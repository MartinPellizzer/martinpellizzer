Nauclea gilletii var. gilletii

Nauclea gilletii var. gilletii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods containing seeds, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

It has a milky sap that can be toxic, which contributes to its common name.
