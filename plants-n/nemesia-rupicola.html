Nemesia rupicola

Nemesia rupicola, commonly known as the rock nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a low-growing perennial that is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Nemesia rupicola is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
