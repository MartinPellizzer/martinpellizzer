Nasturtium alexandrinum

Nasturtium alexandrinum, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is an annual flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, which are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is known for its peppery-tasting seeds and leaves, which are sometimes used in culinary applications. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium alexandrinum is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and bees.
