Neurocarpum argenteum

Neurocarpum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf neurocarpum, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its unique leaf coloration and its ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
