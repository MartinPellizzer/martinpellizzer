Notocactus glaucinus

Notocactus glaucinus, commonly known as the blue spine cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive blue-gray coloration due to its dense covering of fine, white hairs. The plant features short, spiny ribs and produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Notocactus glaucinus is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
