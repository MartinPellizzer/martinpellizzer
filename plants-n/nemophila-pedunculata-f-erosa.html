Nemophila pedunculata f. erosa

Nemophila pedunculata f. erosa, commonly known as the erose blue-eyed Mary, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a soft, hairy texture. This variety is distinguished by its erose (notched) leaf margins, which give it a unique appearance.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded gardens.
