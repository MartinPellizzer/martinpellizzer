Neoregelia magdalenae var. teresae

Neoregelia magdalenae var. teresae, commonly known as the Magdalenae Teresae Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Colombia.

It features vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a unique water-holding structure. The plant produces showy, long-lasting inflorescences with bright red bracts that attract pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
