Nasturtium × astylon

Nasturtium × astylon, commonly known as the hybrid nasturtium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a cross between *Tropaeolum majus* and *Tropaeolum tricolor* and is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright orange, red, or yellow blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage is often used in culinary applications due to its peppery flavor, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Nasturtium × astylon is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.
