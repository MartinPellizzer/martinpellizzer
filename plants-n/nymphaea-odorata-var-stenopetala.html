Nymphaea odorata var. stenopetala

Nymphaea odorata var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct, fragrant scent. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life.

The narrow-petaled variety is distinguished by its more compact flower structure compared to other water lily species.
