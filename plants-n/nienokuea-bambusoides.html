Nienokuea bambusoides

Nienokuea bambusoides, commonly known as the bamboo-like cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem that resembles bamboo, covered in a fibrous, leafless sheath. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem. Its reproductive structures include a cone-like inflorescence that bears both male and female flowers.

Nienokuea bambusoides is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appearance and cultural significance.
