Noccaea boeotica

Noccaea boeotica, commonly known as the Boeotian honesty, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing herb with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnately divided leaves and produces small, round seeds in pods. It is often found in calcareous soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Noccaea boeotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
