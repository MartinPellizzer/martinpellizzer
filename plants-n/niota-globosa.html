Niota globosa

Niota globosa, commonly known as the globular niota, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and clusters of small, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are broad and waxy, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate blooms.

Niota globosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
