Nemesia hemiptera

Nemesia hemiptera, commonly known as the velvetleaf nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving the plant a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
