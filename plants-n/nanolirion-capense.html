Nanolirion capense

Nanolirion capense, commonly known as the Cape nanolirion, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense cluster of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale purple during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
