Neottia pantlingii

Neottia pantlingii, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a short, underground rhizome and a single, slender, leafless stem. Neottia pantlingii thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing on the forest floor or in swampy areas.
