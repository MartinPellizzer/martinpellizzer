Nidularium longiflorum

Nidularium longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered nidularium, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, elongated inflorescences with bright yellow spadices and small, white flowers. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Nidularium longiflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
