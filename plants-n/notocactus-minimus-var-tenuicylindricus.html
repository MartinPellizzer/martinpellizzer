Notocactus minimus var. tenuicylindricus

Notocactus minimus var. tenuicylindricus, commonly known as the narrow-cylindrical miniature notocactus, is a compact cactus species native to southern Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color, covered with sparse, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well-suited for container gardening due to its small size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
