Nerine flexuosa

Nerine flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible nerine, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive hooded shape. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nerine flexuosa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
