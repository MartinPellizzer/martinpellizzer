Neriandra martiana

Neriandra martiana, commonly known as the Malayan flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Neriandra martiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its vibrant floral display.
