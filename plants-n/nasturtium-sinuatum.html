Nasturtium sinuatum

Nasturtium sinuatum, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers and heart-shaped, edible leaves. The plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables. Its flowers and leaves are often used in culinary applications due to their peppery flavor.

Nasturtium sinuatum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
