Nicipe fissurisedula

Nicipe fissurisedula, commonly known as the fissured leaf nicipe, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its leaves are deeply lobed and exhibit a fissured pattern, which is a key botanical feature. The plant belongs to the family Nicipeaceae and is native to arid regions with high sunlight exposure. It has a succulent stem that stores water, aiding in its survival in harsh environments.

The flowers are small and clustered, typically displaying a range of colors from yellow to red.
