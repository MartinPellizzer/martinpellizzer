Nigritella fragrans var. megastachya

Nigritella fragrans var. megastachya, commonly known as the giant fragrant nigritella, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers and robust growth habit.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its inflorescences consist of clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates for its aesthetic appeal.
