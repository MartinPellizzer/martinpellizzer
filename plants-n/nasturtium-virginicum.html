Nasturtium virginicum

Nasturtium virginicum, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is an annual herb with heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant produces edible seeds that are often used in culinary applications. Its stems and leaves are also edible and have a peppery flavor.

Nasturtium virginicum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and wild areas.
