Nerine venusta var. rosea

Nerine venusta var. rosea, commonly known as the pink nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep rose, often with a white throat. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
