Nama palmeri var. argentea

Nama palmeri var. argentea, commonly known as the silver nama, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant in dry climates.
