Notopleura lateriflora

Notopleura lateriflora, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The flowers are typically pink to red in color and bloom during the rainy season. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
