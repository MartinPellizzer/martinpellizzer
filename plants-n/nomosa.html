Nomosa

Nomosa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Nomosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
