Nemacladus sigmoideus

Nemacladus sigmoideus, commonly known as the zigzag mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of various host trees.

Nemacladus sigmoideus is found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
