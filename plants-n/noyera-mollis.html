Noyera mollis

Noyera mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are soft to the touch and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Noyera mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
