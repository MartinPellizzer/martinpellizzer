Narcissus vitekii

Narcissus vitekii, commonly known as the Viteki narcissus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive cup-like shape at the base.

Narcissus vitekii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
