Notocactus werdermannianus f. ferrugineus

Notocactus werdermannianus f. ferrugineus, commonly known as the rusty-furred notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, rust-colored spines, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
