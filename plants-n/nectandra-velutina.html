Nectandra velutina

Nectandra velutina, commonly known as velvet wood or velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elliptical, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. Nectandra velutina is valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.

It also has medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
