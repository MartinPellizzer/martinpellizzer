Neolepisorus lastii, commonly known as the Lastii Shield Fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen fronds that form a compact, shield-like structure, giving it its common name. Its rhizomes are wiry and form a dense network that helps anchor the plant and absorb moisture from the substrate.

Neolepisorus lastii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
