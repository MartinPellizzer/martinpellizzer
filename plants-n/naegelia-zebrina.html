Naegelia zebrina

Naegelia zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that resemble the pattern of a zebra. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. Naegelia zebrina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

It is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water and pruning to maintain its compact, bushy form.
