Nastus aristatus

Nastus aristatus, commonly known as the spiny nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are ovate and often have a slight pubescence, while the stems are covered in fine spines.

The fruit is a small, globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
