Nephrodium aristulatum

Nephrodium aristulatum, commonly known as the arrowhead fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant features delicate, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy coating. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

Nephrodium aristulatum plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygenation for various aquatic organisms.
