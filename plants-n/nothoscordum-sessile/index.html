Nothoscordum Sessile

Nothoscordum sessile is a unique plant native to the southeastern United States. It is known for its striking yellow flowers and strong garlic-like odor. This species thrives in wet, sunny habitats such as marshes and roadside ditches. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, making it easy to overlook. Despite its pungent smell, it plays an important role in supporting pollinators and local ecosystems.
