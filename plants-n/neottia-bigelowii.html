Neottia bigelowii

The plant lacks true leaves and instead has reduced, scale-like structures called pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually white or pale green and are arranged in a spike, often with a single flower at the apex.

Neottia bigelowii is notable for its mycorrhizal relationship with fungi, which plays a crucial role in its nutrient uptake.
