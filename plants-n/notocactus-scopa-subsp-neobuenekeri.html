Notocactus scopa subsp. neobuenekeri

Notocactus scopa subsp.

neobuenekeri, commonly known as the Neobuenekeri Hedgehog Cactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico. It features a distinctive spiny, segmented stem with a grayish-green coloration.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
