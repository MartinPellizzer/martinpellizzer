Notocactus alacriportanus

Notocactus alacriportanus, commonly known as the spiny leaf cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in sharp, spine-like structures, which provide protection against herbivores and reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in full sun with minimal water, making it well-suited for desert environments.

Due to its unique morphology and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and cactus enthusiasts.
