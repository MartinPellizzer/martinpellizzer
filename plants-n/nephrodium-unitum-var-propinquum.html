Nephrodium unitum var. propinquum

Nephrodium unitum var. propinquum, commonly known as the narrowleaf nephrolepis, is a variant of the nephrolepis fern characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant exhibits a delicate, feathery frond structure with closely spaced pinnae. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores for propagation.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor and tropical gardens.
