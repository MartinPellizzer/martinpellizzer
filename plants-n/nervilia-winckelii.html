Nervilia winckelii

Nervilia winckelii, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish-green leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces intricate, showy flowers in shades of purple and white, often with a distinctive hooded labellum. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Nervilia winckelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
