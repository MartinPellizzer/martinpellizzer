Navarretia micromeria

Navarretia micromeria, commonly known as the dwarf navarretia, is a small, flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family and typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces delicate white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves.
