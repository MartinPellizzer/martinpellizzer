Neophloga linearis var. distachya

Neophloga linearis var. distachya, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves and clusters of bright red, flame-like flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a distinctive floral structure with two stamens.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, moist climates.
