Nemastylis grandiflora

Nemastylis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered nemastylis, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive, elongated corolla. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
