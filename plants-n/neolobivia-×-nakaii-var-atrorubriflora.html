Neolobivia × nakaii var. atrorubriflora

Neolobivia × nakaii var. atrorubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered lobivia, is a hybrid cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique coloration and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
