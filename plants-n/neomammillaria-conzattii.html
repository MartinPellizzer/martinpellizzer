Neomammillaria conzattii

Neomammillaria conzattii, commonly known as the Conzatti's pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and typically produces white or pale pink flowers. The plant has a dense covering of white to yellowish spines, which help protect it from herbivores and intense sunlight. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria conzattii is often cultivated for its unique appearance and hardiness.
