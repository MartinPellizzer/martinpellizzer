Neea mollis

Neea mollis, commonly known as the silky neee, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. Its flowers are small and white, clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round berries that are edible.

Neea mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
