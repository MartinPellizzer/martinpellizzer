Neillia tunkinensis var. bibracteolata

Neillia tunkinensis var. bibracteolata, commonly known as the Tunkin's neillia, is a rare flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with hairy bracts that give it its varietal name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
