Nasturtium terrestre var. semipinnatifidum

Nasturtium terrestre var. semipinnatifidum, commonly known as the broadleaf nasturtium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, semi-lobed leaves that are often variegated with bright yellow and green. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred structure. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value and edible qualities make it a popular choice for gardens and culinary use.
