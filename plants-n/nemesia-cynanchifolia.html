Nemesia cynanchifolia

Nemesia cynanchifolia, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial species that produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy undersides, and its stems are usually covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
