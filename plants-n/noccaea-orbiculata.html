Noccaea orbiculata

Noccaea orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by delicate, papery seed pods. Its leaves are rounded and slightly hairy, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
