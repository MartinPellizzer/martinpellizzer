Notholaena argyrostigma

Notholaena argyrostigma, commonly known as silver-stemmed hookless fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, silvery-gray stems that are covered in fine, scale-like structures. The plant produces small, clustered fronds with dark green, leathery leaves that have a distinctive, narrow shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or wooded areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
