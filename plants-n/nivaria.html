Nivaria

Nivaria, commonly known as the snowbell, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that often display a white or pale blue coloration. The plants typically have opposite, simple leaves that are often hairy or pubescent. They are often found in woodland edges, meadows, and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
