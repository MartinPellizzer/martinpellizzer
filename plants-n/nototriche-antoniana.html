Nototriche antoniana

Nototriche antoniana, commonly known as the Antonian rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
