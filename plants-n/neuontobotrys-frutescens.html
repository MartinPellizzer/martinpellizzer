Neuontobotrys frutescens

Neuontobotrys frutescens, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
