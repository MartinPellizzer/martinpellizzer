Neosasamorpha magnifica

Neosasamorpha magnifica, commonly known as the Royal Flame Tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant red and orange hues, often resembling flames. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Neosasamorpha magnifica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
