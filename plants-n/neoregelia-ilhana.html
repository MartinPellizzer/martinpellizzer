Neoregelia ilhana

Neoregelia ilhana, commonly known as the Ilhena bromeliad, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Neoregelia ilhana is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
