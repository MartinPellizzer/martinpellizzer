Nasturtium Perfoliatum

Nasturtium perfoliatum is a vibrant flowering plant known for its striking red and orange blooms. It thrives in sunny locations and is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value. The plant's unique leaves are perfoliate, meaning they have holes through which the stems pass. It is also edible, with both leaves and flowers used in salads and as a natural dye. This versatile plant attracts pollinators and adds a splash of color to any garden space.
