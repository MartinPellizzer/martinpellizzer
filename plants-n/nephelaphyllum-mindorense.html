Nephelaphyllum mindorense

Nephelaphyllum mindorense, commonly known as the Mindoro mistflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in the early morning. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.
