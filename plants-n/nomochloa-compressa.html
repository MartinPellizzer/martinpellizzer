Nomochloa compressa

Nomochloa compressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf duckweed, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ribbon-like leaves that float on the surface of freshwater bodies. Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
