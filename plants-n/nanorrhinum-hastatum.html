Nanorrhinum hastatum

Nanorrhinum hastatum, commonly known as the narrow-beaked daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruit capsules.
