Nectandra tonii

Nectandra tonii, commonly known as the Malabar ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood and aromatic leaves. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while its fruit is a small, woody capsule containing several seeds.

Nectandra tonii is valued for its durable timber and is also used in traditional medicine.
