Nissolia declinata

Nissolia declinata, commonly known as the hanging nissolia, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping, pendulous inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Nissolia declinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
