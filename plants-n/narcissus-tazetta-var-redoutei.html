Narcissus tazetta var. redoutei

Narcissus tazetta var. redoutei, commonly known as the Redoute narcissus, is a fragrant, bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinct, spicy scent. The plant typically blooms in early spring and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is also valued for its use in floral arrangements and as a cut flower.
