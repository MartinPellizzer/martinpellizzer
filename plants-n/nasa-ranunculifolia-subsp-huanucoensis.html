Nasa ranunculifolia subsp. huanucoensis

Nasa ranunculifolia subsp.

huanucoensis, commonly known as the Huanuco buttercup, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its basal leaves with palmate lobes and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Native to the highlands of Peru, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant has a short flowering period and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
