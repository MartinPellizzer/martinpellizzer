Neuractis

Neuractis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that open in the morning and close at dusk. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that float on the surface of water, while its roots are submerged. Neuractis thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical significance is reflected in its cultural and medicinal uses across various traditional practices.
