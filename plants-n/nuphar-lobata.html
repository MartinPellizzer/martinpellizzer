Nuphar lobata

Nuphar lobata, commonly known as the broadleaf water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to North America.

It features large, floating leaves with a distinctive waxy coating that helps repel water. Its flowers are showy, with yellow petals surrounding a central cluster of stamens. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
