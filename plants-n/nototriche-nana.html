Nototriche nana

Nototriche nana, commonly known as the dwarf nototriche, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale blue.

Nototriche nana is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal or mountainous areas.
