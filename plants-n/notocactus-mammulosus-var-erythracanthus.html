Notocactus mammulosus var. erythracanthus

Notocactus mammulosus var. erythracanthus, commonly known as the red-spined cactus, is a slow-growing, columnar cactus native to Brazil.

It features a ribbed stem with prominent, red-brown spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged exterior. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
