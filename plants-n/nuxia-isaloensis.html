Nuxia isaloensis

Nuxia isaloensis, commonly known as the Isalo nuxia, is a unique plant species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant thrives in the arid and rocky environments of the Isalo Massif. Its botanical features include a woody stem and a distinctive flowering pattern.

Nuxia isaloensis is of interest to botanists due to its adaptation to extreme conditions and its role in the local ecosystem.
