Notocactus spegazzinii var. rubescens

Notocactus spegazzinii var. rubescens, commonly known as the Crimson Thorned Cactus, is a unique cactus species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, spiny stems that form a dense, rounded shape. The plant produces bright orange to red flowers during the summer months, adding vibrant color to its appearance. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

The varietal name "rubescens" refers to its distinctive reddish hue, which is particularly noticeable in the stems and flowers.
