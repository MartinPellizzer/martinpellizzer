Nageia eurhyncha

Nageia eurhyncha, commonly known as the Chinese cycads, is a species of cycad native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
