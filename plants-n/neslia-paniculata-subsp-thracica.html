Neslia paniculata subsp. thracica

Neslia paniculata subsp.

thracica, commonly known as the Thrace panicled neslia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Europe. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

It is a perennial species that produces seeds in elongated, cylindrical capsules.
