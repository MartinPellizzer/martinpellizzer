Nasturtium sphaerocarpum

Nasturtium sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe nasturtium, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, round, orange or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Nasturtium sphaerocarpum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a natural pest repellent in gardens.
