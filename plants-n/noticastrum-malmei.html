Noticastrum malmei

Noticastrum malmei, commonly known as the Malme's acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are encased in pods, a typical feature of the Fabaceae family.

Noticastrum malmei is often found in dry, arid environments and is adapted to thrive in poor soil conditions.
