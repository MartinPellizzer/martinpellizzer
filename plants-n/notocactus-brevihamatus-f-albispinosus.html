Notocactus brevihamatus f. albispinosus

Notocactus brevihamatus f. albispinosus, commonly known as the white-spined short-stemmed cactus, is a unique variant of the Notocactus genus native to South America.

This plant is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and dense, white spines that distinguish it from other species. It produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
