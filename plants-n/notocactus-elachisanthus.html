Notocactus elachisanthus

Notocactus elachisanthus, commonly known as the pale-stemmed cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its stems are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Notocactus elachisanthus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
