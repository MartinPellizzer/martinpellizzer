Nephrolepis exaltata var. exauriculata

Nephrolepis exaltata var. exauriculata, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a tropical fern species characterized by its large, pinnately compound fronds.

It features a robust, creeping rhizome that supports the growth of its long, arching leaves. The fronds are typically dark green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters, making it a striking ornamental plant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its lush foliage and is often used in landscaping and indoor gardening.
