Notoptera curviflora

Notoptera curviflora, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, curving inflorescence and striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a waxy coating, which helps retain moisture.

Notoptera curviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
