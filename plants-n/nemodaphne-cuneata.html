Nemodaphne cuneata

Nemodaphne cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daphne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Thymelaeaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It typically grows as an understory shrub in humid, shaded environments.
