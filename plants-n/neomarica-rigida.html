Neomarica rigida

Neomarica rigida, commonly known as the rigid spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features a robust, rhizomatous root system and produces tall, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a greenish hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Neomarica rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
