Nasturtium nudiusculum

Nasturtium nudiusculum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, vibrant orange or red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is an annual or biennial, often found in moist, shady environments. Its flowers are edible and have a peppery taste, similar to watercress.

Nasturtium nudiusculum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
