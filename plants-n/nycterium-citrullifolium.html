Nycterium citrullifolium

Nycterium citrullifolium, commonly known as the citrullifolium nightshade, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow berry that contains several seeds.
