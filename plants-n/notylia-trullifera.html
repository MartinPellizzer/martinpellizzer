Notylia trullifera

Notylia trullifera, commonly known as the turkey tail orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy leaves and its striking, colorful flower spikes that resemble the tail of a turkey. The plant typically grows in shady, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are borne on long, slender stems and can range in color from white to pink and purple.

Notylia trullifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
