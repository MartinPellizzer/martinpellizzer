Nigritella rubra var. minor

Nigritella rubra var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf red nigritella, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and delicate floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
