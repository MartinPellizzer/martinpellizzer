Nothaphoebe cuneata

Nothaphoebe cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nothaphoebe, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the southeastern United States.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas along streams and in wetlands.
