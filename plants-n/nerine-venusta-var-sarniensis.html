Nerine venusta var. sarniensis

Nerine venusta var. sarniensis, commonly known as the Sarnian bellflower, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
