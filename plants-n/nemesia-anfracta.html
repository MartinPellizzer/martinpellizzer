Nemesia anfracta

Nemesia anfracta, commonly known as the rock nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and profuse display of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage is typically covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Nemesia anfracta is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
