Nasturtium neozelandicum

Nasturtium neozelandicum, commonly known as New Zealand nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge, and its stems are hairy and trailing. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

The plant is also known for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery taste similar to watercress.
