Nothoscordum brevispathum

Nothoscordum brevispathum, commonly known as the narrow-pathum garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in umbels. Its leaves are grass-like and aromatic, similar to garlic or onions.

It is often found in wetlands and moist soils, thriving in humid environments.
