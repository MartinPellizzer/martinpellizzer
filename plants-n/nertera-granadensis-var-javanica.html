Nertera granadensis var. javanica

Nertera granadensis var. javanica, commonly known as the Javanese Nertera, is a compact, trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
