Nardurus lachenalii var. tenuiculum

Nardurus lachenalii var. tenuiculum, commonly known as the slender nardus, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding delicate floral interest. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
