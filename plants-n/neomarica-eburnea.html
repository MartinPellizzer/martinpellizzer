Neomarica eburnea

Neomarica eburnea, commonly known as the white lady orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Neomarica eburnea is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
