Nephrodium nockianum

Nephrodium nockianum, commonly known as the Nockian kidney fern, is a unique species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and covered in fine, silky hairs. Nephrodium nockianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
