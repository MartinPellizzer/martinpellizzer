Narcissus nobilis var. leonensis

Narcissus nobilis var. leonensis, commonly known as the Spanish daffodil, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow with a white or pale green throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This variety is valued for its early blooming habit and ornamental appeal.
