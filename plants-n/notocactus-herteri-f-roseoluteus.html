Notocactus herteri f. roseoluteus

Notocactus herteri f. roseoluteus, commonly known as the Roseolate Notocactus, is a hybrid cactus species originating from South America.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and dense covering of bright pink to red spines, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique coloration and compact growth habit, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
