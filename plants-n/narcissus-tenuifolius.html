Narcissus tenuifolius

Narcissus tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a single, erect tepal that is typically narrower than the surrounding ones. Its leaves are long and narrow, giving it the common name "slender-leaved.".

It is valued in gardens for its early spring bloom and hardy nature.
