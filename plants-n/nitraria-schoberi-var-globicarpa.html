Nitraria schoberi var. globicarpa

Nitraria schoberi var. globicarpa, commonly known as the globular nitraria, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, spherical fruits that are typically yellow or red when mature. The plant has small, scale-like leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to harsh environments, with deep roots that allow it to survive in poor soil conditions.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.
