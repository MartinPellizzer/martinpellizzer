Neoschumannia kamerunensis

Neoschumannia kamerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroon grassveld cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, compound leaves. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is adapted to the tropical and subtropical climates of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
