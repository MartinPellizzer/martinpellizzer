Nectandra squarrosa var. pyrifolia

Nectandra squarrosa var. pyrifolia, commonly known as the scarlet flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a waxy coating.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
