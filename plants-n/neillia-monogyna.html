Neillia monogyna

Neillia monogyna, commonly known as the single-seed flax, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Neillia monogyna is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
