Nassauvia argyrophylla

Nassauvia argyrophylla, commonly known as silverleaf nassauvia, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.
