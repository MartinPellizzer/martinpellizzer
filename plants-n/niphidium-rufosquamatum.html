Niphidium rufosquamatum

Niphidium rufosquamatum, commonly known as the red-squamated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, hairy stems and vibrant, often red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in fine, reddish hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Niphidium rufosquamatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
