Notocactus erinaceus var. spinosior

Notocactus erinaceus var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its dense, spiny appearance and cylindrical stems. The plant features a unique arrangement of areoles, from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

The plant is well adapted to arid environments, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and cactus collections.
