Nephrolepis biserrata var. rufescens

Nephrolepis biserrata var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed sword fern, is a varietal form of the common nephrolepis.

It is a fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features long, arching fronds with a distinctive reddish hue on the undersides of its leaves. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with small, serrated pinnae that give it a finely divided appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
