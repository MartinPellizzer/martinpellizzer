Nerine masoniorum

Nerine masoniorum, commonly known as the Mason's nerine, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are evergreen and persist through the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
