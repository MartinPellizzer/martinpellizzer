Nidularium laurentii var. immaculatum

Nidularium laurentii var. immaculatum, commonly known as the White-veined Sword Fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It features elongated, arching fronds with a distinctive white midrib and serrated margins, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish. Its rhizomes form dense mats, allowing it to spread slowly and colonize tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in both indoor and outdoor settings with proper care.
