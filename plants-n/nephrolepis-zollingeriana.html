Nephrolepis zollingeriana

Nephrolepis zollingeriana, commonly known as the Zollinger's sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically arching or drooping. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
