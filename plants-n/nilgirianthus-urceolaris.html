Nilgirianthus urceolaris

Nilgirianthus urceolaris, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
