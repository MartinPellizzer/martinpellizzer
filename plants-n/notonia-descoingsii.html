Notonia descoingsii

Notonia descoingsii, commonly known as the Desert Honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with its deep root system and reduced leaf surface. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are dispersed by animals.

Notonia descoingsii is often found in rocky soils and is an important species in its native ecosystem.
