Niphogeton glaucescens

Niphogeton glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green grass.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and blue-green foliage. Niphogeton glaucescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
