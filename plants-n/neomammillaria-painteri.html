Neomammillaria painteri

Neomammillaria painteri, commonly known as the Painter's Hedgehog Cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, globular stem covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
