Notocactus miniatispinus

Notocactus miniatispinus, commonly known as the miniature spiny cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in small, sharp spines, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
