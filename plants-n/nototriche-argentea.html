Nototriche argentea

Nototriche argentea, commonly known as silver nototriche, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white hairs covering its leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and horticultural contexts.
