Neoalsomitra schefferiana subsp. podagrica

Neoalsomitra schefferiana subsp.

podagrica, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. It has a fibrous root system and features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair.
