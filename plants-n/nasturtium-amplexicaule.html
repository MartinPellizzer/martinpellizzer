Nasturtium amplexicaule

Nasturtium amplexicaule, commonly known as the climbing nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Nasturtium amplexicaule is also valued for its edible leaves and flowers, which have a peppery flavor often used in culinary applications.
