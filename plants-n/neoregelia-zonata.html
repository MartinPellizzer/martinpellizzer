Neoregelia zonata

Neoregelia zonata, commonly known as the Zonate Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with distinct zonation patterns. The plant features a rosette-shaped leaf structure that collects water, supporting its epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are typically red or orange, emerging from the center of the rosette, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Neoregelia zonata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
