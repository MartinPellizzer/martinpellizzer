Nectandra acutangula

Nectandra acutangula, commonly known as the sharp-leaved nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Nectandra acutangula is valued for its essential oils and has traditional uses in local medicine.
