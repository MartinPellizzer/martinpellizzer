Nunnezharia radicalis

Nunnezharia radicalis, commonly known as the deep-rooted fern, is a perennial plant characterized by its robust, fibrous root system that allows it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are pollinated by wind, a common trait among its relatives. Nunnezharia radicalis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
