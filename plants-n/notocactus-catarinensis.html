Notocactus catarinensis

Notocactus catarinensis, commonly known as the Catarinensis Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny areoles. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in the early morning.

Notocactus catarinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
