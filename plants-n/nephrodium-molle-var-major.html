Nephrodium molle var. major

Nephrodium molle var. major, commonly known as the soft kidney fern, is a variant of the soft kidney fern native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in humid climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
