Nigritella megastachya

Nigritella megastachya, commonly known as the giant star grass, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small white to pale blue flowers arranged in a star-like pattern. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a slight waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Nigritella megastachya is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
