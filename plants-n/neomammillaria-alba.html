Neomammillaria alba

Neomammillaria alba, commonly known as the white mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria alba is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique and attractive appearance.
