Neorautanenia orbicularis

Neorautanenia orbicularis, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to taro.

Neorautanenia orbicularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses in traditional cuisines.
