Neomammillaria zephyranthoides

Neomammillaria zephyranthoides, commonly known as the Zephyranthus Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
