Notocactus meonacanthus

Notocactus meonacanthus, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, pale green stems and sparse, spiny areoles. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and nocturnal flowering habit, it is a popular choice for collectors and desert gardeners.
