Nephrodium pulchrum

Nephrodium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful kidney fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, lush fronds that resemble the shape of a kidney, giving it its scientific name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often a deep green, with a slightly glossy texture that enhances its visual appeal.

Nephrodium pulchrum is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
