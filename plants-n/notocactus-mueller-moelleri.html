Notocactus mueller-moelleri

Notocactus mueller-moelleri, commonly known as the Müller-Möller notocactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in collections.
