Nymphaea × intermedia var. polypetala

Nymphaea × intermedia var. polypetala, commonly known as the pink lotus, is a hybrid water lily native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink flowers that bloom on long, upright stalks above the water surface. The leaves are broad, floating, and typically a deep green with a waxy texture. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in ponds and water gardens.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with full sun exposure.
