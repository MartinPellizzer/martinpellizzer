Nephrodium rubidum

Nephrodium rubidum, commonly known as the crimson kidney fern, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, kidney-shaped fronds that display a vibrant red hue on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Nephrodium rubidum plays a role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various small insects and animals.
