Nepeta henanensis

Nepeta henanensis, commonly known as Henan catmint, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple and are arranged in whorls along the stems.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
