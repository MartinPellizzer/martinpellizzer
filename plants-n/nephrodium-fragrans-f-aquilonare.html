Nephrodium fragrans f. aquilonare

Nephrodium fragrans f. aquilonare, commonly known as the aquatic nephroid, is a unique aquatic plant characterized by its delicate, floating leaves and specialized reproductive structures.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically submerged or partially submerged, aiding in pollination by aquatic insects. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it can anchor itself to submerged surfaces.
