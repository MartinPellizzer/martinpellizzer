Neolitsea chunii var. annamensis

Neolitsea chunii var. annamensis, commonly known as the Annam leafy ash or Annam sweet ash, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Laos, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, berry-like fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
