Nasturtium millefolium

Nasturtium millefolium, commonly known as leaf lettuce, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves that resemble those of lettuce, and its bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in temperate climates. It is often grown as an ornamental plant and can also be used in salads due to its peppery flavor.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both gardeners and culinary enthusiasts.
