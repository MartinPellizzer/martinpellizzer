Nephrodium resiniferum

Nephrodium resiniferum, commonly known as the resinous nephroid, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped leaves.

It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant is notable for producing a resinous substance that has been historically used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features include a short stem and broad, waxy leaves that help in water retention.

The plant's ability to secrete resin makes it an interesting subject for botanical and pharmacological study.
