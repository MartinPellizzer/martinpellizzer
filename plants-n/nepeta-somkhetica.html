Nepeta somkhetica

Nepeta somkhetica, commonly known as Thai catnip, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered blue or purple flowers. It is often grown for its fragrant foliage and as a companion plant for cats due to its nepetalactone content.

Nepeta somkhetica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
