Nolana intonsa

Nolana intonsa, commonly known as the Chilean flax or silver flax, is a flowering plant native to Chile.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal areas with sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Nolana intonsa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
