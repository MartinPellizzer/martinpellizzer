Nama palmeri

Nama palmeri, commonly known as the Namib Desert lichen, is a unique plant that thrives in the extreme arid conditions of the Namib Desert.

It is a type of lichen, a symbiotic organism composed of a fungus and a green alga or cyanobacterium. This plant is adapted to survive with minimal water, relying on fog and dew for moisture. Its structure is typically crust-like, allowing it to adhere tightly to surfaces and reduce water loss.

Nama palmeri plays a crucial role in the ecosystem by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.
