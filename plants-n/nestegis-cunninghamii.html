Nestegis cunninghamii

Nestegis cunninghamii, commonly known as the mountain pine or Cunningham's pine, is a species of conifer native to the highlands of New Zealand.

It is a tall, straight tree with a conical crown and scaly, dark brown bark. The leaves are needle-like, arranged in spirals, and are typically a bluish-green color. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This plant is well adapted to the cool, moist climates of its native habitat.
