Neocabreria

Neocabreria, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
