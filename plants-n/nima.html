Nima

Nima, commonly known as the watercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, edible leaves that are often used in salads or as a garnish. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as streams, ponds, and damp soil. Its rapid growth and high nutrient content make it a valuable aquatic vegetable.

Nima is also noted for its peppery flavor and high levels of vitamins and minerals.
