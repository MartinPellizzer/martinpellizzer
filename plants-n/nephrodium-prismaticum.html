Nephrodium prismaticum

Nephrodium prismaticum, commonly known as the prismatic nephroid, is a rare and striking plant characterized by its unique, geometric leaf patterns.

It belongs to the family Nephroidaceae and is native to specific regions with high humidity and acidic soils. The plant's leaves exhibit a prismatic effect due to microscopic structures that refract light, creating a shimmering appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rarity and distinctive appearance, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
