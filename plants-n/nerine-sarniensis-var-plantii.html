Nerine sarniensis var. plantii

Nerine sarniensis var. plantii, commonly known as the Persian lily or the autumn lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This perennial is valued for its late summer to early autumn blooming period, offering a prolonged display of color in the garden.
