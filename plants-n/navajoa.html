Navajoa

Navajoa, commonly known as the Navajoan or Navajoan daisy, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Navajoa is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid environments.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance to Indigenous communities.
