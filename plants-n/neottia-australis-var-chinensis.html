Neottia australis var. chinensis

Neottia australis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with two opposite leaves, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers. It is characterized by its lack of true roots and its ability to absorb nutrients through its leaves.

This variety is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.
