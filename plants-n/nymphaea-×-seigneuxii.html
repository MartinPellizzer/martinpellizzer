Nymphaea × seigneuxii

Nymphaea × seigneuxii, commonly known as the Seigneux's water lily, is a hybrid aquatic plant belonging to the Nymphaeaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers are usually pink to reddish in color, with a prominent yellow center.

Nymphaea × seigneuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in water gardens.
