Nasturtium henryi

Nasturtium henryi, commonly known as Henry's nasturtium, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces edible, peppery-tasting seeds, similar to those of other nasturtium species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary gardens.

Nasturtium henryi is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and hoverflies.
