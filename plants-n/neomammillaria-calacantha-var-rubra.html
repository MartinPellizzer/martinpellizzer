Neomammillaria calacantha var. rubra

Neomammillaria calacantha var. rubra, commonly known as the red-tipped mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale pink spines. The plant produces showy, red or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fleshy stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
