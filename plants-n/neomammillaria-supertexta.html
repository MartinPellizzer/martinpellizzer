Neomammillaria supertexta

Neomammillaria supertexta, commonly known as the Mexican night-blooming cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and flowering habit make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
