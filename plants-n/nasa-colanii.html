Nasa colanii

Nasa colanii, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant blooms that open at night. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Nasa colanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal beauty.
