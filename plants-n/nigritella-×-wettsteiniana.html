Nigritella × wettsteiniana

Nigritella × wettsteiniana, commonly known as the hybrid sweet pepper, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between *Nigritella acutifolia* and *Capsicum wettsteinianum*.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, bell-shaped fruits that range in color from green to red. The plant typically thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate regions. Its foliage is dark green and dense, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This hybrid is valued for both its culinary uses and its role in botanical research due to its genetic diversity.
