Neoporteria paucicostata var. floccosa

Neoporteria paucicostata var. floccosa, commonly known as the woolly neoporteria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique texture, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
