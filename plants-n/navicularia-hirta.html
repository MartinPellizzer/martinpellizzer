Navicularia hirta

Navicularia hirta, commonly known as the hairy navicular, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
