Nintooa longiflora

Nintooa longiflora, commonly known as the long-flower nintooa, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale yellow.

Nintooa longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
