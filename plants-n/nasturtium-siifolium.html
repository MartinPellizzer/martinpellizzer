Nasturtium siifolium

Nasturtium siifolium, commonly known as the leafy nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant is known for its peppery taste, which comes from compounds like glucosinolates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium siifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
