Nania nitida

Nania nitida, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid environments and has a milky sap that is toxic to many animals.
