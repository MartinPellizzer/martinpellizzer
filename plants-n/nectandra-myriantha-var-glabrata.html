Nectandra myriantha var. glabrata

Nectandra myriantha var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves that are usually glabrous on both surfaces. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elongated, woody fruits containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its essential oils and has traditional uses in local medicine and perfumery.
