Nidularium pulverulentum

Nidularium pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty nest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, pale green to brownish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its unique flower structure resembles a nest, giving it its common name.

Nidularium pulverulentum is noted for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
