Narcissus × rogendorfii

Narcissus × rogendorfii, commonly known as the Dutch hybrid narcissus, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow, with a distinctive cup-shaped corona. The plant is native to the Netherlands and was developed through crossbreeding of various narcissus species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often blooming in early spring.

This ornamental plant is prized for its fragrance and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
