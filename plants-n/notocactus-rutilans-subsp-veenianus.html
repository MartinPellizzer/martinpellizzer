Notocactus rutilans subsp. veenianus

Notocactus rutilans subsp.

veenianus, commonly known as the Veenian sunburst cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for arid environments due to its ability to store water in its thick stems.

The subspecies is notable for its unique coloration and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
