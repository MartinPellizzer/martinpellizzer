Nageia chilina

It belongs to the family Araucariaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces cones that are typically clustered at the ends of its branches. It thrives in temperate rainforests and is adapted to moist, well-drained soils.
