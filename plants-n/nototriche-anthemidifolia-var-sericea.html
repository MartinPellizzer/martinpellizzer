Nototriche anthemidifolia var. sericea

Nototriche anthemidifolia var. sericea, commonly known as the silky nototriche, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving the plant its "sericea" designation, which refers to its silky texture. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
