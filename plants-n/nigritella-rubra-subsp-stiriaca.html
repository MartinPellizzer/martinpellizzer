Nigritella rubra subsp. stiriaca

Nigritella rubra subsp.

stiriaca, commonly known as the Stiriaca bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.
