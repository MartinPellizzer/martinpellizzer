Necramium gigantophyllum

Necramium gigantophyllum, commonly known as the giant corpse plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that resembles a funeral shroud, hence its common name. The plant features a thick, waxy stem and broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a sought-after specimen in botanical collections.
