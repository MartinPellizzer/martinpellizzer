Nasturtium cryptanthum

Nasturtium cryptanthum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, butterfly-shaped yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that are often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a slightly peppery taste.

Nasturtium cryptanthum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and edible plants.
