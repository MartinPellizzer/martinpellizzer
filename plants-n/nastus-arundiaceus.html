Nastus arundiaceus

Nastus arundiaceus, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in starch and are used in various culinary applications. Its rhizomes are often harvested for their nutritional value and versatility in cooking.

Nastus arundiaceus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for both its ornamental and edible qualities.
