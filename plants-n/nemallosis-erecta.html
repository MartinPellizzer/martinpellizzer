Nemallosis erecta

Nemallosis erecta, commonly known as the upright nemallosis, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
