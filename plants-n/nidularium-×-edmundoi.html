Nidularium × edmundoi

Nidularium × edmundoi, commonly known as the hybrid spider plant, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Nidularium humile and Nidularium macrophyllum.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, followed by red berries that contain the seeds.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
