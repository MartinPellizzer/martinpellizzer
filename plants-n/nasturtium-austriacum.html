Nasturtium austriacum

Nasturtium austriacum, commonly known as Austrian watercress, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers and edible leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are peppery and can be used in salads or as a seasoning.

Nasturtium austriacum is also known for its ability to purify water and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
