Notonia subulata

Notonia subulata, commonly known as the slender leaf notonia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Notonia subulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
