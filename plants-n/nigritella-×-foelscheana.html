Nigritella × foelscheana

Nigritella × foelscheana, commonly known as the hybrid nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Nigritella × foelscheana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
