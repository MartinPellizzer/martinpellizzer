Nama hispida var. tenuis

Nama hispida var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in small, white, waxy tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are followed by small, round, green fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained sandy soils.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
