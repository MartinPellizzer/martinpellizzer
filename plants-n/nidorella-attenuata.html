Nidorella attenuata

Nidorella attenuata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among horticulturists.

Nidorella attenuata plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
