Nephelephyllum

Nephelephyllum, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a unique plant with striking botanical features.

It belongs to the family Nephelephyllaceae and is characterized by its translucent, waxy leaves that resemble ghostly apparitions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale blue, adding to its ethereal appearance. Nephelephyllum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

Its rare occurrence and unusual morphology make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
