Notholaena marantae subsp. subcordata

Notholaena marantae subsp.

subcordata, commonly known as the subcordate notholaena, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental and decorative plants.

The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This subspecies is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
