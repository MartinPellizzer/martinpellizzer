Narcissus × abilioi var. milagrosus

Narcissus × abilioi var. milagrosus, commonly known as the Milagros narcissus, is a hybrid bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive white center and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing clusters of 5-7 perianth segments. Native to the Iberian Peninsula, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its beauty and unique scent.
