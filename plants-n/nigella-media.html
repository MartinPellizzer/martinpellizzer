Nigella media

Nigella media, commonly known as the common sesame, is an annual herb belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, black, sesame-like seeds that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to gardens and a source of traditional remedies.
