Nama serpylloides

Nama serpylloides, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species adapted to dry conditions.

Nama serpylloides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
