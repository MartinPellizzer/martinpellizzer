Nectandra minima

Nectandra minima, commonly known as the small nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

Nectandra minima is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
