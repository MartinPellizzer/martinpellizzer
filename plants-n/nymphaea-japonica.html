Nymphaea japonica

Nymphaea japonica, commonly known as the Japanese water lily, is a aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features striking white to pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Its roots anchor in the muddy substrate at the bottom of ponds and lakes.

Nymphaea japonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in aquatic habitats.
