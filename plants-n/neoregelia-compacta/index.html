Neoregelia Compacta

Neoregelia compacta is a striking bromeliad known for its vibrant, colorful foliage that adds a tropical flair to any indoor space. Native to the rainforests of South America, this plant thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its rosette-shaped leaves form a central cup that can hold water, making it a unique and low-maintenance houseplant. Easy to care for, it's popular among plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and resilience. Whether placed on a windowsill or in a shaded corner, Neoregelia compacta brings a touch of nature indoors.
