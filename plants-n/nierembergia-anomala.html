Nierembergia anomala

Nierembergia anomala, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nierembergia anomala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
