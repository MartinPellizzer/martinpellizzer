Neoregelia coimbrae

Neoregelia coimbrae, commonly known as the Coimbra's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports aquatic life. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.

Neoregelia coimbrae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.
