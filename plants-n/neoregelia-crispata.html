Neoregelia crispata

Neoregelia crispata, commonly known as the Crimson Vase or the Red Vase, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the central inflorescence, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant features rosettes of fleshy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy, silver-green coating. Neoregelia crispata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush foliage.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
