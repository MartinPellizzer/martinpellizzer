Nuxia gracilis

Nuxia gracilis, commonly known as the slender nuxia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

Nuxia gracilis is often found in fynbos vegetation and is adapted to the region's Mediterranean climate.
