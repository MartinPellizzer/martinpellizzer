Nectandra bahiana

Nectandra bahiana, commonly known as Brazilian sandarac, is a tropical tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by elliptical, woody fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth upper surface.

Nectandra bahiana is valued for its durable timber and has been used traditionally in various applications.
