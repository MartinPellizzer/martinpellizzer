Neorthosis tigrina

Neorthosis tigrina, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, striped flowers that resemble the pattern of a tiger's coat. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with intricate patterns that aid in attracting pollinators.

Neorthosis tigrina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
