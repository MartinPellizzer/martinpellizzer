Nesaea minima

Nesaea minima, commonly known as the small nesaea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale blue flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Nesaea minima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its compact size and subtle beauty.
