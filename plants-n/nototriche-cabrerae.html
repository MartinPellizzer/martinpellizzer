Nototriche cabrerae

Nototriche cabrerae, commonly known as the Cabrera's nototriche, is a species of flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

The flowers are small and yellow, often appearing in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.
