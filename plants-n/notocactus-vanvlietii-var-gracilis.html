Notocactus vanvlietii var. gracilis

Notocactus vanvlietii var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are modified into spines, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

This variety is highly sought after by collectors due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
