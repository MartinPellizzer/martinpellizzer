Nasa rugosa subsp. gracilipes

Nasa rugosa subsp.

gracilipes, commonly known as the slender nasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or violet.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
