Nesynstylis undulata

Nesynstylis undulata, commonly known as the undulating swamp orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific wetland habitats in Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale green or white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or marshy environments. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
