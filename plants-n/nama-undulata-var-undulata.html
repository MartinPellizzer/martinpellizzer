Nama undulata var. undulata

Nama undulata var. undulata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
