Nothoscordum hirtellum

Nothoscordum hirtellum, commonly known as the hairy garlic.

It is a flowering plant in the sunflower family, native to the southeastern United States. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
