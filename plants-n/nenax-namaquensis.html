Nenax namaquensis

Nenax namaquensis, commonly known as theNamaqua starfish, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Nenax namaquensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
