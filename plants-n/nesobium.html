Nesobium

Nesobium, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. They typically grow as compact shrubs with upright stems and are often cultivated as ornamental plants. Nesobium species are adapted to warm, humid climates and thrive in well-drained soil.

Their botanical features make them popular in both garden settings and as houseplants.
