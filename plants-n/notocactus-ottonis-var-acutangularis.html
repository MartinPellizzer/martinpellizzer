Notocactus ottonis var. acutangularis

Notocactus ottonis var. acutangularis, commonly known as the angular notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and often have a spiny appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique shape and ornamental value in succulent collections.
