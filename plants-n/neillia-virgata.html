Neillia virgata

Neillia virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-green foliage and daisy-like white flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
