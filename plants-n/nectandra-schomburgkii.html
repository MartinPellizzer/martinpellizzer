Nectandra schomburgkii

Nectandra schomburgkii, commonly known as the yellow sand-wood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood and lanceolate leaves.
