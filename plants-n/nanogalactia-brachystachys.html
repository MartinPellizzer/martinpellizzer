Nanogalactia brachystachys

Nanogalactia brachystachys, commonly known as the small-stalked galactia, is a small shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
