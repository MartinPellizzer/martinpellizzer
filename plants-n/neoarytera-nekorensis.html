Neoarytera nekorensis

Neoarytera nekorensis, commonly known as the Nekora Tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces dark blue, fleshy berries that are edible and contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
