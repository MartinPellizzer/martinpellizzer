Nhandiroba albiflora

Nhandiroba albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered nhandiroba, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and clustering growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Nhandiroba albiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
