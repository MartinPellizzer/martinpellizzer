Nonea intermedia

Nonea intermedia, commonly known as the silverleaf nonea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Nonea intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
