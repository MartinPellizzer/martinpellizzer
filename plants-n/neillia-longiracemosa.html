Neillia longiracemosa

Neillia longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Neillia longiracemosa thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in tropical and subtropical climates.
