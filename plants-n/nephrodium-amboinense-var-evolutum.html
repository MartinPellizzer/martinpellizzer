Nephrodium amboinense var. evolutum

Nephrodium amboinense var. evolutum, commonly known as the Amboina kidney fern, is a rare and unique fern species native to the Indonesian island of Ambon.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are often curled or rolled when young. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a short, sturdy rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and rarity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
