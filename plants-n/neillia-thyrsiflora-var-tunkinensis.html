Neillia thyrsiflora var. tunkinensis

Neillia thyrsiflora var. tunkinensis, commonly known as the Tunkin's thyrse-flowered neillia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and are arranged oppositely along the stems. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and delicate floral displays.
