Nicolaia grandiligulata

Nicolaia grandiligulata, commonly known as the giant lily, is a striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a tall, upright stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its botanical features include a complex inflorescence with multiple bracts and a distinct, elongated ovary.

Nicolaia grandiligulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its dramatic appearance.
