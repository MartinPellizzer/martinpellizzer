Nertera granadensis var. tetrasperma

Nertera granadensis var. tetrasperma, commonly known as the Four-Seed Nerve Plant, is a compact, trailing perennial native to Madagascar.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, followed by four small, round seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or as a tabletop plant.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a favored ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
