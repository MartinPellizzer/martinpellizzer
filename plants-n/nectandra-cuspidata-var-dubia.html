Nectandra cuspidata var. dubia

Nectandra cuspidata var. dubia, commonly known as the dwarf nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
