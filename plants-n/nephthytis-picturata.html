Nephthytis picturata

Nephthytis picturata, commonly known as the Variegated ZZ Plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, dark green, glossy leaves that often have striking white or yellow variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to store water efficiently, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Nephthytis picturata is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and functional houseplant.
