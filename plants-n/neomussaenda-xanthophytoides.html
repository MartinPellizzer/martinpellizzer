Neomussaenda xanthophytoides

Neomussaenda xanthophytoides, commonly known as the golden leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

Neomussaenda xanthophytoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to various light conditions.
