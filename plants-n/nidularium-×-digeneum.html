Nidularium × digeneum

Nidularium × digeneum, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a rare and intriguing member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Nidularium digeneum and Nidularium × alatum, resulting in unique floral characteristics. The plant typically produces small, spider-like flowers with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
