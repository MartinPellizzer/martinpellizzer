Nama erecta

Nama erecta, commonly known as the erect nama, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Nama erecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
