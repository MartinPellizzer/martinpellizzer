Notocactus neobuenekeri

Notocactus neobuenekeri, commonly known as the Neobuenekeri Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive rib structure and spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters, with a dense, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the ribs during the warmer months.

This cactus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
