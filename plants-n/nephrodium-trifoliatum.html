Nephrodium trifoliatum

It is characterized by its trifoliate (three-part) fronds, which are typically divided into three lanceolate segments. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Nephrodium trifoliatum is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
