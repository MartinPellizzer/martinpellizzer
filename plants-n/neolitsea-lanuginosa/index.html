Neolitsea Lanuginosa

Neolitsea lanuginosa is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China and Vietnam. It is known for its delicate, fluffy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant's leaves are elliptical and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a soft and unique appearance. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in forest understories. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
