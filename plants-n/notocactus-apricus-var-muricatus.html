Notocactus apricus var. muricatus

Notocactus apricus var. muricatus, commonly known as the Spiny Sun Cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in sharp, spiny tubercles, giving it a distinctive, rugged appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
