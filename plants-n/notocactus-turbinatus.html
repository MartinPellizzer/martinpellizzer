Notocactus turbinatus

Notocactus turbinatus, commonly known as the spiral cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of cylindrical stems, which give it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Notocactus turbinatus is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
