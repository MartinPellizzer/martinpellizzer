Nephrolepis lanceolata

Nephrolepis lanceolata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features feathery, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with horizontal underground stems that produce new shoots. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Nephrolepis lanceolata is well-suited for shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
