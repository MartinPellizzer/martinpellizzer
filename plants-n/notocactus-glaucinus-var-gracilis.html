Notocactus glaucinus var. gracilis

Notocactus glaucinus var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender globose cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
