Nerine laticoma subsp. huttoniae

Nerine laticoma subsp.

huttoniae, commonly known as the Hutton's nerine, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa. It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive white throat.

The plant produces clusters of narrow, strap-like leaves that are evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in late summer to early autumn.
