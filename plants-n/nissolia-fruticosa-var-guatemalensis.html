Nissolia fruticosa var. guatemalensis

Nissolia fruticosa var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan nissolia, is a shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, and it thrives in moist, tropical environments.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
