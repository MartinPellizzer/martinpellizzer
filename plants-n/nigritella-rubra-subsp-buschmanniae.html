Nigritella rubra subsp. buschmanniae

Nigritella rubra subsp.

buschmanniae, commonly known as the Buschmann's red nigritella, is a member of the Acanthaceae family. It is characterized by its striking red flowers and lance-shaped leaves.

Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller flowers and more compact growth habit compared to the typical form.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
