Nonatelia sechellarum

Nonatelia sechellarum, commonly known as the Seychelles orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Seychelles Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or in rocky crevices. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and limited genetic diversity, making conservation efforts crucial for its preservation.

Nonatelia sechellarum plays an important ecological role in its native ecosystem, contributing to local biodiversity.
