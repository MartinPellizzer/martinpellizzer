Nasturtium × ligerinum

Nasturtium × ligerinum, commonly known as the hybrid nasturtium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is a cross between *Tropaeolum majus* and *Tropaeolum tricolor*, resulting in a plant with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, while the flowers are typically borne in clusters at the ends of stems. This plant is known for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery, spicy flavor.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary gardens.
