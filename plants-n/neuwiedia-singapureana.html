Neuwiedia singapureana

Neuwiedia singapureana, commonly known as the Singapore flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its foliage. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.

Neuwiedia singapureana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
