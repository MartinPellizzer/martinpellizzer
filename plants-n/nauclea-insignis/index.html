Nauclea Insignis

Velvetleaf nauclea, scientifically known as Nauclea insignis, is a striking tropical shrub native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia. Its dark green, velvety leaves and showy pink or red flowers make it a standout in both natural and cultivated settings. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests and disturbed areas. While it is valued for its ornamental appeal, it also holds cultural and medicinal significance in many regions. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Nauclea insignis is increasingly threatened, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
