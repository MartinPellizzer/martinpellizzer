Nilgirianthus decurrens

Nilgirianthus decurrens, commonly known as the Nilgiri aster, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and purple. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in alpine meadows.

Nilgirianthus decurrens plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
