Notocactus ottoianus

Notocactus ottoianus, commonly known as the Otto's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
