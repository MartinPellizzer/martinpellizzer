Nuxia saligna

Nuxia saligna, commonly known as the silver tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray bark and oval-shaped leaves that turn red in autumn. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, oval berries. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Nuxia saligna is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
