Niedenzua cordata

Niedenzua cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to yams.

Niedenzua cordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
