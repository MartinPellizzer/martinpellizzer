Nectandra cuneatocordata

Nectandra cuneatocordata, commonly known as the broad-heart or heart-shaped wood.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in furniture and construction.
