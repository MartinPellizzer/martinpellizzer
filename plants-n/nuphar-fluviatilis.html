Nuphar fluviatilis

Nuphar fluviatilis, commonly known as the river water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It has broad, floating leaves that are usually green with a waxy surface, and its flowers are large, showy, and typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, dark brown fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Nuphar fluviatilis plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
