Nepenthes × ratcliffiana

Nepenthes × ratcliffiana, commonly known as the hybrid pitcher plant, is a tropical carnivorous plant native to Borneo.

It is a natural hybrid between Nepenthes lowii and Nepenthes rafflesiana, characterized by its large, waxy pitcher-shaped traps. The plant's leaves form elongated stems that can grow up to several meters in height. Its pitchers are typically green or red, with a distinctive lid that helps retain prey.

Nepenthes × ratcliffiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high light intensity for optimal growth.
