Nephrolepis genyi

Nephrolepis genyi, commonly known as the Genyi shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered into sori covered by a protective indusium. Nephrolepis genyi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural collections.
