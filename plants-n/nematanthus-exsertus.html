Nematanthus exsertus

Nematanthus exsertus, commonly known as the Crimson Trumpet Plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is part of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Nematanthus exsertus is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
