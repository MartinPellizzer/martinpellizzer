Narcissus Mion

Daffodil, scientifically known as Narcissus mion, is a vibrant spring flower celebrated for its bright yellow petals and elegant form. Native to Europe and western Asia, it has become a symbol of renewal and hope in many cultures. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its resilience in various climates. Its blooming season typically begins in late winter or early spring, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. With its simple yet striking appearance, the daffodil has inspired artists, poets, and gardeners for centuries.
