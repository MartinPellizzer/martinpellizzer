Notosceptrum brachystachyum

Notosceptrum brachystachyum, commonly known as the short-stalked sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and succulent leaves that help it conserve water. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

Notosceptrum brachystachyum plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert organisms.
