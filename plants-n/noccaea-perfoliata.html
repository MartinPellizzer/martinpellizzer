Noccaea perfoliata

Noccaea perfoliata, commonly known as the leafy spurge, is a flowering plant native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and stems that bear small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and arid environments. It has a deep taproot system that allows it to survive in poor soil conditions.

Noccaea perfoliata is also noted for its medicinal properties, with various compounds used in traditional herbal remedies.
