Nicotiana ybarrensis

Nicotiana ybarrensis, commonly known as the Ybarren's tobacco, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its ornamental, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

It is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of nicotine.
