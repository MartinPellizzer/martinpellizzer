Nuttallia multiflora

Nuttallia multiflora, commonly known as the Florida nutmeg, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in wetlands and swampy areas. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains aromatic, nutmeg-like seeds.

Nuttallia multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland habitats.
