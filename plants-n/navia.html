Navia

Navia, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a bulbous base. Navia thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements contribute to its ornamental value.
