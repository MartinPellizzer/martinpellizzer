Nothoscordum gibbatum

Nothoscordum gibbatum, commonly known as the broadleaf garlic.

The plant features strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its bulbs, though not as pungent as true garlic, have a mild garlic-like scent.
