Norysca madagascariensis

Norysca madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that display a vibrant mix of colors, often featuring purple, white, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of Madagascar. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the rich, organic soil of its native habitat.

Norysca madagascariensis is also notable for its specialized pollination mechanism, which involves specific insect species found only in its native region.
