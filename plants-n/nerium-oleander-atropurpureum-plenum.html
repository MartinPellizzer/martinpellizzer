Nerium oleander atropurpureum-plenum

Nerium oleander atropurpureum-plenum, commonly known as the purple-flowered oleander, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features simple, narrow, evergreen leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It is a shrub or small tree with a robust, woody structure that can grow up to 6 meters tall.

The flowers are rich in nectar and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
