Nissolia dodgei

Nissolia dodgei, commonly known as the dwarf nissolia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, while its stems are somewhat woody.

Nissolia dodgei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in warm climates.
