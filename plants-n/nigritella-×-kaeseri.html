Nigritella × kaeseri

Nigritella × kaeseri, commonly known as the hybrid black-eyed Susan, is a cross between Nigritella and Kaeseria species.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy yellow flowers with dark centers, resembling the classic daisy pattern. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
