Notocactus mueller-melchersii var. winkleri

Notocactus mueller-melchersii var. winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that branches into a tree-like structure, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique form, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
