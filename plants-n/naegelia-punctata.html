Naegelia punctata

Naegelia punctata, commonly known as the spotted naegelia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover. Naegelia punctata is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
