Nerine laticoma subsp. laticoma

Nerine laticoma subsp.

laticoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nerine, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
