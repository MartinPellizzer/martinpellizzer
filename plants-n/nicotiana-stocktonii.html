Nicotiana stocktonii

Nicotiana stocktonii, commonly known as Stockton's tobacco, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple.
