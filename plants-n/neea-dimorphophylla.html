Neea dimorphophylla

Neea dimorphophylla, commonly known as the two-leaf nectarine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Neea dimorphophylla is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
