Neea flavifolia

Neea flavifolia, commonly known as the yellow-flowered neea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Neea flavifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native regions.
