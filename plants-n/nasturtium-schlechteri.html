Nasturtium schlechteri

Nasturtium schlechteri, commonly known as the rock nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its botanical features include a trailing or climbing habit, with small, edible leaves and flowers.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has mild peppery flavors in its foliage.
