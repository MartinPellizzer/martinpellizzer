Navarretia effusa

Navarretia effusa, commonly known as the narrowleaf navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This annual herb typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its flowers are small, white, and arranged in a raceme, often blooming in early spring.

The plant has a low-growing habit with slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.
