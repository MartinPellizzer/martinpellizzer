Nidema boothii var. triandrum

Nidema boothii var. triandrum, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces a dense cluster of flowers on a single stem, with each bloom having a unique, intricate pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
