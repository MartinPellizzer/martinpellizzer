Notocactus schaeferianus

Notocactus schaeferianus, commonly known as the Schaefer's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed, cylindrical stem and sparse, spiny foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid environments.
