Notocactus horstii f. purpureus

Notocactus horstii f. purpureus, commonly known as the Purple-Flowered Horstii Cactus, is a unique hybrid of the Notocactus genus.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to South America, specifically Brazil, and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in cactus collections.
