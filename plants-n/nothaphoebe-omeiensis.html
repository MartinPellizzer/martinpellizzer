Nothaphoebe omeiensis

Nothaphoebe omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese beech.

It is a deciduous tree native to China, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family and produces small, clustered flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
