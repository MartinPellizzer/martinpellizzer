Nerine × traubianthe

Nerine × traubianthe, commonly known as the hybrid nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant features strap-like, grassy foliage that emerges in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely appreciated by gardeners.
