Neoachmandra clemensiae

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the cooler months. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and habitat loss.
