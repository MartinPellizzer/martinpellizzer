Nephthytis swainei

Nephthytis swainei, commonly known as the Swaine's prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green and white patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Nephthytis swainei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
