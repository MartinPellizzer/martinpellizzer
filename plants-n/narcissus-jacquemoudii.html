Narcissus jacquemoudii

Narcissus jacquemoudii, commonly known as the Jacquemoudii daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the western Mediterranean region, particularly found in Spain and France. This plant is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.
