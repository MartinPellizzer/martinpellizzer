Nothoscordum bahiense

Nothoscordum bahiense, commonly known as the Bahia garlic.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Nothoscordum bahiense is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
