Notocactus rubriflorus

Notocactus rubriflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered notocactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually red or orange in hue. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and xerophytic gardens.
