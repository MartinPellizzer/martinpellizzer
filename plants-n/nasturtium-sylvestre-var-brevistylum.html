Nasturtium sylvestre var. brevistylum

Nasturtium sylvestre var. brevistylum, commonly known as the short-stemmed wood nasturtium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive yellow-orange flowers with prominent yellow centers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value and edible flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic and culinary benefits.
