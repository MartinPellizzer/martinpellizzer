Nasturtium trifurcum

Nasturtium trifurcum, commonly known as the three-lobed nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves.
