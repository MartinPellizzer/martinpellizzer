Nallogia

Nallogia, commonly known as the mountain holly, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically leathery and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Nallogia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
