Nauclea Surinamensis

Nauclea surinamensis is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forests. Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
