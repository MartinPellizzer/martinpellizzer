Nemesia floribunda

Nemesia floribunda, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.

Nemesia floribunda is also valued for its use in gardens and as a cut flower.
