Notocactus arachnitis var. minor

Notocactus arachnitis var. minor, commonly known as the miniature spider cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to southern Brazil.

It features a compact, globular shape with a greenish-gray stem covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
