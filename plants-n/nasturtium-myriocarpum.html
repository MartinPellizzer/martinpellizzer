Nasturtium myriocarpum

Nasturtium myriocarpum, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It produces bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive, rounded shape. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also edible, adding a peppery flavor to salads and dishes.
