Nemesia barbata var. minor

Nemesia barbata var. minor, commonly known as the slender nemesia, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to South Africa.

It features delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to white, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and extended flowering period make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, colorful ground cover.
