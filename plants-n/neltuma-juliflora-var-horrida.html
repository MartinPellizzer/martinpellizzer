Neltuma juliflora var. horrida

Neltuma juliflora var. horrida, commonly known as the horrida neltuma, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a woody stem with rough, often hairy foliage that contributes to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is noted for its resilience and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique floral display.
