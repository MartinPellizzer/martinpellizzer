Neolitsea sericea f. angustifolia

Neolitsea sericea f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Chinese tallowtree, is a variant of the Neolitsea sericea species.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by yellowish-orange berries.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
