Nassauvia juniperina

Nassauvia juniperina, commonly known as the silverleaf nassauvia, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates. Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a desirable addition to xeriscaped gardens.
