Nymphaea lutea subf. denticulata

Nymphaea lutea subf.

denticulata, commonly known as the yellow water lily, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom above the water surface.

The leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in shallow waters.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by supporting biodiversity and water filtration.
