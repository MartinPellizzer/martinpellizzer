Naegelia tomentosa

Naegelia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly naegelia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has hairy, grayish leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Naegelia tomentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
