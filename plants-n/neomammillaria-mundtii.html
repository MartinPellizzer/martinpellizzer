Neomammillaria mundtii

Neomammillaria mundtii, commonly known as the Mundt's clustered cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, slow-growing cactus characterized by its dense, globular shape and short, cylindrical stems. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its spines are typically short and white, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Neomammillaria mundtii is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
