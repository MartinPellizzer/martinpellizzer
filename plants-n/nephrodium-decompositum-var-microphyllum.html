Nephrodium decompositum var. microphyllum

Nephrodium decompositum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved kidney fern, is a variant of the kidney fern family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that are typically arranged in a clustered pattern. The fronds are usually bipinnate, with a distinctive kidney-shaped outline. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
