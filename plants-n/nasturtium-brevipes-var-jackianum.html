Nasturtium brevipes var. jackianum

Nasturtium brevipes var. jackianum, commonly known as the Jack's nasturtium, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which are borne on slender, hairy stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.

This variety is also valued for its peppery taste, making it a versatile addition to salads and culinary dishes.
