Nephrodium pleiopodum

It belongs to the family Nephrodioideae and is characterized by its distinctive, clustered fronds that resemble a cluster of small, rounded leaves. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive structure called a sorus, which contains the spore-producing organs. Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, almost velvety texture.
