Nephrodium speciosum

Nephrodium speciosum, commonly known as the ornate kidney fern, is a striking member of the Davalliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that display a distinctive kidney-shaped outline. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its spores are released from sporangia located on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Nephrodium speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
