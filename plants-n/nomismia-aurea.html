Nomismia aurea

Nomismia aurea, commonly known as the golden leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The golden hue of its leaves makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
