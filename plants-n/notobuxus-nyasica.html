Notobuxus nyasica

Notobuxus nyasica, commonly known as the Nyasica milkweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous elliptical seeds, often with a tuft of hair for wind dispersal.

Notobuxus nyasica is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.
