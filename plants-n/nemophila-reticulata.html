Nemophila reticulata

Nemophila reticulata, commonly known as the fairy bells, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Nemophila reticulata is known for its early spring blooming habit, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color to woodland gardens.
