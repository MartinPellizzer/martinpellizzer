Neottia schlechteriana

Neottia schlechteriana, commonly known as the Schlechter's twayblade, is a perennial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a single spike of small, greenish-white flowers that lack petals, typical of the orchid family. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

Neottia schlechteriana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
