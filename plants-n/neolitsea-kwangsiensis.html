Neolitsea kwangsiensis

Neolitsea kwangsiensis, commonly known as Guangxi litsea, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in subtropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
