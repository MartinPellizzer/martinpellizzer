Nestegis

Nestegis, commonly known as the tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to various regions in the Pacific and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Nestegis thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in humid, well-drained soils.

Its unique structure and growth habits make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
