Navarretia pumila

Navarretia pumila, commonly known as the dwarf navarretia, is a low-growing flowering plant native to western North America.

It is characterized by its small, rosette-forming leaves and clusters of delicate, white or pinkish flowers. This species typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
