Nephrodium evolutum

Nephrodium evolutum, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique plant characterized by its intricate, spirally arranged fronds.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with fiddlehead-like fronds that unfurl as they grow. Its leaves are typically bright green and have a delicate, feathery appearance.

Nephrodium evolutum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
