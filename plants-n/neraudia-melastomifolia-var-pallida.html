Neraudia melastomifolia var. pallida

Neraudia melastomifolia var. pallida, commonly known as the pale neraudia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals and prominent stamens.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
