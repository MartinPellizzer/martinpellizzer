Negretia gigantea

Negretia gigantea, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Negretia gigantea is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some regions.
