Notocactus mueller-melchersii var. eugeniae

Notocactus mueller-melchersii var. eugeniae, commonly known as the Eugenia Notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized collections.
