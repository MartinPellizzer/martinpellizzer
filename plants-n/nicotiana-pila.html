Nicotiana pila

Nicotiana pila, commonly known as the common tobacco plant, is a species native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are broad and often have a velvety texture.

Nicotiana pila is known for containing nicotine, a potent alkaloid used in various medicinal and commercial applications.
