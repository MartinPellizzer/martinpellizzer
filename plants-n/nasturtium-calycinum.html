Nasturtium calycinum

Nasturtium calycinum, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. Nasturtium calycinum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and deter aphids in the garden.
