Neomammillaria uncinata

Neomammillaria uncinata, commonly known as the hooked mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its distinctive hooked spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the spring.

Neomammillaria uncinata is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
