Neuroloma Saposhnikovii

Neuroloma saposhnikovii is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its unique foliage and delicate flowers. Despite its beauty, the plant remains understudied due to its limited distribution and challenging habitat. Its name reflects its historical significance in traditional medicine.
