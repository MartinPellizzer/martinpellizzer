Navarretia multiflora

Navarretia multiflora, commonly known as the coastal fairy bellflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It features slender stems that produce clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers during the spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It thrives in coastal climates with mild winters and warm, dry summers.

Navarretia multiflora is often found in coastal dunes and is an important part of the local flora.
