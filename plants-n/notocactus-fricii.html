Notocactus fricii

Notocactus fricii, commonly known as the Frici's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for desert gardens.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
