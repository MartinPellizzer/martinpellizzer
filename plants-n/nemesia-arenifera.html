Nemesia arenifera

Nemesia arenifera, commonly known as the sand nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and arid regions. Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, adapting to harsh environmental conditions.

Nemesia arenifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
