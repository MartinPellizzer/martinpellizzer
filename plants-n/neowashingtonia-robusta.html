Neowashingtonia robusta

Neowashingtonia robusta, commonly known as the desert fan palm, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Neowashingtonia robusta is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
