Nothofagus pullei

Nothofagus pullei, commonly known as the Huaihua Nothofagus, is a species of evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Nothofagus genus, which is part of the Nothofagaceae family, typically found in the Southern Hemisphere. This plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. The tree is known for its ecological importance in forest ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

It thrives in temperate climates with high humidity and well-drained soils.
