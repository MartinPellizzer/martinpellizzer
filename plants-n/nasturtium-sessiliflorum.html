Nasturtium sessiliflorum

Nasturtium sessiliflorum, commonly known as the sessile-flower nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its flowers have a distinctive, slightly peppery scent and are often used in culinary applications.

Nasturtium sessiliflorum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
