Neodissochaeta reticulata

Neodissochaeta reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features delicate, branched stems and small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are usually linear and covered with fine hairs, and it has a distinctive reticulate (net-like) venation pattern on its leaves.

This species is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
