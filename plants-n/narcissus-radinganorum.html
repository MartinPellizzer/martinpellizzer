Narcissus radinganorum

Narcissus radinganorum, commonly known as the Radinganorum daffodil, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow with a white or greenish throat. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are grassy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant spring blooms.
