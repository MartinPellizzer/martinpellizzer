Nissolia firma

Nissolia firma, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nissolia firma is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
