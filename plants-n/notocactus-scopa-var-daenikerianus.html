Notocactus scopa var. daenikerianus

Notocactus scopa var. daenikerianus, commonly known as the Daeniker's crown of thorns, is a rare and striking cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in specialized collections.
