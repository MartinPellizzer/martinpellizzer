Nuxia polycephala

Nuxia polycephala, commonly known as the many-headed nuxia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Nuxia polycephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
