Nervilia pectinata

Nervilia pectinata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Nervilia pectinata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
