Nemesia frutescens

Nemesia frutescens, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming annual or perennial plant with slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nemesia frutescens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
