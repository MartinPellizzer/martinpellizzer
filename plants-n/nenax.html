Nenax

Nenax, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown heartwood, which is highly valued for its durability and rich color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

The tree produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed, contributing to its propagation in natural habitats.
