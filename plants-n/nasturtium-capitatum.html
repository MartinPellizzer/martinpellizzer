Nasturtium capitatum

Nasturtium capitatum, commonly known as the Indian shot or golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its flowers are borne on a single stem, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Nasturtium capitatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant and for its edible flowers and leaves.
