Neolobivia × nakaii var. atrorosiflora

Neolobivia × nakaii var. atrorosiflora, commonly known as the dark red-flowered lobivia, is a hybrid cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its columnar stems and vibrant, dark red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a slow-growing species that requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
