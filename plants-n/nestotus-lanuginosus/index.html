Nestotus Lanuginosus

Nestotus lanuginosus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America. It is known for its soft, woolly leaves and striking, pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and is protected in several conservation areas. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of great interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
