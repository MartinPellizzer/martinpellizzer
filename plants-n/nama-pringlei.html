Nama pringlei

Nama pringlei, commonly known as the Namakwa flower, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, star-shaped flowers. Nama pringlei is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in succulent gardens.
