Narcissus Elegans

Narcissus elegans is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its elegant, fragrant blooms. This perennial plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils and sunny environments. Its flowers, which range from white to pale yellow, often feature a distinctive cup-like shape. Cultivated for both ornamental and medicinal purposes, it has a long history in traditional herbal medicine. With proper care, Narcissus elegans can bloom reliably year after year, making it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
