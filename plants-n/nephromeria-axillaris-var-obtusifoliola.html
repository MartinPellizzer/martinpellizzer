Nephromeria axillaris var. obtusifoliola

Nephromeria axillaris var. obtusifoliola, commonly known as the broad-leaved nephromeria, is a variant of the nephromeria plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact form. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually a pale yellow or greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
