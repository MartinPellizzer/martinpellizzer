Nervilia Kotschyi Var. Purpurata

Nervilia kotschyi var. purpurata is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This unique variant is known for its vivid purple and pink floral hues that stand out against its green foliage. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. The plant's delicate beauty has made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Due to its limited distribution and habitat-specific requirements, it remains a challenging species to cultivate.
