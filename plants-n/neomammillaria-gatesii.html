Neomammillaria gatesii

Neomammillaria gatesii, commonly known as the Gates' pincushion cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is typically adorned with white or pale green spines. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria gatesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
