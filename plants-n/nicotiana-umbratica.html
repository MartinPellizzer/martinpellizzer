Nicotiana umbratica

Nicotiana umbratica, commonly known as the shadow tobacco, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are broad and often have a velvety texture.

It is cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
