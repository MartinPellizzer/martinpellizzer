Nierembergia stuebelii

Nierembergia stuebelii, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
