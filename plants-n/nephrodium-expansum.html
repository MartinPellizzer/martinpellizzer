Nephrodium expansum

Nephrodium expansum, commonly known as the broadleaf nephroid, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces striking, orbicular spore cases that are often brightly colored, aiding in its reproductive process. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in tropical rainforests.

Due to habitat loss, Nephrodium expansum is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
