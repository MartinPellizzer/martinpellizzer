Nuphar advena var. minor

Nuphar advena var. minor, commonly known as the lesser yellow pond-lily, is a aquatic perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes. The plant features broad, floating leaves that are usually yellowish-green in color. Its flowers are pale yellow and bloom in early summer.

The plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various organisms.
