Niebuhria oblongifolia

Niebuhria oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its root system is extensive and deep, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor, sandy soils.
