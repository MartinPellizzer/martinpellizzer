Nephrodium trichophlebium

Nephrodium trichophlebium, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens.
