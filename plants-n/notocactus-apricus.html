Notocactus apricus

Notocactus apricus, commonly known as the sun cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green or yellowish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Notocactus apricus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
