Nunnezharia martiana

Nunnezharia martiana, commonly known as the Martian Moonflower, is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, moon-like white flowers that open at night, emitting a subtle fragrance. The plant has a succulent, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are small, thick, and covered in a waxy layer to reduce water loss.

Nunnezharia martiana is highly valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
