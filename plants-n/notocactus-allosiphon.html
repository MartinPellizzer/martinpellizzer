Notocactus allosiphon

Notocactus allosiphon, commonly known as the red-tipped notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink spines and its cylindrical, segmented stems. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
