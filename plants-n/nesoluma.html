It features vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
