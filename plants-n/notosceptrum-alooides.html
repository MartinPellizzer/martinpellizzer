Notosceptrum alooides

Notosceptrum alooides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved African spear lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil.
