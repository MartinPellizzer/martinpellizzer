Neolitsea sutchuanensis var. gongshanensis

Neolitsea sutchuanensis var. gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan sweetwood tree, is a rare evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, lanceolate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered an endangered species requiring conservation efforts.
