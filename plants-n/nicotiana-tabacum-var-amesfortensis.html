Nicotiana tabacum var. amesfortensis

Nicotiana tabacum var. amesfortensis, commonly known as the Amesfortensis tobacco, is a variant of the tobacco plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This variety is also valued for its use in both ornamental gardening and traditional herbal practices.
