Neoporteria reichei f. carneoflora

Neoporteria reichei f. carneoflora, commonly known as the red-flowered neoporteria, is a striking hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fleshy red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture. It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests.

Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists.
