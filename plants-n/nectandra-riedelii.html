Nectandra riedelii

Nectandra riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's sandarac, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves. Nectandra riedelii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
