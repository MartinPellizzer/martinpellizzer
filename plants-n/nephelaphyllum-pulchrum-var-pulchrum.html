Nephelaphyllum pulchrum var. pulchrum

Nephelaphyllum pulchrum var. pulchrum, commonly known as the Splendid Cloud Fern, is a strikingly beautiful epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble clouds, hence its common name. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its unique foliage features a combination of light green and silvery-white hues, adding visual interest to any collection.

It is also notable for its ability to grow in low-nutrient environments, making it relatively easy to care for.
