Narcissus × abilioi

Narcissus × abilioi, commonly known as the Giant Crowned Narcissus, is a hybrid tulip species originating from the Netherlands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive crown-like arrangement of petals. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring strap-shaped leaves and a robust root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
