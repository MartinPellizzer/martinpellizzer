Nephrolepis westonii

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds with a distinctive, glossy appearance. The plant produces spores on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
