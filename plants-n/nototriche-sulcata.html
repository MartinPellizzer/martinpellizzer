Nototriche sulcata

Nototriche sulcata, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. Nototriche sulcata has a short stem and produces flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white.
