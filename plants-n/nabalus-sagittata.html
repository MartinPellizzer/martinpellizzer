Nabalus sagittata

Nabalus sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly California, and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its narrow, arrow-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as slopes and gravelly areas.

Nabalus sagittata is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant once established.
