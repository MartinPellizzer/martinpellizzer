Noccaea lutescens subsp. drenovskii

Noccaea lutescens subsp.

drenovskii, commonly known as the Drenovskii's yellow cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and compound leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is notable for its ecological significance in supporting local pollinators and its role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
