Neolitsea involucrata

Neolitsea involucrata, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces woody fruits that contain two or three ovate seeds. Neolitsea involucrata is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical and subtropical forests.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
