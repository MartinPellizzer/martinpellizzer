Nocca mollis

Nocca mollis, commonly known as softleaf mallow, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features soft, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Nocca mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
