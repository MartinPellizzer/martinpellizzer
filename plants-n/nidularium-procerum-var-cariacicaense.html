Nidularium procerum var. cariacicaense

Nidularium procerum var. cariacicaense, commonly known as the Cariacica Narrowleaf Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, pale greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or branches. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a distinctive floral structure with elongated petals.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
