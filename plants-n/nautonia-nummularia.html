Nautonia nummularia

Nautonia nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, coin-shaped leaves that are typically silver to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Nautonia nummularia is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.
