Nephrodium fragrans var. aquilonare

Nephrodium fragrans var. aquilonare, commonly known as the aquatic nephroid, is a unique variant of the nephroid plant family.

It is characterized by its submerged foliage and specialized adaptations for aquatic environments. The plant exhibits a distinct morphology with elongated, ribbon-like leaves that facilitate water flow and gas exchange. Its reproductive structures are modified to ensure successful pollination in water.

This variant is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats, contributing to the ecological diversity of aquatic ecosystems.
