Nephelea incana

Nephelea incana, commonly known as the silverleaf nephelea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
