Navarretia Capillaris

California poppy, scientifically known as Navarretia capillaris, is a striking wildflower native to the coastal regions of California. This delicate plant is renowned for its vibrant orange petals and its ability to thrive in harsh, sun-scorched environments. It plays a vital role in supporting local pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, the California poppy faces threats from habitat loss and invasive species. Its resilience and symbolic significance make it an iconic representation of California's natural heritage.
