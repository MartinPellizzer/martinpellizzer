Nelanaregam

Nelanaregam, commonly known as the Indian mulberry, is a deciduous tree native to South Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices. Its bark and leaves contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

Nelanaregam plays an important role in ecological systems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
