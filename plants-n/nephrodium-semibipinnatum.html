Nephrodium semibipinnatum

Nephrodium semibipinnatum, commonly known as the semi-bipinnate nephroid, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, bipinnate fronds that are divided into small, delicate pinnae. The plant has a creeping rhizome with numerous adventitious roots, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically green and glossy, with a slightly serrated margin.

Nephrodium semibipinnatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
