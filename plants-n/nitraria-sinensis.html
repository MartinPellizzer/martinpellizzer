Nitraria sinensis

Nitraria sinensis, commonly known as Chinese saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Nitrariaceae and is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to saline and drought conditions, making it a hardy species in harsh environments. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains seeds coated in a sticky resin.

Nitraria sinensis has been used traditionally in some regions for medicinal and agricultural purposes.
