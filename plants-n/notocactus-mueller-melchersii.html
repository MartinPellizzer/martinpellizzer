Notocactus mueller-melchersii

Notocactus mueller-melchersii, commonly known as the Mueller's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived but highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
