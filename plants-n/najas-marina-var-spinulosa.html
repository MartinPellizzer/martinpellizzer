Najas marina var. spinulosa

Najas marina var. spinulosa, commonly known as the slender water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It has slender, branching stems that are often covered with small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, floating flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found in ponds and lakes.

Its presence is an indicator of good water quality and healthy aquatic ecosystems.
