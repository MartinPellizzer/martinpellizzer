Nomocharis farreri

Nomocharis farreri, commonly known as the red-flowered lilies, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive purple throat. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, sturdy stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Nomocharis farreri is known for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
