Nasturtium nudiusculum var. integrifolium

Nasturtium nudiusculum var. integrifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically entire, giving it its varietal name "integrifolium.". The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers with a distinctive spurred petal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible flowers.
