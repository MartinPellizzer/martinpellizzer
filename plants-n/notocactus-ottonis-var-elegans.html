Notocactus ottonis var. elegans

Notocactus ottonis var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant notocactus, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a smooth, pale green to bluish-gray surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
