Neoporteria heteracantha

Neoporteria heteracantha, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces striking blue or purple star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
