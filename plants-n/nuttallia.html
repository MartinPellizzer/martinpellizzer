Nuttallia

Nuttallia, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spiny leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from blue to purple. These plants are typically found in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing their adaptability to harsh environments. Nuttallia species are often used in ornamental gardening due to their unique appearance and hardy nature.

The genus includes several species, such as Nuttallia frondosa, which is native to the western United States.
