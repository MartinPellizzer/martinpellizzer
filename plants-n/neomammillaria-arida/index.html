Neomammillaria Arida

Neomammillaria arida is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is known for its unique, rounded shape and vibrant green stems adorned with prominent white spines. This plant thrives in harsh, dry environments where water is scarce and temperatures can be extreme. Its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems allows it to survive in conditions that would be fatal to most other plants. Due to its beauty and ecological significance, Neomammillaria arida is a subject of interest for botanists and cactus enthusiasts alike.
