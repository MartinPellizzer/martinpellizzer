Nitrosalsola dzhungarica

Nitrosalsola dzhungarica, commonly known as the Dzungarian nitrate plant, is a halophytic shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, showcasing its adaptation to extreme environmental conditions. The plant is characterized by its succulent leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It plays a role in stabilizing desert soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its ability to accumulate nitrates makes it an important species for ecological and agricultural studies.
