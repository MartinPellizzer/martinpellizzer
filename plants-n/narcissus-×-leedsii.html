Narcissus × leedsii

Narcissus × leedsii, commonly known as the Giant Paperwhite, is a hybrid narcissus species originating from the Netherlands.

It is characterized by its large, white, cup-shaped flowers that often have a subtle greenish tinge. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches, with broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and slightly textured. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its elegant appearance and fragrance.

This variety is well-suited for indoor growing and can be forced to bloom out of season.
