Nerine elwesii

Nerine elwesii, commonly known as the Elwes' nerine, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

Nerine elwesii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
