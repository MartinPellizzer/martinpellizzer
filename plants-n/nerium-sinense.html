Nerium sinense

Nerium sinense, commonly known as the Chinese oleander, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy clusters of pink, red, or white flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to a variety of soil types and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic and should be handled with care.
