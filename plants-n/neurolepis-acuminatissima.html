Neurolepis acuminatissima

Neurolepis acuminatissima, commonly known as the sharp-pointed neurolepis, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a slender, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and have a pointed tip, contributing to its common name.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
