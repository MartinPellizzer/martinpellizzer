Neptunia natans

Neptunia natans, commonly known as floating bladderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its unique ability to float on the surface of water, supported by air-filled sacs in its stems. The plant has small, simple leaves and produces tiny white or pink flowers. It is an aquatic plant that thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Neptunia natans plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
