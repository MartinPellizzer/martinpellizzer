Nasturtium officinale f. microphyllum

Nasturtium officinale f. microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a compact variant of the common nasturtium.

It features small, rounded leaves and vibrant, edible flowers in shades of orange and red. This plant is native to South America and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as a ornamental and culinary plant due to its peppery taste and aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for container gardening and borders in gardens.
