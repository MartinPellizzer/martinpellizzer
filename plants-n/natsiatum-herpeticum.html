Natsiatum herpeticum

Natsiatum herpeticum, commonly known as the Herpetic Natsiatum, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored petals that range from deep red to vibrant yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a slightly serrated edge.

Natsiatum herpeticum is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
