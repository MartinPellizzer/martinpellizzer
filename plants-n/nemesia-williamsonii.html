Nemesia williamsonii

Nemesia williamsonii, commonly known as the William's nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has delicate, hairy leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Nemesia williamsonii prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
