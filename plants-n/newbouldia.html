Newbouldia

Newbouldia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are pinnate, with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Newbouldia thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
