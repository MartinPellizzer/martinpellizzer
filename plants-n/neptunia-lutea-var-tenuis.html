Neptunia lutea var. tenuis

Neptunia lutea var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender yellow water fern, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its yellowish-green foliage helps it absorb sunlight for photosynthesis.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing shelter for small aquatic organisms.
