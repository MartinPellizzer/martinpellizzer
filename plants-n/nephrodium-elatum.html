Nephrodium elatum

Nephrodium elatum, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped fronds that are often bright yellow or golden in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its fronds are compound, with multiple small leaflets arranged in a radiating pattern.

Nephrodium elatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
