Nasturtium flavum

Nasturtium flavum, commonly known as the golden touch, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is known for its peppery taste, similar to watercress. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Nasturtium flavum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in herbal remedies.
