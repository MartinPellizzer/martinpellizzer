Notocactus sessiliflorus var. stegmannii

Notocactus sessiliflorus var. stegmannii, commonly known as the Stegmann's sunburst cactus, is a rare and striking member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in bright yellow spines and bear clusters of vibrant orange to red flowers. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

This variety is highly sought after by collectors due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate.
