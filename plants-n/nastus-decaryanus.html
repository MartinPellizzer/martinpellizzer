Nastus decaryanus

Nastus decaryanus, commonly known as the Decary's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic, containing various cardiac glycosides. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands.

Nastus decaryanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
