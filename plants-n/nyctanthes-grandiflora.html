Nyctanthes grandiflora

Nyctanthes grandiflora, commonly known as night-flowering jasmine, is a deciduous shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom at night. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are dark green in color. It produces small, black, spherical berries that contain the seeds.

Nyctanthes grandiflora is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
