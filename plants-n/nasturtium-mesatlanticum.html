Nasturtium mesatlanticum

Nasturtium mesatlanticum, commonly known as the Atlantic nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Additionally, its edible flowers and leaves are used in culinary applications, adding a peppery flavor to dishes.
