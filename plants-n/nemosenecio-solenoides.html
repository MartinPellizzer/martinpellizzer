Nemosenecio solenoides

Nemosenecio solenoides, commonly known as the Alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist conditions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
