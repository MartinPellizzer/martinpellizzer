Neolitsea viridis

Neolitsea viridis, commonly known as the green flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by spherical, greenish-yellow fruits. Neolitsea viridis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
