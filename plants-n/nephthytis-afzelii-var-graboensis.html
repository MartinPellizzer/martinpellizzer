Nephthytis afzelii var. graboensis

Nephthytis afzelii var. graboensis, commonly known as the Grabo Spider Plant, is a variant of the spider plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically green with a striking silver or white stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect light.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to purify the air and its adaptability to indoor conditions.
