Nymphaea ampla var. speciosa

Nymphaea ampla var. speciosa, commonly known as the pink lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a subtle red or purple hue at the base. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing a habitat for various aquatic life.

The flowers open in the early morning and close by midday, a behavior known as nyctinasty.
