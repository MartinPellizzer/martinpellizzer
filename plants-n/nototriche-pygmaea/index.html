Nototriche Pygmaea

Nototriche pygmaea is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of South America. It is known for its tiny, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. Despite its modest size, this plant plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its survival is threatened by habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare and beautiful species.
