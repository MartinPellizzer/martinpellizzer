Neanotis carnosa

Neanotis carnosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or carnose neanotis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Neanotis carnosa is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
