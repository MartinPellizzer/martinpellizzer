Norysca chinensis

Norysca chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue, depending on the species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Norysca chinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
