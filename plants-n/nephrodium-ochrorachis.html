Nephrodium ochrorachis

Nephrodium ochrorachis, commonly known as the golden kidney fern, is a striking fern species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, kidney-shaped fronds that are deeply lobed and often covered in a fine, golden pubescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its unique leaf structure and vibrant coloration make it a popular ornamental plant in botanical gardens.

Nephrodium ochrorachis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various insects and small vertebrates.
