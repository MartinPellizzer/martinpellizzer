Neoxythece atabapoensis

Neoxythece atabapoensis, commonly known as the Atabapo bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically in the region of the Atabapo River in Colombia. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, linear leaves. It plays a vital role in the ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Due to its ecological importance, it is often found in wetland and riparian habitats.
