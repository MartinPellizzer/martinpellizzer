Neea rufula

Neea rufula, commonly known as the red-flowered neea, is a tropical shrub belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its most distinctive feature is its clusters of bright red flowers, which bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Neea rufula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
