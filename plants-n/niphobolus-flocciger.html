Niphobolus flocciger

Niphobolus flocciger, commonly known as the woolly bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant native to freshwater habitats in North America.

Its leaves are often covered in a dense, woolly coating, which helps reduce water loss and provides camouflage.

Niphobolus flocciger plays a unique role in its ecosystem by controlling insect populations and contributing to nutrient cycling in aquatic environments.
