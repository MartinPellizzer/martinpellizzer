Nesaea kilimandscharica var. ngongensis

It is a perennial herb with a compact growth habit, typically growing in rocky or sandy soils at elevations above 2,500 meters. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, often in shades of red and orange, which bloom during the dry season. It is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including strong sunlight and low humidity.
