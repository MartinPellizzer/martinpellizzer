Napaea dioica f. stellata

Napaea dioica f. stellata, commonly known as the star-forming yellow vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
