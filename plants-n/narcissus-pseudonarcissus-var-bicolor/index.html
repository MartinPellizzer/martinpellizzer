Narcissus Pseudonarcissus Var. Bicolor

Narcissus pseudonarcissus var. bicolor is a striking variant of the daffodil known for its vibrant two-toned petals. This plant typically features a yellow corona surrounded by white or pale yellow outer petals. It blooms in early spring, adding a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. The bicolor variation is a result of genetic diversity within the species. Its beauty and resilience make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
