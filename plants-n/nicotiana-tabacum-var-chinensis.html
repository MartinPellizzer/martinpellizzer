Nicotiana tabacum var. chinensis

Nicotiana tabacum var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese tobacco, is a variant of the tobacco plant native to China.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically violet or blue, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It produces elliptical, dark brown seeds that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in herbal remedies.
