Nothofagus fusca var. colensoi

Nothofagus fusca var. colensoi, commonly known as Colenso's nothofagus, is a variant of the nothofagus genus native to New Zealand.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its lanceolate, glossy leaves and distinctive fruiting bodies. The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, while the cones are small and often clustered. This species thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat and food for various native fauna.
