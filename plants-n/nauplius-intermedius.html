Nauplius intermedius

Nauplius intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate nauplius, is a marine crustacean belonging to the order Anomura.

It is characterized by its elongated, segmented body and a pair of biramous antennae. This species is typically found in shallow coastal waters and is part of the early life stages of certain crab species. Its morphology includes a distinct carapace and well-developed pleopods.
