Niphobolus gralla

Niphobolus gralla, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Niphobolus gralla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
