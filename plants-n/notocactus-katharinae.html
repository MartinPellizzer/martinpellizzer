Notocactus katharinae

Notocactus katharinae, commonly known as the Katherine's notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
