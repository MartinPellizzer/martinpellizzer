Nuxia dentata

Nuxia dentata, commonly known as the toothed nuxia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Nuxia dentata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
