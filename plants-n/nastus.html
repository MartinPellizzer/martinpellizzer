Nastus

Nastus, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Nastus is often found in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to thrive in dry conditions.
