Notocactus linkii var. albispinus

Notocactus linkii var. albispinus, commonly known as the white-spined columnar cactus, is a unique variety of the Notocactus genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features distinctive white spines that contrast with its green or blue-green stem color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens and indoor settings.
