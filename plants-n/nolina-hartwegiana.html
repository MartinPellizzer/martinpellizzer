Nolina hartwegiana

Nolina hartwegiana, commonly known as the desert spineflower, is a shrub-like plant native to the arid regions of northern Mexico and the southwestern United States.

Nolina hartwegiana is well adapted to dry environments, with a deep root system and reduced leaf surface to minimize water loss.
