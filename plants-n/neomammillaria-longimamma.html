Neomammillaria longimamma

Neomammillaria longimamma, commonly known as the long-stemmed mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale blue tubercles. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria longimamma is a popular choice for succulent collectors due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
