Notocactus ottonis var. albispinus

Notocactus ottonis var. albispinus, commonly known as the white-spined columnar cactus, is a unique species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant features distinctive white spines that contrast with its green or bluish-green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens and collections.
