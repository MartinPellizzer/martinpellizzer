Nouhuysia pauciflora

Nouhuysia pauciflora, commonly known as the Cape York lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, recurved shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
