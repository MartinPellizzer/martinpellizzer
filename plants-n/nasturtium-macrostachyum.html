Nasturtium macrostachyum

Nasturtium macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant nasturtium, is a vigorous climbing plant native to South America.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of orange and red, with prominent yellow centers. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of pubescence. This species is known for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often used in gardens for its ornamental and culinary value.
