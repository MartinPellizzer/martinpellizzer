Neomammillaria Eriacantha

Neomammillaria eriacantha is a rare and striking cactus native to the rocky habitats of Mexico. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring a short stem covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces delicate, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
