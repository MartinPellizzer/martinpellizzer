Neoporteria napina f. mebbesii

Neoporteria napina f. mebbesii, commonly known as the Mebbesii form of the narrow-leaved neoporteria, is a rare and unique variant of this plant species.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are often narrower than those of the typical form. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a delicate pink or white hue.

This form is of particular interest to botanists due to its distinct morphology and limited distribution.
