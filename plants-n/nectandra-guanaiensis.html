Nectandra guanaiensis

Nectandra guanaiensis, commonly known as the Guanaiense Laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, followed by elliptical, woody fruits. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils, which have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.

Nectandra guanaiensis plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
