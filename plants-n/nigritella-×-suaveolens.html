Nigritella × suaveolens

Nigritella × suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented nightshade, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, lobed leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is known for its fragrant, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

However, due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
