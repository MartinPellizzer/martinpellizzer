Najas tenuicaulis

Najas tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant belonging to the Najadaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. This plant thrives in freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually borne in clusters and are pollinated by water currents.

Najas tenuicaulis plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
