Narcissus × taitii

Narcissus × taitii, commonly known as the Taiti daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Narcissus.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is native to the island of Taiti in the French South Pacific and thrives in coastal environments. It produces showy blooms in early spring, often with a strong, sweet fragrance.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
