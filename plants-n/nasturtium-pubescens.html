Nasturtium pubescens

Nasturtium pubescens, commonly known as the hairy nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the summer. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery taste, similar to watercress.

Nasturtium pubescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant and for its edible parts.
