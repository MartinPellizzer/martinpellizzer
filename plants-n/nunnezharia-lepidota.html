Nunnezharia lepidota

Nunnezharia lepidota, commonly known as the smooth-leaved nunnezharia, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy on both sides. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
