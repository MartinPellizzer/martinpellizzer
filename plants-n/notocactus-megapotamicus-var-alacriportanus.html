Notocactus megapotamicus var. alacriportanus

Notocactus megapotamicus var. alacriportanus, commonly known as the Alacriportan Cactus, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
