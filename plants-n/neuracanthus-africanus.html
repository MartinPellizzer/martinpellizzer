Neuracanthus africanus

Neuracanthus africanus, commonly known as the African lantern plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that resemble small lanterns. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.
