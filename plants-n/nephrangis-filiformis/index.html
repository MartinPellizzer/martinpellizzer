Nephrangis Filiformis

Nephrangis filiformis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its slender, vine-like stems and small, fragrant blossoms that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. Due to its limited natural habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
