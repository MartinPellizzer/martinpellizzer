Notholaena nudiuscula

Notholaena nudiuscula, commonly known as the slender rock fern, is a small, terrestrial fern native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in woodland or scrub habitats. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with dense, clustered fronds that are usually less than 10 cm in length. Its leaves are pinnately divided with a narrow, linear shape and are often covered in fine, silky hairs.

Notholaena nudiuscula is a hardy species that thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in limestone areas.
