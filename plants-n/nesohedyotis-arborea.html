Nesohedyotis arborea

Nesohedyotis arborea, commonly known as the tree hedyotis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters along the stems.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
