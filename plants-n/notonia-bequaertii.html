Notonia bequaertii

Notonia bequaertii, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its bark is smooth and greyish, and it produces small, edible pods that contain seeds.
