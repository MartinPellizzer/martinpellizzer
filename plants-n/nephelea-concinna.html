Nephelea concinna

Nephelea concinna, commonly known as the slender lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a loose cluster at the top of the stem, and it prefers well-drained soil in full sun.

Nephelea concinna is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
