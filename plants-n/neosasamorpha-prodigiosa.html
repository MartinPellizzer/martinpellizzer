Neosasamorpha prodigiosa

Neosasamorpha prodigiosa, commonly known as the Marvelous Beauty, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bioluminescent petals that emit a soft glow at night. The plant has a unique root system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are large, broad, and have a velvety texture.
