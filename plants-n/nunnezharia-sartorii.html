Nunnezharia sartorii

Nunnezharia sartorii, commonly known as the purple-stemmed morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet or purple stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming in the early morning. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Nunnezharia sartorii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
