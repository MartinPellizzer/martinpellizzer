Neolitsea sieboldii var. prematura

Neolitsea sieboldii var. prematura, commonly known as early-flowering neolitsea, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which bloom early in the spring.
