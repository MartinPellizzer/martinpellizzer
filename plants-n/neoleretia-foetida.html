Neoleretia foetida

Neoleretia foetida, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its distinctive, fragrant flowers that bloom at night. Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
