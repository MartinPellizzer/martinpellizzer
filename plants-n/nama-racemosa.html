Nama racemosa

Nama racemosa, commonly known as the yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Nama racemosa is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
