Nicotiana heterantha

Nicotiana heterantha, commonly known as the heterantha tobacco, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, with a rough texture.

Nicotiana heterantha is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and unique foliage.
