Nolletia zambesica

Nolletia zambesica, commonly known as the Zambesi sandveld grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in grassland ecosystems. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Nolletia zambesica plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small invertebrates.
