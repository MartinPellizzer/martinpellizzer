Nicandra nana

Nicandra nana, commonly known as dwarf nicandra, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is a compact, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often followed by orange or red berries. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Its foliage is characterized by ovate, hairy leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues.
