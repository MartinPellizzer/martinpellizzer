Neomammillaria bucareliensis

Neomammillaria bucareliensis, commonly known as the Bucareli cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and typically measures up to 10 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
