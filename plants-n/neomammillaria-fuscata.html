Neomammillaria fuscata

Neomammillaria fuscata, commonly known as the dark mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dark green to bluish-gray coloration. The plant features prominent areoles with white radial spines and a few central spines. It produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

Neomammillaria fuscata is a slow-growing, drought-tolerant cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
