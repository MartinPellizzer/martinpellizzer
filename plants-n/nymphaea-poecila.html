Nymphaea poecila

Nymphaea poecila, commonly known as the purple lotus, is a water-living plant belonging to the Nymphaeaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Southeast Asia. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, depending on the stage of bloom. Its leaves are broad and floating, with a waxy surface that helps repel water.

The botanical structure of Nymphaea poecila is adapted for aquatic environments, with specialized roots and a buoyant stem system.
