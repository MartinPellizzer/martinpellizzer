Nasturtium officinale var. precosium

Nasturtium officinale var. precosium, commonly known as the early nasturtium, is a vibrant flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces bright orange or yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant is known for its edible leaves and seeds, which have a peppery flavor similar to watercress. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
