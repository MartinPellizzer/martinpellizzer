Nerium latifolium

Nerium latifolium, commonly known as the oleander, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, red, and yellow. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its bark is smooth and grayish, becoming fissured with age.

Nerium latifolium is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
