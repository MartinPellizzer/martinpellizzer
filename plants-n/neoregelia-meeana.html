Neoregelia meeana

Neoregelia meeana, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, waxy leaves that form a rosette shape, creating a central cup that collects rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts and yellow-orange flowers, adding visual interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia meeana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
