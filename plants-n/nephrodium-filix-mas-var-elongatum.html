Nephrodium filix-mas var. elongatum

Nephrodium filix-mas var. elongatum, commonly known as the elongated male fern, is a variant of the male fern species characterized by its elongated fronds.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a distinctive fiddlehead structure, which is a coiled young frond that uncurls as it matures. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent and have a unique, elongated shape compared to the standard male fern.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
