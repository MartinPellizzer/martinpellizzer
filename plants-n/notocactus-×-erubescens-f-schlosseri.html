Notocactus × erubescens f. schlosseri

Notocactus × erubescens f. schlosseri, commonly known as the Red-Flowered Schlosser Notocactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
