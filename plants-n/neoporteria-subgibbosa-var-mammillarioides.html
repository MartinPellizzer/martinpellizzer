Neoporteria subgibbosa var. mammillarioides

Neoporteria subgibbosa var. mammillarioides, commonly known as the mammillarioid neoporteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and is covered in small, white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, typically in the afternoon. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
