Neblinantha

Neblinantha, commonly known as the misty beauty, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, waxy leaves that often appear misty due to the high humidity in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Neblinantha thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
