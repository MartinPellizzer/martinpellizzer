Nephrocarya horizontalis

It is characterized by its distinctive, horizontally oriented fruit that resembles a nutmeg in appearance. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, with a strong aromatic scent.
