Nasturtium nilolicum

Nasturtium nilolicum, commonly known as the Indian cress, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers. Its seeds are edible and have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.

Nasturtium nilolicum is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
