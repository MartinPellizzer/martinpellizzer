Noisettia roquefeuilliana

Noisettia roquefeuilliana, commonly known as the Corsican walnut, is a deciduous tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Juglandaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and large, edible nuts. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a rounded crown.
