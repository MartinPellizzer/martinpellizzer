Notocactus Mammulosus Subsp. Submammulosus

Notocactus mammulosus subsp. submammulosus is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa. Known for its unique, rounded stems and vibrant, spiky flowers, this plant is a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens. Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of horticulture.
