Navarretia foetida

Navarretia foetida, commonly known as the stinkcup, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has a short life cycle, often completing its growth and reproduction within a single growing season.
