Neillia sinensis var. caudata

Neillia sinensis var. caudata, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, dark green leaves that often have a reddish or purple tint on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Neillia sinensis var. caudata is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
