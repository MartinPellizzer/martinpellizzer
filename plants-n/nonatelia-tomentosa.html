Nonatelia tomentosa

Nonatelia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly nonatelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique texture and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty plant collections.
