Neuracanthus africanus var. limpopoensis

Neuracanthus africanus var. limpopoensis, commonly known as the Limpopo starfish, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green.

This variety is known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions, which makes it well-suited for arid environments.
