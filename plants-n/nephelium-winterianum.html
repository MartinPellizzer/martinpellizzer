Nephelium winterianum

Nephelium winterianum, commonly known as the winter mango, is a tropical fruit tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is closely related to the common mango. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, fibrous peel and a sweet, aromatic pulp. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.
