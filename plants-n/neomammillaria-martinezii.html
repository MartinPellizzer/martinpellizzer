Neomammillaria martinezii

Neomammillaria martinezii, commonly known as the Martinez's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a ribbed surface. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Neomammillaria martinezii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
