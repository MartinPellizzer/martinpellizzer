Notocactus leprosorum

Notocactus leprosorum, commonly known as the Leprosy Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
