Narcissus × pujolii

Narcissus × pujolii, commonly known as the Pujol daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Narcissus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a prominent, trumpet-shaped corona. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30–50 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its vibrant yellow petals and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
