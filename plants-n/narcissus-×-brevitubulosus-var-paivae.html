Narcissus × brevitubulosus var. paivae

Narcissus × brevitubulosus var. paivae, commonly known as the Paivai daffodil, is a hybrid daffodil species characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental value.

It typically features single, trumpet-shaped flowers with a yellow or white corona, often with a distinctive green or yellow throat. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are grass-like in appearance, and it is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its early blooming period and vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
