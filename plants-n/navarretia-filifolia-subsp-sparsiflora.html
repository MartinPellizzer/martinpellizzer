Navarretia filifolia subsp. sparsiflora

Navarretia filifolia subsp.

sparsiflora, commonly known as the slender navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and clusters of small white flowers.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies of native flora.
