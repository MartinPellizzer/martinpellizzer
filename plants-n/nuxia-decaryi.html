Nuxia decaryi

Nuxia decaryi, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Nuxia decaryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand dry conditions.
