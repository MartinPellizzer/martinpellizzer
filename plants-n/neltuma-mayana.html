Neltuma mayana

Neltuma mayana, commonly known as the Maya orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Neltuma mayana is a perennial herb that prefers well-drained soil and partial sunlight.

Its unique floral structure and cultural significance make it a valued plant in both horticulture and traditional practices.
