Neomitranthes gracilis

Neomitranthes gracilis, commonly known as the slender starflower, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features delicate, star-shaped flowers that are often white or pale pink, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Neomitranthes gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
