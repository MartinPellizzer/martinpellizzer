Neltuma × vinalillo

Neltuma × vinalillo, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Neltuma and Vinalillo species.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant is native to certain regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
