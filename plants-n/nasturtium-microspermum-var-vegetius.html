Nasturtium microspermum var. vegetius

Nasturtium microspermum var. vegetius, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a compact, annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, edible flowers in shades of orange and yellow, with heart-shaped leaves that are also edible. The plant produces small, round seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary gardening.
