Nasturtium pinnatifidum

Nasturtium pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow and orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its stems are hairy and can grow up to several feet in height, often sprawling along the ground.

Nasturtium pinnatifidum is valued for its ornamental beauty and edible flowers, which have a peppery taste.
