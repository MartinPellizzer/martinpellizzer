Neoporteria robusta

Neoporteria robusta, commonly known as the robust neotropical orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with multiple blooms, each featuring three petals and three sepals, with the lip being highly modified. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Neoporteria robusta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
