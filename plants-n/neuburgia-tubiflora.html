Neuburgia tubiflora

Neuburgia tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Neuburgia tubiflora is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
