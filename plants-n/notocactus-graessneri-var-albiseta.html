Notocactus graessneri var. albiseta

Notocactus graessneri var. albiseta, commonly known as the White Settled Notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, columnar stem with a distinctive white or pale yellow coloration, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the cooler months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
