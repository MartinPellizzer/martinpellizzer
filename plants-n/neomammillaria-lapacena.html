Neomammillaria lapacena

Neomammillaria lapacena, commonly known as the Lapacena pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact, rounded shape and a ribbed structure. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its stems are covered in soft, white spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
