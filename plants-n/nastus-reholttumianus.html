Nastus reholttumianus

Nastus reholttumianus, commonly known as the Red-leaf Nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and yellow flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The edible flowers and leaves are used in culinary applications, adding a peppery flavor to dishes.
