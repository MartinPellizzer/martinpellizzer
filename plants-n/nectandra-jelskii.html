Nectandra jelskii

Nectandra jelskii, commonly known as the Malabar ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by fruit that is a capsule containing several seeds.

Nectandra jelskii is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid, shaded environments.
