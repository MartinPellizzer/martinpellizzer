Nerisyrenia

Nerisyrenia, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and spiny, leaf-like bracts that surround the flower heads. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found along shorelines. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a distinctive addition to coastal gardens.
