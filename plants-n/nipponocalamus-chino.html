Nipponocalamus chino

Nipponocalamus chino, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or Chinese golden bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Nipponocalamus chino is also used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable materials.
