Notoniopsis implexa

Notoniopsis implexa, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spiraling arrangement of fronds, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant features small, delicate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Notoniopsis implexa is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
