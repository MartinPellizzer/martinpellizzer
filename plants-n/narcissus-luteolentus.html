Narcissus luteolentus

Narcissus luteolentus, commonly known as the yellow narcissus, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-shaped corona. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. The bulbs are perennial, producing foliage and flowers in the spring.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
