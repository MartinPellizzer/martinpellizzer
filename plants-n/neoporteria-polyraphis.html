Neoporteria polyraphis

Neoporteria polyraphis, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
