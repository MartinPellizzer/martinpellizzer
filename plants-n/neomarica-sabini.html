Neomarica sabini

Neomarica sabini, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green, broad leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. The plant produces an inflorescence known as a spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

It is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
