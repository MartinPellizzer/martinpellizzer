Narduretia delicatula f. intermedia

Narduretia delicatula f. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate fragrant orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and a unique floral structure that distinguishes it from other orchid species.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
