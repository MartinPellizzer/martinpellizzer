Neoraputia magnifica

Neoraputia magnifica, commonly known as the royal flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from fiery red to deep orange, often with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique inflorescence, which consists of multiple blooms arranged in a spiral. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant flowers.

Neoraputia magnifica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a favorite among tropical plant enthusiasts.
