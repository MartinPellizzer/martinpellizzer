Neyraudia mezii

Neyraudia mezii, commonly known as the Chinese reed or Chinese bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green in color. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. Neyraudia mezii is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and ability to form dense clumps.

It is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
