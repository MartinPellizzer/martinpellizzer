Nemesia versicolor var. oxyceras

Nemesia versicolor var. oxyceras, commonly known as the oxyceras nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and profuse displays of small, showy flowers. The flowers typically come in shades of pink, white, and red, with a distinctive tubular shape and a five-lobed corolla. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
