Nasturtium macrocarpum

Nasturtium macrocarpum, commonly known as the giant nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces edible seeds that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its vibrant blooms and peppery flavor make it both ornamental and edible.
