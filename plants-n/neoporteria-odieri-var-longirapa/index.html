Neoporteria Odieri Var. Longirapa

Neoporteria odieri var. longirapa is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, velvety leaves and unique growth patterns, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This variant of Neoporteria is distinguished by its elongated, lance-shaped foliage that sets it apart from other members of the genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it has become a sought-after specimen in specialized horticulture circles.
