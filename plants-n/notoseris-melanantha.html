Notoseris melanantha

Notoseris melanantha, commonly known as the dark-hued aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its dark purple to black flowers are notable for their striking coloration and attract various pollinators.

Notoseris melanantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
