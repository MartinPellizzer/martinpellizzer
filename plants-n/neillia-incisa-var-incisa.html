Neillia incisa var. incisa

Neillia incisa var. incisa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed neillia, is a species native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, woodland environments. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters during the spring.

Neillia incisa var. incisa is often found in shaded areas and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
