Notocactus harmonianus

Notocactus harmonianus, commonly known as the Harlequin Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, flattened stems that are segmented and often adorned with spines. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in the evening. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to harsh desert conditions.

The plant is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
