Nasturtium densiflorum

Nasturtium densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible leaves and flowers that are often used in culinary applications. Its foliage is covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Nasturtium densiflorum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.
