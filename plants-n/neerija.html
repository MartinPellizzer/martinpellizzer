Neerija

Neerija, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to South Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family and is characterized by its stinging hairs on its leaves and stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its small greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits.

Neerija is often found in forests, wetlands, and along stream banks.
