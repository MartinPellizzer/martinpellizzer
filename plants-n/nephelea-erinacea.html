Nephelea erinacea

Nephelea erinacea, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it a soft, velvety appearance. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Nephelea erinacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
