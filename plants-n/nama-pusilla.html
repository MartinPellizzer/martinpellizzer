Nama pusilla

Nama pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf lily of the Nile, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue or purple flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that can be grown in gardens for its ornamental value.

Nama pusilla is also valued for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
