Nectandra urophylla

Nectandra urophylla, commonly known as the golden sand-wood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The tree is valued for its durable timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
