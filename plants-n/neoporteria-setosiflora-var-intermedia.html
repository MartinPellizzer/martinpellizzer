Neoporteria setosiflora var. intermedia

Neoporteria setosiflora var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate bristly mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant belonging to the family Viscaraceae.

It grows primarily on the branches of host trees, such as eucalyptus and acacia, drawing water and nutrients from them. This variant is notable for its bristly appearance, with hairy structures on its stems and leaves.
