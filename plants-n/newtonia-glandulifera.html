Newtonia glandulifera

Newtonia glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular Newtonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features pinnate leaves with small, glandular hairs that give it its scientific name. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in racemes, and it produces legume pods containing seeds.

Newtonia glandulifera is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.
