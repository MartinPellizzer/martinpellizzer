Nasturtium microphyllum

Nasturtium microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange. Its flowers and leaves are edible, with a peppery flavor similar to watercress.
