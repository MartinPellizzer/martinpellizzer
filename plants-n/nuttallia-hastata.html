Nuttallia hastata

Nuttallia hastata, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its milky sap is a distinguishing feature, and it serves as a host plant for certain butterfly species.
