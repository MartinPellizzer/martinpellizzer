Nemesia gracillima

Nemesia gracillima, commonly known as the slender nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, featuring slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nemesia gracillima is also valued for its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
