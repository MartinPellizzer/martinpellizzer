Nothotaxus chienii

Nothotaxus chienii, commonly known as the Chinese false yew, is a rare evergreen conifer native to China.

It belongs to the Taxaceae family and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and dark green, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
