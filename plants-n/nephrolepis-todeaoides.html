Nephrolepis todeaoides

Nephrolepis todeaoides, commonly known as the lace fern, is a tropical fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a feathery appearance, giving it a graceful and ornamental look. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
