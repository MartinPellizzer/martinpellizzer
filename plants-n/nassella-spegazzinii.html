Nassella spegazzinii

Nassella spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Nassella spegazzinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soils.
