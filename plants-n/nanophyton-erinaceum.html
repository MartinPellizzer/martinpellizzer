Nanophyton erinaceum

Nanophyton erinaceum, commonly known as the spiny scale plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny clusters of small, scale-like leaves that form a compact, cushion-like structure. The plant is adapted to extreme drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

Due to its specialized morphology and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native environment.
