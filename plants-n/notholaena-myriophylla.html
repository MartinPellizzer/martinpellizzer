Notholaena myriophylla

Notholaena myriophylla, commonly known as the fairy moss, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

Notholaena myriophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in woodland ecosystems.
