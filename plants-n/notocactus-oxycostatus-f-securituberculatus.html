Notocactus oxycostatus f. securituberculatus

Notocactus oxycostatus f. securituberculatus, commonly known as the Honeycomb Cactus, is a unique hybrid of the genus Notocactus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, honeycomb-like tubercles that give it a textured appearance. The plant features a short, thick stem with areoles from which spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding a vibrant contrast to its rugged structure.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unusual morphology and resilience.
