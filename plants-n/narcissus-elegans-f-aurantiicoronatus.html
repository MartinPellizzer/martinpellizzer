Narcissus elegans f. aurantiicoronatus

Narcissus elegans f. aurantiicoronatus, commonly known as the Orange Crown Daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar characterized by its striking orange-yellow floral corona.

This variety belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Iberian Peninsula. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers with a distinctive orange crown. It typically blooms in early spring and is prized for its vibrant color and ornamental value in gardens.

The species is relatively hardy and adaptable, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a bold, seasonal display.
