Nidorella sprengelii

Nidorella sprengelii, commonly known as the Sprengel's orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Nidorella sprengelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
