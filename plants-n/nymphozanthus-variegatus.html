Nymphozanthus variegatus

Nymphozanthus variegatus, commonly known as the variegated flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, dark green leaves that are lance-shaped and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nymphozanthus variegatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
