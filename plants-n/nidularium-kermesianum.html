Nidularium kermesianum

Nidularium kermesianum, commonly known as the Kermesian basket plant, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its unique, basket-like inflorescence. The plant features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. Its foliage is broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Nidularium kermesianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
