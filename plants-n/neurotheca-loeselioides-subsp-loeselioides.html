Neurotheca loeselioides subsp. loeselioides

Neurotheca loeselioides subsp.

loeselioides, commonly known as the Cape dune lily, is a perennial herb native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple.

The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found growing on coastal dunes and is well-suited to coastal gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
