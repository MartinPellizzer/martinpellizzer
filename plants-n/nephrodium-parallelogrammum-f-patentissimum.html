Nephrodium parallelogrammum f. patentissimum

Nephrodium parallelogrammum f. patentissimum, commonly known as the broad-leafed nephroid, is a unique fern species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

The fronds are typically broad and ovate, with a leathery texture and a dark green coloration. The plant exhibits a patent form, meaning the pinnae are spread out rather than folded, which is a distinguishing feature of this form. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique structural adaptations and its role in ecological studies of fern diversity.
