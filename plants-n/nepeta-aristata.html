Nepeta aristata

Nepeta aristata, commonly known as catnip, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often grayish-green with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It is known for its strong scent, which is particularly stimulating to cats.

Nepeta aristata thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal use.
