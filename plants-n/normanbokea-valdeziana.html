Normanbokea valdeziana

Normanbokea valdeziana, commonly known as the Valdez's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is often found growing on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature.
