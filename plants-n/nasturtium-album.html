Nasturtium album

Nasturtium album, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and produces edible seeds and leaves that are often used in culinary applications. Its rapid growth habit makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to attract pollinators.

Nasturtium album is also valued for its ornamental appeal and natural pest-repelling properties.
