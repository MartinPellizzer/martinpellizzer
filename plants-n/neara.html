Neara

Neara, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular flowers. The leaves are typically opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving them their distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
