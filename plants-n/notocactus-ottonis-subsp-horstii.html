Notocactus ottonis subsp. horstii

Notocactus ottonis subsp.

horstii, commonly known as the Horstii Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular to cylindrical stem that is typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne in clusters at the stem tips. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
