Nephrodium omeiense

Nephrodium omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense Kidney Fern, is a rare and unique fern species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped fronds, which are deeply lobed and have a delicate, feathery texture. The plant thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth at high altitudes. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of the fern family.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
