Notocactus lenninghausii var. minor

Notocactus lenninghausii var. minor, commonly known as the miniature notocactus, is a compact cactus species native to Brazil.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive green color and a slightly ribbed texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
