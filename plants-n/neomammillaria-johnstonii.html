Neomammillaria johnstonii

Neomammillaria johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's fairy daisy, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its growth is slow, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to well-drained soils.
