Neuroloma fruticulosum

Neuroloma fruticulosum, commonly known as the fruit-bearing neuro plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate botanical structure.

It features a woody stem with dense clusters of small, fleshy fruits that resemble berries. The plant's leaves are broad and slightly lobed, often with a glossy surface. Its flowering pattern is distinctive, with clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers appearing in the axils of the leaves.

The plant is native to specific regions and is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
