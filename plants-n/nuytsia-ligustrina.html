Nuytsia ligustrina

Nuytsia ligustrina, commonly known as the yellow bough hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and grey-green foliage. The plant has a woody structure with thick, leathery leaves that help it conserve water in arid conditions. It produces small, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

Nuytsia ligustrina is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and inland environments.
