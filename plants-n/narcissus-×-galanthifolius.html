Narcissus × galanthifolius

Narcissus × galanthifolius, commonly known as the spring snowflake, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between narcissus and galanthus.

It is characterized by its delicate, white flowers with a yellow center, resembling the snowflake flower. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters and produces foliage that is narrow and grass-like. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
