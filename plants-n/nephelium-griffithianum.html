Nephelium griffithianum

Nephelium griffithianum, commonly known as the red mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The fruit is round, with a thick, reddish-purple rind and a sweet, segmented white flesh. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
