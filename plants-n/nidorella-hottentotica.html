Nidorella hottentotica

Nidorella hottentotica, commonly known as the Hottentot's orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the arid environment.

Nidorella hottentotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
