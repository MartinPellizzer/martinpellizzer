Notocactus mammulosus f. floricomus

Notocactus mammulosus f. floricomus, commonly known as the showy mammulate cactus, is a unique hybrid within the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are covered in dense, white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming during the spring and summer months. Its foliage is adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water efficiently.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
