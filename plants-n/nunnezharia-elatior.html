Nunnezharia elatior

Nunnezharia elatior, commonly known as the greater nunnezharia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It features upright, slender stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or woodland borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
