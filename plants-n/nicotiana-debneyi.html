Nicotiana debneyi

Nicotiana debneyi, commonly known as the Debney's tobacco, is a species native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or serrated.

Nicotiana debneyi is part of the nightshade family and contains alkaloids, though it is not as well-known for its nicotine content as other Nicotiana species.
