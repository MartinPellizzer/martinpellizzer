Neoporteria nidus var. coimascensis

Neoporteria nidus var. coimascensis, commonly known as the Coimascensis Cactus, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape with spiny, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged exterior. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and specific environmental needs, it is considered a conservation concern.
