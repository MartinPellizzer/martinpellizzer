Nerine krigei

Nerine krigei, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are evergreen and remain throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Nerine krigei is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
