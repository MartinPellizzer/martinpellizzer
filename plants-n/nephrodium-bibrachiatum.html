Nephrodium bibrachiatum

Nephrodium bibrachiatum, commonly known as the two-branched kidney fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces upright, lanceolate pinnae with a serrated margin. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores for propagation.
