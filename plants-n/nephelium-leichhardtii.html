Nephelium leichhardtii

Nephelium leichhardtii, commonly known as the rambutan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, spiny fruits that resemble a hairy ball.
