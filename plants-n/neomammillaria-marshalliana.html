Neomammillaria marshalliana

Neomammillaria marshalliana, commonly known as the Marshall's milk barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
