Neuropeltis maingayi

Neuropeltis maingayi, commonly known as the Malabar orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces small, clustered pseudobulbs. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Neuropeltis maingayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
