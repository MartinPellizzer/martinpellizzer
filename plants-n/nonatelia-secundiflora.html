Nonatelia secundiflora

Nonatelia secundiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Nonatelia secundiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a drought-tolerant plant.
