Nasturtium microspermum var. macilentum

Nasturtium microspermum var. macilentum, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a lesser-known variant of the nasturtium genus.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, bright orange or yellow flowers. This plant is native to South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and edible flowers.

The variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
