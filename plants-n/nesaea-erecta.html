Nesaea erecta

Nesaea erecta, commonly known as the upright nesaea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It is native to regions in the Mediterranean and parts of southern Europe.
