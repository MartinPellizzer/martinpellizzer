Neolitsea Latifolia

Neolitsea latifolia is a remarkable evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its fragrant, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical gardens. The species also plays an important role in local ecosystems and biodiversity.
