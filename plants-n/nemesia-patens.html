Nemesia patens

Nemesia patens, commonly known as the spreading nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, downy texture.

Nemesia patens is often grown as a ground cover or in hanging baskets due to its compact growth and vibrant floral display.
