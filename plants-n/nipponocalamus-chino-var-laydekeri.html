Nipponocalamus chino var. laydekeri

Nipponocalamus chino var. laydekeri, commonly known as the Laydeker's bamboo, is a variant of the Chinese bamboo species.

It is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to East Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically green and glossy. It grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
