Nasturtium stenocarpum

Nasturtium stenocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-fruit nasturtium, is a species native to South America.

It is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its vibrant orange or red flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in coastal regions.

Its ornamental value and edible flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and culinary use.
