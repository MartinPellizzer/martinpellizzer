Nesaea erecta f. glabra

Nesaea erecta f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-flowered nesaea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and smooth, glabrous leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
