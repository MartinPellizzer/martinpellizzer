Nidularium terminale

Nidularium terminale, commonly known as the "Spoonleaf Orchid," is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, often clustered in loose inflorescences. Nidularium terminale thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
