Notodanthonia pallida

Notodanthonia pallida, commonly known as the slender grass or slender danthonia, is a small, perennial grass species native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in open, dry habitats such as sandy soils and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale flowers in summer. It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Notodanthonia pallida is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native fauna.
