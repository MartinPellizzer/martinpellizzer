Navarretia pubescens

Navarretia pubescens, commonly known as the silverleaf navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.
