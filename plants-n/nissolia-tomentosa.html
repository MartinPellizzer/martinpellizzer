Nissolia tomentosa

Nissolia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly nissolia, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are generally oval-shaped with a thick, leathery texture.

Nissolia tomentosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
