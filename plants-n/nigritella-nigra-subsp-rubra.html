Nigritella nigra subsp. rubra

Nigritella nigra subsp.

rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered nigritella, is a member of the Rubiaceae family. It is characterized by its striking red petals and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in dense inflorescences.

It is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
