Neomammillaria confusa

Neomammillaria confusa, commonly known as the fuzzy mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria confusa is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
