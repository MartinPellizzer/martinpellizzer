Najas marina var. riedelii

Najas marina var. riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's watermilfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Najadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, whorled leaves. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen.
