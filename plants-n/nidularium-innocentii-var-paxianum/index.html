Nidularium Innocentii Var. Paxianum

Nidularium innocentii var. paxianum is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This unique plant is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. Due to its endangered status, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses. Its beauty and rarity make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
