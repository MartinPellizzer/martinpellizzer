Neosprucea sararensis

Neosprucea sararensis, commonly known as the Sararensis spruce.

It is a rare evergreen shrub native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America. The plant features needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in dense clusters. Its flowers are small, inconspicuous, and typically appear in clusters during the spring season.

Neosprucea sararensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
