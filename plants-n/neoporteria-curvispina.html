Neoporteria curvispina

Neoporteria curvispina, commonly known as the curly-spined holly.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive, curved spines. The plant features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, providing ornamental value in gardens.

Neoporteria curvispina is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
