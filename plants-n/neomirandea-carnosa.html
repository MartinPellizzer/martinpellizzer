Neomirandea carnosa

Neomirandea carnosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish.
