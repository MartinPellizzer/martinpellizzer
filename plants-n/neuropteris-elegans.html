Neuropteris elegans

Neuropteris elegans, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its spore-producing structures, called sporangia, are clustered in small, stalked clusters on the underside of its fronds.
