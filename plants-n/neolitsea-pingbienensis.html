Neolitsea pingbienensis

Neolitsea pingbienensis, commonly known as the Pingbian sweetwood tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan and Guangxi provinces, where it thrives in subtropical forests. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Neolitsea pingbienensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting local biodiversity and contributing to forest ecosystems.
