Nerine bowdenii subsp. wellsii

Nerine bowdenii subsp.

wellsii, commonly known as the Wells' nerine, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa. It produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with six petals and a prominent central stamen.

The plant features strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in late summer to early autumn.
