Nephrodium callipteris

Nephrodium callipteris, commonly known as the slender kidney fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Nephrodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, clustered sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are responsible for reproduction through spores. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and growth habits make it a notable species in botanical studies.
