Nertera cunninghamii

Nertera cunninghamii, commonly known as the silver ponytail plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a trailing evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features long, thin, silver-gray leaves that are often described as resembling a ponytail. It produces small, pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Nertera cunninghamii is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant in shaded areas due to its preference for indirect light and moderate humidity.
