Nothoscordum × borbonicum

Nothoscordum × borbonicum, commonly known as the hybrid garlic chive, is a cross between *Nothoscordum tuberosum* and *Nothoscordum borbonicum*.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America. The plant features strap-like, aromatic leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild garlic-like scent.

Nothoscordum × borbonicum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and fragrance.
