Nerine × haylockii

Nerine × haylockii, commonly known as the hybrid nerine, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting purple or red throat. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers typically bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
