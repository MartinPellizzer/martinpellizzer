Nephelea arborea

Nephelea arborea, commonly known as the tree nephelea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height, featuring a straight trunk and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy surface, while its bark is smooth and greyish in color.

Nephelea arborea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
