Nidularium microps var. bicense

Nidularium microps var. bicense, commonly known as the miniature nest fern, is a unique variant of the nest fern genus Nidularium.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and small, delicate fronds that resemble miniature nests, hence its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a waxy texture, which helps in retaining moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
