Notocactus horstii var. purpureiflorus

Notocactus horstii var. purpureiflorus, commonly known as the purpleflowered notocactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its columnar stems that often branch into clusters, forming a tree-like structure. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

It is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
