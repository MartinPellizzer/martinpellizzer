Notholaena sulphurea var. quinquefidopalmata

Notholaena sulphurea var. quinquefidopalmata, commonly known as the golden fern, is a variant of the golden maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow fronds that contrast with its dark green stems. The plant has a delicate, lace-like appearance with finely divided, palmate leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
