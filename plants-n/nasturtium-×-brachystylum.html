Nasturtium × brachystylum

Nasturtium × brachystylum, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, making it ideal for container gardening and small spaces. The plant produces vibrant, spurred flowers in shades of orange and red, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a peppery taste, often used in culinary applications.

Nasturtium × brachystylum thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, requiring minimal water and care.
