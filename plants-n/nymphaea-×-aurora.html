Nymphaea × aurora

Nymphaea × aurora, commonly known as the Aurora water lily, is a hybrid aquatic plant belonging to the Nymphaeaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life.

The Aurora water lily is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in water gardens.
