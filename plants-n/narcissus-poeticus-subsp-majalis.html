Narcissus poeticus subsp. majalis

Narcissus poeticus subsp.

majalis, commonly known as the spring narcissus, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the temperate regions of Europe. It produces fragrant, cup-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive central bloom.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its blooming period occurs in early spring, adding a fresh floral display to gardens and natural habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical significance in botanical studies.
