Neorosea suffruticosa

Neorosea suffruticosa, commonly known as the pink-flowered shrub, is a perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, woody structure and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.

Neorosea suffruticosa is relatively low maintenance and can thrive in a variety of garden settings.
