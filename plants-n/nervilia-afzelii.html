Nervilia afzelii

Nervilia afzelii, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Nervilia afzelii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
