Nomocharis × notabilis

Nomocharis × notabilis, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a cross between two species of the Nomocharis genus.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive fragrance. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in floral arrangements.
