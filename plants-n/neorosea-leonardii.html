Neorosea leonardii

Neorosea leonardii, commonly known as the Leonard's rose.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink to red petals. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Neorosea leonardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
