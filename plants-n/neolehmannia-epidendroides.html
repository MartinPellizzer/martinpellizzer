Neolehmannia epidendroides

Neolehmannia epidendroides, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires bright, indirect light.

Neolehmannia epidendroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
