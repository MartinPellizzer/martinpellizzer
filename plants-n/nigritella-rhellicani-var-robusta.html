Nigritella rhellicani var. robusta

Nigritella rhellicani var. robusta, commonly known as the robust nigritella, is a rare and striking plant native to the humid lowland forests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, waxy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires protected environments to thrive.
