Nebelia laevis

Nebelia laevis, commonly known as the smooth nebelia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
