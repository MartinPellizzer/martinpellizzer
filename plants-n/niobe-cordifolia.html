Niobe cordifolia

Niobe cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf niobe, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus, and is known for its showy blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in some regions.
