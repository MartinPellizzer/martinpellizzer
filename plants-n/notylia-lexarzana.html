Notylia lexarzana

Notylia lexarzana, commonly known as the red-flowered notylia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Notylia lexarzana thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
