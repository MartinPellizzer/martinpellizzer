Neolitsea sericea

Neolitsea sericea, commonly known as silverleaf magnolia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring smooth, pale grey bark and lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits containing single seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
