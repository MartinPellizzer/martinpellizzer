Nauclea Uncaria

Nauclea uncaria is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is known for its distinctive, palm-like leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. This species thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds, requiring caution when handling.
