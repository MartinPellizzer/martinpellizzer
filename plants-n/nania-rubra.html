Nania rubra

Nania rubra, commonly known as the red flame fern, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feathery fronds that range in color from deep red to vibrant orange, depending on the light exposure. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental displays.

Nania rubra is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it both aesthetically pleasing and beneficial for indoor air quality.
