Nerium

Nerium, commonly known as the oleander, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae.

It consists of several species, with Nerium oleander being the most well-known. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, white, and yellow. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Nerium is native to regions of southern Europe, North Africa, and western Asia, and it is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes.
