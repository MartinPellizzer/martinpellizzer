Nisomenes diffusa

Nisomenes diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features elliptic to ovate leaves that are typically covered in a silvery or grayish pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, blooming in the summer months. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple or black color, often containing several seeds.

Nisomenes diffusa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and waste areas.
