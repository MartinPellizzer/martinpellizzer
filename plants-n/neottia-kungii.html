Neottia kungii

Neottia kungii, commonly known as the Kung's lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and a long, slender flowering stem that bears a cluster of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Neottia kungii is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing nectar for pollinators.
