Nestegis montana

Nestegis montana, commonly known as the mountain nestegis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a rough texture.

Nestegis montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
