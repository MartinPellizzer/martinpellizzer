Nasa glandulosissima

Nasa glandulosissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in spikes. It is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton.

Nasa glandulosissima is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in some regions.
