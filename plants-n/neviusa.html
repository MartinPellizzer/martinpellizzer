Neviusa

Neviusa, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it its characteristic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. Neviusa thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and drought-resistant properties.
