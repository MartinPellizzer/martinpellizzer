Neea Stellulata

Neea stellulata is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific. It is known for its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found growing in shaded, moist environments. Its foliage is lush and green, with leaves that are often variegated in color. Neea stellulata is a popular choice for gardeners looking to add both beauty and interest to their landscape.
