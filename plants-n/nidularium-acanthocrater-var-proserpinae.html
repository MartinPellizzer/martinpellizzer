Nidularium acanthocrater var. proserpinae

Nidularium acanthocrater var. proserpinae, commonly known as the Purple Heart Plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that ranges from deep purple to reddish hues, providing a dramatic visual effect. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of violet and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental displays.
