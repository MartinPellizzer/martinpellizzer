Nuxia siebenlistii

Nuxia siebenlistii, commonly known as the Siebenlist's nuxia, is a succulent shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in drought conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Nuxia siebenlistii is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a resilient plant in harsh environments.
