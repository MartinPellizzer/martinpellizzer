Neonotonia verdcourtii

Neonotonia verdcourtii, commonly known as the Congo orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to the rainforests of Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Neonotonia verdcourtii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in its native regions.
