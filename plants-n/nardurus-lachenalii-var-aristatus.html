Nardurus lachenalii var. aristatus

Nardurus lachenalii var. aristatus, commonly known as the slender nardus, is a grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences, and it is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
