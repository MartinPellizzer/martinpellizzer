Neomammillaria Tegelbergiana

Neomammillaria tegelbergiana is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is known for its striking appearance, featuring clusters of short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, adding a touch of beauty to its rugged form. Despite its delicate blooms, it is highly resilient, thriving in hot, dry conditions with minimal water. This plant is a favorite among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
