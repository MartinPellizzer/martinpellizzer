Nerissa glandulosa

Nerissa glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular nerissa, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, often hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Acanthaceae family.
