Neorosea petitii

Neorosea petitii, commonly known as the small roseleaf plant, is a flowering species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Neorosea petitii prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or shaded borders.
