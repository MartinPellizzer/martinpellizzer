Neomammillaria polycentra

Neomammillaria polycentra, commonly known as the clustered star cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with multiple areoles covered in white to yellowish woolly hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
