Nagelia denticulata var. nervosa

Nagelia denticulata var. nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed nagel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that have finely serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinct veining pattern.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
