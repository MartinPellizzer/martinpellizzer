Notocactus neohorstii f. juvenaliformis

Notocactus neohorstii f. juvenaliformis, commonly known as the Juvenaliformis Notocactus, is a rare and distinctive cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the stem tips. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts and botanists.
