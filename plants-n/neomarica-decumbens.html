Neomarica decumbens

Neomarica decumbens, commonly known as the decumbent orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a creeping rhizome and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically blooms during the rainy season, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Neomarica decumbens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
