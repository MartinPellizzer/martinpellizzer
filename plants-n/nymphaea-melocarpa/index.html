Nymphaea Melocarpa

Pink water lily, scientifically known as Nymphaea melocarpa, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. Its vibrant pink petals and broad, floating leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental ponds and water gardens. This species thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and lakes. The plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms. With its elegant appearance and ecological significance, the pink water lily is both a beautiful and functional addition to wetland habitats.
