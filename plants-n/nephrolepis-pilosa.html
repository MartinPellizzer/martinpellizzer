Nephrolepis pilosa

Nephrolepis pilosa, commonly known as the hairy sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in various cultures.

The leaves are pinnately compound, with a feathery texture that contributes to its ornamental value.
