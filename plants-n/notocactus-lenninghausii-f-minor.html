Notocactus lenninghausii f. minor

Notocactus lenninghausii f. minor, commonly known as the miniature notocactus, is a rare and compact cactus species native to the coastal regions of southern Brazil.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive rib structure that allows it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to its compact form. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts in controlled environments.
