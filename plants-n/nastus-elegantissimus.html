Nastus elegantissimus

Nastus elegantissimus, commonly known as the elegant nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. The plant features striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a distinctive, ornamental appearance.

Nastus elegantissimus is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
