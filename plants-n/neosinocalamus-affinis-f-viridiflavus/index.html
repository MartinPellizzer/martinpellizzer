Neosinocalamus Affinis F. Viridiflavus

Green-yellow bamboo, scientifically known as Neosinocalamus affinis f. viridiflavus, is a rare and striking variant of the bamboo family native to China. This unique plant is distinguished by its vibrant green-yellow foliage, which sets it apart from typical bamboo species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions where the climate is cool and moist. Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts. Its cultivation remains challenging, making it a rare and precious addition to botanical collections.
