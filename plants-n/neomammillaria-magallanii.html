Neomammillaria magallanii

Neomammillaria magallanii, commonly known as the Magallanii Pincushion Cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines and has a distinctive, rounded shape. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
