Narcissus reflexus

Narcissus reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, particularly found in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers with a yellow perianth and a white or yellow corona. It typically blooms in early spring and is characterized by its reflexed (bent backward) petals.

Narcissus reflexus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
