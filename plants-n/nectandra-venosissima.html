Nectandra venosissima

Nectandra venosissima, commonly known as the veiny sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with prominent venation, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed.

The tree is valued for its durable timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
