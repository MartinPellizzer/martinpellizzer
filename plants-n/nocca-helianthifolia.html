Nocca helianthifolia

Nocca helianthifolia, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

The plant features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that resemble those of the true sorrel genus.
