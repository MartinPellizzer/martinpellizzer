Nephrodium crinitum var. nudatum

Nephrodium crinitum var. nudatum, commonly known as the naked fern, is a unique variant of the nephroid fern characterized by its reduced fronds.

This plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with shorter, more compact fronds compared to the typical variety. Its leaves are usually unbranched and have a smooth, delicate texture. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
