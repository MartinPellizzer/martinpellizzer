Neomammillaria capensis

Neomammillaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape milkweed cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to South Africa.

It features a compact, globular stem with a greenish-gray color and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal water once established.

Neomammillaria capensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
