Nama origanifolia var. rotundifolia

Nama origanifolia var. rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf sage, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves that are round or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional medicine.
