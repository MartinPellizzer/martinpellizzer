Nasturtium spinescens

Nasturtium spinescens, commonly known as the spiny nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom throughout the summer. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and is known for its peppery taste, similar to watercress. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium spinescens is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs.
