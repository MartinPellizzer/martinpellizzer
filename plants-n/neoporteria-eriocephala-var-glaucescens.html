Neoporteria eriocephala var. glaucescens

Neoporteria eriocephala var. glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-stemmed spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features upright, branching stems that are often tinged with a blue or bluish-green hue. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are borne in clusters and are surrounded by soft, woolly bracts.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine, sticky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
