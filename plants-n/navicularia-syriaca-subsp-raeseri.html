Navicularia syriaca subsp. raeseri

Navicularia syriaca subsp.

raeseri, commonly known as the Syrian navicular, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purplish in color.

It is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
