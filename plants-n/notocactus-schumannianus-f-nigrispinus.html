Notocactus schumannianus f. nigrispinus

Notocactus schumannianus f. nigrispinus, commonly known as the Black Spine Cactus, is a fascinating member of the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its dark spines. It is native to Brazil and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
