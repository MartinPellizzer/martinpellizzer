Neoporteria atroviridis

Neoporteria atroviridis, commonly known as the dark-green starling plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are typically yellow with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Neoporteria atroviridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
