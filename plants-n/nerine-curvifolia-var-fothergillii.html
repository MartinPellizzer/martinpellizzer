Nerine curvifolia var. fothergillii

Nerine curvifolia var. fothergillii, commonly known as the Fothergill's nerine, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive curved shape. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
