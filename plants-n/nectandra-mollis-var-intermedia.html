Nectandra mollis var. intermedia

Nectandra mollis var. intermedia, commonly known as the yellow sandstone tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, oily leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically green when immature and turn reddish when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction.
