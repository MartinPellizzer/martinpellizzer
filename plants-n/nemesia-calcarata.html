Nemesia calcarata

Nemesia calcarata, commonly known as the rock nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage is typically gray-green, with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

Nemesia calcarata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
