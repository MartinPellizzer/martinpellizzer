Narcissus × humei

Narcissus × humei, commonly known as the Dutch hybrid narcissus, is a flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Narcissus cyclamineus and Narcissus tazetta, resulting in a plant with distinctive characteristics. The flowers are typically white with a yellow center and have a strong, sweet fragrance. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
