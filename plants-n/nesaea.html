Nesaea

Nesaea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and Australia, often growing in moist, shaded environments. Nesaea species typically have slender, upright stems and produce delicate, spider-like flowers with intricate patterns. The flowers are usually small, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that resembles a spider's abdomen.

These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
