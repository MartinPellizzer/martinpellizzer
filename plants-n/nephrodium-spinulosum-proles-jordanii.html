Nephrodium spinulosum proles jordanii

Nephrodium spinulosum proles jordanii, commonly known as the Jordanian spinulose nephroid, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, spiny projections, giving it a rugged appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils with limited moisture.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptation to harsh conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
