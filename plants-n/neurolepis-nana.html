Neurolepis nana

Neurolepis nana, commonly known as the dwarf neurolepis, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features compact, upright stems with small, clustered leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in late spring to early summer.

Neurolepis nana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
