Neomammillaria eschauzieri

Neomammillaria eschauzieri, commonly known as the Eschauzier's milk barrel cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in white, radial spines and prominent areoles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers in late spring, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
