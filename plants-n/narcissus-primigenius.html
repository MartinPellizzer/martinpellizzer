Narcissus primigenius

Narcissus primigenius, commonly known as the Giant Daffodil, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with a prominent central corona. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The bulbs are bulbous and produce multiple stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves.

It is a hardy perennial that blooms in early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to gardens.
