Neriandra aurantiaca

Neriandra aurantiaca, commonly known as the red-flowered neriandra, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which bloom in spring and attract various pollinators. It typically grows in coastal areas and is often found in heathlands and sand dunes.

Neriandra aurantiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
