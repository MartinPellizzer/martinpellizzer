Niruris

Niruris, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, white or pale purple flowers and has a distinctive silvery foliage that gives it its common name. Niruris is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
