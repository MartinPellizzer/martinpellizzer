Neoregelia lillyae

Neoregelia lillyae, commonly known as the Lily's Neoregelia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, colorful bracts that resemble petals, giving it a floral appearance. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. Its rosette-shaped leaves are often covered in a dense, fibrous layer that helps retain moisture.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
