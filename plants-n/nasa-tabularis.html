Nasa tabularis

Nasa tabularis, commonly known as the air plant, is a type of bromeliad that belongs to the Tillandsia genus.

It is a epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has a rosette-shaped structure with tightly packed leaves that collect water and nutrients from the air. Its ability to absorb moisture through its leaves makes it well adapted to humid environments.

Nasa tabularis is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
