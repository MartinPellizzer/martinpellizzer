Neonauclea excelsioides

Neonauclea excelsioides, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals and are usually pink or red in color.

Neonauclea excelsioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
