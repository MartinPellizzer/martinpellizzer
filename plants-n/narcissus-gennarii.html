Narcissus gennarii

Narcissus gennarii, commonly known as the Italian daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, particularly found in regions like Italy and the Iberian Peninsula. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers with bright yellow petals and a white or greenish center. It typically grows in rocky or open habitats and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Narcissus gennarii is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
