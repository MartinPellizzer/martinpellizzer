Neoregelia margaretae

Neoregelia margaretae, commonly known as the Margaretae Sword Fern, is a species of bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces showy, long-lasting inflorescences that range from red to orange, adding visual interest to its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia margaretae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
