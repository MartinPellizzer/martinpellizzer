Novella

Novella, commonly known as the common yew.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree or shrub belonging to the family Taxaceae. The plant is characterized by its reddish bark, needle-like leaves, and small, red berries that contain seeds. It is native to the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in temperate climates.

The yew has a long history of use in traditional medicine and as a symbol in various cultures.
