Noticastrum montevidense

Noticastrum montevidense, commonly known as the Montevidense tamarind, is a species of leguminous tree native to South America, particularly Uruguay and southern Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it ecologically significant in certain regions.
