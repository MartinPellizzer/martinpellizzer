Neuwiedia amboinensis

Neuwiedia amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina orchid, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous small, clustered flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Neuwiedia amboinensis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
