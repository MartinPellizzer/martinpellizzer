Notocactus tabularis subsp. bommeljei

Notocactus tabularis subsp.

bommeljei, commonly known as the Bommeljei cactus, is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height.

The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in the evening. Its stems are covered in a thick, waxy cuticle that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
