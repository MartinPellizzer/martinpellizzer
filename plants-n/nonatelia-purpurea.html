Nonatelia purpurea

Nonatelia purpurea, commonly known as the purple nonatelia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Nonatelia purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
