Nasturtium crispum

Nasturtium crispum, commonly known as the curled nasturtium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, adding a peppery flavor to salads and dishes.

Additionally, the plant is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
