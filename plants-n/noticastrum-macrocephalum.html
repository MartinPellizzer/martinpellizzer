Noticastrum macrocephalum

Noticastrum macrocephalum, commonly known as the large-headed milkwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and coastal forest regions. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
