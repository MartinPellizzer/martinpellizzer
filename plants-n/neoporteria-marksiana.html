Neoporteria marksiana

Neoporteria marksiana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds.

Neoporteria marksiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
