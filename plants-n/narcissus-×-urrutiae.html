Narcissus × urrutiae

Narcissus × urrutiae, commonly known as the Giant Paperwhite, is a hybrid narcissus species characterized by its large, white, cup-shaped flowers.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is known for its strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with broad, strap-like leaves. Its bulbs are relatively large and require well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in gardens for its elegant appearance and winter blooming habit.
