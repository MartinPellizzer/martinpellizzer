Nama origanifolia var. pedunculata

Nama origanifolia var. pedunculata, commonly known as the Cape York germander, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
