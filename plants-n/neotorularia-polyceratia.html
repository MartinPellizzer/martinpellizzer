Neotorularia polyceratia

Neotorularia polyceratia, commonly known as the horned stromboid, is a species of sea snail belonging to the family Strombidae.

It is characterized by its elongated, spiral shell that is often adorned with prominent, horn-like spires. The shell's surface is typically smooth and may exhibit a range of colors, including white, yellow, or brown. This marine gastropod is found in tropical and subtropical waters, often inhabiting shallow coastal areas.

Its anatomy includes a well-developed radula and a muscular foot used for locomotion across rocky substrates.
