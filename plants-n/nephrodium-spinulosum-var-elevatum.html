Nephrodium spinulosum var. elevatum

Nephrodium spinulosum var. elevatum, commonly known as the elevated spiny nephroid, is a rare and unique variant of the nephroid plant family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny morphology and leaf structure, which sets it apart from other members of the genus. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in specific microhabitats, often found in rocky or limestone-rich soils.

Due to its limited distribution and unique features, it is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptation and biodiversity.
