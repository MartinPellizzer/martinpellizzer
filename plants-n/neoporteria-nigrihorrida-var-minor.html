Neoporteria nigrihorrida var. minor

Neoporteria nigrihorrida var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf black-hairy neoporteria, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small stature, typically reaching only a few centimeters in height, and its distinctive dark, hairy foliage. The leaves are usually dark green with a dense covering of fine, blackish hairs, giving it a striking appearance. This variant is notable for its compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
