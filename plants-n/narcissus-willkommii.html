It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, particularly found in regions like Turkey and the Balkans. It typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Narcissus willkommii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
