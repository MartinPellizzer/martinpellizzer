Nasturtium hortense

Nasturtium hortense, commonly known as garden nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright, showy flowers that come in shades of orange, yellow, and red. The plant produces edible leaves and seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with trailing or climbing stems depending on the variety.

Nasturtium hortense thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.
