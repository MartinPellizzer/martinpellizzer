Nostelis minor

Nostelis minor, commonly known as the lesser nostelis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of white or pale blue.

Nostelis minor is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
