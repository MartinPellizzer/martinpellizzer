Nasturtium floridanum

Nasturtium floridanum, commonly known as Florida watercress, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves with a peppery, watercress-like flavor, and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant grows in moist, shady environments such as swamps and stream banks. Its leaves are edible and often used in salads or as a garnish.

The flowers are also attractive to pollinators like bees and butterflies.
