Nezera carnea

Nezera carnea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its fragrant blooms. Nezera carnea thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections and indoor gardening.
