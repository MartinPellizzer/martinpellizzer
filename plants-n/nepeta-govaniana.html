Nepeta govaniana

Nepeta govaniana, commonly known as the Kashmiri catnip, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers arranged in whorled clusters. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Nepeta govaniana is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
