Narcissus majalis

Narcissus majalis, commonly known as the spring narcissus or the white narcissus, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces showy, white flowers with a yellow center, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.

Narcissus majalis is also known for its ability to naturalize and form dense colonies over time.
