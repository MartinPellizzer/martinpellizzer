Nasturtium Sisymbrioides

Nasturtium sisymbrioides is a vibrant flowering plant known for its bright orange and yellow blooms. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is native to regions in North America. This plant is often grown as a decorative and edible species in gardens. Its leaves and flowers are commonly used in salads and culinary dishes. Nasturtium sisymbrioides also serves as a natural pest repellent, making it a valuable addition to organic gardening.
