Nicoraella venusta

Nicoraella venusta, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped lip. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season, and are pollinated by specific insect species.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
