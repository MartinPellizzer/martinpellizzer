Nephrodium excellens

Nephrodium excellens, commonly known as the golden kidney fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its large, intricate fronds that resemble the shape of a kidney. This unique characteristic makes it a popular specimen in botanical gardens and a subject of scientific interest.
