Nectandra pichurim var. cuprea

Nectandra pichurim var. cuprea, commonly known as the red-flowered nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and striking red flowers. Its wood is valued for its durability and aromatic properties, making it useful in traditional crafts and construction.
