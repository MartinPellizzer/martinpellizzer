Neomammillaria nejapensis var. aureispina

Neomammillaria nejapensis var. aureispina, commonly known as the golden spined mammillaria, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to bluish-green body with prominent, golden-yellow spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
