Nubigena tenerifa

Nubigena tenerifa, commonly known as the Canary Island pine, is a unique evergreen tree native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The plant has needle-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems. It produces small, reddish-brown cones that contain seeds.

Nubigena tenerifa is well adapted to the volcanic soils and coastal climates of its native habitat.
