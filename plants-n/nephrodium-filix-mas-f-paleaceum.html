Nephrodium filix-mas f. paleaceum

Nephrodium filix-mas f. paleaceum, commonly known as the paleaceum fern, is a rare and unique variant of the common nephrolepis.

It is characterized by its pale, almost translucent fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant exhibits a delicate, feathery structure with small, lance-shaped pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its morphological variation and potential ecological role in specific microhabitats.
