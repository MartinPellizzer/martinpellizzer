Nasturtium fontanum

Nasturtium fontanum, commonly known as the watercress nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near streams or in damp meadows. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor, similar to watercress.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
