Nephelium callarrie

Nephelium callarrie, commonly known as the mangostan, is a tropical fruit-bearing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen canopy and smooth, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that develop into the familiar purple-skinned fruits. Its fruit contains a segmented, white, juicy aril that is rich in antioxidants and nutrients.
