Nuxia capitata

Nuxia capitata, commonly known as the knobthorn tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive knobby thorns and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

Nuxia capitata is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
