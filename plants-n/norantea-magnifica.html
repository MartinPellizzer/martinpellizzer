Norantea magnifica

Norantea magnifica, commonly known as the Royal Flame Orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display vibrant red and orange hues, often with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its native environment. It thrives in warm, moist conditions and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorching.

Norantea magnifica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
