Nymphozanthus variegatus var. lutescens

Nymphozanthus variegatus var. lutescens, commonly known as the yellow variegated zanthus, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage featuring shades of yellow, green, and white. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
