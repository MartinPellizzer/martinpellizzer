Notocactus floricomus

Notocactus floricomus, commonly known as the Showy Notocactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the warmer months. Its growth habit is typically solitary or in small clusters, and it has a slow growth rate.

The plant is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
