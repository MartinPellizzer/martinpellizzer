Neocalyptrocalyx nectarius

Neocalyptrocalyx nectarius, commonly known as the honeycomb orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.
