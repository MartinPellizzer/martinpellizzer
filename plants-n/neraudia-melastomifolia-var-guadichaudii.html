Neraudia melastomifolia var. guadichaudii

Neraudia melastomifolia var. guadichaudii, commonly known as the Guadichaudii variant of the melastomaceae family, is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to regions of South America.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
