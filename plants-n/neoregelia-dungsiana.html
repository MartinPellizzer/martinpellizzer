Neoregelia dungsiana

Neoregelia dungsiana, commonly known as the Dungsian bromeliad, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant features vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange. Its leaves are typically broad, glossy, and have a waxy texture.

Neoregelia dungsiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
