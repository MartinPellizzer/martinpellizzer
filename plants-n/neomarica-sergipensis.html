Neomarica sergipensis

Neomarica sergipensis, commonly known as the Sergipe orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, producing blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Neomarica sergipensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
