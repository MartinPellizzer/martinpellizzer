Nidularium compactum

Nidularium compactum, commonly known as the compact ground orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
