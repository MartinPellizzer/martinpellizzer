Neoregelia pontualii

Neoregelia pontualii, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange and yellow, creating a visually stunning display. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports small aquatic life. Its flowering stems produce numerous white or pale pink flowers, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Neoregelia pontualii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
