Nerium sibiricum

Nerium sibiricum, commonly known as the Siberian oleander, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture.

Nerium sibiricum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
