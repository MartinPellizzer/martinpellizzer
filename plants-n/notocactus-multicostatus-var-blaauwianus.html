Notocactus multicostatus var. blaauwianus

Notocactus multicostatus var. blaauwianus, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Cactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a bluish-gray coating, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique features, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts and botanists.
