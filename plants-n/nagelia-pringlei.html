Nagelia pringlei

Nagelia pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's nagel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

Nagelia pringlei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
