Nymphaea stellata punctata

Nymphaea stellata punctata, commonly known as the starry water lily, is a aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features showy, white to pink flowers with a star-like pattern, which bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its roots anchor it in muddy substrates, while its stems support the foliage.

Nymphaea stellata punctata plays a role in water purification and serves as a food source for aquatic life.
