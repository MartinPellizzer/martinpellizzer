Nasturtium Integrifolium

Nasturtium integrifolium is a vibrant flowering plant known for its bright orange and yellow blooms. It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is native to South America. This plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and flowers. It also has edible leaves and seeds that are used in culinary applications. Its ability to thrive in various conditions makes it a popular choice for gardeners.
