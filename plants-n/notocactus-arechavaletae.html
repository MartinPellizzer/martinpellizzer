Notocactus arechavaletae

Notocactus arechavaletae, commonly known as the Arechavaleta's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
