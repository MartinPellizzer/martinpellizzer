Nonea pulla subsp. pulla

Nonea pulla subsp.

pulla, commonly known as the small-flowered Nonea, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink to purple in color, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
