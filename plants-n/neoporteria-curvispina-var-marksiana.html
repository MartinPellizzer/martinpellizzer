Neoporteria curvispina var. marksiana

Neoporteria curvispina var. marksiana, commonly known as the Mark's slender-stemmed neoporteria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
