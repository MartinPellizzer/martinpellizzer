Nekemias megalophylla

Nekemias megalophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf cycad, is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, typical of gymnosperms, and its reproductive structures are essential for its propagation. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their aesthetic value.

The species is also of interest to botanists for its evolutionary adaptations and ecological significance in its native habitat.
