Nasa modesta

Nasa modesta, commonly known as the modest nasa, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a mild, tangy flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, Nasa modesta is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
