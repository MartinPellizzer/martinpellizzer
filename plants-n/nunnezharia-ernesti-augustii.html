Nunnezharia ernesti-augustii

Nunnezharia ernesti-augustii, commonly known as the royal fern, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant features a stout, underground rhizome that supports its rapid growth and spreading habit. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, ovate leaflets arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
