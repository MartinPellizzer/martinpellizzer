Neoporteria subgibbosa f. castanea

Neoporteria subgibbosa f. castanea, commonly known as the red-flowered neoporteria, is a unique variant of the neoporteria genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which contrast with its green foliage, making it a visually appealing plant. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas, often in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
