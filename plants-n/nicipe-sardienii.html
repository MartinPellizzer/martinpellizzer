Nicipe sardienii

Nicipe sardienii, commonly known as the Sardinian orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the island of Sardinia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring, producing pinkish-white petals with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forested areas or along stream banks.
