Neja nidorelloides

Neja nidorelloides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Neja nidorelloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
