Notocactus pseudoherteri

Notocactus pseudoherteri, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically blue or bluish-green in color, which help it reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value. It is a slow-growing plant that requires well-draining soil and minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and xeriscaping.
