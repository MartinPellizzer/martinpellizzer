Notanthera orbicularis

Notanthera orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf notanthera, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly circular, leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open habitats.

Notanthera orbicularis is an herbaceous perennial that is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
