Neuberia humilis

Neuberia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf neuberia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features simple, opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Neuberia humilis is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
