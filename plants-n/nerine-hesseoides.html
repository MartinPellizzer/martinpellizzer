Nerine hesseoides

Nerine hesseoides, commonly known as the Hesse's nerine, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive trumpet shape. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are often evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Nerine hesseoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
