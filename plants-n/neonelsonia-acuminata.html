Neonelsonia acuminata

Neonelsonia acuminata, commonly known as the slender sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wetland or swampy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often green or brown in color. Neonelsonia acuminata is characterized by its erect, slender stems and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds.
