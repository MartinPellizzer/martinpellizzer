Nierembergia hippomanica var. densa

Nierembergia hippomanica var. densa, commonly known as the dense flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
