Neogaillonia hymenostephana

Neogaillonia hymenostephana, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Neogaillonia hymenostephana is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
