Neillia opulifolia var. mollis

Neillia opulifolia var. mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green with a soft texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Neillia opulifolia var. mollis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
