Narcissus jonquilla f. henriquesii

Narcissus jonquilla f. henriquesii, commonly known as the Henriquesii narcissus, is a rare and elegant variant of the Jonquilla daffodil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. This subspecies is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained, sunny soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
