Nicotiana obtusifolia var. palmeri

Nicotiana obtusifolia var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's Nicotiana, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky soils. It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and tobacco.
