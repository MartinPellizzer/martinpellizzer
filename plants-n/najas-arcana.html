Najas arcana

Najas arcana, commonly known as the sacred water plant, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Najadaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are often spirally arranged around the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically submerged or just at the water's surface. Najas arcana is known for its ability to thrive in still or slow-moving waters, often found in ponds, lakes, and wetlands.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
