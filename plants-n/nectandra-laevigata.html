Nectandra laevigata

Nectandra laevigata, commonly known as the smooth nectandra, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a dark, woody berry containing several seeds.

Nectandra laevigata is valued for its timber and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
