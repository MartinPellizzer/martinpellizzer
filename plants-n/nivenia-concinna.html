Nivenia concinna

Nivenia concinna, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Nivenia concinna is well adapted to sandy and rocky soils, making it a hardy species in dry climates.
