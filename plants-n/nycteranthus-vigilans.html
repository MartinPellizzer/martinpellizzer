Nycteranthus vigilans

Nycteranthus vigilans, commonly known as the night-flowering morning glory, is a member of the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that open at night and close in the morning. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique blooming pattern.
