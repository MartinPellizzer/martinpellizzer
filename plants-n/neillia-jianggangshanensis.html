Neillia jianggangshanensis

Neillia jianggangshanensis, commonly known as the Jianggangshan lily, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces a single flower per stem, making it a unique addition to botanical collections.
