Notocactus spegazzinii

Notocactus spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Bolivia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and prominent areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
