Narcissus × juratensis

Narcissus × juratensis, commonly known as the Dutch narcissus, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow. The plant is native to the Netherlands and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile garden plant.

The bulbs of Narcissus × juratensis are perennial, allowing for reliable growth and flowering year after year.
