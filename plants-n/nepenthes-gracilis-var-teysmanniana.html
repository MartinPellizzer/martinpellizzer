Nepenthes gracilis var. teysmanniana

Nepenthes gracilis var. teysmanniana, commonly known as the slender pitcher plant, is a carnivorous plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow pitchers that are often green or red in color. The plant produces nectar to attract insects, which then fall into the pitcher and are digested by the plant's digestive enzymes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of light for optimal growth.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
