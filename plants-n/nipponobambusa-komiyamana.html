Nipponobambusa komiyamana

Nipponobambusa komiyamana, commonly known as the Komiyama bamboo, is a species native to Japan.

It is a clumping bamboo that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features slender, green culms with a smooth, pale surface. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
