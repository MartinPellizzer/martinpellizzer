Notocactus ferrugineus

Notocactus ferrugineus, commonly known as the rusty notocactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are typically modified into spines, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Notocactus ferrugineus is well-suited for cultivation in rocky or well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for desert gardens.
