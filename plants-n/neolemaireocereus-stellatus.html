Neolemaireocereus stellatus

Neolemaireocereus stellatus, commonly known as the Star Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green and covered with small, white spines. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
