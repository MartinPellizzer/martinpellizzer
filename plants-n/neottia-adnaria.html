Neottia adnaria

Neottia adnaria, commonly known as the Adnara orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. The plant has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Its structure and growth habits reflect adaptations to its specific ecological niche.
