Nolana tarapacana

Nolana tarapacana, commonly known as the "Cordillera flower" or "Puya," is a flowering plant native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

Nolana tarapacana is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Andean medicine.
