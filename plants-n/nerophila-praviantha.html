Nerophila praviantha

Nerophila praviantha, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Nerophila praviantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
