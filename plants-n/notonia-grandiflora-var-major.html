Notonia grandiflora var. major

Notonia grandiflora var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered notonia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
