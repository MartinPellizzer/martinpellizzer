Notocactus stegmannii

Notocactus stegmannii, commonly known as the Stiegmann's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to dry environments.

Notocactus stegmannii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
