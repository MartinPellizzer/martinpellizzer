Neoregelia cathcartii

Neoregelia cathcartii, commonly known as the Crimson Beauty, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, red bracts that surround its flowering spikes, creating a visually dramatic display. The plant has a rosette-shaped leaf structure that holds water, providing a habitat for small aquatic organisms. Its foliage is often variegated with green and silver tones, adding to its ornamental value.

Neoregelia cathcartii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
