Notocactus ritterianus

Notocactus ritterianus, commonly known as the Ritter's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
