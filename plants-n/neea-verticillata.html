Neea verticillata

Neea verticillata, commonly known as the clustered neee, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences and elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with five petals and a distinct central column.

Neea verticillata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
