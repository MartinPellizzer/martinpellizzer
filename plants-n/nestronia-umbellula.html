Nestronia umbellula

Nestronia umbellula, commonly known as the umbrella lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or near water bodies. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Nestronia umbellula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
