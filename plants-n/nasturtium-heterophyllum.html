Nasturtium heterophyllum

Nasturtium heterophyllum, commonly known as the Indian cress, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its alternate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, edible yellow or orange flowers. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine regions.

Nasturtium heterophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
