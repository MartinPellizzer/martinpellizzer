Nipponanthemum nipponicum

Nipponanthemum nipponicum, commonly known as the Japanese marguerite, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream sides. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Nipponanthemum nipponicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
