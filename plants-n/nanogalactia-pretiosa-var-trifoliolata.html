Nanogalactia pretiosa var. trifoliolata

Nanogalactia pretiosa var. trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaf beauty, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
