Nephrodium lacerum var. uniforme

Nephrodium lacerum var. uniforme, commonly known as the uniform fringed fern, is a variant of the fringed fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures that are typically divided into narrow, serrated segments. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a creeping rhizome that supports its growth habit. Its leaves are often described as having a lacerated appearance due to their finely divided margins.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to shaded environments.
