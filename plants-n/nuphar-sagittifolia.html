Nuphar sagittifolia

Nuphar sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead water plant, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots are submerged in the mud. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine by indigenous peoples.

Nuphar sagittifolia plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
