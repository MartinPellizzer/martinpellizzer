Nonea echioides

Nonea echioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf nonea, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and gravelly soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Nonea echioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
