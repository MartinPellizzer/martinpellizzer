Neesia maritima

Neesia maritima, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical, with a slightly rough texture.

Neesia maritima is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
