Nerium oleander

Nerium oleander, commonly known as the oleander, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, white, and yellow. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a woody stem, making it easily identifiable. Oleander is known for its resilience, thriving in hot, dry climates and poor soil conditions.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic if ingested.
