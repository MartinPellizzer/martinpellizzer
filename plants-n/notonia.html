Notonia

Notonia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the genus *Strychnos* in the family Loganiaceae. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Notonia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
