Noaea spinosissima

Noaea spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny noaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a bushy growth habit and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
