Nephrodium usitatum

Nephrodium usitatum, commonly known as the common kidney fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Nephrolepidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a serrated margin and a dense arrangement of small, clustered sori on the underside.

Nephrodium usitatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
