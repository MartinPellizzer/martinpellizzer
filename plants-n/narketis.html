Narketis

Narketis, commonly known as the common daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its composite flower heads, which consist of a central disc surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. It blooms in spring and summer, producing bright yellow to white flowers.

Narketis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
