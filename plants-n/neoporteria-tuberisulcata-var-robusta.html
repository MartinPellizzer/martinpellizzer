Neoporteria tuberisulcata var. robusta

Neoporteria tuberisulcata var. robusta, commonly known as the robust tuberous orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive tuberous roots and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
