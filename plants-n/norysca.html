Norysca

Norysca, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blossoms that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth. Its roots are fibrous and adapt well to rocky or sandy environments.

Norysca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
