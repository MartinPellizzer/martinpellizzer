Nasturtium officinale f. subasarifolium

Nasturtium officinale f. subasarifolium, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a compact variant of the common nasturtium.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are slightly smaller than those of the standard species. The flowers are typically bright orange or red, with a spurred petal at the back. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible foliage.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens and containers.
