Numisaureum acuminatum

Numisaureum acuminatum, commonly known as the sharp-tipped numis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant prefers well-drained soils and full sun exposure, thriving in arid or semi-arid environments. Its unique morphology and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
