Navaea

Navaea, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking, often blue or purple, ray flowers and silver-green foliage. These plants are typically found in coastal areas, where they thrive in salty, well-drained soils. Navaea species are known for their hardiness and ability to withstand harsh maritime conditions.
