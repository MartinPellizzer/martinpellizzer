Narcissus Pseudonarcissus F. Serratus

Serrated daffodil, scientifically known as Narcissus pseudonarcissus f. serratus, is a striking variant of the common daffodil prized for its unique foliage. This plant features jagged, serrated leaves that contrast beautifully with its vibrant yellow blooms. Native to the British Isles, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Despite its delicate appearance, the serrated daffodil is resilient and easy to cultivate for both novice and experienced gardeners.
