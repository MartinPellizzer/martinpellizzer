Nestotus stenophyllus

Nestotus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nestotus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrow and linear in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale in color, often white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
