Neocollettia gracilis

Neocollettia gracilis, commonly known as the slender lemur orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the rainforests of Madagascar. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, silky hair, contributing to its unique appearance.

Neocollettia gracilis is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanical conservation efforts.
