Navicularia heisteri

Navicularia heisteri, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Navicularia heisteri thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
