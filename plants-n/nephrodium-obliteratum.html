Nephrodium obliteratum

Nephrodium obliteratum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a rare and endangered species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae, characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that grow in a compact, upright form. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
