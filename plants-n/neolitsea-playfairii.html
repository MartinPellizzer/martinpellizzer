Neolitsea playfairii

Neolitsea playfairii, commonly known as the Chinese laurustin, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant features simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by spherical berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Neolitsea playfairii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
