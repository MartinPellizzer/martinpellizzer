Nidularium concentricum

Nidularium concentricum, commonly known as the concentric begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive pattern of veins. Its flowers typically have five petals, with the inner petals being larger and more brightly colored than the outer ones.

Nidularium concentricum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
