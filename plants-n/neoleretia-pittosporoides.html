Neoleretia pittosporoides

Neoleretia pittosporoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Neoleretia pittosporoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
