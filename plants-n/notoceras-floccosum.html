Notoceras floccosum

Notoceras floccosum, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Notoceras floccosum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
