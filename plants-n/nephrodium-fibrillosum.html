Nephrodium fibrillosum

Nephrodium fibrillosum, commonly known as the fibrous kidney fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, finely divided fronds that resemble the structure of a kidney, hence its scientific name. Nephrodium fibrillosum plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystems.
