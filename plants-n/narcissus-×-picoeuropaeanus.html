Narcissus × picoeuropaeanus

Narcissus × picoeuropaeanus, commonly known as the Pico Europa narcissus, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between various narcissus species.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically features narrow, strap-like leaves and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for spring-flowering displays.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is often cultivated for its unique floral characteristics and adaptability to different climates.
