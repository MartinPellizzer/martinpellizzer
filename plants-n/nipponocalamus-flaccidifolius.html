Nipponocalamus flaccidifolius

Nipponocalamus flaccidifolius, commonly known as the flaccidifolius bamboo, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
