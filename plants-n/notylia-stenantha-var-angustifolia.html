Notylia stenantha var. angustifolia

Notylia stenantha var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved notylia, is a variant of the notylia species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with linear, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and relatively rare species within the Notylia genus.
