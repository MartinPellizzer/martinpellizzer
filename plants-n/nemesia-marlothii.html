Nemesia marlothii

Nemesia marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a low-growing habit with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. Nemesia marlothii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
