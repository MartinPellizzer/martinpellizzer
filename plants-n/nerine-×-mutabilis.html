Nerine × mutabilis

Nerine × mutabilis, commonly known as the hybrid lily or the variable nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to naturalize and return year after year.
