Neottia oblonga

It is a small, herbaceous plant with a short, creeping rhizome and a single, erect stem. The plant produces two opposite, broad, ovate leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are pale green or white, with three sepals and three petals, and are typically arranged in a spike.

Neottia oblonga is often found in moist, shaded woodland habitats and is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
