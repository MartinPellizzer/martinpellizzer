Nicotiana kawakamii

Nicotiana kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's tobacco, is a species native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is closely related to other tobacco species.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
