Narcissus ajax

Narcissus ajax, commonly known as the Dutch narcissus, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single, upright flower with a cup-shaped corona. The bloom is typically white or yellow, with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular garden plant.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
