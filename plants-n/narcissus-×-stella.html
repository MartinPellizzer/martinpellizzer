Narcissus × stella

Narcissus × stella, commonly known as the Stella daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Narcissus.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a single stem with a cluster of blooms, making it a popular choice for gardeners. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, turning yellow as the plant enters dormancy.
