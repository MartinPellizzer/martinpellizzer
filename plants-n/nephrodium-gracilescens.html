Nephrodium gracilescens

Nephrodium gracilescens, commonly known as the slender kidney fern, is a delicate fern species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a graceful, arching appearance that gives it its common name. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are characterized by a finely divided structure, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Nephrodium gracilescens is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities and ability to thrive in controlled indoor settings.
