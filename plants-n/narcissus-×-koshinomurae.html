Narcissus × koshinomurae

Narcissus × koshinomurae, commonly known as the Koshinomura daffodil, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a prominent central corona. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–50 cm, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to Japan, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
