Neomarica itatiaica

Neomarica itatiaica, commonly known as the Itatiaia lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Oncidium genus and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and produces inflorescences that can reach up to 3 feet in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Neomarica itatiaica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
