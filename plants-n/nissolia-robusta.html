Nissolia robusta

Nissolia robusta, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Nissolia robusta thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
