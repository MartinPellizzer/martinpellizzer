Neea duckei

Neea duckei, commonly known as the Guangxi neea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Guangxi region, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are elliptical and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

Neea duckei is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is adapted to thrive in acidic soils.
