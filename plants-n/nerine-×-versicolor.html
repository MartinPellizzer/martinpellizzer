Nerine × versicolor

Nerine × versicolor, commonly known as the pink iris lily, is a hybrid perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive violet throat. The plant produces strap-like, grassy leaves that are evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens during that time.
