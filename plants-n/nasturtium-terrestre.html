Nasturtium terrestre

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The plant produces edible leaves and seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. Its stems are hairy and trailing, allowing it to spread across the ground.
