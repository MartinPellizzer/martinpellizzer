Neoregelia abendrothae

Neoregelia abendrothae, commonly known as the silver star bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of broad, silver-green leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences with red, orange, and yellow bracts, which appear in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia abendrothae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
