Neoregelia indecora

Neoregelia indecora, commonly known as the Ornate Sword Fern, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage that displays a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Its rosette-shaped leaves form a central cup that can collect water, supporting small aquatic life.

Neoregelia indecora is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
