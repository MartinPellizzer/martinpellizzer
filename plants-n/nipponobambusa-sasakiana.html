Nipponobambusa sasakiana

Nipponobambusa sasakiana, commonly known as the Sasakawa bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and fine, narrow leaves. This bamboo is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments. It has a clumping growth habit and is relatively cold-hardy compared to other bamboo species.

The plant is also valued for its use in traditional Japanese crafts and landscaping.
