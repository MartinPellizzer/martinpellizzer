Nycteranthus noctiflorus

Its leaves are typically dark green and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in the humid, shaded environments where it grows. The flowers are usually pale yellow to white, with intricate patterns that enhance their visual appeal under moonlight.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, Nycteranthus noctiflorus is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered a vulnerable species.
