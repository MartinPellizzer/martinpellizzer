Nematospermum laevigatum

Nematospermum laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth climbing fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant's root system is fibrous, and it can spread aggressively through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
