Nelia

Nelia, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers open at dusk and close by dawn, adapting to low-light conditions.

Nelia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
