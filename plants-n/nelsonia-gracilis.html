Nelsonia gracilis

Nelsonia gracilis, commonly known as the slender Nelsonia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and delicate white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and forested areas, preferring well-drained soils. It is often found in the understorey of native forests and is adapted to the temperate climate of its native range.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
