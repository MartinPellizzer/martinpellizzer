Nephrolepis biserrata var. glabrata

Nephrolepis biserrata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth holly fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is a terrestrial fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features fronds that are leathery and dark green, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other varieties. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, typical of fern reproduction.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
