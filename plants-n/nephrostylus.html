Nephrostylus

Nephrostylus, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a genus of aquatic plants native to Asia.

It features showy, floating or erect flowers that bloom in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide habitat for aquatic life. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Nephrostylus plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts.
