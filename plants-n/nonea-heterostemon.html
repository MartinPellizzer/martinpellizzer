Nonea heterostemon

Nonea heterostemon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nonea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
