Nemuranthes longicauda

Nemuranthes longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed nemuranthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescences that can reach several feet in length, composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive, elongated shape that gives it its common name.

Nemuranthes longicauda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
