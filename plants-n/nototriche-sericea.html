Nototriche sericea

Nototriche sericea, commonly known as the silky nototriche, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Nototriche sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
