Najas fragilis

Najas fragilis, commonly known as water needle or slender watermilfoil, is a free-floating aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It has slender, thread-like leaves that are typically less than 1 millimeter in diameter, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats on the water surface.

Najas fragilis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and contributing to water oxygenation.
