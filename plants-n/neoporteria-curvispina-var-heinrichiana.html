Neoporteria curvispina var. heinrichiana

Neoporteria curvispina var. heinrichiana, commonly known as the Heiligenstadt neoporteria, is a rare and unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The flowers are fragrant and white, often appearing in clusters, and are a key feature for identification.
