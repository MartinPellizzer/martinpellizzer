Nemastylis multiflora

Nemastylis multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered nemastylis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are small and white or pale green, arranged in dense inflorescences, and bloom during the warmer months.

The species is notable for its adaptability to dry conditions and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
