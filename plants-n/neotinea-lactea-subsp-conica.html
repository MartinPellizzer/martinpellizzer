Neotinea lactea subsp. conica

Neotinea lactea subsp.

conica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkweed, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species.

The plant is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellowish-green, clustered in umbels, and produce a milky sap when damaged.
