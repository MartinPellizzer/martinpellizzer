Nephrodium monocarpum

Nephrodium monocarpum, commonly known as the single-seed kidney fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically bioluminescent in certain conditions. Nephrodium monocarpum is often found in humid, shaded environments in tropical regions.
