Nuxia brachyscypha

Nuxia brachyscypha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dwarf heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, dense clusters of dark green, leathery leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the winter months, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Nuxia brachyscypha is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
