Nerine transvaalensis

Nerine transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal lily, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive white throat. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nerine transvaalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
