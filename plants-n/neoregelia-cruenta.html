Neoregelia cruenta

Neoregelia cruenta, commonly known as the Crimson Fireplant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a compact, water-holding structure at the center. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with showy, red-orange bracts that attract pollinators. Its foliage is often variegated with green and silver tones, adding visual interest.

Neoregelia cruenta thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
