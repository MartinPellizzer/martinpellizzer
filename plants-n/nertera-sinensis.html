Nertera sinensis

Nertera sinensis, commonly known as the silver thread plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Nertera sinensis is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
