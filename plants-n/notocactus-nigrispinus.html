Notocactus nigrispinus

Notocactus nigrispinus, commonly known as the Black-spined Cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces dark, spiny areoles that are often covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom in late spring or early summer.

Notocactus nigrispinus is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.
