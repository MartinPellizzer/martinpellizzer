Nototriche cheilanthifolia

Nototriche cheilanthifolia, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
