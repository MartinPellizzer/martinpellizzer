Nymphaea × omarana var. rosea

Nymphaea × omarana var. rosea, commonly known as the pink lotus, is a hybrid water lily native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to deep red flowers that bloom above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing a habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its flowers are typically fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The botanical features of this variety make it a popular ornamental plant in ponds and water gardens.
