Nothaphoebe baviensis

Nothaphoebe baviensis, commonly known as Bavi's beech, is a rare evergreen tree native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Its bark is smooth and dark brown, while its wood is dense and highly valued for its durability.
