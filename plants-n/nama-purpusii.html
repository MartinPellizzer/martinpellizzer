Nama purpusii

Nama purpusii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a succulent, with a short stem and rosettes of fleshy leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nama purpusii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
