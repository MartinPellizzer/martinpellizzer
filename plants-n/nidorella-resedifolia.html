Nidorella resedifolia

Nidorella resedifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, intensely red flowers with a distinctive, sweet fragrance. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
