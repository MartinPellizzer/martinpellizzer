Nasa lindeniana

Nasa lindeniana, commonly known as the silverleaf linden, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its broad, ovate leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for temperate climates.

Nasa lindeniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
