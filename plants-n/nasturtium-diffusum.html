Nasturtium diffusum

Nasturtium diffusum, commonly known as spreading nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. Nasturtium diffusum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and edible gardens.
