Nasturtium eggersii

Nasturtium eggersii, commonly known as the Eggers' nasturtium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, often with contrasting spots. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are edible and have a peppery flavor. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary gardens.

Nasturtium eggersii is also known for its ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and hoverflies.
