Notopleura longipedunculata

Notopleura longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed notopleura, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated inflorescences that bear small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are usually alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Notopleura longipedunculata is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
