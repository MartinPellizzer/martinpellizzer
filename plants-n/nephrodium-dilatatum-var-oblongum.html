Nephrodium dilatatum var. oblongum

Nephrodium dilatatum var. oblongum, commonly known as the broadleaf nephroid, is a variant of the nephroid plant characterized by its elongated, oblong-shaped leaves.

This plant belongs to the family Nephroidaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. Its leaves are typically broad and have a smooth, waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
