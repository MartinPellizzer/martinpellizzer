Neodunnia atrocyanea

Neodunnia atrocyanea, commonly known as the black-blue leaved dunnia, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost metallic blue leaves that shimmer in the sunlight, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests or alpine meadows.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and somewhat elusive species in the botanical world.
