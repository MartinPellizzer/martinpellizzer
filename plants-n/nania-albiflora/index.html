Nania Albiflora

Nania albiflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its striking white blooms. Found primarily in the high-altitude regions of the Andes, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to the harsh mountain climate. The plant's flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators with their pure white petals. Due to habitat loss, Nania albiflora is now considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
