Nephrodium pellucidum

Nephrodium pellucidum, commonly known as the transparent fern, is a unique species belonging to the family Nephrodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in dense forests. Its reproductive structures include small, spore-bearing capsules that are often hidden among the foliage.

Nephrodium pellucidum is noted for its limited distribution and is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
