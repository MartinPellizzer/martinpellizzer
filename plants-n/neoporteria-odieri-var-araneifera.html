Neoporteria odieri var. araneifera

Neoporteria odieri var. araneifera, commonly known as the Spider-veined Begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate leaf patterns that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically during the spring and summer months.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
