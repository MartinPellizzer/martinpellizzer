Nothoscordum muscorum

Nothoscordum muscorum, commonly known as the musk garlic.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a distinctive garlic-like odor.

Nothoscordum muscorum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
