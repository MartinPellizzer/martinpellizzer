Nasa orbicularis

Nasa orbicularis, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant has oval-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Nasa orbicularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming behavior.
