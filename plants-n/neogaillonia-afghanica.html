Neogaillonia afghanica

Neogaillonia afghanica, commonly known as the Afghan bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a violet or purple tinge. The plant has a fibrous root system and upright, branching stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Neogaillonia afghanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
