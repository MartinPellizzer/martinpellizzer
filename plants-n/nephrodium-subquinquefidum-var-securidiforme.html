Nephrodium subquinquefidum var. securidiforme

Nephrodium subquinquefidum var. securidiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nephroid, is a variant of the nephroid plant characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Nephroidaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

The botanical features of this variant make it distinct from other members of the genus Nephrodium.
