Nasturtium tenue

Nasturtium tenue, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, trailing stems and vibrant, spurred flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow. The plant produces small, edible leaves and seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Nasturtium tenue is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects to gardens.
