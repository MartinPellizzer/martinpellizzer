Nectaroscordum bulgaricum

Nectaroscordum bulgaricum, commonly known as the Balkan garlic, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous stem and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Nectaroscordum bulgaricum is known for its strong, garlic-like odor, which is most noticeable when the flowers are in bloom.
