Narcissus jonquilla var. stellaris

Narcissus jonquilla var. stellaris, commonly known as the Star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms in early spring and is known for its delicate fragrance.

This variety is closely related to other narcissus species and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
