Nerine humilis

Nerine humilis, commonly known as the dwarf nerine, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a contrasting white throat. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and vibrant blooms, it is a popular choice for adding color to gardens with minimal maintenance.
