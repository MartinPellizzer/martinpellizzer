Nicotiana berteroana

Nicotiana berteroana, commonly known as the Bertero's tobacco, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically broad and hairy, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
