Notocampylum mannii

Notocampylum mannii, commonly known as the African marsh orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit. The plant typically features a slender stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in wetland or marshy environments, often near water bodies.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for wetland ecosystems.
