Notocactus ottonis f. elegans

Notocactus ottonis f. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a branching stem with spines and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to rock gardens and desert landscapes.
