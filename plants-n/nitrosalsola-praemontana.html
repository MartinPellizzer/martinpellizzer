Nitrosalsola praemontana

Nitrosalsola praemontana, commonly known as the coastal nitrate plant, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting it to arid and saline environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing near the coastlines where it can tolerate high salt exposure.
