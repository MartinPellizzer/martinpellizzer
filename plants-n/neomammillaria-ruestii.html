Neomammillaria ruestii

Neomammillaria ruestii, commonly known as the Ruestii cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a dense covering of white or pale blue hairs, which helps protect it from intense sunlight.

Neomammillaria ruestii is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
