Nephrolepis exaltata subsp. exaltata

Nephrolepis exaltata subsp.

exaltata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a perennial fern native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features a rhizomatous growth habit with arching fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length.

The fronds are bipinnate, with small, lance-shaped pinnae that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
