Nymania capensis

Nymania capensis, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a hardy and resilient species.

Nymania capensis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
