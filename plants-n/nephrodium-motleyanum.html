Nephrodium motleyanum

Nephrodium motleyanum, commonly known as the Motley's nephroid, is a rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores, contributing to its propagation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
