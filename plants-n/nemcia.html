Nemcia

Nemcia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, followed by bright red berries.

Nemcia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
