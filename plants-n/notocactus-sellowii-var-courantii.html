Notocactus sellowii var. courantii

Notocactus sellowii var. courantii, commonly known as the Sellow's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight, making it a suitable choice for arid or semi-arid climates.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
