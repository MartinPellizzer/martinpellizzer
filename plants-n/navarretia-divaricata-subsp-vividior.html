Navarretia divaricata subsp. vividior

Navarretia divaricata subsp.

vividior, commonly known as the vivid dwarf larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

This subspecies is noted for its vibrant floral display and is often found in regions with cool, temperate climates.
