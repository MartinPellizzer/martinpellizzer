Nirbisia bisma

Nirbisia bisma, commonly known as the red-flowered bellvista, is a striking member of the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves with a serrated margin, and its stems are often hairy. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and botanical collections.
