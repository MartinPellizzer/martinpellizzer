Normantha filiformis

Normantha filiformis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Normantha filiformis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
