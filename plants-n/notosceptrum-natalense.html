Notosceptrum natalense

Notosceptrum natalense, commonly known as Natal mountain grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in mountainous regions, preferring well-drained environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered at the base of the plant.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
