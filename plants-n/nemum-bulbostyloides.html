Nemum bulbostyloides

Nemum bulbostyloides, commonly known as the bulbous starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces a bulbous root system that serves as a storage organ for nutrients. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
