Nephrolepis delicatula

Nephrolepis delicatula, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shady garden areas or indoor cultivation. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the propagation of the plant.

Nephrolepis delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its elegant foliage.
