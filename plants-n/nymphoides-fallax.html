Nymphoides fallax

Nymphoides fallax, commonly known as the golden arrowhead, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in shallow, still water and is often used in water gardens for its ornamental value.

Nymphoides fallax is also valued for its ability to help purify water by absorbing nutrients.
