Nigella stellaris

Nigella stellaris, commonly known as star anise, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces narrow, elongated fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Nigella stellaris thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens and borders.
