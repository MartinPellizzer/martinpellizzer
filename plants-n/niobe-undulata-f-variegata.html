Niobe undulata f. variegata

Niobe undulata f. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Waveleaf, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its unique foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and undulating, with a distinctive variegation pattern of white and green. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden settings. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add visual interest to landscapes.

While it does not produce showy flowers, its foliage alone makes it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
