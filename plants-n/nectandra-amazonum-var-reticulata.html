Nectandra amazonum var. reticulata

Nectandra amazonum var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, rough-textured bark and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by yellowish, fragrant fruits that contain winged seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and swampy areas.

The wood of this species is valued for its durability and distinctive aromatic properties.
