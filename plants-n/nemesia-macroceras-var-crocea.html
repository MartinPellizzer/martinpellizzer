Nemesia macroceras var. crocea

Nemesia macroceras var. crocea, commonly known as the yellow nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
