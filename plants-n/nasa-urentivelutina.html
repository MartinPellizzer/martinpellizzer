Nasa urentivelutina

Nasa urentivelutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf nightshade, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, white to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, yellowish seeds.

Nasa urentivelutina is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
