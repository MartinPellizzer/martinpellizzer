Neobaclea crispifolia

Neobaclea crispifolia, commonly known as the crinkled leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are often crinkled or wavy in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing along forest floors or in undergrowth.

Neobaclea crispifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
