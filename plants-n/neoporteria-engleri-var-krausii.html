Neoporteria engleri var. krausii

Neoporteria engleri var. krausii, commonly known as the Karoo starling plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
