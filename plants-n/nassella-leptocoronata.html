Nassella leptocoronata

Nassella leptocoronata, commonly known as slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Nassella leptocoronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
