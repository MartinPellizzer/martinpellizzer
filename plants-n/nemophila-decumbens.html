Nemophila decumbens

Nemophila decumbens, commonly known as the carpet bugloss, is a low-growing annual flowering plant native to California.

It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has prostrate to ascending stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Nemophila decumbens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
