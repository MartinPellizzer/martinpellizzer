Neoregelia eltoniana

Neoregelia eltoniana, commonly known as the Elton's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange and yellow. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy coating, enhancing its ornamental value.

Neoregelia eltoniana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
