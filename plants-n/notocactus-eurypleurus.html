Notocactus eurypleurus

Notocactus eurypleurus, commonly known as the Desert Cactus or the Eurypleurus Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered in spines, which help to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
