Navia semiserrata

Navia semiserrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctively semi-serrated margin, giving it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Navia semiserrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
