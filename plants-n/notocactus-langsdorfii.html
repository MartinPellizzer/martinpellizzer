Notocactus langsdorfii

Notocactus langsdorfii, commonly known as the Langsdorfii cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but attract pollinators. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to minimize water loss through reduced surface area.

Notocactus langsdorfii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
