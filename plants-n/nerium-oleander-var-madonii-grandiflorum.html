Nerium oleander var. madonii-grandiflorum

Nerium oleander var. madonii-grandiflorum, commonly known as the Madonii Grandiflorum Oleander, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and deep red, often blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It is a hardy shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce multiple flushes of blooms throughout the growing season.
