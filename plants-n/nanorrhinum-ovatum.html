Nanorrhinum ovatum

Nanorrhinum ovatum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Nanorrhinum ovatum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
