Nervilia taiwaniana

Nervilia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan nerve orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a loose spike, which are typical of the Nervilia genus. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
