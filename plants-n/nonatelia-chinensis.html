Nonatelia chinensis

Nonatelia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese starfruit, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is yellow when mature and contains a sweet, juicy pulp with a mild, tangy flavor.
