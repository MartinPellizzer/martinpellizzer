Nectandra lanceolata var. grandifolia

Nectandra lanceolata var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing aromatic seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine and as a source of essential oils.
