Nephrodium apiciflorum

Nephrodium apiciflorum, commonly known as the apiciflorum nephroid, is a rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are often covered in a fine layer of silvery hairs. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that produce spores for propagation.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
