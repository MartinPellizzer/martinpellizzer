Nichallea soyauxii

Nichallea soyauxii, commonly known as the Soyaux's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.
