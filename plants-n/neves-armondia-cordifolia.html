Neves-armondia cordifolia

Neves-armondia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf neves-armondia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Neves-armondia cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
