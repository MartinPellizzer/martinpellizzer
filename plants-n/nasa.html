Nasa

Nasa, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night and close during the day. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. Nasa thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal beauty.
