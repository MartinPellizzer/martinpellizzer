Neomammillaria brandegeei var. magdalensis

Neomammillaria brandegeei var. magdalensis, commonly known as the Magdalena mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its distinctive appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
