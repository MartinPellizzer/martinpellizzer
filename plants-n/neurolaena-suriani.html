Neurolaena suriani

Neurolaena suriani, commonly known as the Brazilian false mallow, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Neurolaena suriani is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
