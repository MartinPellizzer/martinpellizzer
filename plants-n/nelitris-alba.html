Nelitris alba

Nelitris alba, commonly known as the white nelitris, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate white petals and slender, upright stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Nelitris alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
