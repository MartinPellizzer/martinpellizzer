Neodryas llanachagaensis

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, thriving in the understory of dense tropical forests. Its flowers are often small but striking, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.
