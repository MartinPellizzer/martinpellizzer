Nerine pumila

Nerine pumila, commonly known as the dwarf nerine, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pink flowers in the summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is well-suited for rock gardens or containers. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
