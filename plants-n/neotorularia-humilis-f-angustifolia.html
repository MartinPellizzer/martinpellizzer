Neotorularia humilis f. angustifolia

Neotorularia humilis f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed torularia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Its small, daisy-like flowers are usually pale yellow to white, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.
