Nanorrhinum sagittatum

Nanorrhinum sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically green and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Nanorrhinum sagittatum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and modest flowering display.
