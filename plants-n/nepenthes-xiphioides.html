Nepenthes xiphioides

Nepenthes xiphioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pitcher plant, is a carnivorous plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that form distinctive pitcher-shaped traps. The plant's pitchers are typically green or reddish in color and produce a sticky fluid to trap insects. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture.

Nepenthes xiphioides is a popular specimen among carnivorous plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
