Nama aretioides var. aretioides

Nama aretioides var. aretioides, commonly known as the Aretioides Aretioides, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its low-growing habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
