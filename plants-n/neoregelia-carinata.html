Neoregelia carinata

Neoregelia carinata, commonly known as the "Crimson Sword" or "Red Neoregelia," is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and green foliage, with the central rosette of leaves forming a cup that collects water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia carinata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
