Nenga pumila var. pumila

Nenga pumila var. pumila, commonly known as the small nenga or dwarf nenga, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
