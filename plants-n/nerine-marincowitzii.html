Nerine marincowitzii

Nerine marincowitzii, commonly known as the Marincowitz nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom in late summer to early autumn.
