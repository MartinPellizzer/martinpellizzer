Nephrolepis verdissima

Nephrolepis verdissima, commonly known as the golden sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining, moist soil. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies.

Nephrolepis verdissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
