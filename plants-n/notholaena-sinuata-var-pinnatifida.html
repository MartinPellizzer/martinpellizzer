Notholaena sinuata var. pinnatifida

Notholaena sinuata var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the slender fern, is a variant of the species Notholaena sinuata, which belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are often gracefully arching. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually lanceolate to ovate in shape, with a soft, velvety texture.

This variant is distinguished by its more finely divided leaves compared to the typical form, showcasing a unique botanical feature.
