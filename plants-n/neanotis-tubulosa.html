Neanotis tubulosa

Neanotis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from blue to violet. The plant grows in shrubland and rocky habitats, often in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, tubular flowers that attract various pollinators.

Neanotis tubulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
