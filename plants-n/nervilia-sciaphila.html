Nervilia sciaphila

Nervilia sciaphila, commonly known as the shade-loving orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in the understory of forests. The plant has a distinctive appearance with dark green, strap-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. Its root system is specialized for absorbing nutrients from decaying organic matter in the soil.

Nervilia sciaphila is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and unique growth habits.
