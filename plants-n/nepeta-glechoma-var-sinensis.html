Nepeta glechoma var. sinensis

Nepeta glechoma var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese catnip, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features rounded, hairy leaves and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic properties.

This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
