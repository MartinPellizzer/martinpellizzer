Nerine × atrosanguinea

Nerine × atrosanguinea, commonly known as the purple nerine, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from deep purple to almost black, with a contrasting white or pink throat. The plant produces strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are broad and lanceolate in shape. It is typically grown as a bulbous plant, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Nerine × atrosanguinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
