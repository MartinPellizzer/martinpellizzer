Nigritella × wulfeniana

Nigritella × wulfeniana, commonly known as the hybrid black-eyed Susan, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Nigritella, which is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae). The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a dark central disk, giving it its common name. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Nigritella × wulfeniana is typically found in moist, sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
