Nidularium pinelianum

Nidularium pinelianum, commonly known as the silver pineleaf, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen perennial plant features rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the leaf rosette.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
