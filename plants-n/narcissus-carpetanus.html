Narcissus carpetanus

Narcissus carpetanus, commonly known as the carpet narcissus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of fragrant, white or yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing small, cup-shaped blooms that are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
