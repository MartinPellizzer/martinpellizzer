Neltuma Flexuosa F. Subinermis

Neltuma flexuosa f. subinermis is a rare and intriguing plant species known for its unique morphological characteristics. Its delicate structure and subtle coloration make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Despite its beauty, this fern faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
