Narcissus × souliei

Narcissus × souliei, commonly known as the Souliei daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Narcissus.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its cheerful blossoms.
