Nicipe

Nicipe, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom above the water's surface, often in vibrant shades of pink, red, or white. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water, while its roots anchor it to the muddy substrate. Nicipe is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, often representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental ponds and wetland restoration projects.
