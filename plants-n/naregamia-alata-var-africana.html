Naregamia alata var. africana

Naregamia alata var. africana, commonly known as the African winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features compound leaves with distinctive wing-like structures on the leaflets, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by pods containing edible, bean-like seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content, including high protein and calcium levels.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop in some regions.
