Neomammillaria phitauiana

Neomammillaria phitauiana, commonly known as the Phitau's pincushion cactus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
