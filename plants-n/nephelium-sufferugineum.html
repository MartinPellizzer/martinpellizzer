Nephelium sufferugineum

Nephelium sufferugineum, commonly known as the red mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that lead to the development of its distinctive red, segmented fruit. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in various tropical regions around the world.
