Nardosmia speciosa

Nardosmia speciosa, commonly known as the showy nardosmia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Nardosmia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
