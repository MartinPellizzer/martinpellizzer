Nectandra willdenoviana

Nectandra willdenoviana, commonly known as the Java sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored heartwood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed.

The tree is valued for its fragrant timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
