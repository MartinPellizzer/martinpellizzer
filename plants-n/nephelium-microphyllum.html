Nephelium microphyllum

Nephelium microphyllum, commonly known as the mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces a unique fruit with a thick, purple rind and a segmented, white, sweet flesh. The fruit is rich in antioxidants and is valued for its flavor and medicinal properties.

It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
