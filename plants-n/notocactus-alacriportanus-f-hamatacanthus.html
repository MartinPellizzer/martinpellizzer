Notocactus alacriportanus f. hamatacanthus

Notocactus alacriportanus f. hamatacanthus, commonly known as the spiny hedgehog cactus, is a unique hybrid species within the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in sharp, spine-like structures, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. It is native to arid regions of South America and thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in desert gardens and rockeries.
