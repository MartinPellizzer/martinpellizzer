Narvalina fruticosa

Narvalina fruticosa, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit and silver-blue foliage. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Narvalina fruticosa is adapted to salty, windy environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often used in coastal landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
