Neomammillaria schmollii

Neomammillaria schmollii, commonly known as the Schmolli's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and prominent, white to yellowish tubercles. The plant typically produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
