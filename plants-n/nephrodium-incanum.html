Nephrodium incanum

Nephrodium incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf nephroid, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of hair, contributing to its unique appearance.

Nephrodium incanum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
