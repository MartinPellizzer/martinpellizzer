Nephelium leiocarpum

Nephelium leiocarpum, commonly known as the white sapodilla, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a smooth, pale yellow or white skin. Inside, it contains a single seed surrounded by a sweet, creamy pulp.
