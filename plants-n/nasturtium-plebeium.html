Nasturtium plebeium

Nasturtium plebeium, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces bright orange or yellow flowers with four petals. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are often used in herbal remedies.

Nasturtium plebeium is also valued for its peppery taste, making it a popular choice for culinary use.
