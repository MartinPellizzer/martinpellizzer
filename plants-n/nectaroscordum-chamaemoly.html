Nectaroscordum chamaemoly

Nectaroscordum chamaemoly, commonly known as the sweet garlic or meadow garlic, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its umbel-shaped inflorescence and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Nectaroscordum chamaemoly is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its mild garlic scent.
