Neomammillaria decipiens

Neomammillaria decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria decipiens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
