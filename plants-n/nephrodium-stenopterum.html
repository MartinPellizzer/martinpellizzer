Nephrodium stenopterum

Nephrodium stenopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nephroid, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Nephroidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its spores are produced in clusters on the undersides of the fronds, a common feature in ferns.

The plant's morphology reflects its adaptation to humid, tropical climates.
