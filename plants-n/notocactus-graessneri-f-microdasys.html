Notocactus graessneri f. microdasys

Notocactus graessneri f. microdasys, commonly known as the miniature notocactus, is a compact cactus species native to southern Brazil.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that is typically less than 10 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. Its foliage is reduced to spines, and it has a slow growth rate, making it suitable for small indoor spaces.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care in arid environments.
