Nasturtium

Nasturtium, commonly known as *Tropaeolum majus*, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright orange or red blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its tubular flowers contain both male and female reproductive structures, making it a perfect flower.

Nasturtium is also valued for its peppery, edible leaves and seeds, which are used in culinary applications.
