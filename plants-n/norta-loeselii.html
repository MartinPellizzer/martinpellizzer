Norta loeselii

Norta loeselii, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and small, dark purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces berries that are toxic to humans and animals, containing tropane alkaloids. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in shrubland and grassland habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
