Nymphoides humilis

Nymphoides humilis, commonly known as the small water lily, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant features broad, floating leaves that are usually green with a waxy surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and bloom in early summer.

Nymphoides humilis is often used in water gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
