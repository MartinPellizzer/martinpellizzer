Nerine pudica

Nerine pudica, commonly known as the dwarf rain lily, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant blooms during the rainy season, typically from late winter to early spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Nerine pudica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
