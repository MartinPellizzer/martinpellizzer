Neomammillaria moelleriana

Neomammillaria moelleriana, commonly known as the Moeller's milk barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem that typically grows to about 10 centimeters in height. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
