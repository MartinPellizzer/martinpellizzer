Nephrolepis biserrata var. normalis

Nephrolepis biserrata var. normalis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that are typically divided into lance-shaped segments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. Its fronds are usually dark green and glossy, with a serrated margin that gives it its scientific name.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
