Notocactus notabilis

Notocactus notabilis, commonly known as the golden crown cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant features a distinctive crown of spines at the top, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Notocactus notabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
