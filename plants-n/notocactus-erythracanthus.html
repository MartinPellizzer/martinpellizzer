Notocactus erythracanthus

Notocactus erythracanthus, commonly known as the red-spined cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, green stems and prominent red spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens.
