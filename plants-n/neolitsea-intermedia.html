Neolitsea intermedia

Neolitsea intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese laurel, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, blue-black berries.

Neolitsea intermedia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
