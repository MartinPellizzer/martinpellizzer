Nerine laticoma subsp. sandersonii

Nerine laticoma subsp.

sandersonii, commonly known as the sanderson's nerine, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with a prominent yellow throat.

The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
