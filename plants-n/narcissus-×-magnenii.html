Narcissus × magnenii

Narcissus × magnenii, commonly known as the Giant Crowned narcissus, is a hybrid tulip species native to the Netherlands.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive crown-like pattern on the outer petals. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces multiple blooms per stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette at the base.

This variety is prized for its vibrant yellow color and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
