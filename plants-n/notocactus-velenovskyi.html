Notocactus velenovskyi

Notocactus velenovskyi, commonly known as the Velenovsky's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and areoles, which are specialized structures for producing spines and flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise spiny appearance.

Notocactus velenovskyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
