Neomammillaria nivosa

Neomammillaria nivosa, commonly known as the Snowy Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its pale blue to white cylindrical stems, which are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria nivosa is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
