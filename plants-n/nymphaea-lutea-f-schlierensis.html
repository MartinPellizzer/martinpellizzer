Nymphaea lutea f. schlierensis

Nymphaea lutea f. schlierensis, commonly known as the Schlieren yellow water lily, is a rare and distinctive aquatic plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom above the water surface, and broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for aquatic life. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it can receive ample sunlight.
