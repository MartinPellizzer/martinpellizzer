Notocactus grossei f. aureispinus

Notocactus grossei f. aureispinus, commonly known as the golden spined cactus, is a unique hybrid of the Notocactus genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that can grow up to 30 cm in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its spines are typically golden or reddish in color, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
