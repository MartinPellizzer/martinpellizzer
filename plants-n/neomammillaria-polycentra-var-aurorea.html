Neomammillaria polycentra var. aurorea

Neomammillaria polycentra var. aurorea, commonly known as the golden mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive golden hue, particularly on the areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
