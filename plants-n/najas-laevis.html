Najas laevis

Najas laevis, commonly known as the slender watermilfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments across North America.

It has slender, branching stems that are often covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, floating flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Najas laevis is often found in slow-moving waters such as ponds, lakes, and streams.
