Neoporteria subgibbosa var. castanea

Neoporteria subgibbosa var. castanea, commonly known as the red-leafed pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that ranges from deep green to reddish hues, especially in sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles. Its leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, slightly serrated margin.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
