Neckia distans

Neckia distans, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, bright orange to red flowers. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

This plant is also known for its role in supporting monarch butterfly populations due to its nectar-rich flowers.
