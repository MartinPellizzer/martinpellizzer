Nidorella resedifolia subsp. resedifolia

Nidorella resedifolia subsp.

resedifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf nidorella, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has a fibrous root system and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
