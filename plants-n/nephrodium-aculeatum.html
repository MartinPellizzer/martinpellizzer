Nephrodium aculeatum

Nephrodium aculeatum, commonly known as the spiny nephroid, is a unique aquatic plant characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped leaves.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are covered with fine, spiny projections, which help in reducing water flow and providing structural support.

Nephrodium aculeatum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by offering habitat and food for various microorganisms and small aquatic life.
