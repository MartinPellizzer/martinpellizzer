Notocactus langsdorfii var. leprosorum

Notocactus langsdorfii var. leprosorum, commonly known as the Leprous Notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and unique, spiny areoles that bear white, woolly bristles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
