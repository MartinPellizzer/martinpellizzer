Netrostylis capillaris

Netrostylis capillaris, commonly known as the slender nettle orchid, is a herbaceous flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Netrostylis capillaris is an important species for both ecological and botanical study due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences.
