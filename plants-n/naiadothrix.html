Naiadothrix

Naiadothrix, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a unique plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming in late spring or early summer. Naiadothrix thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
