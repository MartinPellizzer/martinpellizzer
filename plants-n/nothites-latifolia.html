Nothites latifolia

Nothites latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf nothite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a broad, flat base and a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters during the spring season. Its flowering period attracts various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Nothites latifolia is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
