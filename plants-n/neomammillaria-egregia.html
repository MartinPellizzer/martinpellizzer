Neomammillaria egregia

Neomammillaria egregia, commonly known as the showy mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria egregia is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
