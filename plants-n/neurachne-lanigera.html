Neurachne lanigera

Neurachne lanigera, commonly known as the silky flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage with slender, cylindrical stems that often grow in clumps. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
