Neoregelia chlorosticta

Neoregelia chlorosticta, commonly known as the Emerald Star Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, waxy leaves that form a rosette shape, creating a central water-holding cup. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences, often with red or orange flowers, which bloom in late summer or early fall. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Neoregelia chlorosticta is popular as a ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
