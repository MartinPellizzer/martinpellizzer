Notocactus sellowii f. cornifer

Notocactus sellowii f. cornifer, commonly known as the Cornifer Notocactus, is a unique variant of the Notocactus genus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This cactus is appreciated for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid environments.
