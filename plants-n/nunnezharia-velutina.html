Nunnezharia velutina

Nunnezharia velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Nunnezharia velutina is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain ecosystems.
