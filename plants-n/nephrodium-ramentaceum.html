Nephrodium ramentaceum

Nephrodium ramentaceum, commonly known as the mossy fern, is a rare and ancient plant species belonging to the family Nephrodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, moss-like appearance, with small, clustered fronds that resemble a carpet of green foliage. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, typically found in shaded, humid forests or rocky crevices.
