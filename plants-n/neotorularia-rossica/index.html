Neotorularia Rossica

Neotorularia rossica is a rare and intriguing species of lichen found in the extreme environments of Antarctica. This unique organism thrives in some of the harshest conditions on Earth, surviving sub-zero temperatures and intense ultraviolet radiation. Its discovery has provided valuable insights into the adaptability of life in extreme climates. The lichen's structure consists of a symbiotic relationship between a fungus and a cyanobacterium. Studying Neotorularia rossica helps scientists understand the limits of biological survival and potential for life on other planets.
