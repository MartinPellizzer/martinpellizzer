Nitraria schoberi var. sibirica

Nitraria schoberi var. sibirica, commonly known as the Siberian nitraria, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves and produces yellowish or reddish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including high salinity and extreme temperatures. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.

Nitraria schoberi var. sibirica plays a role in stabilizing soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
