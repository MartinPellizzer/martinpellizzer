Notocactus securituberculatus

Notocactus securituberculatus, commonly known as the Barbed Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, tuberculate spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise spiky structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique features, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens and collections.
