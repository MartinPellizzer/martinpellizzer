Nageia montana

Nageia montana, commonly known as the mountain pine or the cloud pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and its dense, pyramidal crown, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The tree has needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds. Nageia montana is adapted to grow in high-altitude environments, often in areas with cool, moist climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
