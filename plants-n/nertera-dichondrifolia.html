Nertera dichondrifolia

Nertera dichondrifolia, commonly known as the silver thread plant, is a low-growing, trailing plant native to Madagascar.

It features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or rock gardens.

Due to its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage, it is well-suited for indoor cultivation.
