Nasa aspiazui

Nasa aspiazui, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
