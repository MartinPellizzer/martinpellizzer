Notocactus arnostianus

Notocactus arnostianus, commonly known as the Arnost's cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Notocactus arnostianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
