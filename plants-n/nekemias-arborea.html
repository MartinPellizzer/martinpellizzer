Nekemias arborea

Nekemias arborea, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, fleshy flowers that appear in winter, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Nekemias arborea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
