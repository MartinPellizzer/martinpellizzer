Nelumbium luteum

Nelumbium luteum, commonly known as the yellow lotus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The yellow lotus is also valued for its ornamental appeal in aquatic gardens.
