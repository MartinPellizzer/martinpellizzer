Nemosenecio incisifolius

Nemosenecio incisifolius, commonly known as the cut-leaved alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features basal leaves that are deeply lobed and often have a serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
