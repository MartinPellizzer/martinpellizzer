Neomammillaria magnimamma

Neomammillaria magnimamma, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and prominent, white to light yellow spines. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range from pink to purple, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
