Nonea obtusifolia

Nonea obtusifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf nonea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features basal leaves that are broad and rounded, with a soft, hairy texture. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
