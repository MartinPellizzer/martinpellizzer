Neomammillaria neoelegans var. nigrispina

Neomammillaria neoelegans var. nigrispina, commonly known as the black-spined pincushion cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a smooth, pale green body with dark, spiny tubercles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
