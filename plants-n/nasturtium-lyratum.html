Nasturtium lyratum

Nasturtium lyratum, commonly known as the lyrical nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of orange, yellow, and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often hairy as well. Nasturtium lyratum is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

It is also known for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery taste similar to watercress.
