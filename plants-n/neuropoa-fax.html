Neuropoa fax

Neuropoa fax, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its distinctive, feathery foliage. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are covered in a silvery-white bloom, giving it its common name. Neuropoa fax thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
