Neltuma affinis

Neltuma affinis, commonly known as the slender lily, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Liliaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and tall, slender stems that bear showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Neltuma affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and natural habitats.
