Nasa urens

Nasa urens, commonly known as the "starry night" or "moonflower," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, nocturnal blooms that open at night and close by morning. The plant has small, velvety leaves and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Nasa urens is also valued for its ability to attract nocturnal pollinators like moths.
