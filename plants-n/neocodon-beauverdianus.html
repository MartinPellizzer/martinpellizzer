Neocodon beauverdianus

Neocodon beauverdianus, commonly known as the red-flowered bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique color and bloom time make it a striking addition to any garden setting.
