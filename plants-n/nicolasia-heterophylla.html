Nicolasia heterophylla

Nicolasia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf nicolasia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from ovate to linear, depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, usually appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Nicolasia heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
