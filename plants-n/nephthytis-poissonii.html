Nephthytis poissonii

Nephthytis poissonii, commonly known as the poissonii ivy, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Nephthytis poissonii is also noted for its ability to purify the air, enhancing its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
