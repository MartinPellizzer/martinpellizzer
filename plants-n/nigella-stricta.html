Nigella stricta

Nigella stricta, commonly known as love-in-a-mist, is an annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and produces delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. Its seeds, which are small and black, are often used in cooking and traditional medicine.

Nigella stricta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and edible plants.
