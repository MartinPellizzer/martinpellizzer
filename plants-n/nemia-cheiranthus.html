Nemia cheiranthus

Nemia cheiranthus, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are basal, pinnately divided, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Nemia cheiranthus is adapted to cold climates and is considered a hardy, low-maintenance plant for rock gardens.
