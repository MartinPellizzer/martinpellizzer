Neothymopsis polyantha

Neothymopsis polyantha, commonly known as the clustered thyme.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Neothymopsis polyantha thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
