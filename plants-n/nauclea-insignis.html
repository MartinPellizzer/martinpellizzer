Nauclea insignis

Nauclea insignis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
