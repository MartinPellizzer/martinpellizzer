Nicipe campanulata

Nicipe campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It blooms during the summer months and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Nicipe campanulata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
