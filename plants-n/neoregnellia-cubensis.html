Neoregnellia cubensis

Neoregnellia cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Cuba.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

Neoregnellia cubensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
