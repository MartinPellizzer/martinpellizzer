Nasturtium pyrenaicum var. majus

Nasturtium pyrenaicum var. majus, commonly known as the Pyrenean nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its edible parts, which are prized for their peppery flavor.
