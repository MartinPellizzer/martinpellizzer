Nama sericea

Nama sericea, commonly known as silverleaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines. Nama sericea thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage species for livestock.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil also contributes to soil fertility in degraded landscapes.
