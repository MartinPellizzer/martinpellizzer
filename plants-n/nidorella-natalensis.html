Nidorella natalensis

Nidorella natalensis, commonly known as Natal's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the eastern region of Natal. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Nidorella natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
