Nephrodium × slossoniae

Nephrodium × slossoniae, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Nephrodium and Slossonia.

It exhibits characteristics of both parent species, showcasing a unique combination of leaf morphology and growth habits. The plant typically features fronds that are intermediate in size and shape between its two parent species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its hybrid nature makes it a subject of interest in botanical research and horticultural cultivation.
