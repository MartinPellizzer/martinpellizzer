Nerium coccineum

Nerium coccineum, commonly known as the red oleander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces showy clusters of bright red, pink, or white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a bluish-green hue.

The red oleander is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
