Nama cuatrocienegensis

Nama cuatrocienegensis, commonly known as the four-leaf cactus, is a unique species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in its harsh environment.

Nama cuatrocienegensis is adapted to survive in extreme conditions with minimal water availability.
