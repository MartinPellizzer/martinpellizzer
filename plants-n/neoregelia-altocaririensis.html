Neoregelia altocaririensis

Neoregelia altocaririensis, commonly known as the Altocaririensis bromeliad, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with striking red or purple markings. The plant produces a tall flower spike bearing small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Neoregelia altocaririensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
