Noccaea revelieri

Noccaea revelieri, commonly known as the alpine penny-cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately compound leaves. Noccaea revelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
