Narcissus × piifontianus

Narcissus × piifontianus, commonly known as the Crown Imperial, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that consist of multiple overlapping flowers arranged in a crown-like formation. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with broad, strap-shaped leaves that emerge from the base. Its flowers are usually orange or red in color, with a yellow center, and bloom in early spring.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
