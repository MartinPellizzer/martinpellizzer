Notocactus werdermannianus

Notocactus werdermannianus, commonly known as the Werdermann's notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent structure. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or orange, bloom during the warmer months.

Notocactus werdermannianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
