Notocactus bertinii

Notocactus bertinii, commonly known as the Bertinii Cactus, is a species native to southern Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stems and sparse, white spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late summer.

Notocactus bertinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
