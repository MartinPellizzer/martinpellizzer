Narcissus × felineri var. herminii

Narcissus × felineri var. herminii, commonly known as the Herminii daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Narcissus.

It is characterized by its striking, double-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–30 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its elegant appearance.
