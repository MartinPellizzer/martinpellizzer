Nothaphoebe annamensis

Nothaphoebe annamensis, commonly known as the Annam beech, is a member of the Lauraceae family and is native to Vietnam.

It is a deciduous tree that typically grows in mountainous regions, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, with both male and female flowers occurring on the same plant.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
