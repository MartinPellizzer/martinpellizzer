Niphidium oblanceolatum

Niphidium oblanceolatum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, broad leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water sources.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wetland restoration projects.
