Neoporteria clavata var. parviflora

Neoporteria clavata var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered neoporteria, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are usually pale yellow to white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
