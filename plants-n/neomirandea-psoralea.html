Neomirandea psoralea

Neomirandea psoralea, commonly known as the rock lichen, is a lichenized fungus belonging to the family Roccellaceae.

It is characterized by its crustose growth form, typically forming dense, dark-colored patches on rock surfaces. This species plays a significant role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling in its habitat.
