Nephrodium costale

Nephrodium costale, commonly known as the Costal Nephrolepis, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive fronds.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, slender fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The leaves are usually dark green and have a serrated margin, with a prominent midrib. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
