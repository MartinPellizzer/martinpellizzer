Neosasamorpha tobagenzoana

Neosasamorpha tobagenzoana, commonly known as the Tobago flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tobago.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas and along roadsides.

Neosasamorpha tobagenzoana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
