Notocactus spegazzinii var. alacriportanus

Notocactus spegazzinii var. alacriportanus, commonly known as the Alacriportan Notocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, covered with dense, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
