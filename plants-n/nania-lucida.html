Nania lucida

Nania lucida, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm climates. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

Nania lucida is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its beauty and unique blooming pattern.
