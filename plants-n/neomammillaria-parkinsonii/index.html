Neomammillaria Parkinsonii

Neomammillaria parkinsonii is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its unique appearance, it features short, cylindrical stems covered in soft, white spines. This plant is prized by collectors for its delicate beauty and slow growth rate. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a challenging and sought-after species in the succulent community.
