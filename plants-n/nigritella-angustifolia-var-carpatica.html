Nigritella angustifolia var. carpatica

Nigritella angustifolia var. carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian narrowleaf nigritella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian region.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
