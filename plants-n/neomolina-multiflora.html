Neomolina multiflora

Neomolina multiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Neomolina multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
