Nasturtium × maviei

Nasturtium × maviei, commonly known as the dwarf nasturtium, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from both *Tropaeolum majus* and *Tropaeolum fendleri*.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, making it ideal for container gardening and small spaces. The plant produces vibrant, edible flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, which are commonly used in salads and garnishes. Its leaves are also edible and have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.

Nasturtium × maviei thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and it is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
