Nigella oxypetala subsp.

oxypetala, commonly known as the Kashmiri nigella or black cumin, is a flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Afghanistan, Pakistan, and the Himalayas.
