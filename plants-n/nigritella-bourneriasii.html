Nigritella bourneriasii

Nigritella bourneriasii, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to purple. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often variegated with silver or green markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nigritella bourneriasii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
