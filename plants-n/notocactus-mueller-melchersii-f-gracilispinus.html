Notocactus mueller-melchersii f. gracilispinus

Notocactus mueller-melchersii f. gracilispinus, commonly known as the slender-spined notocactus, is a hybrid cactus species originating from South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its spines are slender and often arranged in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is well-suited for cultivation in arid environments due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
