Nauclea sessilifolia

Nauclea sessilifolia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, opposite, and often elliptical leaves that are leathery and dark green. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Nauclea sessilifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
