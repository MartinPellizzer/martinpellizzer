Nidularium rubens

Nidularium rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered nidularium, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking red inflorescence, which contrasts with its green, strap-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nidularium rubens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
