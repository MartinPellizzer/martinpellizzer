Narcissus × campernellii

Narcissus × campernellii, commonly known as the Campernelle daffodil, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to white. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

Narcissus × campernellii is relatively low maintenance and resistant to many common pests and diseases.
