Neocinnamomum delavayi

Neocinnamomum delavayi, commonly known as the Yunnan cinnamon or Delavay's cinnamon, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in southwestern China. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its bark and leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Neocinnamomum delavayi is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
