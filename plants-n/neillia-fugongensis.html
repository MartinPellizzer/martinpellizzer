Neillia fugongensis

Neillia fugongensis, commonly known as the Yunnan rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically in shades of white and purple. Neillia fugongensis thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
