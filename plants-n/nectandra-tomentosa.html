Nectandra tomentosa

Nectandra tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and ovate, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elliptical, woody fruits. Nectandra tomentosa is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological significance lies in providing habitat and food for various wildlife species in its native rainforests.
