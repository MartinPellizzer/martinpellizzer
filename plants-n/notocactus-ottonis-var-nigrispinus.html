Notocactus ottonis var. nigrispinus

Notocactus ottonis var. nigrispinus, commonly known as the black-spined night cactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark green, cylindrical stems that are often covered with sharp, black spines. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers at night, which are typically short-lived but highly fragrant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its distinctive appearance and nocturnal blooming habit, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
