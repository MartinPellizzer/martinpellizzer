Neocinnamomum fargesii

Neocinnamomum fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan cinnamon or Farges' cinnamon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants such as cinnamon and laurel. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic and have a distinct scent. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Neocinnamomum fargesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its essential oils and medicinal properties.
