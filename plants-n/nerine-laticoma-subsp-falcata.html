Nerine laticoma subsp. falcata

Nerine laticoma subsp.

falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nerine, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its bulbs are relatively hardy and can be planted in the fall for spring blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
