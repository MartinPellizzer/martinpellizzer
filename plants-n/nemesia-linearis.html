Nemesia linearis

Nemesia linearis, commonly known as the slender nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and compact growth habit.

Nemesia linearis is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance beauty.
