Nymphaea nouchali var. cyanea

Nymphaea nouchali var. cyanea, commonly known as the blue lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, waxy blue or purple petals that are highly fragrant, especially at night. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life.

The flowers open in the evening and close during the day, making it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens.
