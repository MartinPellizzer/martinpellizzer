Neuburgia moluccana

Neuburgia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with glossy, dark green leaves that are oppositely arranged. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical, red berries containing seeds. Neuburgia moluccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to the flora of its native region.
