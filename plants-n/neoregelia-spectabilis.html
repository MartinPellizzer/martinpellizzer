Neoregelia spectabilis

Neoregelia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy neoregelia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that surround the central rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant forms a dense rosette that collects rainwater, creating a microhabitat for small organisms. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, white flowers that emerge from the center.

Neoregelia spectabilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
