Nogo chevalieri

Nogo chevalieri, commonly known as the Chevalier's nogo, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that often display a vibrant green hue with subtle variegation. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal.
