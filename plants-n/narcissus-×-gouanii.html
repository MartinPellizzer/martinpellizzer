Narcissus × gouanii

Narcissus × gouanii, commonly known as the Gouanii daffodil, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Narcissus tazetta and Narcissus poeticus.

It is characterized by its large, showy, funnel-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow. The plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is a perennial bulbous plant that produces multiple stems and flowers in early spring.

The Gouanii daffodil is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
