Nasturtium astyloides

Nasturtium astyloides, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Tropaeolaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and vibrant orange or yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible seed pods that are often used in culinary applications. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly peppery taste, similar to other nasturtium species.

Nasturtium astyloides thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.
