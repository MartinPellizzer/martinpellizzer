Neoregelia atroviridifolia

Neoregelia atroviridifolia, commonly known as the dark green foliage neoregelia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its striking, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. Neoregelia atroviridifolia is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
