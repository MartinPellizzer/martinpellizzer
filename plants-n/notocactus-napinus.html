Notocactus napinus

Notocactus napinus, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tall, cylindrical stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for both ornamental and desert gardens.
