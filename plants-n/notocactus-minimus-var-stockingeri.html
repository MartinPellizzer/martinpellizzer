Notocactus minimus var. stockingeri

Notocactus minimus var. stockingeri, commonly known as the Stockinger's miniature notocactus, is a small, compact cactus native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and produces small, white flowers during the warmer months. The plant is characterized by its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in well-draining soil. It requires minimal water and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often sought after by cactus enthusiasts.
