Nilgirianthus neilgherrensis

Nilgirianthus neilgherrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri aster, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in montane forests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Nilgirianthus neilgherrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
