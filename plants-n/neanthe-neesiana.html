Neanthe neesiana

Neanthe neesiana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Neanthe neesiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
