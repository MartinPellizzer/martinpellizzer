Nervilia leguminosarum

Nervilia leguminosarum, commonly known as the common vetch, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets, and its distinctive yellow or orange-pink flowers. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

Nervilia leguminosarum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
