Notocactus araneolarius

Notocactus araneolarius, commonly known as the spider cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
