Narcissus × cadevallii

Narcissus × cadevallii, commonly known as the Cadevallii daffodil, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Narcissus.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically display a vibrant yellow color. The plant is native to the Netherlands and was developed through selective breeding to enhance its ornamental qualities. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its blooming period occurs in early spring, adding a striking display of color to the landscape.
