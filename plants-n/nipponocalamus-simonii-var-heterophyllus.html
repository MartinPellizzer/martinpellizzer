Nipponocalamus simonii var. heterophyllus

Nipponocalamus simonii var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable bamboo, is a variant of the Nipponocalamus simonii species.

It is native to China and is characterized by its distinctive foliage and growth habit. The plant exhibits variation in leaf shape and size, which is reflected in its name "heterophyllus.". It typically grows as a clump-forming bamboo with slender, hollow stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
