Notopleura aligera

Notopleura aligera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, and the plant prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Notopleura aligera is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
