Nephrodium × boottii f. multiflorum

Nephrodium × boottii f. multiflorum, commonly known as the multibract fern, is a hybrid fern species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features compound fronds with multiple pinnae, each bearing small, serrated leaflets. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that produce spores for propagation.
