Najas meiklei

Najas meiklei, commonly known as the narrowleaf watermilfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in North America.

It has slender, branching stems that are often covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically found in clusters near the water's surface. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.

Its rapid growth can sometimes lead to dense vegetation, impacting water flow and clarity.
