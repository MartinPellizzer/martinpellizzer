Nigritella × moritziana

Nigritella × moritziana, commonly known as the hybrid black-eyed Susan, is a perennial flowering plant that belongs to the Asteraceae family.

It is a cross between Nigritella and Moritzia, two genera within the same family, resulting in a unique combination of traits. The plant produces showy yellow ray flowers with dark centers, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Nigritella × moritziana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
