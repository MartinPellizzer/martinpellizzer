Neriacanthus nitidus

Neriacanthus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy leaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often covered in a waxy, glossy coating. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its aesthetic qualities, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
