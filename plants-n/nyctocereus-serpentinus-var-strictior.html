Nyctocereus serpentinus var. strictior

Nyctocereus serpentinus var. strictior, commonly known as the strict night bloomer cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its tall, slender stems and spines. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers that open at night, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its nocturnal blooming habit, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique flowering behavior.
