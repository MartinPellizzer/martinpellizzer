Nephrolepis cordifolia f. aureoglandulosa

Nephrolepis cordifolia f. aureoglandulosa, commonly known as the golden fern, is a varietal form of the common sword fern.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green fronds that are adorned with golden-yellow spore cases along the margins. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are pinnately divided, with each segment bearing small, serrated leaflets.

This ornamental fern is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and easy care requirements.
