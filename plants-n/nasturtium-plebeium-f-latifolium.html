Nasturtium plebeium f. latifolium

Nasturtium plebeium f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf nasturtium, is a variant of the common nasturtium.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of orange and red. This plant is native to South America and thrives in temperate climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and culinary uses.

The foliage and flowers are both edible, making it a versatile addition to gardens and kitchens.
