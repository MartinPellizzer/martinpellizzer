Narica

Narica, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, bright orange or red flower heads that resemble the sun. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sunlight and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Narica is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
