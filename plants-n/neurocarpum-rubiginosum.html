Neurocarpum rubiginosum

Neurocarpum rubiginosum, commonly known as the red-staining orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often adorned with a reddish tinge. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Neurocarpum rubiginosum is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local pollinator populations.
