Notoceras canescens

Notoceras canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Notoceras canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
