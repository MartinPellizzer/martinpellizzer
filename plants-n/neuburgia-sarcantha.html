Neuburgia sarcantha

Neuburgia sarcantha, commonly known as the Neuburgia starflower, is a striking member of the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Neuburgia sarcantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and alpine settings.
