Nototriche megalorrhiza

Nototriche megalorrhiza, commonly known as the giant root nototriche, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its robust root system and its ability to thrive in cold, arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Nototriche megalorrhiza is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
