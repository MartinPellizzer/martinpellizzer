Nunnezia

Nunnezia, commonly known as the "ghost flower," is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the arid regions of northern South America.

The plant has a short blooming period, typically during the dry season, when it produces its unique, ephemeral flowers. Nunnezia thrives in nutrient-poor soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.
