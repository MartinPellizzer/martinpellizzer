Nobula

Nobula, commonly known as the noble lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. Nobula thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its blooming period typically occurs in spring, adding a touch of elegance to gardens and landscapes.
