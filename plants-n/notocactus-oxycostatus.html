Notocactus oxycostatus

Notocactus oxycostatus, commonly known as the sharp-ribbed cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered with spines, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Its growth habit is typically solitary or clustered, and it is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil.

Notocactus oxycostatus is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
