Neomammillaria blossfeldiana

Neomammillaria blossfeldiana, commonly known as the Blossfeld's pincushion cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are borne on short, erect stems. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
