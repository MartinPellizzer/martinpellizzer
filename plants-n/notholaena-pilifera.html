Notholaena pilifera

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often creeping and can form dense colonies.
