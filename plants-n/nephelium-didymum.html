Nephelium didymum

Nephelium didymum, commonly known as the rambutan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, spiny fruit covering. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into the distinctive hairy fruits. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
