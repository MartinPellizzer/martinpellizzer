Nephrodium palatanganum

Nephrodium palatanganum, commonly known as the Palatanganum kidney fern, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Nephrolepidaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with clustered, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that are arranged in clusters on the undersides of the fronds.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
