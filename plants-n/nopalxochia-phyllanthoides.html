Nopalxochia phyllanthoides

Nopalxochia phyllanthoides, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

Nopalxochia phyllanthoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
