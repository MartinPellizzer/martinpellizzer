Neolloydia beguinii var. senilis

Neolloydia beguinii var. senilis, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a rare and ornamental plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-white leaves that contrast beautifully with its dark green stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
