Neltuma castellanosii

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup, typical of many bromeliads. The plant produces striking, colorful inflorescences that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and insects. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure the survival of this unique botanical species.
