Notocactus ottonis var. stenogonus

Notocactus ottonis var. stenogonus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed notocactus, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
