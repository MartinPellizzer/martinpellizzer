Nemepiodon speciosum

Nemepiodon speciosum, commonly known as the ornate spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage and unique floral structures. The plant typically features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and prominent veins. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, which are cup-like structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Nemepiodon speciosum is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in well-drained, moist soils.
