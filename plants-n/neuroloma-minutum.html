Neuroloma minutum

Neuroloma minutum, commonly known as the small nerve plant, is a small, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features delicate, slender stems that often grow close to the ground. The plant produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in small inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, arranged alternately along the stem.

Neuroloma minutum is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal practices.
