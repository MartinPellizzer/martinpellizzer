Nephrodium Ligulatum

Nephrodium ligulatum is a rare and ancient fern species found in specific wetland habitats. It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and unique reproductive structures. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments with rich organic soil. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Studying Nephrodium ligulatum provides valuable insights into the evolution of ferns and ecosystem health.
