Nephrodium peripae

Nephrodium peripae, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its intricate fronds and unique reproductive structures.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, arching fronds that bear small, clustered sori on the underside. Its leaves are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions, a rare trait in ferns.

The silverleaf fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
