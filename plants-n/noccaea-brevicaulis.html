Noccaea brevicaulis

Noccaea brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, white or pink flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. It produces small, round seeds enclosed in a pod, which is a common feature among members of its family.

Noccaea brevicaulis is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
