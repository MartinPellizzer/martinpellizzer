Notospartium torulosum

Notospartium torulosum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Notospartium torulosum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
