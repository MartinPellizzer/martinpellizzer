Notocactus neo-ottoianus var. schuldtii

Notocactus neo-ottoianus var. schuldtii, commonly known as the Schuldt's notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and clusters of vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a smooth, ribbed surface and is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal rainfall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a challenging and specialized plant for growers.
