Nephrodium cheilocarpum

Nephrodium cheilocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nephroid, is a rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Nephrodioideae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, narrow base. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive structure, with sporangia arranged in clusters on the underside of its leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Nephrodium cheilocarpum is considered a species of conservation concern.
