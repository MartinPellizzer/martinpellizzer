Naiadia

Naiadia, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy blue to purple ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers. Naiadia thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded or partially shaded areas.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
