Navia heliophila

Navia heliophila, commonly known as the sun-loving navia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Navia heliophila is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
