Nassella Carettei

Nassella carettei is a striking grass species native to the arid regions of South America. Known for its unique appearance and resilience, it thrives in harsh environments with minimal water. This plant features slender, silver-blue leaves that give it a distinctive visual appeal. Often used in xeriscaping, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens. Its beauty and adaptability make it a valuable addition to ecological and ornamental landscapes.
