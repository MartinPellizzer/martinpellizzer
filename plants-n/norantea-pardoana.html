Norantea pardoana

Norantea pardoana, commonly known as the Pardoan Flamebush, is a strikingly colorful shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom in dense clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the branches. Norantea pardoana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
