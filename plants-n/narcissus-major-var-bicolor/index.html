Narcissus Major Var. Bicolor

Narcissus major var. bicolor is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant, bi-colored flowers. This variety of daffodil showcases a blend of white and yellow petals that create a visually captivating display. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its blooms typically appear in early spring, adding a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This plant is not only ornamental but also holds cultural significance in various regions.
