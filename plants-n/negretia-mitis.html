Negretia mitis

Negretia mitis, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and large, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitats.

Negretia mitis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential economic uses.
