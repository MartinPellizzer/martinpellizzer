Nerium chinense

Nerium chinense, commonly known as Chinese oleander, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth surface.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental species.
