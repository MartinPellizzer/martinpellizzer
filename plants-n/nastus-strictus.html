Nastus strictus

Nastus strictus, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive, pungent odor, and its leaves are edible, often used in salads or as a garnish. The plant grows upright with slender stems and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical name reflects its strict, upright growth habit.
