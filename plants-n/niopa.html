Niopa

Niopa, commonly known as the Christmas rose, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe.

It is characterized by its fleshy, hairy leaves and its striking white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade. Niopa is known for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its flowers are often associated with the Christmas season, hence its common name.
