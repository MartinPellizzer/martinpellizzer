Nephrodium patens

Nephrodium patens, commonly known as the broad-leafed nephroid, is a unique fern species characterized by its distinctive frond morphology.

Its fronds are typically broad and lanceolate, with a prominent midrib and a feathery arrangement of pinnae. The plant exhibits a complex life cycle involving both sporophyte and gametophyte stages. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The botanical features of Nephrodium patens make it an interesting subject for study in plant morphology and ecology.
