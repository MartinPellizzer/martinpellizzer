Nasturtium × subglobosum

Nasturtium × subglobosum, commonly known as the globe-shaped nasturtium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is characterized by its round, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible parts. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its flowers are also used in culinary applications, adding a peppery flavor to dishes.
