Narcissus stellaris

Narcissus stellaris, commonly known as the star daffodil, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers with a yellow corona and white petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a narrow, grass-like foliage and is well adapted to well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Narcissus stellaris is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is closely related to other daffodil species.
