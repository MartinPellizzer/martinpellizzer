Nania elegans

Nania elegans, commonly known as the delicate bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features slender stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or alpine displays. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of down.

Nania elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in cooler climates.
