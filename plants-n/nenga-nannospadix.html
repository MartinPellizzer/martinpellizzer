Nenga nannospadix

Nenga nannospadix, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its unusual reproductive structures, which include a spadix and a spathe.
