Neoporteria nidus var. gerocephala

Neoporteria nidus var. gerocephala, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with distinctive golden or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble small stars, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden areas or as a houseplant.
