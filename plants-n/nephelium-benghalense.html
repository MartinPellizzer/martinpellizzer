Nephelium benghalense

Nephelium benghalense, commonly known as the Bengal longan, is a tropical fruit-bearing tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, round fruits with a smooth, thin skin and a sweet, juicy pulp. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green surface.
