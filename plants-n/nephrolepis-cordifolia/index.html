Nephrolepis Cordifolia

Nephrolepis cordifolia, also known as the Boston fern, is a popular evergreen fern prized for its lush, feathery fronds. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in humid environments and is often used as a decorative plant indoors. Its heart-shaped leaflets give it a distinctive appearance that adds elegance to any space. Easy to care for, it requires moderate watering and indirect light to flourish. This versatile plant is both ornamental and air-purifying, making it a favorite among plant lovers.
