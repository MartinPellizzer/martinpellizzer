Nemophila pedunculata

Nemophila pedunculata, commonly known as the blue-eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Nemophila pedunculata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.
