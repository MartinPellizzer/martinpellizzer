Nerion

Nerion, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

These plants are typically found in coastal areas and are adapted to saline environments. They have distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, often pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. Nerion species are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.

Their ecological role includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for small marine and terrestrial organisms.
