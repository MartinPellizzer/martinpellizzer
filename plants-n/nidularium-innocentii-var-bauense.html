Nidularium innocentii var. bauense

Nidularium innocentii var. bauense, commonly known as the Bauens Bucephalandra, is a rare aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft, slightly acidic water with good water circulation. It is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
