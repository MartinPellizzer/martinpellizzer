Neomolina gnidiifolia

Neomolina gnidiifolia, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in a dense, star-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Neomolina gnidiifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
