Nicodemia rondeletiiflora

Nicodemia rondeletiiflora, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble flames, with bright pink or red bracts. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered within the vibrant bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical areas.
