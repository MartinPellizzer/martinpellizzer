Notocactus intricatus

Notocactus intricatus, commonly known as the intricate notocactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features a smooth, green surface with prominent ribs and spines that are typically reddish in color. It produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, blooming in late spring.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
