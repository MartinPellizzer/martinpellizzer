Notocactus polyacanthus

Notocactus polyacanthus, commonly known as the Pencil Cactus, is a distinctive succulent native to South America.

It features a tall, unbranched stem covered in sharp, spiny tubercles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss in arid environments.

Notocactus polyacanthus is well-suited for xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
