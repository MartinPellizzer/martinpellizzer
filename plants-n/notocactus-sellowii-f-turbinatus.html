Notocactus sellowii f. turbinatus

Notocactus sellowii f. turbinatus, commonly known as the Turbinated Notocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its distinct appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
