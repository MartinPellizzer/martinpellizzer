Nigella bicolor

Nigella bicolor, commonly known as love-in-a-mist, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its seeds, which are small and black, are used in cooking and traditional medicine.

Nigella bicolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal collections.
