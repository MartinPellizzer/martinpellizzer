Nemacladus rigidus var. australis

Nemacladus rigidus var. australis, commonly known as the Australian rigid nemacladus, is a perennial shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
