Neea tenuis

Neea tenuis, commonly known as the slender neea, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. Neea tenuis is often grown as an ornamental plant in shaded gardens due to its attractive foliage and compact size.
