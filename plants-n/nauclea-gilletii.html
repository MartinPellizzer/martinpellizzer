Nauclea gilletii

Nauclea gilletii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow, followed by small, woody fruits. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a slightly hairy texture.

Nauclea gilletii is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
