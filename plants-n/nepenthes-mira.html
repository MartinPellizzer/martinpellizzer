Nepenthes mira

Nepenthes mira, commonly known as the Miri Pitcher Plant, is a tropical pitcher plant native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow pitchers that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces a sticky liquid in its pitchers to trap and digest insects, providing essential nutrients in nutrient-poor soils. Its unique morphology and carnivorous habit make it a fascinating subject in botanical studies.

Nepenthes mira thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.
