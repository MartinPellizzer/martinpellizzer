Nanophyton botschantzevii

Nanophyton botschantzevii, commonly known as the desert moss or rock moss, is a small, mat-forming liverwort that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit, which helps it conserve moisture in harsh conditions. The plant has tiny, leaf-like structures called phyllidia that are arranged in a compact rosette pattern. It is often found growing on rocky substrates or in crevices, where it can access limited water and nutrients.

Nanophyton botschantzevii plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitats.
