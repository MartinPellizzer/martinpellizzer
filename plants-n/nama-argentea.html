Nama argentea

Nama argentea, commonly known as silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves and produces a long, slender stem with bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Nama argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
