Nasa dyeri subsp. dyeri

Nasa dyeri subsp.

dyeri, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Andes mountains of South America. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to silver-colored spines.

The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This succulent is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to adapt to arid environments.
