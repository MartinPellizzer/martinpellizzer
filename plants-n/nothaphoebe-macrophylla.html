Nothaphoebe macrophylla

Nothaphoebe macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf nothaphoebe, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elliptical, woody fruits.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
