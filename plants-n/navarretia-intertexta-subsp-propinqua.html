Navarretia intertexta subsp. propinqua

Navarretia intertexta subsp.

propinqua, commonly known as the slender navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a small annual herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.
