Nastus prolifer

Nastus prolifer, commonly known as the spreading nasturtium, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a cylindrical silique that contains several seeds.

Nastus prolifer is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
