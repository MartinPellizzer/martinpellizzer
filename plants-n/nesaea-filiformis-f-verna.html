Nesaea filiformis f. verna

Nesaea filiformis f. verna, commonly known as the spring ivy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender, creeping stems that produce small, fragrant flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its blooming period occurs in early spring, adding a delicate touch of color to gardens during the cooler months.
