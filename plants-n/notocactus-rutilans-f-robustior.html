Notocactus rutilans f. robustior

Notocactus rutilans f. robustior, commonly known as the robustior form of the sunburst cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a cylindrical, ribbed stem that can grow up to several feet in height, with a distinctive yellowish-orange coloration. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on long, slender pedicels. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, making it well-suited for dry environments.

The varietal form 'robustior' is characterized by its more robust growth and enhanced coloration compared to the typical species.
