Nigritella × fragrans

Nigritella × fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant hybrid nightshade, is a perennial plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that emit a sweet, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and greenhouses.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is often cultivated for both aesthetic and aromatic purposes.
