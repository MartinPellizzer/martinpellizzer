Notocactus schumannianus subsp. claviceps

Notocactus schumannianus subsp.

claviceps, commonly known as the Claviceps notocactus, is a cacti species native to Brazil. It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to reddish in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. It has a slow growth rate and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat challenging species to grow in cultivation.
