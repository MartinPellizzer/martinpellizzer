Nelitris leucocoma

Nelitris leucocoma, commonly known as the white sand lily, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Nelitris leucocoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
