Neomarica humilis

Neomarica humilis, commonly known as the dwarf spider lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Neomarica humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
