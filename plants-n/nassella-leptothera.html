Nassella leptothera

Nassella leptothera, commonly known as slender needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife.
