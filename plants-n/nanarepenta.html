Nanarepenta

Nanarepenta, commonly known as the silverleaf starflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It features small, star-shaped white petals with a subtle silver sheen on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, slightly hairy leaves. It blooms in early spring, producing clusters of delicate flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species.
