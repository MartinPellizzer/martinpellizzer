Nasa formosissima

Nasa formosissima, commonly known as the "beautiful star" or "star flower," is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

Nasa formosissima is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
