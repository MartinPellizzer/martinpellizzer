Nephrodium schkuhrii

Nephrodium schkuhrii, commonly known as the Schkuhrii nephroid, is a rare and unique member of the fern family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori, which are clustered on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
