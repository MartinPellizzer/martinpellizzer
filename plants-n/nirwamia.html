Nirwamia

Nirwamia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Nirwamia thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and vibrant appearance make it a favorite in landscaping and garden settings.
