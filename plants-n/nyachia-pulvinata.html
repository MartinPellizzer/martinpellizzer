Nyachia pulvinata

Nyachia pulvinata, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Nyachia pulvinata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
