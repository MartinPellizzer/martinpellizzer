Nicotiana ipomopsiflora

Nicotiana ipomopsiflora, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
