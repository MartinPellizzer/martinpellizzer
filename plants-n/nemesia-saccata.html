Nemesia saccata

Nemesia saccata, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and hanging baskets.

Nemesia saccata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
