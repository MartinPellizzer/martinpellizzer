Neomammillaria tegelbergiana

Neomammillaria tegelbergiana, commonly known as the Tegelbergiana cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in short, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor succulent collections.
