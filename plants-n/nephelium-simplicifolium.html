Nephelium simplicifolium

Nephelium simplicifolium, commonly known as the red mangostin, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers.
