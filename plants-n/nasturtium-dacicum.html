Nasturtium dacicum

Nasturtium dacicum, commonly known as the Romanian watercress, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are peppery and edible, often used in salads or as a garnish. Its flowers are bright orange or yellow, with four petals and prominent yellow stamens.

Nasturtium dacicum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources.
