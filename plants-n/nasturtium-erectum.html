Nasturtium erectum

Nasturtium erectum, commonly known as the upright nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, edible flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces bright orange or yellow blossoms. Its botanical name, *Tropaeolum majus*, reflects its historical use in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental and edible plants.
