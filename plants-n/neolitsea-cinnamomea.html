Neolitsea cinnamomea

Neolitsea cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon tree or Chinese cinnamon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces drupes that are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils with various medicinal properties.

Neolitsea cinnamomea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
