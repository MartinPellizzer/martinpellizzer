Nopalxochia × horichii

Nopalxochia × horichii, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
