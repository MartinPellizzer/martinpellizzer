Nyctanthes goa

Nyctanthes goa, commonly known as the night-flowering jasmine, is a deciduous shrub native to the coastal regions of India.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that open at night and close during the day. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are glossy in appearance. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Nyctanthes goa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
