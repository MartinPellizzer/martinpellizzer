Neea subcoccinea

Neea subcoccinea, commonly known as the red-stemmed neea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its slender, reddish stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are usually glossy and have a slightly hairy texture.

Neea subcoccinea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
