Notocactus oxycostatus subsp. gracilis

Notocactus oxycostatus subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to southern Brazil. It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and thick, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
