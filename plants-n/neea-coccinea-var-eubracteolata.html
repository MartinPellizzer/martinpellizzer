Neea coccinea var. eubracteolata

Neea coccinea var. eubracteolata, commonly known as the red-flowered neea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or streamside areas. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, often with a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
