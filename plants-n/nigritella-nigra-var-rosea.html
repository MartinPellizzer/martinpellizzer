Nigritella nigra var. rosea

Nigritella nigra var. rosea, commonly known as the roseate blackberry, is a variant of the blackberry plant characterized by its distinctive pink to rose-colored flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces small, dark purple to black berries. The plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture.

The roseate variety is prized for its ornamental value and mild, sweet flavor in its fruit.
