Nasturtium procerum

Nasturtium procerum, commonly known as the slender nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its stems are slender and often trailing, making it suitable for containers or hanging baskets.

Nasturtium procerum is also noted for its edible flowers and leaves, which have a peppery flavor.
