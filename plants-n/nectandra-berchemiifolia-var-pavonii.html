Nectandra berchemiifolia var. pavonii

Nectandra berchemiifolia var. pavonii, commonly known as the pavonii variety of the fragrant wood tree, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and other aromatic trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces dark, glossy berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
