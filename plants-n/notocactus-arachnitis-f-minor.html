Notocactus arachnitis f. minor

Notocactus arachnitis f. minor, commonly known as the miniature spider cactus, is a rare and distinctive cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white, hair-like spines resembling spider legs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.

However, it requires specific growing conditions, including well-draining soil and bright, indirect light, to thrive.
