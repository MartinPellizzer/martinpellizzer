Neocheiropteris ansata f. monstrifera

Neocheiropteris ansata f. monstrifera, commonly known as the giant maidenhair fern, is a rare and striking variant of the maidenhair fern family.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped fronds that can reach lengths of up to 1.5 meters. The fronds are typically dark green with a glossy surface and a distinctive, delicate texture. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

Its unique appearance and size make it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and collections.
