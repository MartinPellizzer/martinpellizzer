Nymphozanthus × rubrodiscus

Nymphozanthus × rubrodiscus, commonly known as the red disc hibiscus, is a hybrid shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, disc-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual element to gardens. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Nymphozanthus × rubrodiscus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical landscaping.
