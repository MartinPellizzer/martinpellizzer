Noronhia congesta

Noronhia congesta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
