Noronhia camptoneura

Noronhia camptoneura, commonly known as the zigzag acanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in clusters.

Noronhia camptoneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
