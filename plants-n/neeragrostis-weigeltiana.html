Neeragrostis weigeltiana

Neeragrostis weigeltiana, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces delicate, feathery seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Neeragrostis weigeltiana is often used in landscaping for its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
