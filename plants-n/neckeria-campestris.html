Neckeria campestris

Neckeria campestris, commonly known as the field neckeria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
