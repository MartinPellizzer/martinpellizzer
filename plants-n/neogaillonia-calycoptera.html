Neogaillonia calycoptera

Neogaillonia calycoptera, commonly known as the velvetleaf or broad-leaved velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, capsule-like fruits. Neogaillonia calycoptera is often found in wetland areas and can thrive in moist, shady environments.
