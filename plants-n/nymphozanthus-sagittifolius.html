Nymphozanthus sagittifolius

Nymphozanthus sagittifolius, commonly known as the arrowleaf balm, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its medicinal properties have also been studied for their potential health benefits.
