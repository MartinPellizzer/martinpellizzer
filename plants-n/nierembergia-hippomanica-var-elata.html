Nierembergia hippomanica var. elata

Nierembergia hippomanica var. elata, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking silver-white foliage and pendant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of blue to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.

It is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
