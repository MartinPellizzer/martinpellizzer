Nymphaea × fletcheri

Nymphaea × fletcheri, commonly known as the Fletcher's water lily, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct, waxy appearance. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.

Nymphaea × fletcheri is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
