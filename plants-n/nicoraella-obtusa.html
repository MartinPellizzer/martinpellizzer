Nicoraella obtusa

Nicoraella obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers with white ray florets. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with a compact and bushy form.
