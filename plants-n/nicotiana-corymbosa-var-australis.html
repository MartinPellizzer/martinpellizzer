Nicotiana corymbosa var. australis

Nicotiana corymbosa var. australis, commonly known as the Australian tobacco plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Australia.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically clustered in corymbs. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal areas.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty in gardens.
