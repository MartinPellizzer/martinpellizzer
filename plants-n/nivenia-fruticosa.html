Nivenia fruticosa

Nivenia fruticosa, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and rocky environments.

Due to its ornamental value and historical use in traditional crafts, it is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic and practical properties.
