Nototriche armeriifolia

Nototriche armeriifolia, commonly known as the woolly nototriche, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are often covered in fine, white trichomes, which give it a distinctive, woolly appearance.

The flowers are usually yellow or white and bloom in the spring or early summer.
