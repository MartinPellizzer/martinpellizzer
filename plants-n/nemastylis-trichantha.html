Nemastylis trichantha

Nemastylis trichantha, commonly known as the hairy nemastylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by white ray florets. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It is native to South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Nemastylis trichantha is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
