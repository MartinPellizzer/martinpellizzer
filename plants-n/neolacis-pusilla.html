Neolacis pusilla

Neolacis pusilla, commonly known as the small lacis.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions in South America. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Neolacis pusilla is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
