Neolobivia × segawae var. rubriviolaciflora

Neolobivia × segawae var. rubriviolaciflora, commonly known as the Ruby Violet Flame Cactus, is a hybrid succulent native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It features a compact, globular shape with ribbed stems that display a striking combination of deep red and violet hues. The plant produces vibrant orange to yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique coloration and hybrid origin, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
