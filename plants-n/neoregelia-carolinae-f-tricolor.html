Neoregelia carolinae f. tricolor

Neoregelia carolinae f. tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Bromeliad, is a striking hybrid bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tricolored foliage that displays shades of red, pink, and green. The plant features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.

This ornamental plant is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
