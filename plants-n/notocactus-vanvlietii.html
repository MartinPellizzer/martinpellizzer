Notocactus vanvlietii

Notocactus vanvlietii, commonly known as the Van Vliet's notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its columnar stem and clusters of small, spiny, yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the sparse soil. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss in its harsh environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
