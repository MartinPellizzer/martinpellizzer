Nebelia globosa

Nebelia globosa, commonly known as the globular nebelia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. Its small, white to pale yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Nebelia globosa is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
