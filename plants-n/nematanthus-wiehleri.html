Nematanthus wiehleri

Nematanthus wiehleri, commonly known as the red-tipped plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers.
