Neohopea isoptera

Neohopea isoptera, commonly known as the white-flowered ironwood, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath.
