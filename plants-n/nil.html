Nil

Nil, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family and is characterized by its stinging hairs that can cause skin irritation. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in spikes.

Nil is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
