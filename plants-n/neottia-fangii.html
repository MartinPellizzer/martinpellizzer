Neottia fangii

Neottia fangii, commonly known as the Fang's twayblade, is a small, herbaceous orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with two opposite leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in a spike. Neottia fangii is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests.
