Nierembergia linariifolia var. linariifolia

Nierembergia linariifolia var. linariifolia, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its charming, fragrant blooms.
