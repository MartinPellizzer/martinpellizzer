Nemastylis purpurea var. minor

Nemastylis purpurea var. minor, commonly known as the small purple nemastylis, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant purple foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically enclosed within a spathe, a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
