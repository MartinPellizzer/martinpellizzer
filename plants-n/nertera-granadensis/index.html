Nertera Granadensis

Nertera granadensis is a small, evergreen flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its compact growth habit and vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts. Nertera granadensis is often used as a trailing plant, adding a touch of elegance to hanging baskets and window sills.
