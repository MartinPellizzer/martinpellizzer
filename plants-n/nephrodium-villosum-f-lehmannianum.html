Nephrodium villosum f. lehmannianum

Nephrodium villosum f. lehmannianum, commonly known as the hairy nephroid, is a fern characterized by its distinctive, leathery, ovate fronds.

The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a short, creeping rhizome and a dense cluster of fertile fronds at the base. Its fronds are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
