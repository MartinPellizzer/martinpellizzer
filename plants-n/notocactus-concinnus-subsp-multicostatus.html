Notocactus concinnus subsp. multicostatus

Notocactus concinnus subsp.

multicostatus, commonly known as the mult costate notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are ribbed and often covered in white or pale blue spines.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This subspecies is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
