Nephrodium cinereum

Nephrodium cinereum, commonly known as the black fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its dark, leathery fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance.

Nephrodium cinereum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
