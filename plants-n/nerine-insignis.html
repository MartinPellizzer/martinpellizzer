Nerine insignis

Nerine insignis, commonly known as the rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a red or purple throat. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in mild climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Nerine insignis is known for its ability to bloom reliably in late summer or early autumn.
