Nasturtium × capillipes

Nasturtium × capillipes, commonly known as the creeping nasturtium, is a hybrid species within the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, edible flowers that come in shades of orange and red. The plant produces heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are also edible and have a peppery taste. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Nasturtium × capillipes is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and as a natural pest deterrent.
