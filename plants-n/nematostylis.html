Nematostylis

Nematostylis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plants are typically small, with slender stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. They produce small, inconspicuous flowers that are often green or yellowish in color.

Nematostylis species are primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in shaded, moist environments.
