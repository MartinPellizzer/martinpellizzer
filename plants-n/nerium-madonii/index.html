Nerium Madonii

Madon's oleander, scientifically known as Nerium madonii, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white throughout the summer months. This plant is highly adaptable, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its resilience and ornamental value have led to its cultivation in various parts of the world. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic, requiring caution in handling and placement.
