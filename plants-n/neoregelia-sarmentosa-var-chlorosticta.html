Neoregelia sarmentosa var. chlorosticta

Neoregelia sarmentosa var. chlorosticta, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, elongated leaves that are dark green with a prominent central stripe, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, long-lasting inflorescences in shades of yellow to orange, which attract pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
