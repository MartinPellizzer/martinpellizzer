Nassella karstenii

Nassella karstenii, commonly known as Karsten's needlegrass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Nassella karstenii is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
