Nemophila menziesii var. liniflora

Nemophila menziesii var. liniflora, commonly known as the slender blue-eyed Mary, is a low-growing annual herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, branched stems and produces small, delicate blue or violet flowers with a white eye. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for wildflower gardens and ecological restoration projects.
