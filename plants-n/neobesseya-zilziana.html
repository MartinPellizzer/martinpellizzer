Neobesseya zilziana

Neobesseya zilziana, commonly known as the Zilzian bottle tree, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, bottle-shaped trunk that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. Its leaves are typically small and clustered at the base of the stem, while the trunk is often covered in a rough, bark-like texture.

Neobesseya zilziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
