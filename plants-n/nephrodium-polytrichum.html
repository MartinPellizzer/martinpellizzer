Nephrodium polytrichum

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often lobed fronds that are typically submerged or floating in freshwater environments. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the undersides of the fronds, producing spores for propagation.

Nephrodium polytrichum plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen through photosynthesis.
