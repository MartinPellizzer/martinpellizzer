Nageia endlicheriana

Nageia endlicheriana, commonly known as the mountain pine or the dwarf pine, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, often twisted trunk and dense, needle-like foliage. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Nageia endlicheriana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing mountainous terrain and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
