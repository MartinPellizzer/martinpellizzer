Nerium carneum

Nerium carneum, commonly known as the red milkweed tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds with silky hairs. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Nerium carneum is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
