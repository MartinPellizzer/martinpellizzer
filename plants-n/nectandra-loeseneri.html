Nectandra loeseneri

Nectandra loeseneri, commonly known as the yellow sand-wood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown heartwood and yellow sapwood. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and is valued for its durable timber. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by elliptical, dark green berries.

Nectandra loeseneri is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
