Narcissus × conspicuus

Narcissus × conspicuus, commonly known as the Dutch narcissus, is a hybrid perennial bulbous plant native to the Netherlands.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 20–40 cm, with grass-like foliage and a single flower per stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Narcissus × conspicuus is valued for its early blooming habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
