Nasturtium Crystallinum

Nasturtium crystallinum is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant, jewel-like flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique name comes from the crystalline appearance of its leaves and petals. This plant is not only visually stunning but also has medicinal and culinary uses. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively unknown species in the botanical world.
