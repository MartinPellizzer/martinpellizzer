Nenga nagensis

Nenga nagensis, commonly known as the Namib Desert orchid, is a rare and specialized plant found exclusively in the hyperarid regions of Namibia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is adapted to extreme desert conditions through its unique morphological features. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color, blending with its arid environment. Its root system is highly efficient at absorbing and storing water, allowing it to survive prolonged dry periods.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological sensitivity, Nenga nagensis is considered a vulnerable species.
