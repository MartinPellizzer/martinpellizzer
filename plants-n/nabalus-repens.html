Nabalus repens

Nabalus repens, commonly known as the creeping navelwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Parietaria.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with slender stems that root at the nodes and spread along the ground. Nabalus repens is often found growing on rocky or disturbed soils in temperate regions.
