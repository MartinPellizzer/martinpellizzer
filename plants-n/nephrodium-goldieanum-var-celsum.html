Nephrodium goldieanum var. celsum

Nephrodium goldieanum var. celsum, commonly known as the golden kidney fern, is a unique variant of the nephrodium genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, kidney-shaped fronds that are typically golden-green in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are compound, with a serrated margin and a velvety texture.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
