Neeragrostis contrerasii

Neeragrostis contrerasii, commonly known as the Mexican bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Neeragrostis contrerasii is also valued for its ability to form dense mats, making it suitable for erosion control in certain environments.
