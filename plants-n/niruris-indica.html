Niruris indica

Niruris indica, commonly known as the Indian nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to regions of South and Southeast Asia, including India and Indonesia. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Niruris indica is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
