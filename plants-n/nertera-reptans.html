Nertera reptans

Nertera reptans, commonly known as the inch plant, is a trailing evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features small, rounded, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slight blue or silver hue. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Nertera reptans is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.
