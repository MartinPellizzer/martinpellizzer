Nemia rubra

Nemia rubra, commonly known as the red nemia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.

Nemia rubra is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
