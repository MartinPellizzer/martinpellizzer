Notelaea microcarpa var. velutina

Notelaea microcarpa var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet-leafed notelaea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

This plant is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
