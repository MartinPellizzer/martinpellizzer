Nuxia rupicola

Nuxia rupicola, commonly known as the rock nuxia, is a hardy shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves that are adapted to withstand arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Nuxia rupicola is valued for its resilience and is used in traditional medicine and ornamental gardening.
