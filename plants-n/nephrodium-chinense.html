Nephrodium chinense

Nephrodium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese kidney fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped fronds that are deeply lobed and often have a velvety texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures include sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Nephrodium chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
