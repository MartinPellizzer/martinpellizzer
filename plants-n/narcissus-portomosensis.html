Narcissus portomosensis

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a white corona, typical of the Amaryllidaceae family. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves and blooms in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal habitats.
