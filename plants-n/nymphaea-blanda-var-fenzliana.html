Nymphaea blanda var. fenzliana

Nymphaea blanda var. fenzliana, commonly known as the Fenzli's water lily, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in the early morning. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental ponds and wetland restoration projects.
