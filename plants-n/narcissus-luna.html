Narcissus luna

Narcissus luna, commonly known as the moon narcissus, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its large, white flowers with a yellow center, resembling a crescent moon. The plant typically blooms in early spring, adding a delicate touch to gardens and natural landscapes. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Narcissus luna is relatively hardy and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
