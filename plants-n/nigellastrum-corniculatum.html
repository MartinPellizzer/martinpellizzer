Nigellastrum corniculatum

Nigellastrum corniculatum, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped seed pods that give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance.

Nigellastrum corniculatum is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
