Nicodemia diversifolia var. lucida

Nicodemia diversifolia var. lucida, commonly known as the shining nicodemia, is a strikingly ornamental plant characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that reflect light brilliantly.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of forests. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season, adding a delicate contrast to its foliage. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a waxy texture that enhances their shine.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings or as a houseplant.
