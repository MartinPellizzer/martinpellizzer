Nidularium cyaneum

Nidularium cyaneum, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Nidularium cyaneum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
