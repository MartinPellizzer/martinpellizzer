Nivenia dispar

Nivenia dispar, commonly known as the mountain holly.

It is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, holly-like leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Nivenia dispar is often found in cloud forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
