Nertera depressa var. papuana

Nertera depressa var. papuana, commonly known as the Papuan Nertera, is a compact, trailing plant native to the tropical regions of Papua.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
