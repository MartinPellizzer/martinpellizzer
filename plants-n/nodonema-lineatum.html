Nodonema lineatum

Nodonema lineatum, commonly known as the slender trailing geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features slender, hairy stems that trail along the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Nodonema lineatum is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit and ornamental value.
