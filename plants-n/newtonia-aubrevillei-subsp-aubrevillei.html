Newtonia aubrevillei subsp. aubrevillei

Newtonia aubrevillei subsp.

aubrevillei, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, compound leaves and showy yellow flowers.

The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in tropical and subtropical regions.
