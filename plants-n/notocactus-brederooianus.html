Notocactus brederooianus

Notocactus brederooianus, commonly known as the Brederoo cactus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stem and sparse, spiny foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
