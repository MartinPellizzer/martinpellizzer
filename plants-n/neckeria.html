Neckeria

Neckeria, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flower heads surrounded by bracts that resemble leaves. The plants typically grow in coastal areas, thriving in saline and sandy soils. Neckeria species are often found in the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
