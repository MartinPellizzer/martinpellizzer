Neraudia melastomifolia f. parvifolia

Neraudia melastomifolia f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved melastomia, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smaller than those of the species, giving it its varietal name. Its flowers are usually clustered in cymes and are often pink or purple in color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
