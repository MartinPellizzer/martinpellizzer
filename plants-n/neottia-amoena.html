Neottia amoena

Neottia amoena, commonly known as the elegant twayblade, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.

The plant has a slender, leafless stem and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are usually arranged in a loose cluster. Neottia amoena is characterized by its lack of true leaves, with photosynthesis occurring through its stem and roots.
