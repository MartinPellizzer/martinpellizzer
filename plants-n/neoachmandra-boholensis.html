Neoachmandra boholensis

Neoachmandra boholensis, commonly known as the Bohol grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow leaves. Native to the Philippines, particularly the island of Bohol, it thrives in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in panicles.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.
