Neomammillaria petterssonii

Neomammillaria petterssonii, commonly known as the Pettersson's nipple cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and typically has 8 to 12 prominent tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
