Notocactus scopa f. marchesii

Notocactus scopa f. marchesii, commonly known as the Marchesi's Crown of Thorns, is a unique hybrid cactus originating from South America.

It features a tall, columnar stem with a dense covering of spines, which provides protection against herbivores and excessive sunlight. The plant produces striking, elongated flowers that range in color from yellow to red, typically blooming in the spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for indoor cultivation, though it requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Despite its ornamental appeal, it is important to handle the plant with care due to its sharp spines.
