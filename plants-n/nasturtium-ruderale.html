Nasturtium ruderale

Nasturtium ruderale, commonly known as field nasturtium, is an annual flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows up to 1 meter in height and thrives in well-drained soil. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in various regions around the world.

The edible leaves and flowers are often used in culinary applications, though the plant is more commonly appreciated for its ornamental value.
