Neomammillaria macdougalii

Neomammillaria macdougalii, commonly known as the Macdougalii cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and is characterized by its dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
