Neomammillaria celsiana

Neomammillaria celsiana, commonly known as the Celsian Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a ribbed structure and produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil. It is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in succulent gardens.

Neomammillaria celsiana is a member of the Cactaceae family and is often grown as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
