Neomarica decora

Neomarica decora, commonly known as the decorative orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Neomarica decora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
