Nymphaea thermalis

Nymphaea thermalis, commonly known as the thermal water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to regions with geothermal activity.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in the early summer. The plant thrives in shallow, warm waters, often found near hot springs or geothermal areas. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.

Nymphaea thermalis plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its unique thermal environments.
