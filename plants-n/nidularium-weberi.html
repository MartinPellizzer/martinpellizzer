Nidularium weberi

Nidularium weberi, commonly known as the Weber's nest fern, is a unique plant belonging to the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped fronds that resemble a nest, hence its common name. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with a dense covering of roots, which helps it anchor to rocky surfaces. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Nidularium weberi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
