Nonea affinis

Nonea affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Nonea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Nonea affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
