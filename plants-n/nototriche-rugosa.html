Nototriche rugosa

Nototriche rugosa, commonly known as the rough nototriche, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Nototriche rugosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
