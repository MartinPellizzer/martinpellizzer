Nivenia spathulata

Nivenia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed nivenia, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in shrubland and coastal areas, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Nivenia spathulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
