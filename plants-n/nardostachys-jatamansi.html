Nardostachys jatamansi

Nardostachys jatamansi, commonly known as Indian valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, woolly leaves and small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant thrives in alpine and subalpine regions, often found in rocky soils at high altitudes. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their sedative and calming properties.
