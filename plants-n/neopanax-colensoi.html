Neopanax colensoi

Neopanax colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit with multiple stems arising from the base. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in panicles, while the fruit is a bright red drupe.

Neopanax colensoi is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
