Nuxia corrugata

Nuxia corrugata, commonly known as the rough nuxia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, dark green leaves with a rough, textured surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Nuxia corrugata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
