Narcissus × romoi var. arenasii

Narcissus × romoi var. arenasii, commonly known as the Arensii daffodil, is a hybrid daffodil species originating from the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
