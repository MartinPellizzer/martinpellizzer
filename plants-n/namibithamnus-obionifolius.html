Namibithamnus obionifolius

Namibithamnus obionifolius, commonly known as the Namib Desert shrub, is a hardy plant adapted to arid environments.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to the Namib Desert in Namibia. The plant has small, narrow leaves that reduce water loss through transpiration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color.

Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater in the dry, sandy soils of its habitat.
