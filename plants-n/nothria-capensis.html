Nothria capensis

Nothria capensis, commonly known as the Cape nothra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Nothria capensis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, showcasing its resilience to harsh environments.
