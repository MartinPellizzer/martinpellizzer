Nephrodium × Uliginosum

Carnation fern, scientifically known as Nephrodium × uliginosum, is a unique and striking plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments. This hybrid fern is prized for its vibrant, colorful fronds that resemble blooming flowers, giving it its common name. Native to parts of Asia, it has become a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal. Its intricate leaf structures and ability to thrive in humid conditions make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to maintain its health and vibrant appearance.
