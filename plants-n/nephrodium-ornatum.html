Nephrodium ornatum

Nephrodium ornatum, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It features a dense, compact growth habit with bright yellow, star-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense clumps. Its foliage is evergreen, maintaining a vibrant appearance throughout the year.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
