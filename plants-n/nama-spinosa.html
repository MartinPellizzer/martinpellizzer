Nama spinosa

Nama spinosa, commonly known as the spiny lichen, is a unique plant that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is a type of lichen, which is a symbiotic organism composed of algae and fungi. The plant has a distinctive appearance, characterized by its rough, spiny texture and dark green to blackish color. Nama spinosa is often found on rock surfaces or in crevices where it can retain moisture.

Due to its hardy nature, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
