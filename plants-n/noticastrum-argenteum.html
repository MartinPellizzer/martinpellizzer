Noticastrum argenteum

Noticastrum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into small, feather-like segments.

Noticastrum argenteum is often used in traditional medicine and is adapted to arid environments.
