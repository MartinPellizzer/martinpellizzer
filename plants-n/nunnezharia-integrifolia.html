Nunnezharia integrifolia

Nunnezharia integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed nunnezharia, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of the southern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth-edged and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, which bloom in the spring. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Nunnezharia integrifolia thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
