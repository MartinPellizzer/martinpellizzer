Nuphar advena var. variegata

Nuphar advena var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated water lily, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that often display striking variegation of green and white. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom above the water surface. Its roots are anchored in the mud at the bottom of ponds and lakes.

Nuphar advena var. variegata plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various water organisms.
