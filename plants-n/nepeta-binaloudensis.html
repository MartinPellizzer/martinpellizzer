Nepeta binaloudensis

Nepeta binaloudensis, commonly known as Binaloud catmint, is a perennial herb native to Iran.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is often fragrant and can be used in traditional medicine.

Nepeta binaloudensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
