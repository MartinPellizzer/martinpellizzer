Nilgirianthus membranaceus

Nilgirianthus membranaceus, commonly known as the Nilgiri sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Nilgirianthus membranaceus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
