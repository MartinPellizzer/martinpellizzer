Nekemias megalophylla var. megalophylla

Nekemias megalophylla var. megalophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf nekemias, is a species native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along riverbanks or in dense forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in terminal inflorescences, and are typically greenish or yellowish in color.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
