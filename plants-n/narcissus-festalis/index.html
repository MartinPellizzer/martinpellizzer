Narcissus Festalis

Narcissus festalis is a rare and delicate flower native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its striking white petals and a yellow-orange center that resembles a sun. This species is often found in rocky soils and shaded forest areas, thriving in cool, moist environments. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now classified as endangered. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of both botanical interest and conservation concern.
