Nepeta nivalis

Nepeta nivalis, commonly known as snow nepeta, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant produces small, fragrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

Nepeta nivalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
