Nemesia pageae

Nemesia pageae, commonly known as the Page's nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as a low-growing perennial, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nemesia pageae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
