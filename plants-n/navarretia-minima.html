Navarretia minima

Navarretia minima, commonly known as the dwarf navarretia, is a small flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, often in coastal areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.
