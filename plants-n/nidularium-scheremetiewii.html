Nidularium scheremetiewii

Nidularium scheremetiewii, commonly known as the Scheremetiew's flax lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
