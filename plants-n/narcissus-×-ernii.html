Narcissus × ernii

Narcissus × ernii, commonly known as the hybrid daffodil, is a cross between various species of narcissus.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped corona and brightly colored petals, typically yellow or white. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It produces showy blooms in early spring, often preceding the leaves.

Narcissus × ernii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is widely cultivated in gardens.
