Neoregelia leviana

Neoregelia leviana, commonly known as the silver star bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water and nutrients. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, often resembling a star. Its foliage is typically covered in a waxy coating that gives it a silvery appearance.

Neoregelia leviana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
