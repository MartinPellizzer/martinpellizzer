Nidularium kautskyanum

Nidularium kautskyanum, commonly known as the Kautsky's nest fern, is a rare and unusual member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a creeping rhizome that produces small, clustered sporangia. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
