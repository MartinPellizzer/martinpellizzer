Neoschroetera tridentata

Neoschroetera tridentata, commonly known as the Trinidad sword fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, arching fronds that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk covered in a dense mass of dark green, leathery leaves. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.

Neoschroetera tridentata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
