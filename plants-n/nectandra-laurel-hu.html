Nectandra laurel-hu

Nectandra laurel-hu, commonly known as the wild cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and bay laurel. The tree is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark and leaves contain essential oils, giving it a distinct spicy aroma.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
