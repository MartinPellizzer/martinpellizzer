Nigella damascena var. oligogyna

Nigella damascena var. oligogyna, commonly known as the Persian flower or love in a bottle, is a compact, annual herbaceous plant native to the Middle East.

It features small, delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces distinctive, three-lobed, gray-green leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy. Its seed pods, which resemble tiny capsules, mature into a striking black color and release seeds when mature.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
