Nicodemia grandifolia

Nicodemia grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaved nicodemia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
