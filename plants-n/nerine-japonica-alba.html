Nerine japonica alba

Nerine japonica alba, commonly known as the white Japanese lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Japan.

It features striking white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Nerine japonica alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
