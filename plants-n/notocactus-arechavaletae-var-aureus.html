Notocactus arechavaletae var. aureus

Notocactus arechavaletae var. aureus, commonly known as the golden notocactus, is a rare cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in yellowish-green spines, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
