Nageia usta

Nageia usta, commonly known as the dwarf fan-leaf cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its compact size, with a short trunk and a rosette of pinnate, fan-shaped leaves.
