Nepeta cryptantha

Nepeta cryptantha, commonly known as catmint, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their calming properties.

Nepeta cryptantha is also valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and fragrance.
