Neomammillaria geminispina

Neomammillaria geminispina, commonly known as the twin-spined milk barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant features prominent, tuberculate ribs that are often adorned with clusters of short, white spines. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Neomammillaria geminispina is well adapted to dry conditions, with a shallow root system and thick, water-storing stems.
