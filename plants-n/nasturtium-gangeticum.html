Nasturtium gangeticum

Nasturtium gangeticum, commonly known as the Indian nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Its stems are hairy and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, making it a striking addition to gardens.

Nasturtium gangeticum is also known for its edible leaves and flowers, which have a peppery flavor and are used in various culinary applications.
