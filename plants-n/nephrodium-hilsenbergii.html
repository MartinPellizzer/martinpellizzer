Nephrodium hilsenbergii

Nephrodium hilsenbergii, commonly known as the Hilsenbergs' nephroid, is a rare and delicate fern native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, lace-like fronds that are typically dark green with a subtle sheen. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in dense tropical forests. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered on the undersides of the fronds.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
