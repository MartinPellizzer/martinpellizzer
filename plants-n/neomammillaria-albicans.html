Neomammillaria albicans

Neomammillaria albicans, commonly known as the white-flowered pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, cylindrical stem. The plant features white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the spring. Its surface is covered with white to pale blue spines, and it has areoles that bear both spines and flowers.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
