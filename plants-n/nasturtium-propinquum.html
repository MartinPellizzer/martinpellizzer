Nasturtium propinquum

Nasturtium propinquum, commonly known as the common nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is an annual or biennial species that thrives in temperate climates. Its flowers are edible and often used in culinary applications.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is known for its peppery flavor.
