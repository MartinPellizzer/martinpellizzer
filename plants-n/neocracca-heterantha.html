Neocracca heterantha

Neocracca heterantha, commonly known as the slender whitebeard orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils, while its leaves are narrow and strap-like.

Neocracca heterantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
