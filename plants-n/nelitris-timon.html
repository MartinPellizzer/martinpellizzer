Nelitris timon

Nelitris timon, commonly known as the Timon's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptation to waterlogged soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its botanical classification places it within the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family.
