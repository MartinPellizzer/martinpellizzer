Normeyera doerriana

Normeyera doerriana, commonly known as the Doerrien's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread and form dense clusters. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Normeyera doerriana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
