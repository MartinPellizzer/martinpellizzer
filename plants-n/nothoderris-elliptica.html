Nothoderris elliptica

Nothoderris elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic derris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The elliptic derris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
