Nidularium deleonii

Nidularium deleonii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Nidularium deleonii is also noted for its slow growth and specific care requirements, making it a challenging but rewarding plant to cultivate.
