Nothaphoebe malabonga

Nothaphoebe malabonga, commonly known as the Malabonga nothaphoebe, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and smooth, grayish bark. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are small and clustered in catkins.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
