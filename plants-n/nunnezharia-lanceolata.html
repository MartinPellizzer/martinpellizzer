Nunnezharia lanceolata

Nunnezharia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nunnezharia, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
