Nissolia diversifolia

Nissolia diversifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf nissolia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are typically white or pale yellow. Nissolia diversifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
