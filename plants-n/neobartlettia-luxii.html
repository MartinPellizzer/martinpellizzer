Neobartlettia luxii

Neobartlettia luxii, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in a waxy coating. Neobartlettia luxii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical research.
