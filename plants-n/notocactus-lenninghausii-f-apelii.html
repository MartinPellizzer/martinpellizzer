Notocactus lenninghausii f. apelii

Notocactus lenninghausii f. apelii, commonly known as the Apelii's columnar cactus, is a rare and unique hybrid within the Notocactus genus.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height, often with a slightly ribbed texture. The plant produces striking, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming during the warmer months. It is native to South America, specifically Brazil, and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts and botanists.
