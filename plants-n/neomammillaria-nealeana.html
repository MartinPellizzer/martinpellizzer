Neomammillaria nealeana

Neomammillaria nealeana, commonly known as the Neale's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria nealeana is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
