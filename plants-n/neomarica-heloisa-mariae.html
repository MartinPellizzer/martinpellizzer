Neomarica heloisa-mariae

Neomarica heloisa-mariae, commonly known as the Mariae's Flame Lily, is a striking member of the Araceae family native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant red spatheae that enclose a yellow spadix, creating a visually dramatic flower structure. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Neomarica heloisa-mariae is a rare species, making it a sought-after plant in botanical gardens and specialized horticulture.
