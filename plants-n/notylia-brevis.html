Notylia brevis

Notylia brevis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved notylia, is a small shrub native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and tea tree species. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers that appear in clusters. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathland environments.
