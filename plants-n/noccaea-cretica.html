Noccaea cretica

Noccaea cretica, commonly known as Cretan stonecress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and basal rosette of leaves. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, demonstrating remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions.

Noccaea cretica is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
