Nephrodium spinulosum subsp. dilatatum

Nephrodium spinulosum subsp.

dilatatum, commonly known as the broad-leaved nephroid, is a fern native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive, slightly curled shape.

The fronds are typically dark green and have a rough, textured surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
