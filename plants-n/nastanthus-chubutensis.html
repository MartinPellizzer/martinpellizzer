Nastanthus chubutensis

Nastanthus chubutensis, commonly known as the Chubut carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Patagonian region of Argentina.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. Nastanthus chubutensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
