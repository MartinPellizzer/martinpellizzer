Nestotus lanuginosus var. lanuginosus

Nestotus lanuginosus var. lanuginosus, commonly known as the silky nestotus, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation.
