Notocactus × schlosseri

Notocactus × schlosseri, commonly known as the Schlosseri hybrid, is a hybrid cactus species resulting from the cross between Notocactus phyllocactus and Notocactus leucopappus.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and prominent areoles. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Notocactus × schlosseri prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
