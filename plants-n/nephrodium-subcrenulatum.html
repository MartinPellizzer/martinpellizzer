Nephrodium subcrenulatum

Nephrodium subcrenulatum, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds along their length. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a serrated margin.

Nephrodium subcrenulatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
