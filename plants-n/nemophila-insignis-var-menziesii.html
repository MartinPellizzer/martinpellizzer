Nemophila insignis var. menziesii

Nemophila insignis var. menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' flower.

It is a low-growing annual herb native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant produces small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
