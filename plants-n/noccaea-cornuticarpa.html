Noccaea cornuticarpa

Noccaea cornuticarpa, commonly known as the horned watercress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine environments.
