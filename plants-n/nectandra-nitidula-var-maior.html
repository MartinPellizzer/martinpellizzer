Nectandra nitidula var. maior

Nectandra nitidula var. maior, commonly known as the glossy nectandra, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval-shaped berries containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its aromatic wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
