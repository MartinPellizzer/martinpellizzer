Notocactus submammulosus var. pampeanus

Notocactus submammulosus var. pampeanus, commonly known as the Pampas notocactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, ribbed stems and sparse, spiny areoles. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing during the warmer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ornamental appearance.
