Nephelium cuspidatum var. robustum

Nephelium cuspidatum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust longan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that give way to edible, sweet, and juicy drupes. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of nutritious fruit.
