Neoporteria castaneoides

Neoporteria castaneoides, commonly known as the red-flowered ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and hardy nature.

Neoporteria castaneoides is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
