Narcissus × pravianoi

Narcissus × pravianoi, commonly known as the Pravian hybrid daffodil, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers with a bright yellow corona and white petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant grows to a height of about 30–50 cm, with strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant display.

The species is a hybrid between Narcissus tazetta and Narcissus poeticus, contributing to its unique floral characteristics.
