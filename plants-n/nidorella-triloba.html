Nidorella triloba

Nidorella triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has three lobes on its leaves, which give it its scientific name, and it thrives in humid, shady environments. Nidorella triloba is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and pleasant scent.

However, it is relatively rare in the wild and requires specific care to grow successfully.
