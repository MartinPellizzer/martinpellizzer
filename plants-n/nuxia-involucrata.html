Nuxia involucrata

Nuxia involucrata, commonly known as the red sand rat plant, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its leaves are small, narrow, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs to reduce water loss.

Nuxia involucrata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
