Notogrammitis gunnii

Notogrammitis gunnii, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Notogrammitis gunnii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
