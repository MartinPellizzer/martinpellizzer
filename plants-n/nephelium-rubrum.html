Nephelium rubrum

Nephelium rubrum, commonly known as the rambutan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, spiny exterior fruit, which gives it a unique appearance. The fruit contains a sweet, white, fleshy aril surrounding the seed. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.
