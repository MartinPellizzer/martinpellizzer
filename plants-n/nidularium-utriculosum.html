Nidularium utriculosum

Nidularium utriculosum, commonly known as the bladderpod orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, inflated seed pods that resemble bladders. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
