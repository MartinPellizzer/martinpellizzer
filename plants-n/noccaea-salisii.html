Noccaea salisii

Noccaea salisii, commonly known as the Salisii's penny-cress, is a small annual plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Noccaea salisii is known for its delicate appearance and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
