Neanotis indica var. deltoidea

Neanotis indica var. deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid leaf sarsaparilla, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive deltoid-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that has been traditionally used in herbal medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional pharmacology.
