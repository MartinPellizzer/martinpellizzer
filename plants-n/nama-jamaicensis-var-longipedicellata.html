Nama jamaicensis var. longipedicellata

Nama jamaicensis var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the Jamaican name plant, is a tropical species native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping in warm climates.
