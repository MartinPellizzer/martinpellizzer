Narcissus fernandesii

Narcissus fernandesii, commonly known as the Fernandes' daffodil, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive orange center. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and grass-like in appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
