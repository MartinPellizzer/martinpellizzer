Nasturtium leptopetalum

Nasturtium leptopetalum, commonly known as the slenderleaf watercress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its small, edible leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are often used in salads and have a peppery flavor similar to watercress.

Nasturtium leptopetalum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
