Nivenia levynsiae

Nivenia levynsiae, commonly known as the Kerguelen Island flax, is a flowering plant native to the subantarctic region of the Kerguelen Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the short summer season. Nivenia levynsiae is adapted to harsh, windy conditions and grows in rocky or sandy soils.

It plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and food for native invertebrates.
