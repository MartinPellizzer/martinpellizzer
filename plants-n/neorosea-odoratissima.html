Neorosea odoratissima

Neorosea odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant rose of the tropics, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white petals and a strong, sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

Neorosea odoratissima thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
