Nototriche hirtipes

Nototriche hirtipes, commonly known as the hairy nototriche, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy surface, contributing to its overall rugged appearance.

Nototriche hirtipes is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
