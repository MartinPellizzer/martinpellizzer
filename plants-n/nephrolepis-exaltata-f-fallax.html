Nephrolepis exaltata f. fallax

Nephrolepis exaltata f. fallax, commonly known as the fallax fern, is a varietal form of the sword fern.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright fronds that are typically shorter and more compact than those of the standard Nephrolepis exaltata. The leaves are leathery and have a distinctive, glossy appearance, often with a dark green hue. This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its elegant and refined appearance.
