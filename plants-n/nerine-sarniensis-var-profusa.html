Nerine sarniensis var. profusa

Nerine sarniensis var. profusa, commonly known as the broad-leafed nerine, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant produces strap-like, broad leaves that are typically a vibrant green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its late summer to early autumn flowering and its ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
