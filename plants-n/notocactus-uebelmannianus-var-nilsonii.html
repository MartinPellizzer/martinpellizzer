Notocactus uebelmannianus var. nilsonii

Notocactus uebelmannianus var. nilsonii, commonly known as the Nilson's Crown of Thorns, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, shrub-like structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its thorny appearance. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems to survive drought conditions.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and resilience, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
