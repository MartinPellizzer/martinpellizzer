Neuracanthus robecchii

Neuracanthus robecchii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, typically blooming during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Neuracanthus robecchii is a popular ornamental plant due to its long-lasting blooms and ease of care.
