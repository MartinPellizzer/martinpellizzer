Nephrodium gracilescens var. didymochlaenoides

Nephrodium gracilescens var. didymochlaenoides, commonly known as the slender kidney fern, is a variant of the kidney fern family with distinctive frond morphology.

Its fronds are typically slender and pinnately compound, featuring a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant exhibits a creeping rhizome system, allowing it to spread vegetatively across moist, shaded environments. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest undergrowth or along stream banks.

The leaves are characterized by their finely divided segments and a pale, almost translucent quality, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
