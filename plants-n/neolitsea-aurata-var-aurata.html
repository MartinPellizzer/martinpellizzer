Neolitsea aurata var. aurata

Neolitsea aurata var. aurata, commonly known as golden flame tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
