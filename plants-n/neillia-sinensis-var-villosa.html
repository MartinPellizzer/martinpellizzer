Neillia sinensis var. villosa

Neillia sinensis var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet or blue flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its specific variety name "villosa.". It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.
