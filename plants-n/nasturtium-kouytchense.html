Nasturtium kouytchense

Nasturtium kouytchense, commonly known as the Andean watercress, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andes region of South America.

The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers with five petals, typical of the Tropaeolaceae family. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery, watercress-like flavor, making it a valued ingredient in local cuisines.
