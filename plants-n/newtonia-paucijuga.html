Newtonia paucijuga

Newtonia paucijuga, commonly known as the slender Newtonia, is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being elliptical and slightly hairy.

The flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and range in color from pale yellow to greenish-white, attracting various pollinators.
