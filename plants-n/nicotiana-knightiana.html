Nicotiana knightiana

Nicotiana knightiana, commonly known as the Knight's tobacco, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the highlands of South America, particularly in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces aromatic leaves.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
