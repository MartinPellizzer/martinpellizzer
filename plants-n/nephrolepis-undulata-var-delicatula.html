Nephrolepis undulata var. delicatula

Nephrolepis undulata var. delicatula, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a variety of the common maidenhair fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with arching, leathery fronds that have a distinctive undulating edge. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a soft, feathery appearance.

This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its elegant foliage.
