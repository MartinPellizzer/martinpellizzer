Nasturtium ovirense

Nasturtium ovirense, commonly known as the swamp nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to wetland areas in South America.

It belongs to the family Tropaeolaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant also plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
