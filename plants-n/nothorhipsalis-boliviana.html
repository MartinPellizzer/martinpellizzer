Nothorhipsalis boliviana

Nothorhipsalis boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian crown of thorns, is a hybrid cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its hybrid nature, it is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in arid environments.
