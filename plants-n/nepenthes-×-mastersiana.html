Nepenthes × mastersiana

Nepenthes × mastersiana, commonly known as the hybrid tropical pitcher plant, is a remarkable species native to Borneo.

It is a hybrid between Nepenthes lowii and Nepenthes rafflesiana, resulting in a plant with unique morphological traits. The plant produces large, vibrant red pitchers that secrete a digestive fluid to trap insects. Its adaptability to various environmental conditions makes it a popular choice for cultivation.

The botanical interest in Nepenthes × mastersiana lies in its hybrid origin and ecological significance in its native habitat.
