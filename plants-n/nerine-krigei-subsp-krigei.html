Nerine krigei subsp. krigei

Nerine krigei subsp.

krigei, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa. It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with a distinctive trumpet-shaped form.

The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in late winter to early spring.
