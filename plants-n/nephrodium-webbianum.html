Nephrodium webbianum

Nephrodium webbianum, commonly known as the Webbian nephroid, is a unique fern-like plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It exhibits a distinctive morphology with its fiddlehead-like young fronds and a complex, branched structure. The plant's leaves are typically divided into numerous small, lance-shaped segments, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Botanically, it is notable for its reproductive structures, which include sporangia that release spores for propagation.
