Notocactus rauschii

Notocactus rauschii, commonly known as the Rausch notocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and ribbed structure, which helps it store water efficiently in its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
