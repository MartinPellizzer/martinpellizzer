Nictitella aspera

Nictitella aspera, commonly known as the rough nictitella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in rough, textured hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom in early summer.

Nictitella aspera is characterized by its distinctive foliage and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
