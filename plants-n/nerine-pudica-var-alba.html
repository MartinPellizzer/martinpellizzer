Nerine pudica var. alba

Nerine pudica var. alba, commonly known as the white rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its bulbs are adapted for storing water and nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
