Naiadothrix longipes

Naiadothrix longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed naiad, is a aquatic plant native to tropical freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that emerge from the water, often in clusters. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the sediment in aquatic habitats.

Naiadothrix longipes plays a role in providing shelter for aquatic organisms and improving water quality through oxygenation.
