Neottia Sinica

Chinese twayblade, scientifically known as Neottia sinica, is a rare and delicate orchid native to eastern Asia. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. This plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its unique biology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
