Nepeta gracilescens

Nepeta gracilescens, commonly known as slender catmint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Nepeta gracilescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild aromatic properties.
