Neoporteria reichei f. aerocarpa

Neoporteria reichei f. aerocarpa, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a unique variant of the neoporteria genus.

This fern is characterized by its delicate, airy fronds that give it a light, ethereal appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens. The plant's fronds are typically arching and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
