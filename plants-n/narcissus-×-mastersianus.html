Narcissus × mastersianus

Narcissus × mastersianus, commonly known as the Crown Imperial, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, tall flower stalks that bear numerous large, showy flowers resembling a crown. The plant produces trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of yellow, orange, or red, depending on the cultivar. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are strap-shaped and form a dense rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
