Nomocharis × finlayorum

Nomocharis × finlayorum, commonly known as the hybrid lilies, is a cross between Nomocharis and other lily species, resulting in a unique blend of characteristics.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to New Zealand, known for its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which appear in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in temperate regions for its vibrant floral display.
