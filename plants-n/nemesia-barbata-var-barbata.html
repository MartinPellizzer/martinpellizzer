Nemesia barbata var. barbata

Nemesia barbata var. barbata, commonly known as the bearded nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which are typically opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and hanging baskets.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period, which can last from spring through early autumn.
