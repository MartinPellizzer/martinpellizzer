Nephrodium odontocarpos

Nephrodium odontocarpos, commonly known as the toothed kidney fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, toothed fronds that resemble the shape of a kidney, hence its common name. The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy in the wild.
