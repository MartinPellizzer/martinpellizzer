Neomolinia

Neomolinia, commonly known as the tallgrass bluestem, is a genus of grasses native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. The plants typically have narrow, bluish-green leaves and produce small, spike-like inflorescences. Neomolinia species are often found in prairie ecosystems and are important components of native grasslands.

They are also valued for their role in erosion control and wildlife habitat.
