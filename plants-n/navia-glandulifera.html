Navia glandulifera

Navia glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular navia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Navia glandulifera thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
