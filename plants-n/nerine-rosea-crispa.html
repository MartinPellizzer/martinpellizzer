Nerine rosea-crispa

Nerine rosea-crispa, commonly known as the crinkled nerine, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, crinkled, rose-pink petals that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
