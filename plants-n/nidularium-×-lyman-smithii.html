Nidularium × lyman-smithii

Nidularium × lyman-smithii, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a rare and fascinating plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers that are often a striking combination of purple and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
