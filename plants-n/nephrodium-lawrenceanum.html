Nephrodium lawrenceanum

Nephrodium lawrenceanum, commonly known as the Lawrencean nephroid, is a rare and unusual fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is characterized by its unique, kidney-shaped fronds that are often brightly colored. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are typically found on the underside of its leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
