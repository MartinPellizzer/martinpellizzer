Narcissus × macleayi

Narcissus × macleayi, commonly known as the Macleay narcissus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a prominent central corona, which is a defining feature of the narcissus genus. Native to South Africa, this species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves, which are evergreen in nature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
