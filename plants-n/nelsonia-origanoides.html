Nelsonia origanoides

Nelsonia origanoides, commonly known as the lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a distinctive citrus aroma.

Nelsonia origanoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its use in herbal teas.
