Nerium oleander var. flore-variegato

Nerium oleander var. flore-variegato, commonly known as the variegated oleander, is a cultivar of the oleander plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring white and green patterns on its lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink or red flowers in the summer months. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscapes across warm climates.
