Notocactus erinaceus f. corynodes

Notocactus erinaceus f. corynodes, commonly known as the spiny cactus or the hedgehog cactus, is a unique hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and a compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the base of the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
