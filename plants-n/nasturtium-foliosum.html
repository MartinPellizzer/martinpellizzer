Nasturtium foliosum

Nasturtium foliosum, commonly known as the leafy nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant is known for its peppery, edible leaves that are often used in salads and culinary applications. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium foliosum also serves as a natural pest repellent, attracting beneficial insects like ladybugs.
