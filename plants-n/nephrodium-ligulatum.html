Nephrodium ligulatum

Nephrodium ligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nephrolepis, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with long, slender fronds that are typically arching or drooping. The leaves are bipinnate, with finely divided segments that give the plant a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant foliage and easy care requirements.
