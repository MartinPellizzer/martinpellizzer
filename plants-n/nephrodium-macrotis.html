Nephrodium macrotis

Nephrodium macrotis, commonly known as the large-leaf nephroid, is a fern-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Nephroidaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its fronds are often bioluminescent, a unique adaptation that aids in attracting pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
