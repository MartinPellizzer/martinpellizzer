Noccaea prostii

Noccaea prostii, commonly known as the European watercress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. This plant typically grows in moist, calcareous environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. It produces small white or greenish flowers and has lobed, toothed leaves that are often submerged or floating.
