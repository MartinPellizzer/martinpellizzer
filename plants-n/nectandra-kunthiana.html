Nectandra kunthiana

Nectandra kunthiana, commonly known as the yellow sandarac tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its essential oils, extracted from the bark and leaves, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Nectandra kunthiana plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
