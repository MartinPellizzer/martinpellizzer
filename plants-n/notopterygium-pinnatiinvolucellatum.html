Notopterygium pinnatiinvolucellatum

Notopterygium pinnatiinvolucellatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features finely divided, pinnately arranged leaves that are often covered in a soft, woolly pubescence. Its small white flowers appear in compact clusters and are followed by small, dry fruits.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
