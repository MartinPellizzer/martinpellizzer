Notocactus laetivirens

Notocactus laetivirens, commonly known as the Lemon Cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in clusters of small, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

Notocactus laetivirens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care.
