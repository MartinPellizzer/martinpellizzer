Nasturtium × stenophyllum

Nasturtium × stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed nasturtium, is a hybrid species within the Tropaeolaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of orange and red. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its flowers are not only ornamental but also edible, with a peppery taste similar to watercress.

Nasturtium × stenophyllum is often used in companion planting to deter pests due to its strong scent and natural pest-repelling properties.
