Nemia capensis

Nemia capensis, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a South African plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its distinctive white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to the harsh coastal climate.

Nemia capensis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
