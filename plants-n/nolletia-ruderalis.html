Nolletia ruderalis

Nolletia ruderalis, commonly known as the field gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which are typically tubular in shape. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Nolletia ruderalis is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
