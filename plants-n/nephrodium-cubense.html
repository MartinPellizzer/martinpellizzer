Nephrodium cubense

Nephrodium cubense, commonly known as the Cuban kidney fern, is a species of fern native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce both roots and new fronds. Its leaves are usually bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.
