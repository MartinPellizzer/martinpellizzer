Narcissus mion

Narcissus mion, commonly known as the Mion narcissus, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, cup-shaped flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces white or yellow blooms with a distinct, sweet fragrance. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and floral arrangements.
