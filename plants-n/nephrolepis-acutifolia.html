Nephrolepis acutifolia

Nephrolepis acutifolia, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, slender fronds that are typically arching or drooping, with a distinctive, serrated edge. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Nephrolepis acutifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
