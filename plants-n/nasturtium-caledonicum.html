Nasturtium caledonicum

Nasturtium caledonicum, commonly known as the Caledonian nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to the island of Tasmania.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant features striking, vibrant yellow or orange flowers with distinctive heart-shaped petals. Its leaves are broad, lobed, and have a slightly hairy texture.
