Nephrodium vestitum

Nephrodium vestitum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Nephrodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Nephrodium vestitum is notable for its ability to absorb moisture through its leaves, making it well adapted to humid climates.
