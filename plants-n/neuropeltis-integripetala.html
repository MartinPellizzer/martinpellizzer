Neuropeltis integripetala

Neuropeltis integripetala, commonly known as the smooth-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a blend of purple and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts interested in exotic orchids.
