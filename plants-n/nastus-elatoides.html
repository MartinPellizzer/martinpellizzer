Nastus elatoides

Nastus elatoides, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange bell-shaped flowers. Nastus elatoides thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
