Neomammillaria ortizrubioana

Neomammillaria ortizrubioana, commonly known as the Ortiz Rubio cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a ribbed stem that can grow up to 15 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
