Nasturtium macrorhizum

Nasturtium macrorhizum, commonly known as the large-rooted nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow, often with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Its roots are thick and fleshy, which help it thrive in moist environments.

Nasturtium macrorhizum is often cultivated as a decorative plant and is also used in herbal remedies due to its peppery taste and medicinal properties.
