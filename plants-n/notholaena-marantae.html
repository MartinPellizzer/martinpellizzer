Notholaena marantae

Notholaena marantae, commonly known as the red-flowered staghorn fern, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that form a rosette shape, providing a lush, tropical appearance. It produces vibrant red or orange inflorescences that bloom in the spring, adding a dramatic visual element to its display.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
