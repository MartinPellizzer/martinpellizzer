Notylia broadwayi

Notylia broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Notylia broadwayi is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist habitats.
