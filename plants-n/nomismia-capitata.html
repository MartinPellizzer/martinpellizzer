Nomismia capitata

Nomismia capitata, commonly known as the clustered grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in dense, spherical heads. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Nomismia capitata is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
