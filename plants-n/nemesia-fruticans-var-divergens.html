Nemesia fruticans var. divergens

Nemesia fruticans var. divergens, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a low-growing, annual plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters along the stems. The plant has hairy, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its rapid growth and prolific flowering make it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and wildflower habitats.
