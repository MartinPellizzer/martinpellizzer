Nemacladus rubescens var. tenuis

Nemacladus rubescens var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender redstem, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its foliage is lance-shaped with a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.
