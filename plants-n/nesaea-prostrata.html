Nesaea prostrata

Nesaea prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate nesaea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that typically spreads across the ground, producing small, tubular flowers. The plant features slender stems covered in fine hairs and possesses opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink and bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.
