Neoporteria bulbocalyx

Neoporteria bulbocalyx, commonly known as the bulb calyx orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a bulbous calyx that resembles a small, rounded bulb. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Neoporteria bulbocalyx is often grown as a rare and exotic ornamental plant due to its unusual appearance and limited distribution.
