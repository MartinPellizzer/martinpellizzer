Nothoscordum uniflorum

Nothoscordum uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower false garlic, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system and strap-shaped leaves. Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated for both its aesthetic value and potential medicinal properties.
