Nidularium marechalii

Nidularium marechalii, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
