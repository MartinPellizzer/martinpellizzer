Nabiasodendron excelsum

Nabiasodendron excelsum, commonly known as the giant sky fern, is a towering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its massive fronds that can reach lengths of over 10 meters. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with high humidity and well-drained soil. Its unique structure allows it to capture sunlight efficiently in its mountainous habitat.
