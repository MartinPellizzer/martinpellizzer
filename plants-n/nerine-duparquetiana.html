Nerine duparquetiana

Nerine duparquetiana, commonly known as the pink nerine, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and distinctive foliage.
