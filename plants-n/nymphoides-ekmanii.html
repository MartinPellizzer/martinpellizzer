Nymphoides ekmanii

Nymphoides ekmanii, commonly known as the dwarf water lily, is a small aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, floating leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. The plant produces delicate white or pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Nymphoides ekmanii is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value.
