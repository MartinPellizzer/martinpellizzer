Neoregelia martinellii

Neoregelia martinellii, commonly known as the Crimson Star Bromeliad, is a striking ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a striking red color. The plant features rosettes of broad, waxy leaves that can vary in color from green to reddish hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.

Neoregelia martinellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
