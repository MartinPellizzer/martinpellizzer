Nestylix

Nestylix, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features long, slender leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in moisture retention. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their unique, flame-like appearance.

Nestylix thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
