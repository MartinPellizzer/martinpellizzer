Nerine radiata

Nerine radiata, commonly known as the rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive red or purple spot at the base. The plant produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, stocky stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Nerine radiata is known for its ability to bloom reliably in the summer months.
