Neomarica nitida

Neomarica nitida, commonly known as the silver sword orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom of tiny, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Neomarica nitida is known for its resilience and ability to adapt to various growing conditions.
