Nothoscordum dynamiandrum

Nothoscordum dynamiandrum, commonly known as the dynamite garlic.

This hybrid plant is a cross between Allium and Nothoscordum species, exhibiting characteristics of both parents. It is a hardy perennial that produces striking purple or white flowers in clusters. The plant is known for its strong garlic-like scent, which is more intense than that of typical onions.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
