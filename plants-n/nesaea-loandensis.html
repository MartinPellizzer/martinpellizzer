Nesaea loandensis

Nesaea loandensis, commonly known as the Loandens mountain orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocky substrates or in dense forest undergrowth. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Nesaea loandensis is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
