Neolitsea kraduengensis

Neolitsea kraduengensis, commonly known as Kradueng tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries.

The tree is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
