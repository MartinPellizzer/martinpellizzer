Nenax hirta

Nenax hirta, commonly known as the hairy nenax, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming during the warmer months. Nenax hirta thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

It is an important plant in local ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
