Nasturtium sylvestre var. rivulare

Nasturtium sylvestre var. rivulare, commonly known as the water nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange or red flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, sharing some characteristics with other cruciferous plants.

Its edible parts, including leaves and flowers, are known for their peppery flavor and potential health benefits.
