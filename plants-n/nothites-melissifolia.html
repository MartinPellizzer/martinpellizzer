Nothites melissifolia

Nothites melissifolia, commonly known as the honeyleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that emit a subtle honey-like fragrance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Nothites melissifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
