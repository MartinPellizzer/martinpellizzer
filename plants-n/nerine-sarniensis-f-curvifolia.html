Nerine sarniensis f. curvifolia

Nerine sarniensis f. curvifolia, commonly known as the curl-leaved nerine, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, strap-like leaves that curve gracefully, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add a dramatic touch to garden landscapes.
