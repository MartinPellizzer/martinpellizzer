Neillia densiflora

Neillia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered neillia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Neillia densiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
