Neomammillaria fertilis

Neomammillaria fertilis, commonly known as the Fertilis Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a smooth, greenish-gray surface covered in short, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
