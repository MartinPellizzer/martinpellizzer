Neyraudia arundinacea var. zollingeri

Neyraudia arundinacea var. zollingeri, commonly known as the Zollinger's bamboo, is a striking ornamental grass native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender, arching culms that are often reddish in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces feathery, panicle-like inflorescences that add visual interest during the growing season. It thrives in partial to full sun and requires well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its rapid growth and elegant form make it a popular choice for privacy screens and ornamental planting.
