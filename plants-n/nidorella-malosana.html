Nidorella malosana

Nidorella malosana, commonly known as the Malosan orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces clusters of white or pale yellow blossoms.

Nidorella malosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
