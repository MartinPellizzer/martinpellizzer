Nigella degenii subsp. barbro

Nigella degenii subsp.

barbro, commonly known as Barbro's fennel, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender, branched stems and finely divided, fern-like leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in loose racemes. Its seeds are small, ovoid, and often have a faintly aromatic scent.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is adapted to cool, rocky habitats.
