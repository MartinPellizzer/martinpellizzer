Nasa cymbopetala

Nasa cymbopetala, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its leaves are broad and silvery, which helps it reflect sunlight and conserve moisture.

Nasa cymbopetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
