Narcissus × montserratii var. vallrutae

Narcissus × montserratii var. vallrutae, commonly known as the Valencia daffodil, is a hybrid daffodil native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its large, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow to orange in color. The plant features a slender, grass-like foliage and is known for its early spring blooming habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
