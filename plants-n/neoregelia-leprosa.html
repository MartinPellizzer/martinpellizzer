Neoregelia leprosa

Neoregelia leprosa, commonly known as the Leprosa Bromeliad, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with vibrant red and orange flowers. Its foliage is often marked with intricate patterns and can vary in color from green to reddish hues.

Neoregelia leprosa is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
