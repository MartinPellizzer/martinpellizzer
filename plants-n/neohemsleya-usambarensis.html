Neohemsleya usambarensis

Neohemsleya usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambara rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky habitats, thriving in the humid and shaded environments of the Usambara Mountains. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, while its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
