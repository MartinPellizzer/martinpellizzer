Nerine laticoma subsp. krigei

Nerine laticoma subsp.

krigei, commonly known as the Krige's nerine, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa. It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive white throat.

The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and succulent, adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

The subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
