Nissolia debilis

Nissolia debilis, commonly known as the slender nissolia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow, followed by small, oval berries.

Nissolia debilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
