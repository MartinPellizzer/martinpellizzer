Nephrodium gongyloides

Nephrodium gongyloides, commonly known as the Chinese kidney fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped fronds that are usually dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Nephrodium gongyloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
