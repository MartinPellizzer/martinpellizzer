Navarretia leucocephala

Navarretia leucocephala, commonly known as the white-flowered navarretia, is a flowering plant native to California and parts of Oregon.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Navarretia leucocephala is also known for its ability to thrive in coastal climates with mild temperatures and moderate rainfall.
