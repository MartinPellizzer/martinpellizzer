Nasturtium uniseriatum

Nasturtium uniseriatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow. The plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants. Its flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their bright colors and ability to attract pollinators.

The plant is also known for its peppery taste, making it a popular choice for edible landscaping.
