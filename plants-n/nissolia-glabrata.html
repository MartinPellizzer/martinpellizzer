Nissolia glabrata

Nissolia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth nissolia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and undergrowth. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy in texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in greenish-white or pale yellow hues.

Nissolia glabrata is notable for its adaptability to humid climates and its role in supporting local insect pollinators.
