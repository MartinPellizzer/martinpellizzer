Nipponocalamus virens var. nebulosus

Nipponocalamus virens var. nebulosus, commonly known as the nebulous bamboo, is a variant of the green bamboo species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, delicate foliage that gives it a misty, ethereal appearance. The plant has slender, upright stems that grow in dense clumps, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a smooth, slightly waxy texture.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
