Notoceras

Notoceras, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly in regions of Argentina, Chile, and Peru. Notoceras species are typically herbaceous perennials, characterized by their hairy stems and showy yellow or orange composite flowers. They often grow in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climates.

The genus includes several species, such as Notoceras acanthocarpum, which is known for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
