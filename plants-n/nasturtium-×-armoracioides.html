Nasturtium × armoracioides

Nasturtium × armoracioides, commonly known as the yellow wood sorrel, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the common nasturtium. The plant is native to Europe and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Nasturtium × armoracioides is also known for its peppery taste, which makes it a useful addition to salads and herbal teas.
