Navia diffusa

Navia diffusa, commonly known as the spreading navia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features a robust root system and erect, branched stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture.

Navia diffusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
