Nerisyrenia linearifolia var. mexicana

Nerisyrenia linearifolia var. mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican linearleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that grow in a delicate, feathery arrangement. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The leaves are typically a deep green, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface.
