Nymphaea thermarum

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open in the early morning and close in the afternoon. The plant thrives in still, mineral-rich waters and has adapted to the unique environmental conditions of its habitat. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing a habitat for various aquatic organisms.
