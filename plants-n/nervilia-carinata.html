Nervilia carinata

Nervilia carinata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Nervilia carinata is known for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
