Nidularium minutum

Nidularium minutum, commonly known as the dwarf spider plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Nidularium minutum is valued for its low maintenance and ornamental appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
