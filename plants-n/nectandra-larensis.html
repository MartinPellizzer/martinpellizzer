Nectandra larensis

Nectandra larensis, commonly known as the Larensis camphor tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic wood, which contains essential oils. It typically grows in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

Nectandra larensis plays a role in local ecosystems and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
