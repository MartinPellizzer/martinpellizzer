Notocactus Apricus Var. Muricatus

The notocactus apricus var. muricatus is a unique and striking cactus native to arid regions of South America. Known for its vibrant orange to red flowers, it adds a bold splash of color to any garden or indoor space. Its spiny stems and compact growth make it both visually appealing and relatively easy to care for. This variety is prized for its resilience and ability to thrive in low-water conditions. Whether grown outdoors or in a pot, it is a fascinating addition to any plant collection.
