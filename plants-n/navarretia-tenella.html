Navarretia tenella

Navarretia tenella, commonly known as the Santa Barbara navarretia, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats. The plant produces delicate white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Navarretia tenella is part of the Portulacaceae family and is known for its ephemeral life cycle, completing its growth and reproduction quickly in response to seasonal rains.
