Nonea karsensis

Nonea karsensis, commonly known as the Kars mountain bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Black Sea region of Turkey.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky and forested areas, thriving in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are elongated and slightly hairy, adapting to its mountainous habitat.

Nonea karsensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
