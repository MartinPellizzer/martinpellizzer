Navia tenuifolia

Navia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf navia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, coastal areas and thrives in well-drained soils. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Navia tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
