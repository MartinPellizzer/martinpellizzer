Nuphar sericea

Nuphar sericea, commonly known as the silky water lily, is a aquatic plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, floating leaves that are covered in a waxy, silky coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots are rhizomatous, allowing it to spread and colonize wetland areas.

Nuphar sericea plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
