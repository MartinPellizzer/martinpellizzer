Nephrolepis niphoboloides

Nephrolepis niphoboloides, commonly known as the silver sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Nephrolepis niphoboloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
