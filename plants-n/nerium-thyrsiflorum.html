Nerium thyrsiflorum

Nerium thyrsiflorum, commonly known as the Arabian oleander, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, featuring dense, dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy clusters of pink, red, or white flowers in terminal thyrses, which bloom throughout the summer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
