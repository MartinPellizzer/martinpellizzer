Noccaea affinis

Noccaea affinis, commonly known as the common rock pepper, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and lobed leaves.
