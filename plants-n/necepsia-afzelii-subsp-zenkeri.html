Necepsia afzelii subsp. zenkeri

Necepsia afzelii subsp.

zenkeri, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the flora in its native habitat.
