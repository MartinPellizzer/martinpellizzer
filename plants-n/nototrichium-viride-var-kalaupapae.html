Nototrichium viride var. kalaupapae

Nototrichium viride var. kalaupapae, commonly known as the Kalaupapa greenweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. Its foliage is often covered in fine, green hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
