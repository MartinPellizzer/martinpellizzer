Notocactus ottonis var. villa-velhensis

Notocactus ottonis var. villa-velhensis, commonly known as the Villa Velhensa Cactus, is a rare and unique cactus species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a white, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
