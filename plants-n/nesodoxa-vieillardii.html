Nesodoxa vieillardii

Nesodoxa vieillardii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in warm climates as a houseplant or garden specimen.
