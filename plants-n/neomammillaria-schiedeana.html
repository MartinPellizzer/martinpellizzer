Neomammillaria schiedeana

Neomammillaria schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiedeana Pincushion Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a compact growth habit with prominent areoles and spines.

Neomammillaria schiedeana is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
