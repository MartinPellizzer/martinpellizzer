Notocactus warasii

Notocactus warasii, commonly known as the Warasii cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system adapted to survive in dry, rocky soils.

Notocactus warasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
