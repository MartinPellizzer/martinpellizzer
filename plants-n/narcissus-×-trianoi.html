Narcissus × trianoi

Narcissus × trianoi, commonly known as the Trianoi daffodil, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a yellow tepal arrangement and a central crown. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
