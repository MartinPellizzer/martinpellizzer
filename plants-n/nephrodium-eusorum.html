Nephrodium eusorum

Nephrodium eusorum, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly appearance, which gives it a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky surfaces or in sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in desert botany and ecological adaptation.
