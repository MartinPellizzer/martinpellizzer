Negretia urens

Negretia urens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a slight pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Negretia urens is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
