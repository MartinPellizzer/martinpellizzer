Neochilenia huascensis

Neochilenia huascensis, commonly known as the Huascensis cactus, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, green stems and spiny areoles. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Neochilenia huascensis is valued for its resilience and is often cultivated in xerophytic gardens.
