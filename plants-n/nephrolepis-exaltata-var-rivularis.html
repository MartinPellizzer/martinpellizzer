Nephrolepis exaltata var. rivularis

Nephrolepis exaltata var. rivularis, commonly known as the water fern, is a variety of the sword fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It features fronds that are typically longer and more delicate compared to the standard Nephrolepis exaltata. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a feathery appearance that adds a lush, tropical look to gardens or indoor spaces.

This variety is well-suited for bog gardens, ponds, or as a hanging plant, where it can thrive in consistently damp conditions.
