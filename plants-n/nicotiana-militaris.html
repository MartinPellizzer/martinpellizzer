Nicotiana militaris

Nicotiana militaris, commonly known as the military tobacco, is a species native to South America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its aromatic foliage and ornamental value.
