Nitraria Sibirica Var. Globicarpa

Nitraria sibirica var. globicarpa is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and the Middle East. It is known for its small, round fruits that resemble tiny globes, hence its common name. This plant thrives in harsh environments with low rainfall and high temperatures. Its ability to survive in extreme conditions makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects. Due to its resilience, it has gained attention for potential use in combating desertification.
