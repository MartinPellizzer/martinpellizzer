Narcissus Pseudonarcissus Proles Bicolor

Narcissus pseudonarcissus proles bicolor is a rare and striking variant of the daffodil known for its unique bicolor petals. This plant exhibits a fascinating blend of colors, often featuring a mix of yellow and white in its blooms. It is native to specific regions where its genetic diversity has led to this distinctive appearance. The plant's beauty has made it a subject of interest among horticulturists and botanists alike. Its cultivation requires careful attention to soil and climate conditions to thrive.
