Nematolepis

Nematolepis, commonly known as the needle grass, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats or tufts. The plants typically have small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered along the stems.
