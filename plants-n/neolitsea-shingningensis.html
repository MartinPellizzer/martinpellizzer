Neolitsea shingningensis

Neolitsea shingningensis, commonly known as the Shingning sweet-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province, growing in subtropical forest environments. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
