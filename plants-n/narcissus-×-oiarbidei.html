Narcissus × oiarbidei

Narcissus × oiarbidei, commonly known as the Oiarbidei daffodil, is a hybrid species within the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a prominent, trumpet-shaped corona. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–50 cm, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

This hybrid is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and resilience in various soil conditions.
