Newcastelia cephalantha var. oblonga

Newcastelia cephalantha var. oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Newcastelia, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
