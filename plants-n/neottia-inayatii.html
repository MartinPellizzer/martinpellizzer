Neottia inayatii

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a slender, leafless stem that typically grows close to the ground. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow color and a lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. Neottia inayatii thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
