Navarretia rattanii

Navarretia rattanii, commonly known as the silverleaf navarretia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils along streambanks and in open woodlands. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray, providing a striking contrast to its pale flowers.

Navarretia rattanii is a short-lived perennial that plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecosystem biodiversity.
