Nama bartlettii

Nama bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's nama, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. It has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with narrow, succulent leaves.

Nama bartlettii plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small fauna.
