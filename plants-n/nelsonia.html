Nelsonia

Nelsonia, commonly known as the Nelson's tree fern, is a genus of tree ferns native to New Zealand.

It consists of several species, including Nelsonia sericea and Nelsonia cunninghamii, which are characterized by their tall, slender trunks and feathery fronds. The plants have a distinctive appearance with a smooth, pale trunk that often has a fibrous sheath. They thrive in moist, shaded environments and are often found in rainforests.

Nelsonia species are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological importance in their native habitats.
