Nectandra reflexa

Nectandra reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed nectandra, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and it is often found in humid, lowland forests.
