Nebropsis hystrix

Nebropsis hystrix, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or yellow. The plant has thick, waxy leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, sunny environments and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
