Nephrodium brachypodum

Nephrodium brachypodum, commonly known as the shortfoot fern, is a unique member of the water fern family, Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, short-stemmed structure and pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are robust and help anchor it to moist, nutrient-rich soils.

The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a lush appearance throughout the year.
