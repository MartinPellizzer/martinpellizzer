Nephrodium firmifolium

Nephrodium firmifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf nephroid, is a rare and unique fern species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a distinctive, fan-like pattern. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and can grow up to several feet in length, making it a striking addition to botanical gardens.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for ecological and conservation studies.
