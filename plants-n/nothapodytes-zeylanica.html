Nothapodytes zeylanica

Nothapodytes zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Sri Lanka and southern India.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

The tree is often found in moist, lowland forests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
