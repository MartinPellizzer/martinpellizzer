Narcissus × alleniae

Narcissus × alleniae, commonly known as the orange daffodil, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with strap-like leaves that are bright green and narrow. Its bulbs are adapted for storing nutrients, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
