Neobracea martiana var. robusta

Neobracea martiana var. robusta, commonly known as the robust neobracea, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
