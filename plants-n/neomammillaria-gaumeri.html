Neomammillaria gaumeri

Neomammillaria gaumeri, commonly known as the Gaumer's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Neomammillaria gaumeri is often cultivated for its compact size and unique appearance.
