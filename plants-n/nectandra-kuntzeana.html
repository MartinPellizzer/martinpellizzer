Nectandra kuntzeana

Nectandra kuntzeana, commonly known as the yellow sand-wood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown heartwood. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Nectandra kuntzeana is valued for its durable timber and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.
