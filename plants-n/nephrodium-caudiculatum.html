Nephrodium caudiculatum

Nephrodium caudiculatum, commonly known as the slender kidney fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
