Nashia armata

Nashia armata, commonly known as the armed nashia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in compact clusters, and bloom in the spring.

Nashia armata is adapted to thrive in harsh environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
