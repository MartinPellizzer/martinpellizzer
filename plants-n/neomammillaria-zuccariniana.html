Neomammillaria zuccariniana

Neomammillaria zuccariniana, commonly known as the Zuccarinian mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by small, reddish fruits. It is a compact, slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
