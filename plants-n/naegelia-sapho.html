Naegelia sapho

Naegelia sapho, commonly known as the Sapho's naegelia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Naegelia sapho is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
