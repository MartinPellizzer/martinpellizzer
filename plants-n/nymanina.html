Nymanina

Nymanina, commonly known as the small white orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plants are characterized by their delicate, white or pale pink flowers and compact growth habit. They typically grow in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.
