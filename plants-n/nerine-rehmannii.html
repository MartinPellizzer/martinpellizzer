Nerine rehmannii

Nerine rehmannii, commonly known as the rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, upward-facing flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, narrow shape. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Nerine rehmannii is known for its ability to bloom reliably in late summer and early autumn.
