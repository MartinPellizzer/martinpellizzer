Nesaea praetermissa

Nesaea praetermissa, commonly known as the lesser nesaea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale blue flowers bloom in early summer, adding a subtle beauty to its habitat.

Nesaea praetermissa is relatively uncommon and is often overlooked due to its modest appearance.
