Narcissus magniobesus

Narcissus magniobesus, commonly known as the giant narcissus, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces a single, upright stem with a cluster of large, white or yellow flowers. It is native to southern Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bulbs of Narcissus magniobesus are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
