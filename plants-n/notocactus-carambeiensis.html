Notocactus carambeiensis

Notocactus carambeiensis, commonly known as the Carambola Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is adapted to arid environments.

The fruit of this plant is edible and has a tangy, citrus-like flavor.
