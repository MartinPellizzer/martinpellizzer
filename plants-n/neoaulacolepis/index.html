Neoaulacolepis

Neoaulacolepis is a prehistoric plant that thrived during the Paleozoic era. It belongs to the group of early land plants known as lycophytes. This ancient species played a crucial role in the development of early terrestrial ecosystems. Its fossilized remains provide valuable insights into the evolution of plant life. Studying Neoaulacolepis helps scientists understand how early plants adapted to life on land.
