Nephrodium siifolium

Nephrodium siifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved nephroid, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive nephroid-shaped leaves.

The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth, waxy surface that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming during the late spring and early summer. The plant is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
