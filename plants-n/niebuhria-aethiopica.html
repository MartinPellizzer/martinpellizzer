Niebuhria aethiopica

Niebuhria aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Ethiopia.

It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems to survive drought conditions.

Niebuhria aethiopica is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
