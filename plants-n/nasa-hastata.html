Nasa hastata

Nasa hastata, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several glossy black seeds.

Nasa hastata is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands.
