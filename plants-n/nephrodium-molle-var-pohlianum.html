Nephrodium molle var. pohlianum

Nephrodium molle var. pohlianum, commonly known as the soft kidney fern, is a variant of the soft kidney fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fiddlehead-like fronds that unfurl into a lush, feathery appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are typically light green, with a soft texture that gives it its common name.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
