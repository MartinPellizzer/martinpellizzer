Natalia

Natalia, commonly known as the Natalia flower, is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Natalia has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsules as its fruit, containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
