Nasa ramirezii

Nasa ramirezii, commonly known as the Ramirez's night-blooming cereus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and its ability to bloom at night, producing large, fragrant white flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening only for a few hours before closing.

Nasa ramirezii is valued for its ornamental beauty and its unique nocturnal blooming habit.
