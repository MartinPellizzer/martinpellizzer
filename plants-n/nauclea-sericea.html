Nauclea sericea

Nauclea sericea, commonly known as silverleaf nauclea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its smooth, silver-gray bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in terminal panicles, and it produces capsule fruits containing several seeds.

Nauclea sericea is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions.
