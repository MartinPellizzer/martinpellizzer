Nerium splendens

Nerium splendens, commonly known as the splendid oleander, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring thick, fleshy leaves and showy clusters of flowers. The flowers are typically pink, red, or white, and they bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The plant is also known for its adaptability to various climates and its ability to withstand drought conditions.
