Neomammillaria subdurispina

Neomammillaria subdurispina, commonly known as the spiny nipple cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
