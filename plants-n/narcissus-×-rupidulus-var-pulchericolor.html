Narcissus × rupidulus var. pulchericolor

Narcissus × rupidulus var. pulchericolor, commonly known as the purple crown narcissus, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit. The flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive purple or violet crown, which is a notable feature of this variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, showcasing its adaptability to challenging environments.
