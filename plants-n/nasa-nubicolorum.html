Nasa nubicolorum

Nasa nubicolorum, commonly known as the cloud forest violet, is a flowering plant native to the Andean cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and white petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, thriving in the cool, humid conditions of its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Nasa nubicolorum is valued not only for its beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
