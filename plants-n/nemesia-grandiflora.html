Nemesia grandiflora

Nemesia grandiflora, commonly known as the common nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive spurred petal, which is a key botanical feature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Nemesia grandiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
