Navia pauciflora

Navia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered navia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has adapted to survive in dry environments with its succulent leaves and deep root system. It produces small, oval fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Navia pauciflora is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
