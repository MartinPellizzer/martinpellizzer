Nothocnide mollissima

Nothocnide mollissima, commonly known as the silky nothocnide, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive tactile quality. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
