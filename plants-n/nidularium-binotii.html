Nidularium binotii

Nidularium binotii, commonly known as the black orchid, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves that form a rosette shape, creating a unique microhabitat. The plant produces strikingly beautiful, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nidularium binotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
