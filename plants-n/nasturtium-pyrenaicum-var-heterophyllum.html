Nasturtium pyrenaicum var. heterophyllum

Nasturtium pyrenaicum var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the Pyrenean nasturtium, is a herbaceous plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage and flowers are edible, often used in salads or as a garnish.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and mild peppery flavor.
