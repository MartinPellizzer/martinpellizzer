Nicipe tortuosa

Nicipe tortuosa, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its twisting, serpentine stems that grow in a helical pattern. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a decorative element in landscaping.
