Nasa profundiserrata

Nasa profundiserrata, commonly known as the deep-toothed nasa, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with deep, serrated lobes that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Nasa profundiserrata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
