Neoporteria curvispina f. minor

Neoporteria curvispina f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf neoporteria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and small spaces due to its compact size.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and subtle floral display.
