Nephelea canescens

Nephelea canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf nephelea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Nephelea canescens is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
