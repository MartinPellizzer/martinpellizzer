Notocactus soldtianus

Notocactus soldtianus, commonly known as the Soldtian Hedgecactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny clusters of stems that form a compact, shrub-like structure. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Notocactus soldtianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
