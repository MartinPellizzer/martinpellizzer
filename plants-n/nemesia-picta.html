Nemesia picta

Nemesia picta, commonly known as the painted nemesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with trailing stems, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Nemesia picta blooms throughout the growing season, adding continuous color to gardens.
