Neomammillaria Zephyranthoides

Neomammillaria zephyranthoides is a rare and striking cactus native to Mexico. It is known for its unique, woolly appearance and delicate, pinkish-white flowers. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its slow growth rate makes it a prized addition to many succulent collections. Due to its limited distribution, it is often considered a conservation concern.
