Normeyera diversicolor

Normeyera diversicolor, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, multicolored flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the rainforests of Central and South America. The plant features long, slender stems that bear clusters of showy, asymmetrical blooms in shades of red, yellow, and blue. Its unique coloration and intricate floral structure make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.

Normeyera diversicolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
