Nerine sarniensis var. alba

Nerine sarniensis var. alba, commonly known as the white rain lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, white, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
