Nothocissus spicifera

Nothocissus spicifera, commonly known as the spiny vine, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Vitaceae family.

It is characterized by its twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

Nothocissus spicifera is typically found in moist, shaded environments in tropical and subtropical regions.
