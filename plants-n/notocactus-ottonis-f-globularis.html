Notocactus ottonis f. globularis

Notocactus ottonis f. globularis, commonly known as the globular notocactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense, rounded stem covered in short, cylindrical spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique shape and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
