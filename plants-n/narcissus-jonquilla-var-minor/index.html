Narcissus Jonquilla Var. Minor

Narcissus jonquilla var. minor is a lesser-known but striking member of the daffodil family. It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and a single, cup-shaped flower with a bright yellow corona. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its compact size and early blooming habit make it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more common daffodil varieties.
