Narcissus salmonii

Narcissus salmonii, commonly known as the salmon narcissus, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its striking, salmon-pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its bulbs are perennial, and it is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
