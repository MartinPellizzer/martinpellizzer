Nigritella suaveolens var. nigroconopsea

Nigritella suaveolens var. nigroconopsea, commonly known as the black-winged nightshade, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, toxic seeds.
