Nonatelia macrophylla

Nonatelia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf nonatelia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Nonatelia macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
