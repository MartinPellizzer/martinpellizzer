Nymphaea fragrans

Nymphaea fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive fragrance. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots anchor it to the bottom of the water body.

Nymphaea fragrans plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
