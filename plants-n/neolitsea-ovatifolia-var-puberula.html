Neolitsea ovatifolia var. puberula

Neolitsea ovatifolia var. puberula, commonly known as the smooth-leafed sandarac tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, Vietnam, and the Philippines.
