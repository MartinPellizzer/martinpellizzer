Nephrolepis schkuhrii

Nephrolepis schkuhrii, commonly known as the Schkuhri's sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Nephrolepis schkuhrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
