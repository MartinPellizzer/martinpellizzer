Nephrodium meniscioides

Nephrodium meniscioides, commonly known as the heart-shaped fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Nephrodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped fronds, which are typically deeply lobed and have a leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that produce spores, typical of ferns.

Nephrodium meniscioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
