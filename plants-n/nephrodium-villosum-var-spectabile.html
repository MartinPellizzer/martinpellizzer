Nephrodium villosum var. spectabile

Nephrodium villosum var. spectabile, commonly known as the showy nephroid, is a striking fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, arching fronds that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant's foliage is typically bright green, with a slightly variegated pattern that enhances its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and aesthetic qualities make it a desirable addition to botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
