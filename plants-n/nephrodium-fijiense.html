Nephrodium fijiense

Nephrodium fijiense, commonly known as the Fiji water fern, is a rare and endemic fern species native to the island of Fiji.

It belongs to the family Nephrodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests along stream banks or in rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are clustered on the undersides of its fronds, aiding in spore dispersal.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Nephrodium fijiense is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts to protect its unique botanical traits.
