Nandina domestica f. gigantea

Nandina domestica f. gigantea, commonly known as the giant heavenly bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its large, showy foliage.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length, giving it a lush, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is a compact shrub that thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
