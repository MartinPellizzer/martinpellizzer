Neomammillaria fragilis

Neomammillaria fragilis, commonly known as the fragile mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant in collections.
