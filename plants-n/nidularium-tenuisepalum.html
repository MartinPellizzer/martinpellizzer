Nidularium tenuisepalum

Nidularium tenuisepalum, commonly known as the slender-sepal nidularium, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a water-holding cup. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
