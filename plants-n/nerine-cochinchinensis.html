Nerine cochinchinensis

Nerine cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped blooms on slender stems, which are often accompanied by narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The bulbs are perennial, allowing the plant to return year after year in suitable climates.
