Notylia carnosiflora

Notylia carnosiflora, commonly known as the fleshy-flowered notylia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, and often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with thick, succulent stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are primarily pollinated by insects, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Notylia carnosiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
