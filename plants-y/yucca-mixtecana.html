Yucca mixtecana

Yucca mixtecana, commonly known as the Mixtec yucca, is a perennial shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence bearing white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are sword-shaped and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Yucca mixtecana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses by indigenous communities.
