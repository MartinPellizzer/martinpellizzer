Yulania xinyangensis

Yulania xinyangensis, commonly known as the Xinyang flower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its showy, single-layered flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, red or orange fruits in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

Yulania xinyangensis prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
