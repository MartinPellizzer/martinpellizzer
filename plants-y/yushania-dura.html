Yushania dura

Yushania dura, commonly known as the hardy bamboo, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, upright culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. Its leaves are long, narrow, and have a smooth texture, often with a slight waxy coating.

Yushania dura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design for its resilience and aesthetic qualities.
