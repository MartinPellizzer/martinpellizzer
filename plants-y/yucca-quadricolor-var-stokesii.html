Yucca quadricolor var. stokesii

Yucca quadricolor var. stokesii, commonly known as Stokes' yucca, is a strikingly colorful plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white to pale blue flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its unique coloration and architectural form make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
