Youngia thunbergiana

Youngia thunbergiana, commonly known as the Japanese bur marigold, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and is often found in wetland environments. The plant has a taproot system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Youngia thunbergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
