Youngia zhenduoi

Youngia zhenduoi, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grayish-green leaves and a hollow, hairy stem. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early summer. It is native to China and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Youngia zhenduoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
