Yulania conspicua

Yulania conspicua, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Yulania conspicua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
