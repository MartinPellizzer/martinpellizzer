Yushania gongshanensis

Yushania gongshanensis, commonly known as Gongshan yushania, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. This plant typically grows in high-altitude forests and is adapted to cool, moist climates. Its flowering inflorescence consists of a panicle, and it produces small, spikelet-based flowers.

Yushania gongshanensis plays a role in local ecosystems and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
