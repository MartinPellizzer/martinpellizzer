Yushania longshanensis

Yushania longshanensis, commonly known as the Longshan bamboo, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 5 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.

Yushania longshanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
