Yushania papyrifera subsp. albocerea

Yushania papyrifera subsp.

albocerea, commonly known as the white-flowered yushania, is a member of the grass family Poaceae. It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Laos.

This subspecies is characterized by its slender, upright stems and pale to white inflorescences that distinguish it from other members of the species. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a pale green color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests at elevations above 1,500 meters.
