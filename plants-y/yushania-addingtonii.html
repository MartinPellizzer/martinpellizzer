Yushania addingtonii

Yushania addingtonii, commonly known as the Addington bamboo, is a grass species native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments, often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. Yushania addingtonii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its natural habitat and supports local biodiversity.
