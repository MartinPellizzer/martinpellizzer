Yucca angustissima var. kanabensis

Yucca angustissima var. kanabensis, commonly known as the Kanab yucca, is a shrub-like plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white or pale green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and resilient species within the Yucca genus.
