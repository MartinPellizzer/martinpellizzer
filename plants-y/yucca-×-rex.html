Yucca × rex

Yucca × rex, commonly known as the queen of the yuccas, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca rigidifolia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking blue-green foliage with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of white to pale blue flowers. It is native to the southwestern United States and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Yucca × rex is a popular ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and architectural form.
