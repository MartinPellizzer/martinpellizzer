Yucca lenneana

Yucca lenneana, commonly known as the Lenean yucca, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stems and large, sword-shaped leaves that are gray-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Yucca lenneana is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
