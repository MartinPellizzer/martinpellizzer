Yulania multiflora

Yulania multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese sand pear, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually gray-green in color. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by edible, sweet, yellow or orange fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Yulania multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruit in its native habitat.
