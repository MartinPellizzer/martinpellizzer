Yulania × mirifolia

Yulania × mirifolia, commonly known as the Shantung pine or Chinese white pine, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Pinus armandii and Pinus morrisoni.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to China, characterized by its slender, pyramidal shape and long, needle-like leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.

Yulania × mirifolia is valued for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various climates.
