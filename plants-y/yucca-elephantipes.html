Yucca elephantipes

Yucca elephantipes, commonly known as the tree of life, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a short, thick trunk with a crown of stiff, blue-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
