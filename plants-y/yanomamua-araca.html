Yanomamua araca

Yanomamua araca, commonly known as the Yanomami arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the genus *Maranta* and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by berries containing edible seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense forests.

The Yanomami people have traditionally used it for both culinary and medicinal purposes.
