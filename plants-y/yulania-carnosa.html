Yulania carnosa

Yulania carnosa, commonly known as the Chinese rose or the evergreen rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and provide a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.

Yulania carnosa is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
