Yucca meldensis

Yucca meldensis, commonly known as the Meldens yucca, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are tough and covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in its dry habitat. The plant produces striking clusters of white or pale blue flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and moths. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to access groundwater in nutrient-poor soils.

Yucca meldensis is well adapted to harsh environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
