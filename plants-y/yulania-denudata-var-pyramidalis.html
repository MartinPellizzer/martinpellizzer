Yulania denudata var. pyramidalis

Yulania denudata var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright, pyramidal growth habit and large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features compound leaves with a serrated margin and produces orange-red to yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscape design.
