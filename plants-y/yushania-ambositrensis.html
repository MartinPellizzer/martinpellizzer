Yushania ambositrensis

Yushania ambositrensis, commonly known as the mountain bamboo, is a perennial grass species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, typical of the grass family. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue.

Yushania ambositrensis plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its native habitat, providing habitat and food for various local wildlife.
