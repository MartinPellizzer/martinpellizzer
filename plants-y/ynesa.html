Ynesa

Ynesa, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are small and yellow, often appearing in clusters during the spring season.

Ynesa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
