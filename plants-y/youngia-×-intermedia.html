Youngia × intermedia

Youngia × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid yellow cosmos, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a cross between Youngia japonica and Youngia gracilis, resulting in a plant with distinctive yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces clusters of small, bright yellow blooms. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
