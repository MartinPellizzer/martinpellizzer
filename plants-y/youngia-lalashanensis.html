Youngia lalashanensis

Youngia lalashanensis, commonly known as the Lalashan dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Youngia lalashanensis is often found in meadows and rocky areas, thriving in temperate climates.
