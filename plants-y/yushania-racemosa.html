Yushania racemosa

Yushania racemosa, commonly known as the clustered bamboo, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that often form dense clumps. The plant produces inflorescences that are typically racemose, consisting of long, drooping panicles. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slightly bluish-green hue.

Yushania racemosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and streamside areas.
