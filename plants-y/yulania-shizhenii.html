Yulania shizhenii

Yulania shizhenii, commonly known as the Shizhen chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, with dark green, lobed leaves. Its flowers bloom in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often in late summer to early autumn.

Yulania shizhenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
