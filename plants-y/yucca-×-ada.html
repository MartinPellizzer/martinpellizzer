Yucca × ada

Yucca × ada, commonly known as the hybrid yucca, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, sword-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces panicles of white or pale yellow flowers in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
