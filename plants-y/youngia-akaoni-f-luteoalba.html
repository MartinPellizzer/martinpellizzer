Youngia akaoni f. luteoalba

Youngia akaoni f. luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-flowered dandelion, is a variant of the common dandelion species.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that contrast with its green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is typically found in disturbed soils and open areas, thriving in temperate climates. It produces a capsule that contains numerous small seeds with white, feathery parachutes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
