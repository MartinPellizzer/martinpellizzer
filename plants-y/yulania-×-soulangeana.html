Yulania × soulangeana

Yulania × soulangeana, commonly known as the hybrid Chinese lantern or the double-flowered chrysanthemum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is a hybrid between Chrysanthemum indicum and Chrysanthemum morifolium. The plant is characterized by its showy, pompon-like flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, yellow, and white. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Yulania × soulangeana is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
