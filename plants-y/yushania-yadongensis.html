Yushania yadongensis

Yushania yadongensis, commonly known as the Yushania from Yadanong, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in moist, shaded areas with well-drained soil. Yushania yadongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of alpine flora.
