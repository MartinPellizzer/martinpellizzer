Yushania yunnanensis

Yushania yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters, with a dense, bushy appearance. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically dark green in color and covered in a fine layer of hair. Yushania yunnanensis thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
