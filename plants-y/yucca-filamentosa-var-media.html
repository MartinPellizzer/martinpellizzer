Yucca filamentosa var. media

Yucca filamentosa var. media, commonly known as the stiffleaf yucca, is a striking evergreen perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features tall, rigid, grass-like foliage that is gray-green in color and has a distinctive, sharp appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with white, bell-shaped flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Its hardy nature and architectural form make it both ornamental and drought-tolerant.
