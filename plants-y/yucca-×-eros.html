Yucca × eros

Yucca × eros, commonly known as the foxtail yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca filamentosa and Yucca schidigera.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Yucca × eros is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
