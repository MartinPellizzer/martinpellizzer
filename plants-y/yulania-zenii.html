Yulania zenii

Yulania zenii, commonly known as the Shandong dwarf chrysanthemum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically produces yellow to orange flowers in the fall, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Yulania zenii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
