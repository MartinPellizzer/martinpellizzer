Yulania cuneatifolia

Yulania cuneatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is closely related to other chrysanthemum species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
