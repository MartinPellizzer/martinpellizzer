Yunorchis pingbianensis

Yunorchis pingbianensis, commonly known as the Pingbian orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral morphology and intricate structure. The plant typically features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are often brightly colored, showcasing a combination of hues that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
