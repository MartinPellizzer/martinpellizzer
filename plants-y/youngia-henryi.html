Youngia henryi

Youngia henryi, commonly known as the Chinese buttercup, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and forest edges.

Youngia henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
