Yushania

Yushania, commonly known as the bamboo grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. These plants are characterized by their tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Yushania species often thrive in cool, moist environments and are adapted to high-altitude habitats.

They play an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in their native ecosystems.
