Yushania weiningensis

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with high altitudes and cool climates. It is often found in forested areas and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
