Yucca × regalis

Yucca × regalis, commonly known as the Adam's needle, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca rigida.

It is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features tall, cylindrical, evergreen leaves that are gray-green in color and have a waxy coating. Its flowering stems produce clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring.

Yucca × regalis is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
