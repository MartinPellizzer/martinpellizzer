Yucca × imperialis

Yucca × imperialis, commonly known as the imperial yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca filamentosa and Yucca flaccida.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is native to the southern United States and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Yucca × imperialis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
