Youngia silhetensis

Youngia silhetensis, commonly known as the Silhet dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Bangladesh.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as riverbanks and wetlands. It has a short stem and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Youngia silhetensis is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological role in its native environment.
