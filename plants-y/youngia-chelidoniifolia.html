Youngia chelidoniifolia

Youngia chelidoniifolia, commonly known as the meadow everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers.
