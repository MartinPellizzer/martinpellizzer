Yaundea

Yaundea, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. Yaundea thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and as a houseplant.
