Yulania

Yulania, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and includes over 200 species. Yulania plants are characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. They are typically herbaceous perennials, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

The genus is closely related to the common sunflower and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
