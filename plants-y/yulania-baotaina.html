Yulania baotaina

Yulania baotaina, commonly known as the Baotian chrysanthemum, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage is usually dark green with a slightly hairy texture.
