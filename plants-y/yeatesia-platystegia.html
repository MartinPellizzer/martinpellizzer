Yeatesia platystegia

Yeatesia platystegia, commonly known as the broad-leafed fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and moist woodlands.

The plant reproduces through spores, which are produced on the underside of its fronds in sporangia.
