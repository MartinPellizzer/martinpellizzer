Yushania ungulata

Yushania ungulata, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched culms that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a slightly fibrous texture.

Yushania ungulata is adapted to cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.
