Yucca filamentosa var. orchioides

Yucca filamentosa var. orchioides, commonly known as the orchid yucca, is a striking plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features tall, grass-like leaves that are gray-green in color and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
