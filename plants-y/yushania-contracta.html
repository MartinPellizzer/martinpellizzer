Yushania contracta

Yushania contracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf yushania, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in width. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories at high elevations.

Yushania contracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
