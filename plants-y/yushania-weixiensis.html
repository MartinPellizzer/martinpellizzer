Yushania weixiensis

Yushania weixiensis, commonly known as the Weixian bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and stabilizing soil.

Yushania weixiensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in Chinese culture.
