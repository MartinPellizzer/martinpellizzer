Yakirra majuscula

Yakirra majuscula, commonly known as the giant yakirra, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the rainy season. Yakirra majuscula is often found growing in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native habitat.
