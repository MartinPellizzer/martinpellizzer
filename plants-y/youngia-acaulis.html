Youngia acaulis

Youngia acaulis, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Youngia acaulis is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
