Yulania fragarigynandria

Yulania fragarigynandria, commonly known as the tree of life, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a tall, straight trunk with smooth, grey bark. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush canopy.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
