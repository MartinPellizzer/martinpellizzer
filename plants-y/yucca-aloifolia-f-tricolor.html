Yucca aloifolia f. tricolor

Yucca aloifolia f. tricolor, commonly known as the tricolor yucca, is a striking plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a variant of the yucca aloifolia species, distinguished by its colorful foliage that displays shades of green, white, and pink. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often lined with white or pale pink markings. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

This ornamental plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
