Yucca × saturnus

Yucca × saturnus, commonly known as the hybrid yucca or Florida yucca, is a perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Yucca filamentosa and Yucca flaccida, resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are blue-green in color and often have a waxy coating. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers.

Yucca × saturnus is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and sandy areas.
