Yushania wightiana

Yushania wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's yushania, is a species of grass native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in moist, shaded areas. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the grass family.

Yushania wightiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
