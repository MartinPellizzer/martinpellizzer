Yulania × pilocarpa

Yulania × pilocarpa, commonly known as the hybrid peony, is a deciduous shrub that belongs to the family Paeoniaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Paeonia suffruticosa (tree peony) and Paeonia lactiflora (Chinese peony), resulting in a plant with unique floral characteristics. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, white, and yellow, often with a double petal structure. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its spring blooms.

Yulania × pilocarpa prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile choice for temperate climates.
