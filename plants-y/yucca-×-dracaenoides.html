Yucca × dracaenoides

Yucca × dracaenoides, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca filamentosa and Dracaena reflexa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with prominent white or yellow variegation. The plant is native to the Caribbean and is well adapted to low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers on long, slender stalks, which are usually followed by clusters of red berries.

Yucca × dracaenoides is known for its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
