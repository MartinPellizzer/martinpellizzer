Yucca flexilis f. ensifolia

Yucca flexilis f. ensifolia, commonly known as the sword yucca, is a striking plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, grass-like leaves that are stiff and sword-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
