Yulania × jigongshanensis

Yulania × jigongshanensis, commonly known as the Jigongshanensis Chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a rough texture, and its stems are sturdy and branched.

Yulania × jigongshanensis is native to China and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to different climates.
