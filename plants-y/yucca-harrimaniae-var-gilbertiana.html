Yucca harrimaniae var. gilbertiana

Yucca harrimaniae var. gilbertiana, commonly known as the Gilbert's yucca, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features tall, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or grayish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, white to pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
