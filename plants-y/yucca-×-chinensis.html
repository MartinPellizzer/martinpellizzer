Yucca × chinensis

Yucca × chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca filamentosa and Yucca flaccida.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its tall, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is native to China and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Yucca × chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and floral display.
