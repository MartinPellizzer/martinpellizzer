Yulania shirenshanensis

Yulania shirenshanensis, commonly known as the Shirenshan yellow flower.

It is a species of the genus Yulania, which belongs to the family Asteraceae. This plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to China. It typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers well-drained soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
