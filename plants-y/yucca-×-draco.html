Yucca × draco

Yucca × draco, commonly known as the dragon yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca filamentosa.

It is a tall, evergreen shrub characterized by its sword-shaped, gray-green leaves that are often lined with sharp spines. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers in the spring. It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Yucca × draco is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
