Yucca angustissima subsp. toftiae

Yucca angustissima subsp.

toftiae, commonly known as Toft's narrowleaf yucca, is a distinctive plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is characterized by its tall, stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are often blue-green in color.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
