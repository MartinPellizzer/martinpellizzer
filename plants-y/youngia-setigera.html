Youngia setigera

Youngia setigera, commonly known as the slender hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Youngia setigera is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
