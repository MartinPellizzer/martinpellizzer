Youngia zhengyiana

Youngia zhengyiana, commonly known as the Chinese false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and a single, showy yellow flower head. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It has small, linear leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
