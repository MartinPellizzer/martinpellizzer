Yucca × nicotrana

Yucca × nicotrana, commonly known as the cross between Yucca filamentosa and Yucca flaccida, is a hybrid plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Yucca × nicotrana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
