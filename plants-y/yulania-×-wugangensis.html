Yulania × wugangensis

Yulania × wugangensis, commonly known as the Wugang chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the genus Yulania, which is part of the larger Chrysanthemum genus.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact growth habit and a wide range of flower colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant typically blooms in the autumn, producing showy, daisy-like flowers that are popular in ornamental horticulture.

It is well adapted to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.
