Yushania stoloniforma

Yushania stoloniforma, commonly known as the bamboo grass, is a perennial plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, particularly in China, and thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, forming dense clumps of slender, hollow stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green coloration.

Yushania stoloniforma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
