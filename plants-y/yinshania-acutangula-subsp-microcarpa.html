Yinshania acutangula subsp. microcarpa

Yinshania acutangula subsp.

microcarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit yinshania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, elongated fruits and compact growth habit. It typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
