Yushania lipingensis

Yushania lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Guizhou provinces. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Yushania lipingensis plays a role in local ecosystems by contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
