Yucca recurvifolia f. elegans

Yucca recurvifolia f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant yucca, is a variant of the yucca plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking white flower clusters in the summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in arid landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and desert gardens.
