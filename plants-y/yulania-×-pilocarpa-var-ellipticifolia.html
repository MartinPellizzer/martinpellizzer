Yulania × pilocarpa var. ellipticifolia

Yulania × pilocarpa var. ellipticifolia, commonly known as the ellipticifolia chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, finely toothed leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red, during the fall season. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is relatively hardy and adaptable, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
