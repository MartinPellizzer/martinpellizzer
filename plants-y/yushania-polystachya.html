Yushania polystachya

Yushania polystachya, commonly known as the giant bamboo or Chinese bamboo, is a tall perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, hollow stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, feathery inflorescences that are often clustered at the top of the stems. Its leaves are long, narrow, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Yushania polystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
