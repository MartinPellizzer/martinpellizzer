Yulania campbellii

Yulania campbellii, commonly known as Campbell's goldbush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small garden spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Yulania campbellii is also valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
