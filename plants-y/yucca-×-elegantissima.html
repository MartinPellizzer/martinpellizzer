Yucca × elegantissima

Yucca × elegantissima, commonly known as the Adam's needle, is a hybrid plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are blue-gray in color and have a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.

Yucca × elegantissima is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
