Yucca × quinnarjenii

Yucca × quinnarjenii, commonly known as the hybrid yucca or Quinn's yucca, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Yucca flaccida and Yucca recurvata.

It is characterized by its tall, stiff, sword-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces large, pendulous white flowers in summer, which are clustered in panicles. It is native to the southwestern United States and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Yucca × quinnarjenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
