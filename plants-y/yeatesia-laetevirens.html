Yeatesia laetevirens

Yeatesia laetevirens, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and a woody stem that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
