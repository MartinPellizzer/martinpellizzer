Yucca × deleuilii

Yucca × deleuilii, commonly known as the hybrid yucca, is a plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca filamentosus.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are usually blue-green in color. The plant produces striking white flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Yucca × deleuilii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
