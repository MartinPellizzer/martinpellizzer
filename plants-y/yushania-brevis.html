Yushania brevis

Yushania brevis, commonly known as the short-bracted yushania, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in forest understories and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Yushania brevis produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicle-like inflorescences.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the flora in its native habitat.
