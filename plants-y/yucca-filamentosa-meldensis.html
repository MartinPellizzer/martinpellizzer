Yucca filamentosa meldensis

Yucca filamentosa meldensis, commonly known as the Meldens yucca, is a perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often covered in white filaments, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers in the summer, which are borne on tall, branched stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
