Yua

Yua, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features striking, inflated, red or orange calyces that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in traditional festivals. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by the characteristic seed pods. Yua thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
