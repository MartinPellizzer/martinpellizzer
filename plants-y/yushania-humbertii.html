Yushania humbertii

Yushania humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert yushania, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green in color. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.

Yushania humbertii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
