Yushania niitakayamensis var. microcarpa

Yushania niitakayamensis var. microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited yushania, is a species of grass native to Japan.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates.

Its botanical features make it an important component of understory vegetation in its native habitat.
