Yinshania albiflora

Yinshania albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered yinshania, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
