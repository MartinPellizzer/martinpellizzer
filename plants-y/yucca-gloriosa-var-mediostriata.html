Yucca gloriosa var. mediostriata

Yucca gloriosa var. mediostriata, commonly known as the variegated yucca, is a striking plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features tall, sword-like leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern of white and green stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear white or pale blue flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its architectural form and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
