Yucca × sprengeri

Yucca × sprengeri, commonly known as the Sprengeri yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca recurvata.

It is a succulent perennial shrub characterized by its tall, narrow, evergreen leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking white flowers in panicles during the summer months, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid regions.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a favored plant in both gardens and botanical collections.
