Yulania denudata var. purpurascens

Yulania denudata var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and showy flowers that range from pink to deep purple, often with a contrasting white or pale pink base. The plant features compound, bipinnate leaves that are green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape.
