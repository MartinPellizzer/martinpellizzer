Youngia

Youngia, commonly known as the common ragwort, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species, most notably Youngia japonica, which is native to East Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and wetlands. It features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers in clustered inflorescences.

The genus is characterized by its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
