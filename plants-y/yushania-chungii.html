Yushania chungii

Yushania chungii, commonly known as the Yushania chungii, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in forested or alpine habitats. Its inflorescence consists of a dense panicle, bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

Yushania chungii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and seasonal flowering.
