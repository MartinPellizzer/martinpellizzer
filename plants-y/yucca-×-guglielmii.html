Yucca × guglielmii

Yucca × guglielmii, commonly known as the Florida yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca aloifolia.

It is a shrub-like plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often blue-gray in color, with a waxy coating. It produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers.

Yucca × guglielmii is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and scrub habitats.
