Yulania japonica var. purpurea

Yulania japonica var. purpurea, commonly known as the Japanese rose or pink peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a deep pink or purple hue. The plant features compound, trifoliate leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate watering.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its historical significance in horticulture.
