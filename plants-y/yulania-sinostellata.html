Yulania sinostellata

Yulania sinostellata, commonly known as the Chinese aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Yulania sinostellata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional Chinese medicine.
