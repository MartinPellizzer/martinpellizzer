Youngia nana

Youngia nana, commonly known as the dwarf sunflower, is a small flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant has short, hairy stems and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs to protect against cold temperatures.

Youngia nana is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
