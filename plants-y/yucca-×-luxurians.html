Yucca × luxurians

Yucca × luxurians, commonly known as the hybrid yucca or Florida yucca, is a plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Yucca flaccida and Yucca filamentosa, resulting in a unique combination of characteristics from both parents. The plant features tall, stiff, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, and it produces tall, branched inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Yucca × luxurians is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
