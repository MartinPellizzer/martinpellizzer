Yushania yongdeensis

Yushania yongdeensis, commonly known as the Yongde bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province, where it grows in mountainous regions. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It plays an important role in its native ecosystem, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various wildlife.

Yushania yongdeensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
