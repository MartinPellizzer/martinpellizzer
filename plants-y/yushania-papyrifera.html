Yushania papyrifera

Yushania papyrifera, commonly known as the bamboo grass or paper bamboo, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in cool, moist environments, often found in forests and alpine meadows. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense clumps, contributing to soil stabilization.

Yushania papyrifera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
