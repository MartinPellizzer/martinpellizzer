Youngia japonica subsp. elstonii

Youngia japonica subsp.

elstonii, commonly known as the Elston's yarrow, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural use.
