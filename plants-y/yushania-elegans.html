Yushania elegans

Yushania elegans, commonly known as the elegant yushania, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lipped flowers, typically in shades of purple or pink. Yushania elegans thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady landscapes.
