Youngia Wilsonii

Youngia wilsonii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is known for its small, daisy-like flowers and its ability to thrive in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in rocky soils and shaded areas, making it a challenging plant to cultivate. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Youngia wilsonii is considered endangered in many parts of its range. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique botanical species.
