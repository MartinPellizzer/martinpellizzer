Yushania lineolata

Yushania lineolata, commonly known as the bamboo grass or lineolated yushania, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense clumps, contributing to soil stabilization.

Yushania lineolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
