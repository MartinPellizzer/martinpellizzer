Yushania flexa

Yushania flexa, commonly known as the flexible yushania, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization.

Yushania flexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
