Yucca × sokrates

Yucca × sokrates, commonly known as the hybrid yucca or sometimes the Spanish dagger, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color and have a waxy coating. The plant produces large, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Yucca × sokrates is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
