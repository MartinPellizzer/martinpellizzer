Yulania pyriformis

Yulania pyriformis, commonly known as the golden flower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Yulania pyriformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
