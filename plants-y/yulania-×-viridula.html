Yulania × viridula

Yulania × viridula, commonly known as the evergreen chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between *Chrysanthemum morifolium* and *Chrysanthemum indicum*.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, single or semi-double yellow flowers. The plant is evergreen, retaining its leaves throughout the year, and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade. It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

Yulania × viridula is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
