Yushania doupengshanensis

Yushania doupengshanensis, commonly known as the Doupengshan bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in Yunnan Province. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant plays a significant role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
