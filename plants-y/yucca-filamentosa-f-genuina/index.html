Yucca Filamentosa F. Genuina

Yucca filamentosa f. genuina is a unique variant of the yucca plant known for its striking appearance and hardy nature. It features narrow, sword-like leaves that are often adorned with soft, white threads along their edges. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it an excellent choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its resilience and low maintenance requirements have made it a popular choice among gardeners in arid regions. The plant's distinctive look and adaptability make it a standout addition to any landscape.
