Yucca × elwesiana

Yucca × elwesiana, commonly known as the stiffleaf yucca, is a hybrid plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, rigid, sword-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces large, terminal clusters of white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for arid landscape designs.
