Yunnanea xylocarpa

Yunnanea xylocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody fruits containing aromatic seeds.
