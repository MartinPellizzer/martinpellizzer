Yushania rolloana

Yushania rolloana, commonly known as the rolled bamboo or mountain bamboo, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit with long, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments at high altitudes, often forming dense clumps. Its culms are hollow and jointed, with a smooth, greenish-brown surface.

Yushania rolloana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
