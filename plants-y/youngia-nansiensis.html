Youngia nansiensis

Youngia nansiensis, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments and has heart-shaped, hairy leaves. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer and are followed by fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Youngia nansiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
