Youngia diversifolia

Youngia diversifolia, commonly known as the diverseleaf youngia, is a flowering plant species native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Youngia diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
