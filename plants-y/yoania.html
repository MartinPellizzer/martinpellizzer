Yoania

Yoania, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense clusters.

Yoania is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and aesthetic appeal.
