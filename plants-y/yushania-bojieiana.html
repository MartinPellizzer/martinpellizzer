Yushania bojieiana

Yushania bojieiana, commonly known as the Bojie bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan Province, where it thrives in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a grass-like texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also studied for its ecological role in its native habitat.
