Youngia poosia

Youngia poosia, commonly known as the slender hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a flat-topped cluster. Native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has a short lifespan, typically completing its life cycle within a single growing season.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and roadside environments.
