Yucca × europa

Yucca × europa, commonly known as the hybrid yucca or Spanish dagger, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky foliage composed of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white or pale yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Yucca × europa is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
