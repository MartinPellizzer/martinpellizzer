Yucca × engelmannii

Yucca × engelmannii, commonly known as the Engelmann yucca, is a hybrid plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, stiff, sword-like leaves that are usually blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Yucca × engelmannii is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements and ornamental value.
