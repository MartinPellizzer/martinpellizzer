Youngia silhetensis subsp. bhutanica

Youngia silhetensis subsp.

bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutanese buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Bhutan and northeastern India. It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers and hairy leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. It has small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.
