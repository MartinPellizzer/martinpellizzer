Yolanda

Yolanda, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has a spreading canopy with pinnate leaves and can grow up to 30 meters tall. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish hue, adding to its visual appeal.

Yolanda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
