Yushania uniramosa

Yushania uniramosa, commonly known as the single-shoot yushania, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Yushania uniramosa plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various forest-dwelling organisms.
