Yulania elliptigemmata

Yulania elliptigemmata, commonly known as the elliptical twig yulania, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings. Its foliage turns vibrant red in the autumn, adding ornamental value to the landscape.

Yulania elliptigemmata is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
