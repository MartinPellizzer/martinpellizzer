Yavia

Yavia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and ragworts. The plants typically have showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white or pink petals. Yavia species are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

They are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
