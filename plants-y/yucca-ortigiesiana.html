Yucca ortigiesiana

Yucca ortigiesiana, commonly known as the Ortilia yucca, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence and sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to dry climates and can thrive in well-drained soils.

Yucca ortigiesiana is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
