Yinshania rupicola subsp. rupicola

Yinshania rupicola subsp.

rupicola, commonly known as the rock yinshania, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in rocky outcrops and along coastal dunes.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
