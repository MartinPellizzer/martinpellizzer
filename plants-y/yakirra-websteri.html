Yakirra websteri

Yakirra websteri, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a perennial plant native to the arid regions of central Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps it retain moisture in harsh environments. Yakirra websteri thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to its native habitat.
