Yushania jaunsarensis

Yushania jaunsarensis, commonly known as the Jaunsar bamboo, is a species of grass found in the Himalayan region of India.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, temperate forests at high altitudes. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Due to its resilience and adaptability, it is also valued for its use in traditional construction and crafts.
