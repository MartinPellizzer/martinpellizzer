Yulania × lenneana

Yulania × lenneana, commonly known as the Shrub Chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Chrysanthemum morifolium and Chrysanthemum indicum.

It is a hardy perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring dense, compact growth with abundant flowering. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, red, and white, during the late summer to early autumn season. Its foliage consists of dark green, serrated leaves that are aromatic when crushed.

Yulania × lenneana is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
