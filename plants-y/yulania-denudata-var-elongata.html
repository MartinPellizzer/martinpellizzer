Yulania denudata var. elongata

Yulania denudata var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are palmately compound, with five to seven leaflets that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces yellow anthers and has a distinctive, fragrant floral scent.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
