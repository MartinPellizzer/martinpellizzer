Yucca filamentosa f. orchioides

Yucca filamentosa f. orchioides, commonly known as the orchid yucca, is a cultivar of the yucca plant characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers.

It features narrow, gray-green leaves that are stiff and sword-shaped, typical of the yucca genus. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its architectural form and seasonal floral display.
