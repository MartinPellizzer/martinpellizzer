Youngia ordosica

Youngia ordosica, commonly known as the Ordos dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of northern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fluffy, white seeds, typical of the genus Youngia. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing food for insects, it is of interest in ecological restoration projects.
