Yulania acuminata

Yulania acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Yulania acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
