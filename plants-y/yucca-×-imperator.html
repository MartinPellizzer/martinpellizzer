Yucca × imperator

Yucca × imperator, commonly known as the Imperial yucca, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Yucca flaccida and Yucca recurvata.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in panicles. It is native to the southwestern United States and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic appearance and low maintenance requirements.
