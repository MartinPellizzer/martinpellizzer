Yoania japonica var. amagiensis

Yoania japonica var. amagiensis, commonly known as the Amagi bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are erect and often branched.
