Yildirimlia gracillima

Yildirimlia gracillima, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces striking clusters of orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. Its stems are flexible and can twine around supports, making it suitable for trellises or arbors.

Yildirimlia gracillima is valued in horticulture for its vibrant floral display and ornamental appeal.
