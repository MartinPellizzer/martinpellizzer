Yushania lanshanensis

Yushania lanshanensis, commonly known as the Lushan yushania, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow leaves. This plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. It is often found in forested areas and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Yushania lanshanensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
