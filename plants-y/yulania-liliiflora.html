Yulania liliiflora

Yulania liliiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that resemble those of the lily, and its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of white, lily-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Yulania liliiflora is also known for its ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions with well-drained soil.
