Yushania pingshanensis

Yushania pingshanensis, commonly known as the Pingshan yushania, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, elongated leaves with a bluish-green coloration. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Yushania pingshanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
