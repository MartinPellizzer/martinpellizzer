Yushania suijiangensis

Yushania suijiangensis, commonly known as the Suijiang bamboo, is a species of grass native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool and moist environments. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife.

Its ornamental value has also made it a subject of interest in horticulture.
