Zygostates densiflora

Zygostates densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered zygostate, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Zygostates densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
