Zephyra violiflora

Zephyra violiflora, commonly known as the sky violet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its delicate, violet-blue petals that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Zephyra violiflora is also valued for its aromatic foliage and its role in attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
