Zygopetalum kegelii

Zygopetalum kegelii, commonly known as the Kegel's zygopetalum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, arranged in a rosette pattern. Zygopetalum kegelii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
