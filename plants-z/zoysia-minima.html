Zoysia minima

Zoysia minima, commonly known as slender zoysia, is a low-growing, turf-forming grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green blades and dense, mat-like growth habit. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Zoysia minima is often used in lawns and landscaping due to its durability and aesthetic appeal.
