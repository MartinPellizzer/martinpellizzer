Zanthoxylum molokaiense

Zanthoxylum molokaiense, commonly known as Molokai prickly ash, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically divided into multiple leaflets with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to red, fleshy fruits. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been used traditionally for medicinal purposes.

Zanthoxylum molokaiense is adapted to the dry, coastal environments of its native habitat.
