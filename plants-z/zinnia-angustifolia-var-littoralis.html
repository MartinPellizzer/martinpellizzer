Zinnia angustifolia var. littoralis

Zinnia angustifolia var. littoralis, commonly known as the coastal zinnia, is a compact, annual flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It features slender, linear leaves and produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is well adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and can tolerate salty air and coastal winds. It thrives in full sun and is often found growing along shorelines and dunes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in coastal environments.
