Zerna paulsenii subsp. turkestanica

Zerna paulsenii subsp.

turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan zerna, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia. It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds. It is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of local ecosystems and traditional pastoral systems.
