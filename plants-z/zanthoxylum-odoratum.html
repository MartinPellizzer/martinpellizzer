Zanthoxylum odoratum

Zanthoxylum odoratum, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Zanthoxylum odoratum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
