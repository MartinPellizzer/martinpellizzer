Zygopetalum brachystalix

Zygopetalum brachystalix, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its roots are fibrous and well-developed, allowing it to thrive in well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Zygopetalum brachystalix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a warm, humid environment.
