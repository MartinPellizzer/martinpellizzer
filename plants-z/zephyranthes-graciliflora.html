Zephyranthes graciliflora

Zephyranthes graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a bulbous plant that produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.

Zephyranthes graciliflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fleeting but striking blooms.
