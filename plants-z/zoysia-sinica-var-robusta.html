Zoysia sinica var. robusta

Zoysia sinica var. robusta, commonly known as the Chinese zoysia, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to a wide range of soil types.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and erosion control.
