Ziziphus jujuba f. allochroa

Ziziphus jujuba f. allochroa, commonly known as the red date tree, is a cultivar of the jujube plant native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive red, fleshy fruit that ripens in the fall. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a dark green color. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit and ornamental value.

It is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions.
