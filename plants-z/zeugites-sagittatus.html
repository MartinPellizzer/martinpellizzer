Zeugites sagittatus

Zeugites sagittatus, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties. Its striking white flowers are followed by a spadix, typical of the Araceae family.

Zeugites sagittatus is also valued as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
