Zannichellia palustris var. major

Zannichellia palustris var. major, commonly known as the greater marsh pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater habitats in Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences, which emerge above the water surface during the summer months.
