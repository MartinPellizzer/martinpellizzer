Zantedeschia hastata

Zantedeschia hastata, commonly known as the purple calla lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink spatulate flowers with a prominent, narrow yellow spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia hastata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
