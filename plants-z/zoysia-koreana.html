Zoysia koreana

Zoysia koreana, commonly known as Korean zoysia, is a perennial grass native to Korea and parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green foliage and dense growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue to white flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Zoysia koreana is known for its drought tolerance and ability to form a thick, weed-resistant turf.
