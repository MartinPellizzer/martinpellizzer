Zephyranthes brasiliensis

Zephyranthes brasiliensis, commonly known as the rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in response to rainfall, often appearing after storms. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are usually dark green and narrow. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Zephyranthes brasiliensis is a popular ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation.
