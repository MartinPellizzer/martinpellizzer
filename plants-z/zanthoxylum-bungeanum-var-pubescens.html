Zanthoxylum bungeanum var. pubescens

Zanthoxylum bungeanum var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its spiny branches and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.

The hairy pubescens variety is distinguished by its dense, glandular hairs on the stems and leaves.
