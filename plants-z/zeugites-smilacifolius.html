Zeugites smilacifolius

Zeugites smilacifolius, commonly known as the climbing smilacifolius, is a vine-like plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, climbing stems with opposite, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits. Its leaves are typically 5-10 cm in length and have a distinctive, slightly serrated margin.

Zeugites smilacifolius is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and thickets.
