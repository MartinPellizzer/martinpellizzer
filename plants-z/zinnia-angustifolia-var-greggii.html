Zinnia angustifolia var. greggii

Zinnia angustifolia var. greggii, commonly known as Gregg's zinnia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and vibrant, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
