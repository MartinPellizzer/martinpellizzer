Zeyheria montana

Zeyheria montana, commonly known as the mountain zeyheria, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Zeyheria montana is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
