Ziziphus acidojujuba

Ziziphus acidojujuba, commonly known as the Chinese date or red date, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It produces small, reddish-brown fruits that are rich in sugars, vitamins, and minerals. The plant has compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in panicles, which attract pollinators.

The fruit is typically harvested when fully ripe, and the plant is valued both for its nutritional content and traditional medicinal uses.
