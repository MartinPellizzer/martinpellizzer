Zollikoferia Chondrilloides

Zollikoferia chondrilloides is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring small, clustered leaves and delicate, pale green flowers. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils at elevations above 3,000 meters. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation to extreme climates.
