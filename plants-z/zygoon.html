Zygoon

Zygoon, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a unique species belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves that add an ornamental appeal to gardens and indoor spaces. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. Zygoon thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
