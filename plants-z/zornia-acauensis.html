Zornia acauensis

Zornia acauensis, commonly known as the Acauense Zornia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.
