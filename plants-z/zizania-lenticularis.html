Zizania lenticularis

Zizania lenticularis, commonly known as the narrowleaf bulrush, is a species of aquatic plant native to the wetlands of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that often form dense stands in shallow water. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that are distinctive and contribute to its ornamental value. It plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Zizania lenticularis is also used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize shorelines and improve water quality.
