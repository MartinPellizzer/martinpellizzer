Zygopetalum hemixanthum

Zygopetalum hemixanthum, commonly known as the pink zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its botanical features include long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
