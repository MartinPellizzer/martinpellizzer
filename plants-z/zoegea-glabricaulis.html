Zoegea glabricaulis

Zoegea glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed zoegea, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy or open woodland habitats. The plant features slender, smooth stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying shades of white or pale yellow.

Zoegea glabricaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
