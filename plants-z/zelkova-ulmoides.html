Zelkova ulmoides

Zelkova ulmoides, commonly known as the Chinese zelkova, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ulmaceae family, which includes elms and plane trees. The tree has a distinctive, rounded crown and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age.

Zelkova ulmoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
