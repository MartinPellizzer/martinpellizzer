Zinnia elegans var. violacea

Zinnia elegans var. violacea, commonly known as the violet zinnia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant violet to deep purple flower heads, which are typically single or semi-double blooms. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Zinnia elegans var. violacea is also known for its long flowering period and resistance to common pests.
