Zingiber velutinum

Zingiber velutinum, commonly known as velvet ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped, hairy leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often grown for its ornamental value.

Zingiber velutinum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
