Zelkova Serrata Var. Stipulacea

Zelkova serrata var. stipulacea is a unique and lesser-known variant of the zelkova tree, native to parts of East Asia. This plant is distinguished by its distinctive leaf shape and subtle stipules, which give it a unique appearance compared to its parent species. While not as widely cultivated as other zelkova varieties, it holds ecological and horticultural significance in its native habitats. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a resilient choice for landscaping and reforestation projects. Despite its quiet presence, this variant deserves greater recognition for its beauty and environmental value.
