Zizyphon jujubum

Zizyphon jujubum, commonly known as Chinese date or jujube, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It produces edible, sweet, and fleshy fruits that are rich in nutrients such as vitamin C and antioxidants. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and yellowish-green, often appearing in clusters, and it is primarily pollinated by insects.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly cultivated for its fruit in temperate climates.
