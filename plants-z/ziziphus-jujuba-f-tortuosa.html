Ziziphus jujuba f. tortuosa

Ziziphus jujuba f. tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted Chinese date, is a cultivar of the Chinese date tree characterized by its unique, twisting growth pattern.

It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically red or reddish-brown when mature. The plant is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of small, dark green leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.

The twisted branches make it a distinctive addition to gardens and landscapes.
