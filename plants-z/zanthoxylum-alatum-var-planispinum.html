Zanthoxylum alatum var. planispinum

Zanthoxylum alatum var. planispinum, commonly known as the flat-spined prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Its branches are covered in small, stiff spines, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

This species is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
