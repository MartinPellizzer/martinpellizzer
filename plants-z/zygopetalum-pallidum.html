Zygopetalum pallidum

Zygopetalum pallidum, commonly known as the pale zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Zygopetalum pallidum is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
