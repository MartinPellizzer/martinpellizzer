Zanthoxylum anison

Zanthoxylum anison, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are edible but have a sharp, spicy taste.
