Zygopetalum discolor

Zygopetalum discolor, commonly known as the variegated zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves, which often display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and moderate humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for collectors and horticulturists.
