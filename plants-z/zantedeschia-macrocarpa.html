Zantedeschia macrocarpa

Zantedeschia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant calla lily, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy white spatulate leaves and striking white or pale yellow flowers with a prominent, spadix-like structure. The plant is characterized by its robust rhizomes and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia macrocarpa is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence and botanical significance.
