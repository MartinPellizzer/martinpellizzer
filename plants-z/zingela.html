Zingela

Zingela, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and produces vibrant blooms in shades of purple, pink, and white. Zingela thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
