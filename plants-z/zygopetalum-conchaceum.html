Zygopetalum conchaceum

Zygopetalum conchaceum, commonly known as the pink zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety pink petals and a distinctive, elongated spur. The plant produces fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink to reddish-purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Zygopetalum conchaceum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
