Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp. szowitsii

Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp.

szowitsii, commonly known as Szowits' betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and produces small, pink to purple flowers in summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
