Zollikoferia leucodon

Zollikoferia leucodon, commonly known as the white lily of the Nile, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces large, showy white flowers with a subtle pinkish hue. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
