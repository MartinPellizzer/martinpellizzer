Zingiber raja

Zingiber raja, commonly known as the "king of ginger," is a rare and ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of India.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species and ornamental gingers. The plant is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. Zingiber raja is typically grown as a houseplant due to its specific care requirements.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and collections.
