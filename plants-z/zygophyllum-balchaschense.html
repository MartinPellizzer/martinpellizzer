Zygophyllum balchaschense

Zygophyllum balchaschense, commonly known as the Balchaschene zygophyllum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Zygophyllaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Its root system is typically deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry conditions.

Zygophyllum balchaschense plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitats and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
