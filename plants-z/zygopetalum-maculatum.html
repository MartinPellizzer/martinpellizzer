Zygopetalum maculatum

Zygopetalum maculatum, commonly known as the spotted zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues, with distinctive dark markings. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant blooms. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its botanical appeal lies in its intricate floral structure and long-lasting blooms, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
