Zebrina pendula var. quadricolor

Zebrina pendula var. quadricolor, commonly known as the four-color spider plant, is a strikingly colorful variant of the spider plant family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and pink. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is particularly popular for its ease of care and ability to cascade beautifully from hanging baskets or shelves.
