Zeuxine polygonoides

Zeuxine polygonoides, commonly known as the slender zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or stream banks. Its flowers are usually blue or violet in color, with five petals and a distinctive corona.

Zeuxine polygonoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
