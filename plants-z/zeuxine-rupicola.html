Zeuxine rupicola

Zeuxine rupicola, commonly known as the rock zeuxine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that bloom in early summer. Zeuxine rupicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
