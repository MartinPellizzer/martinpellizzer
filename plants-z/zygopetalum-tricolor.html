Zygopetalum tricolor

Zygopetalum tricolor, commonly known as the Three-Colored Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tricolored petals that display shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season.

Zygopetalum tricolor is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its striking appearance and unique coloration.
