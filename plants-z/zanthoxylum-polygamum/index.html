Zanthoxylum Polygamum

Zanthoxylum polygamum is a rare and intriguing plant native to specific regions of China. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its distinctive citrus-like scents and medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit, which resembles a peppercorn, is used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Zanthoxylum polygamum is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
