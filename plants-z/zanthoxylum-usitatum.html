Zanthoxylum usitatum

Zanthoxylum usitatum, commonly known as the prickly ash or yellow cedar, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the eastern United States.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for wildlife.
