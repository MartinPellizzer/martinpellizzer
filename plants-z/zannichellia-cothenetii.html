Zannichellia cothenetii

Zannichellia cothenetii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water plantain, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant has elongated, narrow leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a smooth, waxy surface. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in spikes, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Zannichellia cothenetii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small invertebrates and contributing to water filtration.
