Zephyranthes leucantha

Zephyranthes leucantha, commonly known as the white rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Central America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces delicate, white, star-shaped flowers during the rainy season. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and its ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open woodlands and along the edges of swamps.

Zephyranthes leucantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to bloom reliably in moist environments.
