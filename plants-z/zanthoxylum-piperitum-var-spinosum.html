Zanthoxylum piperitum var. spinosum

Zanthoxylum piperitum var. spinosum, commonly known as the spiny Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves and fruits contain bioactive compounds, making it valuable in traditional medicine.

The spiny morphology of the plant serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores.
