Zeuxine debrajiana

Zeuxine debrajiana, commonly known as the Himalayan zigzag root, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows at elevations above 3,000 meters. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Zeuxine debrajiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
