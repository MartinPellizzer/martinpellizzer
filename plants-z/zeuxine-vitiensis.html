Zeuxine vitiensis

Zeuxine vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Vanuatu.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Zeuxine vitiensis is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists due to its restricted distribution and ecological significance.
