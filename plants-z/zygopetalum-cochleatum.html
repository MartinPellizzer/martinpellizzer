Zygopetalum cochleatum

Zygopetalum cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of petals and sepals, which gives it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant typically produces fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

Zygopetalum cochleatum is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitat.
