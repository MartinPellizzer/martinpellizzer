Zycona

Zycona, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its leaves are covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss and reflects sunlight.
