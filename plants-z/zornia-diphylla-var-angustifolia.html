Zornia diphylla var. angustifolia

Zornia diphylla var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
