Zapoteca nervosa

Zapoteca nervosa, commonly known as the Zapotec nettle, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes various species of stinging nettles. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Zapoteca nervosa is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and rocky slopes.
