Zephyranthes susatana

Zephyranthes susatana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for winter gardens and floral arrangements. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

Zephyranthes susatana is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering to thrive.
