Zoysia matrella var. pacifica

Zoysia matrella var. pacifica, commonly known as Pacific zoysia, is a warm-season grass native to Japan and Korea.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fine texture, making it visually appealing. This variety is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, requiring minimal water once established. It spreads through stolons, forming a thick mat that suppresses weeds and prevents erosion.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for lawns and landscaping in coastal and temperate regions.
