Ziziphus sinensis

Ziziphus sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese date or red date tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and simple, alternate leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid climates, making it an important plant in dryland agriculture.
