Zeltnera nesomii

Zeltnera nesomii, commonly known as the San Francisco thistle, is a flowering plant native to California, specifically the San Francisco Bay Area.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet, featuring basal rosettes of spiny, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Zeltnera nesomii has small, oval to linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are surrounded by a series of bracts.

It is adapted to coastal climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
