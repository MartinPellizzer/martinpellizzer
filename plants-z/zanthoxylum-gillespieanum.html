Zanthoxylum gillespieanum

Zanthoxylum gillespieanum, commonly known as the Gillespie prickly ash, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

Zanthoxylum gillespieanum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and along stream banks.
