Zoduba masuca

Zoduba masuca, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves that are often green or purple in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a range of colors, including purple, blue, and white, which bloom from the leaf axils. Zoduba masuca thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to bloom year-round under proper care adds to its appeal as a decorative plant.
