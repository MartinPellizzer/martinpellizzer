Ziziphora bungeana

Ziziphora bungeana, commonly known as Chinese pennyroyal, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ziziphora bungeana is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
