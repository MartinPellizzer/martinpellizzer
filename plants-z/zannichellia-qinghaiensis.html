Zannichellia qinghaiensis

Zannichellia qinghaiensis, commonly known as the Qinghai waterweed, is a species of aquatic plant native to the Qinghai Lake region in China.

It is characterized by its submerged, ribbon-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments with moderate to high light exposure. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various microorganisms and invertebrates.

Zannichellia qinghaiensis is also studied for its potential ecological and medicinal applications.
