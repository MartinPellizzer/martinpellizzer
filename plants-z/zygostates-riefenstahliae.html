Zygostates riefenstahliae

Zygostates riefenstahliae, commonly known as the Riefenstahl's zygostate, is a rare and striking member of the Zygostomataceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that often display a striking coloration, ranging from deep red to vibrant orange. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in spring, and are noted for their intricate, almost lace-like structure.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
