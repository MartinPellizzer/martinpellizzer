Zollernia magnifica

Zollernia magnifica, commonly known as the royal flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers, which resemble flames and bloom during the rainy season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. Zollernia magnifica thrives in warm, moist environments with high humidity and indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
