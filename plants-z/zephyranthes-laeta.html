Zephyranthes laeta

Zephyranthes laeta, commonly known as the pink rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. Zephyranthes laeta is adapted to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
