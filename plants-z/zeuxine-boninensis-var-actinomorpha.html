Zeuxine boninensis var. actinomorpha

Zeuxine boninensis var. actinomorpha, commonly known as the Bonin mountainflower, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a member of the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its adaptability to challenging environments. Its leaves are usually basal, arranged in a rosette, and its stems are often hairy.
