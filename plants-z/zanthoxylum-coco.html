Zanthoxylum coco

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Zanthoxylum coco is valued for its ornamental features and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
