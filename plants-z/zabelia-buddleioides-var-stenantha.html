Zabelia buddleioides var. stenantha

Zabelia buddleioides var. stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved zabelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period, which extends from spring to early summer.
