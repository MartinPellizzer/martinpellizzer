Zingiber ovoideum

Zingiber ovoideum, commonly known as the oval ginger or eggplant ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

Zingiber ovoideum is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal properties.
