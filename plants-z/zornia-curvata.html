Zornia curvata

Zornia curvata, commonly known as the curled zornia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Native to the southern United States, it thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Zornia curvata is an annual herb that plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various insects.
