Ziziphus jujuba var. nana

Ziziphus jujuba var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese date, is a compact, shrub-like variety of the jujube tree.

It features small, round fruits that are typically harvested in the fall, known for their sweet and tangy flavor. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and is native to China. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to produce a good yield.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes as well as for its edible fruits.
