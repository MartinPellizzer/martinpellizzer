Zanthoxylum chiloperone var. angustifolium

Zanthoxylum chiloperone var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.
