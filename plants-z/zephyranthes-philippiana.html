Zephyranthes philippiana

Zephyranthes philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine rain lily, is a bulbous plant native to the Philippines.

It produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Zephyranthes philippiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
