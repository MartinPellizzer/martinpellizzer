Zephyranthes hondurensis

Zephyranthes hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduran rain lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central America, particularly Honduras. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette, and the plant is known for its resilience in tropical and subtropical climates.
