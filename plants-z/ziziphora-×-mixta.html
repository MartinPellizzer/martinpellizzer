Ziziphora × mixta

Ziziphora × mixta, commonly known as the hybrid savory, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Ziziphora capitata and Ziziphora taurica, characterized by its aromatic, slightly minty scent. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Ziziphora × mixta is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its pungent flavor and medicinal properties.
