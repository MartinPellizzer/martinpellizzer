Zinnia acerosa var. guanajuatensis

Zinnia acerosa var. guanajuatensis, commonly known as the Guanajuato zinnia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and vibrant, daisy-like yellow flowers. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It is often found in rocky soils and open habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of its native range.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
