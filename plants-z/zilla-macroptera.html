Zilla macroptera

Zilla macroptera, commonly known as the giant zilla, is a large herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and large size make it a notable species in its native habitat.
