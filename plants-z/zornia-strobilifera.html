Zornia strobilifera

Zornia strobilifera, commonly known as the spikeless zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Zornia strobilifera is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wetlands.
