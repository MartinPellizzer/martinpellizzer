Zebrina Pendula Var. Quadricolor

Zebrina pendula var. quadricolor is a strikingly colorful houseplant known for its vibrant, cascading foliage. This variety showcases a unique combination of four distinct colors, creating a visually stunning display. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its trailing growth habit makes it ideal for hanging baskets or shelves. With proper care, this plant can bring a splash of tropical beauty to any indoor space.
