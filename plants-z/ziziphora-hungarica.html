Ziziphora hungarica

Ziziphora hungarica, commonly known as Hungarian millepertuis, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and fragrant, square stems. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Ziziphora hungarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
