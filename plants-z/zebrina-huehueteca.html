Zebrina huehueteca

Zebrina huehueteca, commonly known as the Mexican water spinach, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features long, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color and covered with small, waxy leaves. The plant thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found near water sources. Its leaves are edible and have a slightly bitter taste, making it a traditional food in some regions.

Zebrina huehueteca is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
