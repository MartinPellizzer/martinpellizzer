Zoegea crinita

Zoegea crinita, commonly known as the silky zoegea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Zoegea crinita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
