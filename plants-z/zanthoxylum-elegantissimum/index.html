Zanthoxylum Elegantissimum

Zanthoxylum elegantissimum is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant red flowers and spiny foliage, it stands out among its relatives in the Rhamnaceae family. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its ornamental appeal, it remains relatively understudied in the scientific community.
