Zeyheria Fluviatilis

Zeyheria fluviatilis is a unique aquatic plant native to South America. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as rivers and lakes. This plant is known for its delicate, floating leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Its ecological importance lies in providing habitat for aquatic life and stabilizing water ecosystems. Despite its modest appearance, Zeyheria fluviatilis plays a vital role in maintaining the balance of its habitat.
