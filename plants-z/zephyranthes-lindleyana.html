Zephyranthes lindleyana

Zephyranthes lindleyana, commonly known as the rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, including parts of Florida and the Caribbean.

Zephyranthes lindleyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to bloom quickly after rainfall.
