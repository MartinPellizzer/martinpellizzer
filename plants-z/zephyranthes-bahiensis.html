Zephyranthes bahiensis

Zephyranthes bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has strap-like leaves and is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates.

Zephyranthes bahiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
