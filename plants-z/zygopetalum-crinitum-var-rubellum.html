Zygopetalum crinitum var. rubellum

Zygopetalum crinitum var. rubellum, commonly known as the red-tipped zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals that range in color from deep red to pink, with a distinctive red or orange tip. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
