Zosima violacea

Zosima violacea, commonly known as the violet zosima, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers and lance-shaped leaves. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
