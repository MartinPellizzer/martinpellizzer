Zingiber jucundum

Zingiber jucundum, commonly known as the Happy Ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant features bright green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and red. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Zingiber jucundum thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
