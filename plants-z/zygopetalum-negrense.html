Zygopetalum negrense

Zygopetalum negrense, commonly known as the Negrense orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically during the dry season. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Zygopetalum negrense is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
