Zygopetalum massangei

Zygopetalum massangei, commonly known as the Massangei zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Zygopetalum massangei thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
