Ziziphus Mauritiana Subsp. Orthacantha

Indian date palm, scientifically known as Ziziphus mauritiana subsp. orthacantha, is a versatile and hardy plant native to the Indian subcontinent. It is valued for its edible fruits, which are rich in nutrients and used in traditional cuisines. The plant thrives in a variety of climates and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. Its ecological importance extends beyond food production, as it provides habitat and sustenance for various wildlife species. Despite its widespread use, the Indian date palm remains relatively underappreciated in global horticultural discussions.
