Zephyranthes pusilla

Zephyranthes pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf rain lily, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It typically grows to a height of only a few inches, with narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate white or pale pink flowers that bloom after rainfall. The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open areas such as savannas and grasslands.
