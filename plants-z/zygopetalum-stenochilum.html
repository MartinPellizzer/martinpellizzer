Zygopetalum stenochilum

Zygopetalum stenochilum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features long, slender petals and a distinctive, narrow lip that sets it apart from other orchid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Zygopetalum stenochilum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.
