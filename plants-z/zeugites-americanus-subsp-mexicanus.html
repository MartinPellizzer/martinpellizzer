Zeugites americanus subsp. mexicanus

Zeugites americanus subsp.

mexicanus, commonly known as the Mexican zeugite, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
