Zygopetalum gemma

Zygopetalum gemma, commonly known as the miniature zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid prized for its compact size and vibrant floral display.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor cultivation and greenhouse environments.

Zygopetalum gemma requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.
