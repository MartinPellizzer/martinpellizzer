Zanonia

Zanonia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. They are often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
