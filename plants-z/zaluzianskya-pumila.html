Zaluzianskya pumila

Zaluzianskya pumila, commonly known as the narrowleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Zaluzianskya pumila is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience in harsh conditions.
