Zephyranthes datensis

Zephyranthes datensis, commonly known as the dawn lily, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms in the early morning, hence its common name, and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.

Zephyranthes datensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
