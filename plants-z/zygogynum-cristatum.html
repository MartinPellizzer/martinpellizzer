Zygogynum cristatum

Zygogynum cristatum, commonly known as the crowned sarsaparilla, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its distinctive crown-like arrangement of leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elongated, woody fruits. Its leaves are compound, with elongated leaflets that give it a unique appearance.
