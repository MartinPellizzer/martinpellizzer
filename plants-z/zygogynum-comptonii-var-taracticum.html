Zygogynum comptonii var. taracticum

Zygogynum comptonii var. taracticum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
