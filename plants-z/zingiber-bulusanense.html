Zingiber bulusanense

Zingiber bulusanense, commonly known as the Mindanao ginger, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with narrow, linear leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.
