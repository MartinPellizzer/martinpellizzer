Zingiber cholmondeleyi

Zingiber cholmondeleyi, commonly known as the African ginger or red ginger, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a reddish tint. Its inflorescence consists of brightly colored, fragrant flowers that range from red to orange.

Zingiber cholmondeleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
