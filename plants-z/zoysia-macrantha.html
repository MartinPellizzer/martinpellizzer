Zoysia macrantha

Zoysia macrantha, commonly known as the broadleaf zoysia, is a warm-season grass native to East Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a dense growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in late summer, which are often pink or red in hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Zoysia macrantha is known for its drought tolerance and ability to form a thick, resilient turf.
