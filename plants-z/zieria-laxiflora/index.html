Zieria Laxiflora

Zieria laxiflora is a unique and delicate flowering plant native to the rainforests of eastern Australia. Known for its small, clustered white flowers and slender, grass-like leaves, it thrives in shady, moist environments. This species is part of the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is often found in rocky or clay soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Zieria laxiflora is considered a rare and endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the face of habitat loss and climate change.
