Zehneria japonica

Zehneria japonica, commonly known as Japanese zehneria, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Zehneria japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
