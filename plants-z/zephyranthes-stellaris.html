Zephyranthes stellaris

Zephyranthes stellaris, commonly known as the star flower or night bloomer, is a species of flowering plant native to the southeastern United States and the Caribbean.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are evergreen in warmer climates.

Zephyranthes stellaris is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or well-drained soils.
