Zoellnerallium andinum

Zoellnerallium andinum, commonly known as the Andean star-of-Bethlehem, is a bulbous plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It produces striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive purple or red throat. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions. Its bulbs are an important food source for local wildlife and have been used by indigenous communities for centuries.

Zoellnerallium andinum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
