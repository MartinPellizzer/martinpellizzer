Zigmaloba

Zigmaloba, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Zigmaloba thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
