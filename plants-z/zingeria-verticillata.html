Zingeria verticillata

Zingeria verticillata, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Zingeria verticillata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
