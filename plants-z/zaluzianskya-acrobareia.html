Zaluzianskya acrobareia, commonly known as the skyrocket lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its tall, spire-like flower stalks that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Zaluzianskya acrobareia is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit and showy display.
