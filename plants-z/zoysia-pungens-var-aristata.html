Zoysia pungens var. aristata

Zoysia pungens var. aristata, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-blue foliage and narrow, upright blades. The plant forms dense, low-growing mats and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, exhibiting moderate drought tolerance.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking color and texture, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.
