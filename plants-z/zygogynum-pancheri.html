Zygogynum pancheri

Zygogynum pancheri, commonly known as the Kashmiri star anise, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces dark brown, capsule-like fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas at higher altitudes.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and pharmacologists alike.
