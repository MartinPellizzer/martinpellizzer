Zexmenia scandens

Zexmenia scandens, commonly known as the climbing zexmenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Zexmeniaceae.

It is characterized by its twining stems that allow it to climb and spread along supports. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Zexmenia scandens is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments.
