Zanthoxylum Myriacanthum Var. Pubescens

Zanthoxylum myriacanthum var. pubescens is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its aromatic and often spiny characteristics. This variant is distinguished by its hairy foliage and distinctive fruit structure. While not widely cultivated, it holds cultural and medicinal significance in traditional practices. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.
