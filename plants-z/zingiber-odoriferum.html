Zingiber odoriferum

Zingiber odoriferum, commonly known as the fragrant ginger or wild ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of yellow and red. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Zingiber odoriferum is valued for its aromatic rhizomes and ornamental appeal in gardens.
