Zinnia laevis

Zinnia laevis, commonly known as the smooth zinnia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Zinnia laevis is an annual herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
