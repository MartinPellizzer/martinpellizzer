Zelkova schneideriana

Zelkova schneideriana, commonly known as the Chinese zelkova, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ulmaceae family, which includes elms and zelkovas. The tree is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It produces small, winged samaras as its fruit, which aid in seed dispersal.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
