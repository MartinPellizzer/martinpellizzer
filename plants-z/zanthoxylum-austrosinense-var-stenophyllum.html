Zanthoxylum austrosinense var. stenophyllum

Zanthoxylum austrosinense var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
