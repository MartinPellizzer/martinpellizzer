Zosterophyllanthos archidiaconi

Zosterophyllanthos archidiaconi, commonly known as the archidiaconal zosterophyll, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the class Zosterophyta.

The plant exhibits a distinct dichotomous branching pattern, which is a key feature of early land plants. Its fossils are often found in Paleozoic sedimentary rocks, providing important insights into early plant evolution.
