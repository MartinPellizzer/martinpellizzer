Zingania minutiflora

Zingania minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered zingania, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
