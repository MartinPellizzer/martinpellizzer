Zehneria perpusilla var. deltifrons

Zehneria perpusilla var. deltifrons, commonly known as the dwarf zehneria, is a compact, low-growing plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically a glossy green, often with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
