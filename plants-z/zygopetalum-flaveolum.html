Zygopetalum flaveolum

Zygopetalum flaveolum, commonly known as the yellow zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically fragrant and have a complex structure typical of the orchid family. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Zygopetalum flaveolum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking color and unique floral morphology.

It requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
