Zexmenia arnottii var. interea

Zexmenia arnottii var. interea, commonly known as the Northern River Grass, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of northern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shallow water or moist soils, often found along riverbanks and floodplains. The plant has slender, grass-like stems with small, narrow leaves that are often submerged or floating. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually pale green to yellowish in color, and bloom during the wet season.
