Zornia orbiculata

Zornia orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, pink to reddish-purple flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom in the summer months.

Zornia orbiculata is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and fields.
