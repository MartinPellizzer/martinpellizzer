Zeuxine somae

Zeuxine somae, commonly known as the Himalayan zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often branched and zigzagging.

Zeuxine somae is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
