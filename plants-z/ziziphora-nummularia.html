Ziziphora nummularia

Ziziphora nummularia, commonly known as roundleaf betony, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny environments. The plant features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Ziziphora nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
