Zygopetalum × bellum

Zygopetalum × bellum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Zygopetalum and Miltoniopsis.

It is a tropical orchid characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like, elongated, and often have a waxy texture.

Zygopetalum × bellum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity for optimal growth.
