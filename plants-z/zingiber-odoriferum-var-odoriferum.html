Zingiber odoriferum var. odoriferum

Zingiber odoriferum var. odoriferum, commonly known as the fragrant ginger or wild ginger, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like turmeric and cardamom. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a strong, spicy fragrance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
