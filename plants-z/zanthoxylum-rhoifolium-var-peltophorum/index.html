Zanthoxylum Rhoifolium Var. Peltophorum

Zanthoxylum rhoifolium var. peltophorum is a rare and striking plant native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its distinctive red berries and compound leaves that catch the eye in forest understories. this variant of zanthoxylum is often found in moist, shaded habitats along riverbanks and creek edges. its ornamental value has made it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and private collections. despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly understood by the general public.
