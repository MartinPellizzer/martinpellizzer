Zapoteca amazonica

Zapoteca amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian zapoteca, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a strong, aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale yellow, blooming during the wet season.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, seed-containing pods.
