Ziziphora acinos

Ziziphora acinos, commonly known as narrow-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Ziziphora acinos is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in traditional medicine.
