Zygogynum tieghemii subsp. tieghemii

Zygogynum tieghemii subsp.

tieghemii, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers.
