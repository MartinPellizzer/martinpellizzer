Zygopetalum lucidum

Zygopetalum lucidum, commonly known as the shining zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant mix of pink and white hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, adapting to its natural habitat in shaded, humid environments.

Zygopetalum lucidum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
