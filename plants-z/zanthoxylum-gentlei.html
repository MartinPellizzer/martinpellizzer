Zanthoxylum gentlei

Zanthoxylum gentlei, commonly known as the gentle prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.
