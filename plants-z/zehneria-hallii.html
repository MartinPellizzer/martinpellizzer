Zehneria hallii

Zehneria hallii, commonly known as the Hall's zehneria, is a rare and striking member of the Gesneriaceae family.

Native to the mountainous regions of western Africa, this plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from pink to red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Zehneria hallii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
