Zornia zeylonensis

Zornia zeylonensis, commonly known as the Ceylon zornia, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural distribution.

Zornia zeylonensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
