Zehneria palmatiloba

Zehneria palmatiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaf zehneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach several feet in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic value.

Zehneria palmatiloba is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in high humidity conditions.
