Zygopetalum gibeziae

Zygopetalum gibeziae, commonly known as the Giant Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, with vibrant purple and yellow hues. The plant has thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, adapting it to its arid mountainous habitat. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme, and it blooms during the cooler months of the year.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
