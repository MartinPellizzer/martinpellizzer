Zoysia tremula

Zoysia tremula, commonly known as the slender zoysia, is a perennial grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, soft foliage and dense growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental and turfgrass applications. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its rhizomatous and stolonal growth patterns allow it to spread and form a thick, resilient turf.

Zoysia tremula is valued for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal in landscaping.
