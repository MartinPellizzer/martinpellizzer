Ziziphora clinopodioides var. rigida

Ziziphora clinopodioides var. rigida, commonly known as rigid betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to both wild and cultivated plantings.
