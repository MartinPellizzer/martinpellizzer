Zephyranthes concolor

Zephyranthes concolor, commonly known as the pink hurricane lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a bulbous herbaceous plant that typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers during the late summer and early fall. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in swampy or moist habitats.
