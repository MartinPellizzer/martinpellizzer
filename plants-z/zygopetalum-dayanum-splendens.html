Zygopetalum dayanum splendens

Zygopetalum dayanum splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features sturdy, erect stems that bear large, colorful blooms with a distinctive lip. Its flowers typically display shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a contrasting white or cream throat.

Zygopetalum dayanum splendens thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix for optimal growth.
