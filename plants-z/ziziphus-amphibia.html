Ziziphus amphibia

Ziziphus amphibia, commonly known as the amphibious ziziphus, is a unique plant species that thrives in wetland environments.

Ziziphus amphibia is adapted to fluctuating water levels, making it a resilient species in marshy and riparian habitats.
