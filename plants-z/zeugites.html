Zeugites

Zeugites, commonly known as a type of rock-forming mineral, is not a plant but a mineral belonging to the zeolite group.

It is characterized by its crystalline structure and ability to absorb water and other substances. Zeugites are typically found in volcanic rocks and are used in various industrial applications. They do not have botanical significance as they are not part of the plant kingdom.

Their properties make them useful in construction and as a soil amendment.
