Ziziphus jujuba var. hysundrica

Ziziphus jujuba var. hysundrica, commonly known as the Chinese date or red date, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or reddish-brown when mature. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, appearing in clusters.

The fruit is rich in sugars and has been used for both culinary and medicinal purposes in traditional Chinese medicine.
