Zoegea baldschuanica

Zoegea baldschuanica, commonly known as the Caucasian zoegea, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a somewhat hairy texture.

Zoegea baldschuanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
