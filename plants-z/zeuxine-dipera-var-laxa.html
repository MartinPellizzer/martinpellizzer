Zeuxine dipera var. laxa

Zeuxine dipera var. laxa, commonly known as the slender zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its slender stems and delicate leaves contribute to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in high-altitude meadows and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
