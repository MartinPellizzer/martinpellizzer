Zygophyllum densispinum

Zygophyllum densispinum, commonly known as the dense-spined zygophyllum, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with small, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its dense spines provide protection from herbivores, while its deep root system allows it to access groundwater.

Zygophyllum densispinum is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient component of desert ecosystems.
