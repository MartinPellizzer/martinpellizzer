Zygophyllum rosowii

Zygophyllum rosowii, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Zygophyllaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Mongolia. The plant is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. It has adapted to harsh environments with its succulent leaves and deep root system for water storage.

Zygophyllum rosowii is often found in sandy soils and can tolerate extreme temperature fluctuations.
