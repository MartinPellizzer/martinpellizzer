Zanthoxylum amapaense

Zanthoxylum amapaense, commonly known as the Amazon prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species. Its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments makes it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem.
