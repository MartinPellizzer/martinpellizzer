Zeuxine viridiflora var. latifolia

Zeuxine viridiflora var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved green-flowered zeuxine, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its lanceolate, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
