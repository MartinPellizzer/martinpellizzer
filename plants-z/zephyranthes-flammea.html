Zephyranthes flammea

Zephyranthes flammea, commonly known as the flame lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a bulbous plant that produces showy, red-orange flowers during the dry season. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are short-lived but striking, often appearing in clusters.

Zephyranthes flammea is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
