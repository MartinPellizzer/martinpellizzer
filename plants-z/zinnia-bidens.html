Zinnia bidens

Zinnia bidens, commonly known as the common zinnia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters, featuring hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets, which are often bilaterally symmetrical.

Zinnia bidens is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
