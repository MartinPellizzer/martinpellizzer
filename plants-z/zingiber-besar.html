Zingiber besar

Zingiber besar, commonly known as the giant ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its rhizomes, which are underground stems, are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Zingiber besar thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
