Zingiber negrosense

Zingiber negrosense, commonly known as the Negros ginger, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in terminal clusters. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The botanical characteristics of Zingiber negrosense make it a unique species within its genus.
