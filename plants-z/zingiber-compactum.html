Zingiber compactum

Zingiber compactum, commonly known as the dwarf ginger or compact ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Zingiber compactum is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
