Zygopetalum mackayi var. charlesworthii

Zygopetalum mackayi var. charlesworthii, commonly known as the Charlesworth's zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often featuring a mix of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific growing conditions, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
