Ziziphora subcapitata

Ziziphora subcapitata, commonly known as the narrow-headed germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny conditions. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Ziziphora subcapitata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
