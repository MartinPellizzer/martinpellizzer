Zygophyllum ifniense

Zygophyllum ifniense, commonly known as the Canary Islands prickly scale.

It is a shrub native to the Canary Islands, characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, exhibiting succulent features to store water. It belongs to the family Zygophyllaceae, which includes other desert plants with similar adaptations.

Zygophyllum ifniense plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
