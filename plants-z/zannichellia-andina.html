Zannichellia andina

Zannichellia andina, commonly known as the Andean water starwort, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. It produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water, often in the spring and summer months.
