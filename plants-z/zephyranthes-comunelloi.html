Zephyranthes comunelloi

Zephyranthes comunelloi, commonly known as the pink hurricane lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and the plant is known for its resilience in tropical and subtropical climates.

Zephyranthes comunelloi is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
