Zygopetalum dayanum

Zygopetalum dayanum, commonly known as the Dayan orchid, is a rare and unique species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive zygomatic structure. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, thriving in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen, while its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and conservationists alike.
