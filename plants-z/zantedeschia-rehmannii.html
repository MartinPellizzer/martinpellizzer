Zantedeschia rehmannii

Zantedeschia rehmannii, commonly known as the white calla lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, slender stem with a single, spatulate leaf that is often variegated with white and green. The plant produces a large, showy white spathe that encloses a cluster of yellowish-green, pollen-rich flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia rehmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
