Zygocarpum rectangulare

Zygocarpum rectangulare, commonly known as the rectangular false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat, composite head, typical of the dandelion-like appearance.
