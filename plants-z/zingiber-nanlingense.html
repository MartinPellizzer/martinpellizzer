Zingiber nanlingense

Zingiber nanlingense, commonly known as the Nanling ginger, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like ginger and turmeric. This plant is characterized by its striking, narrow leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
