Ziziphus mucronata var. glabrata

Ziziphus mucronata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-fruited date palm, is a variant of the date palm native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous fruit surface, which distinguishes it from other varieties of Ziziphus mucronata. The plant produces small, sweet, edible fruits that are often consumed fresh or processed into products like dates. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to hot, dry climates with well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts in its native range.
