Zosterophyllanthos fossulata

Zosterophyllanthos fossulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zosterophyll, is a unique plant species characterized by its specialized morphology.

Its reproductive structures are simple, consisting of sporangia that release spores for dispersal.
