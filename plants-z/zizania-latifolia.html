Zizania latifolia

Zizania latifolia, commonly known as Chinese arrowhead, is a semi-aquatic perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves and cylindrical, branched inflorescences that produce small, fluffy flowers. The plant thrives in wetlands, marshes, and slow-moving water bodies, where it forms dense stands. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese cuisine and medicine.

Zizania latifolia also plays an important ecological role by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
