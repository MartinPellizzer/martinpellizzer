Zaluzianskya venusta

Zaluzianskya venusta, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
