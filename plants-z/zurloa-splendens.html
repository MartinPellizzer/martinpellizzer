Zurloa splendens

Zurloa splendens, commonly known as the Golden Flame Orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and slightly wavy in texture. It thrives in high humidity and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
