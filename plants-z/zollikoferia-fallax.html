Zollikoferia fallax

Zollikoferia fallax, commonly known as the narrow-leaf yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Zollikoferia fallax is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects like ladybugs and lacewings.
