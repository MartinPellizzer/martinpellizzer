Zeuxine lindleyana

Zeuxine lindleyana, commonly known as the mountain zeuxine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, including India, Nepal, and Bhutan. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowers are usually small and yellow, with five petals and numerous stamens, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Zeuxine lindleyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
