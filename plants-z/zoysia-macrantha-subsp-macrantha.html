Zoysia macrantha subsp. macrantha

Zoysia macrantha subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as broadleaf zoysia, is a perennial grass native to Southeast Asia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers in summer, which are clustered in loose panicles. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its dense growth habit makes it a popular choice for lawns and erosion control in tropical and subtropical regions.
