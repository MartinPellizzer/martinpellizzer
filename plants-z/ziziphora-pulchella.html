Ziziphora pulchella

Ziziphora pulchella, commonly known as the pretty ziziphora, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly aromatic, contributing to its medicinal and ornamental value.

Ziziphora pulchella thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
