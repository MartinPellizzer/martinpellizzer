Zeuxine formosana

Zeuxine formosana, commonly known as the Formosan zeuxine, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
