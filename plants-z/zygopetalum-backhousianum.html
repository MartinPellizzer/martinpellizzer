Zygopetalum backhousianum

Zygopetalum backhousianum, commonly known as the Backhouse's zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, often in spring or early summer. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid is well-suited for cultivation in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its beauty and ease of care.
