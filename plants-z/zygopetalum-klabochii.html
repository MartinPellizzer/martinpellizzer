Zygopetalum klabochii

Zygopetalum klabochii, commonly known as the Klaboch's zygopetalum, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the high altitudes of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its arid or semi-arid habitat. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often covered in a fine, silky hair that helps reduce water loss.

The species is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
