Zieria chevalieri

Zieria chevalieri, commonly known as the slender zieria, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the subtropical regions of eastern Australia.

It typically grows in coastal heathlands and rocky soils, where it thrives in well-drained conditions. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers appear in clusters during late spring to early summer.

Zieria chevalieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
