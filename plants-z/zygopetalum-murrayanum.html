Zygopetalum murrayanum

Zygopetalum murrayanum, commonly known as the Murray's zygopetalum, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that range from yellow to orange and red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Zygopetalum murrayanum is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and limited distribution.
