Zingiber natmataungense

Zingiber natmataungense, commonly known as the Natmataung ginger, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Myanmar.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow.
