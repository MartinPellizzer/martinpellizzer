Zantedeschia chloroleuca

Zantedeschia chloroleuca, commonly known as the white calla lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces large, showy white flowers with a distinctive, hood-like spathe surrounding a spadix. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and a preference for moist, well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
