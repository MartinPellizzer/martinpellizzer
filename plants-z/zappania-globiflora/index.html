Zappania Globiflora

Zappania globiflora is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa. It is known for its striking, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
