Ziziphora schizodonta

Ziziphora schizodonta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late summer.

Ziziphora schizodonta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
