Zannichellia palustris var. peltata

Zannichellia palustris var. peltata, commonly known as the broad-leaved water starwort, is a aquatic plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and marshes.
