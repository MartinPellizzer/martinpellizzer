Zephyranthes taubertii

Zephyranthes taubertii, commonly known as the rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and has a short, rhizomatous stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires regular watering to bloom.

Zephyranthes taubertii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
