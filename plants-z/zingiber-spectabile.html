Zingiber spectabile

Zingiber spectabile, commonly known as the spectable ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flower spikes that can reach up to 3 feet in height. Its flowers are typically orange or yellow, with a spicy scent reminiscent of ginger.

Zingiber spectabile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual and aromatic qualities.
