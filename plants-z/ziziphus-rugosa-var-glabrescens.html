Ziziphus rugosa var. glabrescens

Ziziphus rugosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-fruited Chinese date, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears edible fruits that are typically round or oval, with a leathery skin and a sweet, sticky pulp. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in traditional Chinese horticulture.
