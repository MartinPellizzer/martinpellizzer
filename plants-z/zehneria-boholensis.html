Zehneria boholensis

Zehneria boholensis, commonly known as the Bohol spider plant, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Zehneria boholensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
