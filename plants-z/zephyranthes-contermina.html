Zephyranthes contermina

Zephyranthes contermina, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for festive floral displays. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value and ease of cultivation make it a favored plant in gardens and floral arrangements.
