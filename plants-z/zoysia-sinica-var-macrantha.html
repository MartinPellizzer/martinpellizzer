Zoysia sinica var. macrantha

Zoysia sinica var. macrantha, commonly known as Chinese zoysia, is a perennial grass native to Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, dense foliage and creeping growth habit, forming a dense mat. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny white to pale blue flowers. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its adaptability to various climates contributes to its popularity in horticultural settings.
