Zigadenus elegans subsp. glaucus

Zigadenus elegans subsp.

glaucus, commonly known as the blue-flowered death camas, is a member of the Melanthiaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to western North America, particularly found in California and Oregon.
