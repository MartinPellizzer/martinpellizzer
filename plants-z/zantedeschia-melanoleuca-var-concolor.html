Zantedeschia melanoleuca var. concolor

Zantedeschia melanoleuca var. concolor, commonly known as the African calla lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, white, funnel-shaped blooms that are often surrounded by a prominent, dark green spathe. The plant has broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
