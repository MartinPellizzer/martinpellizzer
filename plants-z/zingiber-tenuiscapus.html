Zingiber tenuiscapus

Zingiber tenuiscapus, commonly known as the slender ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Zingiber tenuiscapus thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
