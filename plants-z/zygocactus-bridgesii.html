Zygocactus bridgesii

Zygocactus bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridgess zygocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems that store water, a feature typical of succulent plants. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, which help reduce water loss and deter herbivores.

Zygocactus bridgesii thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
