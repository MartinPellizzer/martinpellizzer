Zygotritonia nyassana

Zygotritonia nyassana, commonly known as the Nyassan spider flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking inflorescence composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Zygotritonia nyassana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
