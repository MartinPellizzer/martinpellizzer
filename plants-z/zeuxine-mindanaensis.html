Zeuxine mindanaensis

Zeuxine mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao zeuxine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Mindanao. The plant is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It prefers moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
