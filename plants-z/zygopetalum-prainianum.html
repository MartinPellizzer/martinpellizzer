Zygopetalum prainianum

Zygopetalum prainianum, commonly known as the Prain's zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
