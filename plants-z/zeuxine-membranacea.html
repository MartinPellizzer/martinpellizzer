Zeuxine membranacea

Zeuxine membranacea, commonly known as the membranous zeuxine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Zeuxine in the family Orchidaceae, which includes orchids. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that often have a distinctive yellow or greenish hue. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, membranous texture, giving it its common name.

Zeuxine membranacea is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
