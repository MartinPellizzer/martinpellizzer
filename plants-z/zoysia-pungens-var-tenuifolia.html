Zoysia pungens var. tenuifolia

Zoysia pungens var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf zoysia, is a low-growing, mat-forming grass native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a fine texture and a bluish-green color that remains vibrant throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by dark, spherical seed heads. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and low-maintenance landscapes.

Its dense growth habit helps suppress weeds and provides erosion control in suitable environments.
