Zygia odoratissima

Zygia odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant zygia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly glossy surface.

Zygia odoratissima is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for its ornamental appeal.
