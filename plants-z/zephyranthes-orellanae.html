Zephyranthes orellanae

Zephyranthes orellanae, commonly known as the Orellana's rain lily, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It produces striking, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom after rainfall, typically during the wet season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

Zephyranthes orellanae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ephemeral beauty.
