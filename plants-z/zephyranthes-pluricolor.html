Zephyranthes pluricolor

Zephyranthes pluricolor, commonly known as the rainbow starflower, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically blooms during the dry season and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the year.

Zephyranthes pluricolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
