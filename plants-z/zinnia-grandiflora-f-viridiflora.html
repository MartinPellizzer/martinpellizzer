Zinnia grandiflora f. viridiflora

Zinnia grandiflora f. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered zinnia, is a hybrid form of the zinnia plant characterized by its unique foliage and floral features.

It is native to the southern United States and thrives in warm, sunny climates. The leaves are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to the flowers. Its blooms are usually smaller than those of the standard zinnia, with a more compact growth habit.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its vibrant colors and hardy nature.
