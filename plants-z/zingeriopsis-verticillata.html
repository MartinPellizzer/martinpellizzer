Zingeriopsis verticillata

Zingeriopsis verticillata, commonly known as the spiral herb, is a small flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists studying plant evolution and adaptation.
