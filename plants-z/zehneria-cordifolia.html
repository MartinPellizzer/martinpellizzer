Zehneria cordifolia

Zehneria cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf zehneria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Zehneria cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
