Zoysia matrella var. macrantha

Zoysia matrella var. macrantha, commonly known as the broadleaf zoysia, is a variant of the zoysia grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fine-textured foliage and a spreading growth habit that forms a thick, carpet-like turf. The plant produces showy, panicle-like inflorescences that are typically pink to reddish in color, adding ornamental value to landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
