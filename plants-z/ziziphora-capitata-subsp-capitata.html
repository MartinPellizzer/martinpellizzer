Ziziphora capitata subsp. capitata

Ziziphora capitata subsp.

capitata, commonly known as the flowering germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
