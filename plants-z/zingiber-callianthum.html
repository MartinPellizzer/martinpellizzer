Zingiber callianthum

Zingiber callianthum, commonly known as the red ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features vibrant red and orange inflorescences that resemble large, ornamental flowers. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a glossy texture.

Zingiber callianthum is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value rather than for culinary or medicinal purposes.
