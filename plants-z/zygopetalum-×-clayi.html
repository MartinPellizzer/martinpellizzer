Zygopetalum × clayi

Zygopetalum × clayi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a captivating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Zygopetalum and Cymbidium, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Zygopetalum × clayi is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
