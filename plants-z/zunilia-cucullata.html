Zunilia cucullata

Zunilia cucullata, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Zunilia cucullata is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
