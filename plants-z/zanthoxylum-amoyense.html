Zanthoxylum amoyense

Zanthoxylum amoyense, commonly known as the Amoy prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces red berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its bark and fruits contain bioactive compounds, making it valuable for both medicinal and ornamental purposes.
