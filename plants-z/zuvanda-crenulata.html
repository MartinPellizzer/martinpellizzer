Zuvanda crenulata

Zuvanda crenulata, commonly known as the serrated leaf succulent, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and serrated, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the warm season. It is well adapted to rocky soils and can thrive in full sun with minimal water.

Due to its striking appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
