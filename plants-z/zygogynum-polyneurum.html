Zygogynum polyneurum

Zygogynum polyneurum, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its edible fruits, which are rich in nutrients and have a buttery texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and nutritional value, it is of interest in both botanical research and potential agricultural applications.
