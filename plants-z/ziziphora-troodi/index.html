Ziziphora Troodi

Ziziphora troodi is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic herbs and medicinal properties. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Ziziphora troodi is considered a conservation concern. Its unique features make it a subject of interest for botanists and ecologists alike.
