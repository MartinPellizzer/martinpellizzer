Zygosepalum revolutum

Zygosepalum revolutum, commonly known as the rolling-sepal orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its unique, spirally twisted sepals that give it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the summer months.

Zygosepalum revolutum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and distinctive floral structure.
