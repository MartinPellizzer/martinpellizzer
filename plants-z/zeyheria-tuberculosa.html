Zeyheria tuberculosa

Zeyheria tuberculosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, velvety flowers in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Zeyheria tuberculosa is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
