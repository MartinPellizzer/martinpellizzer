Zornia diphylla var. leptophylla

Zornia diphylla var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaf zornia, is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are finely divided and give the plant a delicate appearance. The flowers are typically small and clustered in racemes, with a distinctive pink or purple hue. This variant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
