Zeocriton strictum

Zeocriton strictum, commonly known as the rigid zeocriton, is a unique plant species characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive foliage.

It belongs to the family Zeocritonaceae and is native to arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from pale yellow to deep orange.

Zeocriton strictum is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
