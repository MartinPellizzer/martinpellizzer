Zygoon graveolens

Zygoon graveolens, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dark green, oval-shaped leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces toxic, purple berries that are highly poisonous to humans and animals. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it is typically avoided in gardens and natural areas.
