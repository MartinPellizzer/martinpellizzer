Zygogynum balansae

Zygogynum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and oblong, star-shaped fruits that resemble miniature pumpkins. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil to flourish. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly waxy texture, and the plant is often found in secondary forest understories.

Zygogynum balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
