Zehneria umbellata

Zehneria umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped inflorescence. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a spadix, which is typical of the Araceae family.

Zehneria umbellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
