Zwingera floribunda

Zwingera floribunda, commonly known as the showy zwingera, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture in its arid habitat. Zwingera floribunda thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to gardens in temperate climates.
