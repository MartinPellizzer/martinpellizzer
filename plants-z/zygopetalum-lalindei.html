Zygopetalum lalindei

Zygopetalum lalindei, commonly known as the Lalinde's zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Zygopetalum lalindei thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
