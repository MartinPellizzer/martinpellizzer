Ziziphora tenuior

Ziziphora tenuior, commonly known as narrowleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, aromatic leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands.

Ziziphora tenuior is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
