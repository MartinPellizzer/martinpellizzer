Zephyranthes × ajax

Zephyranthes × ajax, commonly known as the rain lily or spider lily, is a hybrid plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in response to rainfall. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in well-drained soil. Its bulbs are underground storage organs that allow it to survive dry periods.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with six petals and a distinctive, narrow corona.
