Zanthoxylum decaryi

Zanthoxylum decaryi, commonly known as the African prickly ash, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often pinnate and have serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are small, red berries that contain several seeds.

Zanthoxylum decaryi is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
