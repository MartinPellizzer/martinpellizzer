Zanonia cissoides

Zanonia cissoides, commonly known as the creeping zanonia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

Zanonia cissoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
