Ziziphora brevicalyx

Ziziphora brevicalyx, commonly known as Chinese feverfew, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, aromatic stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine.

Ziziphora brevicalyx is valued for its potential calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
