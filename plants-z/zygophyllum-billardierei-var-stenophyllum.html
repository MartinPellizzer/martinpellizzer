Zygophyllum billardierei var. stenophyllum

Zygophyllum billardierei var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved zig-zag plant, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zig-zag stems and small, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and linear. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system that allows it to survive in poor soil conditions.

This variety is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in native habitats.
