Zygophyllum miniatum subsp. dielsianum

Zygophyllum miniatum subsp.

dielsianum, commonly known as the dwarf sandmat, is a low-growing shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is adapted to harsh environments, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions. It is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in areas with limited water availability.

Its resilience makes it a valuable species for ecological restoration in degraded landscapes.
