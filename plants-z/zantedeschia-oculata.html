Zantedeschia oculata

Zantedeschia oculata, commonly known as the white calla lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a reddish tinge along the veins. The plant produces elegant, white spatulate flowers with a prominent, showy spathe that encloses the small, clustered true flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia oculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
