Zollikoferia anomala

Zollikoferia anomala, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and its clusters of yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is drought-tolerant and has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
