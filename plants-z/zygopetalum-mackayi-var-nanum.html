Zygopetalum mackayi var. nanum

Zygopetalum mackayi var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf zygopetalum, is a compact orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more prominent. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the old ones. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
