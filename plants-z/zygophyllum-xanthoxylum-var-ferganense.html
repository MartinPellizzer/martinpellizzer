Zygophyllum xanthoxylum var. ferganense

Zygophyllum xanthoxylum var. ferganense, commonly known as the Fergana Zygophyllum, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves adapted to arid environments. The plant belongs to the family Zygophyllaceae, which includes species adapted to dry and saline soils. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in steppe and desert regions.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
