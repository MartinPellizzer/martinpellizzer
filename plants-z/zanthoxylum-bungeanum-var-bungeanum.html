Zanthoxylum bungeanum var. bungeanum

Zanthoxylum bungeanum var. bungeanum, commonly known as Sichuan pepper, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. Its fruit contains aromatic seeds that are widely used in Chinese cuisine for their distinctive numbing flavor.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental and culinary value.
