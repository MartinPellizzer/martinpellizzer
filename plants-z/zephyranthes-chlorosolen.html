Zephyranthes chlorosolen

Zephyranthes chlorosolen, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant typically blooms during the rainy season, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.
