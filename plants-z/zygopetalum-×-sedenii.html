Zygopetalum × sedenii

Zygopetalum × sedenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Zygopetalum pachyanthum and Zygopetalum hancockii.

It is a sympodial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous or upright flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of dark green, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape.

Zygopetalum × sedenii is native to the tropical regions of South America and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
