Zapoteca

Zapoteca, commonly known as the Zapotec wild coffee, is a shrub native to the highlands of Oaxaca, Mexico.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species of coffee and shrubs. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds used in traditional medicine and as a stimulant. Its leaves are often used in local rituals and as a natural remedy for various ailments.

The Zapoteca plant is valued for its ecological and cultural significance in indigenous communities.
