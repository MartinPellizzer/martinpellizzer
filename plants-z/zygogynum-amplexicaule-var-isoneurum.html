Zygogynum amplexicaule var. isoneurum

Zygogynum amplexicaule var. isoneurum, commonly known as the African violet, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that often have a waxy texture and a distinctive heart-shaped base. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the year under proper care. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
