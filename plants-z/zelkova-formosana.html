Zelkova formosana

Zelkova formosana, commonly known as the Formosan zelkova, is a deciduous tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Ulmaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, followed by winged fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
