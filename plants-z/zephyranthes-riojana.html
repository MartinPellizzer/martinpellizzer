Zephyranthes riojana

Zephyranthes riojana, commonly known as the Rioja rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are typically evergreen and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Zephyranthes riojana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
