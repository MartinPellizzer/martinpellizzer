Zygophyllum karatavicum

Zygophyllum karatavicum, commonly known as the Karatavicum sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions. Its root system is extensive, enabling it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Zygophyllum karatavicum plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is often found in desert and steppe ecosystems.
