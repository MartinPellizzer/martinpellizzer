Zephyranthes eggersiana

Zephyranthes eggersiana, commonly known as the rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in open areas such as grasslands and forest edges.

Zephyranthes eggersiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
