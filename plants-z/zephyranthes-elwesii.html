Zephyranthes elwesii

Zephyranthes elwesii, commonly known as the rain lily, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is known for its ability to bloom after rainfall, often during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Zephyranthes elwesii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
