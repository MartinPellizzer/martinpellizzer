Zoysia malaccensis

Zoysia malaccensis, commonly known as the Malacca zoysia, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted blades and a dense, carpet-like growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage is dark green and remains lush throughout much of the year.

Zoysia malaccensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
