Zeocriton secalinum

Zeocriton secalinum, commonly known as the golden grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically golden in color, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Zeocriton secalinum is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep root systems that help it access water from deeper soil layers.
