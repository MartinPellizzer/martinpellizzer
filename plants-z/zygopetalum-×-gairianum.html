Zygopetalum × gairianum

Zygopetalum × gairianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Zygopetalum and Brassolaelia species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Zygopetalum × gairianum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
