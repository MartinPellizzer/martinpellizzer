Zanthoxylum wallichianum

Zanthoxylum wallichianum, commonly known as the Himalayan prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers.
