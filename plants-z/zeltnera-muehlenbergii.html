Zeltnera muehlenbergii

Zeltnera muehlenbergii, commonly known as the Missouri rock violet, is a perennial herb native to the central United States.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in open woodlands and prairies. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy violet-blue flowers in the spring. It is a member of the Violaceae family, which includes violets and related plants.
