Zeuxine ovata

Zeuxine ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf false dragonhead, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on both surfaces. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers with five petals, typical of the Boraginaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
