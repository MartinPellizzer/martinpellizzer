Zanonia bibracteata

Zanonia bibracteata, commonly known as the Cape flatscar, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, leaf-like bracts that surround the flower clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Zanonia bibracteata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
