Ziziphus chinensis

Ziziphus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese date or jujube, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It produces small, yellowish-green fruits that ripen to a reddish-brown color, often referred to as "red dates.". The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Ziziphus chinensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, making it a hardy species suitable for various soil types.

It is also valued for its edible fruits and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
