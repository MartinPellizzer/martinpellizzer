Zingiber longyanjiang

Zingiber longyanjiang, commonly known as the Yunnan ginger.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-white, arranged in loose inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its aromatic rhizomes, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
