Zollingeria dongnaiensis

Zollingeria dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
