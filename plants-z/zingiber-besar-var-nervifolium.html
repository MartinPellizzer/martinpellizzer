Zingiber besar var. nervifolium

Zingiber besar var. nervifolium, commonly known as the giant ginger or large ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
