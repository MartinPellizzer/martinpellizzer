Zebrina pendula var. villosa

Zebrina pendula var. villosa, commonly known as the velvety spider plant, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its cascading foliage and vibrant, variegated leaves.

The leaves are typically a mix of white, green, and pink, creating a visually appealing contrast. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
