Ziziphora orontia

Ziziphora orontia, commonly known as mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorled clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Ziziphora orontia is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
