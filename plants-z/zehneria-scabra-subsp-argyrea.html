Zehneria scabra subsp. argyrea

Zehneria scabra subsp.

argyrea, commonly known as silverleaf zehneria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Zehneria scabra subsp.

argyrea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
