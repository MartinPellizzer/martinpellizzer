Zeuxine odorata

Zeuxine odorata, commonly known as the sweetroot or fragrant zeuxine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that bloom in spring.
