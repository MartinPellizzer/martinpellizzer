Zygopetalum coloratum

Zygopetalum coloratum, commonly known as the Purple Butterfly Orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, butterfly-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, with intricate patterns that resemble butterfly wings.

Zygopetalum coloratum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
