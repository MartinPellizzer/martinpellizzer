Zingiber smilesianum

Zingiber smilesianum, commonly known as the pink ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, pinkish-red flowers with a spicy fragrance. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and aromatic qualities.
