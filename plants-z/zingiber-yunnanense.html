Zingiber yunnanense

Zingiber yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan ginger, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in terminal inflorescences, and are surrounded by prominent, colorful bracts.

This species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and for its potential medicinal properties.
