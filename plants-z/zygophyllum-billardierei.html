Zygophyllum billardierei

Zygophyllum billardierei, commonly known as the silver leaf chenopod, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its gray-green, succulent leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

Zygophyllum billardierei plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
