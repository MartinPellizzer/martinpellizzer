Zygopetalum acuminatum

Zygopetalum acuminatum, commonly known as the Acuminatum Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant color palette, including shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral development. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, arranged in a fan-like pattern around the stem.

Zygopetalum acuminatum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
