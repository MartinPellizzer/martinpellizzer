Zenopogon genistae

The galls are typically found on the leaves of Genista, a genus of flowering plants in the legume family. The plant's botanical features include small, woody shrubs with yellow flowers and compound leaves. The interaction between Zenopogon genistae and Genista is an example of herbivory-induced plant defense responses.
