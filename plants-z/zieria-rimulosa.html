Zieria rimulosa

Zieria rimulosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zieria, is a small evergreen shrub native to the wet tropical regions of Queensland, Australia.

It typically grows in dense, shaded understorey environments of rainforests and moist forested areas. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small, white or cream-colored, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Zieria rimulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
