Zornia brasiliensis var. adenocarpa

Zornia brasiliensis var. adenocarpa, commonly known as the Brazilian zornia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds, typical of many legumes. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
