Zigmaloba sulcata

Zigmaloba sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag succulent, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems that are covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its textured foliage. Zigmaloba sulcata is a hardy succulent that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
