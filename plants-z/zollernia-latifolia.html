Zollernia latifolia

Zollernia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf zollernia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its bark contains compounds that have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.

Zollernia latifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.
