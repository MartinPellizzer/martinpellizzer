Zehneria odorata

Zehneria odorata, commonly known as the fragrant zehneria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces fleshy, red berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Zehneria odorata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both horticultural and ecological landscapes.
