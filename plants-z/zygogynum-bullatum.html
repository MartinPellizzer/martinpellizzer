Zygogynum bullatum

Zygogynum bullatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming a compact rosette structure. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its native arid environment.

Zygogynum bullatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
