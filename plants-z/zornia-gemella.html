Zornia gemella

Zornia gemella, commonly known as the slender zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually hairy and lance-shaped. Its flowers are pale pink to white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Zornia gemella is adapted to arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
