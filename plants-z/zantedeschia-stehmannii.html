Zantedeschia stehmannii

Zantedeschia stehmannii, commonly known as the Stehmann's arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and prominently veined. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers with a white spathe and a yellowish spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia stehmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
