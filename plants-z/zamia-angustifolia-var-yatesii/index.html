Zamia Angustifolia Var. Yatesii

Zamia angustifolia var. yatesii is a rare and unique plant species native to the southeastern United States. It belongs to the cycad family, known for their ancient lineage and prehistoric appearance. This variety is distinguished by its narrow, strap-like leaves and compact, cone-like structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in swampy areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
