Zizia Stricklandii

Zizia stricklandii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads. This species is often found in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to its environment. Due to habitat loss, Zizia stricklandii is considered endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
