Zenobia speciosa var. viridis

Zenobia speciosa var. viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Zenobia, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant green foliage.

It is a variant of the broader Zenobia speciosa species, which belongs to the family Polygonaceae. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its unique coloration and foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
