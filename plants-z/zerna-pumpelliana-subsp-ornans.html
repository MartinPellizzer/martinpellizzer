Zerna pumpelliana subsp. ornans

Zerna pumpelliana subsp.

ornans, commonly known as the ornate zerna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces delicate, clustered inflorescences bearing tiny, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
