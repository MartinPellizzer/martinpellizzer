Zeuxine gilgiana

Zeuxine gilgiana, commonly known as the Himalayan bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

Zeuxine gilgiana is valued both for its beauty and its ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
