Zornia capensis var. capensis

Zornia capensis var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape zornia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the legume family, Fabaceae.
