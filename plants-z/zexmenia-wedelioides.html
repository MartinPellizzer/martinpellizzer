Zexmenia wedelioides

Zexmenia wedelioides, commonly known as the Wedelioides zexmenia, is a rare and unusual plant species native to southern Africa.

The plant produces cones rather than flowers, which are typically male and female, with the female cones bearing a distinctive, fleshy, red or orange aril. Due to its restricted habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered and requires specific conditions to thrive.
