Zhukowskia scintillans

Zhukowskia scintillans, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, giving it a delicate and ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows in well-drained soil and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen, adding a textural element to the landscape.

Zhukowskia scintillans is also noted for its long blooming period, often from late winter to early spring, providing early color in the garden.
