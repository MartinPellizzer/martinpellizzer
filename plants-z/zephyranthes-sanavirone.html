Zephyranthes sanavirone

Zephyranthes sanavirone, commonly known as the Sanavirone rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a bulbous plant that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as savannas and forest edges.

The flowers are typically short-lived but attract pollinators like bees and moths.
