Zehneria hastata

Zehneria hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zehneria, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. Zehneria hastata thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
