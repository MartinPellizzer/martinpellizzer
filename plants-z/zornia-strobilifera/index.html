Zornia Strobilifera

Zornia strobilifera is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the legume family. Native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance, featuring clustered seed pods and delicate foliage, makes it a standout in its ecosystem. Despite its resilience, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked by botanists and gardeners alike. This article explores the biology, habitat, and ecological significance of Zornia strobilifera.
