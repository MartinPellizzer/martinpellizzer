Zanthoxylum mayanum

Zanthoxylum mayanum, commonly known as Mayan prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Central America and the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish or orange-red flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
