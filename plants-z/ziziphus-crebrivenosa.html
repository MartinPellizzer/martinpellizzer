Ziziphus crebrivenosa

Ziziphus crebrivenosa, commonly known as the dense-fruited date palm, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, date-like fruits that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Ziziphus crebrivenosa is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
