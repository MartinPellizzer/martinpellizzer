Zygopetalum flabelliforme

Zygopetalum flabelliforme, commonly known as the flabelliform zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
