Zephyranthes seubertii

Zephyranthes seubertii, commonly known as the rain lily, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous plant that produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant typically blooms after rainfall, hence its common name, and is often found in open, sunny areas. Its leaves are grass-like and narrow, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Zephyranthes seubertii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fleeting yet striking blooms.
