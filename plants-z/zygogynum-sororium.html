Zygogynum sororium

Zygogynum sororium, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, violet-colored flowers that bloom throughout the year under proper care. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular houseplant worldwide.
