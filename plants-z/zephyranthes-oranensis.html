Zephyranthes oranensis

Zephyranthes oranensis, commonly known as the Algerian rain lily, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the rainy season, often after thunderstorms, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular choice for horticultural use in arid regions.
