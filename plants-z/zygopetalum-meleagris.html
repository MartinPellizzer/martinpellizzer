Zygopetalum meleagris

Zygopetalum meleagris, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that feature a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its floral structure includes a spurred lip, which is a common feature in many orchid species.

Zygopetalum meleagris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
