Zenkeria jainii

Zenkeria jainii, commonly known as the Jain's spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in cyathia, a unique floral structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It produces a milky latex, which is a common feature among members of this family.

The plant is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and disturbed areas.
