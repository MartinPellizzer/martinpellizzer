Zygopetalum dormanianum

Zygopetalum dormanianum, commonly known as the Dormanian zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Zygopetalum dormanianum thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
