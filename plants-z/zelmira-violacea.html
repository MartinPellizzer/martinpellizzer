Zelmira violacea

Zelmira violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features striking violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns, often resembling those of true orchids. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Zelmira violacea thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and greenhouses.
