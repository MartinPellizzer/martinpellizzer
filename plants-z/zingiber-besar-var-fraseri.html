Zingiber besar var. fraseri

Zingiber besar var. fraseri, commonly known as Fraser's giant ginger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, broad leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, providing a lush, tropical appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time.

This variety is well-suited for shaded garden areas and is valued for its aesthetic appeal and aromatic qualities.
