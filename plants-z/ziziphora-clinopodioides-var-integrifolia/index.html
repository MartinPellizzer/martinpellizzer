Ziziphora Clinopodioides Var. Integrifolia

Blue mistflower, scientifically known as Ziziphora clinopodioides var. integrifolia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western United States. This perennial herb is prized for its delicate, blue-gray foliage and its clusters of tiny, mist-like flowers that bloom in late summer. Often found in rocky soils and dry landscapes, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens. With its calming blue tones and ecological benefits, the blue mistflower is both an ornamental and a valuable addition to local ecosystems.
