Zygopetalum jorisianum

Zygopetalum jorisianum, commonly known as the Jorisianum zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Zygopetalum jorisianum prefers cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.
