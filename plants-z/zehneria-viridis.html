Zehneria viridis

Zehneria viridis, commonly known as the green zehneria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.

Zehneria viridis is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
