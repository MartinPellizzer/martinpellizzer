Zappania Staechadifolia

Zappania staechadifolia is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, making it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping. Its ability to store water in its leaves helps it survive in harsh, dry environments. Despite its tough exterior, Zappania staechadifolia adds a touch of elegance and resilience to any garden.
