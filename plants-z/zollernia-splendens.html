Zollernia splendens

Zollernia splendens, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Zollernia splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
