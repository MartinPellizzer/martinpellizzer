Zuvanda meyeri

Zuvanda meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's zuvanda, is a unique plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit with small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Zuvanda meyeri thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.
