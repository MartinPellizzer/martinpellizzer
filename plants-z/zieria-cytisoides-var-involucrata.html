Zieria cytisoides var. involucrata

Zieria cytisoides var. involucrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zieria, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathland and coastal environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant communities.
