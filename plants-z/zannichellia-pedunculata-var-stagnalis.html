Zannichellia pedunculata var. stagnalis

Zannichellia pedunculata var. stagnalis, commonly known as the pondweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater habitats in Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces tiny, green flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences, which emerge from the water surface.
