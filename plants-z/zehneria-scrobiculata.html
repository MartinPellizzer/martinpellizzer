Zehneria scrobiculata

Zehneria scrobiculata, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments and indirect light.
