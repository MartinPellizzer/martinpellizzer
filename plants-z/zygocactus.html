Zygocactus

Zygocactus, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming around the holiday season. Zygocactus thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a favored ornamental plant in homes and gardens.
