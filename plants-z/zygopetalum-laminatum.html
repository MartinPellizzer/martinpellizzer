Zygopetalum laminatum

Zygopetalum laminatum, commonly known as the wax plant, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers with a unique lip that is often curled or folded. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Zygopetalum laminatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
