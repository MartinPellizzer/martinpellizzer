Zingiber malaysianum

Zingiber malaysianum, commonly known as the Malaysian ginger or Malayan ginger, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a strong, spicy aroma.

Zingiber malaysianum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in various herbal remedies.
