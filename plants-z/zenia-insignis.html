Zenia insignis

Zenia insignis, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Zenia insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its hardy nature and visual impact.
