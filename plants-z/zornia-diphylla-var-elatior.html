Zornia diphylla var. elatior

Zornia diphylla var. elatior, commonly known as the tall zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Zornia diphylla var. elatior is often found in open woods, fields, and along roadsides.
