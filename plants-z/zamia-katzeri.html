Zamia katzeri

Zamia katzeri, commonly known as the Katzer's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a short, stout trunk and large, feather-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its reproductive structures include a cone-like structure that produces both male and female spores.
