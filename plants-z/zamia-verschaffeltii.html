Zamia verschaffeltii

Zamia verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Verschaffelt's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

Zamia verschaffeltii is dioecious, meaning it has separate male and female plants, and it produces cones rather than flowers.
