Zygophyllum laeve

Zygophyllum laeve, commonly known as the smooth zygophyllum, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Zygophyllum laeve plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
