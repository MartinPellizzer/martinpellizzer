Ziziphus adelensis

Ziziphus adelensis, commonly known as the Adelaide thornapple, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems, which are often thorny, and its compound leaves that are typically alternate. Ziziphus adelensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in dry climates.
