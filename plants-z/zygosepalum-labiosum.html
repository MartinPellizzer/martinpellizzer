Zygosepalum labiosum

Zygosepalum labiosum, commonly known as the pink-flowered zygosepalum, is a unique orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lip-like petals that display a vibrant pink hue. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are small but highly ornamental, often appearing in clusters along the stems.

Zygosepalum labiosum is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
