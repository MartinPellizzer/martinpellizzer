Zoysia matrella var. tenuifolia

Zoysia matrella var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender zoysia, is a low-growing, mat-forming grass native to Asia.

It has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a light to medium green color. The plant is characterized by its dense, fine-textured growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Zoysia matrella var. tenuifolia is known for its durability and ability to withstand foot traffic, making it suitable for lawns and ground covers.
