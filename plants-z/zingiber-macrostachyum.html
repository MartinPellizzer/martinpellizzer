Zingiber macrostachyum

Zingiber macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant ginger or wild ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, orange-red inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. Its rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Zingiber macrostachyum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal properties.
