Zornia tetraphylla var. obovata

Zornia tetraphylla var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf zornia, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its obovate, hairy leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and along stream banks.

It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or through animal activity.
