Zantedeschia Tropicalis

Zantedeschia tropicalis is a striking plant known for its vibrant, arrow-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental beauty. This plant is commonly referred to as the calla lily, though it is botanically distinct from true lilies. It prefers well-draining soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
