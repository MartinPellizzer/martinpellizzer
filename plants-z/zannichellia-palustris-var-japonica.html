Zannichellia palustris var. japonica

Zannichellia palustris var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese water starwort, is a aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. The plant has slender, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface or hang submerged. It produces small, green flowers that are arranged in dense clusters.

This variety is often used in ornamental water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in various aquatic conditions.
