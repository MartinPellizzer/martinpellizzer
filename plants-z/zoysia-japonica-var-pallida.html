Zoysia japonica var. pallida

Zoysia japonica var. pallida, commonly known as the silver zoysia, is a low-growing, fine-textured grass native to Japan.

It features narrow, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant has a dense, carpet-like growth habit and is well-suited for warm climates. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires moderate water and fertilizer.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for lawns and ornamental landscapes.
