Zingiber reflexum

Zingiber reflexum, commonly known as the zig-zag ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its ornamental, strap-shaped leaves that curl and twist in a zig-zag pattern. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often arranged in clusters.

Zingiber reflexum is often cultivated for its unique foliage and is relatively easy to grow in warm, humid conditions.
