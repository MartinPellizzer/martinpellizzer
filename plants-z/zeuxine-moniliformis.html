Zeuxine moniliformis

Zeuxine moniliformis, commonly known as the bearded bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Zeuxine moniliformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
