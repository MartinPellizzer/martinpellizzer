Zanha africana

Zanha africana, commonly known as African zanha, is a tropical shrub native to several African countries.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures containing several seeds.
