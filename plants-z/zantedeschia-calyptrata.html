Zantedeschia calyptrata

Zantedeschia calyptrata, commonly known as the Calla Lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy. The plant produces a single, showy spathe that surrounds a cluster of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia calyptrata is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
