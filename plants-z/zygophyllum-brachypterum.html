Zygophyllum brachypterum

Zygophyllum brachypterum, commonly known as the narrow-winged sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
