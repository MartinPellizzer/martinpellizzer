Zygia palmana

Zygia palmana, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical rainforests. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are often pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip petal. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

Zygia palmana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
