Zexmenia podocephala

Zexmenia podocephala, commonly known as the red-flowered zexmenia, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and unique, elongated flower structure. The plant typically grows in dense, humid forests, where it thrives in the understory with partial shade. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
