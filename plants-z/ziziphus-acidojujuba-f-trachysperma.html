Ziziphus acidojujuba f. trachysperma

Ziziphus acidojujuba f. trachysperma, commonly known as the Chinese date or jujube, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and pinnate, and its yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically orange-red in color, with a sweet and tangy flavor. The fruits are rich in nutrients, including sugars, vitamins, and antioxidants, making them valuable both nutritionally and commercially.

This cultivar is prized for its high fruit quality and is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
