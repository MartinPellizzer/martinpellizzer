Zygopetalum × perrenoudii

Zygopetalum × perrenoudii, commonly known as the Perrenoud's zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture, while the inflorescence is a raceme of fragrant, butterfly-shaped blooms.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
