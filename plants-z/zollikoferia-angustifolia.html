Zollikoferia angustifolia

Zollikoferia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf zollikoferia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
