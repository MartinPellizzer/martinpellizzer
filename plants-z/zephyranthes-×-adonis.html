Zephyranthes × adonis

Zephyranthes × adonis, commonly known as the pink hurricane lily, is a hybrid flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces strap-shaped, evergreen leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Zephyranthes × adonis is also notable for its ability to naturalize and spread through bulbous propagation.
