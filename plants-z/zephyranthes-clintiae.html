Zephyranthes clintiae

Zephyranthes clintiae, commonly known as the rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a central bulb.

Zephyranthes clintiae is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care once established.
