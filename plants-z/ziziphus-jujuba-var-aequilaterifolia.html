Ziziphus jujuba var. aequilaterifolia

Ziziphus jujuba var. aequilaterifolia, commonly known as the Chinese date or jujube, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets that are arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into edible, reddish-brown fruits. The leaves are typically alternate and have a smooth surface with a slightly serrated margin.

This variety is valued for its fruit, which is rich in nutrients and has a sweet, tangy flavor.
