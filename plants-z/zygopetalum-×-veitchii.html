Zygopetalum × veitchii

Zygopetalum × veitchii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Zygopetalum orchid species.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually dark green, strap-shaped, and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Zygopetalum × veitchii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation under proper care.
