Zanthoxylum Kleinii

Zanthoxylum kleinii is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its aromatic and often spiny characteristics. This species is notable for its distinctive appearance, featuring bright red berries and compound leaves. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered an important subject for botanical research.
