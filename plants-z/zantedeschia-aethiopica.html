Zantedeschia aethiopica

Zantedeschia aethiopica, commonly known as the calla lily, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It features a tall, sword-shaped leaf and a showy, white or colored spathe that encloses a cluster of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia aethiopica is also known for its ability to thrive in wet conditions, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
