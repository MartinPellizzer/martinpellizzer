Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp. rigida

Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp.

rigida, commonly known as rigid betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features upright stems covered in dense, hairy foliage, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a serrated margin.

The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats. Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp.

rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
