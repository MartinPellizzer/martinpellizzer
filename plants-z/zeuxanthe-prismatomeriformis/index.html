Zeuxanthe Prismatomeriformis

Prismatomeris coral, scientifically known as Zeuxanthe prismatomeriformis, is a fascinating species of coral that thrives in tropical marine environments. This coral is notable for its vibrant coloration and unique branching structure, which makes it a popular choice among reef aquarium enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is also an important component of coral reef ecosystems, supporting a diverse array of marine life. Its ability to form symbiotic relationships with zooxanthellae algae plays a crucial role in its survival and growth. However, like many corals, it faces significant threats from climate change and ocean acidification.
