Ziziphus lotus

Ziziphus lotus, commonly known as the desert date or sacred fig, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in harsh, dry environments, making it highly drought-resistant.
