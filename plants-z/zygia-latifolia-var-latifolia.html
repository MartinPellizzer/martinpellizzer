Zygia latifolia var. latifolia

Zygia latifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved zygia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Zygia latifolia var. latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
