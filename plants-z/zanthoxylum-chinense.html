Zanthoxylum chinense

Zanthoxylum chinense, commonly known as Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible but have a spicy, citrusy flavor. Zanthoxylum chinense is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in horticulture and herbal practices.
