Zygopetalum bolivianum

Zygopetalum bolivianum, commonly known as the Bolivian zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique zygomatic structure. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the Andes region of Bolivia. Its flowers display a range of colors, including pink, white, and violet, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Zygopetalum bolivianum is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitat.
