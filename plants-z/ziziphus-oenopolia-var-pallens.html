Ziziphus oenopolia var. pallens

Ziziphus oenopolia var. pallens, commonly known as the white-fruited date palm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into fleshy, sweet fruits resembling dates. Its fruit is edible and has been traditionally used for food and medicinal purposes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
