Zygia coccinea var. oriunda

Zygia coccinea var. oriunda, commonly known as the red-flowered zygia, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blossoms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Zygia coccinea var. oriunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
