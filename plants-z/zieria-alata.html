Zieria alata

Zieria alata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zieria, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its compact growth habit. Zieria alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
