Zingiber yingjiangense

Zingiber yingjiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan ginger or Yingjiang ginger, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. Its inflorescence consists of colorful, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
