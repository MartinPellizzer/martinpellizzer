Zygogynum tieghemii subsp. thulium

Zygogynum tieghemii subsp.

thulium, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a member of the Fabaceae family. It is a climbing shrub characterized by its pinnate leaves and small, yellowish flowers.

The plant produces pods that contain small, round seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, typically in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and medicinal contexts.
