Zeuxine thanmoiensis

Zeuxine thanmoiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Zeuxine thanmoiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
