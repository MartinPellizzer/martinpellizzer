Zyrphelis ecklonis subsp. ecklonis

Zyrphelis ecklonis subsp.

ecklonis, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It features erect, branched stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers.

The leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
