Zygostates multiflora

Zygostates multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered zygostates, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, providing year-round visual interest.

Zygostates multiflora prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
