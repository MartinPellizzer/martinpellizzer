Zygophyllum giessii

Zygophyllum giessii, commonly known as the desert sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water. The plant produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the spring. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep groundwater in dry soils.

Zygophyllum giessii plays an important role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
