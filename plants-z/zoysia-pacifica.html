Zoysia pacifica

Zoysia pacifica, commonly known as the Pacific zoysia, is a warm-season grass native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine texture, dense growth habit, and ability to form a lush, green lawn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to tolerate salt spray, making it suitable for coastal landscapes.

Zoysia pacifica is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and durability.
