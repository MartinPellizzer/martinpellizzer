Zingiber ensal

Zingiber ensal, commonly known as the ginger lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Zingiber ensal is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
