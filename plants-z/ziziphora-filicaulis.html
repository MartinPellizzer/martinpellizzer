Ziziphora filicaulis

Ziziphora filicaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Ziziphora filicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
