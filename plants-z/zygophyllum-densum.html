Zygophyllum densum

Zygophyllum densum, commonly known as the denseleaf mistletoe, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Zygophyllaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found as a shrub or subshrub, adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

Zygophyllum densum plays a role in local ecosystems by providing shelter and food for various insects and small animals.
