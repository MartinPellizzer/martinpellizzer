Zingiber medium

Zingiber medium, commonly known as the lesser ginger, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Zingiber medium is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
