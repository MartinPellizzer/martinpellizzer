Zygogynum comptonii var. angustifolium

Zygogynum comptonii var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved zygogynum, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically narrow and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
