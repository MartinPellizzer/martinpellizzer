Zanthoxylum bungeanum var. punctatum

Zanthoxylum bungeanum var. punctatum, commonly known as Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to red, fleshy berries in the autumn. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

The plant is also valued in ornamental horticulture for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature.
