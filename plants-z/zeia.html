Zeia

Zeia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant has a unique ability to purify water and provide habitat for various aquatic life forms. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Zeia plays a significant role in many cultural and spiritual practices across different regions.
