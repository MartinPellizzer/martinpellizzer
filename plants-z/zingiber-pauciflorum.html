Zingiber pauciflorum

Zingiber pauciflorum, commonly known as the rare ginger orchid, is a unique member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. This plant is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Zingiber pauciflorum is considered a rare and endangered species.
