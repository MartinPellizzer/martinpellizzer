Zuckia brandegeei var. plummeri

Zuckia brandegeei var. plummeri, commonly known as the red sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
