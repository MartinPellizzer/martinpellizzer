Zingiber tuanjuum

Zingiber tuanjuum, commonly known as the Tuanjuum ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomes that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers are typically yellow or orange in color.

Zingiber tuanjuum is valued not only for its aromatic rhizomes but also for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
