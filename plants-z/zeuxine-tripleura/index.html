Zeuxine Tripleura

Zeuxine tripleura is a striking member of the Ranunculaceae family known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and botanists alike. The plant's delicate petals and contrasting colors add visual interest to gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, Zeuxine tripleura remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
