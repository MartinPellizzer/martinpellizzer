Zephyranthes gracilifolia var. bijou

Zephyranthes gracilifolia var. bijou, commonly known as the miniature rain lily, is a compact, bulbous plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is well-suited for container gardening due to its small size and ease of care. It blooms in response to rainfall, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
