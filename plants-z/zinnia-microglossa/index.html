Zinnia Microglossa

Mexican zinnia, scientifically known as Zinnia microglossa, is a vibrant and resilient flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico. This species is celebrated for its bright, showy blooms that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a favorite among gardeners and florists alike. Thriving in full sun and well-drained soil, it is a hardy plant that requires minimal water and care. Its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies adds ecological value to any garden. With its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, Mexican zinnia is a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological landscaping.
