Zea mucronata

Zea mucronata, commonly known as the dwarf corn or small corn, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, a common trait among grasses.

Zea mucronata is native to South America and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
