Zaluzianskya bolusii

Zaluzianskya bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
