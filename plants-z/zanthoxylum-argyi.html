Zanthoxylum argyi

Zanthoxylum argyi, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate and have serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

The bark and fruits contain compounds that have been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
