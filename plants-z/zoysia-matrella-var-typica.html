Zoysia matrella var. typica

Zoysia matrella var. typica, commonly known as Japanese lawn grass, is a warm-season grass native to East Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a dark green color and a dense growth habit. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous and stoloniferous growth, allowing it to spread and form a thick, durable turf. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for lawns and ornamental use.

Zoysia matrella var. typica is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to maintain an attractive appearance with minimal maintenance.
