Ziziphora vichodceviana

Ziziphora vichodceviana, commonly known as the Vichodcev's ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has small, narrow leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are pale blue to purple and are arranged in whorls along the stem.

Ziziphora vichodceviana is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory effects.
