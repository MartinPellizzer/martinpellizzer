Zygopetalum burtii var. wallisii

Zygopetalum burtii var. wallisii, commonly known as the Wallisii zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
