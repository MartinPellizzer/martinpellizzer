Zerna Variegata

Zerna variegata is a rare and striking plant known for its unique, colorful foliage. It thrives in specific, often remote environments, making it a subject of both botanical interest and conservation concern. Its leaves display a mesmerizing blend of green, yellow, and white patterns that shift with light and temperature. Despite its beauty, Zerna variegata is highly sensitive to environmental changes, posing challenges for cultivation and preservation. This plant serves as a symbol of nature's delicate balance and the importance of protecting such fragile ecosystems.
