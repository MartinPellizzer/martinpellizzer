Zygopetalum mosenianum

Zygopetalum mosenianum, commonly known as the Mosenian zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that typically feature a unique lip or labellum, which is a distinguishing feature of many orchid species. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Zygopetalum mosenianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
