Zornia decussata

Zornia decussata, commonly known as the common zornia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. Its seed pods are cylindrical and dehiscent, releasing seeds when mature.

Zornia decussata is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions.
