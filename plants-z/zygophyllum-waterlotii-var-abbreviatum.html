Zygophyllum waterlotii var. abbreviatum

Zygophyllum waterlotii var. abbreviatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed desert shrub, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its structure allows it to store water efficiently, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.

It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for small desert animals.
