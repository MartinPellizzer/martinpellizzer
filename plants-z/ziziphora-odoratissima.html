Ziziphora odoratissima

Ziziphora odoratissima, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, aromatic seeds that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or dry gardens.

Ziziphora odoratissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery and medicine.
