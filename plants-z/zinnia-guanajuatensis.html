Zinnia guanajuatensis

Zinnia guanajuatensis, commonly known as the Guanajuato zinnia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

Zinnia guanajuatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
