Zornia paniculata

Zornia paniculata, commonly known as the panicled zornia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and racemose inflorescence. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Zornia paniculata is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides.

It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 1 meter in height, with a fibrous root system.
