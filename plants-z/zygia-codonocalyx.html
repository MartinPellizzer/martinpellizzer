Zygia codonocalyx

Zygia codonocalyx, commonly known as the red-flowered zygia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing plant. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
