Zephyranthes brazosensis var. chlorosolen

It is characterized by its bright green petals and a yellowish-white flower spike, which distinguish it from other Zephyranthes species. Native to the wetlands of Texas, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its unique coloration is due to the presence of chlorophyll in the petals, a trait uncommon in most orchids.
