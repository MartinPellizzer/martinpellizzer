Zygia latifolia var. communis

Zygia latifolia var. communis, commonly known as the broad-leaf zygia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Zygia latifolia var. communis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
