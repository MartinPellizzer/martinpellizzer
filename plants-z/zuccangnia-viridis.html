Zuccangnia viridis

Zuccangnia viridis, commonly known as the green leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil efficiently.

Zuccangnia viridis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
