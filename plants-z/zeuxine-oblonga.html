Zeuxine oblonga

Zeuxine oblonga, commonly known as the slender zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Zeuxine oblonga is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
