Zygopetalum × ballii

Zygopetalum × ballii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cultivated variety resulting from the cross between Zygopetalum orchids.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Zygopetalum × ballii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal blooming.
