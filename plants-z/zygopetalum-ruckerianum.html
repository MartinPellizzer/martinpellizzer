Zygopetalum ruckerianum

Zygopetalum ruckerianum, commonly known as the Rucker's zygopetalum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soil.

Zygopetalum ruckerianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
