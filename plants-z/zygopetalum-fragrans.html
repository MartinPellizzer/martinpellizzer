Zygopetalum fragrans

Zygopetalum fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Zygopetalum fragrans thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
