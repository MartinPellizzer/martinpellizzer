Zygophyllum Rosowii Var. Latifolium

Zygophyllum rosowii var. latifolium is a hardy desert plant known for its unique adaptations to arid environments. It thrives in regions with minimal rainfall and extreme temperature fluctuations. The plant's broad leaves help reduce water loss while maximizing sunlight absorption. Its striking pink or red flowers bloom during brief periods of moisture. This resilient species plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and supporting local wildlife.
