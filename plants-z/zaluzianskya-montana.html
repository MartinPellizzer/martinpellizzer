Zaluzianskya montana

Zaluzianskya montana, commonly known as the mountain zonal geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Zaluzianskya montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
