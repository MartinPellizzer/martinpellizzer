Zeuxine tenuifolia

Zeuxine tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf zeuxine, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and is often found in meadows and forest edges.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
