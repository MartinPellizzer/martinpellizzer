Ziziphora granatensis

Ziziphora granatensis, commonly known as the granate ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and forest-steppe regions. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.

Ziziphora granatensis is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
