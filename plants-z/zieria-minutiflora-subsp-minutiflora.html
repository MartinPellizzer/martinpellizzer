Zieria minutiflora subsp. minutiflora

Zieria minutiflora subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf zieria, is a small shrub native to the south-western region of Western Australia. It typically grows in heathland and dry forest environments, preferring well-drained sandy soils.

The plant features small, narrow leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small and white or pale green, often clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic plants.
