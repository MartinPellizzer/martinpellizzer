Zanthoxylum melanostictum

Zanthoxylum melanostictum, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. Its bark and leaves contain compounds with medicinal properties, making it valuable in herbal remedies.

Zanthoxylum melanostictum thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.
