Zeuxine arisanensis

Zeuxine arisanensis, commonly known as the Japanese zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Zeuxine arisanensis is known for its unique inflorescence, which consists of a spiral arrangement of flowers.
