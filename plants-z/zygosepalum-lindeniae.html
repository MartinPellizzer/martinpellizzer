Zygosepalum lindeniae

Zygosepalum lindeniae, commonly known as the Lindena's zygosepalum, is a rare orchid species native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a few leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its unique floral structure includes a fused sepal that forms a distinctive hood-like shape, which is a key botanical feature.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered and is often found in protected areas.
