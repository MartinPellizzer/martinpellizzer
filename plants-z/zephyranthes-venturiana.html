Zephyranthes venturiana

Zephyranthes venturiana, commonly known as the rain lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade. Its blooming period typically occurs after rainfall, hence its common name.

The flowers are short-lived but often appear in clusters, making it a striking addition to gardens.
