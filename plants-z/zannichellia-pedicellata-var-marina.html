Zannichellia pedicellata var. marina

Zannichellia pedicellata var. marina, commonly known as sea onion, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It thrives in brackish or saltwater environments, making it an important species in estuarine ecosystems.
