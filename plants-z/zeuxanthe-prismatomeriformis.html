Zeuxanthe prismatomeriformis

Zeuxanthe prismatomeriformis, commonly known as the purple-tipped staghorn coral, is a species of zoantharian coral that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It forms dense colonies of polyps with a distinctive, branching morphology resembling staghorn structures. The coral's coloration often includes shades of purple and yellow, which can vary depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in shallow tropical waters, typically in regions with warm, clear waters and ample sunlight.
