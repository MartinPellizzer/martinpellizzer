Ziziphora pungens

Ziziphora pungens, commonly known as the prickly germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a slightly fuzzy texture. The flowers are small, tubular, and typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
