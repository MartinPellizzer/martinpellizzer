Zollernia tango

Zollernia tango, commonly known as the Tango Zollernia, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors, often resembling the hues of a sunset. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its visual appeal.
