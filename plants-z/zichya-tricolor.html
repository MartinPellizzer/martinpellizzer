Zichya tricolor

Zichya tricolor, commonly known as the three-color orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored petals that typically display shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture. Zichya tricolor thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
