Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp. clinopodioides

Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp.

clinopodioides, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties have made it notable in traditional herbal practices.
