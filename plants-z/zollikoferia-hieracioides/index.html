Zollikoferia Hieracioides

Zollikoferia hieracioides is a unique plant known for its striking appearance and hardy nature. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to southern Africa. This plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements. Its resilience makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
