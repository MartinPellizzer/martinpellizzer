Zanonia indica subsp. indica

Zanonia indica subsp.

indica, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family. It is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

indica is used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
