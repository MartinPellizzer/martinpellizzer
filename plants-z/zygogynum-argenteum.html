Zygogynum argenteum

Zygogynum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, waxy hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Zygogynum argenteum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
