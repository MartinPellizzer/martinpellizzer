Zieria prostrata

Zieria prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate zieria, is a low-growing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

Zieria prostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
