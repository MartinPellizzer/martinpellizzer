Zanthoxylum setulosum

Zanthoxylum setulosum, commonly known as the prickly ash or white prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

Zanthoxylum setulosum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and streamside habitats.
