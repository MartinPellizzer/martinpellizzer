Zehneria heterophylla

Zehneria heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf zehneria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves that can be either entire or lobed depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowers are small, white to pale blue, and clustered in loose inflorescences.

Zehneria heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
