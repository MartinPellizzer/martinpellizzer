Ziziphora tomentosa

Ziziphora tomentosa, commonly known as woolly ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and aromatic, small blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are erect and branched.

Ziziphora tomentosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
