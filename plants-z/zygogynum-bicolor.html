Zygogynum bicolor

Zygogynum bicolor, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, tuberous roots and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with heart-shaped leaves that are often velvety in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

Zygogynum bicolor is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
