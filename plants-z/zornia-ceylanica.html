Zornia ceylanica

Zornia ceylanica, commonly known as Ceylon zornia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including Sri Lanka, India, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its seed pods are typically slender and dehiscent, releasing seeds upon maturity.

Zornia ceylanica is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
