Zephyranthes tubiflora

Zephyranthes tubiflora, commonly known as the rain lily, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often blooming after rainfall. Its leaves are strap-like and grassy, and it is known for its rapid growth and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.

Zephyranthes tubiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
