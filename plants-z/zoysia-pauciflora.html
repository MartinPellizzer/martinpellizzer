Zoysia pauciflora

Zoysia pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered zoysia, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, medium-green blades and forms dense, low-growing turf. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Zoysia pauciflora is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and durability.
