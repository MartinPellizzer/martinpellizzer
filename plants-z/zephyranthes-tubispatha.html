Zephyranthes tubispatha

Zephyranthes tubispatha, commonly known as the rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a bulbous plant that produces showy, white or pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Zephyranthes tubispatha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
