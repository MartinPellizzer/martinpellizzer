Zannichellia aschersoniana

Zannichellia aschersoniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water plantain, is a submerged aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in Europe and Asia.

It has long, narrow leaves that are typically ribbon-like in shape, adapted for underwater growth. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in still or slow-moving waters such as ponds and lakes.
