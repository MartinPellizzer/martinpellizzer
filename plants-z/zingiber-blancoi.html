Zingiber blancoi

Zingiber blancoi, commonly known as the Blanco's ginger, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, fragrant inflorescences with yellow and white bracts. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy texture.

Zingiber blancoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
