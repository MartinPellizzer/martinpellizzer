Ziziphus jujuba var. stenocarpa

Ziziphus jujuba var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese date, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It produces small, sweet, edible fruits that are similar to dates but typically smaller and more tart. The plant is characterized by its thorny branches, alternate leaves, and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid climates, making it well-suited for drought-resistant landscaping.

The fruit is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary applications.
