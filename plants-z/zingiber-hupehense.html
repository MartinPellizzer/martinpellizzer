Zingiber hupehense

Zingiber hupehense, commonly known as the Hupeh ginger or Chinese ginger, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, leafy stems with clusters of fragrant, pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often reaching up to 30 cm in length.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
