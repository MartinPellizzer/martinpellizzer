Zinnia darwinii

Zinnia darwinii, commonly known as Darwin's zinnia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy blooms. The plant typically produces daisy-like flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Zinnia darwinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
