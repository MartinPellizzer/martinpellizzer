Zephyranthes hassleriana

Zephyranthes hassleriana, commonly known as the rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

Zephyranthes hassleriana is noted for its rapid growth and ability to bloom within a few weeks of rainfall.
