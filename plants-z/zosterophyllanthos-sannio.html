Zosterophyllanthos sannio

Zosterophyllanthos sannio, commonly known as the Sannio zosterophyll, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the group of stoneworts, which are among the earliest land plants.

The plant thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in nutrient-rich, shallow waters. Its morphology reflects adaptations to aquatic life, with specialized cells for gas exchange and light absorption.
