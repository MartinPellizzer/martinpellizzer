Zygopetalum × gottianum

Zygopetalum × gottianum, commonly known as the Gottian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a mix of pink, red, and white hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its flowers are borne on long, arching inflorescences, and it requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Zygopetalum × gottianum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
