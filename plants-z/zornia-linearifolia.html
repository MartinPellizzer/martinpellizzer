Zornia linearifolia

Zornia linearifolia, commonly known as slender zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, trifoliate leaves.
