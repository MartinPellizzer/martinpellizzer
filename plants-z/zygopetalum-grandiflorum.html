Zygopetalum grandiflorum

Zygopetalum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a combination of pink, white, and purple hues. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush, green backdrop to its striking blooms.

Zygopetalum grandiflorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
