Zanthoxylum leprieurii

Zanthoxylum leprieurii, commonly known as the black prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces red berries that are edible for some wildlife but contain seeds that are dispersed by animals.
