Zanthoxylum rigidum subsp. hasslerianum

Zanthoxylum rigidum subsp.

hasslerianum, commonly known as the Hassler's prickly ash, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States. It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swamps and bottomlands.
