Zoysia × forbesiana

Zoysia × forbesiana, commonly known as the hybrid zoysia, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, dense foliage and a mat-forming growth habit, making it suitable for lawns and ornamental landscapes. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Zoysia × forbesiana is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to form a lush, green turf with minimal maintenance.
