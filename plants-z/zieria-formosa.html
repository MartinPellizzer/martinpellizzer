Zieria formosa

Zieria formosa, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a small evergreen shrub native to the wet tropical rainforests of northeastern Australia.

It features dense, glossy green foliage and produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, moist environments. Zieria formosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
