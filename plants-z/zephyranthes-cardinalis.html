Zephyranthes cardinalis

Zephyranthes cardinalis, commonly known as the red-flowered rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink to red in color, blooming in late summer and early fall.
