Zoysia pungens

Zoysia pungens, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, feathery seed heads. It is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

Zoysia pungens is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
