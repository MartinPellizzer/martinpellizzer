Zygopetalum micranthum

Zygopetalum micranthum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Zygopetalum micranthum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
