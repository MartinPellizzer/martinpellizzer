Zingiber flammeum

Zingiber flammeum, commonly known as the flame ginger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant features lush, dark green, sword-shaped leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Zingiber flammeum is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical settings.
