Zoysia liukiuensis

Zoysia liukiuensis, commonly known as the Ryukyu zoysia, is a perennial grass species native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, dense foliage and a creeping growth habit, forming a dense mat that is well-suited for turf applications. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically light green in color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Zoysia liukiuensis is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade conditions.

It is often used in landscaping and as a groundcover due to its aesthetic appeal and durability.
