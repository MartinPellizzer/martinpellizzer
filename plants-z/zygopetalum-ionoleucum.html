Zygopetalum ionoleucum

Zygopetalum ionoleucum, commonly known as the white zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Zygopetalum ionoleucum prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialized orchid collections.
