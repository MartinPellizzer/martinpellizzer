Zanthoxylum nashii

Zanthoxylum nashii, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually pinnate with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to red, fleshy berries in the fall. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.

Zanthoxylum nashii is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
