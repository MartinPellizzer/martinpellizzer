Ziziphus acidojujuba f. granulata

Ziziphus acidojujuba f. granulata, commonly known as the Chinese date or sand date, is a cultivated variant of the Ziziphus acidojujuba species.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits that are typically covered in a granular, rough texture. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates. Its fruits are edible, sweet, and often used in traditional Chinese medicine and culinary applications.

The botanical features of this variety include its thorny stems, compound leaves, and the distinctive appearance of its fruit.
