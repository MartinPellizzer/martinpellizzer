Zanthoxylum yunnanense

Zanthoxylum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Zanthoxylum yunnanense is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
