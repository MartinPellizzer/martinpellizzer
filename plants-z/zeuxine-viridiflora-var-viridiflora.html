Zeuxine viridiflora var. viridiflora

Zeuxine viridiflora var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered zeuxine, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
