Zanthoxylum bungeanum

Zanthoxylum bungeanum, commonly known as Sichuan pepper, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually pinnate with 5 to 9 leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. Its fruit is widely used in culinary applications, particularly in Chinese cuisine, for its distinctive numbing flavor.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
