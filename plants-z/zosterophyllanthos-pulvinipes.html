Zosterophyllanthos pulvinipes

Zosterophyllanthos pulvinipes, commonly known as the cushion zosterophyllum, is a unique plant with distinctive botanical features.

It belongs to the family Zosterophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form. The plant exhibits specialized structures called pulvini, which are swollen nodes at the base of its stems, aiding in its upright growth. Its leaves are typically small, scale-like, and arranged in dense clusters, contributing to its overall appearance.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments, where it thrives in humid and cool conditions.
