Zannichellia intermedia

Zannichellia intermedia, commonly known as the broad-leaved water starwort, is a aquatic plant that thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams.

It features elongated, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, with smaller, submerged leaves that aid in photosynthesis. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically borne on short stalks above the water. Its root system helps stabilize sediment and improve water quality by absorbing nutrients.

Zannichellia intermedia is an important species for aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
