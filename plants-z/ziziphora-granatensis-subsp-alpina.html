Ziziphora granatensis subsp. alpina

Ziziphora granatensis subsp.

alpina, commonly known as alpine ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes, often in alpine meadows and grasslands.

The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and produces small, pinkish to purple flowers in late summer. Its fibrous root system helps stabilize soil in harsh environments. Ziziphora granatensis subsp.

alpina is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
