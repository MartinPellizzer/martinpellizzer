Zehneria minutiflora

Zehneria minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf zehneria, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with small, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces tiny, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Zehneria minutiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in shaded garden settings due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
