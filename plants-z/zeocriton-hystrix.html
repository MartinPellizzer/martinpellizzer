Zeocriton hystrix

Zeocriton hystrix, commonly known as the spiny cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in sharp, spine-like structures that provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the dry season, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and enabling survival in harsh desert conditions.

Zeocriton hystrix is well adapted to extreme temperatures and low precipitation, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
