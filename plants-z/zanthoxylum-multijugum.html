Zanthoxylum multijugum

Zanthoxylum multijugum, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. Its bark and fruits contain compounds used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Zanthoxylum multijugum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
