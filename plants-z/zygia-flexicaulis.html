Zygia flexicaulis

Zygia flexicaulis, commonly known as the creeping zygia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible or medicinal properties. Zygia flexicaulis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.
