Zygopetalum maxillare f. sanderianum

Zygopetalum maxillare f. sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or red color with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often arranged in two rows along the stem.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific temperature and humidity requirements.
