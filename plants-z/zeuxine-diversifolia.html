Zeuxine diversifolia

Zeuxine diversifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf zeuxine, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes buttercups and other flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
