Zeuxine commelinoides

Zeuxine commelinoides, commonly known as the zigzag grass or zigzag plant, is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the family Commelinaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, lanceolate leaves that often exhibit a zigzag pattern along the stem. The plant produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. Zeuxine commelinoides is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
