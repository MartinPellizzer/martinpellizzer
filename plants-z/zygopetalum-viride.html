Zygopetalum viride

Zygopetalum viride, commonly known as the Emerald Phalaenopsis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green foliage and long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of white or pale green flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Zygopetalum viride is also valued for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year under proper conditions.
