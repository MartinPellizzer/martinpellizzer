Zingiber meleguetta

Zingiber meleguetta, commonly known as the ginger lily, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like true ginger and turmeric. The plant features ornamental, sword-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Zingiber meleguetta is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a cut flower.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
