Zygopetalum brachypetalum var. stenopetalum

Zygopetalum brachypetalum var. stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful blossoms with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
