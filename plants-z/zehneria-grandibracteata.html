Zehneria grandibracteata

Zehneria grandibracteata, commonly known as the giant bract zehneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy bracts that resemble petals, often displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, thriving in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its foliage consists of broad, glossy leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Zehneria grandibracteata is valued in horticulture for its dramatic floral display and adaptability to controlled indoor growing conditions.
