Zingiber isanense

Zingiber isanense, commonly known as the Isan ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important spices like ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly serrated edge.

Zingiber isanense is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
