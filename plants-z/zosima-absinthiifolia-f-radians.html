Zosima absinthiifolia f. radians

Zosima absinthiifolia f. radians, commonly known as the radiata form of the absinthiifolia variety, is a rare and distinctive botanical variant.

It is characterized by its dense, radiating foliage that gives it a unique appearance. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with multiple branches emerging from a central stem. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a silvery-gray underside.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialized horticultural collections.
