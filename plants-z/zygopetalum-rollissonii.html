Zygopetalum rollissonii

Zygopetalum rollissonii, commonly known as the Rollisson's zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive color pattern. The flowers typically feature a white or pale pink lip, contrasting with the darker petals and sepals. This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
