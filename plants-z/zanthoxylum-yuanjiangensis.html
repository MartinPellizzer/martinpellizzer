Zanthoxylum yuanjiangensis

Zanthoxylum yuanjiangensis, commonly known as the Yuanjiang prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
