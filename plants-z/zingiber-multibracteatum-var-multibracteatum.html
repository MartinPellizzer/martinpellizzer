Zingiber multibracteatum var. multibracteatum

Zingiber multibracteatum var. multibracteatum, commonly known as the multibracteatum ginger, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a thick, rhizomatous stem that produces multiple, elongated leaves with a deep green color. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored bracts that range from red to orange. Its flowers are small and clustered, typical of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential use in traditional medicine.
