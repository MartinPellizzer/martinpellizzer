Zingiber porphyrosphaerum

Zingiber porphyrosphaerum, commonly known as the purple sphere ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink inflorescences, which are borne on tall, slender stems. The plant produces small, ornamental flowers arranged in a spherical cluster, giving it its common name. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface.

While not widely cultivated for culinary use, it is valued in horticulture for its unique and showy appearance.
