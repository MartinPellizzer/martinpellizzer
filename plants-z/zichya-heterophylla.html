Zichya heterophylla

Zichya heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf orchid, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant exhibits morphological variation in leaf shape and size, which is a notable botanical feature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability to various microhabitats make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
