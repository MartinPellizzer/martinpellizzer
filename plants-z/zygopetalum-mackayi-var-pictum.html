Zygopetalum mackayi var. pictum

Zygopetalum mackayi var. pictum, commonly known as the painted zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flower spikes that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique coloration.
