Zygopetalum africanum

Zygopetalum africanum, commonly known as the African zygopetalum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, thriving on trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Zygopetalum africanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant blooms.
