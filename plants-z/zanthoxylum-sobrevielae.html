Zanthoxylum sobrevielae

Zanthoxylum sobrevielae, commonly known as the Sierra Madre prickly ash, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
