Zygocactus altensteinii

Zygocactus altensteinii, commonly known as the Altensteinii holiday cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems that are often covered in a waxy, greenish coating. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the winter months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Zygocactus altensteinii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
