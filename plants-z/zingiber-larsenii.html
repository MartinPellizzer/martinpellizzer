Zingiber larsenii

Zingiber larsenii, commonly known as Larsen's ginger, is a rare and lesser-known species within the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Myanmar and Thailand. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a yellow and red coloration.
