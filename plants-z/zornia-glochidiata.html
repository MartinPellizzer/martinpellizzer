Zornia glochidiata

Zornia glochidiata, commonly known as the prickly zornia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round, spiny fruits that contain seeds, and it thrives in dry, sandy soils.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to arid environments.
