Zerna gigantea

Zerna gigantea, commonly known as the giant zerna, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its towering, grass-like structure, which can reach heights of up to three meters. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, providing a distinctive visual contrast. Zerna gigantea is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in its native ecosystems.
