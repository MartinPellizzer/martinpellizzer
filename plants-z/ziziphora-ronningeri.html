Ziziphora ronningeri

Ziziphora ronningeri, commonly known as Ronning's hyssop, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Ziziphora ronningeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
