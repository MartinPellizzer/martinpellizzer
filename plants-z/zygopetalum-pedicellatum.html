Zygopetalum pedicellatum

Zygopetalum pedicellatum, commonly known as the pedicellate zygopetalum, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant orchid blooms. The plant features long, slender pedicels that support its showy, butterfly-shaped flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a compact rosette.

Zygopetalum pedicellatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
