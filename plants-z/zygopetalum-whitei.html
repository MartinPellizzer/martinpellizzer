Zygopetalum whitei

Zygopetalum whitei, commonly known as the White's zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually dark green and lance-shaped, providing a striking contrast to its delicate flowers.

Zygopetalum whitei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
