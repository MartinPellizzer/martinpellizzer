Zingiber subroseum

Zingiber subroseum, commonly known as the pink ginger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant features tall, erect stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant pink or red in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typically displaying a mix of white, yellow, and pink hues.

Zingiber subroseum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
