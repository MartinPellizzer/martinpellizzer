Zinnia elegans

Zinnia elegans, commonly known as the garden zinnia, is a popular annual flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, yellow, orange, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features a bushy, branching structure. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Zinnia elegans thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a favorite among gardeners for its ease of cultivation and long blooming period.
