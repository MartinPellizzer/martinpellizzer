Zerna ciliata

Zerna ciliata, commonly known as the silky grass or velvet grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves that give it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets. Zerna ciliata is often found in moist, temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
