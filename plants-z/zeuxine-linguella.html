Zeuxine linguella

Zeuxine linguella, commonly known as the narrowleaf zigzag root.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, delicate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pale blue or violet, arranged in clusters at the base of the plant.

Zeuxine linguella is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
