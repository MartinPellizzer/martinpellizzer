Zephyranthes robusta

Zephyranthes robusta, commonly known as the rain lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright sunlight. It is known for its ability to bloom after rainfall, hence its common name.

Zephyranthes robusta is relatively low maintenance and is popular in gardens for its ornamental value.
