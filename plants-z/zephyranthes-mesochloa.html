Zephyranthes mesochloa

Zephyranthes mesochloa, commonly known as the pink rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming during the rainy season. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically evergreen, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Zephyranthes mesochloa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
