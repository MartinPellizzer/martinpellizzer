Zephyranthes guatemalensis

Zephyranthes guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It blooms during the rainy season, typically from summer to fall, and is often found in moist, tropical environments.

Zephyranthes guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
