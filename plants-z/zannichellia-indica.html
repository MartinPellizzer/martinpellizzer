Zannichellia indica

Zannichellia indica, commonly known as the water starwort, is a submerged aquatic plant native to Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has long, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged spirally around the stem. It produces small, greenish flowers that are usually submerged, though some may emerge above water.
