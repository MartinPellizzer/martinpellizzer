Zeuxine subquadrata

Zeuxine subquadrata, commonly known as the quadrangular zeuxine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or white. The plant produces follicles as its fruit type, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
