Zygopetalum gramineum

Zygopetalum gramineum, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its grass-like leaves and striking, long-lasting flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of small, fragrant flowers.

Zygopetalum gramineum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
