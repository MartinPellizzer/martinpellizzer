Zephyranthes mataca

Zephyranthes mataca, commonly known as the Mataca rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically evergreen and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Zephyranthes mataca thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom reliably in moist conditions.
