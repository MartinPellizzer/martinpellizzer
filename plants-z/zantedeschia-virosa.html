Zantedeschia virosa

Zantedeschia virosa, commonly known as the giant calla lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces large, showy white or cream-colored spatulate flowers with a prominent yellow spathe. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Zantedeschia virosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
