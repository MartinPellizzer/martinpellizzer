Zephyranthes matogrossensis

Zephyranthes matogrossensis, commonly known as the Matogrossense rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Brazil.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant is adapted to wetland environments and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its flowering period is usually during the rainy season, making it a notable species in tropical ecosystems.

Zephyranthes matogrossensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
