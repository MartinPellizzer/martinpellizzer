Zingiber orbiculatum

Zingiber orbiculatum, commonly known as the globe ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae, and is characterized by its ornamental, round-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its rhizomes are not typically used for culinary purposes but are appreciated for their aesthetic value in gardens.

Zingiber orbiculatum thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
