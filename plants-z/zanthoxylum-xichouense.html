Zanthoxylum xichouense

Zanthoxylum xichouense, commonly known as the Xichou prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.

Zanthoxylum xichouense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
