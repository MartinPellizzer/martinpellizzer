Zygocactus truncatus

Zygocactus truncatus, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored and covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, orange, and white, typically blooming around Christmas time. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Zygocactus truncatus is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
