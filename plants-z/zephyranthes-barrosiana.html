Zephyranthes barrosiana

Zephyranthes barrosiana, commonly known as the rain lily, is a bulbous plant native to the Americas.

It produces showy, white or pink flowers that bloom after rainfall, often in the early morning. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically green and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Zephyranthes barrosiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom quickly after rain.
