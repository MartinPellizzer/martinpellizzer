Zephyranthes acuminata

Zephyranthes acuminata, commonly known as the rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial bulbous plant that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in response to rainfall. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are often evergreen.

Zephyranthes acuminata is well adapted to wet environments and is often found in marshy areas or near water sources.
