Zygopetalum sanderianum

Zygopetalum sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically feature a yellow lip and a contrasting purple or violet upper lip. The plant has long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Zygopetalum sanderianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
