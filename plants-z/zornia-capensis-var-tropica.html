Zornia capensis var. tropica

Zornia capensis var. tropica, commonly known as the tropical zornia, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry garden environments.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening and as a cover crop.
