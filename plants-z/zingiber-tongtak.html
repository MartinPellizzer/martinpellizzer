Zingiber tongtak

Zingiber tongtak, commonly known as the Tongtak ginger, is a tropical herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers like turmeric and cardamom. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically orange or yellow and are followed by small, round fruits.

The rhizomes are valued for their aromatic properties and are used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
