Zygopetalum quadratum

Zygopetalum quadratum, commonly known as the quadratum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces striking flowers with a unique quadrilateral shape, which gives it its scientific name. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Zygopetalum quadratum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
