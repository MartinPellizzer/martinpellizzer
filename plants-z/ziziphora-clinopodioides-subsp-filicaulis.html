Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp. filicaulis

Ziziphora clinopodioides subsp.

filicaulis, commonly known as the broad-leafed betony, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, narrow to broad leaves that are often hairy and have a mint-like scent.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
