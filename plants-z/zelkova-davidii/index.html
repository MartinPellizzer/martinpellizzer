Zelkova Davidii

Zelkova davidii is a rare and ancient tree species native to the mountainous regions of eastern Turkey. Known for its distinctive bark and elegant foliage, it has captivated botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species is often referred to as the "David's elm" due to its historical association with the botanist who first described it. Despite its beauty, Zelkova davidii faces significant threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensuring its survival for future generations.
