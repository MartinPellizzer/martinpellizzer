Zehneria perrieri var. parvula

Zehneria perrieri var. parvula, commonly known as the dwarf zehneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is appreciated by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
