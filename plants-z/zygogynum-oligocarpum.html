Zygogynum oligocarpum

Zygogynum oligocarpum, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in panicles, and it produces edible, star-shaped fruits.

The botanical features of Zygogynum oligocarpum make it well-suited for humid, tropical climates.
