Zygopetalum klugii

Zygopetalum klugii, commonly known as the Klug's zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically display a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Zygopetalum klugii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
