Zygotritonia atropurpurea

Zygotritonia atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple zygotritonia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vibrant purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique coloration, it is highly sought after by horticulturists and gardeners.
