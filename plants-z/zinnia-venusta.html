Zinnia venusta

Zinnia venusta, commonly known as the elegant zinnia, is a striking annual plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its botanical name reflects its ornamental value, with "venusta" meaning beautiful in Latin.

Zinnia venusta thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
