Zephyranthes stenopetala

Zephyranthes stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petalled rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant blooms after rainfall, often in the early morning, and is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
