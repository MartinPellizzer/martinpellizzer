Zygopetalum crinitum var. coeruleum

Zygopetalum crinitum var. coeruleum, commonly known as the blue-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features long, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
