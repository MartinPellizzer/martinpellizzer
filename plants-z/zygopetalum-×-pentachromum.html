Zygopetalum × pentachromum

Zygopetalum × pentachromum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Zygopetalum and Pentachromum species.

It is a tropical orchid characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that often display multiple colors in a single bloom. The plant typically has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, warm environments.

Zygopetalum × pentachromum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral displays.
