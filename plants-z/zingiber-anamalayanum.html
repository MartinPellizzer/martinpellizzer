Zingiber anamalayanum

Zingiber anamalayanum, commonly known as the Philippine ginger, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features rhizomatous stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are often yellow or orange in color.

Zingiber anamalayanum is valued for its aromatic rhizomes, which are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
