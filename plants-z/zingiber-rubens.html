Zingiber rubens

Zingiber rubens, commonly known as the red ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy green surface.

Zingiber rubens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
