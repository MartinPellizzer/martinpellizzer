Zygopetalum micropterum

Zygopetalum micropterum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette formation.

Zygopetalum micropterum thrives in cool, humid environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
