Ziziphus floribunda

Ziziphus floribunda, commonly known as the flowering ziziphus, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. The plant is adapted to drought conditions, with deep roots and waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Its fruit is a small, edible drupe that is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Ziziphus floribunda plays an important role in providing food and habitat for various desert wildlife.
