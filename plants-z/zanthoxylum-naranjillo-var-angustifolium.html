Zanthoxylum naranjillo var. angustifolium

Zanthoxylum naranjillo var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thorny stems are distinctive features that help identify the species in its natural habitat.
