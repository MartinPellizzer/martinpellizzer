Zingiber griffithii

Zingiber griffithii, commonly known as the Himalayan ginger, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and can have a strong, spicy aroma.

Zingiber griffithii is valued for both its ornamental beauty and potential medicinal properties.
