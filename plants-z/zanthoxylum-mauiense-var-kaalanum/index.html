Zanthoxylum Mauiense Var. Kaalanum

Zanthoxylum mauiense var. kaalanum is a rare and unique plant native to the Hawaiian island of Maui. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its aromatic and often spiny characteristics. This variant is distinguished by its distinctive foliage and floral structures, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it faces significant threats to its survival. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of Maui's ecosystems.
