Zingiber album

Zingiber album, commonly known as white ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other ginger species. The plant features tall, erect stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, with a strong, spicy aroma.

Zingiber album is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
