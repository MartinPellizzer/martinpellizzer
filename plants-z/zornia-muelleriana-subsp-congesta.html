Zornia muelleriana subsp. congesta

Zornia muelleriana subsp.

congesta, commonly known as the dense zornia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is native to regions of South America, particularly Argentina and Chile, where it thrives in dry, rocky habitats.
