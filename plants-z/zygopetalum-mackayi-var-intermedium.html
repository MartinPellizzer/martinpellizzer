Zygopetalum mackayi var. intermedium

Zygopetalum mackayi var. intermedium, commonly known as the Intermediate Zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically display a combination of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral development. Its leaves are strap-like, broad, and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique and vibrant floral display.
