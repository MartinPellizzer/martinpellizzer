Zigadenus douglasii

Zigadenus douglasii, commonly known as death bloom, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces toxic alkaloids, making all parts of it highly poisonous to humans and animals. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and sagebrush steppe.
