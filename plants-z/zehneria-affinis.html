Zehneria affinis

Zehneria affinis, commonly known as the silver sword, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of broad, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Zehneria affinis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and low maintenance requirements.
