Zygogynum tanyostigma

Zygogynum tanyostigma, commonly known as the African violet or violet leaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative ornamental. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and horticultural displays.
