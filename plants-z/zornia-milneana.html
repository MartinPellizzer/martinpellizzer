Zornia milneana

Zornia milneana, commonly known as the Hawaiian zornia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and rocky areas. It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
