Zygopetalum labiosum

Zygopetalum labiosum, commonly known as the lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers with a distinct lip that often has a contrasting color. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Zygopetalum labiosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms.
