Zephyranthes gameleirensis

Zephyranthes gameleirensis, commonly known as the gameleirensis rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny environments. Its leaves are strap-like and narrow, often emerging from a bulbous base.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.
