Ziziphus calophylla

Ziziphus calophylla, commonly known as the smooth leaf date bush, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with oval, dark green leaves that have a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, sweet, yellow fruits resembling dates. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Ziziphus calophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in horticulture and traditional medicine.
