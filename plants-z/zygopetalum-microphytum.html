Zygopetalum microphytum

Zygopetalum microphytum, commonly known as the miniature zygopetalum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact orchid with small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a short, upright stem with a few leaves and produces numerous tiny flowers clustered at the top. Its foliage is characterized by narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged closely around the stem.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific humidity and temperature requirements.
