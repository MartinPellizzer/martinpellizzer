Zingiber bradleyanum

Zingiber bradleyanum, commonly known as the Bradley ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers such as turmeric and cardamom. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

Zingiber bradleyanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
