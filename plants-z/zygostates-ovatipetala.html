Zygostates ovatipetala

Zygostates ovatipetala, commonly known as the broad-leafed zygostate, is a unique flowering plant native to the humid lowland forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Zygostataceae and is characterized by its ovate, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing near the forest floor.
