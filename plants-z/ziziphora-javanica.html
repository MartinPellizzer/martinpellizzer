Ziziphora javanica

Ziziphora javanica, commonly known as Java ziziphora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Ziziphora javanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
