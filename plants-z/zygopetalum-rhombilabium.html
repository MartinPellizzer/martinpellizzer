Zygopetalum rhombilabium

Zygopetalum rhombilabium, commonly known as the rhombic-labied zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically feature a velvety purple or violet coloration. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical and subtropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Zygopetalum rhombilabium thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal blooming.
