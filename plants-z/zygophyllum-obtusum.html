Zygophyllum obtusum

Zygophyllum obtusum, commonly known as the bluntleaf glassworts, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

Zygophyllum obtusum plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is an important part of the local flora in its native habitat.
