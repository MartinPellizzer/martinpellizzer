Zygosepalum

Zygosepalum, commonly known as the false orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing as epiphytes on trees or as terrestrial plants in moist environments. The flowers are usually small and showy, with a variety of colors, and they often have a sweet fragrance to attract pollinators.
