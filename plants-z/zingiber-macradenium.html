Zingiber macradenium

Zingiber macradenium, commonly known as the giant ginger or Macradenium ginger, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers and turmeric. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary spice.

Zingiber macradenium is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic qualities.
