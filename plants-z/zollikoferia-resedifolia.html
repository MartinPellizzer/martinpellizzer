Zollikoferia resedifolia

Zollikoferia resedifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic leaves with a distinctive licorice-like scent, and its yellow flowers are arranged in umbrella-like clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas. Its seeds are used in culinary applications, adding a subtle anise flavor to dishes.

Zollikoferia resedifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
