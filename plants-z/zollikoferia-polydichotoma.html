Zollikoferia polydichotoma

Zollikoferia polydichotoma, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a basal rosette of leaves and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its compact size and vibrant blooms.
