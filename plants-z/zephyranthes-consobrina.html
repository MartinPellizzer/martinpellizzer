Zephyranthes consobrina

Zephyranthes consobrina, commonly known as the rain lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the southern United States and Mexico.

The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically evergreen, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Zephyranthes consobrina is often found in open areas such as prairies and woodlands, and it is adapted to dry conditions.
