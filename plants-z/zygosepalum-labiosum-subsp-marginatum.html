Zygosepalum labiosum subsp. marginatum

Zygosepalum labiosum subsp.

marginatum, commonly known as the marginate zygosepalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers.

The plant features elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves, which are typically lance-shaped and covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes and are known for their intricate, spurred petals and vibrant hues.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
