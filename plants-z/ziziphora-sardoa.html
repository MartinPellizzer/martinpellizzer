Ziziphora sardoa

Ziziphora sardoa, commonly known as the Sardonic Ziziphora, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Ziziphora sardoa thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.

Its leaves are aromatic and have been used traditionally in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
