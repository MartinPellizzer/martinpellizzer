Zeuxine exilis

Zeuxine exilis, commonly known as the slender zigzag gentian, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.

Zeuxine exilis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
