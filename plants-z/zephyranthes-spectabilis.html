Zephyranthes spectabilis

Zephyranthes spectabilis, commonly known as the rain lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant typically grows from a bulbous rhizome and produces its blooms after rainfall, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Zephyranthes spectabilis is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
