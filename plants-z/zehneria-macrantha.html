Zehneria macrantha

Zehneria macrantha, commonly known as the giant zehneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, with a waxy, glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Zehneria macrantha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and unique foliage.
