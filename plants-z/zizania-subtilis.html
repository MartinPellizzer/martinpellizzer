Zizania subtilis

Zizania subtilis, commonly known as the slender wild rice, is a species of aquatic grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in shallow freshwater habitats, such as marshes and slow-moving streams. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy coating.

Zizania subtilis plays an important ecological role by providing food and habitat for various aquatic and avian species.
