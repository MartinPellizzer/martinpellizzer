Zygopetalum citrinum

Zygopetalum citrinum, commonly known as the lemon orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish throat, making it visually striking. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Zygopetalum citrinum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity for optimal growth.
