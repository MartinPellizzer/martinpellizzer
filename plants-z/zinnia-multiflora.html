Zinnia multiflora

Zinnia multiflora, commonly known as the Florida zinnia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bushy growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces numerous small, showy blooms throughout the growing season. Zinnia multiflora thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its foliage is covered in fine, hairy leaves that help protect it from drought and pests.
