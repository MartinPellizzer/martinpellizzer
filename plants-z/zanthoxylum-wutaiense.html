Zanthoxylum wutaiense

Zanthoxylum wutaiense, commonly known as the Wutai prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its bark and leaves contain bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

Zanthoxylum wutaiense thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.
