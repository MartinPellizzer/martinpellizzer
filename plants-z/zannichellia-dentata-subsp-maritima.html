Zannichellia dentata subsp. maritima

Zannichellia dentata subsp.

maritima, commonly known as the maritime water starwort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to coastal regions. It typically grows in brackish or saline water environments, such as estuaries and tidal marshes.

The plant has slender, branching stems and small, floating leaves that are oval-shaped with serrated edges. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually greenish-white in color.

It plays an important role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
