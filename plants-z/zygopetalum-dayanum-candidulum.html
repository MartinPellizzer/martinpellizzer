Zygopetalum dayanum candidulum

Zygopetalum dayanum candidulum, commonly known as the white zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive white coloration. The plant features long, slender petals and a narrow, recurved lip, typical of the Zygopetalum genus. It prefers cool, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a delicate beauty to gardens and greenhouses.
