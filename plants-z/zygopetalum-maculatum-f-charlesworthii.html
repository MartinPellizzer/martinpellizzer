Zygopetalum maculatum f. charlesworthii

Zygopetalum maculatum f. charlesworthii, commonly known as the Charlesworthii zygopetalum, is a hybrid orchid with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like petals and a prominent, often contrasting lip that sets it apart from other zygopetalum varieties. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical environments. Its flowers are usually a mix of white and pink hues, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant due to its specific care requirements.
