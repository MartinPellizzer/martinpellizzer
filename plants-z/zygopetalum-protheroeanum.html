Zygopetalum protheroeanum

Zygopetalum protheroeanum, commonly known as the Protheroe's zygopetalum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Zygopetalum protheroeanum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
