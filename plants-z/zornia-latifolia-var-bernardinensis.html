Zornia latifolia var. bernardinensis

Zornia latifolia var. bernardinensis, commonly known as the broadleaf zornia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in wetlands. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
