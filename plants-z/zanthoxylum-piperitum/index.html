Zanthoxylum Piperitum

Zanthoxylum piperitum, commonly known as Sichuan pepper, is a spiny shrub native to eastern Asia. Its fruits are prized for their unique numbing and spicy flavor, often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. With its distinctive appearance and versatile uses, it holds cultural and economic significance in many regions. This article explores the history, uses, and characteristics of this remarkable plant.
