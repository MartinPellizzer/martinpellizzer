Zanthoxylum dipetalum var. dipetalum

Zanthoxylum dipetalum var. dipetalum, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated margins and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some animals but considered toxic to humans. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
