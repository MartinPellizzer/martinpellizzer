Zingiber roseum

Zingiber roseum, commonly known as the pink ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other gingers like turmeric and cardamom. The plant is characterized by its ornamental, fragrant, and brightly colored flowers that range from pink to red. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Zingiber roseum is cultivated for its aesthetic value and has a long history in horticulture.
