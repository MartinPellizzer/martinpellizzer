Zygopetalum ghillanyi

Zygopetalum ghillanyi, commonly known as the Giant Orchid, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, making it one of the most visually impressive orchids. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually a vibrant mix of red, orange, and yellow, with a unique lip that is often darker in color.

Zygopetalum ghillanyi thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
