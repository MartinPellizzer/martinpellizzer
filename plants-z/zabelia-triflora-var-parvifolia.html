Zabelia triflora var. parvifolia

Zabelia triflora var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved zabelia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
