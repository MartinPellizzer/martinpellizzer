Zanthoxylum natalense

Zanthoxylum natalense, commonly known as Natal prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits. Its bark and leaves contain bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in savanna ecosystems.
