Zannichellia repens var. aerea

Zannichellia repens var. aerea, commonly known as the broad-leaf water plantain, is a aquatic plant characterized by its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that float on the water surface.

The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread across the water surface, forming dense mats.
