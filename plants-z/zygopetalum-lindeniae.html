Zygopetalum lindeniae

Zygopetalum lindeniae, commonly known as the Lindenia orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are usually a vibrant mix of red, orange, and yellow, with a unique lip structure.

Zygopetalum lindeniae thrives in warm, moist conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
