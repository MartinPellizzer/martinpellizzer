Zephyranthes pantanalensis

Zephyranthes pantanalensis, commonly known as the Pantanal rain lily, is a flowering plant native to the Pantanal wetlands of Brazil.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in wet, marshy environments. It is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds and rivers.

Its blooming period typically occurs in the late afternoon, hence its common name.
