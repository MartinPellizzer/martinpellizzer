Zuckertia manuelii

Zuckertia manuelii, commonly known as the Manuel's zuckertia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, evergreen leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
