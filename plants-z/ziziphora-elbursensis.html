Ziziphora elbursensis

Ziziphora elbursensis, commonly known as the Elburz ziziphora, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Its small, tubular flowers are usually purple or pink, and it produces small, oval-shaped seeds.

Ziziphora elbursensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
