Zygopetalum rivierei

Zygopetalum rivierei, commonly known as the Riviere's zygopetalum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant blooms. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive lip, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Zygopetalum rivierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
