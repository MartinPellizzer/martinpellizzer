Ziziphora glabrata

Ziziphora glabrata, commonly known as smooth ziziphora, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Ziziphora glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
