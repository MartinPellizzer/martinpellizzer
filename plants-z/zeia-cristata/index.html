Zeia Cristata

Zeia cristata is a rare and striking plant known for its unique, spiky appearance. Native to the high altitudes of a secluded region, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its foliage features a distinct crown-like structure, giving it a regal and otherworldly look. Despite its beauty, it remains largely unknown to the general public. This plant is a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
