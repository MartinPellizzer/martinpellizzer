Ficus popenoei subsp. popenoei

Ficus popenoei subsp.

popenoei, commonly known as the Popenoe's fig, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height.

The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces edible figs that are important for both wildlife and local human consumption.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in its native habitat.
