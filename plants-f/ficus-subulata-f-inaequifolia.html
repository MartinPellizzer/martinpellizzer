Ficus subulata f. inaequifolia

Ficus subulata f. inaequifolia, commonly known as the Variegated Spineless Fig, is a cultivar of the Ficus subulata species, characterized by its unique foliage.

It features narrow, spiny leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern of white and green, adding visual interest to indoor spaces. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying qualities.

Its foliage structure is adapted to provide efficient photosynthesis while minimizing water loss.
