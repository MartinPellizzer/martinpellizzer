Fabiana petunioides

Fabiana petunioides, commonly known as the silverleaf broom, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has a sprawling growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a drought-tolerant species.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid regions.
