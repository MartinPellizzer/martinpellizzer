Fritillaria lanzhouensis

Fritillaria lanzhouensis, commonly known as the Lanzhou fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its bulbs are often used in traditional Chinese medicine, valued for their purported health benefits.

Fritillaria lanzhouensis is also appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens and horticultural collections.
