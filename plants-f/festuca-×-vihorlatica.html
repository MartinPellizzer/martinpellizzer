Festuca × vihorlatica

Festuca × vihorlatica, commonly known as the silvergrass or velvet grass, is a hybrid species within the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Festuca × vihorlatica is native to Europe and is adapted to temperate climates.
