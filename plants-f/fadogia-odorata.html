Fadogia odorata

Fadogia odorata, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by pod fruits containing edible seeds. It is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.

Fadogia odorata is often cultivated for its seeds, which are rich in protein and can be used as a food source.
