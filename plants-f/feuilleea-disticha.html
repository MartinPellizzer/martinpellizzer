Feuilleea disticha

Feuilleea disticha, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a striking evergreen plant native to tropical regions.

It features feathery, delicate fronds that grow in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant's leaves are covered in a fine, silvery coating, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Feuilleea disticha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
