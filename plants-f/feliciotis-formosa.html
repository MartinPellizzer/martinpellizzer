Feliciotis formosa

Feliciotis formosa, commonly known as the golden thread fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically borne on a spadix, a feature common in the Araceae family. Feliciotis formosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
