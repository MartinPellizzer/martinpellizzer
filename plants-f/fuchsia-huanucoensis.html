Fuchsia huanucoensis

Fuchsia huanucoensis, commonly known as the Huanuco fuchsia, is a species native to the Andean region of Peru.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of red and purple, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Fuchsia huanucoensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
