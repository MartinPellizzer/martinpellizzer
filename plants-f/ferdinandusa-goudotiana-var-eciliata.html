Ferdinandusa goudotiana var. eciliata

Ferdinandusa goudotiana var. eciliata, commonly known as the pink-flowered evergreen, is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
