Felicia linearis

Felicia linearis, commonly known as the narrowleaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia linearis is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
