Furcraea foetida

Furcraea foetida, commonly known as the stinking fig or yellow fig, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that are followed by woody, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
