Festuca orizabensis

Festuca orizabensis, commonly known as the Orizaba fescue, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, two-lobed flowers.

Festuca orizabensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
