Ficus Timorensis

Ficus timorensis is a rare and fascinating species of fig tree native to the Indonesian island of Timor. This evergreen plant is known for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in tropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in botanical gardens and private collections worldwide. The tree's adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening. Its presence in both natural and cultivated settings highlights its ecological and aesthetic significance.
