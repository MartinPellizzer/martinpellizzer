Ficus cavaleriei

Ficus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese banyan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits. It is a fast-growing species that can reach heights of up to 20 meters in its natural habitat.

Due to its adaptability, it is also cultivated as an ornamental plant in many parts of the world.
