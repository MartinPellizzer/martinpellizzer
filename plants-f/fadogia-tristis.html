Fadogia tristis

Fadogia tristis, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Fadogia tristis is notable for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
