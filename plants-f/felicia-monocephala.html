Felicia monocephala

Felicia monocephala, commonly known as the yellow daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant produces bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Felicia monocephala is adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms.
