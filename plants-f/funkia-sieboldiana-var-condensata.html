Funkia sieboldiana var. condensata

Funkia sieboldiana var. condensata, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface, and it produces yellow flowers in summer. The plant thrives in shallow water and is often found in marshes and pond edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to help purify water.

Its rhizomes can spread to form dense colonies, making it both a useful and sometimes invasive species.
