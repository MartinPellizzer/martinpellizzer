Feuilleea flexuosa

Feuilleea flexuosa, commonly known as the wavy leaf fern, is a unique plant characterized by its gracefully undulating fronds.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with high moisture levels. The plant's leaves are long, narrow, and exhibit a distinct wavy texture, giving it its common name. Feuilleea flexuosa typically grows from rhizomes, spreading slowly to form dense clusters.

Its botanical structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
