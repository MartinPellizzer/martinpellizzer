Ferula penninervis

Ferula penninervis, commonly known as the Kashmiri ferula, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a tall, herbaceous perennial that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins, giving it its scientific name. Its flowers are yellow and clustered in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

The plant is often found in rocky and alpine environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
