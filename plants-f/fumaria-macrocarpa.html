Fumaria macrocarpa

Fumaria macrocarpa, commonly known as the greater celandine, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant grows up to 1 meter tall and thrives in moist, disturbed soils. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along pathways.

Fumaria macrocarpa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for various health purposes.
