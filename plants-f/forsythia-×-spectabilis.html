Forsythia × spectabilis

Forsythia × spectabilis, commonly known as the golden trumpet bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and parks.
