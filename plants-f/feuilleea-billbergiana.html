Feuilleea billbergiana

Feuilleea billbergiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. Its leaves are thick and leathery, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The flowers bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant.
