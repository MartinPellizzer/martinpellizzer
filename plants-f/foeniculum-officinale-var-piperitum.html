Foeniculum officinale var. piperitum

Foeniculum officinale var. piperitum, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features feathery, finely divided leaves and produces yellow flowers in umbels. The plant is characterized by its aromatic seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing to a height of 1 to 2 meters.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant in both gardens and traditional medicine.
