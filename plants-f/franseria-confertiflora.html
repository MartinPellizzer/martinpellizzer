Franseria confertiflora

Franseria confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow to orange composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged alternately along the stems.

Franseria confertiflora is often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
