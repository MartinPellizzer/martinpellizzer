Festuca loliiformis

Festuca loliiformis, commonly known as slender fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks. Its flowers are small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Festuca loliiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
