Fenixanthes splendens

Fenixanthes splendens, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
