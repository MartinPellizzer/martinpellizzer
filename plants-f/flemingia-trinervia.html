Flemingia trinervia

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are pinnately divided with three main veins, giving it the species name *trinervia*.
