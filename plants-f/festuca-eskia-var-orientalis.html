Festuca eskia var. orientalis

Festuca eskia var. orientalis, commonly known as Siberian grass or oriental fescue, is a low-growing, perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, dark green leaves and produces delicate, blue-gray inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. This plant is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.

Its foliage is also valued for its use in traditional herbal remedies.
