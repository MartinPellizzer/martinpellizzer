Fuchsia aquaviridis

Fuchsia aquaviridis, commonly known as the water green fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from bright yellow to deep green. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded gardens.

Fuchsia aquaviridis is valued for its unique foliage and vibrant floral display.
