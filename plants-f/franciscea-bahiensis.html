Franciscea bahiensis

Franciscea bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahamian starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Bahamas.

It is a small shrub characterized by its star-shaped, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typically found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
