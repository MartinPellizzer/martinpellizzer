Fritillaria messanensis subsp. gracilis

Fritillaria messanensis subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
