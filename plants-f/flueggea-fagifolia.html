Flueggea fagifolia

Flueggea fagifolia, commonly known as the desert pomegranate, is a shrub or small tree native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its root system is extensive and drought-tolerant, allowing it to thrive in harsh, sandy soils.
