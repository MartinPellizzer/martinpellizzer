Festuca santyvesii

Festuca santyvesii, commonly known as the San Ysidro fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Festuca santyvesii is adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
