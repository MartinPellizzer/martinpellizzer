Fadogia cienkowskii var. cienkowskii

Fadogia cienkowskii var. cienkowskii, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a pod that contains several flat, kidney-shaped seeds.

This plant is often found in dry, open woodlands and is adapted to arid conditions.
