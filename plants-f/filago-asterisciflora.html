Filago asterisciflora

Filago asterisciflora, commonly known as the starflower filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is native to southern Africa. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Filago asterisciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
