Feuilleea formosa

Feuilleea formosa, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that reflect light, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the rainy season. Feuilleea formosa thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and elegant blossoms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
