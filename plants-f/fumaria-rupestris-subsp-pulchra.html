Fumaria rupestris subsp. pulchra

Fumaria rupestris subsp.

pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful rock fumitory, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, yellow-orange flowers clustered in racemes.

The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to colonize rocky or gravelly landscapes.
