Furcraea viridis

Furcraea viridis, commonly known as the green sword plant, is a succulent plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow, and sword-like leaves that are usually a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
