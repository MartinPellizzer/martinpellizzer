Fumaria bulbosa

Fumaria bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous fumewort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a short, fleshy stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are alternate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria bulbosa is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
