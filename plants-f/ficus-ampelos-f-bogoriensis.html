Ficus ampelos f. bogoriensis

Ficus ampelos f. bogoriensis, commonly known as the Bogoriensis fig, is a variant of the weeping fig tree.

It is characterized by its long, drooping branches that create a cascading appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

This cultivar is known for its adaptability and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
