Farsetia grandiflora var. angustipetala

Farsetia grandiflora var. angustipetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaf farsetia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are often a glossy green, providing a dramatic visual contrast to its showy, tubular flowers. The flowers, which bloom in spring, are typically bright yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
