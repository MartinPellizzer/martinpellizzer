Felicia hyssopifolia subsp. polyphylla

Felicia hyssopifolia subsp.

polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.

The plant has narrow, slightly hairy leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and compact size, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
