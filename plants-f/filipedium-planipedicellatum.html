Filipedium planipedicellatum

Filipedium planipedicellatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Filipedium planipedicellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
