Ficus krukovii

Ficus krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus krukovii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
