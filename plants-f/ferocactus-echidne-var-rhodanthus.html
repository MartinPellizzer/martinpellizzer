Ferocactus echidne var. rhodanthus

Ferocactus echidne var. rhodanthus, commonly known as the red-figured hedgehog cactus, is a distinctive cactus species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with sharp, spiny areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and hardiness in desert environments.
