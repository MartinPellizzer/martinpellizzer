Forsythia koreana f. autumnalis

Forsythia koreana f. autumnalis, commonly known as the Korean forsythia autumnalis, is a compact shrub native to Korea.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms. It is typically deciduous, shedding its leaves in the fall.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of soil conditions.
