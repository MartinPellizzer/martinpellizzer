Feuilleea lindeniana

Feuilleea lindeniana, commonly known as the silverleaf linden, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring season. Feuilleea lindeniana thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.
