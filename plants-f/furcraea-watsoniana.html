Furcraea watsoniana

Furcraea watsoniana, commonly known as the Watson's sword fern, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of Central America.

It features tall, narrow leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, giving it a dramatic, architectural appearance. The plant is characterized by its robust, fibrous trunk and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its resilience and unique foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
