Faucaria longifolia

Faucaria longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
