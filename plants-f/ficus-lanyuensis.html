Ficus lanyuensis

Ficus lanyuensis, commonly known as the Lanyu fig, is a rare and endemic species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its unique, broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as a tree or shrub, often in moist, shaded environments. Its fruit is a small, green fig that ripens to a yellowish color, attracting various wildlife.
