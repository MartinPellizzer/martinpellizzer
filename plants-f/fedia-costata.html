Fedia costata

Fedia costata, commonly known as the coastal fedia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Fedia costata thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand salt spray and coastal winds.

It plays an important role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for coastal wildlife.
