Fusifilum papillosum

Fusifilum papillosum, commonly known as the spiky leaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its unique, elongated, and spiky leaves.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dense clusters of dark green, lance-shaped fronds. Its leaves are covered in small, rounded papillae, giving it a distinctive textured appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
