Felicia teres

Felicia teres, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. Felicia teres is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and aromatic foliage.

It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
