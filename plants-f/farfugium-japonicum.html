Farfugium japonicum

Farfugium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, giving them a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a cup-like structure. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in regions with suitable climates.
