Festuca chita

Festuca chita, commonly known as Chinese meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and mountainous regions.

Festuca chita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
