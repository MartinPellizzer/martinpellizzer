Falconera fastigiata

Falconera fastigiata, commonly known as the narrowleaf evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern Australia, where it thrives in sandy soils and full sun. Falconera fastigiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand coastal conditions.
