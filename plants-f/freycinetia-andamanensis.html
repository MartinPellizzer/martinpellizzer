Freycinetia andamanensis

Freycinetia andamanensis, commonly known as the Andaman palm, is a species of palm native to the Andaman Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its pinnate leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it a significant component of the island's flora.
