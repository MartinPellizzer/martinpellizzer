Filago carpetana subsp. maroccana

Filago carpetana subsp.

maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan sandstar, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellowish to orange-colored capitula.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and sandy areas. It has small, narrow leaves and produces daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This subspecies is often found in regions of Morocco and parts of the Sahara Desert.
