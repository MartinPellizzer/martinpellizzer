Fortuynia aucheri

Fortuynia aucheri, commonly known as the silver-leafed bellflower, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking silver-gray foliage that contrasts beautifully with its clusters of bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
