Festuca × schisticola

Festuca × schisticola, commonly known as the hybrid tall fescue, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its robust growth habit and tufted foliage, with leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous narrow, spikelet-like flowers. It is widely used in turfgrass applications due to its drought tolerance and adaptability to various soil conditions.

Festuca × schisticola is also valued in ecological restoration projects for its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
