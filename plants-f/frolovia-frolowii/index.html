Frolovia Frolowii

Frolovia frolowii is a rare and enigmatic plant species discovered in the remote highlands of South America. Known for its striking bioluminescent properties, it emits a soft blue glow during twilight hours. This unique characteristic has made it a subject of fascination for botanists and ecologists alike. Despite its beauty, the plant remains elusive and poorly understood due to its limited habitat. Its study offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and ecological resilience.
