Forsteronia alexandri

Forsteronia alexandri, commonly known as the Alexandrian forsteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its flowers are pollinated primarily by birds, particularly sunbirds, due to their shape and nectar content.

Forsteronia alexandri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
