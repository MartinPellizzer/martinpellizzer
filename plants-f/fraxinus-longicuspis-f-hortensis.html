Fraxinus longicuspis f. hortensis

Fraxinus longicuspis f. hortensis, commonly known as the garden ash, is a cultivar of the white ash tree.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring attractive, finely toothed leaves that turn vibrant yellow in autumn. The plant typically grows as a multi-stemmed shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This cultivar is valued for its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping.
