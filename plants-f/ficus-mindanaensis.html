Ficus mindanaensis

Ficus mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao fig, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is a large, evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring broad, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
