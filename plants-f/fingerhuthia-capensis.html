Fingerhuthia capensis

Fingerhuthia capensis, commonly known as the Cape fingerhuthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems with small, spiky leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. Its flowers are typically small and star-shaped, appearing in shades of yellow and orange.

Fingerhuthia capensis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
