Fimbristylis stauntonii var. stauntonii

Fimbristylis stauntonii var. stauntonii, commonly known as the slender flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats such as swampy areas and wetlands. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually green or brown and are arranged in dense clusters.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
