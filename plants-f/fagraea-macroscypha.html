Fagraea macroscypha

Fagraea macroscypha, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets and produces woody, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea macroscypha is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
