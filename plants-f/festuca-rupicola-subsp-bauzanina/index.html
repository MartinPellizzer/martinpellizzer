Festuca Rupicola Subsp. Bauzanina

Festuca rupicola subsp. bauzanina is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in rocky and arid environments. This subspecies is native to regions of western North America, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions. Its compact growth habit and dark green foliage make it a distinctive plant in its habitat. Despite its resilience, it remains underappreciated in botanical studies and horticultural practices. Understanding this plant offers insights into ecological adaptation and conservation in challenging landscapes.
