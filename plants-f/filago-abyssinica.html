Filago abyssinica

Filago abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian filago, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, while its flowers are small and clustered in a composite head.

Filago abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
