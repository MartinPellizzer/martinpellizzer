Fedtschenkoa grandiflora, commonly known as the giant bellflower, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

Fedtschenkoa grandiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
