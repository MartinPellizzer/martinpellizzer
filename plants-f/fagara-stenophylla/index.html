Fagara Stenophylla

Fagara stenophylla is a rare and intriguing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. It belongs to the Rutaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public. Its unique ecological role and potential health benefits make it a fascinating subject for further study.
