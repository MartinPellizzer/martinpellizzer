Fumaria heldreichii

Fumaria heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan smokeplant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fumariaceae family, characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer, often in a raceme arrangement. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Fumaria heldreichii is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and meadows.
