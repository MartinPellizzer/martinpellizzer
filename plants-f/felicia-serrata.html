Felicia serrata

Felicia serrata, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and serrated, giving it its scientific name.

Felicia serrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
