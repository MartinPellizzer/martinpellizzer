Flickingeria eurorum

Flickingeria eurorum, commonly known as the European flickingeria, is a rare and specialized plant species native to specific regions in Europe.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and unique flowering structure, which consists of tightly clustered, pale yellow blooms. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas with rich, well-drained soil. Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for certain ecological conditions.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Flickingeria eurorum is considered endangered in many parts of its native range.
