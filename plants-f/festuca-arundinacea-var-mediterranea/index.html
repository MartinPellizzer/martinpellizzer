Festuca Arundinacea Var. Mediterranea

Festuca arundinacea var. mediterranea is a hardy grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments. This variety is known for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions. Its dense growth habit makes it useful for erosion control and as a forage plant. Despite its tough nature, it adds a fine texture to landscapes and is valued in ecological restoration projects.
