Fumaria viciosoi

Fumaria viciosoi, commonly known as the common fumitory, is a herbaceous annual plant belonging to the Fumariaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces elongated, spiral-shaped seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its leaves are alternate and have a distinctive, slightly hairy surface.
