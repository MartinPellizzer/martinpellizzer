Fumana laevis var. hispidula

Fumana laevis var. hispidula, commonly known as the hairy fumana, is a variant of the fumana plant characterized by its hairy foliage.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to certain regions of South Africa. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in loose inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
