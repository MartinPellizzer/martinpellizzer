Festuca kryloviana f. aristata

Festuca kryloviana f. aristata, commonly known as the tufted fescue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, upright stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. It is native to temperate regions of Asia and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value lies in its fine texture and ability to form dense, low-growing mats.
