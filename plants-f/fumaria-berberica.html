Fumaria berberica

Fumaria berberica, commonly known as yellow glory of the snow, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fumariaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Fumaria berberica is also known for its medicinal properties, having been used in traditional herbal remedies.
