Ficus subulata var. gracillima

Ficus subulata var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender fig or narrowleaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Ficus subulata var. gracillima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
