Filetia mesargyrea

Filetia mesargyrea, commonly known as the silverleaf filetia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
