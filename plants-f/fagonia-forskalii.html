Fagonia forskalii

Fagonia forskalii, commonly known as the desert fagonia, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. It has a deep root system that helps it access underground water sources.

Fagonia forskalii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
