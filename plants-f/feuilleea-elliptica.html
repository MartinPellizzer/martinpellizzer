Feuilleea elliptica

Feuilleea elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical leaf plant, is a flowering species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Feuilleea elliptica thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
