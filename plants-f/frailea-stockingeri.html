Frailea stockingeri

Frailea stockingeri, commonly known as the Stockinger's stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
