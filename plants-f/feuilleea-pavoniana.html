Feuilleea pavoniana

Feuilleea pavoniana, commonly known as the Rainbow Leaf Plant, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, palmate leaves that display a vibrant array of colors, ranging from deep purples to bright greens and reds. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.

Feuilleea pavoniana is relatively low-maintenance, making it accessible for both novice and experienced gardeners.
