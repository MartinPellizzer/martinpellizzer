Ferula Rigida

Ferula rigida is a hardy perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It is known for its tall, spiky stems and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in harsh environments with limited water and intense sunlight. Its fibrous roots have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties. Ferula rigida is both an ecological cornerstone and a symbol of resilience in extreme climates.
