Fumaria media var. vagans

Fumaria media var. vagans, commonly known as the variable yellow weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and bright yellow flowers that resemble those of the common fumitory. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds dispersed by wind and animals.
