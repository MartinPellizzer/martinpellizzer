Felicia bampsiana

Felicia bampsiana, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun.

Felicia bampsiana is often used in xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
