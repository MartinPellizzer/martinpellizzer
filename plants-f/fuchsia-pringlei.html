Fuchsia pringlei

Fuchsia pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep pink to purple. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair. Fuchsia pringlei thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude forests.

Its unique beauty makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
