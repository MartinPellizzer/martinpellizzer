Ficaria verna f. ebulbillosa

Ficaria verna f. ebulbillosa, commonly known as the spring snowflake, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its botanical significance lies in its early blooming habit and its role in supporting early pollinators.
