Forrestia hispida var. mollis

Forrestia hispida var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet forestiera, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and covered in fine, pubescent hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
