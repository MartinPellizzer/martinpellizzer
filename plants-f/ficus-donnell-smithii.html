Ficus donnell-smithii

Ficus donnell-smithii, commonly known as the Donnell-Smith fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. It produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible figs that are often used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
