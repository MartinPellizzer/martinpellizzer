Ficus kingii

Ficus kingii, commonly known as the King's fig, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, round fig fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus kingii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
