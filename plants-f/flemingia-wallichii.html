Flemingia wallichii

Flemingia wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Flemingia wallichii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
