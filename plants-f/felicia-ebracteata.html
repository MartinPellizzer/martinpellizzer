Felicia ebracteata

Felicia ebracteata, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow daisy-like flowers, which bloom from spring to summer. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia ebracteata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
