Ficus colobocarpa

Ficus colobocarpa, commonly known as the broad-leafed fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits that are typically round and yellowish.

Ficus colobocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
