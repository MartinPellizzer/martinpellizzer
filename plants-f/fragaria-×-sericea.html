Fragaria × sericea

Fragaria × sericea, commonly known as the silverleaf strawberry, is a hybrid plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white hairy leaves and small, edible berries. The plant is native to the western United States and thrives in temperate climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.

The berries, while not as flavorful as common strawberries, are used in some culinary applications.
