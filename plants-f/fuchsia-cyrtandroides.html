Fuchsia cyrtandroides

Fuchsia cyrtandroides, commonly known as the New Zealand fuchsia, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in forested areas.

Fuchsia cyrtandroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
