Feuilleea fournieri

Feuilleea fournieri, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, glossy green leaves that often display a subtle golden hue under certain lighting conditions. The plant has a unique rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread slowly across the forest floor. Its leaves are typically divided into lance-shaped segments, giving it a feathery appearance.

Feuilleea fournieri thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
