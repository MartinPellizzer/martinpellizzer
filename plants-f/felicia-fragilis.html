Felicia fragilis

Felicia fragilis, commonly known as the brittle felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its delicate, branching structure and small, white to yellow flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Felicia fragilis is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
