Forsythia japonica f. ovata

Forsythia japonica f. ovata, commonly known as the oval forsythia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its narrow, ovate-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, funnel-shaped flowers in early spring, which are highly ornamental. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

This cultivar is valued for its early blooming habit and compact growth form.
