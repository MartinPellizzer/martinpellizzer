Ferula licentiana var. tunshanica

Ferula licentiana var. tunshanica, commonly known as the Tunshan giant fennel, is a variant of the Ferula genus native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, feathery leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, which are followed by long, narrow seed pods. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in mountainous regions.

The plant has aromatic properties and is valued for its medicinal and culinary uses.
