Fallopia koreana

Fallopia koreana, commonly known as Korean cowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible roots. Fallopia koreana is often found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks.
