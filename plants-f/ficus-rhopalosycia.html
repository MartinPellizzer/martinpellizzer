Ficus rhopalosycia

Ficus rhopalosycia, commonly known as the warty fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its thick, warty bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a smooth, greenish-yellow fig that turns reddish when mature.
