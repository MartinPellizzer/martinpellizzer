Ferocactus peninsulae var. gracilis

Ferocactus peninsulae var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender peninsula cactus, is a columnar cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features a slender, cylindrical stem with prominent areoles and spines that are typically yellowish to brown in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered in the axils of the spines. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
