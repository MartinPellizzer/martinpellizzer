Fritillaria xibeiensis

Fritillaria xibeiensis, commonly known as the Xinbei fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its bulbs are often used in traditional Chinese medicine, valued for their purported health benefits.

Fritillaria xibeiensis is also appreciated in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
