Forestiera hondurensis

Forestiera hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduras forestiera, is a small evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by elongated, woody fruits.

Forestiera hondurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
