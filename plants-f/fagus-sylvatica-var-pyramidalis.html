Fagus sylvatica var. pyramidalis

Fagus sylvatica var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the European beech, is a cultivar of the common beech tree.

It is characterized by its narrow, conical shape, which makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The tree has dark green, glossy leaves that turn yellow in the autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to form a dense canopy.
