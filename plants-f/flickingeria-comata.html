Flickingeria comata

Flickingeria comata, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of South America.

This orchid is characterized by its distinctive hairy leaves and striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in the understory of rainforests. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with new shoots emerging from the base of the previous growth.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
