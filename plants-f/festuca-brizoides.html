Festuca brizoides

Festuca brizoides, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie and steppe ecosystems. Festuca brizoides is drought-tolerant and has a deep root system, making it well adapted to arid conditions.

Its small, spike-like inflorescences produce light blue to purple flowers, contributing to its ornamental value.
