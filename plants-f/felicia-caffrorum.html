Felicia caffrorum

Felicia caffrorum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is well adapted to sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters.

Felicia caffrorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
