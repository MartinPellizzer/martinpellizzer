Ficus columbarum

Ficus columbarum, commonly known as the columnar ficus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and dense, glossy dark green leaves. The plant has a simple, unbranched stem that grows vertically, giving it a unique, column-like appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits.

Ficus columbarum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking visual appeal and adaptability to indoor conditions.
