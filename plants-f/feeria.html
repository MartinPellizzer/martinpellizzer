Feeria

Feeria, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, lantern-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Feeria thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
