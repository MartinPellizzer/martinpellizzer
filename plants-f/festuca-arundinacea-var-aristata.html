Festuca arundinacea var. aristata

Festuca arundinacea var. aristata, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and tufted inflorescences, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in wetlands or along waterways. It has a clump-forming growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Festuca arundinacea var. aristata is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
