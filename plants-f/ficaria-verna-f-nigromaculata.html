Ficaria verna f. nigromaculata

Ficaria verna f. nigromaculata, commonly known as the black-spotted winter jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The leaves are ovate and have a distinctive black or dark purple marking at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
