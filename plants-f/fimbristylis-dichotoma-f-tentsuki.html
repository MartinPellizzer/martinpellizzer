Fimbristylis dichotoma f. tentsuki

Fimbristylis dichotoma f. tentsuki, commonly known as the Tentsuki sedge, is a variant of the sedge genus Fimbristylis.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and spikelets arranged in dense clusters.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
