Freycinetia hollrungii

Freycinetia hollrungii, commonly known as the hollrung's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Its inflorescence consists of a branched cluster of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months.
