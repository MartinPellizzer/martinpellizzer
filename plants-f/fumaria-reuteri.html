Fumaria reuteri

Fumaria reuteri, commonly known as the yellow fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks.

Fumaria reuteri is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
