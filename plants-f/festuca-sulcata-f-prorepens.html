Festuca sulcata f. prorepens

Festuca sulcata f. prorepens, commonly known as the African grass or prorepens fescue, is a cool-season grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

This variety is often used in landscaping and erosion control due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
