Felicia bergeriana

Felicia bergeriana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Felicia bergeriana is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
