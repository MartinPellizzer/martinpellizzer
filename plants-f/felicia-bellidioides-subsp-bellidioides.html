Felicia bellidioides subsp. bellidioides

Felicia bellidioides subsp.

bellidioides, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm.

The plant features bright yellow flowers arranged in a compact, daisy-like cluster, with narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
