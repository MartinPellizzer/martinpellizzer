Fumaria muralis var. curta

Fumaria muralis var. curta, commonly known as the short wall rue, is a variant of the common wall rue, characterized by its shorter stature and compact growth habit.

It is a member of the Papaveraceae family, known for its delicate, often nodding flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in shady, moist environments, often found growing on walls or in rocky crevices.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
