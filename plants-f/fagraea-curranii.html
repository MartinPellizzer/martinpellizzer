Fagraea curranii

Fagraea curranii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, round, orange-red fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea curranii is relatively low-maintenance and adapts well to a variety of soil types.
