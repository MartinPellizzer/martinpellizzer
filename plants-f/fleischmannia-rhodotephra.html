Fleischmannia rhodotephra

Fleischmannia rhodotephra, commonly known as the red-flowered fleischmannia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Fleischmannia rhodotephra is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
