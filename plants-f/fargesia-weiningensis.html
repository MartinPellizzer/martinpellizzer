Fargesia weiningensis

Fargesia weiningensis, commonly known as the Weining bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. This bamboo is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, evergreen foliage. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Fargesia weiningensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
