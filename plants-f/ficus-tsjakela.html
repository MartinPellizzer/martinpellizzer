Ficus tsjakela

Ficus tsjakela, commonly known as the Tsjakela fig, is a species of fig tree native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, featuring a broad, spreading canopy. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible fig fruits.

The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
