Fagraea loheri

Fagraea loheri, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. Fagraea loheri thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
