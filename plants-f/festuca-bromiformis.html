Festuca bromiformis

Festuca bromiformis, commonly known as bromegrass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. The plant has fine, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Festuca bromiformis is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
