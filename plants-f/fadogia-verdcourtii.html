Fadogia verdcourtii

Fadogia verdcourtii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing small, dark brown seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.

Fadogia verdcourtii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
