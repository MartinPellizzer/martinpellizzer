Feeria angustifolia

Feeria angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf feeria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental appeal. Feeria angustifolia thrives in well-drained soils and prefers a warm, sunny climate.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and collections.
