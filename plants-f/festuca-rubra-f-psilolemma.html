Festuca rubra f. psilolemma

Festuca rubra f. psilolemma, commonly known as red fescue with a narrow lemma, is a grass species characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and distinctive leaf morphology.

It features narrow, slightly rolled leaves with a prominent midrib, and its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a compact panicle. The plant is notable for its compact growth and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as lawns and meadows. It has a high tolerance for drought and poor soil conditions, making it a hardy ornamental and turfgrass species.

Its botanical features make it valuable for ecological restoration and landscaping applications.
