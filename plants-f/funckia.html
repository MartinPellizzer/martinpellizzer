Funckia

Funckia, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of broad, silver-green leaves that form a dense, sword-like shape. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of white or pale pink flowers. Funckia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant.
