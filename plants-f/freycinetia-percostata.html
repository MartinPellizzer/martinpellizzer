Freycinetia percostata

Freycinetia percostata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. Freycinetia percostata is adapted to coastal and lowland environments, often growing in sandy or disturbed soils.
