Foetidia asymetrica

Foetidia asymetrica, commonly known as the asymmetrical foetid plant, is a unique species characterized by its unusual leaf morphology.

It features asymmetrical leaves that are often twisted and curling, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical regions.

Its name derives from the strong, unpleasant odor it emits, especially when damaged or during certain times of the year.
