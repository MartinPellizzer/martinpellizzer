Fuchsia confertifolia

Fuchsia confertifolia, commonly known as the clustered fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. Fuchsia confertifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
