Ficus kirkii

Ficus kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Ficus kirkii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.

It prefers warm, humid environments and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
