Fritillaria messanensis var. oranensis

Fritillaria messanensis var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranese fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with contrasting patterns. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is typically found in dry, sunny habitats and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
