Festuca pungens

Festuca pungens, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinctive blue-gray coloration.

Festuca pungens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soils in native plant landscapes.
