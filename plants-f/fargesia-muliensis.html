Fargesia muliensis

Fargesia muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a striking silver-green color. This plant is evergreen and thrives in cool, moist environments, making it well-suited for shaded garden settings. Its flowers, which appear in clusters, are small and white, adding a delicate beauty to the plant.

Fargesia muliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to form dense, compact clumps.
