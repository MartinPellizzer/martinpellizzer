Fibigia thesigeri

Fibigia thesigeri, commonly known as the Cape fynbos.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, typically found in the fynbos biome. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment. It produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Fibigia thesigeri is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
