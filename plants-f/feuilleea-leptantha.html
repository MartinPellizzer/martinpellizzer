Feuilleea leptantha

Feuilleea leptantha, commonly known as the slender-leafed fern, is a delicate perennial plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that form a dense, feathery canopy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and establish a robust root system.

Feuilleea leptantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and partial sunlight.
