Fritillaria gracillima

Fritillaria gracillima, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Fritillaria gracillima is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
