Firmiana borneensis

Firmiana borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean fan palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
