Ficus hauilii

Ficus hauilii, commonly known as the Hauilii fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig fruits.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
