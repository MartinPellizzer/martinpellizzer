Fragaria valderia

Fragaria valderia, commonly known as the Valderia strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine. Its leaves are trifoliate, with a serrated margin, and it thrives in temperate climates.

The fruit has a delicate flavor and is valued for its nutritional content and ornamental appeal.
