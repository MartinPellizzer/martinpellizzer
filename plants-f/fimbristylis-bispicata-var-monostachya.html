Fimbristylis bispicata var. monostachya

Fimbristylis bispicata var. monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spikelet, which is typically borne on a slender stalk.
