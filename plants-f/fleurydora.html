Fleurydora

Fleurydora, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Fleurydora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
