Fritillaria messanensis subsp. oranensis

Fritillaria messanensis subsp.

oranensis, commonly known as the Oran fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with dark markings.

The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its bulbs are adapted for survival in arid conditions, allowing it to endure dry periods.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
