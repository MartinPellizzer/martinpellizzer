Ficus stellaris subsp. stellaris

Ficus stellaris subsp.

stellaris, commonly known as the starfig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. It produces small, white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are often consumed by local wildlife.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
