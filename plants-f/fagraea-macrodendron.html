Fagraea macrodendron

Fagraea macrodendron, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody, cylindrical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea macrodendron is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
