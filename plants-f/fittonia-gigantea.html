Fittonia gigantea

Fittonia gigantea, commonly known as the giant nerve plant, is a strikingly ornamental species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety foliage that displays intricate patterns of red, pink, and white veins. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters in its natural habitat. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, enhancing its visual appeal.

While it prefers shady, humid conditions, it can adapt to various light levels when cultivated indoors.
