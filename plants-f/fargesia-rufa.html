Fargesia rufa

Fargesia rufa, commonly known as the red bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring. Fargesia rufa is a clump-forming plant that grows relatively slowly, making it ideal for small garden spaces.

It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, and is often used as an ornamental plant in landscaping.
