Ferolia scabra

Ferolia scabra, commonly known as the rough-leafed ferolia, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rugged, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by oval, orange-colored fruits. Ferolia scabra is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in Mediterranean flora.
