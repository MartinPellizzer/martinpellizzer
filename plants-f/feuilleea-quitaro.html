Feuilleea quitaro

Feuilleea quitaro, commonly known as the Moonleaf Orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that shimmer with a silvery sheen under sunlight. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the evening. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Feuilleea quitaro is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, shaded conditions.
