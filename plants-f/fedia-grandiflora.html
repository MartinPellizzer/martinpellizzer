Fedia grandiflora

Fedia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered fedia, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color and are arranged alternately along the stem. Fedia grandiflora is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
