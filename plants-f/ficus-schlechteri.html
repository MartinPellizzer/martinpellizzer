Ficus schlechteri

Ficus schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus schlechteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
