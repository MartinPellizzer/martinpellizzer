Filago germanica subsp. lutescens

Filago germanica subsp.

lutescens, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers.

The plant has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
