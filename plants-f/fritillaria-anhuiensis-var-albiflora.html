Fritillaria anhuiensis var. albiflora

Fritillaria anhuiensis var. albiflora, commonly known as the Anhui white-flowered fritillary, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous structure and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive pattern of darker markings. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
