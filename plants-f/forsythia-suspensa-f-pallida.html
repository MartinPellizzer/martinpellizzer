Forsythia suspensa f. pallida

Forsythia suspensa f. pallida, commonly known as the pale forsythia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow flowers, which bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has a bushy growth habit with multiple stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

This variety is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, early-blooming plant.
