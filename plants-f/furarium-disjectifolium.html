Furarium disjectifolium

Furarium disjectifolium, commonly known as the scattered-leaf furarium, is a unique plant characterized by its irregular leaf arrangement.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. The leaves are broad and vary in shape, sometimes appearing disorganized or scattered. Its flowering is rare, with small, inconspicuous blooms that appear in late summer.

This plant is notable for its adaptability and resilience in challenging soil conditions.
