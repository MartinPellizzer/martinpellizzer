Felicia capensis

Felicia capensis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces bright yellow to orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Felicia capensis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
