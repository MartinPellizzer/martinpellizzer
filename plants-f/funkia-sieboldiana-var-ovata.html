Funkia sieboldiana var. ovata

Funkia sieboldiana var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leaved daylily, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
