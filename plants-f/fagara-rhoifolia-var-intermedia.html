Fagara rhoifolia var. intermedia

Fagara rhoifolia var. intermedia, commonly known as the Mexican prickly ash, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
