Ferdinandusa hirsuta

Ferdinandusa hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy fern tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries.

Ferdinandusa hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and horticulture.
