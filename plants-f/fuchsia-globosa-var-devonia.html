Fuchsia globosa var. devonia

Fuchsia globosa var. devonia, commonly known as the Devon fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of showy, violet-pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in cool, moist climates and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
