Fumaria rupestris var. platycarpa

Fumaria rupestris var. platycarpa, commonly known as the rock fumitory, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It features slender, branching stems that often grow along the ground or in crevices. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, adapted to reduce water loss.

It is often found in limestone areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
