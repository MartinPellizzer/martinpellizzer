Fedia caput-bovis var. pomeliana

Fedia caput-bovis var. pomeliana, commonly known as the horned fedia, is a rare and unique variant of the fedia species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like growths on the stem, which are more pronounced in this variety. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are broad and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture that aids in water retention.

The botanical interest of this plant lies in its unusual morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of study in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
