Franseria arborescens

Franseria arborescens, commonly known as the tree franseria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a waxy texture.

Franseria arborescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and floral display.
