Franseria recurva

Franseria recurva, commonly known as the narrowleaf franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Franseria recurva is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
