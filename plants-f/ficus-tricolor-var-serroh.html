Ficus tricolor var. serroh

Ficus tricolor var. serroh, commonly known as the variegated ficus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
