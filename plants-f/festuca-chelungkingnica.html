Festuca chelungkingnica

Festuca chelungkingnica, commonly known as the Chinese meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are usually less than 30 cm tall, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing small, spike-like inflorescences.

Festuca chelungkingnica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a forage plant.
