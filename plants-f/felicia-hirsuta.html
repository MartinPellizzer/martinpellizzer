Felicia hirsuta

Felicia hirsuta, commonly known as the woolly felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. Felicia hirsuta thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value contribute to its widespread cultivation.
