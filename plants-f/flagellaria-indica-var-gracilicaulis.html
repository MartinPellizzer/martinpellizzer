Flagellaria indica var. gracilicaulis

Flagellaria indica var. gracilicaulis, commonly known as the slender flagellaria, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that can grow quite long. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the daisy family. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
