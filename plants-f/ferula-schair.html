Ferula schair

Ferula schair, commonly known as the Persian cow's milk plant, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and the Middle East. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most notable feature is the yellowish-orange flower cluster that appears at the top of the stem.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
