Fragaria vesca var. ananas

Fragaria vesca var. ananas, commonly known as the pineapple strawberry, is a variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pineapple-like scent and flavor, which sets it apart from typical strawberries. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, juicy berries. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique taste and ornamental value.
