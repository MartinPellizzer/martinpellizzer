Ficus patellata

Ficus patellata, commonly known as the Patellata Fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Ficus patellata thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
