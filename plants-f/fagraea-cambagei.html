Fagraea cambagei

Fagraea cambagei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has glossy, elliptical leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea cambagei is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
