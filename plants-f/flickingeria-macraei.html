Flickingeria macraei

Flickingeria macraei, commonly known as the giant leaved orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety green leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in high humidity and warm, moist environments. Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.

Flickingeria macraei is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitat.
