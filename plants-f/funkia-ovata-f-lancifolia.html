Funkia ovata f. lancifolia

Funkia ovata f. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daylily, is a variant of the daylily species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wetland areas.

The flowers typically range in color from yellow to orange, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
