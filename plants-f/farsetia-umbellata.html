Farsetia umbellata

Farsetia umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, upright trunk covered in a thick, fibrous sheath that resembles a crown. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate leaves that form an umbrella-like shape, giving it its common name. Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, with a waxy texture.

Farsetia umbellata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as a decorative indoor plant.
