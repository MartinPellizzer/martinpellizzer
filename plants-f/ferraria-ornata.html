Ferraria ornata

Ferraria ornata, commonly known as the sweet-scented bellflower, is a South African plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are erect and branching.

Ferraria ornata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
