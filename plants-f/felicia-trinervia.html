Felicia trinervia

Felicia trinervia, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Felicia trinervia is a hardy perennial that is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
