Fritillaria nobilis

Fritillaria nobilis, commonly known as the noble fritillary, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow with dark markings. The plant has a fleshy, tunicated bulb that stores nutrients for growth. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from the base of the plant.

Fritillaria nobilis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
