Forsythia × intermedia var. spectabilis

Forsythia × intermedia var. spectabilis, commonly known as the golden bell forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has compound, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and hardy nature make it suitable for hedging and landscape design.
