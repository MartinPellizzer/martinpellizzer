Faramea Anisocalyx Var. Pulchella

Faramea anisocalyx var. pulchella is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Southeast Asia. Its unique foliage features vibrant, lance-shaped leaves with a deep green hue and intricate vein patterns. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and rarity make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and horticultural appreciation.
