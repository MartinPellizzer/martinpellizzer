Fuchsia magellanica var. eburnea

Fuchsia magellanica var. eburnea, commonly known as the silver fuchsia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of pink and purple during the spring and summer. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for shaded garden areas and container planting.
