Fumaria agraria var. pallescens

Fumaria agraria var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale garden fumitory, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as gardens and fields. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The pale garden fumitory is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
