Fargesia sparsiflora

Fargesia sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers in early summer, which are borne on short, hairy stalks. It is a cold-hardy plant that thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil.

Fargesia sparsiflora is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and ability to form dense, compact clumps.
