Filago candolleana

Filago candolleana, commonly known as the Himalayan filago, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk florets. Filago candolleana is known for its resilience to cold climates and is often found in high-altitude meadows.
