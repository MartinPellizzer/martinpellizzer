Fagonia olivieri f. jolyi

Fagonia olivieri f. jolyi, commonly known as the Joly's fagonia, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, often growing on rocky soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
