Ficus murrayana

Ficus murrayana, commonly known as the Murray's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Ficus murrayana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

It prefers warm, humid environments and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
