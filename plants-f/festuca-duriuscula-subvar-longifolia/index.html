Festuca Duriuscula Subvar. Longifolia

Festuca duriuscula subvar. longifolia is a resilient grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and nutrient availability. This subspecies is characterized by its long, slender leaves and compact growth habit. It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and preventing erosion in its native habitat. Due to its hardiness, it is also gaining attention for potential use in restoration projects.
