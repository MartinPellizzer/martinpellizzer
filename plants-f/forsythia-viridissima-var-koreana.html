Forsythia viridissima var. koreana

Forsythia viridissima var. koreana, commonly known as Korean golden trumpet, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to landscape designs.
