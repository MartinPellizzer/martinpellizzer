Ficus hirta var. setosa

Ficus hirta var. setosa, commonly known as the hairy fig or woolly fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a rough texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
