Fadogia schumanniana

Fadogia schumanniana, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and cylindrical pods. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are encased in hard, woody pods, and the plant is often cultivated for its edible seeds and nitrogen-fixing properties.

Fadogia schumanniana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
