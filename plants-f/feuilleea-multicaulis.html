Feuilleea multicaulis

Feuilleea multicaulis, commonly known as the multi-stemmed leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its multiple stems that emerge from a single root system, giving it a bushy appearance. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Feuilleea multicaulis thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to produce small, fragrant flowers makes it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
