Festuca Varia Var. Cyllenica

Bluegrass, scientifically known as Festuca varia var. cyllenica, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Its delicate blue-green foliage and intricate flowering structure make it a standout among grass species. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. While it is not commonly found in gardens, it is increasingly cultivated by enthusiasts who appreciate its unique appearance. Its resilience and aesthetic appeal have made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
