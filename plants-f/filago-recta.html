Filago recta

Filago recta, commonly known as the straight filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces seeds in small, fluffy clusters.

Filago recta is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
