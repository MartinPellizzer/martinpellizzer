Ficus celebica

Ficus celebica, commonly known as the Celebes fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Indonesia, particularly the islands of Sulawesi and the Moluccas. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

The fruit, which is edible, is a small, round fig that ripens to a yellowish color.
