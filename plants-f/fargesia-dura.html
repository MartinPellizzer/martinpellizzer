Fargesia dura

Fargesia dura, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, and thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal. Its leaves are lance-shaped and can reach up to 15 centimeters in length.

Fargesia dura is known for its hardiness and ability to grow in partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
