Froelichia nudicaulis subsp. lanigera

Froelichia nudicaulis subsp.

lanigera, commonly known as the woolly froelichia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
