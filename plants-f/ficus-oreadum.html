Ficus oreadum

Ficus oreadum, commonly known as the rock fig, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along its stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It is well adapted to rocky, coastal environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Ficus oreadum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and attractive foliage.
