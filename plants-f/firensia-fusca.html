Firensia fusca

Firensia fusca, commonly known as the dark firensia, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple foliage and striking orange-red flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and extreme temperature fluctuations. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Firensia fusca is also notable for its unique scent, which attracts specific pollinators like certain species of moths.
