Funckia grandis

Funckia grandis, commonly known as the grand lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and lavender. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are broad and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal. Funckia grandis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both horticultural collections and natural ecosystems.
