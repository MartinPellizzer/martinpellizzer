Foeniculum dulce

Foeniculum dulce, commonly known as fennel, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its feathery, aromatic foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters. The plant produces small, oval fruits that are typically used as a spice. Fennel thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and it is often cultivated for its seeds and leaves.
