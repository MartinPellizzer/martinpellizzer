Fragaria × ananassa var. cuneifolia

Fragaria × ananassa var. cuneifolia, commonly known as the cuneifolia strawberry, is a botanical variant of the cultivated strawberry.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that give it its species name, *cuneifolia*. This variety typically produces small, tart berries with a distinct flavor profile. It is often used in breeding programs to introduce desirable traits such as disease resistance or improved cold hardiness.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
