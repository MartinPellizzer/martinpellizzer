Frailea castanea subvar. spinosior

Frailea castanea subvar. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny frailea, is a compact cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in short, spiny bristles. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid conditions.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
