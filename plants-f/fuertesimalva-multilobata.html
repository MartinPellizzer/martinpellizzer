Fuertesimalva multilobata

Fuertesimalva multilobata, commonly known as the multilobed rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.
