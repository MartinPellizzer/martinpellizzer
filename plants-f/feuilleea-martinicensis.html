Feuilleea martinicensis

Feuilleea martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique feather plant, is a unique species native to the Caribbean island of Martinique.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported healing properties.

Feuilleea martinicensis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
