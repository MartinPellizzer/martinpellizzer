Ficus nuda

Ficus nuda, commonly known as the naked fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often hairless, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden within the leaf axils. Its fruit, a small, round fig, is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.

Ficus nuda thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
