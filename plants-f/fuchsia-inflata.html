Fuchsia inflata

Fuchsia inflata, commonly known as the inflated fuchsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. Its unique fruit, which is inflated and resembles a balloon, is a distinctive feature.

Fuchsia inflata thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
