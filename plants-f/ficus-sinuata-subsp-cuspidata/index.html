Ficus Sinuata Subsp. Cuspidata

Ficus sinuata subsp. cuspidata is a rare and striking plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique leaf shape, featuring deep lobes and a pointed tip that sets it apart from other ficus species. This plant thrives in warm, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening, though it demands careful attention to humidity and light levels. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure specimen among plant enthusiasts.
