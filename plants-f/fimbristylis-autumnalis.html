Fimbristylis autumnalis

Fimbristylis autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn sedge, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale green to brownish in color, and it blooms during the autumn season.

Fimbristylis autumnalis plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
