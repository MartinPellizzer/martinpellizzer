Feuilleea diadema

Feuilleea diadema, commonly known as the Crownleaf Plant, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of red and orange. Its leaves are velvety and have a distinctive crown-like arrangement, giving it its common name.

Feuilleea diadema thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
