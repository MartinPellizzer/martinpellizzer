Faramea grayumiana

Faramea grayumiana, commonly known as the red-flowered faramea, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Faramea grayumiana is native to tropical regions of South America and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
