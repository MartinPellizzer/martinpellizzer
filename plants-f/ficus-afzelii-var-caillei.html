Ficus afzelii var. caillei

Ficus afzelii var. caillei, commonly known as the Caille's fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible, green fig fruits that mature to a yellowish color. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.

Ficus afzelii var. caillei thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
