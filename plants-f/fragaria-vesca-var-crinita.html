Fragaria vesca var. crinita

Fragaria vesca var. crinita, commonly known as the curlytop strawberry, is a variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled leaves and small, edible fruits. This plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest floors. It has a fibrous root system and produces white flowers that bloom in early spring.

The leaves are trifoliate, with a hairy texture that gives it its common name.
