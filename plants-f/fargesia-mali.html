Fargesia mali

Fargesia mali, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its clumping growth habit and slender, green culms. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color, followed by the development of small, triangular seeds. Fargesia mali is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.

It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in shaded, moist environments.
