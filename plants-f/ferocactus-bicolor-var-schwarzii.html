Ferocactus bicolor var. schwarzii

Ferocactus bicolor var. schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwerzii barrel cactus, is a striking cactus native to the deserts of Baja California, Mexico.

It is characterized by its ribbed, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height, with a distinctive blue-green coloration. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a vibrant contrast to its otherwise muted appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
