Fagraea maingayi

Fagraea maingayi, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The tree has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its bark is smooth and reddish-brown in color. Fagraea maingayi is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy blooms and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its various pharmacological properties.
