Filix-mas spinulosa

Filix-mas spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, spiny fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to certain regions of Asia. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and small, spiny projections along the leaflets. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
