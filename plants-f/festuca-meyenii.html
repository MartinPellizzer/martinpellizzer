Festuca meyenii

Festuca meyenii, commonly known as the Yungas grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, dark green blades. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in cloud forests and grasslands. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil in its native habitat.

Festuca meyenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
