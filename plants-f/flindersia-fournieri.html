Flindersia fournieri

Flindersia fournieri, commonly known as the Java flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in humid climates and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

The tree is also valued for its durable wood, which has been historically used in construction and furniture-making.
