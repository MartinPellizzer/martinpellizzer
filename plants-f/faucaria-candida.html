Faucaria candida

Faucaria candida, commonly known as the white stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
