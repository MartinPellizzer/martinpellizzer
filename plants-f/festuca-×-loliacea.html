Festuca × loliacea

Festuca × loliacea, commonly known as tall fescue, is a perennial grass species that is widely cultivated for its ornamental and forage value.

It is a hybrid between Festuca arundinacea and Festuca ovina, exhibiting a combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and fine, dark green foliage that remains dense throughout the growing season. It is highly adaptable to various soil types and can thrive in a range of environmental conditions.

Festuca × loliacea is often used in lawns, pastures, and erosion control due to its durability and resilience.
