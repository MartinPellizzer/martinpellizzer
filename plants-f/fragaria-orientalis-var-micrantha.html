Fragaria orientalis var. micrantha

Fragaria orientalis var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-fruited strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically red when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and erect, hairy stems. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruits.
