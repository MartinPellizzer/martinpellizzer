Fargesia concinna

Fargesia concinna, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, upright culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, typically in late spring. Fargesia concinna is well-suited for shaded environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its growth habit makes it an excellent choice for creating a natural, flowing appearance in garden settings.
