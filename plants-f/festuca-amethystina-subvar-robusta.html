Festuca amethystina subvar. robusta

Festuca amethystina subvar. robusta, commonly known as Amethyst Festuca, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and distinctive purple to violet-colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is fine and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds ornamental value to gardens.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to form attractive, low-maintenance ground cover.
