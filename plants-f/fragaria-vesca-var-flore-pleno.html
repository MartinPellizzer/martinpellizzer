Fragaria vesca var. flore-pleno

Fragaria vesca var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the everbearing strawberry, is a variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is characterized by its dense, double flowers that bloom throughout the growing season, often producing fruit in multiple flushes. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, edible berries similar to the common strawberry. It thrives in cool climates and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and permaculture systems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and extended fruiting period.
