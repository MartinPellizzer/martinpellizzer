Filix-mas cristata var. floridana

Filix-mas cristata var. floridana, commonly known as the Florida basket fern, is a variant of the common holly fern.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy fronds that form a compact, upright rosette. The plant features compound leaves with multiple pinnae, giving it a lush, fern-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to the southeastern United States.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to humid, tropical climates.
