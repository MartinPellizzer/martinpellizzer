Ficus stenothyrsus

Ficus stenothyrsus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, including countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. It produces small, clustered figs that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Ficus stenothyrsus thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
