Fritillaria fujiangensis

Fritillaria fujiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan fritillary, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous stem and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing clusters of pale yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are grass-like and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Fritillaria fujiangensis is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its medicinal and horticultural properties.
