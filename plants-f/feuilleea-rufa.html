Feuilleea rufa

Feuilleea rufa, commonly known as the crimson leaf fern, is a unique plant characterized by its striking red-brown fronds.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are tightly clustered at the base. Feuilleea rufa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant coloration and delicate appearance.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
