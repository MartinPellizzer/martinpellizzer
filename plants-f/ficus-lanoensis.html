Ficus lanoensis

Ficus lanoensis, commonly known as the Lanoensis Fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus lanoensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
