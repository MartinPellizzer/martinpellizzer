Ficus deltoidea var. borneensis

Ficus deltoidea var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean fig, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round fruits that are a vital food source for various wildlife.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
