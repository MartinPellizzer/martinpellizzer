Franciscodendron

Franciscodendron, commonly known as the "Belize hibiscus," is a genus of flowering plants in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Belize, Mexico, and Guatemala. Franciscodendron species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
