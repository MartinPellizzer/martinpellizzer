Feuilleea oblonga

Feuilleea oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf fern, is a species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Feuilleea oblonga is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
