Fagopyrum fagopyrum

Fagopyrum fagopyrum, commonly known as common buckwheat, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright stems and palmate leaves with three to five lobes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Common buckwheat is also cultivated for its edible seeds and has historical use in traditional medicine.
