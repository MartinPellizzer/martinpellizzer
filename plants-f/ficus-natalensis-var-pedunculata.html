Ficus natalensis var. pedunculata

Ficus natalensis var. pedunculata, commonly known as the Natal fig or Natal weeping fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by fig-like fruits that are edible for certain animals but not for humans. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
