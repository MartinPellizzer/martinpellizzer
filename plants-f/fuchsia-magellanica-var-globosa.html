Fuchsia magellanica var. globosa

Fuchsia magellanica var. globosa, commonly known as the globular fuchsia, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, purple, and pink. The leaves are ovate and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. This variety is well-suited for container gardening or as a decorative hedge due to its compact form.

It thrives in cool, moist climates and requires partial to full sun for optimal flowering.
