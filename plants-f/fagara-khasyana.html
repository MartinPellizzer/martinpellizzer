Fagara khasyana

Fagara khasyana, commonly known as the Himalayan fagara, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and India.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal umbels. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to dark purple as they mature.
