Froelichia alata

Froelichia alata, commonly known as the winged lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are often pink or red in color. The plant has distinctive, wing-like bracts that surround its inflorescence, giving it its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Froelichia alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
