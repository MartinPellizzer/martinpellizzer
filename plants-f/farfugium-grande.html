Farfugium grande

Farfugium grande, commonly known as the giant swamp daisy, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features large, waxy, yellow-orange flowers that are surrounded by broad, spoon-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually broad and have a distinctive, wavy margin.

Farfugium grande is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
