Fritillaria messanensis var. hispanica

Fritillaria messanensis var. hispanica, commonly known as the Spanish fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow with a distinctive black netting pattern. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
