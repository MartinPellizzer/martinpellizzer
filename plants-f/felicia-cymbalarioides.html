Felicia cymbalarioides

Felicia cymbalarioides, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for xeriscaping.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
