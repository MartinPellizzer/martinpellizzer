Filago cretensis

Filago cretensis, commonly known as the Cretan filago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, often arranged in a composite head.

Filago cretensis is known for its resilience and is often found in coastal and inland areas of Crete.
