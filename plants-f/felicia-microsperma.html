Felicia microsperma

Felicia microsperma, commonly known as the small-seeded fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, yellow-orange flowers bloom in spring, and the plant produces tiny, elongated seeds.

Felicia microsperma is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
