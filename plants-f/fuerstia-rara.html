Fuerstia rara

Fuerstia rara, commonly known as the rare frosted fern, is a delicate perennial plant native to the temperate rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, silver-green fronds that give it a distinctive, almost frost-like appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

Due to habitat loss, Fuerstia rara is considered endangered, highlighting the need for protective measures to preserve its existence.
