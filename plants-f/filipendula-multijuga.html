Filipendula multijuga

Filipendula multijuga, commonly known as the meadow sweet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. Filipendula multijuga is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
