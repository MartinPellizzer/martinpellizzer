Ficus oleifolia var. memecylifolia

Ficus oleifolia var. memecylifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often display striking red or purple veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
