Fedia puberula

Fedia puberula, commonly known as the silky fedia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
