Fimbristylis decumbens

Fimbristylis decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous tiny, three-ranked flowers. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, typically covered with fine hairs.

Fimbristylis decumbens is commonly found in wetlands, marshes, and other moist habitats across tropical and subtropical regions.
