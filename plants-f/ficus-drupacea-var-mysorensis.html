Ficus drupacea var. mysorensis

Ficus drupacea var. mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the southern regions of India, particularly the state of Karnataka. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit, which is a small fig, is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.
