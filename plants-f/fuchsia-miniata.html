Fuchsia miniata

Fuchsia miniata, commonly known as the miniature fuchsia, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.

Fuchsia miniata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
