Ficus aurita var. celebica

Ficus aurita var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus aurita var. celebica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable environments.
