Flindersia australis

Flindersia australis, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a tropical tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth surface with a glossy appearance. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well adapted to coastal and tropical climates.
