Fagara curbeloi

Fagara curbeloi, commonly known as the Mexican prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces edible fruits, known as tunas, which are used in traditional cuisine. Fagara curbeloi thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
