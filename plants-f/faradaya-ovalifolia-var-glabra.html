Faradaya ovalifolia var. glabra

Faradaya ovalifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth faradaya, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
