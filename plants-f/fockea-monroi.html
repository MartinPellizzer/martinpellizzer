Fockea monroi

Fockea monroi, commonly known as the Cape fockea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
