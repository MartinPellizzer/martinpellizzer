Ficus dimorpha var. scabra

Ficus dimorpha var. scabra, commonly known as the rough-leaved ficus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rough, and textured leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with thick, waxy foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
