Fumaria macrosepala var. atlantica

Fumaria macrosepala var. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic yellowcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and alternate, pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in coastal areas or along riverbanks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
