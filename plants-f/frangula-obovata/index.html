Frangula Obovata

Frangula obovata is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern united states. it is known for its striking red berries that attract birds and other wildlife. the plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. its leaves are broad and oval shaped, turning yellow in the fall. it is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
