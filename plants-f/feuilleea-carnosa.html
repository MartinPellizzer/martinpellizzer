Feuilleea carnosa

Feuilleea carnosa, commonly known as the thickleaf succulent, is a unique plant native to arid regions.

It features thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The leaves are typically fleshy and vary in color from green to reddish tones. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
