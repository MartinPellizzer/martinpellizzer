Fedia ciliata

Fedia ciliata, commonly known as the woolly flax or silky flax, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Fedia ciliata is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its fibrous stems and leaves have been historically used for textile production and medicinal purposes.
