Ficaria verna f. disjuncta

Ficaria verna f. disjuncta, commonly known as the yellow starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of rounded, yellowish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable indicator species for certain ecological conditions.
