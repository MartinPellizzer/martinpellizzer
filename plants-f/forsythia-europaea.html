Forsythia europaea

Forsythia europaea, commonly known as the European forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun.

Forsythia europaea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
