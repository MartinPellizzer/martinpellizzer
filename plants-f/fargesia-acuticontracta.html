Fargesia acuticontracta

Fargesia acuticontracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf evergreen bamboo, is a cold-hardy plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. This bamboo species is known for its slow growth and compact form, making it suitable for small garden spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, and it is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Fargesia acuticontracta is also valued for its ability to provide year-round visual interest in the garden.
