Fragaria nilgerrensis var. mairei

Fragaria nilgerrensis var. mairei, commonly known as the Yunnan strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, edible berries. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its flowers are white with five petals, and the berries are red when ripe, containing small seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a cultivated fruit species.
