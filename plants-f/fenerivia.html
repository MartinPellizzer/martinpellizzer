Fenerivia

Fenerivia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in winter. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Fenerivia is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
