Fatsia polycarpa

Fatsia polycarpa, commonly known as the Japanese fatsia, is a species of flowering plant in the Araliaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Fatsia polycarpa prefers partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
