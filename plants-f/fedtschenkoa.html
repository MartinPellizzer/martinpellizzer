Fedtschenkoa

Fedtschenkoa, commonly known as the giant panda plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to the subtropical regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, giving it a dramatic and striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Fedtschenkoa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
