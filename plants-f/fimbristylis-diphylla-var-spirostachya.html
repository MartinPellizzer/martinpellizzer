Fimbristylis diphylla var. spirostachya

Fimbristylis diphylla var. spirostachya, commonly known as the spiral-stemmed flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture, while its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers.
