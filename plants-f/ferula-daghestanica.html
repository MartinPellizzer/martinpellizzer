Ferula daghestanica

Ferula daghestanica, commonly known as the Caucasian ferula, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stem and large, umbrella-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces yellow flowers and has aromatic, resinous leaves. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Ferula daghestanica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
