Ficus irisana var. validicaudata

Ficus irisana var. validicaudata, commonly known as the Irisana Ficus, is a unique variant of the Ficus genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that often display a gradient of colors, ranging from deep green to variegated white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental foliage makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.
