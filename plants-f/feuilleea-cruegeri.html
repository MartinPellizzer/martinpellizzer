Feuilleea cruegeri

Feuilleea cruegeri, commonly known as the Crimson Leaf Orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that range in color from deep red to emerald green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
