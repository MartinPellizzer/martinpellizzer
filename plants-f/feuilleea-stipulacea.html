Feuilleea stipulacea

Feuilleea stipulacea, commonly known as the leafy stipule plant, is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with prominent stipules along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its growth habit is upright, with a tendency to spread via rhizomes.

Feuilleea stipulacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
