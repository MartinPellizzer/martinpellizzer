Fumaria capreolata var. bastardii

Fumaria capreolata var. bastardii, commonly known as the bastard fumitory, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces elongated, slender capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a common subject in botanical studies and traditional herbal medicine.
