Fugosia thespesioides

Fugosia thespesioides, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers. The plant blooms in early spring, typically from March to May, and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

However, it can be invasive in some areas if not managed carefully.
