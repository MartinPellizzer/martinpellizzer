Ferdinandusa pubescens

Ferdinandusa pubescens, commonly known as the silky fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a soft, lacy appearance. The plant features a short, unbranched stem with dense, hairy foliage that contributes to its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
