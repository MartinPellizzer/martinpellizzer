Forsythia × vitellina

Forsythia × vitellina, commonly known as the golden trumpet bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Forsythia × vitellina is relatively hardy and adaptable to a variety of soil types and growing conditions.
