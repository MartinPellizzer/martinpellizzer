Fargesia conferta

Fargesia conferta, commonly known as the Yunnan evergreen bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, dark green foliage. This plant is a member of the grass family and is known for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded environments. Fargesia conferta is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its graceful appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for creating natural screens or as part of a rock garden.
