Fokienia hodginsii

Fokienia hodginsii, commonly known as the Chinese cliff-leaf.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to China, characterized by its unique foliage and aromatic wood. The plant features long, needle-like leaves that are often clustered in whorls at the ends of the branches. It thrives in temperate climates and is known for its slow growth rate.

Fokienia hodginsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
