Firmiana migeodii

Firmiana migeodii, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The tree produces showy, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
