Fragaria vesca var. elatior

Fragaria vesca var. elatior, commonly known as the wild strawberry or Alpine strawberry, is a variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is a small perennial herb with trifoliate leaves and white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often more intensely flavored than common strawberries. It thrives in cool, temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary uses in specialty foods.
