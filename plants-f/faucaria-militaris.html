Faucaria militaris

Faucaria militaris, commonly known as the soldier's shield, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Faucaria militaris is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
