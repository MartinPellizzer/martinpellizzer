Funkia ovata f. latemarginata

Funkia ovata f. latemarginata, commonly known as the broad-margin arrowhead, is a cultivar of the arrowhead plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are broad and have a distinctive white margin. The plant produces small, white, arrowhead-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various aquatic environments.
