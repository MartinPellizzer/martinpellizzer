Fuchsia macrostemma var. conica

Fuchsia macrostemma var. conica, commonly known as the conical fuchsia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear vibrant pink to purple flowers, which are rich in nectar and attract a variety of pollinators. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
