Freylinia rigida

Freylinia rigida, commonly known as the rigid freylinia, is a member of the Lythraceae family.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, specifically found in the Western Cape region. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Freylinia rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
