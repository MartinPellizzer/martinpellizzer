Foeniculum multiradiatum

Foeniculum multiradiatum, commonly known as the radiate fennel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its aromatic seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both gardens and herbal practices.
