Faucaria tigrina

Faucaria tigrina, commonly known as the tiger tail cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Faucaria tigrina is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and unique aesthetic.
