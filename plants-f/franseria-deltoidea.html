Franseria deltoidea

Franseria deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid franseria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
