Fargesia dracocephala

Fargesia dracocephala, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or blue butterfly bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. This plant is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and striking blue-green foliage that turns a deeper green in winter. It is a cold-hardy plant that thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and shaded borders.

Fargesia dracocephala is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form compact, decorative clumps over time.
