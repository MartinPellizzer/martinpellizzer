Ficus dusenii

Ficus dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that are often heart-shaped. The plant produces small, green figs that are enclosed within a spherical, smooth-walled syconium. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage.

Ficus dusenii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
