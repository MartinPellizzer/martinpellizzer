Felicia knella

Felicia knella, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and dandelions. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Felicia knella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
