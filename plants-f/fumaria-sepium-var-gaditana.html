Fumaria sepium var. gaditana

Fumaria sepium var. gaditana, commonly known as the Spanish glory-of-the-snow, is a variant of the common fumitory.

It is a herbaceous annual plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. Its flowers are small, nodding, and typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and other open habitats.
