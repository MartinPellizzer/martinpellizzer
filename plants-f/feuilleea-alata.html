Feuilleea alata

Feuilleea alata, commonly known as the winged leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are broad and elongated, with a serrated edge that gives them a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Feuilleea alata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
