Filago × similata

Filago × similata, commonly known as the common filago, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange disk flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. Filago × similata typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
