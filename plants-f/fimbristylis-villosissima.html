Fimbristylis villosissima

Fimbristylis villosissima, commonly known as the silky flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in wetland or marshy environments, often forming dense mats on the ground.

Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil, making it ecologically significant in its habitat.
