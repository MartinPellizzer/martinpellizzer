Ficus hirta var. palmatiloba

Ficus hirta var. palmatiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible figs that are typically yellow or green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus hirta var. palmatiloba is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
