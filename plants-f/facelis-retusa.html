Facelis retusa

Facelis retusa, commonly known as the smoothleaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Facelis retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
