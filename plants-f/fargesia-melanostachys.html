Fargesia melanostachys

Fargesia melanostachys, commonly known as the black-stemmed arrow bamboo, is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo native to China.

It features slender, dark green culms that are often tinged with purple or black, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in summer, which are followed by bright red berries. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, upright growth habit.

Fargesia melanostachys prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.
