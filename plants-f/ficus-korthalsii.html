Ficus korthalsii

Ficus korthalsii, commonly known as the silver ficus, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by green, round fruits. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities.

Ficus korthalsii is also known for its slow growth and ease of care, making it a favored choice for novice plant enthusiasts.
