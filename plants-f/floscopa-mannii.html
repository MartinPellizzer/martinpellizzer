Floscopa mannii

Floscopa mannii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a milky sap that is toxic and can cause skin irritation. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often with a velvety texture.
