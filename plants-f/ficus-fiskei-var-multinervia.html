Ficus fiskei var. multinervia

Ficus fiskei var. multinervia, commonly known as the Kauai fig, is a tropical plant native to Hawaii.

It is a member of the Moraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are followed by edible figs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a notable species in Hawaiian flora.
