Flemingia lineata

Flemingia lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed flemingia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale yellow.

Flemingia lineata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
