Ficus branderhorstii

Ficus branderhorstii, commonly known as the Brandes fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus branderhorstii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
