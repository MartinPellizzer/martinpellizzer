Flindersia bourjotiana

Flindersia bourjotiana, commonly known as the Java flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, round fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
