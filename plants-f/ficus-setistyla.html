Ficus setistyla

Ficus setistyla, commonly known as the upright fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and unique, upright growth habit. Its small, clustered flowers are typically followed by edible, round fig fruits.

Ficus setistyla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor environments.
