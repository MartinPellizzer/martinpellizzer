Ficus ellipsoidea

Ficus ellipsoidea, commonly known as the elliptic fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Ficus ellipsoidea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
