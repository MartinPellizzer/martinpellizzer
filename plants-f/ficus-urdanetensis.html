Ficus urdanetensis

Ficus urdanetensis, commonly known as the UrdaNet fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Venezuela. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Ficus urdanetensis is typically found in humid, lowland areas with rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
