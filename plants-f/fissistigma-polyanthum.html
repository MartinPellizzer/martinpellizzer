Fissistigma polyanthum

Fissistigma polyanthum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale pink, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Fissistigma polyanthum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
