Faucaria longidens

Faucaria longidens, commonly known as the long-toothed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a white powdery coating, known as farina. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Faucaria longidens is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.
