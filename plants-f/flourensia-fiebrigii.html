Flourensia fiebrigii

Flourensia fiebrigii, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The flowers bloom in the spring and are pollinated by various insects, including bees and butterflies.
