Filix-mas rigida

Filix-mas rigida, commonly known as the rigid holly fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its robust, upright fronds that can grow up to several feet in height. The plant has a creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically divided into lance-shaped segments with a glossy, dark green appearance.

Filix-mas rigida thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
