Fabiana lanuginosa

Fabiana lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly fabiana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hair. Fabiana lanuginosa is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
