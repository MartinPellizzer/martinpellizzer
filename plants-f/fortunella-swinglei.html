Fortunella swinglei

Fortunella swinglei, commonly known as the calamondin orange, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is closely related to true citrus plants. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, leathery rind and a sour, acidic flavor. Its leaves are glossy and ovate, while its flowers are white and fragrant.
