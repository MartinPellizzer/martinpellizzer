Funkia ovata var. undulata

Funkia ovata var. undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf daylily, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
