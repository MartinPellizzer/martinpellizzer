Felicia abyssinica subsp. abyssinica

Felicia abyssinica subsp.

abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features bright yellow daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its botanical structure includes narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
