Fargesia angustissima

Fargesia angustissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf evergreen bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. This plant is evergreen, retaining its foliage throughout the year, and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Fargesia angustissima is also valued for its ability to form dense clumps, making it an excellent choice for creating natural screens or adding texture to garden spaces.
