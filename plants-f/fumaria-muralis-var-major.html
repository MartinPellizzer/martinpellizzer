Fumaria muralis var. major

Fumaria muralis var. major, commonly known as the wall rue, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Papaveraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer.
