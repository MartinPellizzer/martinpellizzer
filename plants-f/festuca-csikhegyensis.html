Festuca csikhegyensis

Festuca csikhegyensis, commonly known as the Csikhegy fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. Festuca csikhegyensis is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including cold temperatures and limited nutrients.

Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny seeds.
