Feuilleea boliviana

Feuilleea boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian feather plant, is a unique species native to the high-altitude regions of Bolivia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage that resembles the structure of a feather, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, which helps to reflect sunlight and reduce water loss.

Feuilleea boliviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
