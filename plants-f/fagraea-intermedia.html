Fagraea intermedia

Fagraea intermedia, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea intermedia is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
