Feuilleea lusoria

Feuilleea lusoria, commonly known as the Lusoria Leaf Plant, is a rare evergreen shrub native to the subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are usually a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Feuilleea lusoria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low-maintenance in suitable climates.
