Ficus sycomorus

Ficus sycomorus, commonly known as the sacred fig, is a species of fig tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The tree is known for its rapid growth and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. Its fruit, the fig, is edible and has been used for food and traditional medicine.

The plant is also significant in religious and cultural contexts across various African societies.
