Feuilleea aeschynomenoides

Feuilleea aeschynomenoides, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling mist or clouds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
