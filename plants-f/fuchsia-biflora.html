Fuchsia biflora

Fuchsia biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Fuchsia biflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
