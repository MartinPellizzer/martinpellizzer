Fragariastrum speciosum

Fragariastrum speciosum, commonly known as the showy strawberry, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It produces small, edible berries that are typically red, with a sweet and tart flavor. The plant has trifoliate leaves and grows in clusters, often in moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are white with five petals and are pollinated by insects.

The fruit develops from the flower and is an important food source for various wildlife species.
