Ferulago crassicosta

Ferulago crassicosta, commonly known as the broad-ribed ferulago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky or gravelly soils, often in sunny environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have prominent, wavy margins. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clustered inflorescences, blooming in late spring to early summer.

The species is characterized by its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
