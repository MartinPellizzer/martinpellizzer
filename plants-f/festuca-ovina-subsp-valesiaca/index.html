Festuca Ovina Subsp. Valesiaca

Festuca ovina subsp. valesiaca is a hardy grass species commonly found in alpine and subalpine regions. It thrives in rocky soils and cool climates, often growing in mountainous areas across Europe and parts of Asia. This subspecies is known for its dense, low-growing foliage and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife. Due to its resilience, it is often used in ecological restoration projects.
