Filago Pyramidata Var. Obovata

Filago pyramidata var. obovata is a lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. This variant is distinguished by its obovate leaves and compact growth habit, making it well adapted to harsh conditions. It is commonly found in regions with low precipitation and high solar exposure, often growing on rocky or sandy soils. Despite its resilience, it remains underappreciated in botanical studies and horticultural practices. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for further research and conservation efforts.
