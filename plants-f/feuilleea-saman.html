Feuilleea saman

Feuilleea saman, commonly known as the sacred leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional ceremonies and for their medicinal properties.

Feuilleea saman is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for aesthetic purposes.
