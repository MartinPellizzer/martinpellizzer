Flemingia nilgheriensis

Flemingia nilgheriensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri Flemingia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to its native habitat.
