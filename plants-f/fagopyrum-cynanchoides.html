Fagopyrum cynanchoides

Fagopyrum cynanchoides, commonly known as the Chinese buckwheat, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
