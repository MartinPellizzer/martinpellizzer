Ferula koelziana

Ferula koelziana, commonly known as the Tibetan cow parsley, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a distinctive, hollow, cylindrical stem. Its leaves are finely divided and have a strong, spicy odor, similar to other members of its family.
