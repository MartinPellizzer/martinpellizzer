Fouquieria splendens subsp. breviflora

Fouquieria splendens subsp.

breviflora, commonly known as the desert candle, is a shrub-like plant native to the Sonoran Desert. It is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the spring.

The plant has a thick, woody stem that branches into many small, narrow leaves. It is well adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system to access water.

Its slow growth and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in desert regions.
