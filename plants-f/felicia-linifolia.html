Felicia linifolia

Felicia linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, surrounded by narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
