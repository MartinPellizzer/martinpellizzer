Ferocactus chrysacanthus f. rubrispinus

Ferocactus chrysacanthus f. rubrispinus, commonly known as the crimson spines ferocactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems and dense, spiny foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its striking red spines and is often cultivated in desert gardens.
