Felicia karooica

Felicia karooica, commonly known as the Karoo daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. It features a low-growing rosette of silver-gray leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers.

Felicia karooica is often used in ornamental gardening due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.
