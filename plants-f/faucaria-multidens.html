Faucaria multidens

Faucaria multidens, commonly known as the many-toothed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
