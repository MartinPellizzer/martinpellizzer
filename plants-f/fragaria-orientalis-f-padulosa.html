Fragaria orientalis f. padulosa

Fragaria orientalis f. padulosa, commonly known as the Padul strawberry, is a botanical variant of the oriental strawberry.

It is characterized by its small, elongated fruits that are typically red in color and have a delicate flavor. This plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It has a low-growing habit with trifoliate leaves and produces white flowers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
