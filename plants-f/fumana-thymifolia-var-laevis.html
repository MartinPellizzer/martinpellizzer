Fumana thymifolia var. laevis

Fumana thymifolia var. laevis, commonly known as smooth fumana thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that are smooth and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its foliage is used in culinary and medicinal applications, similar to other thyme species.
