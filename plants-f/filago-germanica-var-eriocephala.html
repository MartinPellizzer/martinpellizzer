Filago germanica var. eriocephala

Filago germanica var. eriocephala, commonly known as the hairy filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, ascending stems covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in loose clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
