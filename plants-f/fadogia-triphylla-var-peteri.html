Fadogia triphylla var. peteri

Fadogia triphylla var. peteri, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods containing seeds that are used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry savanna regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
