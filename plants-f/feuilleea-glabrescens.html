Feuilleea glabrescens

Feuilleea glabrescens, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with clusters of small, scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. Feuilleea glabrescens thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden landscapes.
