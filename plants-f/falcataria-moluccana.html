Falcataria moluccana

Falcataria moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, greyish-brown bark. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and often have a lanceolate shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, elongated pods containing flat, dark seeds.

Falcataria moluccana is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in construction and furniture making.
