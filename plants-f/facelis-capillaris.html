Facelis capillaris

Facelis capillaris, commonly known as the silky senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
