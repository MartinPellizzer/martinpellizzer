Fumanopsis laevis

Fumanopsis laevis, commonly known as the smooth fumaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Fumanopsis laevis is primarily known for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
