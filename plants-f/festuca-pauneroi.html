Festuca pauneroi

Festuca pauneroi, commonly known as the Pauneroi fescue, is a species of grass native to certain regions of Europe.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Festuca pauneroi is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and aesthetic qualities.
