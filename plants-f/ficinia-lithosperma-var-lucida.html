Ficinia lithosperma var. lucida

Ficinia lithosperma var. lucida, commonly known as the shining rock fig, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, attracting various bird species.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in drought conditions.
