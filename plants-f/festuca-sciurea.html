Festuca sciurea

Festuca sciurea, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its flowering spikes are slender and often produce small, inconspicuous flowers.

Festuca sciurea is often used in ornamental landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
