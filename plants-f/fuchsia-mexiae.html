Fuchsia mexiae

Fuchsia mexiae, commonly known as the Mexican fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a member of the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink to purple bracts that enclose the smaller, tubular flowers. Fuchsia mexiae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
