Fagraea berteroana var. pogas

Fagraea berteroana var. pogas, commonly known as the Pogas fagraea, is a shrub or small tree native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in cloud forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections in suitable climates.
