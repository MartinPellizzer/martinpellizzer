Fumana spachii var. grandiflora

Fumana spachii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered fumana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, linear seeds in its composite flower heads.

Fumana spachii var. grandiflora is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
