Fumaria muralis proles affinis

Fumaria muralis proles affinis, commonly known as the wall rue, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in rocky or shaded areas and is often found growing on walls or in moist, woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the fumariaceae family.
