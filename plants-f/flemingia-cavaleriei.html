Flemingia cavaleriei

Flemingia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
