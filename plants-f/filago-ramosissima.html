Filago ramosissima

Filago ramosissima, commonly known as the branched filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Filago ramosissima is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
