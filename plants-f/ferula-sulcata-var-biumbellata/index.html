Ferula Sulcata Var. Biumbellata

Ferula sulcata var. biumbellata is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. Known for its unique, spiraling leaf structure and vibrant yellow flowers, it stands out among other members of the Umbelliferae family. This variant of Ferula sulcata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts. Its resilience to harsh conditions makes it a fascinating subject for both scientific study and horticultural interest. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of flora.
