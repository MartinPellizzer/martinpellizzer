Fructesca

Fructesca, commonly known as the golden fruit vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Fructesca thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and attractive fruit clusters.
