Frankenia aucheri

Frankenia aucheri, commonly known as the silverleaf frankenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its overall look. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Frankenia aucheri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
