Festuca venusta

Festuca venusta, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a species of grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Festuca venusta is a perennial species, often forming dense tufts or clumps.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscaping.
