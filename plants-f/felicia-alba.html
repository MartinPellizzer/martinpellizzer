Felicia alba

Felicia alba, commonly known as the white fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. Felicia alba is known for its drought tolerance and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.

Its foliage is typically blue-green, adding visual interest to gardens.
