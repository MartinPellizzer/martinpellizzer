Felicia brachyglossa

Felicia brachyglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its scientific name reflects its distinctive leaf structure and its affinity for sunlight.
