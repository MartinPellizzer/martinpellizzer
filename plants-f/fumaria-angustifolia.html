Fumaria angustifolia

Fumaria angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leafed fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a slightly hairy surface.

Fumaria angustifolia is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
