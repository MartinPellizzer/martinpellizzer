Ficus scassellatii subsp. scassellatii

Ficus scassellatii subsp.

scassellatii, commonly known as the African rubber fig, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by greenish-white fruit that turns red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
