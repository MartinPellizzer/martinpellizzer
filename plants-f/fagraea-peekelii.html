Fagraea peekelii

Fagraea peekelii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its foliage. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Fagraea peekelii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
