Fimbristemma gonoloboides

Fimbristemma gonoloboides, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a dense canopy and smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by yellow or orange fruits resembling starfruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a waxy texture with a serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit, which is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.
