Funtumia latifolia

Funtumia latifolia, commonly known as the African tulip tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.

Funtumia latifolia is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
