Feuilleea coruscans

Feuilleea coruscans, commonly known as the shimmering leaf plant, is a striking species native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that exhibit a metallic sheen under sunlight, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming during the wet season. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil.

Feuilleea coruscans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
