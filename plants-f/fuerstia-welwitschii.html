Fuerstia welwitschii

Fuerstia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschia, is a unique plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant has a woody trunk that can reach heights of over ten meters. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, and its seeds are enclosed in hard, woody capsules.

Fuerstia welwitschii is adapted to extreme arid conditions, with a slow growth rate and a long lifespan, sometimes exceeding 2,000 years.
