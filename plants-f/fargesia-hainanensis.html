Fargesia hainanensis

Fargesia hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan bamboo, is a species native to China, specifically the Hainan Island.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that form dense clumps. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or pale yellow in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its graceful appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
