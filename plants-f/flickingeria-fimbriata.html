Flickingeria fimbriata

Flickingeria fimbriata, commonly known as the fringe-leaved orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves that have a fringed appearance along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
