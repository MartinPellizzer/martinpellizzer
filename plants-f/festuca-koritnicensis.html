Festuca koritnicensis

Festuca koritnicensis, commonly known as the Koritnic grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Festuca koritnicensis is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.

Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny flowers.
