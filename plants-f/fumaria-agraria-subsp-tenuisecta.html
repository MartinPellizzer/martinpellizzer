Fumaria agraria subsp. tenuisecta

Fumaria agraria subsp.

tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed crown-vetch, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer.

The leaves are pinnately divided into several narrow segments, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
