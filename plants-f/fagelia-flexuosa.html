Fagelia flexuosa

Fagelia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy, and often twisted fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spore-bearing cones. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Fagelia flexuosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
