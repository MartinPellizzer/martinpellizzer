Ficus ataktophylla

Ficus ataktophylla, commonly known as the Ataktophylla fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
