Fregirardia vargasii

Fregirardia vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas firebush, is a flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange tubular flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
