Forestiera chiapensis

It belongs to the Oleaceae family, which includes plants like jasmine and lilac. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and opposite, providing a dense canopy in its natural habitat.
