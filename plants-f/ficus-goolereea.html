Ficus goolereea

Ficus goolereea, commonly known as the Goolereea fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.

The fruit, which is a small fig, is edible and supports local wildlife.
