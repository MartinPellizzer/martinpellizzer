Fritillaria chuanganensis

Fritillaria chuanganensis, commonly known as the Chuangan fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are grass-like and often withered by the time flowering occurs.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
