Fumaria schrammii

Fumaria schrammii, commonly known as Schramm's smokeplant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fumariaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Its delicate appearance and early blooming period make it a notable species in its native habitat.
