Fallopia filipes

Fallopia filipes, commonly known as goat's beard, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, branched stems that often grow along the ground or climb over other vegetation. The plant produces small, white to greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense, feathery clusters. Its leaves are alternate, with long petioles and finely divided, thread-like leaflets.

Fallopia filipes is often found in moist environments such as stream banks and woodland edges.
