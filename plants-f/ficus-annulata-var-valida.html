Ficus annulata var. valida

Ficus annulata var. valida, commonly known as the Indian rubber plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. It is a versatile plant that can be grown as a houseplant or outdoors in warm climates.

Ficus annulata var. valida is valued for its air-purifying properties and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
