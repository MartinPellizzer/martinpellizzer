Forsythia × intermedia f. primulina

Forsythia × intermedia f. primulina, commonly known as the primrose-flowered forsythia, is a hybrid shrub valued for its ornamental qualities.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to produce an abundance of showy, yellow flowers. The flowers are typically more delicate and resemble those of primroses, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscape design for its early spring bloom and hardy nature.
