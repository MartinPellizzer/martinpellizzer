Ficus granatum var. minor

Ficus granatum var. minor, commonly known as the Indian rubber plant or small red fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with red or purple veins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that give way to red, fleshy fruits resembling small apples. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular houseplant.
