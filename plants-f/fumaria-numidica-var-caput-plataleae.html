Fumaria numidica var. caput-plataleae

Fumaria numidica var. caput-plataleae, commonly known as the Cape Verde fumitory, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, linear leaves and has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
