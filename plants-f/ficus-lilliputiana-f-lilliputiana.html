Ficus lilliputiana f. lilliputiana

Ficus lilliputiana f. lilliputiana, commonly known as the miniature fig tree, is a compact, slow-growing shrub that thrives in warm, humid environments.

It is characterized by its small, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with creamy white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by tiny, edible fig fruits. It is highly adaptable and can be grown both indoors and outdoors in suitable climates.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and bonsai cultivation.
