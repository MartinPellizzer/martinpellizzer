Fuchsia killipii

Fuchsia killipii, commonly known as the Hawaiian fuchsia, is a species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant features showy, pendulous flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Fuchsia killipii is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and thrives in well-drained soils.
