Fordiophyton zhuangiae

Fordiophyton zhuangiae, commonly known as the Zhuangiae orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions with rich soil. Its flowers are small but striking, with a complex arrangement of petals and sepals that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
