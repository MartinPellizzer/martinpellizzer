Fagopyrum pleioramosum

Fagopyrum pleioramosum, commonly known as the many-branched buckwheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other buckwheat species. The plant is characterized by its upright stems, which can grow up to 1 meter in height, and its simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in late summer.

The plant is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
