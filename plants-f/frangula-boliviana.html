Frangula boliviana

Frangula boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian buckthorn, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Frangula boliviana thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
