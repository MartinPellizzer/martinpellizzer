Ficus rupestris

Ficus rupestris, commonly known as the rock fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits.

Ficus rupestris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
