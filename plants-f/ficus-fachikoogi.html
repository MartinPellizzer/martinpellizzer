Ficus fachikoogi

Ficus fachikoogi, commonly known as the Fakikoogi fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor.

Ficus fachikoogi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
