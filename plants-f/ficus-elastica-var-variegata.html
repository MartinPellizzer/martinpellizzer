Ficus elastica var. variegata

Ficus elastica var. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Rubber Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves with striking white and cream variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify the air and adapt to various indoor conditions further enhances its appeal.
