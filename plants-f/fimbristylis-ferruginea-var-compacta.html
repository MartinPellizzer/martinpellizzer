Fimbristylis ferruginea var. compacta

Fimbristylis ferruginea var. compacta, commonly known as the compact flat sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale brown flowers. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

This variant is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to tolerate periodic flooding.
