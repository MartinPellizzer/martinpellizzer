Festuca hervieri subsp. costei

Festuca hervieri subsp.

costei, commonly known as the Costean fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces compact inflorescences with spikelets that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscaping projects.
