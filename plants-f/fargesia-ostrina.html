Fargesia ostrina

Fargesia ostrina, commonly known as the silver lace bamboo, is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo native to China.

It features elegant, arching stems that are covered in a silvery-gray sheath, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a soft, velvety texture with a pale green color. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping for its graceful form.

This plant is relatively low maintenance and thrives in partial shade to full sun, depending on the climate.
