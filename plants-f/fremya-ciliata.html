Fremya ciliata

Fremya ciliata, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are compound, with small leaflets arranged in a feathery pattern.

Fremya ciliata is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
