Fimbrorchis havilandii

Fimbrorchis havilandii, commonly known as the Haviland's basket orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, flowering stem and distinctive, basket-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as pine flatwoods and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small, greenish-white with a yellowish lip.

Fimbrorchis havilandii is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific habitat conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
