Fuchsia gracilis var. tenella

Fuchsia gracilis var. tenella, commonly known as the compact fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a container plant. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

Fuchsia gracilis var. tenella is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a decorative yet easy-to-care-for plant.
