Fraxinus viridis var. nobilis

Fraxinus viridis var. nobilis, commonly known as the green ash.

It is a deciduous tree native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant features opposite, simple, and serrated leaves that are typically a vibrant green during the growing season. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in catkins, and it produces winged seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The tree is valued for its adaptability and is often used in urban landscaping due to its tolerance of various soil conditions.
