Fagara rubescens var. disperma

Fagara rubescens var. disperma, commonly known as the red-fruited evergreen, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

Fagara rubescens var. disperma is also noted for its aromatic leaves, which are sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
