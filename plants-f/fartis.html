Fartis

Fartis, commonly known as the stinkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae, which includes carrots and parsley.

It is characterized by its small, umbrella-like clusters of white or pale yellow flowers and fern-like, deeply divided leaves. Due to its pungent smell, it is sometimes considered a weed in gardens and agricultural fields.
