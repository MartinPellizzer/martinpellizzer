Felicia joubertinae subsp. glabrescens

Felicia joubertinae subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit with hairy leaves that become smooth as the plant matures.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
