Felicia zeyheri

Felicia zeyheri, commonly known as the blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright blue to purple daisy-like flowers and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually hairy and gray-green in color, providing a contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Felicia zeyheri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
