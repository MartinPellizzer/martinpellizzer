Ferula pratovii

Ferula pratovii, commonly known as the alpine ferula, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and yellowish-green flowers.
