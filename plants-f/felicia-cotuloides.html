Felicia cotuloides

Felicia cotuloides, commonly known as the yellow daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Felicia cotuloides is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
