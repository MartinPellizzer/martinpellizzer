Festuca ovina subsp. bigoudenensis

Festuca ovina subsp.

bigoudenensis, commonly known as the Bigouden bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western Pyrenees and northern Spain. It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

This subspecies thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions with cool, temperate climates. It plays an important role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value also makes it a sought-after plant for gardeners interested in native flora.
