Ficus chewii

Ficus chewii, commonly known as the African rubber plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, including countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Ficus chewii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
