Fernandezia piesikii

Fernandezia piesikii, commonly known as the Kauai fern, is a rare and endemic species found exclusively on the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bi-lobed fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocky outcrops or in forest understories. Its unique morphology and restricted habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists studying Hawaiian flora.

Conservation efforts are crucial due to its vulnerability to habitat loss and invasive species.
