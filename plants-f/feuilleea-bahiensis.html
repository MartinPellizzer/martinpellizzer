Feuilleea bahiensis

Feuilleea bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually pale yellow or white, which bloom during the warmer months. Feuilleea bahiensis thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
