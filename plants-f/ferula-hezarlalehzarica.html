Ferula hezarlalehzarica

Ferula hezarlalehzarica, commonly known as the Persian cow's milk plant, is a member of the Umbelliferae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, particularly Iran. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and umbel-shaped inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, white coating.

The plant produces a milky sap that has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
