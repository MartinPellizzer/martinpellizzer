Freycinetia forbesii

Freycinetia forbesii, commonly known as the Forbess' palm, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that bear small, white flowers, followed by bright orange or red fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
