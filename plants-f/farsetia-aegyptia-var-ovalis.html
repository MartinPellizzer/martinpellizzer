Farsetia aegyptia var. ovalis

Farsetia aegyptia var. ovalis, commonly known as the Egyptian farsetia, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Egypt.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically oval in shape, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
