Festuca tenella var. aristulata

Festuca tenella var. aristulata, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features fine, silver-blue foliage with a dense, tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate floral element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its compact size and striking color make it a popular choice for landscaping and horticultural collections.
