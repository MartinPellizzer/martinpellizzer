Fagopyrum × kuntzei

Fagopyrum × kuntzei, commonly known as the hybrid buckwheat, is a perennial plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Fagopyrum esculentum (common buckwheat) and Fagopyrum tatarinowii (Tatarian buckwheat). The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in early summer. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and potential agricultural uses, it is cultivated in some regions for its seeds and foliage.
