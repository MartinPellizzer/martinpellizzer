Fritillaria messanensis var. algeriensis

Fritillaria messanensis var. algeriensis, commonly known as the Algerian fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or reddish in color, with intricate patterns. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. Its bulbs are adapted for storage and survival in arid conditions, allowing it to endure dry periods.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
