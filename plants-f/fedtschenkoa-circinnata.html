Fedtschenkoa circinnata

Fedtschenkoa circinnata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing in rocky or mountainous regions.
