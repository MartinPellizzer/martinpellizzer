Ficus grossularioides var. subpanduriformis

Ficus grossularioides var. subpanduriformis, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.
