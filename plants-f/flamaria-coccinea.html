Flamaria coccinea

Flamaria coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered flame grass, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright red, tubular flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Flamaria coccinea thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its vibrant color and unique floral structure make it a valuable addition to botanical collections and garden landscapes.
