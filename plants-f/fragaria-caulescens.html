Fragaria caulescens

Fragaria caulescens, commonly known as the slender strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in temperate regions, particularly in South America. The plant features a trailing stem with numerous small, trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are white to pink, and it produces small, edible berries similar to strawberries.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in horticulture.
