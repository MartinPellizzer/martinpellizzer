Feuilleea glomeriflora

Feuilleea glomeriflora, commonly known as the clustered leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and arranged in tight clusters at the ends of the stems.

Feuilleea glomeriflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings.
