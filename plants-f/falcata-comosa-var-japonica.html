Falcata comosa var. japonica

Falcata comosa var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese feather grass, is a ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features delicate, silver-green foliage that forms a dense, low-growing mat. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that add a soft, ethereal texture to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics include narrow, linear leaves and a clumping growth habit.
