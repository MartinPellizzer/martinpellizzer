Ficus moderata

Ficus moderata, commonly known as the small-leaf fig.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae, native to Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically small to medium in size. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible figs that are often consumed by various animals.

Ficus moderata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
