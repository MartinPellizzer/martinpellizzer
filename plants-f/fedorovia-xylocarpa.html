Fedorovia xylocarpa

Fedorovia xylocarpa, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble small balls, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy dark green appearance.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
