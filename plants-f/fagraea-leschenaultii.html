Fagraea leschenaultii

Fagraea leschenaultii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Fagraea leschenaultii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
