Ficus kiloneura

Ficus kiloneura, commonly known as the kiloneura fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, edible figs that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and has a dense canopy that provides habitat for various wildlife species.
