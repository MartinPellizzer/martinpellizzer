Fenestraria rhopalophylla

Fenestraria rhopalophylla, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems with distinctive, window-like openings called fenestrations, which help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
