Fragaria platypetala

Fragaria platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its potential use in horticulture and food production.
