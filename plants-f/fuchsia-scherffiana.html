Fuchsia scherffiana

Fuchsia scherffiana, commonly known as the silver fuchsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are attractive to pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.

Fuchsia scherffiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
