Filipendula koreana f. alba

Filipendula koreana f. alba, commonly known as the Korean white meadow rue, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of white to pale pink flowers. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are typically arranged in panicle inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
