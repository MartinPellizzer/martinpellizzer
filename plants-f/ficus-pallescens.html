Ficus pallescens

Ficus pallescens, commonly known as the silver fig or variegated ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and cream colors. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Ficus pallescens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
