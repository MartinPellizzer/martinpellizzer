Fleischmannia lithophila

Fleischmannia lithophila, commonly known as the lithophytic fleischmannia, is a rare and specialized plant adapted to grow on rock surfaces.

It is native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. This species is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires minimal water.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
