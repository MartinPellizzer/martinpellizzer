Ficus arimensis

Ficus arimensis, commonly known as the Armenian fig, is a tropical plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for its fruit, which is used in local cuisines.
