Fagopyrum chinense

Fagopyrum chinense, commonly known as Chinese buckwheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
