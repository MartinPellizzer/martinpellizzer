Fumaria bastardii

Fumaria bastardii, commonly known as the bastard fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fumariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Fumaria bastardii is primarily found in Europe and parts of western Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
