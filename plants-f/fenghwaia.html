Fenghwaia

Fenghwaia, commonly known as the Chinese cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. Native to China, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Fenghwaia is a dioecious species, meaning individual plants are either male or female.

Its ancient lineage dates back to the Mesozoic era, making it a living fossil with significant botanical and evolutionary importance.
