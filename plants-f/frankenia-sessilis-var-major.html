Frankenia sessilis var. major

Frankenia sessilis var. major, commonly known as coastal frankenia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal bluffs. Its adaptability to salt spray and harsh coastal conditions makes it an important component of coastal ecosystems.

Frankenia sessilis var. major plays a role in stabilizing dune soils and providing habitat for various small organisms.
