Ficus sciarophylla

Ficus sciarophylla, commonly known as the dark leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, pale bark. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, yellowish fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus sciarophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
