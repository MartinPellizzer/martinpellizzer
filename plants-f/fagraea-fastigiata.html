Fagraea fastigiata

Fagraea fastigiata, commonly known as the flame tree or torch tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, columnar growth habit and its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are typically elliptical, leathery, and have a glossy appearance.

Fagraea fastigiata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
