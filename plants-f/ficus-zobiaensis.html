Ficus zobiaensis

Ficus zobiaensis, commonly known as the Zobia fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of western Africa, specifically Nigeria. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are edible and attract various wildlife.

Ficus zobiaensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to a range of growing conditions.
