Felicia tenella var. longifolia

Felicia tenella var. longifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disc of dark brown or black florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
