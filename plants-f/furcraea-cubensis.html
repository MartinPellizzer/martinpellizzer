Furcraea cubensis

Furcraea cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban sword plant, is a succulent perennial native to Cuba.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to yellowish flowers. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Furcraea cubensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and resilience.
