Flueckigera rosea-aenea

Flueckigera rosea-aenea, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators such as bees and moths.
