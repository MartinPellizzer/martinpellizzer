Faberiopsis

Faberiopsis, commonly known as the Chinese broomrape, is a parasitic plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It lacks chlorophyll and relies entirely on its host for nutrients, typically attaching to the roots of other plants. Due to its parasitic nature, it plays a unique role in ecosystem dynamics, though it can be a concern for agricultural crops.
