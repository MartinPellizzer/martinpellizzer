Fargesia lincangensis

Fargesia lincangensis, commonly known as the Lincang bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms that are often covered with a fine layer of white or grayish bloom. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Fargesia lincangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
