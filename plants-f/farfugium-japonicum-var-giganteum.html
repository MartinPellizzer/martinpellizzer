Farfugium japonicum var. giganteum

Farfugium japonicum var. giganteum, commonly known as giant Japanese buttercup, is a striking perennial plant native to Japan.

It features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, with a waxy, dark green surface. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, arranged in a single, prominent inflorescence at the center of the foliage. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Its robust growth and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a dramatic, tropical look in shaded garden spaces.
