Festuca × brinkmannii

Festuca × brinkmannii, commonly known as Brinkmann's fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green foliage and dense growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental and turfgrass applications. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, exhibiting good drought tolerance.

Festuca × brinkmannii is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
