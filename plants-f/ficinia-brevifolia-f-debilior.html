Ficinia brevifolia f. debilior

Ficinia brevifolia f. debilior, commonly known as the slender leaf fig fern, is a botanical variant of the species Ficinia brevifolia.

It is a fern characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves that are typically shorter and more refined compared to the standard form. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage exhibits a light green color with a subtle sheen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This fern is valued for its ability to purify air and add a touch of natural beauty to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
