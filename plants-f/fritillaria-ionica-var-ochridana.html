Fritillaria ionica var. ochridana

Fritillaria ionica var. ochridana, commonly known as the Ochridan Crown Imperial, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountains of North Macedonia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces a tall, slender stem that bears multiple flower clusters, giving it a regal appearance. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from a basal rosette of bulbs.

This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
