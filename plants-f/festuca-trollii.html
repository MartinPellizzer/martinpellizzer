Festuca trollii

Festuca trollii, commonly known as the Troll's fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated. Festuca trollii is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soils.

It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
