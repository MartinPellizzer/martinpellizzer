Fragariastrum Libanoticum

Fragariastrum libanoticum is a rare and intriguing plant species native to the mountainous regions of the Middle East. It belongs to the Rosaceae family, which includes well-known fruits like strawberries and raspberries. Despite its botanical similarities to these familiar plants, it remains largely unknown to the general public. Its unique habitat and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Studying this plant offers insights into adaptation strategies in harsh environments.
