Ficus natalensis var. minor

Ficus natalensis var. minor, commonly known as the Natal fig or dwarf Natal fig, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its widespread cultivation.
