Ficus lucescens

Ficus lucescens, commonly known as the shining fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a waxy texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into edible figs.

Ficus lucescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
