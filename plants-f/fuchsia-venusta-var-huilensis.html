Fuchsia venusta var. huilensis

Fuchsia venusta var. huilensis, commonly known as the Huilens Mountain Fuchsia, is a compact shrub native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that range in color from deep violet to pale pink. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms during the spring and summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers.
