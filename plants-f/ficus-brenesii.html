Ficus brenesii

Ficus brenesii, commonly known as the Guatemalan fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in Guatemala and parts of Mexico. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Ficus brenesii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
