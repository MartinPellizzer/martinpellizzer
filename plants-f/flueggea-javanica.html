Flueggea javanica

Flueggea javanica, commonly known as the Java flueggea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its thick, succulent stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange to red fleshy fruits. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in tropical regions.

The plant's botanical features make it suitable for ornamental use and ecological restoration in suitable climates.
