Festuca Elwendiana

Festuca elwendiana is a rare and delicate grass species native to the mountainous regions of central Europe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky soils. This plant is known for its fine, silver-green foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early summer. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a valuable subject for botanical study and environmental protection.
