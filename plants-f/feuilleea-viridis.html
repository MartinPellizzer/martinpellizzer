Feuilleea viridis

Feuilleea viridis, commonly known as the Emerald Leaf Plant, is a tropical species characterized by its lush, deep green foliage.

Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a waxy texture that reflects light, enhancing its visual appeal. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, which are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits.

Feuilleea viridis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic value and air-purifying qualities.
