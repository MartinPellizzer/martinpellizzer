Fissistigma prismaticum

Fissistigma prismaticum, commonly known as the prismatic cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that store water. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its unique structure allows it to thrive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water.

Fissistigma prismaticum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.
