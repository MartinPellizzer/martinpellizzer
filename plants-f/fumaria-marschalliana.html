Fumaria marschalliana

Fumaria marschalliana, commonly known as the marsh fumewort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as meadows, woodlands, and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

Fumaria marschalliana is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
