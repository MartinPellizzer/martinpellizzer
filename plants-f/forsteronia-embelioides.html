Forsteronia embelioides

Forsteronia embelioides, commonly known as the white star vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has climbing habit, with slender, woody stems that can twine around supports. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Forsteronia embelioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its attractive floral display.
