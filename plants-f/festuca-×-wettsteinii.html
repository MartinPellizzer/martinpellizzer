Festuca × wettsteinii

Festuca × wettsteinii, commonly known as Wettstein's fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous slender florets.

Festuca × wettsteinii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, low-growing lawns.
