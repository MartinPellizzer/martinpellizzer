Fritillaria Messanensis Subsp. Gracilis

Fritillaria messanensis subsp. gracilis is a rare and delicate bulbous plant native to southern Italy. It is known for its striking bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies is distinguished by its slender stems and pale yellow to orange coloration. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
