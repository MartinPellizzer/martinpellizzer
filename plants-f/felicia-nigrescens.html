Felicia nigrescens

Felicia nigrescens, commonly known as the dark felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often surrounded by pale green bracts. Felicia nigrescens typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
