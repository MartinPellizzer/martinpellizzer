Fraxinus Americana Var. Pistaciifolia

Fraxinus americana var. pistaciifolia is a unique variant of the American ash tree known for its striking foliage. This plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green hue that turns vibrant yellow in autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in eastern North America. The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for landscaping and conservation efforts.
