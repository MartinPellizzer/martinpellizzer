Fargesia alpina

Fargesia alpina, commonly known as the alpine bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. This plant is characterized by its slender, clumping growth habit and its ability to thrive in cool, high-altitude environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in panicles.

Fargesia alpina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
