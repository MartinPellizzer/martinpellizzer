Fabricia zeyheri

Fabricia zeyheri, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a climbing legume native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive blue-green hue and produces showy, trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its seeds are encased in a pod that splits open to release the seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Fabricia zeyheri is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
