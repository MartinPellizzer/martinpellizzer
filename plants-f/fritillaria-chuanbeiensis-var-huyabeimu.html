Fritillaria chuanbeiensis var. huyabeimu

Fritillaria chuanbeiensis var. huyabeimu, commonly known as the Huyabeimu fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its bulbs are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
