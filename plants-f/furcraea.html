Furcraea

Furcraea, commonly known as the sword plant or banana tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the asparagus family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Furcraea species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
