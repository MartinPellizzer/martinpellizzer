Felicia hyssopifolia subsp. hyssopifolia

Felicia hyssopifolia subsp.

hyssopifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf felicia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa. It features slender, upright stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or containers.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
