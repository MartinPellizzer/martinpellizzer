Fraxinus excelsior var. atrovirens

Fraxinus excelsior var. atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green ash, is a variant of the common ash tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green foliage, which gives it a striking appearance in both summer and autumn. The leaves are typically ovate or elliptical, with a smooth margin and a serrated edge. This variety is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local wildlife.
