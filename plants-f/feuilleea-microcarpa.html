Feuilleea microcarpa

Feuilleea microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited feuilleea, is a unique plant species characterized by its compact growth habit and specialized foliage.

It typically features narrow, elongated leaves that are densely arranged along its stems, providing a distinct visual appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often overlooked due to their diminutive size. Its fruit is also minute, containing a single seed enclosed in a thin, fibrous covering.

Feuilleea microcarpa is native to specific tropical regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
