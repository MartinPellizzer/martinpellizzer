Filago mareotica

Filago mareotica, commonly known as the Mareotica filago, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant is adapted to saline and drought conditions, making it a common sight in coastal and desert environments. Its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers are typical of the genus Filago.

It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
