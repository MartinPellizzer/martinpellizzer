Fimbristylis leptoclada var. leptoclada

Fimbristylis leptoclada var. leptoclada, commonly known as slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as swamps and seasonally flooded areas. It has small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, and its roots are often submerged in water.

This species is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is adapted to thrive in humid, aquatic environments.
