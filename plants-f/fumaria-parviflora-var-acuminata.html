Fumaria parviflora var. acuminata

Fumaria parviflora var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender yellow-cress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and alternate leaves. It produces slender, hairy stems and has a low-growing habit.
