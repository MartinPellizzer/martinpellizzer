Ficus Elastica Var. Odorata

Ficus elastica var. odorata is a rare and captivating variety of the rubber plant known for its striking foliage and subtle fragrance. This plant is prized by collectors and plant enthusiasts for its unique characteristics that set it apart from other ficus species. Its leaves are thick, glossy, and often display a deep green hue with a hint of variegation. The plant's pleasant scent adds an extra layer of appeal, making it a delightful addition to any indoor space. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to thrive, making it a rewarding challenge for plant lovers.
