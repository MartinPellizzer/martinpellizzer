Forsteronia corylifolia

Forsteronia corylifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf evergreen, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Forsteronia corylifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
