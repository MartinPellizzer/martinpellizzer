Fabiana clarenii

Fabiana clarenii, commonly known as the silverleaf fabiana, is a South African shrub belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Fabiana clarenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
