Feuilleea comosa

Feuilleea comosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Feuilleea comosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking foliage.
