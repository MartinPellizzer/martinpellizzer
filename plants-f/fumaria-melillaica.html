Fumaria melillaica

Fumaria melillaica, commonly known as the Melilla yellowweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, ascending stems with alternate, pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in loose racemes, typical of the genus Fumaria.

The plant is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, making it a hardy species adapted to harsh climates.
