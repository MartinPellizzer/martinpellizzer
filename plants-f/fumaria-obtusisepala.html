Fumaria obtusisepala

Fumaria obtusisepala, commonly known as the blunt-sepal fumewort, is a flowering plant in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
