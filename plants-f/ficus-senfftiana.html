Ficus senfftiana

Ficus senfftiana, commonly known as the Senfft's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus senfftiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
