Fuchsia conica

Fuchsia conica, commonly known as the conical fuchsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with glossy, ovate leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Fuchsia conica is particularly valued for its showy blooms that appear in late spring to early summer.
