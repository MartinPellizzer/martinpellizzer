Filago stellata

Filago stellata, commonly known as the star daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Filago stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
