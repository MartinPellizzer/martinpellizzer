Ficus hiiranensis

Ficus hiiranensis, commonly known as the Hiiranensis Fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Ethiopia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered fig fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Ficus hiiranensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
