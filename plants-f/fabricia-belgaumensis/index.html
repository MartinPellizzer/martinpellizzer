Fabricia Belgaumensis

Fabricia belgaumensis is a rare flowering plant native to the western ghats of india. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its unique leaf structure and small yellow flowers. this species was first discovered in the belgaum region and has since been studied for its ecological significance. due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. its discovery highlights the biodiversity of the western ghats and the need for continued botanical research.
