Forasaccus ciliatus var. laeviglumis

Forasaccus ciliatus var. laeviglumis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed asparagus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered greenish-white flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is native to southern Africa and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
