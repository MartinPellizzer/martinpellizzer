Fragosa reniformis

Fragosa reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped fragosa, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped leaves.

It is native to specific regions with a preference for well-drained soils and partial shade. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance. Its foliage is often noted for its vibrant green color and textured surface.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is relatively rare and often found in moist, woodland environments.
