Fordiophyton Breviscapum

Fordiophyton breviscapum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This species is characterized by its short, stout stems and distinctive umbellate inflorescences. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
