Fimbristylis savannicola

Fimbristylis savannicola, commonly known as the savanna sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in savanna and wetland habitats. The plant has slender, grass-like stems with narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical structure is adapted to withstand periodic flooding and nutrient-poor conditions.
