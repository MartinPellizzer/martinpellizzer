Ficus glareosa var. oblanceolata

Ficus glareosa var. oblanceolata, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, ovate to broadly ovate leaves that are often dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits that turn yellow when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
