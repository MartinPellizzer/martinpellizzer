Firmiana pallens

Firmiana pallens, commonly known as the Chinese parasol tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped leaves that are usually bipinnately compound. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Firmiana pallens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and shade-providing canopy.
