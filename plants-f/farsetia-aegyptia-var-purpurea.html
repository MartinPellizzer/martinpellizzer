Farsetia aegyptia var. purpurea

Farsetia aegyptia var. purpurea, commonly known as the Egyptian farsetia, is a striking plant characterized by its vibrant purple foliage.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is native to regions of North Africa and the Middle East. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of tubular, bright orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique coloration and flowering make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
