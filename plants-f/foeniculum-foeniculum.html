Foeniculum foeniculum

Foeniculum foeniculum, commonly known as fennel, is a flowering plant in the carrot family (Apiaceae).

It is characterized by its feathery, aromatic foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-shaped clusters. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are often used as a spice. Fennel thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and it is native to the Mediterranean region.

Its botanical features make it a popular herb in both culinary and medicinal applications.
