Ficaria verna f. stipitatus

Ficaria verna f. stipitatus, commonly known as the yellow figwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of elliptical, yellowish-green leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters during early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Scrophulariaceae family.
