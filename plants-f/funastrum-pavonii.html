Funastrum pavonii

Funastrum pavonii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf senna, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by long, slender pods containing small, dark seeds. It is native to regions of South America and is often found in moist, shady environments.

Funastrum pavonii is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage.
