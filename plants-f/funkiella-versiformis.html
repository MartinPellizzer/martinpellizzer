Funkiella versiformis

Funkiella versiformis, commonly known as the variable funkiella, is a unique species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Charales.

It is characterized by its branching, filamentous thallus that can vary in form depending on environmental conditions. Its ecological role includes serving as a habitat for microorganisms and contributing to nutrient cycling in aquatic ecosystems.
