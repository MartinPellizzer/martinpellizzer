Forbesia namaquensis

Forbesia namaquensis, commonly known as the Namaqua daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often found in rocky areas and is adapted to arid conditions.

Forbesia namaquensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
