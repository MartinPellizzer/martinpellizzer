Fissia picturata

Fissia picturata, commonly known as the painted fissia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from green to shades of red and purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
