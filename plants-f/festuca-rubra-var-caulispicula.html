Festuca rubra var. caulispicula

Festuca rubra var. caulispicula, commonly known as red fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale green to reddish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in turfgrass and restoration projects.

Its adaptability to various environmental conditions makes it a valuable plant in ecological and horticultural settings.
