Fevillea cordifolia

Fevillea cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf fevillea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Fevillea cordifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.

Its foliage provides a dense canopy, making it suitable for use as a hedge or in container gardening.
