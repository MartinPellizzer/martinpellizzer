Ferula gracillima

Ferula gracillima, commonly known as the slender ferula, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Ferula gracillima is part of the Apiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and spices.
