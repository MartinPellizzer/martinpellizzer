Ficaria transsilvanica

Ficaria transsilvanica, commonly known as the Transylvanian figwort, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves.
