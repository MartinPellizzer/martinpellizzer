Ficus cordata var. marlothii

Ficus cordata var. marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's fig, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, pale green flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
