Ficus vohsenii

Ficus vohsenii, commonly known as the Vohsen's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Ficus vohsenii is typically evergreen and can grow as a shrub or small tree.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
