Flemingia grandiflora

Flemingia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered flemingia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Flemingia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
