Festuca × czarnohorensis

Festuca × czarnohorensis, commonly known as the Czarnohora fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, dense foliage, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, showing good tolerance to moderate drought and frost.

Its botanical features make it suitable for both ecological restoration and garden use.
