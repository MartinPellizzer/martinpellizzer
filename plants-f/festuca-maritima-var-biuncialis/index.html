Festuca Maritima Var. Biuncialis

Festuca maritima var. biuncialis is a rare and hardy grass species native to coastal regions. It thrives in salty, sandy soils and is often found in dunes and shorelines. This variety is distinguished by its unique growth pattern and compact form. It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems. Its resilience makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
