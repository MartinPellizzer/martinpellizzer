Funkia lancifolia var. alba

Funkia lancifolia var. alba, commonly known as the white arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture, and its flowers are white with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant thrives in shallow water and moist soil, often found in wetlands and pond margins. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to help purify water by absorbing nutrients.

Its growth is often encouraged in water gardens for both aesthetic and ecological benefits.
