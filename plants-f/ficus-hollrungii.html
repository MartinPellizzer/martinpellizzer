Ficus hollrungii

Ficus hollrungii, commonly known as the hollrung's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus hollrungii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
