Fillaea

Fillaea, commonly known as the "buttercup plant," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that resemble buttercups. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive ribbed texture. Fillaea species are often found in moist, temperate environments, such as meadows and wetlands.
