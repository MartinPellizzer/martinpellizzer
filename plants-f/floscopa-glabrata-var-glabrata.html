Floscopa glabrata var. glabrata

Floscopa glabrata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth floscopa, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
