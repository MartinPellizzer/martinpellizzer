Fontanesia philliraeoides subsp. fortunei

Fontanesia philliraeoides subsp.

fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's fontanesia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
