Filago germanica var. pyramidata

Filago germanica var. pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramidal filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a pyramidal inflorescence. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
