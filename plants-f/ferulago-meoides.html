Ferulago meoides

Ferulago meoides, commonly known as the mountain fumitory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.

Ferulago meoides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
