Feuilleea gardneri

Feuilleea gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's featherleaf, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of central Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows low to the ground, with narrow, linear leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, pale green flowers during the cooler months, which are often overlooked due to their subdued color.

Feuilleea gardneri is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
