Festuca tracyi

Festuca tracyi, commonly known as Tracy's fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. The plant has fine, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open woodlands and mountainous regions.

Festuca tracyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
