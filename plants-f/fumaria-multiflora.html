Fumaria multiflora

Fumaria multiflora, commonly known as common fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 20 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Fumaria multiflora is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
