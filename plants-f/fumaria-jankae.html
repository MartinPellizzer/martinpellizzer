Fumaria jankae

Fumaria jankae, commonly known as the Janka's smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and Southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae, which includes several species known for their distinctive, often colorful flowers. The plant produces delicate, pendulous flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often resembling small smoke-like tendrils. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and typically have a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its unique botanical appearance.
