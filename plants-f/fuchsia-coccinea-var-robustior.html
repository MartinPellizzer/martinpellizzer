Fuchsia coccinea var. robustior

Fuchsia coccinea var. robustior, commonly known as the robust fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to violet, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage that provides a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a container plant.
