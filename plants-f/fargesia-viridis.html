Fargesia viridis

Fargesia viridis, commonly known as the Green Fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant is evergreen, with a slow growth rate, making it suitable for small spaces or container gardening. It produces small, fragrant flowers in early summer, which are usually pale green or white.

Fargesia viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
