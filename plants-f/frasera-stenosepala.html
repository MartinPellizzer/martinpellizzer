Frasera stenosepala

Frasera stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled frasera, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Frasera stenosepala thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
