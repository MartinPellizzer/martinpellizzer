Forsythia nakaii

Forsythia nakaii, commonly known as the Japanese forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Forsythia nakaii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
