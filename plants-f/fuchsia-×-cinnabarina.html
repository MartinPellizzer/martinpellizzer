Fuchsia × cinnabarina

Fuchsia × cinnabarina, commonly known as the coral fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a distinctive coral-like hue. The plant is native to South America and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular flowers.

Fuchsia × cinnabarina thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
