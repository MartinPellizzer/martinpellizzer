Festuca pallens f. virens

Festuca pallens f. virens, commonly known as the green meadow fescue, is a variant of the tall fescue species.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its fine, dense foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in meadows, lawns, and disturbed soils. Its leaves are generally a lighter green compared to the parent species, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
