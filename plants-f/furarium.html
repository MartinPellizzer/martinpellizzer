Furarium

Furarium, commonly known as the African violets, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically has a rosette of waxy, dark green leaves that form a compact, bushy structure. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Furarium is prized for its ease of care and ability to bloom continuously under proper conditions.
