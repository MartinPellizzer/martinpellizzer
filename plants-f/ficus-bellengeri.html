Ficus bellengeri

Ficus bellengeri, commonly known as the Indian rubber plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and Sri Lanka. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It is a tropical evergreen that thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Ficus bellengeri is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
