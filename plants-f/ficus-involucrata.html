Ficus involucrata

Ficus involucrata, commonly known as the African banyan, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and can grow to impressive heights. The plant has a distinctive aerial root system that allows it to form a dense canopy. Its fruit is small and yellow, containing a single seed.

Ficus involucrata is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity.
