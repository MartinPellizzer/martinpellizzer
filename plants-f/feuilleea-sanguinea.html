Feuilleea sanguinea

Feuilleea sanguinea, commonly known as the Crimson Leaf Plant, is a striking tropical species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to crimson, depending on the light exposure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming during the late spring and summer months. Its unique leaf structure allows it to efficiently capture sunlight, making it well-suited for shaded environments.

Feuilleea sanguinea is also noted for its ability to thrive in high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.
