Fragariastrum arcadiensis

Fragariastrum arcadiensis, commonly known as the Arcadian strawberry, is a rare and native species found in the temperate regions of North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, edible berries that are typically red in color. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces white flowers with five petals.
