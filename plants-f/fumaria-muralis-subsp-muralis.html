Fumaria muralis subsp. muralis

Fumaria muralis subsp.

muralis, commonly known as wall rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae. It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found growing on walls, fences, and rocky areas.

Its leaves are alternate, with a distinctive wavy margin and a hairy surface.
