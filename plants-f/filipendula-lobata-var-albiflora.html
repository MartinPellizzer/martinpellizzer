Filipendula lobata var. albiflora

Filipendula lobata var. albiflora, commonly known as white flowering cinquefoil, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features compound leaves with five leaflets, often with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plants.
