Fuchsia tuberosa

Fuchsia tuberosa, commonly known as the velvet tunic or tropical fuchsia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant produces fleshy, tuberous roots that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms.

Fuchsia tuberosa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
