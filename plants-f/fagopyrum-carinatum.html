Fagopyrum carinatum

Fagopyrum carinatum, commonly known as the Chinese buckwheat, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive triangular leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by triangular, three-lobed seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
