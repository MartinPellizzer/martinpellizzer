Festuca haenkei

Festuca haenkei, commonly known as the alpine bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green leaves and small, blue-green flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Festuca haenkei is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

It is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
