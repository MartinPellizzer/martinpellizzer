Ficus nota

Ficus nota, commonly known as the Nota fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into edible figs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as an understory plant in dense forests.

The Nota fig is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit.
