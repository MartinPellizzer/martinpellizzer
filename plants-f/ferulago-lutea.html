Ferulago lutea

Ferulago lutea, commonly known as the yellow centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Ferulago lutea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
