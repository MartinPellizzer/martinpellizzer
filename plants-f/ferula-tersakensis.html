Ferula tersakensis

Ferula tersakensis, commonly known as the Tersaknsis cow parsley, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers. Ferula tersakensis thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing at high altitudes where it is adapted to harsh climatic conditions.
