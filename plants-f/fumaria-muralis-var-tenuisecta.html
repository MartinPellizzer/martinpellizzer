Fumaria muralis var. tenuisecta

Fumaria muralis var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the slender yellowweed, is a variant of the yellowweed species native to Europe.

It is a flowering plant that typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils, often found in urban environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient and adaptable species.
