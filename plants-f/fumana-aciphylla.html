Fumana aciphylla

Fumana aciphylla, commonly known as the woolly tree fern, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. Fumana aciphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
