Fabiana ericoides

Fabiana ericoides, commonly known as the silverleaf fabiana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features small, narrow leaves covered in a silvery-white bloom of fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Fabiana ericoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
