Faberia cavaleriei

Faberia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese faberia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas and is adapted to temperate climates. Its seeds are encased in pods and can remain viable for several years.

Faberia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
