Fuirena zambesiaca

Fuirena zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesiensis fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that float on the surface of freshwater bodies. The plant thrives in slow-moving or still waters, often found in marshy areas and along riverbanks. Its rhizomes form dense mats that help stabilize the sediment and provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Fuirena zambesiaca plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its wetland habitats.
