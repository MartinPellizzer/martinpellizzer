Ficus hookeri

Ficus hookeri, commonly known as the hookeri fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often lobed or heart-shaped. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus hookeri is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
