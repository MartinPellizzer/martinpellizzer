Fumaria ragusina

Fumaria ragusina, commonly known as the Ragusan smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces delicate, thread-like seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats.

Fumaria ragusina is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
