Festuca durandoi subsp. fontqueri

Festuca durandoi subsp.

fontqueri, commonly known as the Fontqueri fescue, is a perennial grass species native to specific regions of North America. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often blue-green blades.

The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.
