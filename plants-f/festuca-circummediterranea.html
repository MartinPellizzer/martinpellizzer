Festuca circummediterranea

Festuca circummediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to a wide range of climatic conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open habitats such as slopes, meadows, and disturbed areas.

Festuca circummediterranea plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
