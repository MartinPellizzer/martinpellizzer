Filipendula Digitata

Filipendula digitata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is known for its unique, feathery foliage and clusters of soft pink to white blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
