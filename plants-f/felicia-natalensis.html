Felicia natalensis

Felicia natalensis, commonly known as Natal daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Felicia natalensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
