Feuilleea pilosula

Feuilleea pilosula, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a herbaceous species characterized by its soft, hairy foliage.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests. The leaves are broad and ovate, covered in fine, downy hairs that give the plant its common name. Feuilleea pilosula produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
