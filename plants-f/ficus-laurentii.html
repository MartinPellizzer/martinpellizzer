Ficus laurentii

Ficus laurentii, commonly known as the laurel fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Ficus laurentii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
