Fuirena rubiginosa

Fuirena rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stained broom.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its erect, spindly growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces elongated pods that contain several seeds.
