Festuca Laevis Var. Stenantha

Festuca laevis var. stenantha is a unique grass species known for its slender, compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and marshy areas. This variety is characterized by its narrow leaves and dense clumping structure. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits. Its adaptability makes it a useful plant for both landscaping and restoration projects.
