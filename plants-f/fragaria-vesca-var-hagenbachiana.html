Fragaria vesca var. hagenbachiana

Fragaria vesca var. hagenbachiana, commonly known as the Hagenbach strawberry, is a rare variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces tiny, flavorful berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Due to its rarity, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
