Ficus ludens

Ficus ludens, commonly known as the weeping fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a distinctive, cascading pattern. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits that mature to a reddish color. It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in various light conditions.

However, it can be sensitive to changes in its environment, such as temperature and humidity fluctuations.
