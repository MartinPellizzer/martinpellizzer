Faucaria felina subsp. tuberculosa

Faucaria felina subsp.

tuberculosa, commonly known as the Cat's Whiskers, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features small, fleshy, tuberculate leaves that are often covered in a white, powdery coating.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
