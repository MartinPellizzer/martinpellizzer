Felicia retorta

Felicia retorta, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Felicia retorta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
