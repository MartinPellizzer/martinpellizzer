Fargesia tengchongensis

Fargesia tengchongensis, commonly known as the Tengchong bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by dark blue berries. Fargesia tengchongensis thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
