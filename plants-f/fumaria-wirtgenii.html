Fumaria wirtgenii

Fumaria wirtgenii, commonly known as the Wirtgen's smokeplant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that resemble smoke. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Fumaria wirtgenii is a perennial herb that is native to parts of Europe and Asia.
