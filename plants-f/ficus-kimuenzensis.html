Ficus kimuenzensis

Ficus kimuenzensis, commonly known as the Kimuen Ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Kenya, specifically the Kimuen area in the Aberdare Range. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. It produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts.
