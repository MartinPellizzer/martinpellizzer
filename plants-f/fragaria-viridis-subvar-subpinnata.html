Fragaria viridis subvar. subpinnata

Fragaria viridis subvar. subpinnata, commonly known as the green strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It features trifoliate leaves with a serrated margin and small, heart-shaped leaves on the stems. The plant produces small, green berries that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and interesting species within the Rosaceae family.
