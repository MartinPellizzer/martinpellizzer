Feuilleea macrocephala

Feuilleea macrocephala, commonly known as the giant-headed leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. The leaves are thick and waxy, which helps the plant retain moisture in arid conditions.

This plant is native to tropical regions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
