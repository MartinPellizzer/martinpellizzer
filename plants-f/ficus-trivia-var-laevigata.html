Ficus trivia var. laevigata

Ficus trivia var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved fig.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae, characterized by its glossy, smooth leaves and small, clustered fig fruits. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage. It prefers warm, humid environments and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
