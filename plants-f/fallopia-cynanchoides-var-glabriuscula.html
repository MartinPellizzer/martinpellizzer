Fallopia cynanchoides var. glabriuscula

Fallopia cynanchoides var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth cowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive leaf shapes and often reddish stems. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture, and it produces small, clustered flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
