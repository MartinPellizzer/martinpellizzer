Fimbristylis nana

Fimbristylis nana, commonly known as dwarf flat-sedge, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets. It thrives in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas.
