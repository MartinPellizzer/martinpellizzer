Fadogia pobeguinii

Fadogia pobeguinii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural studies.
