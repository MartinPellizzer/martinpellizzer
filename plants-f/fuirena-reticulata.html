Fuirena reticulata

Fuirena reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers with intricate, net-like vein patterns on the petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Fuirena reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
