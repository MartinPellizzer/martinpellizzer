Ficus mildbraedii

Ficus mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, edible figs. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Ficus mildbraedii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
