Favratia zoysii

Favratia zoysii, commonly known as the Zosia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Favratia zoysii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
