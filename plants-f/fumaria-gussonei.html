Fumaria gussonei

Fumaria gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's smokeplant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that resemble smoke. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Fumaria gussonei is typically found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
