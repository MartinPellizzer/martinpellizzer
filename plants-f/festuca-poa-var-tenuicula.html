Festuca poa var. tenuicula

Festuca poa var. tenuicula, commonly known as slender meadow grass, is a variant of the Poa genus within the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, delicate blades and is typically found in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and along stream banks. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.

Its botanical features make it an important species for studying grassland ecosystems.
