Felicia gariepina

Felicia gariepina, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually erect and branching.

Felicia gariepina is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
