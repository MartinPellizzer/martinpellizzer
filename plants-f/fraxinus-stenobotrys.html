Fraxinus stenobotrys

Fraxinus stenobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ash.

It is a deciduous tree native to parts of Asia, including China and the Indian subcontinent. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, dark green upper surface and a lighter, often pubescent underside. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in catkins, and it produces small, winged seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
