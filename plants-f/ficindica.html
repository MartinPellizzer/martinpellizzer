Ficindica

Ficindica, commonly known as the Indian rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are glossy and dark green. Ficindica is also valued for its rubber-like sap, which has been historically used in various industrial applications.
