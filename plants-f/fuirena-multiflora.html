Fuirena multiflora

Fuirena multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese mountain holly, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Fuirena multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide winter interest.
