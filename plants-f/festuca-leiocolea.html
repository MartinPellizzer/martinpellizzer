Festuca leiocolea

Festuca leiocolea, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Festuca leiocolea has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
