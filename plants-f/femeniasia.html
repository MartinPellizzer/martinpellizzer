Femeniasia

Femeniasia, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking yellow or orange bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. Femeniasia is typically found in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
