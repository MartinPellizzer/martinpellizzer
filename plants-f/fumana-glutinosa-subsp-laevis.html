Fumana glutinosa subsp. laevis

Fumana glutinosa subsp.

laevis, commonly known as the smooth fumana, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves that are typically smooth on the upper surface.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in grassland and woodland habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
