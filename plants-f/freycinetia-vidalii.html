Freycinetia vidalii

Freycinetia vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and cuisine. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its roots and leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its aromatic properties.

Freycinetia vidalii thrives in humid, coastal environments and is often found in mangrove ecosystems.
