Fontainea rostrata

Fontainea rostrata, commonly known as the red-flowered fontainea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of striking red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Fontainea rostrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
