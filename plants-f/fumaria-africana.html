Fumaria africana

Fumaria africana, commonly known as the African smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, ascending stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat various ailments.
