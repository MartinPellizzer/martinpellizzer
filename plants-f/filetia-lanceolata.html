Filetia lanceolata

Filetia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf fillet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, usually appearing in shades of pink or purple.

The plant is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
