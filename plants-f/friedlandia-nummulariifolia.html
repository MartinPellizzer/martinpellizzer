Friedlandia nummulariifolia

Friedlandia nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are rounded and silver-white in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
