Flindersia schottiana

Flindersia schottiana, commonly known as the white-flowered flindersia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine.
