Fritillaria taipaiensis f. platyphylla

Fritillaria taipaiensis f. platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved fritillaria, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange.
