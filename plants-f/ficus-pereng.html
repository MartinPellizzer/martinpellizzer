Ficus pereng

Ficus pereng, commonly known as the Pereng fig, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has broad, glossy leaves that are often heart-shaped or oval in form. Its fruit, a small fig, is edible and contains a sweet, soft pulp.

Ficus pereng is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
