Felicia abyssinica var. schimperi

Felicia abyssinica var. schimperi, commonly known as the Abyssinian fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water conservation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
