Frailea schilinzkyana

Frailea schilinzkyana, commonly known as the Schilinzky's fairy cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, compact cactus with a globular shape and typically features a single, short stem. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green body. Its leaves are modified into spines, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Frailea schilinzkyana is well adapted to drought conditions and requires minimal care, making it a popular choice for collectors and desert gardeners.
