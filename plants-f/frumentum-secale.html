Frumentum secale

Frumentum secale, commonly known as barley grass, is a member of the Poaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage.

It is typically found in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are often rich in chlorophyll, contributing to its vibrant green color. Barley grass is known for its high nutritional value, containing various vitamins and minerals.

It is often used in herbal medicine and as a food supplement due to its health benefits.
