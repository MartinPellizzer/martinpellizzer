Festuca Varia Subsp. Xanthina

Festuca varia subsp. xanthina is a unique grass species known for its striking yellowish foliage. It is native to parts of Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a soft, textured look. Despite its beauty, it requires careful maintenance to flourish in different environments.
