Feuilleea californica

Feuilleea californica, commonly known as the California leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
