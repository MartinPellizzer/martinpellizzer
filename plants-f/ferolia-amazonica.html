Ferolia amazonica

Ferolia amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the common starfruit. The plant produces fragrant white flowers and has glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.

Ferolia amazonica is valued not only for its unique fruit but also for its potential medicinal properties.
