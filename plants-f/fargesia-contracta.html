Fargesia contracta

Fargesia contracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf evergreen bamboo, is a shrub-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its leaves are narrow, elongated, and have a bluish-green hue with a prominent midrib.

Fargesia contracta is well-suited for rock gardens and shaded areas due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
