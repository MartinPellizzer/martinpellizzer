Fioria dictyocarpa

Fioria dictyocarpa, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are elongated and have a net-like venation pattern, which is a distinctive botanical feature.

The flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insects.
