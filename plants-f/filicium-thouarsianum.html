Filicium thouarsianum

Filicium thouarsianum, commonly known as the Thouars' fern, is a species of fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Filicales and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a graceful, arching appearance.

Filicium thouarsianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
