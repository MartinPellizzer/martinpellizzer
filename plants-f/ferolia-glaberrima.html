Ferolia glaberrima

Ferolia glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth ferolia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers and oval-shaped, leathery leaves. Ferolia glaberrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact growth habit and fragrant blossoms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
