Fissistigma polyanthum var. ellipticum

Fissistigma polyanthum var. ellipticum, commonly known as the Chinese starflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its compact growth habit makes it an excellent choice for borders or container planting.
