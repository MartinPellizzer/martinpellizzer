Felicia aethiopica subsp. aethiopica

Felicia aethiopica subsp.

aethiopica, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
