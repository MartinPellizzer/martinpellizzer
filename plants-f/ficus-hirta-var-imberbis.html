Ficus hirta var. imberbis

Ficus hirta var. imberbis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved fig.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
