Funkia lancifolia var. univittata

Funkia lancifolia var. univittata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daylily, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow margins. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
