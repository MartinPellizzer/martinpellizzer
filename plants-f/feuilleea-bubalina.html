Feuilleea bubalina

Feuilleea bubalina, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a unique tropical plant characterized by its intricate, feathery fronds.

Its leaves are typically bright green with a metallic sheen, and they grow in a dense, cascading pattern. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Feuilleea bubalina is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance.

It is also noted for its ability to purify the air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
