Forsythia giraldiana

Forsythia giraldiana, commonly known as the Chinese forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the autumn. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Forsythia giraldiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
