Fumaria capreolata f. sciophila

Fumaria capreolata f. sciophila, commonly known as the shade-loving fumitory, is a botanical variant of the common fumitory.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and delicate, spurred flowers. This form thrives in shaded environments, making it suitable for woodland or partially shaded gardens. Its leaves are alternate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early summer, contributing to its ornamental value in low-light conditions.
