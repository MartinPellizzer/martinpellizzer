Festuca ovina f. tenuifolia

Festuca ovina f. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender goatgrass, is a cool-season grass with narrow, grass-like leaves.

It is characterized by its fine, delicate foliage and is often found in alpine and rocky habitats. The plant typically grows in compact tufts, with flowering spikes that appear in early summer. It is adapted to harsh environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for various ecological conditions.
