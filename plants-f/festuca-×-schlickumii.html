Festuca × schlickumii

Festuca × schlickumii, commonly known as the Schlickum fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and dense growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental and turfgrass applications. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It prefers well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, thriving in temperate climates.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is often used in landscaping and erosion control.
