Firmiana linearicarpa

Firmiana linearicarpa, commonly known as the Chinese fan-palm, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and air pollution.
