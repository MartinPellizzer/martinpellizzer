Fremontodendron Californicum

Fremontodendron californicum is a striking shrub native to the western United States. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife. Its resilience and beauty make it a popular choice for landscaping and conservation efforts.
