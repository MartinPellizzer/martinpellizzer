Ficus fulva var. minor

Ficus fulva var. minor, commonly known as the variegated rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive cream or yellow variegation patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by pale green, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
