Festuca heterophylla var. robusta

Festuca heterophylla var. robusta, commonly known as the robust bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its dense growth helps prevent erosion and provides habitat for small insects and animals.

Festuca heterophylla var. robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
