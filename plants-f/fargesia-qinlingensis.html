Fargesia qinlingensis

Fargesia qinlingensis, commonly known as the Qinling evergreen bamboo, is a species native to China's Qinling Mountains.

It is a clump-forming bamboo that typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters. The plant features slender, smooth culms with a light green to grayish-green color. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a slightly hairy texture on the underside.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
