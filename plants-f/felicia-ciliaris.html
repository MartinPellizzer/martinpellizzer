Felicia ciliaris

Felicia ciliaris, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Felicia ciliaris is a perennial herb that produces small, daisy-like blooms throughout the growing season.

It is also known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
