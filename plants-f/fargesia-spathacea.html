Fargesia spathacea

Fargesia spathacea, commonly known as the Sichuan bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers clustered in spathaceous bracts, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fargesia spathacea is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
