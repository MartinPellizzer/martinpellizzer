Fagraea pachyclados

Fagraea pachyclados, commonly known as the flame tree or torch tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. Its woody stems are often swollen at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance.
