Frankenia laevis subsp. laevis

Frankenia laevis subsp.

laevis, commonly known as the smooth frankenia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters.

The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are smooth and succulent, adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and drought tolerance, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.
