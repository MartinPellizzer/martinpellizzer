Flindersia chatawayana

Flindersia chatawayana, commonly known as the Chatawayan flamewood, is a tropical tree native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, lance-shaped leaves. The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
