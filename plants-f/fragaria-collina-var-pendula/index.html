Fragaria Collina Var. Pendula

Fragaria collina var. pendula is a unique variant of the wild strawberry known for its trailing growth habit and delicate appearance. This plant thrives in cooler climates and is often found in rocky or alpine environments. Its small, sweet berries are not only edible but also hold cultural and ecological significance. The pendulous stems add an ornamental quality that makes it a popular choice for gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species within the broader Fragaria family.
