Fargesia pachyclada

Fargesia pachyclada, commonly known as the thick-stemmed arrow bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its sturdy, upright stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for shaded gardens.

Fargesia pachyclada is a clump-forming bamboo that grows slowly and requires well-drained soil.
