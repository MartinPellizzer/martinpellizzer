Festuca Halleri Subsp. Stenantha

Festuca halleri subsp. stenantha is a rare and delicate grass species native to high altitude environments in the Alps. It thrives in rocky and arid conditions where few plants can survive. This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth and narrow leaves that help reduce water loss. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable plant in its natural habitat. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the face of climate change and habitat loss.
