Frankenia densa

Frankenia densa, commonly known as coastal bluemouth, is a perennial shrub native to coastal regions of the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Frankenia densa thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.

It is well adapted to salt spray and can tolerate periods of drought.
