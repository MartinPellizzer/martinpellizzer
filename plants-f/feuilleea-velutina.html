Feuilleea velutina

Feuilleea velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Feuilleea velutina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
