Freycinetia superba

Freycinetia superba, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces long, branched inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers. Its fibrous leaves are used in traditional crafts and construction.

Freycinetia superba thrives in coastal and lowland areas, often growing near mangroves and tidal zones.
