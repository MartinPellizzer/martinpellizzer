Fagraea borneensis

Fagraea borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean fagraea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are often followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
