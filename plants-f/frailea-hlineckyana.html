Frailea hlineckyana

Frailea hlineckyana, commonly known as the Hlinecky's stonecrop, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its compact, low-growing structure with thick, fleshy stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Frailea hlineckyana is often cultivated as a decorative succulent due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
