Faucaria acutipetala

Faucaria acutipetala, commonly known as the sharp-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
