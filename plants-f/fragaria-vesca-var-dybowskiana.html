Fragaria vesca var. dybowskiana

Fragaria vesca var. dybowskiana, commonly known as the Dybowskiana wild strawberry, is a variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with trifoliate leaves and small, white flowers that develop into red berries. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland undergrowth. Its berries are edible and have a delicate, sweet flavor.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticulture.
