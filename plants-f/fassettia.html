Fassettia

Fassettia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented stems that resemble the branches of a tree. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, orange, and white, typically blooming during the holiday season. Fassettia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
