Fumaria officinalis subsp. tenuiflora

Fumaria officinalis subsp.

tenuiflora, commonly known as slender yellowcress, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features slender, branching stems that grow up to 30 cm in height, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

This species is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for its purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory effects.
