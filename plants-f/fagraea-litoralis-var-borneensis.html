Fagraea litoralis var. borneensis

Fagraea litoralis var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean fagraea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
