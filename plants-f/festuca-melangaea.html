Festuca melangaea

Festuca melangaea, commonly known as silver hair veldtgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-tinged leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to whitish flowers.

Festuca melangaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
