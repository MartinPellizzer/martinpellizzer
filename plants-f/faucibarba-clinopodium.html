Faucibarba clinopodium

Faucibarba clinopodium, commonly known as narrowleaf betony, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and produces small, pale blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
