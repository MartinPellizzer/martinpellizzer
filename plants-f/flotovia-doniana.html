Flotovia doniana

Flotovia doniana, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a dense canopy, making it a significant component of tropical rainforests. Its wood is valued for its strength and is used in construction and furniture-making.

The plant also plays an important role in supporting local biodiversity by providing habitat and food for various animals.
