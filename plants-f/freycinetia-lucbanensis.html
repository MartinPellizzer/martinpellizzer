Freycinetia lucbanensis

Freycinetia lucbanensis, commonly known as the Lucban palm, is a species of palm native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish flowers, followed by dark blue, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The Lucban palm is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in its native region.
