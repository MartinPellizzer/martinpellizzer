Feuilleea ruiziana

Feuilleea ruiziana, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a unique plant native to the humid tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that emit a subtle golden hue under sunlight. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a soft, velvety texture.

Feuilleea ruiziana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in indoor gardens and tropical landscapes.
