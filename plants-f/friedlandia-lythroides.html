Friedlandia lythroides

Friedlandia lythroides, commonly known as the red-flowered kowhai, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, red aril. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
