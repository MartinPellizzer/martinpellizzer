Fumaria muralis var. lebelii

Fumaria muralis var. lebelii, commonly known as the wall rue, is a variant of the common fumaria.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe, characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly pubescent surface. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found growing on walls, roadsides, and in waste areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
