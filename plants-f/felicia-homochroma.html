Felicia homochroma

Felicia homochroma, commonly known as the silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage and produces yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Felicia homochroma is drought-tolerant and has ornamental value in gardens.
