Flexularia compressa

Flexularia compressa, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It features long, narrow, silver-green leaves that are often described as resembling spider legs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Flexularia compressa is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
