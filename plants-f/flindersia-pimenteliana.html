Flindersia pimenteliana

Flindersia pimenteliana, commonly known as the Pimentel's flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually dramatic plant. The tree has large, pinnate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Flindersia pimenteliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its showy blooms and unique foliage.
