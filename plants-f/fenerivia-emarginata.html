Fenerivia emarginata

Fenerivia emarginata, commonly known as the Margined Fenerivia, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that often display a distinctive margin with a pale or contrasting edge. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, thriving in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Due to habitat loss, Fenerivia emarginata is considered a rare and endangered species.
