Ficus natalensis subsp. natalensis

Ficus natalensis subsp.

natalensis, commonly known as Natal fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae. It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered fig fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor environments.

The species is also valued for its ability to purify air and thrive in low-light conditions.
