Feuilleea mangensis

Feuilleea mangensis, commonly known as the Mangrove Leaf Plant, is a unique species native to tropical coastal regions.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in humid environments. The plant thrives in saline soils and is often found in mangrove ecosystems. Its root system is adapted to filter out salt, allowing it to survive in brackish water.

Feuilleea mangensis plays a crucial role in stabilizing coastal soils and supporting local biodiversity.
