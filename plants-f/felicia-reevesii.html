Felicia reevesii

Felicia reevesii, commonly known as the Reeves' fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color.

Felicia reevesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
