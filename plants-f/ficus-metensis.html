Ficus metensis

Ficus metensis, commonly known as the Metens fig tree, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its fruit, a small fig, is edible and attracts various wildlife.

Ficus metensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
