Forsythia × mandschurica

Forsythia × mandschurica, commonly known as the Mandschurian forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Forsythia × mandschurica is relatively hardy and adaptable to a variety of soil types and light conditions.
