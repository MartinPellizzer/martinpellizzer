Fabago major

Fabago major, commonly known as the large-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. Fabago major thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a nectar source for pollinators and its ornamental value in horticulture.
