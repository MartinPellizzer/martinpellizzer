Flemingia congesta

Flemingia congesta, commonly known as the clustered flemingia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, and it produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds.

Flemingia congesta is often found in moist, tropical environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
