Fagraea robusta

Fagraea robusta, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea robusta is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
