Freireodendron Sessiliflorum

Mexican flame tree, scientifically known as Freireodendron sessiliflorum, is a strikingly ornamental tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America. It is celebrated for its vibrant, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The tree's bold appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces, despite its relatively short lifespan. Its rapid growth and unique foliage add a dramatic flair to any landscape. However, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive, making it a rewarding but demanding plant for growers.
