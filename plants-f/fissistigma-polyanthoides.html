Fissistigma polyanthoides

Fissistigma polyanthoides, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Fissistigma polyanthoides blooms during the summer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
