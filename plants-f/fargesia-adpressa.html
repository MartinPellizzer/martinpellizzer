Fargesia adpressa

Fargesia adpressa, commonly known as the flat bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western China, particularly in Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. This plant is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and slender, often slightly curved stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Fargesia adpressa is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
