Funkia japonica

Funkia japonica, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and slightly wavy. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in late summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for wetland gardens and pond edges.
