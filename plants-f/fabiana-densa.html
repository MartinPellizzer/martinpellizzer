Fabiana densa

Fabiana densa, commonly known as the dense fabiana, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshade plants. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Fabiana densa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
