Fumaria algeriensis

Fumaria algeriensis, commonly known as Algerian smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces elongated, bead-like seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Fumaria algeriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local herbal medicine.
