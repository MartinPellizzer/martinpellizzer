Ficus obscura var. kunstleri

Ficus obscura var. kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstleri fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
