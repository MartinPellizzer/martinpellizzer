Ferula jaeschkeana var. parkeriana

Ferula jaeschkeana var. parkeriana, commonly known as the Parker's giant fennel, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, yellowish-green umbels of flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, with a strong licorice-like scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to hot, dry climates.
