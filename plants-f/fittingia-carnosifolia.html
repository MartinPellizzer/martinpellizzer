Fittingia carnosifolia

Fittingia carnosifolia, commonly known as the fleshy-leaved fittingia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy, often reddish stems and leaves, which are adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
