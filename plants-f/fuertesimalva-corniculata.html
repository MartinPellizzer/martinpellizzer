Fuertesimalva corniculata

Fuertesimalva corniculata, commonly known as the horned rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid and sunny conditions of its native habitat. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in the region's flora.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
