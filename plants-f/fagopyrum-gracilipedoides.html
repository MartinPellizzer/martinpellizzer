Fagopyrum gracilipedoides

Fagopyrum gracilipedoides, commonly known as slender-leaved buckwheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
