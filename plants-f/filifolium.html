Filifolium

Filifolium, commonly known as the silky filifolium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Filifolium thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
