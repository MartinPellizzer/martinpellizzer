Fumaria solida

Fumaria solida, commonly known as the solid smokeplant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Fumaria solida is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.
