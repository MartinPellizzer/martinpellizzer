Fraga sterilis

Fraga sterilis, commonly known as the barren strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, white flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in rocky and dry environments, making it a resilient species. Despite its name, it is not related to the true strawberry but is named for its similar appearance.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
