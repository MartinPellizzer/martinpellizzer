Feuilleea bella

Feuilleea bella, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of pale pink and white, which bloom during the early morning. Feuilleea bella thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its unique appearance and elegant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental displays.
