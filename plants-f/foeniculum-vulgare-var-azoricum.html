Foeniculum vulgare var. azoricum

Foeniculum vulgare var. azoricum, commonly known as Azorean fennel, is a variant of the common fennel native to the Azores.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its aromatic, feathery foliage and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal environments.

Its distinctive flavor and essential oils make it a valuable plant in both traditional and modern herbal practices.
