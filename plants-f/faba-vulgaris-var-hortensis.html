Faba vulgaris var. hortensis

Faba vulgaris var. hortensis, commonly known as the garden bean, is a cultivated variety of the broad bean.

It is characterized by its large, edible seeds that are typically harvested when mature. The plant is an annual legume that thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces flowering stems that bear pods containing the seeds.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and is widely grown for both culinary and agricultural purposes.
