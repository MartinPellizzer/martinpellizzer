Festuca gigantea var. nemoralis

Festuca gigantea var. nemoralis, commonly known as the silver hair veldtgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, woodlands, and rocky areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Festuca gigantea var. nemoralis is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
