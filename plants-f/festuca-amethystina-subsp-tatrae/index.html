Festuca Amethystina Subsp. Tatrae

Festuca amethystina subsp. tatrae is a rare and striking grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It is known for its distinctive purple-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it a standout in alpine environments. This subspecies thrives in rocky and moist soils, often found in high-altitude meadows and slopes. Its unique coloration and ecological significance have made it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
