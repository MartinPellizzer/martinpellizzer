Filago floribunda

Filago floribunda, commonly known as the flowering filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It is native to parts of Africa and is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils.

Filago floribunda is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
