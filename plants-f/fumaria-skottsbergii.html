Fumaria skottsbergii

Fumaria skottsbergii, commonly known as the Skottsberg's smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually slender and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and branching.

Fumaria skottsbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
