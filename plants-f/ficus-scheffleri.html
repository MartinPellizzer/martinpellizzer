Ficus scheffleri

Ficus scheffleri, commonly known as the Variegated Ficus or the Variegated Rubber Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which adds visual interest to any setting. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its ability to adapt to indoor conditions makes it a popular choice for houseplants.
