Filix-mas cristata var. clintoniana

Filix-mas cristata var. clintoniana, commonly known as the Clinton's basket fern, is a varietal form of the common holly fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tightly curled fronds that resemble a basket, giving it its common name. The plant is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several feet in length.

This fern is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
