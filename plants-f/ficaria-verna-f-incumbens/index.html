Ficaria Verna F. Incumbens

Ficaria verna f. incumbens is a striking variation of the buttercup plant known for its delicate, bright yellow blooms. This cultivar features a compact growth habit and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade. The plant's unique foliage and flower structure make it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts. Its beauty and resilience make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated settings.
