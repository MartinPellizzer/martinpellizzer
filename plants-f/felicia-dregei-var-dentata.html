Felicia dregei var. dentata

Felicia dregei var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features delicate, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of stems. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
