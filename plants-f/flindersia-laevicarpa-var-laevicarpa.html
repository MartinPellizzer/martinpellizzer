Flindersia laevicarpa var. laevicarpa

Flindersia laevicarpa var. laevicarpa, commonly known as the smooth-flindersia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy green. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
