Filix × dowellii

Filix × dowellii, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Filix mas* and *Filix communis*.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that typically grow in a compact, clump-forming habit. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a distinctive, arching appearance, often reaching up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or garden settings.

Filix × dowellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated for its elegant foliage.
