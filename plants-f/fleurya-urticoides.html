Fleurya urticoides

Fleurya urticoides, commonly known as the stinging nettle tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes many stinging plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy, often stinging leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its defensive properties against herbivores.
