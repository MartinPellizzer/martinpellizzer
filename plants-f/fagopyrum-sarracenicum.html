Fagopyrum sarracenicum

Fagopyrum sarracenicum, commonly known as the Japanese buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

It is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
