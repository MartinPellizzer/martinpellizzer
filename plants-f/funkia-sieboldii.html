Funkia sieboldii

Funkia sieboldii, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically light green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in late summer, arranged in umbels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Funkia sieboldii is often used in water gardens and wetland landscapes due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
