Fernandezia dalstroemii

Fernandezia dalstroemii, commonly known as the Darwin's fern, is a rare and unique fern species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble those of traditional ferns, though it exhibits some distinctive structural features. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in cloud forests at high elevations. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, contributing to its limited distribution and ecological specificity.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Fernandezia dalstroemii is considered a vulnerable species with limited conservation efforts in place.
