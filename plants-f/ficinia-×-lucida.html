Ficinia × lucida

Ficinia × lucida, commonly known as the silver sword, is a hybrid plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in coastal environments, characterized by its rosette-forming structure. The leaves are broad, sword-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape plant.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in harsh coastal conditions.
