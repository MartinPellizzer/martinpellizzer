Fumaria neapolitana

Fumaria neapolitana, commonly known as the Naples cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Papaveraceae family.

Fumaria neapolitana is primarily grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and historical use in herbal medicine.
