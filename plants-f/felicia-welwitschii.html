Felicia welwitschii

Felicia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow composite flowers and linear, gray-green leaves. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, white fruits that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.

Felicia welwitschii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
