Feuilleea obovata

Feuilleea obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed evergreen, is a perennial plant native to the subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, obovate-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green on top with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Feuilleea obovata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
