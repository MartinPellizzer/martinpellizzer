Ficus indica var. gelderi

Ficus indica var. gelderi, commonly known as the Gelder's fig, is a variant of the Indian fig tree.

It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. The plant features broad, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
