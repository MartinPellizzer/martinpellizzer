Fimbristylis diphylla var. gracilis

Fimbristylis diphylla var. gracilis, commonly known as slender false spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are narrow and tapering, with a fibrous texture, and it produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variant is distinguished by its more delicate appearance compared to other forms of Fimbristylis diphylla.
