Fargesia canoviridis

Fargesia canoviridis, commonly known as the silver fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that have a silvery-blue hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs. This plant is evergreen and thrives in cool, moist environments, making it well-suited for shaded garden areas or woodland settings. Its growth habit is clump-forming, with a compact and elegant appearance.

Fargesia canoviridis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
