Faba sepium

Faba sepium, commonly known as the broad bean or fava bean, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It is often cultivated for both culinary and agricultural purposes.

Faba sepium thrives in temperate climates and is well-adapted to various soil types.
