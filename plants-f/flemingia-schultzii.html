Flemingia schultzii

Flemingia schultzii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. Flemingia schultzii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and savannas.
