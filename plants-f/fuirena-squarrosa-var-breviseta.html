Fuirena squarrosa var. breviseta

Fuirena squarrosa var. breviseta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Fuirena, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry, well-drained conditions.
