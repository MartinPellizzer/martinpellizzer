Feuilleea laeta

Feuilleea laeta, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, frond-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a subtle golden hue on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

Feuilleea laeta is also noted for its ability to purify the air, making it both aesthetically pleasing and beneficial for indoor spaces.
