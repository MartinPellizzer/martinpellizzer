Frankenia grandifolia var. micrantha

Frankenia grandifolia var. micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved frankenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas with a Mediterranean climate. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also known for its ability to tolerate saline conditions, making it adaptable to coastal environments.
