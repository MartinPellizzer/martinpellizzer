Festuca soratana

Festuca soratana, commonly known as slender fescue, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in areas with high humidity and partial shade.

Festuca soratana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
