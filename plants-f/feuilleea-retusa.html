Feuilleea retusa

Feuilleea retusa, commonly known as the smooth-leaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are borne on slender stalks. Its root system is fibrous and helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soil.

Feuilleea retusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
