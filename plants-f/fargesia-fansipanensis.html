Fargesia fansipanensis

Fargesia fansipanensis, commonly known as the Fan Mountain bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and gracefully arching foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.

Fargesia fansipanensis is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
