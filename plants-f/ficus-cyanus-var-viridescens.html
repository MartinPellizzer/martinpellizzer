Ficus cyanus var. viridescens

Ficus cyanus var. viridescens, commonly known as the Blue Fig or Azure Fig, is a striking plant characterized by its vibrant blue-green foliage.

This variant of the Ficus cyanus species exhibits a unique coloration due to the presence of a specific pigment, which gives its leaves their distinctive hue. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a glossy texture that enhances the plant's visual appeal. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.
