Freycinetia bassa

Freycinetia bassa, commonly known as the slender pandanus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Freycinetia bassa is often used in traditional crafts and has cultural significance in many Pacific Island communities.
