Fumaria capreolata var. speciosa

Fumaria capreolata var. speciosa, commonly known as the showy fumitory, is a flowering plant in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, often bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
