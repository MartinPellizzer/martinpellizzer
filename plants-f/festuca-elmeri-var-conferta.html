Festuca elmeri var. conferta

Festuca elmeri var. conferta, commonly known as Elmer's fescue, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, contributing to its ornamental value.

Festuca elmeri var. conferta is valued for its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil in harsh, mountainous conditions.
