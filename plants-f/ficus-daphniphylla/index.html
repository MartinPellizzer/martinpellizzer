Ficus Daphniphylla

Ficus daphniphylla is a unique and visually striking plant native to southern China. Known for its distinctive aerial roots and glossy, waxy leaves, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening. This evergreen fig species thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its ability to climb and form a dense canopy makes it ideal for hanging baskets or shelves. With proper care, Ficus daphniphylla can become a stunning centerpiece in any home or office space.
