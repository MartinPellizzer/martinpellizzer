Fuchsia minutiflora var. hidalgensis

Fuchsia minutiflora var. hidalgensis, commonly known as the Hidalgo fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
