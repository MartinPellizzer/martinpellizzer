Ferreirea

Ferreirea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Ferreirea is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain regions.
