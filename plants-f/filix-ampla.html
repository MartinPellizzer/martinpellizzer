Filix ampla

Filix ampla, commonly known as the giant fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, arching fronds that can reach lengths of up to 10 feet. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a thick, fleshy rootstock that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are compound, with a feathery appearance and are typically bright green.

Filix ampla thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
