Ficaria verna var. africana

Ficaria verna var. africana, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers with white petals, often appearing in early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with rosettes of waxy, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its cheerful blooms and hardy nature.
