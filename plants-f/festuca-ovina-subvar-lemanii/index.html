Festuca Ovina Subvar. Lemanii

Festuca ovina subvar. lemanii is a unique grass species native to alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky and moist environments, often found in mountain meadows and slopes. This plant is known for its fine, tufted growth and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Its presence is an indicator of healthy, undisturbed ecosystems. Due to its rarity, it is often protected in conservation efforts.
