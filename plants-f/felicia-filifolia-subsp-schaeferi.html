Felicia filifolia subsp. schaeferi

Felicia filifolia subsp.

schaeferi, commonly known as the Schaefer's felicia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa. It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with succulent stems that store water.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
