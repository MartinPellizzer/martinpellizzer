Fumaria turbinata

Fumaria turbinata, commonly known as the turban fumewort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Fumariaceae family, characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces long, slender seed pods that release seeds by wind.

Fumaria turbinata is often found in disturbed soils and can be an indicator plant for certain ecological conditions.
