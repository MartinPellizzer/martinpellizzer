Fedia radiata var. patellaria

Fedia radiata var. patellaria, commonly known as the Patellaria fedia, is a rare and distinctive plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique leaf morphology, featuring broad, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and prominent venation patterns. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the wet season, followed by the development of woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.
