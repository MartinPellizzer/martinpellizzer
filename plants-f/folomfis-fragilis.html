Folomfis fragilis

Folomfis fragilis, commonly known as the brittle leaf fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features finely divided, light green fronds that give it a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil. Its rapid growth and fragile foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

However, it is sensitive to temperature changes and requires careful maintenance to flourish.
