Frankenia fecunda

Frankenia fecunda, commonly known as the coastal frankenia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to its arid habitat. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in harsh coastal environments.

Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves and a compact growth habit that helps it conserve moisture.
