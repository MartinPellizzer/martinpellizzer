Feuilleea distachya

Feuilleea distachya, commonly known as the two-branched leaf plant, is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Feuilleea distachya is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
