Fritillaria oranensis

Fritillaria oranensis, commonly known as the Algerian fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Fritillaria oranensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
