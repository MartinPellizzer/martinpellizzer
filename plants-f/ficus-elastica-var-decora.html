Ficus elastica var. decora

Ficus elastica var. decora, commonly known as the decorative rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy foliage and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It is a popular houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.

The rubber plant is also known for its ability to produce small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries.
