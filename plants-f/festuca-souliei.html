Festuca souliei

Festuca souliei, commonly known as the Chinese meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and rocky environments.

Festuca souliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its visual texture and resilience.
