Flemingia sootepensis

Flemingia sootepensis, commonly known as the Sootep flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in lowland areas. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Flemingia sootepensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
