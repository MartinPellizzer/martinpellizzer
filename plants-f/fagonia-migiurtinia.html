Fagonia migiurtinia

Fagonia migiurtinia, commonly known as the desert fagonia, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, spine-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. It has a deep root system that helps it access underground moisture.

Fagonia migiurtinia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
