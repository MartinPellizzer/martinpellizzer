Filago evacoides

Filago evacoides, commonly known as the slender filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, hairy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Filago evacoides is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
