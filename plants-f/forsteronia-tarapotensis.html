Forsteronia tarapotensis

Forsteronia tarapotensis, commonly known as the Peruvian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its ornamental value. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
