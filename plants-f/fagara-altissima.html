Fagara altissima

Fagara altissima, commonly known as the high mountain soursop, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces a large, spiny fruit that contains a single large seed, often used in traditional medicine.
