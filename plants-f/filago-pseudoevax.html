Filago pseudoevax

Filago pseudoevax, commonly known as the false sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid or rocky environments.
