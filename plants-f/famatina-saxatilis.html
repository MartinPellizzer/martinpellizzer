Famatina saxatilis

Famatina saxatilis, commonly known as the "Cape of Good Hope" or "Cape" plant, is a member of the Fabaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub that features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Famatina saxatilis is native to southern Africa and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
