Ficus nasuta var. glabrata

Ficus nasuta var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits that are often consumed by both humans and animals. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
