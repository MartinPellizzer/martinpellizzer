Fuchsia × floribunda

Fuchsia × floribunda, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep purples to vibrant reds. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Fuchsia × floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
