Ficus peroblonga

Ficus peroblonga, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Ficus peroblonga is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments makes it a popular choice for those seeking to add greenery to their spaces.
