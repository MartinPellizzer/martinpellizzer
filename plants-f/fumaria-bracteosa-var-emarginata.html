Fumaria bracteosa var. emarginata

Fumaria bracteosa var. emarginata, commonly known as the emarginate yellowcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate yellow flowers and basal leaves with toothed margins. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
