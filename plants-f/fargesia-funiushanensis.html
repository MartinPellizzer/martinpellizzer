Fargesia funiushanensis

Fargesia funiushanensis, commonly known as the Funiushan bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant is typically found in mountainous regions, growing in cool, moist environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its elegant appearance.

Fargesia funiushanensis is a clump-forming plant that can reach heights of up to 3 meters, making it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
