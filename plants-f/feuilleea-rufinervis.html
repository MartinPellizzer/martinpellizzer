Feuilleea rufinervis

Feuilleea rufinervis, commonly known as the red-veined leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its striking foliage.

Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with prominent, reddish veins that contrast sharply with the dark green background. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has a woody stem that becomes more pronounced with age, adding to its structural complexity.

Feuilleea rufinervis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its distinctive appearance and ease of care.
