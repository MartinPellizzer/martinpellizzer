Feuilleea linearis

Feuilleea linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, linear leaves that grow in a dense, upright cluster.

Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant's foliage is typically a vibrant green, with some varieties exhibiting a silvery or bluish hue on the underside of the leaves. Feuilleea linearis has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread slowly and form a compact, attractive ground cover.

It is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
