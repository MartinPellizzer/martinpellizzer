Ficus geocharis

Ficus geocharis, commonly known as the dwarf rubber plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical, greenish fruits that mature to a reddish hue. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal maintenance needs.

Its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions makes it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant growers.
