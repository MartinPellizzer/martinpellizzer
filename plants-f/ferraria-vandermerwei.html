Ferraria vandermerwei

Ferraria vandermerwei, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, white or pale blue flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, blooming in late winter to early spring.

Ferraria vandermerwei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
