Frankenia interioris var. conspicua

Frankenia interioris var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy frankenia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and drought tolerance.
