Festuca inops var. spiralis

Festuca inops var. spiralis, commonly known as spiral fescue, is a grass species characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often exhibit a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and hardiness in cooler climates.
