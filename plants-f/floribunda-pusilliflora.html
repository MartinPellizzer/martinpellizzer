Floribunda pusilliflora

Floribunda pusilliflora, commonly known as the dwarf flowering bush, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to tropical regions.

It features small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, or red, depending on the variety. The plant has dense, dark green foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Floribunda pusilliflora is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ease of maintenance.
