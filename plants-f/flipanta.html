Flipanta

Flipanta, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and elusive plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that appear to shimmer in the wind, giving it its ethereal reputation. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its unique morphology includes a short stem and a single, elongated bloom that opens only under specific environmental conditions.

Due to its rarity and striking appearance, Flipanta is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
