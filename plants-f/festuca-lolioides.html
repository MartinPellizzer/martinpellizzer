Festuca lolioides

Festuca lolioides, commonly known as slender fescue, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.

Festuca lolioides is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its resilience to environmental stress.
