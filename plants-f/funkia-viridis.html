Funkia viridis

Funkia viridis, commonly known as the green arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to eastern North America.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shallow water and moist soil, often found in wetlands and ponds.

Funkia viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to help stabilize aquatic ecosystems.
