Ficus ischnopoda var. subcylindrica

Ficus ischnopoda var. subcylindrica, commonly known as the narrow-cylinder fig, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible figs that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for small spaces and container gardening.
