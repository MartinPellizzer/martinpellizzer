Fuchsia macrostemma var. globosa

Fuchsia macrostemma var. globosa, commonly known as the globular fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
