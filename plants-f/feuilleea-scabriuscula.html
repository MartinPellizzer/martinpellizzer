Feuilleea scabriuscula

Feuilleea scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough-leaved fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered fronds that are covered in a rough, scaly texture. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, serrated pinnae that contribute to its distinctive appearance.

Feuilleea scabriuscula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
