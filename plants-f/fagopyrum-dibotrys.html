Fagopyrum dibotrys

Fagopyrum dibotrys, commonly known as the Chinese buckwheat, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by triangular, winged seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas or on rocky slopes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes other plants like sorrel and rhubarb.
