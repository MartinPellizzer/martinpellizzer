Frankenia ericifolia var. latifolia

Frankenia ericifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf frankenia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and sandy habitats.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
