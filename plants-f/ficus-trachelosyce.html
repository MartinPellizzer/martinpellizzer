Ficus trachelosyce

Ficus trachelosyce, commonly known as the white-veined fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. Ficus trachelosyce is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
