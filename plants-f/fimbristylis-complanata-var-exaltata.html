Fimbristylis complanata var. exaltata

Fimbristylis complanata var. exaltata, commonly known as the tall flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, where it thrives in shallow water or saturated soil. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often pale green to brown in color. It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and is often found in coastal or inland wetland habitats.

Fimbristylis complanata var. exaltata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
