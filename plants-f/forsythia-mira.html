Forsythia mira

Forsythia mira, commonly known as the Chinese lantern bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Forsythia mira is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
