Ficus pleiadenia

Ficus pleiadenia, commonly known as the Variegated Ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or light green variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Ficus pleiadenia is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
