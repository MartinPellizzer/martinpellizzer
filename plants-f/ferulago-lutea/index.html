Ferulago Lutea

Ferulago lutea is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow blooms. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This perennial herb is often found in mountainous regions and dry grasslands. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and ornamental landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
