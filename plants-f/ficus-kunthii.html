Ficus kunthii

Ficus kunthii, commonly known as the Kunth's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or red when mature. Ficus kunthii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Its growth habit includes both as a shrub and a small tree, depending on environmental factors.
