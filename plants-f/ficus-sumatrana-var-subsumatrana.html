Ficus sumatrana var. subsumatrana

Ficus sumatrana var. subsumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Indonesia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation in subtropical regions.
