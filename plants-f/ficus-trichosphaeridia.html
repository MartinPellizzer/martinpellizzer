Ficus trichosphaeridia

Ficus trichosphaeridia, commonly known as the hairy sphere fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its unique, hairy, spherical fruit which is often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Ficus trichosphaeridia is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties.
