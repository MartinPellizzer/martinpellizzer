Fragaria elatior var. magna

Fragaria elatior var. magna, commonly known as the giant strawberry, is a cultivated variant of the strawberry plant characterized by its large, showy flowers and fruit.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions, typically growing in cool climates with well-drained soil. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces clusters of bright red, juicy berries that are prized for their size and flavor. It is often grown in gardens for ornamental purposes and as a food source.

Due to its vigorous growth, it requires regular pruning and care to maintain its health and productivity.
