Fuchsia desiderata

Fuchsia desiderata, commonly known as the velvet tassel fuchsia, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant produces showy, bracted inflorescences that are often described as resembling tassels, hence its common name. Its foliage consists of glossy, ovate leaves that are typically a deep green.

Fuchsia desiderata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.
