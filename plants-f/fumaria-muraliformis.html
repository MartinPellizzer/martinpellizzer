Fumaria muraliformis

Fumaria muraliformis, commonly known as the wall fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils, often found along walls or in cracks in stone. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its fruit is a capsule that releases tiny seeds.

This species is noted for its ability to thrive in harsh environments, making it a hardy and resilient plant.
