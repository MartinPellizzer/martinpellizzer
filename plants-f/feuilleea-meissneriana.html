Feuilleea meissneriana

Feuilleea meissneriana, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that have a metallic sheen, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the wet season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment, and it thrives in partial shade with high humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
