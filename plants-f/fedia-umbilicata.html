Fedia umbilicata

Fedia umbilicata, commonly known as the umbilicated fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Marsileaceae.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically four-lobed and resemble a four-leaf clover. Fedia umbilicata plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small invertebrates.
