Frankenia pallida var. lucianae

Frankenia pallida var. lucianae, commonly known as the narrowleaf frankenia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, glandular leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal dunes or open woodlands. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a notable species in its native habitat.

The variety lucianae is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Frankenia pallida.
