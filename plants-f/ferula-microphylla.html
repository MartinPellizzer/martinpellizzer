Ferula microphylla

Ferula microphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ferula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Ferula microphylla is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
