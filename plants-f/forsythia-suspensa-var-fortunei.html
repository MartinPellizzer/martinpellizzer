Forsythia suspensa var. fortunei

Forsythia suspensa var. fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese lantern forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant has arching branches and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. It is relatively hardy and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique flowering habit and vibrant color make it a striking addition to landscapes.
