Felicia schenckii

Felicia schenckii, commonly known as the Schenck's felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular flowers, typical of the family. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Felicia schenckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
