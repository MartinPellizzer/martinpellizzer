Ficus psychotriifolia

Ficus psychotriifolia, commonly known as the Psychotriifolia Fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves that often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are typically clustered in a spherical structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Ficus psychotriifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
