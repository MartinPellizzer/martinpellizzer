Felicia serrulata

Felicia serrulata, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves that give it its scientific name, "serrulata," meaning "serrated.". It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Felicia serrulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
