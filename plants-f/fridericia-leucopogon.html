Fridericia leucopogon

Fridericia leucopogon, commonly known as silverleaf fridericia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. Fridericia leucopogon is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
