Fimbristylis ambigua

Fimbristylis ambigua, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often forming dense mats near water bodies. Its slender stems are usually less than 30 cm tall, with narrow, grass-like leaves.
