Fissistigma Manubriatum

Coral tree, scientifically known as Fissistigma manubriatum, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa. It is renowned for its vibrant, coral-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The tree's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces around the world. Its ability to thrive in a variety of climates adds to its appeal among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With proper care, the Coral tree can become a focal point of any landscape, offering both beauty and ecological benefits.
