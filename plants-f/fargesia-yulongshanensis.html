Fargesia yulongshanensis

Fargesia yulongshanensis, commonly known as the Yulongshan bamboo, is a rare and cold-hardy species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is a clumping bamboo characterized by its slender, upright stems and gracefully arching leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters and thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, making it an attractive ornamental plant for gardens in temperate climates.

Fargesia yulongshanensis is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
