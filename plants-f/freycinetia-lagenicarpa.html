Freycinetia lagenicarpa

Freycinetia lagenicarpa, commonly known as the bottlebrush cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the family Cycadaceae.

Freycinetia lagenicarpa is a slow-growing, long-lived species that plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
