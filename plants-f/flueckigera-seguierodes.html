Flueckigera seguierodes

Flueckigera seguierodes, commonly known as the Seguierode orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.
