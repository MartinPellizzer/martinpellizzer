Firmiana rubriflora

Firmiana rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered firmiana, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and floral display. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
