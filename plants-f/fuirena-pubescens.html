Fuirena pubescens

Fuirena pubescens, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a striking orchid species native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often purple or pink flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures, contributing to its common name. It thrives in humid environments with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Fuirena pubescens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
