Filipendula ulmaria var. pleniflora

Filipendula ulmaria var. pleniflora, commonly known as Queen of the Meadow, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and abundant, fluffy white to pinkish flower heads.
