Fibigia suffruticosa var. macrostyla

Fibigia suffruticosa var. macrostyla, commonly known as the large-fruited fibigia, is a shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by distinctive, fleshy fruits. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, making it well-suited for rocky or dry environments.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphological features and ecological adaptability.
