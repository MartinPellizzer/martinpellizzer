Feuilleea jiringa

Feuilleea jiringa, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green with a silvery sheen on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil. Its root system is fibrous, allowing it to anchor firmly in its habitat.

Feuilleea jiringa is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
