Flemingia coccinea

Flemingia coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered flemingia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers, which are often visited by bees and other pollinators. Flemingia coccinea is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
