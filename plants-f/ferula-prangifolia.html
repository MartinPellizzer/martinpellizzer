Ferula prangifolia

Ferula prangifolia, commonly known as the Persian cow parsley, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescences and aromatic foliage. Ferula prangifolia is often found in rocky and arid environments, adapting well to harsh climatic conditions.
