Fagelia aquatica

Fagelia aquatica, commonly known as water hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant thrives in moist or aquatic environments, often found near water bodies. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Fagelia aquatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
