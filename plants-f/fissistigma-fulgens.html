Fissistigma fulgens

Fissistigma fulgens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dazzling display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fissistigma fulgens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
