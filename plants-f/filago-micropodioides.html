Filago micropodioides

Filago micropodioides, commonly known as the small-leaf filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along riverbanks.

Filago micropodioides is valued for its traditional uses in herbal medicine.
