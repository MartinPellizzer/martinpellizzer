Frankenia bucharica subsp. transkaratavica

Frankenia bucharica subsp.

transkaratavica, commonly known as the Karatava frankenia, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region. It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climatic conditions. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the leaves, which help reduce water loss.

This subspecies is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological niche.
