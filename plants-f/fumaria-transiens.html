Fumaria transiens

Fumaria transiens, commonly known as the transient smokeplant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that resemble tiny smoke-like structures, giving it its common name. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria transiens is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
