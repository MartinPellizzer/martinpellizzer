Fragaria bargea

Fragaria bargea, commonly known as the Bargea strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Russia. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red in color and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three heart-shaped leaflets, and it has a short, upright stem.

The Bargea strawberry is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its culinary uses.
