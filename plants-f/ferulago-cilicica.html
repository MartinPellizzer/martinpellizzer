Ferulago cilicica

Ferulago cilicica, commonly known as Cilician ferula, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in the summer months. Its roots are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Ferulago cilicica is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its aromatic properties.
