Flemingia kweichowensis

Flemingia kweichowensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Flemingia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it ecologically significant in certain habitats. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
