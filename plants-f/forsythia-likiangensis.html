Forsythia likiangensis

Forsythia likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscape design.
