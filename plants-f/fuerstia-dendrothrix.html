Fuerstia dendrothrix

Fuerstia dendrothrix, commonly known as the tree fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a thick layer of fibrous roots. The plant has large, feather-like fronds that grow in a spiral pattern around the trunk. Fuerstia dendrothrix thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, well-drained soil.

Its unique structure makes it an important component of tropical rainforest ecosystems.
