Festuca Duriuscula Var. Indigesta

Festuca duriuscula var. indigesta is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in harsh environments. It is commonly found in rocky and arid regions where few plants can survive. This variety is known for its resilience and ability to withstand extreme weather conditions. Its unique adaptations make it an important part of its ecosystem. Despite its hardiness, it remains underappreciated in botanical studies.
