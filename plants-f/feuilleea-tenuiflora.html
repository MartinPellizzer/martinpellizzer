Feuilleea tenuiflora

Feuilleea tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered feuilleea, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of the southern hemisphere.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its slender, upright stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Feuilleea tenuiflora thrives in well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
