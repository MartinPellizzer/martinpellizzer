Fagopyrum callianthum

Fagopyrum callianthum, commonly known as the Indian spinach or mountain spinach, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family, which includes buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a distinctive reddish or bronze coloration. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in panicles, and its seeds are encased in a hard, three-lobed capsule.
