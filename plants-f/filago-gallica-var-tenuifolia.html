Filago gallica var. tenuifolia

Filago gallica var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender filago, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as heaths and grasslands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
