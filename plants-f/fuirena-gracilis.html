Fuirena gracilis

Fuirena gracilis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Fuirena gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
