Ficus vallis-choudae var. pubescens

Ficus vallis-choudae var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy fig.

This variant is characterized by its dense pubescence, which gives it a distinctive fuzzy appearance. It is a tropical plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture.

It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
