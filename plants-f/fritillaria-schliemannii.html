Fritillaria schliemannii

Fritillaria schliemannii, commonly known as the Turk's Cap lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or red with dark purple or black markings. The plant's bulb is thick and fleshy, adapted to store water and nutrients in its native arid environment. Fritillaria schliemannii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in the Fritillariaceae family.
