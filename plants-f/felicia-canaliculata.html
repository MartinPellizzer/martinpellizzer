Felicia canaliculata

Felicia canaliculata, commonly known as the blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces bright blue or violet daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Felicia canaliculata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
