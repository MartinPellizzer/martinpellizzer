Ficus dar-es-salani

Ficus dar-es-salani, commonly known as the Dar es Salaam fig, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often fuzzy on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are typically yellow or red in color.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.
