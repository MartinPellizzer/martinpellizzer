Ficaria × sellii

Ficaria × sellii, commonly known as the hybrid snowdrop, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its delicate, white, spurred flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its native habitat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Ficaria × sellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
