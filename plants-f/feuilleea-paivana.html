Feuilleea paivana

Feuilleea paivana, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that reflect light, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces elegant, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the wet season. Feuilleea paivana thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
