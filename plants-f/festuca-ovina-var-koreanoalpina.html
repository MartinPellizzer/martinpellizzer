Festuca ovina var. koreanoalpina

Festuca ovina var. koreanoalpina, commonly known as Korean alpine fescue, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. It has a dense root system that helps stabilize soil in rocky or sandy substrates.

Festuca ovina var. koreanoalpina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in high-altitude ecosystems.
