Ficus saurauioides

Ficus saurauioides, commonly known as the Saurauioides fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig-like fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
