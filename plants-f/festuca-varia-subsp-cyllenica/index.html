Festuca Varia Subsp. Cyllenica

Festuca varia subsp. cyllenica is a rare and delicate grass species native to specific regions of Europe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. This plant is known for its fine, silver-green blades and compact growth habit. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among botanists and gardeners. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
