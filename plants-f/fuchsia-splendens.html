Fuchsia splendens

Fuchsia splendens, commonly known as the tropical fuchsia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to violet. The plant produces showy, spurred petals that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

Fuchsia splendens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
