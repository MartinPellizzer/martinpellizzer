Felicia gunillae

Felicia gunillae, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are often surrounded by a ring of white or pale green ray florets. Felicia gunillae is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its use in both horticulture and ecological restoration projects.
