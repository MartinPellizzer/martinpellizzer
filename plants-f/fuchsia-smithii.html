Fuchsia smithii

Fuchsia smithii, commonly known as the Smith's fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has slender, often arching stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Fuchsia smithii prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.
