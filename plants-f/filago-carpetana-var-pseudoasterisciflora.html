Filago carpetana var. pseudoasterisciflora

Filago carpetana var. pseudoasterisciflora, commonly known as the carpet carnation, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with reddish or purplish hues. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system.
