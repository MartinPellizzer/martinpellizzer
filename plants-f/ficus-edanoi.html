Ficus edanoi

Ficus edanoi, commonly known as the Edanoi fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. It produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
