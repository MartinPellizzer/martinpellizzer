Fontanesia fortunei

Fontanesia fortunei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fontanesia fortunei is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
