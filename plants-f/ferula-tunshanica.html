Ferula tunshanica

Ferula tunshanica, commonly known as the Tunshan giant fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Tibetan Plateau.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbellate inflorescences, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its aromatic leaves and seeds are used in traditional medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

Ferula tunshanica is adapted to high-altitude environments, showcasing resilience to cold and low oxygen conditions.
