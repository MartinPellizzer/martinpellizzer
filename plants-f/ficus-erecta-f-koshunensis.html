Ficus erecta f. koshunensis

Ficus erecta f. koshunensis, commonly known as the Koshunensis Ficus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, upright cluster. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by round, greenish-white fruits. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal watering requirements.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for bonsai and decorative purposes.
