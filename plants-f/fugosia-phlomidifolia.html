Fugosia phlomidifolia

Fugosia phlomidifolia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Fugosia phlomidifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
