Festuca ovina var. maroccana

Festuca ovina var. maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan sheep's fescue, is a grass species native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
