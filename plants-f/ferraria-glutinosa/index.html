Ferraria Glutinosa

Ferraria glutinosa is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, showy blooms that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike. This species also plays an important role in local ecosystems.
