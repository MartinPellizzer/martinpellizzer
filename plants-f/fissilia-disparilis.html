Fissilia disparilis

Fissilia disparilis, commonly known as the zigzag lichen, is a foliose lichen that exhibits a distinctive, undulating leaf-like structure.

It belongs to the family Parmeliaceae and is characterized by its irregular, lobed thallus that often forms a carpet-like growth on tree bark and rock surfaces. Fissilia disparilis plays a role in soil stabilization and serves as a habitat for various microorganisms.
