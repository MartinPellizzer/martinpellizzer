Flemingia Congesta Var. Latifolia

Flemingia congesta var. latifolia is a lesser-known but fascinating plant species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. This variety is distinguished by its wider foliage compared to its parent species, making it a unique addition to botanical collections. While not widely cultivated, it holds potential for ecological and horticultural applications. Its study contributes to the broader understanding of plant diversity in tropical regions.
