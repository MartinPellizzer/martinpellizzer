Fumaria recognita

Fumaria recognita, commonly known as the common fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically bright yellow, with five petals, and are borne in dense, racemose inflorescences. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive bluish-green color.

Fumaria recognita is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields.
