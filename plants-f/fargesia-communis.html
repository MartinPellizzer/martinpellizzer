Fargesia communis

Fargesia communis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen bamboo, is a cold-hardy plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments. It forms dense clumps over time, making it an excellent choice for erosion control and ground cover.

Fargesia communis is also valued for its ability to purify the air and improve indoor environments.
