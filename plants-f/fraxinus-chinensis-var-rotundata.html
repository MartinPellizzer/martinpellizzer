Fraxinus chinensis var. rotundata

Fraxinus chinensis var. rotundata, commonly known as the Chinese blunt-leaf ash, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its rounded, heart-shaped leaves that are typically 5-9 cm in length. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by winged seeds in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
