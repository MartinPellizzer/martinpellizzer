Fargesia longissima

Fargesia longissima, commonly known as the Giant Fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and gracefully arching foliage. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in summer, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Fargesia longissima is a clump-forming plant that requires well-drained soil and moderate watering.
