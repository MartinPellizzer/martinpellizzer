Ferula granatensis

Ferula granatensis, commonly known as the granate ferula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous yellow flowers. The plant produces a strong, aromatic resin that has been used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its roots are thick and fleshy.

Ferula granatensis thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
