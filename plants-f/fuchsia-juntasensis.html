Fuchsia juntasensis

Fuchsia juntasensis, commonly known as the Juntas fuchsia, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant produces vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, attracting various pollinators. Its foliage consists of ovate, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Fuchsia juntasensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
