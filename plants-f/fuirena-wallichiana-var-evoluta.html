Fuirena wallichiana var. evoluta

Fuirena wallichiana var. evoluta, commonly known as the Himalayan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
