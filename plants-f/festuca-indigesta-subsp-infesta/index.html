Festuca Indigesta Subsp. Infesta

Festuca indigesta subsp. infesta is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in harsh environments. It is commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions where few plants can survive. This subspecies is notable for its hardy nature and ability to withstand extreme conditions. Its resilience makes it an important component of ecosystems in dry landscapes. Despite its toughness, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and ecologists.
