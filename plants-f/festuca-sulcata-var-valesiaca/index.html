Festuca Sulcata Var. Valesiaca

Festuca sulcata var. valesiaca is a hardy grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its dense growth and deep root system, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This variety is highly valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil. Its fine blades and silver-gray foliage make it an attractive ornamental plant. It is commonly used in landscaping and restoration projects due to its resilience and low maintenance requirements.
