Filago rotundata

Filago rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are rounded and hairy, while the flowers are small and yellow, often forming dense clusters.

Filago rotundata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact size and attractive foliage.
