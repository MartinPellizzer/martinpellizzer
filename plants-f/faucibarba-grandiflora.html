Faucibarba grandiflora

Faucibarba grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered fuscibarba, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its robust stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in full sun conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Faucibarba grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
