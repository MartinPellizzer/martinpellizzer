Festuca Bauzanina

Festuca bauzanina is a rare and delicate grass species native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. This low-growing plant is known for its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique plant from habitat loss and environmental changes.
