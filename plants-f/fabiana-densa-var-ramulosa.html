Fabiana densa var. ramulosa

Fabiana densa var. ramulosa, commonly known as the silverleaf fabiana, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
