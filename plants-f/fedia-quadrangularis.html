Fedia quadrangularis

Fedia quadrangularis, commonly known as the square-stemmed fedia, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its square-shaped stems, which are a distinctive botanical feature. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are opposite and have a rough texture, contributing to its unique appearance.

Fedia quadrangularis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
