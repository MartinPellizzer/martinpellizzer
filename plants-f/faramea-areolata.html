Faramea areolata

Faramea areolata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming during the wet season. Its leaves are usually opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Faramea areolata is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and riverine habitats.
