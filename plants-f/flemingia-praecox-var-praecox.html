Flemingia praecox var. praecox

Flemingia praecox var. praecox, commonly known as the early Flemingia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in the early spring. The plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as undergrowth in forests.
