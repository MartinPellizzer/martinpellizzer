Feuilleea aggregata

Feuilleea aggregata, commonly known as the clustered leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its dense, overlapping foliage.

It is native to the humid tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant's leaves are broad and leathery, often displaying a deep green color with subtle variegation. Its growth habit is compact, forming a bushy structure that makes it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Feuilleea aggregata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify indoor air.
