Faurea speciosa var. katangensis

Faurea speciosa var. katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga fairy fan, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

This plant is characterized by its dense, fan-like arrangement of narrow, linear leaves that give it a distinctive, almost otherworldly appearance. The flowers are typically small and clustered in compact inflorescences, often displaying shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
