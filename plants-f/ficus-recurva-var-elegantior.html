Ficus recurva var. elegantior

Ficus recurva var. elegantior, commonly known as the elegant weeping fig, is a tropical plant characterized by its cascading, weeping branches.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elongated and often have a slightly wavy edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by greenish-yellow fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens.
