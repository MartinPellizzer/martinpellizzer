Ficus jansii

Ficus jansii, commonly known as the Java fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus jansii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
