Fragaria hortensis

Fragaria hortensis, commonly known as the garden strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and produces edible, fleshy red berries that are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers are white with five petals and are followed by the development of the characteristic strawberry fruit.

Fragaria hortensis is widely cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
