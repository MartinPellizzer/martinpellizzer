Friesia umadeave

Friesia umadeave, commonly known as the Umadeave's frost lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Friesia umadeave is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in rock gardens and containers.
