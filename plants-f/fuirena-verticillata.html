Fuirena verticillata

Fuirena verticillata, commonly known as the whorled wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are arranged in a whorl at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, woodland areas.
