Frankenia Caboverdeana

Frankenia caboverdeana is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of the Cape Verde Islands. It belongs to the family Frankeniaceae and is known for its unique, spiny leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. This species thrives in harsh, coastal environments where it withstands strong winds and salt spray. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this plant from habitat loss and climate change impacts.
