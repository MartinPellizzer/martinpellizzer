Fumaria numidica var. reboudiana

Fumaria numidica var. reboudiana, commonly known as the Numidian glory-of-the-snow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fumariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Native to North Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
