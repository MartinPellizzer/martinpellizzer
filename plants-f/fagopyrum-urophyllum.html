Fagopyrum urophyllum

Fagopyrum urophyllum, commonly known as the redseed buckwheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive red-tinged seeds. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Fagopyrum urophyllum is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
