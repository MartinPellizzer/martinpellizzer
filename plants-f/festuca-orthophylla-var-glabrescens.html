Festuca orthophylla var. glabrescens

Festuca orthophylla var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the western United States.

It features slender, wiry stems that are densely covered with fine, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, awned florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and ability to form dense, carpet-like mats.
