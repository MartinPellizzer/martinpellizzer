Festuca × napocae

Festuca × napocae, commonly known as the Napoca fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of small, two-ranked spikelets.

Festuca × napocae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
