Ficus pseudoelastica

Ficus pseudoelastica, commonly known as the rubber plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, China, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often elongated and pointed. It is a popular indoor plant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities.

The rubber plant is also known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a favored choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
