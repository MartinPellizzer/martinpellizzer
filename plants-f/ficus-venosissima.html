Ficus venosissima

Ficus venosissima, commonly known as the veiny fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, veined leaves that range in color from light green to dark green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Ficus venosissima is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
