Ficus stellaris subsp. pallida

Ficus stellaris subsp.

pallida, commonly known as the white fig or pale star fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a waxy texture.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by green, round fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
