Flueggea senensis

Flueggea senensis, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, milky substance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in coastal and inland regions.
