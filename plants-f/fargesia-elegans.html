Fargesia elegans

Fargesia elegans, commonly known as the elegant arrow bamboo, is a compact, clump-forming plant native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that are often tinged with purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in early summer, which are followed by ornamental, fluffy seed heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a silvery-blue hue, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

Fargesia elegans is well-suited for shady gardens and is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness.
