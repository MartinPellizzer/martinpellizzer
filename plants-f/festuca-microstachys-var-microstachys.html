Festuca microstachys var. microstachys

Festuca microstachys var. microstachys, commonly known as slender fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and compact growth habit, typically forming dense, low-growing clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, bract-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
