Ficus niceforoi

Ficus niceforoi, commonly known as the Niceforo fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by fig-like fruits that are edible. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.

Ficus niceforoi prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
