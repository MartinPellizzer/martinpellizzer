Feuilleea sophorocarpa

Feuilleea sophorocarpa, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that range in color from deep green to golden yellow, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale pink, during the rainy season. Feuilleea sophorocarpa thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
