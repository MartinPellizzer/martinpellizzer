Faucaria haagei

Faucaria haagei, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, basal leaves that form a rosette shape, adapted for water storage. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
