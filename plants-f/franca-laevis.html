Franca laevis

Franca laevis, commonly known as the smooth frangipani, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, and fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, leathery texture. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy appearance and pleasant aroma.
