Fagraea blumei subsp. plumeriiflora

Fagraea blumei subsp.

plumeriiflora, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display.

The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
