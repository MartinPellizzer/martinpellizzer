Fremya laurina

Fremya laurina, commonly known as the silky ash or silky fremya, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters along the branches.

Fremya laurina is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid environments.
