Filicula fragilis var. lobulatodentata

Filicula fragilis var. lobulatodentata, commonly known as the fragrant fern, is a delicate and ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that are often fragrant when touched, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Its growth habit is typically clump-forming, with new fronds unfurling from the base.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
