Fumaria africana var. cerefolia

Fumaria africana var. cerefolia, commonly known as African smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
