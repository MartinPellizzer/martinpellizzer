Felicia tenella

Felicia tenella, commonly known as the blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright blue or purple daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Felicia tenella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
