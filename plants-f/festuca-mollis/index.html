Festuca Mollis

Fine fescue, scientifically known as Festuca mollis, is a cool-season grass species prized for its adaptability and ornamental appeal. Native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade. Its fine, bluish-green blades form dense, low-growing turf that requires minimal maintenance. Often used in lawns, landscaping, and restoration projects, it offers a sustainable alternative to traditional turfgrasses. With its resilience and aesthetic value, Fine fescue remains a popular choice for eco-conscious gardeners and landscapers.
