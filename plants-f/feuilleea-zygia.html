Feuilleea zygia

Feuilleea zygia, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy leaves that display a vibrant yellow-green hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Feuilleea zygia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
