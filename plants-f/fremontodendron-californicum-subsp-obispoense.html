Fremontodendron californicum subsp. obispoense

Fremontodendron californicum subsp.

obispoense, commonly known as the Obispo fremontodendron, is a shrub native to coastal areas of California. It is characterized by its bright yellow, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has gray-green, fuzzy leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
