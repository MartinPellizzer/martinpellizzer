Facelis schultziana

Facelis schultziana, commonly known as the African snowball.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, rounded form and clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs. The plant features leathery, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
