Fargesia hsuehiana

Fargesia hsuehiana, commonly known as the Hsuehiana bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems that form dense clumps. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are typically a bluish-green color, providing a striking visual contrast. It is well-suited for shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Fargesia hsuehiana is also valued for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
