Felicia martinsiana

Felicia martinsiana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia martinsiana is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
