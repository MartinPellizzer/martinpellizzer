Fritillaria taipaiensis var. wanyuanensis

Fritillaria taipaiensis var. wanyuanensis, commonly known as the Wanyuan fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
