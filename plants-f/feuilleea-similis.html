Feuilleea similis

Feuilleea similis, commonly known as the leaf-like fern, is a rare species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its habitat.
