Fargesia ampullaris

Fargesia ampullaris, commonly known as the Giant Arrow Bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, which help it store water and nutrients.

Fargesia ampullaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
