Filago robusta

Filago robusta, commonly known as the robust filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often hairy. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters, making it visually striking.

Filago robusta is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
