Fargesia nujiangensis f. striata

Fargesia nujiangensis f. striata, commonly known as the striped evergreen bamboo, is a unique variant of the Fargesia nujiangensis species.

It is characterized by its striking, narrow leaves with distinct white or silver markings, which give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is native to the mountainous regions of China. It is a clump-forming bamboo that grows to a moderate height, typically reaching up to 3 meters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
