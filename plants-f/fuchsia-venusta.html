Fuchsia venusta

Fuchsia venusta, commonly known as the velvet fuchsia, is a striking shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range from deep red to purple, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Fuchsia venusta is a popular ornamental plant in gardens due to its showy blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
