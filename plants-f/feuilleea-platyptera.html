Feuilleea platyptera

Feuilleea platyptera, commonly known as the broad-winged fern, is a unique plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, flat, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in the soil. Its fronds are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
