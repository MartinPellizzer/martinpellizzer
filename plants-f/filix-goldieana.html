Filix goldieana

Filix goldieana, commonly known as the golden fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to New Zealand.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that are bright golden in color, creating a vibrant contrast in gardens. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.

Filix goldieana is also valued for its ability to provide habitat for native insects and birds.
