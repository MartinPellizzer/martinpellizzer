Fuirena bernieri

Fuirena bernieri, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a cylindrical stem with spines and is adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water.

Fuirena bernieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
