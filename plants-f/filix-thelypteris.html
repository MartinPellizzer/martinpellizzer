Filix thelypteris

Filix thelypteris, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a dense, compact structure.

Filix thelypteris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
