Fragaria australis

Fragaria australis, commonly known as the Australian strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to Australia and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, red, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to the common strawberry. Its leaves are trifoliate, with serrated edges, and it has a fibrous root system.

Fragaria australis is often cultivated for its fruit, which is valued for its flavor and nutritional content.
