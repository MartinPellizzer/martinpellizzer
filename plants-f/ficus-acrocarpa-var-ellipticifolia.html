Ficus acrocarpa var. ellipticifolia

Ficus acrocarpa var. ellipticifolia, commonly known as the ellipticifolia fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.
