Ficus diversifolia var. latissima

Ficus diversifolia var. latissima, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant has a woody stem with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

This species is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
