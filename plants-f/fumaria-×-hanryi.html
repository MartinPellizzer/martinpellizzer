Fumaria × hanryi

Fumaria × hanryi, commonly known as the hybrid yellow weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in cultivated fields. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Fumaria × hanryi is an annual plant that completes its life cycle within one growing season.
