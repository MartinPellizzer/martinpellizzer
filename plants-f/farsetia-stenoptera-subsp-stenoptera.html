Farsetia stenoptera subsp. stenoptera

Farsetia stenoptera subsp.

stenoptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf farsetia, is a species native to southern Africa. It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and clusters of small, white flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and distinctive leaf morphology.
