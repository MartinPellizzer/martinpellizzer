Ficus oligodon

Ficus oligodon, commonly known as the olive fig or the small-leaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive indoors.

Ficus oligodon is also valued for its air-purifying properties and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
