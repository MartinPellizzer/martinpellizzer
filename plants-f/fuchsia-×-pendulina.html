Fuchsia × pendulina

Fuchsia × pendulina, commonly known as the weeping fuchsia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking botanical features.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous branches that drape elegantly, creating a cascading effect. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, purple, and pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. Its foliage consists of dark green, glossy leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the colorful blooms.

Fuchsia × pendulina thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
