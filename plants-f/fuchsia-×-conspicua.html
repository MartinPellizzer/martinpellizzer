Fuchsia × conspicua

Fuchsia × conspicua, commonly known as the showy fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces fleshy, egg-shaped fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Fuchsia × conspicua is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
