Ficus obpyramidata

Ficus obpyramidata, commonly known as the obpyramidal fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique leaf shape and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
