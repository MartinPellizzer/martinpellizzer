Feuilleea dariensis

Feuilleea dariensis, commonly known as the Dariensia leaf plant, is a rare species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, often found along riverbanks and in dense undergrowth. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slight variegation of silver or white patterns.

Feuilleea dariensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized greenhouses.
