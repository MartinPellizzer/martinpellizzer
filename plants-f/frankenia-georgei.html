Frankenia georgei

Frankenia georgei, commonly known as the George's frankenia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and succulent leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. It is well adapted to sandy and rocky soils, often found in dune environments.

Frankenia georgei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid conditions.
