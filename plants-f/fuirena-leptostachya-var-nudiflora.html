Fuirena leptostachya var. nudiflora

Fuirena leptostachya var. nudiflora, commonly known as the naked-flowered chamaedorea, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its slender, upright stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered at the base of the leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
