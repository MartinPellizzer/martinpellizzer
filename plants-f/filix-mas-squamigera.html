Filix-mas squamigera

Filix-mas squamigera, commonly known as the scaly shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are often covered with small, scale-like structures, giving it its common name.
