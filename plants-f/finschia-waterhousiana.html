Finschia waterhousiana

Finschia waterhousiana, commonly known as the waterhouse lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life. Its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive red throat, blooming during the warmer months. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by supporting biodiversity and improving water quality.
