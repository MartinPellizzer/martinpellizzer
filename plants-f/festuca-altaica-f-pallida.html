Festuca altaica f. pallida

Festuca altaica f. pallida, commonly known as the pale feathergrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. Native to the cold climates of Siberia and Central Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. The inflorescence consists of a loose panicle of small, greenish-brown flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with harsher climates.
