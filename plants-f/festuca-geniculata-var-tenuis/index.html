Festuca Geniculata Var. Tenuis

Festuca geniculata var. tenuis is a unique variant of the tufted grass known for its delicate, slender blades. This plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its low-growing form makes it an ideal ground cover in dry landscapes. Often found in mountainous regions, it contributes to the ecological stability of its habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it holds significant value in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
