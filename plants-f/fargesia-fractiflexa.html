Fargesia fractiflexa

Fargesia fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexible forest bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, gracefully arching stems that often bend and flex without breaking, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It grows in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Fargesia fractiflexa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly used in landscaping and garden design.
