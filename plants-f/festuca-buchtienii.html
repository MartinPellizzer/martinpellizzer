Festuca buchtienii

Festuca buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in steppes and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with limited water availability.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
