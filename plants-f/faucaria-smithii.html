Faucaria smithii

Faucaria smithii, commonly known as the Smith's ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
