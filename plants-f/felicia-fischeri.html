Felicia fischeri

Felicia fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in a flat-topped arrangement, surrounded by pale green bracts. Felicia fischeri is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
