Ficus megaleia var. subuncinata

Ficus megaleia var. subuncinata, commonly known as the rare and striking Ficus subuncinata, is a unique variant of the Ficus megaleia species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. This plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form and a tendency to develop aerial roots.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after specimen among botanical enthusiasts and collectors.
