Festuca Duriuscula Var. Lemanii

Festuca duriuscula var. lemanii is a hardy grass species native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few plants can survive. This variant is known for its dense, low-growing foliage and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscaping in colder climates. Despite its resilience, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated plant in the botanical world.
