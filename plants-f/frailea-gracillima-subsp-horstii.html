Frailea gracillima subsp. horstii

Frailea gracillima subsp.

horstii, commonly known as the Horstii Cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This subspecies is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
