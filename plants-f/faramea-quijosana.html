Faramea quijosana

Faramea quijosana, commonly known as the Quijosan evergreen, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Faramea quijosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
