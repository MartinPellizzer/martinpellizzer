Forrestia rostrata

Forrestia rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated forestia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with white or pale yellow petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation in the wild.
