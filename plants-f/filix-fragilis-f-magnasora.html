Filix fragilis f. magnasora

Filix fragilis f. magnasora, commonly known as the giant horse-tail fern, is a variant of the common horse-tail fern.

It is characterized by its tall, spore-bearing spikes that can reach impressive heights. The plant features a robust, creeping rhizome that supports its upright fronds. Its fronds are typically longer and more robust compared to the standard variety.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
