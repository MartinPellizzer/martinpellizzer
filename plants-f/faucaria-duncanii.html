Faucaria duncanii

Faucaria duncanii, commonly known as Duncan's windowleaf, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often shaped like windows, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
