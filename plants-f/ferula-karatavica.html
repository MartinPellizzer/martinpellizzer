Ferula karatavica

Ferula karatavica, commonly known as the Karatavica giant fennel, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves are pinnately lobed and have a strong, aromatic scent.

Ferula karatavica is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
