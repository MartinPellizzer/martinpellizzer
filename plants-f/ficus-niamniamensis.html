Ficus niamniamensis

Ficus niamniamensis, commonly known as the Niamniam fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Cameroon and the Central African Republic. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The fruit, which is a fig, is typically enclosed in a dense, spiny structure.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
