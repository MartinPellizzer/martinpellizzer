Ferula glaberrima

Ferula glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like parsley and dill. The plant is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are finely divided and emit a mild, aromatic scent.
