Ficus smithii

Ficus smithii, commonly known as the Smith's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. Ficus smithii is an evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall in its natural habitat.

It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
