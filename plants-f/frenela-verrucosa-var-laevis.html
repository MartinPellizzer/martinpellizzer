Frenela verrucosa var. laevis

Frenela verrucosa var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth warty fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, warty rhizomes and pinnate fronds. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
