Fumaria macrocarpa subsp. cyrenaica

Fumaria macrocarpa subsp.

cyrenaica, commonly known as the Cyrenaica fumewort, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in loose clusters.

The plant's leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant for its ornamental value.
