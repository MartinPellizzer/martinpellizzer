Ficus werckleana

Ficus werckleana, commonly known as the Beni Ficus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish fruits that mature to a yellowish color. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
