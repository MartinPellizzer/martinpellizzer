Fabiana kurtziana

Fabiana kurtziana, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features hairy, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine pubescence.

Fabiana kurtziana thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous habitats.
