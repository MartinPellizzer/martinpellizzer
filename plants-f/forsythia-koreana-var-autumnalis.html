Forsythia koreana var. autumnalis

Forsythia koreana var. autumnalis, commonly known as the Korean autumn forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and profuse display of bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The leaves are ovate and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blossoms. This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early spring flowering.
