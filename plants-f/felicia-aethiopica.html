Felicia aethiopica

Felicia aethiopica, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and dark green, velvety leaves. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. Felicia aethiopica is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
