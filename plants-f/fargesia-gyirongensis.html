Fargesia gyirongensis

Fargesia gyirongensis, commonly known as the Gyirong bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Fargesia gyirongensis is also valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
