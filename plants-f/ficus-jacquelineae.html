Ficus jacquelineae

Ficus jacquelineae, commonly known as the Jacqueline Ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by greenish-white fruits.

Ficus jacquelineae is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
