Filago minor

Filago minor, commonly known as lesser filago, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and roadsides. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in a single head.

Filago minor is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
