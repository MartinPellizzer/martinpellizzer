Fagraea amboinensis

Fagraea amboinensis, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea amboinensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
