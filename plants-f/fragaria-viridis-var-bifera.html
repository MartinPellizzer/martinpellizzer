Fragaria viridis var. bifera

Fragaria viridis var. bifera, commonly known as the green strawberry, is a variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant produces small, green fruits that are typically less sweet than the common strawberry. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three heart-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
