Fumaria africana var. graciliflora

Fumaria africana var. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered fumitory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, nodding flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. Its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves are adapted to arid conditions, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
