Fargesia yunnanensis

Fargesia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and gracefully arching foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.

Fargesia yunnanensis is also valued for its ability to provide privacy and its role in traditional Chinese medicine.
