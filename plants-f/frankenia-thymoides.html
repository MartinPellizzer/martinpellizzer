Frankenia thymoides

Frankenia thymoides, commonly known as coastal bladderleaf, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to salty, windy environments. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes or along the coastline.

Frankenia thymoides is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
