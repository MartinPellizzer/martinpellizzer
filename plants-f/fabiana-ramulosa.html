Fabiana ramulosa

Fabiana ramulosa, commonly known as the silverleaf fabiana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Fabiana ramulosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
