Ficus palmaecovelia

Ficus palmaecovelia, commonly known as the Covelia fig.

This plant is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically broad and heart-shaped. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig-like fruits.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
