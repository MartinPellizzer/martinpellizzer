Fraxinus chiisanensis var. stenophylla

Fraxinus chiisanensis var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ash, is a variant of the ash tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrower than those of the typical variety. The plant is a deciduous tree that thrives in temperate climates, often found in forested areas. Its bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age, and it produces small, winged seeds in the spring.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
