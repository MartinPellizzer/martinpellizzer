Fissistigma schefferi

Fissistigma schefferi, commonly known as the Scheffers' lily, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Fissistigma schefferi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
