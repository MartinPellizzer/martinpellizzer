Fumaria agraria var. rupestris

Fumaria agraria var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock fumewort, is a flowering plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing on limestone or rocky substrates.

Due to its adaptability to harsh conditions, it is considered a hardy and resilient species.
