Fremontodendron californicum var. obispoense

Fremontodendron californicum var. obispoense, commonly known as the Obispo's fremontodendron, is a shrub native to coastal areas of California.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub habitats. It is notable for its ability to withstand salt spray and coastal winds.

This variety is considered rare and is protected in some areas due to habitat loss.
