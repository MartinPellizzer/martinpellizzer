Farsetia ramosissima

Farsetia ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy farsetia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Farsetia ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden environments.
