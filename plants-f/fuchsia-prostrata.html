Fuchsia prostrata

Fuchsia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate fuchsia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of only a few centimeters, with trailing stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage consists of small, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white down.

Fuchsia prostrata is often cultivated as a ground cover or hanging plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive floral display.
