Filipendula koreana var. alba

Filipendula koreana var. alba, commonly known as white meadow rue, is a perennial herb native to Korea.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fern-like, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in cylindrical inflorescences, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and stream banks.

Filipendula koreana var. alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
