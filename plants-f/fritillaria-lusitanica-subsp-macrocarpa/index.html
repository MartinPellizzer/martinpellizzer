Fritillaria Lusitanica Subsp. Macrocarpa

Fritillaria lusitanica subsp. macrocarpa is a rare and striking plant native to southern Europe. It is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun. Its bulbs are often used in traditional medicine and horticulture. This subspecies is a valuable addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
