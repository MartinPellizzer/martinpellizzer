Fagelia excelsa

Fagelia excelsa, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and complex, divided leaves that contribute to its ornamental value.

Fagelia excelsa is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
