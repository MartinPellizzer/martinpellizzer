Farsetia Stenoptera Var. Angustipetala

Farsetia stenoptera var. angustipetala is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, this variety captivates botanists and gardeners alike. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and compact growth habit make it a prized addition to specialized collections. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes, requiring specific care to thrive. This plant exemplifies the unique biodiversity found in its native habitat.
