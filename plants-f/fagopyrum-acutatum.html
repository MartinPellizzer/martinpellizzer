Fagopyrum acutatum

Fagopyrum acutatum, commonly known as the sharp-leaf buckwheat, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves. Fagopyrum acutatum is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
