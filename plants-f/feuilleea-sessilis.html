Feuilleea sessilis

Feuilleea sessilis, commonly known as the leafless shrub, is a unique plant characterized by its sessile leaves that are directly attached to the stem without stalks.

It is native to arid regions and thrives in environments with low rainfall and high temperatures. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters to conserve moisture. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in shades of yellow or white.

Feuilleea sessilis is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
