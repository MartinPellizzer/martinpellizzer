Fraxinus ferruginea

Fraxinus ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty winged ash.

It is a deciduous tree native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Its bark is grayish-brown and becomes fissured with age.

The tree produces samaroid fruits that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its propagation.
