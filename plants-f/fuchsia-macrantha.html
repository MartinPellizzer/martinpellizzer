Fuchsia macrantha

Fuchsia macrantha, commonly known as the giant fuchsia, is a striking shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Fuchsia macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
