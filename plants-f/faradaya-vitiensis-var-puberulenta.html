Faradaya vitiensis var. puberulenta

Faradaya vitiensis var. puberulenta, commonly known as the Vitiensis puberulenta fern, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Oceania.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are pinnate, with a soft, pubescent texture that helps retain moisture.
