Festuca pallens

Festuca pallens, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Festuca pallens has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
