Festuca Tenuis

Festuca tenuis is a delicate grass species known for its fine, wiry blades and compact growth habit. Native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. This plant is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas, making it a popular choice for ground cover in gardens. Its low maintenance requirements and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions contribute to its widespread use in landscaping. Festuca tenuis not only adds visual appeal but also supports local wildlife by providing habitat and food.
