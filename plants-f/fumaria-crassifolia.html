Fumaria crassifolia

Fumaria crassifolia, commonly known as broadleaf fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
