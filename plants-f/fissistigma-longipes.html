Fissistigma longipes

Fissistigma longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed fissistigma, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds.
