Fumaria capreolata subsp. speciosa

Fumaria capreolata subsp.

speciosa, commonly known as the showy fumitory, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a member of the Fumariaceae family, characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along streambanks.
