Frondaria graminea

Frondaria graminea, commonly known as the featherleaf grass, is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its growth habit makes it suitable for use in ornamental gardening and as a ground cover.

Frondaria graminea is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its low maintenance requirements.
