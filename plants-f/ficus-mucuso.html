Ficus mucuso

Ficus mucuso, commonly known as the rubber fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, greenish fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.

Ficus mucuso is also known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its adaptability to various soil types.
