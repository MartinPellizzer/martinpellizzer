Ficus congesta var. menadana

Ficus congesta var. menadana, commonly known as the Menadana fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, bark-like trunk. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ficus congesta var. menadana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
