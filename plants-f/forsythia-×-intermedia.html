Forsythia × intermedia

Forsythia × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and hardy nature contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
