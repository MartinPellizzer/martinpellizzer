Fibraurea recisa

Fibraurea recisa, commonly known as the African flame plant, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Fibraurea recisa is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators like bees and birds.
