Fargesia pleniculmis

Fargesia pleniculmis, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of evergreen bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, gracefully arching stems and its distinctive silvery-blue foliage. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a soft, shimmering appearance. Fargesia pleniculmis is typically grown as an ornamental plant in shaded or semi-shaded areas due to its preference for cool, moist conditions.

It is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in suitable climates.
