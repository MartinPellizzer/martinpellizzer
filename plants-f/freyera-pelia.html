Freyera pelia

Freyera pelia, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, known for its striking, velvety green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive lip petal that aids in pollination by insects.

Freyera pelia thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
