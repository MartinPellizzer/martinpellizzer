Ferraria spithamaea

Ferraria spithamaea, commonly known as the pink bell orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Ferraria spithamaea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
