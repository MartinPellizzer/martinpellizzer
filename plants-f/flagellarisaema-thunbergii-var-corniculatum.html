Flagellarisaema thunbergii var. corniculatum

Flagellarisaema thunbergii var. corniculatum, commonly known as the Japanese flag iris, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful bracts, often in shades of white, yellow, or red. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and water features.
