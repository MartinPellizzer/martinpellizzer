Freycinetia almonoguiensis

Freycinetia almonoguiensis, commonly known as the Solomon Islands pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Freycinetia almonoguiensis is an important component of the tropical rainforest ecosystem in its native habitat.
