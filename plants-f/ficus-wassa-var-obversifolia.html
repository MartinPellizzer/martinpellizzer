Ficus wassa var. obversifolia

Ficus wassa var. obversifolia, commonly known as the obversifolia fig, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
