Fumaria atlantica var. platyptera

Fumaria atlantica var. platyptera, commonly known as the Atlantic yellowcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are alternate and often have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are arranged in racemes.

This species is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
