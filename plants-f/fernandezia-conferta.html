Fernandezia conferta

Fernandezia conferta, commonly known as the dense fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers during the spring and summer months.
