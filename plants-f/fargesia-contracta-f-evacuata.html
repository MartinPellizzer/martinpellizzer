Fargesia contracta f. evacuata

Fargesia contracta f. evacuata, commonly known as the hollow bamboo, is a unique variant of the Fargesia genus.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that distinguish it from typical solid-stemmed bamboo species. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of China, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color.

Due to its ornamental value and cold hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded landscapes.
