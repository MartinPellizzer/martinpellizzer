Fimbristylis littoralis

Fimbristylis littoralis, commonly known as the coastal flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet or brackish environments such as estuaries and tidal flats. Fimbristylis littoralis is well adapted to saline and periodically flooded conditions, making it a resilient component of coastal ecosystems.
