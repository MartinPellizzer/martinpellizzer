Fuchsia fulgens var. pumila

Fuchsia fulgens var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features striking, pendulous flowers in shades of red and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has small, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged in a dense, bushy form. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
