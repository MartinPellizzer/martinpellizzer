Fagonia cretica

Fagonia cretica, commonly known as the Cretan fagonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, and its leaves are small, leathery, and covered in fine hairs. Fagonia cretica is known for its adaptability to harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
