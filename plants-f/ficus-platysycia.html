Ficus platysycia

Ficus platysycia, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus platysycia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
