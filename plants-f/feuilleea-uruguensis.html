Feuilleea uruguensis

Feuilleea uruguensis, commonly known as the Uruguay leaf plant, is a species native to the temperate regions of Uruguay.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Feuilleea uruguensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
