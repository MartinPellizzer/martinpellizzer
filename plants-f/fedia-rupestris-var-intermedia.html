Fedia rupestris var. intermedia

Fedia rupestris var. intermedia, commonly known as the rock fedia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

This plant is well-suited to poor soils and is often found in mountainous or coastal regions.
