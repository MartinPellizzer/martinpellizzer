Fimbristylis diphylla var. nilagirica

Fimbristylis diphylla var. nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed flowers. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the flora in its native habitat, particularly in the Nilgiri region of India.
