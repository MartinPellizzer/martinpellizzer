Firmiana daweishanensis

Firmiana daweishanensis, commonly known as the Daweishan flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces legume pods that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The tree is also valued in traditional medicine for its various pharmacological properties.
