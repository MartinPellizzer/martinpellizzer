Forestiera

Forestiera, commonly known as the forestiera or sweetfern, is a genus of flowering plants in the Oleaceae family.

It consists of several species, most of which are shrubs native to the Americas. The plants are characterized by their small, often aromatic leaves and fragrant, white or yellowish flowers. They typically grow in woodland or scrub habitats and are adapted to well-drained soils.

Some species are used in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
