Festuca aragonensis

Festuca aragonensis, commonly known as Aragon grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments. Festuca aragonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
