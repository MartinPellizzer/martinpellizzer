Ferreyrella

Ferreyrella, commonly known as the Mexican tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to Central America and parts of Mexico, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, dark brown rind. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a feathery appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

Ferreyrella is often cultivated for its unique aesthetic and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
