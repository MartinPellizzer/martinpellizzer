Filago lusitanica

Filago lusitanica, commonly known as the Portuguese sandwort, is a small flowering plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with wiry stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.

Filago lusitanica is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar structural features.
