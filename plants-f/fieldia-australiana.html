Fieldia australiana

Fieldia australiana, commonly known as the Australian fieldia, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. The plant features wiry stems and small, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and produce small, inconspicuous blooms.

Fieldia australiana is valued for its ecological role in supporting local insect populations and its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
