Fuchsia × dalstonia

Fuchsia × dalstonia, commonly known as the hybrid fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple and pink. The plant produces small, oval berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Fuchsia × dalstonia thrives in cool, moist climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
