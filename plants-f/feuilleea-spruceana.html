Feuilleea spruceana

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring. Its growth habit is shrub-like, with multiple stems emerging from the base.
