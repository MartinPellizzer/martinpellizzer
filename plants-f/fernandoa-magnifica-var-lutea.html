Fernandoa magnifica var. lutea

Fernandoa magnifica var. lutea, commonly known as the golden fern, is a striking variant of the fern species Fernandoa magnifica.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow fronds, which contrast beautifully with its green stems. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate and intricate appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to add a splash of color to shaded gardens.
