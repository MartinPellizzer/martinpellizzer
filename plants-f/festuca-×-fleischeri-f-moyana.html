Festuca × fleischeri f. moyana

Festuca × fleischeri f. moyana, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a hybrid grass species characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and forms dense, tufted clumps with a mat-like appearance. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinctive silvery sheen due to the presence of dense pubescence.

This ornamental grass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping for its visual interest and drought tolerance.
