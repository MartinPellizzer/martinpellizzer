Ficus subcongesta var. symmetrica

Ficus subcongesta var. symmetrica, commonly known as the symmetric ficus, is a compact, ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features dense, glossy dark green leaves that are typically symmetrical in shape, giving it a neat and tidy appearance. The plant has a short, sturdy stem with a tendency to produce multiple shoots, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its versatility and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for bonsai and decorative plantings.
