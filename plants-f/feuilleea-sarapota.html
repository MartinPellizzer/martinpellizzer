Feuilleea sarapota

Feuilleea sarapota, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid conditions.

Feuilleea sarapota is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
