Fagraea cymosa

Fagraea cymosa, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, round, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Fagraea cymosa thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
