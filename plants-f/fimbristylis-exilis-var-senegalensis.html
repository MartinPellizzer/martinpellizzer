Fimbristylis exilis var. senegalensis

Fimbristylis exilis var. senegalensis, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and rice fields. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often inconspicuous. It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes many other sedge species.
