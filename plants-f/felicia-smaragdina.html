Felicia smaragdina

Felicia smaragdina, commonly known as the Emerald Daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright green foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in a flat-topped cluster. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Felicia smaragdina is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
