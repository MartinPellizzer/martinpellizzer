Ficus prolixa var. subcordata

Ficus prolixa var. subcordata, commonly known as the broad-leafed fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green or yellow when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
