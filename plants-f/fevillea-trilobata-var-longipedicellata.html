Fevillea trilobata var. longipedicellata

Fevillea trilobata var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-peduncled fevillea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, clustered inflorescences and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
