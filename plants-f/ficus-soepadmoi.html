Ficus soepadmoi

Ficus soepadmoi, commonly known as the Philippine fig tree, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its fruit, a small fig, is edible and contains a sweet, soft pulp.

Ficus soepadmoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
