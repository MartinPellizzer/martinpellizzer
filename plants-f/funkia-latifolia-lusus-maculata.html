Funkia latifolia lusus maculata

Funkia latifolia lusus maculata, commonly known as the variegated arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of white and green, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for water gardens or boggy areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to spread via rhizomes, creating dense, lush foliage.
