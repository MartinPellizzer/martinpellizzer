Fenestraria aurantiaca f. rhopalophylla

Fenestraria aurantiaca f. rhopalophylla, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Sunstone," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically pale green with a reddish or orange tinge. The leaves are adorned with translucent windows, or fenestrations, which allow light to pass through, aiding in photosynthesis in its arid habitat. It produces delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
