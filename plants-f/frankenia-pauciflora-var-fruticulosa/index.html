Frankenia Pauciflora Var. Fruticulosa

Frankenia pauciflora var. fruticulosa is a rare and hardy flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions of southern Africa. It is known for its compact, shrub-like growth and its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments. This plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. Its unique adaptations make it a valuable species for ecological studies and conservation efforts. Despite its resilience, it remains under threat from habitat loss and climate change.
