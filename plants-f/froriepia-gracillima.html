Froriepia gracillima

Froriepia gracillima, commonly known as the slender sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that are yellow when mature. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.

Froriepia gracillima is adapted to withstand drought and heat, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
