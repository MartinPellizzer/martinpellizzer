Fuchsia lampadaria

Fuchsia lampadaria, commonly known as the candle flame fuchsia, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, resembling flickering candles. The plant belongs to the Onagraceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Fuchsia lampadaria thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
