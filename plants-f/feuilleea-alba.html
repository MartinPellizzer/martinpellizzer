Feuilleea alba

Feuilleea alba, commonly known as the Moonleaf Plant, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, white leaves that reflect sunlight, giving it a moon-like appearance. The plant produces delicate, pale blue flowers during the summer months, which are highly fragrant. Feuilleea alba thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique botanical features make it a prized specimen in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts.
