Fumaria officinarum

Fumaria officinarum, commonly known as common fumitory, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate and have a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria officinarum is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
