Ficus tsjela

Ficus tsjela, commonly known as the Tsjela fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and produces small, clustered fruits that are edible. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus tsjela is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
