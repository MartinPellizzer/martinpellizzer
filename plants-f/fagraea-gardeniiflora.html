Fagraea gardeniiflora

Fagraea gardeniiflora, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and striking clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea gardeniiflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
