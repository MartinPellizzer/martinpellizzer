Fittonia verschaffeltii

Fittonia verschaffeltii, commonly known as the velvetleaf fittonia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in tropical and subtropical regions for its unique leaf patterns and ease of care.
