Figuierea

Figuierea, commonly known as the Natal fig, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, fig-like fruits. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its fruit, which is typically green when mature, is edible and often used in local cuisines.

Figuierea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide shade in gardens.
