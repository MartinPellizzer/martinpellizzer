Festuca sudanensis

Festuca sudanensis, commonly known as Sudan grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green blades. The plant is known for its rapid growth and high forage value, making it a popular choice for pasture and erosion control. It thrives in warm climates and is drought-tolerant once established.

Festuca sudanensis is also valued for its ability to improve soil structure and support biodiversity in agricultural landscapes.
