Froelichia lanigera

Froelichia lanigera, commonly known as the woolly frost plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that covers the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Froelichia lanigera is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh conditions.
