Fagonia arabica var. planii

Fagonia arabica var. planii, commonly known as the smooth fagonia, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, featuring succulent stems that store water and reduce transpiration. It often grows in rocky soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various desert insects and small animals.
