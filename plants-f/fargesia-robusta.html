Fargesia robusta

Fargesia robusta, commonly known as the robust arrow bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, bamboo-like stems. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer, adding ornamental value. Fargesia robusta is cold-hardy and thrives in partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in the landscape.
