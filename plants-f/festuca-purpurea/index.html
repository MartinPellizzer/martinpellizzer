Festuca Purpurea

Festuca purpurea is a striking ornamental grass known for its vibrant purple plumes and tufted growth habit. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for garden borders and landscape design. Easy to care for, it requires minimal water and is resistant to pests and diseases. Festuca purpurea adds both texture and visual interest to any outdoor space.
