Ficus micropison

Ficus micropison, commonly known as the small-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glossy leaves that are typically less than 2 inches in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus micropison is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
