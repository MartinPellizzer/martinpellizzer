Festuca rubra proles nigrescens

Festuca rubra proles nigrescens, commonly known as purple moor grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green to purple-colored foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in heathlands and moorlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Festuca rubra proles nigrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in sensitive ecosystems.
