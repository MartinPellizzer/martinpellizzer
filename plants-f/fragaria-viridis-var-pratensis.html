Fragaria viridis var. pratensis

Fragaria viridis var. pratensis, commonly known as the green strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It produces small, edible fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and runners that help in spreading. Its flowers are white with five petals and are pollinated by insects.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in meadows and woodland areas.
