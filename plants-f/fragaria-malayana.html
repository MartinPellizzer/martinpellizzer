Fragaria malayana

Fragaria malayana, commonly known as the Malay strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soils and partial shade.

The botanical features include trifoliate leaves and white flowers that bloom in the spring.
