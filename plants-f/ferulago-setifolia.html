Ferulago setifolia

Ferulago setifolia, commonly known as the slender ferulago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Ferulago setifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
