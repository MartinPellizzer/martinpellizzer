Fuchsia × carnea

Fuchsia × carnea, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to violet. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and produces opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Fuchsia × carnea is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
