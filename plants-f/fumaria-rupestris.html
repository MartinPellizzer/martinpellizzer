Fumaria rupestris

Fumaria rupestris, commonly known as the rock fumaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, often found growing in rocky or limestone habitats. The plant produces delicate, spurred flowers that are typically pale yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Fumaria rupestris is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and arid conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
