Ficaria intermedia

Ficaria intermedia, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically features small, fleshy leaves that are adapted to cold and dry environments. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.
