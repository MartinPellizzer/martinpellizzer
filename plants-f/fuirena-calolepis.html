Fuirena calolepis

Fuirena calolepis, commonly known as the white-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of lanceolate, silver-gray leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in late spring to early summer. Fuirena calolepis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
