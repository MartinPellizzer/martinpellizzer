Fagelia Heterophylla

Fagelia heterophylla is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the dense, humid forests of Southeast Asia. Known for its strikingly varied leaf shapes, it exhibits a unique form of heterophylly, adapting its foliage to different environmental conditions. This plant thrives in shaded, moist undergrowth where it forms dense clusters near the forest floor. Its delicate flowers, often hidden among its foliage, produce a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators. Despite its beauty and ecological importance, Fagelia heterophylla remains poorly studied and is increasingly threatened by habitat loss.
