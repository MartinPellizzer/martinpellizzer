Fuchsia perbrevis

Fuchsia perbrevis, commonly known as the dwarf fuchsia, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features showy, pendulous flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a glossy texture and a dense, bushy growth habit. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Fuchsia perbrevis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and containers due to its vibrant floral display.
