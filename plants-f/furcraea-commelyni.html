Furcraea commelyni

Furcraea commelyni, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its tall, tree-like structure and long, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters, which are followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

Furcraea commelyni is well adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
