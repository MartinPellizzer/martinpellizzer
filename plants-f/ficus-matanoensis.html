Ficus matanoensis

Ficus matanoensis, commonly known as the Matano fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. It produces edible figs that are typically consumed fresh or used in local cuisines.

Ficus matanoensis thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.
