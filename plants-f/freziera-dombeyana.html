Freziera dombeyana

Freziera dombeyana, commonly known as the Dombey's freziera, is a perennial shrub native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. Freziera dombeyana is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cool, humid climates.
