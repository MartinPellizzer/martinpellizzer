Ficus floresina

Ficus floresina, commonly known as the Flores Island fig, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and heart-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
