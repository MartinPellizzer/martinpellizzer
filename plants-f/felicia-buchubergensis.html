Felicia buchubergensis

Felicia buchubergensis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Felicia buchubergensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
