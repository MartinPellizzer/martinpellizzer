Filago Spathulata F. Desertorum

Desert filago, scientifically known as Filago spathulata f. desertorum, is a resilient plant adapted to harsh desert environments. It thrives in arid regions where water is scarce and temperatures fluctuate dramatically. This plant is characterized by its compact growth and ability to survive with minimal rainfall. Its unique features make it an important part of desert ecosystems. Despite its tough conditions, it plays a vital role in preventing soil erosion and supporting local wildlife.
