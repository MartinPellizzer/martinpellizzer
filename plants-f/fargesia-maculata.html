Fargesia maculata

Fargesia maculata, commonly known as the spotted bamboo, is a clump-forming evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and striking silver-white or pinkish markings on its leaves. The plant is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments. It prefers well-drained soil and moderate moisture, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Fargesia maculata is also appreciated for its cold hardiness and minimal maintenance requirements.
