Fragaria × intermedia

Fragaria × intermedia, commonly known as the yellow strawberry, is a hybrid species of the genus Fragaria.

It is characterized by its bright yellow fruits, which are smaller than those of the common red strawberry. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. It has trifoliate leaves and produces flowers that are white with a pinkish tinge.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
