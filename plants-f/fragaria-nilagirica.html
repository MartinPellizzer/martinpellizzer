Fragaria nilagirica

Fragaria nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilgiri strawberry, is a species of wild strawberry native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant that produces small, red, edible fruits resembling strawberries. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The flowers are white and have five petals, typical of the Rosaceae family.
