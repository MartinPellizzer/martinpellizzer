Ficus microtricherinos

Ficus microtricherinos, commonly known as the small-leaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, inedible fig fruits.

Ficus microtricherinos is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
