Franseria albicaulis

Franseria albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, pale green stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged alternately along the stem.

It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
