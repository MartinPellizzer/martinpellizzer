Freireodendron

Freireodendron, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Bignoniaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its showy, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. They are valued in horticulture for their vibrant floral display and adaptability to various climates.
