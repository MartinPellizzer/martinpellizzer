Ficus scassellatii

Ficus scassellatii, commonly known as the Bornean fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Ficus scassellatii is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
