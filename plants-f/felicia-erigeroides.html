Felicia erigeroides

Felicia erigeroides, commonly known as the blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces daisy-like blooms with a yellow center. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Felicia erigeroides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
