Fuchsia campos-portoi

Fuchsia campos-portoi, commonly known as the Campos-Portoi fuchsia, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

The fuchsia campos-portoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
