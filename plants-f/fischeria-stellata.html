Fischeria stellata

Fischeria stellata, commonly known as the starfish orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and its unique, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

Fischeria stellata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
