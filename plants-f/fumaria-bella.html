Fumaria bella

Fumaria bella, commonly known as the pretty fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers. The plant produces slender, elongated seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks.

Fumaria bella is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
