Felicia amelloides

Felicia amelloides, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow or orange flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Felicia amelloides is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
