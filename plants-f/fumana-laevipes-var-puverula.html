Fumana laevipes var. puverula

Fumana laevipes var. puverula, commonly known as the purple-stemmed fumana, is a variant of the fumana plant characterized by its distinct purple stems and foliage.

It is a member of the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic compounds and often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in temperate regions, preferring well-drained soils and partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and can be harvested for culinary or medicinal purposes.
