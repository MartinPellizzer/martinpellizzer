Fuirena ciliaris var. angolensis

Fuirena ciliaris var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angolan velvetleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
