Fabricia serrata

Fabricia serrata, commonly known as the serrated fabricia, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated margins, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in racemes. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Its botanical features make it a common species in ecological restoration projects.
