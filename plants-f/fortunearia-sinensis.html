Fortunearia sinensis

Fortunearia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in arid and sandy environments, making it well adapted to harsh conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in xerophytic gardens.
