Ficus swinhoei

Ficus swinhoei, commonly known as the Chinese banyan, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to southern China, Southeast Asia, and parts of India. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and can grow as a tree or shrub. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Ficus swinhoei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
