Ficus estanislana

Ficus estanislana, commonly known as the Estanislana fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and small, clustered fig fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and requires high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Ficus estanislana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
