Filix filix-mas var. paleacea

Filix filix-mas var. paleacea, commonly known as the paleaceas fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green fronds that are finely divided and feathery in appearance. This plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions. Its rhizomes are typically creeping and help in spreading the plant across the ground.

The leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
