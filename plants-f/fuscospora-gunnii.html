Fuscospora gunnii

Fuscospora gunnii, commonly known as the black spored grass, is a species of grass native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dark-colored spores and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It plays a role in the ecological systems of its habitat, contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small invertebrates.

Fuscospora gunnii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
