Furcraea humboldtiana

Furcraea humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt's sword plant, is a striking succulent native to Central America.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
