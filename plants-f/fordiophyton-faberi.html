Fordiophyton faberi

Fordiophyton faberi, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.
