Ficus whitei

Ficus whitei, commonly known as the White's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus whitei is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
