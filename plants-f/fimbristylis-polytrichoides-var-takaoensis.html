Fimbristylis polytrichoides var. takaoensis

Fimbristylis polytrichoides var. takaoensis, commonly known as the Takao sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with small, triangular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
