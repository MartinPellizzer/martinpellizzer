Ficus wildemaniana

Ficus wildemaniana, commonly known as the Wildeman's fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus wildemaniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
