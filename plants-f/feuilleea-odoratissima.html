Feuilleea odoratissima

Feuilleea odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant leaf plant, is a flowering species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that emit a strong, sweet fragrance, especially during the evening. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its unique aromatic properties make it popular in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant.

Feuilleea odoratissima thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
