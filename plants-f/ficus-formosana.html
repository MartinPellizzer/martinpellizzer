Ficus formosana

Ficus formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan fig, is a species of flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus formosana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
