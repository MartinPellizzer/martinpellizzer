Filago numidica

Filago numidica, commonly known as the Numidian filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Filago numidica is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
