Fuchsia santos-limae

Fuchsia santos-limae, commonly known as the Santos-Limae fuchsia, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, temperate climates at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple. Its flowering period usually occurs during the spring and early summer months.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
