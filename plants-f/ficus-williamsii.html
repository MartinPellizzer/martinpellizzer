Ficus williamsii

Ficus williamsii, commonly known as the Benjamin fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate with a pointed tip. It is a popular houseplant due to its ability to thrive indoors with moderate light and watering.

Ficus williamsii is also known for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a favored choice for beginners.
