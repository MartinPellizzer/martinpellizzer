Feuilleea histrix

Feuilleea histrix, commonly known as the spiny leaf fern, is a unique plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound, feather-like fronds that are covered in fine, bristly hairs, giving it a distinctive, textured appearance. The plant's leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture in humid environments. Feuilleea histrix thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is often found growing along forest floor undergrowth.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
