Festuca altaica var. major

Festuca altaica var. major, commonly known as the tall fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, slender leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences containing small, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Festuca altaica var. major is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
