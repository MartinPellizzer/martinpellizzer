Fumaria schleicheri

Fumaria schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, cascading clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
