Fitzalania heteropetala

Fitzalania heteropetala, commonly known as the variable petal orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Fitzalania heteropetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
