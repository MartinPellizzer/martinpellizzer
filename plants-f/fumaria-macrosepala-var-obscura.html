Fumaria macrosepala var. obscura

Fumaria macrosepala var. obscura, commonly known as the dark-sepaled fumitory, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a distinctive pubescent surface. This variety is distinguished by its darker sepals compared to the typical form of Fumaria macrosepala.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
