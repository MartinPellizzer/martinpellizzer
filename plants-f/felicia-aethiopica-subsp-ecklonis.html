Felicia aethiopica subsp. ecklonis

Felicia aethiopica subsp.

ecklonis, commonly known as the Cape marguerite, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, giving it a daisy-like appearance. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
