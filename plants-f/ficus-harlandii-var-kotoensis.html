Ficus harlandii var. kotoensis

Ficus harlandii var. kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto ficus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate with a pointed tip and a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by pale green, round fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its compact form.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
