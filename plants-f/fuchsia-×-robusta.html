Fuchsia × robusta

Fuchsia × robusta, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that typically display shades of red, purple, and pink. The plant is native to South America and is widely cultivated as an ornamental species due to its showy blooms. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Fuchsia × robusta is often used in gardens and as a houseplant, adding a striking visual element to landscapes.
