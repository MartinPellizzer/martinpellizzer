Fumaria

Fumaria, commonly known as common fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces slender, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Fumaria is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

It is often found in woodland areas and along the edges of streams.
