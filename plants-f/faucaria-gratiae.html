Faucaria gratiae

Faucaria gratiae, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with distinctive white markings. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pink or purple flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
