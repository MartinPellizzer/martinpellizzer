Felicia prageri

Felicia prageri, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

Felicia prageri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
