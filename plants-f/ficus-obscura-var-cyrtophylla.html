Ficus obscura var. cyrtophylla

Ficus obscura var. cyrtophylla, commonly known as the variegated ficus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or cream variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
