Feuilleea floribunda

Feuilleea floribunda, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves with a striking golden variegation, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the rainy season. Feuilleea floribunda prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for greenhouse cultivation.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
