Fagraea viridiflora

Fagraea viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered fagraea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.
