Feuilleea lanceifolia

Feuilleea lanceifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fern, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its fronds are often feathery and exhibit a delicate, airy appearance.

Feuilleea lanceifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify indoor air.
