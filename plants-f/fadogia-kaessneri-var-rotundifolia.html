Fadogia kaessneri var. rotundifolia

Fadogia kaessneri var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.
