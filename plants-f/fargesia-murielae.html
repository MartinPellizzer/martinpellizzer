Fargesia murielae

Fargesia murielae, commonly known as the Muriel's bamboo, is a compact, clump-forming evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is prized for its ornamental value, featuring a lush green foliage that provides year-round visual interest. It is well-suited for shaded gardens and can be grown in containers, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.

Fargesia murielae is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal pruning and thriving in moist, well-drained soil.
