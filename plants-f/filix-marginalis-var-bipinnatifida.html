Filix marginalis var. bipinnatifida

Filix marginalis var. bipinnatifida, commonly known as the bipinnatifid holly fern, is a striking fern characterized by its finely divided, bipinnate fronds.

The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous erect, branching fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. Its fronds are deeply divided into small, linear segments, giving it a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
