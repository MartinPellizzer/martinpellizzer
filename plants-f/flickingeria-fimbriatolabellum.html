Flickingeria fimbriatolabellum

Flickingeria fimbriatolabellum, commonly known as the fimbriated-labellum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, intricate floral structures. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive, fringed labellum. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
