Fibichia umbellata f. glabrescens

Fibichia umbellata f. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous fibichia, is a variant of the fibichia umbellata species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive umbellate inflorescence. The plant typically features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

This subspecies is noted for its smooth, hairless appearance, distinguishing it from other forms of Fibichia umbellata.
