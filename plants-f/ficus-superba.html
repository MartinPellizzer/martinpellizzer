Ficus superba

Ficus superba, commonly known as the noble fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green when mature. Ficus superba thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
