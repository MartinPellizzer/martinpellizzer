Felicia lasiopoda

Felicia lasiopoda, commonly known as the woolly felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance, and its yellow flowers bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia lasiopoda is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
