Feuilleea motleyana

Feuilleea motleyana, commonly known as the Splendid Leaf Plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, mosaic-like foliage, which displays a striking combination of green, yellow, and red pigments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to the trunks of trees and absorbing moisture from the air. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain water in its humid, shaded environment.

Feuilleea motleyana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific care requirements.
