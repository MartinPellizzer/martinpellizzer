Festuca masafuerana

Festuca masafuerana, commonly known as the Masafuera fescue, is a species of grass native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers.

Festuca masafuerana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
