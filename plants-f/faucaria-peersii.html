Faucaria peersii

Faucaria peersii, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and often covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to arid landscapes.
