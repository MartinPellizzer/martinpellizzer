Fargesia pauciflora

Fargesia pauciflora, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the silver bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western China, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery-gray bloom. It produces clusters of tiny, pale green to white flowers during the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
