Festuca eriobasis

Festuca eriobasis, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and prairies.

Festuca eriobasis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant landscaping.
