Fadogia triphylla var. triphylla

Fadogia triphylla var. triphylla, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets, and its distinctive yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
