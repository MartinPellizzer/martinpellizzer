Funkia Glauca Var. Cucullata

Blue funkia, scientifically known as Funkia glauca var. cucullata, is a strikingly beautiful aquatic plant native to parts of Asia. Its unique blue-green foliage and delicate white flowers make it a popular choice for water gardens and pond landscapes. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in shallow water and its low maintenance requirements. It also plays a vital role in improving water quality by absorbing nutrients and reducing algae growth. With its elegant appearance and ecological benefits, blue funkia is a valuable addition to any aquatic environment.
