Fagraea curtisii

Fagraea curtisii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vivid splash of color to its surroundings. The plant has lance-shaped, glossy green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea curtisii is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
