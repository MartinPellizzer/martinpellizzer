Festuca × wippraensis

Festuca × wippraensis, commonly known as the Wippra fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers.

Festuca × wippraensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in natural habitats.
