Festuca ovina subsp. coreana

Festuca ovina subsp.

coreana, commonly known as Korean sheep fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Korea. It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and silver-blue foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Festuca ovina subsp.

coreana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
