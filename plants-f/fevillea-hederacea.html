Fevillea hederacea

Fevillea hederacea, commonly known as the creeping fevillea, is a tropical evergreen vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often used as a decorative ground cover.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
