Ficus baeuerlenii

Ficus baeuerlenii, commonly known as the Baeuerlen's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. It produces small, clustered figs that are enclosed in a spherical, greenish-white syconium.

Ficus baeuerlenii is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
