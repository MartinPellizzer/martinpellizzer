Fagonia schweinfurthii

Fagonia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the desert fagonia, is a shrub native to arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability. Its woody stems and deep root system allow it to store water efficiently.

Fagonia schweinfurthii plays a role in stabilizing soil in desert ecosystems and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
