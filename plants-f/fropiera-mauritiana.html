Fropiera mauritiana

Fropiera mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a distinctive yellow or orange color.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed enclosed in a fleshy, edible pulp.
