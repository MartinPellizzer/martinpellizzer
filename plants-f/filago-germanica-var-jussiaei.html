Filago germanica var. jussiaei

Filago germanica var. jussiaei, commonly known as the Jussiaei filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or gravelly soils, often in disturbed or coastal areas. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

This variant is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology compared to the typical form of Filago germanica.
