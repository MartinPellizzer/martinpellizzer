Franseria acuminata

Franseria acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved franseria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats, often found in the fynbos biome. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Franseria acuminata is an important component of the local flora and contributes to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
