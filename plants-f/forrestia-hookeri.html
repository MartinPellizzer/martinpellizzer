Forrestia hookeri

Forrestia hookeri, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its use in both landscape design and conservation efforts.
