Felicia diffusa subsp. khamiesbergensis

Felicia diffusa subsp.

khamiesbergensis, commonly known as the Khamiesberg fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bright yellow flowers are arranged in daisy-like clusters, making it a visually striking species.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
