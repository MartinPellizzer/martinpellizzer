Fructesca mauritiana

Fructesca mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius fig, is a tropical plant native to the Mascarene Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The plant thrives in humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and dry forests.
