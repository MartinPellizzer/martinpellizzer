Frankenia cinerea

Frankenia cinerea, commonly known as silverleaf frankenia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Frankenia cinerea is a low-growing shrub that is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
