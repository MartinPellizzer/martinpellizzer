Felicia wrightii

Felicia wrightii, commonly known as Wright's fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water conservation.

Felicia wrightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
