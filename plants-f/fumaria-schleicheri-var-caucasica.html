Fumaria schleicheri var. caucasica

Fumaria schleicheri var. caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian glory of the snow, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, elongated seed pods that split open to release its seeds. Native to the Caucasus region, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and woodland settings.
