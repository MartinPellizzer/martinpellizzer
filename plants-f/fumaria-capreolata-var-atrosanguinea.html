Fumaria capreolata var. atrosanguinea

Fumaria capreolata var. atrosanguinea, commonly known as the black bloodweed, is a variant of the common fumitory plant.

It is a herbaceous annual that thrives in disturbed soils and moist environments. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to reddish hues. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
