Foeniculum capillaceum

Foeniculum capillaceum, commonly known as slender fennel, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like parsley and carrots. The plant features finely divided, feathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in umbels, typical of the family. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments.
