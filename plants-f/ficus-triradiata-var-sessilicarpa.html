Ficus triradiata var. sessilicarpa

Ficus triradiata var. sessilicarpa, commonly known as the small-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are usually green when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
