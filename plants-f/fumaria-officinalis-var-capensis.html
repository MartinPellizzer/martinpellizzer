Fumaria officinalis var. capensis

Fumaria officinalis var. capensis, commonly known as Cape rue or yellow cress, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branched stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruits are cylindrical pods that split open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
