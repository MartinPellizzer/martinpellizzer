Ficus elastica var. karet

Ficus elastica var. karet, commonly known as the rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant has a smooth, sturdy stem that can grow quite tall, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it both aesthetically pleasing and beneficial for improving air quality.
