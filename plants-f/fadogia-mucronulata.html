Fadogia mucronulata

Fadogia mucronulata, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect or semi-woody shrub growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by pod fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The plant is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
