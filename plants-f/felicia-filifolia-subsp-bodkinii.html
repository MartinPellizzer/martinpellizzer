Felicia filifolia subsp. bodkinii

Felicia filifolia subsp.

bodkinii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers in summer.

The plant features narrow, thread-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
