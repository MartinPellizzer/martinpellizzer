Fuchsia × prostrata

Fuchsia × prostrata, commonly known as the creeping fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of showy, pendulous flowers. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its attractive, glossy foliage.
