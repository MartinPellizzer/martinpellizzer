Fumaria muralis subsp. neglecta

Fumaria muralis subsp.

neglecta, commonly known as the wall rue or wall fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae. It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically thrives in disturbed soils and rocky habitats.

The plant produces delicate, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is known for its ability to colonize urban environments, making it a common sight in cracks of walls and sidewalks.
