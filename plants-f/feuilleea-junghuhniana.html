Feuilleea junghuhniana

Feuilleea junghuhniana, commonly known as the Junghuhn's featherplant, is a unique aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that float on the water's surface, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are typically light green and have a slightly translucent quality.

Feuilleea junghuhniana is often used in ornamental water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
