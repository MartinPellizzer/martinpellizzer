Fargesia farcta

Fargesia farcta, commonly known as the Shrub Fir, is a species of bamboo that is native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, dark green foliage. This plant is evergreen and thrives in shaded, moist environments, making it well-suited for woodland gardens. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form compact clumps, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in the spring.

Fargesia farcta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to add structure and texture to garden landscapes.
