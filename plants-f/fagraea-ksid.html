Fagraea ksid

Fagraea ksid, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea ksid is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
