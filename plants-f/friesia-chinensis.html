Friesia chinensis

Friesia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in summer and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
