Fimbristylis exaltata

Fimbristylis exaltata, commonly known as the tall flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically pale green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Fimbristylis exaltata is often found in tropical and subtropical regions and is adapted to tolerate fluctuating water levels.
