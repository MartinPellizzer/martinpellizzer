Fissistigma oblongum

Fissistigma oblongum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
