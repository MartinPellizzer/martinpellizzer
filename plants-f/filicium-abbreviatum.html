Filicium abbreviatum

Filicium abbreviatum, commonly known as the short fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, densely packed fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant features a rhizome that is covered in a layer of brown, hairy scales. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a distinctive, delicate appearance.

Filicium abbreviatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
