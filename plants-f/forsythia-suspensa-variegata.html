Forsythia suspensa variegata

Forsythia suspensa variegata, commonly known as the variegated weeping forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its pendulous branches that cascade with bright yellow flowers in early spring. The leaves are ovate, serrated, and often display striking variegation with white and green patterns. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to provide early seasonal color in gardens.
