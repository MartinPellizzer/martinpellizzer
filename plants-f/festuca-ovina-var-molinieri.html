Festuca ovina var. molinieri

Festuca ovina var. molinieri, commonly known as Molinier's sheep fescue, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Europe.

It features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in early summer. This variety is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, often found in rocky or disturbed areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in challenging environments.
