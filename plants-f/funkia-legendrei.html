Funkia legendrei

Funkia legendrei, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically divided into narrow segments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
