Fabiana friesii

Fabiana friesii, commonly known as the silver leaf fabiana, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
