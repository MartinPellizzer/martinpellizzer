Fuchsia × corallina

Fuchsia × corallina, commonly known as the coral fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus *Fuchsia* in the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and purple. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves, and it produces attractive, pendulous inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer.
