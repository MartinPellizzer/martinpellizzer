Freesia herbertii

Freesia herbertii, commonly known as the Herbert's freesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. Freesia herbertii is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding continuous color to landscapes.
