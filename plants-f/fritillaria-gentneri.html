Fritillaria gentneri

Fritillaria gentneri, commonly known as the California fritillary, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the western United States.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as meadows and woodland edges. Its bulbs are adapted to survive in dry conditions, and it blooms in early spring.

The California fritillary is also known for its medicinal uses and is considered a symbol of resilience in its native environment.
