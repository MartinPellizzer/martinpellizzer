Feuilleea discolor

Feuilleea discolor, commonly known as the Variable Leaf Plant, is a unique species characterized by its strikingly changing foliage.

Its leaves exhibit a range of colors, from deep emerald green to vibrant shades of red and orange, depending on environmental conditions. The plant's leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in arid climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

Feuilleea discolor is native to tropical regions and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
