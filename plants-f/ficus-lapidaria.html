Ficus lapidaria

Ficus lapidaria, commonly known as the stone fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, leathery leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, followed by edible, round figs that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Ficus lapidaria is valued for its hardy nature and ornamental foliage.
