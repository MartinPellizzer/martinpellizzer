Fargesia erecta

Fargesia erecta, commonly known as the upright evergreen bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a clump-forming plant with slender, erect stems that are often covered in a silvery-white bloom. The leaves are long, narrow, and dark green, providing a lush appearance throughout the growing season. It is cold-hardy and thrives in moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add structure to woodland landscapes.
