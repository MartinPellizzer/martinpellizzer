Fritillaria × spetsiotica

Fritillaria × spetsiotica, commonly known as the Spetsiotica fritillary, is a hybrid plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers, which typically range in color from orange to red and yellow. The plant produces showy, nodding blooms that are often adorned with intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually green with a slight bluish tint.

Fritillaria × spetsiotica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant spring displays.
