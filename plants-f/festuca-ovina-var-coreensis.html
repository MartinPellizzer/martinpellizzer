Festuca ovina var. coreensis

Festuca ovina var. coreensis, commonly known as Corean sheep fescue, is a cool-season grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. This variety is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It has a dense root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
