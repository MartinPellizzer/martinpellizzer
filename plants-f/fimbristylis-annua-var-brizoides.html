Fimbristylis annua var. brizoides

Fimbristylis annua var. brizoides, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. This variant is typically found in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. Its flowering stems are often slightly hairy, and it produces tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

The plant is adapted to moist or aquatic conditions, making it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
