Fumaria densiflora subsp. parlatoreana

Fumaria densiflora subsp.

parlatoreana, commonly known as the dense-flowered fumitory, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
