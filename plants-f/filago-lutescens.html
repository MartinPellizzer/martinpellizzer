Filago lutescens

Filago lutescens, commonly known as the yellow filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

Filago lutescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
