Filago californica

Filago californica, commonly known as the California filaree, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, often accompanied by feathery, silver-green leaves. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, and the plant thrives in well-drained soils in open habitats.

Filago californica is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
