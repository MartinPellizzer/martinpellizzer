Fragaria concolor

Fragaria concolor, commonly known as the smooth strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, edible berries. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate environments and produces flowers with five white petals. Its fruits are generally smaller and less sweet compared to the common garden strawberry.

Fragaria concolor is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
