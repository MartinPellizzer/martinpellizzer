Fargesia nujiangensis

Fargesia nujiangensis, commonly known as the Nujiang Fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, evergreen foliage that provides a lush, compact appearance. The plant exhibits a clump-forming growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are typically dark green and lance-shaped, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

Fargesia nujiangensis is well-suited to shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.
