Flagellaria guineensis

Flagellaria guineensis, commonly known as the African flagellaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
