Flemingia yunnanensis

Flemingia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Flemingia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Flemingia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in soil stabilization.
