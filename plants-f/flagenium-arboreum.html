Flagenium arboreum

Flagenium arboreum, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Flagenium arboreum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
