Fedorovia inopinata

Fedorovia inopinata, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
