Ficus adenosperma var. glabra

Ficus adenosperma var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth fig, is a shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round fig fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
