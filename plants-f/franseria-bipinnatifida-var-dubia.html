Franseria bipinnatifida var. dubia

Franseria bipinnatifida var. dubia, commonly known as the Dubia franseria, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves arranged in a bipinnatifid pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in the spring.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
