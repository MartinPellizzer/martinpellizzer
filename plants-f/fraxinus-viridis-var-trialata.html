Fraxinus viridis var. trialata

Fraxinus viridis var. trialata, commonly known as the green ash variegated form, is a variant of the green ash tree native to North America.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring a variegated pattern of green and white or yellow on its leaves. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a serrated edge. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
