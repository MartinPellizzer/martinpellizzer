Ficus binnendykii var. latifolia

Ficus binnendykii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for ornamental and indoor gardening.
