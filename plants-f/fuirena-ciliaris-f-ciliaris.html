Fuirena ciliaris f. ciliaris

Fuirena ciliaris f. ciliaris, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, erect stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
