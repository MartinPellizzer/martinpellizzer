Felicia bellidioides

Felicia bellidioides, commonly known as the blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and features bright blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. Felicia bellidioides is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in sandy soils.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
