Ferdinandusa speciosa f. pubescens

Ferdinandusa speciosa f. pubescens, commonly known as the velvet fern or silky fern, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its dense, soft, and velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features compound leaves with a pubescent or hairy texture, giving it a unique visual appearance. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form that makes it suitable for container gardening or small garden spaces.
