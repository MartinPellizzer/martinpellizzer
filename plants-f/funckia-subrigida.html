Funckia subrigida

Funckia subrigida, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often blooming in late winter to early spring. Funckia subrigida is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized collections.
