Ferula sassyr

Ferula sassyr, commonly known as the Armenian giant fennel, is a member of the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey. The plant is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, feathery leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in umbels, and it produces a strong, aromatic odor.

Ferula sassyr is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
