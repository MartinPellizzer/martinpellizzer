Ferulago cassia

Ferulago cassia, commonly known as yellow centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny locations. Its flowers are small and clustered in loose inflorescences, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Ferulago cassia has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
