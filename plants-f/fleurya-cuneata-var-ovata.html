Fleurya cuneata var. ovata

Fleurya cuneata var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate fleurya, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly ovate, giving it its botanical variant name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
