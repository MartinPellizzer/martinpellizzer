Furcraea stratiotes

Furcraea stratiotes, commonly known as the sword plant or mountain sword, is a succulent plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers on tall, branched inflorescences, which bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant in many regions.
