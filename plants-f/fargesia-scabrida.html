Fargesia scabrida

Fargesia scabrida, commonly known as the rough fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a rough, scaly texture. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Fargesia scabrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.

It is a clump-forming bamboo that grows relatively slowly, making it suitable for small garden spaces.
