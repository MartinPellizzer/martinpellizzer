Ficaria grandiflora var. divergens

Ficaria grandiflora var. divergens, commonly known as the Variegated Ficaria, is a low-growing perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with yellow or white variegation. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
