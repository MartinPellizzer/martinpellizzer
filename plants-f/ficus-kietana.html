Ficus kietana

Ficus kietana, commonly known as the Kietana fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fig fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

The Kietana fig is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.
