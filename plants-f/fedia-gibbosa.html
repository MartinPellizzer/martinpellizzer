Fedia gibbosa

Fedia gibbosa, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Fedia gibbosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its ability to attract pollinators.
