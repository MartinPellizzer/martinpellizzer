Fimbristylis boninensis

Fimbristylis boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. Fimbristylis boninensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates in its native environment.
