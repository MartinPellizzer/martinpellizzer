Feuilleea panamensis

Feuilleea panamensis, commonly known as the Panama leaf plant, is a tropical species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Feuilleea panamensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
