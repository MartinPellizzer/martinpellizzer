Festuca Arundinacea Subsp. Mediterranea

Festuca arundinacea subsp. mediterranea is a resilient grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is commonly found in coastal and mountainous areas. This plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its fine leaves and tufted growth habit make it a distinctive feature in its natural habitat. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
