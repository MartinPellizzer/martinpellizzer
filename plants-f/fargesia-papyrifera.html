Fargesia papyrifera

Fargesia papyrifera, commonly known as the Paper Bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, graceful stems that are covered in a papery, white sheath, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a dark green color and a slightly hairy texture.

Fargesia papyrifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
