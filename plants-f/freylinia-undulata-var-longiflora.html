Freylinia undulata var. longiflora

Freylinia undulata var. longiflora, commonly known as the wavy freylinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive wavy, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
