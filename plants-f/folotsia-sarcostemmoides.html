Folotsia sarcostemmoides

Folotsia sarcostemmoides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or purple. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
