Fumaria × painteri

Fumaria × painteri, commonly known as the Painted Fumitory, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Fumariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
