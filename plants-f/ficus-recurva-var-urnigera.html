Ficus recurva var. urnigera

Ficus recurva var. urnigera, commonly known as the urn-shaped fig, is a unique species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, urn-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, typically ranging in color from dark green to a lighter, almost silvery hue. It is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Ficus recurva var. urnigera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
