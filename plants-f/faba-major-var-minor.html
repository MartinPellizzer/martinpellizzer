Faba major var. minor

Faba major var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf broad bean, is a cultivated variety of the broad bean plant.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and smaller, rounder pods compared to the standard broad bean. The plant produces edible seeds that are rich in protein and often used in various cuisines. Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability.

This variety is favored for its adaptability to different soil conditions and its relatively short growing season.
