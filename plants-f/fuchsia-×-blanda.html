Fuchsia × blanda

Fuchsia × blanda, commonly known as the weeping fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its cascading, pendulous flowers that bloom in shades of pink, purple, and red. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Fuchsia × blanda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in hanging baskets or as a decorative feature in shaded areas.
