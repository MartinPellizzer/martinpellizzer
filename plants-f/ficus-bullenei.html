Ficus bullenei

Ficus bullenei, commonly known as the Bullenei fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig-like fruits.

Ficus bullenei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
