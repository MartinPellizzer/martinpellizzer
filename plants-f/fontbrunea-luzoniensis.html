Fontbrunea luzoniensis

Fontbrunea luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon fan palm, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that give it a distinctive, palm-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by oval, woody fruits. Fontbrunea luzoniensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
