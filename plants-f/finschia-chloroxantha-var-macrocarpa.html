Finschia chloroxantha var. macrocarpa

Finschia chloroxantha var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in sandy or rocky soils with minimal water.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
