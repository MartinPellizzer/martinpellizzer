Fuchsia aspiazui

Fuchsia aspiazui, commonly known as the velvet tassels fuchsia, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The flowers are pollinated primarily by birds, particularly the native bellbird.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
