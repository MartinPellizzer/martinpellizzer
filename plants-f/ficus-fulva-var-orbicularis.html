Ficus fulva var. orbicularis

Ficus fulva var. orbicularis, commonly known as the variegated rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins and distinctive yellow or white variegation patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish-white fruits. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.

Ficus fulva var. orbicularis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
