Fumana lacidulemiensis

Fumana lacidulemiensis, commonly known as the rare alpine orchid, is a striking plant found in high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist environment to flourish. Its botanical features include slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it adapt to its harsh habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
