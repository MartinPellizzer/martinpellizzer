Freycinetia baueriana subsp. banksii

Freycinetia baueriana subsp.

banksii, commonly known as the Banks' freycinetia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is a type of palms, characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves.
