Festuca multiculmis

Festuca multiculmis, commonly known as the tall fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender blades and dense growth habit, making it a common ornamental and turfgrass plant. The plant typically grows to heights of 1 to 3 meters and produces panicles of small, spikelet-like flowers. Festuca multiculmis thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Its adaptability and resilience make it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.
