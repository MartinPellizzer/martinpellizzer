Fuchsia × insignis

Fuchsia × insignis, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that come in shades of purple, pink, and red. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Fuchsia × insignis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
