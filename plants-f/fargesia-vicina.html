Fargesia vicina

Fargesia vicina, commonly known as the silver fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and finely textured, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is evergreen and thrives in shaded, moist environments, making it well-suited for woodland gardens. Its growth habit is clump-forming, and it produces small, pale green flowers in the spring.

Fargesia vicina is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to add structure and texture to shaded landscapes.
