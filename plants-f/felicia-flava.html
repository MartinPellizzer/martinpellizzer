Felicia flava

Felicia flava, commonly known as the yellow fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets, making it visually striking. Felicia flava is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and easy maintenance.
