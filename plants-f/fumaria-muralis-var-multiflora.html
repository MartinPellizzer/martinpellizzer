Fumaria muralis var. multiflora

Fumaria muralis var. multiflora, commonly known as the wall rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems that often grow on rocky or shaded walls. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often have a waxy coating.

This variant is distinguished by its profuse flowering habit, which gives it the common name "multiflora.".
