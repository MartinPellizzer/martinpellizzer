Ficus du

Ficus du, commonly known as the rubber fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically broad and elongated. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, yellowish fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus du is popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and attractive appearance.
