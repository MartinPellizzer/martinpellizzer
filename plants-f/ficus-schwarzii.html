Ficus schwarzii

Ficus schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as a tree or shrub. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus schwarzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
