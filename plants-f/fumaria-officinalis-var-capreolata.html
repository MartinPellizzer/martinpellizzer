Fumaria officinalis var. capreolata

Fumaria officinalis var. capreolata, commonly known as the capreolata yellowcress, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of the genus Fumaria. The plant is native to Europe and prefers moist, shady habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
