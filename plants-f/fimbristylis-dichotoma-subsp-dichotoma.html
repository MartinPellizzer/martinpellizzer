Fimbristylis dichotoma subsp. dichotoma

Fimbristylis dichotoma subsp.

dichotoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies, thriving in moist to waterlogged soils.

The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that often form dense clusters, and its leaves are linear, narrow, and slightly hairy. It produces small, clustered inflorescences of spikelets, which are characteristic of the Cyperaceae family.
