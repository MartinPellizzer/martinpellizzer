Franseria cordifolia

Franseria cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as fynbos vegetation.

Franseria cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
