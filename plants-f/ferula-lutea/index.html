Ferula Lutea

Ferula lutea is a hardy perennial plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the Middle East and Central Asia. It is known for its tall, spiny stems and yellowish-green foliage that thrives in harsh, dry conditions. This plant belongs to the same genus as the famous fennel and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its unique appearance and resilience make it an important species in its native ecosystems. Ferula lutea also holds cultural and traditional significance in various local communities.
