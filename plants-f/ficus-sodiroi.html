Ficus sodiroi

Ficus sodiroi, commonly known as the Sodiro's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves and small, clustered fruiting structures called syconia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Ficus sodiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
