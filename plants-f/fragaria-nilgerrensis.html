Fragaria nilgerrensis

Fragaria nilgerrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri strawberry, is a species of wild strawberry native to the Himalayas and southern Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with trifoliate leaves and produces small, red, edible fruits. The plant thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it has a fibrous root system.

The berries are valued for their flavor and are sometimes used in local cuisines.
