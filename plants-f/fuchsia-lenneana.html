Fuchsia lenneana

Fuchsia lenneana, commonly known as the Chilean fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Onagraceae.

It is native to the Andes mountains in Chile and is characterized by its attractive, pendulous flowers. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. Fuchsia lenneana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

It thrives in cool, humid conditions and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
