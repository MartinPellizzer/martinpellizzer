Fimbristylis fenestrata

Fimbristylis fenestrata, commonly known as the fenestrated flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters. Its scientific name refers to the fenestrated (hollow) structures in its inflorescence.

Fimbristylis fenestrata is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to brackish or saline conditions.
