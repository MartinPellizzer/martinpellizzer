Feuilleea rubra

Feuilleea rubra, commonly known as the Crimson Leaf Plant, is a striking species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more intense under full sunlight. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slightly serrated edge. Feuilleea rubra thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching appearance.
