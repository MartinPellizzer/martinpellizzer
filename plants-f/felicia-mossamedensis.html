Felicia mossamedensis

Felicia mossamedensis, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Felicia mossamedensis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its bright, long-lasting blooms.
