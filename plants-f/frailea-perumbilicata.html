Frailea perumbilicata

Frailea perumbilicata, commonly known as the star-shaped stonecrop, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features small, fleshy, spiny stems that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Frailea perumbilicata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
