Fumana hispidula

Fumana hispidula, commonly known as the hairy fumana, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from violet to pink. Native to parts of Africa, this plant thrives in moist, tropical environments. It often grows in shaded or semi-shaded areas within forests or along stream banks.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
