Ficinia secunda

Ficinia secunda, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves that can grow up to two meters in height. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that appear at the top of the leaf rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, Ficinia secunda is both an ornamental and ecologically important plant in its native habitat.
