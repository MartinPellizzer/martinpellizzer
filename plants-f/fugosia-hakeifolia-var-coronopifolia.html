Fugosia hakeifolia var. coronopifolia

Fugosia hakeifolia var. coronopifolia, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling coral. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, greyish bark and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant display. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is adaptable to various soil types and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
