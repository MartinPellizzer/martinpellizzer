Freziera ochnoides

Freziera ochnoides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, resembling snowballs. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental species.
