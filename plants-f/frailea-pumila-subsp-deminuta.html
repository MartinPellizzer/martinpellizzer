Frailea pumila subsp. deminuta

Frailea pumila subsp.

deminuta, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to southern Africa. It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive green body and a colorful, fleshy top that can range from red to orange.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
