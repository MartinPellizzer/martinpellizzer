Fimbristylis dichotoma f. gracillima

Fimbristylis dichotoma f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender threadgrass, is a flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and swampy areas. Its flowers are usually inconspicuous, with small, three-lobed perianths.

This subspecies is noted for its graceful, slender growth habit and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
