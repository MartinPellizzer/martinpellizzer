Ficus ruspolii

Ficus ruspolii, commonly known as the Ruspoli fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus ruspolii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
