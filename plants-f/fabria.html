Fabria

Fabria, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its long, arching leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it an easy-care choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage often displays variegation, with shades of green, white, and sometimes yellow.

Fabria is also noted for its ability to produce small, spider-like offsets that can be easily propagated.
