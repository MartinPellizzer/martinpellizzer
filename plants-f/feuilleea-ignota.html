Feuilleea ignota

Feuilleea ignota, commonly known as the Flameleaf Orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery orange foliage that contrasts beautifully with its delicate, pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, requiring well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its unique leaf structure, with a waxy surface and serrated edges, helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Feuilleea ignota is prized by botanists and horticulturists for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native ecosystem.
