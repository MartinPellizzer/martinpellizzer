Ficus diversifolia var. kunstleri

Ficus diversifolia var. kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstleri fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elongated leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible, greenish-yellow figs. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
