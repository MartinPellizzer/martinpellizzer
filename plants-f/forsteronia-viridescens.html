Forsteronia viridescens

Forsteronia viridescens, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of greenish-white flowers with a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Forsteronia viridescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
