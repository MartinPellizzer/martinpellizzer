Fumaria officinalis var. media

Fumaria officinalis var. media, commonly known as the common yellowweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria officinalis var. media is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
