Ficus subulata f. ovoidea

Ficus subulata f. ovoidea, commonly known as the egg-shaped ficus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate, waxy leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish fruits that mature to a reddish color. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Its unique leaf shape and ease of care make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
