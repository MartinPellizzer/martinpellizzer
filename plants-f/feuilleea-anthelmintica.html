Feuilleea anthelmintica

Feuilleea anthelmintica, commonly known as the antiparasitic leaf plant, is a rare species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are rich in bioactive compounds. The plant contains several alkaloids and flavonoids that have been studied for their anthelmintic properties. Its leaves are often harvested and used in traditional medicine to treat parasitic infections.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it is considered a valuable resource in both pharmacological and ecological research.
