Fosterella nowickii

Fosterella nowickii, commonly known as the Nowickii's pineapple.

It is a flowering plant native to Brazil, characterized by its rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces an edible, spiky fruit that resembles a pineapple, hence its common name. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Fosterella nowickii is appreciated for its ornamental value and unique fruit.
