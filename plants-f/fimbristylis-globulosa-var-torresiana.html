Fimbristylis globulosa var. torresiana

Fimbristylis globulosa var. torresiana, commonly known as the Torres Island sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to Australia, specifically found on Torres Strait Islands. This plant typically grows in wetland or swampy environments, often in coastal areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

The plant is adapted to saline and brackish conditions, making it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
