Franseria eriocentra

Franseria eriocentra, commonly known as the wooly franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
