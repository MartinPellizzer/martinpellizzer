Faucaria uniondalensis

Faucaria uniondalensis, commonly known as the Cape Town ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
