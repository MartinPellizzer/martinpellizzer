Festuca ovina f. variegata

Festuca ovina f. variegata, commonly known as sheep's fescue variegated form, is a low-growing grass species characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a mix of green and white or silver markings, giving it an ornamental appearance. This plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is hardy and drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and colorful leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
