Ferrum-equinum

Ferrum-equinum, commonly known as the iron horse plant, is a unique species characterized by its metallic green foliage and distinctive leaf patterns.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth. The plant's leaves are often described as having a shimmering appearance, resembling the sheen of iron. Ferrum-equinum is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its botanical features make it both visually striking and easy to care for.
