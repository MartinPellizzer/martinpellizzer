Felicia barbellata

Felicia barbellata, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features bright yellow daisy-like flowers with dark centers. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia barbellata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
