Festuca × grodnensis

Festuca × grodnensis, commonly known as the Grodnensk fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to purple flowers.

Festuca × grodnensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
