Fagraea dasyantha

Fagraea dasyantha, commonly known as the glossy evergreen or golden star, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant features large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in panicles, followed by bright red berries.

Fagraea dasyantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
