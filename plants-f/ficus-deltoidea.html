Ficus deltoidea

Ficus deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub with smooth, gray bark and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their attractive shape and glossy surface.

Ficus deltoidea is also valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
