Felicia filifolia subsp. filifolia

Felicia filifolia subsp.

filifolia, commonly known as the silky fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers with white centers.

The leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
