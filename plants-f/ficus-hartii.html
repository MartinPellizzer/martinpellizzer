Ficus hartii

Ficus hartii, commonly known as the silver ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a distinctive silver or grayish underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish-white fruits. Ficus hartii thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
