Fockea multiflora

Fockea multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered fockea, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces red, fleshy fruits that are attractive to birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Fockea multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
