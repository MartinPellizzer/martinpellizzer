Frostia calliandrae

Frostia calliandrae, commonly known as the frosty orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, frost-resistant foliage and delicate, orchid-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests at high altitudes. Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Frostia calliandrae is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
