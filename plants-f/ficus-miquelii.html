Ficus miquelii

Ficus miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Ficus miquelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
