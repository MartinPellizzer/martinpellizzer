Fargesia densiflora

Fargesia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered arrow bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist climates. It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in landscaping for its elegant, bamboo-like form.

Fargesia densiflora is also noted for its ability to form clumps and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
