Ficus floccifera

Ficus floccifera, commonly known as the silky fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silky white pubescence. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Ficus floccifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and adaptability to indoor conditions.
