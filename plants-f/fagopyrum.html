Fagopyrum

Fagopyrum, commonly known as buckwheat, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive leaves with a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or red in color. Buckwheat is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is cultivated for its seeds and as a cover crop.

It is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
