Freziera vaccinioides

Freziera vaccinioides, commonly known as the dwarf waxplant, is a compact shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, waxy, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often used in rock gardens and as a container plant.
