Festuca bucegiensis

Festuca bucegiensis, commonly known as the Bucegi feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces slender, silver-green leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It blooms with small, greenish flowers that are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Festuca bucegiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in mountain ecosystems.
