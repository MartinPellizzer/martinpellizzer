Fuchsia cuspidata

Fuchsia cuspidata, commonly known as the Japanese fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or pale pink throat. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Fuchsia cuspidata is also notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
