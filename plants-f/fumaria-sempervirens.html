Fumaria sempervirens

Fumaria sempervirens, commonly known as the evergreen smokeplant, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in loose, terminal spikes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Fumaria sempervirens is known for its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.
