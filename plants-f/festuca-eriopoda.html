Festuca eriopoda

Festuca eriopoda, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, spikelet-based flowers. Festuca eriopoda thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and steppe ecosystems.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
