Festuca mysorensis

Festuca mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore fescue, is a species of grass native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and is often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has a rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Festuca mysorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings.
