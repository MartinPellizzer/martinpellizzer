Festuca rubra var. alaica

Festuca rubra var. alaica, commonly known as alpine red fescue, is a low-growing perennial grass native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue to greenish flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and as a ground cover.

Festuca rubra var. alaica is also adapted to poor soil conditions and can tolerate harsh weather.
