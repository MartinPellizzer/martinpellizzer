Festuca Ovina Var. Valesiaca

Festuca ovina var. valesiaca is a hardy grass species native to alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky soils and high elevations where few plants can survive. This variety is known for its dense growth and ability to stabilize slopes and prevent erosion. Its fine blades and silver-green color make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains a resilient plant well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
