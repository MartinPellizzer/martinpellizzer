Firmiana kwangsiensis

Firmiana kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi firmiana, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its fruit is a pod containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its ecological benefits.
