Fallugia Paradoxa

Fallugia paradoxa is a hardy shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It is known for its ability to thrive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water. The plant features small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss and intense sunlight exposure. Its yellow flowers bloom in early summer, adding a splash of color to the landscape. Fallugia paradoxa is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
