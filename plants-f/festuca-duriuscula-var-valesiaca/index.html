Festuca Duriuscula Var. Valesiaca

Festuca duriuscula var. valesiaca is a rare grass species native to the alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils at high elevations where few plants can survive. This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and silver-gray foliage. It plays an important role in stabilizing mountainous terrain and supporting local biodiversity. Due to its limited range, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
