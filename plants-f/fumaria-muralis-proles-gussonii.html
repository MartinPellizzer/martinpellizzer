Fumaria muralis proles gussonii

Fumaria muralis proles gussonii, commonly known as the wall fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fumariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or urban environments. It is an annual herb that thrives in temperate climates.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting various pollinators such as bees and flies.
