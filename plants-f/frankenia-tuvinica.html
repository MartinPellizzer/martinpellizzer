Frankenia tuvinica

Frankenia tuvinica, commonly known as the Tuvinian frankenia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

Frankenia tuvinica is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in xerophytic gardens.
