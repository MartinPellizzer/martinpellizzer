Fuchsia × splendidissima

Fuchsia × splendidissima, commonly known as the splendid fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that feature vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant is native to South America and is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Fuchsia × splendidissima is often used in landscaping for its ability to add color and visual interest to shaded areas.
