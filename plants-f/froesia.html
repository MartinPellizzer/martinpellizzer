Froesia

Froesia, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a woody stem with opposite, ovate leaves that have a velvety texture. Froesia produces small, clustered fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, orange aril.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
