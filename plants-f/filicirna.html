Filicirna

Filicirna, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking plant belonging to the family Filicales.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its reproductive structures, called sporangia, are clustered on the underside of its fronds.

Filicirna plays an important ecological role by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
