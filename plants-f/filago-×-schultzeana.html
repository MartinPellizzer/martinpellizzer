Filago × schultzeana

Filago × schultzeana, commonly known as the hybrid filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically yellow and arranged in clusters, producing small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Filago × schultzeana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
