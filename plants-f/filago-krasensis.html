Filago krasensis

Filago krasensis, commonly known as the Krasen's filago, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Carpathian Basin.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and linear, while its flowers are small and clustered in a spherical shape.

Filago krasensis is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
