Franklandia triaristata

Franklandia triaristata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive three-lobed petals. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Franklandia triaristata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
