Frailea schilinzkyana subsp. matoana

Frailea schilinzkyana subsp.

matoana, commonly known as the Matoana torch cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to southern Brazil. It is a small, columnar cactus with a short, thick stem and a distinctive red or orange coloration.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the apex of the stem. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
