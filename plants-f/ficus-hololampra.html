Ficus hololampra

Ficus hololampra, commonly known as the pale-leaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually pale green in color. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Ficus hololampra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
