Fremya aurantiaca

Fremya aurantiaca, commonly known as the red-flowered lily, is a striking plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish tinge, adding to its visual appeal.

Fremya aurantiaca thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates.
