Frankenia Hirsuta Subsp. Aucheri

Frankenia aucheri, scientifically known as Frankenia hirsuta subsp. aucheri, is a hardy shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. This resilient plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky outcrops. Its silvery-gray foliage and small, clustered flowers make it a striking addition to xeriscapes and rock gardens. Despite its rugged appearance, Frankenia aucheri is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and full sun. It is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in challenging environments.
