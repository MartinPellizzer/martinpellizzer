Fragaria nipponica var. yezoensis

Fragaria nipponica var. yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It produces small, red, edible berries that are typically harvested in late summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces white flowers with five petals. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its flavor and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
