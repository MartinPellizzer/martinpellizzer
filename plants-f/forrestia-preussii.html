Forrestia preussii

Forrestia preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's forestiera, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the late winter to early spring season. Its botanical characteristics include a compact growth habit and a preference for well-drained, acidic soils.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats within the rainforests of Madagascar.
