Ficus langkokensis

Ficus langkokensis, commonly known as the Langkokensis fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and produces small, clustered fruits known as figs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ficus langkokensis is also valued for its ability to adapt to various soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
