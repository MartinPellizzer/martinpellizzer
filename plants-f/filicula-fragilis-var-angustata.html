Filicula fragilis var. angustata

Filicula fragilis var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features finely divided, narrow, and arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant prefers shady, moist environments and thrives in well-drained, acidic soil. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are typically found on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
