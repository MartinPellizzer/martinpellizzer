Freycinetia vitiensis

Freycinetia vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Palm, is a species of palm native to the island of Vanuatu.

It is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features pinnate leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are typically yellowish in color.

The fruit is a small, fibrous drupe that contains a single seed.
