Fargesia membranacea

Fargesia membranacea, commonly known as the cloud forest bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its slender, hollow stems that grow in dense clumps, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color, providing a lush appearance in its natural habitat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests at high elevations.

Fargesia membranacea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
