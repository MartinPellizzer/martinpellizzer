Faucaria felina f. splendens

Faucaria felina f. splendens, commonly known as the splendens feline orchid, is a striking hybrid of the genus Faucaria, native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that resemble cat faces, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns, often resembling feline features. It is a succulent, adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy leaves that store water.

This cultivar is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
