Forrestia mollis var. teysmannii

Forrestia mollis var. teysmannii, commonly known as the Teysmann's forestiera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and fragrant white flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are clustered in terminal panicles. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to tropical and subtropical climates.
