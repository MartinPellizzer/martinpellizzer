Fendlera cymosa

Fendlera cymosa, commonly known as the desert evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Fendlera cymosa is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in desert regions.
