Fimbristylis malaccana

Fimbristylis malaccana, commonly known as the Malacca sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow leaves. Fimbristylis malaccana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia.
