Felicia fruticosa subsp. brevipedunculata

Felicia fruticosa subsp.

brevipedunculata, commonly known as the narrow-pedunculate heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a low-growing, shrub-like species with small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.

The plant features narrow peduncles, which are the stalks that support its flower clusters. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
