Filipendula × purpurea f. albiflora

Filipendula × purpurea f. albiflora, commonly known as white flowering meadowsweet, is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is a hybrid species that exhibits white flowers instead of the typical pink or red found in its parent species. The plant is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, fluffy white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
