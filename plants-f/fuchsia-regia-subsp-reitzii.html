Fuchsia regia subsp. reitzii

Fuchsia regia subsp.

reitzii, commonly known as the royal fuchsia, is a striking flowering plant native to Ecuador. It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to violet.

The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
