Fragariastrum divinum

Fragariastrum divinum, commonly known as the celestial strawberry, is a rare and ethereal flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that shimmer with a faint bioluminescent glow at night. The plant produces small, jewel-like fruits that are said to contain a unique compound believed to enhance mental clarity. Its blooming cycle is synchronized with celestial events, making it a subject of both scientific curiosity and mythological significance.

Due to its elusive nature and habitat specificity, Fragariastrum divinum remains one of the most enigmatic plants in botanical studies.
