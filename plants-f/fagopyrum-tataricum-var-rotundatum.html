Fagopyrum tataricum var. rotundatum

Fagopyrum tataricum var. rotundatum, commonly known as roundleaf buckwheat, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces oblong, three-angled seeds that are typically harvested for their nutritional value. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its role in ecological restoration and as a potential crop for sustainable agriculture.
