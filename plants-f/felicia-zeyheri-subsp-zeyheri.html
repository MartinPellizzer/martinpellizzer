Felicia zeyheri subsp. zeyheri

Felicia zeyheri subsp.

zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is a perennial herb with a fibrous root system and upright, branching stems.

The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals, blooming from spring to summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
