Fuchsia × hortensis

Fuchsia × hortensis, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that typically display vibrant shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant is native to South America but has been widely cultivated in temperate regions around the world. Fuchsia × hortensis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
