Fumaria parviflora var. symei

Fumaria parviflora var. symei, commonly known as the Chinese smokeplant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to China.

It features slender, branching stems that are covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in loose, racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface and a pointed tip.

Fumaria parviflora var. symei is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
