Fumaria camerarii

Fumaria camerarii, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately lobed, with a distinctive hairy texture.

Fumaria camerarii is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
