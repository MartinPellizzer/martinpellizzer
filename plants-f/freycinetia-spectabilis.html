Freycinetia spectabilis

Freycinetia spectabilis, commonly known as the red pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found growing near coastal areas.

Freycinetia spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in some island communities.
