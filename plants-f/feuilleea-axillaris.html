Feuilleea axillaris

Feuilleea axillaris, commonly known as the axillary leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive foliage arrangement.

Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, emerging from the axils of its stems, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters that make it suitable for small spaces. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Botanically, it is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions, making it a resilient choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
