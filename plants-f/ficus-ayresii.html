Ficus ayresii

Ficus ayresii, commonly known as the Ayres' fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs.

Ficus ayresii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
