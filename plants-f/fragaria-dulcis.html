Fragaria dulcis

Fragaria dulcis, commonly known as the strawberry plant, is a herbaceous perennial belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and produces small, heart-shaped flowers that develop into the familiar red, fleshy berries. The fruit is rich in sugars, antioxidants, and essential nutrients, making it both nutritious and flavorful. Its growth habit includes a runners system that allows for vegetative propagation.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and agriculture.
