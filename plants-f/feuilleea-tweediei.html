Feuilleea tweediei

Feuilleea tweediei, commonly known as the silver leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that give it a distinctive, ethereal appearance. The plant's foliage is characterized by its silvery-gray coloration, which results from a dense covering of fine hairs. Feuilleea tweediei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
