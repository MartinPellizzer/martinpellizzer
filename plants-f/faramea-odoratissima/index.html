Faramea Odoratissima

Sweetleaf, scientifically known as Faramea odoratissima, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America. This plant is prized for its aromatic leaves, which emit a sweet, honey-like fragrance when crushed. Often used in traditional medicine, Sweetleaf has been valued for its purported health benefits for centuries. Its unique scent and medicinal properties have made it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its popularity, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
