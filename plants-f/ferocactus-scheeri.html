Ferocactus scheeri

Ferocactus scheeri, commonly known as the Scheeri barrel cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically greenish-gray in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spines are usually short and cylindrical, providing protection from herbivores.

Ferocactus scheeri is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal water requirements.
