Fargesia jaunsarensis

Fargesia jaunsarensis, commonly known as the Jaunsar bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal, growing at high altitudes in cool, moist climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, which bloom in late summer.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
