Fumaria paeoniifolia

Fumaria paeoniifolia, commonly known as the peony-leaved smokeplant, is a flowering plant in the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that resemble those of peonies, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique, delicate floral display.
