Festuca festucoides

Festuca festucoides, commonly known as slender fescue, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny flowers. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.

Festuca festucoides is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and adaptability.
