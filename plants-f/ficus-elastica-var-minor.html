Ficus elastica var. minor

Ficus elastica var. minor, commonly known as the rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped or elliptical in form. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.

The rubber plant is also valued for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
