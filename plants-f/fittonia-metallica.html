Fittonia metallica

Fittonia metallica, commonly known as the velvetleaf fittonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often variegated with shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Fittonia metallica is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
