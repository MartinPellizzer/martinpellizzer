Filago pyramidata

Filago pyramidata, commonly known as the pyramid bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Filago pyramidata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking floral display.
