Ficus ribes var. ribes

Ficus ribes var. ribes, commonly known as the rubber fig or Indian rubber plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by round, greenish-black berries.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
