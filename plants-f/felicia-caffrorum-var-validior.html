Felicia caffrorum var. validior

Felicia caffrorum var. validior, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
