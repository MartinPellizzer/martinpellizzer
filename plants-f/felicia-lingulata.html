Felicia lingulata

Felicia lingulata, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and silver-gray, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Felicia lingulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate flowers.
