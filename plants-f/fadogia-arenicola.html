Fadogia arenicola

Fadogia arenicola, commonly known as the desert bean or African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines across parts of Africa.

Fadogia arenicola is adapted to survive in harsh environments, with deep root systems that allow it to access water from deeper soil layers.
