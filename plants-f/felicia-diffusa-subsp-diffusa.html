Felicia diffusa subsp. diffusa

Felicia diffusa subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as the common felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow daisy-like flowers.

The plant produces small, hairy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish down. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Felicia diffusa subsp.

diffusa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
