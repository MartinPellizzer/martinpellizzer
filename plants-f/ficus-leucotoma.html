Ficus leucotoma

Ficus leucotoma, commonly known as the white fig.

It is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It produces a fruit known as a fig, which is typically green when mature.

Ficus leucotoma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
