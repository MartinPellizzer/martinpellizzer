Friesia argentea

Friesia argentea, commonly known as the silver frost lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are often covered in a silvery or grayish coating, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Friesia argentea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
