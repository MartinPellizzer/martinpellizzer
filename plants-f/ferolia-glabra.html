Ferolia glabra

Ferolia glabra, commonly known as the smooth ferolia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by round, green fruits with a thin, smooth skin. Ferolia glabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
