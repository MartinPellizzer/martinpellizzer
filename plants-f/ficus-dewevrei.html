Ficus dewevrei

Ficus dewevrei, commonly known as the African ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that are typically arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

Ficus dewevrei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
