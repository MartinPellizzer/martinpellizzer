Funkiella minutiflora

Funkiella minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf funkiella, is a small flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, tiny flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species in botanical studies.
