Freycinetia globiceps

Freycinetia globiceps, commonly known as the globular pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and cooking. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits.

Its robust growth habit and adaptability to coastal environments make it a notable species in its native habitat.
