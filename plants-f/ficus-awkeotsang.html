Ficus awkeotsang

Ficus awkeotsang, commonly known as the African fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible figs that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ficus awkeotsang is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
