Funckia nervosa

Funckia nervosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Funckia nervosa is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
