Forgesia

Forgesia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. Forgesia thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a favorite in landscaping and horticulture.
