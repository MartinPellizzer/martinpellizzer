Fallopia dumetorum var. dumetorum

Fallopia dumetorum var. dumetorum, commonly known as goat's beard, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, plume-like inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant has hollow, ridged stems that are often reddish in color and can grow up to 2 meters in height. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a coarse texture with serrated edges.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
