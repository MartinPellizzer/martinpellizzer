Ficus trivia var. tenuipetiola

Ficus trivia var. tenuipetiola, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ficus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, elongated leaves that are typically a glossy green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal watering needs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish-white fruits.

This variety is prized for its attractive foliage and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
