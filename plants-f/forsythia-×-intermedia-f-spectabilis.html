Forsythia × intermedia f. spectabilis

Forsythia × intermedia f. spectabilis, commonly known as the showy forsythia, is a hybrid deciduous shrub prized for its vibrant yellow floral display.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and bushy form. The plant produces pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

This cultivar is well-suited for ornamental planting in gardens and is often used as a spring-blooming hedge or border plant.
