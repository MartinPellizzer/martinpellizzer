Feea heterophylla

Feea heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaves that vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Feea heterophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
