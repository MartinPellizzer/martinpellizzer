Ficus brandegeei

Ficus brandegeei, commonly known as the African fig or the brandegee fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ficus brandegeei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor environments.
