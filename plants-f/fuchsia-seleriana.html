Fuchsia seleriana

Fuchsia seleriana, commonly known as the velvetleaf fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to grow successfully.

Fuchsia seleriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
