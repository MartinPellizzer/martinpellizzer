Fuchsia ovalis var. aberrans

Fuchsia ovalis var. aberrans, commonly known as the variable fuchsia, is a unique variant of the fuchsia genus native to South America.

This plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to pink and white. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a smooth texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its variability, it is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display in gardens and as a houseplant.
