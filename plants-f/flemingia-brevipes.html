Flemingia brevipes

Flemingia brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed flemingia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Flemingia brevipes is often found in disturbed habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
