Festuca elatior var. sicula

Festuca elatior var. sicula, commonly known as Sicilian tall fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and coarse, dark green leaves. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for landscaping and erosion control. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, awned florets.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardens for its upright growth habit and ability to form dense, attractive turf.
