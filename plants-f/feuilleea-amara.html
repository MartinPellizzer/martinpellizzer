Feuilleea amara

Feuilleea amara, commonly known as the bitter leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions.

It features elongated, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are known for their strong, bitter taste, which is attributed to the presence of various alkaloids.

Feuilleea amara is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
