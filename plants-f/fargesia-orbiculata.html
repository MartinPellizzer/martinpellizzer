Fargesia orbiculata

Fargesia orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaf bamboo, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its clumping growth habit and slender, greenish-brown stems. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in late spring, which are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Fargesia orbiculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
