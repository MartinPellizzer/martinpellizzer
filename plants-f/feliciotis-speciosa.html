Feliciotis speciosa

Feliciotis speciosa, commonly known as the splendour lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and smooth, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Feliciotis speciosa is often cultivated for its beauty in gardens and as a houseplant.
