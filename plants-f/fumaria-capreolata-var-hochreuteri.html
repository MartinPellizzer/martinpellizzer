Fumaria capreolata var. hochreuteri

Fumaria capreolata var. hochreuteri, commonly known as the yellow horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers with prominent, horn-like sepals. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. It has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and its stems are erect and branching.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
