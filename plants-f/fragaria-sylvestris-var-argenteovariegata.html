Fragaria sylvestris var. argenteovariegata

Fragaria sylvestris var. argenteovariegata, commonly known as the silver-variegated wild strawberry, is a variant of the wild strawberry plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white variegation on its leaves, which sets it apart from typical wild strawberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are similar in flavor to common strawberries but often have a more intense taste. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its unique fruit.
