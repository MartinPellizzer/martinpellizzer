Festuca Duriuscula Var. Longifolia

Festuca duriuscula var. longifolia is a hardy grass species known for its resilience in harsh environments. It thrives in rocky and arid regions, making it a common sight in mountainous areas. The plant's long, slender leaves give it a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other grass varieties. Despite its tough nature, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. This variety is often overlooked but is a vital part of its ecosystem.
