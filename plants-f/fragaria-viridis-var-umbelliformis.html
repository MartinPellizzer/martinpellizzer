Fragaria viridis var. umbelliformis

Fragaria viridis var. umbelliformis, commonly known as the green strawberry, is a variant of the common strawberry plant.

It is characterized by its small, green berries that are typically less sweet and more acidic than the typical red strawberries. The plant produces clusters of fruit, known as umbels, which give it its specific variety name. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
