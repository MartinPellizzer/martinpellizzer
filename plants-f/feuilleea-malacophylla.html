Feuilleea malacophylla

Feuilleea malacophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with a dense rosette of lance-shaped, silver-gray leaves. The plant's foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive, almost metallic appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Feuilleea malacophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant and unique visual appeal.
