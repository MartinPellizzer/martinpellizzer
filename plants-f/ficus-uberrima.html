Ficus uberrima

Ficus uberrima, commonly known as the noble fig tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large, evergreen tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall, featuring a broad, umbrella-like canopy. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus uberrima is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in humid, well-drained soils.
