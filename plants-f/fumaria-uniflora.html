Fumaria uniflora

Fumaria uniflora, commonly known as the one-flowered smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges.

Fumaria uniflora is known for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
