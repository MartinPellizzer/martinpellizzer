Fimbristylis macrantha

Fimbristylis macrantha, commonly known as the broad-leaved sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

The inflorescence consists of a spike of small, three-lobed flowers, which are usually green or reddish in color.
