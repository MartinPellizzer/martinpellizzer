Fimbristylis laniceps

Fimbristylis laniceps, commonly known as the hairy fringed sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Australia, and the Pacific Islands.

Its leaves are usually covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinct textured appearance.

Fimbristylis laniceps produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are often surrounded by specialized bracts.
