Fumana thymifolia var. glabra

Fumana thymifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved fumana, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered umbels of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
