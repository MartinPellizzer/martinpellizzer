Fuchsia caracasana

Fuchsia caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas fuchsia, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Fuchsia caracasana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its ornate floral display.
