Fuchsia × hendersonii

Fuchsia × hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep magenta to soft pink. The plant produces small, oval berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fuchsia × hendersonii is valued in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
