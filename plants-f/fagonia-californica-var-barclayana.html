Fagonia californica var. barclayana

Fagonia californica var. barclayana, commonly known as the California fagonia, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its gray-green, scale-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
