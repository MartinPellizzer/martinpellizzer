Filago cretensis subsp. cretensis

Filago cretensis subsp.

cretensis, commonly known as the Cretaceous filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the family.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in open habitats such as slopes and cliffs.

Due to its historical significance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically valuable species.
