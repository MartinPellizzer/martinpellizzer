Ferula schischkinii

Ferula schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's giant fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, yellowish-green flower heads that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and aromatic, with a strong licorice-like scent.

Ferula schischkinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
