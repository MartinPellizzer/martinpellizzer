Fessia Bisotunensis

Fessia bisotunensis is a rare and enigmatic plant native to a limited region in Iran. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing remarkable adaptations to its harsh surroundings. This species is notable for its unique floral structure and limited geographic distribution. Due to habitat destruction and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Studying Fessia bisotunensis offers valuable insights into ecological resilience and biodiversity preservation.
