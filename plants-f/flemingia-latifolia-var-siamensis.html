Flemingia latifolia var. siamensis

Flemingia latifolia var. siamensis, commonly known as the Siam flatleaf acacia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that are often arranged in clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
