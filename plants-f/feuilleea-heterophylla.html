Feuilleea heterophylla

Feuilleea heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf fern, is a unique plant species characterized by its morphological variability.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant exhibits heterophylly, meaning it produces leaves of different shapes and sizes depending on environmental conditions. Its fronds are typically divided into numerous small segments, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Feuilleea heterophylla is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
