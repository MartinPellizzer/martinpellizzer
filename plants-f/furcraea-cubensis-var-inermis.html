Furcraea cubensis var. inermis

Furcraea cubensis var. inermis, commonly known as the Cuban sword plant, is a striking succulent native to the Caribbean.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers, though it is not commonly cultivated for its blooms. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and resilience in dry climates.
