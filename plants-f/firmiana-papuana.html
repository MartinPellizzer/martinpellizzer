Firmiana papuana

Firmiana papuana, commonly known as the Papuan firmiana, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, often appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation in natural habitats.
