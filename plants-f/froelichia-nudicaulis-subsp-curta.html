Froelichia nudicaulis subsp. curta

Froelichia nudicaulis subsp.

curta, commonly known as the short-stemmed froelichia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the southeastern United States. It typically features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered leaves with a waxy texture.

The plant produces tiny, pale green to yellowish flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and botanical studies.
