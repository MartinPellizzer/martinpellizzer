Fuchsia macropetala

Fuchsia macropetala, commonly known as the large-flowered fuchsia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Fuchsia macropetala is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
