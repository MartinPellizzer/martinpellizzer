Falcata ovalifolia

Falcata ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf falcata, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly curved at the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its bark is thick and fibrous, making it resistant to pests and diseases.

Falcata ovalifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
