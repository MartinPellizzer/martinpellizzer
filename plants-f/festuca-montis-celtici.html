Festuca montis-celtici

Festuca montis-celtici, commonly known as the Celtic mountain fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the high-altitude regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. This plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. Festuca montis-celtici is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small invertebrates.
