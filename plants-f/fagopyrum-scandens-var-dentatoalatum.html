Fagopyrum scandens var. dentatoalatum

Fagopyrum scandens var. dentatoalatum, commonly known as the hairy buckwheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with serrated margins that contribute to its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late summer.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
