Filicium decipiens f. apterum

Filicium decipiens f. apterum, commonly known as the false fern, is a fern-like plant belonging to the family Filicales.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices, where it can thrive in humid conditions.
