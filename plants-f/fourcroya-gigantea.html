Fourcroya gigantea

Fourcroya gigantea, commonly known as the giant fourcroya, is a striking plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to two feet in diameter. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by dark purple berries.
