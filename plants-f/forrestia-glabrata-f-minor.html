Forrestia glabrata f. minor

Forrestia glabrata f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf forestiera, is a compact shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a glossy dark green, with a smooth, glabrous surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a desirable choice for ornamental horticulture.
