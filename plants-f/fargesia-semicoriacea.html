Fargesia semicoriacea

Fargesia semicoriacea, commonly known as the Sichuan bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China, particularly in the Sichuan province, and is characterized by its slender, clumping growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Fargesia semicoriacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
