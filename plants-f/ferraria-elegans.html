Ferraria elegans

Ferraria elegans, commonly known as the silver leaf ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silvery sheen. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ferraria elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
