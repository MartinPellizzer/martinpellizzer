Ficaria varia

Ficaria varia, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, showy yellow flowers with prominent stamens. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that help it store water, adapting to its often arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
