Furcraea aitonii

Furcraea aitonii, commonly known as the Aiton's furcraea, is a perennial plant native to southern Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by long, narrow fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
