Feuilleea championii

Feuilleea championii, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves with a distinctive golden hue along the edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the wet season. Feuilleea championii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
