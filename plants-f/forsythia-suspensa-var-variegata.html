Forsythia suspensa var. variegata

Forsythia suspensa var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated weeping forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its pendulous branches that cascade with clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The leaves are typically variegated, featuring white or yellow edges against a dark green background. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to add vertical interest to gardens.
