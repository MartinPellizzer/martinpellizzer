Fuirena stricta subsp. stricta

Fuirena stricta subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf fuirena, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
