Faramea axillaris

Faramea axillaris, commonly known as the velvetleaf or waxleaf faramea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green foliage that often has a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Faramea axillaris is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
