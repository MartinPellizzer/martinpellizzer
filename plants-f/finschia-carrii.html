Finschia carrii

Finschia carrii, commonly known as the Tasmanian mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Finschia carrii thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in heathlands and mountainous regions.

Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
