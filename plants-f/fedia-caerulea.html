Fedia caerulea

Fedia caerulea, commonly known as the blue fedia, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue flowers, which bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. Fedia caerulea thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
