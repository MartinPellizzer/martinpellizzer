Ficus tonsa var. leptodictya

Ficus tonsa var. leptodictya, commonly known as the glossy leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy, and dark green leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits that mature to a reddish color. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
