Festuca myuros var. bromoides

Festuca myuros var. bromoides, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Festuca myuros var. bromoides is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, low-maintenance turf-like areas.
