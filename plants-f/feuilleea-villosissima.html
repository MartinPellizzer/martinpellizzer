Feuilleea villosissima

Feuilleea villosissima, commonly known as the velvety leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its soft, hairy foliage.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, ovate leaves covered in fine, white trichomes that give it a velvety texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Feuilleea villosissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
