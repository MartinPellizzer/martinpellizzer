Ficus sphaeridiophora

Ficus sphaeridiophora, commonly known as the spherical fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its unique, round, and sometimes swollen fruit, which is a key feature in its identification. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Ficus sphaeridiophora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
