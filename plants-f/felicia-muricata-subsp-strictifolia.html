Felicia muricata subsp. strictifolia

Felicia muricata subsp.

strictifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is popular among gardeners in arid climates.
