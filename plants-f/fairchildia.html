Fairchildia

Fairchildia, commonly known as the "false hibiscus," is a genus of flowering plants in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

Fairchildia species typically have palmate leaves and produce capsules as their fruit.

They are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
