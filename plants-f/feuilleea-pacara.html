Feuilleea pacara

Feuilleea pacara, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its feathery, delicate fronds that give it a soft, airy appearance. The plant's leaves are typically dark green on the upper surface and silvery-white on the underside, creating a striking visual contrast. Feuilleea pacara thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
