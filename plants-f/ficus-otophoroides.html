Ficus otophoroides

Ficus otophoroides, commonly known as the ear-shaped fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, ear-like leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are typically round and smooth. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus otophoroides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
