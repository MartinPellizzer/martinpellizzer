Feuilleea contortisiliqua

Feuilleea contortisiliqua, commonly known as the twisted-leaf fern, is a rare tropical plant characterized by its uniquely contorted fronds.

Its leaves are elongated and spirally twisted, creating an intricate, almost sculptural appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in dense rainforests. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spore-bearing cones.

Its botanical significance lies in its unusual morphology and potential medicinal properties.
