Fragaria rubricaulis

Fragaria rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and small, edible berries. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces fruit that is similar in taste to common strawberries.

This species is often found in woodland areas and is valued for both its ornamental and culinary uses.
