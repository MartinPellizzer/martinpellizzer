Frailea pumila var. maior

Frailea pumila var. maior, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Brazil.

It features a swollen, rounded stem that is typically green or red, with a dense covering of white or pale blue hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its small size and ability to thrive in bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
