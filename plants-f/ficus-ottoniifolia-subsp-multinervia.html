Ficus ottoniifolia subsp. multinervia

Ficus ottoniifolia subsp.

multinervia, commonly known as the African rubber plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves.

The plant has a woody stem and can grow as an upright shrub or small tree. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a prominent venation pattern.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
