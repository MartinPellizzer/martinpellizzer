Ferreirea praecox

Ferreirea praecox, commonly known as the early flowering ferreirea, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, temperate climates. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white hair. Its flowers are pale yellow and appear in clusters, blooming early in the growing season.

Ferreirea praecox is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
