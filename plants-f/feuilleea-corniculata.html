Feuilleea corniculata

Feuilleea corniculata, commonly known as the horned leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with distinctive horn-like protrusions along the edges, giving it its unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming in early summer. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Feuilleea corniculata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
