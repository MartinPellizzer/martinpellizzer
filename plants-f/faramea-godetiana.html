Faramea godetiana

Faramea godetiana, commonly known as the Godetiana or the African violet relative, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to regions of Africa.

It features attractive, glossy, dark green leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Faramea godetiana is valued for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
