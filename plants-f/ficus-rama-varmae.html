Ficus rama-varmae

Ficus rama-varmae, commonly known as the Rama Varma ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South and Southeast Asia, particularly India. The plant is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves and a robust, woody stem that can grow both as a tree or shrub. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
