Faucaria nemorosa

Faucaria nemorosa, commonly known as the rock fig or desert rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
