Fargesia decurvata

Fargesia decurvata, commonly known as the Curved Fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and gracefully arching leaves that give it a soft, flowing appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens. Its foliage is a deep green, often with a bluish tinge, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring.

Fargesia decurvata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in cool, humid climates.
