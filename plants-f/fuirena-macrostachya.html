Fuirena macrostachya

Fuirena macrostachya, commonly known as the giant spiderwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Fuirena macrostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
