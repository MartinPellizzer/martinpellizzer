Ficus Dilatata

Ficus dilatata is a unique and adaptable plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environments, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening. This species is characterized by its glossy leaves and distinctive aerial roots that help it climb and anchor itself. Its versatility has made it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists alike. Ficus dilatata not only adds aesthetic value to spaces but also plays a role in local ecosystems where it naturally occurs.
