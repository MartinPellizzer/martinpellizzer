Ficus Amblyphylla

Ficus amblyphylla is a unique species of fig tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking foliage and distinctive leaf shapes that set it apart from other ficus varieties. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more common ficus species.
