Felicia burkei

Felicia burkei, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. It typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun.

Felicia burkei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
