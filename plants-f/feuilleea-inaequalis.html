Feuilleea inaequalis

Feuilleea inaequalis, commonly known as the irregular leaf plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its asymmetrical, lance-shaped leaves that exhibit a striking contrast in coloration, ranging from deep green to variegated yellow and white. The plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are not only aesthetically pleasing but also have a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Feuilleea inaequalis is relatively low-maintenance, requiring regular watering and well-draining soil to flourish.
