Feuilleea punctata

Feuilleea punctata, commonly known as the Spotted Leaf Plant, is a unique species characterized by its striking foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a velvety texture and a distinctive pattern of dark green spots. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. It is native to tropical regions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Feuilleea punctata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
