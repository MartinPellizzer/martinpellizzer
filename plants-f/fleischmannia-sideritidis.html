Fleischmannia sideritidis

Fleischmannia sideritidis, commonly known as the ironstone fleischmannia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Fleischmannia sideritidis thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to rock gardens and xeriscaping.
