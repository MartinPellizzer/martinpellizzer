Fargesia hackelii

Fargesia hackelii, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in panicles during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Fargesia hackelii is a hardy perennial that is well-suited for garden use in temperate climates.
