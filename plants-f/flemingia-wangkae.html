Flemingia wangkae

Flemingia wangkae, commonly known as the Wangkae acacia, is a shrub native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is adapted to humid, tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Flemingia wangkae is often found in shaded understorey environments within dense forests.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
