Fuchsia macrostemma

Fuchsia macrostemma, commonly known as the large-flowered fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces both male and female flowers on the same inflorescence, making it monoecious. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Fuchsia macrostemma thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
