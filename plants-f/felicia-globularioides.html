Felicia globularioides

Felicia globularioides, commonly known as the globular daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and bright yellow central disc surrounded by white ray florets. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Felicia globularioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
