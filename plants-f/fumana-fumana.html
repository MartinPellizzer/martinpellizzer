Fumana fumana

Fumana fumana, commonly known as the smoky bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Fumana fumana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
