Festuca × belensis var. propinqua

Festuca × belensis var. propinqua, commonly known as the slender meadow fescue, is a hybrid grass species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in open, sunny environments. It is often found in meadows, pastures, and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of ecological restoration projects.
