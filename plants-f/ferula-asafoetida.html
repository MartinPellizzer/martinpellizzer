Ferula asafoetida

Ferula asafoetida, commonly known as the wild asafoetida, is a flowering plant native to the dry regions of Iran, Afghanistan, and Pakistan.

Its roots are the primary source of the spice and medicinal substance known as asafoetida.

Ferula asafoetida is valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a spice in various cuisines.
