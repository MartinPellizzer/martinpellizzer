Fagopyrum odontopterum

Fagopyrum odontopterum, commonly known as buckwheat, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its triangular, serrated leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces triangular, three-lobed seeds that are rich in nutrients and used in various culinary and medicinal applications. Buckwheat is also valued for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its role in ecological landscaping.

Its rapid growth and deep root system make it an effective plant for erosion control.
