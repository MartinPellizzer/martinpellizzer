Fimbristylis subtristachya

Fimbristylis subtristachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers, typically in the summer months. Its botanical structure is adapted to tolerate waterlogged conditions, making it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
