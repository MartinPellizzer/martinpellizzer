Flemingia angusta

Flemingia angusta, commonly known as the narrowleaf flemingia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Flemingia angusta is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
