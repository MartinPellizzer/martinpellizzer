Festuca kunmingensis

Festuca kunmingensis, commonly known as Kunming fescue, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscaping.
