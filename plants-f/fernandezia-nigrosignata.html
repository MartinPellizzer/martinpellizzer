Fernandezia nigrosignata

Fernandezia nigrosignata, commonly known as the black-streaked fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green with dark streaks. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Fernandezia nigrosignata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
