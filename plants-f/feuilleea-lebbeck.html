Feuilleea lebbeck

Feuilleea lebbeck, commonly known as the golden banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often a vibrant green, and its distinctive, sprawling canopy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Feuilleea lebbeck is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses.
