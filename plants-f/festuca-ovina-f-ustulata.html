Festuca ovina f. ustulata

Festuca ovina f. ustulata, commonly known as the scorched sheepgrass, is a variant of the sheepgrass species.

It is a low-growing, perennial grass characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or arctic environments.

This form is distinguished by its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
