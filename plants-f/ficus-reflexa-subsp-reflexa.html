Ficus reflexa subsp. reflexa

Ficus reflexa subsp.

reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed fig, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub with smooth, dark green leaves that are often lobed or serrated.

The plant produces small, clustered fig fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. Ficus reflexa subsp.

reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
