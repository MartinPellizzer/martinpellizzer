Fimbrorchis linearifolia

Fimbrorchis linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
