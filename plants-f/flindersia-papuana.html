Flindersia papuana

Flindersia papuana, commonly known as the papuan flame tree, is a tropical tree native to the Indo-Pacific region, including parts of Indonesia and Papua New Guinea.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
