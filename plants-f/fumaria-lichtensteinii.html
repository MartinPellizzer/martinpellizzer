Fumaria lichtensteinii

Fumaria lichtensteinii, commonly known as the Lichtenstein's smokeplant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria lichtensteinii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
