Faramea elegans

Faramea elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf faramea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features attractive, glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Faramea elegans is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
