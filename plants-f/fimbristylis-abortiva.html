Fimbristylis abortiva

Fimbristylis abortiva, commonly known as the narrowleaf spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wet meadows.
