Fugosia hakeifolia

Fugosia hakeifolia, commonly known as the Hakea leaf tree, is a unique plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and its ability to thrive in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces striking, cone-like inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are often leathery and arranged in a compact, bushy form, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Fugosia hakeifolia is valued for its resilience to coastal conditions and its ability to provide habitat for local wildlife.
