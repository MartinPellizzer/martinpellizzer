Fedia

Fedia, commonly known as the African mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its dark, heavy wood with a rich grain pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. Fedia is valued for its durable timber, which is used in furniture and construction.
