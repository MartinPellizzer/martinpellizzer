Feuilleea venosa

Feuilleea venosa, commonly known as the Veinleaf Plant, is a unique species characterized by its striking, veined foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and elongated, with a deep green color and prominent white or silver veins that run throughout the leaf surface. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.

Feuilleea venosa requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight to flourish.
