Fuchsia dependens

Fuchsia dependens, commonly known as the weeping fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant has slender, drooping stems that give it a distinctive cascading appearance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Fuchsia dependens thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
