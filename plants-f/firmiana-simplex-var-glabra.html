Firmiana simplex var. glabra

Firmiana simplex var. glabra, commonly known as the Chinese parasol tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped leaves that are typically glabrous, or smooth, as indicated by its varietal name. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by distinctive, spiny fruit pods. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and shade-providing canopy.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
