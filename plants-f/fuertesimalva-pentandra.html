Fuertesimalva pentandra

Fuertesimalva pentandra, commonly known as the Canary Island pine, is a species of pine native to the Canary Islands.

It is a tall, evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The tree has long, needle-like leaves that are arranged in pairs along the branches. Its cones are woody and typically measure around 5 to 8 centimeters in length.

This plant is well adapted to the volcanic soils and coastal climates of its native habitat.
