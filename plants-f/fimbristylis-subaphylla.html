Fimbristylis subaphylla

Fimbristylis subaphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flat sedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Fimbristylis subaphylla plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
