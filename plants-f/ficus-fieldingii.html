Ficus fieldingii

Ficus fieldingii, commonly known as the Fielding's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus fieldingii is often grown as a houseplant due to its adaptability and attractive foliage.
