Frailea perumbilicata var. spinosior

Frailea perumbilicata var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny frailea, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, spiny areoles, which are characteristic of the Cactaceae family. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged foliage. Its spines are typically white to yellow and can be quite sharp, providing protection from herbivores.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid environments with minimal water.
