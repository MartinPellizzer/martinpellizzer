Felicia kraussii

Felicia kraussii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to arid environments. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation.

Felicia kraussii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its bright, cheerful blooms.
