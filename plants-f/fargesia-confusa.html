Fargesia confusa

Fargesia confusa, commonly known as the Yunnan arrow bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, clumping growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Fargesia confusa is well-suited for rock gardens and as a decorative element in woodland settings.
