Fargesia zayuensis

Fargesia zayuensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and gracefully arching foliage, which give it an elegant appearance. This plant is known for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. Its foliage is typically a deep green, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen.

Fargesia zayuensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
