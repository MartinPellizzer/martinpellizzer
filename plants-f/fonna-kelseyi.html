Fonna kelseyi

Fonna kelseyi, commonly known as the desert bellflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low water availability. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve moisture.

Fonna kelseyi plays an important ecological role by providing nectar for pollinators in its native habitat.
