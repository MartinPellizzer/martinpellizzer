Freycinetia excelsa

Freycinetia excelsa, commonly known as the giant pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters. Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

Freycinetia excelsa is also valued for its fibrous leaves, which have traditional uses in basket-making and thatching.
