Festuca arundinacea var. incrustata

Festuca arundinacea var. incrustata, commonly known as velvet fescue, is a perennial grass species characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.

It features fine, dark green foliage with a distinctive velvet-like texture due to the presence of a waxy coating on the leaves. This variety is well adapted to cool, moist climates and thrives in partial to full shade.
