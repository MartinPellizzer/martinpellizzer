Ficus gongoensis

Ficus gongoensis, commonly known as the African fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green to yellow in color. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ficus gongoensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
