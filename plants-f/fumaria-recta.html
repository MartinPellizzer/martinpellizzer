Fumaria recta

Fumaria recta, commonly known as the straight fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fumariaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, dark seeds. Fumaria recta thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
