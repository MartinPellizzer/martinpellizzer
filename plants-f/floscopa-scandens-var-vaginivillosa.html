Floscopa scandens var. vaginivillosa

Floscopa scandens var. vaginivillosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf climbing plant, is a variant of the species Floscopa scandens.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
