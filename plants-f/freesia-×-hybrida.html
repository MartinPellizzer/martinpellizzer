Freesia × hybrida

Freesia × hybrida, commonly known as the garden freesia, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in a wide range of colors, including white, yellow, orange, and pink. The flowers typically bloom in early spring and are known for their strong, sweet scent. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

It is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as cut flowers.
