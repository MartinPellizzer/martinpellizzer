Franseria sanctae-gertrudis

Franseria sanctae-gertrudis, commonly known as the Gertrude's franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Franseria sanctae-gertrudis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
