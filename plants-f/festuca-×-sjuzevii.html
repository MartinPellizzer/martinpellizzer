Festuca × sjuzevii

Festuca × sjuzevii, commonly known as the Sjuzevii fescue, is a hybrid grass species originating from the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, two-lobed flowers.

Festuca × sjuzevii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
