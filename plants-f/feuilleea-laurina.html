Feuilleea laurina

Feuilleea laurina, commonly known as the golden laurel, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Feuilleea laurina is also valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine.
