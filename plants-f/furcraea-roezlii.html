Furcraea roezlii

Furcraea roezlii, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its dramatic appearance, it is often used in tropical landscapes and as a houseplant.
