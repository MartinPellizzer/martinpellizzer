Fargesia solida

Fargesia solida, commonly known as the Yunnan evergreen bamboo, is a species native to China.

It is a clump-forming bamboo characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is evergreen, retaining its foliage throughout the year, which makes it suitable for ornamental landscaping. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Fargesia solida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
