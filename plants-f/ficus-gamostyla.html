Ficus gamostyla

Ficus gamostyla, commonly known as the golden fig or the sweet fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into edible fruits. The fruit is typically yellow or orange when ripe and has a sweet, tropical flavor.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and for its edible fruit in tropical regions.
