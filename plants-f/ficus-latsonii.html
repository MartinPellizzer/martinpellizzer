Ficus latsonii

Ficus latsonii, commonly known as the Guangxi fig, is a species native to southern China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus latsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
