Fumaria capreolata var. devoniensis

Fumaria capreolata var. devoniensis, commonly known as the Devon yellow-crown fumitory, is a flowering plant native to the United Kingdom.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often sprawling.

This variety is distinguished by its unique floral coloration and is considered a rare and localized species.
