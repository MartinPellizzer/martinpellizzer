Felicia cymbalariae subsp. ionops

Felicia cymbalariae subsp.

ionops, commonly known as the narrowleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant has finely divided, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in dry, open habitats.

This species is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
