Festuca akhanii

Festuca akhanii, commonly known as the Akhanyan fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes, making it resilient in harsh climates.

Its ecological importance includes soil stabilization and providing forage for wildlife.
