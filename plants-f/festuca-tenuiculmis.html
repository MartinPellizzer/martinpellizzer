Festuca tenuiculmis

Festuca tenuiculmis, commonly known as slender meadow grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has fine, slender leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in early summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.

Festuca tenuiculmis is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
