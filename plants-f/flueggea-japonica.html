Flueggea japonica

Flueggea japonica, commonly known as Japanese flueggea, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the spring.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
