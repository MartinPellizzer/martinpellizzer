Faterna

Faterna, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, buoyant stalks that rise above the water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the surface of ponds and lakes. Its botanical name, *Nelumbo nucifera*, reflects its historical and cultural significance in various civilizations.

The faterna plant is also valued for its symbolic meanings in religion and medicine.
