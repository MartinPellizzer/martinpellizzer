Ficus sohotonensis

Ficus sohotonensis, commonly known as the Sohoton fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Ficus sohotonensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

Its adaptability to various light conditions makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
