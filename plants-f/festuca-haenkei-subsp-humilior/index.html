Festuca Haenkei Subsp. Humilior

Festuca haenkei subsp. humilior is a low-growing grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh, alpine environments where few plants can survive. This subspecies is known for its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to withstand cold and poor soil conditions. Its fine, blue-green foliage makes it a striking addition to rock gardens and alpine landscapes. Due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal, it has become a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, drought-tolerant plants.
