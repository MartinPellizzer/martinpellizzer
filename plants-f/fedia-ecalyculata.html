Fedia ecalyculata

Fedia ecalyculata, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Fedia ecalyculata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
