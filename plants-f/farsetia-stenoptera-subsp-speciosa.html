Farsetia stenoptera subsp. speciosa

Farsetia stenoptera subsp.

speciosa, commonly known as the showy farsetia, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family. It is characterized by its vibrant, spurred flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of glossy, ovate leaves with a deep green hue.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
