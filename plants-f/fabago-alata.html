Fabago alata

Fabago alata, commonly known as the winged balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf structure, which features prominent wing-like veins running along the blade. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. Fabago alata thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
