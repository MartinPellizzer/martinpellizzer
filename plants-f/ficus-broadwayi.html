Ficus broadwayi

Ficus broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are typically consumed raw.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for both decorative and medicinal purposes.
