Fargesia sylvestris

Fargesia sylvestris, commonly known as the silver fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and gracefully arching foliage. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late spring. Fargesia sylvestris is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.

It prefers cool, moist environments and is relatively low maintenance once established.
