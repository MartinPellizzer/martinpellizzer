Fuchsia × colensoi

Fuchsia × colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters and produces showy bracts that surround the true flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Fuchsia × colensoi is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display.
