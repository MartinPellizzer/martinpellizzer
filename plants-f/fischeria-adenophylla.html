Fischeria adenophylla

Fischeria adenophylla, commonly known as the pink fairy tern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces small, round, black seeds enclosed in a fleshy, red capsule. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions.

Fischeria adenophylla is a low-growing plant that typically reaches a height of around 30 centimeters.
