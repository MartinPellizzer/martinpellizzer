Furcraea cabuya var. cabuya

Furcraea cabuya var. cabuya, commonly known as the Cabuya plant, is a striking member of the Asphodelaceae family native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall inflorescences with clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

Its architectural form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
