Ferula licentiana var. licentiana

Ferula licentiana var. licentiana, commonly known as the Licentian fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, aromatic leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbel-like clusters, followed by long, narrow seed pods. Its roots are thick and fibrous, often used in traditional medicine.

This species is valued for its essential oils and is cultivated for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
