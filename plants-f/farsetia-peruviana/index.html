Farsetia Peruviana

Farsetia peruviana is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in Peru. Known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This species belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants prized for their beauty. Despite its allure, Farsetia peruviana remains relatively unknown outside of specialized horticultural circles. Its unique appearance and challenging cultivation requirements make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts.
