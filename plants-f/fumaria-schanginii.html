Fumaria schanginii

Fumaria schanginii, commonly known as the Schangin's smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer, often in clusters at the tips of the stems. Fumaria schanginii is typically found in rocky or gravelly habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is a herbaceous perennial that is relatively uncommon in cultivation but appreciated for its unique appearance and ecological value.
