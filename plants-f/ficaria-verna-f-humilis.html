Ficaria verna f. humilis

Ficaria verna f. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf winter beauty, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, waxy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant has a mat-forming habit, with succulent, dark green leaves that are somewhat fleshy. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
