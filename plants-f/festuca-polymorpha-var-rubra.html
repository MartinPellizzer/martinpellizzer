Festuca polymorpha var. rubra

Festuca polymorpha var. rubra, commonly known as red mountain grass, is a variant of the common bentgrass species.

It is characterized by its reddish stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. It has a clump-forming growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The botanical features of Festuca polymorpha var. rubra make it well adapted to harsh mountain conditions.
