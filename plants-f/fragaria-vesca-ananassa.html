Fragaria vesca ananassa

Fragaria vesca ananassa, commonly known as the strawberry plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, red, fleshy fruits that are rich in seeds. The plant is native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is widely cultivated for its edible fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The flowers are typically white with a pinkish tinge and produce fruit within a few weeks of blooming.
