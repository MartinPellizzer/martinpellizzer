Fagopyrum polygonum

Fagopyrum polygonum, commonly known as Chinese buckwheat, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and triangular leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in a hard, spiky shell, which aids in seed dispersal.

The plant is often cultivated for its nutritional value, as its seeds are rich in protein and minerals.
