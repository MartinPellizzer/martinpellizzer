Fragaria indica

Fragaria indica, commonly known as the Indian strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the Himalayas and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, red, edible berries that are similar in appearance to true strawberries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Fragaria indica is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
