Festuca sulcata f. strictiflora

Festuca sulcata f. strictiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered tall fescue, is a variant of the tall fescue species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, spikelet-like flowers. It is adapted to a wide range of soil types and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for various landscaping and agricultural applications.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fine texture and ability to form a dense turf.
