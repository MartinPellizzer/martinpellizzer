Fumaria muralis var. affinis

Fumaria muralis var. affinis, commonly known as the wall rue, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or wall crevices, thriving in poor, well-drained soils. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately divided, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
