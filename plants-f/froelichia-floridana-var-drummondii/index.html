Froelichia Floridana Var. Drummondii

Froelichia floridana var. drummondii is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of the southeastern united states. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its distinctive floral characteristics. this variety is named after the botanist who first described it, highlighting its scientific importance. due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces threats to its survival. conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and ecologically significant plant.
