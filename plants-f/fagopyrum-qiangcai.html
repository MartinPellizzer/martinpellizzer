Fagopyrum qiangcai

Fagopyrum qiangcai, commonly known as Qiangcai buckwheat, is a perennial herb native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau in China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright stem and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its unique ecological adaptations, it plays a significant role in the local flora of its native region.
