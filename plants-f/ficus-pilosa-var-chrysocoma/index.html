Ficus Pilosa Var. Chrysocoma

Ficus pilosa var. chrysocoma is a unique variant of the common fig tree known for its striking foliage. This plant features dense clusters of small, velvety leaves that give it a lush, almost moss-like appearance. It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant. Despite its ornamental value, it is also appreciated for its air-purifying qualities. Its distinctive look makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and interior designers alike.
