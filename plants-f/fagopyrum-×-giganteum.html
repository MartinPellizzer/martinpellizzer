Fagopyrum × giganteum

Fagopyrum × giganteum, commonly known as the giant buckwheat, is a perennial plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in landscaping.
