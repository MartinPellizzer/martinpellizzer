Fargesia chungii

Fargesia chungii, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its clumping growth habit and slender, erect culms. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are often a vibrant green, providing an ornamental appeal in gardens. It is cold-hardy and thrives in shaded or partially sunny conditions, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Fargesia chungii is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and its ecological benefits in natural habitats.
