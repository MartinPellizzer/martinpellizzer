Filago Spathulata Var. Desertorum

Filago spathulata var. desertorum is a resilient plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and extreme temperatures. This variant is known for its compact growth and specialized adaptations to survive in desert conditions. Its unique morphology includes small, spiny leaves and a short, sturdy stem. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
