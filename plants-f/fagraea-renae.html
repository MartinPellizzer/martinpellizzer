Fagraea renae

Fagraea renae, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring, giving it its common name. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, red fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea renae is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
