Festuca Mediterranea

Festuca mediterranea is a hardy grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in rocky and coastal areas where other plants struggle to grow. This plant is known for its resilience to drought and salt spray. Its fine, silver-green leaves make it a popular choice for landscaping. Festuca mediterranea plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
