Festuca calamaria var. minor

Festuca calamaria var. minor, commonly known as the lesser calamity fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control and ornamental purposes.
