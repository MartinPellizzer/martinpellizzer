Frailea castanea var. perumbilicata

Frailea castanea var. perumbilicata, commonly known as the Orange-Flowered Stone Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Brazil.

It features a globular stem with a smooth, pale green surface that develops a reddish tinge when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces vibrant orange flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
