Fuchsia × bacillaris

Fuchsia × bacillaris, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that typically range in color from deep red to purple. The plant is native to South America and is widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
