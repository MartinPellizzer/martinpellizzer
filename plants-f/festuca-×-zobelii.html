Festuca × zobelii

Festuca × zobelii, commonly known as Zobel's fescue, is a hybrid grass species that combines traits from two parent species, Festuca rubra and Festuca ovina.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green foliage and tufted growth habit, making it suitable for ornamental and turfgrass applications. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its durability and aesthetic appeal.

Festuca × zobelii is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its low maintenance requirements.
