Festuca gussonis

Festuca gussonis, commonly known as the Tuscan meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Festuca gussonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
