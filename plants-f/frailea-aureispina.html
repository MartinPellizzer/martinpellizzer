Frailea aureispina

Frailea aureispina, commonly known as the golden spine cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in sharp, yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
