Fedorovia

Fedorovia, commonly known as the false heather, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Ericaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are shrubs characterized by their small, often clustered flowers and evergreen foliage. They are generally low-maintenance and thrive in well-drained conditions.
