Falconeria

Falconeria, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, supported by long, flexible stems. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom above the water, often with a fragrant scent. Its roots anchor it in muddy substrates, while its leaves provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Falconeria is valued for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance in various traditions.
