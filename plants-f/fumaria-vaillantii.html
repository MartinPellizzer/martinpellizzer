Fumaria vaillantii

Fumaria vaillantii, commonly known as the Vaillant's smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, often in a spiral arrangement along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and typically have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria vaillantii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
