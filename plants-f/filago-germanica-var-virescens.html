Filago germanica var. virescens

Filago germanica var. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered filago, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, hairy stems that grow up to 60 cm in height, often branching towards the top. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is often found in dry meadows and rocky soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded environments.
