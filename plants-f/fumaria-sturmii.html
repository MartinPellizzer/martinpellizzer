Fumaria sturmii

Fumaria sturmii, commonly known as the Sturm's smokeplant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces elongated, seed-containing pods that mature into a distinctive, smoke-like appearance, giving it its common name. Fumaria sturmii thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
