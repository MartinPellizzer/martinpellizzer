Festuca Communis Var. Valesiaca

Festuca communis var. valesiaca is a hardy grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments. Known for its fine, tufted growth and silver-blue foliage, this plant adds a striking visual element to its natural habitat. Its ability to withstand harsh weather conditions makes it a resilient addition to rocky landscapes. This variety is both ecologically significant and aesthetically pleasing in horticultural settings.
