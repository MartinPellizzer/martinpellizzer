Fagraea spatiosa

Fagraea spatiosa, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warmer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Fagraea spatiosa thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
