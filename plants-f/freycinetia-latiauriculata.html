Freycinetia latiauriculata

Freycinetia latiauriculata, commonly known as the broad-leafed freycinetia, is a species of cycad native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that are often a deep green in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow to green flowers arranged in a branched inflorescence, which is typical of the Cycadaceae family. Its growth habit is slow, and it can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

Freycinetia latiauriculata is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
