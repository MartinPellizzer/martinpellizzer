Feuilleea maritima

Feuilleea maritima, commonly known as the coastal leaf plant, is a hardy species native to coastal regions.

It thrives in saline and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh maritime environments. The plant features narrow, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss and reflect intense sunlight. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, producing small, pale blue blooms.

Feuilleea maritima plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion along shorelines.
