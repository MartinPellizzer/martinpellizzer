Fritillaria tenella var. micrantha

Fritillaria tenella var. micrantha, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or red, with a distinctive cap-like structure at the top. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to temperate climates.

The bulbs are perennial and can be propagated through division or seed.
