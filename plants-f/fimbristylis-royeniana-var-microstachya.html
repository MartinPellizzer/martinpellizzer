Fimbristylis royeniana var. microstachya

Fimbristylis royeniana var. microstachya, commonly known as the small-stalked flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers are small and inconspicuous.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in moist environments and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
