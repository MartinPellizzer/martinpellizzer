Ferula nevskii

Ferula nevskii, commonly known as the Nevskii's giant fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, yellowish-green flower heads that are composed of numerous small florets. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its roots are thick and fleshy.

Ferula nevskii is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
