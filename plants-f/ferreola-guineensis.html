Ferreola guineensis

Ferreola guineensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.
