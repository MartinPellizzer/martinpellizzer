Ferula stricta

Ferula stricta, commonly known as the rigid fennel, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, which bloom in the summer months. Its aromatic seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Ferula stricta is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
