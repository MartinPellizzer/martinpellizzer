Frenela actinostrobus

Frenela actinostrobus, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is a tall, tree-like fern that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features a slender, unbranched trunk with a crown of large, feather-like fronds. Its fronds are pinnately compound and can grow up to 3 meters in length.

This species is native to New Zealand and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
