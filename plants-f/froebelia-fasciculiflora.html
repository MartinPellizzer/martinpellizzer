Froebelia fasciculiflora

Froebelia fasciculiflora, commonly known as the clustered froebelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Froebelia fasciculiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
