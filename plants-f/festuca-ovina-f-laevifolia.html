Festuca ovina f. laevifolia

Festuca ovina f. laevifolia, commonly known as sheep's fescue, is a variant of the sheep fescue species.

It is a cool-season grass characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly rolled, with a bluish-green coloration. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed sites.

This form is distinguished by its smooth, hairless leaf surfaces, contributing to its unique appearance.
