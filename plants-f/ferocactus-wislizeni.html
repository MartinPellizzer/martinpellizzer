Ferocactus wislizeni

Ferocactus wislizeni, commonly known as the wislizen's barrel cactus, is a species native to the deserts of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are typically borne on the upper part of the stem. Ferocactus wislizeni is a slow-growing plant that can live for several decades, making it a long-lived component of desert ecosystems.
