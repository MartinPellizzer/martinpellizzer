Ficinia micrantha

Ficinia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered figsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. Ficinia micrantha thrives in wetland environments and is often found in swamps, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
