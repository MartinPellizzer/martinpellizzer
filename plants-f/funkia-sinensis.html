Funkia sinensis

Funkia sinensis, commonly known as Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and wetland landscapes.
