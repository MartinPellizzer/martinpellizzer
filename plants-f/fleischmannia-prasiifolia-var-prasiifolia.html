Fleischmannia prasiifolia var. prasiifolia

Fleischmannia prasiifolia var. prasiifolia, commonly known as the green-leaf fleischmannia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in arid landscapes.
