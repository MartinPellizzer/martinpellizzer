Felicia pusilla

Felicia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers with a central disk, and it has a compact, low-growing habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Felicia pusilla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
