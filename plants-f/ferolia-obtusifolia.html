Ferolia obtusifolia

Ferolia obtusifolia, commonly known as the blunt-leafed ferolia, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves with a smooth margin. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by oval, yellow fruits. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study due to its adaptability to arid conditions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
