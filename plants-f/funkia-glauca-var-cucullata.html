Funkia glauca var. cucullata

Funkia glauca var. cucullata, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a bluish tint, and it produces striking white to pale purple flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soil, making it a popular choice for water gardens and pond edges. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily, and it is often used for erosion control in wetland environments.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to support aquatic life.
