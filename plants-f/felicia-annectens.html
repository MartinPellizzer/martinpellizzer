Felicia annectens

Felicia annectens, commonly known as the yellow daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia annectens is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
