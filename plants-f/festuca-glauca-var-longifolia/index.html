Festuca Glauca Var. Longifolia

Festuca glauca var. longifolia is a striking ornamental grass known for its blue-green foliage and delicate, feather-like seed heads. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This variety is prized for its ability to form dense, low-growing clumps that add texture and visual interest to gardens. Its cold hardiness makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. With its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a versatile plant for both landscape design and ecological restoration.
