Fumaria abyssinica

Fumaria abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces slender, tubular fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Fumaria abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
