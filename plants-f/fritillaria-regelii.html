Fritillaria regelii

Fritillaria regelii, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils. It blooms in early spring and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.

Fritillaria regelii is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
