Fagraea blumei

Fagraea blumei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically lance-shaped and have a glossy surface. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several winged seeds, which aid in its dispersal.

Fagraea blumei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
