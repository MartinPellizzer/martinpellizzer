Festuca petriei f. tenuifolia

Festuca petriei f. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a cool-season grass species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant typically forms dense mats, often found in rocky or alpine environments. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Festuca petriei f. tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in naturalized landscapes.
