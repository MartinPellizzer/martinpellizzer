Fimbristylis subtricephala

Fimbristylis subtricephala, commonly known as the three-headed sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.
