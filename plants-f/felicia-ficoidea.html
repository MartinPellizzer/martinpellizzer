Felicia ficoidea

Felicia ficoidea, commonly known as the "Golden Groundsel," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance. Felicia ficoidea is a low-growing perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
