Fritillaria minor

Fritillaria minor, commonly known as the lesser crown imperial, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with contrasting black markings. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a basal rosette of bulbs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Fritillaria minor is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and dramatic floral display.
