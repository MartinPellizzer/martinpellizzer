Fuchsia × atkinsonii

Fuchsia × atkinsonii, commonly known as the Atkinson's fuchsia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant is native to New Zealand and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. Its foliage consists of ovate, glossy leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Fuchsia × atkinsonii thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically grown in partial shade.
