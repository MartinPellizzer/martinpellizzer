Fissistigma latifolium var. ovoideum

Fissistigma latifolium var. ovoideum, commonly known as the broad-leafed fissistigma, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
