Fargesia yuanjiangensis

Fargesia yuanjiangensis, commonly known as the Yuanjiang bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright culms that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Fargesia yuanjiangensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.
