Fumaria calcarata

Fumaria calcarata, commonly known as the rock fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. Its leaves are alternate, often with a slight waxy coating, and the flowers are borne in clusters along the stems.

Fumaria calcarata is often found in open, sunny habitats such as hillsides and rocky outcrops.
