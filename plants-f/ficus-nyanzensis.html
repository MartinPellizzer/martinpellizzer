Ficus nyanzensis

Ficus nyanzensis, commonly known as the Nyanza fig, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

The Nyanza fig is often found in moist, shaded areas near rivers and in dense forests.
