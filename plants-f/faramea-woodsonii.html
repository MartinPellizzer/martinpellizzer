Faramea woodsonii

Faramea woodsonii, commonly known as the Florida holly.

It is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and parts of Georgia. This evergreen shrub or small tree has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, white flowers followed by red berries. It is characterized by its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Faramea woodsonii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
