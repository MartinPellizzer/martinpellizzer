Fimbristylis diphylla var. tuberculata

Fimbristylis diphylla var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate flatsedge, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, triangular stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth or slightly textured surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along water edges.
