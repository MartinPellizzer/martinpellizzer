Fumana glutinosa var. viridis

Fumana glutinosa var. viridis, commonly known as the green sticky fumana, is a variant of the fumana plant characterized by its distinctive green foliage.

This plant is native to certain regions of Africa and is known for its sticky resinous secretions on its leaves. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that aids in water retention. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical environments.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
