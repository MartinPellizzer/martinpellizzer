Feuilleea speciosa

Feuilleea speciosa, commonly known as the golden leaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that range in color from deep emerald to coppery gold. The plant produces fragrant, orchid-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the rainy season. Feuilleea speciosa thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
