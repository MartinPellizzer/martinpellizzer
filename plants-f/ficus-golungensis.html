Ficus golungensis

Ficus golungensis, commonly known as the Golungensis fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Ficus golungensis thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
