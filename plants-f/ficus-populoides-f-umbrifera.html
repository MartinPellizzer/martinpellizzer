Ficus populoides f. umbrifera

Ficus populoides f. umbrifera, commonly known as the shady fig or umbra fig, is a rare and unique cultivar of the ficus genus.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black foliage, which gives it a striking appearance. The leaves are typically large, glossy, and have a velvety texture. This variety thrives in low-light conditions, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
