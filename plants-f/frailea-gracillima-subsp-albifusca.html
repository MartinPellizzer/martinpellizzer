Frailea gracillima subsp. albifusca

Frailea gracillima subsp.

albifusca, commonly known as the white-fleshed fairy cactus, is a succulent native to southern Africa. It features a compact, rounded stem with a white or pale green coloration, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the cooler months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
