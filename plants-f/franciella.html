Franciella

Franciella, commonly known as the "moss rose," is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
