Fargesia denudata

Fargesia denudata, commonly known as the naked fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a dark green color. The plant is evergreen and thrives in cool, moist environments, making it suitable for shaded garden areas. Its growth habit is clumping, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in the spring.

Fargesia denudata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to create a dense, decorative screen in landscaping.
