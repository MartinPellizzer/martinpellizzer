Frailea schilinzkyana subsp. schilinzkyana

Frailea schilinzkyana subsp.

schilinzkyana, commonly known as the Schilinzky's frailea, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warm season, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
