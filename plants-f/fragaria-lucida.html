Fragaria lucida

Fragaria lucida, commonly known as the shining strawberry, is a species of wild strawberry native to North America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are typically covered in a whitish bloom. These berries are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in local cuisines.

Fragaria lucida thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
