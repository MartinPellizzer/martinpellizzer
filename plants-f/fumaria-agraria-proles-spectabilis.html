Fumaria agraria proles spectabilis

Fumaria agraria proles spectabilis, commonly known as the showy fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branching.

The showy fumitory is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
