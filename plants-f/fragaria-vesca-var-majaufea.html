Fragaria vesca var. majaufea

Fragaria vesca var. majaufea, commonly known as the Majaufea strawberry, is a rare variant of the woodland strawberry.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, intensely flavored berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and is typically found in temperate regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is often cultivated in specialty gardens.
