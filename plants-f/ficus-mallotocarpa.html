Ficus mallotocarpa

Ficus mallotocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese rubber fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a smooth, round, greenish-yellow berry that turns red when mature.

Ficus mallotocarpa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
