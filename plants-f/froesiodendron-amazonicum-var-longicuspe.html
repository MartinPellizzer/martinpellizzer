Froesiodendron amazonicum var. longicuspe

Froesiodendron amazonicum var. longicuspe, commonly known as the long-pointed froesiodendron, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its bark is smooth and dark brown, often marked with shallow fissures.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
