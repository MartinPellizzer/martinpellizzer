Ficus aculeata var. indecora

Ficus aculeata var. indecora, commonly known as the Variegated Ficus or the Variegated Natal Ficus, is a popular houseplant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant has smooth, waxy leaves with a glossy appearance and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
