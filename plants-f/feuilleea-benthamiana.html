Feuilleea benthamiana

Feuilleea benthamiana, commonly known as the Bentham's leaf plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is fibrous and well-developed, allowing it to thrive in moist, well-drained soil.

Feuilleea benthamiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden setting.
