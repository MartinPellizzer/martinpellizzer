Faucaria

Faucaria, commonly known as the ice plant, is a genus of succulent plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy, often star-shaped flowers. They typically grow in rocky or sandy soils and are adapted to arid environments. Faucaria species are often used in xeriscaping due to their drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Their unique appearance and resilience make them popular among succulent enthusiasts.
