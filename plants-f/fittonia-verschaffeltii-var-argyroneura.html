Fittonia verschaffeltii var. argyroneura

Fittonia verschaffeltii var. argyroneura, commonly known as the silver-veined fittonia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with intricate, silver-white veins that create a striking visual contrast. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant due to its ornamental leaf patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and terrariums.
