Ficus schinzii

Ficus schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It produces edible figs that are typically consumed fresh or used in local cuisines.

Ficus schinzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
