Ficus microtus

Ficus microtus, commonly known as the little fig or dwarf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus microtus is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
