Furcraea antillana

Furcraea antillana, commonly known as the Antilles sword plant, is a succulent plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves that are often lined with small spines. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Furcraea antillana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
