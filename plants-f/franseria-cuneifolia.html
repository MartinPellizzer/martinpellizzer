Franseria cuneifolia

Franseria cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Franseria cuneifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to well-drained soils.
