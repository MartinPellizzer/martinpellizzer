Ferraria plicata

Ferraria plicata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Ferraria plicata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
