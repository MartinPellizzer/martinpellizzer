Ficus mariae

Ficus mariae, commonly known as the Marie fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit, a small fig-like structure, is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.

Ficus mariae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
