Filago paniculata

Filago paniculata, commonly known as the panicled filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features a slender, branched stem with small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in paniculate inflorescences, giving it a delicate appearance.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.
