Ficus endothrix

Ficus endothrix, commonly known as the silver fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a distinctive silver or pale underside. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Ficus endothrix is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
