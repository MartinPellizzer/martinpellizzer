Fagraea obovata-javana

Fagraea obovata-javana, commonly known as the Java glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are ovate in shape and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Fagraea obovata-javana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its use in traditional medicine.
