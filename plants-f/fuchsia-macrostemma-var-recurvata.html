Fuchsia macrostemma var. recurvata

Fuchsia macrostemma var. recurvata, commonly known as the recurved fuchsia, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant produces pendulous clusters of blooms, which attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage consists of dark green, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
