Fraxinus ornus subsp. cilicica

Fraxinus ornus subsp.

cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician hornbeam, is a subspecies native to the eastern Mediterranean region. It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils.

The plant features smooth, gray-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive serrated margin. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow catkins that appear in early spring, followed by small, round fruits.

The subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
