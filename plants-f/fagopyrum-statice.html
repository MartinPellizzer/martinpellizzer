Fagopyrum statice

Fagopyrum statice, commonly known as Tibetan buckwheat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including the Tibetan Plateau. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, fluffy white or pale pink bracts that resemble small flowers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
