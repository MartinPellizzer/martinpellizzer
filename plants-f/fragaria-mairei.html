Fragaria mairei

Fragaria mairei, commonly known as the Maire strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, edible berries. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and has fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit, similar to the common strawberry, is often used in culinary applications.

Fragaria mairei is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in traditional Chinese medicine.
