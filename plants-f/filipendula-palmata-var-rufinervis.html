Filipendula palmata var. rufinervis

Filipendula palmata var. rufinervis, commonly known as the red-veined meadow beauty, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its red-veined leaves are a distinguishing feature, and it blooms from June to August.
