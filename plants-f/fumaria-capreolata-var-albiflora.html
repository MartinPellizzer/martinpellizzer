Fumaria capreolata var. albiflora

Fumaria capreolata var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered fumitory, is a variant of the fumitory plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pale yellow to white, with five petals, and are borne in racemes along the stem. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

This plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
