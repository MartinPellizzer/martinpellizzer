Felicia minima

Felicia minima, commonly known as the dwarf fleur-de-lis, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, featuring bright yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Felicia minima is also noted for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
