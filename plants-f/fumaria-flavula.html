Fumaria flavula

Fumaria flavula, commonly known as the yellow cranesbill, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has a trailing habit, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are alternate and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

Fumaria flavula is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in woodland and grassland habitats.
