Fockea gracilis

Fockea gracilis, commonly known as the slender fockea, is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Fockea gracilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
