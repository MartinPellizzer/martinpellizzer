Ficus trivia var. trivia

Ficus trivia var. trivia, commonly known as the weeping fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a cascading pattern. The plant has a thick, woody stem that supports its drooping branches. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
