Fessia purpurea

Fessia purpurea, commonly known as the purple fessia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
