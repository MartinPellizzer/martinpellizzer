Felicia burchellii

Felicia burchellii, commonly known as the yellow daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are dispersed by the wind. Felicia burchellii is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and hardiness contribute to its use in gardens and landscaping.
