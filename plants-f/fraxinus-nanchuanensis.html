Fraxinus nanchuanensis

Fraxinus nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan ash, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with opposite arrangement. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, followed by samaroid fruits that are dispersed by wind.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its graceful appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
