Fragariopsis discolor

Fragariopsis discolor, commonly known as the variable strawberry bush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, hairy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit, which resembles a small strawberry, is edible and has a tart flavor.

Fragariopsis discolor is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
