Ficus mangenotii

Ficus mangenotii, commonly known as the Manguet's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus mangenotii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor conditions.
