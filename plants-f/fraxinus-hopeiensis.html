Fraxinus hopeiensis

Fraxinus hopeiensis, commonly known as the Hope's ash, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The tree has opposite, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers appear in clusters and are followed by winged fruits.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
