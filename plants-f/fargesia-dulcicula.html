Fargesia dulcicula

Fargesia dulcicula, commonly known as the sweet-scented arrow bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fargesia dulcicula is a slow-growing plant that is well-suited for shaded garden areas.
