Fadyenia laurifolia

Fadyenia laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery, dark green leaves that give it a lush, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red fruits containing seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
