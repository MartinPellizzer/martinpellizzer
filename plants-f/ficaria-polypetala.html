Ficaria polypetala

Ficaria polypetala, commonly known as the many-petalled buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers with numerous petals, typically arranged in a cup-like shape, and is known for its attractive, daisy-like appearance. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ficaria polypetala is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
