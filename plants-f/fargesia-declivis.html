Fargesia declivis

Fargesia declivis, commonly known as the cliff bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is well adapted to rocky and mountainous environments, often growing on slopes and cliffs.

Fargesia declivis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
