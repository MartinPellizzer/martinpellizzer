Feuilleea ferrugineohirta

Feuilleea ferrugineohirta, commonly known as the rusty-hairy leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, reddish-brown stems and serrated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Feuilleea ferrugineohirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
