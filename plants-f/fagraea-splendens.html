Fagraea splendens

Fagraea splendens, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in late winter or early spring. The plant has compound leaves with elliptic to ovate leaflets, and its bark is smooth and reddish-brown. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The golden flame tree is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
