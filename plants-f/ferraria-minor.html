Ferraria minor

Ferraria minor, commonly known as the dwarf iron plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
