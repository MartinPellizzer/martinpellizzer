Flaveria peruviana

Flaveria peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and appear in dense clusters, attracting various pollinators.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
