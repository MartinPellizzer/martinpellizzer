Facolos

Facolos, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its distinctive silvery foliage.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves covered in fine, white hairs that give it a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
