Friesia fruticosa

Friesia fruticosa, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces vibrant, tubular flowers that are usually orange or red, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Friesia fruticosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
