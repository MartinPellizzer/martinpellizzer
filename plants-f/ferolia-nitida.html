Ferolia nitida

Ferolia nitida, commonly known as the smooth lemon tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a citrus-like flavor. Ferolia nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
