Fragaria tormentilla

Fragaria tormentilla, commonly known as the tormentilla strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in cool, moist environments and has a compact growth habit.

Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, and it is valued for both its ornamental and culinary uses.
