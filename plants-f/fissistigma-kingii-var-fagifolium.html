Fissistigma kingii var. fagifolium

Fissistigma kingii var. fagifolium, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically in late spring to early summer. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a distinctive ovate shape with a serrated margin. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental tree is often cultivated for its showy blooms and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
