Forrestia rostrata var. leiocarpa

Forrestia rostrata var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-seeded forestia, is a variant of the forestia genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its propagation.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
