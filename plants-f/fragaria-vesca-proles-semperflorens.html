Fragaria vesca proles semperflorens

Fragaria vesca proles semperflorens, commonly known as the everblooming strawberry, is a hybrid cultivar of the woodland strawberry.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse flowering throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically smaller than those of common strawberries but have a similar flavor profile. It thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and mild fruit flavor.
