Fargesia stenoclada

Fargesia stenoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaved evergreen bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters, typically in the spring. Fargesia stenoclada is a hardy, evergreen bamboo that thrives in shaded, moist environments.
