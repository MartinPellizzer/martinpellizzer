Filago carpetana

Filago carpetana, commonly known as the Carpathian filago, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Filago carpetana is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in mountainous regions.
