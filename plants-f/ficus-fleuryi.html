Ficus fleuryi

Ficus fleuryi, commonly known as the Fleury's fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ficus fleuryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
