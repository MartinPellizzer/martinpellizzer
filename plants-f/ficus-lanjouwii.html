Ficus lanjouwii

Ficus lanjouwii, commonly known as the Lanjouwii fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Indonesia and is typically found in lowland tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and produces small, clustered fruits that are edible. It thrives in humid environments with high levels of indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
