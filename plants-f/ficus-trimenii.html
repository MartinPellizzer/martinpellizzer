Ficus trimenii

Ficus trimenii, commonly known as the Bornean fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, greenish flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus trimenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
