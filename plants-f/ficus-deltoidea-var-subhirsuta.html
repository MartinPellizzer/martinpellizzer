Ficus deltoidea var. subhirsuta

Ficus deltoidea var. subhirsuta, commonly known as the hirsute fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus deltoidea var. subhirsuta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
