Feuilleea cayennensis

Feuilleea cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne leaf plant, is a tropical species native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

Feuilleea cayennensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens and landscapes.
