Fargesia sagittatinea

Fargesia sagittatinea, commonly known as the arrowleaf bamboo, is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo native to China.

It features slender, upright stems that are covered in a smooth, grayish-brown bark. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a pointed tip, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance.

This species is valued for its ability to provide privacy and its adaptability to various soil types.
