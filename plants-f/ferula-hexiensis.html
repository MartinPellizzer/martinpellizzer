Ferula hexiensis

Ferula hexiensis, commonly known as the Chinese giant fennel, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, yellow flowers arranged in umbrella-like clusters, typical of the Apiaceae family. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a strong, aromatic scent.

Ferula hexiensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
