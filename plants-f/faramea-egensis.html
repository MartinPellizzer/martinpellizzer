Faramea egensis

Faramea egensis, commonly known as the African evergreen shrub, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.
