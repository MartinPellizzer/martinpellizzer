Feuilleea divaricata

Feuilleea divaricata, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a species native to tropical regions.

It features fronds that are bipinnate, with a distinctive silvery sheen on the underside. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Feuilleea divaricata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
