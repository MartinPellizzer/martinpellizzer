Ficus somae

Ficus somae, commonly known as the Somalian fig, is a species of flowering plant native to Somalia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit, a small fig, is edible and has a sweet, soft texture.

Ficus somae thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.
