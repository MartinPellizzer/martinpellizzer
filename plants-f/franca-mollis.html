Franca mollis

Franca mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to several meters in height. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a fragrant aroma.

Franca mollis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
