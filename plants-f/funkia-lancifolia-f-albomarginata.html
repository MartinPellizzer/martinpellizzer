Funkia lancifolia f. albomarginata

Funkia lancifolia f. albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin, giving it a unique visual appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow water, often in marshes or along the edges of ponds. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in water gardens and wetland restoration projects.
