Fargesia exposita

Fargesia exposita, commonly known as the silver fargesia, is a cold-hardy evergreen bamboo species native to China.

It features slender, upright canes with a silvery-blue sheen on its leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, panicle-like flowers in late spring, adding ornamental value. It is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.

Fargesia exposita is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
