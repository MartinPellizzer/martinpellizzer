Fragaria vesca var. ananassa

Fragaria vesca var. ananassa, commonly known as the strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces red, fleshy fruits that are rich in seeds and have a sweet, aromatic flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This variety is widely cultivated for both its ornamental value and as a fruit crop.
