Friedlandia hexandra

Friedlandia hexandra, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Friedlandia hexandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
