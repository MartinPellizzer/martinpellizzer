Flemingia lacei

Flemingia lacei, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. Flemingia lacei thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
