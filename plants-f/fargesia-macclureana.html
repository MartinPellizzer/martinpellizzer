Fargesia macclureana

Fargesia macclureana, commonly known as the MacClure's bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its clumping growth habit and slender, bamboo-like stalks. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It is often cultivated as a landscape plant due to its attractive foliage and cold hardiness.

Fargesia macclureana prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.
