Festuca rubra var. hondoensis

Festuca rubra var. hondoensis, commonly known as Hondo bentgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Japan.

It features fine, tufted blades with a bluish-green color and is often used in ornamental landscaping. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for ground cover and erosion control in specific climates.
