Ficus subsumatrana

Ficus subsumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered fruits known as figs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
