Fragariastrum ancistrifolium

Fragariastrum ancistrifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three broad, ovate leaflets. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are aggregate berries, similar to strawberries, and are edible.

This plant is typically found in temperate regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soil.
