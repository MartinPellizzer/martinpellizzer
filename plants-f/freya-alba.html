Freya alba

Freya alba, commonly known as the white freya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are often broad and pointed. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for gardens in cooler climates.

Freya alba is relatively easy to care for, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
