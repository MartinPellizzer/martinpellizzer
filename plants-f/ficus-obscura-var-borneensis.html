Ficus obscura var. borneensis

Ficus obscura var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean fig, is a species native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant has dark, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a slightly waxy texture. Its fruit, a small fig, is edible and serves as an important food source for various wildlife.

The Bornean fig is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
