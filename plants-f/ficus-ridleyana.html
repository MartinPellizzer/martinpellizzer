Ficus ridleyana

Ficus ridleyana, commonly known as the ridley fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Ficus ridleyana is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various indoor environments.

Its ability to thrive in low-light conditions makes it a popular choice for houseplants.
