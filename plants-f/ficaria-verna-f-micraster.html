Ficaria verna f. micraster

Ficaria verna f. micraster, commonly known as the dwarf snowflake, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming habit with hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine white coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
