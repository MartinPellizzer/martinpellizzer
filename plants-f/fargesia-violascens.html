Fargesia violascens

Fargesia violascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered bamboo, is a species of flowering bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in late spring. Fargesia violascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its graceful appearance.

It prefers cool, moist environments and is relatively low maintenance once established.
