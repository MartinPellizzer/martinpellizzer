Flemingia strobilifera var. fruticulosa

Flemingia strobilifera var. fruticulosa, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its flowers are a popular food source for butterflies, particularly the monarch butterfly.
