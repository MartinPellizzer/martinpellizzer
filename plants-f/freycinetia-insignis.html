Freycinetia insignis

Freycinetia insignis, commonly known as the red-veined arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, starchy tubers that are used in various cuisines.
