Feuilleea langsdorffii

Feuilleea langsdorffii, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bright yellow or golden in color. The plant thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil. Its unique leaf structure helps it absorb moisture efficiently, making it well-suited for tropical climates.

Feuilleea langsdorffii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
