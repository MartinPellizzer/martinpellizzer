Ficus hypsophila

Ficus hypsophila, commonly known as the white sapphire fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by small, round fruits.

Ficus hypsophila is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
