Ficus nebulosilvana

Ficus nebulosilvana, commonly known as the misty forest fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in humid environments and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
