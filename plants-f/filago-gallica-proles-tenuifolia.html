Filago gallica proles tenuifolia

Filago gallica proles tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-fruited filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

Filago gallica proles tenuifolia is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
