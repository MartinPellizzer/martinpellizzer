Fabiana patagonica var. foliosa

Fabiana patagonica var. foliosa, commonly known as the Patagonian leafy fabiana, is a perennial shrub native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh, arid conditions of its native habitat. Its leaves are typically broad and leathery, providing a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and resilience in challenging environments.
