Frenela endlicheri var. mucronata

Frenela endlicheri var. mucronata, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a dense, rounded growth habit and is often found in rocky, coastal areas. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.
