Fissistigma parvifolium

Fissistigma parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf fissistigma, is a member of the Malvaceae family, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.
