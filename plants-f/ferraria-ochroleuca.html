Ferraria ochroleuca

Ferraria ochroleuca, commonly known as the yellow bell orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a short, erect stem.

Ferraria ochroleuca is a perennial herb that is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
