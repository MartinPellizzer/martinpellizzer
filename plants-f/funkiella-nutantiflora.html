Funkiella nutantiflora

Funkiella nutantiflora, commonly known as the waveling orchid, is a unique flowering plant characterized by its pendulous, wavelike inflorescence.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features vibrant, fragrant blossoms that range in color from deep purples to soft yellows. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy texture that aids in water retention.

Due to its striking appearance and rare occurrence, Funkiella nutantiflora is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
