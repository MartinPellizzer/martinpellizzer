Ficus disticha subsp. calodictya

Ficus disticha subsp.

calodictya, commonly known as the Variegated Ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or cream variegation patterns.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish-yellow fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
