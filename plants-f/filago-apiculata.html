Filago apiculata

Filago apiculata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in summer, forming clusters at the tips of the branches.

Filago apiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
