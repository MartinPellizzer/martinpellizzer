Funkia subcordata var. taquetii

Funkia subcordata var. taquetii, commonly known as the Taquet's arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically light to dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to water gardens. It thrives in shallow water and is often used in pond landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable conditions.
