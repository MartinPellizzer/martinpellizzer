Finlaysonia lanuginosa

Finlaysonia lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky finlaysonia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires careful protection.
