Flourensia hirtissima

Flourensia hirtissima, commonly known as the rough flourensia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy, composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.
