Fahrenheitia

Fahrenheitia, commonly known as the heat-sensitive orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its delicate, violet-blue petals and a unique ability to respond to temperature changes by altering its blooming cycle. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Its botanical significance lies in its adaptive mechanisms to extreme climates.

Due to its rarity and sensitivity, Fahrenheitia is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
