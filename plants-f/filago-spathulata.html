Filago spathulata

Filago spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf filago, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and oblanceolate, with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in umbels, giving it a delicate appearance.

Filago spathulata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
