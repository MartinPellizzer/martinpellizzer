Faxonia

Faxonia, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Faxonia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
