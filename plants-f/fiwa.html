Fiwa

Fiwa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves and showy blooms. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, white, pink, and blue.

Fiwa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
