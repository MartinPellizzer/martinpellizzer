Fedia olitaria

Fedia olitaria, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant wood, which is highly valued for its scent and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits containing hard, smooth seeds.

The tree thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry forest or woodland habitats.
