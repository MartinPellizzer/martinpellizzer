Fuirena tenuis

Fuirena tenuis, commonly known as the slender lichen, is a species of lichen that belongs to the family Parmeliaceae.

It is characterized by its thin, foliose thallus that is typically pale green to yellowish in color. Due to its delicate structure, it is sensitive to environmental changes and is used as an indicator species for air quality.
