Ficus vasta var. glabrescens

Ficus vasta var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed ficus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and waxy to the touch. This variety thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its growth habit is bushy, with multiple stems branching out from the base.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and attractive appearance.
