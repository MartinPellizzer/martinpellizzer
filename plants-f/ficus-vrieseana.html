Ficus vrieseana

Ficus vrieseana, commonly known as the Vriesia fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Ficus vrieseana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
