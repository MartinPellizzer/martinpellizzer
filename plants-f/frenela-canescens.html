Frenela canescens

Frenela canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf frenel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Frenela canescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
