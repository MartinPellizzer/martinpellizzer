Fargesia caduca

Fargesia caduca, commonly known as the deciduous evergreen bamboo, is a unique species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms that are often covered with a white powdery coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in panicle clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Fargesia caduca is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
