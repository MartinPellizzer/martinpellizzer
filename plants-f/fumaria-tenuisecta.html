Fumaria tenuisecta, commonly known as the slender yellowcress, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Fumaria tenuisecta is a short-lived perennial that plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for early pollinators.
