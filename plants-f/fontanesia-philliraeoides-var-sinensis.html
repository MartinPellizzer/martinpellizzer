Fontanesia philliraeoides var. sinensis

Fontanesia philliraeoides var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking, fiery red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
