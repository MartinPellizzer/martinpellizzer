Forrestia hispida var. calva

Forrestia hispida var. calva, commonly known as the smooth forestiera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and oval in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and seasonal floral display.
