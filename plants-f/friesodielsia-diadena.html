Friesodielsia diadena

Friesodielsia diadena, commonly known as the red-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink in color, blooming in late summer. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and compact growth habit.
