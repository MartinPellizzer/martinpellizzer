Ficinia Fastigiata

Fastigiate fig, scientifically known as Ficinia fastigiata, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. This unique plant is prized for its narrow, upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in harsh, saline environments. Its dense foliage and compact form make it an excellent choice for coastal gardens and landscape design. The Fastigiate fig is also notable for its ornamental value, with glossy leaves that provide year-round visual interest. Due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal, it has become a popular plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
