Fadogia graminea

Fadogia graminea, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Fadogia graminea is valued for its nutritional content and potential medicinal properties.
