Filago germanica var. longibracteata

Filago germanica var. longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and grasslands. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

The variety is distinguished by its elongated bracts, which give it its specific name.
