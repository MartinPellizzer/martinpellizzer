Fritillaria przewalskii var. tessellata

Fritillaria przewalskii var. tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated fritillary, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that display a unique tessellated pattern. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes with cool, moist conditions.
