Fagonia chilensis var. palmeri

Fagonia chilensis var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's fagonia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of northern Chile.

It is characterized by its gray-green, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to withstand harsh desert conditions, including high temperatures and low rainfall. It has a woody stem with a rough, fissured bark.

This variety is often found in coastal and inland desert environments, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.
