Froelichia lanata

Froelichia lanata, commonly known as the woolly froelichia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
