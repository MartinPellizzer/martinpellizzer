Farsetia linifolia

Farsetia linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf farsetia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Farsetia linifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
