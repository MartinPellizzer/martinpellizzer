Funkia ovata f. marginata

Funkia ovata f. marginata, commonly known as the variegated arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves.

The leaves are typically variegated with white or yellow margins, creating a visually appealing contrast against the dark green central portion. This plant thrives in shallow water, often found in ponds and wetlands, where it spreads through rhizomes. It produces small, white flowers that emerge above the water surface, adding to its ornamental value.

The plant is known for its ability to purify water and provide habitat for aquatic life.
