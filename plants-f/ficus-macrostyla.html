Ficus macrostyla

Ficus macrostyla, commonly known as the large-leaf fig.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences called syconia. Its fruit, a greenish-yellow fig, is edible and often used in local cuisines.

Ficus macrostyla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
