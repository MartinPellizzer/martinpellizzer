Faujasia flexuosa

Faujasia flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Faujasia flexuosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
