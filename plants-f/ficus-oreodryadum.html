Ficus oreodryadum

Ficus oreodryadum, commonly known as the mountain fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are usually opposite or whorled. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Ficus oreodryadum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
