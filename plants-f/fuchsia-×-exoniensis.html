Fuchsia × exoniensis

Fuchsia × exoniensis, commonly known as the Exeter fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–2 meters, with dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Fuchsia × exoniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
