Ficus glabella var. nesophila

Ficus glabella var. nesophila, commonly known as the Neophila Ficus, is a rare and unique variant of the Ficus glabella species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a thick, woody stem that can develop aerial roots. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and collectors.
