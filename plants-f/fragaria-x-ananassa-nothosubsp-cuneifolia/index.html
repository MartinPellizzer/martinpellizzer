Fragaria × Ananassa Nothosubsp. Cuneifolia

Fragaria × ananassa nothosubsp. cuneifolia is a rare and intriguing hybrid plant that combines traits from the common strawberry and other wild strawberry species. This subspecies is known for its unique leaf shape, featuring narrow, pointed leaves that distinguish it from typical strawberry varieties. It thrives in cooler climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. While not widely cultivated, it has gained attention among horticulturists and botanists for its potential in breeding programs. Its existence highlights the complexity and beauty of plant hybridization in nature.
