Feuilleea rufescens

Feuilleea rufescens, commonly known as the crimson leaf fern, is a unique plant native to the humid tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety rufescens leaves that exhibit a deep red to brown hue, especially when exposed to direct sunlight. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with multiple branching stems that support its broad, lance-shaped foliage. Feuilleea rufescens thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soil and requires high humidity and indirect light to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
