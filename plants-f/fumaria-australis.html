Fumaria australis

Fumaria australis, commonly known as the Australian smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, ascending stems and trifoliate leaves, which are divided into three small leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Fumaria australis is notable for its unique appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
