Ficus sundaica var. impressicostata

Ficus sundaica var. impressicostata, commonly known as the Sunda ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often marked with distinctive wavy edges. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its ability to adapt to various indoor conditions makes it a popular choice for houseplants.
