Ficus subulata f. driveri

Ficus subulata f. driveri, commonly known as the Driver's ficus, is a rare and unique cultivar of the ficus genus.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among horticultural enthusiasts.
