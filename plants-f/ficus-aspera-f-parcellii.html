Ficus aspera f. parcellii

Ficus aspera f. parcellii, commonly known as the Parcell's fig.

This variant is a compact, shrub-like form of the common fig tree, characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and small, rounded leaves. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, edible figs that are typically consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
