Festuca gigantea f. triflora

Festuca gigantea f. triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered tall fescue, is a cultivar of the tall fescue grass species.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and dark green, coarse foliage. The plant is typically found in temperate climates and is often used in landscaping and turfgrass applications. It produces multiple flower heads per stem, which contribute to its ornamental value.

This variety is known for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil conditions.
