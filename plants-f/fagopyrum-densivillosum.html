Fagopyrum densivillosum

Fagopyrum densivillosum, commonly known as the dense-hairy buckwheat, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Fagopyrum densivillosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in rocky environments.
