Frumentum hordeum

Frumentum hordeum, commonly known as barley.

It is a cereal grain that belongs to the grass family Poaceae. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has a robust stem covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, with a bluish-green color, and the flowers are arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

Barley produces small, three-lobed seeds that are harvested when mature and dried.
