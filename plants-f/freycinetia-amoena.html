Freycinetia amoena

Freycinetia amoena, commonly known as the red-flowered pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves and striking red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are often used in traditional crafts and as a source of aromatic oils.

Freycinetia amoena thrives in humid, coastal environments and is valued for its ornamental and cultural significance.
