Ficus schweinfurthii

Ficus schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African rubber fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.

The plant is also valued for its ability to purify the air and its aesthetic appeal.
