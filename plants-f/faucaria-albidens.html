Faucaria albidens

Faucaria albidens, commonly known as the White Faucaria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
