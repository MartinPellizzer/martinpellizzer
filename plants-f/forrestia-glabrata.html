Forrestia glabrata

Forrestia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth forestiera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped and smooth in texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
