Fuchsia × reflexa

Fuchsia × reflexa, commonly known as the common fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep purples to bright pinks. The plant produces showy bracts that are often mistaken for petals, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Fuchsia × reflexa is widely cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in horticultural research due to its genetic diversity.
