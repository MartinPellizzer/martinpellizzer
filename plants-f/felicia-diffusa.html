Felicia diffusa

Felicia diffusa, commonly known as the common fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, featuring hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, blooming from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Felicia diffusa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
