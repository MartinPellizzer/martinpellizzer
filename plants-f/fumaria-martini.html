Fumaria martini

Fumaria martini, commonly known as the common fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

Fumaria martini is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.
