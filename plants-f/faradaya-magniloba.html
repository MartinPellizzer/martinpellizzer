Faradaya magniloba

Faradaya magniloba, commonly known as the giant faradaya, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it requires specific humidity and light conditions to thrive.
