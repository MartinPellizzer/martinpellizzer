Fagopyrum × heterocarpum

Fagopyrum × heterocarpum, commonly known as the hybrid buckwheat, is a perennial plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive triangular leaves with a hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from green to red, depending on the stage of development. Its seeds are encased in a hard, spiky shell, which aids in seed dispersal.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional herbal medicine.
