Funkia ovata f. latifolia

Funkia ovata f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant characterized by its wide, arrow-shaped leaves.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to aquatic conditions. Its leaves are usually a vibrant green, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to help stabilize wetland soils.
