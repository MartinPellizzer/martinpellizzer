Fontainea picrosperma

Fontainea picrosperma, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant, fiery appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fontainea picrosperma is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture-making.
