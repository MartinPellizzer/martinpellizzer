Fagelia extensa

Fagelia extensa, commonly known as the broadleaf fagelia, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by oval, woody fruits containing several seeds. Fagelia extensa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
