Festuca fagetina

Festuca fagetina, commonly known as red fescue, is a cool-season grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green blades and tufted growth habit. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and shaded areas. Festuca fagetina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and turfgrass mixtures.

It is also an important species in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
