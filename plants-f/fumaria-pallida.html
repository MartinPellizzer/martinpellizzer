Fumaria pallida

Fumaria pallida, commonly known as the pale fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

Fumaria pallida is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
