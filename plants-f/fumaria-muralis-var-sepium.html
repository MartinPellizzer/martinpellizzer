Fumaria muralis var. sepium

Fumaria muralis var. sepium, commonly known as wall rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in rocky or wall crevices, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in urban and rural wall habitats across Europe.
