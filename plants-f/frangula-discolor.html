Frangula discolor

Frangula discolor, commonly known as the yellow toadflax, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers and smooth, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush communities.

Frangula discolor has been used in traditional medicine for its potential laxative properties.
