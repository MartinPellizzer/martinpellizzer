Fargesia nivalis

Fargesia nivalis, commonly known as the silver fir or snow fir, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the禾本科 (Poaceae) family and is characterized by its graceful, arching stems and dense, feather-like foliage. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a silvery or grayish underside, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Fargesia nivalis is well-suited for shady, moist environments and is often used in landscape design for its aesthetic and hardy nature.
