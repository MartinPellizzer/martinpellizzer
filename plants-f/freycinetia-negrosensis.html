Freycinetia negrosensis

Freycinetia negrosensis, commonly known as the Negros palm, is a species of palm native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by dark blue to black berries. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
