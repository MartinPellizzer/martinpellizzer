Ficus milletii

Ficus milletii, commonly known as the African rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, inedible fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.

Ficus milletii prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
