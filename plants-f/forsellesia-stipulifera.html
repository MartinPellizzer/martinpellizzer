Forsellesia stipulifera

Forsellesia stipulifera, commonly known as the red-flowered pandanus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional cooking and crafts. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found growing near water sources.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular species in horticulture.
