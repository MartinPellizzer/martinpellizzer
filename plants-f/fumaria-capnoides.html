Fumaria capnoides

Fumaria capnoides, commonly known as the yellow loosestrife, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Fumaria capnoides is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
