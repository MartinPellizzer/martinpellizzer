Forrestia Hispida Var. Mollis

Forrestia hispida var. mollis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern China. It is known for its soft, velvety leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. This variety is a lesser-known cousin of the more common Forrestia hispida, often overlooked in horticultural circles. Its unique appearance and subtle beauty make it a sought-after plant among collectors and botanical enthusiasts. Due to its limited natural habitat and slow growth, it is considered a challenging but rewarding specimen to cultivate.
