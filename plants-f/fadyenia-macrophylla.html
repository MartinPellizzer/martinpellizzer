Fadyenia macrophylla

Fadyenia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf fadyenia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Fadyenia macrophylla is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
