Fuziifilix pilosella

Fuziifilix pilosella, commonly known as the hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with five leaflets, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Fuziifilix pilosella is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.
