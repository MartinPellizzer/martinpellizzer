Felicia eylesii

Felicia eylesii, commonly known as the Eyles' fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Felicia eylesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
