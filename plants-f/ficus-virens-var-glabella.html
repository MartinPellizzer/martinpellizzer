Ficus virens var. glabella

Ficus virens var. glabella, commonly known as the glossy fig or velvet fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, waxy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden within the leaves.

Ficus virens var. glabella is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
