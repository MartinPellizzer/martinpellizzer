Filago psilantha

Filago psilantha, commonly known as the slender filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.

Filago psilantha is often found growing in sandy soils and along rocky outcrops.
