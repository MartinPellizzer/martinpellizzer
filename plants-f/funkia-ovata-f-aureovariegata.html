Funkia ovata f. aureovariegata

Funkia ovata f. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant characterized by its bright yellow and green variegated foliage.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, with a waxy texture that enhances its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shallow water and is often cultivated in ponds or wet gardens for its vibrant color and ease of care.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological water gardens.
