Feidanthus striatellus

Feidanthus striatellus, commonly known as the streaked pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dark green, glossy leaves with distinct white or silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Feidanthus striatellus is relatively low maintenance and suitable for garden landscapes.
