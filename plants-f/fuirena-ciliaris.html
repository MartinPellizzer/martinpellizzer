Fuirena ciliaris

Fuirena ciliaris, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that are often overlooked due to their subtle nature.

Fuirena ciliaris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
