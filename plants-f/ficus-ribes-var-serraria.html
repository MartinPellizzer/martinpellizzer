Ficus ribes var. serraria

Ficus ribes var. serraria, commonly known as the serrated fig.

This evergreen shrub or small tree is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. It features glossy, dark green leaves that are serrated along the edges, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
