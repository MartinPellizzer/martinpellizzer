Fraxinus minutepunctata

Fraxinus minutepunctata, commonly known as the dwarf ash.

It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to the eastern United States. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and green, appearing in catkin-like clusters, and it produces small, winged seeds.

The bark is typically smooth and gray, with some shallow fissures.
