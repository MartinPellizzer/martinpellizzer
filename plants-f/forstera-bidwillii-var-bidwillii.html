Forstera bidwillii var. bidwillii

Forstera bidwillii var. bidwillii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock heath, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, preferring full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
