Filago × media

Filago × media, commonly known as the hybrid meadow fleabane, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows and roadsides. The plant produces showy yellow to orange composite flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Filago × media is characterized by its ability to hybridize with other Filago species, leading to genetic diversity in its populations.
