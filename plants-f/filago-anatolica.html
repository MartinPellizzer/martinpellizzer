Filago anatolica

Filago anatolica, commonly known as Anatolian filago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Turkey.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Filago anatolica is not widely cultivated but is of interest to botanists due to its ecological adaptations and potential medicinal properties.
