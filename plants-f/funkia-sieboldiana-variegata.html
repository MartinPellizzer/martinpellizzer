Funkia sieboldiana variegata

Funkia sieboldiana variegata, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, variegated leaves with white and green patterns, which add visual interest to water gardens. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often growing along the edges of ponds and streams. Its flowers are small and white, appearing in clusters, and it blooms during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to help purify water by absorbing nutrients.
