Ficaria fascicularis

Ficaria fascicularis, commonly known as the snowflake plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ficaria fascicularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
