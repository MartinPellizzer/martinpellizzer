Fagraea ternatana

Fagraea ternatana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea ternatana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
