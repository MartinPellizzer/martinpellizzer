Ficaria roberti

Ficaria roberti, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial with delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has basal leaves that are spoon-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Ficaria roberti is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
