Franseria intricata

Franseria intricata, commonly known as the intricate franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually yellow or white.

Franseria intricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
