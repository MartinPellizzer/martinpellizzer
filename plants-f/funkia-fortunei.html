Funkia fortunei

Funkia fortunei, commonly known as the Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically floating or partially submerged, with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to water gardens. It thrives in shallow water and is often used in pond landscaping.

Its rhizomes are also used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
