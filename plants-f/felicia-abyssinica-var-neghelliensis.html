Felicia abyssinica var. neghelliensis

Felicia abyssinica var. neghelliensis, commonly known as the Abyssinian fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features daisy-like flowers with bright yellow disc florets surrounded by white to pale pink ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
