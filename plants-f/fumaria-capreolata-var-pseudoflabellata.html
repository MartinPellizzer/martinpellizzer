Fumaria capreolata var. pseudoflabellata

Fumaria capreolata var. pseudoflabellata, commonly known as the false flabella.

This variant is a member of the Fumariaceae family and is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, pale yellow flowers. The leaves are usually alternate and have a distinctive, pinnately divided structure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
