Fragaria suchiana

Fragaria suchiana, commonly known as the Armenian strawberry, is a species of wild strawberry native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant with trifoliate leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, red, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to common strawberries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
