Fumaria sepium subsp. rugosa

Fumaria sepium subsp.

rugosa, commonly known as the rough yellowcress, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many flowering plants with small, cross-shaped flowers.
