Ficus erecta var. beecheyana

Ficus erecta var. beecheyana, commonly known as the Beechey's fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are typically arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, spherical fig fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
