Fumaria sativa

Fumaria sativa, commonly known as common fumitory, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, elongated seeds. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
