Ficus balica var. colfsii

Ficus balica var. colfsii, commonly known as the Colfsii Fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
