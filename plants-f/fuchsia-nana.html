Fuchsia nana

Fuchsia nana, commonly known as the dwarf fuchsia, is a compact shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep pink to purple, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Fuchsia nana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and containers due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
