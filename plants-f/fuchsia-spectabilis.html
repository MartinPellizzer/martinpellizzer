Fuchsia spectabilis

Fuchsia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple and pink. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Fuchsia spectabilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
