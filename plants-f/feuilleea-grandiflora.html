Feuilleea grandiflora

Feuilleea grandiflora, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, broad leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Feuilleea grandiflora thrives in high humidity and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vivid floral display.
