Ficus rubra

Ficus rubra, commonly known as the red fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India and China. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves that range in color from dark green to reddish hues. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.
