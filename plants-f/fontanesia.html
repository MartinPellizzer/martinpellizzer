Fontanesia

Fontanesia, commonly known as the "cotton tree," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fluffy, cotton-like seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation. Its leaves are typically large and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.
