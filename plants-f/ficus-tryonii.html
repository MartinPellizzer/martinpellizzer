Ficus tryonii

Ficus tryonii, commonly known as the Tryon's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It prefers warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Ficus tryonii is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
