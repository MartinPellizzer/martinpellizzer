Ferraria ixioides

Ferraria ixioides, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, producing clusters of pink to purple blooms.

Ferraria ixioides is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
