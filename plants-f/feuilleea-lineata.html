Feuilleea lineata

Feuilleea lineata, commonly known as the lined leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its striking, elongated leaves with distinct linear patterns.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's foliage exhibits a gradient of green to deep emerald, with subtle variegation along the leaf margins. Feuilleea lineata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.

Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for indoor or outdoor garden spaces.
