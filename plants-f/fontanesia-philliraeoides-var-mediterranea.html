Fontanesia philliraeoides var. mediterranea

Fontanesia philliraeoides var. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean fontanesia, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
