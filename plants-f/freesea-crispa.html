Freesea crispa

Freesea crispa, commonly known as the crinkled sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive, crinkled leaves and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments. Freesea crispa is a hardy perennial that is well adapted to salt spray and strong coastal winds.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
