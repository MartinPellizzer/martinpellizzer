Forexeta

Forexeta, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of purple and pink, which bloom in summer. The plant has a robust root system and upright, branching stems that support its vibrant inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Forexeta is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
