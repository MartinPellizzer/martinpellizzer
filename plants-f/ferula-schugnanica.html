Ferula schugnanica

Ferula schugnanica, commonly known as the Schugnan ferula, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots and parsley, and is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and feathery leaves. Ferula schugnanica thrives in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
