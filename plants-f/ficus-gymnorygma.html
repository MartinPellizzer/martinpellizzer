Ficus gymnorygma

Ficus gymnorygma, commonly known as the African fig.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus gymnorygma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
