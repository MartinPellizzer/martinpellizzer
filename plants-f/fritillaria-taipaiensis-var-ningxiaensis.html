Fritillaria taipaiensis var. ningxiaensis

Fritillaria taipaiensis var. ningxiaensis, commonly known as the Ningxia fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the arid regions of northern China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers, which typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, linear leaves and has a deep root system adapted to survive in dry conditions.

It is often found growing in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
