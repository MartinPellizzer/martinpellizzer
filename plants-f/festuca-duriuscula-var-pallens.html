Festuca duriuscula var. pallens

Festuca duriuscula var. pallens, commonly known as the pale festuca, is a low-growing grass species native to arctic and alpine regions.

It typically forms dense, mat-like growths that help stabilize soil in harsh environments. The plant has narrow, bluish-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to cold climates and can survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of tundra and mountain ecosystems.
