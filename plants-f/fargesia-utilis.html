Fargesia utilis

Fargesia utilis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western China and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is deciduous, shedding its leaves in the fall, and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Fargesia utilis is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic value and ease of cultivation.
