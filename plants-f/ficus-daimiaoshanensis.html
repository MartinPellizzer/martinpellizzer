Ficus daimiaoshanensis

Ficus daimiaoshanensis, commonly known as the Daimiao Shan fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

The fruit, which is a small fig, is edible and serves as a food source for various wildlife species.
