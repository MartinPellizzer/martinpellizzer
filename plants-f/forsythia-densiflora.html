Forsythia densiflora

Forsythia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color and turn yellow in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and hardy nature make it adaptable to various garden conditions.
