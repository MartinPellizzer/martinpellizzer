Fargesia ferax

Fargesia ferax, commonly known as the Yunnan bamboo, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its clumping growth habit. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are typically green or slightly bluish in color. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, providing a fine texture to the plant's appearance.

Fargesia ferax is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
