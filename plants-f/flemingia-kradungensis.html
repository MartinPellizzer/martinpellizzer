Flemingia kradungensis

Flemingia kradungensis, commonly known as the Thai flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Flemingia kradungensis is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of timber in its native regions.
