Faramea sessiliflora var. pedunculata

Faramea sessiliflora var. pedunculata, commonly known as the clustered faramea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features compound leaves with elliptical to ovate leaflets arranged in an alternate pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Rubiaceae family.
