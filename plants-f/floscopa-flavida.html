Floscopa flavida

Floscopa flavida, commonly known as the yellow-flowered floscopa, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically tubular with a prominent central disk. The plant is native to regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in dry, sandy soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Floscopa flavida is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
