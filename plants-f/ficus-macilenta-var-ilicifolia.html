Ficus macilenta var. ilicifolia

Ficus macilenta var. ilicifolia, commonly known as the slender fig or narrow-leaved fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered fig fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus macilenta var. ilicifolia is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
