Fagraea stenophylla

Fagraea stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fagraea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Fagraea stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
