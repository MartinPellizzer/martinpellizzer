Fuchsia × penduliflora

Fuchsia × penduliflora, commonly known as the weeping fuchsia, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from different fuchsia species.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red. The plant is native to South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soil in shaded to partially shaded environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Fuchsia × penduliflora is also valued for its adaptability to container gardening, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and vertical gardens.
