Filipendula filipendula

Filipendula filipendula, commonly known as meadow sweet, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, pinkish-white flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and a tall, slender stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Filipendula filipendula is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
