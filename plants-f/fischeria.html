Fischeria

Fischeria, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, round fruits that resemble lanterns, which is where its common name originates. Fischeria thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
