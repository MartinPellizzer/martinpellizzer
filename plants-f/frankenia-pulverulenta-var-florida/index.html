Frankenia Pulverulenta Var. Florida

Frankenia pulverulenta var. florida is a rare and resilient plant native to the southeastern United States. This coastal shrub thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune ecosystems. Known for its small, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow flowers, it plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal habitats. Despite its hardiness, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
