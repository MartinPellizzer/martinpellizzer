Faucaria latipetala

Faucaria latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of its arid habitat. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
