Fleroya stipulosa

Fleroya stipulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink, often blooming in clusters at the ends of its stems.
