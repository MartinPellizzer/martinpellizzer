Ficus stenoclada

Ficus stenoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered fruits called syconia, which are enclosed within a greenish-white receptacle. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
