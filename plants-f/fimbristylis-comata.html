Fimbristylis comata

Fimbristylis comata, commonly known as the hairy flat sedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and Australia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, typically blooming during the rainy season.
