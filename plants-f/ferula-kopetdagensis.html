Ferula kopetdagensis

Ferula kopetdagensis, commonly known as the Kopetdag fennel, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand harsh, dry conditions. Its tall, slender stems can reach up to 2 meters in height, and it produces yellowish-green flowers in umbrella-like clusters.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits.
