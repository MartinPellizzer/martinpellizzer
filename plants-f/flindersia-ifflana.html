Flindersia ifflana

Flindersia ifflana, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets, which are arranged alternately along the stem. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its natural dispersal.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and public spaces for its visual impact.
