Fumaria densiflora var. dubia

Fumaria densiflora var. dubia, commonly known as the dense-flowered fumitory, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria densiflora var. dubia is known for its ability to thrive in temperate climates and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
