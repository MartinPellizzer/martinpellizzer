Forsteronia multinervia var. glabriuscula

Forsteronia multinervia var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smoothleaf forsteria, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like plant characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and compact growth habit. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a glossy surface and a prominent midrib. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often blooming in late summer.

This plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
