Fugosia latifolia

Fugosia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf fugosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Fugosia latifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
