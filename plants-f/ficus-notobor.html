Ficus notobor

Ficus notobor, commonly known as the southern ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to parts of southern Africa, including South Africa, Namibia, and Botswana. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are edible and serve as a food source for various animals.

Ficus notobor thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
