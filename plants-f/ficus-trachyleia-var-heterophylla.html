Ficus trachyleia var. heterophylla

Ficus trachyleia var. heterophylla, commonly known as the Variegated Ficus, is a unique variant of the Ficus trachyleia species.

It is characterized by its striking variegation, featuring patches of white, yellow, and green on its leaves. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with broad, glossy foliage that adds visual interest to indoor spaces. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor environments.
