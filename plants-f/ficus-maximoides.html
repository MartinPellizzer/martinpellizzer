Ficus maximoides

Ficus maximoides, commonly known as the giant ficus or Philippine ficus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, greenish fruits that turn red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus maximoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to indoor conditions.
