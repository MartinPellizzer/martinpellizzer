Fragaria duplex

Fragaria duplex, commonly known as the double strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family.

It is native to the eastern United States and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to regular strawberries but have a more delicate flavor. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
