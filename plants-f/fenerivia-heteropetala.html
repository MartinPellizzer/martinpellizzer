Fenerivia heteropetala

Fenerivia heteropetala, commonly known as the variable-flowered cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like appearance with a stout trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces both male and female cones, which are essential for its reproductive process. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts, and the plant is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Fenerivia heteropetala is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
