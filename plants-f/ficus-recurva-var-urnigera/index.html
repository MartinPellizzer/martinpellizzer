Ficus Recurva Var. Urnigera

Ficus recurva var. urnigera is a unique and striking plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its distinctive, waxy leaves that often display a rich, deep green or even reddish hue, this variety stands out among other ficus species. It is prized for its adaptability to indoor environments and its ability to thrive with minimal care. The plant's elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts. Its versatility and visual appeal have contributed to its growing popularity in modern interior design.
