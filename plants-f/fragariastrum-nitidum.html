Fragariastrum nitidum

Fragariastrum nitidum, commonly known as the smooth strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, aggregate fruits that resemble strawberries. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas. Its flowers are white with five petals and are followed by the characteristic red, fleshy fruits.

The fruit is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor, making it a valuable plant for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
