Fimbristylis tamaensis

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
