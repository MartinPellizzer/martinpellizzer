Funkia latifolia lusus aureomaculata

Funkia latifolia lusus aureomaculata, commonly known as the golden-spotted arrowhead, is a hybrid aquatic plant with striking foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or gold spots, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shallow water and wetlands, preferring full to partial sunlight. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies.

It is often used in water gardens and pond landscapes for its ornamental value and ability to help control algae growth.
