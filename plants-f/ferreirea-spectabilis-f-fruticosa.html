Ferreirea spectabilis f. fruticosa

Ferreirea spectabilis f. fruticosa, commonly known as the Florida star jasmine, is a shrub-like plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value comes from its pleasant scent and delicate appearance.
