Flagellaria plicata

Flagellaria plicata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally twisted stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are elongated and opposite, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Flagellaria plicata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
