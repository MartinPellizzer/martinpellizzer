Frailea gracillima subsp. gracillima

Frailea gracillima subsp.

gracillima, commonly known as the slender rock cactus, is a species native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscape designs.
