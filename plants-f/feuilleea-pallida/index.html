Feuilleea Pallida

White leaf plant, scientifically known as Feuilleea pallida, is a rare and striking species native to the misty highlands of Southeast Asia. Its most distinguishing feature is its pale, almost translucent foliage that shimmers under indirect light. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires careful attention to maintain its unique appearance. Despite its delicate look, Feuilleea pallida is remarkably resilient when provided with the right conditions. It has become a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
