Freycinetia urdanetensis

Freycinetia urdanetensis, commonly known as the Urdanet palm, is a species native to Cuba.

It is a tall, slender palm characterized by its pinnate leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into drupes, which are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
