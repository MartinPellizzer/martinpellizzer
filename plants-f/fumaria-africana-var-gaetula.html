Fumaria africana var. gaetula

Fumaria africana var. gaetula, commonly known as the African smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, cascading clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract beneficial insects make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
