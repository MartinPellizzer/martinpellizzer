Ficus urnigera

Ficus urnigera, commonly known as the urn fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. It produces small, clustered fig fruits that are edible and often consumed by various wildlife species.

Ficus urnigera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
