Festuca ovina var. alpina

Festuca ovina var. alpina, commonly known as alpine sheep fescue, is a perennial grass species native to mountainous regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit, fine leaves, and compact inflorescence. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil in alpine ecosystems.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for erosion control in rocky or degraded landscapes.
