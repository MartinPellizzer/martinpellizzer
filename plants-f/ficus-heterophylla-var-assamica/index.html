Ficus Heterophylla Var. Assamica

Ficus heterophylla var. assamica is a unique species of fig tree known for its striking variation in leaf shape. This plant is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in warm and moist environments. Its foliage displays a fascinating transition from broad, glossy leaves to narrow, pointed ones as it matures. Often grown as a houseplant, it is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
