Fumaria agraria var. erostrata

Fumaria agraria var. erostrata, commonly known as the scarlet fumitory, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, creeping growth habit. The plant produces small, bright red flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branched.

This species is often found in dry, sunny habitats like rocky soils and grasslands.
