Filicium somalense

Filicium somalense, commonly known as the Somali fern, is a species of fern native to Somalia.

It belongs to the order Filicales and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near water sources in its natural habitat. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Filicium somalense plays a role in maintaining soil stability and biodiversity in its native ecosystem.
