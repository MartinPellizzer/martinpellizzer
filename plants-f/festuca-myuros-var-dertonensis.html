Festuca myuros var. dertonensis

Festuca myuros var. dertonensis, commonly known as the Dertonensian meadow fescue, is a grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to blue-green flowers.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soils.
