Fallopia cynanchoides var. cynanchoides

Fallopia cynanchoides var. cynanchoides, commonly known as giant knotweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, robust stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough, waxy texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its rhizomatous growth system allows it to spread aggressively, making it a problematic invasive species in many regions.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along riverbanks and in wetlands.
