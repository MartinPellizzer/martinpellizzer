Franseria lancifolia

Franseria lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved franseria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens.
