Ficus sorsogonensis

Ficus sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Province fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically the province of Sorsogon. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered fig fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
