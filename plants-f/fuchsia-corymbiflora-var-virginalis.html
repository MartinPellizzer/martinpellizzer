Fuchsia corymbiflora var. virginalis

Fuchsia corymbiflora var. virginalis, commonly known as the Virgin's fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming throughout the summer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

This variety is noted for its ability to produce abundant blooms even in shaded conditions.
