Ficus erecta var. yamadorii

Ficus erecta var. yamadorii, commonly known as the Japanese rubber plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.
