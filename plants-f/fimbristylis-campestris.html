Fimbristylis campestris

Fimbristylis campestris, commonly known as the field sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, meadows, and along stream banks. Fimbristylis campestris plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
