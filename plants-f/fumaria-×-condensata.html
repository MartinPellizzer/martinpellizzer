Fumaria × condensata

Fumaria × condensata, commonly known as the hybrid yellow cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Fumaria officinalis and Fumaria parviflora, characterized by its slender, erect stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along riverbanks. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in racemes.

This species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
