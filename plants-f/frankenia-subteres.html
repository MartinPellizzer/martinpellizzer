Frankenia subteres

Frankenia subteres, commonly known as the narrow-leaved frankenia, is a flowering plant native to arid and coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, narrow leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its adaptations include water-storing leaves and a deep root system to survive in drought conditions.

Frankenia subteres is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
