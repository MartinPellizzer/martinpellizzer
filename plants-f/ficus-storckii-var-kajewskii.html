Ficus storckii var. kajewskii

Ficus storckii var. kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewskii fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its adaptability makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments.
