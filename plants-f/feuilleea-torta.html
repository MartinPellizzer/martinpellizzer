Feuilleea torta

Feuilleea torta, commonly known as the curled leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, wavy foliage.

Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's leaves are notable for their curling patterns, which give it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow during the spring and summer months.

Feuilleea torta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
