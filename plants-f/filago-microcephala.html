Filago microcephala

Filago microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is native to regions of southern Africa. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Filago microcephala is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.
