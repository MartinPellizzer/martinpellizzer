Funkia sieboldiana

Funkia sieboldiana, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features striking arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers in late summer, which bloom in clusters on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or near water features.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and pond margins.
