Ficaria ficarioides

Ficaria ficarioides, commonly known as the fairy slipper, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
