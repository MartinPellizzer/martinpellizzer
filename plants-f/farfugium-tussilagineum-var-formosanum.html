Farfugium tussilagineum var. formosanum

Farfugium tussilagineum var. formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan coughwort, is a perennial herb native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, broad, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine white pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in folk remedies for respiratory ailments.
