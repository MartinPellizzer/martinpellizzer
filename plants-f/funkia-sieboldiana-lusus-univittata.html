Funkia sieboldiana lusus univittata

Funkia sieboldiana lusus univittata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf daylily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to garden settings.
