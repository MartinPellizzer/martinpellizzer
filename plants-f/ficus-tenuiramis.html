Ficus tenuiramis

Ficus tenuiramis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green when mature. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Ficus tenuiramis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
