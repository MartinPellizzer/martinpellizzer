Forsythia × densiflora

Forsythia × densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is known for its early spring blooming period. The plant produces bright yellow, funnel-shaped flowers that appear before the leaves, creating a striking visual display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Forsythia × densiflora is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
