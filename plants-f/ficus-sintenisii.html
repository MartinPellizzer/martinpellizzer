Ficus sintenisii

Ficus sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often shaped like a shield. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are typically green when mature. It is a popular houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.

Ficus sintenisii thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
