Fernelia axillaris

Fernelia axillaris, commonly known as the rock fern, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink, which bloom in early spring. Fernelia axillaris thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
