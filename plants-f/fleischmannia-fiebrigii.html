Fleischmannia fiebrigii

Fleischmannia fiebrigii, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking yellow flowers with red centers. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
