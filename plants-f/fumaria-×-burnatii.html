Fumaria × burnatii

Fumaria × burnatii, commonly known as the hybrid common fumitory, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on long pedicels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Fumaria × burnatii is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate and attractive floral display.
