Fimbristylis aphylla var. gracilis

Fimbristylis aphylla var. gracilis, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often forming dense mats near water bodies. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are usually small and clustered.
