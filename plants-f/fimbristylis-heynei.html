Fimbristylis heynei

Fimbristylis heynei, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and rhizomatous growth habit.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist to submerged soil conditions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often inconspicuous. Its flowering spikes are usually erect and can be found at the base of the plant.

Fimbristylis heynei is an important component of wetland ecosystems, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
