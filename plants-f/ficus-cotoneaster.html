Ficus cotoneaster

Ficus cotoneaster, commonly known as the Chinese fig, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, round, greenish fruits that mature to a red or purple color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Ficus cotoneaster is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
