Fuchsia × brewsteri

Fuchsia × brewsteri, commonly known as the New Zealand fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and features dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Fuchsia × brewsteri is valued for its long blooming season and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
