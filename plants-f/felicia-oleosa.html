Felicia oleosa

Felicia oleosa, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. It typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and long blooming period.
