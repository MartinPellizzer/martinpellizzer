Ficus polita

Ficus polita, commonly known as the velvet fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that have a velvety texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically hidden within the leaves.

Ficus polita is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
