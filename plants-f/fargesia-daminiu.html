Fargesia daminiu

Fargesia daminiu, commonly known as the Yunnan arrow bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes. Fargesia daminiu is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a unique addition to horticultural collections.
