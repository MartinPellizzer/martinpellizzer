Ficus diospyrifolia

Ficus diospyrifolia, commonly known as the red-fig or red-leaf fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its distinctive reddish leaves, which are often heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by edible fig fruits.

Ficus diospyrifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
