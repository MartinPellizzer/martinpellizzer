Forsythia viridissima var. bronxensis

Forsythia viridissima var. bronxensis, commonly known as the Bronx forsythia, is a variant of the forsythia plant native to the Bronx area of New York.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
