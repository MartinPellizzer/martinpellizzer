Fumaria muralis var. gracilis

Fumaria muralis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender wall rue, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the poppy family (Papaveraceae).

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils, often found along walls or in crevices. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is a short-lived perennial that thrives in temperate climates and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
