Ficus phaeosyce

Ficus phaeosyce, commonly known as the dark fig or black fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its dark, almost black, foliage and smooth, glossy leaves. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature.

Ficus phaeosyce is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
