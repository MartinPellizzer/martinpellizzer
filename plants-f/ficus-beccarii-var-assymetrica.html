Ficus beccarii var. assymetrica

Ficus beccarii var. assymetrica, commonly known as the asymmetric ficus, is a unique variant of the Ficus beccarii species.

It is characterized by its irregular, asymmetrical leaf shape, which sets it apart from other ficus varieties. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.

The plant's foliage is dense and glossy, making it a popular choice for interior decorating.
