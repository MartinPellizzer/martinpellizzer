Frailea cataphracta subsp. duchii

Frailea cataphracta subsp.

duchii, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a compact, cylindrical stem with a bluish-green coloration, giving it its common name.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
