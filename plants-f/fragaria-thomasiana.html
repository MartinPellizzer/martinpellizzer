Fragaria thomasiana

Fragaria thomasiana, commonly known as Thomas's strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to South America, specifically found in countries like Colombia and Ecuador. The plant produces small, edible berries that are similar in appearance to common strawberries. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and less commonly cultivated species.
