Fedia auricula

Fedia auricula, commonly known as the auriculate fedia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gesneriaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive auriculate (ear-shaped) leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
