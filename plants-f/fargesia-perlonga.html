Fargesia perlonga

Fargesia perlonga, commonly known as the longleaf arrow bamboo, is a clump-forming evergreen bamboo native to China.

It features slender, arching culms that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a compact, upright fashion. It is well-suited for shaded or partially shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Fargesia perlonga is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in cool, humid climates.
