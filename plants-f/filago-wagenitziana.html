Filago wagenitziana

Filago wagenitziana, commonly known as the Wagenitz's filago, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. Filago wagenitziana is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
