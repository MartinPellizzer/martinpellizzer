Firmiana philippinensis

Firmiana philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flower clusters that appear in late summer. The leaves are compound, with long, slender leaflets that give the tree a feathery appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and along roadsides.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to provide shade.
