Filix bipinnata

Filix bipinnata, commonly known as the giant fern or giant chain fern, is a large tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, bipinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are divided into numerous small, serrated segments, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Filix bipinnata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
