Fimbripetalum, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, tassel-like inflorescences that give it its common name. The plants typically have opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Fimbripetalum species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.
