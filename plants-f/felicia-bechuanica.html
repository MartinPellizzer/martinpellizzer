Felicia bechuanica

Felicia bechuanica, commonly known as the Bechuanian daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in rocky areas and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates.

Felicia bechuanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in rock gardens and dry landscaping.
