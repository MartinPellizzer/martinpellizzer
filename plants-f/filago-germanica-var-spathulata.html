Filago germanica var. spathulata

Filago germanica var. spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal rosettes of broad, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a single, elongated inflorescence. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
