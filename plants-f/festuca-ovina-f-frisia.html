Festuca ovina f. frisia

Festuca ovina f. frisia, commonly known as frisian sheep's fescue, is a cool-season grass native to the northern regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and dense growth habit, making it well-suited for turf and erosion control. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its rhizomatous and stoloniferous growth patterns allow it to form a dense sod, enhancing its ornamental and functional value.

Festuca ovina f. frisia is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
