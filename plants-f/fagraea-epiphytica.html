Fagraea epiphytica

Fagraea epiphytica, commonly known as the silver tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, silver-gray bark and large, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries. It is often found growing as an epiphyte on other trees in rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in its native habitats.
