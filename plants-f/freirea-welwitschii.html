Freirea welwitschii

Freirea welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschia, is a unique plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia and Angola.

It is characterized by its two large, leathery leaves that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant has a thick, woody trunk and is adapted to survive in arid conditions with minimal rainfall. Freirea welwitschii is considered a living fossil, with a lineage dating back over 100 million years.
