Feuilleea barbata

Feuilleea barbata, commonly known as the featherleaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that are finely divided and give the plant a lacy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Feuilleea barbata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage, which adds texture and visual interest to any landscape.
