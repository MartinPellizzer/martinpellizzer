Ficus obscura var. angustata

Ficus obscura var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elongated leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
