Feuilleea plumifera

Feuilleea plumifera, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that give it its common name, and its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservatories.

Feuilleea plumifera is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
