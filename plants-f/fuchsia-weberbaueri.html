Fuchsia weberbaueri

Fuchsia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Ecuadorian fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Fuchsia weberbaueri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
