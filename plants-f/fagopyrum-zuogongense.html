Fagopyrum zuogongense

Fagopyrum zuogongense, commonly known as the Zhaogong buckwheat, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive triangular leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.
