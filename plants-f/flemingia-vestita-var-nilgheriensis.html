Flemingia vestita var. nilgheriensis

Flemingia vestita var. nilgheriensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri Flemingia, is a shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments within the montane forests. It has a woody stem with smooth, pale bark and produces small, pod-like fruits.

This species is of interest in ecological studies due to its role in local biodiversity and potential medicinal properties.
