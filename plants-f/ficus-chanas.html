Ficus chanas

Ficus chanas, commonly known as the Chinese banyan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and can grow to impressive heights with a wide, spreading canopy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by round, yellowish fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.

Ficus chanas is also noted for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in both indoor and outdoor environments.
