Fuchsia colombiana

Fuchsia colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian fuchsia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
