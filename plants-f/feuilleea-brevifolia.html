Feuilleea brevifolia

Feuilleea brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, broad leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters along the stems. Feuilleea brevifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
