Furcraea samalana

Furcraea samalana, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking succulent plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white flowers, followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a source of fiber.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
