Fargesia longiuscula

Fargesia longiuscula, commonly known as the long-leaf fargesia, is a species of bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. This plant is known for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. The foliage remains evergreen throughout the year, providing a lush appearance in gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a privacy screen in landscaping.
