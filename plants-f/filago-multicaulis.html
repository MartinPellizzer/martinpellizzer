Filago multicaulis

Filago multicaulis, commonly known as the many-stemmed filago, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its slender, branched stems and small, inconspicuous flowers make it a subtle yet resilient plant.

Filago multicaulis is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
