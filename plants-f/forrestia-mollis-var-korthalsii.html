Forrestia mollis var. korthalsii

Forrestia mollis var. korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthalsii forestiera, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a glossy texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening or as a specimen plant. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, thriving in humid, tropical conditions.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
