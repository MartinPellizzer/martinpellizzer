Fuchsia pubescens

Fuchsia pubescens, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Fuchsia pubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
