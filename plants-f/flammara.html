Flammara

Flammara, commonly known as the flameflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vivid, flame-like blossoms that range in color from deep red to orange. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers.

Flammara thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
