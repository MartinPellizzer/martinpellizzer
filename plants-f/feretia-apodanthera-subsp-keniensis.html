Feretia apodanthera subsp. keniensis

Feretia apodanthera subsp.

keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan feretia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to East Africa, particularly Kenya.

The plant features a rhizomatous stem with fleshy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
