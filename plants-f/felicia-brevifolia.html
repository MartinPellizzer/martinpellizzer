Felicia brevifolia

Felicia brevifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Felicia brevifolia is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
