Farfugium japonicum var. nokozanense

Farfugium japonicum var. nokozanense, commonly known as the Nokozan yellow crownbeard, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of large, broad, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, arranged in a distinctive crown-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and floral display.
