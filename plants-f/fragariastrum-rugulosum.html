Fragariastrum rugulosum

Fragariastrum rugulosum, commonly known as the rough strawberry, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a low-growing, sprawling habit with trifoliate leaves that have a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that develop into edible berries with a tart flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and flavor, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental and culinary purposes.
