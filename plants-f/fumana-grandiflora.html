Fumana grandiflora

Fumana grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora fumana, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red or orange. The plant belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture, adding to its visual appeal.
