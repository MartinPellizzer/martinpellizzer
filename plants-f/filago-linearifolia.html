Filago linearifolia

Filago linearifolia, commonly known as the slender filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
