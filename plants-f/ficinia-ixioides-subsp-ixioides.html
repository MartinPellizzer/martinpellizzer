Ficinia ixioides subsp. ixioides

Ficinia ixioides subsp.

ixioides, commonly known as the New Zealand swamp spinach, is a herbaceous plant native to New Zealand. It features heart-shaped, succulent leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

The plant grows in wetlands and swampy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in hue.

The species is valued for its edible leaves, which have a mild, spinach-like flavor.
