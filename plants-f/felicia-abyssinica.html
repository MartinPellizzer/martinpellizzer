Felicia abyssinica

Felicia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disc of tubular flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Felicia abyssinica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
