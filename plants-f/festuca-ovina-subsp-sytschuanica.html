Festuca ovina subsp. sytschuanica

Festuca ovina subsp.

sytschuanica, commonly known as the Chinese sheep fescue, is a perennial grass species native to China. It typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments.

The plant has slender, wiry stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize soils.
