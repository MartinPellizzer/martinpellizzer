Flagellaria indica var. australiensis

Flagellaria indica var. australiensis, commonly known as the Australian flagellaria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, leafy stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has a creeping growth habit and can form dense mats, making it a common weed in disturbed areas.
