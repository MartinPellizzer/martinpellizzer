Forsteronia linearis

Forsteronia linearis, commonly known as the slender forsteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Forsteronia linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
