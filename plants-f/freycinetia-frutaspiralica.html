Freycinetia frutaspiralica

Freycinetia frutaspiralica, commonly known as the spiral fruit cycad, is a unique species belonging to the family Cycadaceae.

It is characterized by its distinct spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its leaves are typically broad and strap-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Despite its ornamental appeal, it is a slow-growing plant that requires specific environmental conditions to thrive.
