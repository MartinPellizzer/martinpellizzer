Festuca Ciliata Var. Glabra

Festuca ciliata var. glabra is a hardy grass species native to temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. It is known for its fine, dense foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. This variety is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in landscaping. Its low-growing habit makes it ideal for ground cover and erosion control. With its silver-green leaves and adaptability, it remains a popular choice among gardeners and ecologists alike.
