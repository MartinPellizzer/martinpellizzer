Frankenia ericifolia subsp. caboverdeana

Frankenia ericifolia subsp.

caboverdeana, commonly known as the Cape Verde firebush, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands. It is a shrub characterized by its small, dense leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh coastal conditions. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the leaves and stems, which help reduce water loss.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
