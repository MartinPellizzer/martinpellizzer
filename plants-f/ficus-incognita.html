Ficus incognita

Ficus incognita, commonly known as the unknown fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green or yellowish in color. Ficus incognita is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various indoor conditions.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
