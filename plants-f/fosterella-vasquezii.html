Fosterella vasquezii

Fosterella vasquezii, commonly known as the Vasquez's pineapple.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. This plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater.
