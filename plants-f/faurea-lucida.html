Faurea lucida

Faurea lucida, commonly known as the silverleaf faurea, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California. The plant is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy foliage that reflects sunlight, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring.

Faurea lucida is adapted to coastal climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
