Festuca eugenii

Festuca eugenii, commonly known as the Eugen's fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the high altitudes of the Alps and surrounding regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous climates. Festuca eugenii is often found in alpine meadows and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
