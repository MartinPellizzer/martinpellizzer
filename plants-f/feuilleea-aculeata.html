Feuilleea aculeata

Feuilleea aculeata, commonly known as the spiky leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are elongated and covered in fine, needle-like structures, giving it a prickly texture. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ornamental gardening.
