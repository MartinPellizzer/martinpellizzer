Flomosia nemarosa

Flomosia nemarosa, commonly known as the water flomosia, is a rare aquatic plant found in freshwater habitats.

It has slender, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, aiding in photosynthesis. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers that bloom in summer, adding to its ornamental value. Its root system helps stabilize sediment and improve water quality.

Due to its specialized habitat requirements, it is considered a sensitive indicator of aquatic ecosystem health.
