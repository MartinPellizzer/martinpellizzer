Ficus volkensii

Ficus volkensii, commonly known as the African fig tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in syconia, which are specialized inflorescences. Ficus volkensii is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
