Floscopa leiothyrsa

Floscopa leiothyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved floscopa, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and typically wind-pollinated flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and yellow, forming dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Floscopa leiothyrsa is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats, where it thrives in full sun and well-drained soils.
