Ficus afeelii

Ficus afeelii, commonly known as the African rubber fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically heart-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by fig-like fruits that are edible for some animals but not for humans. Ficus afeelii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.

It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to flourish.
