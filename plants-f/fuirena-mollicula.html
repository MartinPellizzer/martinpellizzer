Fuirena mollicula

Fuirena mollicula, commonly known as the silky broomrape, is a parasitic plant that lacks chlorophyll and cannot perform photosynthesis.

The plant has a slender, yellowish stem covered in soft, hair-like structures that give it its common name. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters.

Fuirena mollicula is found in various parts of Europe and is considered a rare and elusive species.
