Funtumia gilletii

Funtumia gilletii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The tree has a thick, buttressed trunk and broad, palmate leaves that provide dense shade. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.

Funtumia gilletii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
