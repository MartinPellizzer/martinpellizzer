Fumaria bastardii var. maurorum

Fumaria bastardii var. maurorum, commonly known as the Maurorian Corydalis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish flowers. The plant produces delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in woodland areas.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
