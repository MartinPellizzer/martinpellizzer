Feronia lucida

Feronia lucida, commonly known as the shining feronia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, orange-red fruits that are rich in vitamin C. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and aromatic properties.

Feronia lucida thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
