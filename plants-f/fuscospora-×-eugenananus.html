Fuscospora × eugenananus

Fuscospora × eugenananus, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a rare and intriguing plant resulting from the cross between two distinct fern species.

It exhibits a combination of morphological traits from both parent plants, including unique frond structures and spore characteristics. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are small and dispersed by wind, contributing to its limited distribution.

Due to its rarity and hybrid nature, it is of interest to botanists studying plant evolution and genetic diversity.
