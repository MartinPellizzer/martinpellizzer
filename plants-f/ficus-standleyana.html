Ficus standleyana

Ficus standleyana, commonly known as the silver fig or standley fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. It is an evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height under optimal conditions.

Ficus standleyana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor environments.
