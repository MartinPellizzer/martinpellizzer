Ficus celebica var. ovata

Ficus celebica var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus celebica var. ovata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
