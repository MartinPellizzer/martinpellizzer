Ficus virens var. dasycarpa

Ficus virens var. dasycarpa, commonly known as the rough-seeded fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves that are typically leathery in texture. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are usually green to reddish in color when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
