Ferula atlantica

Ferula atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and feathery, aromatic leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in a complex, branched inflorescence. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine.

Ferula atlantica is valued for its essential oils and has adaptability to various soil types.
