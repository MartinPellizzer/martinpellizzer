Fernandezia robusta

Fernandezia robusta, commonly known as the robust fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. Fernandezia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
