Ficus altimeraloo var. laeta

Ficus altimeraloo var. laeta, commonly known as the Happy Tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, broad leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish-white fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
