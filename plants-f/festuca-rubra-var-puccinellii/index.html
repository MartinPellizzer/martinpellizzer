Festuca Rubra Var. Puccinellii

Festuca rubra var. puccinellii is a hardy grass species known for its resilience in challenging environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is commonly found in coastal and inland dunes. This plant plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion. Its fine, tufted growth habit makes it an attractive addition to landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains largely underappreciated in horticultural circles.
