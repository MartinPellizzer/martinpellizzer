Fagraea thwaitesii

Fagraea thwaitesii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea thwaitesii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
