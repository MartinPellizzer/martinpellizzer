Fimbristylis allenii

Fimbristylis allenii, commonly known as the Allen's sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience in waterlogged environments.
