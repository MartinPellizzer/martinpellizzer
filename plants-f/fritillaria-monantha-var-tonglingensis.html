Fritillaria monantha var. tonglingensis

Fritillaria monantha var. tonglingensis, commonly known as the Tongling fritillary, is a perennial bulbous plant native to China.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that are typically orange or reddish in color, often with contrasting purple or black markings. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are grass-like in appearance, and it produces a single flower per stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
