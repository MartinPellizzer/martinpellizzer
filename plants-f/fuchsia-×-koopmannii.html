Fuchsia × koopmannii

Fuchsia × koopmannii, commonly known as the hybrid fuchsia, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to purple, often with a distinctive white throat. The plant produces pendulous clusters of flowers, which are typically borne on long, flexible stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Fuchsia × koopmannii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
