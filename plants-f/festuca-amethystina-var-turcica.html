Festuca amethystina var. turcica

Festuca amethystina var. turcica, commonly known as the Turcican violetgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It features fine, dark green foliage with a bluish-gray sheen, and produces delicate, violet-blue flower spikes in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often used in rock gardens and alpine landscapes due to its ornamental value and hardiness.

Festuca amethystina var. turcica is a member of the Poaceae family and is valued for its compact growth habit and seasonal color.
