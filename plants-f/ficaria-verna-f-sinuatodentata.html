Ficaria verna f. sinuatodentata

Ficaria verna f. sinuatodentata, commonly known as the variegated winter cabbage, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a hybrid form of Ficaria verna, characterized by its distinctive, lobed, and often variegated leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from February to April. It is native to Central and Southern Europe and thrives in cool, moist environments.

This cultivar is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to provide color during the winter months.
