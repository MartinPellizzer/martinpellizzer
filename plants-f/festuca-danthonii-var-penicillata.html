Festuca danthonii var. penicillata

Festuca danthonii var. penicillata, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its fine, delicate appearance and is often used in ornamental gardening.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
