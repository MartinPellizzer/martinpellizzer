Forrestia monosperma

Forrestia monosperma, commonly known as the African sand forest tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and dense, rounded crown. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy forest habitats.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
