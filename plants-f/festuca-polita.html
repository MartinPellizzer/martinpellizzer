Festuca polita

Festuca polita, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, soft foliage and a bluish-green coloration, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and prairies, and it thrives in well-drained soils. Festuca polita is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and its ability to form dense, attractive lawns.

It is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and adaptability.
