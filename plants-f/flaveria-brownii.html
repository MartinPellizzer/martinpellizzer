Flaveria brownii

Flaveria brownii, commonly known as the brownstem fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has slender stems that are often tinged with brown, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Flaveria brownii is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
