Fimbristylis andongensis var. glabra

Fimbristylis andongensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Andong sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves that are smooth to the touch. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of sedges. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in wetland or marshy environments.

This variant is distinguished by its lack of hair on the leaves, which sets it apart from other varieties within the species.
