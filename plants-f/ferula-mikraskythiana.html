Ferula mikraskythiana

Ferula mikraskythiana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ferula, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, typical of the family, and its taproot is thick and fleshy. It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
