Fallopia

Fallopia, commonly known as cowhead, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polygonaceae.

These plants are characterized by their slender, often climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. They are typically found in temperate regions and can thrive in a variety of soil types. Some species produce small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
