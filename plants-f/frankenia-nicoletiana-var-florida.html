Frankenia nicoletiana var. florida

Frankenia nicoletiana var. florida, commonly known as Florida beach morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found along coastal areas and dunes. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that plays a role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
