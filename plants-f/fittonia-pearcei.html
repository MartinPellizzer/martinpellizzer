Fittonia pearcei

Fittonia pearcei, commonly known as the velvetleaf fittonia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green veins. Fittonia pearcei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive leaves and relatively easy care requirements.
