Fontanesia philliraeoides var. fortunei

Fontanesia philliraeoides var. fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's fontanesia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in panicles, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
