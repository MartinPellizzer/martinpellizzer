Fagelia laxa

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. Fagelia laxa is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forests and undergrowth.
