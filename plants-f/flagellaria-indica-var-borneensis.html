Flagellaria indica var. borneensis

Flagellaria indica var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean flagellaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow to orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its visual appeal in gardens.
