Flueggea japonica var. intermedia

Flueggea japonica var. intermedia, commonly known as the Japanese flueggea, is a succulent plant native to East Asia.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
