Festuca sulcata f. portensis

Festuca sulcata f. portensis, commonly known as the Portensis tall fescue, is a cool-season grass species native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and coarse, dark green foliage, which can grow up to 1 meter in height under optimal conditions. This variety is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in a range of soil types, making it a popular choice for landscaping and turfgrass applications. It produces dense, hardy turf that requires minimal maintenance once established.

The plant is also valued for its adaptability to various climates and its resistance to pests and diseases.
