Fagelia erioclada

Fagelia erioclada, commonly known as the woolly fagelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, woolly appearance. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture, while the flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. It produces small, dark purple fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
