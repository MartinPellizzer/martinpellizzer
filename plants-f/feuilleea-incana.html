Feuilleea incana

Feuilleea incana, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features feathery, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water sources. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Feuilleea incana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture for landscaping and indoor gardening.
