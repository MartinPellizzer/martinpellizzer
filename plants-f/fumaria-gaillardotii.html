Fumaria gaillardotii

Fumaria gaillardotii, commonly known as the Gaillardot's smokeplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pale yellow to greenish flowers that resemble smoke. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are alternate and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria gaillardotii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
