Ficus suborbicularis

Ficus suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are glossy and have a smooth texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs that are typically consumed raw.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
