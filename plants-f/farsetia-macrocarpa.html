Farsetia macrocarpa

Farsetia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant farsetia, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The flowers are small and clustered in umbels, typically white or greenish in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
