Feuilleea unguis-cati var. latifolia

Feuilleea unguis-cati var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved cat's claw, is a unique plant with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its wide, waxy leaves that are often serrated along the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange. Its stems are typically woody and can grow in both upright and creeping forms.

This variety is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, well-drained soil conditions.
