Felicia zeyheri subsp. linifolia

Felicia zeyheri subsp.

linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
