Fumaria capreolata var. burchellii

Fumaria capreolata var. burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, linear to lanceolate, and often have a hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native environment.
