Funkia ovata

Funkia ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf daylily, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features strap-like, broad leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Funkia ovata is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
