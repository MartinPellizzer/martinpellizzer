Fargesia pallens

Fargesia pallens, commonly known as the silver fargesia, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its graceful, arching stems and finely divided, silver-gray leaves that provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late spring, which are clustered in panicle-like inflorescences. It is well-suited for shaded or partially shaded garden environments and is often used as a ornamental plant in landscaping.

Fargesia pallens is relatively low-maintenance, requiring well-drained soil and moderate watering.
