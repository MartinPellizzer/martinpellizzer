Festuca ovina var. polita

Festuca ovina var. polita, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curled, giving it a silky appearance.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive turf.
