Flemingia sericans

Flemingia sericans, commonly known as the Java flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in urban landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
