Felicia abyssinica subsp. neghelliensis

Felicia abyssinica subsp.

neghelliensis, commonly known as the Abyssinian felicia, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant produces yellow-orange ray florets surrounded by a dense array of tubular disk florets. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller size and more compact growth habit compared to the main species.
