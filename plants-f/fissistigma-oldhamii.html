Fissistigma oldhamii

Fissistigma oldhamii, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces oval, woody fruits that contain smooth, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas with a humid climate.
