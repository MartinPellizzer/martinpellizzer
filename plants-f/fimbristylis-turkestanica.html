Fimbristylis turkestanica

Fimbristylis turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. Fimbristylis turkestanica is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to thrive in challenging wetland conditions.
