Felicia amoena subsp. latifolia

Felicia amoena subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers.

The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
