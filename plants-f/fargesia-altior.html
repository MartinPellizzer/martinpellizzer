Fargesia altior

Fargesia altior, commonly known as the tall evergreen bamboo, is a clump-forming plant native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, green stems that grow in dense, upright clusters. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface, providing an elegant appearance. It is cold-hardy and thrives in partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This plant is prized for its ornamental value and ability to create a natural, privacy-screen effect in landscapes.
