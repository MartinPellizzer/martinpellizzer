Fritillaria camschatcensis var. keisukei

Fritillaria camschatcensis var. keisukei, commonly known as the Keisukei fritillary, is a rare variant of the Siberian fritillary.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its bulbs are adapted to survive harsh winter conditions, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This variant is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique coloration.
