Festuca × oenensis

Festuca × oenensis, commonly known as the hybrid meadow fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, leafy stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous florets, which are typically green to purplish in color. Festuca × oenensis is adapted to a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight.

It is often used in landscaping and for erosion control due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
