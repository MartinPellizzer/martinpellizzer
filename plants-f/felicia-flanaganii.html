Felicia flanaganii

Felicia flanaganii, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright blue or purple flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a desirable ornamental plant.

Felicia flanaganii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
