Feuilleea macrophylla

Feuilleea macrophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf fern, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense colonies over time.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
