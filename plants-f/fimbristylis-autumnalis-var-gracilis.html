Fimbristylis autumnalis var. gracilis

Fimbristylis autumnalis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. This variant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. The plant is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to tolerate saline conditions.
