Fumaria media var. muralis

Fumaria media var. muralis, commonly known as the wall rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or wall crevices, often found in disturbed soils. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slight pubescence.

This species is noted for its ability to thrive in harsh environments, making it a characteristic plant of urban and rural walls.
