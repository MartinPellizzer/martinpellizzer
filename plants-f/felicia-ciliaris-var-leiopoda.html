Felicia ciliaris var. leiopoda

Felicia ciliaris var. leiopoda, commonly known as the silky fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long blooming period.
