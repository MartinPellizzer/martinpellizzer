Floribunda

Floribunda, commonly known as a hybrid rose, is a type of rose that combines the best traits of hybrid tea roses and shrub roses.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and abundant, continuous flowering throughout the growing season. The plant typically produces clusters of vibrant, colorful blooms that come in a wide range of hues. Floribunda roses are drought-tolerant and require minimal pruning, making them ideal for gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants.

They are also resistant to common rose diseases, enhancing their suitability for a variety of garden environments.
