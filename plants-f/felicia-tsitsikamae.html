Felicia tsitsikamae

Felicia tsitsikamae, commonly known as the Knobbed Felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems with narrow, gray-green leaves that have a distinctive waxy coating. Its showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the cooler months.

Felicia tsitsikamae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
