Filix pinnata

Filix pinnata, commonly known as the giant fern or horse's tail fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, creeping rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a lush, ornamental appearance.

Filix pinnata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
