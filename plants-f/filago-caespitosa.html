Filago caespitosa

Filago caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted filago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, forming low, compact clumps. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

Filago caespitosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
