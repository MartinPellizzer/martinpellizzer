Ficus neurocarpa

Ficus neurocarpa, commonly known as the Indian rubber fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Thailand, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as either a shrub or a small tree. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, round, greenish berry that turns yellow when ripe.
