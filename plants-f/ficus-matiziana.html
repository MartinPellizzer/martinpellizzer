Ficus matiziana

Ficus matiziana, commonly known as the Matiziana fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Moraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fig-like fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus matiziana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
