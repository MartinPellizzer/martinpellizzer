Filix setigera

Filix setigera, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread along the forest floor. Its leaves are pinnate, with a soft, fuzzy texture, and they often have a bluish-green hue.

Filix setigera thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
