Fragaria × ananassa var. variegata

Fragaria × ananassa var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated strawberry, is a hybrid cultivar of the common strawberry.

It is characterized by its variegated foliage, featuring white or yellow markings on the leaves, which distinguish it from typical strawberries. The plant produces small, flavorful berries similar to standard strawberries, though they may be slightly less abundant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and edible fruits.
