Faberia silhetensis subsp. silhetensis

Faberia silhetensis subsp.

silhetensis, commonly known as the Silhet faberia, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of Bangladesh. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along riverbanks and in forest understories.

Its botanical features include hairy stems and pods that contain several seeds, making it an important species in local ecosystems.
