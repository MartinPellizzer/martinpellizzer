Ficus elastica

Ficus elastica, commonly known as the rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant has a robust root system and can grow quite large, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its adaptability and ease of care make it a favored houseplant worldwide.
