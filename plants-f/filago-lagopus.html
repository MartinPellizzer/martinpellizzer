Filago lagopus

Filago lagopus, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or yellow flowers that hang from the stem. It thrives in cold, moist environments and is adapted to withstand harsh winter conditions.
