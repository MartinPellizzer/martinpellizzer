Fluminaria

Fluminaria, commonly known as the winter jasmine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in winter and early spring. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. Native to regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Fluminaria is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cover ground effectively.
