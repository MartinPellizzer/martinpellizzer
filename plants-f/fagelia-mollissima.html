Fagelia mollissima

Fagelia mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Fagelia mollissima is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
