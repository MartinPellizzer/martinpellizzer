Festuca rubra var. marocana

Festuca rubra var. marocana, commonly known as Moroccan red fescue, is a perennial grass species native to North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and dense, carpet-like foliage. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences with small, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance.

Festuca rubra var. marocana is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to withstand moderate foot traffic.
