Felicia stenophylla

Felicia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Felicia stenophylla is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
