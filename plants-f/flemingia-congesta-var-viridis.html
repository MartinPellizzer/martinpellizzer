Flemingia congesta var. viridis

Flemingia congesta var. viridis, commonly known as green flemingia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, white to yellowish flowers are arranged in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
