Festuca turkestanica

Festuca turkestanica, commonly known as Turkestan fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky, or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.

Festuca turkestanica is also used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
