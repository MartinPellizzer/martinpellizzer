Festuca chuquisacae

Festuca chuquisacae, commonly known as bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in turfgrass mixes for its durability and aesthetic appeal.

Festuca chuquisacae is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and recover quickly from wear.
