Fedia olitcria

Fedia olitcria, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring and are pollinated by specific insect species.

Fedia olitcria is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
