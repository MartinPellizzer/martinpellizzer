Feuilleea bicolor

Feuilleea bicolor, commonly known as the Two-Colored Leaf Plant, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored leaves, which display a vibrant green on one side and a deep crimson on the other. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, waxy leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. Feuilleea bicolor thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its unusual leaf coloration and its ability to add a touch of exotic beauty to any space.
