Frankenia ericifolia subsp. latifolia

Frankenia ericifolia subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf frankenia, is a shrub native to coastal regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments. Its leaves are typically broad and leathery, providing a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
