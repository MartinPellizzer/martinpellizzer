Ferulago commutata

Ferulago commutata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ferulago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in umbels, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Ferulago commutata is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
