Fuirena umbellata var. unguiculata

Fuirena umbellata var. unguiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cow-wheat, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinctive, narrow base. This plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is commonly found in woodland edges and grassy areas.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is of interest in ecological studies due to its specific habitat preferences.
