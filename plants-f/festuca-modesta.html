Festuca modesta

Festuca modesta, commonly known as slender fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Festuca modesta is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
