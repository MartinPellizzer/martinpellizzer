Ficus calyptroceras var. elliotiana

Ficus calyptroceras var. elliotiana, commonly known as the Elliot's fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor environments. It has a shallow root system, making it suitable for container gardening.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical landscapes.
