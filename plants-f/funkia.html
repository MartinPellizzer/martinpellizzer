Funkia

Funkia, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Araceae.

These aquatic or semi-aquatic plants are native to Asia, Australia, and the Americas. They are characterized by their arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive, spadix-bearing inflorescences. Funkia species often grow in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Their ornamental value makes them popular in water gardens and pond landscapes.
