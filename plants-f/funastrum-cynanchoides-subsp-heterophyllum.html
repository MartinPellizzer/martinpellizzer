Funastrum cynanchoides subsp. heterophyllum

Funastrum cynanchoides subsp.

heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in racemes, and its fruit is a legume containing several seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
