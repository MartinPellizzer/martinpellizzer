Forsythia × intermedia var. densiflora

Forsythia × intermedia var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered forsythia, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has arching, slender branches covered with small, ovate leaves that turn dark green in summer. Its dense inflorescences consist of numerous flowers clustered in racemes, giving it a striking appearance.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its early spring blooms and hardy nature.
