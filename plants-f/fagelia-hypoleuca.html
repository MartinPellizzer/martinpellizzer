Fagelia hypoleuca

Fagelia hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered fagelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a glossy dark green surface. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in terminal inflorescences, adding ornamental value.

Fagelia hypoleuca is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
