Ficus laeta

Ficus laeta, commonly known as the silver ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a subtle silvery sheen on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by round, greenish-white fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Ficus laeta is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
