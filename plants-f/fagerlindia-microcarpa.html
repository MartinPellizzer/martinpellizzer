Fagerlindia microcarpa

Fagerlindia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf kaffir lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, narrow, glossy leaves that are often used in culinary applications. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, citrus-like fruits. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and a preference for warm, humid climates.

Fagerlindia microcarpa is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
