Fornicium uniflorum

Fornicium uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower fornicium, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, solitary flower that emerges from a basal rosette of fleshy, succulent leaves. The plant's botanical structure includes a short, thick stem that supports the single, showy bloom, which typically features vibrant colors such as red or orange. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
