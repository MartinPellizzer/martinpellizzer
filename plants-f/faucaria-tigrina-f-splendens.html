Faucaria tigrina f. splendens

Faucaria tigrina f. splendens, commonly known as the Splendens Tiger's Head.

This plant is a succulent with distinctive, spiky, upward-facing flowers that are typically pink or red. It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating called farina. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
