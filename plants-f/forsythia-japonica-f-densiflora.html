Forsythia japonica f. densiflora

Forsythia japonica f. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Japanese forsythia, is a compact shrub characterized by its profuse display of bright yellow flowers.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical flower clusters that are densely packed with small, bright yellow flowers. It is native to Japan and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and early spring flowering, making it a popular choice in gardens.
