Fagonia chilensis var. insularis

Fagonia chilensis var. insularis, commonly known as the Easter Island fagonia, is a shrub native to the Chatham Islands.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, spine-tipped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to the region's maritime climate.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
