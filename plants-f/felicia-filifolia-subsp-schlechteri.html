Felicia filifolia subsp. schlechteri

Felicia filifolia subsp.

schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's featherflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant produces small, bright yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
