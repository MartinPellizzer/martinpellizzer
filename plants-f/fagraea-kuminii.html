Fagraea kuminii

Fagraea kuminii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, opposite, and often leathery leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. Fagraea kuminii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
