Fargesia brevipaniculata

Fargesia brevipaniculata, commonly known as the short-panicle evergreen bamboo, is a cold-hardy shrub-like plant native to China.

It features slender, upright canes that are covered in small, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale green to white flowers in late spring to early summer. It is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.

Fargesia brevipaniculata is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
