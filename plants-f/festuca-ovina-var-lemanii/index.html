Festuca Ovina Var. Lemanii

Festuca ovina var. lemanii is a low-growing grass species native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few plants can survive. This variety is known for its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Its fine, silver-green leaves give it a distinctive appearance that makes it popular in landscaping. Despite its hardiness, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish.
