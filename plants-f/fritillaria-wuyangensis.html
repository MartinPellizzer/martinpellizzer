Fritillaria wuyangensis

Fritillaria wuyangensis, commonly known as the Wuyang fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its bulbs are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
