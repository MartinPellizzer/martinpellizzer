Filago jussiaei

Filago jussiaei, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that allow it to spread effectively across its native range.

Filago jussiaei is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
