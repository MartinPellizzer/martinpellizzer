Ficus hochstetteri var. glabior

Ficus hochstetteri var. glabior, commonly known as the Hochstetter's fig, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible, green fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
