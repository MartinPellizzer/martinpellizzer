Forsythia suspensa f. aureovariegata

Forsythia suspensa f. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated weeping forsythia, is a cultivar of the weeping forsythia, characterized by its striking yellow and white variegated leaves.

This plant is a deciduous shrub native to China, known for its cascading branches that create a dramatic, waterfall-like appearance. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive yellow-green to white variegation pattern. It produces fragrant, bright yellow flowers in the spring, which bloom in clusters along the branches.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
