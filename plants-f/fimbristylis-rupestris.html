Fimbristylis rupestris

Fimbristylis rupestris, commonly known as rock sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Fimbristylis rupestris is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
