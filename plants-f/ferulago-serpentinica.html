Ferulago serpentinica

Ferulago serpentinica, commonly known as the serpentine fumitory, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky, calcareous, or serpentine soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Ferulago serpentinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
