Ficus eriobotryoides var. callei

Ficus eriobotryoides var. callei, commonly known as the Calle's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
