Flagellaria indica f. angustifolia

Flagellaria indica f. angustifolia, commonly known as slender flagellaria, is a variant of the slender morning glory.

It is a climbing herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its stems are often hairy and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
