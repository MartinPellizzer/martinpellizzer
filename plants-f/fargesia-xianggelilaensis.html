Fargesia xianggelilaensis

Fargesia xianggelilaensis, commonly known as the Xianggelilaensis bush bamboo, is a species of shrub bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and slender, culm-like stems that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Fargesia xianggelilaensis is valued for its hardiness and ability to provide year-round interest in the garden.
