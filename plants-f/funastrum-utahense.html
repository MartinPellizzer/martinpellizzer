Funastrum utahense

Funastrum utahense, commonly known as Utah funastrum, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and small, elliptic leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Funastrum utahense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in rocky or disturbed areas.
