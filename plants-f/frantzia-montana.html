Frantzia montana

Frantzia montana, commonly known as the mountain frantzia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Frantzia montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
