Fargesia nujiangensis f. lanpingensis

Fargesia nujiangensis f. lanpingensis, commonly known as the Lanping evergreen bamboo, is a variant of the Nujiang bamboo species.

It is a clump-forming bamboo that typically grows in dense, upright clusters. The plant features slender, dark green culms with a smooth, almost metallic sheen. It is native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

This ornamental bamboo is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
