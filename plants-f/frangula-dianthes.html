Frangula dianthes

Frangula dianthes, commonly known as the common fumitory, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Frangula dianthes is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
