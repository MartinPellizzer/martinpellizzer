Fadogia audruana

Fadogia audruana, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a legume belonging to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural systems.
