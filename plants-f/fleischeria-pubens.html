Fleischeria pubens

Fleischeria pubens, commonly known as the hairy fleischeria, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, glandular leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Fleischeria pubens is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.
