Fimbristylis × itaru-itoana

Fimbristylis × itaru-itoana, commonly known as the Itaru-Itoana sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and swampy areas. Its flowers are small and clustered, with three stamens and a single pistil, typical of the sedge family.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils.
