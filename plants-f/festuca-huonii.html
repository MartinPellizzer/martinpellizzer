Festuca huonii

Festuca huonii, commonly known as Huon grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Southern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, erect flowering stems. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Festuca huonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
