Fibichia umbellata f. major

Fibichia umbellata f. major, commonly known as the large-flowered fibichia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, umbellate inflorescences that bear bright yellow flowers with prominent, thread-like filaments. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as grasslands and open woodlands. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and often have a rough texture.

Fibichia umbellata f. major is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
