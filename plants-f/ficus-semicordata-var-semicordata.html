Ficus semicordata var. semicordata

Ficus semicordata var. semicordata, commonly known as the weeping fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by green, oval-shaped fruits that mature to a yellowish color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscaping.
