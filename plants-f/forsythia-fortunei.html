Forsythia fortunei

Forsythia fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese lantern bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is largely due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various climates.
