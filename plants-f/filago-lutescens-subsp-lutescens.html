Filago lutescens subsp. lutescens

Filago lutescens subsp.

lutescens, commonly known as the yellow filago, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This subspecies is native to southern Africa and is often found in regions with mild, Mediterranean climates.
