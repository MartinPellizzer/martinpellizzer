Fleischmannia panamensis

Fleischmannia panamensis, commonly known as the Panama fleischmannia, is a flowering plant native to Central America, particularly Panama.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spineless stems and clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in coastal areas with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and they bloom during the dry season.

Fleischmannia panamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
