Ficus stapfii

Ficus stapfii, commonly known as the African ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, including countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

Ficus stapfii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
