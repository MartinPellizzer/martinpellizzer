Fraxinus chinensis var. rhynchophylla

Fraxinus chinensis var. rhynchophylla, commonly known as the Chinese ash or Chinese winged ash, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged samaras, which aid in seed dispersal. The tree has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its wood is valued for its strength and is used in construction and furniture-making.
