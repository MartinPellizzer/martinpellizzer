Fuchsia arborescens f. tenuis

Fuchsia arborescens f. tenuis, commonly known as the slender fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy dark green. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its delicate foliage, it requires protection from strong winds and direct sunlight.
