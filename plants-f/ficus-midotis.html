Ficus midotis

Ficus midotis, commonly known as the Midotis fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are edible and often consumed by local wildlife. Ficus midotis thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
