Ficus palawanensis

Ficus palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found on the island of Palawan. The plant is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit, which is a small, round fig, is edible and plays a role in local ecosystems.
