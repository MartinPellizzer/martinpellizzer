Fagraea elliptica

Fagraea elliptica, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Fagraea elliptica is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
