Felicia maritima

Felicia maritima, commonly known as sea daisies, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has silver-gray foliage and thrives in sandy or rocky soils, making it well adapted to maritime environments. It is drought-tolerant and often found in coastal dunes and cliffs.

Felicia maritima plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for small insects and birds.
