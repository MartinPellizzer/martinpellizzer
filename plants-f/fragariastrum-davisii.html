Fragariastrum davisii

Fragariastrum davisii, commonly known as Davis's strawberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, red, edible berries that resemble strawberries. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often found in shaded, forested areas where it contributes to the understory vegetation.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
