Francoa lyrata

Francoa lyrata, commonly known as the lyrata francoa, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle purple hue. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Francoa lyrata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
