Fritillaria delphinensis var. burnatii

Fritillaria delphinensis var. burnatii, commonly known as the Burnat's bellflower, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue or white with a prominent central blotch. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
