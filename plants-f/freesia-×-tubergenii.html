Freesia × tubergenii

Freesia × tubergenii, commonly known as the tuberous freesia, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including white, yellow, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and produces tuberous roots that allow it to survive in adverse conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and pleasant aroma.
