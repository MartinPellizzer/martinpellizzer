Fragaria nilgerrensis subsp. hayatae

Fragaria nilgerrensis subsp.

hayatae, commonly known as the Japanese mountain strawberry, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly larger fruit size and more robust growth compared to other Fragaria species. It thrives in cool, temperate climates and is often found in forested mountain areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited but notable use in local traditional medicine.
