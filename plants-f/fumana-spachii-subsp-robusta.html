Fumana spachii subsp. robusta

Fumana spachii subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust fumana, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae. It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in regions such as South Africa and Namibia.

The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and pinnately divided leaves. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

The subspecies robusta is distinguished by its robust stems and larger leaves compared to other subspecies of Fumana spachii.
