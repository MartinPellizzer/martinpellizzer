Fritillaria tianshanica

Fritillaria tianshanica, commonly known as the Tianshan fritillary, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Tianshan Mountains in China.

It features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a thick, fleshy bulb that stores nutrients, allowing it to survive in its alpine habitat. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapting to the harsh, cold climate.

Fritillaria tianshanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
