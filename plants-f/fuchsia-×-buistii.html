Fuchsia × buistii

Fuchsia × buistii, commonly known as the New Zealand fuchsia, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with a distinctive yellow or green throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Fuchsia × buistii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
