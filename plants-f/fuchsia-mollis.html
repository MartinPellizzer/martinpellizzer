Fuchsia mollis

Fuchsia mollis, commonly known as the velvet tangle, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Fuchsia mollis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and shaded garden areas.
