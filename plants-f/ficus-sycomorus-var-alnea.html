Ficus sycomorus var. alnea

Ficus sycomorus var. alnea, commonly known as the African fig or desert fig, is a variant of the sycomore fig native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

This plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It features thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry climates. The fruit, which is a small, edible fig, is an important food source for various wildlife and local communities.

The plant's ability to thrive in harsh environments makes it an important component of desert ecosystems.
