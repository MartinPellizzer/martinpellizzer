Ficus dendrocida

Ficus dendrocida, commonly known as the silver fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible figs, which are often used in local cuisines. Ficus dendrocida thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ability to adapt to various indoor conditions makes it a popular choice for houseplants.
