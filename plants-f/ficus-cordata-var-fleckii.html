Ficus cordata var. fleckii

Ficus cordata var. fleckii, commonly known as the variegated rubber plant, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which adds visual interest to indoor spaces. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by green, round fruits that mature to a reddish color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
