Furiolobivia

Furiolobivia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, velvety foliage that ranges from silver to deep green. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming during the cooler months. Furiolobivia thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
