Festuca scheuchzeri

Festuca scheuchzeri, commonly known as the Alpine bentgrass, is a perennial grass native to high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in cold, moist environments such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to harsh climatic conditions, exhibiting frost resistance and efficient water use.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
