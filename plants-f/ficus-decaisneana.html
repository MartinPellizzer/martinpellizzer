Ficus decaisneana

Ficus decaisneana, commonly known as the Decaisne's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green when mature. It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall in its natural habitat.

Ficus decaisneana is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
