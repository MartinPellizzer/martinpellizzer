Funium

Funium, commonly known as the peace lily, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and white, spatula-shaped flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular indoor plant. Its air-purifying properties also make it a favored choice for improving indoor air quality.

Funium is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal watering and moderate humidity.
