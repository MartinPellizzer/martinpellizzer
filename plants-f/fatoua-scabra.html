Fatoua scabra

Fatoua scabra, commonly known as the rough fatoua, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features upright stems covered in stiff, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss. Its small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Fatoua scabra is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
