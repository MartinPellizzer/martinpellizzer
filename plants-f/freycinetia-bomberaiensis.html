Freycinetia bomberaiensis

Freycinetia bomberaiensis, commonly known as the Bomberai palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the island of Bomberai in the Indonesian province of West Kalimantan. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in inflorescences, followed by dark blue-black berries.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
