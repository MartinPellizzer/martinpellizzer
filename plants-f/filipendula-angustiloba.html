Filipendula angustiloba

Filipendula angustiloba, commonly known as narrow-leaved meadow sweet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Filipendula angustiloba is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local pollinators.
