Furcraea gigantea var. mediopicta

Furcraea gigantea var. mediopicta, commonly known as the Variegated Furcraea, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central America.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, making it a dramatic addition to gardens or indoor spaces.
