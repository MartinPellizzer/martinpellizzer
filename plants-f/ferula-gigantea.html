Ferula gigantea

Ferula gigantea, commonly known as the giant fennel, is a tall, aromatic plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a thick, branched stem and large, feathery leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces yellow flowers in umbels, which are followed by long, slender seed pods. Its roots are thick and fleshy, containing compounds used for their medicinal properties.

Ferula gigantea is also valued for its essential oils, which have been used in perfumery and herbal remedies.
