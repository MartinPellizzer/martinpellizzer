Felicia esterhuysenii

Felicia esterhuysenii, commonly known as the Esterhuysen's fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with white ray florets.

Felicia esterhuysenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
