Funkia tardiflora

Funkia tardiflora, commonly known as the late-flowering daylily, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features strap-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and water features.
