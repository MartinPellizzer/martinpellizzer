Freycinetia anomala

Freycinetia anomala, commonly known as the red pandanus, is a tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a member of the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped, fibrous leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Its fruit is a large, fibrous structure that contains several seeds.

Freycinetia anomala is often used in traditional crafts and has cultural significance in many island communities.
