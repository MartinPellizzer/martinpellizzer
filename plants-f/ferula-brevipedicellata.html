Ferula brevipedicellata

Ferula brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed ferula, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Ferula brevipedicellata thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
