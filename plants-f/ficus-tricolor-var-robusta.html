Ficus tricolor var. robusta

Ficus tricolor var. robusta, commonly known as the variegated ficus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements.
