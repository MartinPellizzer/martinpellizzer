Ficus Piperifolia Var. Borneensis

Ficus piperifolia var. borneensis is a unique species of fig tree native to the rainforests of Borneo. This plant is known for its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display a vibrant green color. It thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish. Its small, inedible fruits are seldom harvested by humans. This plant is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
