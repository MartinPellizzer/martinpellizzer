Fagraea sparei

Fagraea sparei, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. Fagraea sparei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
