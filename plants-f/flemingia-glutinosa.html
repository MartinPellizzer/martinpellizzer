Flemingia glutinosa

Flemingia glutinosa, commonly known as sticky flemingia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a sticky resin. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Flemingia glutinosa thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its sticky secretions have been studied for potential medicinal and industrial applications.
