Fuchsia minimiflora

Fuchsia minimiflora, commonly known as the miniature fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Fuchsia minimiflora thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
