Fagara beniensis

Fagara beniensis, commonly known as the Benin fagara, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces elliptical, smooth-skinned fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
