Feuilleea globulifera

Feuilleea globulifera, commonly known as the globe-leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its spherical, fleshy leaves that resemble small globes.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil. The plant's foliage is dense and often features a waxy surface, which helps reduce water loss. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Feuilleea globulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
