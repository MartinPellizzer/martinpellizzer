Furcraea altissima

Furcraea altissima, commonly known as the tall sword plant, is a striking succulent native to southern Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and long, sword-shaped leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in panicles during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
