Ficus oleracea var. oleracea

Ficus oleracea var. oleracea, commonly known as the edible fig tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Moraceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish flowers that develop into the familiar fig fruit. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its fruit is edible, sweet, and commonly used in culinary applications.

It is also valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to grow in various soil types.
