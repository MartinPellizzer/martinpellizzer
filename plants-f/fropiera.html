Fropiera

Fropiera, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions.

It features broad, waxy leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Fropiera thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.
