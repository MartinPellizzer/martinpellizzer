Fontquera

Fontquera, commonly known as the Spanish bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Fontquera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
