Fingardia lokonensis

Fingardia lokonensis, commonly known as the Lokon mountain orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms that typically appear in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink.

Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, Fingardia lokonensis is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
