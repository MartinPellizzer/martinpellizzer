Fadogia agrestis

Fadogia agrestis, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain flat, kidney-shaped seeds covered in a velvety coating. It is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in warm, humid climates.

Fadogia agrestis is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
