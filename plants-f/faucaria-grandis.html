Faucaria grandis

Faucaria grandis, commonly known as the giant stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, Faucaria grandis is well-suited for arid climates.
