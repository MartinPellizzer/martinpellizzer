Ficus bussei var. longipetiolata

Ficus bussei var. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves with long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a robust root system and can grow quite tall under optimal conditions. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by round, yellowish fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor environments.
