Fagraea megalantha

Fagraea megalantha, commonly known as the giant fagraea, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is a large evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
