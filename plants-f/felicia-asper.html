Felicia asper

Felicia asper, commonly known as the rough felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features gray-green, hairy leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Felicia asper is also known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
