Fumaria agraria var. tigertensis

Fumaria agraria var. tigertensis, commonly known as the Tigert's Glory, is a variant of the common fumitory plant.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, trailing stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along fences.
