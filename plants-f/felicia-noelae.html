Felicia noelae

Felicia noelae, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk, often surrounded by white or pale pink ray florets. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Felicia noelae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
