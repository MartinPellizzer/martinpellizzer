Ficus indecora

Ficus indecora, commonly known as the variegated ficus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and yellow variegation, which adds visual interest to indoor spaces. The plant has a compact growth habit and can be pruned to maintain its shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Ficus indecora is popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and attractive foliage.
