Feuilleea cynometroides

Feuilleea cynometroides, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces elegant, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
