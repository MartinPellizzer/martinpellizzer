Ficus callophylla var. minor

Ficus callophylla var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by edible, greenish-white fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
