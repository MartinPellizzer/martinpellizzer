Ficus comptonii

Ficus comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, clustered figs that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Ficus comptonii is also popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
