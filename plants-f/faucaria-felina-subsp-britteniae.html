Faucaria felina subsp. britteniae

Faucaria felina subsp.

britteniae, commonly known as the cat's paw plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa. It features small, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge.

The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
