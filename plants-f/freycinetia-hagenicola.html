Freycinetia hagenicola

Freycinetia hagenicola, commonly known as the Hagen's pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae.

It is native to the Pacific islands, particularly parts of Fiji and Tonga. The plant is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and for thatching. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color.

Freycinetia hagenicola thrives in coastal and lowland areas, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.
