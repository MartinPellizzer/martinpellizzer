Feuilleea crassipes

Feuilleea crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leafed marsh plant, is a herbaceous species native to wetland environments.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Feuilleea crassipes plays a vital role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems by preventing soil erosion.
