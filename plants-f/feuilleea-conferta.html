Feuilleea conferta

Feuilleea conferta, commonly known as the denseleaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. The plant features feathery, compound leaves that are arranged in a tight, rosette-like formation. Its rhizomatous structure allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats over time.

Feuilleea conferta is often used in ornamental horticulture for its lush, green appearance and air-purifying qualities.
