Ferula sjugatensis

Ferula sjugatensis, commonly known as the Kashmiri ferula, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic compounds and often hollow stems. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels, typical of the family. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, pungent odor.

Ferula sjugatensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
