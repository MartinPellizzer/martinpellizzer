Fuchsia coccinea var. chonotica

Fuchsia coccinea var. chonotica, commonly known as the Chonotica fuchsia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to violet. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
