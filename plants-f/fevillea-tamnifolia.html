Fevillea tamnifolia

Fevillea tamnifolia, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are covered in tiny, golden spore cases. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Fevillea tamnifolia requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish.
