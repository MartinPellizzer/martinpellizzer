Ficus meistosyce

Ficus meistosyce, commonly known as the Meistosyce fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered fruiting bodies known as syconia. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

The tree can grow up to several meters tall, with a dense canopy that provides shade in shaded environments.
