Fagara Dugandii

Fagara dugandii is a striking plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia and Ecuador. It is known for its unique, waxy leaves that shimmer in the dappled light of its natural habitat. This species belongs to the Bombacaceae family, which includes other notable plants like the ceiba tree. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice among collectors and gardeners. Despite its beauty, Fagara dugandii remains a relatively rare and underappreciated plant in the botanical world.
