Forsellesia planitierum

Forsellesia planitierum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture from the dry soil, and it has a short lifespan, often completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
