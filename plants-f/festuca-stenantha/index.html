Festuca Stenantha

Festuca stenantha is a unique grass species known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance. Native to specific regions, it thrives in harsh environments where few plants can survive. Its narrow leaves and compact growth make it easily identifiable among other grasses. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. This plant is often overlooked but deserves recognition for its resilience and beauty.
