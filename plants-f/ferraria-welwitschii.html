Ferraria welwitschii

Ferraria welwitschii, commonly known as the yellow bell orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in the succulent Karoo region. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Ferraria welwitschii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
