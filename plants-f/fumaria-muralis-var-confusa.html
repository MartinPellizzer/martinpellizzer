Fumaria muralis var. confusa

Fumaria muralis var. confusa, commonly known as the wall rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. This variety is typically found growing on walls and in rocky areas, thriving in sunny to partially shaded conditions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and often overlooked member of the fumaria family.
