Fadogia chlorantha var. chlorantha

Fadogia chlorantha var. chlorantha, commonly known as the African blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a climbing habit and small, capsule-like fruits.

It is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional uses in local cultures.
