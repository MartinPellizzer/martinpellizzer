Feuilleea serianthes

Feuilleea serianthes, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant wood that is highly valued for its scent and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape with a smooth texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spherical fruits containing several seeds.

Due to overharvesting, it is now considered an endangered species in many regions.
