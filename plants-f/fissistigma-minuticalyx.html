Fissistigma minuticalyx

Fissistigma minuticalyx, commonly known as the Chinese hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with serrated edges.

Fissistigma minuticalyx is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
