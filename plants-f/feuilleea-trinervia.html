Feuilleea trinervia

Feuilleea trinervia, commonly known as the three-veined leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate leaf structure.

Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with three distinct veins running from the base to the tip, creating a distinctive trinervate pattern. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pale yellow.

Feuilleea trinervia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper moisture and light conditions.
