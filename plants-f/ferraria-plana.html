Ferraria plana

Ferraria plana, commonly known as the flat ferraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands.

Ferraria plana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
