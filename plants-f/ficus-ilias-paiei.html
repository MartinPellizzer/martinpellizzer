Ficus ilias-paiei

Ficus ilias-paiei, commonly known as the Ivory Coast fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Ivory Coast and surrounding areas. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fig-like fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
