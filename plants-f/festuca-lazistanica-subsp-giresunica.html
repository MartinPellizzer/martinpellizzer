Festuca lazistanica subsp. giresunica

Festuca lazistanica subsp.

giresunica, commonly known as Giresun fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Turkey. It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, temperate habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Botanically, it is distinguished by its chromosome number and specific morphological traits that set it apart from other subspecies of Festuca.
