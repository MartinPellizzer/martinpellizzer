Ficus finlayana

Ficus finlayana, commonly known as the Mulu fig, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Myanmar.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in the understory of dense forests. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
