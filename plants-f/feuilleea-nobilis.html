Feuilleea nobilis

Feuilleea nobilis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that exhibit a distinctive silvery-blue hue, which helps reflect intense sunlight. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming during the rainy season. Feuilleea nobilis thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
