Ficus urbaniana

Ficus urbaniana, commonly known as the urban ficus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India and China. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible figs, which are a food source for various animals.

Ficus urbaniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
