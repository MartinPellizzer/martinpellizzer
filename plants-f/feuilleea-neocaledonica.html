Feuilleea neocaledonica

Feuilleea neocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia feather plant, is a unique species native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are covered in a fine, waxy layer that helps retain moisture in its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
