Flueggea jaburan

Flueggea jaburan, commonly known as the Java flueggea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Flueggea jaburan is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
