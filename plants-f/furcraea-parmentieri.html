Furcraea parmentieri

Furcraea parmentieri, commonly known as the Parmentier's furcraea, is a striking succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, narrow, sword-like leaves that are usually blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both gardens and indoor collections.
