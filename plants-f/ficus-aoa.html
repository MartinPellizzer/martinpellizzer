Ficus aoa

Ficus aoa, commonly known as the Aoa fig, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into edible fig fruits. Ficus aoa thrives in humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
