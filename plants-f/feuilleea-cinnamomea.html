Feuilleea cinnamomea

Feuilleea cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon leaf plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, broad leaves that emit a distinct cinnamon scent when crushed. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are rich in essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.

Feuilleea cinnamomea is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor ambiance.
