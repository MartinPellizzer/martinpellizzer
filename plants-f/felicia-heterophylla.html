Felicia heterophylla

Felicia heterophylla, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Felicia heterophylla is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
