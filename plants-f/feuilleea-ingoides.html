Feuilleea ingoides

Feuilleea ingoides, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Feuilleea ingoides thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
