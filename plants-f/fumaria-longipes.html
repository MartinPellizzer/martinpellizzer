Fumaria longipes

Fumaria longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed fumitory, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds, typical of the genus Fumaria. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its delicate appearance and historical use in traditional medicine make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
