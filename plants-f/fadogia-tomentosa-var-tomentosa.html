Fadogia tomentosa var. tomentosa

Fadogia tomentosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the velvet gourd or African bottle gourd, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant is characterized by its hairy, waxy leaves and distinctive, bottle-shaped fruits that can vary in color from green to yellow. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
