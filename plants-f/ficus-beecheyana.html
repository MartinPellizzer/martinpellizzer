Ficus beecheyana

Ficus beecheyana, commonly known as the Beechey fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, clustered figs that are usually green to yellowish in hue.

Ficus beecheyana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
