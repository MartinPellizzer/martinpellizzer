Fuchsia pringsheimii

Fuchsia pringsheimii, commonly known as the New Zealand fuchsia, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Fuchsia pringsheimii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
