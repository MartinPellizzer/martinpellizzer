Feuilleea peruviana

Feuilleea peruviana, commonly known as the Andean leaf plant, is a strikingly unique species native to the high-altitude regions of the Peruvian Andes.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often adorned with a subtle iridescence, giving it an almost otherworldly appearance.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
