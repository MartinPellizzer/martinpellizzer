Forrestia mollissima

Forrestia mollissima, commonly known as the silky forestia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that is covered in a fine, silky texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by yellowish-orange berries. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
