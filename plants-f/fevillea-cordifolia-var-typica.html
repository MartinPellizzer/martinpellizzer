Fevillea cordifolia var. typica

Fevillea cordifolia var. typica, commonly known as the heartleaf fevillea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Fevillea cordifolia var. typica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
