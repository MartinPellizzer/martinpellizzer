Fedia graciliflora subsp. sulcata

Fedia graciliflora subsp.

sulcata, commonly known as the slender-flowered fedia, is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences.

The plant produces pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a botanically significant species with limited distribution.
