Frankenia caboverdeana

Frankenia caboverdeana, commonly known as the Cape Verde firebush, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, which helps reduce water loss in arid climates.

Frankenia caboverdeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
