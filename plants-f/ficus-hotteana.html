Ficus hotteana

Ficus hotteana, commonly known as the hotteana fig, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are often hidden within a smooth, greenish husk. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ficus hotteana is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
