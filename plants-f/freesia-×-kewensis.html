Freesia × kewensis

Freesia × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew freesia, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including yellow, orange, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, with strap-like, grassy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Freesia × kewensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in floral arrangements.
