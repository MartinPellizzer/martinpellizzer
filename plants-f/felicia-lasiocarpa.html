Felicia lasiocarpa

Felicia lasiocarpa, commonly known as the woolly felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, surrounded by soft, white, woolly hairs. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a similar fuzzy coating, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Felicia lasiocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
