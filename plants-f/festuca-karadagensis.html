Festuca karadagensis

Festuca karadagensis, commonly known as Karadag fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Turkey.

It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
