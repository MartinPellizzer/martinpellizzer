Fumaria normanii

Fumaria normanii, commonly known as the Norman's smokeplant, is a member of the Fumariaceae family.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features slender, ascending stems that bear small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in loose racemes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Fumaria normanii is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
