Faucaria felina

Faucaria felina, commonly known as the cat's paw plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are shaped like miniature cat paws, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
