Filago polycephala

Filago polycephala, commonly known as the many-headed filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators.

Filago polycephala is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
