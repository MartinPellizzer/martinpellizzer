Fleischmannia laxa

Fleischmannia laxa, commonly known as the slender fleischmannia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, typically in clusters at the ends of the stems. Fleischmannia laxa is often found in rocky soils and is considered a rare species due to its limited distribution.
