Ferulago barrelieri

Ferulago barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's ferula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features tall, slender stems and produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Ferulago barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
