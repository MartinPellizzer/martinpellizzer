Feuilleea lucida

Feuilleea lucida, commonly known as the Shimmering Leaf Plant, is a unique species native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent leaves that reflect light in a way that appears almost iridescent. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with multiple stems that often branch out near the base. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and emit a faint, sweet fragrance.

Feuilleea lucida is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires high humidity to thrive.
