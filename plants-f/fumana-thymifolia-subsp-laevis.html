Fumana thymifolia subsp. laevis

Fumana thymifolia subsp.

The flowers are small and white, arranged in compact umbels, and the plant produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps. It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in sandy or rocky soils.
