Festuca Rigida Var. Hemipoa

Festuca rigida var. hemipoa is a hardy grass species native to cold climates. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few plants can survive. This variety is known for its dense, low-growing foliage and winter-hardiness. Its ability to withstand extreme temperatures makes it a valuable plant for landscaping in harsh regions. Despite its resilience, it remains a lesser-known gem in the world of ornamental grasses.
