Filago germanica var. prostrata

Filago germanica var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate German filago, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features a mat-forming habit with slender, prostrate stems that root at the nodes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are linear and hairy, often with a reddish hue when mature.

It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
