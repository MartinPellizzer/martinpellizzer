Fuchsia × experscandens

Fuchsia × experscandens, commonly known as the royal fuchsia, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Fuchsia × experscandens thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
