Fraxinus retusa var. calcicola

Fraxinus retusa var. calcicola, commonly known as the limestone ash.

It is a variant of the European ash tree adapted to calcareous soils. This plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite leaves that are often hairy beneath. The small, greenish-yellow flowers appear in clusters and are followed by winged fruits.

It is typically found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats across southern Europe.
