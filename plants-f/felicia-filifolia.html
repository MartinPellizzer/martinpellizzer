Felicia filifolia

Felicia filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Felicia filifolia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in poor soils.
