Fabiana Australis

Fabiana australis is a hardy shrub native to southern South America known for its resilience in harsh environments. It features small, oval leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of tiny, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. It is commonly used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its drought tolerance. Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
