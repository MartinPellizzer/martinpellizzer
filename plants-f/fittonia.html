Fittonia

Fittonia, commonly known as the nerve plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are often variegated, featuring shades of red, pink, white, and green, which make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Fittonia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is known for its compact growth habit and is often used as a decorative plant in terrariums and as a houseplant.
