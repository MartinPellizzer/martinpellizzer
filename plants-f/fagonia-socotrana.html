Fagonia socotrana

Fagonia socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cactus, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and modified leaves. The plant is adapted to the arid and semi-arid climate of its native habitat, exhibiting features such as succulent tissues for water storage. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding a striking visual element to the landscape.

Fagonia socotrana is also notable for its role in the local ecosystem and its status as a rare, endemic species.
