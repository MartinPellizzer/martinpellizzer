Forsythia suspensa f. atrocaulis

Forsythia suspensa f. atrocaulis, commonly known as the blackstem forsythia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black stems that contrast with its bright yellow, pendulous flowers. The plant produces showy, raceme-like clusters of flowers in early spring, typically before the leaves emerge. Its foliage consists of narrow, oval leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it a popular choice in gardens and landscapes.
