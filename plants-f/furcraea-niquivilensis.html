Furcraea niquivilensis

Furcraea niquivilensis, commonly known as the Niquivilensis spinefoot, is a succulent plant native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or dry forest habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
