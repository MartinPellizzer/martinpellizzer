Flindersia strzeleckiana var. latifolia

Flindersia strzeleckiana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved flindersia, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
