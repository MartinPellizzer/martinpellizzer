Faurea argentea, commonly known as silver tree or silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Proteaceae.

It is native to the coastal regions of South Africa, particularly in the Western Cape. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. It produces clusters of small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in spring.
