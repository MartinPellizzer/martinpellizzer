Ferulago cinerea

Ferulago cinerea, commonly known as gray ferula, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a tall, slender stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a panicle inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, grayish-white down.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
