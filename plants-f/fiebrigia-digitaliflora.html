Fiebrigia digitaliflora

Fiebrigia digitaliflora, commonly known as the digitate lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Fiebrigia digitaliflora is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
