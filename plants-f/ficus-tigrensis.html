Ficus tigrensis

Ficus tigrensis, commonly known as the Trinidad fig, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are usually oval-shaped with a pointed tip. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits.

The Trinidad fig is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food in its native habitat.
