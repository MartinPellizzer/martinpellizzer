Ficus bukitrayaensis

Ficus bukitrayaensis, commonly known as the Bukitraya fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, clustered figs that are enclosed in a syconium, a unique structure typical of fig species.

Ficus bukitrayaensis is often found in lowland rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
