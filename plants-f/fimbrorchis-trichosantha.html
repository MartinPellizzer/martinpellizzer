Fimbrorchis trichosantha

Fimbrorchis trichosantha, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Fimbrorchis trichosantha is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
