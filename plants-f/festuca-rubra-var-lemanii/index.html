Festuca Rubra Var. Lemanii

Festuca rubra var. lemanii is a hardy, low-growing grass that thrives in moist, shady environments. Known for its fine texture and dense growth habit, it is often used in landscaping and erosion control. This variety is particularly valued for its ability to form lush, carpet-like lawns in challenging conditions. It is native to parts of Europe and has become a popular choice in garden design. Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a versatile plant for both natural and cultivated settings.
