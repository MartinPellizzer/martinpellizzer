Fumaria ajmasiana

Fumaria ajmasiana, commonly known as the Ajmasian smokeplant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish or greenish flowers that resemble smoke. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Native to Central Asia, it thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its delicate appearance and unique floral structure make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
