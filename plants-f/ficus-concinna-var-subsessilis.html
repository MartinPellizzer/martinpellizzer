Ficus concinna var. subsessilis

Ficus concinna var. subsessilis, commonly known as the small-leaf fig, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, followed by edible figs that are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
