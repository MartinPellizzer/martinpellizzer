Forsythia suspensa var. latifolia

Forsythia suspensa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and profuse display of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
