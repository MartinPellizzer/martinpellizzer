Flemingia lineata var. lineata

Flemingia lineata var. lineata, commonly known as the slender flemingia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
