Faujasia squamosa

Faujasia squamosa, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool and moist climates. The plant features a rosette of fleshy, succulent leaves that are covered in a waxy, scaly coating. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white, pink, or purple.

Faujasia squamosa is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
