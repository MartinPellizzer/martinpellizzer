Forestiera selleana

Forestiera selleana, commonly known as the Selleana forestiera, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a dense canopy that offers habitat for small wildlife.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
