Fumaria rupestris var. subalata

Fumaria rupestris var. subalata, commonly known as the alpine yellow rocket, is a low-growing perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cool, high-altitude environments.

This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and pale yellow floral coloration.
