Festuca Riloensis

Festuca riloensis is a rare grass species native to the mountainous regions of Bulgaria. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments. This plant is known for its delicate blue-green foliage and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species facing habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve its unique ecological role.
