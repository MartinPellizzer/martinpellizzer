Ficus oleracea var. pugnans

Ficus oleracea var. pugnans, commonly known as the fighting fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and can grow as an upright shrub or small tree. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
