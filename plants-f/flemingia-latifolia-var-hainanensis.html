Flemingia latifolia var. hainanensis

Flemingia latifolia var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan Flemingia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of China, particularly Hainan Island.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, woody pods containing flat, seed-like structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
