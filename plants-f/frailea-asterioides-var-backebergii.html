Frailea asterioides var. backebergii

Frailea asterioides var. backebergii, commonly known as the Backeberg frailea, is a compact, cespitose cactus native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of short, cylindrical stems that are ribbed and often covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
