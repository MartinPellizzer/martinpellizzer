Fagus cochinchinensis

Fagus cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Chinese beech, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple, ovate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green catkins and is notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
