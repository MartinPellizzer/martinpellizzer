Forsythia togashii

Forsythia togashii, commonly known as the Japanese forsythia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, funnel-shaped flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Forsythia togashii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
