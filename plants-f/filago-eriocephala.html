Filago eriocephala

Filago eriocephala, commonly known as the woolly filago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a hairy stem and leaves, and its flowers are typically yellow or pale green. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Filago eriocephala is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
