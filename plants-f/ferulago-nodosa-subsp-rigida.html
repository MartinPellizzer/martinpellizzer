Ferulago nodosa subsp. rigida

Ferulago nodosa subsp.

rigida, commonly known as rigid nodding ferulago, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features erect, branched stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
