Fischeria martiana

Fischeria martiana, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Fischeria martiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
