Fragaria pistillaris

Fragaria pistillaris, commonly known as the slender strawberry, is a flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, edible berries that are typically red in color and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces white flowers with five petals. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in open, sunny areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject in both horticulture and ecological studies.
