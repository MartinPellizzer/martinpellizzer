Felicia amoena subsp. stricta

Felicia amoena subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf felicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
