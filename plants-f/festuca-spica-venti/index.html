Festuca Spica-Venti

Festuca spica-venti is a unique grass species known for its distinctive wind-swept appearance. This plant thrives in open, windy environments where it adapts to frequent air movement. Its narrow leaves and tufted growth pattern make it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. The name "spica-venti" translates to "wind spike," reflecting its characteristic form. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
