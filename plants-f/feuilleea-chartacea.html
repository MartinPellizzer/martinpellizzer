Feuilleea chartacea

Feuilleea chartacea, commonly known as the parchment leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its thin, papery leaves that resemble parchment.

The leaves are typically light green to pale yellow, with a smooth, waxy texture that helps reduce water loss. It is native to arid regions and thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Its slow growth and distinctive foliage make it a popular choice for low-maintenance indoor gardening.
