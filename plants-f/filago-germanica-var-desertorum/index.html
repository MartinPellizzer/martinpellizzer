Filago Germanica Var. Desertorum

Filago germanica var. desertorum is a resilient plant species adapted to harsh, arid environments. It thrives in sandy soils and is commonly found in desert regions across central and southern Europe. This variant of the common filago exhibits unique morphological traits that help it survive extreme conditions. Its small, fluffy seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize new areas efficiently. Despite its tough environment, this plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
