Festuca brevissima f. pallida

Festuca brevissima f. pallida, commonly known as the pale festuca, is a low-growing grass species characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cold, alpine environments. The plant typically forms dense mats, with narrow leaves and small, pale blue-green inflorescences. It is well adapted to harsh conditions, including poor soil and high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental appearance and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
