Ficinia Bicolor

Ficinia bicolor is a rare and unique plant native to the remote regions of New Zealand. It is known for its striking bi-color leaves, which display a combination of green and white hues. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular subject among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, Ficinia bicolor is considered a species of conservation concern.
