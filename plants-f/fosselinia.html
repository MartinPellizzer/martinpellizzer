Fosselinia

Fosselinia, commonly known as the glassy fan palm, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tree-like structure with a single trunk and a crown of feather-like leaves. The plant's leaves are typically light green and have a waxy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Fosselinia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
