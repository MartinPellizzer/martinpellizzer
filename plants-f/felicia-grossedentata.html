Felicia grossedentata

Felicia grossedentata, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Felicia grossedentata is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
