Ficus pisifera var. obscura

Ficus pisifera var. obscura, commonly known as the dark fig tree, is a variant of the Ficus pisifera species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant is a deciduous tree that can grow quite large, often reaching heights of several meters. Its fruit, which is a small, edible fig, is produced in clusters and is an important food source for various animals.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some regions for their purported health benefits.
