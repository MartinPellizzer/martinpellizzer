Festuca gracillima var. gracillima

Festuca gracillima var. gracillima, commonly known as slender fescue, is a low-growing grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and dense, mat-forming growth habit. This plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. It is drought-tolerant and can withstand harsh weather conditions, making it a hardy ornamental and ecological plant.

Its small, spike-like inflorescences produce light blue to purple flowers, adding visual interest in late summer.
