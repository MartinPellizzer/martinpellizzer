Ficus cannonii

Ficus cannonii, commonly known as the Cannon's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the family Moraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Thailand and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are usually oval in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig-like fruits.

Ficus cannonii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
