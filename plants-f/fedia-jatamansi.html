Fedia jatamansi

Fedia jatamansi, commonly known as Indian valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, opposite leaves and clusters of small, white to greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments at high altitudes. Its rhizomes and roots are the primary parts used in traditional medicine for their calming properties.

Fedia jatamansi is valued for its essential oils and is often used in herbal remedies for anxiety and sleep disorders.
