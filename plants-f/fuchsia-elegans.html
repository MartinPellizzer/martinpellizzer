Fuchsia elegans

Fuchsia elegans, commonly known as the elegant fuchsia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep pink to violet. The plant produces small, oval berries that are typically orange or red when mature. Fuchsia elegans thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses due to its striking floral display.
