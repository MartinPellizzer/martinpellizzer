Francoa alba

Francoa alba, commonly known as white francoa, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage is broad, lance-shaped, and often has a bluish-green hue.

Francoa alba is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
