Fagraea congesta

Fagraea congesta, commonly known as the clustered fagraea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
