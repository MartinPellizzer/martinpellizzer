Festuca Maritima Subsp. Hemipoa

Festuca maritima subsp. hemipoa is a hardy grass species native to coastal regions. It thrives in salty and windy environments, often found along shorelines and dunes. This subspecies is known for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its dense growth pattern makes it an important plant for coastal ecosystems. Despite its resilience, it remains relatively obscure in botanical discussions.
