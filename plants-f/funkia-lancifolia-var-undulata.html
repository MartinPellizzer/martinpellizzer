Funkia lancifolia var. undulata

Funkia lancifolia var. undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf daylily, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a wavy margin, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms in garden settings.
