Fimbristylis insignis

Fimbristylis insignis, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats. The plant has grass-like leaves that are usually narrow and tapering. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flower clusters.

Fimbristylis insignis is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in wetlands and along water edges.
