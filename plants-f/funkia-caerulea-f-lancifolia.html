Funkia caerulea f. lancifolia

Funkia caerulea f. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf daylily, is a cultivated form of the daylily plant.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep blue. The plant is a perennial herbaceous species native to East Asia, particularly China. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and water gardens.
