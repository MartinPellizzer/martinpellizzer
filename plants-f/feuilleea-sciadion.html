Feuilleea sciadion

Feuilleea sciadion, commonly known as the shadow leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

It possesses broad, waxy leaves that reflect sunlight, aiding in its survival in shaded environments. The plant's foliage is often a deep green, with some cultivars displaying variegated patterns of white and yellow. Feuilleea sciadion is native to tropical regions and prefers moist, well-draining soil.

Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a compact, bushy growth habit.
