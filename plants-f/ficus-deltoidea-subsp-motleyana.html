Ficus deltoidea subsp. motleyana

Ficus deltoidea subsp.

motleyana, commonly known as the Motley's fig, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia.

The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The leaves are glossy and have a smooth texture, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
