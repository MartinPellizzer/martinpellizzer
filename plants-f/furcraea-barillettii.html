Furcraea barillettii

Furcraea barillettii, commonly known as the Barillettii sword fern, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish-green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
