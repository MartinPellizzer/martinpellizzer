Ficus gomelleira

Ficus gomelleira, commonly known as the Brazilian fig tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Moraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible fig fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ficus gomelleira is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.
