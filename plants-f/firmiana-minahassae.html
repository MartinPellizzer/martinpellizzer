Firmiana minahassae

Firmiana minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically bright green and have a distinctive, almost feather-like appearance. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant splash of color to its surroundings. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used in traditional crafts and construction.
