Felicia monticola

Felicia monticola, commonly known as the mountain felicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it produces small, narrow leaves.

Felicia monticola is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
