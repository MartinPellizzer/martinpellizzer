Ficus pisifera var. microtus

Ficus pisifera var. microtus, commonly known as the dwarf fig tree, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are usually green to yellowish in hue. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
