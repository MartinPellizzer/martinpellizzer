Fumaria officinalis var. pycnantha

Fumaria officinalis var. pycnantha, commonly known as the clustered fumitory, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces elongated, ribbed seed pods that contain numerous tiny, brown seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along roadsides.

The species is known for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
