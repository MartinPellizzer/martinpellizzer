13 Best Benefits Of Corydalis Yanhusuo

Corydalis yanhusuo, also known as Yangonese Corydalis or Yanhusuo, is a species of flowering plant in the poppy family that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

The bioactive compounds responsible for its benefits include tetrahydroprotoberberine alkaloids, such as tetrahydroberberine and columbamine, as well as other compounds like protopine and allocryptopine.

Corydalis yanhusuo has been shown to reduce pain by inhibiting pain pathways in the body, relieve anxiety by affecting the brain's neurotransmitters, and decrease inflammation by suppressing the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes.

These properties make Corydalis yanhusuo a promising natural remedy for managing pain, anxiety, and inflammation.
