cassia senna uses

constipation

Cassia senna is a natural laxative derived from the leaves of the cassia tree, traditionally used to treat constipation and promote bowel regularity. The senna content in cassia senna works by stimulating the muscles in the intestines to contract and move stool through the digestive system, providing relief from constipation. However, long-term use of cassia senna can lead to dependence and is generally recommended for short-term relief under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

constipation

high blood pressure

Cassia senna is a natural herb that has been traditionally used to help lower high blood pressure due to its sennoside content, which helps to relax blood vessels and improve circulation. Studies have shown that cassia senna can also help to reduce inflammation and improve overall cardiovascular health. However, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using cassia senna, as its effects may interact with certain medications or worsen underlying health conditions.

high blood pressure

sore throat

Cassia senna is a natural herbal remedy that has been traditionally used to alleviate sore throats due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. The anthraquinones present in cassia senna help to reduce swelling and ease pain, providing quick relief from a sore throat. By consuming cassia senna tea or taking it in supplement form, individuals can promote a healthy recovery and prevent further irritation to the throat.
