Silybum marianum, also known as milk thistle, is a flowering plant in the daisy family native to the Mediterranean region.

The health benefits of milk thistle include protecting the liver from toxins, improving liver function, and reducing inflammation, making it a popular natural remedy for conditions such as liver disease, jaundice, and cirrhosis.

Milk thistle is often prepared as a herbal supplement in capsule or tablet form, and can also be found in teas, tinctures, and extracts.
