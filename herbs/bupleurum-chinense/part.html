11 Bupleurum Chinense Best Medicinal Parts

1. Root

Bupleurum chinense root is a traditional medicinal herb used in Chinese medicine, known for its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. The root is rich in saikosaponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects, including reducing fever and alleviating pain. Bupleurum chinense root is often used in combination with other herbs to treat various health conditions, such as colds, flu, and digestive issues.

2. Whole Plant

Bupleurum chinense whole plant is used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antipyretic, and analgesic properties. The plant's roots are particularly valued for their ability to reduce fever and alleviate pain, while its leaves and stems are used to treat various respiratory issues. The whole plant is also employed to treat various gastrointestinal problems, including diarrhea and indigestion, due to its anti-inflammatory and soothing effects.

3. Leaf

Bupleurum chinense leaf is primarily used in traditional medicine due to its flavonoids and alkaloids, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The leaf is believed to have a regulating effect on the body's yin and yang balance, making it useful in treating conditions such as fever and sore throats. In addition, it is also used to treat various gastrointestinal issues, including diarrhea and indigestion.

4. Rhizome
