peral

Allium sativum peral is a variety of garlic known for its distinctive flavor and aroma, often used in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisine. It is typically harvested when mature, with the bulbs being separated and dried to preserve their flavor and nutritional properties. Peral garlic is prized for its pungency and is often used in traditional medicine, where it is valued for its potential health benefits and antibacterial properties.
