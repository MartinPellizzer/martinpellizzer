Centella asiatica (gotu kola)

What is Centella asiatica?

Centella asiatica, commonly known as gotu kola, is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Apiaceae and is a member of the genus Centella.

It is a perennial herb native to Asia and is also known as Indian pennywort.

The taxonomy of C. asiatica places it within the kingdom Plantae, class Magnoliopsida, order Apiales, and family Apiaceae.
