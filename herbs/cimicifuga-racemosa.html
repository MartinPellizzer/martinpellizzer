Cimicifuga racemosa (black cohosh)

What is Cimicifuga racemosa?

Cimicifuga racemosa, commonly known as black cohosh, is a perennial plant classified within the family Ranunculaceae.

This species is further grouped into the genus Cimicifuga, which is part of the order Ranunculales and the subclass Magnoliidae.

Taxonomically, it is placed in the kingdom Plantae and phylum Magnoliophyta.
