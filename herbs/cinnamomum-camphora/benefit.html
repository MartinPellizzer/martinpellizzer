9 Best Benefits Of Cinnamomum Camphora

Cinnamomum camphora, also known as camphor tree or gajabhar, is an evergreen tree native to Asia and Australia, known for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

The bioactive compounds present in this plant, including camphor, borneol, and alpha-terpineol, have been found to possess various health benefits.

The camphor in Cinnamomum camphora has been shown to reduce inflammation and relieve fever by its antipyretic and anti-inflammatory properties, while the borneol has been found to have a calming effect, helping to heal anxiety and promote relaxation.

Additionally, the alpha-terpineol in this plant has been known to exhibit antimicrobial and antiviral properties, further enhancing its medicinal value.
