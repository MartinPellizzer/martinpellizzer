crocus sativus preparations

extract

Crocus sativus extract is a natural compound derived from the saffron flower, known for its vibrant yellow-orange color and numerous health benefits. Rich in antioxidants and carotenoids, it has been traditionally used in medicine and culinary applications, particularly in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine. The extract has also been studied for its potential anti-inflammatory and neuroprotective properties, making it a popular ingredient in modern health supplements.

extract

cream

Crocus sativus cream is a luxurious and highly prized skincare product derived from the delicate flowers of the saffron crocus plant. Rich in anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, this cream is said to have numerous benefits for the skin, including reducing fine lines and wrinkles, soothing redness and irritation, and promoting a radiant and youthful complexion. Its unique blend of natural ingredients and high concentration of saffron extract make it a sought-after treatment for those seeking a natural and effective approach to skincare.

cream

oil

Crocus sativus oil, also known as safflower oil, is a highly prized and expensive oil extracted from the dried stigmas of the saffron crocus flower. Rich in antioxidants and carotenoids, this oil has been traditionally used to color and flavor various foods, as well as in traditional medicine to promote relaxation and reduce inflammation. Its distinct golden hue and earthy aroma make it a sought-after ingredient in perfumery and aromatherapy applications.

oil

capsule

Crocus sativus capsule is a dried, seed-filled pod derived from the saffron crocus plant, primarily cultivated for its highly valued stigmas. The capsule contains numerous tiny, black seeds and is typically harvested when mature and dry, to ensure the seeds are not exposed to moisture, which can cause them to germinate. The seeds within the capsule are an important component of the saffron crocus plant's life cycle, as they hold the potential for growth and the continuation of the species.

capsule
