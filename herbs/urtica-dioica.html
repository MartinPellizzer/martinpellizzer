Urtica Dioica: Complete Medicinal Profile

Urtica dioica, also known as common nettle or stinging nettle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and Asia.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its anti-inflammatory and antihistamine effects, which can be attributed to its high content of vitamins A, C, and K, as well as minerals like calcium and iron.

The health benefits of Urtica dioica include relief from arthritis, digestive issues, and seasonal allergies, making it a popular herbal remedy.

Urtica dioica can be prepared in various forms, including tea, capsules, and infusions, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties.
