valeriana officinalis preparations

tincture

Valeriana officinalis tincture is a natural remedy derived from the root of the valerian plant, known for its calming and sedative properties. It is often used to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and improve sleep quality by interacting with the body's GABA receptors and promoting a sense of tranquility. Valeriana officinalis tincture can be taken sublingually or added to water as a soothing tea to help alleviate symptoms of insomnia, restlessness, and stress.

tincture

extract

Valeriana officinalis extract is a natural remedy derived from the roots of the valerian plant, commonly used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality due to its sedative properties. Studies have shown that this extract contains valerenic acid and isovaleric acid, which contribute to its calming effects on the nervous system. Valeriana officinalis extract is often used as a dietary supplement or added to herbal teas to alleviate anxiety and promote a restful night's sleep.
