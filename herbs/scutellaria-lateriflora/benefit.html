9 Best Benefits Of Scutellaria Lateriflora

Scutellaria lateriflora, also known as skullcap, is a perennial herb that belongs to the mint family and is native to North America.

The bioactive compounds found in Scutellaria lateriflora, including flavonoids and phenolic acids, are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

The plant has been traditionally used to reduce anxiety by promoting relaxation and calming effects, alleviate depression by regulating mood and emotions, and decrease stress by mitigating its negative effects on the body.

These benefits are attributed to the plant's ability to modulate the activity of neurotransmitters and its antioxidant properties, making it a popular natural remedy for mental health and well-being.
