Astragalus membranaceus (huang qi)

What is Astragalus membranaceus?

Astragalus membranaceus, commonly known as Huang Qi, is a plant species that belongs to the family Fabaceae and is classified within the genus Astragalus.

It is further grouped into the subfamily Faboideae and the tribe Galegeae.

At the species level, Astragalus membranaceus is often distinguished from its closely related species, Astragalus membranaceus var. mongholicus and Astragalus membranaceus var. mongholicus var. membranaceus.
