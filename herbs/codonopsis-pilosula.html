Side Effects

Codonopsis pilosula side effects are often observed in individuals who consume it in excess or for extended periods.

These side effects include gastrointestinal issues such as nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and stomach cramps, as well as headaches, dizziness, and fatigue.

People with pre-existing conditions like bleeding disorders, high blood pressure, and kidney or liver disease are more likely to experience these side effects, as well as pregnant or breastfeeding women who should be cautious due to potential interactions with their child's development.

Precautions should also be taken by individuals with allergies to plants in the Campanulaceae family, as well as those who are prone to sedation or have a history of respiratory problems.
