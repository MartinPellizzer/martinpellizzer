artemisia absinthium preparations

tincture

Artemisia absinthium tincture is a potent herbal remedy made from the leaves of the wormwood plant, traditionally used in folk medicine for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties. The tincture is often used to alleviate digestive issues, such as indigestion and nausea, and can also be used topically to treat skin irritations and wounds. However, it is essential to use artemisia absinthium tincture with caution, as excessive consumption can be toxic due to its high thujone content.
