Polygonum Multiflorum: Complete Medicinal Profile

Polygonum multiflorum, also known as He Shou Wu, is a perennial plant species native to East Asia, belonging to the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae).

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries, valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-aging effects.

The health benefits of Polygonum multiflorum include improving cognitive function, promoting hair growth, and enhancing overall well-being.

He Shou Wu is often prepared as a decoction, powder, or tablet, and can also be consumed as a dietary supplement to harness its potential health benefits.
