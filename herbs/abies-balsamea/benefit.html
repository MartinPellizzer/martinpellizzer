13 Best Benefits Of Abies Balsamea

The bioactive compounds present in Abies balsamea, including terpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant has been found to reduce inflammation, promote wound healing, and alleviate respiratory problems, such as bronchitis and asthma, due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.

The terpene and flavonoid content in Abies balsamea also exhibits antimicrobial and antioxidant activities, contributing to its overall therapeutic benefits.
