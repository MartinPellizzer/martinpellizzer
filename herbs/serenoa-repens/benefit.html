11 Best Benefits Of Serenoa Repens

Serenoa repens, also known as saw palmetto, is a small, shrub-like plant native to the southeastern United States.

The bioactive compounds found in Serenoa repens, including fatty acids, plant sterols, and polyphenols, are responsible for its potential health benefits.

Research has shown that Serenoa repens can treat benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), improve urinary flow, and support prostate health due to its ability to inhibit the conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT), a hormone that contributes to BPH development.

By alleviating these symptoms, Serenoa repens may provide relief for individuals experiencing BPH-related issues.

Featured Study

According to Public health nutrition, Serenoa repens, extracted from the saw palmetto, has been found to decrease nocturia (reducing the number of nighttime urinations) and improve peak urine flow, as well as increase the improvement of urinary tract symptoms in men with symptomatic benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) compared to those receiving placebo.
