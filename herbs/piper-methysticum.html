Piper methysticum, also known as kava, is a plant native to the Pacific Island region.

It is known for its medicinal properties, which include pain relief, anxiety reduction, and muscle relaxation due to the presence of kavalactones.

The health benefits of kava include improved sleep quality, reduced stress levels, and relief from symptoms of depression and anxiety.

Kava is traditionally consumed in the Pacific Islands as a tea or in herbal preparations such as tinctures, capsules, and topical creams, although its use is subject to regulations and recommended dosages.
