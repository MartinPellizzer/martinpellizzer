Side Effects

Dioscorea villosa side effects are diverse and can be severe in certain individuals.

Additionally, people with kidney or liver problems may experience complications such as kidney damage, liver failure, and electrolyte imbalances due to the plant's potential to cause dehydration and electrolyte loss.

Precautions should be taken to avoid consuming Dioscorea villosa in large quantities or by individuals with pre-existing conditions, especially those taking blood thinners, diabetes medications, or potassium supplements.

  • Dizziness and lightheadedness: Consuming large amounts of the plant may cause dizziness and lightheadedness due to its effect on blood pressure and circulation.

  • Skin rash: A skin rash may develop due to an allergic reaction or sensitivity to the plant's compounds.
  • Urination problems: The plant may cause increased urination due to its diuretic properties, leading to dehydration if not balanced with adequate fluid intake.
