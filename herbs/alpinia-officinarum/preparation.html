alpinia officinarum preparations

extract

Alpinia officinarum extract is a derivative of the Alpinia officinarum plant, commonly known as Galangal, which is native to Southeast Asia and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. The extract is rich in bioactive compounds, including gingerols and shogaols, which have been found to possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Research has also suggested that Alpinia officinarum extract may have potential in supporting digestive health and reducing symptoms of nausea and vomiting.

extract

tincture

Alpinia officinarum tincture is a homeopathic remedy derived from the root of the Galangal plant, traditionally used to treat digestive issues and nausea. It is often used to alleviate symptoms associated with motion sickness and morning sickness during pregnancy. The tincture is typically taken in small doses, and its effectiveness may vary depending on individual circumstances and medical conditions.

tincture

Additionally, alpinia officinarum oil has a distinctive, pungent aroma and is often used in perfumery and culinary applications due to its unique flavor profile.
