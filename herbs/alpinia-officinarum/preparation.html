1. Extract

Alpinia officinarum extract is derived from the rhizomes of the Galangal plant, a species native to Southeast Asia, and has been traditionally used for its medicinal and culinary properties. Studies have shown that alpinia officinarum extract may also possess antioxidant and antiseptic properties, making it a promising natural remedy for various health conditions.
