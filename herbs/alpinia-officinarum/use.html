alpinia officinarum uses

common cold

Alpinia officinarum is a plant-based remedy that has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of the common cold, such as congestion and cough. The essential oil extracted from its rhizomes is often used in aromatherapy to help ease respiratory issues and promote relaxation. However, its effectiveness in treating the common cold is largely anecdotal and requires further scientific research for confirmation.
