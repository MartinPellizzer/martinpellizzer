9 Best Benefits Of Crataegus Oxyacantha

Crataegus oxyacantha, also known as the common hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe, western Asia, and northwestern Africa.

The bioactive compounds found in Crataegus oxyacantha, such as flavonoids, oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs), and triterpenoids, are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Studies have shown that consuming Crataegus oxyacantha extracts can help reduce blood pressure, improve cardiovascular health by preventing the formation of blood clots and reducing inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant properties, which help protect against cell damage caused by free radicals.

Overall, the benefits of Crataegus oxyacantha make it a popular herbal remedy for cardiovascular health and other conditions.

1. Reduces blood pressure

Crataegus oxyacantha reduces blood pressure by lowering systolic and diastolic blood pressure levels in the body, resulting in a healthier cardiovascular system.

This benefit is important because high blood pressure is a major risk factor for heart disease, stroke, and kidney disease, which can lead to disability and even death if left unmanaged.

The reduction of blood pressure in Crataegus oxyacantha is attributed to its bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which have been shown to exhibit vasodilatory and antioxidant properties.

To take advantage of this benefit, Crataegus oxyacantha can be consumed as a tea or supplement, with a standard dosage of 250-500 mg of dried extract per day, under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
