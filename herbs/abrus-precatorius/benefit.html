The abrus precatorius plant is believed to exhibit this benefit due to its rich content of bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which have been shown to possess vasodilatory and antioxidant properties, helping to lower blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health.
