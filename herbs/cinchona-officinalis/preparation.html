cinchona officinalis preparations

extract

Cinchona officinalis extract is a natural alkaloid derived from the bark of the Cinchona tree, native to the Andean region of South America. This extract has been used for centuries to treat various health conditions, including malaria, due to its quinine content, which has potent antimalarial and anti-inflammatory properties. Cinchona officinalis extract is still used today in some herbal remedies and pharmaceutical applications, offering a natural alternative for managing certain health issues.

extract

elixir

Cinchona officinalis elixir is a traditional herbal remedy derived from the bark of the Cinchona tree, native to the Andean region of South America. This elixir is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to prevent and treat malaria, due to the presence of quinine, a natural compound with antimalarial and anti-inflammatory effects. For centuries, Cinchona officinalis elixir has been used to alleviate fevers and other health issues associated with malaria, earning it a reputation as a valuable natural remedy.
