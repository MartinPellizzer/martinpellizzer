7 Best Benefits Of Ammi Visnaga

Ammi visnaga, also known as the toothpick plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, known for its medicinal properties.

The bioactive compounds present in Ammi visnaga, including sesquiterpenes and coumarins, particularly umbelliferone and bergapten, contribute to its therapeutic benefits.

These compounds have been shown to relieve arthritis, reduce inflammation, and alleviate pain by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and modulating the immune response.

By harnessing the anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties of Ammi visnaga, individuals can potentially find relief from chronic conditions and improve their overall quality of life.
