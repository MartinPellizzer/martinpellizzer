Azadirachta indica (neem)

What is Azadirachta indica?

Azadirachta indica, commonly known as Neem, is a plant species that belongs to the family Meliaceae and is classified within the genus Azadirachta.

The classification of Neem follows the ranks: Kingdom: Plantae, Clade: Angiosperms, Clade: Eudicots, Clade: Sapindales, Family: Meliaceae, Genus: Azadirachta, and Species: A. indica.

This classification reflects the plant's evolutionary relationships and places it within the larger family of Meliaceae, which consists of over 600 species of flowering plants.
