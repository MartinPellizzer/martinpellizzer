aesculus hippocastanum preparations

extract

Aesculus hippocastanum extract is a concentrated form of the horse chestnut plant, derived from the seeds and leaves. It is commonly used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which are believed to aid in the treatment of conditions such as varicose veins and swelling. Additionally, the extract has been studied for its potential in supporting cardiovascular health and reducing the risk of oxidative stress.

extract

tincture

Aesculus hippocastanum tincture is a herbal remedy derived from the seeds of the horse chestnut tree, traditionally used to treat various health issues including inflammation, swelling, and circulatory problems. This tincture is believed to possess anti-inflammatory properties, which may help alleviate symptoms such as edema and varicose veins. When used under the guidance of a healthcare professional, aesculus hippocastanum tincture may offer relief from these conditions, promoting overall vascular health and well-being.

tincture

cream

Aesculus hippocastanum cream is a topical treatment derived from the seed oil of the horse chestnut tree, which has been used for centuries to soothe and calm skin irritations. It is known for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it an effective remedy for conditions such as eczema, acne, and minor wounds. Regular application of aesculus hippocastanum cream can help to reduce redness, itching, and swelling, promoting a smoother and healthier appearance of the skin.

cream

Rich in anti-inflammatory properties, it is often used in aromatherapy to soothe and calm the mind and body, reducing stress and promoting relaxation. capsule

Aesculus hippocastanum capsule is a distinctive and intriguing feature of the horse chestnut tree, characterized by its spiky, green or brown coating and containing 2-4 large seeds. The capsule itself is typically 5-10 cm in length, and it splits open when mature to reveal the seeds within. After splitting, the capsule dries and persists on the tree for some time, serving as a reminder of the horse chestnut's unique reproductive cycle.

capsule

gum

Aesculus hippocastanum gum is a type of resin obtained from the horse chestnut tree, primarily used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive. It has been employed in various applications, including wound healing, anti-inflammatory treatments, and as a fixative in perfumes and fragrances. The gum's properties make it a valuable ingredient in the production of skincare products and pharmaceuticals.

gum
