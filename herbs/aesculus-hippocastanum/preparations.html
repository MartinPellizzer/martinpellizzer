aesculus hippocastanum preparations

tablet

Aesculus hippocastanum is a herbal tablet derived from the horse chestnut plant, commonly used to treat and prevent varicose veins and swelling. The active ingredients in these tablets work to reduce inflammation and improve circulation, providing relief from painful symptoms. Aesculus hippocastanum tablets are often recommended as a natural alternative to pharmaceutical treatments for circulatory issues.

lotion

Aesculus hippocastanum is a key ingredient in various skincare products, known for its soothing and moisturizing properties. This extract from the horse chestnut tree is often used to calm irritated skin and reduce inflammation, making it a popular choice for those with sensitive skin. When formulated into a lotion, aesculus hippocastanum can provide long-lasting hydration and comfort to dry, itchy skin.

capsule

Aesculus hippocastanum is a deciduous tree commonly known as the horse chestnut, and its capsule is a spiky, green fruit that contains several seeds. The capsule typically dries and turns brown as it matures, often persisting on the tree throughout the winter months. Once the capsule has opened, it releases its seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals, allowing the tree to propagate and spread.
