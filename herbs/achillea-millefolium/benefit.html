13 Best Benefits Of Achillea Millefolium

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine.

The bioactive compounds present in yarrow, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and sesquiterpenes, are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Yarrow has been found to reduce inflammation, ease pain, and soothe digestion due to its anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, which are attributed to the presence of compounds such as apigenin and chamazulene.

These benefits make yarrow a valuable herb for addressing various health issues and promoting overall well-being.
