angelica sinensis preparations

extract

Angelica sinensis extract is a traditional herbal remedy derived from the roots of the angelica plant, native to China and used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries. Its bioactive compounds, including ferulic acid and ligustilide, have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-aging properties, making it a popular ingredient in skincare and wellness products. Studies have also suggested that angelica sinensis extract may have neuroprotective effects, potentially helping to prevent or alleviate conditions such as dementia and Alzheimer's disease.

extract

tincture

Angelica sinensis tincture is a concentrated herbal extract derived from the roots of the dong quai plant, traditionally used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote women's health and balance the body's energy. Rich in bioactive compounds, it is believed to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-anxiety properties, making it a popular natural remedy for menstrual issues and stress relief. When taken as directed, the tincture may help alleviate symptoms such as cramps, mood swings, and fatigue associated with the menstrual cycle.

infusion

Angelica sinensis infusion is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy made from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant, also known as dong quai, which has been used for centuries to promote women's health and balance the body's energy. The infusion is often consumed as a tea, typically steeped in hot water for 5-10 minutes, and is believed to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that can help to alleviate menstrual cramps and other symptoms associated with PMS. Regular consumption of Angelica sinensis infusion is also thought to support overall well-being and vitality, particularly in women during menopause.
