angelica sinensis preparations

extract

Angelica sinensis extract is a traditional herbal remedy derived from the roots of the angelica plant, native to China and used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries. Its bioactive compounds, including ferulic acid and ligustilide, have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-aging properties, making it a popular ingredient in skincare and wellness products. Studies have also suggested that angelica sinensis extract may have neuroprotective effects, potentially helping to prevent or alleviate conditions such as dementia and Alzheimer's disease.

extract
