13 Best Benefits Of Rehmannia Glutinosa

Rehmannia glutinosa, also known as Chinese foxglove or di huang, is a plant that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

The bioactive compounds present in Rehmannia glutinosa, including polysaccharides, alkaloids, and saponins, are responsible for its medicinal properties.

These compounds have been found to reduce inflammation, soothe digestive issues, and help lower blood pressure due to their anti-inflammatory, prebiotic, and vasodilatory effects.

By reducing inflammation and promoting a healthy digestive system, Rehmannia glutinosa may also help alleviate symptoms of various diseases and conditions.

This page lists the most important health benefits of Rehmannia glutinosa.

1. Reduces inflammation

Rehmannia glutinosa reduces inflammation by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes and cytokines, thereby decreasing redness, swelling, and pain in affected areas.

This benefit is important as chronic inflammation is a major risk factor for various diseases, such as arthritis, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease, which can lead to symptoms like joint pain, numbness, and increased risk of heart attacks.

The anti-inflammatory properties of Rehmannia glutinosa are attributed to its bioactive constituents, including iridoid glycosides, such as rehmannioside A and B, and polysaccharides, which have been shown to modulate the immune response and reduce inflammation.

To harness the anti-inflammatory benefits of Rehmannia glutinosa, it is best to use it as a decoction or tea, or in combination with other herbs in a formula, as these preparations have been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to reduce inflammation and promote healing.
