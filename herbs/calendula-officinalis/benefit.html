13 Best Benefits of Calendula Officinalis

1. Reduces Inflammation

Calendula officinalis reduces inflammation by promoting the healing of wounds and injuries, while also soothing redness and discomfort in the affected area. The anti-inflammatory properties in calendula officinalis, specifically triterpenoids and flavonoids, work to reduce swelling and ease pain. This makes calendula officinalis an effective natural remedy for treating various inflammatory conditions, including eczema, acne, and sunburn.

2. Has Anti-Inflammatory Effects

Calendula officinalis has anti-inflammatory effects, which make it a valuable natural remedy for soothing and calming irritated skin and reducing redness and swelling. The anti-inflammatory properties of calendula can also help to alleviate joint pain and muscle soreness, making it a popular choice for treating conditions like arthritis and sprains. Additionally, calendula's anti-inflammatory effects can aid in the healing process of wounds by promoting tissue repair and reducing inflammation.

3. Has Antioxidant Properties

Calendula officinalis has antioxidant properties that help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals, which can contribute to signs of aging and skin conditions. These antioxidant properties also aid in reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing, making calendula a valuable ingredient in skincare products. Additionally, the antioxidants in calendula may help to neutralize oxidative stress, which can contribute to various chronic diseases.

4. Fights Cancer Cells

Additionally, calendula officinalis has been found to induce apoptosis (cell death) in cancer cells, further supporting its potential as an anticancer agent.

Regular consumption of calendula officinalis may help to reduce blood pressure and improve overall cardiovascular health.
