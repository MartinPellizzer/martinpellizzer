9 Atropa Belladonna Best Medicinal Parts

1. Root

Atropa belladonna root, also known as deadly nightshade root, has been used in traditional medicine for its antispasmodic and sedative properties. However, it's essential to note that Atropa belladonna root can be highly toxic and should only be used under the guidance of a qualified medical professional.

2. Fruit

Atropa belladonna fruit, commonly known as deadly nightshade berries, are highly toxic and contain the tropane alkaloids scopolamine and atropine. These compounds can cause a range of symptoms including hallucinations, delirium, and tachycardia due to their anticholinergic effects. However, in small, carefully controlled quantities, the fruit is sometimes used in traditional medicine to treat conditions such as spasms and cramps.

5. Stem

However, the stem's medicinal value must be carefully balanced due to its potential toxicity and risk of overdose.
