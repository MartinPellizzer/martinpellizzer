Chamomilla recutita (german chamomile)

What is Chamomilla recutita?

Chamomilla recutita, commonly known as German chamomile, is a flowering plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is classified within the genus Matricaria, which is a group of daisy-like plants.

The species recutita is further distinguished from other related species within the genus, such as Matricaria chamomilla.
