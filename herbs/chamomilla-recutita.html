Chamomilla recutita, also known as German chamomile, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family that is native to Europe and Western Asia.

The health benefits of Chamomilla recutita include reducing stress and anxiety, promoting relaxation, and soothing digestive issues, as well as alleviating symptoms of menstrual cramps and insomnia.

Side Effects

Chamomilla recutita side effects are diverse and can be experienced by individuals who consume it in large quantities or have sensitive skin.

Common side effects include dizziness, nausea, and allergic reactions such as hives, itching, and swelling, particularly among those who are prone to allergies.

Pregnant women, children, and individuals with liver or kidney diseases are also more likely to experience side effects like stomach upset, diarrhea, and increased heart rate.

Precautions include taking the recommended dosage, avoiding consumption if you have a history of allergies, and using diluted chamomile tea or topical creams to minimize the risk of adverse reactions.

The possible side effects of Chamomilla recutita are listed below.

  • Allergic reactions: Some people may be allergic to chamomile and experience symptoms such as hives, itching, and difficulty breathing.
  • Dizziness and lightheadedness: Chamomile can cause dizziness and lightheadedness, especially when taken in large doses or combined with other medications.
  • Digestive issues: Chamomile may cause stomach upset, nausea, and diarrhea in some individuals, especially when taken in large amounts.
  • Increased bleeding: Chamomile may increase the risk of bleeding in people taking anticoagulant medications or experiencing bleeding disorders.
  • Interactions with medications: Chamomile may interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners, diabetes medications, and sedatives, leading to adverse effects.
  • Skin irritation: Chamomile essential oil can cause skin irritation, including redness, itching, and rashes, especially when applied topically.
  • Sleep disturbances: Chamomile can cause drowsiness and sleep disturbances, especially when taken in large doses or combined with other sedatives.
