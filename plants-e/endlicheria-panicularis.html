Endlicheria panicularis

Endlicheria panicularis, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually covered in a fine layer of white or gray hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns red when mature.
