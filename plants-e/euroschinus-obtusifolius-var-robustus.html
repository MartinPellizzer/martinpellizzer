Euroschinus obtusifolius var. robustus

Euroschinus obtusifolius var. robustus, commonly known as the broad-leaved euroschinus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its robust growth habit makes it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
