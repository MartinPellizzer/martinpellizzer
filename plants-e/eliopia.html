Eliopia

Eliopia, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant has small, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained soil, making it well-suited for dry environments. Eliopia is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for gardens in drought-prone areas.
