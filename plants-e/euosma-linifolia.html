Euosma linifolia

Euosma linifolia, commonly known as California boxwood, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit.

Euosma linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
