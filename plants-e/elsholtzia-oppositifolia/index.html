Elsholtzia Oppositifolia

Elsholtzia oppositifolia is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its opposite leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found at high altitudes. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a fascinating subject for botanists and gardeners alike.
