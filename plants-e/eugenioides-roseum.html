Eugenioides roseum

Eugenioides roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered eugenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners interested in tropical plants.
