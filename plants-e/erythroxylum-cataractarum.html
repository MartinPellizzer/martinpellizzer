Erythroxylum cataractarum

Erythroxylum cataractarum, commonly known as the red river holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Erythroxylum cataractarum is often found in moist, shaded areas within tropical forests.
