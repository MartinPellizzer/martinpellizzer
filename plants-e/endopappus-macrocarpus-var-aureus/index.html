Endopappus Macrocarpus Var. Aureus

The endopappus macrocarpus var. aureus is a rare and striking plant native to the western regions of the united states. it is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. the plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. its unique morphology and ecological significance have made it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. despite its beauty, it remains under threat due to habitat loss and invasive species.
