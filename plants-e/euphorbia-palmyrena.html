Euphorbia palmyrena

Euphorbia palmyrena, commonly known as the Palmyrene spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
