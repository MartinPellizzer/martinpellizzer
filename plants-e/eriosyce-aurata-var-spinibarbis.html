Eriosyce aurata var. spinibarbis

Eriosyce aurata var. spinibarbis, commonly known as the spiny-tailed sun rose, is a species of cactus native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
