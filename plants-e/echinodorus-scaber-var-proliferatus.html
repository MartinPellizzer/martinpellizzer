Echinodorus scaber var. proliferatus

Echinodorus scaber var. proliferatus, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often marked with dark green veins. This variety is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a wide range of water conditions. It is commonly used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and its role in oxygenating the water.

The plant also provides shelter and breeding grounds for various aquatic organisms.
