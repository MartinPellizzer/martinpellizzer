Epidendrum hagsateri

Epidendrum hagsateri, commonly known as the Hagsater's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
