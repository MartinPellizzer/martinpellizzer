Eugenia pseudoverticilliflora

Eugenia pseudoverticilliflora, commonly known as the false verticilliflorous eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a strong aromatic scent.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
