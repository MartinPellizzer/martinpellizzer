Epidendrum buchtienii

Epidendrum buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
