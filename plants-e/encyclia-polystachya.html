Encyclia polystachya

Encyclia polystachya, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of multiple racemes bearing showy, colorful blooms.

The leaves are usually long and strap-like, adapted for efficient water absorption in its natural habitat.
