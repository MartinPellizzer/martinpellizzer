Elsholtzia polystachya

Elsholtzia polystachya, commonly known as Chinese blueweed, is a perennial herb native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its essential oils contain compounds with potential medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

Elsholtzia polystachya is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
