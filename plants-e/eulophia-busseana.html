Eulophia busseana

Eulophia busseana, commonly known as the Busse's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Eulophia busseana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
