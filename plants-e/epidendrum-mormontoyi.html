Epidendrum mormontoyi

Epidendrum mormontoyi, commonly known as the Mormontoyi orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and cloud forests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Epidendrum mormontoyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
