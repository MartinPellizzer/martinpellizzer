Eragrostis stenothyrsa

Eragrostis stenothyrsa, commonly known as narrow-panicle bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Eragrostis stenothyrsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in arid and semi-arid environments.
