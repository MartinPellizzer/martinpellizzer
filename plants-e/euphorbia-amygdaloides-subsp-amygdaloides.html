Euphorbia amygdaloides subsp. amygdaloides

Euphorbia amygdaloides subsp.

amygdaloides, commonly known as the smooth tree spurge, is a shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features a woody stem with a smooth, grayish bark and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters.

The plant has a milky sap that is toxic and can cause skin irritation. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its structure and appearance make it a distinctive and hardy plant in arid environments.
