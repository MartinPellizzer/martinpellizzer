Epidendrum ensatum

Epidendrum ensatum, commonly known as the Sword Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers that are typically green or yellow with red or purple markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum ensatum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
