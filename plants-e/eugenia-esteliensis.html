Eugenia esteliensis

Eugenia esteliensis, commonly known as the Esteli myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
