Eriostemon apricus

Eriostemon apricus, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its soft, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a aromatic scent when crushed.

Eriostemon apricus thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and open woodlands.
