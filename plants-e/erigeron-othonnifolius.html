Erigeron othonnifolius

Erigeron othonnifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has finely divided, grass-like leaves. It produces small blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
