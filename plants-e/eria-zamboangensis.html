Eria zamboangensis

Eria zamboangensis, commonly known as the Zamboanga orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white with intricate patterns, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
