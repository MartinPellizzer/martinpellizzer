Eriocaulon nepalense var. nepalense

Eriocaulon nepalense var. nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
