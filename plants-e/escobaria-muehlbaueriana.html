Escobaria muehlbaueriana

Escobaria muehlbaueriana, commonly known as Muehlbauer's liveforever, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, gray-green leaves. Escobaria muehlbaueriana is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
