Epidendrum octandrum

Epidendrum octandrum, commonly known as the Showy Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually broad and oval, arranged in a compact rosette.

Epidendrum octandrum is prized in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
