Eriosema polystachyum var. fulvum

Eriosema polystachyum var. fulvum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has small, trifoliate leaves. It is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant yellow blooms.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
