Echeveria sheldonii

Echeveria sheldonii, commonly known as the Sheldon's Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Echeveria sheldonii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
