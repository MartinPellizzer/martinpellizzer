Eriocoma heterophylla

Eriocoma heterophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriocoma, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid environments.

Eriocoma heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
