Epidendrum paniculorotundifolium

Epidendrum paniculorotundifolium, commonly known as the Round-leafed Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage. The leaves are ovate to rounded, often with a waxy texture and a dark green color. It produces racemose inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

This species is well-suited for cultivation in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
