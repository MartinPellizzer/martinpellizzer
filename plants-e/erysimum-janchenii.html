Erysimum janchenii

Erysimum janchenii, commonly known as the Chilean wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Erysimum janchenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
