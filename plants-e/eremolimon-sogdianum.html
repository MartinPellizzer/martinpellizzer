Eremolimon sogdianum

Eremolimon sogdianum, commonly known as the Sogdian stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in the summer months. It is well adapted to rocky soils and can often be found growing on slopes or in dry grasslands.

Due to its resilience and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
