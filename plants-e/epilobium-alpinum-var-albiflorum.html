Epilobium alpinum var. albiflorum

Epilobium alpinum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the alpine willow herb, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
