Eutaxia diffusa

Eutaxia diffusa, commonly known as the spreading eutaxia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers, which are typically arranged in a cylindrical shape. Eutaxia diffusa is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
