Epidendrum paniculatum var. linearifolium

Epidendrum paniculatum var. linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaved epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
