Euphorbia fanshawei

Euphorbia fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese Spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique cyathium structure, which is typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
