Eroteum Ochnaceum

Coral tree, scientifically known as Eroteum ochnaceum, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Africa. It is prized for its vibrant, coral-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring a broad, spreading canopy and glossy green leaves. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and urban landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
