Epidendrum pomecense

Epidendrum pomecense, commonly known as the Pomegranate Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flower spikes that can reach up to two meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom sequentially along the inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum pomecense is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
