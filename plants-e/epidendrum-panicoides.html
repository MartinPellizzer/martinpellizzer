Epidendrum panicoides

Epidendrum panicoides, commonly known as the Panicled Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Epidendrum panicoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a warm, humid environment.
