Erigeron nanus

Erigeron nanus, commonly known as the dwarf fleabane, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters and features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in open, sunny areas such as meadows and prairies.

Erigeron nanus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
