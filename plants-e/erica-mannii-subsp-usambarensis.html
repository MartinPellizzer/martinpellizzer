Erica mannii subsp. usambarensis

Erica mannii subsp.

usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambarensis heath, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants.

The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
