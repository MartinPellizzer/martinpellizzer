Eleocharis acicularis f. filiformis

Eleocharis acicularis f. filiformis, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, thread-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences. This variant is typically found in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense spikes.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and semi-aquatic ecosystems.
