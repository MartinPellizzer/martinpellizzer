Eulophia panganiana

Eulophia panganiana, commonly known as the Panganian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces erect, slender stems with few leaves. The plant blooms with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia panganiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
