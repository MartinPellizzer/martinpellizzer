Erythroxylum brevipes var. spinescens

Erythroxylum brevipes var. spinescens, commonly known as the spiny copal tree, is a species native to parts of South America, particularly in Brazil and Peru.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and dark green, elliptical leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.

This species is of interest for its potential medicinal properties and ecological significance in its native habitat.
