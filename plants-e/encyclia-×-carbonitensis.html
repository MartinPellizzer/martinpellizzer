Encyclia × carbonitensis

Encyclia × carbonitensis, commonly known as the Carbonitensis Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-shaped leaves and blooms with pale yellow to white petals adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
