Eugenia brownsbergii

Eugenia brownsbergii, commonly known as the Brown's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
