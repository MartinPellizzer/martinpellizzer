Erysimum asperum var. elatum

Erysimum asperum var. elatum, commonly known as the tall wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has rough, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
