Erica coccinea subsp. coccinea

Erica coccinea subsp.

coccinea, commonly known as the scarlet heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and vibrant red flowers.

The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Erica coccinea subsp.

coccinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
