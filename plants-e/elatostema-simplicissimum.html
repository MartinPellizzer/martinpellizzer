Elatostema simplicissimum

Elatostema simplicissimum, commonly known as the small-leaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streams. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive waxy coating. Its flowers are tiny and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a yellowish-green color.

Elatostema simplicissimum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
