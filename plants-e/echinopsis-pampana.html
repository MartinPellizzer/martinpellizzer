Echinopsis pampana

Echinopsis pampana, commonly known as the Pampa Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Echinopsis pampana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
