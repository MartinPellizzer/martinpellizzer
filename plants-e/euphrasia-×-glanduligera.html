Euphrasia × glanduligera

Euphrasia × glanduligera, commonly known as the glandular eyebright, is a hybrid species within the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glandular surface, giving it its common name.

Euphrasia × glanduligera is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
