Eucalyptus hemilampra

Eucalyptus hemilampra, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed form and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, producing a distinctive aromatic scent.

Eucalyptus hemilampra plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
