Echinolaena oplismenoides

Echinolaena oplismenoides, commonly known as the slender burrgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spiky seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland grasslands.

Echinolaena oplismenoides is an important component of native vegetation, providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
