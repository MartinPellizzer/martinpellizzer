Eschenbachia stricta var. stricta

Eschenbachia stricta var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eschenbachia, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom during the cooler months. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
