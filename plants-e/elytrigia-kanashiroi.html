Elytrigia kanashiroi

Elytrigia kanashiroi, commonly known as the Japanese needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Elytrigia kanashiroi thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
