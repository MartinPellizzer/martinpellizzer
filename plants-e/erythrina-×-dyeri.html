Erythrina × dyeri

Erythrina × dyeri, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a hybrid legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its bark is smooth with a reddish hue. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often used as an ornamental tree due to its vibrant floral display.

The seeds are encased in spiny pods, which contain several hard, angular seeds.
