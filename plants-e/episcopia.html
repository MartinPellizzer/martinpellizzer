Episcopia

Episcopia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumpfia and is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that resemble foliage. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, typically blooming during the holiday season. It is a tropical plant that prefers cooler temperatures and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
