Erica procera

Erica procera, commonly known as the tall heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. Erica procera produces fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple during the summer months.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
