Eragrostis leptotricha

Eragrostis leptotricha, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny flowers.

Eragrostis leptotricha is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
