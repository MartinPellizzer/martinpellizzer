Erigeron andicola f. pleiocephalus

Erigeron andicola f. pleiocephalus, commonly known as the Andean fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets.
