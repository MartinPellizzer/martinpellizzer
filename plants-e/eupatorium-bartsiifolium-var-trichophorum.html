Eupatorium bartsiifolium var. trichophorum

Eupatorium bartsiifolium var. trichophorum, commonly known as the silky eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely hairy, often silver-gray leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, which attract various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and hardy plant.
