Euphorbia gossypina subsp. gossypina

Euphorbia gossypina subsp.

gossypina, commonly known as the cotton spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features succulent, branched stems that are often covered in a white, powdery coating.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow with a red or purple center. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a waxy texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny environments.
