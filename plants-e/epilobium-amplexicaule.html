Epilobium amplexicaule

Epilobium amplexicaule, commonly known as redstem or redstem willow, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect or ascending stems that are often reddish in color, especially when young. The leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and have a hairy surface, while the flowers are typically pink or red, arranged in terminal or axillary clusters. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and roadside areas.

This plant is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
