Eulophia abyssinica var. aurantiaca

Eulophia abyssinica var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Red-flowered African Orchid, is a striking orchid native to Ethiopia.

It features vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to the dry, mountainous environments where it thrives.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid conditions.
