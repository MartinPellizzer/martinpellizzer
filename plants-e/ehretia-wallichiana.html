Ehretia wallichiana

Ehretia wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan mahua, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

It is often found in moist, mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
