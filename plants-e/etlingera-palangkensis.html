Etlingera palangkensis

Etlingera palangkensis, commonly known as the Palangkensian torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking red and orange inflorescences, which resemble torches, hence its common name. Its leaves are large, lance-shaped, and often have a glossy surface.

Etlingera palangkensis is typically grown for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
