Eugenia sphenophylla

Eugenia sphenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is often found in heathlands and is known for its aromatic foliage.

Eugenia sphenophylla is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
