Epidendrum humile

Epidendrum humile, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features slender, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and thrives in warm, moist climates.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
