Erythroxylum nitidulum

Erythroxylum nitidulum, commonly known as the glossy evergreen shrub, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that have a waxy texture, giving them a shiny appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, followed by bright red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
