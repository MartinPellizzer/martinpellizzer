Escallonia Pulverulenta

Escallonia pulverulenta is a striking evergreen shrub native to south-western australia. it features dense, glossy foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. this plant is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in coastal conditions. it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardeners in mild climates.
