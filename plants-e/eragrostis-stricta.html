Eragrostis stricta

Eragrostis stricta, commonly known as rigid lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Eragrostis stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
