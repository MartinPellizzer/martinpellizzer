Epidendrum mathewsii

Epidendrum mathewsii, commonly known as the Mathews' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically has long, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum mathewsii is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
