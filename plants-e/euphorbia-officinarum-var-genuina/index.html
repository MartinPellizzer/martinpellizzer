Euphorbia Officinarum Var. Genuina

Euphorbia officinarum var. genuina is a striking succulent known for its unique, spiky appearance and vibrant green color. Native to the Canary Islands, this plant thrives in arid and coastal environments. It features a short, thick stem with clusters of small, leaf-like structures called phyllodes. The plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its architectural form and low maintenance requirements. Its ability to store water makes it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
