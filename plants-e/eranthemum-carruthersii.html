Eranthemum carruthersii

Eranthemum carruthersii, commonly known as the mountain eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

Eranthemum carruthersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
