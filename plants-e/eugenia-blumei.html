Eugenia blumei

Eugenia blumei, commonly known as the Blume's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia blumei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
