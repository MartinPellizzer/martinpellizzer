Echites pellieri

Echites pellieri, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

Echites pellieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
