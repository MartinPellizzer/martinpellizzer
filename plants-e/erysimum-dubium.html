Erysimum dubium

Erysimum dubium, commonly known as the alpine wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its foliage is characterized by lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
