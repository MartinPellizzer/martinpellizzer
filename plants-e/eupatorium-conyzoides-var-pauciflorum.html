Eupatorium conyzoides var. pauciflorum

Eupatorium conyzoides var. pauciflorum, commonly known as lesser boneset, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as wetlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive, aromatic odor. Its small, clustered white flowers bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
