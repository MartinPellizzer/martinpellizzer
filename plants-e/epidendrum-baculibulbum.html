Epidendrum baculibulbum

Epidendrum baculibulbum, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are often bright red or orange with a contrasting yellow lip. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, helping it store water in its native habitat.

Epidendrum baculibulbum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
