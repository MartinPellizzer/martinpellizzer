Epidendrum lindenianum

Epidendrum lindenianum, commonly known as the Showy Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with contrasting patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in varying conditions.

The species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
