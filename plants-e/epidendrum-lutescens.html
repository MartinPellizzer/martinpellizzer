Epidendrum lutescens

Epidendrum lutescens, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.

The plant's unique appearance and resilience make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
