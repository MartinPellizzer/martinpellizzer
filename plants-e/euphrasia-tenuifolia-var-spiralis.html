Euphrasia tenuifolia var. spiralis

Euphrasia tenuifolia var. spiralis, commonly known as spiral narrow-leaved eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, spiral-arranged leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers with prominent dark markings, typical of the Scrophulariaceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
