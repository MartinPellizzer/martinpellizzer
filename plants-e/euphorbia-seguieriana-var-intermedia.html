Euphorbia seguieriana var. intermedia

Euphorbia seguieriana var. intermedia, commonly known as the variegated euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
