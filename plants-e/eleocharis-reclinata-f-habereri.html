Eleocharis reclinata f. habereri

Eleocharis reclinata f. habereri, commonly known as the Habereri flatsedge, is a variant of the flatsedge genus Eleocharis.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Its leaves are usually slender and cylindrical, with a smooth surface.

The inflorescences are composed of numerous tiny, spikelet-like structures that are often clustered at the tips of the stems.
