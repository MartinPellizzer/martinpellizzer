Eugenia toxanatolica

Eugenia toxanatolica, commonly known as the red-eyed tree frog plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy texture.

Eugenia toxanatolica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
