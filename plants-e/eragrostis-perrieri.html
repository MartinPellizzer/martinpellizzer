Eragrostis perrieri

Eragrostis perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's grass, is a species of flowering grass native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in grassland habitats, often in areas with well-drained soils. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that helps it blend into its natural surroundings.

Eragrostis perrieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
