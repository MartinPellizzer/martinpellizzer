Elatostema Frutescens

Elatostema frutescens is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. It is known for its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking easy-to-care-for plants. Despite its modest appearance, Elatostema frutescens plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
