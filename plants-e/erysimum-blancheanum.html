Erysimum blancheanum

Erysimum blancheanum, commonly known as the Blancheanum Wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to alpine and subalpine flora collections.
