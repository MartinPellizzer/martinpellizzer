Erigeron curvifolius

Erigeron curvifolius, commonly known as the curlyleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields, and it thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and often curl at the edges, giving it its common name.

It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
