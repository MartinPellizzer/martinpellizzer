Eumachia Depauperata

Coralbean, scientifically known as Eumachia depauperata, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. Its unique appearance, featuring vibrant red or orange bean-like pods, makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its eye-catching features, the plant is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies, adding ecological benefits to gardens. However, it is important to note that the plant can be invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
