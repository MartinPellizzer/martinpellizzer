Epilobium rubricaule

Epilobium rubricaule, commonly known as red-stemmed willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as along stream banks and in wet meadows. The plant features slender, reddish stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

It produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
