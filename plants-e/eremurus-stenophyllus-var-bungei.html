Eremurus stenophyllus var. bungei

Eremurus stenophyllus var. bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's Spearflower, is a striking perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence composed of numerous yellow to orange-red tubular flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a central stem.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in arid gardens for its unique visual impact.
