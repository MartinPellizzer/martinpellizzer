Encyclia × ortizii

Encyclia × ortizii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Encyclia fraterna and Encyclia tecta.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico, characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
