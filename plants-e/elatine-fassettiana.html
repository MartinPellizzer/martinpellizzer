Elatine fassettiana

Elatine fassettiana, commonly known as the slender waterwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and streams. The plant has slender, branched stems that are often submerged, with tiny, opposite leaves that are usually less than 1 millimeter in length. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, which appear above the water surface.

Elatine fassettiana is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed aquatic habitats.
