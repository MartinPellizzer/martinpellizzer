Escallonia resinosa var. spiraeifolia

Escallonia resinosa var. spiraeifolia, commonly known as the spiraeoid escallonia, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It features attractive, fragrant white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.
