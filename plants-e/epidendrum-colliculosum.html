Epidendrum colliculosum

Epidendrum colliculosum, commonly known as the clustered epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and often produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native habitat.

Epidendrum colliculosum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
