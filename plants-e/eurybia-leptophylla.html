Eurybia leptophylla

Eurybia leptophylla, commonly known as the slenderleaf sunflower, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to eastern North America and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and yellow composite flowers. Eurybia leptophylla is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
