Eremosparton schumannii

Eremosparton schumannii, commonly known as the Schumann's desert lily, is a perennial shrub native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, woody stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for survival in harsh, semi-desert environments.
