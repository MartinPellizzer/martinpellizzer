Echinops hussonii

Echinops hussonii, commonly known as the Husson's thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, globular shape and is often found in rocky or dry soils. The plant produces purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture.

Echinops hussonii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
