Erodium × anaristatum

Erodium × anaristatum, commonly known as the California buckwheat, is a hybrid plant species native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important part of local ecosystems and a common sight in arid and semi-arid regions.
