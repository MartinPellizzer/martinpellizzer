Epidendrum ariasii

Epidendrum ariasii, commonly known as the Arias' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum ariasii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
