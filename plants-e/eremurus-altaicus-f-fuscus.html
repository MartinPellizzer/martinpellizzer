Eremurus altaicus f. fuscus

Eremurus altaicus f. fuscus, commonly known as the dark form of the Altai spirea, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
