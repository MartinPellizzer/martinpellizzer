Euphorbia infernidialis

Euphorbia infernidialis, commonly known as the hellfire euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with its spiny, cylindrical stems and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by colorful bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its toxic sap, handling the plant requires caution.
