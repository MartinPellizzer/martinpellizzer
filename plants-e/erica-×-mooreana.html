Erica × mooreana

Erica × mooreana, commonly known as the Cape heath or mooreana heath, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces vibrant pink or red flowers in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Erica × mooreana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover in coastal areas.
