Eriocaulon nilagirense

Eriocaulon nilagirense, commonly known as the Nilagiri marsh plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the wetlands of the Nilgiri Mountains in southern India.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes grass-like plants with small, clustered flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth and narrow, linear leaves that often form a rosette. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, typically appearing in late summer to early autumn.

Eriocaulon nilagirense thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or marshy habitats.
