Epidendrum excisum var. grandiflorum

Epidendrum excisum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and long, pendulous stems that bear numerous showy flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns. This variety thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
