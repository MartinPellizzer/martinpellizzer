Eleocharis microcarpa var. filiculmis

Eleocharis microcarpa var. filiculmis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clusters at the base.

This variety is adapted to moist or aquatic habitats and plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.
