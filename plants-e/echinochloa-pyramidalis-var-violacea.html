Echinochloa pyramidalis var. violacea

Echinochloa pyramidalis var. violacea, commonly known as purple panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, tufted foliage. The plant produces spikelets that are typically purple or violet in color, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

This variety is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its striking color and texture.
