Eriastrum sapphirinum

Eriastrum sapphirinum, commonly known as the blue eriastrum, is a flowering plant native to California.

Its leaves are gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Eriastrum sapphirinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for native pollinators.
