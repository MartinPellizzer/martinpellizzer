Eulophia pulchra

Eulophia pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that can range from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Eulophia pulchra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
