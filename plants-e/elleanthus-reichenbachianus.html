Elleanthus reichenbachianus

Elleanthus reichenbachianus, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. It produces small, cylindrical seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal.

Elleanthus reichenbachianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
