Eugenia Moluccana

Eugenia moluccana is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Indonesia. It is known for its fragrant flowers and edible fruits that are highly valued in local cuisines. The plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their medicinal properties. This versatile plant plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts.
