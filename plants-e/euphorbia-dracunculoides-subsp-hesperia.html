Euphorbia dracunculoides subsp. hesperia

Euphorbia dracunculoides subsp.

hesperia, commonly known as the Mexican milkweed, is a succulent shrub native to Mexico. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by specialized structures called cyathia. It is adapted to arid environments and has a milky sap that can be toxic.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a notable species in the Euphorbiaceae family.
