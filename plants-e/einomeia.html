Einomeia

Einomeia, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces striking, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas.

Einomeia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
