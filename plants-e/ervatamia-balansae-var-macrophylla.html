Ervatamia balansae var. macrophylla

Ervatamia balansae var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved ervatamia, is a rare and striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal spikes, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure.
