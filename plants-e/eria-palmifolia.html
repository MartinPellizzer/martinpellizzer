Eria palmifolia

Eria palmifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Eria palmifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance.

The plant requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
