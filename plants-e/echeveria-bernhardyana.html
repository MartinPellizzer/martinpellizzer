Echeveria bernhardyana

Echeveria bernhardyana, commonly known as the "Blue Elf" or "Blue Echeveria," is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features dense, compact rosettes of bluish-green, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of purple or pink during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant.
