Ehretia dicksonii var. glabrescens

Ehretia dicksonii var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smoothleaf ehretia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glabrous surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are borne in clusters, adding ornamental value to the plant.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
