Eugenia calycolpoides

Eugenia calycolpoides, commonly known as the Queensland holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to northern Australia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. These flowers give way to small, edible berries that are typically red or orange in color.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
