Echidnopsis chrysantha subsp. filipes

Echidnopsis chrysantha subsp.

filipes, commonly known as the coastal flax, is a member of the Asphodelaceae family and is native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
