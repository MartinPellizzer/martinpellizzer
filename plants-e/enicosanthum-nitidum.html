Enicosanthum nitidum

Enicosanthum nitidum, commonly known as the glossy hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Enicosanthum nitidum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
