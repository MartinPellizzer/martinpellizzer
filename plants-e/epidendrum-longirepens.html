Epidendrum longirepens

Epidendrum longirepens, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length, with numerous small, greenish-white flowers clustered along the stem. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and delicate flowers make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
