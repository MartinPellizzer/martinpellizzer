Echinocactus pampeanus

Echinocactus pampeanus, commonly known as the Pampas hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny, segmented appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to low water conditions.

Its structure provides protection against herbivores and extreme temperatures.
