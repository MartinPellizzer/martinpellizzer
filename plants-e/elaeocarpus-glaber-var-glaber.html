Elaeocarpus glaber var. glaber

Elaeocarpus glaber var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
