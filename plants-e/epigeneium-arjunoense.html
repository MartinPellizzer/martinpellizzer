Epigeneium arjunoense

Epigeneium arjunoense, commonly known as the Java slipper orchid, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

Epigeneium arjunoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
