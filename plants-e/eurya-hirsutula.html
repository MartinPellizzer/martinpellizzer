Eurya hirsutula

Eurya hirsutula, commonly known as the hairy alder, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Indonesia. Eurya hirsutula is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
