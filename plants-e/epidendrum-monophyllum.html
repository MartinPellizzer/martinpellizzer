Epidendrum monophyllum

Epidendrum monophyllum, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, lemon-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum monophyllum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
