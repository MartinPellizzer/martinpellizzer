Euphrasia crassiuscula subsp. eglandulosa

Euphrasia crassiuscula subsp.

eglandulosa, commonly known as the glandless eyebright, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent yellow markings.
