Epidendrum claesianum

Epidendrum claesianum, commonly known as the Golden Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, golden-orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
