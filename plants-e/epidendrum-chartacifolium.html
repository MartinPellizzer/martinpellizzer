Epidendrum chartacifolium

Epidendrum chartacifolium, commonly known as the chartaceous-leaf epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments.

Its unique leaf structure and flowering pattern make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
