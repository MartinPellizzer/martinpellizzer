Erigeron armeriifolius

Erigeron armeriifolius, commonly known as the slender fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers bloom in shades of white to pale yellow.
