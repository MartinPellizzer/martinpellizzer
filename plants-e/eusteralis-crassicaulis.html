Eusteralis crassicaulis

Eusteralis crassicaulis, commonly known as the broadstemmed eusteralis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features robust, upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Eusteralis crassicaulis is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, demonstrating adaptability to different environmental conditions.
