Etlingera steringophora

Etlingera steringophora, commonly known as the red torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the small, yellow flowers, creating an eye-catching display. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface.
