Encyclia × gracilis

Encyclia × gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia × gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
