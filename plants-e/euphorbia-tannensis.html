Euphorbia tannensis

Euphorbia tannensis, commonly known as the Tannen Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Euphorbia tannensis is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
