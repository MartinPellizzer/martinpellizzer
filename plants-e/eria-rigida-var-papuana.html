Eria rigida var. papuana

Eria rigida var. papuana, commonly known as the Papuan hardy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Indonesia and Papua.

It features erect, hairy stems that bear several oval, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with three sepals and three petals, typical of the Orchidaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts.
