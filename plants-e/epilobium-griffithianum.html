Epilobium griffithianum

Epilobium griffithianum, commonly known as Griffith's willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, rocky habitats. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
