Erysimum confine

Erysimum confine, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
