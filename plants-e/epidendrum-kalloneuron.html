Epidendrum kalloneuron

Epidendrum kalloneuron, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear numerous showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum kalloneuron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
