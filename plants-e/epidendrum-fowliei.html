Epidendrum fowliei

Epidendrum fowliei, commonly known as the Fowlie's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the rainy season, and its inflorescence is often racemose, bearing numerous small to medium-sized blooms. Epidendrum fowliei has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

It is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
