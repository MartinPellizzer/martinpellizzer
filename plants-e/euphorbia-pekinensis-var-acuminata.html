Euphorbia pekinensis var. acuminata

Euphorbia pekinensis var. acuminata, commonly known as the Chinese Spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has a spiny, branched structure with a distinctive crown of leaves at the top. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
