Euphorbia remyi var. wahiawana

Euphorbia remyi var. wahiawana, commonly known as the Wahiawana euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and small, clustered flowers that range from yellow to greenish-white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typical of its coastal habitat. Its unique morphology includes thick, ribbed leaves and a compact growth habit.

This variety is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local wildlife.
