Epidendrum aerides

Epidendrum aerides, commonly known as the orchid of the sky, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with intricate patterns. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Epidendrum aerides is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
