Encyclia regnelliana

Encyclia regnelliana, commonly known as the regnellian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features a short rhizome with a few pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal inflorescences and can be found in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

Encyclia regnelliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
