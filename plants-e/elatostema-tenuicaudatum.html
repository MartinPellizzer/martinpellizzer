Elatostema tenuicaudatum

Elatostema tenuicaudatum, commonly known as the slender-tailed elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces tiny, greenish-white flowers clustered in umbels. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil efficiently.

Elatostema tenuicaudatum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
