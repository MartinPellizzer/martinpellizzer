Eragrostis philippica

Eragrostis philippica, commonly known as Philippine lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields and disturbed areas.

Eragrostis philippica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
