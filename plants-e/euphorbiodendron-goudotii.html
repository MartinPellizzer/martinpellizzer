Euphorbiodendron goudotii

Euphorbiodendron goudotii, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in drought-tolerant gardens.
