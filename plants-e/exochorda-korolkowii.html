Exochorda korolkowii

Exochorda korolkowii, commonly known as the snowball bush, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators and adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has a compact, rounded growth habit with dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its frost-resistant nature allows it to thrive in temperate climates.
