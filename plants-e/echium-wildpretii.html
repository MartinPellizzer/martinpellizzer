Echium wildpretii

Echium wildpretii, commonly known as the Canary Island fennel, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous violet-blue flowers. The plant has silver-gray, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Echium wildpretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual presence.
