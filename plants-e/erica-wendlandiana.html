Erica wendlandiana

Erica wendlandiana, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica wendlandiana is often used in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
