Eupatorium buniifolium var. saucechicoense

Eupatorium buniifolium var. saucechicoense, commonly known as saucechico, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in parts of Cuba and the Bahamas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as coastal areas and wetlands.
