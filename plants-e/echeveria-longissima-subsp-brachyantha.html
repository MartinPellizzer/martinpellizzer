Echeveria longissima subsp. brachyantha

Echeveria longissima subsp.

brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico. It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating.

The leaves are narrow and pointed, giving the plant a distinctive, elegant appearance. It produces clusters of showy pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months.

This species is well-suited for rock gardens and container planting due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
