Eryngium octophyllum

Eryngium octophyllum, commonly known as the eight-leaved thistle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its spiny leaves and blue or purple flowers.
