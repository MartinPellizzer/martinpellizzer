Euphrasia tenuifolia

Euphrasia tenuifolia, commonly known as slender eyebright, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent yellow markings, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has delicate, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Euphrasia tenuifolia is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
