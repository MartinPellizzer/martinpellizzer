Eugenia glaucescens

Eugenia glaucescens, commonly known as the blue eucalyptus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like or small tree-like in growth habit. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Eugenia glaucescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
