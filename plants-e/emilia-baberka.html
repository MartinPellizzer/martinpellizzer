Emilia baberka

Emilia baberka, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces small, bright yellow flowers with distinctive red markings. The plant grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Emilia baberka thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its flowers bloom throughout the summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and natural habitats.
