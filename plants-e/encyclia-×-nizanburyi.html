Encyclia × nizanburyi

Encyclia × nizanburyi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia frondosa.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its hybrid nature makes it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
