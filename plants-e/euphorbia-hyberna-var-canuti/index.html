Euphorbia Hyberna Var. Canuti

Euphorbia hyberna var. canuti is a unique and striking plant native to the Canary Islands. Known for its vibrant green leaves and distinctive red or orange bracts, it captivates with its bold visual appeal. This succulent thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for arid environments. Its ability to store water in its thick leaves helps it survive in harsh conditions. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap, so care must be taken when handling it.
