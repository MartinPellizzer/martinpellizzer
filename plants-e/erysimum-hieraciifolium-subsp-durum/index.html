Erysimum Hieraciifolium Subsp. Durum

Erysimum hieraciifolium subsp. durum is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the rocky slopes of southern Europe. It is known for its delicate, yellow flowers and finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This subspecies thrives in harsh, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. Due to its limited habitat and declining populations, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
