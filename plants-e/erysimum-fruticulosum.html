Erysimum fruticulosum

Erysimum fruticulosum, commonly known as the fruticose ermine.

This plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of yellow flowers. It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The leaves are pinnately divided, giving the plant a feathery appearance.

It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate, dainty blooms.
