Eustoma russellianum f. flaviflorum

Eustoma russellianum f. flaviflorum, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet.

Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.
