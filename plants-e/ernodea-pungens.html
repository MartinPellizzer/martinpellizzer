Ernodea pungens

Ernodea pungens, commonly known as the spiny morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Ernodea pungens is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils.
