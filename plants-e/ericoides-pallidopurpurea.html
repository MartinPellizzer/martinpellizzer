Ericoides pallidopurpurea

Ericoides pallidopurpurea, commonly known as the pale purple heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves that are typically glossy and arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale purple to white, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland or woodland habitats.

Ericoides pallidopurpurea is an ornamental plant appreciated for its compact growth habit and attractive floral display.
