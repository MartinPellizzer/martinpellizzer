Elleanthus norae

Elleanthus norae, commonly known as the rare mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
