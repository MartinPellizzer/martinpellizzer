Epilobium confertifolium var. tasmanicum

Epilobium confertifolium var. tasmanicum, commonly known as Tasmanian willow, is a shrub native to Tasmania, Australia.

It features slender, willow-like stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It produces small, clustered flowers with pink to reddish petals, typically blooming in spring.

This plant is adapted to moist, cool environments and is often found in streamside habitats.
