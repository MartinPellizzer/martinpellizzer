Empleurum serrulatum var. ensatum

Empleurum serrulatum var. ensatum, commonly known as the serrated emplurum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
