Eucharis ruthiana

Eucharis ruthiana, commonly known as the Snowbell plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family, Araceae, and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped white flowers. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with cream or yellow markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eucharis ruthiana is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
