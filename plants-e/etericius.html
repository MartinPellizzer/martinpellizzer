Etericius

Etericius, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, nocturnal white flowers that open at night and close by dawn. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a rapid growth rate, making it popular for ornamental purposes. Its botanical name, Etericius, reflects its unique flowering behavior tied to lunar cycles.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
