Elionurus rostratus

Elionurus rostratus, commonly known as the rostrated grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often reddish in color.

Elionurus rostratus is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in regions with well-drained soil.
