Erythrina smithiana

Erythrina smithiana, commonly known as the Smith's flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are clustered in dense panicles. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
