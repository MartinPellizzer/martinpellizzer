Erophila × glabrescens-majuscula

Erophila × glabrescens-majuscula, commonly known as the hybrid marsh woundwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It typically grows in shallow waters or moist soils, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered with a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, often with a purple or reddish hue.

This species is noted for its adaptability to fluctuating water levels and its role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems.
