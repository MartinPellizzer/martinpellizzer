Eragrostis buchtienii

Eragrostis buchtienii, commonly known as the narrowleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant.

Its ecological role includes providing forage for livestock and supporting local biodiversity.
