Epidendrum olorteguii

Epidendrum olorteguii, commonly known as the Olortegui's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually narrow and dark green, with a waxy coating to retain moisture.

Epidendrum olorteguii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
