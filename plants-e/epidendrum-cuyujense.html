Epidendrum cuyujense

Epidendrum cuyujense, commonly known as the Cuyujense orchid, is a species native to the Andes region of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its inflorescence consists of multiple showy, violet or purple flowers, which bloom during the warmer months.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
