Eugenia stokesii

Eugenia stokesii, commonly known as Stokes' eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
