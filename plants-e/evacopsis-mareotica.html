Evacopsis mareotica

Evacopsis mareotica, commonly known as the Mareotica spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Nile Delta region of Egypt.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in saline and alkaline soils. The plant features slender, branching stems covered with small, waxy leaves. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

Evacopsis mareotica is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
