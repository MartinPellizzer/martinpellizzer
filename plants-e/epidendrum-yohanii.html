Epidendrum yohanii

Epidendrum yohanii, commonly known as the Yohan's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environments.

Epidendrum yohanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
