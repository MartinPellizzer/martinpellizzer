Erica × broadleyana

Erica × broadleyana, commonly known as the Heather plant, is a hybrid evergreen shrub belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae).

It is native to the Western Cape of South Africa and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and profuse flowering. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica × broadleyana is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
