Echinodorus tenellus f. apanecae

Echinodorus tenellus f. apanecae, commonly known as the Apanecan swordplant, is a compact, aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green color, often with a slight reddish or bronze hue along the edges. This variety is well-suited for planted aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a range of water conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen in captivity.

The plant is prized for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in both freshwater and brackish environments.
