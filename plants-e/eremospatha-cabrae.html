Eremospatha cabrae

Eremospatha cabrae, commonly known as the Cape coral tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, coral-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has woody stems with a distinctive branching pattern, and its leaves are simple and alternate. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and rocky areas.

The Cape coral tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
