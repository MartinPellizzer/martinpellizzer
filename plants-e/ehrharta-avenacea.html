Ehrharta avenacea

Ehrharta avenacea, commonly known as slender oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Ehrharta avenacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control due to its dense root system.
