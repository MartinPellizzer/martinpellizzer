Excoecaria suberosa

Excoecaria suberosa, commonly known as the rubber tree or tree of heaven, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, corky bark, which provides resistance to pests and diseases. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
