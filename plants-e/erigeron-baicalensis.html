Erigeron baicalensis

Erigeron baicalensis, commonly known as the Chinese fleabane, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of a central yellow disk surrounded by white to pale blue ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often lance-shaped and serrated.

Erigeron baicalensis is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
