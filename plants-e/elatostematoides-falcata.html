Elatostematoides falcata

Elatostematoides falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elatostemataceae.

It is characterized by its small, falcate (sickle-shaped) leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale yellow flowers, which are often borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Elatostematoides falcata is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
