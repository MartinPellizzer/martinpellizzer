Eulophia elegantula

Eulophia elegantula, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Eulophia elegantula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
