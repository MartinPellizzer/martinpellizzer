Esenbeckia collina subsp. collina

Esenbeckia collina subsp.

collina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved esenbeckia, is a herbaceous plant native to South America. It typically grows in moist, shady environments, often found in forest understories.

The plant features elongated, narrow leaves with a smooth, often glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and are typically greenish-white in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
