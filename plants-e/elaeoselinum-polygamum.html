Elaeoselinum polygamum

Elaeoselinum polygamum, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange ray florets and dark brown disk florets, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Elaeoselinum polygamum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
