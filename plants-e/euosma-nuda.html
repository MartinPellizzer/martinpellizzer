Euosma nuda

Euosma nuda, commonly known as the slender euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
