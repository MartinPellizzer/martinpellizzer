Eulophia sphaerocarpa

Eulophia sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, globular pseudobulbs and long, slender leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia sphaerocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
