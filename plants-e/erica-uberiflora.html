Erica uberiflora

Erica uberiflora, commonly known as the honeycomb heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth and its distinctive, honeycomb-like flower clusters. Erica uberiflora typically blooms in spring, producing small, pale yellow to orange flowers.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique ecosystem in the Western Cape region of South Africa.
