Elatostema elmeri

Elatostema elmeri, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear in early spring. Elatostema elmeri thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest floors or along stream banks.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in cool climates.
