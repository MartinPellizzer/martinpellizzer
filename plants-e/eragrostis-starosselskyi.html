Eragrostis starosselskyi

Eragrostis starosselskyi, commonly known as the Starosselsky's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and erosion control.
