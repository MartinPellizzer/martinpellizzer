Echeveria sonianevadensis

Echeveria sonianevadensis, commonly known as the Sonianevadensis Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its coloration.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
