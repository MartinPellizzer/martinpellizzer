Ethulia conyzoides subsp. conyzoides

Ethulia conyzoides subsp.

conyzoides, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Ethulia conyzoides subsp.

conyzoides is known for its hardy nature and is often used in traditional medicine.
