Erica brachycentra

Erica brachycentra, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. It produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months.

Erica brachycentra is well adapted to acidic, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
