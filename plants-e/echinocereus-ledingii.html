Echinocereus ledingii

Echinocereus ledingii, commonly known as the Ledingii cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Echinocereus ledingii is adapted to arid environments and is relatively drought-tolerant.
