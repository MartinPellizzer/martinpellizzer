Erica kirstenii

Erica kirstenii, commonly known as the Kirsten's heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Erica kirstenii typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique biome in the Western Cape region of South Africa.
