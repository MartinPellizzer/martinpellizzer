Erythraea pickeringii

Erythraea pickeringii, commonly known as the Pickering's erythraea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
