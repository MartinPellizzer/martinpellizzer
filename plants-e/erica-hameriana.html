Erica hameriana

Erica hameriana, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica hameriana is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salt spray and drought conditions.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive choice for rock gardens and container planting.
