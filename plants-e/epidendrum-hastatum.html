Epidendrum hastatum

Epidendrum hastatum, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with distinctive spurred petals. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum hastatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
