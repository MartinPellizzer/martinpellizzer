Epidendrum dasyanthum

Epidendrum dasyanthum, commonly known as the Wooly Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can range in color from white to pink and purple.

Epidendrum dasyanthum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
