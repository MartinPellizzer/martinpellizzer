Engelhardia spicata var. spicata

Engelhardia spicata var. spicata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed engelhardia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Engelhardia spicata var. spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
