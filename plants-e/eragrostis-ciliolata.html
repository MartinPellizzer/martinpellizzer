Eragrostis ciliolata

Eragrostis ciliolata, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Eragrostis ciliolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
