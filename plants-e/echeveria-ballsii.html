Echeveria ballsii

Echeveria ballsii, commonly known as the Mexican snowball, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are densely packed and form a compact, rounded shape. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Echeveria ballsii is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
