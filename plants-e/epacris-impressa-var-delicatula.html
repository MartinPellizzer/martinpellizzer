Epacris impressa var. delicatula

Epacris impressa var. delicatula, commonly known as the slender epacris, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
