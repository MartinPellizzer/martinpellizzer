Emilia hockii

Emilia hockii, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces slender, twisted seed pods that release tiny seeds when mature. Emilia hockii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for dry garden environments.
