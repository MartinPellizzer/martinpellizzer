Epidendrum chugurense

Epidendrum chugurense, commonly known as the Chuguren orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, arranged in a fan shape.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
