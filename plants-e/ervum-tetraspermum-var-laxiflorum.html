Ervum tetraspermum var. laxiflorum

Ervum tetraspermum var. laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered cowpea, is a variant of the cowpea species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated pods and smaller, less dense flowers compared to other varieties. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate to lanceolate in shape.

This variety is valued for its edible pods and seeds, which are used in various cuisines around the world.
