Eragrostis virletii

Eragrostis virletii, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and drooping inflorescences that give it a weeping appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Eragrostis virletii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its unique visual characteristics.
