Euphrasia cebennensis var. edouardii

Euphrasia cebennensis var. edouardii, commonly known as the Edouard's eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
