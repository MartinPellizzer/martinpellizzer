Eugenia toaensis

Eugenia toaensis, commonly known as the Toaense Myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.

Eugenia toaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
