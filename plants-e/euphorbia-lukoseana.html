Euphorbia lukoseana

Euphorbia lukoseana, commonly known as the Lukose's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant is characterized by its succulent stems and small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
