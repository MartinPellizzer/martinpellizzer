Eryngium babadaghense

Eryngium babadaghense, commonly known as the Babadagh thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of Turkey.

Eryngium babadaghense is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
