Ericoila verticillata

Ericoila verticillata, commonly known as the spiral heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that are arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. Ericoila verticillata thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
