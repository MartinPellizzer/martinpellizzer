Eugenia brachystachys

Eugenia brachystachys, commonly known as the small-stemmed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, dark red berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia brachystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional medicine.
