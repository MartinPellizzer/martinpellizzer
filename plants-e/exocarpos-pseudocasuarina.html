Exocarpos pseudocasuarina

Exocarpos pseudocasuarina, commonly known as the slender hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are woody, follicles that split open to release seeds.
