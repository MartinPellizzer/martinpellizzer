Eulophia flammea

Eulophia flammea, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elongated and strap-like, providing structural support.

Eulophia flammea thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
