Eragrostis deflexa

Eragrostis deflexa, commonly known as the drooping grass or slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its drooping inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, spikelet-based flowers.

Eragrostis deflexa is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its distinctive appearance and adaptability.
