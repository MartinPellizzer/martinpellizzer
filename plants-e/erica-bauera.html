Erica bauera

Erica bauera, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Erica bauera thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
