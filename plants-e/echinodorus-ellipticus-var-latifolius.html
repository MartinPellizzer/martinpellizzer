Echinodorus ellipticus var. latifolius

Echinodorus ellipticus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. This variety is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and capacity to provide habitat for aquatic life.

The plant also contributes to water filtration by absorbing nutrients and heavy metals.
