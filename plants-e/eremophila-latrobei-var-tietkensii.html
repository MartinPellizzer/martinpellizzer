Eremophila latrobei var. tietkensii

Eremophila latrobei var. tietkensii, commonly known as the Tietkens' eremophila, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grey-green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is adapted to low rainfall and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
