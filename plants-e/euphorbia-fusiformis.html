Euphorbia fusiformis

Euphorbia fusiformis, commonly known as the spindle euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, cylindrical stem that is often green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are the modified flower clusters typical of the Euphorbia genus. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
