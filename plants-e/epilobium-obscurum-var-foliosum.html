Epilobium obscurum var. foliosum

Epilobium obscurum var. foliosum, commonly known as the dark willow herb, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and the flowers are small, pink to purple, and clustered in dense spikes.

This species is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in riparian habitats.
