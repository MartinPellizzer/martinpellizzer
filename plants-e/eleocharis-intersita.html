Eleocharis intersita

Eleocharis intersita, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often unbranched and bear small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually green or brown and are surrounded by bracts.

Eleocharis intersita plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
