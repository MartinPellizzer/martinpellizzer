Episcia hastata

Episcia hastata, commonly known as the "Shiny Leaf" or "Radiator Plant," is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage is thick and waxy, with a distinctive glossy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Episcia hastata is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
