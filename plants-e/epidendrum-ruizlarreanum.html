Epidendrum ruizlarreanum

Epidendrum ruizlarreanum, commonly known as the Ruizlarrean orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its inflorescence is often branched, and the leaves are elongated with a waxy texture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
