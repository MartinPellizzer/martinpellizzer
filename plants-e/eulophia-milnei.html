Eulophia milnei

Eulophia milnei, commonly known as the Milne's eulophia, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, lance-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Eulophia milnei is often found in forested areas, growing on trees or in shaded undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
