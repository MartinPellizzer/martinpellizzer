Epidendrum nanoecallosum

Epidendrum nanoecallosum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features delicate, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered somewhat rare in the wild.
