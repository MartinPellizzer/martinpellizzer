Eversmannia hedysaroides

Eversmannia hedysaroides, commonly known as the Mongolian milkvetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and grasslands. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is used in traditional medicine.

Its seeds are also a food source for various wildlife species.
