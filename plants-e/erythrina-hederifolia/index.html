Erythrina Hederifolia

Erythrina hederifolia is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant red flowers and climbing habit. Native to regions across Asia and Africa, it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features compound leaves and produces clusters of bright orange or red blooms. It is often used in ornamental gardening and traditional medicine. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for landscapers and botanists alike.
