Euphrasia salisburgensis proles cuprea

Euphrasia salisburgensis proles cuprea, commonly known as the coppery eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early summer.
