Eulophia scripta var. concolor

Eulophia scripta var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, spurred orchid flowers in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
