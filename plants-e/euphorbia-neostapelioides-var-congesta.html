Euphorbia neostapelioides var. congesta

Euphorbia neostapelioides var. congesta, commonly known as the Compact Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
