Elisanthe quadriloba

Elisanthe quadriloba, commonly known as the four-lobed elisanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive four-lobed leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Elisanthe quadriloba is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
