Epipactis × conquensis

Epipactis × conquensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Epipactis hederacea and Epipactis purpurata.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Central America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb with a dense rosette of dark green, hairy leaves. Its inflorescence consists of multiple showy, greenish-yellow to brownish flowers with a distinctive lip.

Epipactis × conquensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
