Euphrona

Euphrona, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling small, pendant-like clusters. The tree has pinnately compound leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

The plant is also valued for its fast growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
