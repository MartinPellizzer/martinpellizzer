Erica patersonia

Erica patersonia, commonly known as the Paterson's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. Erica patersonia thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
