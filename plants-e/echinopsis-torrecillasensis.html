Echinopsis torrecillasensis

Echinopsis torrecillasensis, commonly known as the Torrecillas hedgehog cactus, is a species native to northern Argentina.

It is characterized by its spiny, globular stems that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
