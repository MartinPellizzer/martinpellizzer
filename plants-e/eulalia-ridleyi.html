Eulalia ridleyi

Eulalia ridleyi, commonly known as the Ridley's grass, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. This plant typically grows in wetland or swampy areas, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils. Eulalia ridleyi is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
