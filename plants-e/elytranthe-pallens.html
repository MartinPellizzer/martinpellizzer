Elytranthe pallens

Elytranthe pallens, commonly known as the silver leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Elytranthe pallens is also noted for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
