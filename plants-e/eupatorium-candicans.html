Eupatorium candicans

Eupatorium candicans, commonly known as the white iron plant, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, tropical environments. The plant features showy, white to pale pink flower heads that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are usually opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Eupatorium candicans is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
