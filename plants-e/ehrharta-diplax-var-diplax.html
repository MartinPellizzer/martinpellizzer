Ehrharta diplax var. diplax

Ehrharta diplax var. diplax, commonly known as silver grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are slightly hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice in xeriscaping.
