Echinocoryne holosericea

Echinocoryne holosericea, commonly known as the white sand dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive white, fluffy seed heads. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in a composite head.

Echinocoryne holosericea is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal dunes.
