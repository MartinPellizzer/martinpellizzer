Elatinoides ramosissima

Elatinoides ramosissima, commonly known as the slender elatin, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Elatinaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and succulent leaves. The plant is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland dunes. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
