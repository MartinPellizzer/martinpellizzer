Erica parviporandra

Erica parviporandra, commonly known as the small-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Erica parviporandra thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
