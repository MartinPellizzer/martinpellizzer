Epidendrum carautaense

Epidendrum carautaense, commonly known as the Carauta orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
