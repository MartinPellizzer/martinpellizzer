Eragrostis porosa var. parvula

Eragrostis porosa var. parvula, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its drooping, feathery inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has small, spikelet-based flowers that are often wind-pollinated.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid and semi-arid regions.
