Equisetum telmateia f. breve

Equisetum telmateia f. breve, commonly known as the dwarf scouring rush, is a fern-like plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched stems that are covered in small, scale-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its spore-producing structures, called strobili, are located at the tips of the stems.

This variety is notable for its compact size and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
