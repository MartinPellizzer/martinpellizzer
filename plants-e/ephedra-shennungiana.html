Ephedra shennungiana

Ephedra shennungiana, commonly known as the Chinese ephedra, is a flowering plant native to northern China.

It belongs to the family Ephedraceae and is characterized by its jointed, branched stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in cones, typical of the genus Ephedra. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its historical use in traditional medicine, it has been the subject of both scientific research and conservation efforts.
