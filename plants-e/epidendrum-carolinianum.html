Epidendrum carolinianum

Epidendrum carolinianum, commonly known as the Carolina epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and swampy areas. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It is a perennial species that thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade.

The botanical characteristics of Epidendrum carolinianum make it an attractive and relatively easy-to-care-for orchid for gardeners.
