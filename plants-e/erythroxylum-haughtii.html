Erythroxylum haughtii

Erythroxylum haughtii, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into elliptical, orange-red berries. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
