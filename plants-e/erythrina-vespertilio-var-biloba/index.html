Erythrina Vespertilio Var. Biloba

Velvet bean tree, scientifically known as Erythrina vespertilio var. biloba, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is recognized for its vibrant red flowers and distinctive foliage that make it a popular ornamental species. The tree also holds cultural and medicinal significance in various regions where it is traditionally used. Its seeds contain valuable nutrients and have been studied for their potential health benefits. Despite its beauty, the plant can be toxic if ingested, requiring caution in cultivation and handling.
