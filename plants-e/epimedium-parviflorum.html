Epimedium parviflorum

Epimedium parviflorum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium parviflorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate cold climates.
