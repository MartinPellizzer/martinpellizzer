Echinocactus asterioides

Echinocactus asterioides, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its spines are short and pale, providing protection from herbivores.

Echinocactus asterioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
