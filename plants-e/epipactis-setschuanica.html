Epipactis setschuanica

Epipactis setschuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.

Epipactis setschuanica is often found in rocky or clay soils and thrives in temperate climates.
