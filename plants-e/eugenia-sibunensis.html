Eugenia sibunensis

Eugenia sibunensis, commonly known as the Sibun wild guava, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces edible, fragrant berries that are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and biodiversity conservation efforts.
