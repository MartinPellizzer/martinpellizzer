Erica esteriana subsp. swartbergensis

Erica esteriana subsp.

swartbergensis, commonly known as the Swartberg heath, is a perennial shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heaths and heaths.

The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
