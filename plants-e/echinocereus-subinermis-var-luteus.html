Echinocereus subinermis var. luteus

Echinocereus subinermis var. luteus, commonly known as the yellow hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant features a ribbed stem covered in white to pale green spines, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
