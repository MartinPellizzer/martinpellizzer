Echinocactus sickmannii

Echinocactus sickmannii, commonly known as the sickmann's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny, ribbed stems and a distinctive crown of flowers. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
