Epidendrum dactyloclinium

Epidendrum dactyloclinium, commonly known as the silver-veined epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features long, narrow leaves with distinctive silver veins, giving it its common name. It produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow.

This epiphytic orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
