Euphrasia officinalis subsp. alpina

Euphrasia officinalis subsp.

alpina, commonly known as the Alpine eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe. It typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats at high elevations.

The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported eye and respiratory health benefits.
