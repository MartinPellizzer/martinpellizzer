Elatostema sinopurpureum

Elatostema sinopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-veined elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features elliptical leaves with a distinctive purple-veined pattern, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema sinopurpureum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in traditional medicine.
