Eugenia nesiotica

Eugenia nesiotica, commonly known as the red-eyed treefrog plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

Eugenia nesiotica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
