Eupyrena glabra

Eupyrena glabra, commonly known as the smooth eupyrena, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels. The leaves are typically narrow and finely divided, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges.
