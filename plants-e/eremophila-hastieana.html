Eremophila hastieana

Eremophila hastieana, commonly known as the pink eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the plant family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and a deep root system.

Eremophila hastieana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
