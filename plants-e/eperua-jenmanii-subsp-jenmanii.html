Eperua jenmanii subsp. jenmanii

Eperua jenmanii subsp.

jenmanii, commonly known as the Jenman's eperua, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the rainforests of northern Australia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive fruit pods.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its bark is generally smooth and may have a reddish or brownish hue, while its wood is valued for its durability and workability.

This subspecies is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in humid tropical climates.
