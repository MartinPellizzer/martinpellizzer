Eriostemon scaber subsp. latifolius

Eriostemon scaber subsp.

latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf eriostemon, is a member of the Rutaceae family. It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, short hairs.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It is native to eastern Australia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This sub-species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
