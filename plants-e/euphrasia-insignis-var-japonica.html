Euphrasia insignis var. japonica

Euphrasia insignis var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese eyebright, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.

This variety is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
