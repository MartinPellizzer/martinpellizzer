Euphorbia schaijesii

Euphorbia schaijesii, commonly known as the Schaijes' spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its short, branched stems and small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces unique, colorful cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in arid environments and is well adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its striking appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
