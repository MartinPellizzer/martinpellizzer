Elatostema rhizomatosum

Elatostema rhizomatosum, commonly known as the creeping spearplant, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively. It produces tiny greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Elatostema rhizomatosum is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to partial sunlight.
