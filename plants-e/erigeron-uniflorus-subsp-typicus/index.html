Erigeron Uniflorus Subsp. Typicus

Erigeron uniflorus subsp. typicus is a delicate flowering plant native to the western united states. it belongs to the aster family and is known for its small blue to purple flowers. this subspecies is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists. despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
