Epidendrum macphersonii

Epidendrum macphersonii, commonly known as the MacPherson's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and long, linear leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Epidendrum macphersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
