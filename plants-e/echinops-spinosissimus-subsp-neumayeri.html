Echinops spinosissimus subsp. neumayeri

Echinops spinosissimus subsp.

neumayeri, commonly known as the Neumayer's thistle, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its spiny, spherical inflorescence and gray-green foliage.

The plant produces small blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
