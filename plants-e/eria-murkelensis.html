Eria murkelensis

Eria murkelensis, commonly known as the Murkel's orchid, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eria murkelensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
