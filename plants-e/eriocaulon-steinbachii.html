Eriocaulon steinbachii

Eriocaulon steinbachii, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences. This species is often found in wetland environments, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively and form dense mats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and semi-aquatic ecosystems.
