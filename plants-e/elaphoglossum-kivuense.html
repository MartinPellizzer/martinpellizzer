Elaphoglossum kivuense

Elaphoglossum kivuense, commonly known as the Kivu holly fern, is a rare and endemic species found in the mountainous regions of the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rock faces or in forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
