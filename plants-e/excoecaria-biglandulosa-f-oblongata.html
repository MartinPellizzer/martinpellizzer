Excoecaria biglandulosa f. oblongata

Excoecaria biglandulosa f. oblongata, commonly known as the broad-leaved croton, is a tropical shrub characterized by its distinctive, broad, waxy leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. Its foliage is evergreen, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange-red berries.
