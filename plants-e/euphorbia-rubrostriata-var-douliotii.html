Euphorbia rubrostriata var. douliotii

Euphorbia rubrostriata var. douliotii, commonly known as the red-striated euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems covered in a distinctive red or pinkish hue, with yellowish-green leaves that are often spiny. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on branched inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
