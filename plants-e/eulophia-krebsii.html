Eulophia krebsii

Eulophia krebsii, commonly known as the Krebs' eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive yellow lip. Eulophia krebsii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
