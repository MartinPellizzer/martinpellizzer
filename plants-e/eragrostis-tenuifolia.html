Eragrostis tenuifolia

Eragrostis tenuifolia, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce light blue to purple flowers. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies, woodlands, and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Eragrostis tenuifolia is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and hardiness.
