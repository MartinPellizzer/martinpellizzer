Eranthemum crenulatum var. grandiflorum

Eranthemum crenulatum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features a compact growth habit with upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
