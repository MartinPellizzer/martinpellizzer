Erodium cicutarium var. arenicola

Erodium cicutarium var. arenicola, commonly known as sand eryngo, is a low-growing annual herb native to Mediterranean regions.

It features small, yellowish-white flowers and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in disturbed areas. It has a branched stem and produces numerous small, capsule-like fruits.

Sand eryngo is known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
