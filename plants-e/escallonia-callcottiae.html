Escallonia callcottiae

Escallonia callcottiae, commonly known as the Callcott's escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and seasonal blooms.
