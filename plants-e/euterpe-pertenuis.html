Euterpe pertenuis

Euterpe pertenuis, commonly known as the slender palm or the yellow palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of elongated, arching leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are followed by elongated, yellowish fruits. Euterpe pertenuis is adapted to the humid, tropical conditions of its native habitat, thriving in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils.

It plays a role in the ecological diversity of the Amazon, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
