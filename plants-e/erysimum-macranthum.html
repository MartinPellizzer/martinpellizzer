Erysimum macranthum

Erysimum macranthum, commonly known as the showy shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, lobed leaves that resemble a shepherd's purse. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Erysimum macranthum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
