Eugenia onesima

Eugenia onesima, commonly known as the "Java apple," is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in tropical climates and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
