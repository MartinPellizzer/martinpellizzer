Echeveria harmsii

Echeveria harmsii, commonly known as the Harms' echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as farina. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
