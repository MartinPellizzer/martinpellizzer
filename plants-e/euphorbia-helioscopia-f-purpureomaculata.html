Euphorbia helioscopia f. purpureomaculata

Euphorbia helioscopia f. purpureomaculata, commonly known as the purple-spotted spurge, is a flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features upright stems covered in soft, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by vibrant green bracts. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a distinctive purple or reddish marking.

It is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
