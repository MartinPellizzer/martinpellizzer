Erodium choulettianum

Erodium choulettianum, commonly known as the yellow field bean, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces small, pea-like flowers that range from pink to deep red. Its fruit is a hairy, elongated pod containing several elliptical seeds.

This species is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
