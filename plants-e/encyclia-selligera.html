Encyclia selligera

Encyclia selligera, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, narrow leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
