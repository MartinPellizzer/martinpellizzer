Epidendrum posadarum

Epidendrum posadarum, commonly known as the Posada's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Epidendrum posadarum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
