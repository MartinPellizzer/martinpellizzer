Euonymus haberi

Euonymus haberi, commonly known as the Haber's spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange-red berries that are attractive to birds. Euonymus haberi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
