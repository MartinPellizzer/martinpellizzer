Echinodorus grandiflorus var. aureus

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. aureus, commonly known as the golden sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are bright green with a distinctive yellow midrib, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high lighting and can grow in a variety of water conditions.

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. aureus is relatively easy to care for and is known for its resilience in both planted tanks and natural water bodies.
