Eupatorium neglectum

Eupatorium neglectum, commonly known as boneset, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium neglectum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
