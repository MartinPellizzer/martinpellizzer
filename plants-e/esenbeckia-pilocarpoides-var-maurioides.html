Esenbeckia pilocarpoides var. maurioides

Esenbeckia pilocarpoides var. maurioides, commonly known as the Maurioides esenbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant features elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and unique floral structure.
