Eragrostis bromoides

Eragrostis bromoides, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in open habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife.
