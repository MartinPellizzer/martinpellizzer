Erigeron croaticus

Erigeron croaticus, commonly known as the Croatian fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
