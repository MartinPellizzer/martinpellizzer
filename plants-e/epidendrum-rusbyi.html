Epidendrum rusbyi

Epidendrum rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.
