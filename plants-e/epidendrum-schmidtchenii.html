Epidendrum schmidtchenii

Epidendrum schmidtchenii, commonly known as the Schmidtchen's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Epidendrum schmidtchenii is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
