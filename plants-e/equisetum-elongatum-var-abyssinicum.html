Equisetum elongatum var. abyssinicum

Equisetum elongatum var. abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian horsetail, is a perennial plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, jointed stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, spore-bearing cones at the base of its stems, which are essential for reproduction. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ancient lineage dates back to the Carboniferous period, making it a living fossil with unique botanical features.
