Echites congestus

Echites congestus, commonly known as the clustered echites, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has smooth, green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Echites congestus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact size and attractive floral display.

It is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
