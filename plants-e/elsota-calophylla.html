Elsota calophylla

Elsota calophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by flat, linear seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Elsota calophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
