Ervum dalmaticum

Ervum dalmaticum, commonly known as the Dalmatian vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, creeping stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Ervum dalmaticum plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
