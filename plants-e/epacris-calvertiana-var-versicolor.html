Epacris calvertiana var. versicolor

Epacris calvertiana var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to white and purple. The plant typically grows in heathland and open forest habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It is a shrub that can reach up to 1 meter in height, with lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant flowers.
