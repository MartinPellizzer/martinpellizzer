Erysimum idae

Erysimum idae, commonly known as the Ida mountain rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of oblanceolate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, bright yellow, and arranged in racemes, typical of the mustard family.

It is adapted to grow in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
