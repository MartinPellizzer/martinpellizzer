Euphorbia boiteaui var. spirosticha

Euphorbia boiteaui var. spirosticha, commonly known as the spiral euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant features thick, fleshy stems that store water, typical of succulents adapted to arid environments. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters, adding to its visual appeal.

It is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.
