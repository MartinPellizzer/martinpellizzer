Encyclia osmantha

Encyclia osmantha, commonly known as the Osmantha Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical and subtropical environments.

Encyclia osmantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
