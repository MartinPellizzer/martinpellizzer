Epigeneium naviculare

Epigeneium naviculare, commonly known as the boat-shaped epigeneium, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its aerial roots and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white, followed by berries that are edible for some bird species.
