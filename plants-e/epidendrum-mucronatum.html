Epidendrum mucronatum

Epidendrum mucronatum, commonly known as the Sharp-tipped Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum mucronatum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
