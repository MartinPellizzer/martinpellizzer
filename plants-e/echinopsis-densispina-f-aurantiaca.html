Echinopsis densispina f. aurantiaca

Echinopsis densispina f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange-tipped Moon Cactus, is a fascinating cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of orange and yellow. The plant features prominent, spiny areoles and produces small, white flowers that attract nocturnal pollinators. It is a popular choice for collectors due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

This hybrid form is prized for its unique coloration and ornamental value in succulent gardens.
