Exomicrum densiflorum

Exomicrum densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower exomicrum, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.
