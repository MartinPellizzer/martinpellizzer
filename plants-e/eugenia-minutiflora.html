Eugenia minutiflora

Eugenia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Eugenia minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
