Erica elimensis var. elimensis

Erica elimensis var. elimensis, commonly known as the Elim heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
