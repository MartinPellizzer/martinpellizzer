Epidendrum candelabrum

Epidendrum candelabrum, commonly known as the candlestick orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that resemble a candlestick, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Epidendrum candelabrum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
