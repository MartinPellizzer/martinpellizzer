Eugenia venulosa

Eugenia venulosa, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that have a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often clustered together, followed by edible, yellowish fruits. Its fruit contains a single seed and is sometimes used in local cuisines.

Eugenia venulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
