Echinops melitenensis

Echinops melitenensis, commonly known as the Melitene thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the eastern Mediterranean region, particularly found in areas of Turkey and Syria. The plant is characterized by its spiny, spherical heads of pale blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a rough, hairy texture.

Echinops melitenensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
