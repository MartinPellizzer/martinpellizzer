Eugenia mindanaensis

Eugenia mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao eugenia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest in horticulture and conservation efforts.
