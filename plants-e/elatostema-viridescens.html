Elatostema viridescens

Elatostema viridescens, commonly known as the green-stemmed elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate. Its flowers are inconspicuous, producing tiny white or greenish blooms, and it relies on insects for pollination.

Elatostema viridescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
