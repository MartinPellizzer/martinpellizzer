Eriosema buchananii var. subprostratum

Eriosema buchananii var. subprostratum, commonly known as the prostrate eriosema, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to cream in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
