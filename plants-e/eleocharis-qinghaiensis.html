Eleocharis qinghaiensis

Eleocharis qinghaiensis, commonly known as the Qinghai sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the Qinghai Lake region in China, where it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences.
