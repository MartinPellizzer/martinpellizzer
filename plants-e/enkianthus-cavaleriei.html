Enkianthus cavaleriei

Enkianthus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often resembling lanterns. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Enkianthus cavaleriei is also valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
