Eronema

Eronema, commonly known as the tree of heaven, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It consists of several species, with Eronema zeyheri being the most well-known. The plant is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. It produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Eronema is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
