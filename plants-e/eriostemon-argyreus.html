Eriostemon argyreus

Eriostemon argyreus, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are pollinated by birds and insects, contributing to its ecological importance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
