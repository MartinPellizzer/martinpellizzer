Eriochilus petricola

Eriochilus petricola, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
