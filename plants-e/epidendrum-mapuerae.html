Epidendrum mapuerae

Epidendrum mapuerae, commonly known as the Mapuera Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a glossy surface and a pointed tip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
