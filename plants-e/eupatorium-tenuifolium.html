Eupatorium tenuifolium

Eupatorium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It is known for its aromatic foliage and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early fall, attracting various pollinators.
