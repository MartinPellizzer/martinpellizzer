Echinodorus × portoalegrensis

Echinodorus × portoalegrensis, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often cultivated in ponds and aquariums. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense vegetation.

Echinodorus × portoalegrensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
