Epidendrum abbreviatum

Epidendrum abbreviatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has dark green, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum abbreviatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
