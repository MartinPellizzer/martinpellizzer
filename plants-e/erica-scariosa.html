Erica scariosa

Erica scariosa, commonly known as the scaly heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. Erica scariosa typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
