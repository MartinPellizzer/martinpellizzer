Epidendrum matudae

Epidendrum matudae, commonly known as the Matuda's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum matudae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
