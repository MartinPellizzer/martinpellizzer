Echites annularis

Echites annularis, commonly known as the ringed echites, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with opposite leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters at the base of the plant.

Echites annularis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal areas.
