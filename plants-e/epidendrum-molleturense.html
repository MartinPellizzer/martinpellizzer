Epidendrum molleturense

Epidendrum molleturense, commonly known as the Molleturen Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial plant that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. It belongs to the Epidendraceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in some regions.
