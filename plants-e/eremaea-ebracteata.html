Eremaea ebracteata

Eremaea ebracteata, commonly known as the spinifex eremaea, is a shrub native to arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers, typically appearing in spring. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Eremaea ebracteata is well adapted to extreme conditions, making it a resilient component of desert ecosystems.
