Eriocaulon huanchacanum

Eriocaulon huanchacanum, commonly known as the Huanchacan spike-rush, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes various species of spike-rushes and their relatives. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. It typically grows in wet, marshy habitats, thriving in humid and shaded environments.
