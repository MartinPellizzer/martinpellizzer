Erica malleolaris

Erica malleolaris, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes various shrubs and evergreen plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica malleolaris thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive foliage.
