Eulalia brevifolia

Eulalia brevifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf pampas grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, white plumes during the flowering season. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It has a fibrous root system and can tolerate a variety of soil types.

Eulalia brevifolia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
