Eriosema villosum

Eriosema villosum, commonly known as the silky eriosema, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its foliage is typically pubescent, giving it a soft, velvety appearance.
