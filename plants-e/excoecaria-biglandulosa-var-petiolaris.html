Excoecaria biglandulosa var. petiolaris

Excoecaria biglandulosa var. petiolaris, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and distinctive yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a milky sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
