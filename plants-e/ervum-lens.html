Ervum lens

Ervum lens, commonly known as the lentil, is an annual legume belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, lens-shaped seeds that come in a variety of colors, including green, yellow, red, and brown. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces clusters of small, white or purple flowers. Its pod-like fruits contain 2–5 seeds, which are harvested when mature.

Lentils are known for their ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making them a valuable crop for sustainable agriculture.
