Eulophia stricta

Eulophia stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Eulophia stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
