Erica bolusiae var. bolusiae

Erica bolusiae var. bolusiae, commonly known as the Bolus heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica bolusiae var. bolusiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
