Encyclia sabanensis

Encyclia sabanensis, commonly known as the Saban orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow or white flowers with prominent lips. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Encyclia sabanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
