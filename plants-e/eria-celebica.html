Eria celebica

Eria celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a pseudobulb structure, which helps it store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

Eria celebica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its beauty and unique floral structure.
