Exostigma rivulare

Exostigma rivulare, commonly known as the river exostigma, is a rare aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically submerged or partially submerged. Its root system is well-developed, allowing it to anchor in muddy or sandy substrates.
