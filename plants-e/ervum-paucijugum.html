Ervum paucijugum

Ervum paucijugum, commonly known as the sparse-jugum vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with few leaflets and small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces leguminous pods that contain several small, rounded seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare species in certain regions.
