Eryngium brasiliense

Eryngium brasiliense, commonly known as Brazilian thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its spiny, blue-green leaves and purple to pink flowers. Eryngium brasiliense is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
