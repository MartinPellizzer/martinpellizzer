Escallonia myrtilloides var. myrtilloides

Escallonia myrtilloides var. myrtilloides, commonly known as the dwarf evergreen honeysuckle, is a compact shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its evergreen nature and ability to attract pollinators.
