Erica × willmorei

Erica × willmorei, commonly known as the hybrid heather, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, producing shades of white, pink, and red. Erica × willmorei thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
