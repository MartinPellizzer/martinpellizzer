Eriostemon nodiflorus var. lasiocalyx

Eriostemon nodiflorus var. lasiocalyx, commonly known as the silky eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.

It is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
