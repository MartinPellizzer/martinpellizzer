Eragrostis delicatula

Eragrostis delicatula, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, drooping inflorescences and fine, needle-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its botanical features include small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are often tinged with purple or pink.

Eragrostis delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
