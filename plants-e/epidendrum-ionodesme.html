Epidendrum ionodesme

Epidendrum ionodesme, commonly known as the violet-veined orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and a few leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often have a violet or purple hue with intricate veining.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
