Enicostema hyssopifolium

Enicostema hyssopifolium, commonly known as the woollyleaf sida, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in clusters.

Enicostema hyssopifolium is characterized by its robust root system and its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
