Erythranthe × maculosa

Erythranthe × maculosa, commonly known as the spotted monkeyflower, is a hybrid plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Phrymaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as streambanks and woodland edges. It has oval, hairy leaves and produces showy, spurred flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
