Equisetum diffusum var. paucidentatum

Equisetum diffusum var. paucidentatum, commonly known as the slender horsetail, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its jointed, hollow stems that are typically green to yellow-green in color. The plant produces small, spore-bearing cones at the base of its stems, which are used for reproduction. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its simple, needle-like leaves are arranged in whorls around the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
