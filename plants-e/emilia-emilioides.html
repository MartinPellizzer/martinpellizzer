Emilia emilioides

Emilia emilioides, commonly known as the Emilia or Mexican daisy, is an annual flowering plant native to Mexico.

It features bright yellow to orange daisy-like flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Emilia emilioides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
