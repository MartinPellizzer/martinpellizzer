Escallonia × rigida

Escallonia × rigida, commonly known as the hardy escallonia, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or red flowers in the spring. The plant is evergreen, with a compact, rounded form that makes it suitable for hedges or ornamental gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Escallonia × rigida is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it a low-maintenance choice for gardeners.
