Eucomis regia

Eucomis regia, commonly known as the pineapple lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves, creating a unique architectural appearance. The plant produces large, showy flower spikes that resemble pineapples, hence its common name. Its flowers are typically white or pink, with a distinctive, spiky bract at the base.

Eucomis regia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
