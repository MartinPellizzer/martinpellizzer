Eleocharis erratica

Eleocharis erratica, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. This species is typically found in wetland habitats, including marshes and shallow water areas. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that often grow close to the ground.
