Echium stenosiphon subsp. glabrescens

Echium stenosiphon subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the narrow-lobed thornweed, is a member of the Boraginaceae family. It is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and narrow, lanceolate leaves.
