Echinocereus scheeri subsp. scheeri

Echinocereus scheeri subsp.

scheeri, commonly known as the Scherri's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico. It is a low-growing, spine-tipped cactus with a compact, globular shape.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
