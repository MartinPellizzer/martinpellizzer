Elatine schkuhriana

Elatine schkuhriana, commonly known as the Schkuhriana waterwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. Elatine schkuhriana plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
