Encyclia fowliei

Encyclia fowliei, commonly known as the Fowlie's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in the summer months. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

Encyclia fowliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
