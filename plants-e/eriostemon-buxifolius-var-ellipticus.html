Eriostemon buxifolius var. ellipticus

Eriostemon buxifolius var. ellipticus, commonly known as the elliptic-leaved eriostemon, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically a dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.
