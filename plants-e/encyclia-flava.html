Encyclia flava

Encyclia flava, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in a raceme along the stem. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Encyclia flava thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
