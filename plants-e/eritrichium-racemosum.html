Eritrichium racemosum

Eritrichium racemosum, commonly known as the clustered forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features slender, hairy stems that bear clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its showy flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
