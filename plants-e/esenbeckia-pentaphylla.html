Esenbeckia pentaphylla

Esenbeckia pentaphylla, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. The fruit is a yellow or orange berry that contains several seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruit.
