Ericameria laricifolia

Ericameria laricifolia, commonly known as silverleaf erica.

It is a shrub native to the western United States, particularly in California and Oregon. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to a dense covering of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense heads, typically blooming in the spring.

Ericameria laricifolia is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.
