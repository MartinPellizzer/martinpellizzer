Eria rostriflora

Eria rostriflora, commonly known as the rostriflorous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Eria rostriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
