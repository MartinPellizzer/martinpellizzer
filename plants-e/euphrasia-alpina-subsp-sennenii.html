Euphrasia alpina subsp. sennenii

Euphrasia alpina subsp.

sennenii, commonly known as the Alpine eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in mountainous areas of the Alps and is valued for its medicinal properties.
