Erythrodes chinensis

Erythrodes chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese red-flowered orchid, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its striking red petals and green sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous forests. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native habitat.
