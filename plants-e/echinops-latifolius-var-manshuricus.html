Echinops latifolius var. manshuricus

Echinops latifolius var. manshuricus, commonly known as the Manshurian thistle, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of eastern Asia.

It features striking spherical flower heads that are typically blue to purple in color, surrounded by spiny bracts. The plant has gray-green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
