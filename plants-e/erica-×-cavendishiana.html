Erica × cavendishiana

Erica × cavendishiana, commonly known as the hybrid heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and long flowering period.

Erica × cavendishiana is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
