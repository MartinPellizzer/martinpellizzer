Euphorbia goyazensis

Euphorbia goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis spurge, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and unique floral structures. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are often reduced to small, scale-like structures, while its flowers are clustered in cyathia, a specialized inflorescence.

Euphorbia goyazensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking appearance.
