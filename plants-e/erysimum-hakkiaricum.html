Erysimum hakkiaricum

Erysimum hakkiaricum, commonly known as the Hakkiaric Wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard and wallflower species. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing adaptability to arid conditions.

Its foliage is characterized by narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss.
