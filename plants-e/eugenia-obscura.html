Eugenia obscura

Eugenia obscura, commonly known as the dark eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, dark purple to black berries that are typically harvested when fully ripe. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its flowers are small and white or pale pink.

Eugenia obscura is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
