Erica kraussiana

Erica kraussiana, commonly known as the Cape heath or Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in spring and summer. Erica kraussiana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
