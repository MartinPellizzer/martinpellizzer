Erigeron × riklii

Erigeron × riklii, commonly known as the New England aster, is a hybrid perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are gray-green in color. Its flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets, which are usually pink to purple in hue.

Erigeron × riklii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
