Echeveria semivestita var. floresiana

Echeveria semivestita var. floresiana, commonly known as the Floresian Snowflake, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with a powdery coating that gives it a frosty appearance. The plant produces clusters of white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
