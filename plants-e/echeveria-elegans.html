Echeveria elegans

Echeveria elegans, commonly known as the "White Lion" or "Mother of Millions," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or purple flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
