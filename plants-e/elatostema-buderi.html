Elatostema buderi

Elatostema buderi, commonly known as the silverleaf elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white or silver hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, which are often borne on slender stalks.

Elatostema buderi is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in shady gardens.
