Evaiezoa burseriana

Evaiezoa burseriana, commonly known as the Guatemalan birch, is a small evergreen tree native to the highlands of Guatemala.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes frankincense and myrrh trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by spherical, orange-red fruits.

Evaiezoa burseriana is valued for its aromatic resin, which has been used traditionally in local medicine.
