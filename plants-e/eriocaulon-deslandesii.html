Eriocaulon deslandesii

Eriocaulon deslandesii, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features a dense, grass-like foliage with narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spike of small, white to pale green flowers.
