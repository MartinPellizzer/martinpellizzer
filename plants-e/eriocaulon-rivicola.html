Eriocaulon rivicola

Eriocaulon rivicola, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in wetland environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

Eriocaulon rivicola plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of freshwater ecosystems.
