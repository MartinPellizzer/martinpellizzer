Eriocaulon elegans

Eriocaulon elegans, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

Eriocaulon elegans produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale flowers.
