Euclea schimperi var. daphnoides

Euclea schimperi var. daphnoides, commonly known as the Daphnoides euclea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
