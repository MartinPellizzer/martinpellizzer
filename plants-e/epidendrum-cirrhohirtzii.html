Epidendrum cirrhohirtzii

Epidendrum cirrhohirtzii, commonly known as the red-tipped epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red-tipped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
