Escallonia tubar

Escallonia tubar, commonly known as the tubular escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with a glossy, dark green appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. These flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Escallonia tubar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
