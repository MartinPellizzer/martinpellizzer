Exosolenia

Exosolenia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Exosolenia species are typically evergreen and thrive in humid, well-drained soil conditions. They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and ease of care.

The genus includes several species, such as Exosolenia plicata and Exosolenia rupicola, which are appreciated for their vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
