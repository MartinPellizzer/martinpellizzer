Ephedra Distachya Subvar. Linnaei

Ephedra distachya subvar. linnaei is a unique plant belonging to the Ephedraceae family. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia and parts of China. This shrub is known for its distinctive morphology and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions. Its stems are typically woody and branched, with small, scale-like leaves. The plant has been historically used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
