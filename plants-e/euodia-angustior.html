Euodia angustior

Euodia angustior, commonly known as narrowleaf Chinese sandpaper tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain small, hard seeds. Euodia angustior is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
