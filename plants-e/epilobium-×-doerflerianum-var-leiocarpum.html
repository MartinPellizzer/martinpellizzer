Epilobium × doerflerianum var. leiocarpum

Epilobium × doerflerianum var. leiocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-seeded willowherb, is a hybrid plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and riverbanks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and produces showy pink to purple flowers in the summer months. Its flowers are arranged in spikes and are pollinated primarily by insects.

The species is characterized by its distinctive smooth, hairless seeds that are dispersed by wind.
