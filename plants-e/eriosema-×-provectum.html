Eriosema × provectum

Eriosema × provectum, commonly known as the African violet, is a hybrid plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
