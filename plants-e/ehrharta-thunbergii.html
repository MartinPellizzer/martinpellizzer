Ehrharta thunbergii

Ehrharta thunbergii, commonly known as common Bermuda grass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, dark green blades and dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant has rhizomes and stolons that allow it to spread rapidly and form a dense turf. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it a popular choice for lawns and erosion control.

Its botanical features include small, spike-like inflorescences and leathery, narrow leaves.
