Elaphoglossum macilentum

Elaphoglossum macilentum, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, brownish layer of rhizomorphs.

Elaphoglossum macilentum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its elegant foliage.
