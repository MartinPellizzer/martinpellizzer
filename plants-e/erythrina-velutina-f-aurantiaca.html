Erythrina velutina f. aurantiaca

Erythrina velutina f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange flame acacia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features compound leaves with three leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes due to its showy blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
