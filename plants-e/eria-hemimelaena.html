Eria hemimelaena

Eria hemimelaena, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a raceme. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens and greenhouses.
