Echinospermum lappula var. squarrosum

Echinospermum lappula var. squarrosum, commonly known as the rough lappula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This variety is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in temperate climates.
