Effusiella sparsiflora

Effusiella sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered effusiella, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has slender, often hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.
