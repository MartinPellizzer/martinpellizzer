Elymus stenachyrus

Elymus stenachyrus, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

Elymus stenachyrus is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
