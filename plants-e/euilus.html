Euilus

Euilus, commonly known as the common euilus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered white or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a mild, aromatic scent.

Euilus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
