Epidendrum deltochilum

Epidendrum deltochilum, commonly known as the Delta-lobed Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and can range in color from white to pink, with a distinctive fragrance.

Epidendrum deltochilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
