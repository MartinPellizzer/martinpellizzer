Echeveria compressicaulis

Echeveria compressicaulis, commonly known as the compact echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and striking appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
