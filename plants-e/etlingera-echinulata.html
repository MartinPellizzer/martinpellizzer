Etlingera echinulata

Etlingera echinulata, commonly known as the spiny ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Etlingera echinulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
