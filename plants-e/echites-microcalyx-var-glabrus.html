Echites microcalyx var. glabrus

Echites microcalyx var. glabrus, commonly known as the smooth echites, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth texture that distinguishes this variety from others in the species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
