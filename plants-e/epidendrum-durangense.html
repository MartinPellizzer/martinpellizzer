Epidendrum durangense

Epidendrum durangense, commonly known as the Durango Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its erect, branched growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in two rows along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum durangense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
