Epidendrum cyclopterum

Epidendrum cyclopterum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Moon, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
