Euphorbia cylindroclada

Euphorbia cylindroclada, commonly known as the cylindrical spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branched stem covered in short, cylindrical spines, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures, further adapting to its harsh climate.

Euphorbia cylindroclada is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
