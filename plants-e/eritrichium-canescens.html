Eritrichium canescens

Eritrichium canescens, commonly known as the hoary eryngo, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and along roadsides. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

Eritrichium canescens produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall.
