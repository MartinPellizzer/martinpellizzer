Eriosema pycnanthum var. veadeirense

Eriosema pycnanthum var. veadeirense, commonly known as the Veadeirense eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer.
