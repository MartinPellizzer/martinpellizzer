Eriostemon buxifolius subsp. obovatus

Eriostemon buxifolius subsp.

obovatus, commonly known as the broad-leaved eriostemon, is a shrub native to Australia. It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its glossy, ovate to elliptical leaves.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
