Euphrasia salisburgensis var. bergadensis

Euphrasia salisburgensis var. bergadensis, commonly known as the Bergadenskaya eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
