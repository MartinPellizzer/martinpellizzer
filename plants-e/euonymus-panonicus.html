Euonymus panonicus

Euonymus panonicus, commonly known as the Panonic spurge or European euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, simple, and often elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a slightly rough texture.

Euonymus panonicus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
