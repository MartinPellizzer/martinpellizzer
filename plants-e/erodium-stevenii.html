Erodium stevenii

Erodium stevenii, commonly known as Steven's bedstraw, is a small annual herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, pink or white flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, with a hairy texture, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Erodium stevenii is often found in arid environments and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient soils.
