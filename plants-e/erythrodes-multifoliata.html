Erythrodes multifoliata

Erythrodes multifoliata, commonly known as the multileaf erythrodes, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and compound leaves with multiple lobes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
