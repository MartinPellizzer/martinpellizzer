Echinodorus grandiflorus var. longiscapus

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. longiscapus, commonly known as the giant sword plant, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, elongated leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, giving it a dramatic appearance in freshwater aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular fertilization to maintain its lush green coloration. It is often used as a background plant due to its height and dense foliage.

Additionally, it produces small white flowers that bloom during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value.
