Eugenia egensis var. latifolia

Eugenia egensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf eugenia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

The leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications in its native regions.
