Erica × nelsonii

Erica × nelsonii, commonly known as the Natal heath, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica × nelsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.

Its flowering season usually occurs in late winter to early spring.
