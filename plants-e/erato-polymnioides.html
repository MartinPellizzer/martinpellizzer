Erato polymnioides

Erato polymnioides, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
