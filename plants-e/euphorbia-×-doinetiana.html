Euphorbia × doinetiana

Euphorbia × doinetiana, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, though they are not true flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia × doinetiana is also known for its ability to store water in its leaves and stems, which helps it survive in harsh conditions.
