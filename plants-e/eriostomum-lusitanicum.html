Eriostomum lusitanicum

Eriostomum lusitanicum, commonly known as the Iberian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its stems are erect and often branched.
