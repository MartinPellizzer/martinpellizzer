Eugenia glazioviana var. pauciflora

Eugenia glazioviana var. pauciflora, commonly known as the Glaziov's eugenia, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
