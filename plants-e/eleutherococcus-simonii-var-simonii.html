Eleutherococcus simonii var. simonii

Eleutherococcus simonii var. simonii, commonly known as Siberian ginseng, is a deciduous shrub native to the forests of eastern Russia, China, and Korea.

Its adaptability to cold climates makes it a hardy and resilient plant in temperate regions.
