Eriostemon myoporoides subsp. leichhardtii

Eriostemon myoporoides subsp.

leichhardtii, commonly known as the Leichhardt's eriostemon, is a small shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia. It typically grows in heathlands and rocky soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale blue, and appear in clusters during the spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native garden settings.
