Elatostema grandidentatum

Elatostema grandidentatum, commonly known as the broad-toothed elatostema, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and delicate, white flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a short stem and produces small, inconspicuous fruits that disperse its seeds.

Its leaves are typically oval or broad-elliptic with prominent teeth along the margins.
