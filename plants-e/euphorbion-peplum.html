Euphorbion peplum

Euphorbion peplum, commonly known as the peplum euphorb, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit with smooth, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green, surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
