Encyclia argentinensis f. maior

Encyclia argentinensis f. maior, commonly known as the Argentine Encyclia, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that form a rosette, creating a water-holding cup. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid-like plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
