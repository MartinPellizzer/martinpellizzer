Eriope macrostachya var. villosa

Eriope macrostachya var. villosa, commonly known as the woolly eriope, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique texture and vibrant floral display.
