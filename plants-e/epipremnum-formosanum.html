Epipremnum formosanum

Epipremnum formosanum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Epipremnum formosanum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ornamental appeal.
