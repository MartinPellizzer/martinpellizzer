Eurya boninensis

Eurya boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin eurya, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Bonin Islands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
