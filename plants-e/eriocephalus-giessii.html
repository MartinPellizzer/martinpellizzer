Eriocephalus giessii

Eriocephalus giessii, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant is often found in rocky areas and along coastal regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Eriocephalus giessii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
