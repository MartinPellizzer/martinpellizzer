Elytrigia pulcherrima

Elytrigia pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful grass or slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and soil stabilization.
