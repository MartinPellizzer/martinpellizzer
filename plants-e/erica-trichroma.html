Erica trichroma

Erica trichroma, commonly known as the tri-color heath, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and its clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that typically appear in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica trichroma is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
