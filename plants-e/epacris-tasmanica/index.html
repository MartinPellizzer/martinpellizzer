Epacris Tasmanica

Epacris tasmanica is a unique flowering plant native to Tasmania, known for its striking pink and white blooms. It thrives in the cool, moist environments of the region's high-altitude regions. This plant is part of the Heath family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native flora. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to other Tasmanian plants.
