Euonymus lawsonii f. salicifolius

Euonymus lawsonii f. salicifolius, commonly known as the willow-leafed spindle tree, is a cultivar of the Euonymus genus characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features elongated, willow-like leaves that are typically dark green and have a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are often clustered in pairs, adding ornamental value in the fall. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This cultivar is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
