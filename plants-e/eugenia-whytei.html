Eugenia whytei

Eugenia whytei, commonly known as the Whyte's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia whytei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
