Eugenia daaouiensis var. glabriflora

Eugenia daaouiensis var. glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered eugenia, is a shrub or small tree native to the humid forests of western Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes many aromatic and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
