Encyclia leucantha

Encyclia leucantha, commonly known as the white-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Encyclia leucantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
