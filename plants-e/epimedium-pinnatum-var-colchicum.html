Epimedium pinnatum var. colchicum

Epimedium pinnatum var. colchicum, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a perennial plant native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are typically golden to reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage, which remains attractive throughout the growing season.
