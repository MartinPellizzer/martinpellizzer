Eremurus bactrianus

Eremurus bactrianus, commonly known as the Bactrian spire, is a striking plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in the spring. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Eremurus bactrianus is a perennial plant that thrives in full sun and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
