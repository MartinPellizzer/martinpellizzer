Eria oligotricha

Eria oligotricha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often erect stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces showy, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pink.

Eria oligotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
