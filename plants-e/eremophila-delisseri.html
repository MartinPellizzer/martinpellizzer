Eremophila delisseri

Eremophila delisseri, commonly known as the Delisser's eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Eremophila delisseri is often used in native gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
