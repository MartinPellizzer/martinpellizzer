Eukrania

Eukrania, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescences that produce bright orange or red tubular flowers. The plants are native to southern Africa and are often cultivated for their striking appearance. Eukrania species are typically evergreen shrubs with tough, leathery leaves.

They thrive in full sun and well-drained soil, making them suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.
