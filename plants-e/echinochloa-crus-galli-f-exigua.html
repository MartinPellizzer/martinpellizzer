Echinochloa crus-galli f. exigua

Echinochloa crus-galli f. exigua, commonly known as slender barnyardgrass, is a grass species belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown spikelets. It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural fields.

Its rapid growth and high reproductive capacity make it a challenging species to manage in cultivated areas.
