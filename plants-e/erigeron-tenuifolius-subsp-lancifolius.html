Erigeron tenuifolius subsp. lancifolius

Erigeron tenuifolius subsp.

lancifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils.

The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, daisy-like, with white petals surrounding a yellow center.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
