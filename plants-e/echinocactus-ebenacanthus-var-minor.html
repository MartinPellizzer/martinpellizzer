Echinocactus ebenacanthus var. minor

Echinocactus ebenacanthus var. minor, commonly known as the small hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal water once established. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for desert gardens.
