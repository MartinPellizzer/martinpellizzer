Ervum ciliare

Ervum ciliare, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces trifoliate leaves composed of three small, heart-shaped leaflets. Its pods are typically slender and contain several oval seeds.

Ervum ciliare is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or as a cover crop.
