Encyclia kingsii

Encyclia kingsii, commonly known as the Kings' Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette, aiding in water retention.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
