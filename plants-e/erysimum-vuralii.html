Erysimum vuralii

Erysimum vuralii, commonly known as the Vural's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

Erysimum vuralii is typically found in rocky or limestone habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
