Eupatorium densifolium

Eupatorium densifolium, commonly known as denseleaf thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium densifolium is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
