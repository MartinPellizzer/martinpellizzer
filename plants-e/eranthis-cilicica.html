Eranthis cilicica

Eranthis cilicica, commonly known as the Cyprus primrose, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with hairy surfaces, and its flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive purple or brownish glandular spot at the base. The plant produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous small, dry seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Eranthis cilicica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
