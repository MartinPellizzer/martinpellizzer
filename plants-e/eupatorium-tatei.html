Eupatorium tatei

Eupatorium tatei, commonly known as Japanese eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, pinkish-white composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Eupatorium tatei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
