Echinocereus reichenbachii var. chisoensis

Echinocereus reichenbachii var. chisoensis, commonly known as the Chisos hedgehog cactus, is a columnar cactus native to the Chisos Mountains in Texas.

It features a tall, spiny stem with a ribbed structure that helps reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to full sun and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in desert gardens.
