Eragrostis longifolia

Eragrostis longifolia, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces fluffy, pale blue to silver-gray seed heads. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Eragrostis longifolia is also valued for its role in erosion control and wildlife habitat.
