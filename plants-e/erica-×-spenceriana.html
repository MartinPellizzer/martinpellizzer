Erica × spenceriana

Erica × spenceriana, commonly known as the Spenser's heath, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Erica × spenceriana is well-suited to acidic, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
