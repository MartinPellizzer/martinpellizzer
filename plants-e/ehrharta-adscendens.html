Ehrharta adscendens

Ehrharta adscendens, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Ehrharta adscendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
