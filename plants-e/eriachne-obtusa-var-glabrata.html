Eriachne obtusa var. glabrata

Eriachne obtusa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth eriachne, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or coastal environments, often found in dune ecosystems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are smooth to the touch, as indicated by its varietal name "glabrata.". Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are clustered at the base of the stem.

This variety is adapted to tolerate saline and arid conditions, making it a resilient component of coastal flora.
