Euphorbia striatella var. microsciadia

Euphorbia striatella var. microsciadia, commonly known as the miniature spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, cylindrical stems covered in a waxy cuticle that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
