Eugeniopsis clausseniana var. glabrata

Eugeniopsis clausseniana var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved eugeniopsis, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are smooth and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
