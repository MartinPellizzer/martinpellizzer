Eriogonum piperi var. ochrocephalum

Eriogonum piperi var. ochrocephalum, commonly known as the yellowhead buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like sorrel and buckwheat.
