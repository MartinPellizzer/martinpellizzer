Eremurus iae

Eremurus iae, commonly known as the Japanese sasparilla, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a rosette at the base.

Eremurus iae thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and borders.
