Ecbolium linneanum var. oblongum

Ecbolium linneanum var. oblongum, commonly known as the narrowleaf ecbolium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth margin and a slightly serrated edge, often tinged with a pale underside. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the spring and are followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
