Echinodorus × opacus

Echinodorus × opacus, commonly known as the black velvet plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are often variegated with lighter green or silver markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions, making it a versatile choice for aquariums. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge above the water surface.

Echinodorus × opacus is valued for its aesthetic appeal and its role in providing oxygen and shelter for aquatic life.
