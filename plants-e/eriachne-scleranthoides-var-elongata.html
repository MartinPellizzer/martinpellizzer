Eriachne scleranthoides var. elongata

Eriachne scleranthoides var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated spinifex grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

This variety is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a common component of spinifex grasslands.
