Euploca paniculata

Euploca paniculata, commonly known as the silver leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant is herbaceous, with a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
