Eutriana oligostachya

Eutriana oligostachya, commonly known as the slender leaved water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in aquatic gardening for its ornamental value and ability to improve water quality.
