Eulophia oedoplectron

Eulophia oedoplectron, commonly known as the black orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, dark violet to black flowers with yellowish-green markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia oedoplectron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
