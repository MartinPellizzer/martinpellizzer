Euphorbia serpens var. microphylla

Euphorbia serpens var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling form and small, rounded leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative ground cover.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
