Eustegia

Eustegia, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray leaves, which are covered in fine hairs. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and southern Europe, Eustegia thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is often found growing along the edges of the sea, where it is adapted to salty and windy conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add a touch of coastal beauty to their landscapes.
