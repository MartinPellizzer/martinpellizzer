Erucastrum zanonii

Erucastrum zanonii, commonly known as the Zanonii cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its small, alternate leaves and clusters of tiny, yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological diversity.

The plant's seeds are small and capsule-like, dispersing through wind and animal activity.
