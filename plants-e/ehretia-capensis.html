Ehretia capensis

Ehretia capensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
