Equisetum × trachyodon

Equisetum × trachyodon, commonly known as the rough horsetail, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of both Equisetum ramosissimum and Equisetum trachydon.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a tall, unbranched stem with whorls of small, needle-like leaves. Its reproductive structures include spore-producing cones at the top of the stem.

Equisetum × trachyodon is notable for its ancient lineage, belonging to one of the oldest groups of vascular plants.
