Eucomis punctata var. concolor

Eucomis punctata var. concolor, commonly known as the white pineapple lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers resembling miniature pineapples. The plant produces sword-like, grassy leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

This variety is prized for its unique floral display and is often used in landscape design for its ornamental value.
