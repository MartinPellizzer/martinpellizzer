Enneastemon barteri

Enneastemon barteri, commonly known as the Barter's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for those seeking to add color and interest to their botanical collections.
