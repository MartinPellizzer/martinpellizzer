Epilobium alpinum

Epilobium alpinum, commonly known as alpine willow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. It produces clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers during the summer months.

Epilobium alpinum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
