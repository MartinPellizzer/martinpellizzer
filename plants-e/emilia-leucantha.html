Emilia leucantha

Emilia leucantha, commonly known as white emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Emilia leucantha is often cultivated for its delicate flowers and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
