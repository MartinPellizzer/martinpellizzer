Euodia merrillii

Euodia merrillii, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement and emit a distinctive fragrance. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by small, round fruits.

Euodia merrillii is valued for its wood, which is used in traditional Chinese medicine and for crafting.
