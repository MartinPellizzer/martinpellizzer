Epipactis ovalis

Epipactis ovalis, commonly known as the oval helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a tall, erect stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip and are borne in loose clusters.

Epipactis ovalis is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and is often found in association with other orchid species.
