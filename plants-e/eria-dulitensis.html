Eria dulitensis

Eria dulitensis, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Eria dulitensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
