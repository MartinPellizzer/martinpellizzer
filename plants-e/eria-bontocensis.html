Eria bontocensis

Eria bontocensis, commonly known as the Bontoc orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the mountainous regions of the Bontoc area. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
