Erica keeromsbergensis

Erica keeromsbergensis, commonly known as the Keeromsberg heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica keeromsbergensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
