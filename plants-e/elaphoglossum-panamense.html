Elaphoglossum panamense

Elaphoglossum panamense, commonly known as the Panamanian holly fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. Native to the humid forests of Central and South America, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant's rhizomes are often covered in a dense, velvety layer of brownish hairs.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in tropical botanical studies.
