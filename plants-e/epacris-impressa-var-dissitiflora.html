Epacris impressa var. dissitiflora

Epacris impressa var. dissitiflora, commonly known as the mountain banksia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers.
