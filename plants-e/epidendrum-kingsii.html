Epidendrum kingsii

Epidendrum kingsii, commonly known as the Kings' Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, long-lasting inflorescences that produce clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Epidendrum kingsii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
