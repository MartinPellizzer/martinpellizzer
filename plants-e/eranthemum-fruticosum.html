Eranthemum fruticosum

Eranthemum fruticosum, commonly known as the shrub eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates. Its foliage is usually lance-shaped and can be either evergreen or deciduous depending on the species.

Eranthemum fruticosum is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
