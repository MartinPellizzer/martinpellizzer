Etlingera rubrolutea

Etlingera rubrolutea, commonly known as the red-and-yellow torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red and yellow inflorescences, which resemble torches and are highly ornamental. Its leaves are broad, glossy, and often have a reddish hue on the underside.

Etlingera rubrolutea is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
