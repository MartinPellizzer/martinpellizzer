Eremurus robustus var. elwesianus

Eremurus robustus var. elwesianus, commonly known as the Elwes' sasparilla, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like flower stalks that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or arid landscapes.
