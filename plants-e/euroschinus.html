Euroschinus

Euroschinus, commonly known as the European ash or European white ash, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Oleaceae.

It consists of a single species, Euroschinus europaeus, which is native to southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the spring.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
