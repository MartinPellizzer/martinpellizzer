Eriospermum villosum

Eriospermum villosum, commonly known as the woolly eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators.
