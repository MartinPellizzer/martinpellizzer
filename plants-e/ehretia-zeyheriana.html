Ehretia zeyheriana

Ehretia zeyheriana, commonly known as the Zeyher's ehretia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by round, fleshy fruits that are often eaten by birds and mammals.
