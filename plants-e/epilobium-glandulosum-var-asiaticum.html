Epilobium glandulosum var. asiaticum

Epilobium glandulosum var. asiaticum, commonly known as the Asiatic willow herb, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features erect stems that are often glandular and covered with fine hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a hairy surface and a slightly serrated edge. It produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks.
