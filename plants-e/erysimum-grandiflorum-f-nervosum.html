Erysimum grandiflorum f. nervosum

Erysimum grandiflorum f. nervosum, commonly known as the nervy wallflower, is a botanical variant of the common wallflower.

It is characterized by its finely veined leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found growing in rocky or gravelly habitats, preferring full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant, colorful plants.
