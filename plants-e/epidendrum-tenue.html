Epidendrum tenue

Epidendrum tenue, commonly known as the slender epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, adapted to retain moisture.

Epidendrum tenue is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
