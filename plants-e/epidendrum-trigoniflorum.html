Epidendrum trigoniflorum

Epidendrum trigoniflorum, commonly known as the Triangular Flower Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum trigoniflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
