Euploca confertifolia var. confertifolia

Euploca confertifolia var. confertifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved euploca, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often densely clustered at the base of the stems. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically arranged in spikes and come in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
