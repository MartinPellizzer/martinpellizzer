Eria liparoides

Eria liparoides, commonly known as the coral orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, brightly colored flowers that range from red to pink, often resembling coral in appearance. Eria liparoides thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
