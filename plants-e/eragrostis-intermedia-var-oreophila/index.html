Eragrostis Intermedia Var. Oreophila

Bluegrass, scientifically known as Eragrostis intermedia var. oreophila, is a striking ornamental grass native to the mountainous regions of North America. Its delicate blue-green foliage and feathery plumes create a soft, ethereal appearance that makes it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of growing conditions. With its unique color and texture, it adds visual interest to both wild and cultivated spaces. Its beauty and resilience have earned it a special place in the world of horticulture.
