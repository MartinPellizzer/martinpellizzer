Elsholtzia argyi

Elsholtzia argyi, commonly known as Chinese germander, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color and bloom in summer.

Elsholtzia argyi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
