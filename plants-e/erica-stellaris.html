Erica stellaris

Erica stellaris, commonly known as the star heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late winter to early spring. Erica stellaris thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
