Eryngium spinosepalum var. medium

Eryngium spinosepalum var. medium, commonly known as the medium thistle, is a perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features striking blue to purple spiny flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a basal rosette of silver-green, spiny leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its structure is typical of the Asteraceae family, with composite flower heads made up of numerous small florets.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy addition to native gardens.
