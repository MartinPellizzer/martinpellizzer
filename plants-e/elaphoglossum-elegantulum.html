Elaphoglossum elegantulum

Elaphoglossum elegantulum, commonly known as the elegant holly fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to the forests of Southeast Asia.

Elaphoglossum elegantulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
