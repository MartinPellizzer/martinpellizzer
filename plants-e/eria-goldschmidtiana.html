Eria goldschmidtiana

Eria goldschmidtiana, commonly known as the golden eria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
