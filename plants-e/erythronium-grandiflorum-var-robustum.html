Erythronium grandiflorum var. robustum

Erythronium grandiflorum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust yellow trillium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features a basal rosette of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces a single, showy flower with three broad, white petals and a yellow throat, often blooming in early spring. It prefers moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shaded areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
