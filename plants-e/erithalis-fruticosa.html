Erithalis fruticosa

Erithalis fruticosa, commonly known as the yellow wood or redwood, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Erithalis fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
