Eugenia vrieseana

Eugenia vrieseana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit, which is small and fleshy, is edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
