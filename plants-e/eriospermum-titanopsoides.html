Eriospermum titanopsoides

Eriospermum titanopsoides, commonly known as the giant eriosperm, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that can grow up to several meters in height, adorned with large, paddle-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by distinctive, spiky seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
