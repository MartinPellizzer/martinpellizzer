Elymus sinoflexuosus

Elymus sinoflexuosus, commonly known as the Chinese flexible grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fine, hairless leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

Elymus sinoflexuosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
