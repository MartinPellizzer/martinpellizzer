Eugenia parkeri

Eugenia parkeri, commonly known as the pink hibiscus mealybug plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

However, it is also known to host the pink hibiscus mealybug, which can be a pest in some regions.
