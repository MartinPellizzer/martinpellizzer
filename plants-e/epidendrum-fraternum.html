Epidendrum fraternum

Epidendrum fraternum, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in the winter months, often around Christmas, hence its common name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum fraternum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
