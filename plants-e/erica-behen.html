Erica behen

Erica behen, commonly known as the common heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has needle-like leaves that are dark green and covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica behen is valued for its ornamental appeal and is widely cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
