Echinopsis × imperialis

Echinopsis × imperialis, commonly known as the Imperial night bloomer, is a hybrid cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and large, showy white flowers that bloom at night. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring spines for protection and water storage in its thick, fleshy stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and nocturnal blooming habit, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
