Encyclia jenischiana

Encyclia jenischiana, commonly known as the Jenisch's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing light green to white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia jenischiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
