Eupatorium ortegae

Eupatorium ortegae, commonly known as the Ortega's thoroughwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Eupatorium ortegae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
