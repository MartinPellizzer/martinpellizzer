Euchlaena perennis

Euchlaena perennis, commonly known as the everlasting grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses and related plants.
