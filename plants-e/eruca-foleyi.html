Eruca foleyi

Eruca foleyi, commonly known as the Foleys rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant features erect, branched stems and pinnately lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

Eruca foleyi is valued for its peppery leaves, which can be used in culinary applications.
