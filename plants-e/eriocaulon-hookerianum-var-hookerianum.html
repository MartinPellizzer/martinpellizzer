Eriocaulon hookerianum var. hookerianum

Eriocaulon hookerianum var. hookerianum, commonly known as Hooker's spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands.
