Echites arborescens

Echites arborescens, commonly known as the tree echites, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas within its native habitat.
