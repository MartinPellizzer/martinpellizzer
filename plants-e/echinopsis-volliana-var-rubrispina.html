Echinopsis volliana var. rubrispina

Echinopsis volliana var. rubrispina, commonly known as the red-spined moon cactus, is a striking cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red spines and a pale, often pink or white, spherical body. The plant typically grows in arid environments, thriving in well-draining soil and full sun exposure. Its flowers, which appear in summer, are usually yellow or orange and are borne at the top of the plant.

This species is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
