Eugenia operculata var. paniala

Eugenia operculata var. paniala, commonly known as the Paniala eugenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a slightly serrated margin.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
