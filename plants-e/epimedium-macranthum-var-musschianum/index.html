Epimedium Macranthum Var. Musschianum

Fairy bells, scientifically known as Epimedium macranthum var. musschianum, are a striking addition to any woodland garden. These hardy plants are prized for their delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to the forests of China, they thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil. Their foliage is evergreen, providing visual interest throughout the year. With their unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, fairy bells are a favorite among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
