Euphrasia viscosa

Euphrasia viscosa, commonly known as sticky eyebright, is a small herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features upright stems covered with fine, sticky hairs, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in whorls along the stem.

Euphrasia viscosa is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
