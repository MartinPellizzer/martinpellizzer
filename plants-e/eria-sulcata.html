Eria sulcata

Eria sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a raceme. Eria sulcata thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
