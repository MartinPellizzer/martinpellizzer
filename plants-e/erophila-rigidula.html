Erophila rigidula

Erophila rigidula, commonly known as rigid filaree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the stems are erect and somewhat fibrous.

Erophila rigidula is often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides, where it thrives in full sun.
