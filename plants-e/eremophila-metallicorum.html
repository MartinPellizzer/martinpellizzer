Eremophila metallicorum

Eremophila metallicorum, commonly known as the silver-leaf eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and efficient water use.

Eremophila metallicorum is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
