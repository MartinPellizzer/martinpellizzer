Ecbolium linneanum var. cuspidatum

Ecbolium linneanum var. cuspidatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ecbolium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, lanceolate leaves with a pointed tip, giving it its botanical name "cuspidatum.". The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
