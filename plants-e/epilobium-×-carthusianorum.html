Epilobium × carthusianorum

Epilobium × carthusianorum, commonly known as the Carthusian pink, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Epilobium × carthusianorum is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
