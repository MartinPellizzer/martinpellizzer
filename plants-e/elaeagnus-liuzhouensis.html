Elaeagnus liuzhouensis

Elaeagnus liuzhouensis, commonly known as the Liuzhou silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves that are densely covered with short, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, edible berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
