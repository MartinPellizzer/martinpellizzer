Eragrostis simplex

Eragrostis simplex, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its native range.
