Epidendrum × rubroticum

Epidendrum × rubroticum, commonly known as the red-flowered epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum × rubroticum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
