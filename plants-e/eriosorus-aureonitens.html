Eriosorus aureonitens

Eriosorus aureonitens, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of polypodiaceae native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange fronds, which are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are pinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped base.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
