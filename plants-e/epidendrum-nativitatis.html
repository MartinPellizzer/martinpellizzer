Epidendrum nativitatis

Epidendrum nativitatis, commonly known as the Christmas Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in the winter months, typically around December to January. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous bright yellow to orange flowers, often with a contrasting red lip. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Epidendrum nativitatis is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
