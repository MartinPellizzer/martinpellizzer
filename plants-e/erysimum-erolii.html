Erysimum erolii

Erysimum erolii, commonly known as the Erol's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are typically lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Erysimum erolii is often found growing in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
