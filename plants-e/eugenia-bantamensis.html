Eugenia bantamensis

Eugenia bantamensis, commonly known as the Bantam eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by small, round berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
