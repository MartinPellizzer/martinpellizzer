Epilobium × scardicola

It is characterized by its erect or ascending growth habit and is typically found in moist, disturbed habitats such as streambanks and wet meadows. Epilobium × scardicola is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and thrive in wet conditions.
