Erysimum longifolium

Erysimum longifolium, commonly known as the longleaf erisimum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features linear, gray-green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Erysimum longifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
