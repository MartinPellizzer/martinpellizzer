Eltroplectris pauciflora

Eltroplectris pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a single, erect stem that bears a few flowers. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered at the top of the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eltroplectris pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
