Eremaea pilosa

Eremaea pilosa, commonly known as the woolly eremaea, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant has small, green or reddish stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically small and yellow or greenish, appearing in clusters during the warmer months.

Eremaea pilosa is well adapted to drought and can thrive in poor soil conditions.
