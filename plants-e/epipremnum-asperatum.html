Epipremnum asperatum

Epipremnum asperatum, commonly known as the Satin Pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are slightly textured, giving it a unique appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its stems can grow long and vine-like, often requiring support such as a trellis or moss pole.

Epipremnum asperatum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to indoor environmental health.
