Excoecaria diversifolia

Excoecaria diversifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
