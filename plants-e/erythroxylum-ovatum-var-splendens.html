Erythroxylum ovatum var. splendens

Erythroxylum ovatum var. splendens, commonly known as the splendour coca, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Erythroxylaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, with a glossy surface and a strong, bitter scent.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in cloud forests at elevations between 1,500 and 2,500 meters.
