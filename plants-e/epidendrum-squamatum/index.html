Epidendrum Squamatum

Epidendrum squamatum is a fascinating orchid species native to the tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring velvety leaves and striking, long-lasting blooms. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its ability to grow on trees or in rocky areas makes it a popular choice for epiphytic cultivation. With its elegant form and vibrant flowers, Epidendrum squamatum is a beloved addition to many botanical collections.
