Eriocaulon pseudomutatum

Eriocaulon pseudomutatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like appearance and flowering spikes. Native to wetland habitats, it thrives in moist, often acidic soils. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
