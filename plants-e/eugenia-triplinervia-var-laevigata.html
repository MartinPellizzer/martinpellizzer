Eugenia triplinervia var. laevigata

Eugenia triplinervia var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
