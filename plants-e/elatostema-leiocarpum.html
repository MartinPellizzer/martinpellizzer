Elatostema leiocarpum

Elatostema leiocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-fruit elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a short, rounded stem. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are often eaten by birds and small mammals.

Elatostema leiocarpum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
