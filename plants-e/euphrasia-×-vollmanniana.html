Euphrasia × vollmanniana

Euphrasia × vollmanniana, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a hybrid species within the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine, hairy structures.
