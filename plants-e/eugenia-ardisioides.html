Eugenia ardisioides

Eugenia ardisioides, commonly known as the purple leaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often turn reddish-purple in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, sweet-tart berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Eugenia ardisioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
