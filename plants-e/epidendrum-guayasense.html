Epidendrum guayasense

Epidendrum guayasense, commonly known as the Guayas Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in various colors, including yellow and orange.

Its botanical significance lies in its role within its ecosystem and its potential for horticultural interest.
