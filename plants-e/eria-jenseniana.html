Eria jenseniana

Eria jenseniana, commonly known as the Jensen's eria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.
