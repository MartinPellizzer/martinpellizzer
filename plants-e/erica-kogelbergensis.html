Erica kogelbergensis

Erica kogelbergensis, commonly known as Kogelberg heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Erica kogelbergensis is well adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region, with drought-resistant properties.
