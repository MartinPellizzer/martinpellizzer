Eriosorus hispidulus var. dominicensis

Eriosorus hispidulus var. dominicensis, commonly known as the Dominican velvet fern, is a variant of the velvet fern species native to the Caribbean.

It is a small, mat-forming fern that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety fronds that are covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

This variety is notable for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in tropical gardens.
