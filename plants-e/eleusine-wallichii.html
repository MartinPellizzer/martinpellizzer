Eleusine wallichii

Eleusine wallichii, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to brownish flowers.

Eleusine wallichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
