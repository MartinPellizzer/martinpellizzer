Eulophia ericophila

Eulophia ericophila, commonly known as the Ericaceous Eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with narrow, grass-like leaves, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal spikes and exhibit a range of colors, from white to pink and purple.

Eulophia ericophila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
