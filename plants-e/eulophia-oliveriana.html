Eulophia oliveriana

Eulophia oliveriana, commonly known as the Oliver's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Eulophia oliveriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
