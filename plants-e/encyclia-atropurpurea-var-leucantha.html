Encyclia atropurpurea var. leucantha

Encyclia atropurpurea var. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. Its flowers are white or pale pink, with a distinctive purple lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
