Eranthemum filiforme

Eranthemum filiforme, commonly known as the threadleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens for its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms.
