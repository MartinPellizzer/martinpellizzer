Elatine gussonei

Elatine gussonei, commonly known as the slender waterwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and tiny, opposite leaves that are often submerged in water. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically borne in clusters near the water's surface. Elatine gussonei thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its rapid growth and ability to colonize moist habitats make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
