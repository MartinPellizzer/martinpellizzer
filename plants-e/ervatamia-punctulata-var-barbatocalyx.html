Ervatamia punctulata var. barbatocalyx

Ervatamia punctulata var. barbatocalyx, commonly known as the woolly leaf erythroxylum, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, hairy texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
