Ectrosiopsis aruensis

Ectrosiopsis aruensis, commonly known as the Aru starling tree, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are small and fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and birds.

The wood of Ectrosiopsis aruensis is valued for its strength and is used in local crafts and construction.
