Eria peraffinis

Eria peraffinis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, gracefully curved stems. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a fragrant scent. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Eria peraffinis thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
