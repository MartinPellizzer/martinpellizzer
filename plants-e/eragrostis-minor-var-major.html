Eragrostis minor var. major

Eragrostis minor var. major, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping. The plant typically grows in dry, open areas and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates. Its seed heads are slender and often form dense clusters, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Blue grama is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
