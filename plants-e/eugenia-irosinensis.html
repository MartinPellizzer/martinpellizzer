Eugenia irosinensis

Eugenia irosinensis, commonly known as the Irosin eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, dark purple to black berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

It thrives in humid, coastal environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
