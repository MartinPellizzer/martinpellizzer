Eria fimbriloba

Eria fimbriloba, commonly known as the fringed-leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive fringed margin. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Eria fimbriloba thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
