Epilobium microphyllum

Epilobium microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in racemes, and bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
