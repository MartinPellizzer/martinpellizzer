Euphorbia spinosa var. melitensis

Euphorbia spinosa var. melitensis, commonly known as the Malta spurge, is a succulent shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thick, ribbed stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has adapted to arid environments with its water-storing tissues and reduced leaves. Its sap contains toxic compounds, which can cause skin irritation.

It is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas, thriving in full sun.
