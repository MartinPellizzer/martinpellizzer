Eugenia micrantha

Eugenia micrantha, commonly known as the small-fruited eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped, glossy, and arranged alternately along the stems.

The small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.
