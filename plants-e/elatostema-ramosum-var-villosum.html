Elatostema ramosum var. villosum

Elatostema ramosum var. villosum, commonly known as the hairy elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance.
