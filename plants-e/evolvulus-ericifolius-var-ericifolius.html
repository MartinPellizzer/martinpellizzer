Evolvulus ericifolius var. ericifolius

Evolvulus ericifolius var. ericifolius, commonly known as the hairy morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for various landscape applications.
