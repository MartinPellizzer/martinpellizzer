Eremophila decussata

Eremophila decussata, commonly known as the zigzag eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink and purple. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy choice for drought-tolerant gardens.

Eremophila decussata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to native wildlife.
