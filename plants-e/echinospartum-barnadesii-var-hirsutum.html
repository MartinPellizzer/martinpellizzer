Echinospartum barnadesii var. hirsutum

Echinospartum barnadesii var. hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy echinospartum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, cone-shaped inflorescence. The plant features hairy foliage and produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is particularly adapted to dry, well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
