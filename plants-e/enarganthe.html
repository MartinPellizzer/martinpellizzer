Enarganthe

Enarganthe, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating species. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and sturdy, supporting its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Enarganthe is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a cut flower.
