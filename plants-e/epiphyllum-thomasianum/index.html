Epiphyllum Thomasianum

Epiphyllum thomasianum is a unique cactus species native to parts of Central and South America. It is known for its striking, nocturnal blooms that appear only during the night. Unlike many cacti, it often grows on trees or rocks rather than in the ground. Its flowers are large, fragrant, and come in shades of white and pink. This plant is both a botanical curiosity and a symbol of resilience in challenging environments.
