Eriochloa fouchei

Eriochloa fouchei, commonly known as the Fouche's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brown florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Eriochloa fouchei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
