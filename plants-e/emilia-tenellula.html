Emilia tenellula

Emilia tenellula, commonly known as the slender emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that are covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, blooming from late spring to early summer. Emilia tenellula thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
