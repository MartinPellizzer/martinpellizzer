Erasma

Erasma, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by triangular, winged seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both wet and dry conditions.

Its rapid growth makes it a common weed in agricultural fields.
