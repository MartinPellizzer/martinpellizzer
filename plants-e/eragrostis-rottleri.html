Eragrostis rottleri

Eragrostis rottleri, commonly known as Rottler's lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna ecosystems.

Eragrostis rottleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
