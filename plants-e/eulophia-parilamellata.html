Eulophia parilamellata

Eulophia parilamellata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and elongated, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia parilamellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
