Epidendrum cogniauxianum

Epidendrum cogniauxianum, commonly known as the Cogniaux's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, typically featuring a short, thick stem and a cluster of broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum cogniauxianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
