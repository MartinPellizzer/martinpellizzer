Ebnerella eschauzieri

Ebnerella eschauzieri, commonly known as the Schauzier's thistle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Ebnerella eschauzieri is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.
