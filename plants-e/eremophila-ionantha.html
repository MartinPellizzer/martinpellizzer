Eremophila ionantha

Eremophila ionantha, commonly known as the slender eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and striking pink to purple flowers. The plant is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in harsh environments.

Eremophila ionantha is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
