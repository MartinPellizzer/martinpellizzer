Ericameria palmeri var. palmeri

Ericameria palmeri var. palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's ericameria, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, such as desert scrub and chaparral. It has a compact growth habit and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to harsh, dry climates.

The species is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
