Erigeron breviscapus var. alboradiatus

Erigeron breviscapus var. alboradiatus, commonly known as the white-rayed fleabane, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white ray florets and yellow disc florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. It has a trailing or ascending growth habit and produces small, hairy leaves.

Its flowers bloom in the summer and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
