Elatostema gyrocephalum var. pubicaule

Elatostema gyrocephalum var. pubicaule, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves. The leaves are often covered in fine, pubescent hairs, giving the plant its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique subject for study in tropical botany.
