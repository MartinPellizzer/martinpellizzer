Elaphoglossum beitelii

Elaphoglossum beitelii, commonly known as the Beitel's holly fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant features a creeping rhizome with sporangia clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded garden landscapes.
