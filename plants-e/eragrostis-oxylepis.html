Eragrostis oxylepis

Eragrostis oxylepis, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
