Enydra woollsii

Enydra woollsii, commonly known as Woolly Enydra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually covered in a fine, woolly coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Enydra woollsii is often found in open grasslands and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
