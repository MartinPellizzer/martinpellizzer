Euphorbia lambiorum

Euphorbia lambiorum, commonly known as the lamb's ear euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique floral structures. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Euphorbia lambiorum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
