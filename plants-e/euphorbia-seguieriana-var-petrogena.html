Euphorbia seguieriana var. petrogena

Euphorbia seguieriana var. petrogena, commonly known as the rock euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, multi-stemmed structure with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flower clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates.
