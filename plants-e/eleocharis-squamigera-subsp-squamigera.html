Eleocharis squamigera subsp. squamigera

Eleocharis squamigera subsp.

squamigera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a smooth or slightly rough texture.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
