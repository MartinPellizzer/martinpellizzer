Epidendrum × nocteburneum

Epidendrum × nocteburneum, commonly known as the night-burning epidendron, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is known for its striking, night-blooming flowers that emit a subtle fragrance, attracting nocturnal pollinators. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. Its inflorescence consists of multiple pendulous spikes, each bearing numerous showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
