Elleanthus ligularis

Elleanthus ligularis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

Elleanthus ligularis is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
