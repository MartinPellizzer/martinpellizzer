Eurebutia minuscula

Eurebutia minuscula, commonly known as the dwarf moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically small and may be present during the growing season but are often absent during dormancy.

Eurebutia minuscula is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
