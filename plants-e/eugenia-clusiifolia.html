Eugenia clusiifolia

Eugenia clusiifolia, commonly known as the red torch tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically reddish or black when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Eugenia clusiifolia is also valued for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil types and its resilience to urban environments.
