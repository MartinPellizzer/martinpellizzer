Euacer macrocarpon

Euacer macrocarpon, commonly known as the giant seed-bearing plant, is a unique species characterized by its large, showy fruits.

It belongs to the Euaceraceae family and is native to specific regions with tropical climates. The plant exhibits a distinct morphology with thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. Its fruits, which can grow up to several inches in diameter, are adapted for seed dispersal by animals.
