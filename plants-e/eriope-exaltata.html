Eriope exaltata

Eriope exaltata, commonly known as the tall eriope, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Eriope exaltata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
