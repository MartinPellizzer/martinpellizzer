Echinodorus radicans

Echinodorus radicans, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It thrives in freshwater environments and is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 20 inches in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to spread rapidly and form dense underwater foliage. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality by absorbing nutrients.

Echinodorus radicans also provides shelter and breeding grounds for various aquatic species.
