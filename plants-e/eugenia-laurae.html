Eugenia laurae

Eugenia laurae, commonly known as the Laura's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Eugenia laurae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
