Elaphoglossum repens

Elaphoglossum repens, commonly known as the running holly fern, is a small, delicate fern native to the moist forests of Southeast Asia.

Elaphoglossum repens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in maintaining forest floor ecosystems.
