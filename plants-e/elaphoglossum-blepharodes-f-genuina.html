Elaphoglossum blepharodes f. genuina

Elaphoglossum blepharodes f. genuina, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly fern, is a fern species belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, linear leaves that give it a delicate, lacy appearance. The fronds are typically evergreen, with a dark green color and a slightly glossy texture. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
