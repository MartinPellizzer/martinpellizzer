Eremurus chinensis

Eremurus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese desert candle, is a striking perennial plant native to the arid regions of China.

It belongs to the genus Eremurus, which is part of the family Asteraceae, also known as the daisy family. The plant is characterized by its tall, hollow stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height, supporting a dense cluster of yellowish-green flower heads. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a unique, candle-like appearance.

Eremurus chinensis is well adapted to harsh, dry environments, making it a hardy and resilient plant.
