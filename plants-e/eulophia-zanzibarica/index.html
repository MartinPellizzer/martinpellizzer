Eulophia Zanzibarica

Eulophia zanzibarica is a unique orchid native to the coastal regions of East Africa. This plant is known for its striking appearance and adaptability to tropical climates. It features tall, slender stems and elegant, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that add to its visual appeal. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare species, making it a prized find among plant enthusiasts.
