Euonymus japonicus var. latifolius

Euonymus japonicus var. latifolius, commonly known as Japanese spindlewood, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
