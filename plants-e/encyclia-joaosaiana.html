Encyclia joaosaiana

Encyclia joaosaiana, commonly known as the Joaosaiana orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizome and multiple stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or brown markings. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Encyclia joaosaiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
