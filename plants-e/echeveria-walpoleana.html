Echeveria walpoleana

Echeveria walpoleana, commonly known as the Potosi rose, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
