Eria leptocarpa

Eria leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and the plant produces a single inflorescence with several floral buds.

It is often found in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
