Euphorbia ovalleana

Euphorbia ovalleana, commonly known as the ovalleaf spurges, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are surrounded by a crown of bracts. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Euphorbia ovalleana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
