Epigeneium chapaense

Epigeneium chapaense, commonly known as the Chapa's epigeneium, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its aerial roots and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
