Eruca aurea

Eruca aurea, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Eruca aurea is sometimes cultivated as a leafy green, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
