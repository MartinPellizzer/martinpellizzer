Epistephium matogrossense

Epistephium matogrossense, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and specialized roots adapted for nutrient absorption.

Epistephium matogrossense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
