Euthemis engleri

Euthemis engleri, commonly known as the Engler's euphorbia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its succulent, spiny stems and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
