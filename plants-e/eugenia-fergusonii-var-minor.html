Eugenia fergusonii var. minor

Eugenia fergusonii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is noted for its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, pleasant scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
