Erica × ciliaritetralix

Erica × ciliaritetralix, commonly known as the coastal heath, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the coastal regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form with small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers, which appear in spring, are typically pink or purple and are clustered in dense, compact inflorescences.

Erica × ciliaritetralix is well adapted to harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
