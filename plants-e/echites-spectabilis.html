Echites spectabilis

Echites spectabilis, commonly known as the showy echites, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Echites spectabilis is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
