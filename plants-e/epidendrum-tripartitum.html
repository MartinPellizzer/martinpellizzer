Epidendrum tripartitum

Epidendrum tripartitum, commonly known as the Three-parted Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Epidendrum tripartitum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
