Eria unguiculata

Eria unguiculata, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like structures on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eria unguiculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
