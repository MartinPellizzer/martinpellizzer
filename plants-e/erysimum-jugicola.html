Erysimum jugicola

Erysimum jugicola, commonly known as the desert wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid environments.
