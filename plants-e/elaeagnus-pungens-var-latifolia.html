Elaeagnus pungens var. latifolia

Elaeagnus pungens var. latifolia, commonly known as silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are followed by small, reddish berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
