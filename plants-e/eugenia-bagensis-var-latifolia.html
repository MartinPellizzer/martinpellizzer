Eugenia bagensis var. latifolia

Eugenia bagensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters. The fruit is a small, smooth, and edible berry that turns red when mature.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and culinary uses.
