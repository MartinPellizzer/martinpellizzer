Erica embothriifolia var. embothriifolia

Erica embothriifolia var. embothriifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of pale pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months.

Erica embothriifolia var. embothriifolia is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
