Eutoca

Eutoca, commonly known as the golden bell, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
