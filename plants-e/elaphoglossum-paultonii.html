Elaphoglossum paultonii

Elaphoglossum paultonii, commonly known as the Paulton's harts-tongue fern, is a small, delicate fern native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The fronds are usually dark green, with a leathery texture and finely serrated margins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky crevices.
