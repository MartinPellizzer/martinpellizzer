Erythrocephalum stuhlmannii

Erythrocephalum stuhlmannii, commonly known as the pinkhead bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with grey-green, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters, making it a striking addition to gardens.

It is well adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
