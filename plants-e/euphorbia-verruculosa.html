Euphorbia verruculosa

Euphorbia verruculosa, commonly known as the疣茎大戟, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, branched stem covered in small, wart-like bumps, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers surrounded by leaf-like bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its fleshy stems.

Euphorbia verruculosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
