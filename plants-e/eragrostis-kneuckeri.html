Eragrostis kneuckeri

Eragrostis kneuckeri, commonly known as Kneucker's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, exhibiting a clump-forming growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color, and its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often pale green to reddish in hue. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a hardy plant in dry environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
