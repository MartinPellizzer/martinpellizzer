Epilobium santa-cruzense var. cruzense

Epilobium santa-cruzense var. cruzense, commonly known as Santa Cruz willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along the coastline. The plant features erect, branched stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, pink to purple flowers appear in terminal clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This species is adapted to the harsh coastal climate and plays a role in stabilizing dune ecosystems.
