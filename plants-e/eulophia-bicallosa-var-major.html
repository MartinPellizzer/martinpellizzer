Eulophia bicallosa var. major

Eulophia bicallosa var. major, commonly known as the broad-leaved eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
