Euchlaena

Euchlaena, commonly known as the golden club, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking yellow spadix. Native to the wetlands of North America, it thrives in moist, shady environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered around the spadix.

Euchlaena is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
