Eupatorium hualienense

Eupatorium hualienense, commonly known as Hualien eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium hualienense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
