Euphorbia captiosa

Euphorbia captiosa, commonly known as the Showy Spurge, is a perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves and stems, which are typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular ornamental plant.
