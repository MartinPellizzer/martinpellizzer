Euodia fatraina

Euodia fatraina, commonly known as the fatraintree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by small, red berries that are edible but not widely consumed. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
