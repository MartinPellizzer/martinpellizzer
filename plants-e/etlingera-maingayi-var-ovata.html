Etlingera maingayi var. ovata

Etlingera maingayi var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences with bright red bracts and yellow-orange flowers. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
