Elsholtzia bodinieri

Elsholtzia bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese germander, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in summer.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
