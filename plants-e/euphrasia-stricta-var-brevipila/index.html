Euphrasia Stricta Var. Brevipila

Euphrasia stricta var. brevipila is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Europe. It is known for its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This variant of Euphrasia is distinguished by its short, compact growth habit and unique leaf structure. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare botanical treasure from environmental threats.
