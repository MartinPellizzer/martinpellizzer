It is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and is characterized by its dense, glandular hairs that give it a sticky texture. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine, silky hairs.
