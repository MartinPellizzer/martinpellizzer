Eupatorium pedunculosum

Eupatorium pedunculosum, commonly known as swamp milkweed, is a perennial herb native to wetland areas of North America.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its showy inflorescences attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.

Eupatorium pedunculosum is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.
