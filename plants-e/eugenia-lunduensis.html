Eugenia lunduensis

Eugenia lunduensis, commonly known as the Lundu eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Tanzania and Mozambique. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often have a strong aromatic scent.

Eugenia lunduensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
