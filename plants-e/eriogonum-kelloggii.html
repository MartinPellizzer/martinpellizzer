Eriogonum kelloggii

Eriogonum kelloggii, commonly known as Kellogg's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiky inflorescence and small, white to pinkish flowers.
