Erysimum capsellinum

Erysimum capsellinum, commonly known as wall rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor, often used in salads or as a seasoning.

Erysimum capsellinum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
