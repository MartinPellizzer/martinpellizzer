Escallonia misella

Escallonia misella, commonly known as the silverleaf escallonia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late spring, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Escallonia misella is also valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
