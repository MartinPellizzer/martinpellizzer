Elytrigia tilcarensis

Elytrigia tilcarensis, commonly known as the Tilcarensis bentgrass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
