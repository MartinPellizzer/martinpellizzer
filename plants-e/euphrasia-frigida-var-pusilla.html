Euphrasia frigida var. pusilla

Euphrasia frigida var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf lady's tears, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent dark markings.
