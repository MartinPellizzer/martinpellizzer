Evolvulus balansae

Evolvulus balansae, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers that open in the morning and close in the afternoon. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain several seeds. It is a herbaceous annual plant with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves.

Evolvulus balansae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
