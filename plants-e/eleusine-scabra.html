Eleusine scabra

Eleusine scabra, commonly known as rough panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are clustered along the stems. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and wastelands.

Eleusine scabra is a hardy species that can tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
