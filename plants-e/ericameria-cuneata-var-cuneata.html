Ericameria cuneata var. cuneata

Ericameria cuneata var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrowleaf ericameria, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky soils and open woodlands. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to dry conditions and has a deep root system to access water.

Ericameria cuneata var. cuneata is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in landscaping.
