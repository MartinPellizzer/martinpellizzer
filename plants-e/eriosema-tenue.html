Eriosema tenue

Eriosema tenue, commonly known as the slender eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to yellow flowers that are often clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and also bear a fine pubescence.

Eriosema tenue is typically found in grassland habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.
