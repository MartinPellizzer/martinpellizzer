Epidendrum cobanense

Epidendrum cobanense, commonly known as the Coban orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, arching stems with clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Epidendrum cobanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
