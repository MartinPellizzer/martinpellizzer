Euphoria longan

Euphoria longan, commonly known as the longan tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and edible, juicy fruits. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. The fruit, which is about the size of a cherry, has a brown, leathery exterior and a sweet, floral flavor.

The tree is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
