Erica lutescens

Erica lutescens, commonly known as the yellow heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Erica lutescens typically thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded conditions.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
