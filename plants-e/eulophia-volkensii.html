Eulophia volkensii

Eulophia volkensii, commonly known as the Volken's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers with prominent purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia volkensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
