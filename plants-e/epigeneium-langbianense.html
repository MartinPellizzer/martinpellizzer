Epigeneium langbianense

Epigeneium langbianense, commonly known as the Langbian epigeneium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Epigeneium langbianense is typically found in shaded, humid environments within dense forests.
