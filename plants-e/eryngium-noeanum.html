Eryngium noeanum

Eryngium noeanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking spiny leaves and purple-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a robust root system and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.

Eryngium noeanum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
