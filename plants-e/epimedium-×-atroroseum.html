Epimedium × atroroseum

Epimedium × atroroseum, commonly known as the red-veined barrenwort, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in shady, moist environments, often found in woodland areas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins, which add ornamental value to gardens. Its flowers are inconspicuous, appearing in early spring, and it is known for its hardiness and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.

Epimedium × atroroseum is popular in horticulture for its attractive foliage and resilience.
