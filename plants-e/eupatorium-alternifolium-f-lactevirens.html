Eupatorium alternifolium f. lactevirens

Eupatorium alternifolium f. lactevirens, commonly known as the white eupatorium, is a variant of the common eupatorium species.

It is characterized by its alternate leaf arrangement and distinctive white to pale green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late summer to early autumn.

This form is notable for its unique coloration and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
