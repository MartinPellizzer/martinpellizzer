Echeveria lindsayana

Echeveria lindsayana, commonly known as the Lindsay's Echeveria, is a species of flowering plant in the Crassulaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. Echeveria lindsayana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
