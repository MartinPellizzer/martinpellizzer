Eutacta rulei var. compacta

Eutacta rulei var. compacta, commonly known as the compact eutacta, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful foliage that ranges from deep red to intense green. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and possess a waxy texture, which helps the plant retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat. It thrives in partial shade and requires consistent humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
