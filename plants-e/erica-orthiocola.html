Erica orthiocola

Erica orthiocola, commonly known as the upright heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related species. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white.

Erica orthiocola thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
