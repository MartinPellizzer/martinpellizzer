Eugenia prieurii var. robusta

Eugenia prieurii var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta eugenia, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, dark purple or black berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
