Etlingera sorsogonensis

Etlingera sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Island ginger, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, typical of the ginger family.
