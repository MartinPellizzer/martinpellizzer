Edraianthus × linifolius

Edraianthus × linifolius, commonly known as the hybrid everlasting, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features silver-gray, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-tolerant nature. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
