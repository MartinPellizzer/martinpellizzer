Evolvulus delicatus

Evolvulus delicatus, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate, and often silvery-white flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces tendrils that help it climb and spread. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often found in gardens and along fences.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
