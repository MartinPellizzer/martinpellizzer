Eriachne pulchella

Eriachne pulchella, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread across various regions.

Eriachne pulchella is often found in coastal and inland areas, showing adaptability to different environmental conditions.
