Elaeocarpus beccarii subsp. beccarii

Elaeocarpus beccarii subsp.

beccarii, commonly known as the Bornean elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, white or pinkish flowers.

The plant produces woody fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the genus Elaeocarpus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
