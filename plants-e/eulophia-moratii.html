Eulophia moratii

Eulophia moratii, commonly known as the Morat's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its leaves are usually strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Eulophia moratii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
