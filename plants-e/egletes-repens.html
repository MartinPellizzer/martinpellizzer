Egletes repens

Egletes repens, commonly known as the prostrate egletes, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to arid conditions.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is well-suited to dry, sunny environments.
