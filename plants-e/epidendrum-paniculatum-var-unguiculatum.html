Epidendrum paniculatum var. unguiculatum

Epidendrum paniculatum var. unguiculatum, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in pendulous racemes, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
