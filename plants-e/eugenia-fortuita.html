Eugenia fortuita

Eugenia fortuita, commonly known as the lucky eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and dark, glossy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and private collections.
