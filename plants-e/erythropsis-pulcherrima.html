Erythropsis pulcherrima

Erythropsis pulcherrima, commonly known as the red beauty orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and pink flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for its showy blooms.

Erythropsis pulcherrima thrives in warm climates with high humidity and requires careful watering to maintain its health.
