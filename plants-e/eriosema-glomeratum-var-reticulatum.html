Eriosema glomeratum var. reticulatum

Eriosema glomeratum var. reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined eriosema, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The leaves are typically trifoliate, with a hairy texture that gives the plant its common name. The flowers are small, pale yellow, and have a unique reticulated pattern on the petals.

This variant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
