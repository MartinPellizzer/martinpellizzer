Exostema caribaeum var. pubescens

Exostema caribaeum var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy exostema, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found growing in shaded, moist areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
