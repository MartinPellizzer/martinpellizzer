Eugenia vaga var. pumila

Eugenia vaga var. pumila, commonly known as the dwarf eugenia, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often followed by edible, red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
