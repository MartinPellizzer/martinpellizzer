Echeveria nuyooensis

Echeveria nuyooensis, commonly known as the Nuyoo's Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purple hue. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
