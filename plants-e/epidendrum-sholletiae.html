Epidendrum sholletiae

Epidendrum sholletiae, commonly known as the Shollet's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, elliptic, and often have a waxy texture.

Epidendrum sholletiae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
