Exeria

Exeria, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, used in traditional herbal remedies.

Exeria thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
