Exacum subverticillatum

Exacum subverticillatum, commonly known as the spiral flower or spiral vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant is a member of the Primulaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
