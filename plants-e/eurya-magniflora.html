Eurya magniflora

Eurya magniflora, commonly known as the large-flowered eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and have a leathery texture.

Eurya magniflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
