Epidendrum lehmannii

Epidendrum lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum lehmannii is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and outdoor gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
