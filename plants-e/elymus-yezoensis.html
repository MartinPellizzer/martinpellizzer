Elymus yezoensis

Elymus yezoensis, commonly known as Yezo grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, feather-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Elymus yezoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in stabilizing soil and supporting wildlife.
