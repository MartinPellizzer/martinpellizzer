Erica globulifera

Erica globulifera, commonly known as the globular heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes various shrubs and evergreens. The plant is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. It produces bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in spring.

Erica globulifera is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
