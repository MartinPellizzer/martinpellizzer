Eulophia lejolyana

Eulophia lejolyana, commonly known as the Lejoly's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a greenish tinge. Eulophia lejolyana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
