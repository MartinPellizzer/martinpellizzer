Echeveria spectabilis

Echeveria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
