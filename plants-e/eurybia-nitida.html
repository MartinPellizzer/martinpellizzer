Eurybia nitida

Eurybia nitida, commonly known as silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. It has a taproot system and is drought-tolerant, making it well adapted to its environment.

Eurybia nitida is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
