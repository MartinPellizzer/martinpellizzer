Epidendrum cochleatum var. pallidum

Epidendrum cochleatum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale spiral orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces striking, spiral-shaped pseudobulbs. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
