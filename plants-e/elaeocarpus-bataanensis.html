Elaeocarpus bataanensis

Elaeocarpus bataanensis, commonly known as the Bataan elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain a single seed, typical of the genus Elaeocarpus. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland and montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
