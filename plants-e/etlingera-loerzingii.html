Etlingera loerzingii

Etlingera loerzingii, commonly known as the white ginger orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of northern Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white flowers. The plant's foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
