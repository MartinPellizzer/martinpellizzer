Elaterium gemellum

Elaterium gemellum, commonly known as the twinleaf elaterium, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elateriaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that often appear in pairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
