Eruca vesicaria var. albiflora

Eruca vesicaria var. albiflora, commonly known as white-flowered garden rocket, is a variant of the common rocket plant.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial herb native to southern Europe. The plant features lobed, hairy leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its seeds are typically harvested for culinary use, similar to other members of the Brassicaceae family.

The white-flowered variant is prized for its mild flavor and ornamental value in gardens.
