Erysimum szechuanense

Erysimum szechuanense, commonly known as Sichuan wallflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions.
