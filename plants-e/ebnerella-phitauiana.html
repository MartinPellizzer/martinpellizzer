Ebnerella phitauiana

Ebnerella phitauiana, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees and rocks without being parasitic. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Ebnerella phitauiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific care requirements.
