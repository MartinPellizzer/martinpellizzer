Eria eriaeoides

Eria eriaeoides, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Eria eriaeoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
