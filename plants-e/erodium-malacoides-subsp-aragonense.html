Erodium malacoides subsp. aragonense

Erodium malacoides subsp.

aragonense, commonly known as the Aragon false pepper, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

The plant features small, pink to purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its unique seed structure.
