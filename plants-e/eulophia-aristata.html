Eulophia aristata

Eulophia aristata, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, elongated inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive greenish hue, and it blooms during the late spring to early summer.

Eulophia aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
