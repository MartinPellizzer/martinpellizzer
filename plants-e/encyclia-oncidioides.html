Encyclia oncidioides

Encyclia oncidioides, commonly known as the Oncidioid Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia oncidioides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
