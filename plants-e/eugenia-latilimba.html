Eugenia latilimba

Eugenia latilimba, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
