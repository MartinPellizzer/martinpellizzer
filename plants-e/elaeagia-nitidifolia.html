Elaeagia nitidifolia

Elaeagia nitidifolia, commonly known as the shining-leaf elaeagia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeaginaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, orange-red berries. Elaeagia nitidifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
