Epidendrum chanchamayodifforme

Epidendrum chanchamayodifforme, commonly known as the Chanchamayo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Peru.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
