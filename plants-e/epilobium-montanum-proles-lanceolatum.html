Epilobium montanum proles lanceolatum

Epilobium montanum proles lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate willow herb, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a hairy texture, and it has a woody stem that becomes more prominent with age.

It is often found in open, sunny areas and is adapted to withstand cold and harsh environmental conditions.
