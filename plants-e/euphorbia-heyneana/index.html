Euphorbia Heyneana

Euphorbia heyneana is a striking succulent native to western africa known for its unique appearance and hardy nature. this plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water and help it survive in arid environments. its bright green color and spiky leaves make it a popular choice among cactus and succulent enthusiasts. it is often grown as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements. despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap that can cause skin irritation, so caution is advised when handling it.
