Encyclia diguetii

Encyclia diguetii, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Encyclia diguetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
