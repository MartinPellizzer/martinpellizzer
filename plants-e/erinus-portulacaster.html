Erinus portulacaster

Erinus portulacaster, commonly known as the silverleaf eryngo, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. Erinus portulacaster is often used in rock gardens and as a ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.
