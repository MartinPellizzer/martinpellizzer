Escallonia × rubricalyx

Escallonia × rubricalyx, commonly known as the red-leafed escallonia, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that displays a striking red or bronze coloration, especially in cooler weather. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Escallonia × rubricalyx is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
