Ehretia acuminata var. pyrifolia

Ehretia acuminata var. pyrifolia, commonly known as the Chinese mahogany or Chinese evergreen, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a shade tree.
