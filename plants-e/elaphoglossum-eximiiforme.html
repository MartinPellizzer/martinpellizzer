Elaphoglossum eximiiforme

Elaphoglossum eximiiforme, commonly known as the slender holly fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Elaphoglossum eximiiforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
