Epipactis thunbergii

Epipactis thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodlands and thrives in rich, humusy soil. It is a perennial herb that produces a single, erect stem bearing its floral display.

Epipactis thunbergii is known for its ability to form dense colonies and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
