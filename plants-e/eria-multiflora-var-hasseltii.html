Eria multiflora var. hasseltii

Eria multiflora var. hasseltii, commonly known as the Hasselt's eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique floral display and is valued in both horticultural and conservation contexts.
