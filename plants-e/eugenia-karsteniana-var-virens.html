Eugenia karsteniana var. virens

Eugenia karsteniana var. virens, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty Eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that give it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
