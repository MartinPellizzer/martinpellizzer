Eyrythalia floribunda

Eyrythalia floribunda, commonly known as the Crimson Bloom Orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces fragrant flowers in clusters, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in humid environments.

Eyrythalia floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
