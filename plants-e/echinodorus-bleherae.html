Echinodorus bleherae

Echinodorus bleherae, commonly known as the Amazonian sword plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light exposure and nutrient-rich substrates. It is often cultivated in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to improve water quality.

Echinodorus bleherae is also known for its resilience and adaptability to various aquatic conditions.
