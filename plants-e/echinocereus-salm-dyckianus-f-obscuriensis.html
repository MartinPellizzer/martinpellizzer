Echinocereus salm-dyckianus f. obscuriensis

Echinocereus salm-dyckianus f. obscuriensis, commonly known as the dark-stemmed hedgehog cactus, is a columnar cactus native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Mexico.

It features a distinctive dark green to reddish stem with spiny areoles, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
