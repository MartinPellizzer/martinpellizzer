Eragrostis multicaulis

Eragrostis multicaulis, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and prairies. Its rhizomatous and stolonal growth allows it to spread and form dense stands.

Eragrostis multicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
