Echinocereus stramineus subsp. stramineus

Echinocereus stramineus subsp.

stramineus, commonly known as the pale hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico. It is a columnar cactus with a ribbed, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height.

The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
