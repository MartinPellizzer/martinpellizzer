Epidendrum leucopyramis

Epidendrum leucopyramis, commonly known as the white-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically has strap-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum leucopyramis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
