Eupatorium balansae f. foliosa

Eupatorium balansae f. foliosa, commonly known as the foliaceous eupatorium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive foliage, with broad, often lobed leaves that contribute to its ornamental value. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to certain regions of Central America and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
