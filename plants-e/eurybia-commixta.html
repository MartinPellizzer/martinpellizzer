Eurybia commixta

Eurybia commixta, commonly known as the common wood aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are usually blue to purple and bloom in late summer to early fall.

Eurybia commixta is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
