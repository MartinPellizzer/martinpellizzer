Euphorbia handeniensis

Euphorbia handeniensis, commonly known as the Handeni euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green, surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stems for survival.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
