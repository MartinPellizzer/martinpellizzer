Eurybia cydoniifolia

Eurybia cydoniifolia, commonly known as the pineapple weed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.
