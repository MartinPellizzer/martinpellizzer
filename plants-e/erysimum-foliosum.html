Erysimum foliosum

Erysimum foliosum, commonly known as the silverleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and prefers full sun. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants.

Erysimum foliosum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
