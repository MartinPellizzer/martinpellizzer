Euphrasia × brevipila var. reayensis

Euphrasia × brevipila var. reayensis, commonly known as the Reay's eyebright, is a hybrid species within the genus Euphrasia, which belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that resemble eyeballs, a feature that gives the genus its common name. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
