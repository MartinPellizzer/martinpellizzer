Echinospermum deflexum var. grandiflorum

Echinospermum deflexum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the largeflowered echinospermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow to orange composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to arid environments.

It is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
