Eragrostis kalinaensis

Eragrostis kalinaensis, commonly known as Kalina's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, two-lobed flowers.

Eragrostis kalinaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and aesthetic qualities.
