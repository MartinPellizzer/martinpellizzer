Epicranthes undecifila

Epicranthes undecifila, commonly known as the lace-leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and showy yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.
