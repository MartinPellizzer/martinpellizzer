Eriospermum bakerianum var. seineri

Eriospermum bakerianum var. seineri, commonly known as the Baker's eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
