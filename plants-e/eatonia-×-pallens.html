Eatonia × pallens

Eatonia × pallens, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Eatonia and Pallens species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
