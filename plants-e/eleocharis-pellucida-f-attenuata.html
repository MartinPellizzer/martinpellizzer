Eleocharis pellucida f. attenuata

Eleocharis pellucida f. attenuata, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and shallow water areas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This subspecies is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to moist, nutrient-poor soils.
