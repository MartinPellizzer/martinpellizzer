Eutaxia microphylla var. diffusa

Eutaxia microphylla var. diffusa, commonly known as the slender eutaxia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
