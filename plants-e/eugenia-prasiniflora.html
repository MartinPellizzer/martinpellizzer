Eugenia prasiniflora

Eugenia prasiniflora, commonly known as the green-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Eugenia prasiniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
