Erysimum tenellum

Erysimum tenellum, commonly known as the dwarf wallflower, is a low-growing plant native to southern Europe.

It produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a compact, mat-forming habit with hairy, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found growing on rocky or gravelly slopes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
