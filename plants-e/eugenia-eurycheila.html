Eugenia eurycheila

Eugenia eurycheila, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

Eugenia eurycheila is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
