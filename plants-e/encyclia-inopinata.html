Encyclia inopinata

Encyclia inopinata, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, leafy growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Encyclia inopinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, moist conditions.
