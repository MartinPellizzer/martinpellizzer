Elymus Alienus

Elymus alienus is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It is native to regions across the western United States and parts of northern Mexico. This plant is often found in disturbed soils and can tolerate extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study. Understanding Elymus alienus can provide valuable insights into plant adaptation and resilience in harsh climates.
