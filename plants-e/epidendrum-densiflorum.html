Epidendrum densiflorum

Epidendrum densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually elliptical and dark green, with a smooth surface.

Epidendrum densiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
