Epiphyllopsis gaertneri var. serrata

Epiphyllopsis gaertneri var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated epiphyllum, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a climbing plant with fleshy, ribbed stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces showy, nocturnal flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Due to its unique appearance and flowering habits, it is a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
