Eurya oblonga var. stylosa

Eurya oblonga var. stylosa, commonly known as the slender leaf eurya, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive foliage and seasonal flowers.
