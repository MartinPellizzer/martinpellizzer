Eriospermum dielsianum subsp. dielsianum

Eriospermum dielsianum subsp.

dielsianum, commonly known as the Diels' eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
