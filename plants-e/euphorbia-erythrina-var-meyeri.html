Euphorbia erythrina var. meyeri

Euphorbia erythrina var. meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's Crown of Thorns, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, spiny stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers surrounded by fleshy, red bracts. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are often reduced to small, scale-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
