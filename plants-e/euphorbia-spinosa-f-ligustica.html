Euphorbia spinosa f. ligustica

Euphorbia spinosa f. ligustica, commonly known as the Ligustica spiny euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and lobed leaves, which are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.
