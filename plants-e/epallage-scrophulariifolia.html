Epallage scrophulariifolia

Epallage scrophulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf epallage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae, which includes many species with showy flowers. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically tubular and come in shades of pink or purple.

Epallage scrophulariifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
