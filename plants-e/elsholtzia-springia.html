Elsholtzia springia

Elsholtzia springia, commonly known as the spring elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs in the spring, hence its common name.

Elsholtzia springia is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as an ornamental plant in gardens.
