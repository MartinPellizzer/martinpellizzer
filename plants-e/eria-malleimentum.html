Eria malleimentum

Eria malleimentum, commonly known as the sticky orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, sticky secretions on its leaves and stems. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are small and often white or pale green, with a delicate appearance.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among experienced orchid growers.
