Eugenia longiracemosa

Eugenia longiracemosa, commonly known as the longracemose eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit, which is typically a small, smooth, reddish berry, is edible and often used in local cuisines.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in tropical forests.
