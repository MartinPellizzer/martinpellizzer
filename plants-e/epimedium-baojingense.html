Epimedium baojingense

Epimedium baojingense, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features delicate, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge on the underside. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels.

Epimedium baojingense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to partial shade and well-drained soil.
