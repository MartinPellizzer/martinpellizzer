Epilobium × sericeum

Epilobium × sericeum, commonly known as silverweed, is a hybrid plant species belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Epilobium × sericeum is an herbaceous perennial that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes, making it a persistent and adaptable species in various habitats.
