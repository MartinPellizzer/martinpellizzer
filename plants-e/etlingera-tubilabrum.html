Etlingera tubilabrum

Etlingera tubilabrum, commonly known as the red ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related species. The plant is characterized by its striking, bright red inflorescence that resembles a torch, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a glossy texture.
