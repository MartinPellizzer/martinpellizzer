Etlingera balikpapanensis

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking red-orange inflorescences, which are often used in floral arrangements. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, typically with a glossy surface.
