Erica klotzschii

Erica klotzschii, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica klotzschii produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators.

Its dense foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
