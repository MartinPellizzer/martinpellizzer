Evolvulus nanus

Evolvulus nanus, commonly known as the small morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has prostrate or trailing growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Evolvulus nanus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and easy care requirements.
