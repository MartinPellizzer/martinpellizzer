Eriogonum allenii

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
