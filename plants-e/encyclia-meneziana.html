Encyclia meneziana

Encyclia meneziana, commonly known as the Menezes orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia meneziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
