Erythrodes serripetala

Erythrodes serripetala, commonly known as the serrated redleaf plant, is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions of South America.

It features lanceolate leaves with serrated margins, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some horticultural settings.
