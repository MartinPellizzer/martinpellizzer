Euphrasia × tenuis

Euphrasia × tenuis, commonly known as the slender eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with dark markings. The plant produces slender, upright stems and has hairy leaves that are usually lance-shaped. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Euphrasia × tenuis is often found growing in clusters and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
