Erica bedfordiana

Erica bedfordiana, commonly known as the Bedford heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Erica bedfordiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
