Eupatorium ixodes

Eupatorium ixodes, commonly known as boneset, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The leaves are typically opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture. Boneset thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in herbal remedies.
