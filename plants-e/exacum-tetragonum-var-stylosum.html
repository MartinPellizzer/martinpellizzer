Exacum tetragonum var. stylosum

Exacum tetragonum var. stylosum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental value.
