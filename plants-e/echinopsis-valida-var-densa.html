Echinopsis valida var. densa

Echinopsis valida var. densa, commonly known as the dense echinopsis, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces spiny areoles that bear both flowers and spines, typical of the Cactaceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
