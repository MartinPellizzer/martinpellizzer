Euphrasia insignis var. nummularia

Euphrasia insignis var. nummularia, commonly known as the pennywort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, delicate yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has rounded, coin-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
