Eurya groffii var. zhengkangensis

Eurya groffii var. zhengkangensis, commonly known as the Zhengkang eurya, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a glossy surface and a smooth margin. It thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
