Erica × griffithsii

Erica × griffithsii, commonly known as the Griffiths' heath, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are leathery and evergreen, providing year-round interest in the garden.

Erica × griffithsii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
