Echinocereus pectinatus var. rubrispinus

Echinocereus pectinatus var. rubrispinus, commonly known as the red spined hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with ribbed stems and spines that range from white to reddish in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is often cultivated in arid gardens and rockeries.
