Eulophia lenbrassii

Eulophia lenbrassii, commonly known as the Malayan sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces tall, slender stems with numerous linear, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Eulophia lenbrassii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
