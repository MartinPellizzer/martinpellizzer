Eriocaulon willdenovianum var. fergusonii

Eriocaulon willdenovianum var. fergusonii, commonly known as the Ferguson's flat sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in poorly drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish flowers.

The species is notable for its ecological importance in wetland habitats and its role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.
