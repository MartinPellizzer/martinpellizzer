Epidendrum fuertesii

Epidendrum fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertes' Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum fuertesii is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
