Eranthemum variabile var. molle

Eranthemum variabile var. molle, commonly known as the soft eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
