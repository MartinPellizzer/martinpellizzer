Erioneuron

Erioneuron, commonly known as the red mistflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant features showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

Erioneuron thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
