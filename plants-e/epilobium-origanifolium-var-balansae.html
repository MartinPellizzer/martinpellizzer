Epilobium origanifolium var. balansae

Epilobium origanifolium var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae willowherb, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are typically pink to reddish-purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in riparian zones and open woodlands.

This variant is notable for its horticultural value and ability to attract pollinators.
