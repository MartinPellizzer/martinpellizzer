Espeletia grandiflora f. reducta

Espeletia grandiflora f. reducta, commonly known as the small-flowered espeletia, is a compact shrub native to the high-altitude grasslands of South America.

It features dense, woolly foliage that helps it withstand harsh, cold climates. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers clustered in a showy inflorescence. It is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and requires full sun exposure.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
