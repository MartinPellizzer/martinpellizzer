Euodia viridans

Euodia viridans, commonly known as the green flame tree, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its vibrant green, flame-like flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and produces small, fleshy fruits that are yellow when mature. Euodia viridans is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
