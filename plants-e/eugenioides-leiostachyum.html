Eugenioides leiostachyum

Eugenioides leiostachyum, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elliptical, woody pods containing flat, edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and partial shade.
