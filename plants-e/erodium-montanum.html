Erodium montanum

Erodium montanum, commonly known as mountain bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Erodium montanum is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
