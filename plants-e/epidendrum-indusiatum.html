Epidendrum indusiatum

Epidendrum indusiatum, commonly known as the Indian Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, often green or reddish-brown pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer or early fall. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

Epidendrum indusiatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
