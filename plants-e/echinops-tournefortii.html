Echinops tournefortii

Echinops tournefortii, commonly known as the desert globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Echinops tournefortii is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
