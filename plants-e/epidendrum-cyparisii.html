Epidendrum cyparisii

Epidendrum cyparisii, commonly known as the Cyparissus orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features striking, dark green, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble small cypress trees, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum cyparisii is relatively easy to care for and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
