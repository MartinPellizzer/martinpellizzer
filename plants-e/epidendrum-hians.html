Epidendrum hians

Epidendrum hians, commonly known as the skyward orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, slender stems that often bear clusters of showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically produces lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. Its flowers are borne on terminal inflorescences and are known for their striking coloration and fragrance.

Epidendrum hians is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
