Epidendrum calocheilum

Epidendrum calocheilum, commonly known as the Showy Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with contrasting red or purple markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Epidendrum calocheilum is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
