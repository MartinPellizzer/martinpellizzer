Eritheis

Eritheis, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It features striking red flowers with a distinctive spider-like appearance, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Eritheis is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes other ornamental and sometimes toxic plants.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown in gardens with proper care.
