Erigeron gracilis

Erigeron gracilis, commonly known as slender fleabane, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, prairies, and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. Its foliage is usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is often found in ecological restoration projects.
