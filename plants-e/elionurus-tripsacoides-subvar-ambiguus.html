Elionurus tripsacoides subvar. ambiguus

Elionurus tripsacoides subvar. ambiguus, commonly known as the ambiguous elionurus, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native range.
