Eria mansfeldiana

Eria mansfeldiana, commonly known as the Mansfeld's orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and elegant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Eria mansfeldiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
