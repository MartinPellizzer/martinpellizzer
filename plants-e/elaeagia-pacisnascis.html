Elaeagia pacisnascis

Elaeagia pacisnascis, commonly known as the Peaceful Bloom, is a flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Elaeagia pacisnascis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
