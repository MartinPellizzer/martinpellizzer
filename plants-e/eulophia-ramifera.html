Eulophia ramifera

Eulophia ramifera, commonly known as the branched eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and broad, strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months. Eulophia ramifera thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
