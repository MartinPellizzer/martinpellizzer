Encyclia randii

Encyclia randii, commonly known as the Rand's pineapple orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers resembling pineapples. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Encyclia randii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
