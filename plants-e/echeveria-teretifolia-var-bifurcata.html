Echeveria teretifolia var. bifurcata

Echeveria teretifolia var. bifurcata, commonly known as the Split Leaf Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often split or lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant purple-pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its compact form.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
