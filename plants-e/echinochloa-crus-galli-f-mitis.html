Echinochloa crus-galli f. mitis

Echinochloa crus-galli f. mitis, commonly known as slender barnyardgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown bracts. It is often found in disturbed soils and is a common weed in agricultural fields.

Its rapid growth and high reproductive capacity make it a challenging weed to control.
