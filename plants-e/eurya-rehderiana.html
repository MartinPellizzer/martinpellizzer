Eurya rehderiana

Eurya rehderiana, commonly known as the Chinese holly or red holly, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by bright red berries that are an ornamental feature. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Eurya rehderiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide winter interest through its berries.
