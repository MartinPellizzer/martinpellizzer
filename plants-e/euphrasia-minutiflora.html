Euphrasia minutiflora

Euphrasia minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features small, delicate yellow flowers with dark spots, resembling eyes, which give it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Euphrasia minutiflora is part of the Scrophulariaceae family and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
