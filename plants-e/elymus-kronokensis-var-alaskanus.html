Elymus kronokensis var. alaskanus

Elymus kronokensis var. alaskanus, commonly known as Alaska wild rye, is a perennial grass native to the northern regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of lakes and rivers.
