Eragrostis longiradiata

Eragrostis longiradiata, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, awned flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Eragrostis longiradiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
