Epidendrum costanense

Epidendrum costanense, commonly known as the Costa Rican Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Its blooming period usually occurs during the spring and early summer months.
