Ebenidium bourgaei

Ebenidium bourgaei, commonly known as the African ebony tree, is a member of the Ebenaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and fine grain. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Ebenidium bourgaei typically thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
