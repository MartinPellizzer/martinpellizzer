Eriocaulon desulavii

Eriocaulon desulavii, commonly known as the swamp spike-rush, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments, where it forms dense, grass-like clumps. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with a fine, silky hair that gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences, typical of the Poaceae family.
