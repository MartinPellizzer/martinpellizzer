Epidendrum gentryi

Epidendrum gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the production of showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences that bear numerous small, clustered blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum gentryi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
