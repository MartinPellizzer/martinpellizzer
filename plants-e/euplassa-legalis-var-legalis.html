Euplassa legalis var. legalis

Euplassa legalis var. legalis, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

However, it is considered invasive in some areas due to its rapid spread and competitive nature.
