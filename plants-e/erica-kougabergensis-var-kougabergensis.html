Erica kougabergensis var. kougabergensis

Erica kougabergensis var. kougabergensis, commonly known as the Kougaberg heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat. It features dense, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white.

This variety is noted for its resilience and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
