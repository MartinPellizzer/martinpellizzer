Euphorbia multiformis var. microphylla

Euphorbia multiformis var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, low-growing stems covered in small, green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green cyathia, which are its distinctive floral structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
