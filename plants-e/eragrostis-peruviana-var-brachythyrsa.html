Eragrostis peruviana var. brachythyrsa

Eragrostis peruviana var. brachythyrsa, commonly known as the Peruvian lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
