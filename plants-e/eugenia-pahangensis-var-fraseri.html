Eugenia pahangensis var. fraseri

Eugenia pahangensis var. fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's eugenia, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature, containing edible seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
