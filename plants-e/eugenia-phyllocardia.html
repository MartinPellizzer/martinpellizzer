Eugenia phyllocardia

Eugenia phyllocardia, commonly known as the red-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark red leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces edible berries that are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
