Eurya candicans

Eurya candicans, commonly known as the silverleaf eurya, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that have a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. Eurya candicans thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
