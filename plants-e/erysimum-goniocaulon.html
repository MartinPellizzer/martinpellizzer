Erysimum goniocaulon

Erysimum goniocaulon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and compact, branched structure. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Erysimum goniocaulon is well adapted to rocky and sunny habitats, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
