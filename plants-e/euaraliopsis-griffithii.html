Euaraliopsis griffithii

Euaraliopsis griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India and Sri Lanka, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
