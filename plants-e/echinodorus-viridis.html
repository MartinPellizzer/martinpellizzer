Echinodorus viridis

Echinodorus viridis, commonly known as the Green Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and can grow up to 30 inches in length. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Echinodorus viridis is also known for its ability to help maintain water quality by absorbing nutrients and reducing algae growth.
