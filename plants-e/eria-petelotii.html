Eria petelotii

Eria petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Eria petelotii typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
