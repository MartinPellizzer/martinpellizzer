Encholirium ascendens

Encholirium ascendens, commonly known as the ascending lily, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. Encholirium ascendens is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.
