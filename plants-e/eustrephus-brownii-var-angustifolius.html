Eustrephus brownii var. angustifolius

Eustrephus brownii var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in a spherical inflorescence. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
