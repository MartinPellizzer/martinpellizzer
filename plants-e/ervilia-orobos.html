Ervilia orobos

Ervilia orobos, commonly known as the African orchid or the yellow orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a long, slender stem with several leaves.

The flowers are borne on a spike and are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants.
