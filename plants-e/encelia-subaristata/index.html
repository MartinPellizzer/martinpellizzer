Encelia Subaristata

Encelia subaristata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes and other coastal habitats. This species is known for its small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ecologically important plant.
