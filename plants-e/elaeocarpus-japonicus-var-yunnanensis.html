Elaeocarpus japonicus var. yunnanensis

Elaeocarpus japonicus var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan elaeocarpus, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and showy white to pink flowers. The fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
