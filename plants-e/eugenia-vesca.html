Eugenia vesca

Eugenia vesca, commonly known as the myrtle or small myrtle, is a flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. Its fruits are small, dark berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications.

Eugenia vesca is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
