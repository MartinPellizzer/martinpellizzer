Ewyckia galeata

Ewyckia galeata, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, golden-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ewyckia galeata is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in local communities.
