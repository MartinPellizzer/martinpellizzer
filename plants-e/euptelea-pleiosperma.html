Euptelea pleiosperma

Euptelea pleiosperma, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broad-leafed magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Eupteleaceae and is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, and it is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
