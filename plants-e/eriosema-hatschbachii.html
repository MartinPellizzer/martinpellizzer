Eriosema hatschbachii

Eriosema hatschbachii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite and have a rough, hairy texture, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Eriosema hatschbachii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
