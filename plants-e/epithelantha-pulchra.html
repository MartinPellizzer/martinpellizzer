Epithelantha pulchra

Epithelantha pulchra, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and bright yellow flowers that resemble a Mexican hat. The plant has a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a striking visual element to its desert habitat.

Epithelantha pulchra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
