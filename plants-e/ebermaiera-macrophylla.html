Ebermaiera macrophylla

Ebermaiera macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf ebermaiera, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
