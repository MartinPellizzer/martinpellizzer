Epipremnopsis hainanensis

Epipremnopsis hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan epipremnum, is a climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and aerial roots. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with some varieties exhibiting variegation.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb and trail in indoor settings.
