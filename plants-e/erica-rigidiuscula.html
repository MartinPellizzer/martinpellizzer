Erica rigidiuscula

Erica rigidiuscula, commonly known as the rigid heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Erica rigidiuscula typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant pink or white flowers that attract pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation, a unique ecosystem in the Cape region.
