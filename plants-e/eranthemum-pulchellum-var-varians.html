Eranthemum pulchellum var. varians

Eranthemum pulchellum var. varians, commonly known as the variable eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowering period extends throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical areas.
