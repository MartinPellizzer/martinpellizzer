Etlingera corrugata

Etlingera corrugata, commonly known as the rough-leaf torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its elongated, dark green leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its inflorescence consists of showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters.
