Episcia melittifolia var. typica

Episcia melittifolia var. typica, commonly known as the honeybee episcia, is a vibrant trailing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and bright green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and indoor gardens.
