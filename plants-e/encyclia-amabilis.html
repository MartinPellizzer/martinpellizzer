Encyclia amabilis

Encyclia amabilis, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a yellow base and red or orange markings. The plant typically blooms in late summer, with flowers that emit a sweet, spicy scent. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Encyclia amabilis is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
