Echinocereus russanthus subsp. weedinii

Echinocereus russanthus subsp.

weedinii, commonly known as the Weedin's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a columnar cactus with a spiny, cylindrical stem that often branches near the base.

The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. Its areoles are covered with white, silky hairs, and it has a dense covering of spines.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
