Eugenia perpuncticulata

Eugenia perpuncticulata, commonly known as the spotted eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, clustered in small inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a deep red or purple color.
