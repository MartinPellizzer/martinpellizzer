Euphorbia nicaisei

Euphorbia nicaisei, commonly known as the Nicaise's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often spiny stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
