Echeveria Spathulifolia

Spatulate echeveria, scientifically known as Echeveria spathulifolia, is a striking succulent native to Mexico. Its rosette-shaped leaves are broad and spatulate, giving it a unique, almost geometric appearance. The plant is prized for its vibrant foliage, which ranges from deep green to bluish-gray depending on light exposure. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. With its compact growth habit and minimal maintenance requirements, it is a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
