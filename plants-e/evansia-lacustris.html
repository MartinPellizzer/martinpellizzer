Evansia lacustris

Evansia lacustris, commonly known as the lake evansia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically small and fleshy, adapted to store water.

Evansia lacustris is often found in sandy soils near lakes and rivers, where it plays a role in stabilizing the local ecosystem.
