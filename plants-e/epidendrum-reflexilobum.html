Epidendrum reflexilobum

Epidendrum reflexilobum, commonly known as the pink-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive pinkish throat. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a sturdy base for its flowering stems.

Epidendrum reflexilobum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
