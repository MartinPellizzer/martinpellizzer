Eugenia cabanasensis

Eugenia cabanasensis, commonly known as the Cabanas guava, is a small evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or red when mature. Eugenia cabanasensis is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical and subtropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
