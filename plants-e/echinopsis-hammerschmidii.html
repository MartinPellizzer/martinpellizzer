Echinopsis hammerschmidii

Echinopsis hammerschmidii, commonly known as the Hammerschmidii cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, green stems and dense clusters of white flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It produces spines that provide protection from herbivores and reduce water loss through transpiration.

Echinopsis hammerschmidii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
