Erythrochilus multiflorus

Erythrochilus multiflorus, commonly known as the red-lip orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-lipped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are small, clustered, and often have a distinctive red or pink coloration.

Erythrochilus multiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
