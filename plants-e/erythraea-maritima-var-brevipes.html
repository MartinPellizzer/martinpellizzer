Erythraea maritima var. brevipes

Erythraea maritima var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed sea erythraea, is a rare flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered leaves that are adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its roots are specialized to tolerate saline soils, making it a unique example of halophytic vegetation.
