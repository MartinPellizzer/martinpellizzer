Ellisiana

Ellisiana, commonly known as the fairy duster, is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spiky, pink or red bracts that resemble small dusters, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known as the ice plant family, and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. Ellisiana thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which bloom throughout the summer months.
