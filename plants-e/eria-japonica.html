Eria japonica

Eria japonica, commonly known as Japanese spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a cluster of leaves and flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria japonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
