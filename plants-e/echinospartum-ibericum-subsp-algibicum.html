Echinospartum ibericum subsp. algibicum

Echinospartum ibericum subsp.

algibicum, commonly known as the Iberian thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It features erect stems with spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
