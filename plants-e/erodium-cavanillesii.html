Erodium cavanillesii

Erodium cavanillesii, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and open woodland areas.

The species is characterized by its slender stems and is part of the Geraniaceae family.
