Echeveria chiclensis

Echeveria chiclensis, commonly known as the Mexican snowflake, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in the spring, which bloom atop tall, branched stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its color and shape.

Echeveria chiclensis is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
