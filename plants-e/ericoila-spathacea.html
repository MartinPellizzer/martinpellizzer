Ericoila spathacea

Ericoila spathacea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Ericoila spathacea thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forest understories.
