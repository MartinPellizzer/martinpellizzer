Erica × pallens

Erica × pallens, commonly known as the Heath Erica, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica × pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to form dense, low-growing thickets.

It is often used in coastal and rocky garden settings due to its hardiness and adaptability.
