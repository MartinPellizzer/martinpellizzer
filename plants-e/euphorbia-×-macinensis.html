Euphorbia × macinensis

Euphorbia × macinensis, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cup-shaped structure called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stems.

Euphorbia × macinensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
