Erodion botrydium

Erodion botrydium, commonly known as the clustered erodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
