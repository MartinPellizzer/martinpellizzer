Echinopsis santacruzensis

Echinopsis santacruzensis, commonly known as the Santa Cruz Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to northern Argentina.

It is a columnar cactus with a ribbed, spiny stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy white or pink flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick stems.

Its structure and appearance make it a popular choice for succulent collections and rock gardens.
