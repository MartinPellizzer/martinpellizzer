Erucaria pinnata subsp. uncata

Erucaria pinnata subsp.

uncata, commonly known as the silverleaf erucaria, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine, silky hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often pinnately veined.

This species is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing drought tolerance and resilience to poor soil conditions.
