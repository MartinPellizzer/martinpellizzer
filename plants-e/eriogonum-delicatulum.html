Eriogonum delicatulum

Eriogonum delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Eriogonum delicatulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
