Eremophila berryi

Eremophila berryi, commonly known as the pink-flowered eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It is adapted to dry environments and is often found in sandy soils.

Eremophila berryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
