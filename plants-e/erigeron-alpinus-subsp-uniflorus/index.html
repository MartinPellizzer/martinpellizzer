Erigeron Alpinus Subsp. Uniflorus

Erigeron alpinus subsp. uniflorus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe. It thrives in rocky soils and high altitudes where few species can survive. The plant is characterized by its single, showy flower and narrow, silver-green leaves. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
