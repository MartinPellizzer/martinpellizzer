Epidendrum lankesteri

Epidendrum lankesteri, commonly known as the Orchid of the Andes, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, adapted for absorbing moisture from the air. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments with good air circulation.

Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural cultivation.
