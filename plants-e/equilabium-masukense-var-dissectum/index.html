Equilabium Masukense Var. Dissectum

Equilabium masukense var. dissectum is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of Indonesia. Known for its delicate, fern-like foliage, this variant displays a unique dissection pattern that sets it apart from other members of its species. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, making it a sought-after addition to tropical gardens and greenhouses. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. This plant not only adds aesthetic value but also plays a vital role in its ecosystem.
