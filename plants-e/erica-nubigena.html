Erica nubigena

Erica nubigena, commonly known as the cloud heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white. Erica nubigena thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to cool, moist climates.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats at high altitudes.
