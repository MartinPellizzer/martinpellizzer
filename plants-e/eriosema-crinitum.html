Eriosema crinitum

Eriosema crinitum, commonly known as the hairy eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive hairy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.

Eriosema crinitum is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators.
