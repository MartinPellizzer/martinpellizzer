Eragrostis filiformis

Eragrostis filiformis, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, thread-like leaves and delicate, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a clump-forming growth habit and is often found in semi-arid regions.

Eragrostis filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its graceful appearance.
