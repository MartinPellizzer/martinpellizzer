Elatostema longipetiolatum

Elatostema longipetiolatum, commonly known as the long-petioled elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features delicate, opposite leaves with long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-white in color, and it produces small, fleshy berries.

Elatostema longipetiolatum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
