Eurya latifolia variegata

Eurya latifolia variegata, commonly known as the variegated waxleaf eurya, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it an attractive addition to gardens and indoor spaces.
