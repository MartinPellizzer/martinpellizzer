Elaeagnus cyanea

Elaeagnus cyanea, commonly known as the blue elaeagnus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-green foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment. Its fruits are edible and have a sweet, nutty flavor.

Elaeagnus cyanea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
