Epidendrum wendlandianum

Epidendrum wendlandianum, commonly known as the Wendland's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

Epidendrum wendlandianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
