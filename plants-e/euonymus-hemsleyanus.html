Euonymus hemsleyanus

Euonymus hemsleyanus, commonly known as the Chinese spindle tree, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Cucurbitaceae and is characterized by its opposite, oval-shaped leaves that turn red in autumn. However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
