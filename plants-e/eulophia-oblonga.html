Eulophia oblonga

Eulophia oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green in color. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow.

Eulophia oblonga thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
