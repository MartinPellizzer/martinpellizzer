Eragrostis brasiliensis var. delta

Eragrostis brasiliensis var. delta, commonly known as delta panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
