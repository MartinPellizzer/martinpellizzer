Ehretia austin-smithii

Ehretia austin-smithii, commonly known as Austin's ehretia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, elliptical fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a smooth margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
