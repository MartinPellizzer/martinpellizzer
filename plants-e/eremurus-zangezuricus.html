Eremurus zangezuricus

Eremurus zangezuricus, commonly known as the Zangezur Spearthistle, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence that resembles a spear, composed of numerous narrow, bract-like leaves. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Eremurus zangezuricus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in alpine or rocky environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
