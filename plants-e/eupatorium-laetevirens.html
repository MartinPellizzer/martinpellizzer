Eupatorium laetevirens

Eupatorium laetevirens, commonly known as the cheerful green eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white to pale blue, arranged in dense, flat-topped clusters.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
