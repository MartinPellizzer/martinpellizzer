Erica bibax

Erica bibax, commonly known as the two-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes various shrubs and evergreens. The plant is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pale pink or white flowers. Erica bibax typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
