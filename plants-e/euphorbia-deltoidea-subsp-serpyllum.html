Euphorbia deltoidea subsp. serpyllum

Euphorbia deltoidea subsp.

serpyllum, commonly known as the arrowleaf euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized flower-like structures.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often toxic species.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
