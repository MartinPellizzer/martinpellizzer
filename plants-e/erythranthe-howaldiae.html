Erythranthe howaldiae

Erythranthe howaldiae, commonly known as the Howald's fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, showy flowers and distinctive seed pods. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats.
