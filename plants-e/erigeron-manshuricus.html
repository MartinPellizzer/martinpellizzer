Erigeron manshuricus

Erigeron manshuricus, commonly known as the Manchurian fleabane, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. Erigeron manshuricus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
