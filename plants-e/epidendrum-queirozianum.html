Epidendrum queirozianum

Epidendrum queirozianum, commonly known as the Queiroz's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to white, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
