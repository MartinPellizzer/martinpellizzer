Episteira coccinea

Episteira coccinea, commonly known as the red epistle fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its striking red-tinged fronds. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its fronds are pinnately divided with a distinctive reddish hue, especially when young.

Episteira coccinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
