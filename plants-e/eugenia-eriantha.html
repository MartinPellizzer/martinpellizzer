Eugenia eriantha

Eugenia eriantha, commonly known as the mountain satinash, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. Eugenia eriantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a humid climate to flourish.
