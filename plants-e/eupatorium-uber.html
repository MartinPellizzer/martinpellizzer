Eupatorium uber

Eupatorium uber, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of tiny, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Eupatorium uber is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
