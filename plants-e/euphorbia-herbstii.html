Euphorbia herbstii

Euphorbia herbstii, commonly known as the Herbst's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.

Euphorbia herbstii is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
