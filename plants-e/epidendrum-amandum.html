Epidendrum amandum

Epidendrum amandum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves, which are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored, fragrant flowers that range from pink to red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum amandum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home environment.
