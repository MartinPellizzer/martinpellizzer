Eucryphia × penwithensis

Eucryphia × penwithensis, commonly known as the Penwith eucryphia, is a hybrid plant native to the British Isles.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its ornamental, fragrant, and often showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of pink to white, bell-shaped blooms in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and glossy, providing an attractive evergreen structure throughout the year.

Eucryphia × penwithensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
