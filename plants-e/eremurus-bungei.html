Eremurus bungei

Eremurus bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's goat's beard, is a striking perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Eremurus, which is part of the Asteraceae family, commonly known as the sunflower family. The plant is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its most notable feature is its large, showy inflorescence composed of many yellow to orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Eremurus bungei is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
