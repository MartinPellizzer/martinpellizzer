Erythrina tomentosa var. longicauda

Erythrina tomentosa var. longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed coral tree, is a tropical legume characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves and vibrant red flowers.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features a woody stem with a thick, corky bark and produces pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping and as a source of natural dyes.
