Eulophia angustilabellata

Eulophia angustilabellata, commonly known as the narrow-lip eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces a single, slender leaf and a spike of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its flowers are characterized by narrow, elongated lips and are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Eulophia angustilabellata thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
