Epilobium × lindblomianum

Epilobium × lindblomianum, commonly known as the hybrid willowherb, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae).

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers with four petals, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and disturbed sites.

Epilobium × lindblomianum is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
