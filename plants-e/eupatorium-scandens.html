Eupatorium scandens

Eupatorium scandens, commonly known as American thoroughwort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist woodlands and along streambanks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. It is often found in wet or damp habitats and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its botanical name reflects its historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
