Epidendrum stenoselaginella

Epidendrum stenoselaginella, commonly known as the narrow-leaved epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in a raceme, with a pale green to yellowish coloration.

This epiphytic orchid requires well-draining substrate and consistent moisture to thrive.
