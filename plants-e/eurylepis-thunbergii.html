Eurylepis thunbergii

Eurylepis thunbergii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
