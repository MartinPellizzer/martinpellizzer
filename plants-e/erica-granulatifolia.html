Erica granulatifolia

Erica granulatifolia, commonly known as the granulate heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. This shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, leathery leaves.

Erica granulatifolia is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often used in rock gardens.
