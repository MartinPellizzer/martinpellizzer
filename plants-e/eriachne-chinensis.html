Eriachne chinensis

Eriachne chinensis, commonly known as Chinese grass or Chinese needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinct blue-green coloration.

Eriachne chinensis is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
