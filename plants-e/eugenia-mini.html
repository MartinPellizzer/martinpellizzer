Eugenia mini

Eugenia mini, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. Eugenia mini thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and aromatic properties.
