Epidendrum rhopalostele

Epidendrum rhopalostele, commonly known as the green spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and pseudobulbs that support its foliage. The plant produces showy, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This species is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
