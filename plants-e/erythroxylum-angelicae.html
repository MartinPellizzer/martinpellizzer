Erythroxylum angelicae

Erythroxylum angelicae, commonly known as the Angelicae Erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

The plant is notable for its potential medicinal properties, though it is not as widely studied as other species in the Erythroxylum genus.
