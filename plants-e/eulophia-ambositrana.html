Eulophia ambositrana

Eulophia ambositrana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid native habitat.

Eulophia ambositrana is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty.
