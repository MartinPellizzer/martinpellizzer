Epidendrum cardiophyllum

Epidendrum cardiophyllum, commonly known as the Heartleaf Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum cardiophyllum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
