Eugenia glandulosissima

Eugenia glandulosissima, commonly known as the glossy corymbia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and rainforest habitats.

Eugenia glandulosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
