Eriospermum seineri

Eriospermum seineri, commonly known as the sticky everlasting or desert everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Eriospermum seineri is adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
