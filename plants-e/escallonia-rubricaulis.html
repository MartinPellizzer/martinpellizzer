Escallonia rubricaulis

Escallonia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed escallonia, is a shrub native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features red-brown stems that are often covered in a layer of flaky bark. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Escallonia rubricaulis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
