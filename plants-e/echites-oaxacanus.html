Echites oaxacanus

Echites oaxacanus, commonly known as the Oaxacan milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its latex is milky and contains toxic compounds, which deter herbivores.
