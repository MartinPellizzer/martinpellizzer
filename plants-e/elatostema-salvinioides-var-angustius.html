Elatostema salvinioides var. angustius

Elatostema salvinioides var. angustius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants, and are usually greenish-white in color.

The species is characterized by its ability to form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
