Epidendrum × foulquieri

Epidendrum × foulquieri, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Epidendrum nocturnum and Epidendrum secundum.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that are often covered in a dense layer of white or brown hair. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is native to Mexico and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
