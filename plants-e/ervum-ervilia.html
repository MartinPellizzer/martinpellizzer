Ervum ervilia

Ervum ervilia, commonly known as the common vetch, is an annual legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of conditions, including poor soils.

Ervum ervilia is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural settings.
