Encyclia elegantula

Encyclia elegantula, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are borne on short, erect stems. Encyclia elegantula has linear to lance-shaped leaves and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
