Elatostema henriquesii

Elatostema henriquesii, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full to partial sunlight.

Elatostema henriquesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
