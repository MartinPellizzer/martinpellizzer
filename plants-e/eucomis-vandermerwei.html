Eucomis vandermerwei

Eucomis vandermerwei, commonly known as the white-headed pineapple.

This plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa. It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and a tall, branched inflorescence that bears small, white flowers. The plant produces edible, fleshy berries that are often consumed raw or cooked.
