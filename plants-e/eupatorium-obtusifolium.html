Eupatorium obtusifolium

Eupatorium obtusifolium, commonly known as roughleaf boneset, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other familiar flowering plants.

Eupatorium obtusifolium is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
