Euphorbia schoenlandii

Euphorbia schoenlandii, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, spiny stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
