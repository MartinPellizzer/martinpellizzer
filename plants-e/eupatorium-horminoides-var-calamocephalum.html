Eupatorium horminoides var. calamocephalum

Eupatorium horminoides var. calamocephalum, commonly known as the slender false dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded or partially shaded areas. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
