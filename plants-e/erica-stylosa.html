Erica stylosa

Erica stylosa, commonly known as the slender heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other shrubs adapted to acidic soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in spring. Erica stylosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is a hardy, evergreen shrub that is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value.
