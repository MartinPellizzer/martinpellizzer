Eragrostis speirostachya

Eragrostis speirostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and grasslands.

Eragrostis speirostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
