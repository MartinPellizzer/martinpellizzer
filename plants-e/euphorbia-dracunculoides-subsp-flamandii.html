Euphorbia dracunculoides subsp. flamandii

Euphorbia dracunculoides subsp.

flamandii, commonly known as the Flamand's spurge, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It features thick, ribbed stems and produces striking yellow-green flowers in clusters.

The plant has a spiny, branched structure and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are typically small and fleshy, storing water to aid in survival.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
