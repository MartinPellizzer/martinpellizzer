Erica rufescens

Erica rufescens, commonly known as the red heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, blooming in spring and early summer. Erica rufescens thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
