Epidendrum lopezii

Epidendrum lopezii, commonly known as Lopez's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are ovate and covered with a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
