Euphorbia tongchuanensis

Euphorbia tongchuanensis, commonly known as the Tongchuan spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in a structure called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stems for survival.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
