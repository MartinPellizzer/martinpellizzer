Erica setacea

Erica setacea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to the Cape region of South Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Erica setacea is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and thrives in full sun.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardy nature and attractive floral display.
