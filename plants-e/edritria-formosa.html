Edritria formosa

Edritria formosa, commonly known as the beautiful edritria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Edritria formosa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
