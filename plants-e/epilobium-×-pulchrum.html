Epilobium × pulchrum, commonly known as the showy willowherb, is a hybrid species within the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. Epilobium × pulchrum is an herbaceous perennial that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
