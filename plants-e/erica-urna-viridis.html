Erica urna-viridis

Erica urna-viridis, commonly known as the green urn heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heaths and heather. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and its clusters of small, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Erica urna-viridis thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
