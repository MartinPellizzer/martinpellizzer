Epilobium junceum f. teucriifolium

Epilobium junceum f. teucriifolium, commonly known as the teucriifolium form of common willowherb, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color and bear opposite, lanceolate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The flowers are typically pink to reddish-purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and streambanks.

This plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support pollinator populations.
