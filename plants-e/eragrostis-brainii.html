Eragrostis brainii

Eragrostis brainii, commonly known as the Blue Panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences that produce small, whitish to purplish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland and savanna habitats. It is drought-tolerant and can grow in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens and as a landscape plant.
