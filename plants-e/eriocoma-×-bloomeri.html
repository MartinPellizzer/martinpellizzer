Eriocoma × bloomeri

Eriocoma × bloomeri, commonly known as the hybrid eriocoma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine, woolly coating.

Eriocoma × bloomeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.
