Eucosia afzelii

Eucosia afzelii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Eucosia afzelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
