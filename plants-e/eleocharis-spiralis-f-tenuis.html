Eleocharis spiralis f. tenuis

Eleocharis spiralis f. tenuis, commonly known as slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight sheathing base, characteristic of many sedges.

This variety is distinguished by its reduced, elongated inflorescence compared to the typical form of Eleocharis spiralis.
