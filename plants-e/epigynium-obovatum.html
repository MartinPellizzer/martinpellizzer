Epigynium obovatum

Epigynium obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate epigynium, is a plant characterized by its unique botanical structure.

It belongs to the family Solanaceae, commonly known as the nightshade family. The plant features a distinctive epigynous flower arrangement, where the ovary is positioned below the attachment point of the sepals and petals. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, contributing to its common name.

This species is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid and shaded environments.
