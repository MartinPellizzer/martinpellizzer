Eriogonum dudleyanum

Eriogonum dudleyanum, commonly known as Dudley's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, edible seeds that are a food source for various wildlife.

Dudley's buckwheat is often found in coastal and inland regions of California and Oregon.
