Epipremnum moluccanum

Epipremnum moluccanum, commonly known as the Satin Leaf, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically green with a silvery or white variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Epipremnum moluccanum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
