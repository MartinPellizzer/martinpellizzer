Excoecaria guianensis

Excoecaria guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan croton, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and yellow, clustered in inflorescences, and it produces orange-red berries that contain seeds.
