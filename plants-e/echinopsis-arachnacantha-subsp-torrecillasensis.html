Echinopsis arachnacantha subsp. torrecillasensis

Echinopsis arachnacantha subsp.

torrecillasensis, commonly known as the Torrecillas echinopsis, is a columnar cactus native to Bolivia. It features a slender, ribbed stem with a smooth, pale green surface.

The plant produces large, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized cactus collections.
