Epidendrum chisquillense

Epidendrum chisquillense, commonly known as the Chisquillense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with fleshy, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum chisquillense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
