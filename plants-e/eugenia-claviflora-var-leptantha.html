Eugenia claviflora var. leptantha

Eugenia claviflora var. leptantha, commonly known as the slender-leaved eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in horticulture.
