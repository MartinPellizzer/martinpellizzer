Euphrasia fennica

Euphrasia fennica, commonly known as the Finnish eyebright, is a small perennial herb native to northern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Euphrasia fennica is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and healing properties.
