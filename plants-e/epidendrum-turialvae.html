Epidendrum turialvae

Epidendrum turialvae, commonly known as the Turialva Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with stems that grow horizontally and produce leaves along their length. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes, with showy, often fragrant blooms in shades of white, yellow, or pink.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
