Eriosema glomeratum var. overlaetii

Eriosema glomeratum var. overlaetii, commonly known as the yellow globular eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are hairy and often clustered at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
