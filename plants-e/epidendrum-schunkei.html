Epidendrum schunkei

Epidendrum schunkei, commonly known as the Schunke's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and thrives in humid, warm environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and can grow up to several feet in length.

Epidendrum schunkei is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
