Erechtites gunnii

Erechtites gunnii, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, violet-purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and a robust root system that helps it thrive in various soil types. It is often found in wetlands and along stream banks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for pollinators.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
