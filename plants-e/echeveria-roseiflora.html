Echeveria roseiflora

Echeveria roseiflora, commonly known as the rose-flowered echeveria, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a fine, powdery coating. Echeveria roseiflora is prized for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
