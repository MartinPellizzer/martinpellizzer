Evax

Evax, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by winged fruits. It is often found in disturbed areas and can become invasive in certain regions.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Malvaceae, which includes cotton and hibiscus.
