Eragrostis Squamata

Eragrostis squamata is a hardy grass species known for its distinctive silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in poor soils and harsh climates. Often used in xeriscaping and erosion control, this plant is valued for its low maintenance and drought tolerance. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. With its resilience and aesthetic appeal, Eragrostis squamata stands out as a versatile and adaptable plant.
