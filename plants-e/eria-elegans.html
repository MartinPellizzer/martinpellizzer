Eria elegans

Eria elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is a terrestrial orchid with slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
