Elaphoglossum xanthoneuron

Elaphoglossum xanthoneuron, commonly known as the yellow-veined holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately compound fronds with a distinctive yellow venation pattern. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, this fern thrives in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are typically leathery and have a finely divided structure, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
