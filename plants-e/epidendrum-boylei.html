Epidendrum boylei

Epidendrum boylei, commonly known as the Boyle's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It is known for its relatively compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Epidendrum boylei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
