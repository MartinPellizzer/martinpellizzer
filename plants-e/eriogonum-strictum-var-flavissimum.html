Eriogonum strictum var. flavissimum

Eriogonum strictum var. flavissimum, commonly known as the yellowleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The species produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open grasslands and chaparral.

This plant is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its adaptability to arid environments.
