Echidnopsis scutellata subsp. scutellata

Echidnopsis scutellata subsp.

scutellata, commonly known as the Cape Town daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, from late autumn to early spring. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal among gardeners.
