Enkianthus deflexus var. deflexus

Enkianthus deflexus var. deflexus, commonly known as the flexible enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its clusters of bell-shaped, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly serrated margin. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
