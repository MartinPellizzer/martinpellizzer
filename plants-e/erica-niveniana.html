Erica niveniana

Erica niveniana, commonly known as the Highland heath, is a species of heath plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in early summer. Erica niveniana thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

It plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native environment.
