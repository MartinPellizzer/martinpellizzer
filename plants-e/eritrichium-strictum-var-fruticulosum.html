Eritrichium strictum var. fruticulosum

Eritrichium strictum var. fruticulosum, commonly known as the hairy eriophyllum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
