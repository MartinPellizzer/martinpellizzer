Echites vauthieri

Echites vauthieri, commonly known as the Vauthier's ghost flower, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, ephemeral blooms that appear only under specific environmental conditions. The plant has a fibrous root system adapted to survive in nutrient-poor soils. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
