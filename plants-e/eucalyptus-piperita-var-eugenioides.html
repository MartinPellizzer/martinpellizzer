Eucalyptus piperita var. eugenioides

Eucalyptus piperita var. eugenioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mallee, is a species native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub characterized by its aromatic, silver-blue foliage. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a rough, textured surface. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters, which are typically visited by nectar-feeding birds and insects.

This plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in mallee woodlands.
