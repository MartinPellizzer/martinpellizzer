Eugenia sihanakensis

Eugenia sihanakensis, commonly known as the Sihanakensis Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and unique botanical features, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
