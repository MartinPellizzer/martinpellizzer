Echites tenuicaulis var. latifolius

Echites tenuicaulis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf echites, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a slender, branching stem covered in soft, hairy foliage. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring.

This plant is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
