Epilobium × gandogeriamun

Epilobium × gandogeriamun, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a native, hardy plant for water features or moist gardens.
