Erysimum austriacum

Erysimum austriacum, commonly known as the Austrian wallflower, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to Central Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Erysimum austriacum is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
