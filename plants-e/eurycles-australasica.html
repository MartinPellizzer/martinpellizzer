Eurycles australasica

Eurycles australasica, commonly known as the Australian cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. Native to Australia, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades.

Its reproductive structures include a large, cone-like structure that produces both male and female spores.
