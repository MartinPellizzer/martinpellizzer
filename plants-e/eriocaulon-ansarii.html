Eriocaulon ansarii

Eriocaulon ansarii, commonly known as the Ansari's spike-moss, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, hairy layer, which helps in reducing water loss.

Eriocaulon ansarii is notable for its ability to thrive in both still and flowing water conditions.
