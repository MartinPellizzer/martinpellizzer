Eupatorium sexcostatum

Eupatorium sexcostatum, commonly known as roughhead boneset, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant has serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
