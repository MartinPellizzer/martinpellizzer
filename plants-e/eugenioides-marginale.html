Eugenioides marginale

Eugenioides marginale, commonly known as the margins of the eugenia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded environments of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Eugenioides marginale is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
