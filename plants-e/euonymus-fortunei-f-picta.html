Euonymus fortunei f. picta

Euonymus fortunei f. picta, commonly known as the Variegated Spindle Tree, is a cultivar of the Euonymus fortunei species.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring white and green patterns on its leaves. The plant is a compact, evergreen shrub that thrives in a variety of light conditions. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hedges and foundation plantings.
