Eugenioides laxiflorum

Eugenioides laxiflorum, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are often fragrant. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that mature to a dark purple color.

Eugenioides laxiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in warm climates for its foliage and floral display.
