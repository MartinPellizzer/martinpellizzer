Euphorbia heptagona var. fulvispina

Euphorbia heptagona var. fulvispina, commonly known as the yellow spined euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking yellow spines and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
