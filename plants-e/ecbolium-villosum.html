Ecbolium villosum

Ecbolium villosum, commonly known as the silky salsify, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, leafy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces long, slender roots that are sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ecbolium villosum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
