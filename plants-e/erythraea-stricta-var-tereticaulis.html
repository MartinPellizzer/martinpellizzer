Erythraea stricta var. tereticaulis

Erythraea stricta var. tereticaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed erythraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xerophytic gardens.
