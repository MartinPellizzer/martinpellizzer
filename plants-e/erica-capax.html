Erica capax

Erica capax, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly known as the heath or heather family. This evergreen shrub typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, dark blue to black berries.

Erica capax is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
