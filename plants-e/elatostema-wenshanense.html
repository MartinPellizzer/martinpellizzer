Elatostema wenshanense

Elatostema wenshanense, commonly known as the Wenshan elatostema, is a member of the Elatostemaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to China, specifically the Wenshan region in Yunnan Province. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, white flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, dry fruits.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
