Ervatamia officinalis

Ervatamia officinalis, commonly known as the lesser centaury, is a small annual herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.

Its seeds are often used in herbal remedies for their purported digestive and tonic effects.
