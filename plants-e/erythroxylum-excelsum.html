Erythroxylum excelsum

Erythroxylum excelsum, commonly known as the New Zealand velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Erythroxylaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its attractive foliage and floral display.
