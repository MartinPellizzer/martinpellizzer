Erythranthe guttata

Erythranthe guttata, commonly known as the spotted pincushion flower, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, rocky habitats. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Erythranthe guttata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
