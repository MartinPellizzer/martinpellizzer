Emilia libeniana

Emilia libeniana, commonly known as the Ethiopian bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, seed-containing fruits.

Emilia libeniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
