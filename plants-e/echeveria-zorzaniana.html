Echeveria zorzaniana

Echeveria zorzaniana, commonly known as the Zorzaniana Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
