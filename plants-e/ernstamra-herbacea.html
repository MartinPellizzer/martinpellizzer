Ernstamra herbacea

Ernstamra herbacea, commonly known as the fragile leaf plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine, hair-like structures that help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted for storing water, which is a key survival trait in its native habitat.

Ernstamra herbacea is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, where it thrives in full sun.
