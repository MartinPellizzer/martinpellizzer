Epidendrum schistochilum

Epidendrum schistochilum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It typically grows on rocky slopes and in open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous fragrant, violet to purple flowers. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Epidendrum schistochilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
