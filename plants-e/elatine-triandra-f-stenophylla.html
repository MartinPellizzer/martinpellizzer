Elatine triandra f. stenophylla

Elatine triandra f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water starwort, is a aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and tiny, clustered flowers that typically bloom in wetland environments. This fern-like plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its structure includes rhizomes that help anchor it in muddy substrates.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for aquatic invertebrates.
