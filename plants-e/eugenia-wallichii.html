Eugenia wallichii

Eugenia wallichii, commonly known as the Indian bay leaf or Malabar kino, is a small evergreen tree native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to bay leaves.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
