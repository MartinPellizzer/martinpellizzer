Euphorbia bivonae

Euphorbia bivonae, commonly known as the Christmas star or starburst euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with fleshy, green stems that are covered in small, star-shaped structures called cyathia. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers during the summer months, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
