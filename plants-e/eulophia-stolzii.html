Eulophia stolzii

Eulophia stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces linear, grass-like leaves and bears inflorescences with showy, spurred flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Eulophia stolzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
