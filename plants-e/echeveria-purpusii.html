Echeveria purpusii

Echeveria purpusii, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers on tall stalks during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Echeveria purpusii is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
