Epidendrum microtum

Epidendrum microtum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, dark green leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum microtum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
