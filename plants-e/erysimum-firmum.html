Erysimum firmum

Erysimum firmum, commonly known as the New Zealand wallflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Erysimum firmum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
