Erechtites hieraciifolius var. cacalioides

Erechtites hieraciifolius var. cacalioides, commonly known as the curly dock, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features heart-shaped, wavy-edged leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a loose cluster, which bloom in the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and can reach considerable depth, allowing it to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
