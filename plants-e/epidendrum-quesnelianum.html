Epidendrum quesnelianum

Epidendrum quesnelianum, commonly known as the Quesnel's epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems arising from a rhizome. Its flowers are usually clustered in spikes, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.
