Eranthemum velutinum

Eranthemum velutinum, commonly known as velvetleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a prominent central tube. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
