Epilobium lactiflorum

Epilobium lactiflorum, commonly known as the milkweed of the genus Epilobium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. It produces capsules that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
