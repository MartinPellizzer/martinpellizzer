Eragrostis matogrossensis f. glabrescens

Eragrostis matogrossensis f. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous matogrossense grass, is a botanical variant of the species Eragrostis matogrossensis.

It is characterized by its reduced pubescence, giving it a smoother appearance compared to the typical form. This grass is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its morphology includes slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves, making it well adapted to its environment.
