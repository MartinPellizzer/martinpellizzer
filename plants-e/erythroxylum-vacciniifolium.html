Erythroxylum vacciniifolium

Erythroxylum vacciniifolium, commonly known as the red leaf erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that turn reddish in color as they mature. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests.
