Epacris × cregii

Epacris × cregii, commonly known as the hybrid epacris, is a species native to Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant typically features dense clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Epacris × cregii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
