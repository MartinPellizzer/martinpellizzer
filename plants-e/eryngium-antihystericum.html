Eryngium antihystericum

Eryngium antihystericum, commonly known as the hysteric eryngo, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its spiky, blue or purple flower heads. Eryngium antihystericum is also known for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
