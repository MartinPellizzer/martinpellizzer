Eugenia penasii

Eugenia penasii, commonly known as the Pena's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the high Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Eugenia penasii is often found in moist, well-drained soils at higher elevations.
