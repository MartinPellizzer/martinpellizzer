Eriogonum compositum var. lancifolium

Eriogonum compositum var. lancifolium, commonly known as the lanceleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed areas due to its adaptability.
