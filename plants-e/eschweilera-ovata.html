Eschweilera ovata

Eschweilera ovata, commonly known as the pink-veined evergreen, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with distinctive pink veins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Eschweilera ovata is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its wood is also used in local crafts and construction.
