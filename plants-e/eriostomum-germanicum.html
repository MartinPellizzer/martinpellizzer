Eriostomum germanicum

Eriostomum germanicum, commonly known as the German erythronium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive red or purple throat. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Eriostomum germanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
