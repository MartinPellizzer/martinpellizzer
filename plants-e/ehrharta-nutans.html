Ehrharta nutans

Ehrharta nutans, commonly known as slender false grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in dense clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Ehrharta nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its fine texture and drought tolerance.
