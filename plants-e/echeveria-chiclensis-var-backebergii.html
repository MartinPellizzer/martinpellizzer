Echeveria chiclensis var. backebergii

Echeveria chiclensis var. backebergii, commonly known as the Backebergii Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powdery wax. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and easy care requirements.
