Eriospermum cylindricum

Eriospermum cylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, erect stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
