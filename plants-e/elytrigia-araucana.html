Elytrigia araucana

Elytrigia araucana, commonly known as the Araucaria grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces spikelets that are often clustered at the base of the stems, with small, awned lemmas. Its leaves are narrow and blade-like, typically with a bluish-green hue.

Elytrigia araucana is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions, including high-altitude and coastal regions.
