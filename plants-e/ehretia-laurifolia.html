Ehretia laurifolia

Ehretia laurifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ehretia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along its branches. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by yellowish-orange fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
