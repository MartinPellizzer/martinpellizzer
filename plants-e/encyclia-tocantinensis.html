Encyclia tocantinensis

Encyclia tocantinensis, commonly known as the Tocantins orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia tocantinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
