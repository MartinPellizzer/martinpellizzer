Elaphoglossum mexicanum, commonly known as the Mexican holly fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Elaphoglossum mexicanum is native to Mexico and parts of Central America, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates.
