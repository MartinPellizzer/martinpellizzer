Euroschinus elegans

Euroschinus elegans, commonly known as the elegant euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branched stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
