Erechtites hieraciifolius var. intermedius

Erechtites hieraciifolius var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate to ovate, with a hairy surface and a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
