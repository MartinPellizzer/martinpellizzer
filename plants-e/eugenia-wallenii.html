Eugenia wallenii

Eugenia wallenii, commonly known as the Wallenii cherry or Wallenii myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pinkish flowers. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature.

Eugenia wallenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
