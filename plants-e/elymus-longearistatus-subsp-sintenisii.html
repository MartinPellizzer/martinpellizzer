Elymus longearistatus subsp. sintenisii

Elymus longearistatus subsp.

sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis' wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to central Asia. It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, spike-like inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is valued for its hardiness and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
