Erica festa

Erica festa, commonly known as the summer heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Erica festa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
