Ehrharta melicoides

Ehrharta melicoides, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Ehrharta melicoides is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
