Erica hanekomii

Erica hanekomii, commonly known as the Hanekom's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heaths and heathers. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. Erica hanekomii produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or white, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with acidic soil conditions.
