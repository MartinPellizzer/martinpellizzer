Etlingera isip

Etlingera isip, commonly known as the red torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble torches, which are actually modified leaves surrounding the small, yellow flowers. Etlingera isip is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
