Echinopsis rhodotricha

Echinopsis rhodotricha, commonly known as the red-hairy echinopsis, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, red-brown spines. The plant produces striking white or yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
