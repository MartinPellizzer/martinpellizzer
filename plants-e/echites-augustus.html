Echites augustus

Echites augustus, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically spiny, serving as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

Echites augustus is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
