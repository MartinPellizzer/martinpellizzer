Erica kammanassieae

Erica kammanassieae, commonly known as the Kammanassie heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils in rocky or mountainous areas.

Erica kammanassieae is well adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region, with a dormant period during the summer months.
