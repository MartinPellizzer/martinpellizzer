Epidendrum grahamii

Epidendrum grahamii, commonly known as Graham's epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Epidendrum grahamii is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.
