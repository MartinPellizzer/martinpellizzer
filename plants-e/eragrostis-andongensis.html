Eragrostis andongensis

Eragrostis andongensis, commonly known as the Andong grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale green to brownish florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Eragrostis andongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
