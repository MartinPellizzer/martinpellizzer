Echinocereus longisetus var. delaetii

Echinocereus longisetus var. delaetii, commonly known as the Delaetii hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spine-tipped cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in clusters of white to pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has a dense covering of white to pale blue bristles, which help to reduce water loss and provide protection from herbivores. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
