Encyclia rhynchophora

Encyclia rhynchophora, commonly known as the narrow-lobed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow throat and purple or red markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia rhynchophora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
