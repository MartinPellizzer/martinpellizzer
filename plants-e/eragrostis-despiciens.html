Eragrostis despiciens

Eragrostis despiciens, commonly known as the slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Eragrostis despiciens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
