Elatine Graeca

Elatine graeca is a small, aquatic plant commonly found in shallow freshwater environments. It belongs to the family Elatinaceae and is known for its delicate, grass-like appearance. This species thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and often grows in clusters. Its simple structure and rapid growth make it an important part of wetland ecosystems. Despite its modest size, Elatine graeca plays a vital role in maintaining ecological balance.
