Euphorbia petaloidea var. nicolletii

Euphorbia petaloidea var. nicolletii, commonly known as the Nicollet's Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a short, woody stem with dense clusters of spiny, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in late spring, which are surrounded by colorful, petal-like bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
