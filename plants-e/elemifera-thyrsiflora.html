Elemifera thyrsiflora

Elemifera thyrsiflora, commonly known as the golden thorn.

This plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellowish flowers. The leaves are small, leathery, and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal rainfall.
