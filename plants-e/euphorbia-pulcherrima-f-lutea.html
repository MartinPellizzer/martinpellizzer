Euphorbia pulcherrima f. lutea

Euphorbia pulcherrima f. lutea, commonly known as the flame tree or Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bright orange to red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking appearance during the holiday season. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and as a potted specimen.
