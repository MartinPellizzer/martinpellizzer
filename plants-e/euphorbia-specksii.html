Euphorbia specksii

Euphorbia specksii, commonly known as the speckled euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with distinctive, waxy, green leaves that have a speckled appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia specksii is also known for its ability to store water in its leaves, which helps it survive in harsh conditions.
