Eulophia chilangensis

Eulophia chilangensis, commonly known as the Chilango orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinct lip, and it produces small, dark brown seeds.

Eulophia chilangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
