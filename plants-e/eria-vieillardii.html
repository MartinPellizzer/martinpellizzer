Eria vieillardii

Eria vieillardii, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

Eria vieillardii is known for its delicate appearance and unique blooming cycle.
