Enteropogon acicularis

Enteropogon acicularis, commonly known as the spear grass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale yellow to brown flowers. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
