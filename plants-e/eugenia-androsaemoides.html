Eugenia androsaemoides

Eugenia androsaemoides, commonly known as the Androsaemoides eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in subtropical gardens.
