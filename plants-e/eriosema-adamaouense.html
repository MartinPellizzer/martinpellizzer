Eriosema adamaouense

Eriosema adamaouense, commonly known as the Adamou's eriosema, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
