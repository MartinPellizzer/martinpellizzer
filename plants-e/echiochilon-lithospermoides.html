Echiochilon lithospermoides

Echiochilon lithospermoides, commonly known as the rock pepper or stone pepper, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
