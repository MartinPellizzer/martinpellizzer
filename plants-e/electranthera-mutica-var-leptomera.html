Electranthera mutica var. leptomera

Electranthera mutica var. leptomera, commonly known as the slender leaf elecampane, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and horticultural interest.
