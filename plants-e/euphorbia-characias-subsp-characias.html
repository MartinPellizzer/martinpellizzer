Euphorbia characias subsp. characias

Euphorbia characias subsp.

characias, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like.

The plant produces yellowish-green flower clusters called cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and unique floral structures.
