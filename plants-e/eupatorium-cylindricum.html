Eupatorium cylindricum

Eupatorium cylindricum, commonly known as narrowleaf boneset, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium cylindricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
