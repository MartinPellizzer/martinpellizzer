Emilia petitiana

Emilia petitiana, commonly known as the dwarf emilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, with small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Emilia petitiana is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and easy care requirements.
