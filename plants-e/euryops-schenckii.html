Euryops schenckii

Euryops schenckii, commonly known as the Schenck's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers surrounded by pale green ray florets. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits called achenes, which are dispersed by wind.

Euryops schenckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
