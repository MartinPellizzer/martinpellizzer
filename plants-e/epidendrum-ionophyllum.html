Epidendrum ionophyllum

Epidendrum ionophyllum, commonly known as the Ionophyllum Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum ionophyllum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm climates.
