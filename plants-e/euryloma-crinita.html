Euryloma crinita

Euryloma crinita, commonly known as the hairy euryloma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is often found in grassland habitats.
