Erysimum bungei

Erysimum bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with four petals, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
