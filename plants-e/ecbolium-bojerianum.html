Ecbolium bojerianum

Ecbolium bojerianum, commonly known as the desert thorn, is a perennial shrub native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It thrives in hot, dry climates and is adapted to poor soil conditions.

Ecbolium bojerianum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
