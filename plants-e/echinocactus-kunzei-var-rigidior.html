Echinocactus kunzei var. rigidior

Echinocactus kunzei var. rigidior, commonly known as the rigid hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are covered in spines, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to desert conditions.
