Eriosema gracillimum

Eriosema gracillimum, commonly known as the slender eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
