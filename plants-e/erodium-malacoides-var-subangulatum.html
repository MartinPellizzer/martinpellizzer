Erodium malacoides var. subangulatum

Erodium malacoides var. subangulatum, commonly known as the narrow-angled bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, ascending stems and small, opposite leaves that are often narrowly elliptical in shape.
