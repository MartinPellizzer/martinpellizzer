Eragrostis karasbergensis

Eragrostis karasbergensis, commonly known as Karasberg grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, contributing to soil stabilization.

Botanically, it is characterized by its robust root system and drought tolerance.
