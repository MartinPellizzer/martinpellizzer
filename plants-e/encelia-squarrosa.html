Encelia squarrosa

Encelia squarrosa, commonly known as the desert encelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features gray-green, fuzzy leaves and produces yellow, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions.

Encelia squarrosa is adapted to withstand drought and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
