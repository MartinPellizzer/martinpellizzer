Eriocaulon anshiense

Eriocaulon anshiense, commonly known as the Anshi marsh plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Anshi region, where it thrives in wetland and marsh environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences and are adapted for aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, Eriocaulon anshiense is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
