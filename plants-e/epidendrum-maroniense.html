Epidendrum maroniense

Epidendrum maroniense, commonly known as the Maroni orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored blooms, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
