Epidendrum subaquilum

Epidendrum subaquilum, commonly known as the Water Dragon orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like flower spikes that can reach up to two meters in length. The plant produces numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
