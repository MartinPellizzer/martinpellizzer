Euphrasia officinalis subsp. stricta

Euphrasia officinalis subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.

This subspecies is valued in traditional medicine for its purported eye health benefits.
