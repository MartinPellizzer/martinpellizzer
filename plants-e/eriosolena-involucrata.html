Eriosolena involucrata

Eriosolena involucrata, commonly known as the velvetleaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves and yellow flower heads.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. Eriosolena involucrata is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
