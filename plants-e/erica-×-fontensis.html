Erica × fontensis

Erica × fontensis, commonly known as the hybrid heath, is a shrub native to the western Cape of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica × fontensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its flowering period usually occurs in late summer to early autumn, producing a range of colors from white to pink and red.
