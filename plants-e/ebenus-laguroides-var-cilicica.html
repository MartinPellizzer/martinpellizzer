Ebenus laguroides var. cilicica

Ebenus laguroides var. cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician thornvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, hooked spines, which provide protection against herbivores. Its small, yellow flowers appear in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise rugged appearance.

The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive botanical profile.
