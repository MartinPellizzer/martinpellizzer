Eranthemum ecbolium

Eranthemum ecbolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and showy, clustered flowers. Eranthemum ecbolium is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
