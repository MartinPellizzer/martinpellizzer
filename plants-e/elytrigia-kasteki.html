Elytrigia kasteki

Elytrigia kasteki, commonly known as the Kastek's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and wetlands. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, a common trait among grasses.

Elytrigia kasteki is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
