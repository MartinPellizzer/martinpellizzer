Erythroxylum micranthum

Erythroxylum micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered erythroxylum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Erythroxylaceae and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers and elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
