Elatostema kupeiense

Elatostema kupeiense, commonly known as the Chinese dewplant, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, succulent leaves. The leaves are typically green with a waxy surface, and the plant produces tiny, greenish flowers in clusters. It is native to China and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Elatostema kupeiense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
