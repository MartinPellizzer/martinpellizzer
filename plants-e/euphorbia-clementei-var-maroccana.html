Euphorbia clementei var. maroccana

Euphorbia clementei var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to North Africa.

It features a thick, branching stem covered in spiny, leafless stems that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flowers surrounded by bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
