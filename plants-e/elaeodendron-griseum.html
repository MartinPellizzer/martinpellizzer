Elaeodendron griseum

Elaeodendron griseum, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features large, showy flowers with five petals, often in shades of red, yellow, or orange. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a rough texture.

Elaeodendron griseum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
