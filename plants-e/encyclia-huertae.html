Encyclia huertae

Encyclia huertae, commonly known as the Huerta Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

Encyclia huertae thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.
