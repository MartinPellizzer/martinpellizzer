Eulophia sumatrana

Eulophia sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran eulophia, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its inflorescence is often branched, with numerous small to medium-sized blooms.

Eulophia sumatrana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
