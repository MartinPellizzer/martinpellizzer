Erigeron foliosus var. franciscensis

Erigeron foliosus var. franciscensis, commonly known as Franciscan fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.
