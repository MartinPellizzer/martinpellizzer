Epidendrum polyschistum

Epidendrum polyschistum, commonly known as the spotted epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered with a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Epidendrum polyschistum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
