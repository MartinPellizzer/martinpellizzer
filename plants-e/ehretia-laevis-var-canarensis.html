Ehretia laevis var. canarensis

Ehretia laevis var. canarensis, commonly known as the white-flowered ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern India.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
