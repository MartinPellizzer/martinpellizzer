Echinopsis Rhodotricha Var. Chacoana

Echinopsis rhodotricha var. chacoana is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America. Known for its vibrant pink spines and large, showy flowers, it captivates both botanists and gardeners alike. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for desert gardens. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its sharp spines and sensitivity to overwatering.
