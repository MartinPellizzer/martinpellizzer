Erigeron reductus var. reductus

Erigeron reductus var. reductus, commonly known as the narrowleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.
