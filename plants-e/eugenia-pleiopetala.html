Eugenia pleiopetala

Eugenia pleiopetala, commonly known as the many-petaled eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
