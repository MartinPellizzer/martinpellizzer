Epimedium × sinense

Epimedium × sinense, commonly known as barrenwort, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year. It produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries.

Epimedium × sinense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded woodland environments.
