Eugenia retusa

Eugenia retusa, commonly known as the smooth eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter beneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. Eugenia retusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
