Eriosolena montana var. macrophylla

Eriosolena montana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf eriosolena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often found in forest understories and along stream banks.

Its foliage and flowers are important for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
