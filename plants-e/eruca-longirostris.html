Eruca longirostris

Eruca longirostris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pinnately compound leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by elongated, slender pods containing seeds. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often used in culinary applications and has ornamental value in gardens.
