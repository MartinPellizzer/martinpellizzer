Elaphoglossum cristatum

Elaphoglossum cristatum, commonly known as the cristate harts-tongue fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, crisped leaf margins that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its fronds are usually narrow and elongated, with a leathery texture.
