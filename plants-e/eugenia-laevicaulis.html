Eugenia laevicaulis

Eugenia laevicaulis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Eugenia laevicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden environments.
