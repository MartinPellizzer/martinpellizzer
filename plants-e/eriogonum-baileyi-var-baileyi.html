Eriogonum baileyi var. baileyi

Eriogonum baileyi var. baileyi, commonly known as Bailey's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers.
