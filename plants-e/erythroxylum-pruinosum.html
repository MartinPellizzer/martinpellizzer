Erythroxylum pruinosum

Erythroxylum pruinosum, commonly known as the silky balsa, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its smooth, silvery-tomentose leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, oval seeds.

Erythroxylum pruinosum is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
