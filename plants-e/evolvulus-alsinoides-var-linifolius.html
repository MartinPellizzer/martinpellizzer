Evolvulus alsinoides var. linifolius

Evolvulus alsinoides var. linifolius, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it suitable for various garden settings.
