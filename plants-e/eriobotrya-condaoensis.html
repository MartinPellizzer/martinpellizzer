Eriobotrya condaoensis

Eriobotrya condaoensis, commonly known as the Chinese white flowering crabapple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental white flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree produces small, yellow or red fruits that are edible but not widely consumed. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.
