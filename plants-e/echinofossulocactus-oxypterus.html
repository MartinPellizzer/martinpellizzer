Echinofossulocactus oxypterus

Echinofossulocactus oxypterus, commonly known as the red spiny cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, where it thrives with minimal water. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and cactus enthusiasts.
