Epidendrum strictum

Epidendrum strictum, commonly known as the rigid epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, spurred petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum strictum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
