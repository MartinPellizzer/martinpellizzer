Epidendrum giganteum

Epidendrum giganteum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, often displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has long, arching stems that can grow up to 10 feet in length, supporting clusters of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum giganteum is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
