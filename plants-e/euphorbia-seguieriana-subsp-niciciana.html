Euphorbia seguieriana subsp. niciciana

Euphorbia seguieriana subsp.

niciciana, commonly known as the Cape York euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many other succulent and shrub species.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
