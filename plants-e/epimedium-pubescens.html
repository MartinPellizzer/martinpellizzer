Epimedium pubescens

Epimedium pubescens, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, branched pedicels. Epimedium pubescens thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
