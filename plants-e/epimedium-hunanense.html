Epimedium hunanense

Epimedium hunanense, commonly known as the Hunan barbatan, is a species of flowering plant in the Berberidaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Hunan province, and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a glossy texture.

Epimedium hunanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and woodland settings.
