Euphorbia spathulata var. mexicana

Euphorbia spathulata var. mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican spathulate euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in a cup-like structure called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
