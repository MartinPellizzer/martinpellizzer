Epidendrum calanthum subsp. revertianum

Epidendrum calanthum subsp.

revertianum, commonly known as the red-veined epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central America. It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments.

The plant features strap-like leaves and produces vibrant orange to red flowers with a distinctive veined pattern. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is appreciated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical studies.
