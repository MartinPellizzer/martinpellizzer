Eryngium goulartii

Eryngium goulartii, commonly known as the Brazilian thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Eryngium goulartii typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
