Echinopsis × albisetata

Echinopsis × albisetata, commonly known as the white-spined moon cactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a central spination pattern with white to pale yellow spines, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
