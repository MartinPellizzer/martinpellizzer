Eleutherococcus pilosulus

Eleutherococcus pilosulus, commonly known as the hairy eleuthero, is a deciduous shrub native to the Far East, particularly found in regions of China, Korea, and Russia.

Eleutherococcus pilosulus is valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its role in traditional medicine.
