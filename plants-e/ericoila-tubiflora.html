Ericoila tubiflora

Ericoila tubiflora, commonly known as the woolly heath, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Floral Kingdom in South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Ericoila tubiflora is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region, with periods of drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species within its native habitat.
