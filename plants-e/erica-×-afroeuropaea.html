Erica × afroeuropaea

Erica × afroeuropaea, commonly known as the African heath or European heath, is a hybrid shrub that combines traits from Erica species native to both Africa and Europe.

It typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Erica × afroeuropaea is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
