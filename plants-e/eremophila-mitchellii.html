Eremophila mitchellii

Eremophila mitchellii, commonly known as Mitchell's eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Eremophila mitchellii is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
