Elatostema viridicaule

Elatostema viridicaule, commonly known as the green-stemmed elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, pubescent hairs. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Elatostema viridicaule produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically pollinated by insects.
