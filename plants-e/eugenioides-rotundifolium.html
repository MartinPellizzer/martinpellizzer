Eugenioides rotundifolium

Eugenioides rotundifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a shrub native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, which are followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
