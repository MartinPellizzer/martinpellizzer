Euphrasia stricta subsp. pseudosuecica

Euphrasia stricta subsp.

pseudosuecica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
