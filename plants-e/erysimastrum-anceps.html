Erysimastrum anceps

Erysimastrum anceps, commonly known as the narrowleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and blue to purple in color.

Erysimastrum anceps is often found in open habitats such as grasslands, sagebrush steppe, and rocky slopes.
