Elatine alsinastrum var. terrestris

Elatine alsinastrum var. terrestris, commonly known as the common waterwort, is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, streambanks, and shallow water areas.
