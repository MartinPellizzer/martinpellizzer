Eremurus × warei

Eremurus × warei, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a perennial plant belonging to the genus Eremurus in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, showy yellow flower heads that bloom in late spring. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

The hybrid is valued for its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
