Epimedium chlorandrum

Epimedium chlorandrum, commonly known as the golden epimedium, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant features rhizomatous growth and thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of leathery, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens due to its unique floral display and hardy nature.
