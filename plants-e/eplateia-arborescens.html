Eplateia arborescens

Eplateia arborescens, commonly known as the tree eplateia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, covered with soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, often with a waxy coating.

Eplateia arborescens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
