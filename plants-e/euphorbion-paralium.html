Euphorbion paralium

Euphorbion paralium, commonly known as the Paralium euphorbion, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

Euphorbion paralium is adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments, making it a common sight in tropical forests.
