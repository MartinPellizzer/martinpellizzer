Eulophia ovalifolia

Eulophia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinct greenish or purplish hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
