Eremanthus veadeiroensis

Eremanthus veadeiroensis, commonly known as the Veadeiro's eremanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

Eremanthus veadeiroensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and shelter for small fauna.
