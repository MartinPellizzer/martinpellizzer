Echinodorus densinervis

Echinodorus densinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a prominent central vein. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge above the water surface.

Echinodorus densinervis is known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water conditions and is a hardy choice for both beginners and experienced aquarists.
