Erica plukenetii subsp. lineata

Erica plukenetii subsp.

lineata, commonly known as the linear heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions. Erica plukenetii subsp.

lineata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
