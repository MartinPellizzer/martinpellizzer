Echinocactus palmeri

Echinocactus palmeri, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stem and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a spiny exterior, which helps protect it from herbivores and excessive water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Echinocactus palmeri is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
