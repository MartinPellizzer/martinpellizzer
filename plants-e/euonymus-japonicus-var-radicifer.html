Euonymus japonicus var. radicifer

Euonymus japonicus var. radicifer, commonly known as Japanese spurge or radicifer euonymus, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces red berries that are attractive to birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
