Embelia djalonensis

Embelia djalonensis, commonly known as African star anise, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
