Erythrostemon Exostemma

Erythrostemon exostemma is a rare and lesser-known flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, known for its showy and often bell-shaped flowers. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique red and purple blooms make it a striking addition to any botanical collection. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly studied by the wider plant community.
