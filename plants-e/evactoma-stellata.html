Evactoma stellata

Evactoma stellata, commonly known as the star-shaped lily, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-like appearance, with petals arranged in a radial pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, fragrant flowers during the spring season.
