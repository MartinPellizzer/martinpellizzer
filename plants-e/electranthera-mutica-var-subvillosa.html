Electranthera mutica var. subvillosa

Electranthera mutica var. subvillosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This variant is characterized by its soft, velvety texture on the foliage, which gives it a distinctive tactile quality. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a broad base and a tapering tip. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, arranged in dense clusters.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
