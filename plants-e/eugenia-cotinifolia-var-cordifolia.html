Eugenia cotinifolia var. cordifolia

Eugenia cotinifolia var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine and cuisine in some regions.
