Eclipta scabriuscula

Eclipta scabriuscula, commonly known as rough eclipta, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in open, sunny habitats.

Its leaves are rough to the touch, hence the species name "scabriuscula," which means "slightly rough.".
