Eriostemon Pleurandroides

Eriostemon bluebell, scientifically known as Eriostemon pleurandroides, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of New Zealand. With its vibrant blue and purple blooms, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded forest understories. Its unique morphology and striking coloration make it a standout among native flora. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure, highlighting the need for greater conservation efforts.
