Eugenia nigerina

Eugenia nigerina, commonly known as the black eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its dark purple to black fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The flowers are small, white, and fragrant, often appearing in clusters.
