Eriachne pallida

Eriachne pallida, commonly known as the slender grass or pale eriachne, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale or whitish in color, and it is adapted to survive in low-nutrient environments.

Eriachne pallida plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
