Eucharis × grandiflora var. moorei

Eucharis × grandiflora var. moorei, commonly known as the Moore's Amazon lily, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle pinkish hue. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
