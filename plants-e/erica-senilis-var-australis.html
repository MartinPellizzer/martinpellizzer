Erica senilis var. australis

Erica senilis var. australis, commonly known as the South African heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. Its leaves are evergreen, leathery, and arranged in whorls around the stems.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
