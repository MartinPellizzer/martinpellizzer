Euxena cebennensis

Euxena cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna euphorbia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellowish-green sap. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often surrounded by specialized structures called cyathia.

Euxena cebennensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
