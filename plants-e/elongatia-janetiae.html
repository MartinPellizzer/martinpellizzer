Elongatia janetiae

Elongatia janetiae, commonly known as the Janet's elongated fern, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length, making it one of the tallest ferns in its habitat. The plant thrives in high humidity and partial shade, typically growing along the forest floor or on shaded rock faces. Its leaves are deeply divided, with a feathery appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

Elongatia janetiae plays a vital role in the ecosystem by providing shelter and food for various small organisms.
