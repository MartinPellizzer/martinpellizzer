Epidendrum tipuloideum

Epidendrum tipuloideum, commonly known as the Tipuloid Orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It belongs to the Epidendraceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
