Elythranthera emarginata

Elythranthera emarginata, commonly known as the marginate orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features slender, hairy stems and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are elongated and opposite, often covered in fine pubescence.

Elythranthera emarginata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
