Evodia aromatica

Evodia aromatica, commonly known as Chinese prickly ash, is a small evergreen shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves contain essential oils with antimicrobial properties, contributing to its medicinal value.

Evodia aromatica thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental and therapeutic uses.
