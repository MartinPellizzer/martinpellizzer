Eulophia orthoplectra var. rugulosa

Eulophia orthoplectra var. rugulosa, commonly known as the rugulose eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, cane-like stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
