Echeveria pulidonis

Echeveria pulidonis, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powdery wax. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
