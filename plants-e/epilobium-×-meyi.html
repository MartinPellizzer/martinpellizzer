Epilobium × meyi

Epilobium × meyi, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a species native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender, often glandular-stemmed structure. The plant produces showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Epilobium × meyi is often found in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and roadsides.
