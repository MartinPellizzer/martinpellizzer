Elaeocarpus royenii

Elaeocarpus royenii, commonly known as the Royal Elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.

Elaeocarpus royenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitats.
