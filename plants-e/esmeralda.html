Esmeralda

Esmeralda, commonly known as the Emerald Gem, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It features vibrant green leaves that are glossy and textured, often with a waxy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom in late spring. Esmeralda thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
