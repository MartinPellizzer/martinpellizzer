Erophila verna var. americana

Erophila verna var. americana, commonly known as spring beauty, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
