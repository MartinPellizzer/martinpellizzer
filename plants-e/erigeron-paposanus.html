Erigeron paposanus

Erigeron paposanus, commonly known as the Pahute Meadow Aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy blue to purple composite flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and rocky slopes. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
