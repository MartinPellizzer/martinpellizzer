Euphrasia gracilis subsp. ericetorum

Euphrasia gracilis subsp.

ericetorum, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-orange flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems and bloom in early spring. Euphrasia gracilis subsp.
