Eugenia spiciflora

Eugenia spiciflora, commonly known as the spike-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences and aromatic leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia spiciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrant foliage and showy flowers.
