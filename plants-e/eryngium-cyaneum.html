Eryngium cyaneum

Eryngium cyaneum, commonly known as the blue thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, bract-like leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Eryngium cyaneum typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
