Enkianthus nipponicus

Enkianthus nipponicus, commonly known as the Japanese enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan and China.

Enkianthus nipponicus thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and woodland settings.
