Epimedium jingzhouense

Epimedium jingzhouense, commonly known as the Jingzhou barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately divided leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in horticulture and traditional medicine.
