Eugenia robusta var. firma

Eugenia robusta var. firma, commonly known as the robusta eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that transition from green to red as they ripen. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its aromatic foliage and potential medicinal properties.
