Epacris conspicua

Epacris conspicua, commonly known as the showy epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright pink or red flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators such as birds and insects. Epacris conspicua thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
