Euphorbia turcomanica var. tigridis

Euphorbia turcomanica var. tigridis, commonly known as the Turkestan spurge, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers surrounded by colorful bracts, which are often more showy than the actual blooms. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging growing conditions.
