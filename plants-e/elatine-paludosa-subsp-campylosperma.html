Elatine paludosa subsp. campylosperma

Elatine paludosa subsp.

campylosperma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic herbaceous plant. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and streams.

The plant has slender, branching stems that are often submerged or partially submerged. Its leaves are narrow and linear, arranged in whorls around the stems.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and borne in clusters, with four to six stamens and a single pistil.
