Euphrasia alpina subsp. pulchra

Euphrasia alpina subsp.

pulchra, commonly known as the Alpine eyebright, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It features small, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, showy eyespots that attract pollinators.

The plant typically has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high altitudes. Euphrasia alpina subsp.

pulchra is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
