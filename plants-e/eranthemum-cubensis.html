Eranthemum cubensis

Eranthemum cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
