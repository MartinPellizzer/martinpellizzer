Eritrichium barbigerum

Eritrichium barbigerum, commonly known as the bearded eritrichium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine collections.
