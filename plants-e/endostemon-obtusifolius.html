Endostemon obtusifolius

Endostemon obtusifolius, commonly known as the blunt-leaf endostemon, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clustered inflorescences.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.
