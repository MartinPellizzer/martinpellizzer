Euphorbia radians var. radians

Euphorbia radians var. radians, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
