Erica velitaris var. hemisphaerica

Erica velitaris var. hemisphaerica, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants.
