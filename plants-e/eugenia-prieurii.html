Eugenia prieurii

Eugenia prieurii, commonly known as the Prieur's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.

Eugenia prieurii prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.
