Eugenia obtusata

Eugenia obtusata, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically small, reddish berries that develop from the flowers.

Eugenia obtusata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
