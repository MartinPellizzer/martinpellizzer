Eugenia stephanii var. latifolia

Eugenia stephanii var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf eugenia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens.
