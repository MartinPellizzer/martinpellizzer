Eragrostis willdenowii

Eragrostis willdenowii, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and panicle-shaped inflorescences that give it a weeping appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has fibrous roots and produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Eragrostis willdenowii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its delicate, grass-like foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
