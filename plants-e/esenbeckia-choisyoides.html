Esenbeckia choisyoides

Esenbeckia choisyoides, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, while the fruit is a fleshy, star-shaped berry.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other tropical regions for horticultural purposes.
