Eupatorium verbenaceum

Eupatorium verbenaceum, commonly known as boneset, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture, while its roots are fibrous and used in traditional medicine.

Boneset is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating fever and respiratory ailments.
