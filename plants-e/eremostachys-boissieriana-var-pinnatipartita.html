Eremostachys boissieriana var. pinnatipartita

Eremostachys boissieriana var. pinnatipartita, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremostachys, is a perennial flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
