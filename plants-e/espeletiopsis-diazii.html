Espeletiopsis diazii

Espeletiopsis diazii, commonly known as the Colombian nutmeg tree, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to the Andean region of Colombia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains aromatic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and local cuisine.
