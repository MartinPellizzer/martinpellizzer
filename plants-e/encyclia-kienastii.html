Encyclia kienastii

Encyclia kienastii, commonly known as the Kienast's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces erect, lance-shaped leaves and bears racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Encyclia kienastii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
