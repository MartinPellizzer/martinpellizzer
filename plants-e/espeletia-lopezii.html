Espeletia lopezii

Espeletia lopezii, commonly known as the Lopezia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth form and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, arranged in a compact inflorescence.
