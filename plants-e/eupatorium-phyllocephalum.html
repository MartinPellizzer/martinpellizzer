Eupatorium phyllocephalum

Eupatorium phyllocephalum, commonly known as the broadleaf thoroughwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, white to pale purple flower heads appear in late summer and are arranged in clustered inflorescences.

The species is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
