Elephantopus nudicaulis

Elephantopus nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender elephant's foot, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has a distinctive, fleshy, tuberous root system that resembles an elephant's foot, giving it its common name. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It typically blooms with yellow flowers in the summer months, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
