Eranthemum verticillatum

Eranthemum verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Eranthemum verticillatum is known for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
