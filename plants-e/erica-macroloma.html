Erica macroloma

Erica macroloma, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, adding a delicate beauty to its natural habitat. Erica macroloma thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions.
