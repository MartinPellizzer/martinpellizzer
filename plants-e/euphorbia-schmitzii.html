Euphorbia schmitzii

Euphorbia schmitzii, commonly known as Schmitz's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
