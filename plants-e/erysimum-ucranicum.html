Erysimum ucranicum

Erysimum ucranicum, commonly known as the Ucranicum wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbages and mustards. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Erysimum ucranicum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
