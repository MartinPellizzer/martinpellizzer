Epipremnum luzonense

Epipremnum luzonense, commonly known as the Philodendron hederaceum var. 'Luzonense', is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for indoor hanging baskets.

Epipremnum luzonense is relatively low maintenance, making it an ideal plant for beginners.
