Eriocaulon triangulare

Eriocaulon triangulare, commonly known as the triangular spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact, spikelike inflorescence. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers clustered at the tips of its stems.

It is often found in moist soils and is adapted to environments with high humidity and partial shade.
