Eranthemum latifolium

Eranthemum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates. Its growth habit is herbaceous, with the stems often branching out near the base.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes.
