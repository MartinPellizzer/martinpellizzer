Epimedium ikariso

Epimedium ikariso, commonly known as Japanese barbatory, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental, trifoliate leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels. Epimedium ikariso thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

It is valued for its hardiness and ability to add visual interest to shaded landscapes.
