Epidendrum hueycantenangense

Epidendrum hueycantenangense, commonly known as the Huéycantén Angel Wings, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its long, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and lavender. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
