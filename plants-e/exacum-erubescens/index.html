Exacum Erubescens

Exacum erubescens is a delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant pink blooms and trailing habit. Native to parts of Africa, it thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. This plant is popular among gardeners for its beauty and ease of care. Its flowers bloom throughout the summer, adding a splash of color to gardens or hanging baskets. With proper sunlight and watering, Exacum erubescens can become a delightful addition to any indoor or outdoor space.
