Epidendrum prietoi

Epidendrum prietoi, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum prietoi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
