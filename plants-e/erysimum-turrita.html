Erysimum turrita

Erysimum turrita, commonly known as the turrited wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features pinnately divided leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Erysimum turrita thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
