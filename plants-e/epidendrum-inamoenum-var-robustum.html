Epidendrum inamoenum var. robustum

Epidendrum inamoenum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hair, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
