Epidendrum acinacifolium

Epidendrum acinacifolium, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum acinacifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
