Eugenia euosma var. rufescens

Eugenia euosma var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-fruited eugenia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit, which is reddish when mature, is edible and contains several seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
