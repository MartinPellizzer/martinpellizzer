Encholirium Subsecundum

Encholirium subsecundum is a striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. Known for its vibrant, spiky red bracts, it adds a dramatic touch to any indoor or outdoor space. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors. With proper care, it can flourish and bring a burst of color to any garden or home.
