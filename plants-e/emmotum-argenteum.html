Emmotum argenteum

Emmotum argenteum, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a central brown or black disk. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive, daisy-like blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Emmotum argenteum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
