Euphrasia officinalis var. flava

Euphrasia officinalis var. flava, commonly known as the yellow eyebright, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
