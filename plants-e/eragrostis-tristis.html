Eragrostis tristis

Eragrostis tristis, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Eragrostis tristis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
