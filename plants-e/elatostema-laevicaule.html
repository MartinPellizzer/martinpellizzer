Elatostema laevicaule

Elatostema laevicaule, commonly known as the smoothleaf elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy, with a distinctive waxy coating. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema laevicaule is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
