Epipactis willdenovii

Epipactis willdenovii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and a single, showy flower with a greenish-yellow lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a complex root system and produces a capsule containing numerous small, dark seeds.

Epipactis willdenovii is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in association with other orchid species.
