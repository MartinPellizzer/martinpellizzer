Euodia hortensis f. laciniosa

Euodia hortensis f. laciniosa, commonly known as the laceleaf euodia, is a unique variant of the euodia plant characterized by its finely serrated, lace-like leaves.

Its foliage exhibits a distinctive, almost feathery appearance due to the elongated, laciniate leaf margins.
