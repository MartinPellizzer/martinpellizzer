Echeveria stolonifera

Echeveria stolonifera, commonly known as "Mother of Millions," is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces offsets and small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
