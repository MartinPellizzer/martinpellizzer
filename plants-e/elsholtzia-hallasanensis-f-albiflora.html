Elsholtzia hallasanensis f. albiflora

Elsholtzia hallasanensis f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered mountain hyssop, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Jeju Island, South Korea.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its mountainous habitat.

This variety is distinguished by its pale floral color, setting it apart from other forms of Elsholtzia.
