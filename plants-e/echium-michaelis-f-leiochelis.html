Echium michaelis f. leiochelis

Echium michaelis f. leiochelis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. This subspecies is distinguished by its smoother stem compared to other forms of Echium michaelis.
