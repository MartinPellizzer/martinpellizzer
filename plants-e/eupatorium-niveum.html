Eupatorium niveum

Eupatorium niveum, commonly known as white boneset, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems covered in fine white hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the late summer and early fall. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium niveum is typically found in moist habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks.
