Espera

Espera, commonly known as the Espera plant or *Cleome hassleriana*, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a beneficial addition to gardens.
