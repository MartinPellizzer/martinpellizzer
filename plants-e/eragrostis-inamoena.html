Eragrostis inamoena

Eragrostis inamoena, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Eragrostis inamoena is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
