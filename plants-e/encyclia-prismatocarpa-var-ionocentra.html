Encyclia prismatocarpa var. ionocentra

Encyclia prismatocarpa var. ionocentra, commonly known as the Ionocentra orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often with a yellowish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
