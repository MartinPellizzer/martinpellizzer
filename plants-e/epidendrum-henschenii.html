Epidendrum henschenii

Epidendrum henschenii, commonly known as the Henschen's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are elliptical and covered with a fine, white pubescence.

Epidendrum henschenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
