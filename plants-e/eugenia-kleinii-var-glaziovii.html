Eugenia kleinii var. glaziovii

Eugenia kleinii var. glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in subtropical gardens.
