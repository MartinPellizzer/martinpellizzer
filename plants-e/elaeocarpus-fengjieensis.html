Elaeocarpus fengjieensis

Elaeocarpus fengjieensis, commonly known as the Fengjie elaeocarpus, is a rare evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves and showy white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically orange or red in color. It thrives in humid, tropical or subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.
