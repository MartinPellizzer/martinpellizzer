Eriogonum brevicaule var. micranthum

Eriogonum brevicaule var. micranthum, commonly known as the dwarf flaxflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in dry, rocky habitats. It is often found in sagebrush steppe and coastal dunes.

This variety is notable for its compact size and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
