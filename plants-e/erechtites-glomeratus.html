Erechtites glomeratus

Erechtites glomeratus, commonly known as the clustered eupatorium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy inflorescences. The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
