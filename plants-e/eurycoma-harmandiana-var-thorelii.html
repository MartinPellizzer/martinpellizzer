Eurycoma harmandiana var. thorelii

Eurycoma harmandiana var. thorelii, commonly known as the Bornean wild quinine tree, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in lowland and hill forest areas. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.
