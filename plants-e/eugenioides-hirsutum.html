Eugenioides hirsutum

Eugenioides hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy eugenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its hairy foliage and fragrant, white to pinkish flowers. Eugenioides hirsutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
