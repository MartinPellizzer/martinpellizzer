Exochorda grandiflora var. alberti

Exochorda grandiflora var. alberti, commonly known as the Alberta honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
