Erangelia

Erangelia, commonly known as the "silver leaf plant," is a unique species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-white leaves that reflect sunlight, helping it thrive in harsh, sunny environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pale blue and lavender, which bloom during the cooler months. Its roots are deep and fibrous, allowing it to access moisture from underground water sources.

Due to its medicinal properties, Erangelia is also valued in traditional herbal remedies.
