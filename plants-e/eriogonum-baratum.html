Eriogonum baratum

Eriogonum baratum, commonly known as the San Joaquin bursage, is a flowering plant native to California and Nevada.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, clustered flowers. Eriogonum baratum is often found in chaparral and scrubland ecosystems.
