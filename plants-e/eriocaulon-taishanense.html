Eriocaulon taishanense

Eriocaulon taishanense, commonly known as the Taishan spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Taishan region, and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and wetlands. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale green flowers. It plays an important ecological role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
