Eragrostis mauritii

Eragrostis mauritii, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Eragrostis mauritii is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
