Epilobium roseum var. anagallidifolium

Epilobium roseum var. anagallidifolium, commonly known as the slender willow herb, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems that often grow in moist, disturbed soils. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive willow-like appearance. Its flowers are pink to reddish-purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is closely related to other Epilobium species and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.
