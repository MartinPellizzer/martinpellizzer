Escallonia illinita var. stenopetala

Escallonia illinita var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant is notable for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Escallonia illinita var. stenopetala is also valued for its resistance to pests and diseases.
