Eupatorium enixum

Eupatorium enixum, commonly known as the slender boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open woods and along stream banks, preferring partial to full sunlight. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its small, white to pale blue flowers are clustered in flat-topped umbels, blooming in late summer.

Eupatorium enixum is part of the Asteraceae family and is known for its aromatic, slightly medicinal properties.
