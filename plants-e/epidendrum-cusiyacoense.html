Epidendrum cusiyacoense

Epidendrum cusiyacoense, commonly known as the Cusiyaco Orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
