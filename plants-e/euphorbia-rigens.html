Euphorbia rigens

Euphorbia rigens, commonly known as the upright spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem covered in small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Euphorbia rigens is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
