Elatostema filicoides var. eufilicoides

Elatostema filicoides var. eufilicoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spikes, and it is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
