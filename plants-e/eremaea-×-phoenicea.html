Eremaea × phoenicea

Eremaea × phoenicea, commonly known as the coastal spinifex.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of southern Australia. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

Eremaea × phoenicea is well adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal dunes and salt-affected areas.
