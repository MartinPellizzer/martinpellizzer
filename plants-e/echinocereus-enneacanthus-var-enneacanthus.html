Echinocereus enneacanthus var. enneacanthus

Echinocereus enneacanthus var. enneacanthus, commonly known as the Arizona hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a tall, spiny stem and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its spines provide protection from herbivores and help reduce water loss through transpiration.
