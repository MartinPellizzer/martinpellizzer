Eugenia dicrana

Eugenia dicrana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia dicrana is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
