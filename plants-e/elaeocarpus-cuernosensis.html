Elaeocarpus cuernosensis

Elaeocarpus cuernosensis, commonly known as the horned elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like projections on its leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface.

Elaeocarpus cuernosensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
