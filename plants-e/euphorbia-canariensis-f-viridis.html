Euphorbia canariensis f. viridis

Euphorbia canariensis f. viridis, commonly known as the Green Canary Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, fleshy stems that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are the modified flower clusters characteristic of the Euphorbia genus. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
