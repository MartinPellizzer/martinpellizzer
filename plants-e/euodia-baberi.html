Euodia baberi

Euodia baberi, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark brown bark that exudes a resin when cut. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Euodia baberi is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
