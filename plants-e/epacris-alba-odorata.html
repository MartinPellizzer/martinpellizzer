Epacris alba-odorata

Epacris alba-odorata, commonly known as the white-fragrant epacris, is a flowering plant native to the eastern regions of Australia.

Epacris alba-odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
