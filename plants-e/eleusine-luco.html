Eleusine luco

Eleusine luco, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along waterways.

Botanically, it is valued for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
