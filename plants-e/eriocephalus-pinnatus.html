Eriocephalus pinnatus

Eriocephalus pinnatus, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are pinnately arranged, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
