Ekmania

Ekmania, commonly known as the African orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. They typically grow in woodland areas and are adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Ekmania species are herbaceous perennials, often featuring strap-like leaves and a compact growth habit.

Their unique floral structures make them popular among botanists and horticultural enthusiasts.
