Euphorbia × figertii

Euphorbia × figertii, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow or orange, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Euphorbia × figertii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
