Erythrina haerdii

Erythrina haerdii, commonly known as the flame tree or the Australian flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, making it a visually striking plant. The plant has trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being elliptic to ovate in shape. Its bark is smooth and greyish, often peeling in thin layers.

Erythrina haerdii is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, and it is commonly used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
