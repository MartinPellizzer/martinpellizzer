Epidendrum lamprochilum

Epidendrum lamprochilum, commonly known as the silver-lipped epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy, silver-gray coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, showy lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum lamprochilum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor orchid enthusiasts.
