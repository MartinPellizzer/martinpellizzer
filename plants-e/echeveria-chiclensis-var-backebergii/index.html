Echeveria Chiclensis Var. Backebergii

Echeveria chiclensis var. backebergii is a striking succulent native to Mexico known for its vibrant rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves. This variety is prized by collectors for its unique coloration and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its ability to bloom with showy pink flowers adds to its ornamental appeal. With proper care, this plant can become a stunning centerpiece in any succulent collection.
