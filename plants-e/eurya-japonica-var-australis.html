Eurya japonica var. australis

Eurya japonica var. australis, commonly known as the Australian eurya, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, glossy green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. These flowers are followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for birds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and woodland habitats.
