Epidendrum morganii

Epidendrum morganii, commonly known as the Morgan's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored blooms that range from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum morganii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
