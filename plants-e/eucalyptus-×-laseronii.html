Eucalyptus × laseronii

Eucalyptus × laseronii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf malleecush, is a hybrid eucalyptus species native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a compact growth habit and often forms dense thickets. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
