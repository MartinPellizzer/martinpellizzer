Euphorbia squamosa var. wilhelmsiana

Euphorbia squamosa var. wilhelmsiana, commonly known as the Wilhelmsian spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a caudex, which is a swollen stem base, and produces spiny, segmented stems that are covered in small, scale-like structures. The plant has yellow flowers that appear in clusters, and it is adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
