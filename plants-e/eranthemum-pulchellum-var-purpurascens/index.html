Eranthemum Pulchellum Var. Purpurascens

Eranthemum pulchellum var. purpurascens is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, showy blooms, this variety displays a unique blend of colors that range from deep red to soft pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it ideal for both garden beds and container gardening. Its popularity has grown due to its ease of care and long-lasting floral display.
