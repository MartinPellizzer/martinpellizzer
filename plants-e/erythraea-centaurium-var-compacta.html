Erythraea centaurium var. compacta

Erythraea centaurium var. compacta, commonly known as the compact centaury, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, blue to violet flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a reddish hue. It thrives in dry, sunny grasslands and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its compact growth form makes it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural habitats.
