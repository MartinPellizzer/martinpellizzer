Elaphoglossum peltatum f. flabellatum

Elaphoglossum peltatum f. flabellatum, commonly known as the flabellate elaphoglossum, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical regions of the Americas. The fronds are typically leathery and have a distinctive, fan-like appearance in the mature stages. This fern thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
