Eragrostis maypurensis var. densiuscula

Eragrostis maypurensis var. densiuscula, commonly known as the dense dwarf panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, tufted foliage, which helps it thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It has fibrous roots that aid in soil stabilization and water retention.

This variety is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it contributes to ecological diversity and provides habitat for small fauna.
