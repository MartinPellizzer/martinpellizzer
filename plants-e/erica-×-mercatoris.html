Erica × mercatoris

Erica × mercatoris, commonly known as the hybrid heather, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica × mercatoris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form compact, attractive mounds.

It is also valued for its resilience and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
