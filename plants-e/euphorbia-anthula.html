Euphorbia anthula

Euphorbia anthula, commonly known as the silver euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branching structure with thick, fleshy stems covered in a whitish, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers surrounded by green bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
