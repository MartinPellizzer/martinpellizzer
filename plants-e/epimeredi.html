Epimeredi

Epimeredi, commonly known as the "ghost plant," is a rare and elusive species of liverwort.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that appear to be almost invisible against certain backgrounds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique morphology and low visibility make it difficult to study in the wild.
