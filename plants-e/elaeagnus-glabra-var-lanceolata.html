Elaeagnus glabra var. lanceolata

Elaeagnus glabra var. lanceolata, commonly known as silverthorn, is a shrub native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its rapid growth and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental and hedging purposes.
