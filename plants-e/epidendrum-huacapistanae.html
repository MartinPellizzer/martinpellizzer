Epidendrum huacapistanae

Epidendrum huacapistanae, commonly known as the Huacapistanae Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Peru.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in cloud forests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen foliage.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
