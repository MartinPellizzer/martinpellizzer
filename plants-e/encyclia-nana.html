Encyclia nana

Encyclia nana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often resembling miniature orchids. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded conditions.

Encyclia nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled environment.
