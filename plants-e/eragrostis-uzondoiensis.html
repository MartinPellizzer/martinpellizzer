Eragrostis uzondoiensis

Eragrostis uzondoiensis, commonly known as the Uzondoi grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa, particularly Nigeria.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets.

Botanically, it is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its potential use in ecological restoration projects.
