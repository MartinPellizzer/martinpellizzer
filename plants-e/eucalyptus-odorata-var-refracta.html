Eucalyptus odorata var. refracta

Eucalyptus odorata var. refracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a variant of the eucalyptus species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often grey-green in color. The plant produces small, globular flower buds that range in color from white to pale yellow. It is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variant is valued for its aromatic oils and is often used in traditional and modern applications.
