Erodium manescavi var. luxurians

Erodium manescavi var. luxurians, commonly known as the purplestem stickyburr, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, sticky, bristly fruits that aid in seed dispersal. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
