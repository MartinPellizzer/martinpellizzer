Epidendrum montisillinicense

Epidendrum montisillinicense, commonly known as the Sierra de las Minas Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees in humid, tropical environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates.

Epidendrum montisillinicense is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
