Eleocharis uniglumis subsp. uniglumis

Eleocharis uniglumis subsp.

uniglumis, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence composed of small, spike-like clusters of flowers.

The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, including marshes, swamps, and the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

It plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and stabilizing soil.
