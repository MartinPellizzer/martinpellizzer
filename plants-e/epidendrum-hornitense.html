Epidendrum hornitense

Epidendrum hornitense, commonly known as the Horned Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with bright indirect light.

Its unique appearance and blooming habits make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
