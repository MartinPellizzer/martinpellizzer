Eugenia vallsiana

Eugenia vallsiana, commonly known as the Vallsian evergreen, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
