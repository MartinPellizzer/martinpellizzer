Epidendrum kautskyi

Epidendrum kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum kautskyi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
