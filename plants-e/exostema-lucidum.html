Exostema lucidum

Exostema lucidum, commonly known as the shining leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
