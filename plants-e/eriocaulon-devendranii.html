Eriocaulon devendranii

Eriocaulon devendranii, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, grass-like appearance with slender, erect stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clusters at the base.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphological features.
