Evolvulus linifolius var. linearis

Evolvulus linifolius var. linearis, commonly known as the slender blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its flowers have a short blooming period, typically lasting only a few days.
