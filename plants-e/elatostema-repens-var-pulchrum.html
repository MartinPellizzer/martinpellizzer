Elatostema repens var. pulchrum

Elatostema repens var. pulchrum, commonly known as the silverleaf elatostema, is a low-growing perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically appearing in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
