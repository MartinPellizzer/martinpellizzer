Endymion

Endymion, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night and close by dawn. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with woody stems and dark green, ovate leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
