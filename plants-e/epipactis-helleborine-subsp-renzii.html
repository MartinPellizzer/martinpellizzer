Epipactis helleborine subsp. renzii

Epipactis helleborine subsp.

renzii, commonly known as the alpine helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae. It is characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip.

This subspecies is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, growing in rocky or grassy habitats at high elevations. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces small, rounded pseudobulbs.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas of Europe and parts of Asia.
