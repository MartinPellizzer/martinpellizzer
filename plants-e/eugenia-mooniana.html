Eugenia mooniana

Eugenia mooniana, commonly known as the moonflower eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its pale flowers.

Eugenia mooniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties in horticulture.
