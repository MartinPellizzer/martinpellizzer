Eribroma oblonga

Eribroma oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eribroma, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are elongated, narrow, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Eribroma oblonga is typically found in grassy areas and along the edges of woodlands.
