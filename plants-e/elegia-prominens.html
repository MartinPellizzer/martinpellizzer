Elegia prominens

Elegia prominens, commonly known as the common water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and ditches. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often floating or partially submerged. Its leaves are small, oval, and arranged alternately along the stem.

It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.
