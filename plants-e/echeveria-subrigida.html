Echeveria subrigida

Echeveria subrigida, commonly known as the silver echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
