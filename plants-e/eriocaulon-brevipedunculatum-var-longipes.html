Eriocaulon brevipedunculatum var. longipes

Eriocaulon brevipedunculatum var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed marsh grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
