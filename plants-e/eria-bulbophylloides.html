Eria bulbophylloides

Eria bulbophylloides, commonly known as the bulbophyll orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually fleshy and adapted to store water, reflecting its preference for humid, shaded environments.

Eria bulbophylloides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
