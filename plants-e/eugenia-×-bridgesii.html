Eugenia × bridgesii

Eugenia × bridgesii, commonly known as the Java apple or Java pineapple.

It is a hybrid shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round, and have a sweet, acidic flavor, often used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses in tropical regions.
