Epidendrum filicaule var. itatiayae

Epidendrum filicaule var. itatiayae, commonly known as the Itatiaya Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces pendulous flower spikes with numerous small, fragrant blossoms in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
