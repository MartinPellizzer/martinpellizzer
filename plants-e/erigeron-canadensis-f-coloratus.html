Erigeron canadensis f. coloratus

Erigeron canadensis f. coloratus, commonly known as the showy fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
