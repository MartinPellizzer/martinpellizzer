Eragrostis uvida

Eragrostis uvida, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its drooping, silver-gray foliage and slender, wiry stems that create a distinctive weeping appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and have a slightly hairy texture, while the inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green flowers.

Eragrostis uvida is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its unique visual appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
