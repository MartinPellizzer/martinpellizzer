Epilobium angustifolium f. leucanthum

Epilobium angustifolium f. leucanthum, commonly known as the white-flowered willowherb, is a flowering plant in the evening primrose family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as stream banks and meadows. It is a herbaceous perennial with a sprawling growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
