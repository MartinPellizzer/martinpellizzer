Euphorbia oaxacana

Euphorbia oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca spurge, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a succulent shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and leaves, adapted to store water. Its flowers are small and clustered in a structure called a cyathium, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
