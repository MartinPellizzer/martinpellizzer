Epidendrum saximontanum

Epidendrum saximontanum, commonly known as the Sierra Madre Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, often epiphytic on trees or stones. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive purple or red lip, and it blooms during the summer months.

Epidendrum saximontanum is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
