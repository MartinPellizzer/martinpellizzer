Elatostema filicoides var. vitiense

Elatostema filicoides var. vitiense, commonly known as the Vitiense Elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has slender, often prostrate stems that produce small, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female inflorescences, and it relies on wind pollination.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
