Eriocaulon pallidum

Eriocaulon pallidum, commonly known as the pale spikerush, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, characterized by its grass-like appearance and specialized adaptations for aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale flowers.
