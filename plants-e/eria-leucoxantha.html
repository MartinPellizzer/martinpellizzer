Eria leucoxantha

Eria leucoxantha, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrate for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical gardens.
