Erythrina latissima

Erythrina latissima, commonly known as the broadleaf coral tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant red flowers and feathery foliage.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are clustered in dense, raceme-like inflorescences. Erythrina latissima is known for its rapid growth and ability to produce a profusion of showy blooms throughout the growing season.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical areas.
