Echinopsis fiebrigii

Echinopsis fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is a solitary, globular plant with a short, thick stem and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces large, showy white or pink flowers during the cooler months. Its leaves are typically absent, and it is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

Echinopsis fiebrigii is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
