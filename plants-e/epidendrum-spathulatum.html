Epidendrum spathulatum

Epidendrum spathulatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and greenhouses.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
