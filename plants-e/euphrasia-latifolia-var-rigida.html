Euphrasia latifolia var. rigida

Euphrasia latifolia var. rigida, commonly known as rigid leaf eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically broad and stiff, giving it the "rigida" variety name.
