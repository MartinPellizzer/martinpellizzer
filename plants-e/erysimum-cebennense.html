Erysimum cebennense

Erysimum cebennense, commonly known as the Cebennense wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It produces bright yellow flowers with four petals, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found growing in rocky or disturbed soils. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in full sun.

Erysimum cebennense is also known for its tolerance to poor soil conditions and drought.
