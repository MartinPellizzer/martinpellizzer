Echinodorus grandiflorus var. longibracteatus

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. longibracteatus, commonly known as the broadleaf waterweed, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically bright green and slightly serrated along the edges. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with small, white to pale purple flowers. It thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters with moderate to high light levels.

This variety is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
