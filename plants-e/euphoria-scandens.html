Euphoria scandens

Euphoria scandens, commonly known as the climbing euphorbia, is a perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and distinctive, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it contains toxic sap and should be handled with care.
