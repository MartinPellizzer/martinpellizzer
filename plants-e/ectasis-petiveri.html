Ectasis petiveri

Ectasis petiveri, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its growth habit is herbaceous, with a tendency to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Ectasis petiveri is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
