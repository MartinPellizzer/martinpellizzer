Erythrodes foliosa

Erythrodes foliosa, commonly known as the red-leafed foliosa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage, which contrasts with its smaller, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Erythrodes foliosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant appearance.
