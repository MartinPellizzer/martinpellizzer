Epidendrum plagiophyllum

Epidendrum plagiophyllum, commonly known as the silver sword orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to silver hairs. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum plagiophyllum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
