Eulophia rueppelii

Eulophia rueppelii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Eulophia rueppelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
