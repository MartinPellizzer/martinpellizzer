Elaphoglossum membranaceum var. columbiensis

Elaphoglossum membranaceum var. columbiensis, commonly known as the Columbia membranous holly fern, is a fern species native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky stream banks.
