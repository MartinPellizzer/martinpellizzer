Epipactis pallens

Epipactis pallens, commonly known as the pale helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a showy, pale green to yellowish flower spike. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are lipless and have a distinct, spurred structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Epipactis pallens is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in association with other orchid species.
