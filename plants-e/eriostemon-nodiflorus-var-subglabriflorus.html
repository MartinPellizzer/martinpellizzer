Eriostemon nodiflorus var. subglabriflorus

Eriostemon nodiflorus var. subglabriflorus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers.
