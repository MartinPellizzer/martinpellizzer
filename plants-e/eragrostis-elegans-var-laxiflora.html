Eragrostis elegans var. laxiflora

Eragrostis elegans var. laxiflora, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers, which are arranged in a loose, open cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
