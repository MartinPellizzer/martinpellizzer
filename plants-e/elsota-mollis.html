Elsota mollis

Elsota mollis, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Elsota mollis is known for its rapid growth and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
