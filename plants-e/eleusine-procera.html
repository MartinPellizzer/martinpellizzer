Eleusine procera

Eleusine procera, commonly known as love grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that gives it a distinctive appearance.

Eleusine procera is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
