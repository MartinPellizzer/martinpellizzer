Euterpe trichoclada

Euterpe trichoclada, commonly known as the pink-barked palm, is a species native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, erect trunk covered in a distinctive pinkish-brown bark. The plant produces large, feather-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its inflorescences are typically branched and bear small, yellowish flowers.

Euterpe trichoclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
