Ehretia breviflora

Ehretia breviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring and summer. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Ehretia breviflora is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as mountain forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
