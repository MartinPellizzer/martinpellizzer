Euphorbia splendens var. andranovelonana

Euphorbia splendens var. andranovelonana, commonly known as the Madagascar spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
