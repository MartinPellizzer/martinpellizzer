Eria tomohonensis

Eria tomohonensis, commonly known as the Tomohon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in montane forest areas. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, with intricate patterns on the petals.

Eria tomohonensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
