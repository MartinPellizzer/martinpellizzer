Erythrodes nobilis

Erythrodes nobilis, commonly known as the noble red orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, emitting a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Erythrodes nobilis is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
