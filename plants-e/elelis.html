Elelis

Elelis, commonly known as the "tropical orchid," is a member of the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers.

It typically features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Elelis is prized for its ornamental value, with flowers that come in a variety of colors including white, pink, and yellow.

It is often cultivated in gardens or as a potted plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
