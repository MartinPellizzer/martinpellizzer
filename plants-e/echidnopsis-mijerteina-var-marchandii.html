Echidnopsis mijerteina var. marchandii

Echidnopsis mijerteina var. marchandii, commonly known as the Marchand's rock agave, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh environments, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
