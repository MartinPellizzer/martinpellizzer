Eriophyllum nudicaule

Eriophyllum nudicaule, commonly known as the narrowleaf biscuitbrush, is a flowering plant native to California and parts of Oregon.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Eriophyllum nudicaule is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soils and support local pollinator populations.
