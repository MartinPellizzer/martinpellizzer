Emilia tenuicaulis

Emilia tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender emilia, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Emilia tenuicaulis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
