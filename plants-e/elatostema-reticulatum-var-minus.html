Elatostema reticulatum var. minus

Elatostema reticulatum var. minus, commonly known as the small-net elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern, giving it its scientific name. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
