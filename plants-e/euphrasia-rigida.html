Euphrasia rigida

Euphrasia rigida, commonly known as rigid eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that bear small, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, showy eyespots. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered with fine, downy hairs.

Euphrasia rigida is valued in traditional medicine for its potential healing properties, particularly for eye and respiratory ailments.
