Eria cymbidiifolia var. longipes

Eria cymbidiifolia var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
