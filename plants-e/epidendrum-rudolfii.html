Epidendrum rudolfii

Epidendrum rudolfii, commonly known as the Rudolf's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and elegant foliage.
