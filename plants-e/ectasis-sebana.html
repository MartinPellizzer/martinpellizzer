Ectasis sebana

Ectasis sebana, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, reducing water loss.

Ectasis sebana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to high temperatures and low rainfall.
