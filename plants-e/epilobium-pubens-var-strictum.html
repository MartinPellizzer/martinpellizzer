Epilobium pubens var. strictum

Epilobium pubens var. strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf willow herb, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pink to red, and clustered in dense spikes, blooming in early summer.

It is often found in wet meadows, stream banks, and other damp habitats.
