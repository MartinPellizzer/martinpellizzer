Ericameria microphylla

Ericameria microphylla, commonly known as narrowleaf erica.

It is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. This species is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellowish flowers. Ericameria microphylla typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native wildlife.
