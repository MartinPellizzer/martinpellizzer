Etlingera subviridis

Etlingera subviridis, commonly known as the green torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and striking orange-red inflorescences. Its flowers are often used in traditional ceremonies and as ornamental plants due to their vibrant color.
