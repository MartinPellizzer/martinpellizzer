Epimedium × citrinum

Epimedium × citrinum, commonly known as the golden beard.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Berberidaceae family and is native to China. It is characterized by its dense, bright yellow foliage that emerges in early spring. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in late spring, which are borne on short pedicels.

Epimedium × citrinum is well-suited for woodland gardens and thrives in partial shade with moist, well-drained soil.
