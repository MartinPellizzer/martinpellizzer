Eugenioides minus

Eugenioides minus, commonly known as the small eugenia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, aromatic scent.
