Epidendrum josianae

Epidendrum josianae, commonly known as the Josian's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has a sympodial growth habit, with new shoots arising from the base of the previous growth.

Epidendrum josianae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
