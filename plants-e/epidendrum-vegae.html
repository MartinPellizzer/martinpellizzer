Epidendrum vegae

Epidendrum vegae, commonly known as the Vege Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
