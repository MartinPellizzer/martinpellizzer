Eurycles rotundifolia

Eurycles rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaved eurycles, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its round, broad leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms, it is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
