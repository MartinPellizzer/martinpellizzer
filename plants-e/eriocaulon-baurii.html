Eriocaulon baurii

Eriocaulon baurii, commonly known as the Baur's spikegrass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which is characterized by its grass-like appearance and specialized floral structures. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments, often in areas with poor drainage. Eriocaulon baurii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
