Eupatorium blumenavii f. major

Eupatorium blumenavii f. major, commonly known as the giant eupatorium, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
