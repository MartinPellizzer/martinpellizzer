Eurya japonica var. acuminata

Eurya japonica var. acuminata, commonly known as the Japanese holly or acuminata eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green in color.
