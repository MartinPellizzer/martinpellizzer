Eriocalia minor

Eriocalia minor, commonly known as the lesser eriocalia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female structures, and are usually greenish-yellow in color.

Eriocalia minor is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora in temperate and subtropical regions.
