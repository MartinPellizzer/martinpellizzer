Euphorbia gorenflotii

Euphorbia gorenflotii, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, woody stem with a crown of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces striking yellow-orange cyathia, which are specialized floral structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
