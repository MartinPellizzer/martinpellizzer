Elatostema japonicum var. majus

Elatostema japonicum var. majus, commonly known as Japanese elatostema, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable subject for ecological and horticultural study.
