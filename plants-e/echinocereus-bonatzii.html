Echinocereus bonatzii

Echinocereus bonatzii, commonly known as the Bonatzii cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months, which are borne on the upper part of the stem. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

Echinocereus bonatzii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
