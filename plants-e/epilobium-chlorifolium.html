Epilobium chlorifolium

Epilobium chlorifolium, commonly known as the yellow willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant grows in moist, often shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Epilobium chlorifolium is often used in herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
