Equisetum × trachyodon var. continentale

Equisetum × trachyodon var. continentale, commonly known as the giant horsetail, is a perennial plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, jointed stems that are covered in small, scale-like structures. The plant produces spore-bearing cones at the top of its stems, which are typically found in moist, shaded environments. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and ecological benefits.
