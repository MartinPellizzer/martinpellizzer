Elaphoglossum feejeense

Elaphoglossum feejeense, commonly known as the Feejeen's holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green with a slight metallic sheen. Native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with short, creeping stems that produce both roots and new shoots.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
