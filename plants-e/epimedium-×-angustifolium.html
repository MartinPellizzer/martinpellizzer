Epimedium × angustifolium

Epimedium × angustifolium, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and its distinctive, trifoliate leaves that are typically dark green and leathery. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland settings.

Epimedium × angustifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
