Epipactis chinensis

Epipactis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese helleborine, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces inflorescences with showy, greenish-yellow flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive lip that is often darker in color and adorned with intricate patterns.

Epipactis chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
