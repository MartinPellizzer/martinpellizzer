Eriospermum sphaerophyllum

Eriospermum sphaerophyllum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, rounded leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spherical seed capsules that are covered in fine, hairy structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Eriospermum sphaerophyllum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
