Eulophia lindleyana

Eulophia lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has broad, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Eulophia lindleyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
