Excoecaria albicans var. venulosa

Excoecaria albicans var. venulosa, commonly known as the white-veined croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, variegated foliage.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
