Erigeron myosotis subsp. skottsbergii

Erigeron myosotis subsp.

skottsbergii, commonly known as the Skottsberg's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. Its blue to violet flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
