Ehretia taiwaniana

Ehretia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan ehretia, is a small evergreen tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, blue-black berries.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas of mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
