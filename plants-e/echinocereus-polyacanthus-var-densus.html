Echinocereus polyacanthus var. densus

Echinocereus polyacanthus var. densus, commonly known as the dense hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with dense, spiny areoles that give it its common name. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
