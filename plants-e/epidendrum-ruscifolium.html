Epidendrum ruscifolium

Epidendrum ruscifolium, commonly known as the sword-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum ruscifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
