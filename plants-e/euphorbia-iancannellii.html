Euphorbia iancannellii

Euphorbia iancannellii, commonly known as the Sicilian spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a thick, fleshy stem with spines and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The plant has a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Euphorbia iancannellii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
