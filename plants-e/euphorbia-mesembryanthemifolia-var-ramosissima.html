Euphorbia mesembryanthemifolia var. ramosissima

Euphorbia mesembryanthemifolia var. ramosissima, commonly known as the tree euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features a dense, branched stem that can grow up to several meters tall, with a woody base and fleshy, ribbed branches. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are usually small, scale-like, and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact and bushy appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
