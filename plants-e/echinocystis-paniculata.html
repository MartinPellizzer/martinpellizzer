Echinocystis paniculata

Echinocystis paniculata, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, spiny fruits. Its fruit contains edible seeds, though the fruit itself is often bitter.

Echinocystis paniculata is native to the Americas and is commonly found in disturbed habitats and along roadsides.
