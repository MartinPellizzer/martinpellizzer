Epidendrum clowesii

Epidendrum clowesii, commonly known as the Clowes' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features striking, long-lasting inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that contrast with its deep green foliage. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are often fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under proper care.

Epidendrum clowesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
