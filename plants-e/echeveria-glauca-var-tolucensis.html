Echeveria glauca var. tolucensis

Echeveria glauca var. tolucensis, commonly known as Tolucensis Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery blue or white coating, known as a bloom. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
