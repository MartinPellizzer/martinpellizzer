Eurya nitida var. siamensis

Eurya nitida var. siamensis, commonly known as the Siam evergreen or Siamese eurya, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
