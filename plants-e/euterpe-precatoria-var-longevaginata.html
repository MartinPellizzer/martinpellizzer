Euterpe precatoria var. longevaginata

Euterpe precatoria var. longevaginata, commonly known as the long sheath banana, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in taste to the common banana but are less widely cultivated.
