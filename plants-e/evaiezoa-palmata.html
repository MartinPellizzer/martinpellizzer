Evaiezoa palmata

Evaiezoa palmata, commonly known as the broadleaf palm, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can reach lengths of up to three meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red fruits containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
