Elatostema auriculatum

Elatostema auriculatum, commonly known as the ear-shaped elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive auriculate base, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, often appearing in spring or early summer.

Elatostema auriculatum is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.
