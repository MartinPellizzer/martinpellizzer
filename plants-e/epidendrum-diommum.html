Epidendrum diommum

Epidendrum diommum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with strap-like leaves that are usually bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments with good air circulation.

The roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the surrounding air and substrates.
