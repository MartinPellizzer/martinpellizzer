Eugenia barriei

Eugenia barriei, commonly known as the Barrie's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
