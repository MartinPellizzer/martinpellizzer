Eryngium floribundum var. angustifolium

Eryngium floribundum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features gray-green, spiny leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces dense clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to native gardens.

Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a valuable plant for pollinators and landscape design.
