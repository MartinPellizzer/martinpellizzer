Erythranthe × smithii

Erythranthe × smithii, commonly known as the hybrid fireweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.
