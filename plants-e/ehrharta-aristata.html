Ehrharta aristata

Ehrharta aristata, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue to white flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Ehrharta aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in ecological restoration projects.
