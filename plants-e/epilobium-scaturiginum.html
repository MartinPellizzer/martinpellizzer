Epilobium scaturiginum

Epilobium scaturiginum, commonly known as the purple willow-herb, is a flowering plant in the Onagraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often disturbed soils, such as along stream banks and in wet meadows. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing nectar for pollinators.
