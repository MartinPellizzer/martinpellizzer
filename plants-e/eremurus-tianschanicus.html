Eremurus tianschanicus

Eremurus tianschanicus, commonly known as the Tianshan spearflower, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence that resembles a spear. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
