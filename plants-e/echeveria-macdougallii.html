Echeveria macdougallii

Echeveria macdougallii, commonly known as Macdougall's Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with purple at the edges. The plant produces clusters of pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
