Eulophia variopicta

Eulophia variopicta, commonly known as the variegated eulophia, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including pink, white, and yellow. Eulophia variopicta thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for both indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
