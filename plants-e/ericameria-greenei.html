Ericameria greenei

Ericameria greenei, commonly known as the Greene's ericoid, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine layer of white, fuzzy hairs.

Ericameria greenei is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
