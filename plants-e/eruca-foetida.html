Eruca foetida

Eruca foetida, commonly known as stinkcress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. It has a strong, unpleasant odor, which is a distinguishing feature.

Eruca foetida is an annual herb that is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and spread.
