Exacum quinquenervium subsp. linearifolium

Exacum quinquenervium subsp.

linearifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved African violet, is a species native to tropical Africa. It is a flowering plant characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers.

The leaves are elongated and linear, giving it a distinct appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This subspecies is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
