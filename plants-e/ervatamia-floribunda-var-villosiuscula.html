Ervatamia floribunda var. villosiuscula

Ervatamia floribunda var. villosiuscula, commonly known as the silky ervatamia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has silvery-gray, fuzzy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
