Eria cymbidiifolia var. pandanifolia

Eria cymbidiifolia var. pandanifolia, commonly known as the pandan-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, paddle-shaped leaves that resemble pandan leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
