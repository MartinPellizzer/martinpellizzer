Euphorbia gaubae var. velutina

Euphorbia gaubae var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique flower-like structures. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
