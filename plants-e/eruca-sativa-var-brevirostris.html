Eruca sativa var. brevirostris

Eruca sativa var. brevirostris, commonly known as the broad-leafed rocket, is a cultivated variety of the wild mustard plant.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy and have a peppery, mustard-like flavor. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in cool climates and is often grown as a leafy vegetable. It produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is valued for its culinary uses and is commonly found in gourmet and health food markets.
