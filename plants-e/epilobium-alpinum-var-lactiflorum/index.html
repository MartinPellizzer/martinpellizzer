Epilobium Alpinum Var. Lactiflorum

Epilobium alpinum var. lactiflorum is a striking alpine plant known for its vibrant pink flowers and hardy nature. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in mountainous regions across Europe and Asia. This variant of Epilobium alpinum is prized for its ornamental value and ecological importance. Its unique floral structure and ability to withstand harsh weather make it a resilient species. The plant plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
