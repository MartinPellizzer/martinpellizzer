Erodium cicutarium subsp. dunense

Erodium cicutarium subsp.

dunense, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bedstraw, is a low-growing annual herb native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has slender stems and narrow, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
