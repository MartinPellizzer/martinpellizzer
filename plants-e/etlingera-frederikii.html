Etlingera frederikii

Etlingera frederikii, commonly known as the white flame orchid, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, candle-like inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are long, narrow, and slightly fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
