Erythraea nuttallii

Erythraea nuttallii, commonly known as the redroot false nettle, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes plants commonly known as nettles. The plant is characterized by its reddish stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.
