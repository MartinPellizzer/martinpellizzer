Eurya obovata f. platyphylla

Eurya obovata f. platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf eurya, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are broader than those of the typical species, giving it its distinguishing feature. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage and berries make it an attractive addition to botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
