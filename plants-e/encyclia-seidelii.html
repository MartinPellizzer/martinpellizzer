Encyclia seidelii

Encyclia seidelii, commonly known as the Seidel's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a greenish-yellow hue and a prominent lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Encyclia seidelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
