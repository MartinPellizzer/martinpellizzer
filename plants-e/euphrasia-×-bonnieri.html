Euphrasia × bonnieri

Euphrasia × bonnieri, commonly known as the spotted gentian, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers with yellow spots, which are typically tubular in shape. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, where it thrives in moist, shaded habitats. Euphrasia × bonnieri is often found growing in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
