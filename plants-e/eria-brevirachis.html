Eria brevirachis

Eria brevirachis, commonly known as the short-stemmed eria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
