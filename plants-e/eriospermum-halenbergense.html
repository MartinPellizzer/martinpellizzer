Eriospermum halenbergense

Eriospermum halenbergense, commonly known as the Halenberg's eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Eriospermum halenbergense is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
