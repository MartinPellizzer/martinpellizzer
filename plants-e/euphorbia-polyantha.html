Euphorbia polyantha

Euphorbia polyantha, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, woody stem with dense clusters of small, spiny leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green花朵, which are actually modified structures called cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
