Excoecaria acerifolia var. cuspidata

Excoecaria acerifolia var. cuspidata, commonly known as the scarlet flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves and fruits contain toxic compounds, making it important to handle with care.
