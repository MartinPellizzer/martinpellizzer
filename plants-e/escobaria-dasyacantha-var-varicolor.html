Escobaria dasyacantha var. varicolor

Escobaria dasyacantha var. varicolor, commonly known as the variegated liveforever, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit. The plant features pale green to blue-gray foliage with striking variegation, often adorned with red or pinkish hues along the edges. Its flowers, which appear in spring, are typically yellow or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance.

This species is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves.
