Epiprinus

Epiprinus, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. They typically have thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in their native environment. Epiprinus species are often cultivated as ornamental plants due to their aesthetic appeal.

They thrive in well-draining soil and require indirect sunlight to flourish.
