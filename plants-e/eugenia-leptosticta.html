Eugenia leptosticta

Eugenia leptosticta, commonly known as the slender-streaked eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and are clustered in small inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a dark purple color, containing several seeds.
