Erigeron semibarbatus var. semibarbatus

Erigeron semibarbatus var. semibarbatus, commonly known as the semibarbatus fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
