Encyclia magdalenae

Encyclia magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalena orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a green throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia magdalenae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm climates.
