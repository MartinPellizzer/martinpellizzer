Epidendrum floribundum var. convexum

Epidendrum floribundum var. convexum, commonly known as the convex epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored blossoms, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
