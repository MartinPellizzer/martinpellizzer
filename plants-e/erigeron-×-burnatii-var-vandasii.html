Erigeron × burnatii var. vandasii

Erigeron × burnatii var. vandasii, commonly known as the Vandasii fleabane, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically range in color from white to pale blue or pink. The plant features slender stems and narrow, hairy leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Botanically, it is noted for its composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk florets.
