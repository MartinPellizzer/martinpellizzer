Epilobium minutiflorum

Epilobium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as along stream banks, roadsides, and wet meadows. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Epilobium minutiflorum is often found in wetland habitats and is adapted to thrive in environments with high moisture levels.
