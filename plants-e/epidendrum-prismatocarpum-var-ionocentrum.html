Epidendrum prismatocarpum var. ionocentrum

Epidendrum prismatocarpum var. ionocentrum, commonly known as the Ionocentrum Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a contrasting purple or red lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing multiple stems that can reach several feet in length. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a dense, velvety layer of brownish-green foliage.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral displays.
