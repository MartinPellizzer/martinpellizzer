Etlingera angustifolia

Etlingera angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often with a slightly serrated edge.
