Epilobium purpurascens

Epilobium purpurascens, commonly known as purple willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils, and is often found near water bodies such as streams and wet meadows. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and arranged in dense clusters, blooming in early summer.

Epilobium purpurascens is also known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
