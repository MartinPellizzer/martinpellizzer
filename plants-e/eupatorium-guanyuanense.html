Eupatorium guanyuanense

Eupatorium guanyuanense, commonly known as Guanyuan eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are usually opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Eupatorium guanyuanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
