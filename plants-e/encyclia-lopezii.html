Encyclia lopezii

Encyclia lopezii, commonly known as the Lopez's Encyclia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia lopezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
