Ermania lanuginosa

Ermania lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly ermania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique texture and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
