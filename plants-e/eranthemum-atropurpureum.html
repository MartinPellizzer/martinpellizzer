Eranthemum atropurpureum

Eranthemum atropurpureum, commonly known as purple flame geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant purple to red flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
