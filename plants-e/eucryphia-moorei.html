Eucryphia moorei

Eucryphia moorei, commonly known as the Moore's eucryphia, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are showy, with pink or red petals and a distinctive, waxy appearance.

Eucryphia moorei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
