Eriachne axillaris

Eriachne axillaris, commonly known as the slender grass or slender eriachne, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland areas. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often concealed within the leaf sheaths.

Eriachne axillaris is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
