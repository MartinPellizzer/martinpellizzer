Epidendrum boissierianum

Epidendrum boissierianum, commonly known as the Boissier's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
