Edraianthus wettsteinii subsp. wettsteinii

Edraianthus wettsteinii subsp.

wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's edraianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is a perennial species characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, tubular flowers.

The plant typically produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
