Epimedium sinense

Epimedium sinense, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive, often wavy margin.

Epimedium sinense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shady, woodland environments.
