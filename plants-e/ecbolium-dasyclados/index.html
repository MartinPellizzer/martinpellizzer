Ecbolium Dasyclados

Blue mist shrub, scientifically known as Ecbolium dasyclados, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of South America. Its name comes from the soft, bluish-gray foliage that gives it a mist-like appearance, especially in the early morning light. This hardy shrub is well adapted to dry climates and can thrive in poor soil conditions. It produces small, pale blue flowers that add a delicate touch to its overall appearance. Despite its beauty, the Blue mist shrub remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles, making it a rare and intriguing choice for gardeners.
