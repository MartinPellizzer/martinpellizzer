Epidendrum angustisegmentum

Epidendrum angustisegmentum, commonly known as the narrow-segmented epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking, pendulous flower spikes that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
