Eriocaulon similitepalum

Eriocaulon similitepalum, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. It typically grows in wet, swampy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Eriocaulon similitepalum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
