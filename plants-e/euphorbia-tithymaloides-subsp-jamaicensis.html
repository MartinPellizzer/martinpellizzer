Euphorbia tithymaloides subsp. jamaicensis

Euphorbia tithymaloides subsp.

jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to the Caribbean. It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow花朵 that are often clustered in cyathia, a unique floral structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
