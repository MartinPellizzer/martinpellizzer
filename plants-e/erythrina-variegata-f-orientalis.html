Erythrina variegata f. orientalis

Erythrina variegata f. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental coral tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow flowers that bloom in dense, racemose inflorescences. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval to lanceolate leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, while its wood is hard and durable.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
