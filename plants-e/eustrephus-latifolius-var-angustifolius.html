Eustrephus latifolius var. angustifolius

Eustrephus latifolius var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eustrephus, is a variant of the broadleaf eustrephus species.

It is characterized by its narrower, elongated leaves compared to the typical variety. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a slightly serrated edge.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule-like fruits containing seeds.
