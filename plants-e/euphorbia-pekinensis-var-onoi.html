Euphorbia pekinensis var. onoi

Euphorbia pekinensis var. onoi, commonly known as the Peking spurge or Ono spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a unique, globular inflorescence called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings.
