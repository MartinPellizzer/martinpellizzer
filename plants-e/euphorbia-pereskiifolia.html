Euphorbia pereskiifolia

Euphorbia pereskiifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with its silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green花朵, which are typical of the Euphorbia genus. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
