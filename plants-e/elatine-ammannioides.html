Elatine ammannioides

Elatine ammannioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf waterwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wetlands. The plant has slender, branching stems with opposite, linear leaves that are often less than 1 centimeter in length. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with four to six stamens and a single pistil.

Elatine ammannioides is often found in nutrient-rich waters and can be an indicator of clean, well-oxygenated water.
