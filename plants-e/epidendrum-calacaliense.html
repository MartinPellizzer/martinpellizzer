Epidendrum calacaliense

Epidendrum calacaliense, commonly known as the Calacali Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear numerous, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum calacaliense is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
