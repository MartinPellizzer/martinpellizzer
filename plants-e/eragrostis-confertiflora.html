Eragrostis confertiflora

Eragrostis confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are narrow with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences, which are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Eragrostis confertiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
