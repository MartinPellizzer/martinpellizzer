Euodia oligantha

Euodia oligantha, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

Euodia oligantha is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
