Echinopsis smrziana

Echinopsis smrziana, commonly known as the Smrzian ghost cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a spiny exterior, which helps protect it from herbivores and excessive water loss. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in succulent gardens and botanical collections.
