Erica totta

Erica totta, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Erica totta typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in coastal or mountainous regions.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
