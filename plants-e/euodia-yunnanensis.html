Euodia yunnanensis

Euodia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan euodia, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its wood is valued for its fragrance and durability, and it is also used in the preparation of medicinal compounds.

Euodia yunnanensis is typically found in subtropical regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.
