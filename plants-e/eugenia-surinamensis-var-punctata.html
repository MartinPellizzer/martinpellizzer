Eugenia surinamensis var. punctata

Eugenia surinamensis var. punctata, commonly known as the spotted eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Guyana. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinct white or pale spots. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink in color.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.
