Euodia floribunda

Euodia floribunda, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its aromatic properties make it valuable in both ornamental and medicinal contexts.

Euodia floribunda thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
