Epidendrum peperomia

Epidendrum peperomia, commonly known as the Peperomia Epidendrum, is a hybrid plant that combines characteristics of both Epidendrum and Peperomia species.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding ornamental value.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
