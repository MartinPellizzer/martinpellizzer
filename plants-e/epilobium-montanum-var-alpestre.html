Epilobium montanum var. alpestre

Epilobium montanum var. alpestre, commonly known as alpine willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to high-altitude regions of the Alps and other mountainous areas.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are pink to reddish, clustered in dense spikes, and bloom in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing mountainous soils.
