Euodia lyi

Euodia lyi, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Euodia lyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
