Erigeron biolettii

Erigeron biolettii, commonly known as the desert blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Its leaves are generally narrow and grayish-green, adapted to reduce water loss in harsh conditions.
