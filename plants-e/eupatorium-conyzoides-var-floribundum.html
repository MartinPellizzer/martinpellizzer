Eupatorium conyzoides var. floribundum

Eupatorium conyzoides var. floribundum, commonly known as the tropical blueweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers in dense clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
