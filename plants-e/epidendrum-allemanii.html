Epidendrum allemanii

Epidendrum allemanii, commonly known as the Orchid of the Amazon, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically has long, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum allemanii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
