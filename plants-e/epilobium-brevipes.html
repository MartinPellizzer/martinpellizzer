Epilobium brevipes

Epilobium brevipes, commonly known as narrowleaf willow herb, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pink to reddish-purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late summer.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in partial to full sunlight.
