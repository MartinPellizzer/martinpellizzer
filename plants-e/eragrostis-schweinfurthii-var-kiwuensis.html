Eragrostis schweinfurthii var. kiwuensis

Eragrostis schweinfurthii var. kiwuensis, commonly known as the East African lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is adapted to dry, open habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in savannas and grasslands, contributing to the ecological structure of these environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of local biodiversity and potential for ecological restoration.
