Epilobium antarcticum

Epilobium antarcticum, commonly known as Antarctic willow, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in cold, high-altitude environments. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink to purple flowers. It is adapted to harsh conditions, including strong winds and limited nutrient availability.

Epilobium antarcticum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife in its native range.
