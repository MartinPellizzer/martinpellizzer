Elatostema caudiculatum

Elatostema caudiculatum, commonly known as the cushion elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant has a compact, cushion-like growth habit with closely packed leaves. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Elatostema caudiculatum produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
