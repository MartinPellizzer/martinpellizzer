Erica krugeri

Erica krugeri, commonly known as the Kruger's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense foliage. Its flowers, typically pink or white, bloom in spring and are arranged in clusters.

Erica krugeri thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
