Estevesia

Estevesia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It is native to regions of South America and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain areas, impacting local ecosystems.
