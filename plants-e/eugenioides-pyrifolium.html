Eugenioides pyrifolium

Eugenioides pyrifolium, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in the spring. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Eugenioides pyrifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental tree due to its striking floral display.
