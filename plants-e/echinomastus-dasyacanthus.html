Echinomastus dasyacanthus

Echinomastus dasyacanthus, commonly known as the spiked echinomastus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the growing season, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its unique morphology and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
