Epipactis lucana

Epipactis lucana, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Epipactis lucana is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in association with other orchid species.
