Eulophia richardsiae

Eulophia richardsiae, commonly known as the Richards' eulophia, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged along a slender stem.

Eulophia richardsiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
