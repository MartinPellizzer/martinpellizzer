Eriocoma hendersonii

Eriocoma hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's eriocoma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
