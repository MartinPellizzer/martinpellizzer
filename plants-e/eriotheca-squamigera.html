Eriotheca squamigera

Eriotheca squamigera, commonly known as the silky tree, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
