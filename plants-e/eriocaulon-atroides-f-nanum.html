Eriocaulon atroides f. nanum

Eriocaulon atroides f. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf black rush, is a compact, herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its short, dark green leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its unique texture and adaptability to shaded environments.
