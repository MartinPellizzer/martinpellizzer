Eriosphaera salviifolia

Eriosphaera salviifolia, commonly known as the Salvia-leaved eriosphaera, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
