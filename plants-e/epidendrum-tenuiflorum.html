Epidendrum tenuiflorum

Epidendrum tenuiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flower orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Epidendrum tenuiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled environment.
