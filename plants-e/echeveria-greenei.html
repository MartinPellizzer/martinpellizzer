Echeveria greenei

Echeveria greenei, commonly known as the Greene's Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Echeveria greenei is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and easy care requirements.
