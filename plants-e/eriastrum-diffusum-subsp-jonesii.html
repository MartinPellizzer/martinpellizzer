Eriastrum diffusum subsp. jonesii

Eriastrum diffusum subsp.

jonesii, commonly known as the desert bidens, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a low-growing annual herb with small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has hairy, linear leaves and produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of desert ecosystems.
