Erucago

Erucago, commonly known as the erucoid plant, is a species characterized by its unique botanical features.

It typically grows as an herbaceous plant with elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Its seeds are encased in a hard, waxy coating, which aids in their dispersal.

Erucago is often found in moist, temperate environments and is valued for its medicinal and industrial applications.
