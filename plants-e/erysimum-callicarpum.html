Erysimum callicarpum

Erysimum callicarpum, commonly known as the showy honesty, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Erysimum callicarpum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
