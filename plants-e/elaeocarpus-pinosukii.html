Elaeocarpus pinosukii

Elaeocarpus pinosukii, commonly known as the Mindanao elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Mindanao. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 15 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin.

The flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, and the fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
