Escallonia angustifolia var. coquimbensis

Escallonia angustifolia var. coquimbensis, commonly known as the Coquimbo escallonia, is a shrub native to Chile.

It features narrow, evergreen leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in temperate regions.
