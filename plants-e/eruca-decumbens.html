Eruca decumbens

Eruca decumbens, commonly known as field rocket, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant has slender, sprawling stems that often lie close to the ground, with small, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, typical of the mustard family.

The plant is known for its peppery flavor and is sometimes used in culinary applications.
