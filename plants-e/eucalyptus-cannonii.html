Eucalyptus cannonii

Eucalyptus cannonii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grey-green foliage and smooth, flaky bark. The plant typically grows in heathland and sandplain environments, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, globular flower buds that open to reveal white or pale blue flowers.

Eucalyptus cannonii is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in areas with low rainfall.
