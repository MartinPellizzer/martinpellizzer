Euphrasia rotundifolia

Euphrasia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved eyebright, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are rounded and have a velvety texture, while its stems are erect and hairy.
