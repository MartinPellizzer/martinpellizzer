Euphrasia gibbsiae subsp. pulvinestris

Euphrasia gibbsiae subsp.

pulvinestris, commonly known as the pulvinate eyebright, is a small herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
