Echeveria argentinensis var. argentinensis

Echeveria argentinensis var. argentinensis, commonly known as the Argentinian rose, is a compact, succulent plant native to Argentina.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
