Eupatoriastrum

Eupatoriastrum, commonly known as the common eupatorium, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species that are native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. These plants are typically herbaceous, with opposite leaves and showy composite flower heads. They often thrive in moist environments and are known for their aromatic properties.
