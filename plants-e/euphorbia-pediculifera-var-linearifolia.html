Euphorbia pediculifera var. linearifolia

Euphorbia pediculifera var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in a waxy cuticle that helps reduce water loss. The leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged oppositely along the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized flower-like structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.
