Eurycaulis nieuwenhuisii

Eurycaulis nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the New Guinea daisy, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Eurycaulis nieuwenhuisii is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
