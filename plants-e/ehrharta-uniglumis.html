Ehrharta uniglumis

Ehrharta uniglumis, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
