Euryops multifidus var. namibensis

Euryops multifidus var. namibensis, commonly known as the Namibian starburry, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to semi-desert conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
