Erythremia aphylla

Erythremia aphylla, commonly known as the leafless erythranthe, is a unique member of the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, with only a few small, scale-like structures present at the base of the stem. Due to its specialized morphology, it is adapted to arid environments and is considered a rare and delicate species.
