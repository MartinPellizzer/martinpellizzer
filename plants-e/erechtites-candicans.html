Erechtites candicans

Erechtites candicans, commonly known as the white crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, branched stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive feature is the fluffy, white to pale yellow flower heads that resemble a crown, which bloom in late summer.
