Epilobium alpinum var. fontanum

Epilobium alpinum var. fontanum, commonly known as alpine willow herb, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
