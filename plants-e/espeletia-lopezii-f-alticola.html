Espeletia lopezii f. alticola

Espeletia lopezii f. alticola, commonly known as the cloud forest espeletia, is a unique plant species native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth form and dense, woolly foliage. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in cloud forests where it benefits from high humidity and partial shade. Its structure includes a central stem with tightly packed leaves that help retain moisture and protect against strong winds.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its mountainous habitat.
