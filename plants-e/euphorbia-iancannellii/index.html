Euphorbia Iancannellii

Euphorbia iancannellii is a rare and striking succulent native to a small region in southern Africa. Known for its unique, spiky appearance and vibrant green color, this plant is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens. Its small, clustered leaves and unusual flowers make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic, so caution is advised when handling it.
