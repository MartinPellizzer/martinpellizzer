Epidendrum chlorinum

Epidendrum chlorinum, commonly known as the green orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum chlorinum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
