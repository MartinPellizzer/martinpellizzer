Epidendrum alpestre

Epidendrum alpestre, commonly known as the alpine epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Epidendrum alpestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
