Echeveria lutea

Echeveria lutea, commonly known as the yellow echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or bluish tinge. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Echeveria lutea is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
