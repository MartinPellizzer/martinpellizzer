Eugenia strictissima

Eugenia strictissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. It produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia strictissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
