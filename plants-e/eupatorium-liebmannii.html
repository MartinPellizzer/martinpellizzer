Eupatorium liebmannii

Eupatorium liebmannii, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium liebmannii is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
