Etlingera pavieana subsp. pavieana

Etlingera pavieana subsp.

pavieana, commonly known as the Pale-veined Lady's Tresses, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale venation and relatively compact floral structure compared to other members of the genus.
