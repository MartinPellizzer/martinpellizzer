Euphorbia celastroides var. niuensis

Euphorbia celastroides var. niuensis, commonly known as the Niuensis Spurge, is a succulent shrub native to southern China.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
