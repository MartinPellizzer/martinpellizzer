Erica holtii

Erica holtii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre heath, is a flowering plant native to California and Baja California.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Erica holtii is adapted to dry conditions and is an important species in its native ecosystems.
