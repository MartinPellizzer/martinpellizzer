Eriope teucrioides

Eriope teucrioides, commonly known as the silverleaf germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture with a grayish-green hue. It produces small, yellowish flowers in terminal clusters during the summer months.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
