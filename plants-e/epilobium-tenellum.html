Epilobium tenellum

Epilobium tenellum, commonly known as slender willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often boggy habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Epilobium tenellum is often found in wet meadows, riverbanks, and other damp environments.
