Eminium regelii

Eminium regelii, commonly known as the Regel's eminium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of blue or purple. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Eminium regelii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
