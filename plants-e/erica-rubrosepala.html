Erica rubrosepala

Erica rubrosepala, commonly known as the red-sepal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, with showy sepals that are distinctly colored. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica rubrosepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
