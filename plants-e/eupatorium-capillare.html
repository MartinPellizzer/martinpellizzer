Eupatorium capillare

Eupatorium capillare, commonly known as slender boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Eupatorium capillare is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
