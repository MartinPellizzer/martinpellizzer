Eugenia sericiflora

Eugenia sericiflora, commonly known as the silky leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, silky layer of hair. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, reddish-purple berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
