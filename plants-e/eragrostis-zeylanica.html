Eragrostis zeylanica

Eragrostis zeylanica, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil stabilization and can be found in various parts of Asia and Africa.
