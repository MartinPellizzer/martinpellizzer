Erysimum salangense

Erysimum salangense, commonly known as the Salangena wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Erysimum salangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
