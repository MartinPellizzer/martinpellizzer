Euodia pilulifera

Euodia pilulifera, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that resemble pills, hence its common name. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Euodia pilulifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
