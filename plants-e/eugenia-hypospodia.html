Eugenia hypospodia

Eugenia hypospodia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
