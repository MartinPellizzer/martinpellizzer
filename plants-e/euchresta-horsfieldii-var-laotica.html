Euchresta horsfieldii var. laotica

Euchresta horsfieldii var. laotica, commonly known as the Laotian spurge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves, which are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
