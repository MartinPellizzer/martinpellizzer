Eremostachys speciosa var. brevicaulis

Eremostachys speciosa var. brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremostachys, is a perennial succulent native to arid regions of North America.

It features compact, fleshy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to rock gardens and arid landscapes.
