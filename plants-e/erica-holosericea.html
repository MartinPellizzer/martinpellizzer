Erica holosericea

Erica holosericea, commonly known as the silver leaf heath, is a species of shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with silvery-white undersides, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Erica holosericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
