Eugenia kalbreyeri

Eugenia kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

Eugenia kalbreyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
