Erythrina × neillii

Erythrina × neillii, commonly known as the hybrid flame tree, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its striking red or orange flowers.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is a hybrid between Erythrina indica and Erythrina lysistemon. The plant features compound leaves with three leaflets and produces large, showy inflorescences that bloom in summer.

It is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and ability to thrive in warm climates.
