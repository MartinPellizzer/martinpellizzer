Euphorbia sieboldiana f. idzuensis

Euphorbia sieboldiana f. idzuensis, commonly known as the Idzuensis spurge, is a rare and striking variant of the Euphorbia genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant, paddle-shaped leaves that often display a striking yellow or orange hue. This succulent plant is native to Japan, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners interested in unusual plants.

However, it is important to note that all parts of this plant contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
