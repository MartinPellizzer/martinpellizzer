Eragrostis cilianensis var. leersioides

Eragrostis cilianensis var. leersioides, commonly known as the Cilian grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
