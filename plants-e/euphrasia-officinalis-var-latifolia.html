Euphrasia officinalis var. latifolia

Euphrasia officinalis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.

The leaves and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported eye health benefits.
