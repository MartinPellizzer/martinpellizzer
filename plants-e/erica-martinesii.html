Erica martinesii

Erica martinesii, commonly known as the Martinesii heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Erica martinesii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to harsh, rocky environments.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
