Echinocephalum latifolium

Echinocephalum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf echinocephalum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It produces small yellow flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
