Epidendrum sphaerostachyum

Epidendrum sphaerostachyum, commonly known as the globe-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its robust, upright stems that often bear a dense covering of small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Epidendrum sphaerostachyum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
