Exacum Tenue

Exacum tenue is a delicate flowering plant known for its elegant clusters of small, star-shaped blooms. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. This evergreen herbaceous plant is popular among gardeners for its long blooming season and low maintenance requirements. Its vibrant flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple, adding a touch of color to any garden setting. Whether grown in containers or in the ground, Exacum tenue is a versatile choice for both novice and experienced horticulturists.
