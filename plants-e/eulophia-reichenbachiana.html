Eulophia reichenbachiana

Eulophia reichenbachiana, commonly known as the Reichenbach's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or near streams. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings, and it produces numerous small, fragrant blooms.

Eulophia reichenbachiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivation.
