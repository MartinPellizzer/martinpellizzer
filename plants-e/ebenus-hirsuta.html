It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers. Native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces yellowish or greenish flowers that bloom in early spring.
