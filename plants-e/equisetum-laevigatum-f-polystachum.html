Equisetum laevigatum f. polystachum

Equisetum laevigatum f. polystachum, commonly known as the smooth horsetail, is a variant of the common horsetail.

It is a perennial fern-like plant that belongs to the Equisetaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, jointed stems covered in small, scale-like structures. It typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils, often in shaded environments.

This fern is known for its ability to thrive in wetlands and along stream banks.
