Eria beccariana

Eria beccariana, commonly known as the Beccarian's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Eria beccariana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
