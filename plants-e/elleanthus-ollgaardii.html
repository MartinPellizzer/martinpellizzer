Elleanthus ollgaardii

Elleanthus ollgaardii, commonly known as the Ollgaard's lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, hairless blades that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Elleanthus ollgaardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
