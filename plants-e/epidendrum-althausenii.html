Epidendrum althausenii

Epidendrum althausenii, commonly known as the Althausen's epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Epidendrum althausenii requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
