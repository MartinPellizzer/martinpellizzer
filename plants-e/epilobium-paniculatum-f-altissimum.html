Epilobium paniculatum f. altissimum

Epilobium paniculatum f. altissimum, commonly known as the tall willowherb, is a flowering plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers arranged in paniculate inflorescences, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

This species is often found in moist habitats such as meadows, streambanks, and wetlands.
