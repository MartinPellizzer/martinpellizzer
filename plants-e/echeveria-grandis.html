Echeveria grandis

Echeveria grandis, commonly known as the giant echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape, often with a bluish or grayish hue. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
