Eugenia grifensis

Eugenia grifensis, commonly known as the Grifens' eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, leathery leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
