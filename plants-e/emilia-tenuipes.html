Emilia tenuipes

Emilia tenuipes, commonly known as the slender emilia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Emilia tenuipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
