Elatostema latifolium var. acaule

Elatostema latifolium var. acaule, commonly known as the broad-leaved elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are broad and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. The varietal form acaule is distinguished by the absence of a distinct stem, with the leaves directly emerging from the base.
