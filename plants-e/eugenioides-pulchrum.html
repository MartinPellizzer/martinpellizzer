Eugenioides pulchrum

Eugenioides pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful eugenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenioides pulchrum is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine.
