Eria subaliena

Eria subaliena, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Eria subaliena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
