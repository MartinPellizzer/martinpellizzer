Evolvulus strictus

Evolvulus strictus, commonly known as the common blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing annual herb with trailing stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, showy blue or violet flowers that are typically tubular in shape. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Evolvulus strictus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
