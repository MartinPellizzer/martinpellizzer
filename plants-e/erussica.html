Erussica

Erussica, commonly known as the common sandthrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. Erussica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
