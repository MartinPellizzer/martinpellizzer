Epidendrum iltisorum

Epidendrum iltisorum, commonly known as the Iltisorum Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and blooms in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum iltisorum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
