Encyclia cordigera

Encyclia cordigera, commonly known as the heartleaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often heart-shaped, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with reddish-brown markings, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Encyclia cordigera is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.
