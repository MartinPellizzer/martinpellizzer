Eriocaulon plumbeum

Eriocaulon plumbeum, commonly known as the silver moss or blue moss, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is a small, mat-forming plant that typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it its characteristic appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale blue in color.

Eriocaulon plumbeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in bog gardens or as a ground cover in humid climates.
