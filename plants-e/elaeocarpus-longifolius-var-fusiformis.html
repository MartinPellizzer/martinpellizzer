Elaeocarpus longifolius var. fusiformis

Elaeocarpus longifolius var. fusiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
