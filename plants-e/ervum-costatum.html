Ervum costatum

Ervum costatum, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, bright yellow flowers that have five petals. The leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. The plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and gardens.

It is known for its rapid growth and ability to bloom quickly in response to environmental changes.
