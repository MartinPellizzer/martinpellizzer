Euphorbia monensis

Euphorbia monensis, commonly known as the Monensis Spurge, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It features a cylindrical stem with a smooth, greenish-gray bark and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has adapted to harsh environments by storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its leaves are typically narrow and may fall off during dry periods.

Euphorbia monensis is known for its resilience and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
