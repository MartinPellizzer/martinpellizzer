Ehretia cymosa var. zenkeri

Ehretia cymosa var. zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's ehretia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in cymes, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in secondary forests or along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and floral display.
