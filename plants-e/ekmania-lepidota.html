Ekmania lepidota

Ekmania lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody fruits containing elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
