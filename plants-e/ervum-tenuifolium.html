Ervum tenuifolium

Ervum tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It has been used historically in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
