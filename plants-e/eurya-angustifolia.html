Eurya angustifolia

Eurya angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and typically grows in forested areas. Eurya angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its evergreen foliage and seasonal blooms.
