Elateriospermum

Elateriospermum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette of silver-blue, fleshy leaves that form a dense, sword-like structure. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in spring, which are arranged in a composite head typical of the daisy family. Elateriospermum is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favored ornamental plant in arid regions.
