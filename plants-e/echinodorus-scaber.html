Echinodorus scaber

Echinodorus scaber, commonly known as the spiny sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are slightly serrated along the edges, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. It produces small, white flowers that rise above the water surface, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Echinodorus scaber is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for both beginners and experienced aquarists.
