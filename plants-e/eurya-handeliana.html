Eurya handeliana

Eurya handeliana, commonly known as the Chinese holly or red holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, adding to its ornamental value.
