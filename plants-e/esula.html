Esula

Esula, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture in its natural habitat. Esula thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
