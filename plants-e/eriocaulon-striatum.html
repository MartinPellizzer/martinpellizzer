Eriocaulon striatum

Eriocaulon striatum, commonly known as the stripe grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Eriocaulon striatum is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
