Episcia kohlerioides

Episcia kohlerioides, commonly known as the silver lace begonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
