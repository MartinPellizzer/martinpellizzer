Euodia schullei var. simplicifolia

Euodia schullei var. simplicifolia, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It features a dense, rounded crown and dark green, leathery leaves that are simple and elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, round fruits containing smooth, black seeds. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
