Epidendrum panegyricum

Epidendrum panegyricum, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Epidendrum panegyricum is prized by horticulturists for its beauty and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
