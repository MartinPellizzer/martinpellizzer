Eugenia longicuspis

Eugenia longicuspis, commonly known as the long-spined eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are glossy and elliptical in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale pink and are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
