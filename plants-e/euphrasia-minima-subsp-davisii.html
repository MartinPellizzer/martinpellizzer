Euphrasia minima subsp. davisii

Euphrasia minima subsp.

davisii, commonly known as Davis's lesser eyebright, is a small annual herb native to the western United States. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has delicate, hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers with two lips. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.
