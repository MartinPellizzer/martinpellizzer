Erica vlokii

Erica vlokii, commonly known as the Vlok's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom in summer. Erica vlokii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
