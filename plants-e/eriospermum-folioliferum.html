Eriospermum folioliferum

Eriospermum folioliferum, commonly known as the leafy eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are adapted to store water, reflecting its succulent nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Eriospermum folioliferum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
