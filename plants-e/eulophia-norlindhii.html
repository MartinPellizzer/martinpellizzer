Eulophia norlindhii

Eulophia norlindhii, commonly known as the Norlindh orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia norlindhii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
