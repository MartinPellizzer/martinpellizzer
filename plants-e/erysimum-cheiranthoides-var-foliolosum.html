Erysimum cheiranthoides var. foliolosum

Erysimum cheiranthoides var. foliolosum, commonly known as the leafy wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of pinnately lobed, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, four-petaled flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage and flowers contribute to its ornamental value in horticultural settings.
