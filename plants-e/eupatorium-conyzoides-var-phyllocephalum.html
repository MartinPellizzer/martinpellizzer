Eupatorium conyzoides var. phyllocephalum

Eupatorium conyzoides var. phyllocephalum, commonly known as the broadleaf eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture. This species is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
