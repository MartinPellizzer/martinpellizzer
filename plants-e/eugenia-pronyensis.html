Eugenia pronyensis

Eugenia pronyensis, commonly known as the Prony's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland forests.

Eugenia pronyensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
