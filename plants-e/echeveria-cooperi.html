Echeveria cooperi

Echeveria cooperi, commonly known as 'Red Flash', is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with red and purple margins. The plant produces showy, violet-pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
