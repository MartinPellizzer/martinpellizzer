Epidendrum sanchoi var. exasperatum

Epidendrum sanchoi var. exasperatum, commonly known as the Sancho's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent moisture in its growing medium.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
