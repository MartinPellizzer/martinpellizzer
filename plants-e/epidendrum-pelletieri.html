Epidendrum pelletieri

Epidendrum pelletieri, commonly known as the Pelletier's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum pelletieri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
