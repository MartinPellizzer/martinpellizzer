Echium stenosiphon subsp. lindbergii

Echium stenosiphon subsp.

lindbergii, commonly known as the narrow-sheath echium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
