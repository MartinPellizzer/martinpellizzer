Eulophia taiwanensis

Eulophia taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs.

Eulophia taiwanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
