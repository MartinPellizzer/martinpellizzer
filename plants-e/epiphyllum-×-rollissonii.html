Epiphyllum × rollissonii

Epiphyllum × rollissonii, commonly known as the moon cactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are often segmented and covered in soft, spiny areoles. The plant produces large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink, yellow, and even red, typically blooming at night. Its botanical name reflects its hybrid origin, combining traits from Epiphyllum and other related species.

Despite its delicate appearance, it is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
