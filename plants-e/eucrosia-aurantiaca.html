Eucrosia aurantiaca

Eucrosia aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden crocus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Eucrosia in the family Colchicaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble those of the true crocus.
