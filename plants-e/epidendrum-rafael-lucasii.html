Epidendrum rafael-lucasii

Epidendrum rafael-lucasii, commonly known as the Rafael-Lucas Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, featuring a pseudobulb-like structure that supports its growth. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
