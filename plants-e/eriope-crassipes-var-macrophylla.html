Eriope crassipes var. macrophylla

Eriope crassipes var. macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaved eriope, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It is often found in coastal dunes and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The varietal name 'macrophylla' refers to its broad, large leaves, which distinguish it from other varieties of Eriope.
