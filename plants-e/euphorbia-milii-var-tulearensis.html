Euphorbia milii var. tulearensis

Euphorbia milii var. tulearensis, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments. Its leaves are typically thick and waxy, adapted to reduce water loss.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
