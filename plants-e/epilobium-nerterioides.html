Epilobium nerterioides

Epilobium nerterioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features slender stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pinkish to white, and arranged in clusters, blooming in the summer months.

Epilobium nerterioides is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, high-altitude conditions.
