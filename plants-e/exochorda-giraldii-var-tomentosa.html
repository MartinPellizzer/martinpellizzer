Exochorda giraldii var. tomentosa

Exochorda giraldii var. tomentosa, commonly known as the silver-leafed spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded form and clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that provides ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental hedges and borders.
