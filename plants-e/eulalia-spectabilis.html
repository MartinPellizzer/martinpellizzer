Eulalia spectabilis

Eulalia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, with long, narrow leaves that are often silver-gray in color. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes that are highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, and is often used in landscape design for its visual appeal.

Eulalia spectabilis is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in natural habitats.
