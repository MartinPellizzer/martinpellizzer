Elaeagnus isensis

Elaeagnus isensis, commonly known as the Chinese silverberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-green leaves and small, edible berries that ripen in the fall. The plant has a distinctive, aromatic foliage and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Elaeagnus isensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
