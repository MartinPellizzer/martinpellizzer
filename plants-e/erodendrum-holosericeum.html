Erodendrum holosericeum

Erodendrum holosericeum, commonly known as the silky erodendrum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

Erodendrum holosericeum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry conditions.
