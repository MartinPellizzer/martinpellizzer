Eria latiuscula

Eria latiuscula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically clustered along its stems. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often in clusters. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
