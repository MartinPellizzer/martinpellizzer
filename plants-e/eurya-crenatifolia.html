Eurya crenatifolia

Eurya crenatifolia, commonly known as the serrated leaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as India, China, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with wavy, serrated edges. It produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically borne in the axils of the leaves.

The fruit is a small, capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
