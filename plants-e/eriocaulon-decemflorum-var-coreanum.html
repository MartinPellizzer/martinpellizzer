Eriocaulon decemflorum var. coreanum

Eriocaulon decemflorum var. coreanum, commonly known as the Korean star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white flowers during the summer months. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in freshwater habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
