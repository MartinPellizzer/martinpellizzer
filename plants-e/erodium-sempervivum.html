Erodium sempervivum

Erodium sempervivum, commonly known as common stormweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink or purple and appear in clusters, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

Erodium sempervivum is adapted to a wide range of soil types and can thrive in disturbed or sunny environments.
