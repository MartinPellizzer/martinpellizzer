Esenbeckia stephani

Esenbeckia stephani, commonly known as the Brazilian star-of-Bahia, is a shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has aromatic leaves with a distinct, spicy scent, which is often used in traditional medicine. Its woody stems are covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Esenbeckia stephani is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
