Epidendrum johnstonii

Epidendrum johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that are typically orange or red in color, often with contrasting yellow or green markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum johnstonii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
