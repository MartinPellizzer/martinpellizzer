Eriocaulon laniceps

Eriocaulon laniceps, commonly known as the woolly spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas. The leaves are usually linear and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a soft appearance.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
