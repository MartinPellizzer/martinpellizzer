Euphoria danura

Euphoria danura, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant yellow and orange petals that resemble flames, making it a visually captivating species. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and rare occurrence make it a sought-after specimen among botanists and plant enthusiasts.
