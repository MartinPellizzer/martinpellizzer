Eugenia scalariformis

Eugenia scalariformis, commonly known as the scalloped eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that have a distinctive scalloped edge. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to reddish when mature.
