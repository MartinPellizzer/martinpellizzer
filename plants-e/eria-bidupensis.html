Eria bidupensis

Eria bidupensis, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces striking purple and pink flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria bidupensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
