Elytrigia scythica

Elytrigia scythica, commonly known as Scythian grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing minute flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.

Elytrigia scythica is often found in disturbed or marginal environments, showing adaptability to various soil conditions.
