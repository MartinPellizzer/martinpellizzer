Eupatorium reevesii

Eupatorium reevesii, commonly known as Chinese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Eupatorium reevesii is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
