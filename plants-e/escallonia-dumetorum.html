Escallonia dumetorum

Escallonia dumetorum, commonly known as the Chilean firebush, is a shrub native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Escallonia dumetorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
