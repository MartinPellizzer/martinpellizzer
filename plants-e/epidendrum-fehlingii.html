Epidendrum fehlingii

Epidendrum fehlingii, commonly known as the Fehling's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its inflorescence is a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red.

Epidendrum fehlingii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.
