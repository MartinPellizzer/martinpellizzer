Enterolobium barnebianum

Enterolobium barnebianum, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a tropical leguminous tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree's fruit is a long, flat pod that contains several edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food.

The plant also has nitrogen-fixing capabilities, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
