Eltroplectris triloba

Eltroplectris triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces small, showy blossoms in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.
