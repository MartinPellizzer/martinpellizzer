Eriocactus claviceps

Eriocactus claviceps, commonly known as the crown of thorns cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines that provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptations, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
