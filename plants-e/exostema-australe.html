Exostema australe

Exostema australe, commonly known as the Australian flatback spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, flat, greenish-blue leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
