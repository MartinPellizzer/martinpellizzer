Elaphoglossum sinensiumbrarum

Elaphoglossum sinensiumbrarum, commonly known as the Chinese holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a finely divided, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Elaphoglossum sinensiumbrarum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
