Eltroplectris kuhlmanniana

Eltroplectris kuhlmanniana, commonly known as the Kuhlmann's sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright fronds with a distinctive, serrated margin. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or rocky crevices. The fronds are usually evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters, making it a visually appealing addition to shaded gardens.
