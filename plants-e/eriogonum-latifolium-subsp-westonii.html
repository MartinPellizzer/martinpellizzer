Eriogonum latifolium subsp. westonii

Eriogonum latifolium subsp.

westonii, commonly known as the broadleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers.
