Erica russeliana

Erica russeliana, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a low-growing shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica russeliana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
