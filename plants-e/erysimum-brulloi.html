Erysimum brulloi

Erysimum brulloi, commonly known as the Brullo's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial plant with bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found growing in rocky or disturbed soils. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with four-petaled flowers.

Erysimum brulloi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
