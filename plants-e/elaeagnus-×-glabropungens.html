Elaeagnus × glabropungens

Elaeagnus × glabropungens, commonly known as silverthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soils.

Elaeagnus × glabropungens is commonly used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
