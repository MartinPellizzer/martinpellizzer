Echinodorus grisebachii var. minor

Echinodorus grisebachii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf Amazon sword, is a compact aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, waxy, dark green leaves that are slightly undulated and often have a bluish tint. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light and nutrient-rich substrates. It is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Its growth rate is moderate, making it suitable for both beginner and experienced aquarists.
