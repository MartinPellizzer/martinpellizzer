Epilobium pubens f. major

Epilobium pubens f. major, commonly known as the large-flowered willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Epilobium pubens f. major is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
