Erythracanthus pohlianus

Erythracanthus pohlianus, commonly known as the Pohli's erythracanthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
