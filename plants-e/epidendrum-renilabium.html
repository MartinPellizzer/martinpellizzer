Epidendrum renilabium

Epidendrum renilabium, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white with a distinctive red lip. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum renilabium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
