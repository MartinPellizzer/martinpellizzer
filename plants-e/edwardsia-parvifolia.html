Edwardsia parvifolia

Edwardsia parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved edwardsia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Edwardsia parvifolia is noted for its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
