Epidendrum septipartitum

Epidendrum septipartitum, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display a gradient of hues from red to yellow. The plant produces pendulous, showy flowers that are often arranged in a raceme, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are typically elliptical and medium green, with a waxy texture.

Epidendrum septipartitum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
