Eulophia praestans

Eulophia praestans, commonly known as the showy eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Eulophia praestans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
