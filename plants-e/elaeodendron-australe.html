Elaeodendron australe

Elaeodendron australe, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in spring, creating a vibrant display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Elaeodendron australe is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
