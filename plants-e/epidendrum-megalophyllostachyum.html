Epidendrum megalophyllostachyum

Epidendrum megalophyllostachyum, commonly known as the giant leaf Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique foliage and striking blooms make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural collections.
