Eleutherococcus stenophyllus f. angustissimus

Eleutherococcus stenophyllus f. angustissimus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Siberian ginseng, is a rare and distinctive variant of the Siberian ginseng species.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. This plant is valued for its adaptogenic properties and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in cool, temperate climates and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Its botanical features make it an important subject in both horticultural and medicinal research.
