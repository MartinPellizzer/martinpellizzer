Epilobium barbeyanum

Epilobium barbeyanum, commonly known as the Barbey's willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Epilobium barbeyanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
