Epidendrum melistagum

Epidendrum melistagum, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central America.

It features long, pendant-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum melistagum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
