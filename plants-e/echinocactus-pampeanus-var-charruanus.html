Echinocactus pampeanus var. charruanus

Echinocactus pampeanus var. charruanus, commonly known as the Charruan Hedgehog Cactus, is a succulent native to Argentina.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, spiny areoles and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardiness.

Its habitat includes rocky soils and open grasslands in the Pampas region.
