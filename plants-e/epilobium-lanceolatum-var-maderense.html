Epilobium lanceolatum var. maderense

Epilobium lanceolatum var. maderense, commonly known as Mader's willowherb, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils along stream banks and in woodlands.
