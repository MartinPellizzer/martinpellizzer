Euphrasia nemorosa subvar. glabrescens

Euphrasia nemorosa subvar. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth lady's tears, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family, commonly referred to as the broomrape family. This subspecies is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, which become less prominent as the plant matures.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
