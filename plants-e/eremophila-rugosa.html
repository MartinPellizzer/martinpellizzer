Eremophila rugosa

Eremophila rugosa, commonly known as the rough eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of pink or red tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
