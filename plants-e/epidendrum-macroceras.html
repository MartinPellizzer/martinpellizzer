Epidendrum macroceras

Epidendrum macroceras, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features long, arching stems that bear clusters of large, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a distinctive red lip. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Epidendrum macroceras is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
