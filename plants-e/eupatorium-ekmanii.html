Eupatorium ekmanii

Eupatorium ekmanii, commonly known as the Cape Verde ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Its flowering period occurs during the dry season, and it is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Eupatorium ekmanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
