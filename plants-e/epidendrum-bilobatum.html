Epidendrum bilobatum

Epidendrum bilobatum, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendulous inflorescences with striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum bilobatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
