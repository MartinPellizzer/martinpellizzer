Eulophia longipedunculata

Eulophia longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant flowers. The plant typically has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces showy, yellowish-green to white blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Eulophia longipedunculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
