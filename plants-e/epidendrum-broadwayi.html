Epidendrum broadwayi

Epidendrum broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with elongated pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in loose clusters, and are notable for their intricate, spurred petals.

Epidendrum broadwayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under appropriate conditions.
