Epidendrum imantophyllum

Epidendrum imantophyllum, commonly known as the magnetic orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It prefers warm temperatures and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and floral fragrance make it a striking addition to any collection.
