Epidendrum rigidiflorum

Epidendrum rigidiflorum, commonly known as the rigid-flowered epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sturdy, upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum rigidiflorum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
