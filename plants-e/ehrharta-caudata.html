Ehrharta caudata

Ehrharta caudata, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a clumping growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

Ehrharta caudata is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and moderate drought.
