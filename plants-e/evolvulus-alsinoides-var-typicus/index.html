Evolvulus Alsinoides Var. Typicus

Blue morning glory, scientifically known as Evolvulus alsinoides var. typicus, is a flowering plant native to the Americas. It is recognized for its vibrant blue petals that open in the early morning sun. This species thrives in warm climates and is often found in gardens and along roadsides. Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
