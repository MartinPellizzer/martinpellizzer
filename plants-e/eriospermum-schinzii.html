Eriospermum schinzii

Eriospermum schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
