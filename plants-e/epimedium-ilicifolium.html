Epimedium ilicifolium

Epimedium ilicifolium, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that are often divided into lance-shaped segments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Epimedium ilicifolium is valued for its ornamental foliage and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
