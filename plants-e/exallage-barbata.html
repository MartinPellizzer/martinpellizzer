Exallage barbata

Exallage barbata, commonly known as the barbed spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, often appearing in late summer.

Exallage barbata is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
