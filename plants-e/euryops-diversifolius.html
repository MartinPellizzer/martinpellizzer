Euryops diversifolius

Euryops diversifolius, commonly known as the variable leaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are variable in shape, which gives it its scientific name, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
