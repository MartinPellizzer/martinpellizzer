Euphorbia gokakensis

Euphorbia gokakensis, commonly known as the Gokakensi spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
