Echeveria runyonii var. macabeana

Echeveria runyonii var. macabeana, commonly known as the Macabeana Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring or summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
