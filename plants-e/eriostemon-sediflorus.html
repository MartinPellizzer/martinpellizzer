Eriostemon sediflorus

Eriostemon sediflorus, commonly known as the soft-leaved mistflower, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as cliffs and rocky shores.
