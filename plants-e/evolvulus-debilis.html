Evolvulus debilis

Evolvulus debilis, commonly known as morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and distinctive blue or violet flowers that open in the morning and close in the afternoon. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, spiral-shaped seed pods. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Evolvulus debilis is also known for its climbing habit and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
