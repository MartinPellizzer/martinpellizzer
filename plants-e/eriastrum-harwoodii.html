Eriastrum harwoodii

Eriastrum harwoodii, commonly known as Harwood's eriastrum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial species belonging to the aster family (Asteraceae). The plant features small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Eriastrum harwoodii is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
