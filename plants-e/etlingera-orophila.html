Etlingera orophila

Etlingera orophila, commonly known as the mountain ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.
