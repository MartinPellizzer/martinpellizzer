Epidendrum sonsonense

Epidendrum sonsonense, commonly known as the Sonsonense orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that typically range from red to orange, with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences, and its leaves are elliptical with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum sonsonense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
