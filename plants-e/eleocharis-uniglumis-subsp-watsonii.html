Eleocharis uniglumis subsp. watsonii

Eleocharis uniglumis subsp.

watsonii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers.

The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

It is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in waterlogged soils.
