Erica blancheana

Erica blancheana, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

Erica blancheana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
