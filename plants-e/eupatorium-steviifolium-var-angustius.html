Eupatorium steviifolium var. angustius

Eupatorium steviifolium var. angustius, commonly known as narrow-leaved boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to the northeastern United States.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to lavender in color, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a serrated margin, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
