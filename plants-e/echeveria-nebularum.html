Echeveria nebularum

Echeveria nebularum, commonly known as the Cloudy Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
