Echeveria × graessneri

Echeveria × graessneri, commonly known as the Blue Elf or Blue Echeveria, is a hybrid succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a fine layer of white powdery bloom. The plant produces vibrant violet to purple flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its unique coloration and compact form make it a striking addition to any succulent collection.
