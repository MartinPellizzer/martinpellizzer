Eulophia hirsuta

Eulophia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching growth habit and its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous small, colorful blooms. Eulophia hirsuta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
