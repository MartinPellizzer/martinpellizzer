Erysimum × cheiri var. senoneri

Erysimum × cheiri var. senoneri, commonly known as the candytuft, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically produces fluffy, spherical seed heads that add visual interest in the late season. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
