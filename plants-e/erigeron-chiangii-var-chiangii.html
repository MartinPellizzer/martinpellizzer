Erigeron chiangii var. chiangii

Erigeron chiangii var. chiangii, commonly known as the Chiang's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like appearance with white to pale blue ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It produces small, dandelion-like seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
