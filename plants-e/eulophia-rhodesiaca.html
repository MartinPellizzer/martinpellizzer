Eulophia rhodesiaca

Eulophia rhodesiaca, commonly known as the Rhodesian eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowering stems. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves and bears intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

Eulophia rhodesiaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
