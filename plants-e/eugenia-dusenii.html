Eugenia dusenii

Eugenia dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and leathery, elliptical leaves. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters.

Eugenia dusenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
