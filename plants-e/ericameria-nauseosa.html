Ericameria nauseosa

Ericameria nauseosa, commonly known as bitterleaf rabbitbrush, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

Ericameria nauseosa is also known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it an important component of its native ecosystem.
