Epilobium × romieuxii

Epilobium × romieuxii, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

Epilobium × romieuxii is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
