Erysimum grandiflorum var. dertosense

Erysimum grandiflorum var. dertosense, commonly known as the Desert Sunflower, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It features basal rosettes of lanceolate, silvery-green leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with showy ray florets and prominent central cones. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, exhibiting drought tolerance once established.

Its botanical structure supports its adaptation to harsh desert environments, making it a resilient and ornamental plant.
