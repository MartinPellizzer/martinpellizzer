Epidendrum chinense var. naevosum

Epidendrum chinense var. naevosum, commonly known as the Variegated Chinese Vanda, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves with distinctive light green or white markings. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting mix.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
