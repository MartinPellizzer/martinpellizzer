Emmotum nitens

Emmotum nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a taproot system and produces a capsule fruit containing numerous seeds.

Emmotum nitens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
