Epidendrum trachychilum

Epidendrum trachychilum, commonly known as the silver-leaf epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or purple markings, arranged in a raceme.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
