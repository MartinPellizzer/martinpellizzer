Erica gigantea

Erica gigantea, commonly known as the giant heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica gigantea is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
