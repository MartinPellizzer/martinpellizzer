Equilabium acaule

Equilabium acaule, commonly known as the stemless bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of a distinct stem, with leaves directly emerging from the base. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
