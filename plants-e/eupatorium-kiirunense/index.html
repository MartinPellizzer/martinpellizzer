Eupatorium Kiirunense

Eupatorium kiirunense is a lesser-known flowering plant native to parts of East Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This species is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it has garnered interest among botanists and conservationists.
