Erica amalophylla

Erica amalophylla, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces vibrant purple or pink flowers during the summer months.

Erica amalophylla is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
