Eupatorium latifolium

Eupatorium latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar flowering plants.

Its showy blooms attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
