Encyclia peraltensis

Encyclia peraltensis, commonly known as the Palti orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia peraltensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
