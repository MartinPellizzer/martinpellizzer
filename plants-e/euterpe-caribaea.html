Euterpe caribaea

Euterpe caribaea, commonly known as the Jamaican banana or Guinean banana, is a species of plant in the Musaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows as a clump of tall, leafy stems. The plant produces large, elongated bananas that are usually harvested when they are still green. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Euterpe caribaea is often cultivated for its edible fruits and is also used in traditional medicine.
