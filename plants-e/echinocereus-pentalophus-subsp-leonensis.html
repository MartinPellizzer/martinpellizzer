Echinocereus pentalophus subsp. leonensis

Echinocereus pentalophus subsp.

leonensis, commonly known as the Leonensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in prominent, white spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as a decorative cactus in arid landscapes.
