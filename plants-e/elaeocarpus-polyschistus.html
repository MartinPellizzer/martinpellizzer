Elaeocarpus polyschistus

Elaeocarpus polyschistus, commonly known as the mountain elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, a feature common among members of its family.

Elaeocarpus polyschistus is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
