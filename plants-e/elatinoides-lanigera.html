Elatinoides lanigera

Elatinoides lanigera, commonly known as the woolly elatin, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Elatinaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and hairy foliage. The plant is often found in sandy or coastal environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its structure includes a low-growing habit with prostrate stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

Elatinoides lanigera is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
