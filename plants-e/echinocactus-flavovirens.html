Echinocactus flavovirens

Echinocactus flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a yellowish tinge. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
