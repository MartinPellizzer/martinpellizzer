Eugenia barriosana

Eugenia barriosana, commonly known as the Barrios's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
