Epidendrum lembotylosum

Epidendrum lembotylosum, commonly known as the Lembotylosum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
