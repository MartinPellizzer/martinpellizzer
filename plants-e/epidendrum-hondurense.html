Epidendrum hondurense

Epidendrum hondurense, commonly known as the Honduran Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct lip that is often darker in color.

Epidendrum hondurense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
