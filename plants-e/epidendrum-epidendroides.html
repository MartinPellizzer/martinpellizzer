Epidendrum epidendroides

Epidendrum epidendroides, commonly known as the green-lipped epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a yellowish lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity.

Epidendrum epidendroides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor growers.
