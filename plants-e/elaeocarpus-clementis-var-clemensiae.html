Elaeocarpus clementis var. clemensiae

Elaeocarpus clementis var. clemensiae, commonly known as the Malayan white flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its attractive, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically encased in a fleshy, red or yellow pulp. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental beauty and is found in tropical and subtropical regions.
