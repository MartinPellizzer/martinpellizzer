Encyclia × raganii

Encyclia × raganii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia fraterna.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a yellow throat and purple or violet petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid species that showcases the genetic diversity within the Encyclia genus.
