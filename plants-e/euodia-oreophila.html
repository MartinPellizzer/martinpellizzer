Euodia oreophila

Euodia oreophila, commonly known as the mountain euodia, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural research.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
