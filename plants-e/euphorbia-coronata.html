Euphorbia coronata

Euphorbia coronata, commonly known as the Crowned Spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in a crown-like arrangement, giving it its common name. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Euphorbia coronata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
