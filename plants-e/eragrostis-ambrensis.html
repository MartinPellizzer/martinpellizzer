Eragrostis ambrensis

Eragrostis ambrensis, commonly known as the Ambrosia grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
