Erodium ciconium var. genuinum

Erodium ciconium var. genuinum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces delicate pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its seeds are tiny and covered in a sticky coating, which aids in their dispersal by adhering to animals or clothing.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
