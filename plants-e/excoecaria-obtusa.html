Excoecaria obtusa

Excoecaria obtusa, commonly known as the rubber tree or croton, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple underside. Its flowers are small and yellow, and it produces orange to red berries that contain seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
