Eadesia

Eadesia, commonly known as the starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It produces a distinctive fruit with a bright yellow, star-shaped exterior and a sweet, tangy pulp. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit is often cultivated for its edible pulp and is also used in traditional medicine.
