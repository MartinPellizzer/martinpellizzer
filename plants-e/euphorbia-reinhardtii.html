Euphorbia reinhardtii

Euphorbia reinhardtii, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its spiny, segmented stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers surrounded by green bracts. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
