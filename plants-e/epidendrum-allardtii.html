Epidendrum allardtii

Epidendrum allardtii, commonly known as the Allardt's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a glossy surface and a pointed apex.

Epidendrum allardtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
