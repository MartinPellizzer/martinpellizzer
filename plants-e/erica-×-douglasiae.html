Erica × douglasiae

Erica × douglasiae, commonly known as the Cross-leaved Heath, is a hybrid shrub native to the Scottish Highlands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Erica × douglasiae thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
