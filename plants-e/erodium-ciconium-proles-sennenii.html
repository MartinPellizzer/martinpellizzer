Erodium ciconium proles sennenii

Erodium ciconium proles sennenii, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Leguminosae, though it is not a true legume.
