Englerastrum djalonense

Englerastrum djalonense, commonly known as the West African rock orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is native to regions in West Africa.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
