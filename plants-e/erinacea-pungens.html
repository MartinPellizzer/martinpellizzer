Erinacea pungens

Erinacea pungens, commonly known as the spiny hakea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.
