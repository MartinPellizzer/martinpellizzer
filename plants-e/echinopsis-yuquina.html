Echinopsis yuquina

Echinopsis yuquina, commonly known as the Yuquina cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces large, showy white or pink flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and xeriscaping.
