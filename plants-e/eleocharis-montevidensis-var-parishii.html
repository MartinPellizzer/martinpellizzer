Eleocharis montevidensis var. parishii

Eleocharis montevidensis var. parishii, commonly known as the Paraguay sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. This species is native to South America and is often found in regions with high humidity and saturated soil conditions.
