Erythroxylum membranaceum

Erythroxylum membranaceum, commonly known as the red mallow or Brazilian mallow, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its palmate leaves and showy, pink or red flowers. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
