Epidendrum brachyblastum

Epidendrum brachyblastum, commonly known as the short-stemmed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense, velvety coating.

Epidendrum brachyblastum prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
