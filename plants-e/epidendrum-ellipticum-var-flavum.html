Epidendrum ellipticum var. flavum

Epidendrum ellipticum var. flavum, commonly known as the Yellow Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, pendulous flower spikes with yellow-orange blossoms that are highly fragrant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements.
