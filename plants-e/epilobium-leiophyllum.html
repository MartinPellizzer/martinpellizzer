Epilobium leiophyllum

Epilobium leiophyllum, commonly known as smooth willow herb, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. The plant features slender, slightly hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.
