Echeveria argentea

Echeveria argentea, commonly known as the Silver Dust Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces pink to purple flowers on tall stalks during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact growth and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
