Echinocereus weinbergii subsp. venustus

Echinocereus weinbergii subsp.

venustus, commonly known as the Venus's thorn cactus, is a species native to the United States, particularly Arizona and New Mexico. It is a columnar cactus with a ribbed stem and spines that are often reddish in color.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand arid conditions.
