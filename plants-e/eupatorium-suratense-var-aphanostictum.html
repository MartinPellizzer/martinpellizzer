Eupatorium suratense var. aphanostictum

Eupatorium suratense var. aphanostictum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed boneset, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, aromatic stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
