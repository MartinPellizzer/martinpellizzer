Echinocereus engelmannii var. robustior

Echinocereus engelmannii var. robustior, commonly known as the robust hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a dense covering of spines and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a striking addition to desert gardens.
