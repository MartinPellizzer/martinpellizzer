Elaeocarpus kalabitii

Elaeocarpus kalabitii, commonly known as the Bornean elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy white to pink flowers. The tree produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
