Eremophila brownii var. viridiflora

Eremophila brownii var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a unique color contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
