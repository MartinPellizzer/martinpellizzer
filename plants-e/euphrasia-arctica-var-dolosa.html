Euphrasia arctica var. dolosa

Euphrasia arctica var. dolosa, commonly known as the Arctic eyebright, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, rocky habitats and is adapted to cold climates.
