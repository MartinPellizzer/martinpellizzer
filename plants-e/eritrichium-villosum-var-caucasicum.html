Eritrichium villosum var. caucasicum

Eritrichium villosum var. caucasicum, commonly known as the Caucasian velvetleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features delicate, hairy leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance.

It is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
