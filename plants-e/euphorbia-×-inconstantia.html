Euphorbia × inconstantia

Euphorbia × inconstantia, commonly known as the variable spurge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, often ribbed stems and leaves that can vary in shape and texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is native to southern Africa and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Euphorbia × inconstantia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
