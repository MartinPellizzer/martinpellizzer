Eragrostis cilianensis

Eragrostis cilianensis, commonly known as Cilician lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and disturbed areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, showing drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local vegetation and a potential candidate for ecological restoration projects.
