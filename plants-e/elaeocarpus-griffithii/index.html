Elaeocarpus Griffithii

Elaeocarpus griffithii is a rare and unique plant native to the eastern Himalayas. It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is known for its striking blue flowers and ornamental value. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
