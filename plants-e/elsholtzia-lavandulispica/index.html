Elsholtzia Lavandulispica

Elsholtzia lavandulispica is a unique and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. Known for its fragrant, lavender-like flowers, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This perennial herb belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. Its delicate appearance and strong scent make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and fragrance. Despite its appeal, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native habitat.
