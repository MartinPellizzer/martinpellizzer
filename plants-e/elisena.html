Elisena

Elisena, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces elongated, hanging clusters of blooms that are highly ornamental. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Elisena thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
