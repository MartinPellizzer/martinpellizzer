Erica imperialis

Erica imperialis, commonly known as the imperial heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant produces dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Erica imperialis is known for its resilience to drought and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
