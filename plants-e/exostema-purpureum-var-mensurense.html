Exostema purpureum var. mensurense

Exostema purpureum var. mensurense, commonly known as the purple exostema, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple foliage and produces small, showy flowers in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are leathery and have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to humid conditions.
