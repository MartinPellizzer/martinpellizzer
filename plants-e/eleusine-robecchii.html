Eleusine robecchii

Eleusine robecchii, commonly known as false rice, is a flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. It often grows in wetland habitats and can become invasive in certain environments.

Eleusine robecchii is sometimes cultivated as a forage crop or used in traditional medicine.
