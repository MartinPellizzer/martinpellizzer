Elatostema laxisericeum

Elatostema laxisericeum, commonly known as the silky-stemmed elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of tiny white or greenish petals, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Elatostema laxisericeum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
