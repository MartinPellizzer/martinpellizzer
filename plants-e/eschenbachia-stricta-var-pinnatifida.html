Eschenbachia stricta var. pinnatifida

Eschenbachia stricta var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eschenbachia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
