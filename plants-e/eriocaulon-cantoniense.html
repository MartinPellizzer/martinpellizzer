Eriocaulon cantoniense

Eriocaulon cantoniense, commonly known as the Guangdong spikegrass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southeastern China.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes various aquatic and semi-aquatic grasses. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish bloom. Its flowering spikes are typically borne at the top of the stems, producing small, inconspicuous flowers.

Eriocaulon cantoniense is often found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds.
