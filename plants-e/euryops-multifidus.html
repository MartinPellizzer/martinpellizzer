Euryops multifidus

Euryops multifidus, commonly known as the multibract sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant has a bushy growth habit with finely divided, fern-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Euryops multifidus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
