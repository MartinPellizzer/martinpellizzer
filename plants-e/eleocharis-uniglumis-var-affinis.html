Eleocharis uniglumis var. affinis

Eleocharis uniglumis var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers. This variety typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly rough texture.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
