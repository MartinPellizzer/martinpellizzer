Eriocaulon ithyphyllum

Eriocaulon ithyphyllum, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spike-like inflorescences. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in wetlands and swampy areas. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to waterlogged soils.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and marsh ecosystems.
