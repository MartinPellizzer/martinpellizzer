Eugenia glabrata

Eugenia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, edible berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenia glabrata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
