Elwendia stewartiana

Elwendia stewartiana, commonly known as Stewart's elwendia, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil.
