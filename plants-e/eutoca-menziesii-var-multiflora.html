Eutoca menziesii var. multiflora

Eutoca menziesii var. multiflora, commonly known as the Pacific silver fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves. The tree produces male and female cones, with the female cones developing into seed-bearing structures that release winged seeds. Its bark is thick and scaly, providing some resistance to pests and diseases.
