Eugenia barnesii

Eugenia barnesii, commonly known as the Barnes' eugenia, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped, glossy, and emit a mild aromatic scent.

The fruit, which is small and berry-like, is edible and often used in local cuisines.
