Escallonia × demissa

Escallonia × demissa, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a mild, sweet fragrance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pink, which bloom in spring. Its ornamental value comes from its dense foliage and showy blossoms, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Escallonia × demissa is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade.
