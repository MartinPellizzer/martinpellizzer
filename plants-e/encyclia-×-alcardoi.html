Encyclia × alcardoi

Encyclia × alcardoi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia fraterna.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico, characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
