Encyclia baculus

Encyclia baculus, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain.

Encyclia baculus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
