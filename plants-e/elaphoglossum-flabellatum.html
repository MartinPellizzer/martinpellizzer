Elaphoglossum flabellatum

Elaphoglossum flabellatum, commonly known as the fan-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often form a fan-like shape, giving it its common name. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically evergreen, with a leathery texture and a deep green color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
