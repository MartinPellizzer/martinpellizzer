Eucrosia tubiflora

Eucrosia tubiflora, commonly known as the golden crown imperial, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette at the base. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive crown-like arrangement at the base of each bloom.

Eucrosia tubiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
