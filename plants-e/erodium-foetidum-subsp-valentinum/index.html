Erodium Foetidum Subsp. Valentinum

Erodium foetidum subsp. valentinum is a lesser-known member of the geranium family known for its distinctive appearance and unique characteristics. This plant is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, where it thrives in rocky and coastal environments. Despite its strong, often unpleasant odor, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties. It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, adding a subtle touch to gardens. Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it is a popular choice for those seeking low-maintenance, resilient plants.
