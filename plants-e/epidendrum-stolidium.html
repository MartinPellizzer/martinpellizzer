Epidendrum stolidium

Epidendrum stolidium, commonly known as the stiff epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its upright, branching stems. The plant produces showy, lance-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Epidendrum stolidium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
