Epidendrum fusagasugaense

Epidendrum fusagasugaense, commonly known as the Fusagasuga Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, typically found on trees in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its vibrant floral display.
