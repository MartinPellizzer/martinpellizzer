Erigeron maximus var. minor

Erigeron maximus var. minor, commonly known as the lesser blanketflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its fibrous root system and hairy stems help it survive in arid conditions.
