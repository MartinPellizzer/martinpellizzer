Ericoides nitidum

Ericoides nitidum, commonly known as the glossy heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with leathery, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and moorlands.

Ericoides nitidum is a low-growing shrub that is well adapted to cool, moist climates.
