Euphrasia alpina subsp. alpina

Euphrasia alpina subsp.

alpina, commonly known as the Alpine eyebright, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats.

The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive, spurred petals. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
