Eschweilera perumbonata

Eschweilera perumbonata, commonly known as the Perumbonate ceiba, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and can grow up to 40 meters tall. The tree produces showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its wood is valued for its strength and durability, though it is not widely used commercially.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat and food for numerous species.
