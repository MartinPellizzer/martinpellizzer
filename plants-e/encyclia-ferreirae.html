Encyclia ferreirae

Encyclia ferreirae, commonly known as the Ferreira's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia ferreirae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
