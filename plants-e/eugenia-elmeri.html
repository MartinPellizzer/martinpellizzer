Eugenia elmeri

Eugenia elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia elmeri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
