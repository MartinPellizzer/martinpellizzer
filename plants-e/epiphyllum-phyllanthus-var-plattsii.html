Epiphyllum phyllanthus var. plattsii

Epiphyllum phyllanthus var. plattsii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are often brightly colored and can grow in a bushy or trailing form. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor environments.
