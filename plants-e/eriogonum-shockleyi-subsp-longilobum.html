Eriogonum shockleyi subsp. longilobum

Eriogonum shockleyi subsp.

longilobum, commonly known as the long-lobed buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers.
