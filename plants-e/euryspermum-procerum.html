Euryspermum procerum

Euryspermum procerum, commonly known as the tall euphorbia, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, milky sap. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
