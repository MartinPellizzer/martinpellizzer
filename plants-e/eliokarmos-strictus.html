Eliokarmos strictus

Eliokarmos strictus, commonly known as the rigid elokarmos, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, often branching towards the upper portion. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Eliokarmos strictus is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
