Epidendrum pilcuense

Epidendrum pilcuense, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with numerous blooms in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum pilcuense is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
