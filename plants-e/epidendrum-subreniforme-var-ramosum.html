Epidendrum subreniforme var. ramosum

Epidendrum subreniforme var. ramosum, commonly known as the ramose epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing multiple stems from a single base. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, with a distinctive lip structure.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
