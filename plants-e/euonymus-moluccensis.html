Euonymus moluccensis

Euonymus moluccensis, commonly known as the Moluccan spurge, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are distinctive and attractive.

Euonymus moluccensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
