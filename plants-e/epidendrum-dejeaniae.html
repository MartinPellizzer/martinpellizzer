Epidendrum dejeaniae

Epidendrum dejeaniae, commonly known as the Dejeaniae Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light. It is a pseudobulbous orchid, meaning it has swollen stems that store water and nutrients.

Epidendrum dejeaniae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
