Epicranthes cyanotriche

Epicranthes cyanotriche, commonly known as the blue-haired cypress, is a member of the Cupressaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like leaves that are typically a bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, rounded cones that are often clustered at the tips of the branches. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in cool, humid climates.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
