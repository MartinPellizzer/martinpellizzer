Eburopetalum borneense

Eburopetalum borneense, commonly known as the Bornean starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and distinctive yellowish-white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that resemble starfruits, though they are not related to the common starfruit (Carambola). The plant is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and ecological significance in its native habitat.
