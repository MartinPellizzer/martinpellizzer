Erodium hirtum var. cyrenaicum

Erodium hirtum var. cyrenaicum, commonly known as the Cyprus eryngo, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and garden cultivation.
