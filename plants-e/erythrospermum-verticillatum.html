Erythrospermum verticillatum

Erythrospermum verticillatum, commonly known as the red-flowered honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves.
