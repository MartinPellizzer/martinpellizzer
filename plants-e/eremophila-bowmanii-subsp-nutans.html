Eremophila bowmanii subsp. nutans

Eremophila bowmanii subsp.

nutans, commonly known as the Bowman's emu bush, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to red, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it is well-suited to low-water conditions.
