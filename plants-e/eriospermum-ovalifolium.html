Eriospermum ovalifolium

Eriospermum ovalifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Eriospermum ovalifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
