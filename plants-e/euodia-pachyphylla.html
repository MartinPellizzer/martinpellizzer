Euodia pachyphylla

Euodia pachyphylla, commonly known as the thick-leaved euodia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Euodia pachyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
