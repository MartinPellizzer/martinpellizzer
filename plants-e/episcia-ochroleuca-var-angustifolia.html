Episcia ochroleuca var. angustifolia

Episcia ochroleuca var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved episcia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that often resemble small flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
