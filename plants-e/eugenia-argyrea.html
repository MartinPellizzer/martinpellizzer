Eugenia argyrea

Eugenia argyrea, commonly known as silverleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, reddish-purple berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.

Eugenia argyrea is also valued in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.
