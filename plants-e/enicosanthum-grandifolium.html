Enicosanthum grandifolium

Enicosanthum grandifolium, commonly known as the grandifoliate beauty, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warm season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
