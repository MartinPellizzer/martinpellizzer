Eragrostis passa

Eragrostis passa, commonly known as the narrowleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Eragrostis passa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
