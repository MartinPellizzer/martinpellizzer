Ethulia bicostata subsp. bicostata

Ethulia bicostata subsp.

bicostata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daphne, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The subspecies is distinguished by its consistent leaf morphology and floral structure compared to other subspecies of the genus.
