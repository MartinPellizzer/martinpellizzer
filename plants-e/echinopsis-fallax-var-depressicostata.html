Echinopsis fallax var. depressicostata

Echinopsis fallax var. depressicostata, commonly known as the Candelabra Cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem that often branches into a candelabra-like shape. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and nocturnal blooming habit make it a striking addition to succulent collections.
