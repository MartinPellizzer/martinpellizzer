Encyclia piracanjubensis

Encyclia piracanjubensis, commonly known as the Piracanjubensis Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow and green hues. The plant typically has a pseudobulb and grows from a rhizome, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are often arranged in a raceme.

This orchid prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
