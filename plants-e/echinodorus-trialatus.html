Echinodorus trialatus

Echinodorus trialatus, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

Echinodorus trialatus is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters, making it a hardy choice for both beginners and experienced aquarists.
