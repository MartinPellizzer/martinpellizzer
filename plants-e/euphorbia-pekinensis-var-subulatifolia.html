Euphorbia pekinensis var. subulatifolia

Euphorbia pekinensis var. subulatifolia, commonly known as the Pekin euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to China.

It features unique, spiky, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flowers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Euphorbia pekinensis var. subulatifolia is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.
