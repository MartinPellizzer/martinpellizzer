Erucastrum obtusangulum

Erucastrum obtusangulum, commonly known as the broad-leafed erucaceous, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species with characteristic cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and has lance-shaped, hairy leaves.
