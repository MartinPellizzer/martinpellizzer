Euphorbia pekinensis var. japonensis

Euphorbia pekinensis var. japonensis, commonly known as the Japanese crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, spiny stem that can grow up to several feet in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower clusters, known as cyathia, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
