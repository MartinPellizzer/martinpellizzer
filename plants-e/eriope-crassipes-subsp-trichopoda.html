Eriope crassipes subsp. trichopoda

Eriope crassipes subsp.

trichopoda, commonly known as the hairy eriope, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas with a Mediterranean climate.

The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually pink or purple, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
