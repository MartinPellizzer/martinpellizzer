Erigeron paniculatus

Erigeron paniculatus, commonly known as the blueweed or blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

The plant typically grows in open fields and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. Erigeron paniculatus is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
