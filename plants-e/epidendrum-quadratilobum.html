Epidendrum quadratilobum

Epidendrum quadratilobum, commonly known as the Quadrilobed Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Epidendrum quadratilobum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
