Edwardsia chrysophylla var. lanaiensis

Edwardsia chrysophylla var. lanaiensis, commonly known as the Lanai dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its yellow, dandelion-like flowers and basal rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the arid conditions of its native environment. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.
