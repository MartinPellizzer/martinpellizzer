Erysimum Pubicalyx

Erysimum pubicalyx is a striking member of the Brassicaceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique growth habits. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its compact stature and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The species is also valued for its resilience to drought and mild frost. With its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements, Erysimum pubicalyx is a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
