Eugenia winzerlingii

Eugenia winzerlingii, commonly known as the Winzerling's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
