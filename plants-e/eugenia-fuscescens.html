Eugenia fuscescens

Eugenia fuscescens, commonly known as the black myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are typically small, round, and dark purple when mature.

Eugenia fuscescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
