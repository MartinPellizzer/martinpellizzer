Eulophia latifolia

Eulophia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow lip. The plant has erect, grass-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia latifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
