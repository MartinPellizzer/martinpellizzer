Eragrostis glareosa

Eragrostis glareosa, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, drooping inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and spike-like, often appearing in clusters along the stems.

Eragrostis glareosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its graceful appearance.
