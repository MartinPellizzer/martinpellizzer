Eriastrum ertterae

Eriastrum ertterae, commonly known as the San Benito dandelion, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Eriastrum ertterae is adapted to arid environments and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
