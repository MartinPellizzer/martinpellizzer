Elatostema cuneiforme var. cuneiforme

Elatostema cuneiforme var. cuneiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its preference for well-drained soil.
