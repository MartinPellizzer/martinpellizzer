Epilobium parviflorum var. intermedium

Epilobium parviflorum var. intermedium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as riverbanks, wet meadows, and muddy soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, pink to purple flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It is part of the Onagraceae family and is known for its adaptability to wet environments.
