Echinodorus ranunculoides

Echinodorus ranunculoides, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length, providing dense coverage in aquariums. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light levels and nutrient-rich substrates. It is known for its ability to oxygenate water and serve as a habitat for various aquatic life.

Echinodorus ranunculoides is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits in both natural and artificial aquatic settings.
