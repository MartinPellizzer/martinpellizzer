Euodia hortensis f. monophylla

Euodia hortensis f. monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf Chinese frankincense tree, is a unique variant of the Euodia genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive single leaf, which sets it apart from other members of the species. This plant is native to southern China and is often cultivated for its aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The leaves are typically evergreen, and the plant produces small, fragrant flowers.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic uses.
