Echinopsis fallax var. cylindrica

Echinopsis fallax var. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems and bright green color, which help it store water efficiently. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique shape and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
