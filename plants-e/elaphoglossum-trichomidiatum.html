Elaphoglossum trichomidiatum

Elaphoglossum trichomidiatum, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of short, hairy structures. Elaphoglossum trichomidiatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in temperate regions with high humidity.
