Elmeria pinetorum

Elmeria pinetorum, commonly known as the pine tree fern, is a unique plant that combines features of both ferns and conifers.

It is a tropical evergreen with a distinctive, tree-like structure and a crown of feathery fronds. The plant has a short, trunk-like stem covered in a layer of moss-like tissue, which helps retain moisture. Its fronds are typically arranged in a rosette pattern and can grow up to several feet in length.

Elmeria pinetorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
