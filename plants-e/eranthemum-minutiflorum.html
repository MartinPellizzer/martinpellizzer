Eranthemum minutiflorum

Eranthemum minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered eranthemum, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Due to its modest size and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
