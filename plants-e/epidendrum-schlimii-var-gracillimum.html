Epidendrum schlimii var. gracillimum

Epidendrum schlimii var. gracillimum, commonly known as the Graceful Epidendrum, is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
