Eleocharis acicularis var. bella

Eleocharis acicularis var. bella, commonly known as the slender spike rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. This variety is typically found in wetland habitats such as marshes and pond edges. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland biodiversity.
