Euphrasia lanceolata var. catalaunica

Euphrasia lanceolata var. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
