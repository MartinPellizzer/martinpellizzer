Equisetum arvense var. duffortianum

Equisetum arvense var. duffortianum, commonly known as the Duffort's field horsetail, is a variant of the common horsetail.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its spiky, jointed stems and whorled leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It reproduces through spores and has a unique structure with a central stem and branches.

This variety is notable for its aggressive growth and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
