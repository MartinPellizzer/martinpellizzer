Epidendrum ingramii

Epidendrum ingramii, commonly known as the Ingram's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum ingramii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
