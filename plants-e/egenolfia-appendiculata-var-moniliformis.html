Egenolfia appendiculata var. moniliformis

Egenolfia appendiculata var. moniliformis, commonly known as the chainfruit plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces elongated, bead-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
