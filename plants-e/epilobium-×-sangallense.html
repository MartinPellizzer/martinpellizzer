Epilobium × sangallense

Epilobium × sangallense, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Epilobium × sangallense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting insect life.
