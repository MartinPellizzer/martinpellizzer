Eremostachys sarawschanica f. subcanescens

Eremostachys sarawschanica f. subcanescens, commonly known as the hairy eremostachys, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its woody base and hairy, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.
