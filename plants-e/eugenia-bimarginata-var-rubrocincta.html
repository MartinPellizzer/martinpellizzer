Eugenia bimarginata var. rubrocincta

Eugenia bimarginata var. rubrocincta, commonly known as the red-banded eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive red margin, which gives it its common name. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, bright red berries.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
