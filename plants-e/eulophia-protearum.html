Eulophia protearum

Eulophia protearum, commonly known as the Protea Orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Eulophia protearum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
