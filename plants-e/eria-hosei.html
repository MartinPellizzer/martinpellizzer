Eria hosei

Eria hosei, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Eria hosei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
