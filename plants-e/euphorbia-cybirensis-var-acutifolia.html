Euphorbia cybirensis var. acutifolia

Euphorbia cybirensis var. acutifolia, commonly known as the sharpleaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with thick, fleshy stems that store water, typical of succulents. The leaves are narrow and pointed, giving it its common name, and are usually a deep green color. It produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are the modified floral structures characteristic of the Euphorbia genus.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
