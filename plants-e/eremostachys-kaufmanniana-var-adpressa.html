Eremostachys kaufmanniana var. adpressa

Eremostachys kaufmanniana var. adpressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf eremostachys, is a perennial succulent native to arid regions of central Asia.

It features compact, densely packed rosettes of narrow, blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems with small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in rocky, well-drained soils.
