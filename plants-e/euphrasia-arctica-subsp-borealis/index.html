Euphrasia Arctica Subsp. Borealis

Euphrasia arctica subsp. borealis is a rare and resilient plant found in the Arctic tundra. It thrives in harsh, cold environments where few other species can survive. This subspecies is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and hairy leaves that help protect it from the extreme weather. Its presence is an indicator of healthy, undisturbed polar ecosystems. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into adaptation and survival in extreme climates.
