Evolvulus Alsinoides Var. Linnaeanus

Blue morning glory, scientifically known as Evolvulus alsinoides var. linnaeanus, is a vibrant flowering plant native to the Americas. It is prized for its striking blue and purple blossoms that appear in the early morning, hence its common name. This annual vine thrives in warm climates and is often found in gardens and along roadsides. Its rapid growth and easy care make it a popular choice among gardeners. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
