Encyclia campylostalix

Encyclia campylostalix, commonly known as the Campylostalix orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, arranged in clusters on the stem.

The leaves are elongated and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental value in cultivation.
