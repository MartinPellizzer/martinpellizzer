Eugenia microcalyx var. obovata

Eugenia microcalyx var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate eugenia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. The leaves are elliptical or obovate in shape, with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible and have a tart flavor.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
