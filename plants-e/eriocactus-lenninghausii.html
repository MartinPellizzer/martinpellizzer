Eriocactus lenninghausii

Eriocactus lenninghausii, commonly known as the Leninghaus's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
