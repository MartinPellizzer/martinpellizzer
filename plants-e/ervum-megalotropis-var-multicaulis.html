Ervum megalotropis var. multicaulis

Ervum megalotropis var. multicaulis, commonly known as the multicaulis cowpea, is a variant of the cowpea species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its bushy growth habit and multiple stems that arise from the base, giving it a dense appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires full sunlight for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a valuable crop for both food and forage in agricultural systems.
