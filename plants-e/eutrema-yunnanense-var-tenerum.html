Eutrema yunnanense var. tenerum

Eutrema yunnanense var. tenerum, commonly known as the Yunnan sea lettuce, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or yellow, and it produces small, round seeds.

This variant is noted for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
