Echeveria laresensis

Echeveria laresensis, commonly known as the Laresensis Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
