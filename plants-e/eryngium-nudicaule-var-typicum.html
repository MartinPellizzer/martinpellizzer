Eryngium nudicaule var. typicum

Eryngium nudicaule var. typicum, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a basal rosette of gray-green, spine-tipped leaves that are narrow and linear. The plant produces tall, spiky flower heads composed of small blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky areas.

Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
