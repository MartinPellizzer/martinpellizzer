Embelia abyssinica

Embelia abyssinica, commonly known as African star anise, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. Its aromatic properties make it valuable in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant is also used in local cuisines for its distinctive flavor.
