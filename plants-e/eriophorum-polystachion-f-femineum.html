Eriophorum polystachion f. femineum

Eriophorum polystachion f. femineum, commonly known as the slender cotton grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like inflorescences that produce small, white to pale pink flowers. This subspecies is typically found in moist, acidic environments such as bogs and wet meadows. Its growth habit is low and compact, with narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native habitat.
