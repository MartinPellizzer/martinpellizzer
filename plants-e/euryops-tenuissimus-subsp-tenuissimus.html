Euryops tenuissimus subsp. tenuissimus

Euryops tenuissimus subsp.

tenuissimus, commonly known as the narrowleaf bushveld daisies, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish to orange-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, daisy-like flower heads.

It is native to southern Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
