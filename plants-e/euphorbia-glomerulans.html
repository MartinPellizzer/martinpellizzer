Euphorbia glomerulans

Euphorbia glomerulans, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a milky latex when damaged, which is a common feature among the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
