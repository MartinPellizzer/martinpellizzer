Elaeagnus Ohashii

Elaeagnus ohashii is a unique species of shrub native to Japan. It is known for its fragrant leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun conditions. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
