Euosma latifolia

Euosma latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf euosma, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its flowers are small and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters, and it produces small, dark berries.

Euosma latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and fragrance.
