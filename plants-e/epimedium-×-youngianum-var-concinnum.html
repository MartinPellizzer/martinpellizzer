Epimedium × youngianum var. concinnum

Epimedium × youngianum var. concinnum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a deciduous perennial herb native to China.

It features attractive, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bronze to reddish in color, especially in spring. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring before the leaves fully emerge. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and early blooming habit.
