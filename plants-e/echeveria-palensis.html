Echeveria palensis

Echeveria palensis, commonly known as the Mexican snowball, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Echeveria palensis is popular in rock gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
