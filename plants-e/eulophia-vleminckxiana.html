Eulophia vleminckxiana

Eulophia vleminckxiana, commonly known as the Vleminckx's eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, leafy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Eulophia vleminckxiana is noted for its ornamental value and is relatively rare in cultivation.
