Eragrostis jeffreysii

Eragrostis jeffreysii, commonly known as Jeffreys' lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or purplish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Eragrostis jeffreysii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
