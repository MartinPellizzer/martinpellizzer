Elemifera hexandra

Elemifera hexandra, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and ornamental gardening.
