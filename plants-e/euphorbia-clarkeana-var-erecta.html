Euphorbia clarkeana var. erecta

Euphorbia clarkeana var. erecta, commonly known as the upright euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branched stem system with thick, fleshy leaves that are often reduced to small, scale-like structures. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
