Euphrasia parviflora subsp. glabrescens

Euphrasia parviflora subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the small-flowered eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features slender, upright stems that bear small, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, spurred petals.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, hairy textures that become smoother as the plant matures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
