Eria dalatensis

Eria dalatensis, commonly known as the Dalat orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria dalatensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
