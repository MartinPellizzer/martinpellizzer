Elaeocarpus sikkimensis

Elaeocarpus sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the Himalayan regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan, growing in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Its fruit is a woody, spherical drupe that contains a single seed, and it plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
