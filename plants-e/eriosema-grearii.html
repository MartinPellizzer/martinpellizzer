Eriosema grearii

Eriosema grearii, commonly known as the silverleaf eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Eriosema grearii is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
