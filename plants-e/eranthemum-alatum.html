Eranthemum alatum

Eranthemum alatum, commonly known as the winged eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, wing-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eranthemum alatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
