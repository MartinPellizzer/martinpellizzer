Eugenioides guianense

Eugenioides guianense, commonly known as the Guianese eugenia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its ecological role includes supporting a variety of insect and bird species.
