Euptelea polyandra

Euptelea polyandra, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the silver magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China and Korea.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Euptelea polyandra is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
