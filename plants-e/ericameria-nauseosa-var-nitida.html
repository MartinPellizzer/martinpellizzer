Ericameria nauseosa var. nitida

Ericameria nauseosa var. nitida, commonly known as silverleaf ericameria, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Ericameria nauseosa var. nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
