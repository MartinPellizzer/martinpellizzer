Edraianthus kitaibelii var. mediterraneus

Edraianthus kitaibelii var. mediterraneus, commonly known as the Mediterranean edraianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial species characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
