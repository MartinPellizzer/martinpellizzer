Embothrium gilliesii

Embothrium gilliesii, commonly known as the New Zealand honeysuckle, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in small, terminal umbels, and the plant is known for its fragrant, nectar-rich blossoms.

Embothrium gilliesii is often found in coastal and lowland forest environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
