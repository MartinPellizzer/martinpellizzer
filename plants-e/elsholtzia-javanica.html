Elsholtzia javanica

Elsholtzia javanica, commonly known as Javanese elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties.

Elsholtzia javanica is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
