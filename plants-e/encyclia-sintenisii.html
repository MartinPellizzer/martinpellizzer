Encyclia sintenisii

Encyclia sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting purple or brown markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia sintenisii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
