Ecbolium lancifolium

Ecbolium lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ecbolium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant or for its nitrogen-fixing properties.
