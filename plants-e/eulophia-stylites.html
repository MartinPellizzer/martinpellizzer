Eulophia stylites

Eulophia stylites, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, spike-like inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Eulophia stylites thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
