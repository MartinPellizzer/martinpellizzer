Erica brevicaulis

Erica brevicaulis, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features compact, evergreen foliage and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or white. It is well adapted to coastal and mountainous environments, often thriving in areas with high sunlight exposure.

Erica brevicaulis is a hardy plant that is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative shrub.
