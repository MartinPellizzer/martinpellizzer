Elymus yushuensis

Elymus yushuensis, commonly known as the Yushu needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cold climates and well-drained soils. Elymus yushuensis plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.

Its resilience to harsh conditions makes it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
