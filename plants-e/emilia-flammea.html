Emilia flammea

Emilia flammea, commonly known as the flame emilia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their striking color and showy appearance.

Emilia flammea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and ease of growth.
