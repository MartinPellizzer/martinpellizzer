Echinocactus platyacanthus f. visnaga

Echinocactus platyacanthus f. visnaga, commonly known as the Visnaga prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems and spiny, paddle-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Its distinctive appearance and hardiness make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
