Echeveria × haworthioides

Echeveria × haworthioides, commonly known as the "Haworthioides Echeveria," is a hybrid succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
