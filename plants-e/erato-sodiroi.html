Erato sodiroi

Erato sodiroi, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Erato sodiroi is often found in moist, shaded areas in tropical forests.
