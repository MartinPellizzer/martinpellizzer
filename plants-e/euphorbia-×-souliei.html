Euphorbia × souliei

Euphorbia × souliei, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and striking red or yellow flowers that appear in summer. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its leaves are typically green or red, depending on the variety, and are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Euphorbia × souliei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and unique appearance.
