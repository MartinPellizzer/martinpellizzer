Euphorbia niciciana

Euphorbia niciciana, commonly known as the Niciciana spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a cup-like structure called a cyathium. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Euphorbia niciciana is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.
