Euphorbia franchetii

Euphorbia franchetii, commonly known as the Franchet's spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
