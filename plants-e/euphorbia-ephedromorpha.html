Euphorbia ephedromorpha

Euphorbia ephedromorpha, commonly known as the red-stemmed euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, branching stems and produces clusters of yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.

Euphorbia ephedromorpha is also known for its ability to thrive in well-draining soil and full sun conditions.
