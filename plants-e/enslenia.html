Enslenia

Enslenia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It has a sprawling growth habit and produces berries that are toxic to humans and animals.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
