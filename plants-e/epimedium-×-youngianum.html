Epimedium × youngianum

Epimedium × youngianum, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a hybrid evergreen perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, leathery, heart-shaped leaves that remain green throughout the year. The plant produces showy, nodding yellow flowers in late spring, which are borne on tall, erect stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium × youngianum is valued for its ornamental foliage and hardy nature, suitable for USDA hardiness zones 4 to 8.
