Elatostema actinodromum

Elatostema actinodromum, commonly known as the star-leaved elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understoreys. The plant features a rosette of narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, which are characteristic of the Elatostema genus.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in rocky or clay-rich soils.
