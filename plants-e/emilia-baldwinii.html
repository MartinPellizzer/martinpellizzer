Emilia baldwinii

Emilia baldwinii, commonly known as the Baldwin's emilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. Emilia baldwinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and easy care requirements.
