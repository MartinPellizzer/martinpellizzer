Euonymus paniculatus

Euonymus paniculatus, commonly known as the fragrant winterberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red berries that appear in the fall and persist through winter. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that turn yellow in autumn before falling. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its seasonal color.

Its berries are an important food source for birds during the winter months.
