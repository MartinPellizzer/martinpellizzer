Elatostema laetum

Elatostema laetum, commonly known as the silverleaf stellaria, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact rosette of broad, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to rock gardens or borders.

Elatostema laetum is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
