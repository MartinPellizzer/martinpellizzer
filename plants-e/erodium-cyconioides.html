Erodium cyconioides

Erodium cyconioides, commonly known as the false crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender stems and small, pink to purple flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain insects.
