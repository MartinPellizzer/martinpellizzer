Eugenioides benthanianum

Eugenioides benthanianum, commonly known as the benthanian eugenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

Eugenioides benthanianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
