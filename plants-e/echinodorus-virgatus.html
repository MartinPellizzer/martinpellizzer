Echinodorus virgatus

Echinodorus virgatus, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate the water. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Echinodorus virgatus is also known for its ability to help control algae growth in aquariums.
