Eulophia milnei var. norlindhii

Eulophia milnei var. norlindhii, commonly known as the Norlindh orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, dark green foliage and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of flowers, each with a distinctive, spurred lip that aids in pollination.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal.
