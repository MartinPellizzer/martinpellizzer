Epidendrum lindleyanum var. centerae

Epidendrum lindleyanum var. centerae, commonly known as the Center's Epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often used in horticultural breeding programs to develop new orchid hybrids.
