Eugenia ochneocarpa

Eugenia ochneocarpa, commonly known as the purple-leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark purple leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to purple as it matures.

Eugenia ochneocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and potential medicinal properties.
