Eremophila rigens

Eremophila rigens, commonly known as the rigid emu bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, upright leaves and spikes of tubular flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance and fire resistance. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to red, attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.

Eremophila rigens is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value in dry climates.
