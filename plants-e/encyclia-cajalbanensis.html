Encyclia cajalbanensis

Encyclia cajalbanensis, commonly known as the Cajalban orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces racemes of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia cajalbanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
