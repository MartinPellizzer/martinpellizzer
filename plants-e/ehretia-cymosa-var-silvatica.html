Ehretia cymosa var. silvatica

Ehretia cymosa var. silvatica, commonly known as the silverleaf ehretia, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery or grayish pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
