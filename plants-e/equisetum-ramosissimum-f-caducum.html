Equisetum ramosissimum f. caducum

Equisetum ramosissimum f. caducum, commonly known as the slender horsetail, is a fern-like plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its jointed, hollow stems covered in small, scale-like structures. The plant produces spore-bearing cones at the top of its stem, which are typically found in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as along stream banks or in wetlands.

This fern-like plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in wet conditions.
