Eriogonum campanulatum subsp. orendense

Eriogonum campanulatum subsp.

orendense, commonly known as the Orend's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is known for its ornamental value and is often found in arid and semi-arid environments.
