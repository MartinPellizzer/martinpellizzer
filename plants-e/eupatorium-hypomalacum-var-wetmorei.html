Eupatorium hypomalacum var. wetmorei

Eupatorium hypomalacum var. wetmorei, commonly known as the wetmore's boneset, is a perennial herb native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale purple flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sometimes endangered species.
