Eriosema flemingioides

Eriosema flemingioides, commonly known as the silverleaf eriosema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous characteristics. The plant features hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months.
