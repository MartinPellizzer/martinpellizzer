Euklisia valida

Euklisia valida, commonly known as the silverleaf dewberry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite and have a distinctive silvery sheen due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

Euklisia valida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
