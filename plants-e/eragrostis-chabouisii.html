Eragrostis chabouisii

Eragrostis chabouisii, commonly known as the Chabouis' lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green color.

Eragrostis chabouisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
