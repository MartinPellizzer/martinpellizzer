Erica sitiens

Erica sitiens, commonly known as the Sitien heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in spring.

Erica sitiens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
