Erica cryptanthera

Erica cryptanthera, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in winter, adding a striking contrast to its evergreen foliage. Erica cryptanthera thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
