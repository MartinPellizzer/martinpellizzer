Eremurus altaicus

Eremurus altaicus, commonly known as the Altai spearwort, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight hairy texture.

Eremurus altaicus is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
