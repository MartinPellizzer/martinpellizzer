Euphorbia cyparissias

Euphorbia cyparissias, commonly known as the Cyprus spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a branched stem with a grayish-green color and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

However, it contains toxic sap and should be handled with care.
