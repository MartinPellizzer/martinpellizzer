Eragrostis planiculmis

Eragrostis planiculmis, commonly known as flatleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, flat leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often hairy and arranged in a panicle.

Eragrostis planiculmis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
