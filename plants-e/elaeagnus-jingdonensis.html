Elaeagnus jingdonensis

Elaeagnus jingdonensis, commonly known as the Chinese silverberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-hairy leaves and small, yellowish-green fruits that are edible for birds and some mammals. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil improvement. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky areas.

Elaeagnus jingdonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
