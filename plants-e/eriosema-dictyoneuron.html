Eriosema dictyoneuron

Eriosema dictyoneuron, commonly known as the velvet leaf eriosema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with a velvety texture due to dense pubescence, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in racemes, with a distinctive yellow or greenish hue.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
