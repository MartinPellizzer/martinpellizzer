Eria yunnanensis

Eria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China, particularly Yunnan province.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, stout rhizome and produces a single, erect stem bearing several lance-shaped leaves. The plant is notable for its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Eria yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
