Erigeron pseudotenuicaulis

Erigeron pseudotenuicaulis, commonly known as the slenderstem fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are usually white or pale blue.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
