Emelista obtusifolia

Emelista obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved emelista, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are broad and rounded, with a soft, hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Emelista obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
