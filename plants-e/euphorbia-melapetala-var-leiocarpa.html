Euphorbia melapetala var. leiocarpa

Euphorbia melapetala var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, branched stem covered in a waxy cuticle that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, scale-like structures, a characteristic adaptation to arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
