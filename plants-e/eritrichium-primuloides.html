Eritrichium primuloides

Eritrichium primuloides, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers are typically arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and high-altitude environments.

Eritrichium primuloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in pollinator habitats.
