Epipactis graminifolia

Epipactis graminifolia, commonly known as the grassy helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and broad, grass-like leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces intricate, greenish-yellow flowers that are often adorned with dark markings. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Epipactis graminifolia is a semi-evergreen species, with its foliage persisting through mild winters.
