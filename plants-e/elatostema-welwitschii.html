Elatostema welwitschii

Elatostema welwitschii, commonly known as the velvetleaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of small, greenish-white blooms arranged in clusters.

Elatostema welwitschii is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in association with other understory plants.
