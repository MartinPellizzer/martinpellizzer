Erysimum asperum var. stellatum

Erysimum asperum var. stellatum, commonly known as the starry wallflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, often with a slightly succulent texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
