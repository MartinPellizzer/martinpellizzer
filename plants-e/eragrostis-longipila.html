Eragrostis longipila

Eragrostis longipila, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its drooping inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are often wind-pollinated.

Eragrostis longipila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its unique appearance.
