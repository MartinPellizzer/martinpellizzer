Elatostema sinense var. longicornutum

Elatostema sinense var. longicornutum, commonly known as the Chinese elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive waxy coating. Its flowers are inconspicuous, usually greenish-white, and appear in clusters.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid conditions, making it a common sight in subtropical regions.
