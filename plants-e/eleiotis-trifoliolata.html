Eleiotis trifoliolata

Eleiotis trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaf eleiotis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
