Ehretia serrata var. obovata

Ehretia serrata var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed ehretia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, obovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
