Eulophia durbanensis

Eulophia durbanensis, commonly known as the Durban eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the subtropical regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its inflorescence consists of several showy, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinct lip.

Eulophia durbanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
