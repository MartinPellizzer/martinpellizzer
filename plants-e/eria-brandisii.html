Eria brandisii

Eria brandisii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters on tall, slender stems. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Eria brandisii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
