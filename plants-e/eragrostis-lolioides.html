Eragrostis lolioides

Eragrostis lolioides, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its inflorescence consists of dense, cylindrical spikelets that are often tinged with reddish hues.

Eragrostis lolioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
