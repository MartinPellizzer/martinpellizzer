Epidendrum fujimorianum

Epidendrum fujimorianum, commonly known as the Fujimori's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Epidendrum fujimorianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
