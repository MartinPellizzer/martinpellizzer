Epidendrum galianoi

Epidendrum galianoi, commonly known as the Galiano Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or limestone soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
