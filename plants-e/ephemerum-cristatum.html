Ephemerum cristatum

Ephemerum cristatum, commonly known as the crested ephemeral, is a rare and short-lived plant species native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, crested growth pattern, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically blooms with small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow during its brief lifespan. It thrives in sandy soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to harsh, dry environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Ephemerum cristatum is considered a vulnerable species.
