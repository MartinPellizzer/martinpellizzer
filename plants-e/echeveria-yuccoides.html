Echeveria yuccoides

Echeveria yuccoides, commonly known as the Yucca-like Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
