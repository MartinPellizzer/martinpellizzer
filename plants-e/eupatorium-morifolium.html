Eupatorium morifolium

Eupatorium morifolium, commonly known as Chinese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that are often hairy and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
