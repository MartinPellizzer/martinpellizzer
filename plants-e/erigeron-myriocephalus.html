Erigeron myriocephalus

Erigeron myriocephalus, commonly known as the blueweed or blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

Erigeron myriocephalus is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.
