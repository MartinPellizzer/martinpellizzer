Eriocaulon angustisepalum

Eriocaulon angustisepalum, commonly known as narrow-sepal eriocaulon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its small, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces delicate white or pale pink flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It typically grows in wet, marshy environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Eriocaulon angustisepalum is known for its ecological importance in maintaining wetland ecosystems.
