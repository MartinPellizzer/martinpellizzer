Eulophia multicolor

Eulophia multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Eulophia multicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
