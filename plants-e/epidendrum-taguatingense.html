Epidendrum taguatingense

Epidendrum taguatingense, commonly known as the Taguatinga Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
