Euryops linifolius

Euryops linifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Euryops linifolius is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
