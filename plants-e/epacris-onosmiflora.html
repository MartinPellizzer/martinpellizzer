Epacris onosmiflora

Epacris onosmiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved epacris, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Epacris onosmiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
