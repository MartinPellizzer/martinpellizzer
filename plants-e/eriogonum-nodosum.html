Eriogonum nodosum

Eriogonum nodosum, commonly known as the clustered buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.
