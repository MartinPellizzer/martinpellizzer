Eriosyce marksiana

Eriosyce marksiana, commonly known as the Marks' eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of white, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid environments. It produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by cactus enthusiasts and botanists.
