Erica pura

Erica pura, commonly known as the sweet heather, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica pura is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it forms dense shrubs.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
