Erysimum nasturtioides

Erysimum nasturtioides, commonly known as the yellow wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress species. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.
