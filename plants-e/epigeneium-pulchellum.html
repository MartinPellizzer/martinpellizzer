Epigeneium pulchellum

Epigeneium pulchellum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries.

Epigeneium pulchellum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along stream banks.
