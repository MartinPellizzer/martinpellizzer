Euphorbia Pekinensis Var. Watanabei

Euphorbia pekinensis var. watanabei is a striking succulent known for its unique, spiky appearance and vibrant coloration. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water to flourish. Its foliage consists of fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that often display shades of green, red, and pink. The plant is also called the "Chinese lantern" due to its resemblance to traditional festive decorations. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap that can cause skin irritation, so care must be taken when handling it.
