Elatostema gracilifolium

Elatostema gracilifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Elatostema gracilifolium is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
