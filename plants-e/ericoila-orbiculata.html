Ericoila orbiculata

Ericoila orbiculata, commonly known as the orbiculate ericoid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or heathland environments.

Ericoila orbiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
