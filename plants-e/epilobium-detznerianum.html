Epilobium detznerianum

Epilobium detznerianum, commonly known as the Detzner's willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as riverbanks and wet meadows. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Epilobium detznerianum is often found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
