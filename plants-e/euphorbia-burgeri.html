Euphorbia burgeri

Euphorbia burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its arid environment. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Euphorbia burgeri is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun conditions.
