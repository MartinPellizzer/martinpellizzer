Epimedium elongatum

Epimedium elongatum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a distinctive, elongated shape.

Epimedium elongatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to shady, moist woodland environments.
