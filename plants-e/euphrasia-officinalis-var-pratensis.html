Euphrasia officinalis var. pratensis

Euphrasia officinalis var. pratensis, commonly known as the meadow eyebright, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported eye health benefits.
