Epidendrum melinoacron

Epidendrum melinoacron, commonly known as the golden spike epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green striped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
