Eucalyptus scyphoidea

Eucalyptus scyphoidea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed form and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically small and clustered in umbels, with white or pale pink petals. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.

This plant is valued for its hardiness and is used in both ecological restoration and horticultural settings.
