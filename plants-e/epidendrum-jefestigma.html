Epidendrum jefestigma

Epidendrum jefestigma, commonly known as the Jefestigma orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features linear to strap-shaped leaves and produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its inflorescence is often branched, with multiple flowers arranged along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
