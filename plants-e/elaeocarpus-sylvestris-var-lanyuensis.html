Elaeocarpus sylvestris var. lanyuensis

Elaeocarpus sylvestris var. lanyuensis, commonly known as the Lanyu elaeocarpus, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive floral structures. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruits are woody capsules that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

This species is adapted to humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest regions.
