Elaeocarpus celebicus

Elaeocarpus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
