Eupatorium amygdalinum f. elegans

Eupatorium amygdalinum f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant false dragonhead, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that contribute to its delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
