Evoista caroliniana

Evoista caroliniana, commonly known as the Carolina lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features strap-like leaves that are typically green and grass-like in appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellowish base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
