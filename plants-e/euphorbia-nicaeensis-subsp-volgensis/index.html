Euphorbia Nicaeensis Subsp. Volgensis

Nicaean spurge, scientifically known as Euphorbia nicaeensis subsp. volgensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. This member of the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae, is distinguished by its unique morphology and ecological significance. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant's bright yellow flowers and milky sap make it both visually appealing and biologically interesting. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Nicaean spurge is increasingly threatened, making conservation efforts essential for its survival.
