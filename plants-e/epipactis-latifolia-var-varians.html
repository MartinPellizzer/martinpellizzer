Epipactis latifolia var. varians

Epipactis latifolia var. varians, commonly known as the broad-leaved helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. This variant is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has a complex root system and is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is a member of the orchid family and exhibits specialized pollination mechanisms.
