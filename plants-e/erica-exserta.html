Erica exserta

Erica exserta, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. Erica exserta produces clusters of pink or white flowers during the summer months.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
