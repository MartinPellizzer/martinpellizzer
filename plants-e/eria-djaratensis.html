Eria djaratensis

Eria djaratensis, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from decaying organic matter in the soil.

Eria djaratensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
