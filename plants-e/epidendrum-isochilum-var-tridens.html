Epidendrum isochilum var. tridens

Epidendrum isochilum var. tridens, commonly known as the Trident Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features upright, branching stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with three prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
