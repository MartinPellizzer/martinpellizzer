Eremostachys boissieriana var. pinnatifida

Eremostachys boissieriana var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the pinnatifid eremostachys, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It is well adapted to harsh environments, with deep root systems that help it survive in poor soil and low moisture conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in xerophytic gardens.
