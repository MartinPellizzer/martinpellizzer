Epipactis helleborine var. rectilinguis

Epipactis helleborine var. rectilinguis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. This variety is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to other helleborine species. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream sides.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is often found in association with other orchid species.
