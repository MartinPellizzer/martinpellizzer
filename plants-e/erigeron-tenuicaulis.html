Erigeron tenuicaulis

Erigeron tenuicaulis, commonly known as slenderstem fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It features slender, hairy stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer.

Its foliage is usually gray-green, with narrow, linear leaves that help reduce water loss in arid conditions.
