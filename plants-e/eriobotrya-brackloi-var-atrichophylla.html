Eriobotrya brackloi var. atrichophylla

Eriobotrya brackloi var. atrichophylla, commonly known as the glossy pear, is a variant of the Chinese white pear species.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by edible, yellow or orange fruits. It is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary uses.
