Euphoria cambodiana

Euphoria cambodiana, commonly known as Cambodian euphoria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with unique floral structures. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture.

Euphoria cambodiana is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
