Erodium absinthoides subsp. elatum

Erodium absinthoides subsp.

elatum, commonly known as tall erodium, is a flowering plant in the family Leguminosae. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are typically dispersed by wind. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in dry, sandy soils.
