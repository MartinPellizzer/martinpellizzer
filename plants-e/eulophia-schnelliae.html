Eulophia schnelliae

Eulophia schnelliae, commonly known as the Schneelie orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce pseudobulbs. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Eulophia schnelliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
