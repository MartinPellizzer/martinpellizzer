Erica perrieri

Erica perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its low-growing shrub form and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica perrieri thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
