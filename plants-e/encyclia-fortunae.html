Encyclia fortunae

Encyclia fortunae, commonly known as the Fortuna's orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Encyclia fortunae thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
