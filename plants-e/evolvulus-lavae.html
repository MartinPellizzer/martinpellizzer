Evolvulus lavae

Evolvulus lavae, commonly known as the Hawaiian blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with small, blue to violet flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and upland areas.

Evolvulus lavae is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and plays a role in local ecosystems.
