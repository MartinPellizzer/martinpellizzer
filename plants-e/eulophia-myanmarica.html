Eulophia myanmarica

Eulophia myanmarica, commonly known as the Myanmar eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces racemose inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

Eulophia myanmarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
