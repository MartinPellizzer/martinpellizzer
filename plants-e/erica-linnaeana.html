Erica linnaeana

Erica linnaeana, commonly known as the Linnaean heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or red, blooming in spring and summer.

Erica linnaeana is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
