Epipactis helleborine var. densiflora

Epipactis helleborine var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces clusters of greenish-yellow to brownish flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with purple or brown markings. It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in woodlands and along stream banks.

This variety is notable for its dense flowering and its role in the ecological interactions of its habitat.
