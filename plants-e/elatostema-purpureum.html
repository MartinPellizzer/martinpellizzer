Elatostema purpureum

Elatostema purpureum, commonly known as purple-stemmed elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elatostemaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant typically grows as a small, herbaceous perennial with a compact, rosette-like structure. Its leaves are usually opposite, with a waxy coating and sometimes a purple or reddish hue.

It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often accompanied by a distinctive, fleshy calyx.
