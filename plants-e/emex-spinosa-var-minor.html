Emex spinosa var. minor

Emex spinosa var. minor, commonly known as lesser emex, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 30 cm in height, featuring slender stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

The seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread across various regions.
