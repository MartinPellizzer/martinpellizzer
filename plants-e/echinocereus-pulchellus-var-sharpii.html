Echinocereus pulchellus var. sharpii

Echinocereus pulchellus var. sharpii, commonly known as the Sharpii Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments. Its stems are ribbed and can store water, allowing it to thrive in low-water conditions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in desert gardens.
