Euodia hortensis

Euodia hortensis, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a small evergreen shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its spiny branches and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, round berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Euodia hortensis thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.
