Epipactis nordeniorum

Epipactis nordeniorum, commonly known as the northern helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to northern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a single, showy flower with a distinctive, spurred lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It produces a small, greenish-brown capsule as its fruit, containing numerous tiny seeds.

Epipactis nordeniorum is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
