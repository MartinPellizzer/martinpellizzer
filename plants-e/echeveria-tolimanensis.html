Echeveria tolimanensis

Echeveria tolimanensis, commonly known as the Tolimanensis Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-gray in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
