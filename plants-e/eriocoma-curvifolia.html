Eriocoma curvifolia

Eriocoma curvifolia, commonly known as the curlyleaf eriocoma, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Eriocoma curvifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and resilience.
