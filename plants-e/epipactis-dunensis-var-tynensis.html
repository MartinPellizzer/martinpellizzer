Epipactis dunensis var. tynensis

Epipactis dunensis var. tynensis, commonly known as the Tynensis helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its striking, greenish-yellow flowers with purple-brown markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as woodlands and rocky slopes. It produces a single spike of flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is known for its specialized pollination mechanism, relying on specific flies for reproduction.
