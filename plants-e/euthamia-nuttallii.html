Euthamia nuttallii

Euthamia nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to dry prairies, meadows, and open woods. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
