Euphorbia schinzii subsp. schinzioides

Euphorbia schinzii subsp.

schinzioides, commonly known as the Schinz's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a short, thick stem with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating.

The plant produces small, yellowish flower clusters known as cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
