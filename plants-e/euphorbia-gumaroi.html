Euphorbia gumaroi

Euphorbia gumaroi, commonly known as the Gumaroi spurge, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are its modified flower structures. Its bark is smooth and often covered in a white, waxy coating.

Euphorbia gumaroi is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand extreme temperatures and minimal rainfall.
