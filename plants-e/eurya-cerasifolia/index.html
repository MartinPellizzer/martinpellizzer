Eurya Cerasifolia

Eurya cerasifolia is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia, known for its vibrant red berries and ornamental value. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories across China and Japan. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, adding visual interest throughout the year. The berries, which appear in late summer, are edible and favored by birds and small mammals. Eurya cerasifolia is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
