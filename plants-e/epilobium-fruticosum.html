Epilobium fruticosum

Epilobium fruticosum, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a perennial shrub native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamin C and other nutrients. The plant has alternate, narrow leaves and produces flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often growing along coastlines and in wetlands.

Epilobium fruticosum is valued for its ecological benefits and has been used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible fruits.
