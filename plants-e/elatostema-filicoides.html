Elatostema filicoides

Elatostema filicoides, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wet, marshy environments and has a creeping habit with numerous stolons. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Elatostema filicoides is known for its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils and is often found in swampy or riparian habitats.
