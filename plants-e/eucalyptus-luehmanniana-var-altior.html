Eucalyptus luehmanniana var. altior

Eucalyptus luehmanniana var. altior, commonly known as the tall mallee eucalypt, is a species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and smooth, often greyish bark. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and typically have a bluish-green hue. It produces small, globular flower buds that open to reveal white or pale pink flowers.

This variety is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-resistant plant.
