Eulophia evrardii

Eulophia evrardii, commonly known as the Evrard's eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stem and clustered inflorescence. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Eulophia evrardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
