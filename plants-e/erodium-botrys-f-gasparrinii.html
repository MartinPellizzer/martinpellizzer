Erodium botrys f. gasparrinii

Erodium botrys f. gasparrinii, commonly known as the Gasparrini's filaree, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.
