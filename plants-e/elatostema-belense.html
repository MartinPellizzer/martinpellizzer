Elatostema belense

Elatostema belense, commonly known as the Brazilian snowflake, is a member of the Elatostemaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to Brazil, typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense inflorescences, and often have a distinctive white or pale coloration.

Elatostema belense is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
