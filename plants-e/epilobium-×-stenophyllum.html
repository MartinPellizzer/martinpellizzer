Epilobium × stenophyllum

Epilobium × stenophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved willowherb, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, often nitrogen-rich soils, such as those found in wet meadows, marshes, and along riverbanks. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter, adorned with lance-shaped, hairy leaves. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Epilobium × stenophyllum is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a characteristic species of calcareous wetlands.
