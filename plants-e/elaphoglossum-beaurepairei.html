Elaphoglossum beaurepairei

Elaphoglossum beaurepairei, commonly known as the Beaurepaire holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically green with a slightly hairy underside. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are usually bioluminescent, a unique feature that aids in attracting nocturnal pollinators.

Its intricate leaf structure and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
