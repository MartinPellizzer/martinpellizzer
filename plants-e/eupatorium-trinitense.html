Eupatorium trinitense

Eupatorium trinitense, commonly known as the Trinidad heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Eupatorium trinitense is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
