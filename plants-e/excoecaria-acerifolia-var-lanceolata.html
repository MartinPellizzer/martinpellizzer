Excoecaria acerifolia var. lanceolata

Excoecaria acerifolia var. lanceolata, commonly known as the flame tree or the red flame tree, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking red or orange flowers.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces capsules as fruits, which contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.
