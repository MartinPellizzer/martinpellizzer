Electranthera mutica var. holotricha

Electranthera mutica var. holotricha, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
