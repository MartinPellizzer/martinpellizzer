Euphorbia fastuosa

Euphorbia fastuosa, commonly known as the showy spurge, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant yellow bracts that surround the small, greenish flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its toxic sap, it requires careful handling to avoid skin irritation.
