Ephemerantha dahlemensis

Ephemerantha dahlemensis, commonly known as the Dahlem ephemeral, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom briefly during the summer months. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its deep root system. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water in its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it is considered a vulnerable species.
