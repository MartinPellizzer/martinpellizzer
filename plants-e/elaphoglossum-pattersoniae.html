Elaphoglossum pattersoniae

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces spore-bearing sori that are arranged in a distinctive, linear pattern along the margins of the fronds.
