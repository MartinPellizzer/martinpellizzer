Euterpe mollissima

Euterpe mollissima, commonly known as the pink-berried palm, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, pink flowers that develop into edible, sweet fruits. Its fruits are an important food source for both wildlife and local human populations.
