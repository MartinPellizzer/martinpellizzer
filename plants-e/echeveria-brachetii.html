Echeveria brachetii

Echeveria brachetii, commonly known as the Shingleleaf Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features dense, compact rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring. It is well-suited for rock gardens and container planting due to its drought tolerance and compact growth habit.

Its foliage and flowering make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
