Epidendrum fruticosum

Epidendrum fruticosum, commonly known as the shrub orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the production of long, pendulous flower spikes. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum fruticosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
