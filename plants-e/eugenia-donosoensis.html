Eugenia donosoensis

Eugenia donosoensis, commonly known as the Donoso's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments at mid to high elevations.

The fruit, which is a small, fleshy berry, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.
