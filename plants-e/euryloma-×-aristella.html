Euryloma × aristella

Euryloma × aristella, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Euryloma grandiflora and Euryloma aristata.

It is a herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in its natural habitat.

Euryloma × aristella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
