Eriocaulon majusculum

Eriocaulon majusculum, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often floating on the water's surface. It thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving waters with high sunlight exposure.

Eriocaulon majusculum is often used in water gardens and as a decorative plant in ponds.
