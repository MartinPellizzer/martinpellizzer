Epipremnum sorsogonense

Epipremnum sorsogonense, commonly known as the Sorsogonense philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, tropical conditions and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its aerial roots allow it to attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for indoor hanging baskets or vertical gardens.

Epipremnum sorsogonense is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and functional plant.
