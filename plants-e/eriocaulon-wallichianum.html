Eriocaulon wallichianum

Eriocaulon wallichianum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered spikelets that bear tiny flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland environments such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and establish in moist soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and wetland ecosystems.
