Eulophia ovalis var. bainesii

Eulophia ovalis var. bainesii, commonly known as the Baines' oval eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate, dark green leaves that are often slightly hairy. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
