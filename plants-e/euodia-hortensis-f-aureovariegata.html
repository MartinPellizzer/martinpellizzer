Euodia hortensis f. aureovariegata

Euodia hortensis f. aureovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated Chinese tallowtree, is a varietal form of the Chinese tallowtree.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, characterized by its striking variegated leaves with golden and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.
