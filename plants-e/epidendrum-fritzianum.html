Epidendrum fritzianum

Epidendrum fritzianum, commonly known as the Fritz's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of light green to white blooms with a subtle fragrance, often appearing in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum fritzianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
