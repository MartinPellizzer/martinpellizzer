Erysimum merxmuelleri

Erysimum merxmuelleri, commonly known as the Mexican wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
