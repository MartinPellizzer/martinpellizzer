Eriosema rigidum

Eriosema rigidum, commonly known as the rigid eriosema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, rigid stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Eriosema rigidum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky habitats.
