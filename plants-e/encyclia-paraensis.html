Encyclia paraensis

Encyclia paraensis, commonly known as the Paraensis orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Encyclia paraensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and conservation efforts.
