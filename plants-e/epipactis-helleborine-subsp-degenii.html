Epipactis helleborine subsp. degenii

Epipactis helleborine subsp.

degenii, commonly known as the Degen's helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and a distinctive, showy inflorescence with greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It produces a single, erect stem that bears several flowers with a spurred lip.

This orchid is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and is often found in association with mycorrhizal fungi.
