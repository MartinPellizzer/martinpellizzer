Euphorbia togakusensis var. ozensis

Euphorbia togakusensis var. ozensis, commonly known as the Ozone euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are characteristic of the Euphorbia genus. It is adapted to grow in dry, rocky environments, making it a hardy species.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
