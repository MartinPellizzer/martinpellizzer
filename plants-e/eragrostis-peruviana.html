Eragrostis peruviana

Eragrostis peruviana, commonly known as Peruvian lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Eragrostis peruviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
