Erica hesseana

Erica hesseana, commonly known as the Hesse heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in spring.

Erica hesseana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in acidic soil conditions.
