Eriocaulon paludosum

Eriocaulon paludosum, commonly known as the swamp spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of white or pale yellow flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in moist soils.

Eriocaulon paludosum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
