Euphorbia ramipressa

Euphorbia ramipressa, commonly known as the desert spurge, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to survive in dry environments. Its leaves are typically small and sometimes reduced to spines, which help reduce water loss.

Euphorbia ramipressa is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, sun-exposed conditions.
