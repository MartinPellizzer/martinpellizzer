Elatostema laevissimum var. puberulum

Elatostema laevissimum var. puberulum, commonly known as the velvetleaf stellaria, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
