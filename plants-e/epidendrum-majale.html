Epidendrum majale

Epidendrum majale, commonly known as the Mexican flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features showy, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant has long, arching stems with fleshy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum majale is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
