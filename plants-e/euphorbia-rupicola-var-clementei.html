Euphorbia rupicola var. clementei

Euphorbia rupicola var. clementei, commonly known as the Clement's rock spurge, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, helping to reduce water loss in dry conditions.

This variety is noted for its resilience and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
