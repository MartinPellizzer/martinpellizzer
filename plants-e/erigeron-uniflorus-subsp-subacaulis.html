Erigeron uniflorus subsp. subacaulis

Erigeron uniflorus subsp.

subacaulis, commonly known as the slender fleabane, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features a low, spreading growth habit with hairy stems and small, daisy-like white flowers. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a bluish-green hue.

This subspecies is characterized by its reduced stature and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
