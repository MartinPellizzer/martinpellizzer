Euphorbia myrsinites subsp. rechingeri

Euphorbia myrsinites subsp.

rechingeri, commonly known as the silver leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dense, silver-white foliage that reflects sunlight, giving it a striking appearance.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique flower structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
