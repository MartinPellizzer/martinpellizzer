Elaphoglossum longifolium var. tunguraguensis

Elaphoglossum longifolium var. tunguraguensis, commonly known as the Tunguragua holly fern, is a variant of the long-leaf holly fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern characterized by its delicate, lanceolate leaves that are typically bright green. The fronds are usually unifacial, with a smooth, glossy surface and a distinct midrib. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
