Epidendrum isthmi

Epidendrum isthmi, commonly known as the Isthmian orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of orchids.
