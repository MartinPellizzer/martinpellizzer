Epidendrum bricenoorum

Epidendrum bricenoorum, commonly known as the Briceno's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a waxy texture, aiding in water retention.

Epidendrum bricenoorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
