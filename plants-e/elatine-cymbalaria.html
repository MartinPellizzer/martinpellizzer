Elatine cymbalaria

Elatine cymbalaria, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating on the surface of water. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Elatine cymbalaria thrives in moist or aquatic environments, such as ponds, ditches, and wet meadows.

Its rapid growth and ability to colonize water bodies make it an important component of aquatic ecosystems.
