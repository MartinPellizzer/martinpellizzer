Eupatorium schimperianum

Eupatorium schimperianum, commonly known as the Schimper's boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct, aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Eupatorium schimperianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
