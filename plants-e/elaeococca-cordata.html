Elaeococca cordata

Elaeococca cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. Elaeococca cordata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
