Ecbolium linneanum var. laetevirens

Ecbolium linneanum var. laetevirens, commonly known as the yellow ecbolium, is a variant of the ecbolium plant characterized by its bright yellow foliage.

It is a perennial herb native to certain regions of Europe, particularly in the Mediterranean area. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and has small, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are usually small and greenish-yellow, appearing in clusters.

The variety laetevirens is distinguished by its consistently vibrant yellow coloration throughout the growing season.
