Elatostema pedunculatum var. puberulum

Elatostema pedunculatum var. puberulum, commonly known as the velvet leaf stellaria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves that are densely covered with fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers with five petals, typically arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a common ground cover in its native habitat.
