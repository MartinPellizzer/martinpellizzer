Evolvulus echioides

Evolvulus echioides, commonly known as the curly mistletoe, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, sunny environments. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
