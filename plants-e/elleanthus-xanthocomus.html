Elleanthus xanthocomus

Elleanthus xanthocomus, commonly known as the yellow daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disc of smaller flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to full sun exposure. Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Elleanthus xanthocomus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
