Euphorbia brevirama var. supraterra

Euphorbia brevirama var. supraterra, commonly known as the dwarf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing form with thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow cyathia, which are its characteristic flower-like structures. Its leaves are typically narrow and may have a slight waxy coating to reduce water loss.

It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its hardy nature and minimal care requirements.
