Embelia ribes var. garciniifolia

Embelia ribes var. garciniifolia, commonly known as the Garcinia-leafed embelia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
