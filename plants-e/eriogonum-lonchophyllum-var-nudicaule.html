Eriogonum lonchophyllum var. nudicaule

Eriogonum lonchophyllum var. nudicaule, commonly known as the naked-stemmed buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. This variety is distinguished by its lack of leafy stems, giving it a unique appearance among other buckwheat species.
