Elaphoglossum macahense

Elaphoglossum macahense, commonly known as the Macahue fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that typically grow in a rosette or clustered formation. Elaphoglossum macahense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
