Ehretia phillipsonii

Ehretia phillipsonii, commonly known as the Philippine ehretia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers clustered in terminal panicles, followed by small, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.
