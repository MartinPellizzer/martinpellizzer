Euterpe decurrens

Euterpe decurrens, commonly known as the Brazilian palm or buriti palm, is a tall, slender palm native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences that bear both male and female flowers. Its fruit is a yellowish, fibrous drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is often found in wetland and riparian habitats, where it plays an important ecological role.
