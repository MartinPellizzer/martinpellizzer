Epidendrum oncidioides var. mooreanum

Epidendrum oncidioides var. mooreanum, commonly known as the Moorean Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and compact size, making it suitable for both hanging baskets and tabletop displays.
