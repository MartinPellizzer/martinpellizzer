Epidendrum bractescens

Epidendrum bractescens, commonly known as the Orange-flowered Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers and upright, branching growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its pseudobulbs are segmented and covered in a thick layer of velvety tissue.

The flowers are borne on terminal inflorescences and are known for their showy, fragrant appearance.
