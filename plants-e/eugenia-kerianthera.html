Eugenia kerianthera

Eugenia kerianthera, commonly known as the kerianthera myrtle, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Eugenia genus.
