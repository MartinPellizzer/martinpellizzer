Eugenioides martinicense

Eugenioides martinicense, commonly known as the Martinique guava, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the islands of Martinique and Guadeloupe. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit, similar to a guava, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
