Encyclia hollandae

Encyclia hollandae, commonly known as the Holland's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, which is a unique adaptation to its tropical environment. Its leaves are strap-like and evergreen, providing a lush appearance throughout the year.

Encyclia hollandae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
