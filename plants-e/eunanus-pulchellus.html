Eunanus pulchellus

Eunanus pulchellus, commonly known as the beautiful spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has smooth, green, succulent leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Eunanus pulchellus is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
