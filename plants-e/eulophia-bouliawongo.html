Eulophia bouliawongo

Eulophia bouliawongo, commonly known as the Bouliawong orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce pseudobulbs. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and exhibit a range of colors, often with intricate patterns.

Eulophia bouliawongo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
