Elemifera sylvatica

Elemifera sylvatica, commonly known as the forest elemi tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to temperate regions.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and flowers that produce a resinous sap. The plant belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is known for its medicinal properties. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a smooth margin and a glossy surface.
