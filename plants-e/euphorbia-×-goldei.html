Euphorbia × goldei

Euphorbia × goldei, commonly known as the golden crown euphorbia, is a hybrid plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, spineless stems and vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
