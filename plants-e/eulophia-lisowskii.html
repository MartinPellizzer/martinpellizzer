Eulophia lisowskii

Eulophia lisowskii, commonly known as the Lisowskii's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and several fleshy, elliptical pseudobulbs. The plant produces racemose inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

Eulophia lisowskii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticultural collections.
