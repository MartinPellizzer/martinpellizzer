Eupatorium variegatum

Eupatorium variegatum, commonly known as the variegated thoroughwort, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking variegated foliage with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Eupatorium variegatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
