Eulophia phillipsiae

Eulophia phillipsiae, commonly known as the Phillip's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and elongated leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with dark markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Eulophia phillipsiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
