Erblichia odorata var. mollis

Erblichia odorata var. mollis, commonly known as the fragrant velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are soft and velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered together. Its aromatic leaves emit a sweet, spicy scent, which is most noticeable when crushed.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
