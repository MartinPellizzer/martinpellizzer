Euphorbia sciadophila f. orbicularis

Euphorbia sciadophila f. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular euphorbia, is a unique variant of the species characterized by its distinctive orbicular or rounded leaf shape.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features a short, thick stem with clustered, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its flowers are small and yellowish, typically emerging in clusters, and it is known for its drought tolerance and ability to store water in its leaves.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unusual appearance and low maintenance requirements.
