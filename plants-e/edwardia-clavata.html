Edwardia clavata

Edwardia clavata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in a compact, upright form and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.

Edwardia clavata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
