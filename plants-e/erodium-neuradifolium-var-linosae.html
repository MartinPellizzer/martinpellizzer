Erodium neuradifolium var. linosae

Erodium neuradifolium var. linosae, commonly known as the slender erodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that are arranged in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
