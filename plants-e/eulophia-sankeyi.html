Eulophia sankeyi

Eulophia sankeyi, commonly known as the Sankey's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with several pale yellow to greenish-white blooms, often adorned with subtle markings. Eulophia sankeyi thrives in moist, shaded habitats and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
