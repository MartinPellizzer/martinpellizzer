Embreea

Embreea, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Embreea is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
