Epidendrum rothii

Epidendrum rothii, commonly known as the Red-tipped Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that contrast with its dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum rothii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
