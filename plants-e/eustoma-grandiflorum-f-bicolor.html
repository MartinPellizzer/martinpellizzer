Eustoma grandiflorum f. bicolor

Eustoma grandiflorum f. bicolor, commonly known as the double-flowered lisianthus, is a hybrid cultivar of the lisianthus plant.

It is characterized by its unique bicolor flowers, which typically feature two distinct colors on the same bloom. The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are popular in floral arrangements.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
