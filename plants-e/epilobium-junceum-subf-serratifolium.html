Epilobium junceum subf. serratifolium

Epilobium junceum subf.

serratifolium, commonly known as the serratedleaf willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments.

The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually pink to purple in color.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude habitats.
