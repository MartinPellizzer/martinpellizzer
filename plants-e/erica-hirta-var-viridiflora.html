Erica hirta var. viridiflora

Erica hirta var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered heath, is a low-growing shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, bright green flowers that bloom in spring, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its evergreen nature. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
