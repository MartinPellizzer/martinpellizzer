Ericameria nauseosa var. glabrata

Ericameria nauseosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth goldenbush, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, yellowish-green foliage and produces small, fluffy yellow flowers in the spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is adapted to arid environments.

Its leaves are typically hairless, giving it the "glabrata" designation in its scientific name.
