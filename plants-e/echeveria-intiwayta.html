Echeveria intiwayta

Echeveria intiwayta, commonly known as the Intiwayta Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, and often bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces striking pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
