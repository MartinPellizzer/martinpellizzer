Elaeagnus difficilis var. brevistyla

Elaeagnus difficilis var. brevistyla, commonly known as the narrow-styled silverberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-hairy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
