Etlingera Maingayi

Etlingera maingayi is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. This species is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and botanists. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
