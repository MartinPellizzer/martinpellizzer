Eulophia minimiflora

Eulophia minimiflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, linear leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are arranged in a spike and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Eulophia minimiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
