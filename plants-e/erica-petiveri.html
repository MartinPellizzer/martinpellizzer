Erica petiveri

Erica petiveri, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica petiveri is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

It is also valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
