Eleusine africana

Eleusine africana, commonly known as African lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically green or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Eleusine africana is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
