Erica Penicilliformis Var. Chrysantha

Erica penicilliformis var. chrysantha is a rare and striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers, it adds a splash of color to its natural habitat during the summer months. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species in the broader horticultural community.
