Eragrostis pycnostachys

Eragrostis pycnostachys, commonly known as narrow-leaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences, which are typically green to reddish in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in dry climates.

Eragrostis pycnostachys is often used in landscaping and erosion control due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
