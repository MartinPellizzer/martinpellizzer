Epiphyton allenii

Epiphyton allenii, commonly known as the Allen's epiphyte, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. This species typically thrives in humid, tropical environments, often found in the canopies of rainforests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often white or pale in color, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
