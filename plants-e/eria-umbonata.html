Eria umbonata

Eria umbonata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its erect, compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. Eria umbonata thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in tropical gardens.
