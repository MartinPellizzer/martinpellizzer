Euodia ternata

Euodia ternata, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces a resin that has been traditionally used in medicine and perfumery. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Euodia ternata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
