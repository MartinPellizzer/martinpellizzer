Erica tenuis

Erica tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in spring.

Erica tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained, acidic conditions.
