Euonymus hupehensis var. brevipedunculatus

Euonymus hupehensis var. brevipedunculatus, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for birds. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and year-round visual interest.
