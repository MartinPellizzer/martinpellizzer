Epidendrum evelynae

Epidendrum evelynae, commonly known as the Evelyn's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are characterized by their vibrant colors, often ranging from yellow to orange.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
