Eriotheca uniflora

Eriotheca uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower eriotheca, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a fine, hairy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
