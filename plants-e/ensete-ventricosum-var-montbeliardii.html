Ensete ventricosum var. montbeliardii

Ensete ventricosum var. montbeliardii, commonly known as the Montbeliard banana, is a variant of the African giant banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of yellow, fleshy fruits that are typically consumed when ripe. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is cultivated for its edible fruit and is also valued for its ornamental appearance.
