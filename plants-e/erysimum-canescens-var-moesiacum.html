Erysimum canescens var. moesiacum

Erysimum canescens var. moesiacum, commonly known as the Moesia wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features erect, branched stems covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, lanceolate, and covered in a similar fuzzy coating.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
