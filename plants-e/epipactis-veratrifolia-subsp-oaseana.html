Epipactis veratrifolia subsp. oaseana

Epipactis veratrifolia subsp.

oaseana, commonly known as the Oasean helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe. It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a single, showy flower with a distinctive yellow and green lip.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and wet meadows. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanism involving specific insects.

Its subspecific name, *oaseana*, refers to its occurrence in areas with aquatic or marshy environments.
