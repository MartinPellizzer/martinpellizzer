Eriocaulon matopense

Eriocaulon matopense, commonly known as the Matopense spikegrass, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes various aquatic and semi-aquatic grasses. The plant features slender, erect stems that bear dense clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often submerged or floating in water.

Eriocaulon matopense plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
