Elaeagnus difficilis var. difficilis

Elaeagnus difficilis var. difficilis, commonly known as silverberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray bark and small, fragrant flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Elaeagnus difficilis var. difficilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
