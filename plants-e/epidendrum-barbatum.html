Epidendrum barbatum

Epidendrum barbatum, commonly known as the hairy epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, violet to white blooms. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, often with a hairy texture, which gives it its common name.

Epidendrum barbatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
