Ericoila aestiva

Ericoila aestiva, commonly known as the summer heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, leathery leaves. It produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months.

Ericoila aestiva thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
