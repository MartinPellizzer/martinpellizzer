Euphrasia tricolor

Euphrasia tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that display three distinct colors: yellow, white, and purple, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Euphrasia tricolor is part of the Scrophulariaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
