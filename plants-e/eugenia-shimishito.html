Eugenia shimishito

Eugenia shimishito, commonly known as the Japanese snowbell, is a small evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its delicate blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Eugenia shimishito is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscape design.
