Emilia reddyi

Emilia reddyi, commonly known as the Reddy's emilia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Emilia reddyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
