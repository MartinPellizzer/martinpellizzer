Eugenia spissifolia

Eugenia spissifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
