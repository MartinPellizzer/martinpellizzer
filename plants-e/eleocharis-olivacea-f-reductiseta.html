Eleocharis olivacea f. reductiseta

Eleocharis olivacea f. reductiseta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, reduced spikelets and narrow, linear leaves. This fern-like plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in humid conditions. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in dense clusters, typical of the Cyperaceae family.

The plant is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils.
