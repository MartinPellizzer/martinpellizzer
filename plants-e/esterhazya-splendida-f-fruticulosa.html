Esterhazya splendida f. fruticulosa

Esterhazya splendida f. fruticulosa, commonly known as the splendid esterhazya, is a unique cultivar of the esterhazya plant, characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit.

It features dense foliage with bright, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, making it an ornamental choice for gardens. The plant is native to specific regions in Central Asia and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. It is noted for its resilience to drought and moderate frost, making it suitable for a variety of climates.

Its botanical interest lies in its distinct morphology and potential for use in horticultural displays.
