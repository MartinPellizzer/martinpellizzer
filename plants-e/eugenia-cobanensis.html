Eugenia cobanensis

Eugenia cobanensis, commonly known as the Coban beauty, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a pleasant aromatic scent.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.
