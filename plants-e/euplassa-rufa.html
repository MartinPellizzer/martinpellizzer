Euplassa rufa

Euplassa rufa, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red veins contrasting with its greenish-white petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a unique blend of colors that range from pink to deep red.

Euplassa rufa is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators in its native habitats.
