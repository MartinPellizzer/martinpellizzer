Eragrostis purpusii

Eragrostis purpusii, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and produces fluffy, pinkish-white inflorescences that resemble small clouds. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas. It is drought-tolerant and can withstand moderate grazing.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid and semi-arid regions.
