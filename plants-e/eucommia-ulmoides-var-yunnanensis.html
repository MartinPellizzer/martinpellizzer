Eucommia ulmoides var. yunnanensis

Eucommia ulmoides var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Eucommia, is a variant of the Chinese boxwood tree native to southwestern China.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its thick, bark-like trunk and alternate, simple leaves. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine, where its bark is used to treat hypertension and other ailments.

It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
