Epidendrum stictoglossum

Epidendrum stictoglossum, commonly known as the red-lipped epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are borne on branched inflorescences and can last for several weeks.

Epidendrum stictoglossum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
