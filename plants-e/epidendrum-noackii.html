Epidendrum noackii

Epidendrum noackii, commonly known as the Noack's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Epidendrum noackii is known for its ability to bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.
