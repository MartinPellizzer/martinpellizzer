Elatostema parvulum

Elatostema parvulum, commonly known as the small-leaved elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and often has small, white or pale green flowers. Its stems are often prostrate or creeping, allowing it to spread along the ground.

Elatostema parvulum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
