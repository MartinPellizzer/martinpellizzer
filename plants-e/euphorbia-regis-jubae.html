Euphorbia regis-jubae

Euphorbia regis-jubae, commonly known as the royal crown euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branched stem with a crown-like appearance, often adorned with small, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
