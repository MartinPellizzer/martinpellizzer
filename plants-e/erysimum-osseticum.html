Erysimum osseticum

Erysimum osseticum, commonly known as the Ossetian wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers. It has basal leaves that are lobed and may have a slightly hairy texture.

Erysimum osseticum is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cooler, temperate climates.
