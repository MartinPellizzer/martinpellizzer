Epidendrum guanacasense

Epidendrum guanacasense, commonly known as the Guanacaste Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically arranged in racemes. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often covered in a fine pubescence.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
