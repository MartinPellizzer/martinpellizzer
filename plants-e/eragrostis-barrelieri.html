Eragrostis barrelieri

Eragrostis barrelieri, commonly known as Barrelier's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Eragrostis barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
