Epidendrum aquaticoides

Epidendrum aquaticoides, commonly known as the water orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It thrives in humid environments, often growing on trees or rocks near water sources. The plant features long, slender stems and clusters of small, white or greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Epidendrum aquaticoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
