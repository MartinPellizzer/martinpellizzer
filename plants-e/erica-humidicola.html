Erica humidicola

Erica humidicola, commonly known as the wetland heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is well adapted to coastal and mountainous regions with high humidity.

Erica humidicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
