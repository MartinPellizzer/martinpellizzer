Eugenia francavilleana

Eugenia francavilleana, commonly known as the French Guiana eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly French Guiana and parts of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within lowland rainforests.

Eugenia francavilleana is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
