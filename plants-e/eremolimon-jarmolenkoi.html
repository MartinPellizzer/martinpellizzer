Eremolimon jarmolenkoi

Eremolimon jarmolenkoi, commonly known as the Armenian rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of Armenia and Turkey.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers. Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is occasionally cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
