Erica tradouwensis

Erica tradouwensis, commonly known as the Tradouw heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. This evergreen shrub typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal areas. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, dark-colored berries.

Erica tradouwensis is adapted to withstand coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
