Ericoides comosum

Ericoides comosum, commonly known as the hairy heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Ericoides comosum thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and coastal areas.

It is a hardy plant that is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
