Echeveria gigantea

Echeveria gigantea, commonly known as the giant jade plant, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in diameter. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
