Equisetum azoricum

Equisetum azoricum, commonly known as the Azorean horsetail, is a perennial plant native to the Azores.

It is characterized by its slender, jointed stems and whorled arrangement of small, needle-like leaves. The plant belongs to the Equisetaceae family and is closely related to other horsetails. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or calcareous soils.

Equisetum azoricum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms in its native ecosystems.
