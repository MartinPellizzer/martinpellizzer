Echinocactus gibbosus f. fennellii

Echinocactus gibbosus f. fennellii, commonly known as the Fennell's Hedgehog Cactus, is a distinctive cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems and prominent, spiny areoles. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
