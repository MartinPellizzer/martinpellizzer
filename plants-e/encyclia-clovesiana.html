Encyclia clovesiana

Encyclia clovesiana, commonly known as the Clove Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Encyclia clovesiana thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and distinctive floral scent.
