Eriocaulon nosoriense

Eriocaulon nosoriense, commonly known as the narrow-leaved papyrus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
