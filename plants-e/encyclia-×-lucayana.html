Encyclia × lucayana

Encyclia × lucayana, commonly known as the Lucayan orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow throat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia × lucayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
