Epidendrum riofrioae

Epidendrum riofrioae, commonly known as the Rio Frio orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. Its blooming period typically occurs during the spring and early summer.

Epidendrum riofrioae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
