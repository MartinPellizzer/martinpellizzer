Equilabium petiolare

Equilabium petiolare, commonly known as the slender-stalked orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated petioles that support its lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
