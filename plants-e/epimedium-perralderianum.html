Epimedium perralderianum

Epimedium perralderianum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that remains vibrant throughout the year. The plant features showy, bright yellow flowers that emerge in early spring, often before the leaves are fully developed. Epimedium perralderianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, woodland conditions.
