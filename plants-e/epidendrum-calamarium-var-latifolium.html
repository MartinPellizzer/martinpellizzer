Epidendrum calamarium var. latifolium

Epidendrum calamarium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity to flourish.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
