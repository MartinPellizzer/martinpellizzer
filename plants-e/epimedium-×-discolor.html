Epimedium × discolor

Epimedium × discolor, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features attractive, shield-shaped leaves that are typically bronze to reddish in color, especially in the spring. The plant produces delicate, upright racemes of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves fully expand. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium × discolor is valued for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
