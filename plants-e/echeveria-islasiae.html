Echeveria islasiae

Echeveria islasiae, commonly known as the Isla de Isla Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple-pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
