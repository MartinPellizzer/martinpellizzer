Echites pubiflorus

Echites pubiflorus, commonly known as the hairy-flowered echites, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in whorled arrangements along the stems.

Echites pubiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
