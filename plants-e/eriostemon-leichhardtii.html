Eriostemon leichhardtii

Eriostemon leichhardtii, commonly known as the Leichhardt's eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in groups of three. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
