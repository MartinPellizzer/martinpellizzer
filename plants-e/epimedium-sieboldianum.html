Epimedium sieboldianum

Epimedium sieboldianum, commonly known as the Japanese barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, fern-like leaves that emerge in early spring. Epimedium sieboldianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
