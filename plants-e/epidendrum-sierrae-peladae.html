Epidendrum sierrae-peladae

Epidendrum sierrae-peladae, commonly known as the Sierra Pelada Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
