Erucaria crassifolia

Erucaria crassifolia, commonly known as the thick-leaved erucaria, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found growing in rocky soils or on cliffs.

Its unique adaptations make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for xerophytic gardens.
