Erechtites hieraciifolius

Erechtites hieraciifolius, commonly known as the curlycup gumweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, curly leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in a compact cluster at the end of each stem. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and along roadsides.

Erechtites hieraciifolius is a perennial species that is both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
