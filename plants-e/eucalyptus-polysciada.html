Eucalyptus polysciada

Eucalyptus polysciada, commonly known as the narrow-leaf malleeflower, is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is part of the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known eucalyptus species. Its bark is often flaky and can vary in color from white to grey.

Eucalyptus polysciada is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in mallee woodlands and heathlands.
