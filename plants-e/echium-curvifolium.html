Echium curvifolium

Echium curvifolium, commonly known as the bent-leaved fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and curved, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Echium curvifolium is often found in regions with mild, Mediterranean climates.
