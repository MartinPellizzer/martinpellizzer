Eriocaulon andongense

Eriocaulon andongense, commonly known as the Andong marsh plant, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually greenish-white in color.

Eriocaulon andongense plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter for microorganisms and invertebrates.
