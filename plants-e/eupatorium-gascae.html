Eupatorium gascae

Eupatorium gascae, commonly known as the slender boneset, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly rough texture.
