Epidendrum luckei

Epidendrum luckei, commonly known as the Lucky Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It prefers warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum luckei is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
