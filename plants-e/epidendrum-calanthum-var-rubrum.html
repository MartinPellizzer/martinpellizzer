Epidendrum calanthum var. rubrum

Epidendrum calanthum var. rubrum, commonly known as the red-flowered epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers. The plant typically has strap-like, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
