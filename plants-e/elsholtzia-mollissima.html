Elsholtzia mollissima

Elsholtzia mollissima, commonly known as woolly elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid climates.
