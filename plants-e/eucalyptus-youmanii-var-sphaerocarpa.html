Eucalyptus youmanii var. sphaerocarpa

Eucalyptus youmanii var. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular eucalyptus, is a unique variant of the eucalyptus genus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive globular-shaped inflorescences, which are a striking feature of the plant. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, often greyish upper surface. This species is known for its aromatic oils, which contribute to its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
