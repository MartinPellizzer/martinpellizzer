Eugenia decipiens

Eugenia decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often mistaken for other species due to their appearance. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Eugenia decipiens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
