Encyclia Widgrenii

Encyclia widgrenii is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. This unique plant thrives in humid, shaded environments where it clings to tree trunks and branches. Its striking flowers, often in shades of white and yellow, make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Encyclia widgrenii is highly endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
