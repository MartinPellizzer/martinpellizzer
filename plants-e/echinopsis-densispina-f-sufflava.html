Echinopsis densispina f. sufflava

Echinopsis densispina f. sufflava, commonly known as the Orange Ghost Cactus, is a fascinating succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to yellow spines. The plant produces striking, large flowers that range from yellow to orange, often blooming at night. Its fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
