Euonymus dielsianus var. fertilis

Euonymus dielsianus var. fertilis, commonly known as the fertilis euonymus, is a variant of the euonymus genus native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
