Edgeworthia albiflora

Edgeworthia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its graceful, arching branches and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has oval, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its pale blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Edgeworthia albiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
