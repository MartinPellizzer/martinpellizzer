Echium pulchrum

Echium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and hairy, spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces a tall, branching stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Echium pulchrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
