Eulalia Quadrinervis

Eulalia grass, scientifically known as Eulalia quadrinervis, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America. This plant is prized for its elegant, feathery plumes that sway gracefully in the wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its ability to add texture and movement to any setting enhances its appeal among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, Eulalia quadrinervis is a versatile addition to modern botanical collections.
