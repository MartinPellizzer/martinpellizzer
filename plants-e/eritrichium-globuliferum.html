Eritrichium globuliferum

Eritrichium globuliferum, commonly known as the globular eritrichium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its unique globular seed heads are a distinctive feature, adding to its ornamental value.

Eritrichium globuliferum is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
