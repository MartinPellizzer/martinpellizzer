Elsholtzia densa var. calycocarpa

Elsholtzia densa var. calycocarpa, commonly known as the California elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, aromatic stems and small, opposite leaves with a grayish-green color. Its flowers are usually pale blue to purple and are clustered in dense, terminal spikes.

It is often found in chaparral and sagebrush habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.
