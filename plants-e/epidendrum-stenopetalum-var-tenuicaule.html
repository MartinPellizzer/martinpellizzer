Epidendrum stenopetalum var. tenuicaule

Epidendrum stenopetalum var. tenuicaule, commonly known as the narrow-leaved epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom sequentially along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
