Erysimum patisiliquosum

Erysimum patisiliquosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
