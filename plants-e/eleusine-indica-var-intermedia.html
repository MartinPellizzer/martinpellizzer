Eleusine indica var. intermedia

Eleusine indica var. intermedia, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its adaptability makes it a common weed in various environments.
