Epidendrum hawkesii

Epidendrum hawkesii, commonly known as the Hawkes' orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features upright, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Epidendrum hawkesii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
