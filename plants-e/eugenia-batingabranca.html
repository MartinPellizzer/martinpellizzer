Eugenia batingabranca

Eugenia batingabranca, commonly known as the Batinga branca myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.
