Epidendrum witherspooniorum

Epidendrum witherspooniorum, commonly known as the Witherspoon's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
