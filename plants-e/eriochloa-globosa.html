Eriochloa globosa

Eriochloa globosa, commonly known as globular grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Eriochloa globosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
