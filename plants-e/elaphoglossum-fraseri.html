Elaphoglossum fraseri

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically divided into narrow, serrated segments. The plant produces small, yellowish spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. Its botanical features make it a distinctive and relatively rare fern in its native habitat.
