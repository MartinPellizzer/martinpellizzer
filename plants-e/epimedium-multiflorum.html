Epimedium multiflorum

Epimedium multiflorum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, shield-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium multiflorum is valued for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
