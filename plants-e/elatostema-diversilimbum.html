Elatostema diversilimbum

Elatostema diversilimbum, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its succulent leaves help it store water, adapting it to arid environments.

Elatostema diversilimbum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
