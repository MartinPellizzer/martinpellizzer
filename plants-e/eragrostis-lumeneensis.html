Eragrostis lumeneensis

Eragrostis lumeneensis, commonly known as the Lumeneen grass, is a perennial grass species native to specific regions of Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, linear leaves with a rough texture and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its fibrous root system and wind-pollinated flowers.
