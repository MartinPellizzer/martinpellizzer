Epidendrum cornanthera

Epidendrum cornanthera, commonly known as the Cornish orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is a herbaceous plant with tall, erect stems that bear clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically pink or purple and have a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
