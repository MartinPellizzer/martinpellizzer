Erythroxylum pringlei

Erythroxylum pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its small, green leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains tiny, black seeds.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
