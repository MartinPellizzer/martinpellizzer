Eugenia pustulescens

Eugenia pustulescens, commonly known as the rough-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

Eugenia pustulescens is often found in humid, shaded environments within rainforests.
