Ervum amoenum var. pallidum

Ervum amoenum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale yellow vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, trailing stems with small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
