Erythrococca atrovirens var. flaccida

Erythrococca atrovirens var. flaccida, commonly known as the flaccid erythrococca, is a variant of the erythrococca plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in yellow or orange hues. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with a rough, fissured bark.

This variant is noted for its unique leaf morphology and is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological adaptations.
