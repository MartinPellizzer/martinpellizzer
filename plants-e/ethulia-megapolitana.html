Ethulia megapolitana

Ethulia megapolitana, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Ethulia megapolitana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
