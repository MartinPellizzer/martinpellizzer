Euodia lucida

Euodia lucida, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark brown bark that exudes a resin when wounded. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by small, round fruits that contain smooth, dark seeds. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

Euodia lucida is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
