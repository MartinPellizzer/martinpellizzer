Eriodendron grandiflorum

Eriodendron grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered eriodendron, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
