Euphorbia splendens var. bevilaniensis

Euphorbia splendens var. bevilaniensis, commonly known as the Bevilani's crown of thorns, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often tinged with red, and it produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and is well adapted to arid environments. It is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.

However, it contains toxic sap and should be handled with care.
