Echinocactus stuemeri var. tilcarensis

Echinocactus stuemeri var. tilcarensis, commonly known as the Tilcarensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise spiny appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for desert gardens and rockeries.
