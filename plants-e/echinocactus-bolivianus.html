Echinocactus bolivianus

Echinocactus bolivianus, commonly known as the Bolivian hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny, ribbed stems and thick, fleshy leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
