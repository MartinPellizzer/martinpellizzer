Ehrharta eburnea

Ehrharta eburnea, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-blue foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in panicles. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
