Epidendrum physodes

Epidendrum physodes, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with showy, orchid-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum physodes is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
