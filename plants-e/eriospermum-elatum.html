Eriospermum elatum

Eriospermum elatum, commonly known as the silverleaf eriosperm, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.

Eriospermum elatum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
