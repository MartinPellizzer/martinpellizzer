Echinofossulocactus multiareolatus

Echinofossulocactus multiareolatus, commonly known as the multi-areola echinofossulocactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are ribbed and covered with spines, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
