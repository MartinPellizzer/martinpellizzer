Espeletia × pozoensis

Espeletia × pozoensis, commonly known as the Pozoensis heath, is a hybrid plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces striking inflorescences with showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
