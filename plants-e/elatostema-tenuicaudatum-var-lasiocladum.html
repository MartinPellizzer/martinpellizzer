Elatostema tenuicaudatum var. lasiocladum

Elatostema tenuicaudatum var. lasiocladum, commonly known as the silky leaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, elliptical leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, producing yellow or white petals.

The varietal form, lasiocladum, is distinguished by its dense, silky pubescence on the leaves and stems.
