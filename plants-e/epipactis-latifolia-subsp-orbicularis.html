Epipactis latifolia subsp. orbicularis

Epipactis latifolia subsp.

orbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leaved helleborine, is a herbaceous perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It produces a single spike of flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

The subspecies orbicularis is distinguished by its more rounded leaf shape compared to other subspecies of Epipactis latifolia.
