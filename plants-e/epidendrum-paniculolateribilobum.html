Epidendrum paniculolateribilobum

Epidendrum paniculolateribilobum, commonly known as the coral-tipped epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of small, brightly colored flowers. Its foliage consists of broad, glossy leaves that are often dark green in color.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
