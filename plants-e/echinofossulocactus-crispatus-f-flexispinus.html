Echinofossulocactus crispatus f. flexispinus

Echinofossulocactus crispatus f. flexispinus, commonly known as the curled spiny cactus, is a unique cactus species native to specific regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in prominent, spiny tubercles, giving it a rugged appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species in the wild.
