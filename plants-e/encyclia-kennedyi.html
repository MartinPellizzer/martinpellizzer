Encyclia kennedyi

Encyclia kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure with a strikingly colored spur. The plant typically grows in rocky or wooded habitats, thriving in well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinct, elongated lip.

Encyclia kennedyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
