Epidendrum gratiosum var. linearifolium

Epidendrum gratiosum var. linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaved epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
