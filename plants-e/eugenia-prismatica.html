Eugenia prismatica

Eugenia prismatica, commonly known as the prism tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to red when mature.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic foliage and potential medicinal properties.
