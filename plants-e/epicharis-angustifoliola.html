Epicharis angustifoliola

Epicharis angustifoliola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved epicharis, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible and have a slightly sweet, tangy flavor.
