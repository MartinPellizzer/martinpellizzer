Epilobium × heerianum

Epilobium × heerianum, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its erect or spreading growth habit. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and disturbed sites.

Epilobium × heerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for pollinators.
