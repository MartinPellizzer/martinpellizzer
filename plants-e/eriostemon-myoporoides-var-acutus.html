Eriostemon myoporoides var. acutus

Eriostemon myoporoides var. acutus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myopor, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils in coastal or sandy environments. It is often found in heathlands and open woodlands, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
