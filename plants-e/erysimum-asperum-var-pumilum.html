Erysimum asperum var. pumilum

Erysimum asperum var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf wallflower, is a low-growing, annual or biennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and cheerful blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, colorful addition to their landscape.
