Epidendrum huegelianum

Epidendrum huegelianum, commonly known as the Hugel's epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple stems that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense racemes, with showy, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and yellow.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity for optimal growth.
