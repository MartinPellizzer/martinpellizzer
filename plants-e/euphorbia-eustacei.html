Euphorbia eustacei

Euphorbia eustacei, commonly known as the Eustace's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with a distinctive crown of fleshy, greenish-yellow leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Euphorbia eustacei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardiness.
