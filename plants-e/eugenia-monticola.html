Eugenia monticola

Eugenia monticola, commonly known as the mountain eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and dark, glossy leaves. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests.

Eugenia monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
