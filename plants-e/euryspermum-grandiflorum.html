Euryspermum grandiflorum

Euryspermum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered euryspermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets surrounded by white ray florets. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
