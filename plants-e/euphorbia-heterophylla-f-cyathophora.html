Euphorbia heterophylla f. cyathophora

Euphorbia heterophylla f. cyathophora, commonly known as the velvetleaf spurge, is a succulent shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized flower clusters typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated as a garden plant.
