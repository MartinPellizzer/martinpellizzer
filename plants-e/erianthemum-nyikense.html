Erianthemum nyikense

Erianthemum nyikense, commonly known as the Nyikense geranium, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features upright stems and serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and grassland habitats.

Erianthemum nyikense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
