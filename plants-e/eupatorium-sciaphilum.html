Eupatorium sciaphilum

Eupatorium sciaphilum, commonly known as the shade-loving boneset, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Its flowering period typically occurs in late summer to early autumn.

Eupatorium sciaphilum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
