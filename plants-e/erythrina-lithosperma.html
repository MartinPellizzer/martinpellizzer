Erythrina lithosperma

Erythrina lithosperma, commonly known as the red torch tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets, and its stems are often woody and branched. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Erythrina lithosperma is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
