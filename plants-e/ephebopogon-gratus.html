Ephebopogon gratus

Ephebopogon gratus, commonly known as the graceful grass.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to certain regions of Africa. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in clusters.

Ephebopogon gratus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its delicate appearance.
