Eugenia stewardsonii

Eugenia stewardsonii, commonly known as the Stewartson's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and leathery, elliptical leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, smooth, yellowish berry that ripens in the dry season.
