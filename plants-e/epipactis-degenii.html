Epipactis degenii

Epipactis degenii, commonly known as the Degen's helleborine, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive appearance, featuring dark green, lance-shaped leaves and a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a complex life cycle, relying on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

Epipactis degenii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
