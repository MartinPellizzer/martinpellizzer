Erica rubercalyx

Erica rubercalyx, commonly known as the red-stem heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica rubercalyx thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, low-growing thickets.
