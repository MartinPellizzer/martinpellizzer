Epidendrum dolichopus

Epidendrum dolichopus, commonly known as the long-armed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with long, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum dolichopus is a popular choice for collectors due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
