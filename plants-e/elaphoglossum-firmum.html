Elaphoglossum firmum

Elaphoglossum firmum, commonly known as the firm fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and densely veined. Its small, clustered spore-producing structures are an important feature for its reproductive cycle.
