Epithelantha spinosior subsp. huastecana

Epithelantha spinosior subsp.

huastecana, commonly known as the Huastec prickly pear, is a cactus species native to Mexico. It is characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant, yellowish-orange flowers.

The plant belongs to the Cactaceae family and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it stores water in its fleshy stems.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses by indigenous communities.
