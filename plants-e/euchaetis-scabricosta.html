Euchaetis scabricosta

Euchaetis scabricosta, commonly known as the rough-stemmed euchaetis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its rough, ribbed stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.

Euchaetis scabricosta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
