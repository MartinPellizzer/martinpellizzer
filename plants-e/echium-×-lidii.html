Echium × lidii

Echium × lidii, commonly known as the hybrid evening primrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Echium vulgare and Echium plantagineum, two native species found in Europe and North America. Echium × lidii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
