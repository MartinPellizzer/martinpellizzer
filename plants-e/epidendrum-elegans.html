Epidendrum elegans

Epidendrum elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum elegans is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
