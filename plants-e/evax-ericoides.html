Evax ericoides

Evax ericoides, commonly known as the dwarf heath.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western United States, particularly California. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. It produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring.

Evax ericoides thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.
