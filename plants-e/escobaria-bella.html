Escobaria bella

Escobaria bella, commonly known as the pink fairy fan.

It is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. Its leaves are fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss.

Escobaria bella thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
