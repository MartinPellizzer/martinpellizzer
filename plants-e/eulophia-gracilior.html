Eulophia gracilior

Eulophia gracilior, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
