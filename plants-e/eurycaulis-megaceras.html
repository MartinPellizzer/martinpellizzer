Eurycaulis megaceras

Eurycaulis megaceras, commonly known as the giant horned orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers and unique morphological features.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant exhibits pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves that contribute to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and feature prominent, horn-like structures on the petals.

Eurycaulis megaceras is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
