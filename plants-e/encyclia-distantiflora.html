Encyclia distantiflora

Encyclia distantiflora, commonly known as the distant-flowered orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often featuring a yellow lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Encyclia distantiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
