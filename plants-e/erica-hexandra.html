Erica hexandra

Erica hexandra, commonly known as the mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in spring. Erica hexandra is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
