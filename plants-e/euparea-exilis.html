Euparea exilis

Euparea exilis, commonly known as the slender euparea, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters. This species is typically found in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in alpine or arctic regions. Euparea exilis is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
