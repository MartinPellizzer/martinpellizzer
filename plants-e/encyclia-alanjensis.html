Encyclia alanjensis

Encyclia alanjensis, commonly known as the Alanjens orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with several pale yellow to greenish-white blooms. It prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Encyclia alanjensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
