Ebenus mannii

Ebenus mannii, commonly known as the African rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in the spring. Ebenus mannii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
