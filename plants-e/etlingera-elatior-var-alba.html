Etlingera elatior var. alba

Etlingera elatior var. alba, commonly known as the white torch ginger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of large, white, fragrant flowers that resemble torches. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color.
