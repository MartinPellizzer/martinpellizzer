Excoecaria caffra

Excoecaria caffra, commonly known as the Natal rubber tree, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
