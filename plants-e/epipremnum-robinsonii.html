Epipremnum robinsonii

Epipremnum robinsonii, commonly known as the neon philodendron, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically bright green with a striking neon yellow or white margin. The plant is a member of the Araceae family and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various light conditions. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or shelves.

Epipremnum robinsonii is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and indirect sunlight to thrive.
