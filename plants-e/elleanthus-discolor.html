Elleanthus discolor

Elleanthus discolor, commonly known as the white daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of white ray florets surrounding a yellow disc. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. Elleanthus discolor has small, linear leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
