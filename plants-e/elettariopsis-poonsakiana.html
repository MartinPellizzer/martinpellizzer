Elettariopsis poonsakiana

Elettariopsis poonsakiana, commonly known as the Thai star anise, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by dark brown, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface with a distinct fragrance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional medicine.
