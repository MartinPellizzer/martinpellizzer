Epidendrum stellatum

Epidendrum stellatum, commonly known as the star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, racemed inflorescences with small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum stellatum is relatively easy to care for and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
