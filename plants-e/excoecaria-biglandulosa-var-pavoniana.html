Excoecaria biglandulosa var. pavoniana

Excoecaria biglandulosa var. pavoniana, commonly known as the pavoniana croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, palmate leaves that display a striking array of colors, often with a red or orange hue. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
