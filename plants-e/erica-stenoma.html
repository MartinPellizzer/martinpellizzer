Erica stenoma

Erica stenoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many shrubs and evergreens adapted to acidic soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in summer. Erica stenoma thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
