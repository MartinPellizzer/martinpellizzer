Echinocereus pulchellus var. amoenus

Echinocereus pulchellus var. amoenus, commonly known as the Pink Hedgehog Cactus, is a succulent native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in sharp, reddish spines and produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established. It is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
