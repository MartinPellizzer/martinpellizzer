Elsholtzia griffithii var. griffithii

Elsholtzia griffithii var. griffithii, commonly known as Griffith's elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats at elevations ranging from 2,000 to 4,000 meters.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual interest.
