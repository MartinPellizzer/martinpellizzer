Epidendrum calamarium var. longifolium

Epidendrum calamarium var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved whale orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, giving it a distinctive, graceful appearance. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
