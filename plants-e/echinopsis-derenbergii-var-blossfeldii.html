Echinopsis derenbergii var. blossfeldii

Echinopsis derenbergii var. blossfeldii, commonly known as the Blossfeld's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its ability to produce vibrant, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. The plant features a thick, ribbed stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Its flowers, which can be white, pink, or purple, bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
