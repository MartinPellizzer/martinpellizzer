Elatostema parvum var. parvum

Elatostema parvum var. parvum, commonly known as the small-leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has small, opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and it produces tiny, capsule-like fruits.

Elatostema parvum var. parvum is often found in low-light conditions and is adapted to thrive in humid climates.
