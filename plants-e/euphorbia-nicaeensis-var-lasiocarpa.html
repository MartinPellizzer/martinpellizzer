Euphorbia nicaeensis var. lasiocarpa

Euphorbia nicaeensis var. lasiocarpa, commonly known as the Sicilian spurge, is a succulent plant native to the island of Sicily.

It features a low-growing, branched stem covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically gray-green, with a waxy surface, and the plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is adapted to rocky or arid environments.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and resilience in Mediterranean climates.
