Eragrostis brasiliensis var. a

Eragrostis brasiliensis var. a, commonly known as Brazilian sprangletop, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Botanically, it is notable for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and its role in local ecosystems.
