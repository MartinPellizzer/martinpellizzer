Eurya tonkinensis

It is native to Southeast Asia, including Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. Eurya tonkinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
