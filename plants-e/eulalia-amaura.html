Eulalia amaura

Eulalia amaura, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features slender, silver-blue blades that are often used in ornamental landscaping. The plant produces fluffy, pale pink flower heads that appear in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Eulalia amaura is valued for its delicate appearance and low maintenance requirements.
