Encyclia goyazensis

Encyclia goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
