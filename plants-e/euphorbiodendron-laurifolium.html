Euphorbiodendron laurifolium

Euphorbiodendron laurifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf euphorbia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green, with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are typically surrounded by colorful bracts. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to xerophytic and ornamental gardens.
