Encyclia pauciflora

Encyclia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact, low-growing structure.

Encyclia pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
