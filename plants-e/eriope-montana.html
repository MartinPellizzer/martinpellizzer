Eriope montana

Eriope montana, commonly known as the mountain eriope, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Eriope montana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
