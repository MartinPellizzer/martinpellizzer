Echium swartzii

Echium swartzii, commonly known as the Swartz's fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Echium swartzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and unique appearance.
