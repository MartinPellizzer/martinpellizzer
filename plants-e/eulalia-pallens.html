Eulalia pallens

Eulalia pallens, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its silvery-blue foliage, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces fluffy, white plumes during the summer months, which are often used in floral arrangements. Eulalia pallens thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often found in open woodlands, prairies, and along roadsides.
