Epimedium macranthum var. sulphureum

Epimedium macranthum var. sulphureum, commonly known as the golden fairy or sulphur epimedium, is a deciduous perennial plant native to China.

It features attractive, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that turn red or bronze in the fall. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and unusual flower color.
