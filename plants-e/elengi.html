Elengi

Elengi, commonly known as Indian star anise, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruit that resembles the star anise used in cooking. The plant belongs to the family Rutaceae and is valued for its aromatic seeds and leaves. Elengi is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its antispasmodic and carminative effects.

Its botanical features include compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.
