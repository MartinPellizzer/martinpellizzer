Euphorbia yildirimlii

Euphorbia yildirimlii, commonly known as the Turkish spurge, is a succulent plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in a unique, clustered structure called a cyathium. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
