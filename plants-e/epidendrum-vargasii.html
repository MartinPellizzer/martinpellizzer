Epidendrum vargasii

Epidendrum vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in clusters. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Epidendrum vargasii thrives in humid, well-drained environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
