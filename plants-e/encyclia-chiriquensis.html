Encyclia chiriquensis

Encyclia chiriquensis, commonly known as the Chiriqui orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It prefers humid, shady environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Encyclia chiriquensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in local ecosystems.
