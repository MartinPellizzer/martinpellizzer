Eriogonum luteolum var. saltuarium

Eriogonum luteolum var. saltuarium, commonly known as the saltbush eriogonum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and is typically found in saline or arid environments. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in harsh conditions. It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to thrive in poor soils.
