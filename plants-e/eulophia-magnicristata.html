Eulophia magnicristata

Eulophia magnicristata, commonly known as the large-crested eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers with prominent crests. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often near streams or in rocky areas. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or white with a distinctive, intricate pattern.

Eulophia magnicristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
