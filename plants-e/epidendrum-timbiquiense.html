Epidendrum timbiquiense

Epidendrum timbiquiense, commonly known as the Timbiqui orchid, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white bloom.

Epidendrum timbiquiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
