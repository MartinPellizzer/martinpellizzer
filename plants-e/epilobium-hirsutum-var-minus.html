Epilobium hirsutum var. minus

Epilobium hirsutum var. minus, commonly known as the lesser willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect or slightly branched stems covered in soft, glandular hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a hairy surface. It produces clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers in the summer months.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
