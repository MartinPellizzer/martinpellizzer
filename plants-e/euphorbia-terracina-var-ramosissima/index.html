Euphorbia Terracina Var. Ramosissima

Euphorbia terracina var. ramosissima is a striking succulent native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. Known for its dense, branching structure and vibrant green foliage, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It produces unique yellowish-green cyathia, which are its specialized flowers, often mistaken for blooms. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap that can cause skin irritation, requiring caution when handling. This resilient plant is popular among gardeners for its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
