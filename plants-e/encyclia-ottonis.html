Encyclia ottonis

Encyclia ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a greenish-brown lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Encyclia ottonis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
