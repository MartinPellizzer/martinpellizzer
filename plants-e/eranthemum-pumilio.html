Eranthemum pumilio

Eranthemum pumilio, commonly known as the small eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Eranthemum pumilio thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
