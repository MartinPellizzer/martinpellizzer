Eupatorium tacotanum f. apodum

Eupatorium tacotanum f. apodum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boneset, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, white to pale blue, and appear in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

This variant is distinguished by its reduced leaf size compared to the typical form of Eupatorium tacotanum.
