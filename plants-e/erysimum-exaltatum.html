Erysimum exaltatum

Erysimum exaltatum, commonly known as the tall wallflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has alternate, pinnately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum exaltatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
