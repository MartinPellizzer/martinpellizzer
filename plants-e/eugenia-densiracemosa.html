Eugenia densiracemosa

Eugenia densiracemosa, commonly known as the dense raceme eugenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy dark green surface.

Eugenia densiracemosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral displays.
