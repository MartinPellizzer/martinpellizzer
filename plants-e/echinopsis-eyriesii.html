Echinopsis eyriesii

Echinopsis eyriesii, commonly known as the Easter cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and clusters of bright pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant. Its flowers are typically tubular and bloom in clusters, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Echinopsis eyriesii requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
