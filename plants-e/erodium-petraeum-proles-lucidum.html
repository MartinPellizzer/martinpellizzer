Erodium petraeum proles lucidum

Erodium petraeum proles lucidum, commonly known as the glabrous rock erodium, is a flowering plant native to arid and rocky habitats in southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb with delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of pink or violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to harsh, dry environments. Its seeds are small and dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize disturbed or barren areas.

The species is notable for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.
