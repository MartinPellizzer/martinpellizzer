Eremophila platythamnos subsp. exotrachys

Eremophila platythamnos subsp.

exotrachys, commonly known as the broad-leaved eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in arid conditions.
