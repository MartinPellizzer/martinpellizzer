Epimedium macranthum f. roseum

Epimedium macranthum f. roseum, commonly known as the roseum variety of barrenwort, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant pink to red foliage.

This fern-like perennial thrives in shady, moist environments and is native to the mountainous regions of China. Its leaves are broad, leathery, and often display a striking coloration that enhances garden landscapes. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are less showy than its foliage.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to woodland gardens.
