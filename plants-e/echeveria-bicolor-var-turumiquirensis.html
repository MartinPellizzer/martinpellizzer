Echeveria bicolor var. turumiquirensis

Echeveria bicolor var. turumiquirensis, commonly known as the Turumiquirensis Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container plantings.
