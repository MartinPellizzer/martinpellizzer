Eritrichium

Eritrichium, commonly known as blueweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Brassicaceae.

These plants are characterized by their small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to blue and purple. Eritrichium species typically have basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape. They are often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky soils.
