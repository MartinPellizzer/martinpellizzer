Encyclia dressleri

Encyclia dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal inflorescences and can persist for several weeks.

Encyclia dressleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
