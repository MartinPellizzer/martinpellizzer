Eritrichium alyssoides

Eritrichium alyssoides, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped with toothed edges. Its flowers are blue or violet, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early summer.

Eritrichium alyssoides is adapted to cold, alpine environments and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
