Eutrema altaicum

Eutrema altaicum, commonly known as the mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of fleshy, succulent leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers in early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species often found in alpine and subalpine ecosystems.
