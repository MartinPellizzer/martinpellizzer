Euonymus alatus var. rotundatus

Euonymus alatus var. rotundatus, commonly known as the winged euonymus, is a cultivar of the Eurasian native species Euonymus alatus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winged stems that are covered in small, scale-like structures. The leaves are typically oval or rounded, with a smooth texture and a deep green color. This plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that persist into the winter.

It is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
