Euodia compacta var. meionocarpa

Euodia compacta var. meionocarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited Chinese tallowtree, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark purple, smooth fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruiting structure.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
