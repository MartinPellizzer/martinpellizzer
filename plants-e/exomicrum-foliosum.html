Exomicrum foliosum

Exomicrum foliosum, commonly known as the leafy exomicrum, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate leaf structure.

It belongs to the family Exomicraceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant features elongated, serrated leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat. Its flowering pattern is less common, with blooms appearing sporadically and often in shades of pale yellow or green.
