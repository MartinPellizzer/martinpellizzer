Echeveria lanceolata var. aloides

Echeveria lanceolata var. aloides, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are slightly pointed and arranged in a compact, star-shaped formation. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
