Eulophia renschiana

Eulophia renschiana, commonly known as the Rensch's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in racemes, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has erect, branching stems and linear, evergreen leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Eulophia renschiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
