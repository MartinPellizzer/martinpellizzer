Elatostema rupestre

Elatostema rupestre, commonly known as the rock elatostem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in spring.

Elatostema rupestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
