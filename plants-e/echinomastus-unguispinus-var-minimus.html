Echinomastus unguispinus var. minimus

Echinomastus unguispinus var. minimus, commonly known as the dwarf horned milkweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features small, hairy stems and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant's leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
