Eryngium juresianum

Eryngium juresianum, commonly known as the Juresian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has gray-green, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Eryngium juresianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
