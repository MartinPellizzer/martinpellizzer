Echites cymosus

Echites cymosus, commonly known as the clustered echites, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to arid conditions.

Echites cymosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
