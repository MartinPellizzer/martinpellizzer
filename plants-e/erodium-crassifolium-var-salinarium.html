Erodium crassifolium var. salinarium

Erodium crassifolium var. salinarium, commonly known as saltbush erodium, is a perennial herb native to coastal areas of southern Australia.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from spring to autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid or saline environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes or salt marshes.

Its adaptability to harsh coastal conditions makes it an important component of coastal ecosystems.
