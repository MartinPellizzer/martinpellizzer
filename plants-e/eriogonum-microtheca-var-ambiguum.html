Eriogonum microtheca var. ambiguum

Eriogonum microtheca var. ambiguum, commonly known as the ambiguous leaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences of small, white to pale pink flowers.
