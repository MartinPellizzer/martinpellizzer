Ehrharta longifolia var. robusta

Ehrharta longifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust false oat, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
