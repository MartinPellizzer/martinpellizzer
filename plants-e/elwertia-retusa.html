Elwertia retusa

Elwertia retusa, commonly known as the smooth elwertia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Elwertia retusa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
