Eriocereus spinosissimus

Eriocereus spinosissimus, commonly known as the spiny night blooming cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a perennial cacti that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features spiny stems and produces fragrant, white flowers at night. Its flowers are usually short-lived, opening only during the evening.

The plant is well adapted to drought conditions and requires minimal water to thrive.
