Erysimum pacificum

Erysimum pacificum, commonly known as the Pacific wallflower, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant features bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Erysimum pacificum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
