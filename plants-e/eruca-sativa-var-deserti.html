Eruca sativa var. deserti

Eruca sativa var. deserti, commonly known as desert rocket, is a hardy, flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, elongated leaves that are covered in a fine layer of hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is well adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments due to its deep root system and ability to conserve water.

This variety is often used in xeriscaping and has potential as a nutritious, edible plant in sustainable agriculture.
