Euphorbia schizoloba

Euphorbia schizoloba, commonly known as the Split-leaf Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive, branched stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are often lobed or split, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stems.

Euphorbia schizoloba is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
