Erigeron Glabellus Var. Yukonensis

Erigeron glabellus var. yukonensis is a striking perennial plant native to the Yukon region of Canada. It is known for its fluffy white flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. This variety is a hardy member of the Asteraceae family, often found in alpine and rocky habitats. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plant. With its elegant look and resilience, it adds beauty and ecological value to any landscape.
