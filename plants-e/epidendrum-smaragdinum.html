Epidendrum smaragdinum

Epidendrum smaragdinum, commonly known as the Emerald Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green color, often with a waxy texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, green, and yellow, arranged in a raceme.

Epidendrum smaragdinum is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
