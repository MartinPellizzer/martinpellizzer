Echinodorus × maculatus

Echinodorus × maculatus, commonly known as the spotted sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features long, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow spots, giving it a striking appearance. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is commonly used in aquariums for its aesthetic value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow in a variety of water temperatures.

Echinodorus × maculatus is also known for its ability to help maintain water quality by absorbing nutrients and reducing algae growth.
