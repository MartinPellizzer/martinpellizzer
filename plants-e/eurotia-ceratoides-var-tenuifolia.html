Eurotia ceratoides var. tenuifolia

Eurotia ceratoides var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It grows in a compact, shrub-like form with a branching structure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
