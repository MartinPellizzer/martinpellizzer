Epipactis thunbergii var. manshurica

Epipactis thunbergii var. manshurica, commonly known as the Manchurian marsh helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a tall, erect flowering stem that bears a single, showy greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and wetlands. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates with high humidity.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
