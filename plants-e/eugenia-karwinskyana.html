Eugenia karwinskyana

Eugenia karwinskyana, commonly known as the karwinsky's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces edible, red, fleshy berries that are often used in local cuisines.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
