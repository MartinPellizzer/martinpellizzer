Encyclia almasii

Encyclia almasii, commonly known as the Almasii orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with contrasting red or purple markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia almasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
