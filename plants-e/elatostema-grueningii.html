Elatostema grueningii

Elatostema grueningii, commonly known as the silverleaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and arranged in clusters.

Elatostema grueningii is part of the Elatostemaceae family, which is characterized by its unique leaf morphology and specialized reproductive structures.
