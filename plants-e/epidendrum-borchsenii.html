Epidendrum borchsenii

Epidendrum borchsenii, commonly known as the Borchsenius Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Brazil and Colombia. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a distinctive red or purple lip, and it produces showy, clustered blooms.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
