Echinospermum stylosum

Echinospermum stylosum, commonly known as the silky blueweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has hairy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
