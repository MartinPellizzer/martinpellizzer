Echinocereus enneacanthus

Echinocereus enneacanthus, commonly known as the Arizona hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is a low-growing, sprawling cactus with cylindrical stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is well adapted to arid environments.
