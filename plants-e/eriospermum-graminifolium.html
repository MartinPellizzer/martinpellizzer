Eriospermum graminifolium

Eriospermum graminifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow disk flowers that are surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Eriospermum graminifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
