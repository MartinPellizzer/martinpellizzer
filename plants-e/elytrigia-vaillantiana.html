Elytrigia vaillantiana

Elytrigia vaillantiana, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Elytrigia vaillantiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
