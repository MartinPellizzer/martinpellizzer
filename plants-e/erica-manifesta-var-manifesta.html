Erica manifesta var. manifesta

Erica manifesta var. manifesta, commonly known as the common heath, is a flowering shrub native to the western Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming from spring to early summer. Its evergreen leaves are narrow and leathery, providing year-round structure to the landscape.

Erica manifesta var. manifesta is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
