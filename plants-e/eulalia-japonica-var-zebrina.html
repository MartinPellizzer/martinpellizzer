Eulalia japonica var. zebrina

Eulalia japonica var. zebrina, commonly known as the Zebra Grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Japan.

It features tall, slender culms that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a feathery texture that adds visual interest in gardens. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late summer, which are typically borne in loose panicles.

This variety is prized for its vibrant coloration and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
