Epidendrum holmnielsenii

Epidendrum holmnielsenii, commonly known as the Holm Nielsen's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
