Eriophorum tenellum f. axillare

Eriophorum tenellum f. axillare, commonly known as the slender cottongrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense, cushion-like clumps. The plant produces small, fluffy inflorescences that resemble cotton, which are characteristic of the genus Eriophorum. It thrives in moist, acidic environments such as bogs and wet meadows.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
