Epacris refulgens

Epacris refulgens, commonly known as the shining epacris, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Epacris refulgens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
