Erica tenella var. gracilior

Erica tenella var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves. Its flowers, which are typically pink or purple, bloom in late summer and early autumn.

Erica tenella var. gracilior thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.
