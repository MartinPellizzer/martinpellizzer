Etlingera bromeliopsis

Etlingera bromeliopsis, commonly known as the purple torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant purple bracts that surround a tall, narrow flower spike. Its leaves are elongated, glossy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
