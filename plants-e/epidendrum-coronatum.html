Epidendrum coronatum

Epidendrum coronatum, commonly known as the Crowned Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces numerous racemes of fragrant blooms, which appear during the rainy season. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum coronatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
