Eschscholzia caespitosa

Eschscholzia caespitosa, commonly known as the bush poppy, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Eschscholzia caespitosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
