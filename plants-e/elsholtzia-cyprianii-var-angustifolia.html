Elsholtzia cyprianii var. angustifolia

Elsholtzia cyprianii var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves that are typically narrow and linear, with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue to violet in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
