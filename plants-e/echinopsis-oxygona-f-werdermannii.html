Echinopsis oxygona f. werdermannii

Echinopsis oxygona f. werdermannii, commonly known as the Werdermann's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to grow properly.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
