Euphrosyne ambrosiifolia

Euphrosyne ambrosiifolia, commonly known as the sacred leaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and small, clustered white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Annonaceae and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal significance.
