Eriospermum calcaratum

Eriospermum calcaratum, commonly known as the rock eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and distinctive, woolly appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
