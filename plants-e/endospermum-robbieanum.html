Endospermum robbieanum

Endospermum robbieanum, commonly known as the Queensland bottle tree, is a distinctive Australian plant characterized by its thick, bottle-shaped trunk that stores water.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is native to the northeastern regions of Queensland. The plant features a crown of small, leathery leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange or red fruits.

Its unique morphology and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it an important species in its native habitat.
