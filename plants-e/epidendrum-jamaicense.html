Epidendrum jamaicense

Epidendrum jamaicense, commonly known as the Jamaican Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Jamaica and parts of the Caribbean.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories or along rocky slopes. The plant features erect, branching stems with fleshy leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are showy, with vibrant colors ranging from yellow to red, and are arranged in racemes.

Epidendrum jamaicense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
