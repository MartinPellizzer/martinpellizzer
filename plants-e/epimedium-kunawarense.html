Epimedium kunawarense

Epimedium kunawarense, commonly known as the Japanese barbatan, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a deep green color and a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in panicles.

Epimedium kunawarense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
