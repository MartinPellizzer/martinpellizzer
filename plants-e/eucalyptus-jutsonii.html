Eucalyptus jutsonii

Eucalyptus jutsonii, commonly known as the Jutson's mallee, is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub native to Western Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its rough, fibrous bark and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, globular flower clusters that are usually pale green or yellowish in color.

Eucalyptus jutsonii is often found in open woodlands and is an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
