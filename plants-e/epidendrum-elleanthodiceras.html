Epidendrum elleanthodiceras

Epidendrum elleanthodiceras, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, arranged in racemes along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum elleanthodiceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
