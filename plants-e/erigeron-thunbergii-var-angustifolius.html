Erigeron thunbergii var. angustifolius

Erigeron thunbergii var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved fleabane, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
