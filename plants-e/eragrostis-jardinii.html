Eragrostis jardinii

Eragrostis jardinii, commonly known as the silvergrass or garden silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as rocky soils and disturbed areas. Its small, spike-like inflorescence contains numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Eragrostis jardinii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
