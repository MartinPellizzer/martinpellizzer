Erysimum bryoides

Erysimum bryoides, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Erysimum bryoides is adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a hardy species suitable for rock gardens.
