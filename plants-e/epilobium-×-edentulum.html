Epilobium × edentulum

Epilobium × edentulum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and riverbanks. The plant features slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its showy, pink to purple flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in late summer.

It is often found in wetland environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
