Edraianthus graminifolius f. australis

Edraianthus graminifolius f. australis, commonly known as the Australian grass-like ericaceae, is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically forms dense mats, with small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.
