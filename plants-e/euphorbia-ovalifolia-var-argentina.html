Euphorbia ovalifolia var. argentina

Euphorbia ovalifolia var. argentina, commonly known as the Argentine euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South America.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are oval-shaped and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
