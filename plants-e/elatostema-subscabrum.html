Elatostema subscabrum

Elatostema subscabrum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed false shamrock, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema subscabrum is often found in wetlands and is adapted to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
