Erica glabripes

Erica glabripes, commonly known as the smooth heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica glabripes is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
