Erysimum munzuriense

Erysimum munzuriense, commonly known as the desert wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

Erysimum munzuriense is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of these regions.
