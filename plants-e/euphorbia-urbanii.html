Euphorbia urbanii

Euphorbia urbanii, commonly known as the Urban's spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its succulent, spiny stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the genus Euphorbia. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Euphorbia urbanii is relatively low-maintenance and can be cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
