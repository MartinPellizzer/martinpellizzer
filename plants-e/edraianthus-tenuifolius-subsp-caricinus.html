Edraianthus tenuifolius subsp. caricinus

Edraianthus tenuifolius subsp.

caricinus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved edraianthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.

The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early summer. It is often found in alpine or subalpine regions, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions.
