Echioglossum javanicum

Echioglossum javanicum, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose spike, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires specific care to grow successfully in cultivation.
