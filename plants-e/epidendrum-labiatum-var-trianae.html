Epidendrum labiatum var. trianae

Epidendrum labiatum var. trianae, commonly known as the Trianas Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
