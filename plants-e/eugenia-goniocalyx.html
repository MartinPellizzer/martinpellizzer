Eugenia goniocalyx

Eugenia goniocalyx, commonly known as the red-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Eugenia goniocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
