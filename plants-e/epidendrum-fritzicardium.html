Epidendrum fritzicardium

Epidendrum fritzicardium, commonly known as the Fritz Cardium Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
