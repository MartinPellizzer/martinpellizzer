Erythroxylum citrifolium var. latifolium

Erythroxylum citrifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf yellow elder, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain seeds.

The plant is often found in open habitats such as savannas and coastal areas.
