Eulophia bainesii

Eulophia bainesii, commonly known as the Baines' eulophia, is a species of orchid native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous white or cream-colored flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia bainesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
