Ecklonea capensis

Ecklonea capensis, commonly known as the Cape ecklonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Ecklonea capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
