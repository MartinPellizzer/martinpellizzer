Encyclia belizensis

Encyclia belizensis, commonly known as the Belize encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers emerging from the base of the leaf rosette. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia belizensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
