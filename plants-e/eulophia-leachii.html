Eulophia leachii

Eulophia leachii, commonly known as the Leach's eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, often branched stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a contrasting lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia leachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
