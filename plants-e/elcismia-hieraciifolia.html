Elcismia hieraciifolia

Elcismia hieraciifolia, commonly known as the hairy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically arranged in a five-leaflet pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance.
