Euphorbia segetalis f. robusta

Euphorbia segetalis f. robusta, commonly known as the robust euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
