Ehretia psilosiphon

Ehretia psilosiphon, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters and features simple, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
