Eugenia kleinii var. kleinii

Eugenia kleinii var. kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and dark green, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when ripe.
