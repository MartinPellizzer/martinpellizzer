Echinopsis melanopotamica

Echinopsis melanopotamica, commonly known as the Melanopotamian cactus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive dark green to bluish-gray coloration. The plant features areolate ribs and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its structure and appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
