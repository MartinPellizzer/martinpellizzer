Eugenioides glomeratum

Eugenioides glomeratum, commonly known as the clustered eugenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its flowers are usually pink or white and attract various pollinators such as bees and birds.

The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture with a distinct aromatic scent.
