Erica nodiflora var. delapsa

Erica nodiflora var. delapsa, commonly known as the Delapsa heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
