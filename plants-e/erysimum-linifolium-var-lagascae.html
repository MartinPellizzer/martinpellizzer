Erysimum linifolium var. lagascae

Erysimum linifolium var. lagascae, commonly known as the Lagasca wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features upright, branched stems covered in dense clusters of bright yellow flowers. The leaves are linear and grass-like, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
