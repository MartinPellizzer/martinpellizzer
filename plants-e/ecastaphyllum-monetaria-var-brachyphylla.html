Ecastaphyllum monetaria var. brachyphylla

Ecastaphyllum monetaria var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed money plant, is a variant of the money plant species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter stems compared to the typical variety. The leaves are typically broad and glossy, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in humid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
