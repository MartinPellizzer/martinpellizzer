Eriostemon myoporoides subsp. queenslandicus

Eriostemon myoporoides subsp.

queenslandicus, commonly known as the Queensland myopor, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in rainforest or coastal scrub habitats.

The plant features glossy, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It is often found in the subtropical regions of Queensland.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
