Euphorbia maresii subsp. balearica

Euphorbia maresii subsp.

balearica, commonly known as the Balearic spurge, is a succulent shrub native to the Balearic Islands. It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy cuticle.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a distinctive cyathium, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it is adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
