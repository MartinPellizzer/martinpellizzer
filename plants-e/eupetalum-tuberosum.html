Eupetalum tuberosum

Eupetalum tuberosum, commonly known as the white edelweiss, is a flowering plant native to the high alpine regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dense white flower heads surrounded by fuzzy, silver-green bracts. The plant thrives in cold, sunny environments and is adapted to grow at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its flowers bloom in late summer, producing a striking appearance that resembles a miniature sun.

Eupetalum tuberosum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
