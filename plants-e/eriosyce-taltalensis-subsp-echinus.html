Eriosyce taltalensis subsp. echinus

Eriosyce taltalensis subsp.

echinus, commonly known as the Taltal spine cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to coastal regions of Chile. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, spiny areoles.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. It is well adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system that helps it survive in sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
