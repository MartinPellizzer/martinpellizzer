Encyclia lutzenbergeri var. major

Encyclia lutzenbergeri var. major, commonly known as the giant orchid or large encyclia, is a species of orchid native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, tropical environments.

This orchid is often found growing epiphytically on trees or rocks in shaded, moist forests.
