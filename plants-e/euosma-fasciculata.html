Euosma fasciculata

Euosma fasciculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurel trees and bay leaves. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic. It produces small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Euosma fasciculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and foliage.
