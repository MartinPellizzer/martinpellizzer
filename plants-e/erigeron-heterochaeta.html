Erigeron heterochaeta

Erigeron heterochaeta, commonly known as the woolly blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Erigeron heterochaeta is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
