Epidendrum miriamiae

Epidendrum miriamiae, commonly known as Miriam's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with vibrant flowers. The plant typically features narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum miriamiae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
