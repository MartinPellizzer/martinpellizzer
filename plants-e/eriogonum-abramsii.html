Eriogonum abramsii

Eriogonum abramsii, commonly known as Abrams' buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as hillsides and sagebrush steppe. It has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.
