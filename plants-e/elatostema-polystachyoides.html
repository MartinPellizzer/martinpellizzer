Elatostema polystachyoides

Elatostema polystachyoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf stelleria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are often borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are characterized by their smooth margins and a slightly heart-shaped base.

Elatostema polystachyoides is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
