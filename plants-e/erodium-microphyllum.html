Erodium microphyllum

Erodium microphyllum, commonly known as dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to regions of southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb with slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in the early morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gardens.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many parts of the world.
