Ehrharta diffusa

Ehrharta diffusa, commonly known as silky grass or velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, dense foliage and produces small, pale green to brownish spikelets. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along water bodies. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Ehrharta diffusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
