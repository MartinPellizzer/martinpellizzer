Erigeron clokeyi var. clokeyi

Erigeron clokeyi var. clokeyi, commonly known as the Clokey's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.
