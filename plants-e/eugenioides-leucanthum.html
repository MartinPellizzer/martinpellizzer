Eugenioides leucanthum

Eugenioides leucanthum, commonly known as the white-flowered eugenioid, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its flowers are borne in clusters and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
