Eupatorium betoniciforme f. alternifolium

Eupatorium betoniciforme f. alternifolium, commonly known as the alternate-leaved boneset, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, alternate leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
