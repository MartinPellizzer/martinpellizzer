Ehretia championii

Ehretia championii, commonly known as the Indian snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
