Espeletia griffinii

Espeletia griffinii, commonly known as the Griffin's espeletia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean highlands of Colombia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-like growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
