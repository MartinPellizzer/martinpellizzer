Elatostema frutescens var. parvifolia

Elatostema frutescens var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are usually white or pale green, clustered in umbels.
