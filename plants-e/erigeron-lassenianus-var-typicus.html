Erigeron lassenianus var. typicus

Erigeron lassenianus var. typicus, commonly known as the Lassen's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces small blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
