Euscaphis japonica f. lanata

Euscaphis japonica f. lanata, commonly known as the woolly Japanese witchhazel, is a rare and unique variant of the witchhazel genus.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on the stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to Japan and thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.
