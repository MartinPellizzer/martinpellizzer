Euonymus wardii

Euonymus wardii, commonly known as the African boxwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are small and yellowish, appearing in clusters, and it produces red, fleshy fruits that are edible for some animals.

Euonymus wardii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
