Epidendrum micron

Epidendrum micron, commonly known as the miniature epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features compact, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
