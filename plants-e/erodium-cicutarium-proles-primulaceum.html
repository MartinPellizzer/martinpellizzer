Erodium cicutarium proles primulaceum

Erodium cicutarium proles primulaceum, commonly known as the common stork's bill, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Primulaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and distinctive spurred flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in roadsides, fields, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many regions.
