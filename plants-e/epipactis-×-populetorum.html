Epipactis × populetorum

Epipactis × populetorum, commonly known as the hybrid green orchid, is a natural hybrid between Epipactis helleborine and Epipactis palustris.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and moist meadow habitats in Europe. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a spike, with a distinctive lip-like structure. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Epipactis × populetorum is often found in association with other orchid species in its native habitat.
