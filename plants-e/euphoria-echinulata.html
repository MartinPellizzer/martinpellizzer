Euphoria echinulata

Euphoria echinulata, commonly known as the spiny euphoria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes many species with unique floral structures. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture.
