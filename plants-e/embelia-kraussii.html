Embelia kraussii

Embelia kraussii, commonly known as the African star anise, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, small, white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are opposite and oval-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Embelia kraussii is valued for its medicinal properties and is commonly used in herbal remedies.
