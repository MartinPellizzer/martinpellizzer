Eugenia longistyla

Eugenia longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that develops after flowering.

Eugenia longistyla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
