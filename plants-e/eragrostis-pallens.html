Eragrostis pallens

Eragrostis pallens, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of slender, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to bluish flowers.

Eragrostis pallens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
