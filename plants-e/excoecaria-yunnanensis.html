Excoecaria yunnanensis

Excoecaria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan croton, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often brightly colored leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
