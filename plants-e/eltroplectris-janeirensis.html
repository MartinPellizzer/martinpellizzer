Eltroplectris janeirensis

Eltroplectris janeirensis, commonly known as the Brazilian marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to the wetlands of Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in swampy or seasonally flooded areas. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are adapted to thrive in waterlogged soils, and it plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat.

Eltroplectris janeirensis is valued for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
