Eugenia tahanensis

Eugenia tahanensis, commonly known as the Tahan's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas with high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and less-studied species within its native range.
