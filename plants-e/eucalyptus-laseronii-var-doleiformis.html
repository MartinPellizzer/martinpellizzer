Eucalyptus laseronii var. doleiformis

Eucalyptus laseronii var. doleiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a shrub or small tree native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish bloom. The plant produces small, globular flower spikes that are typically greenish-white in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is part of the Eucalyptus laseronii species group, which includes several other closely related taxa.
