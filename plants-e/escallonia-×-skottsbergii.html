Escallonia × skottsbergii

Escallonia × skottsbergii, commonly known as the red escallonia, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Escallonia × skottsbergii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and adaptability to various garden settings.
