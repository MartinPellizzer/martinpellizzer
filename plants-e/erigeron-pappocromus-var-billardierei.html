Erigeron pappocromus var. billardierei

Erigeron pappocromus var. billardierei, commonly known as the slender blueweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while the flowers are small, blue to purple, and arranged in a flat-topped cluster.
