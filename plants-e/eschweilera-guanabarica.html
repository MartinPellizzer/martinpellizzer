Eschweilera guanabarica

Eschweilera guanabarica, commonly known as the Guanabara wax palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark purple fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
