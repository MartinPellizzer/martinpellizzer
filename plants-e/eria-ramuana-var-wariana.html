Eria ramuana var. wariana

Eria ramuana var. wariana, commonly known as the Wariana orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It prefers indirect light and consistent moisture, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
