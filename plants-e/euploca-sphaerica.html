Euploca sphaerica

Euploca sphaerica, commonly known as the globe sedge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, globular inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.

Euploca sphaerica is often found in still or slow-moving water, where it plays a role in stabilizing aquatic environments.
