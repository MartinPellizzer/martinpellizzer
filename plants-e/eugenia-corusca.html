Eugenia corusca

Eugenia corusca, commonly known as the silverleaf eucalyptus, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Eugenia corusca is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to different soil types.
