Euklisia mildredae

Euklisia mildredae, commonly known as the Mildred's mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps. The plant features showy, white to pale yellow inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Euklisia mildredae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
