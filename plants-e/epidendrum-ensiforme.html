Epidendrum ensiforme

Epidendrum ensiforme, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum ensiforme is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
