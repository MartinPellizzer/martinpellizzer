Ericoides crassisepalum

Ericoides crassisepalum, commonly known as the broad-sepal ericoid, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and evergreen foliage, typically found in acidic soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are often pink or white in color. Ericoides crassisepalum is native to regions with cool, moist climates, such as parts of South America.

Its botanical features make it an ornamental plant suitable for shaded garden environments.
