Eugenia afzelii

Eugenia afzelii, commonly known as the African cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. Eugenia afzelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit includes both shrub and tree forms, depending on the environmental conditions.
