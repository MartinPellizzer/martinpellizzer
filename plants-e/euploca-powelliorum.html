Euploca powelliorum

Euploca powelliorum, commonly known as Powell's euploca, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as rocky soils and dry slopes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in desert ecosystems.
