Eugenia mekongensis

Eugenia mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a glossy surface, while its fruit is a small, dark purple drupe.

Eugenia mekongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
