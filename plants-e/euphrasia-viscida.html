Euphrasia viscida

Euphrasia viscida, commonly known as sticky eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, sticky coating.

Euphrasia viscida is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
