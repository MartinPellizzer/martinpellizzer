Epidendrum parra-sanchezii

Epidendrum parra-sanchezii, commonly known as the Parra-Sanchez Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and blooms in shades of yellow to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
