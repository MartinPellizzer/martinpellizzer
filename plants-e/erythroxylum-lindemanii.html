Erythroxylum lindemanii

Erythroxylum lindemanii, commonly known as the Lindeman's coca, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, orange-red berries that contain seeds.

This species is closely related to the coca plants used in traditional medicine and has similar chemical properties.
