Eruca sativa subsp. stenocarpa

Eruca sativa subsp.

stenocarpa, commonly known as narrow-fruited wall rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers in racemes, which are followed by narrow, elongated pods containing seeds. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.

This subspecies is often used in Mediterranean cuisine, particularly in salads and as a seasoning.
