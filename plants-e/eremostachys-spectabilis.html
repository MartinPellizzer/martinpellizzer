Eremostachys spectabilis

Eremostachys spectabilis, commonly known as the showy eremostachys, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, gray-green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
