Eucomis jacquinii

Eucomis jacquinii, commonly known as the white fountain plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features a dense, white to pale blue inflorescence that resembles a fountain, emerging from a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Eucomis jacquinii is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
