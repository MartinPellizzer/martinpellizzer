Eriocaulon nantoense var. satsumense

Eriocaulon nantoense var. satsumense, commonly known as the Satsuma rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of Japan and China. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, acidic soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
