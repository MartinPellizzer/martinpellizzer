Eutelia

Eutelia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rutaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly rough texture.

Eutelia is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
