Elemifera floridana

Elemifera floridana, commonly known as the Florida elemi.

It is a rare shrub native to the coastal regions of Florida, characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces a resinous sap that has been historically used for its aromatic properties. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in maritime hammocks.

Due to habitat loss, it is listed as endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
