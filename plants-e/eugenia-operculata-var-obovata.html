Eugenia operculata var. obovata

Eugenia operculata var. obovata, commonly known as the obovate evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, obovate-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
