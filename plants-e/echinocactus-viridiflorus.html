Echinocactus viridiflorus

Echinocactus viridiflorus, commonly known as the green-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in prominent, spiny areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
