Eyrythalia canaliculata

Eyrythalia canaliculata, commonly known as the redstem sedges, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and wetlands. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, and it produces distinctive, three-angled stems.
