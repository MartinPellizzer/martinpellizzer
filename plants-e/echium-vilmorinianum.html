Echium vilmorinianum

Echium vilmorinianum, commonly known as the Vilmorin's evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, oily seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
