Elaeocarpus kusaiensis

Elaeocarpus kusaiensis, commonly known as the Kusaiensis elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a hard, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
