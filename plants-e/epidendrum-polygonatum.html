Epidendrum polygonatum

Epidendrum polygonatum, commonly known as the Chinese violet or violet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its leaves are long, narrow, and slightly fleshy, adapted to retain moisture.

Epidendrum polygonatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, humid conditions.
