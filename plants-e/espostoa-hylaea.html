Espostoa hylaea

Espostoa hylaea, commonly known as the hylaean cactus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny appearance. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique structure and flowering habits make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
