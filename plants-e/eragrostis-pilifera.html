Eragrostis pilifera

Eragrostis pilifera, commonly known as silky grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hairs that cover the stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Eragrostis pilifera is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
