Erysimum Nudiusculum

Yellow wallflower, scientifically known as Erysimum nudiusculum, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the rocky soils of southern Europe. With its vivid yellow blooms and compact growth habit, it is a favorite among gardeners seeking both color and resilience. This species thrives in well-drained conditions and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens and borders. Its delicate petals and sturdy stems add a touch of elegance to any landscape. Despite its small size, the yellow wallflower packs a powerful visual impact, earning its place as a cherished ornamental plant.
