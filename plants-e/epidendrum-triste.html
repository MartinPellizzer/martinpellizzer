Epidendrum triste

Epidendrum triste, commonly known as the sad orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, pale green or white flowers. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Epidendrum triste is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden setting.
