Epidendrum marginatum

Epidendrum marginatum, commonly known as the marginated orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or grayish pubescence. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum marginatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
