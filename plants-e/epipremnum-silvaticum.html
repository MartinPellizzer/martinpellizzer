Epipremnum silvaticum

Epipremnum silvaticum, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically light green with a glossy texture. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its aerial roots allow it to attach to surfaces as it climbs, making it a popular choice for indoor hanging baskets or vertical gardens.

Epipremnum silvaticum is also known for its air-purifying properties, contributing to its popularity as a houseplant.
