Epidendrum amplexigastrium

Epidendrum amplexigastrium, commonly known as the clasping stomach Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often wrap around the stem, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum amplexigastrium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
