Epidendrum teretifolium var. powellianum

Epidendrum teretifolium var. powellianum, commonly known as the Powell's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to grayish hairs. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
