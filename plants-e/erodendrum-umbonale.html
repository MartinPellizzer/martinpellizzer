Erodendrum umbonale

Erodendrum umbonale, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
