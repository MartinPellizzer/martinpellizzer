Eulophia faberi

Eulophia faberi, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinct, intricate pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Eulophia faberi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
