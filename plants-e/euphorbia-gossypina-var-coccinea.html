Euphorbia gossypina var. coccinea

Euphorbia gossypina var. coccinea, commonly known as the redleaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and striking red to orange foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are the modified inflorescences typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
