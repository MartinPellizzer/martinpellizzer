Edusaron

Edusaron, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps reflect sunlight and reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. Edusaron is adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in its native ecosystems.
