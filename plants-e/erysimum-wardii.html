Erysimum wardii

Erysimum wardii, commonly known as the Mexican wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Erysimum wardii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
