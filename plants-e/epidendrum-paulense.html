Epidendrum paulense

Epidendrum paulense, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Epidendrum paulense is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
