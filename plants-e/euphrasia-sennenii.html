Euphrasia sennenii

Euphrasia sennenii, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy hairs, and it produces small, clustered yellow flowers with distinctive, spurred petals. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly wavy margin.

Euphrasia sennenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
