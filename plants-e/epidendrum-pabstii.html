Epidendrum pabstii

Epidendrum pabstii, commonly known as the Pabst's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically features broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum pabstii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
