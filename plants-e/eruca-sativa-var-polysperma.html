Eruca sativa var. polysperma

Eruca sativa var. polysperma, commonly known as the Italian rocket or wild rocket, is a biennial or annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and basal rosette of pinnately lobed, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in racemes, which are followed by long, slender pods containing several seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in cultivated fields.

This variety is valued for its peppery flavor and is used in culinary and medicinal applications.
