Eriosema irwinii

Eriosema irwinii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a bushy growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
