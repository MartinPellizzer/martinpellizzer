Eugenia balansae

Eugenia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae apple or wild apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Its fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that ripens in late summer.

Eugenia balansae is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
