Eugenia biflora var. pallens

Eugenia biflora var. pallens, commonly known as the pale-flowered eugenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its pale blooms.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
