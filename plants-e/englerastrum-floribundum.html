Englerastrum floribundum

Englerastrum floribundum, commonly known as the golden starbursh, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and drought-tolerant ornamental plant.
