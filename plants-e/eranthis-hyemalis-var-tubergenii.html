Eranthis hyemalis var. tubergenii

Eranthis hyemalis var. tubergenii, commonly known as the winter aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom early in the spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has a short, stout rhizome and produces tubular, yellow petals with a prominent central stamen. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded garden settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular ornamental plant in temperate regions.
