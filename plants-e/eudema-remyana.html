Eudema remyana

Eudema remyana, commonly known as the silverleaf eudema, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and requires a cool, humid environment.
