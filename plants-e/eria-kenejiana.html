Eria kenejiana

Eria kenejiana, commonly known as the Kenejian orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
