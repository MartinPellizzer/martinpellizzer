Erlangea hispida

Erlangea hispida, commonly known as the rough erlangea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erlangea hispida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
