Epigeneium radicosum

Epigeneium radicosum, commonly known as the hairy epigeneium, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its aerial rhizomes and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are often hidden within a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
