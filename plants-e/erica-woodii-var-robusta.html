Erica woodii var. robusta

Erica woodii var. robusta, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a dense, evergreen shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica woodii var. robusta is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its drought tolerance and hardy nature.
