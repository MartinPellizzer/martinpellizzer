Erigeron hultenii

Erigeron hultenii, commonly known as Hulten's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
