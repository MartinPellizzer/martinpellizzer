Evolvulus gracillimus

Evolvulus gracillimus, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South America.

It features small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet, blooming in the morning. The plant has slender, trailing stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
