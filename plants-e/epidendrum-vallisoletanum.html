Epidendrum vallisoletanum

Epidendrum vallisoletanum, commonly known as the Vallisoletan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features erect, branching stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Epidendrum vallisoletanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
