Encyclia × camagueyensis

Encyclia × camagueyensis, commonly known as the Camaguey orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting red or brown lip. Its leaves are dark green, strap-like, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
