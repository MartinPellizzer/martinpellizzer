Eupatorium fortunei

Eupatorium fortunei, commonly known as Chinese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features aromatic, opposite leaves that are usually gray-green in color and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Eupatorium fortunei is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
