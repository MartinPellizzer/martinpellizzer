Eriospermum transvaalense

Eriospermum transvaalense, commonly known as the Transvaal eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
