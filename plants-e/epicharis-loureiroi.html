Epicharis loureiroi

Epicharis loureiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
