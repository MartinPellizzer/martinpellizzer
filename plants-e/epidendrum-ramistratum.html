Epidendrum ramistratum

Epidendrum ramistratum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Andes, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
