Epipactis hyblaea

Epipactis hyblaea, commonly known as the Sicilian helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It produces small, greenish-brown fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Epipactis hyblaea is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in association with other orchid species.
