Epipactis kezlinekii

Epipactis kezlinekii, commonly known as the Kezlinek's helleborine, is a rare orchid species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive lip structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors or stream banks. It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in many regions.
