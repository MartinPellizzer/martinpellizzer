Erysimum longifolium var. ruscinonense

Erysimum longifolium var. ruscinonense, commonly known as the Ruscinonense Wallflower, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branching stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height, adorned with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a slightly serrated margin. This variety is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
