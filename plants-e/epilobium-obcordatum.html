Epilobium obcordatum

Epilobium obcordatum, commonly known as narrowleaf willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist soils along stream banks, wet meadows, and other riparian habitats.
