Eupatorium skutchii

Eupatorium skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium skutchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
