Elaphoglossum perrierianum

Elaphoglossum perrierianum, commonly known as the Perrier's holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnately compound leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The fronds are usually erect and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, with a slender, creeping rhizome. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
