Evolvulus vimineus

Evolvulus vimineus, commonly known as creeping blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces small, blue or violet flowers. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover in gardens. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming throughout the summer months.
