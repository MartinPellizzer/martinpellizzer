Epiadena bicolor

Epiadena bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive bi-colored fronds, with one side green and the other pale or whitish. Its fronds are lance-shaped and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter, making it a striking addition to botanical gardens.

Epiadena bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
