Evolvulus guianensis

Evolvulus guianensis, commonly known as the "blue morning glory," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many climbing and trailing plants.
