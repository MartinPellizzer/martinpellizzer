Eulophia lambii

Eulophia lambii, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with intricate patterns, and it produces numerous small, showy blooms.

Eulophia lambii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
