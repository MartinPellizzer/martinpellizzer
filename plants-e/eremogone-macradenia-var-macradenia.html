Eremogone macradenia var. macradenia

Eremogone macradenia var. macradenia, commonly known as the Macradenia eremogone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive sheath-like base. The plant produces small, pale blue to white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in drought conditions.
