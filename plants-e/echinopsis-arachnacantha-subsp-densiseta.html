Echinopsis arachnacantha subsp. densiseta

Echinopsis arachnacantha subsp.

densiseta, commonly known as the dense-hair echinopsis, is a cacti species native to South America, particularly Argentina. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white spines.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collections and rock gardens.
