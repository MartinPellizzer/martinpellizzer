Elaeocarpus darlacensis

Elaeocarpus darlacensis, commonly known as the Darlac elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
