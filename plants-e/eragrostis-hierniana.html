Eragrostis hierniana

Eragrostis hierniana, commonly known as Hiern's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
