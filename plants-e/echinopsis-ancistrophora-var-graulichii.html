Echinopsis ancistrophora var. graulichii

Echinopsis ancistrophora var. graulichii, commonly known as the Graulich's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is a slow-growing cactus with a short, cylindrical stem covered in spines. The plant produces large, showy white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
