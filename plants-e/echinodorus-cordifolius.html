Echinodorus cordifolius

Echinodorus cordifolius, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate water. It prefers moderate to high light levels and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Echinodorus cordifolius is also known for its ability to help control algae growth in aquariums.
