Epidendrum stamfordianum var. pictum

Epidendrum stamfordianum var. pictum, commonly known as the Painted Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns resembling brushstrokes. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces long, arching inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
