Epidendrum thurstoniorum

Epidendrum thurstoniorum, commonly known as the Thurston's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum thurstoniorum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
