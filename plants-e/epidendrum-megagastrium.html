Epidendrum megagastrium

Epidendrum megagastrium, commonly known as the giant stomach orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often adorned with fleshy, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with numerous flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.

Epidendrum megagastrium thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
