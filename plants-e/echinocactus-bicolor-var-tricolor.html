Echinocactus bicolor var. tricolor

Echinocactus bicolor var. tricolor, commonly known as the Tricolor Hedgehog Cactus, is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that display a unique tricolor pattern of yellow, green, and red. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a prized addition to desert gardens and collections.
