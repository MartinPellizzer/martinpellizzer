Epidendrum microglossum

Epidendrum microglossum, commonly known as the miniature tongue orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a slender, upright stem with numerous small, linear leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, producing small, white to pale yellow blooms with a distinctive, tongue-shaped lip.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its compact size and elegant appearance.
