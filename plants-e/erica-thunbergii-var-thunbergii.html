Erica thunbergii var. thunbergii

Erica thunbergii var. thunbergii, commonly known as the common heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing pink or white flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Erica thunbergii var. thunbergii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive ground cover.
