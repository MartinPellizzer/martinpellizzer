Erysimum odoratum

Erysimum odoratum, commonly known as the sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in early spring. Erysimum odoratum is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
