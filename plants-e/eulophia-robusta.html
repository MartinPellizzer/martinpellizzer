Eulophia robusta

Eulophia robusta, commonly known as the robust eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant has broad, fleshy leaves that are often clustered at the base. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers with three sepals and three petals.

Eulophia robusta thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
