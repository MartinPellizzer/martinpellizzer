Epidendrum spatella

Epidendrum spatella, commonly known as the Spatulate Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, spatulate-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a subtle greenish tinge. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum spatella is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
