Echeveria semivestita var. semivestita

Echeveria semivestita var. semivestita, commonly known as the Silver Dust Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of violet or purple flowers during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
