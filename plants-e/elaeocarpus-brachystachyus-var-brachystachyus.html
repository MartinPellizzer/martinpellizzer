Elaeocarpus brachystachyus var. brachystachyus

Elaeocarpus brachystachyus var. brachystachyus, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain one or two seeds. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in lowland to montane rainforests.

This species is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
