Euphrasia officinalis subsp. kerneri

Euphrasia officinalis subsp.

kerneri, commonly known as the Kern's eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring.
