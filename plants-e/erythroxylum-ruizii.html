Erythroxylum ruizii

Erythroxylum ruizii, commonly known as the Ruiz's erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, red berries that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by animals.
