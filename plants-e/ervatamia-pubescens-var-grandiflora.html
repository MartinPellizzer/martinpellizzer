Ervatamia pubescens var. grandiflora

Ervatamia pubescens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered ervatamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
