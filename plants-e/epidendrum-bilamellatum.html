Epidendrum bilamellatum

Epidendrum bilamellatum, commonly known as the two-lobed epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically has long, narrow leaves and can grow up to several feet in height. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum bilamellatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or greenhouse setting.
