Epidendrum elegantissimum

Epidendrum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid that typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.

Epidendrum elegantissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
