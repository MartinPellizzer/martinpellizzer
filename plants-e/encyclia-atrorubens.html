Encyclia atrorubens

Encyclia atrorubens, commonly known as the dark red Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous, showy flowers with dark red petals and a yellow lip. Its flowers bloom in the spring and are characterized by their intricate, spurred structure.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
