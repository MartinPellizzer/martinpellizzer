Eucharis cyaneosperma

Eucharis cyaneosperma, commonly known as the blue-veined arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a blue-veined pattern. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Eucharis cyaneosperma is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
