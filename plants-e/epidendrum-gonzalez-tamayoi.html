Epidendrum gonzalez-tamayoi

Epidendrum gonzalez-tamayoi, commonly known as the Gonzalez-Tamayo's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
