Echinops opacifolius

Echinops opacifolius, commonly known as the woolly thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical seed heads and silvery, fuzzy leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant. Its flowers are blue to purple in color and bloom in the summer months.

Echinops opacifolius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
