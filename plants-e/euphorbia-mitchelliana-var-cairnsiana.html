Euphorbia mitchelliana var. cairnsiana

Euphorbia mitchelliana var. cairnsiana, commonly known as the Cairns euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to Australia.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cup-like structure called a cyathium. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
