Epacris traversii

Epacris traversii, commonly known as the pink epacris, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.
