Eulophia piscicelliana

Eulophia piscicelliana, commonly known as the fishhook orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with dark markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia piscicelliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
