Eleogiton digitata var. dissoluta

Eleogiton digitata var. dissoluta, commonly known as the slender spider-plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, thin, grass-like leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. This variety is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
