Enkianthus dunnii

Enkianthus dunnii, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often resembling lanterns. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Enkianthus dunnii is also valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
