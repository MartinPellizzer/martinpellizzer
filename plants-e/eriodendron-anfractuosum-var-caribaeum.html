Eriodendron anfractuosum var. caribaeum

Eriodendron anfractuosum var. caribaeum, commonly known as the Caribbean flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed sites.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
