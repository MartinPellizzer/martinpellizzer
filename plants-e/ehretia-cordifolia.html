Ehretia cordifolia

Ehretia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf ehretia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
