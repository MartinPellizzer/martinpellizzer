Erysimum macilentum

Erysimum macilentum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from yellow to orange.

Erysimum macilentum is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
