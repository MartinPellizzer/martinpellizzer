Eriophyllum lanatum var. integrifolium

Eriophyllum lanatum var. integrifolium, commonly known as the Coast Daisy, is a perennial herb native to coastal regions of California and Oregon.

It features grayish-green, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.

Its distinctive appearance and ecological benefits make it an important species in coastal ecosystems.
