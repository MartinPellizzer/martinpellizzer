Epidendrum latorreorum

Epidendrum latorreorum, commonly known as the Latorre's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum latorreorum is a hardy orchid that is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
