Ermania linearis

Ermania linearis, commonly known as the slender ermania, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its linear, grass-like leaves are typically narrow and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ermania linearis is often found in moist, woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
