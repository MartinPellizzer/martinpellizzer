Eriostemon myoporoides var. minor

Eriostemon myoporoides var. minor, commonly known as the small myopor, is a compact shrub native to Australia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, often with a slight fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
