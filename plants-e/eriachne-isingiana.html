Eriachne isingiana

Eriachne isingiana, commonly known as the slender grassvetch, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Eriachne isingiana is adapted to withstand drought and is an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
