Erysimum concinnum

Erysimum concinnum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its flowers are yellow or orange and bloom in early spring.
