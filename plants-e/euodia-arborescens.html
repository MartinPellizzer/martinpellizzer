Euodia arborescens

Euodia arborescens, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, smooth, and greyish bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elliptical, leathery fruits. Its leaves are opposite, elliptic, and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

Euodia arborescens is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
