Erica splendens

Erica splendens, commonly known as the flame heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Erica splendens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and resilience in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
