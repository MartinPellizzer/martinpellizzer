Ehretia changjiangensis

Ehretia changjiangensis, commonly known as the Changjiang Ehretia, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
