Erysimum virgatum var. juranum

Erysimum virgatum var. juranum, commonly known as the Juranum wallflower, is a perennial plant native to Central Europe.

It features bright yellow, semi-double flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has upright, branched stems covered in fine, silvery hairs. Its foliage consists of oval, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
