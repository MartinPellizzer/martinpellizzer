Embelia jussiaei

Embelia jussiaei, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers and red, fleshy berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.
