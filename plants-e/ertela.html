Ertela

Ertela, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves and stems that help it conserve water in harsh environments. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Ertela is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
