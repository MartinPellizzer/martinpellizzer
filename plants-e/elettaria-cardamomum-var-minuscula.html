Elettaria cardamomum var. minuscula

Elettaria cardamomum var. minuscula, commonly known as dwarf cardamom, is a compact variety of the cardamom plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic capsules that contain the prized spice, cardamom seeds. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1-2 meters, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor profile and is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
