Eryngium multifidum var. transiens

Eryngium multifidum var. transiens, commonly known as the transitory thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking blue to purple spiky flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
