Elymus acicularis

Elymus acicularis, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, needle-like leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. This plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides, fields, and dunes. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Elymus acicularis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
