Epidendrum fuscatum var. virescens

Epidendrum fuscatum var. virescens, commonly known as the Emerald Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with clusters of fragrant, greenish-white flowers that have a subtle scent. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
