Eremurus × isabellinus

Eremurus × isabellinus, commonly known as the Isabelline Spearthistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Eremurus, which is part of the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in early summer. It has fibrous roots and hairy stems, which help it conserve water in its native habitat.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and resilience.
