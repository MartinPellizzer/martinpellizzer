Euryloma × leucantha

Euryloma × leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered euryloma, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers, which bloom in terminal clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its elegant floral display.
