Eragrostis volkensii

Eragrostis volkensii, commonly known as Volkens' lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight bluish-gray hue.

Eragrostis volkensii is often found in open woodland and grassland habitats, and it is adapted to dry and semi-arid conditions.
