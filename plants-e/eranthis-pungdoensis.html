Eranthis pungdoensis

Eranthis pungdoensis, commonly known as the Korean spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Korea.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 15-30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down.

Eranthis pungdoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early blooming habit.
