Elaeocarpus palimlimensis

Elaeocarpus palimlimensis, commonly known as the Palimlim tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves with prominent veins. The tree produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by woody, spherical fruits containing one or two seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the understory vegetation in lowland and montane rainforests.
