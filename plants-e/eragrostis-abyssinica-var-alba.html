Eragrostis abyssinica var. alba

Eragrostis abyssinica var. alba, commonly known as white abyssinian grass, is a perennial grass species native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-green foliage and produces fluffy, white inflorescences that resemble clouds. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil stabilization and may have potential for forage in certain regions.
