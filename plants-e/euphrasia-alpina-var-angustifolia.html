Euphrasia alpina var. angustifolia

Euphrasia alpina var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved eyebright, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
