Epidendrum scabrum var. parviflorum

Epidendrum scabrum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
