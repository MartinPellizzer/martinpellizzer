Eugenia ancorifera

Eugenia ancorifera, commonly known as the anchovy guava, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and oval-shaped, glossy leaves. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.
