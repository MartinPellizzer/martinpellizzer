Erica lerouxiae

Erica lerouxiae, commonly known as the Leroux's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This species is characterized by its low-growing, evergreen shrub form with dense foliage. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Erica lerouxiae thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
