Eriostemon filifolius

Eriostemon filifolius, commonly known as the silky-leaved eriostemon, is a small shrub native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia.

It features delicate, slender leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It is well adapted to sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is often cultivated in native gardens and conservation areas.
