Echinops gondarensis

Echinops gondarensis, commonly known as the Gondar thistle, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, spherical crown of leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. Its structure includes a robust central stem with tightly packed, spiny bracts that protect the flowering heads.

Echinops gondarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
