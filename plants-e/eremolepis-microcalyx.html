Eremolepis microcalyx

Eremolepis microcalyx, commonly known as the small-flowered eremolepis, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Its botanical structure supports its role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects in its native habitat.
