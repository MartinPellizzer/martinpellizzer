Epidendrum nonchinense

Epidendrum nonchinense, commonly known as the Orchid of the Andes, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and can grow up to several feet in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum nonchinense is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and ease of cultivation.
