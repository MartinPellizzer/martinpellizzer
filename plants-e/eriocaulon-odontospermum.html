Eriocaulon odontospermum

Eriocaulon odontospermum, commonly known as the tooth-sperm grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which is characterized by its aquatic or semi-aquatic growth habits. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of freshwater bodies.
