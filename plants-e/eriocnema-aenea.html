Eriocnema aenea

Eriocnema aenea, commonly known as the silverleaf acanthus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves covered in dense, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
