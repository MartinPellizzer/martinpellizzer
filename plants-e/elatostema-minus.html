Elatostema minus

Elatostema minus, commonly known as the small elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a compact rosette structure with thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in its native habitat. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and are borne on short stalks.

Elatostema minus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
