Epidendrum palmifolium

Epidendrum palmifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum palmifolium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
