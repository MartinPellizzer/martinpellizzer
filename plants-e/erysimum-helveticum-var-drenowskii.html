Erysimum helveticum var. drenowskii

Erysimum helveticum var. drenowskii, commonly known as the Drenowskii wallflower, is a rare and striking plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a hardy perennial in colder climates.
