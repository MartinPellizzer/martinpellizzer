Eria foliosa

Eria foliosa, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that resemble feathers. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the warmer months. Eria foliosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
