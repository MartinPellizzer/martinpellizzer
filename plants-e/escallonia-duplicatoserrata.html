Escallonia duplicatoserrata

Escallonia duplicatoserrata, commonly known as the Australian snowball, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features showy, rounded clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It is evergreen and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Escallonia duplicatoserrata is drought-tolerant and suitable for coastal gardens due to its salt tolerance.
