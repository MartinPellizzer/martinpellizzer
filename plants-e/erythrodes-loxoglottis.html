Erythrodes loxoglottis

Erythrodes loxoglottis, commonly known as the red-stemmed larkspur, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers and hairy, branched stems. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, it thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. It is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
