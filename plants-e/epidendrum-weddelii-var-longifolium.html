Epidendrum weddelii var. longifolium

Epidendrum weddelii var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features gracefully arching stems that produce multiple clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
