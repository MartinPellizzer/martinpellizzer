Epidendrum criniferum

Epidendrum criniferum, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its popularity among orchid enthusiasts.
