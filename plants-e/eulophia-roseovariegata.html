Eulophia roseovariegata

Eulophia roseovariegata, commonly known as the rose-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and elegant, pendulous inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink or white with distinctive veining, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
