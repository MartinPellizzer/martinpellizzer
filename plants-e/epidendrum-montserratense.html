Epidendrum montserratense

Epidendrum montserratense, commonly known as the Montserrat Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean island of Montserrat.

It is a herbaceous plant with a sympodial growth habit, producing multiple stems that grow horizontally along the ground. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green and covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are usually white or pale pink with a distinctive, elongated lip.

This species is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
