Euroschinus obtusifolius var. obtusifolius

Euroschinus obtusifolius var. obtusifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved euroschinus, is a species native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its simple, alternate, and broadly ovate to elliptical leaves. The leaves are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter green underneath, with a leathery texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
