Euphrasia × venusta

Euphrasia × venusta, commonly known as the pretty eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent dark markings that resemble eyes, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, oval, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Euphrasia × venusta is valued both for its aesthetic qualities and its historical use in herbal medicine.
