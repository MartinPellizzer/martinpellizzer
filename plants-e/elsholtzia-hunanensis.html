Elsholtzia hunanensis

Elsholtzia hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, tubular flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its aromatic properties make it a subject of interest in both traditional medicine and horticulture.
