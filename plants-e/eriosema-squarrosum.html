Eriosema squarrosum

Eriosema squarrosum, commonly known as the rough eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Eriosema squarrosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
