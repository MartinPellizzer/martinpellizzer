Eriogonum latum

Eriogonum latum, commonly known as the broadleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes other plants known for their distinctive leaf structures.
