Eulophia ecalcarata

Eulophia ecalcarata, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in rocky or limestone habitats and prefers well-drained soil.

Eulophia ecalcarata is often found in mountainous regions and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
