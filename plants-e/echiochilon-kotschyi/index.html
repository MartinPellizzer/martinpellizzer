Echiochilon Kotschyi

Echiochilon kotschyi is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This species is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. Due to its limited habitat and threats from environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
