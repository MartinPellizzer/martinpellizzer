Elaphoglossum crinipes

Elaphoglossum crinipes, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are divided into numerous fine, hair-like segments, giving it a feathery appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The fronds are typically evergreen, with a light green color and a soft texture.

Elaphoglossum crinipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
