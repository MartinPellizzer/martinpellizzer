Elytrigia attenuatiglumis

Elytrigia attenuatiglumis, commonly known as the slender glume grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, two-lobed glumes.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in low-nutrient soils.
