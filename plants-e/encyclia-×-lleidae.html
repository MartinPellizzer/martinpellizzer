Encyclia × lleidae

Encyclia × lleidae, commonly known as the Lleida orchid, is a hybrid orchid resulting from the cross between Encyclia fraterna and Encyclia tenuis.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Iberian Peninsula, particularly in Spain. The plant produces slender, upright pseudobulbs that bear lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow or greenish lip.

Encyclia × lleidae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
