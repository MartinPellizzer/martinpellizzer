Eriotheca estevesiae

Eriotheca estevesiae, commonly known as the Estevesea ceiba, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces showy, white or yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The species is noted for its ecological importance in providing habitat and food for diverse rainforest organisms.
