Echinodorus martii var. major

Echinodorus martii var. major, commonly known as Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 20 inches in length, providing excellent coverage in aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular nutrient supplementation to maintain its vibrant green color. It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality by absorbing nitrates.

Echinodorus martii var. major is also known for its hardiness and adaptability to a range of water conditions.
