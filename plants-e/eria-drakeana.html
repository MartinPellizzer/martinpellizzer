Eria drakeana

Eria drakeana, commonly known as the Drake's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces small, elongated pseudobulbs.

Eria drakeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
