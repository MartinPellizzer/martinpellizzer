Eria chandrasekharanii

Eria chandrasekharanii, commonly known as the Chandrasekharanii orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
