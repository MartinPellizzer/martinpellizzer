Electra

Electra, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus *Koelreuteria* and is characterized by its showy, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Electra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
