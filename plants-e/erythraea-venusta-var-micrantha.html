Erythraea venusta var. micrantha

Erythraea venusta var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered erythraea, is a species native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, often with a slight pubescence.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
