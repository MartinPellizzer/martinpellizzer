Encephalartos lanatus

Encephalartos lanatus, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a species of cycad native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, fan-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, silky hairs. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its leaves are typically a deep green color and can grow up to 1.5 meters in length.

Encephalartos lanatus is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
