Epidendrum brachycolumna

Epidendrum brachycolumna, commonly known as the short-stemmed epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features compact, upright pseudobulbs and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Epidendrum brachycolumna is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
