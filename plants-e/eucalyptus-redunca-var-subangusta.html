Eucalyptus redunca var. subangusta

Eucalyptus redunca var. subangusta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often grey-green in color. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with rough, fissured bark on its trunk. It produces small, globular flower clusters that are usually pale green or yellowish in color.

This variety is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions of southern Australia.
