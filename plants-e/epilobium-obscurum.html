Epilobium obscurum

Epilobium obscurum, commonly known as dark willow herb, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters. Its scientific name reflects its dark, often hairy appearance.

It is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other plants like willowherb and evening primrose.
