Echinodorus × barthii

Echinodorus × barthii, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

Echinodorus × barthii is also known for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nutrients and providing habitat for aquatic life.
