Erechtites Quadridentatus Var. Glandulosus

Erechtites quadridentatus var. glandulosus is a unique and lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family. This variant is characterized by its glandular hairs and distinctive leaf structure. It thrives in specific ecological niches, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its blooming period adds a subtle yet important role to local ecosystems. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in botanical studies and horticultural practices.
