Euphrasia × ruebeliana

Euphrasia × ruebeliana, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a hybrid species within the genus Euphrasia, which belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, erect stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Euphrasia × ruebeliana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with partial shade.
