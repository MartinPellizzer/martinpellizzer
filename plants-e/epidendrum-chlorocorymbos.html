Epidendrum chlorocorymbos

Epidendrum chlorocorymbos, commonly known as the green-veined epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, dark green leaves with prominent white veins. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum chlorocorymbos is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
