Epilobium watsonii

Epilobium watsonii, commonly known as Watson's willowherb, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and showy pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.
