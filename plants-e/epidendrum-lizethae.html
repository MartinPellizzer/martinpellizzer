Epidendrum lizethae

Epidendrum lizethae, commonly known as the Lizetha's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum lizethae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
