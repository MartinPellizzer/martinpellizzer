Elyna laxa

Elyna laxa, commonly known as the slender elyna, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and lanceolate, with a slightly hairy surface.

Elyna laxa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
