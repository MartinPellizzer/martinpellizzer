Elaeodendron arboreum

Elaeodendron arboreum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in summer, creating a vibrant display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Elaeodendron arboreum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and traditional medicine.
