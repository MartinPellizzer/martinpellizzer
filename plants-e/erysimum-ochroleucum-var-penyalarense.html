Erysimum ochroleucum var. penyalarense

Erysimum ochroleucum var. penyalarense, commonly known as the Penyalarense Wallflower, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Azores.

It is a herbaceous perennial with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the mild, moist climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern.
