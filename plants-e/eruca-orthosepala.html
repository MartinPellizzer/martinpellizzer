Eruca orthosepala

Eruca orthosepala, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor similar to that of arugula.
