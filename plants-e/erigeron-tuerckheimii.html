Erigeron tuerckheimii

Erigeron tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

Its showy blue to purple flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

Erigeron tuerckheimii is often found in desert scrub and chaparral ecosystems, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
