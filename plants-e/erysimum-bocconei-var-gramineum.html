Erysimum bocconei var. gramineum

Erysimum bocconei var. gramineum, commonly known as the California wallflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that give it its varietal name "gramineum.". It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
