Escobaria duncanii

Escobaria duncanii, commonly known as Duncan's liveforever, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features dense clusters of fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its small, white flowers bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its desolate habitat.

Escobaria duncanii is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soils, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
