Euodia compacta

Euodia compacta, commonly known as the Chinese laurustin, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fragrant, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, orange-red fruits. Euodia compacta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.
