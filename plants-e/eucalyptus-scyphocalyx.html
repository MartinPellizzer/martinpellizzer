Eucalyptus scyphocalyx

Eucalyptus scyphocalyx, commonly known as the white globular eucalypt, is a species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive white, globular flower buds that open to reveal creamy-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a greyish-blue hue.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
