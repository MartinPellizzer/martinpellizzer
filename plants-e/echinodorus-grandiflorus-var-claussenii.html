Echinodorus grandiflorus var. claussenii

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. claussenii, commonly known as the Congo Bucephalandra, is a striking aquatic plant native to the Congo River basin.

It features dark green, broad leaves that are often adorned with a waxy, bluish sheen, giving it a unique appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for aquariums. It produces small, white flowers that are rarely seen in cultivation, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and ease of care in both freshwater and brackish environments.
