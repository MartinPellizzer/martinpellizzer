Elatine cirrhosa

Elatine cirrhosa, commonly known as the slender waterwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and wetlands. The plant has thin, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Elatine cirrhosa is often found in nutrient-rich waters and can serve as an indicator of water quality.
