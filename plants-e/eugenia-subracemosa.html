Eugenia subracemosa

Eugenia subracemosa, commonly known as the small raceme eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often have a slight aromatic scent.

Eugenia subracemosa is typically found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
