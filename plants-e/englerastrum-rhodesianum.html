Englerastrum rhodesianum

Englerastrum rhodesianum, commonly known as the Rhodesian star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth pattern and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are typically arranged in a star-like formation, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Englerastrum rhodesianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
