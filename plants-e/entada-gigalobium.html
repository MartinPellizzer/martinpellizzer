Entada gigalobium

Entada gigalobium, commonly known as the giant legume or African umbrella tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, umbrella-like leaves.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant features long, pendulous pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fibrous, spongy material. Its rapid growth and unique appearance make it a popular ornamental species in tropical gardens.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
