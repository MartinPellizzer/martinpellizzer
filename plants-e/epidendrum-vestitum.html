Epidendrum vestitum

Epidendrum vestitum, commonly known as the velvet epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces striking, pendulous flower spikes with orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It prefers warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum vestitum is relatively easy to care for and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
