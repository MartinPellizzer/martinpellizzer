Epidendrum trinitatis

Epidendrum trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad violet, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, often with a waxy coating.

It blooms during the summer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
