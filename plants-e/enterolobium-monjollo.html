Enterolobium monjollo

Enterolobium monjollo, commonly known as the pink pod tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves and distinctive pink to purple pods that hang in clusters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its roots have a symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria, enhancing soil fertility.

The wood of Enterolobium monjollo is lightweight and used in construction and furniture making.
