Espeletia caldasii

Espeletia caldasii, commonly known as the Caldasia espeletia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. This plant is typically found in high-altitude grasslands, where it thrives in cool, dry conditions. Its rosette-like growth form helps it conserve water and withstand harsh environmental conditions.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
