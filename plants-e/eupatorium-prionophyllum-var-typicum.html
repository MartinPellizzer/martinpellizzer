Eupatorium prionophyllum var. typicum

Eupatorium prionophyllum var. typicum, commonly known as the slender boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a slightly rough texture.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
