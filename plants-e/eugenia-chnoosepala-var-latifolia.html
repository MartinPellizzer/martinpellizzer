Eugenia chnoosepala var. latifolia

Eugenia chnoosepala var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit, which is typically red or purple, is edible and has a sweet, acidic flavor.

It is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
