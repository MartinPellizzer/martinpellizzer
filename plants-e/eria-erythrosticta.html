Eria erythrosticta

Eria erythrosticta, commonly known as the red-streaked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blossoms that are typically pink or purple with red streaks. Eria erythrosticta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
