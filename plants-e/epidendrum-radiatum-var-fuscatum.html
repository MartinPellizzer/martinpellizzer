Epidendrum radiatum var. fuscatum

Epidendrum radiatum var. fuscatum, commonly known as the dark-leaved Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
