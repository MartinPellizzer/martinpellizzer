Eugenia ligustrina var. minensis

Eugenia ligustrina var. minensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Minshan evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature, and it is valued for its resilience in tropical and subtropical climates.
