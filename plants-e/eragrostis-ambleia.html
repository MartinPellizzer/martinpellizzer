Eragrostis ambleia

Eragrostis ambleia, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and drooping inflorescences, which give it a delicate, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its slender, wiry stems and narrow leaves make it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.

Eragrostis ambleia is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
