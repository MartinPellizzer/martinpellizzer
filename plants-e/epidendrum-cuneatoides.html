Epidendrum cuneatoides

Epidendrum cuneatoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid that typically grows as a rhizomatous vine or epiphyte, often found in shaded, humid environments. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy, with a prominent midrib. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

Epidendrum cuneatoides is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
