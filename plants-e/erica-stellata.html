Erica stellata

Erica stellata, commonly known as the star heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its hardy nature.

Erica stellata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to adapt to coastal environments.
