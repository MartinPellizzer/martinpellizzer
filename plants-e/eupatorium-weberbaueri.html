Eupatorium weberbaueri

Eupatorium weberbaueri, commonly known as Weberbauer's boneset, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and streambanks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium weberbaueri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
