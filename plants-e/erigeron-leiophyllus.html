Erigeron leiophyllus

Erigeron leiophyllus, commonly known as the smoothleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
