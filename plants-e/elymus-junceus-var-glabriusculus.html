Elymus junceus var. glabriusculus

Elymus junceus var. glabriusculus, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its leaves are generally smooth and hairless, which is reflected in its varietal name "glabriusculus.".

This species plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
