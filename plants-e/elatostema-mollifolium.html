Elatostema mollifolium

Elatostema mollifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf stellaria, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It features small, alternate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety hair, giving it a soft appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitats.
