Embelia philippinensis

Embelia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine embelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy texture.

The fruit is edible and has been traditionally used in local medicine for its purported health benefits.
