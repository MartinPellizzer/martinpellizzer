Eriocaulon zambesiense

Eriocaulon zambesiense, commonly known as the Zambesi spike-rush, is a member of the Eriocaulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and spike-like inflorescences. Native to southern Africa, it typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikes, and it produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments.
