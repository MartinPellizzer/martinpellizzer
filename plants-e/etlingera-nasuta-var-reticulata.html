Etlingera nasuta var. reticulata

Etlingera nasuta var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate torch ginger, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, red-orange inflorescences with a distinctive reticulated pattern on the bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil.
