Epidendrum llatasii

Epidendrum llatasii, commonly known as the Llatasii Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires moderate light for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
