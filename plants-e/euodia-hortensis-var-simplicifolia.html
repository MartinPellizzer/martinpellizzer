Euodia hortensis var. simplicifolia

Euodia hortensis var. simplicifolia, commonly known as Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves with serrated edges and produces yellowish-green flowers in spring. The plant is characterized by its thorny stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its fruits are small, red berries that mature in autumn.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.
