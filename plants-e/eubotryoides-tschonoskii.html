Eubotryoides tschonoskii

Eubotryoides tschonoskii, commonly known as the Tschonoskii's rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Siberia. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
