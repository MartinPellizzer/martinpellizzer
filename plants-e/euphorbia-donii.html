Euphorbia donii

Euphorbia donii, commonly known as the golden milkweed, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branched stem covered in soft, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in late summer, which are surrounded by colorful bracts that resemble petals. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Euphorbia donii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
