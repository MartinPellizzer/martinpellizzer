Emilia ramulosa

Emilia ramulosa, commonly known as the blueweed or emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has alternate, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Emilia ramulosa is often found in disturbed soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
