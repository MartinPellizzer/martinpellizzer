Eupatorium fuscorubrum

Eupatorium fuscorubrum, commonly known as red-stemmed thoroughwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its reddish stems and showy, pink to purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium fuscorubrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
