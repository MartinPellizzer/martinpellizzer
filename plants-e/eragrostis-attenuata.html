Eragrostis attenuata

Eragrostis attenuata, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a flowering grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, blue-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairie and steppe habitats.

Eragrostis attenuata is a hardy perennial that plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
