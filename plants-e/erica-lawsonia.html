Erica lawsonia

Erica lawsonia, commonly known as the Cape heath or red heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to red, depending on the species. Erica lawsonia thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
