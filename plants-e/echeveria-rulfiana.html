Echeveria rulfiana

Echeveria rulfiana, commonly known as the Rulf's echeveria, is a species of flowering plant in the Crassulaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves. This succulent is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
