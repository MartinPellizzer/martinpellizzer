Elionurus papuanus

Elionurus papuanus, commonly known as the Papuan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Papua and nearby islands.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered in inflorescences.

Elionurus papuanus plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small vertebrates.
