Eleocharis fistulosa var. obtusetrigona

Eleocharis fistulosa var. obtusetrigona, commonly known as the broad-blade spike-rush, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, spike-like inflorescences and is typically found in wetland habitats such as marshes and shallow water areas. It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.

The variety obtusetrigona is distinguished by its broader leaf blades compared to other varieties of Eleocharis fistulosa.
