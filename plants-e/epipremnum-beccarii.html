Epipremnum beccarii

Epipremnum beccarii, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing evergreen vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically light green with a silvery or white underside, giving it its common name. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are not typically noticeable.

Epipremnum beccarii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to climb surfaces such as walls or trellises.
