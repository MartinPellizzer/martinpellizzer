Eupatorium crithmifolium

Eupatorium crithmifolium, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silvery, feathery foliage. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Eupatorium crithmifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
