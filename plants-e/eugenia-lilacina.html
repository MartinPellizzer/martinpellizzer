Eugenia lilacina

Eugenia lilacina, commonly known as the lilac-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, lilac-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Eugenia lilacina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
