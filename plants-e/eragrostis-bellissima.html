Eragrostis bellissima

Eragrostis bellissima, commonly known as the beautiful lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fine, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Eragrostis bellissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
