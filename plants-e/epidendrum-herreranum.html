Epidendrum herreranum

Epidendrum herreranum, commonly known as the Herreranum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Epidendrum herreranum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
