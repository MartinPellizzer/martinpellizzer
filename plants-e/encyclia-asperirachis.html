Encyclia asperirachis

Encyclia asperirachis, commonly known as the rough-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive greenish hue. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia asperirachis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
