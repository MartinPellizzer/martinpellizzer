Eriogonum palmerianum

Eriogonum palmerianum, commonly known as Palmer's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and plains. It has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Palmer's buckwheat is adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert regions.
