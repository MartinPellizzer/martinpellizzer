Eragrostis weigeltiana

Eragrostis weigeltiana, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, exhibiting drought tolerance once established.

Its fine texture and striking color make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and erosion control projects.
