Epidendrum spondiadum

Epidendrum spondiadum, commonly known as the coral spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences with clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum spondiadum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
