Epidendrum verrucipes

Epidendrum verrucipes, commonly known as the velvety epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity to flourish.

Epidendrum verrucipes is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
