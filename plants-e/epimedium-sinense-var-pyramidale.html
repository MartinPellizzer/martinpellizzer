Epimedium sinense var. pyramidale

Epimedium sinense var. pyramidale, commonly known as the Chinese barberry or barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, erect pedicels. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
