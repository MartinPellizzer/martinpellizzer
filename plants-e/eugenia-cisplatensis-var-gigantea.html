Eugenia cisplatensis var. gigantea

Eugenia cisplatensis var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant velvetleaf, is a large shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
