Erica velitaris var. velitaris

Erica velitaris var. velitaris, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces vibrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica velitaris var. velitaris is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its drought tolerance and hardy nature.
