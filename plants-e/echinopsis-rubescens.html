Echinopsis rubescens

Echinopsis rubescens, commonly known as the red echinopsis, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical or slightly elongated stem, which is often covered in spines. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Echinopsis rubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
