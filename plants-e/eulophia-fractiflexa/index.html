Eulophia Fractiflexa

Eulophia fractiflexa is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique and delicate appearance, this plant features intricate, often fragrant blossoms. It thrives in humid environments with ample sunlight and well-drained soil. The species is notable for its ability to adapt to various microclimates. Its beauty and rarity make it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
