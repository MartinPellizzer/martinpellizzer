Encyclia mariae

Encyclia mariae, commonly known as the Marie's Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of dark green, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or purple markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia mariae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
