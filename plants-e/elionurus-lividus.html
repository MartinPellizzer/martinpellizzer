Elionurus lividus

Elionurus lividus, commonly known as the blue grass or slender grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually white or pale blue, appearing in late summer.

Elionurus lividus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
