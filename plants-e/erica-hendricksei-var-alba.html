Erica hendricksei var. alba

Erica hendricksei var. alba, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate appearance to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
