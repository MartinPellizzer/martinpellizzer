Erysimum Eriopodum

Erysimum eriopodum is a small, flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is known for its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. It is often found in coastal areas and open woodlands. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
