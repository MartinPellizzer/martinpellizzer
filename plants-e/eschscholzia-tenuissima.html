Eschscholzia tenuissima

Eschscholzia tenuissima, commonly known as the slender poppy, is a low-growing annual herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, hairy stems and small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Eschscholzia tenuissima is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
