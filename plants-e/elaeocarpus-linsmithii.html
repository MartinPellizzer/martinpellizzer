Elaeocarpus linsmithii

Elaeocarpus linsmithii, commonly known as the Linsmith's elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, and it is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
