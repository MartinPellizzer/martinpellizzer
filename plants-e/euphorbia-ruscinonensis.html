Euphorbia ruscinonensis

Euphorbia ruscinonensis, commonly known as the Ruscinonese spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
