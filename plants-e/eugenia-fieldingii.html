Eugenia fieldingii

Eugenia fieldingii, commonly known as the Fielding's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia fieldingii thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories.
