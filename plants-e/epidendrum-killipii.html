Epidendrum killipii

Epidendrum killipii, commonly known as the Killip's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically blooms during the spring and summer months, showcasing its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum killipii is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
