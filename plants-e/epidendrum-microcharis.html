Epidendrum microcharis

Epidendrum microcharis, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender stem with numerous small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its roots are often epiphytic, meaning they can grow on trees or other surfaces.

Epidendrum microcharis is prized for its compact size and delicate appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
