Eremobium aegyptiacum var. lineare

Eremobium aegyptiacum var. lineare, commonly known as the linear eremobium, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, succulent growth habit and produces showy, sparsely branched inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple and are borne on long, slender stems.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
