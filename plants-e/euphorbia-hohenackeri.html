Euphorbia hohenackeri

Euphorbia hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. The plant has a spiny exterior, which serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia hohenackeri is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh conditions.
