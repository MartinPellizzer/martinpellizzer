Eurychanes

Eurychanes, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and spiny, leaf-like bracts that surround the flower head. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in coastal environments. Its structure includes a woody stem with alternate, narrow leaves.

Eurychanes is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
