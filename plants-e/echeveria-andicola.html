Echeveria andicola

Echeveria andicola, commonly known as the Andean rose, is a succulent native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Echeveria andicola is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
