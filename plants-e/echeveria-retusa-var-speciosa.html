Echeveria retusa var. speciosa

Echeveria retusa var. speciosa, commonly known as the Shiny Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray to silver-green in color. The leaves are covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives them a shiny appearance. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements.
