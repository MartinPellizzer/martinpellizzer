Eriachne aurea var. virens

Eriachne aurea var. virens, commonly known as the green silky grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it can become invasive in certain environments.
