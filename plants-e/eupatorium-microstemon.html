Eupatorium microstemon

Eupatorium microstemon, commonly known as small-stamen boneset, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small and clustered in spherical heads, typically blooming in late summer to early fall.

Eupatorium microstemon is often found in moist habitats such as meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks.
