Epimedium pinnatum var. integrifolium

Epimedium pinnatum var. integrifolium, commonly known as the "Chinese barrenwort," is a perennial herb native to China.

It features striking, shield-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are borne on arching stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, adding ornamental value to the landscape.
