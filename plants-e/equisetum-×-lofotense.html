Equisetum × lofotense

Equisetum × lofotense, commonly known as the Lofoten horsetail, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, jointed stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, spore-bearing cones at the tips of its stems, which are a key feature of its reproductive biology. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands.

Equisetum × lofotense is often found in northern regions, including the Lofoten Islands of Norway, where it contributes to the local flora.
