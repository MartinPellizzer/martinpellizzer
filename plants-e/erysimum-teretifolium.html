Erysimum teretifolium

Erysimum teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has linear, often hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum teretifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
