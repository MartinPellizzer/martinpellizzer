Eriocaulon quinquangulare subsp. quinquangulare

Eriocaulon quinquangulare subsp.

quinquangulare, commonly known as the five-angled spikerush, is a member of the Poaceae family. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences.
