Etlingera kenyalang

Etlingera kenyalang, commonly known as the Kenyan ginger or mountain ginger, is a tropical plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and aromatic leaves. The plant produces striking, pale yellow to white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a distinct, spicy fragrance.

Etlingera kenyalang is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
