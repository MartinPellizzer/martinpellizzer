Eragrostis chariis

Eragrostis chariis, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Eragrostis chariis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
