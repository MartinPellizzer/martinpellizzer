Erodium × cariotii

Erodium × cariotii, commonly known as the hybrid common stormweed, is a flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Erodium cicutarium and Erodium hyssopifolium, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia. It produces small, pink or purple flowers and has a prostrate or upright growth habit depending on environmental conditions.

Due to its adaptability, it is often considered a weed in agricultural and garden settings.
