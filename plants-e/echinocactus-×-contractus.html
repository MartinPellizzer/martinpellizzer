Echinocactus × contractus

Echinocactus × contractus, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a hybrid succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and densely packed, bright green to yellowish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. Its thick, water-storing stems help it survive in arid environments.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
