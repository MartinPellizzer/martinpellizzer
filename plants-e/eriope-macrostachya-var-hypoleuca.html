Eriope macrostachya var. hypoleuca

Eriope macrostachya var. hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered eriope, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners interested in ornamental plants.

The variety hypoleuca is distinguished by its lighter floral coloration compared to other varieties of Eriope macrostachya.
