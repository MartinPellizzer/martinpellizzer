Everardia lanata

Everardia lanata, commonly known as the woolly everardia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a soft, hairy texture.

Everardia lanata is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
