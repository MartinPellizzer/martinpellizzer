Elaeagnus pungens var. simonii

Elaeagnus pungens var. simonii, commonly known as silver leaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The leaves are also known to have a slight fragrance and can be used in traditional medicine.
