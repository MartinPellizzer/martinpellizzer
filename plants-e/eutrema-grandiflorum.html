Eutrema grandiflorum

Eutrema grandiflorum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-gray foliage, which help it thrive in salty, windy environments.
