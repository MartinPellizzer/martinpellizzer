Echinopsis Aurea Var. Dobeana

Dobeana echinopsis, scientifically known as Echinopsis aurea var. dobeana, is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It is prized for its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in harsh environments with minimal water. This variety is distinguished by its tall, cylindrical stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its unique shape and vibrant color make it a popular choice for both collectors and gardeners. Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, Dobeana echinopsis has become a sought-after plant in the world of succulent cultivation.
