Elatostema parvum

Elatostema parvum, commonly known as the smallleaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Elatostema parvum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora.
