Elatostema acuminatum var. striolatum

Elatostema acuminatum var. striolatum, commonly known as the veined prostrate water star, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a sprawling habit with thin, succulent stems that often root at the nodes. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a distinctive veined pattern that gives it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.
