Eupatorium hymenophyllum

Eupatorium hymenophyllum, commonly known as the fern-leaf ironweed, is a striking evergreen perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round interest in the garden.

Eupatorium hymenophyllum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a versatile addition to shaded landscapes.
