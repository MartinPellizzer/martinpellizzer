Eremostachys laciniata var. superba

Eremostachys laciniata var. superba, commonly known as the beautiful leaf eremostachys, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, deeply lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of silvery hair. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and alpine displays.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a sought-after plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
