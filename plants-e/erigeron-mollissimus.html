Erigeron mollissimus

Erigeron mollissimus, commonly known as woolly fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed areas.

The flowers are usually white or pale blue, with yellow centers, and attract various pollinators.
