Eriolarynx australis

Eriolarynx australis, commonly known as the southern lily.

It is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Eriolarynx australis is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
