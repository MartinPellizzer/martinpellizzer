Erythraea calycosa var. nana

Erythraea calycosa var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf erythraea, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to reddish in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants.
