Erica atherstonei

Erica atherstonei, commonly known as the Atherstone heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica atherstonei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
