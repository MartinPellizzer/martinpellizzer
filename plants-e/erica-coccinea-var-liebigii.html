Erica coccinea var. liebigii

Erica coccinea var. liebigii, commonly known as the red heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
