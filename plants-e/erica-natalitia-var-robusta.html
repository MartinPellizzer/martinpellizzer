Erica natalitia var. robusta

Erica natalitia var. robusta, commonly known as the Natal heath, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and needle-like leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy environments.
