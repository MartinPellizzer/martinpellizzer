Eriostemon correifolius

Eriostemon correifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf eriostemon, is a species of flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are characterized by their broad, ovate shape and a slightly hairy surface.

Eriostemon correifolius is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.
