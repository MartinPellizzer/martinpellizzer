Etlingera fulgens

Etlingera fulgens, commonly known as the flame ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its foliage. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a glossy texture.
