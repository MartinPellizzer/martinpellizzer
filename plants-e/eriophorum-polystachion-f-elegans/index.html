Eriophorum Polystachion F. Elegans

Eriophorum polystachion f. elegans is a striking plant known for its delicate, feathery appearance. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. This plant is often found in wetland environments, thriving in moist soils and shallow waters. Its elegant structure makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining wetland biodiversity.
