Emilia marlothiana

Emilia marlothiana, commonly known as the marloth's emilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Emilia marlothiana is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
