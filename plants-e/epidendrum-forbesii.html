Epidendrum forbesii

Epidendrum forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum forbesii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
