Evolvulus helichrysoides

Evolvulus helichrysoides, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a trailing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along surfaces. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The flowers have a delicate, sweet fragrance and are pollinated by insects.
