Euphrasia crosby-smithii

Euphrasia crosby-smithii, commonly known as Crosby-Smith's eyebright, is a small annual herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features delicate, hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, showy eyespots. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, glandular hairs.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
