Episcia glabra

Episcia glabra, commonly known as the smooth episcia, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Episcia glabra is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
