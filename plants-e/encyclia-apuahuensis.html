Encyclia apuahuensis

Encyclia apuahuensis, commonly known as the Apuahuan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces erect, branching pseudobulbs that support narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and arranged in racemose inflorescences, often in shades of yellow and green.

Encyclia apuahuensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.
