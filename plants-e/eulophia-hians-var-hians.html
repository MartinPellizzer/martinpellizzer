Eulophia hians var. hians

Eulophia hians var. hians, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
