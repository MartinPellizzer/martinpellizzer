Eriachne avenacea

Eriachne avenacea, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and disturbed soils.

Eriachne avenacea is often found in open areas and is adapted to both temperate and subtropical climates.
