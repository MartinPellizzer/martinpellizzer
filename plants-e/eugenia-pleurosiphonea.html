Eugenia pleurosiphonea

Eugenia pleurosiphonea, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia pleurosiphonea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
