Epidendrum stalkyi

Epidendrum stalkyi, commonly known as the Stalk's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems that can grow up to several feet in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum stalkyi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
