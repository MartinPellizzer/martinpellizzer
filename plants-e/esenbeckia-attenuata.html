Esenbeckia attenuata

Esenbeckia attenuata, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy berries that are edible.
