Euodia spectabilis

Euodia spectabilis, commonly known as the fragrant leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Rutaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elliptical, orange-red fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its aromatic properties.
