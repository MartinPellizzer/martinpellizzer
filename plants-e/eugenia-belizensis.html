Eugenia belizensis

Eugenia belizensis, commonly known as the Belize holly, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Belize and northern Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

Eugenia belizensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential ecological benefits.
