Echiochilon baricum

Echiochilon baricum, commonly known as the Himalayan spurge, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features a stout, erect stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Echiochilon baricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
