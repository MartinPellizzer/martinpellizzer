Epidendrum capense

Epidendrum capense, commonly known as the Cape Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum capense is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
