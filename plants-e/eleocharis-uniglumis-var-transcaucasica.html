Eleocharis uniglumis var. transcaucasica

Eleocharis uniglumis var. transcaucasica, commonly known as the Transcaucasian spike-rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often clustered together. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet at the tip of each stem.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a fibrous texture.
