Erianthemum dregei

Erianthemum dregei, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light. Its flowers bloom throughout the year in warm climates, making it a popular houseplant.

Erianthemum dregei is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of care in indoor gardening.
