Eria forbesiana

Eria forbesiana, commonly known as the Forbes' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Eria forbesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
