Eleocharis compressa var. acutisquamata

Eleocharis compressa var. acutisquamata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, pointed leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, ponds, and along the edges of streams.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to moist, nutrient-poor soils.
