Elephantopus hypomalacus

Elephantopus hypomalacus, commonly known as the narrowleaf elephantopus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
