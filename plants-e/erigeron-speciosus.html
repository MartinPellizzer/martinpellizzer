Erigeron speciosus

Erigeron speciosus, commonly known as the showy fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with white ray florets surrounding a yellow disk.
