Elytranthe Albida

Elytranthe albida is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America. Known for its striking white petals and compact growth habit, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is often found in cloud forests and rocky slopes where it adapts to harsh conditions. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Elytranthe albida is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
