Encyclia grisebachiana

Encyclia grisebachiana, commonly known as the Guatemalan orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the dry season. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Encyclia grisebachiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
