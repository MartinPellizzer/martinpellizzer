Eugenia creaghii

Eugenia creaghii, commonly known as the creaghii eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface.
