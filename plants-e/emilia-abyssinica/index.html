Emilia Abyssinica

Emilia abyssinica is a striking flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia. Known for its vibrant, spherical blooms that resemble small lanterns, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and conservation. Despite its beauty, Emilia abyssinica remains relatively unknown to the wider public.
