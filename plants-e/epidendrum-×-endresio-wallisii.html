Epidendrum × endresio-wallisii

Epidendrum × endresio-wallisii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Epidendrum nocturnum and Epidendrum secundum.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple stems that can reach several feet in length. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and can be white, pink, or purple, depending on the specific cultivar. This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
