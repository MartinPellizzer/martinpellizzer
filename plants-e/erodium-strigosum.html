Erodium strigosum

Erodium strigosum, commonly known as sticky eryngo, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with five petals. Its leaves are covered in a fine, sticky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
