Euphrasia × brevipila f. eglandulosa

Euphrasia × brevipila f. eglandulosa, commonly known as the glandular eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate and covered with glandular dots, which give it its common name.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional use in herbal medicine.
