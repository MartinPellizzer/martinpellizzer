Eulophia duperreana

Eulophia duperreana, commonly known as the Duperrean orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and distinctive pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in two rows along the stem.

Eulophia duperreana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
