Epidendrum pachysepalum

Epidendrum pachysepalum, commonly known as the thick-sepaled epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features dense, fleshy roots that help it survive in its natural habitat with limited moisture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
