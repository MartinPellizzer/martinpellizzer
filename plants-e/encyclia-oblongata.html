Encyclia oblongata

Encyclia oblongata, commonly known as the oblong orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia oblongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
