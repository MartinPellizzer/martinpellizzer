Epidendrum libiae

Epidendrum libiae, commonly known as the Libia Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum libiae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
