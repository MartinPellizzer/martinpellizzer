Echinocereus scheeri var. nigrispinus

Echinocereus scheeri var. nigrispinus, commonly known as the black spined hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features cylindrical stems covered in dark, spiny tubercles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
