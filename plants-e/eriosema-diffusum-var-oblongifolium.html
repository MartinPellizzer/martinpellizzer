Eriosema diffusum var. oblongifolium

Eriosema diffusum var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features hairy stems and lanceolate to oblong leaves that are often glandular-dotted. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
