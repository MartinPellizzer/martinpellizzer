Eragrostis cilianensis subsp. major

Eragrostis cilianensis subsp.

major, commonly known as the large panicgrass, is a grass species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, while the flowers are small and wind-pollinated.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
