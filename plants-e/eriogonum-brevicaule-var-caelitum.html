Eriogonum brevicaule var. caelitum

Eriogonum brevicaule var. caelitum, commonly known as the skyblue wildrye, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, sky-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
