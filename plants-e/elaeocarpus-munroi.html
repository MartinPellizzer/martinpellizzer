Elaeocarpus munroi

Elaeocarpus munroi, commonly known as the mountain elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and showy white flowers. The tree produces small, spherical fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland to montane forest habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
