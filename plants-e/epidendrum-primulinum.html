Epidendrum primulinum

Epidendrum primulinum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum primulinum is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
