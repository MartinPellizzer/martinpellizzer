Epipactis luzonensis

Epipactis luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon helleborine, is an orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces a tall flowering stem bearing numerous pale green to white flowers with distinctive lip structures. It thrives in moist, shaded forest understories and is adapted to the humid climate of Luzon.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
