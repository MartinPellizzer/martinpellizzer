Elleanthus lindenii var. cymbibracteus

Elleanthus lindenii var. cymbibracteus, commonly known as the Cape York flatleaf, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, flat clusters, typical of the Myrtaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
