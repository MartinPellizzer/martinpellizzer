Eupatorium Rotundifolium Var. Lanceolatum

Eupatorium rotundifolium var. lanceolatum is a striking native plant commonly found in moist woodland areas across eastern North America. Known for its showy purple flowers and distinctive lance-shaped leaves, it adds vibrant color to the late summer landscape. This variety stands out from the typical round-leaved eupatorium due to its elongated foliage. It thrives in partial shade and is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles.
