Embelia nyassana

Embelia nyassana, commonly known as African star anise, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers and small, berry-like fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit, which ripens to a dark purple color, contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
