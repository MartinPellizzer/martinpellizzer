Elatostema Tutuilense

Elatostema tutuilense is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes many species known for their stinging hairs. This particular species is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate white flowers. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is considered an endangered plant.
