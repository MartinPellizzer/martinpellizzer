Euphorbia amygdaloides var. biennis

Euphorbia amygdaloides var. biennis, commonly known as the silver leaf euphorbia, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and unique, spiny stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its toxic sap, it requires careful handling to avoid skin irritation.
