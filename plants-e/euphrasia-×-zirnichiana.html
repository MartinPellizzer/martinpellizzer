Euphrasia × zirnichiana

Euphrasia × zirnichiana, commonly known as the hybrid eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Euphrasia × zirnichiana is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and disturbed soils, thriving in partial shade to full sun.
