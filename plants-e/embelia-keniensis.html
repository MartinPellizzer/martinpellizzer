Embelia keniensis

Embelia keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan embelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
