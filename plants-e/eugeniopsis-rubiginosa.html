Eugeniopsis rubiginosa

Eugeniopsis rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stained eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
