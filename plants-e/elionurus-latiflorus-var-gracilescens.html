Elionurus latiflorus var. gracilescens

Elionurus latiflorus var. gracilescens, commonly known as the slender-flowered elionurus, is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale yellow in color.

This variant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
