Epidendrum phyllocharis

Epidendrum phyllocharis, commonly known as the golden leaf epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves that are often a vibrant green with a slight yellowish hue. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellow-green flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum phyllocharis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
