Epimedium musschianum var. multifoliolatum

Epimedium musschianum var. multifoliolatum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features striking, bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has dark green, leathery foliage with a distinctive, lobed leaf shape. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its early blooming habit.
