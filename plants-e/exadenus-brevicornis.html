Exadenus brevicornis

Exadenus brevicornis, commonly known as the narrow-horned fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with clustered roots, and its fronds are usually pinnately divided with a smooth, leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in dense forest undergrowth.
