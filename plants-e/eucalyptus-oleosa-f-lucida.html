Eucalyptus oleosa f. lucida

Eucalyptus oleosa f. lucida, commonly known as the lucid eucalyptus, is a variant of the slender gum tree.

It is characterized by its smooth, often greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are typically glaucous underneath. The plant produces small, globular flower clusters that are usually pale green or yellowish in color. It is native to southern Australia and thrives in dry, open habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its aromatic oils and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
