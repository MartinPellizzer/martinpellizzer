Erica sphaeroidea

Erica sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globular heath, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family Ericaceae.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Erica sphaeroidea is well adapted to dry, nutrient-poor environments and is often found in coastal areas.
