Erica cederbergensis

Erica cederbergensis, commonly known as the Cedarberg heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers during the summer months.

Erica cederbergensis thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
