Elaeagnus kanaii var. osmastonii

Elaeagnus kanaii var. osmastonii, commonly known as the Japanese silverberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and small, yellowish-green berries that appear in the fall. The plant has a rapid growth rate and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
