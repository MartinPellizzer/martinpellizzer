Eulophia decaryana

Eulophia decaryana, commonly known as the silver sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a silvery or pale green sheen. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct, fragrant scent. Eulophia decaryana thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to high light exposure.

Its growth habit is generally sympodial, with new growth emerging from the base of the plant.
