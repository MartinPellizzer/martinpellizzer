Eucalyptus wandoo

Eucalyptus wandoo, commonly known as the wandoo, is a medium to large evergreen tree native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom. The tree produces small, globular flower clusters that range in color from white to pink, which attract various pollinators. Its timber is valued for its strength and durability, making it useful in construction and furniture-making.

The plant is also known for its aromatic oils, which have medicinal and industrial applications.
