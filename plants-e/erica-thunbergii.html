Erica thunbergii

Erica thunbergii, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves and produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months. The plant is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Erica thunbergii is also valued for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
