Eugenia robustior

Eugenia robustior, commonly known as the broadleaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are typically broad and leathery, with a glossy appearance.

Eugenia robustior is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
