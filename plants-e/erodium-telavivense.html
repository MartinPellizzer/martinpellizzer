Erodium telavivense

Erodium telavivense, commonly known as the Tel Aviv erodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides, contributing to local biodiversity.
