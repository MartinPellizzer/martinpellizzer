Erianthemum aethiopicum

Erianthemum aethiopicum, commonly known as African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Erianthemum aethiopicum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
