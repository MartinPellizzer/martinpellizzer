Eranthis keiskei

Eranthis keiskei, commonly known as the Japanese spring beauty, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features showy, yellow flowers with five petals that bloom in early spring. The plant has delicate, hairy leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in woodland habitats and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Eranthis keiskei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and early blooming habit.
