Erodium cicutarium var. acaule

Erodium cicutarium var. acaule, commonly known as the slender field bean, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Its fruit is a slender, twisted pod that contains several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
