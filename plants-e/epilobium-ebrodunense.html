Epilobium ebrodunense

Epilobium ebrodunense, commonly known as the Ebro River willow herb, is a flowering plant species native to southern Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as riverbanks and meadows. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, pink to purple flowers in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.
