Eumecanthus subsinuatus

Eumecanthus subsinuatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature white petals surrounding a central yellow disk. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Eumecanthus subsinuatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
