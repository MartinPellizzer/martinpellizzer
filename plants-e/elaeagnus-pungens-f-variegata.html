Elaeagnus pungens f. variegata

Elaeagnus pungens f. variegata, commonly known as silver leaf buckthorn, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silvery-white leaves that contrast with its dark green foliage, creating a striking visual effect. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.

Elaeagnus pungens f. variegata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
