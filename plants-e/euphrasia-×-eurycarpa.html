Euphrasia × eurycarpa

Euphrasia × eurycarpa, commonly known as the broad-fruited eyebright, is a hybrid plant species within the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its fruits are distinctive for their broad, fleshy structure, which aids in seed dispersal.
