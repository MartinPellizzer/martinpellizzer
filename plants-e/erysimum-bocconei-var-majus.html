Erysimum bocconei var. majus

Erysimum bocconei var. majus, commonly known as the Sierra Madre wallflower, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and linear, gray-green leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants.

This variety is often found in open habitats such as hillsides and chaparral.
