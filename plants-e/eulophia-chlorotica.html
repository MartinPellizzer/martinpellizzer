Eulophia chlorotica

Eulophia chlorotica, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Eulophia chlorotica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
