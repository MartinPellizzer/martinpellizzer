Eleusine stricta

Eleusine stricta, commonly known as rigid grass or slender indigo, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny blue or purple flowers. Eleusine stricta is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its growth habit makes it a common weed in agricultural fields and along roadsides.
