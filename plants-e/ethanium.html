Ethanium

Ethanium, commonly known as the moonflower, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is renowned for its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Ethanium thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
