Euphorbia × andrefandrovana

Euphorbia × andrefandrovana, commonly known as the Andrefanona euphorbia, is a hybrid plant native to Madagascar.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces unique cyathia, which are cup-shaped inflorescences containing small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and adaptability to poor soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in dry gardens.
