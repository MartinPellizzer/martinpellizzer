Elaeagnus sinensis

Elaeagnus sinensis, commonly known as silver berry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters, followed by edible, red berries that are rich in nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Elaeagnus sinensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
