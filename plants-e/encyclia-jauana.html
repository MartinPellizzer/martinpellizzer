Encyclia jauana

Encyclia jauana, commonly known as the Jauana orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive greenish tint. Encyclia jauana thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
