Eupatorium orbignyanum

Eupatorium orbignyanum, commonly known as the orbignya boneset, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged oppositely along the stem. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is known for its aromatic properties.

Eupatorium orbignyanum is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
