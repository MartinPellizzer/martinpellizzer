Epimedium × sulphureum

Epimedium × sulphureum, commonly known as the golden monkshood, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained, acidic soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a leathery texture, adding ornamental value throughout the growing season.

Epimedium × sulphureum is also valued for its ability to naturalize and provide long-lasting ground cover.
