Euphorbion helioscopium

Euphorbion helioscopium, commonly known as the sun-seeking spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, ribbed stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or green. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in full sun and well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Euphorbion helioscopium is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.
