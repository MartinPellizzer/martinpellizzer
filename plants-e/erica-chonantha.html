Erica chonantha

Erica chonantha, commonly known as the silver leaf heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. This plant is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. Erica chonantha produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers during the summer months.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation, a unique biome in South Africa.
