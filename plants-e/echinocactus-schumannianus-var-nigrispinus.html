Echinocactus schumannianus var. nigrispinus

Echinocactus schumannianus var. nigrispinus, commonly known as the Black-spined Hedgehog Cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in dense, dark green spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for desert gardens and cactus collections.
