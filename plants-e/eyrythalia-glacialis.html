Eyrythalia glacialis

Eyrythalia glacialis, commonly known as the glacier erythra.

This rare alpine plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink. It thrives in high-altitude environments, typically found in mountainous regions with cold climates. The plant has a compact growth form, with hairy leaves that help insulate it against freezing temperatures.

Its botanical features are adapted to survive in nutrient-poor, rocky soils and harsh weather conditions.
