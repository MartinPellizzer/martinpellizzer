Eugenia cryptocardia

Eugenia cryptocardia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia cryptocardia is typically found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.
