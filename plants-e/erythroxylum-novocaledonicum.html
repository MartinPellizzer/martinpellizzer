Erythroxylum novocaledonicum

Erythroxylum novocaledonicum, commonly known as the New Caledonia chili or the red cacao, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia in the South Pacific.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its erect, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing elliptical, red seeds.
