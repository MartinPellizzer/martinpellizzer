Erica goatcheriana var. petrensis

Erica goatcheriana var. petrensis, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
