Eriocephala nigrispina

Eriocephala nigrispina, commonly known as the black-spined eriocephala, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. The plant has dark, spiny stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Eriocephala nigrispina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
