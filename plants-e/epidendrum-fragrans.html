Epidendrum fragrans

Epidendrum fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a faint fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum fragrans is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
