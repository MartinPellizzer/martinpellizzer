Encephalartos altensteinii var. parvifolius

Encephalartos altensteinii var. parvifolius, commonly known as the dwarf knobbed cycad, is a rare and distinctive cycad species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and its unique, knob-like structures on the trunk. The plant produces small, pale green leaves that are arranged in a tight rosette pattern. Its inflorescences are typically concealed within the foliage, and it produces both male and female cones.
