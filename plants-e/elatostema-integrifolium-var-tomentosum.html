Elatostema integrifolium var. tomentosum

Elatostema integrifolium var. tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly leaf stellaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Australia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a woolly appearance. The plant produces tiny, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Its unique foliage and compact growth make it a distinctive addition to native plant gardens.
