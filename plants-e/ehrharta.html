Ehrharta

Ehrharta, commonly known as tall fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the warmer regions of the Old World.

It is characterized by its coarse, upright growth habit and dark green, narrow leaves. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that contain small, spikelet-like flowers. Ehrharta is highly adaptable and thrives in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for turfgrass.

Its deep root system provides excellent erosion control and drought tolerance.
