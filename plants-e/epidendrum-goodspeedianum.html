Epidendrum goodspeedianum

Epidendrum goodspeedianum, commonly known as the Goodspeed's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptical, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Epidendrum goodspeedianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
