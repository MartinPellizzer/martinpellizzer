Echeveria backebergii

Echeveria backebergii, commonly known as the Cape Town echeveria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers on tall, branching stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
