Eremophila fraseri subsp. fraseri

Eremophila fraseri subsp.

fraseri, commonly known as Fraser's eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It is part of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit.

The plant produces striking spikes of tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
