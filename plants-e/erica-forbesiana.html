Erica forbesiana

Erica forbesiana, commonly known as the Forbes' heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Erica forbesiana thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
