Erigeron acris subsp. droebachensis

Erigeron acris subsp.

droebachensis, commonly known as the Droebachense Fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.
