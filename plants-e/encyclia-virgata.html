Encyclia virgata

Encyclia virgata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia virgata is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
