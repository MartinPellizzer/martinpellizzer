Euphrasia × aspera

Euphrasia × aspera, commonly known as the rough pimpernel, is a hybrid plant in the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has hairy, often glandular leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in meadows, woodland edges, and along roadsides.

Euphrasia × aspera is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open areas, making it a common sight in temperate regions.
