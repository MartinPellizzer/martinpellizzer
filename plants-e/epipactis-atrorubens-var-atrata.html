Epipactis atrorubens var. atrata

Epipactis atrorubens var. atrata, commonly known as the dark red helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has strap-like leaves that are dark green in color. It produces showy, spurred flowers that are pollinated by insects, particularly bees.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
