Epidendrum microcardium

Epidendrum microcardium, commonly known as the miniature pink epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Epidendrum microcardium is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
