Erysimum longirostre

Erysimum longirostre, commonly known as the long-beaked wallflower, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features linear, gray-green leaves with a rough texture, and its stems are typically hairy. Erysimum longirostre thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
