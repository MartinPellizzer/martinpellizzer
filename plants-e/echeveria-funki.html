Echeveria funki

Echeveria funki, commonly known as the Funki Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purple tint. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
