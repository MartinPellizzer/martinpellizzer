Eupatorium maximiliani var. pauciflorum

Eupatorium maximiliani var. pauciflorum, commonly known as the slender eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinators.
