Eragrostis distans

Eragrostis distans, commonly known as the slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Eragrostis distans is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
