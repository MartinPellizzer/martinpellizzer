Epimedium macranthum var. musschianum

Epimedium macranthum var. musschianum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and low maintenance requirements.
