Eriocaulon woodii var. minor

Eriocaulon woodii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf marsh grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in marshes and along the edges of water bodies.
