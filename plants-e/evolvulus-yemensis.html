Evolvulus yemensis

Evolvulus yemensis, commonly known as Yemen morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Yemen.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, dry climates and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
