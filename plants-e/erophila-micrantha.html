Erophila micrantha

Erophila micrantha, commonly known as small-flowered erophila, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and wetlands. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with four petals. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

Erophila micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
