Eugenia korthalsiana

Eugenia korthalsiana, commonly known as the Korthals's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that ripens to a dark purple or red color.

Eugenia korthalsiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
