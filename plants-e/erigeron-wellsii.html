Erigeron wellsii

Erigeron wellsii, commonly known as the Blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its blue to violet flower heads that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a basal rosette of gray-green leaves and can reach heights of up to 30 inches.

Erigeron wellsii is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
