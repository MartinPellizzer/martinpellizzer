Escobaria Strobiliformis Var. Durispina

Escobaria strobiliformis var. durispina is a unique and resilient plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. Known for its compact, cushion-like growth habit, this succulent thrives in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. Its gray-green foliage and striking yellow flowers make it a standout in desert landscapes. The variety durispina is distinguished by its thick, spiny stems and robust, drought-resistant structure. This plant is not only a botanical curiosity but also an important component of its native ecosystem.
