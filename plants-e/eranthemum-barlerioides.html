Eranthemum barlerioides

Eranthemum barlerioides, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eranthemum barlerioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
