Euphorbia minxianensis

Euphorbia minxianensis, commonly known as the Minxian spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
