Euodia alba

Euodia alba, commonly known as the white euodia, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are small, green drupes that turn yellow when mature.

Euodia alba is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
