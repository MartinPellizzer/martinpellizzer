Embelia blinii

Embelia blinii, commonly known as the Chinese embelia or small-leaved embelia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
