Epidendrum berbicense

Epidendrum berbicense, commonly known as the Berbice Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly found in Guyana and Suriname.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees and other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
