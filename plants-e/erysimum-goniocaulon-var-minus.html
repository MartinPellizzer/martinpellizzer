Erysimum goniocaulon var. minus

Erysimum goniocaulon var. minus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive grayish-green color. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
