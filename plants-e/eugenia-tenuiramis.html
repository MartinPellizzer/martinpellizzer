Eugenia tenuiramis

Eugenia tenuiramis, commonly known as the narrow-rammed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia tenuiramis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
