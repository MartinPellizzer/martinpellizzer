Erysimum cheiranthus var. subviride

Erysimum cheiranthus var. subviride, commonly known as the greenleaf wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, greenish-gray foliage. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
