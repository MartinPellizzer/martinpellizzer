Eria micrantha

Eria micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Eria micrantha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in local ecosystems.
