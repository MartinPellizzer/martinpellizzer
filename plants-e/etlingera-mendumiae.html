Etlingera mendumiae

Etlingera mendumiae, commonly known as the Mendum's torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which resemble torches, and its aromatic leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The rhizomes of Etlingera mendumiae are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
