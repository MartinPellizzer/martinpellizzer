Eugenia biflora var. lancea

Eugenia biflora var. lancea, commonly known as the lanceleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
