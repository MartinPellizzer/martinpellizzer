Epilobium × foliosum

Epilobium × foliosum, commonly known as the common willowherb, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Epilobium × foliosum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support diverse wildlife.
