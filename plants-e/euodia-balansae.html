Euodia balansae

Euodia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae prickly ash, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Euodia balansae is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.
