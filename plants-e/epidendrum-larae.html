Epidendrum larae

Epidendrum larae, commonly known as Lara's epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, featuring a short, thick stem that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum larae is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
