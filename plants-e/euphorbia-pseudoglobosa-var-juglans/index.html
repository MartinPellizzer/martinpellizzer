Euphorbia Pseudoglobosa Var. Juglans

Euphorbia pseudoglobosa var. juglans is a unique and striking plant known for its distinctive, globe-shaped stems. Native to southern Africa, this succulent thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its waxy, blue-green leaves are adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant is also called the "blue moon" due to its striking coloration. It is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts for its beauty and low maintenance requirements.
