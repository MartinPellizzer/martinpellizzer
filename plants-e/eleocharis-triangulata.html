Eleocharis triangulata

Eleocharis triangulata, commonly known as the triangular spike-rush, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Eleocharis triangulata plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms.
