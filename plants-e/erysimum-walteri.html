Erysimum walteri

Erysimum walteri, commonly known as Walter's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces bright yellow or orange flowers. It has hairy stems and lobed leaves, and it blooms from spring to early summer.

Walter's wallflower is often found in open woodlands and along streambanks.
