Echinospermum bungei

Echinospermum bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's echinospermum, is a flowering plant native to central and southern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers that are typically arranged in a clustered inflorescence. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
