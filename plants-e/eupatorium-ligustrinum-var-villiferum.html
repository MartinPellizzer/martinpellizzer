Eupatorium ligustrinum var. villiferum

Eupatorium ligustrinum var. villiferum, commonly known as the purplestem false dragonhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are typically purplish in color, especially when mature. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink to purple in hue, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or wetlands.
