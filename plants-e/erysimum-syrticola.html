Erysimum syrticola

Erysimum syrticola, commonly known as the Syrticola wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Erysimum syrticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
