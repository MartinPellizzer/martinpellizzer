Epipremnum elegans f. ternatensis

Epipremnum elegans f. ternatensis, commonly known as the Variegated Philodendron, is a cultivar of the Epipremnum elegans species.

It is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of white and green patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
