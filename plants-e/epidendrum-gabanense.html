Epidendrum gabanense

Epidendrum gabanense, commonly known as the Gabanese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Epidendrum gabanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
