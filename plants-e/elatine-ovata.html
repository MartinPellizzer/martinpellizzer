Elatine ovata

Elatine ovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and stream margins. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often floating or submerged, and it produces tiny, star-shaped flowers. Elatine ovata is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through both sexual reproduction and vegetative means.

It plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
