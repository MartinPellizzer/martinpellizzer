Echium humile var. saharicum

Echium humile var. saharicum, commonly known as the Saharan fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing annual herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
