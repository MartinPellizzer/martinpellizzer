Encyclia santos-dumontii

Encyclia santos-dumontii, commonly known as the Santos-Dumont orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, typically blooming during the summer months. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered with a thick, leathery epidermis.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
