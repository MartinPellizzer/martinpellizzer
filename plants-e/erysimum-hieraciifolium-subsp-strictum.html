Erysimum hieraciifolium subsp. strictum

Erysimum hieraciifolium subsp.

strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features basal and cauline leaves that are linear to lanceolate, often with a hairy texture.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
