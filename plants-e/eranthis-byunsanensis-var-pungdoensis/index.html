Eranthis Byunsanensis Var. Pungdoensis

Eranthis byunsanensis var. pungdoensis is a rare and elegant flower native to the mountainous regions of South Korea. This delicate plant is known for its early blooming habit, often appearing in late winter or early spring. Its bright yellow petals and compact size make it a standout addition to any garden or natural setting. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native habitat. Cultivation of this variety requires careful attention to soil and climate conditions to ensure its survival.
