Epipactis gigantea var. manshurica

Epipactis gigantea var. manshurica, commonly known as the Manshurian giant helleborine, is a species of orchid native to the Russian Far East and northeastern China.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rosette of broad, dark green leaves that form a dense basal cluster. The plant produces tall, erect stems bearing showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas.

Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
