Epidendrum zunigae

Epidendrum zunigae, commonly known as the Zuniga's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum zunigae is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
