Eriobotrya elliptica var. petelotii

Eriobotrya elliptica var. petelotii, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It features broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by yellow, edible fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and culinary uses.
