Escallonia concinna

Escallonia concinna, commonly known as the dwarf escallonia, is a flowering shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Escallonia concinna prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
