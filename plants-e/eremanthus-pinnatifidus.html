Eremanthus pinnatifidus

Eremanthus pinnatifidus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Eremanthus pinnatifidus is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky areas.
