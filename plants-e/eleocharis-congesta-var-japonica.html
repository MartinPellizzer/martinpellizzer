Eleocharis congesta var. japonica

Eleocharis congesta var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese spike-rush, is a flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, thriving in temperate climates. Its rhizomes form dense colonies, contributing to soil stabilization in aquatic ecosystems.

The species is native to East Asia, including Japan, Korea, and parts of China.
