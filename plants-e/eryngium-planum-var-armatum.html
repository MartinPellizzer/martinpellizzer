Eryngium planum var. armatum

Eryngium planum var. armatum, commonly known as the spiked eryngo, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a compact, spiky appearance with blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has rigid, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique architecture and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
