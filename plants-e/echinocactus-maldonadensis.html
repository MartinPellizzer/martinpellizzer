Echinocactus maldonadensis

Echinocactus maldonadensis, commonly known as the Maldonadensis cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
