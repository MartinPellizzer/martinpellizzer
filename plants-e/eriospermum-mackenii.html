Eriospermum mackenii

Eriospermum mackenii, commonly known as the Mackenzie's eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has a compact, bushy appearance. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
