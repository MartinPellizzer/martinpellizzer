Eupatorium celebicum

Eupatorium celebicum, commonly known as Java boneset, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and have a rough texture.

Eupatorium celebicum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
