Epidendrum mainauanum

Epidendrum mainauanum, commonly known as the Mainau orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Mexico.

It features long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
