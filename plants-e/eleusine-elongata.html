Eleusine elongata

Eleusine elongata, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Eleusine elongata is a perennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
