Euphorbia seguieriana var. virgata

Euphorbia seguieriana var. virgata, commonly known as the virgata euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem with a smooth, waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flower clusters surrounded by spiny bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
