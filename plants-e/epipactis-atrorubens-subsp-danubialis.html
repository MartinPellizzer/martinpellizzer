Epipactis atrorubens subsp. danubialis

Epipactis atrorubens subsp.

danubialis, commonly known as the Danube helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features dark green, pleated leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched flowering stems.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to the nominate form, particularly in leaf shape and flower structure.
