Erythroxylum ovatum var. angustifolium

Erythroxylum ovatum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved copal tree, is a species native to Central America and parts of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
