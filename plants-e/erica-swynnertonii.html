Erica swynnertonii

Erica swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically found in the Western Cape region. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the summer months.

Erica swynnertonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural environment.
