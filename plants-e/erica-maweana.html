Erica maweana

Erica maweana, commonly known as the Maweana heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Erica maweana typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant pink or white flowers that attract pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
