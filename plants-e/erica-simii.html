Erica simii

Erica simii, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Erica simii produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often blooming in spring and summer.

Its dense foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and habitat conservation.
