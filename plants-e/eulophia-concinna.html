Eulophia concinna

Eulophia concinna, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the rainy season. Eulophia concinna is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.
