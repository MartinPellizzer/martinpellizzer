Euphorbia Hypericifolia Var. Bracteolaris

Variegated euphorbia, scientifically known as Euphorbia hypericifolia var. bracteolaris, is a striking succulent native to South Africa. Its unique foliage features a mix of green, white, and yellow tones, creating a visually captivating appearance. This plant is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water. It is also notable for its toxic sap, which requires careful handling to avoid skin irritation. Despite its beauty, it remains a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
