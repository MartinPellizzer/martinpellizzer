Echinopsis schieliana

Echinopsis schieliana, commonly known as the Schiel's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant has a spiny exterior, with areoles that bear both spines and flowers. It is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Echinopsis schieliana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and indoor settings.
