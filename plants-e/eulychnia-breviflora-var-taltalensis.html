Eulychnia breviflora var. taltalensis

Eulychnia breviflora var. taltalensis, commonly known as the Taltal cholla, is a flowering plant native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the brief rainy season. It has adapted to the extreme arid conditions with a deep root system and water-storing tissues.

This variety is notable for its resilience and is often found growing in rocky soils along coastal areas.
