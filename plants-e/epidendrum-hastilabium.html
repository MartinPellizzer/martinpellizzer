Epidendrum hastilabium

Epidendrum hastilabium, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation.
