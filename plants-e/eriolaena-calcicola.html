Eriolaena calcicola

Eriolaena calcicola, commonly known as the limestone lilly pilly, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that appear in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats. It has small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color.

Eriolaena calcicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
