Eriosyce strausiana subsp. pachacoensis

Eriosyce strausiana subsp.

pachacoensis, commonly known as the Pachacoensis Eriosyce, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Peru. It is a small, spineless cactus that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils at higher elevations.

The plant features a short, cylindrical stem with a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. Its flowers are pale yellow to orange, opening in the morning and closing by midday.

This subspecies is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
