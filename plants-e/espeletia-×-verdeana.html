Espeletia × verdeana

Espeletia × verdeana, commonly known as the Green Mountain heath, is a hybrid plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth form and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in cloud forests and paramo ecosystems.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
