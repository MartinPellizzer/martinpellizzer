Eupatorium pauciflorum

Eupatorium pauciflorum, commonly known as slenderleaf boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, and it produces small, clustered flower heads.

Eupatorium pauciflorum is often found in the eastern United States and is known for its medicinal properties.
