Eugenia everettii

Eugenia everettii, commonly known as the everett's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia everettii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native regions.
