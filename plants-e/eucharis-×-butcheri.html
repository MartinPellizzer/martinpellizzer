Eucharis × butcheri

Eucharis × butcheri, commonly known as the Amazonian white lily, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family (Araceae) and is characterized by its large, waxy, white flowers that resemble lilies. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a typical feature of aroids. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eucharis × butcheri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display.
