Eritrichium nanum subsp. jankae

Eritrichium nanum subsp.

jankae, commonly known as the Janka's dwarf fleabane, is a small annual herbaceous plant native to the western United States. It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and dry meadows.

The plant has slender stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and covered in fine hairs.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
