Eulophia ledermannii

Eulophia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Eulophia ledermannii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
