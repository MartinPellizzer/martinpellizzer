Epidendrum almasii

Epidendrum almasii, commonly known as the Almasii's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native arid environment.

Epidendrum almasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
