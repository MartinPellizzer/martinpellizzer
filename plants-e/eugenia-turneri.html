Eugenia turneri

Eugenia turneri, commonly known as the Mexican cherry or Turneri's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and dark red berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a strong aromatic scent.

Eugenia turneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
