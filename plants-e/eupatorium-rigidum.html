Eupatorium rigidum

Eupatorium rigidum, commonly known as stiff boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its stiff, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Eupatorium rigidum is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
