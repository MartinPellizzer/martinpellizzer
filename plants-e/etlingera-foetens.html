Etlingera foetens

Etlingera foetens, commonly known as the stinking ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its name derives from the strong, unpleasant odor emitted by its flowers.
