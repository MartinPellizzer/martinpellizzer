Euphorbia guntensis

Euphorbia guntensis, commonly known as the Gunt's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features a short, thick stem with paired, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized flower structures typical of the Euphorbia genus. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
