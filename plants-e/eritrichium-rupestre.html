Eritrichium rupestre

Eritrichium rupestre, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, blue or violet flowers with five petals. The plant typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and tundra environments. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

Eritrichium rupestre is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting pollinators in high-altitude ecosystems.
