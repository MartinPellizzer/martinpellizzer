Eriosema × prunelloides

Eriosema × prunelloides, commonly known as the hybrid bluebell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South Africa, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
