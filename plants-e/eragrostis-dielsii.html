Eragrostis dielsii

Eragrostis dielsii, commonly known as the narrowleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, often growing in full sun.

Eragrostis dielsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
