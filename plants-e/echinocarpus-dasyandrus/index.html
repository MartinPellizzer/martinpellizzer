Echinocarpus Dasyandrus

Echinocarpus tree, scientifically known as Echinocarpus dasyandrus, is a unique species native to certain regions of South America. This tree is characterized by its distinctive spiny fruits, which give it its common name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in humid forests and along riverbanks. The plant has both ornamental and practical uses, valued for its hardy nature and ecological benefits. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in diverse ecosystems.
