Epipactis helleborine var. varians

Epipactis helleborine var. varians, commonly known as the variable helleborine, is a perennial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments.

It is characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and striking violet to purple flowers. The plant is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Its flowers are highly specialized, with a unique structure that facilitates pollination by specific insects.

The species is notable for its ability to hybridize with other Epipactis species, contributing to its genetic diversity.
