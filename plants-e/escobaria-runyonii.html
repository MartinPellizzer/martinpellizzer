Escobaria runyonii

Escobaria runyonii, commonly known as Runyon's liveforever, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its compact, low-growing form with fleshy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are typically borne on short stems. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Escobaria runyonii is a hardy species that thrives in full sun and requires minimal water once established.
