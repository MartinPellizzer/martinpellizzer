Eurybia beckleri

Eurybia beckleri, commonly known as the pink sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of North America.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, rounded leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals arranged in a showy cluster.
