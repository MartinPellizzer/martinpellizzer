Eucalyptus remota

Eucalyptus remota, commonly known as the slender gum, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and smooth, often greyish bark. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and typically have a bluish-green hue. It produces small, globular flower clusters that range in color from white to pale yellow.

The plant is well adapted to dry, inland environments and is often found in open forests or woodlands.
