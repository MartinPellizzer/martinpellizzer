Eleocharis tetraquetra f. tetraquetra

Eleocharis tetraquetra f. tetraquetra, commonly known as the common spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in temperate regions. This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in wetland habitats.
