Epidendrum sinense

Epidendrum sinense, commonly known as the Chinese Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often white or pale yellow, with a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

Epidendrum sinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
