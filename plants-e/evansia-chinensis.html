Evansia chinensis

Evansia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are succulent and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for harsh, dry environments.

Evansia chinensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
