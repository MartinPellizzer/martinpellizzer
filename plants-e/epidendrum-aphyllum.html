Epidendrum aphyllum

Epidendrum aphyllum, commonly known as the leafless epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. This unique orchid lacks leaves, relying instead on its roots for photosynthesis. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive spurred shape.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
