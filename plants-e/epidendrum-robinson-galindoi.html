Epidendrum robinson-galindoi

Epidendrum robinson-galindoi, commonly known as the Robinson-Galindo's epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
