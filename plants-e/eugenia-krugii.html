Eugenia krugii

Eugenia krugii, commonly known as the Krug's eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round, yellowish drupe that contains a single seed.

Eugenia krugii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its aromatic properties.
