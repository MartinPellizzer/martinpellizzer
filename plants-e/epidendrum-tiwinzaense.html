Epidendrum tiwinzaense

Epidendrum tiwinzaense, commonly known as the Tiwinza Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to South America, specifically Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
