Ericoila pedicellata

Ericoila pedicellata, commonly known as the clustered heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ericoila pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
