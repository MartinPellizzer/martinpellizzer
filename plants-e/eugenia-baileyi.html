Eugenia baileyi

Eugenia baileyi, commonly known as the Bailey's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia baileyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
