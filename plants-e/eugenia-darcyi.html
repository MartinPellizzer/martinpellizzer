Eugenia darcyi

Eugenia darcyi, commonly known as the Darcy's eucalyptus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of eastern Australia, particularly in Queensland. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, round, edible fruits that are often consumed by birds and other wildlife.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
