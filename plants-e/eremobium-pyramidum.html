Eremobium pyramidum

Eremobium pyramidum, commonly known as the pyramid bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Southwestern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a distinctive, upright growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant has a fibrous root system adapted to survive in dry conditions, and its leaves are typically narrow and succulent. Eremobium pyramidum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is well-suited to harsh, low-nutrient environments.
