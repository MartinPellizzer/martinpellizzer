Echinocactus caespititius

Echinocactus caespititius, commonly known as the rock hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a slow-growing, columnar cactus with a dense, spiny appearance. The plant features a compact, rounded shape with clusters of spines that give it a distinctive look. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in the summer months.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
