Epidendrum haenkeanum

Epidendrum haenkeanum, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow lips, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Epidendrum haenkeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
