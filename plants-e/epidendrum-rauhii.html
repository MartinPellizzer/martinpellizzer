Epidendrum rauhii

Epidendrum rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can reach up to 3 feet in length. The plant produces numerous fragrant, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum rauhii is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
