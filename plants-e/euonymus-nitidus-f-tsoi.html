Euonymus nitidus f. tsoi

Euonymus nitidus f. tsoi, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that remains glossy throughout the growing season.

It features small, inconspicuous yellowish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that add ornamental value in the fall. The plant has a bushy growth habit and is relatively low-maintenance, making it suitable for hedges or as a specimen plant in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, and it is relatively resistant to pests and diseases.

This cultivar is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
