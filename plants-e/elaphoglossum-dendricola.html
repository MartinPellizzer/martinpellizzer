Elaphoglossum dendricola

Elaphoglossum dendricola, commonly known as the tree fern Elaphoglossum, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a delicate, lace-like frond structure with a central rachis and numerous pinnae. Its rhizomes are fibrous and contribute to its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Elaphoglossum dendricola is notable for its compact size and ornamental value in botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
