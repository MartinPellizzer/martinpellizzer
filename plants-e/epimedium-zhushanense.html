Epimedium zhushanense

Epimedium zhushanense, commonly known as the Zhushan barbatum, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically bright green and have a leathery texture. It produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels.

Epimedium zhushanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and shade-tolerant nature.
