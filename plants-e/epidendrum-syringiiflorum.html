Epidendrum syringiiflorum

Epidendrum syringiiflorum, commonly known as the Purple Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum syringiiflorum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm climates.
