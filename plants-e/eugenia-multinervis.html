Eugenia multinervis

Eugenia multinervis, commonly known as the pink hibiscus mealybug plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
