Emilia biensis

Emilia biensis, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers with a central brown disk, which bloom in clusters. The plant is herbaceous, with upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Emilia biensis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
