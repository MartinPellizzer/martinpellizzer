Equisetum × kochianum

Equisetum × kochianum, commonly known as the hybrid horsetail, is a perennial plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its jointed, hollow stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces spore-bearing cones at the tips of its stems, which are often visible in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as along stream banks or in wetlands.

Equisetum × kochianum is notable for its ancient lineage, dating back to the Paleozoic era, and its unique morphology.
