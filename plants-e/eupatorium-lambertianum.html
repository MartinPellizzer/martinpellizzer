Eupatorium lambertianum

Eupatorium lambertianum, commonly known as Lambert's boneset, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and typically grows in moist, woodland areas. The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. Its leaves are opposite, serrated, and often have a strong, aromatic scent.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
