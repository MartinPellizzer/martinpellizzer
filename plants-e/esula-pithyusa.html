Esula pithyusa

Esula pithyusa, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Esula pithyusa is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
