Epimedium grandiflorum var. coelestre

Epimedium grandiflorum var. coelestre, commonly known as the "Chinese barrenwort," is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features rhizomatous growth and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to woodland gardens.
