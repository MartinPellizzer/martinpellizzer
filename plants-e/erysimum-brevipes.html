Erysimum brevipes

Erysimum brevipes, commonly known as the shortstem wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are short and often sprawling.

It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral.
