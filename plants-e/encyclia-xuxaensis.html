Encyclia xuxaensis

Encyclia xuxaensis, commonly known as the Xuxa's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia xuxaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
