Echeveria × haageana

Echeveria × haageana, commonly known as the "Mexican snowball," is a hybrid succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray to green in color, often with a powdery white coating. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
