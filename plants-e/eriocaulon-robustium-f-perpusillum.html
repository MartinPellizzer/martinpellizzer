Eriocaulon robustium f. perpusillum

Eriocaulon robustium f. perpusillum, commonly known as the dwarf flat sedge, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a dense rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for ornamental use in water gardens and bog landscapes.
