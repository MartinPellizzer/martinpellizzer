Erythroxylum calyptratum

Erythroxylum calyptratum, commonly known as the Calyptrate Erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elliptical, reddish-brown fruits. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth margin and a slightly pubescent surface.

Erythroxylum calyptratum is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique fruit structure.
