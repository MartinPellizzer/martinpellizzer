Excoecaria agallocha var. lancifolia

Excoecaria agallocha var. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and bright, showy flowers that range from yellow to red. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm regions.
