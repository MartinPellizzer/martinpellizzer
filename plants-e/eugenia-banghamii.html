Eugenia banghamii

Eugenia banghamii, commonly known as the Bangham's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the wet tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that is edible and contains a single seed.
