Euphrasia officinalis f. brevidens

Euphrasia officinalis f. brevidens, commonly known as the short-leaved eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy, narrow leaves.
