Euodia subcaudata

Euodia subcaudata, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, dark brown seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some Asian cultures.

Euodia subcaudata thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
