Eremogone juncea var. glabra

Eremogone juncea var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple. It is adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils with minimal water.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
