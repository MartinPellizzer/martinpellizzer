Epilobium × ceretanum

Epilobium × ceretanum, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as riverbanks, wet meadows, and roadside ditches. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy surface. It produces clusters of small, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
