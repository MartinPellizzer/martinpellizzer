Epidendrum brachycladium var. crassipes

Epidendrum brachycladium var. crassipes, commonly known as the thick-leaved epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
