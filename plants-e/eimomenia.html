Eimomenia

Eimomenia, commonly known as the "moonflower," is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

The plant belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its fragrant blooms. Eimomenia typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves.
