Eria kaniensis var. kaniensis

Eria kaniensis var. kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
