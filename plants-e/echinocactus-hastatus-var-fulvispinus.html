Echinocactus hastatus var. fulvispinus

Echinocactus hastatus var. fulvispinus, commonly known as the golden spine cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in bright yellow spines, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique features, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and collections.
