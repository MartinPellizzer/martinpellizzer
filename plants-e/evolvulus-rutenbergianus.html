Evolvulus rutenbergianus

Evolvulus rutenbergianus, commonly known as the Rutenberg morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking quick coverage.
