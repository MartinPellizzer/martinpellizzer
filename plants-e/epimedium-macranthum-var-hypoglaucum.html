Epimedium macranthum var. hypoglaucum

Epimedium macranthum var. hypoglaucum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces upright, reddish stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and early blooming habit.
