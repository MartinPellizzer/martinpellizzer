Echinocereus scheeri var. koehresianus

Echinocereus scheeri var. koehresianus, commonly known as the Arizona hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring. The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments with minimal rainfall. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
