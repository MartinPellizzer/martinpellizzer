Erigeron philippii var. tragopogonoides

Erigeron philippii var. tragopogonoides, commonly known as the Mexican fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in the summer months.
