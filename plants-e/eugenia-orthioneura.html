Eugenia orthioneura

Eugenia orthioneura, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and parts of Georgia. The plant features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, reddish-black drupe that ripens in the fall.

Eugenia orthioneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
