Eugenia sphaerosperma

Eugenia sphaerosperma, commonly known as the rounded-fruited eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, rounded fruits that are typically red or purple in color. It produces fragrant flowers and has glossy, dark green leaves.
