Encyclia × bajamarensis

Encyclia × bajamarensis, commonly known as the Bajamar hybrid orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting purple or red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Encyclia × bajamarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms.
