Eruca vesicaria subvar. oleracea

Eruca vesicaria subvar. oleracea, commonly known as garden rocket or roquette, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, glandular hairs that give it a peppery aroma. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by slender, elongated pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and lobed, with a slightly bitter taste.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
