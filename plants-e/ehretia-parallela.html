Ehretia parallela

Ehretia parallela, commonly known as the Chinese mahogany or small-leaved ehretia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant.
