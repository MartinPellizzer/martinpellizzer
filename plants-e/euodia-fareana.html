Euodia fareana

Euodia fareana, commonly known as the African laurustin, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically orange or red in color. Euodia fareana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
