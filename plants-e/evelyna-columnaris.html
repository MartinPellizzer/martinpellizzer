Evelyna columnaris

Evelyna columnaris, commonly known as the columnar cactus, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that grow in a vertical, column-like form. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. It produces yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months.

Evelyna columnaris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
