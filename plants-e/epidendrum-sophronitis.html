Epidendrum sophronitis

Epidendrum sophronitis, commonly known as the Purple-veined Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are characterized by their vivid purple and white coloration.

Epidendrum sophronitis is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
