Eragrostis minor var. roborovskii

Eragrostis minor var. roborovskii, commonly known as Roborovskii grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It features fine, hair-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and hardy.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
