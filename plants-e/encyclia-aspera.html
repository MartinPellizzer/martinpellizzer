Encyclia aspera

Encyclia aspera, commonly known as the rough encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a short, upright stem with several fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with a distinctive, rough-textured appearance.

Encyclia aspera is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
