Epimedium ogisui

Epimedium ogisui, commonly known as Japanese barbatos, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in late spring, typically in shades of yellow or orange. Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it suitable for ground cover in shaded gardens.

Epimedium ogisui is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to moist, woodland environments.
