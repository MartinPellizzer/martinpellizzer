Eragrostis cimicina

Eragrostis cimicina, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its drooping, fountain-like growth habit and fine, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed sites.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscaping for its unique appearance.
