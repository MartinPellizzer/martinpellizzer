Erica cremea

Erica cremea, commonly known as the cream heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white or cream-colored flowers. Erica cremea typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
