Echites grandiflorus var. major

Echites grandiflorus var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered echites, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
