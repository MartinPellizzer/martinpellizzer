Epimedium shuichengense

Epimedium shuichengense, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and appear in clusters during late spring to early summer.

Epimedium shuichengense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to partial shade and well-drained soil.
