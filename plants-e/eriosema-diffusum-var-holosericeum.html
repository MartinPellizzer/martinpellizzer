Eriosema diffusum var. holosericeum

Eriosema diffusum var. holosericeum, commonly known as the silky eriosema, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, sprawling stems and trifoliate leaves with finely toothed margins. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its hairy foliage and stems give it a distinctive, soft appearance.

It is often found in grassy areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
