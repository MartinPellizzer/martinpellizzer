Eupatorium graminifolium

Eupatorium graminifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters called capitula. It thrives in moist, sunny to partially shaded environments, often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and pollinator support.
