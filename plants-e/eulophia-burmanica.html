Eulophia burmanica

Eulophia burmanica, commonly known as the Burmese eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing showy, spurred flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid forest floor.

Eulophia burmanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
