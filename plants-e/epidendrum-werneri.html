Epidendrum werneri

Epidendrum werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting dark throat. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, typically arranged in a fan shape.

Epidendrum werneri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
