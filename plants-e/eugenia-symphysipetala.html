Eugenia symphysipetala

Eugenia symphysipetala, commonly known as the white eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that is typically yellow when mature.
