Episcia decumbens

Episcia decumbens, commonly known as the velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. Episcia decumbens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
