Erica anthura

Erica anthura, commonly known as the purple heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Erica anthura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
