Euphorbia Collina Var. Minor

Euphorbia collina var. minor is a unique and striking succulent native to arid regions of the southwestern united states. this compact plant features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that help it store water in harsh environments. it produces delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. its ability to thrive with minimal care makes it an excellent addition to any drought-tolerant landscape.
