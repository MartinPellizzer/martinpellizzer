Erica × hiemalis

Erica × hiemalis, commonly known as the winter heather, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the British Isles and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late winter or early spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Erica × hiemalis is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
