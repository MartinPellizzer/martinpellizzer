Echeveria adunca

Echeveria adunca, commonly known as the Mexican snowflake, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray or silver in color, with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Echeveria adunca is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
