Eulophia debeerstii

Eulophia debeerstii, commonly known as the De Beer's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Eulophia debeerstii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
