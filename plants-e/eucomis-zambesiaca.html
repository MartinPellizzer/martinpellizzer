Eucomis zambesiaca

Eucomis zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesi torch or Zambesi pineapple, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky bracts that resemble a torch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Eucomis zambesiaca is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance and ease of care.
