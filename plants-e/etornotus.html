Etornotus

Etornotus, commonly known as the false sandalwood, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It consists of a single species, Etornotus alatus, which is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The genus is noted for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife and its potential use in traditional medicine.
