Erythrina breviflora f. oaxacana

Erythrina breviflora f. oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca coral tree, is a variant of the coral tree species native to Mexico.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its vibrant red flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental cultivation.

Its striking floral display makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
