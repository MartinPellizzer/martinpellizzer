Eria canaliculata

Eria canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, pleated leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural collections.
