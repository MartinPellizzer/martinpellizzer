Epidendrum christensonii

Epidendrum christensonii, commonly known as the Christenson's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It features erect, hairy stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers with elongated petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum christensonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
