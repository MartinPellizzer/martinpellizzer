Eulophia johnstonii

Eulophia johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, leafy stems and produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often near streams or in mountainous regions. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, and it is known for its strong, pleasant fragrance.

Eulophia johnstonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
