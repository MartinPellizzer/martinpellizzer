Erica brunneoalba

Erica brunneoalba, commonly known as the yellow heath, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family, Ericaceae.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in coastal regions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow, depending on the variety. Erica brunneoalba is evergreen, with dense, needle-like leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
