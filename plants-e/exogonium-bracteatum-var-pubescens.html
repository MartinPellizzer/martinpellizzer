Exogonium bracteatum var. pubescens

Exogonium bracteatum var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvet leaf plant, is a tropical species characterized by its dense, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is native to Central and South America. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a prominent midrib and a pubescent surface that gives it its common name. It produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in humid, shaded environments.
