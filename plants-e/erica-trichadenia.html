Erica trichadenia

Erica trichadenia, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in spring and summer. Erica trichadenia is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
