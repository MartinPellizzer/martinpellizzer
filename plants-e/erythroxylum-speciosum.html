Erythroxylum speciosum

Erythroxylum speciosum, commonly known as the showy erythroxylum, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly in regions of Brazil and Peru.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.
