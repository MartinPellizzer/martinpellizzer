Epidendrum leopardinum

Epidendrum leopardinum, commonly known as the leopard orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that often display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are borne on long, branching inflorescences, and it blooms throughout the year in suitable conditions.

Epidendrum leopardinum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
