Epidendrum ekmanii

Epidendrum ekmanii, commonly known as the Ekman's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy bloom. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum ekmanii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
