Euterpe zephyria

Euterpe zephyria, commonly known as the wind banana, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can reach up to three meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that eventually develop into edible berries. These berries are typically sweet and have a mild, tropical flavor.

Euterpe zephyria is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and for its edible fruits.
