Euphorbia taourirtensis

Euphorbia taourirtensis, commonly known as the Atlas Mountain spurge, is a succulent plant native to Morocco.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, spine-tipped leaves and yellowish-green flower clusters. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its water-storing stems. Its growth habit is typically low and compact, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Euphorbia taourirtensis is also noted for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
