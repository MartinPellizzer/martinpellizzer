Epidendrum ardens

Epidendrum ardens, commonly known as the fiery torch orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling torches. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted to thrive in humid environments. Epidendrum ardens prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
