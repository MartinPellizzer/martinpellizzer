Euphorbia mundii

Euphorbia mundii, commonly known as the Desert Thorn, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually small and spiny, providing some protection from herbivores.

Euphorbia mundii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
