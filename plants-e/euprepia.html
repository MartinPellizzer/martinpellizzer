Euprepia

Euprepia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking silver or gray-green foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a velvety texture, making them visually appealing. Euprepia species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

They thrive in well-drained soil and require bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
