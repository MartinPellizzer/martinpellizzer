Echites melaleucus

Echites melaleucus, commonly known as the silver bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and cylindrical, bottlebrush-like flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest habitats.

Echites melaleucus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
