Euphorbia splendens var. vulcani

Euphorbia splendens var. vulcani, commonly known as the volcanic euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and striking, spiky leaves that range from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique inflorescences. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
