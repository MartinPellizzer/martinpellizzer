Eatonella nivea

Eatonella nivea, commonly known as the snow lily, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, shaded woodland areas. It is a member of the Liliaceae family and is known for its ephemeral blooming cycle.
