Erodium hoefftianum

Erodium hoefftianum, commonly known as the common strewer, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with trifoliate leaves and small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its rapid growth and prolific seed production make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
