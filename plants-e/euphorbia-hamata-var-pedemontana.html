Euphorbia hamata var. pedemontana

Euphorbia hamata var. pedemontana, commonly known as the Montane Spiny Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
