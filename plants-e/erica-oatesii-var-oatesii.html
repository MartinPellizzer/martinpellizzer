Erica oatesii var. oatesii

Erica oatesii var. oatesii, commonly known as Oates' heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica oatesii var. oatesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
