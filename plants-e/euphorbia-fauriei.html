Euphorbia fauriei

Euphorbia fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has a unique ability to store water in its stems, making it drought-tolerant. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and may have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Euphorbia fauriei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and adaptability to various growing conditions.
