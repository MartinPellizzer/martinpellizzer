Erodium atomarium

Erodium atomarium, commonly known as the common stork's bill, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pinkish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a distinctive, curved capsule that resembles a stork's bill, giving the plant its common name.

Erodium atomarium is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
