Escallonia patens

Escallonia patens, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, typically in late winter to early spring. Escallonia patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also known for its hardiness and resistance to pests and diseases.
