Eruca sativa var. dasycarpa

Eruca sativa var. dasycarpa, commonly known as the woolly wallower, is a variant of the common rocket plant.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor similar to other Eruca species.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes as well as for its culinary use.
