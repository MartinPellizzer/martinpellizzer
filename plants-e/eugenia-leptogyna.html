Eugenia leptogyna

Eugenia leptogyna, commonly known as the slender-lobed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

Eugenia leptogyna is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
