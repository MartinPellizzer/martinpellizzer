Eriachne aristidea var. elegans

Eriachne aristidea var. elegans, commonly known as the slender grass or elegant eriachne, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually blue-green in color. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in a panicle, producing small, pale flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native garden landscapes.
