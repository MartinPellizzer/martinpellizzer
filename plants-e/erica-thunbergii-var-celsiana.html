Erica thunbergii var. celsiana

Erica thunbergii var. celsiana, commonly known as the Celsian heath, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, needle-like foliage that is typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to coastal and rocky garden environments.
