Erysimum maderense

Erysimum maderense, commonly known as the Mader's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Erysimum maderense is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cool, sunny environments.
