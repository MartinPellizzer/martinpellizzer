Erica globiceps subsp. globiceps

Erica globiceps subsp.

globiceps, commonly known as the globular heath, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and small, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces clusters of bright red or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun exposure.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form compact, attractive shapes.
