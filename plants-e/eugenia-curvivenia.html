Eugenia curvivenia

Eugenia curvivenia, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. These flowers give way to small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
