Embelia dielsii

Embelia dielsii, commonly known as the African star anise, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise, hence its common name. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its bark is rough and reddish in color.

Embelia dielsii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
