Eugenia rubrocincta

Eugenia rubrocincta, commonly known as the red-banded eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often tinged with red or purple along the midrib. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, adding to its ornamental value.
