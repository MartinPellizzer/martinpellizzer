Erythroxylum sphaeranthum

Erythroxylum sphaeranthum, commonly known as the red globemallow, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spherical or globose flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Erythroxylum sphaeranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
