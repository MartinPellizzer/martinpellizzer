Eupatorium chinense var. oppositifolium

Eupatorium chinense var. oppositifolium, commonly known as Chinese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its medicinal properties have been utilized in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
