Enterosora sinuata

Enterosora sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sinuous stems that twist and turn in a zigzag pattern. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Enterosora sinuata is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and undergrowth areas.
