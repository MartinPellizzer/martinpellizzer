Epimedium grandiflorum var. koreanum

Epimedium grandiflorum var. koreanum, commonly known as the Korean barbatan, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Korea.

It features rhizomatous growth and heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge beneath. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and early blooming habit.
