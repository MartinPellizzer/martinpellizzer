Eriodictyon californicum f. linearis

Eriodictyon californicum f. linearis, commonly known as the linear yerba santa, is a variety of the yerba santa plant native to California.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been historically used by indigenous peoples for its purported healing benefits.
