Eugenia louvelii var. suborbiculata

Eugenia louvelii var. suborbiculata, commonly known as the broad-leafed eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
