Euphorbia rzedowskii

Euphorbia rzedowskii, commonly known as the Mexican spurge, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, often blue-green leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes many species with unique floral structures called cyathia. Euphorbia rzedowskii is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick stems and leaves.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
