Eugenia aboukirensis

Eugenia aboukirensis, commonly known as the Kribi eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Cameroon, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible berries that are used in local cuisines. Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its bark contains compounds with potential medicinal properties.
