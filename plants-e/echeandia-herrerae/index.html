Echeandia Herrerae

Echeandia herrerae is a rare and unique plant native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking violet-blue flowers. This species is particularly interesting due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat requirements. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from threats like deforestation and climate change. Its discovery and study offer valuable insights into biodiversity and ecological adaptation.
