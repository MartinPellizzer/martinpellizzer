Eria iodantha

Eria iodantha, commonly known as the silver waxy plant, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Eria iodantha is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
