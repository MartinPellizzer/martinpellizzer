Ericameria linifolia

Ericameria linifolia, commonly known as silverleaf ericameria, is a shrub native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and sagebrush habitats.

Ericameria linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
