Elaeagnus crispa var. parvifolia

Elaeagnus crispa var. parvifolia, commonly known as silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, reddish fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Elaeagnus crispa var. parvifolia is also valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various climates.
