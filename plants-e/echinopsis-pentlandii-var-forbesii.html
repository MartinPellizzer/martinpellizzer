Echinopsis pentlandii var. forbesii

Echinopsis pentlandii var. forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a cylindrical stem with a distinctive white or pale green coloration, often adorned with spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
