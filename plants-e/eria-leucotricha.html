Eria leucotricha

Eria leucotricha, commonly known as the white-hair orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. Its leaves are elongated and often covered with fine, white hairs, giving it its common name.

Eria leucotricha is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
