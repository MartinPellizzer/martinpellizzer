Elaphoglossum beddomei

Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori, which are protected by a thin, brown indusium.

Elaphoglossum beddomei is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its habitat.
