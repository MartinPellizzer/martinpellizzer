Eragrostis stenostachya var. floribunda

Eragrostis stenostachya var. floribunda, commonly known as the flowering bluestem, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with blue or purple hues. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that becomes more pronounced during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
