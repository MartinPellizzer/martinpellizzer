Eurya oblonga

Eurya oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf eurya, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes many evergreen and deciduous shrubs and small trees. The plant features elliptical to ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by bright red, fleshy berries.

Eurya oblonga is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
