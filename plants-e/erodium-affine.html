Erodium affine

Erodium affine, commonly known as sticky weed or small morning glory, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It has small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in the early morning. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered with fine, sticky hairs.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.
