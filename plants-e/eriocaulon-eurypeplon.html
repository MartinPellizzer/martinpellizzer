Eriocaulon eurypeplon

Eriocaulon eurypeplon, commonly known as the broad-leaved spike-rush, is a member of the Eriocaulaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as swampy areas and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and cylindrical, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

The small, white flowers are clustered in dense spikes, contributing to its ornamental appeal in aquatic or bog gardens.
