Erysimum zeybekianum

Erysimum zeybekianum, commonly known as the Turkish wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
