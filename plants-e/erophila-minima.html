Erophila minima

Erophila minima, commonly known as the small erophila, is a small flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves.

Erophila minima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
