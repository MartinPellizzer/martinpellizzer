Erysimum barbarea

Erysimum barbarea, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, typically from March to June, and it has pinnately divided leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in open fields.

Erysimum barbarea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
