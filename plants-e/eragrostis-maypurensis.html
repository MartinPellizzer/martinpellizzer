Eragrostis maypurensis

Eragrostis maypurensis, commonly known as the Maypure grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and have a bluish-green hue, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

Eragrostis maypurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
