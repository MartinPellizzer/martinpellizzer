Eulophia alta var. alba

Eulophia alta var. alba, commonly known as the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

The flowers have a delicate, fragrant scent and are often used in traditional floral arrangements.
