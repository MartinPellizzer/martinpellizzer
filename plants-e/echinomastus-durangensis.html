Echinomastus durangensis

Echinomastus durangensis, commonly known as the Durango prickly pear, is a cacti species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and paddle-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by edible fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh desert conditions.

Its structure and growth patterns make it well-suited for survival in regions with limited water availability.
