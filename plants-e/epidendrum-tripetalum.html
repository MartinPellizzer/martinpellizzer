Epidendrum tripetalum

Epidendrum tripetalum, commonly known as the Three-lobed Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and display a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Epidendrum tripetalum is a hardy orchid that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
