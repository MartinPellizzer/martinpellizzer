Eugenia veadeirensis

Eugenia veadeirensis, commonly known as the Veadeirensis Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
