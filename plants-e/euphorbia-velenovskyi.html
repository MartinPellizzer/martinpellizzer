Euphorbia velenovskyi

Euphorbia velenovskyi, commonly known as the Velenovsky's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features a short, thick stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia velenovskyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
