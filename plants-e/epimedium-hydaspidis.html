Epimedium hydaspidis

Epimedium hydaspidis, commonly known as the golden monkshood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features rhizomatous growth and has trifoliate leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and ability to naturalize over time.
