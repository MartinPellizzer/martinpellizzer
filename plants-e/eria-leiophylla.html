Eria leiophylla

Eria leiophylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf eria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria leiophylla is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
