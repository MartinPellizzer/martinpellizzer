Eratica gunnii

Eratica gunnii, commonly known as the Gunn's erica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a rockery plant.
