Epidendrum nocturnum var. minor

Epidendrum nocturnum var. minor, commonly known as the night orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has long, arching stems with broad, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique blooming pattern and ornamental appeal.
