Eleocharis valleculosa var. valleculosa

Eleocharis valleculosa var. valleculosa, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences that bear small, clustered flowers. This plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded soils, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively and colonize new areas.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
