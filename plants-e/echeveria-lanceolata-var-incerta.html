Echeveria lanceolata var. incerta

Echeveria lanceolata var. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain lanceleaf echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy texture. The leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom, which helps reduce water loss. It produces clusters of pink to magenta flowers during the spring and summer months.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
