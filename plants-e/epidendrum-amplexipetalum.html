Epidendrum amplexipetalum

Epidendrum amplexipetalum, commonly known as the clasping-petal epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters of bright yellow or orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum amplexipetalum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
