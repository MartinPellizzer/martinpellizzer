Epidendrum undulatum

Epidendrum undulatum, commonly known as the wavy leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, wavy-edged leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum undulatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
