Eriospermum herporrhizum

Eriospermum herporrhizum, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with succulent, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

Eriospermum herporrhizum is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its resilience and ornamental value.
