Eria cycloglossa

Eria cycloglossa, commonly known as the spiral-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiral-shaped lip that gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Eria cycloglossa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
