Epidendrum exile

Epidendrum exile, commonly known as the slender epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often appearing in clusters along the stems.

Epidendrum exile is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
