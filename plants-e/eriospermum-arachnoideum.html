Eriospermum arachnoideum

Eriospermum arachnoideum, commonly known as the woolly eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its overall appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and interesting species in the botanical world.
