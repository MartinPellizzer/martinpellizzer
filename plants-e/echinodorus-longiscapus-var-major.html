Echinodorus longiscapus var. major

Echinodorus longiscapus var. major, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 20 inches in length, making it a popular choice for aquariums and ponds. The plant thrives in warm, tropical waters with moderate to high light exposure and requires regular fertilization to maintain its vibrant coloration. It is often used to create a lush, natural appearance in aquatic environments.

Additionally, it can produce small, white flowers that emerge above the water surface, adding to its ornamental value.
