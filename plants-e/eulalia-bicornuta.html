Eulalia bicornuta

Eulalia bicornuta, commonly known as the two-horned grass, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-horned inflorescence, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in temperate climates. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Eulalia bicornuta is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
