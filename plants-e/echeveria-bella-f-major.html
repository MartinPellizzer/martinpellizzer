Echeveria bella f. major

Echeveria bella f. major, commonly known as the Giant Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are somewhat elongated and pointed. The plant produces showy clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
