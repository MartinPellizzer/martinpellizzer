Eriocalia

Eriocalia, commonly known as the Christmas star or star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant belonging to the genus Eriocalia in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers bloom in winter, giving it its common name.

Eriocalia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
