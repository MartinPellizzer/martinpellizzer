Encyclia widgrenii

Encyclia widgrenii, commonly known as the Widgren's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a deep red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia widgrenii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
