Erica aneimena

Erica aneimena, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly referred to as the heath or heather family. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica aneimena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a ground cover.
