Encyclia chimborazoensis

Encyclia chimborazoensis, commonly known as the Chimborazo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, showcasing its vibrant pink and white petals. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
