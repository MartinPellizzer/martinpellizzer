Espostoa lanata var. sericata

Espostoa lanata var. sericata, commonly known as the silky woolly nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the evening. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a thick layer of fine hairs.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
