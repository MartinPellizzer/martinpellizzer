Erythranthe hallii

Erythranthe hallii, commonly known as the Hall's redstem, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
