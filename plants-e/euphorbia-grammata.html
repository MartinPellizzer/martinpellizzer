Euphorbia grammata

Euphorbia grammata, commonly known as the grammata spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with numerous small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Euphorbia grammata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
