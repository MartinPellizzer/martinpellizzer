Erepsia roseoalba

Erepsia roseoalba, commonly known as the rose-white erica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erepsia roseoalba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
