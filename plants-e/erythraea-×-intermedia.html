Erythraea × intermedia

Erythraea × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid erythraea, is a fascinating plant resulting from the cross between Erythraea calycina and Erythraea ramosa.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
