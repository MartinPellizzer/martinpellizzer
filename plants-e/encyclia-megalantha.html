Encyclia megalantha

Encyclia megalantha, commonly known as the giant encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Encyclia megalantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
