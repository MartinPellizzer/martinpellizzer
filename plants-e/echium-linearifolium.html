Echium linearifolium

Echium linearifolium, commonly known as the slender fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Echium linearifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
