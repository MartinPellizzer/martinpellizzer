Eulophia buchananii

Eulophia buchananii, commonly known as the Buchanan's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and features elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia buchananii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or greenhouse setting.
