Eucalyptus bloxsomei

Eucalyptus bloxsomei, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallee, is a species of eucalyptus native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-stemmed growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, globular flower clusters that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to poor soil conditions.

Eucalyptus bloxsomei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
