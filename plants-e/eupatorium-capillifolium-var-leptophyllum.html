Eupatorium capillifolium var. leptophyllum

Eupatorium capillifolium var. leptophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. It is often found in the eastern United States and has a distinctive aromatic scent.

Its foliage and flowering period make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
