Eriocaulon aethiopicum

Eriocaulon aethiopicum, commonly known as the African bulbous grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It features a compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense, basal clump. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in suitable climates.
