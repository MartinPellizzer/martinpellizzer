Echinocactus haynii

Echinocactus haynii, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, spiny, globular shape and is covered in dense, yellowish-green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and as a houseplant.
