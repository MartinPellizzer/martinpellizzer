Echium australe var. coincyanum

Echium australe var. coincyanum, commonly known as the Blueweed or the Australian Forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
