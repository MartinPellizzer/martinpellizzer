Eugenia campestris

Eugenia campestris, commonly known as the broadleaf eucalyptus, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Eugenia campestris is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
