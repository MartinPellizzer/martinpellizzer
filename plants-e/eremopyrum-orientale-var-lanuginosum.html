Eremopyrum orientale var. lanuginosum

Eremopyrum orientale var. lanuginosum, commonly known as the silky eremopyrum, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to dry conditions. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to brown flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing sandy soils.
