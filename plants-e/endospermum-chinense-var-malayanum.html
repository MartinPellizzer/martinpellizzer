Endospermum chinense var. malayanum

Endospermum chinense var. malayanum, commonly known as the Malayan milk tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Its latex is milky and can be used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
