Eucalyptus olida

Eucalyptus olida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed malleeflower, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant is part of the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known eucalyptus species. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in mallee woodlands.

Eucalyptus olida is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
