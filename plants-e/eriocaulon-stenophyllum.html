Eriocaulon stenophyllum

Eriocaulon stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered spikelets that bear tiny flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
