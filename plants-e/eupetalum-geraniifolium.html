Eupetalum geraniifolium

Eupetalum geraniifolium, commonly known as the alpine geranium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features palmate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive, fern-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Eupetalum geraniifolium is valued for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably in cooler climates.
