Epilobium adnatum var. heribaudii

Epilobium adnatum var. heribaudii, commonly known as the Heribaud's willow herb, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.
