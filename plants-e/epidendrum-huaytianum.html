Epidendrum huaytianum

Epidendrum huaytianum, commonly known as the Huaytian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Epidendraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white lips, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
