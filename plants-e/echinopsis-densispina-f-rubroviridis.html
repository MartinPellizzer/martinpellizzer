Echinopsis densispina f. rubroviridis

Echinopsis densispina f. rubroviridis, commonly known as the red-viridian echinopsis, is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and dense, spiny stems that provide protection against herbivores and extreme weather. The plant produces striking, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to vibrant green, depending on the variety. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to survive in its natural habitat.

This cultivar is prized by succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging environments.
