Epilobium × matrense

Epilobium × matrense, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and disturbed areas.

Epilobium × matrense is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, supporting various pollinators.
