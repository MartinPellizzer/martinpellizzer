Eurya pullenii

Eurya pullenii, commonly known as the Chinese holly or Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub in tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and often have a waxy texture.
