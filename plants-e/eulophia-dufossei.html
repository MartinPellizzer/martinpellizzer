Eulophia dufossei

Eulophia dufossei, commonly known as the Dufosse's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colorful flowers that often feature vibrant yellow and red hues. The plant typically produces a single or few racemes of flowers, each with a distinctive, elongated lip. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Eulophia dufossei is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories.
