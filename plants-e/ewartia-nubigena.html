Ewartia nubigena

Ewartia nubigena, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a rare and unusual plant species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant produces small, delicate fronds that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat.
