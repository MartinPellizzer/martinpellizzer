Euphrasia × favratii

Euphrasia × favratii, commonly known as the hybrid eyebright, is a flowering plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Euphrasia longifolia and Euphrasia robbiae, both native to Europe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
