Eugenioides odoratissimum

Eugenioides odoratissimum, commonly known as the sweet-scented eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that emit a strong, sweet aroma. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
