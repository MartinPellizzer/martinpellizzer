Eriosphaera rotundifolia

Eriosphaera rotundifolia, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a powdery white coating. The plant produces small yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by tiny, round seed pods. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
