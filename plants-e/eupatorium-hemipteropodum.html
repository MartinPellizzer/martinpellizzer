Eupatorium hemipteropodum

Eupatorium hemipteropodum, commonly known as the African boneset, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often aromatic. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters, which attract various pollinators.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine for treating ailments such as fever and inflammation.
