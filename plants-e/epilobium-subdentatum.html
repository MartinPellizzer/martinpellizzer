Epilobium subdentatum

Epilobium subdentatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and streambanks. The plant features slender, sometimes hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a serrated edge. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and are usually pink to purple in color.

It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.
