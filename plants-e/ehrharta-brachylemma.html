Ehrharta brachylemma

Ehrharta brachylemma, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
