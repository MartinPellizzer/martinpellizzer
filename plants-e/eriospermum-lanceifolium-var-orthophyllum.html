Eriospermum lanceifolium var. orthophyllum

Eriospermum lanceifolium var. orthophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in loose clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to survive in dry, rocky soils.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
