Epacris impressa var. rubicunda

Epacris impressa var. rubicunda, commonly known as the red-stemmed heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular red or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in heathlands and forest margins.
