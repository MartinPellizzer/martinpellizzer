Echinodorus magdalenensis

Echinodorus magdalenensis, commonly known as Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Echinodorus magdalenensis is also known for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nitrates and other nutrients.
