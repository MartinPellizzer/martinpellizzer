Erysimum microstylum

Erysimum microstylum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Erysimum microstylum is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
