Echeveria munizii

Echeveria munizii, commonly known as the Muniz's Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its color and shape.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
