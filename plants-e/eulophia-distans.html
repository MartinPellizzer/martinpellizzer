Eulophia distans

Eulophia distans, commonly known as the distant eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, arranged in a raceme, and are known for their subtle fragrance.

Eulophia distans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
