Elatostema recticaudatum

Elatostema recticaudatum, commonly known as the narrow-tailed elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features a creeping rhizome with opposite, ovate leaves that have a waxy surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and are borne in clusters.

Elatostema recticaudatum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
