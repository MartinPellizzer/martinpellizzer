Evea campyloneuroides

Evea campyloneuroides, commonly known as the zigzag hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
