Epicranthes glebodactyla

Epicranthes glebodactyla, commonly known as the sticky leaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
