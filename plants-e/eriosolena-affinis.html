Eriosolena affinis

Eriosolena affinis, commonly known as the velvetleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and riverbanks. Its stems are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Eriosolena affinis is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
