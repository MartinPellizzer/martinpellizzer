Epimedium × roseum

Epimedium × roseum, commonly known as the barrenwort or firecracker plant, is a deciduous perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has attractive, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium × roseum is also valued for its ability to tolerate moist conditions and its low maintenance requirements.
