Escallonia × rockii

Escallonia × rockii, commonly known as the rock rose.

It is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often with a slight grayish hue.

Escallonia × rockii is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge due to its hardy nature.
