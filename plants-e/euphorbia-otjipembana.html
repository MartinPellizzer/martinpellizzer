Euphorbia otjipembana

Euphorbia otjipembana, commonly known as the Otjipemba spurge, is a shrub native to Namibia and South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has a unique structure with a central stem and multiple branches, and it produces a milky latex when damaged. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

Euphorbia otjipembana is often found in rocky soils and is a component of the local flora in its native range.
