Euphorbia × ambohipotsiensis

Euphorbia × ambohipotsiensis, commonly known as the hybrid euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in a structure called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
