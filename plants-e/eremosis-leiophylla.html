Eremosis leiophylla

Eremosis leiophylla, commonly known as the smoothleaf eremosis, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to low rainfall conditions.

Eremosis leiophylla is often found in shrublands and is a common sight in the Karoo region of South Africa.
