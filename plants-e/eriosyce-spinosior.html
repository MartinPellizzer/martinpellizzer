Eriosyce spinosior

Eriosyce spinosior, commonly known as the spiny eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in dense, spiny areoles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but attract various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
