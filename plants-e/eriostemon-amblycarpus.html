Eriostemon amblycarpus

Eriostemon amblycarpus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in coastal regions and prefers sandy or gravelly soils.

Eriostemon amblycarpus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
