Elettariopsis procurrens

Elettariopsis procurrens, commonly known as the creeping elettariopsis, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that grow in dense clusters, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers in the summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
