Elatine gracilis

Elatine gracilis, commonly known as slender water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and streams. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged, with tiny, white flowers that appear in late summer. Elatine gracilis is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats on the water surface.

It plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
