Eulophia kisanfuensis

Eulophia kisanfuensis, commonly known as the Kisanfu orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, producing clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia kisanfuensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
