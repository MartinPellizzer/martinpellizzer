Eriogonum ericifolium var. thornei

Eriogonum ericifolium var. thornei, commonly known as the Thorne's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This variety is notable for its unique morphology and ecological importance in its native habitat.
