Elatostema hanseatum

Elatostema hanseatum, commonly known as the Hanseatic elatostem, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema hanseatum is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in montane regions.
