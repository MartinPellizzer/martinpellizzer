Elatostema robustum

Elatostema robustum, commonly known as the velvetleaf stellaria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

Elatostema robustum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
