Eupatorium lysimachioide var. estrellense

Eupatorium lysimachioide var. estrellense, commonly known as Estrellense boneset, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant has serrated leaves and produces a distinctive, aromatic foliage that is often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in moist, sunny habitats and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and native plant gardening.
