Echeveria desmetiana columnaris

Echeveria desmetiana columnaris, commonly known as the Columnar Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with cylindrical, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.

This variety is prized for its unique shape and attractive foliage.
