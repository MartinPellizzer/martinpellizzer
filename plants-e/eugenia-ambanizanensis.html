Eugenia ambanizanensis

Eugenia ambanizanensis, commonly known as the Ambanizanese guava, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in parts of Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. Its fruits are typically small, round, and have a sweet, aromatic flavor.
