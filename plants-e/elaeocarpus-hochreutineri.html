Elaeocarpus hochreutineri

Elaeocarpus hochreutineri, commonly known as the Hochreutiner's elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
