Euphorbia rigoi

Euphorbia rigoi, commonly known as the rigid spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with numerous cylindrical, spiny leaves that are covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic flower-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia rigoi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
