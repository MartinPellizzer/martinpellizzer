Eragrostis cilianensis var. major

Eragrostis cilianensis var. major, commonly known as the large Cilian grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and rocky areas. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small spikelets, which are usually green to reddish in color.

This grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a forage plant.
