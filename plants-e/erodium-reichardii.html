Erodium reichardii

Erodium reichardii, commonly known as sticky lentil or redstem stinkweed, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and often edible or medicinal properties. Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it is sometimes considered a weed.
