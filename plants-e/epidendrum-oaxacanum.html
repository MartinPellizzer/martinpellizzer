Epidendrum oaxacanum

Epidendrum oaxacanum, commonly known as the Oaxaca orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often covered in a waxy cuticle.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
