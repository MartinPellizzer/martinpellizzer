Echeveria novogaliciana

Echeveria novogaliciana, commonly known as the Mexican snowball, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a succulent with rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces showy clusters of white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
