Echites virescens

Echites virescens, commonly known as the green echites, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, which helps it conserve water in harsh conditions.

Echites virescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
