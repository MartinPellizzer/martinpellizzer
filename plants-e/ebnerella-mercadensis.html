Ebnerella mercadensis

Ebnerella mercadensis, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ebnerella mercadensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
