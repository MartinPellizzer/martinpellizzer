Echites grandiflorus var. minor

Echites grandiflorus var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered echites, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal umbels. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky coastal habitats.
