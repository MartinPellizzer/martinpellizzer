Ervatamia continentalis var. pubiflora

Ervatamia continentalis var. pubiflora, commonly known as the pubiflora ervatamia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and drought tolerance.
