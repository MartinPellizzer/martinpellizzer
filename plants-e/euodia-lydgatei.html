Euodia lydgatei

Euodia lydgatei, commonly known as the Lydgate's euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain aromatic seeds. Euodia lydgatei is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and pharmacological study.
