Echinocactus ottonis var. paraguayensis

Echinocactus ottonis var. paraguayensis, commonly known as the Paraguayan hedgehog cactus, is a species native to northern Argentina and southern Paraguay.

It is a spineless cactus with a short, thick stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
