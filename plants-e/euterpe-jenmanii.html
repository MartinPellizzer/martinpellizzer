Euterpe jenmanii

Euterpe jenmanii, commonly known as the Jenman's palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fruits that are similar in appearance to acoelous fruits. Euterpe jenmanii thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It plays a significant role in the ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
