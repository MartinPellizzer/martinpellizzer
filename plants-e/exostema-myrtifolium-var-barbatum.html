Exostema myrtifolium var. barbatum

Exostema myrtifolium var. barbatum, commonly known as the barbate exostema, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually appealing species within the Exostemataceae family.
