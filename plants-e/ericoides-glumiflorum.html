Ericoides glumiflorum

Ericoides glumiflorum, commonly known as the woolly heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and moorland environments.

Ericoides glumiflorum is well adapted to cold and nutrient-poor conditions, making it a resilient species in temperate climates.
