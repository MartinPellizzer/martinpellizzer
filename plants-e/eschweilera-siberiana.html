Eschweilera siberiana

Eschweilera siberiana, commonly known as the Siberian angel's trumpet, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Eschweilera siberiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
