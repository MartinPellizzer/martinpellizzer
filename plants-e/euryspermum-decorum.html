Euryspermum decorum

Euryspermum decorum, commonly known as the ornamental euryspermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
