Escallonia illinita var. pubicalicina

Escallonia illinita var. pubicalicina, commonly known as the glossy escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that is typically a deep green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its berries are red and edible, though not particularly palatable to humans.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
