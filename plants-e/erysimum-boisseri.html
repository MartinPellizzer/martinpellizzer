Erysimum boisseri

Erysimum boisseri, commonly known as the rock wallaby bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Erysimum boisseri is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
