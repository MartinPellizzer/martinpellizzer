Epidendrum altum

Epidendrum altum, commonly known as the tall epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum altum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
