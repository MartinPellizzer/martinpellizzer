Euphrasia stricta var. suecica

Euphrasia stricta var. suecica, commonly known as the Swedish eyebright, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to northern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent, showy spots. The plant has hairy leaves that are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
