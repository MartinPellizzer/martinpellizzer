Elaeocarpus zollingeri var. changii

Elaeocarpus zollingeri var. changii, commonly known as the Chang's elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that are typically reddish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited commercial use.
