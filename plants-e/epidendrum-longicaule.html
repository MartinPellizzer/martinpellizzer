Epidendrum longicaule

Epidendrum longicaule, commonly known as the tall stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is typically epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural cultivation.
