Eria dolichocarpa

Eria dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-stemmed eria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to retain moisture.

Eria dolichocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
