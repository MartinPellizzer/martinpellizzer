Eugenia flavovirens var. pallida

Eugenia flavovirens var. pallida, commonly known as the pale eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in understory layers of tropical forests.

The fruit, which is small and edible, is typically consumed by various bird species, aiding in seed dispersal.
