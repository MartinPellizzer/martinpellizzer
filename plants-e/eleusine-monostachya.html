Eleusine monostachya

Eleusine monostachya, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands.

Eleusine monostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
