Eleocharis neozelandica

Eleocharis neozelandica, commonly known as New Zealand spike-rush, is a grass-like plant native to New Zealand.

Eleocharis neozelandica plays an important ecological role in supporting aquatic and terrestrial wildlife in its native environment.
