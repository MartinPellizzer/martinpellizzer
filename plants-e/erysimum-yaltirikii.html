Erysimum yaltirikii

Erysimum yaltirikii, commonly known as the Yaltırıkii wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the plant typically blooms in early spring.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and relatively low maintenance requirements.
