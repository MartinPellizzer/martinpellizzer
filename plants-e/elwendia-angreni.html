Elwendia angreni

Elwendia angreni, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its delicate pink to white flowers and fragrant scent. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Elwendia angreni is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its use in traditional medicine.
