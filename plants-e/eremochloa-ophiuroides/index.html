Eremochloa Ophiuroides

Eremochloa ophiuroides is a hardy grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia and Africa. It is known for its ability to thrive in extreme conditions with minimal water and nutrients. The plant forms dense clumps with narrow, gray-green leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. Its resilience makes it a valuable plant for erosion control and land rehabilitation. Despite its tough exterior, it holds ecological significance in its native habitats.
