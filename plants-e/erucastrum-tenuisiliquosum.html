Erucastrum tenuisiliquosum

Erucastrum tenuisiliquosum, commonly known as the slender silique mustard, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, elongated pods called silique, which contain the seeds. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It has small, green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat obscure species within the mustard family.
