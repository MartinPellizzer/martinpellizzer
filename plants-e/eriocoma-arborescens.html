Eriocoma arborescens

Eriocoma arborescens, commonly known as the tree eriocoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine, silky hair.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions where it thrives in full sun.
