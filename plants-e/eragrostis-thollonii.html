Eragrostis thollonii

Eragrostis thollonii, commonly known as the narrowleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Eragrostis thollonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
