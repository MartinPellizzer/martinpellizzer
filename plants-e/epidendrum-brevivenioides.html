Epidendrum brevivenioides

Epidendrum brevivenioides, commonly known as the short-stemmed epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Epidendrum brevivenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
