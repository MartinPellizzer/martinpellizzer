Erythrospermum novogranatense

Erythrospermum novogranatense, commonly known as the Crimson Beauty, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Colombia and Venezuela.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, preferring a tropical or subtropical climate.
