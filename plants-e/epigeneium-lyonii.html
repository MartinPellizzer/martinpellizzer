Epigeneium lyonii

Epigeneium lyonii, commonly known as Lyon's epigeneium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. Its flowers, which are usually red or orange, bloom in clusters and are adapted for pollination by birds.
