Eranthemum roseum

Eranthemum roseum, commonly known as the rose geranium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, rose-scented leaves and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with rounded, glossy green leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in clusters during the warmer months.

Eranthemum roseum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
