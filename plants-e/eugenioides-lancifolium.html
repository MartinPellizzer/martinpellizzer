Eugenioides lancifolium

Eugenioides lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.

The plant has also been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to the presence of various bioactive compounds.
