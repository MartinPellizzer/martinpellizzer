Eupatorium schiedeanum var. virgatum

Eupatorium schiedeanum var. virgatum, commonly known as the slender boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features tall, slender stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
