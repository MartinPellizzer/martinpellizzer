Eulophia serrata

Eulophia serrata, commonly known as the serrated eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia serrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a member of the Orchidaceae family.
