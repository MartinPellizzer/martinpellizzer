Epimedium pubigerum

Epimedium pubigerum, commonly known as the barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in late spring, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodlands and rocky slopes.

Epimedium pubigerum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
