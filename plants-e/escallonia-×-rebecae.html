Escallonia × rebecae

Escallonia × rebecae, commonly known as the pink escallonia, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Escallonia × rebecae is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
