Euodia semecarpifolia

Euodia semecarpifolia, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically harvested for their medicinal properties. Its bark and leaves contain compounds used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
