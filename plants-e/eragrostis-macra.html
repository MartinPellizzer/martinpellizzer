Eragrostis macra

Eragrostis macra, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
