Encyclia chloroleuca var. yotocoensis

Encyclia chloroleuca var. yotocoensis, commonly known as the Yotoco orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green, often with a yellow or greenish throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
