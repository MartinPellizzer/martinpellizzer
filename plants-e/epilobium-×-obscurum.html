Epilobium × obscurum

Epilobium × obscurum, commonly known as the dark willow herb, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with four pink or purplish petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady habitats such as stream banks and woodland edges.

Epilobium × obscurum is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of North America.
