Eranthemum albiflorum

Eranthemum albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
