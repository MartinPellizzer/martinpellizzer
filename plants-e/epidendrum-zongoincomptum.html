Epidendrum zongoincomptum

Epidendrum zongoincomptum, commonly known as the Zongoi Incomptum Orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, arching stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are characterized by their pale green to white coloration with a slight pinkish tinge.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
