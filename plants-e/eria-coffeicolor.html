Eria coffeicolor

Eria coffeicolor, commonly known as the coffee-colored orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically have a pale yellow or cream color with reddish streaks.

Eria coffeicolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
