Eschweilera lurida

Eschweilera lurida, commonly known as the pink-veined evergreen, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves with distinct pink veins. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are woody capsules containing several seeds.

Eschweilera lurida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
