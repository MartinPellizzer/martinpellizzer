Ehretia cassinifolia

Ehretia cassinifolia, commonly known as the Chinese mahogany or Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters tall. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in terminal panicles.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
