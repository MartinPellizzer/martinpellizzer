Elettaria linearicrista

Elettaria linearicrista, commonly known as the slender cardamom, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the ginger family, Zingiberaceae, and is characterized by its linear, narrow leaves and compact inflorescences. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is cultivated for its aromatic properties.
