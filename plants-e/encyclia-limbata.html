Encyclia limbata

Encyclia limbata, commonly known as the limbed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia limbata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
