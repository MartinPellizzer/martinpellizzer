Erica turneri

Erica turneri, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. Erica turneri thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and attractive appearance.
