Excoecaria lanceolaria

Excoecaria lanceolaria, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and floral arrangements.
