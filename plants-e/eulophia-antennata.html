Eulophia antennata

Eulophia antennata, commonly known as the orchid with the antenna-like appendage, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a greenish hue. Eulophia antennata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
