Epidendrum floribundum var. lilacinum

Epidendrum floribundum var. lilacinum, commonly known as the Lilac Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, lilac-pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
