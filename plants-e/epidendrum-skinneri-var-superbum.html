Epidendrum skinneri var. superbum

Epidendrum skinneri var. superbum, commonly known as the Giant Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It has long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
