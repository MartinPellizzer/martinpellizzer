Eulophia stenopetala

Eulophia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled eulophia, is a flowering orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and pseudobulbs that support its leaf structure. The plant produces slender, erect inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Eulophia stenopetala thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in horticulture.
