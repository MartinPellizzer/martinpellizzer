Erysimum pignattii

Erysimum pignattii, commonly known as the Pignatti's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is often found growing in rocky or coastal areas. The plant has basal and stem leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape. It produces small, elongated fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Erysimum pignattii is typically perennial and thrives in well-drained soils.
