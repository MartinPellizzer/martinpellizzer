Erodium numidicum

Erodium numidicum, commonly known as seaside geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in coastal areas and well-drained soils, often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

Erodium numidicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
