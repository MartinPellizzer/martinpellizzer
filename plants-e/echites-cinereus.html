Echites cinereus

Echites cinereus, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives them their distinctive appearance.

Echites cinereus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
