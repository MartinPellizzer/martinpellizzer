Epidendrum praetervisum

Epidendrum praetervisum, commonly known as the Purple-veined Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Epidendrum praetervisum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
