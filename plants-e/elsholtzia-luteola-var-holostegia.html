Elsholtzia luteola var. holostegia

Elsholtzia luteola var. holostegia, commonly known as yellow mountain betony, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features aromatic, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often gray-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its medicinal properties have been traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine.
