Elatostema sinuatum var. pedunculatum

Elatostema sinuatum var. pedunculatum, commonly known as the slender leaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive sinuous margin, and it produces clusters of tiny white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread vegetatively.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its humid, tropical habitat.
