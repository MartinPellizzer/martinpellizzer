Echeveria fulgens var. fulgens

Echeveria fulgens var. fulgens, commonly known as the firecracker plant, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with a powdery white coating. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
