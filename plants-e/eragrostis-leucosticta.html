Eragrostis leucosticta

Eragrostis leucosticta, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that turns a striking blue-green color in the summer. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that mature into fluffy, light brown seeds. Blue grama is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native landscapes.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for wildlife.
