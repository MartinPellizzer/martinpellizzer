Esenbeckia runyonii

Esenbeckia runyonii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal bloom make it a striking addition to any garden.
