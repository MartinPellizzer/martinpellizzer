Erysimum Lusitanicum

Erysimum lusitanicum is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa. It is known for its bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its hardy nature. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike.
