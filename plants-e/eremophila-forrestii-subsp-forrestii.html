Eremophila forrestii subsp. forrestii

Eremophila forrestii subsp.

forrestii, commonly known as the Forest's eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

This subspecies is often found in heathlands and open woodlands across southeastern Australia.
