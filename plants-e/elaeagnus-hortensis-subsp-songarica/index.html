Elaeagnus Hortensis Subsp. Songarica

Elaeagnus hortensis subsp. songarica is a lesser-known member of the Elaeagnus genus known for its hardy nature and ornamental value. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this shrub thrives in harsh climates with poor soil conditions. It is characterized by its silvery foliage and fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. Often used in landscaping for its resilience and low maintenance requirements, it is gaining popularity among gardeners. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in many botanical circles.
