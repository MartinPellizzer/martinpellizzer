Eragrostis elongatocompressa

Eragrostis elongatocompressa, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, compressed stems that grow in a weeping or drooping habit, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green color, which helps it stand out in its native habitats. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This plant is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
