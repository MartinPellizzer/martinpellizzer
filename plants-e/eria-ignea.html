Eria ignea

Eria ignea, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking orange-red flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Eria ignea is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and unique appearance.
