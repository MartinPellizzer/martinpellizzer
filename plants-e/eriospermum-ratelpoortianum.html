Eriospermum ratelpoortianum

Eriospermum ratelpoortianum, commonly known as the Ratelpoort eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, cylindrical stems and clustered, daisy-like flowers.
