Eremostachys glanduligera

Eremostachys glanduligera, commonly known as the glandular eremostachys, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Eremostachys glanduligera is often found in rocky soils and is a component of steppe and desert ecosystems.
