Erodium salmoneum

Erodium salmoneum, commonly known as redstem filaree, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and gardens.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
