Elleanthus strobilifer

Elleanthus strobilifer, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow or orange flower heads with prominent central disks. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Elleanthus strobilifer is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
