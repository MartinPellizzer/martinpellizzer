Epidendrum kraenzlinii

Epidendrum kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, often with a waxy coating, and the roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.

This orchid prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
