Eschscholzia Caespitosa Var. Hypecoides

Eschscholzia caespitosa var. hypecoides is a rare and striking member of the poppy family known for its vibrant orange blooms. Native to the coastal regions of California, this variant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and mild coastal climates. Its compact growth habit and dense foliage make it a unique addition to native gardens and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and invasive species. This plant serves as both a symbol of California's natural heritage and a reminder of the importance of conservation efforts.
