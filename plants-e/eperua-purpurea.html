Eperua purpurea

Eperua purpurea, commonly known as the purple eperua, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking purple-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Eperua purpurea is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional uses in local communities.
