Elatostema spinulosum

Elatostema spinulosum, commonly known as the spiked elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, prickly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually greenish-white and clustered in small inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Elatostema spinulosum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
