Eugenioides paraense

Eugenioides paraense, commonly known as the Paraense Eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
