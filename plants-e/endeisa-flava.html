Endeisa flava

Endeisa flava, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and is often found in humid, shaded environments. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant flowers.

Endeisa flava is primarily grown for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.
