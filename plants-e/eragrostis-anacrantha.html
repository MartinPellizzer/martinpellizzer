Eragrostis anacrantha

Eragrostis anacrantha, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to the United States.

It typically grows in open, sunny areas such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, wiry stems and narrow, blue-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, spike-like inflorescence produces numerous tiny seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Eragrostis anacrantha is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in various ecological settings.
