Epidendrum langlassei

Epidendrum langlassei, commonly known as the Langlasse's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum langlassei is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
