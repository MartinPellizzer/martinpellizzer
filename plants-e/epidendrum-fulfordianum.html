Epidendrum fulfordianum

Epidendrum fulfordianum, commonly known as the Fulford's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It prefers warm temperatures and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its flowering season usually occurs during the spring and summer months.
