Eranthemum leuconeurum

Eranthemum leuconeurum, commonly known as the silverleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Eranthemum leuconeurum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
