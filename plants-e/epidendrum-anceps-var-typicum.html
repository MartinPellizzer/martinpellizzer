Epidendrum anceps var. typicum

Epidendrum anceps var. typicum, commonly known as the Crimson Lip Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red lip and green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. It produces fragrant, long-lasting flowers in clusters, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
