Erica goatcheriana var. drakensteinensis

Erica goatcheriana var. drakensteinensis, commonly known as the Drakensberg heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and drought tolerance.
