Euphorbia kaleniczenkoi

Euphorbia kaleniczenkoi, commonly known as the Kaleniczenko's spurge, is a succulent plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in a structure called a cyathium, which is a common feature in the spurge family.

It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stems to survive in dry conditions.
