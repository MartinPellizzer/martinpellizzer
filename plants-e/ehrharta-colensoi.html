Ehrharta colensoi

Ehrharta colensoi, commonly known as Colenso's grass, is a perennial grass species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
