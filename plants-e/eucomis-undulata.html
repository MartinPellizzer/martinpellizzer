Eucomis undulata

Eucomis undulata, commonly known as the curly pineapple or African pineapple, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often curled or twisted, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Eucomis undulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add texture and interest to gardens.
