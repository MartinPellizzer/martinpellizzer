Erechtites glossanthus

Erechtites glossanthus, commonly known as the glossy leaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer, surrounded by green, fuzzy leaves. The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to 3 feet tall. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a distinctive, velvety texture.

Erechtites glossanthus is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and along stream banks.
