Erechtites minimus

Erechtites minimus, commonly known as the small bur marigold, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. Erechtites minimus thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
