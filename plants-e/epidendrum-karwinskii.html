Epidendrum karwinskii

Epidendrum karwinskii, commonly known as the Karwinski's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, cane-like stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow or orange petals and a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum karwinskii is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
