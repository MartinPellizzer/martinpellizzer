Erythroxylum banaoense

Erythroxylum banaoense, commonly known as the Banao enigma, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in Panama and Costa Rica. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and elliptical leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
