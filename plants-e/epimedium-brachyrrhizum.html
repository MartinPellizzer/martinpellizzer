Epimedium brachyrrhizum

Epimedium brachyrrhizum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are often divided into segments, and it produces yellowish-green flowers in late spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium brachyrrhizum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
