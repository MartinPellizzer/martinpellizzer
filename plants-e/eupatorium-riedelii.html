Eupatorium riedelii

Eupatorium riedelii, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, purple to pink flower heads that resemble coneflowers. Eupatorium riedelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
