Eltroplectris cogniauxiana

Eltroplectris cogniauxiana, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and a single, erect stem. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, spurred lip.

It is native to Madagascar and is valued for its unique floral structure and ornamental appeal.
