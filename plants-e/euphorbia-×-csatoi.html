Euphorbia × csatoi

Euphorbia × csatoi, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by colorful bracts, which are often mistaken for petals. It is native to regions in Africa and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Euphorbia × csatoi is known for its ability to store water, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
