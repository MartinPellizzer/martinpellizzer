Epidendrum cryptopateras

Epidendrum cryptopateras, commonly known as the hidden stem epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
