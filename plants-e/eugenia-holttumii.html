Eugenia holttumii

Eugenia holttumii, commonly known as the Holttum's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Eugenia holttumii is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
