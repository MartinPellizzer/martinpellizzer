Euodia aubertia

Euodia aubertia, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish flowers and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain aromatic seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.

Euodia aubertia thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
