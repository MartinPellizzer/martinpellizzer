Epipactis atrorubens var. stenopetala

Epipactis atrorubens var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and its showy, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with prominent purple spots. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, elliptical seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.
