Epidendrum dactylodes

Epidendrum dactylodes, commonly known as the Lemon-scented Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a lemon-like scent. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum dactylodes is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
