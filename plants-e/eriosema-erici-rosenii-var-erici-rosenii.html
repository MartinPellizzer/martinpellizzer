Eriosema erici-rosenii var. erici-rosenii

Eriosema erici-rosenii var. erici-rosenii, commonly known as the yellow-flowered eriosema, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with prominent stamens, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
