Epidendrum espiritu-santense

Epidendrum espiritu-santense, commonly known as the Espiritu Santo Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean island of Espiritu Santo.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
