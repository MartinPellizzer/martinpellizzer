Evolvulus speciosus

Evolvulus speciosus, commonly known as blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous annual with trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardens and as a ground cover.
