Epidendrum fuscum

Epidendrum fuscum, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, pale green to brown flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

Epidendrum fuscum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
