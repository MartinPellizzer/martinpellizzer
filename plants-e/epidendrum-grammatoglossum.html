Epidendrum grammatoglossum

Epidendrum grammatoglossum, commonly known as the Christmas Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters during the holiday season. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
