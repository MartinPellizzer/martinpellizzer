Eragrostis mollior

Eragrostis mollior, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, drooping stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
