Erythrorhipsalis cribrata

Erythrorhipsalis cribrata, commonly known as the red-leafed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and fleshy, often reddish-colored leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.
