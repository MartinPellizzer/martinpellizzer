Erysimum vassilczenkoi

Erysimum vassilczenkoi, commonly known as the Armenian wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

Erysimum vassilczenkoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
