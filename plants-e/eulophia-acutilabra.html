Eulophia acutilabra

Eulophia acutilabra, commonly known as the sharp-leafed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia acutilabra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
