Ehretia urceolata

Ehretia urceolata, commonly known as the urn-shaped ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive urn-shaped fruits, which are typically yellow or orange when mature. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value.

Ehretia urceolata is often cultivated for its unique appearance and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
