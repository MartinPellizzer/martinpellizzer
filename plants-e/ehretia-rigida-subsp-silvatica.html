Ehretia rigida subsp. silvatica

Ehretia rigida subsp.

silvatica, commonly known as the forest ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to parts of Southeast Asia. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.

The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in axillary inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that splits open to release seeds.
