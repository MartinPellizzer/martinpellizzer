Eriosyce senilis subsp. coimasensis

Eriosyce senilis subsp.

coimasensis, commonly known as the Coimasensis Pincushion Cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to Argentina. It is characterized by its dense, globular stem that resembles a pincushion, covered with white, woolly spines.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in cactus gardens.
