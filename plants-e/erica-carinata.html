Erica carinata

Erica carinata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and has small, needle-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Erica carinata is well adapted to harsh, sunny conditions and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
