Encyclia krugii

Encyclia krugii, commonly known as the Krug's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a yellowish-green hue and prominent purple markings. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia krugii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
