Epidendrum harmsianum

Epidendrum harmsianum, commonly known as the Harms' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear clusters of pendant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum harmsianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
