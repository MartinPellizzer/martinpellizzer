Euphorbia reinhardtii var. bilocularis

Euphorbia reinhardtii var. bilocularis, commonly known as the two-chambered spurges, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces unique, colorful cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Due to its toxic sap, it requires careful handling and is not recommended for indoor environments with children or pets.
