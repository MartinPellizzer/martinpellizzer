Euodia hupehensis

Euodia hupehensis, commonly known as Chinese bitter orange, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, citrus-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture.

Euodia hupehensis is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
