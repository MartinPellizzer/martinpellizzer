Ethulia gariepina

Ethulia gariepina, commonly known as the Gariep daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange ray florets and dark brown disc florets. Ethulia gariepina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
