Etlingera elegans

Etlingera elegans, commonly known as the white ginger or white torch ginger, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
