Echeveria minutiflora

Echeveria minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature flower echeveria, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight to maintain its compact shape.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in rock gardens.
