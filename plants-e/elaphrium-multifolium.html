Elaphrium multifolium

Elaphrium multifolium, commonly known as the mountain flax, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the western United States.

The plant produces delicate blue to violet flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense racemes. Elaphrium multifolium thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
