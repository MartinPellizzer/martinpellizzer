Euodia forbesii

Euodia forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by round, yellowish fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Euodia forbesii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.
