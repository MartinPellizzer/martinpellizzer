Euphorbia mixta

Euphorbia mixta, commonly known as the mixed spurge, is a perennial plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, branching stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to arid gardens.

Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to landscapes.
