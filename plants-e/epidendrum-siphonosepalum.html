Epidendrum siphonosepalum

Epidendrum siphonosepalum, commonly known as the Siphonosepal Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elliptical and covered with a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The sepals and petals are elongated, giving the flower a distinctive, elegant appearance.
