Enkianthus sichuanensis

Enkianthus sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Enkianthus sichuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique beauty.
