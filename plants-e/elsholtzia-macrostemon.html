Elsholtzia macrostemon

Elsholtzia macrostemon, commonly known as Chinese elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to lavender flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Elsholtzia macrostemon is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
