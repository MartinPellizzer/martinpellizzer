Eremostachys baldschuanica var. trautvetteriana

Eremostachys baldschuanica var. trautvetteriana, commonly known as the Trautvetter's feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and delicate, feathery inflorescences that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest in gardens.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-maintenance landscapes.
