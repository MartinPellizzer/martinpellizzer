Encyclia kundergraberi

Encyclia kundergraberi, commonly known as the Kundergraberi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the highlands of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, greenish-white flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
