Ehretia rupestris

Ehretia rupestris, commonly known as the rock ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in well-drained conditions. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are often dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round berries.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable climates.
