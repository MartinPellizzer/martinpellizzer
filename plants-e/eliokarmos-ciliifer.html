Eliokarmos ciliifer

Eliokarmos ciliifer, commonly known as the fringed leaf elokarmos, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are elongated and have fine, hair-like structures along the edges, giving them a fringed appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
