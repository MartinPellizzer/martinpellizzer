Eriope simplex

Eriope simplex, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Eriope simplex is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
