Eulophia pretoriensis

Eulophia pretoriensis, commonly known as the Pretoria orchid, is a tropical orchid native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Eulophia pretoriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
