Epilepis

These plants are characterized by their small, scale-like leaves and their ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. They play a significant role in soil stabilization and provide habitat for various microorganisms.

The reproductive structures of Epilepis, known as strobila, contain the spores necessary for its propagation.
