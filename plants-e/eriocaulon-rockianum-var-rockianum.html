Eriocaulon rockianum var. rockianum

Eriocaulon rockianum var. rockianum, commonly known as the rock fern, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, characterized by its small, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically forms dense mats or cushions, which help it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are small and clustered, often producing a distinctive, fluffy appearance.

Eriocaulon rockianum var. rockianum is well adapted to poor soils and is often found in crevices or on rock surfaces.
