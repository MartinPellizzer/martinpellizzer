Endotis pyrenaica

Endotis pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Endotis pyrenaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
