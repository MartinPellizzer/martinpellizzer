Echeveria macrantha

Echeveria macrantha, commonly known as the Showy Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wool. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear showy, violet-pink flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Echeveria macrantha is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
