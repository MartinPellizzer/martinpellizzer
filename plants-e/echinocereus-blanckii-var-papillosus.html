Echinocereus blanckii var. papillosus

Echinocereus blanckii var. papillosus, commonly known as the Papillate Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, spiny cactus that typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments. The plant features a short, cylindrical stem covered with white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in the spring.

This variety is well adapted to drought conditions and requires minimal water once established.
