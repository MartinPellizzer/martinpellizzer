Echinodorus reticulatus

Echinodorus reticulatus, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It has broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length, providing a lush, green appearance in aquariums. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light and nutrient-rich substrates. It is known for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nitrates and other harmful substances.

Echinodorus reticulatus is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
