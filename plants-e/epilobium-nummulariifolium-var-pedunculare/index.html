Epilobium Nummulariifolium Var. Pedunculare

Epilobium nummulariifolium var. pedunculare is a unique variant of the common willowherb known for its striking appearance and adaptability. This plant is often found in rocky and coastal areas where it thrives in harsh conditions. Its small, round leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers make it a standout in local flora. Despite its hardy nature, it is relatively rare and requires specific environmental conditions to flourish. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and ecological significance.
