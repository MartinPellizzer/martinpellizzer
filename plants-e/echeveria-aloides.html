Echeveria aloides

Echeveria aloides, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-gray or silver in color. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
