Elaphoglossum ortegae

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically bright green and somewhat leathery in texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight.
