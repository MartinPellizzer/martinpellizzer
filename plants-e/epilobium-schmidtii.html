Epilobium schmidtii

Epilobium schmidtii, commonly known as Schmidt's willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Eurasia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are arranged in spike-like clusters and are usually pink to reddish in color.

Epilobium schmidtii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats.
