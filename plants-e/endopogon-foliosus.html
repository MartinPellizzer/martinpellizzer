Endopogon foliosus

Endopogon foliosus, commonly known as the leafy endopogon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
