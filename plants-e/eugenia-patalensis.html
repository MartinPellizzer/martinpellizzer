Eugenia patalensis

Eugenia patalensis, commonly known as the Patala myrtle, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a fragrant, waxy texture.

Eugenia patalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
