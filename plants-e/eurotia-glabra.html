Eurotia glabra

Eurotia glabra, commonly known as the smooth eurotia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairless. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically arranged in terminal and axillary inflorescences. Eurotia glabra is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering habit.
