Epidendrum orthophyllum

Epidendrum orthophyllum, commonly known as the white-veined epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in tropical regions with high humidity. The plant features long, arching stems that bear multiple clusters of showy, white or pale green flowers with intricate veining. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This species is well-suited for greenhouse cultivation due to its preference for warm, moist conditions.
