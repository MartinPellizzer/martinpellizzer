Echinocactus denudatus var. anisitsii

Echinocactus denudatus var. anisitsii, commonly known as the Anisitsi Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny areoles, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
