Erysimum damirliense

Erysimum damirliense, commonly known as the Damirli mustard, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. It produces small, bright yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its foliage is characterized by serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair.
