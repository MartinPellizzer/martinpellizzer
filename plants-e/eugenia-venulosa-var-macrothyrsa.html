Eugenia venulosa var. macrothyrsa

Eugenia venulosa var. macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-thyrsed eugenia, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in indigenous communities.
