Euphorbia yunnanensis

Euphorbia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique cluster called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stems for survival.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
