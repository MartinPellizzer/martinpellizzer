Epilobium glabellum var. junceum

Epilobium glabellum var. junceum, commonly known as narrowleaf willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the northern hemisphere.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and riverbanks. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces spikes of pink to reddish-purple flowers. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in temperate regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and wetland restoration projects.
