Epithema brunonis var. longipetiolatum

Epithema brunonis var. longipetiolatum, commonly known as the long-petioled epithema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, slender petioles, giving it its varietal name, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to tolerate salt spray.
