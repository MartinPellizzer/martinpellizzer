Ekmaniopappus mikanioides

Ekmaniopappus mikanioides, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and clusters of small, bright orange to red flowers. The plant produces fluffy, white seed heads that aid in wind pollination. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental or for its medicinal properties.

Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture that helps reduce water loss.
