Ectasis banksia

Ectasis banksia, commonly known as the yellow banksia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical inflorescence. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand the dry conditions of its habitat.
