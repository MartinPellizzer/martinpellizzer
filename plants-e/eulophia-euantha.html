Eulophia euantha

Eulophia euantha, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia euantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance.
