Elaphoglossum xiphoides

Elaphoglossum xiphoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically arching or pendulous in growth habit. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with a dense covering of brown, hairy scales. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its small, bright green leaves are often adorned with a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
