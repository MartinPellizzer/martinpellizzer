Epidendrum arachnoideum

Epidendrum arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces intricate, spider-like inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and delicate blooms make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
