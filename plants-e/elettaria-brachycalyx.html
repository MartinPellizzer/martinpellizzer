Elettaria brachycalyx

Elettaria brachycalyx, commonly known as the lesser cardamom, is a member of the ginger family, Zingiberaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant produces small, aromatic, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a mild, spicy aroma.

The plant is valued for its seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
