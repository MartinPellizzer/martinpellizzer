Epacris impressa f. milliganii

Epacris impressa f. milliganii, commonly known as the Milligan's epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or mauve in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal heathlands.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
