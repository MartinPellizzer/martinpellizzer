Erica Radicans Subsp. Schlechteri

Erica radicans subsp. schlechteri is a hardy evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It is known for its dense, compact growth and vibrant evergreen foliage. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its ability to withstand drought makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. With its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements, it is a favorite among gardeners seeking resilient, decorative plants.
