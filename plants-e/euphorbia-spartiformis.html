Euphorbia spartiformis

Euphorbia spartiformis, commonly known as the Spiny Spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry conditions. Its leaves are often reduced to small spines, which help minimize water loss.

Euphorbia spartiformis is a hardy plant that requires minimal water and full sun exposure to flourish.
