Ehretia corymbosa

Ehretia corymbosa, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus or white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
