Encyclia tigrina

Encyclia tigrina, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces pendulous racemes of blooms, each with a three-lobed lip that is often marked with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. Encyclia tigrina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
