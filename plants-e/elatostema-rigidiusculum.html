Elatostema rigidiusculum

Elatostema rigidiusculum, commonly known as the rigid leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. The plant features narrow, stiff leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, three-lobed fruits.

Elatostema rigidiusculum is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
