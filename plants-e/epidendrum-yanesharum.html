Epidendrum yanesharum

Epidendrum yanesharum, commonly known as the Yanesharum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the understory of forests. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are characterized by their pale green to yellowish coloration with intricate patterns.

Epidendrum yanesharum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
