Epilobium davuricum var. arcticum

Epilobium davuricum var. arcticum, commonly known as Arctic willow or arctic rose, is a herbaceous plant native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and small, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically thrives in cold, moist environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Epilobium davuricum var. arcticum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil in tundra and mountainous areas.
