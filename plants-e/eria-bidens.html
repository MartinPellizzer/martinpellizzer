Eria bidens

Eria bidens, commonly known as the two-toothed eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disk florets and white ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and wetlands. It has hairy stems and leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Eria bidens is often found in tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
