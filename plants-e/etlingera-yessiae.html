Etlingera yessiae

Etlingera yessiae, commonly known as the Yessie's torch ginger, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The genus Etlingera is known for its ornamental value, and E. yessiae is no exception, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
