Epidendrum fragrans var. ionoleucum

Epidendrum fragrans var. ionoleucum, commonly known as the fragrant epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or gray veins. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
