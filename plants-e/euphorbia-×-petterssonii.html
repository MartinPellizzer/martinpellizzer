Euphorbia × petterssonii

Euphorbia × petterssonii, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cup-shaped structure called a cyathium. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Euphorbia × petterssonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
