Euphorbia tshuiensis var. tuvinica

Euphorbia tshuiensis var. tuvinica, commonly known as the Tuvinian spurge, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

The species is characterized by its ability to store water in its stems, making it suitable for xerophytic conditions.
