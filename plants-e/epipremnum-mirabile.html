Epipremnum mirabile

Epipremnum mirabile, commonly known as the golden pothos, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves that often display a striking golden variegation. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and vertical landscaping.

Epipremnum mirabile is also notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its tolerance to low light conditions.
