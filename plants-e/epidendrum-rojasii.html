Epidendrum rojasii

Epidendrum rojasii, commonly known as the Crimson Lip Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically display a combination of red and yellow hues. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Epidendrum rojasii thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
