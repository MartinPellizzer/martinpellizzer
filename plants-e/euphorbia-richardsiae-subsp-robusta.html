Euphorbia richardsiae subsp. robusta

Euphorbia richardsiae subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the Richardsia euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
