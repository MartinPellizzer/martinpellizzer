Epidendrum linearifolium

Epidendrum linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaf epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
