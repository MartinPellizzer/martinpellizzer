Elaeagnus montana var. ovata

Elaeagnus montana var. ovata, commonly known as silver leaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, powdery wax, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Elaeagnus montana var. ovata is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh conditions.
