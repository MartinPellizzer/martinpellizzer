Erysimum duriaei

Erysimum duriaei, commonly known as the yellow wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family plants.

Erysimum duriaei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
