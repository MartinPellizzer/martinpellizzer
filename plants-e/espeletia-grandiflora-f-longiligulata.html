Espeletia grandiflora f. longiligulata

Espeletia grandiflora f. longiligulata, commonly known as the long-ligulate espeletia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
