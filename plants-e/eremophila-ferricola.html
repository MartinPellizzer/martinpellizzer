Eremophila ferricola

Eremophila ferricola, commonly known as the ironbark eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are often narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Eremophila ferricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
