Epidendrum luteolum

Epidendrum luteolum, commonly known as the Yellow Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and can bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Epidendrum luteolum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
