Eugenia kleinii var. aristata

Eugenia kleinii var. aristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, edible berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
