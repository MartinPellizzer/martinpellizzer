Eugenia furfuracea

Eugenia furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy eucalyptus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rough, hairy bark and small, elliptical leaves. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.
