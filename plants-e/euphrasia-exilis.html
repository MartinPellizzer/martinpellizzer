Euphrasia exilis

Euphrasia exilis, commonly known as slender eyebright, is a small herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features delicate, yellowish-green flowers with prominent yellow spots, which are characteristic of the Scrophulariaceae family. Its leaves are opposite, oval, and covered in fine hairs.

Euphrasia exilis is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported eye health benefits.
