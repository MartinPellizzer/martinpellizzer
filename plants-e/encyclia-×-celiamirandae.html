Encyclia × celiamirandae

Encyclia × celiamirandae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia frondosa.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Mexico. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support strap-like, dark green leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow with purple or brown markings, arranged in racemes.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil and partial shade.
