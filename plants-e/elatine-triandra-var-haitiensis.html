Elatine triandra var. haitiensis

Elatine triandra var. haitiensis, commonly known as Haitian waterwort, is a small aquatic plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Haiti.

It typically grows in freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has thin, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small, green flowers that are clustered near the water's surface.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to various aquatic habitats and its role in supporting aquatic ecosystems.
