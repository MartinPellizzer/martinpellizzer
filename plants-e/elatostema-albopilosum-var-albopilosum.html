Elatostema albopilosum var. albopilosum

Elatostema albopilosum var. albopilosum, commonly known as the white-hairy elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a compact rosette of fleshy, elliptical leaves that are densely covered in white, silky hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence.

The species is noted for its unique leaf morphology and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
