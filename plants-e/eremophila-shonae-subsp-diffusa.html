Eremophila shonae subsp. diffusa

Eremophila shonae subsp.

diffusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eremophila, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, grey-green leaves and clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and fire resilience. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in heathlands and sandy soils.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant communities.
