Eriachne basedowii

Eriachne basedowii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to Australia, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue or white flowers.

Eriachne basedowii is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often found in coastal dunes or inland shrublands.
