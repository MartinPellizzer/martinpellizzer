Engelhardia nudiflora var. crenata

Engelhardia nudiflora var. crenata, commonly known as the smooth-flowered engelhardia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that appear before the leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is usually elliptical or ovate, with a leathery texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
