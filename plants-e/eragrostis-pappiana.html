Eragrostis pappiana

Eragrostis pappiana, commonly known as the slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Eragrostis pappiana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small wildlife.
