Elaeocarpus stipularis var. stipularis

Elaeocarpus stipularis var. stipularis, commonly known as the slender-flowered elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape with a glossy surface. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, woody seeds.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
