Epiphyllum crenatum var. kimnachii

Epiphyllum crenatum var. kimnachii, commonly known as the Kimnach's orchid cactus, is a hybrid species belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America and is characterized by its flat, leaf-like stems that are often covered in white or pale green spines. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming at night. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
