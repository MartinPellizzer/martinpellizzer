Epidendrum solomonii

Epidendrum solomonii, commonly known as Solomon's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. It produces pendulous flower spikes with numerous small, fragrant blossoms in shades of white, yellow, and orange.

This species is well-suited for cultivation in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
