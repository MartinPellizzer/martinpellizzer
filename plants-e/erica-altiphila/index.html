Erica Altiphila

Erica altiphila is a unique evergreen shrub known for its ability to thrive in extreme high-altitude environments. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions. Its small, leathery leaves and delicate pink or white flowers make it both resilient and visually striking. Erica altiphila plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its arid habitat. Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it has become a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
