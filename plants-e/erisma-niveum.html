Erisma niveum

Erisma niveum, commonly known as the snowbell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped white flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are hairy and grayish-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Erisma niveum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
