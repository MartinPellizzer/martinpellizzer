Epidendrum aemulum

Epidendrum aemulum, commonly known as the miniature epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a compact growth habit with short, fleshy stems and clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, often appearing in shades of white, yellow, or green.

Epidendrum aemulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
