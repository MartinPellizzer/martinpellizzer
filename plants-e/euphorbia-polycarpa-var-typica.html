Euphorbia polycarpa var. typica

Euphorbia polycarpa var. typica, commonly known as the Christmas star or starburst euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with bright green, waxy leaves that are arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique floral structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
