Echium petraeum

Echium petraeum, commonly known as the rock spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces striking blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is well adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Echium petraeum is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
