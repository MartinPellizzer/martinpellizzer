Echinocactus Gibbosus F. Leucodictyus

Echinocactus gibbosus f. leucodictyus is a rare and striking variant of the golden barrel cactus. Known for its unique white to light green spines, it stands out among its green-hued relatives. Native to the arid regions of Mexico, it thrives in harsh, sun-scorched environments. This fascinating plant is highly sought after by collectors for its distinctive appearance. Its cultivation requires careful attention to light, water, and soil conditions.
