Encyclia patens var. patens

Encyclia patens var. patens, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, broad, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitat.
