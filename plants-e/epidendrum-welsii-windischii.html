Epidendrum welsii-windischii

Epidendrum welsii-windischii, commonly known as the Welsii Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant produces linear, often hairy leaves that are typically arranged in two rows along the stem. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
