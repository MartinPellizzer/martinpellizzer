Erophila nana

Erophila nana, commonly known as small erophila, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are white or pale blue and are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Erophila nana is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
