Eriosyce glabrescens

Eriosyce glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth eriosyce, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae, native to Argentina.

Eriosyce glabrescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
