Eriocaulon sellowianum var. longifolium

Eriocaulon sellowianum var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with elongated, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, preferring moist, acidic soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which are often submerged or nearly so.

This variety is distinguished by its longer leaves compared to other varieties within the same species.
