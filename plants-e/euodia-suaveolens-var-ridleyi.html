Euodia suaveolens var. ridleyi

Euodia suaveolens var. ridleyi, commonly known as the fragrant lauraceae, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, dark berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are often used in perfumery due to their pleasant scent.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
