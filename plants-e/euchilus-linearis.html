Euchilus linearis

Euchilus linearis, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
