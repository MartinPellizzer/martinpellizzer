Echium minutiflorum

Echium minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas. It has hairy, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Echium minutiflorum is an annual plant that plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
