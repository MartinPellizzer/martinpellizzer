Echinomastus warnockii

Echinomastus warnockii, commonly known as the Warnock's echinomastus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Echinomastus warnockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
