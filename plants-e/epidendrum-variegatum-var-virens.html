Epidendrum variegatum var. virens

Epidendrum variegatum var. virens, commonly known as the Green Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers that are often tinged with yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in both indoor and outdoor settings.
