Eragrostis holstii

Eragrostis holstii, commonly known as the silverleaf lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous pale green to yellowish florets.

Eragrostis holstii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
