Elsholtzia rugulosa

Elsholtzia rugulosa, commonly known as the woolly elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features slender, aromatic stems covered in fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, blooming in summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a fragrant, mint-like aroma.

Elsholtzia rugulosa is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
