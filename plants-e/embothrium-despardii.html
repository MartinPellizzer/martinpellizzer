Embothrium despardii

Embothrium despardii, commonly known as the Despard's firetree, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its bright red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions, often found in cloud forests.

Embothrium despardii is valued for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
