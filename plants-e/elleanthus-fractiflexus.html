Elleanthus fractiflexus

Elleanthus fractiflexus, commonly known as the flexible flameflower, is a unique plant characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences.

The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural areas. Elleanthus fractiflexus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its eye-catching floral display.
