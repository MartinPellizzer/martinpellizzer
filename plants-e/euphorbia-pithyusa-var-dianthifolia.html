Euphorbia pithyusa var. dianthifolia

Euphorbia pithyusa var. dianthifolia, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with small, star-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking yellow-orange cyathia, which are its modified inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
