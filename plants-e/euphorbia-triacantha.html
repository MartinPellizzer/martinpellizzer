Euphorbia triacantha

Euphorbia triacantha, commonly known as the spiny cactus or goat's horn cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems covered in sharp, spiny tubercles. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by unique, gelatinous fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its spines and toxic sap, it requires careful handling.
