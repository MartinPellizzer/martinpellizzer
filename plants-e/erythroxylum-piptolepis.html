Erythroxylum piptolepis

Erythroxylum piptolepis, commonly known as the Mexican pepper plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is also known for its potential medicinal uses and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
