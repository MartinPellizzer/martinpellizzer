Erigeron thunbergii f. albiflorus

Erigeron thunbergii f. albiflorus, commonly known as the Whiteflower Blueweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features showy, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It is native to eastern Asia and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

This variety is distinguished by its pale flowers, which contrast with the typical blue or purple hues of the species.
