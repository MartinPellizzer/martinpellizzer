Epidendrum kockii

Epidendrum kockii, commonly known as the Kock's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems and is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum kockii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
