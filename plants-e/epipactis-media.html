Epipactis media

Epipactis media, commonly known as the common helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It has a complex life cycle involving mycorrhizal associations with fungi.

Epipactis media is known for its ability to hybridize with other orchid species, contributing to its genetic diversity.
