Epidendrum lavendulum

Epidendrum lavendulum, commonly known as the lavender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking lavender-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has elliptical, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Epidendrum lavendulum is relatively easy to care for and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
