Erithalis cymosa

Erithalis cymosa, commonly known as the clustered erithalis, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Erithalis cymosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
