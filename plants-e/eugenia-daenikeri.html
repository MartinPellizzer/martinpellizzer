Eugenia daenikeri

Eugenia daenikeri, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Eugenia daenikeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
