Elatostema membranaceum

Elatostema membranaceum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces tiny greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Elatostema membranaceum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
