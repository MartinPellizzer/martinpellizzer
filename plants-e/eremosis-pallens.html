Eremosis pallens

Eremosis pallens, commonly known as the pale eremosis, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Eremosis pallens is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.

Its botanical features make it a hardy species well-suited for xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
