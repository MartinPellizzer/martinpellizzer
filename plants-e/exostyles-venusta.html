Exostyles venusta

Exostyles venusta, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
