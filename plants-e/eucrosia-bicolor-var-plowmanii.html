Eucrosia bicolor var. plowmanii

Eucrosia bicolor var. plowmanii, commonly known as the Plowman's goldenstar, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, often with a contrasting red or orange center. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
