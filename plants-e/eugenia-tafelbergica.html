Eugenia tafelbergica

Eugenia tafelbergica, commonly known as the Tafelberg eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
