Epilobium gemmiferum

Epilobium gemmiferum, commonly known as the gemmiferous willow herb, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Epilobium gemmiferum is known for its ability to thrive in cold climates and is often found in alpine and tundra environments.
