Erica mackayana f. eburnea

Erica mackayana f. eburnea, commonly known as the silver-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking silver-gray foliage. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in spring.

This evergreen shrub thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
