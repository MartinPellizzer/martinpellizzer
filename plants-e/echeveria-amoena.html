Echeveria amoena

Echeveria amoena, commonly known as the "Pink Flowering Echeveria," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
