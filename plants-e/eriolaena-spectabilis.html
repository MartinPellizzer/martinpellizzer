Eriolaena spectabilis

Eriolaena spectabilis, commonly known as the golden lily, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and are highly ornamental. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Eriolaena spectabilis thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
