Etlingera penicillata

Etlingera penicillata, commonly known as the red torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking red and orange flower spikes, which are often used in floral arrangements. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface.
