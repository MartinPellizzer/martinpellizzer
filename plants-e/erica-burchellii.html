Erica burchellii

Erica burchellii, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica burchellii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ecological benefits.
