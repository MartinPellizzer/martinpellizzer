Elatostema longifolium

Elatostema longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, with white or pale green petals.

Elatostema longifolium is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
