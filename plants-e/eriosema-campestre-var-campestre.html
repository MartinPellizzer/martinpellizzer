Eriosema campestre var. campestre

Eriosema campestre var. campestre, commonly known as the field eriosema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branched stems that often lie prostrate or are slightly ascending. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.
