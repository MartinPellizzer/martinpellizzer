Elaeocarpus forbesii

Elaeocarpus forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its growth habit includes a straight trunk and dense foliage, making it suitable for ornamental purposes.

The species is also valued for its potential ecological roles in forest ecosystems.
