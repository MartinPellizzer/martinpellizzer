Eremosis callilepis

Eremosis callilepis, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, helping to conserve water.

Eremosis callilepis is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adds vibrant color to the landscape.
