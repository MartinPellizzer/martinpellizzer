Eria isochila

Eria isochila, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, upright growth habit and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or mountainous areas. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Eria isochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
