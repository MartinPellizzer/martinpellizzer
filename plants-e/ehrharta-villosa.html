Ehrharta villosa

Ehrharta villosa, commonly known as silky grass or velvet grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, soft, and hairy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Ehrharta villosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
