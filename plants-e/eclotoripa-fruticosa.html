Eclotoripa fruticosa

Eclotoripa fruticosa, commonly known as the red-fruited shrub, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 3 meters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its most distinctive feature is its bright red, fleshy fruits, which contain several seeds.

Eclotoripa fruticosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and riverbanks.
