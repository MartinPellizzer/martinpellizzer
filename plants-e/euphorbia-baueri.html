Euphorbia baueri

Euphorbia baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, branched stem covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia baueri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
