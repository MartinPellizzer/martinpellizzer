Euonymus chenkangensis

Euonymus chenkangensis, commonly known as the Chenkang euonymus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
