Eriosema × woodii

Eriosema × woodii, commonly known as the African violet, is a hybrid plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
