Epidendrum seriatum

Epidendrum seriatum, commonly known as the Shiny Lip Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically has dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum seriatum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
