Epidendrum bonitense

Epidendrum bonitense, commonly known as the Bonito Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long, and often colorful inflorescences that can reach several feet in length. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Epidendrum bonitense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
