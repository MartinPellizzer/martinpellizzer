Erysimum duriaei subsp. duriaei

Erysimum duriaei subsp.

duriaei, commonly known as the Duriaei Wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, bright yellow or orange flowers in early spring.

The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
