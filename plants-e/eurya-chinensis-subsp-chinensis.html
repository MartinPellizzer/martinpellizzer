Eurya chinensis subsp. chinensis

Eurya chinensis subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia. It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of white or yellowish flowers.
