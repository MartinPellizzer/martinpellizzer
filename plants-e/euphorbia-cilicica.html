Euphorbia cilicica

Euphorbia cilicica, commonly known as the Cilicia spurge, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features succulent, ribbed stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is characterized by its milky sap, which can be irritating to the skin. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Euphorbia cilicica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
