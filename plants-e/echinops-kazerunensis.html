Echinops kazerunensis

Echinops kazerunensis, commonly known as the Kazerun thistle, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, spherical seed heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are gray-green and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow.

Echinops kazerunensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
