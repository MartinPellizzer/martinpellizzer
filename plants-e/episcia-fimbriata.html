Episcia fimbriata

Episcia fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed waxy plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with intricate, fringed margins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Episcia fimbriata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
