Eria propinqua

Eria propinqua, commonly known as the small orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Eria propinqua is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
