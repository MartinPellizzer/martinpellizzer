Eupatorium heptaneurum

Eupatorium heptaneurum, commonly known as white boneset, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and is often found in moist, wooded areas. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

White boneset is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
