Emilia longiramea

Emilia longiramea, commonly known as the long-stemmed emilia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Emilia longiramea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
