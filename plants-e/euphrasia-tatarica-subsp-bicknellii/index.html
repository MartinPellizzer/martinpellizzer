Euphrasia Tatarica Subsp. Bicknellii

Euphrasia tatarica subsp. bicknellii is a rare and lesser-known member of the Orobanchaceae family. Native to specific regions in Eastern Europe and parts of Russia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its delicate, yellowish flowers and hairy stems. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered in many areas. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
