Epimedium × youngianum f. rubrum

Epimedium × youngianum f. rubrum, commonly known as the red-leaf epimedium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking reddish foliage, which becomes more vibrant in cooler temperatures. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in March or April. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique leaf color and low maintenance requirements.
