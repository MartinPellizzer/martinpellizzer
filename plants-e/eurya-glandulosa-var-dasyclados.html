Eurya glandulosa var. dasyclados

Eurya glandulosa var. dasyclados, commonly known as the glossy eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically elliptical, leathery, and have a glossy sheen, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variant is notable for its distinct floral structure and is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
