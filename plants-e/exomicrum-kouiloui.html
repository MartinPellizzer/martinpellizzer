Exomicrum kouiloui

Exomicrum kouiloui, commonly known as the Kouilou wild bean, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to purple.

The seeds are enclosed in tough, leathery pods, which help in seed dispersal through the environment.
