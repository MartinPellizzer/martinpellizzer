Echinops rectangularis

Echinops rectangularis, commonly known as the rigid thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical seed heads that are covered in bracts. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is drought-tolerant. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellow flowers in the spring.

Echinops rectangularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and hardy nature.
