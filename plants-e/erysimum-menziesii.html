Erysimum menziesii

Erysimum menziesii, commonly known as the yellow wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is part of the Brassicaceae family.

Erysimum menziesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environments.
