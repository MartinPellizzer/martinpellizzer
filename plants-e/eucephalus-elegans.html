Eucephalus elegans

Eucephalus elegans, commonly known as the silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features silvery-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
