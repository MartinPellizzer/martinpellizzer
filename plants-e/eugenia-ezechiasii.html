Eugenia ezechiasii

Eugenia ezechiasii, commonly known as the Ezechiasii Myrtle, is a shrub native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers, which are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature, and they are edible for some wildlife.

Eugenia ezechiasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in subtropical gardens.
