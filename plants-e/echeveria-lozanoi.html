Echeveria lozanoi

Echeveria lozanoi, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers in the spring. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
