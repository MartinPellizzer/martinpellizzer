Euonymus sanguineus var. camptoneurus

Euonymus sanguineus var. camptoneurus, commonly known as the red-spotted bugloss, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flower color and adaptability to various growing conditions.
