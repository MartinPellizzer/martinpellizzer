Epacris impressa var. tenuis

Epacris impressa var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender leek-leaf heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.
