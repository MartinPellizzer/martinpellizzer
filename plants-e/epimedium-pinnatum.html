Epimedium pinnatum

Epimedium pinnatum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The plant thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Epimedium pinnatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
