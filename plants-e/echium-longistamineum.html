Echium longistamineum

Echium longistamineum, commonly known as the long-stamen echium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. Its unique feature is the elongated stamens that extend beyond the petals, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
