Epimedium glandulosopilosum

Epimedium glandulosopilosum, commonly known as the fuzzy leaf barbatan, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with a distinctive fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in panicles. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and early blooming habit.
