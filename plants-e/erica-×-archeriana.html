Erica × archeriana

Erica × archeriana, commonly known as the Heath 'Archeriana', is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens, borders, or as a container plant due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
