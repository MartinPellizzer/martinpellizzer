Elaphoglossum gracile

Elaphoglossum gracile, commonly known as the slender fern, is a small, delicate fern species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Elaphoglossum gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
