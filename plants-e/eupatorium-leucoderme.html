Eupatorium leucoderme

Eupatorium leucoderme, commonly known as white eupatorium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium leucoderme is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
