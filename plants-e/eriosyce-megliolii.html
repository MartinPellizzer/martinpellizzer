Eriosyce megliolii

Eriosyce megliolii, commonly known as the Puna Puna, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering that helps it retain moisture in its arid environment. The plant typically grows in rocky, high-altitude regions where it is exposed to strong winds and intense sunlight. Eriosyce megliolii is well adapted to extreme conditions, making it a resilient and unique member of the cactus family.
