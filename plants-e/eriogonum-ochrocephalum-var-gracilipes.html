Eriogonum ochrocephalum var. gracilipes

Eriogonum ochrocephalum var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers.
