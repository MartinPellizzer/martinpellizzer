Eria simplex

Eria simplex, commonly known as the simple eria, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eria simplex is a perennial herb that prefers well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
