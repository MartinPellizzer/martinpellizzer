Elatostema densistriolatum

Elatostema densistriolatum, commonly known as the dense-striated elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. The plant features a short, creeping stem with numerous small, heart-shaped leaves that are densely covered in fine, thread-like hairs. Its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, which are often hidden among the foliage.

Elatostema densistriolatum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its unique leaf structure that aids in water retention.
