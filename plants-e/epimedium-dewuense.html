Epimedium dewuense

Epimedium dewuense, commonly known as Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically evergreen, with a deep green hue. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in early spring before the leaves fully emerge.

Epimedium dewuense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
