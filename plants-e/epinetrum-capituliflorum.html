Epinetrum capituliflorum

Epinetrum capituliflorum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, linear leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Epinetrum capituliflorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
