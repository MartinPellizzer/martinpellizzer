Epidendrum lagenomorphum

Epidendrum lagenomorphum, commonly known as the giant bottle orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, bottle-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice for experienced orchid growers.
