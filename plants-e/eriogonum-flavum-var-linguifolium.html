Eriogonum flavum var. linguifolium

Eriogonum flavum var. linguifolium, commonly known as the yellow flax or golden bush, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and distinctive leaf morphology.
