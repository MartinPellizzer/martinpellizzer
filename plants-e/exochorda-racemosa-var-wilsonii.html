Exochorda racemosa var. wilsonii

Exochorda racemosa var. wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's snowball bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its showy floral display.

The variety is noted for its hardiness and adaptability to a range of soil types.
