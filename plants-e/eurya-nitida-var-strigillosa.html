Eurya nitida var. strigillosa

Eurya nitida var. strigillosa, commonly known as the glossy eurya, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
