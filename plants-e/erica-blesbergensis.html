Erica blesbergensis

Erica blesbergensis, commonly known as the Blesberg heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica blesbergensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
