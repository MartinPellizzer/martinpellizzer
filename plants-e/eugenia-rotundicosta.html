Eugenia rotundicosta

Eugenia rotundicosta, commonly known as the rounded-ribbed eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with thick, leathery leaves that have a distinctive rounded ribbing. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, aromatic berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
