Encyclia oncidioides var. gravida

Encyclia oncidioides var. gravida, commonly known as the heavy-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers with yellow and brown markings. The flowers are typically borne on erect, branching inflorescences and bloom during the summer months. This species is adapted to shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
