Eria mcgregorii

Eria mcgregorii, commonly known as McGregor's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
