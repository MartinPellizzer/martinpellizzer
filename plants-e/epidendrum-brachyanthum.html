Epidendrum brachyanthum

Epidendrum brachyanthum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive orange or red lip. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum brachyanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
