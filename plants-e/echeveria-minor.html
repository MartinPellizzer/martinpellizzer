Echeveria minor

Echeveria minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaf echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Echeveria minor is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
