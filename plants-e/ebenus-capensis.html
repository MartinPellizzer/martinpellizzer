Ebenus capensis

Ebenus capensis, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and rocky areas.

Ebenus capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
