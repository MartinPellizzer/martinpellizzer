Elaeocarpus clementis var. kostermansii

Elaeocarpus clementis var. kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves and showy white flowers. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds. This species is often found in lowland and montane forests, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecological systems.
