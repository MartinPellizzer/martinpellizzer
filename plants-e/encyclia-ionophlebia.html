Encyclia ionophlebia

Encyclia ionophlebia, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia ionophlebia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
