Elsholtzia stauntonii f. albiflora

Elsholtzia stauntonii f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white to pale pink, and clustered in whorls along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
