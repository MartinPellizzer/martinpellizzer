Eritrichium laxum

Eritrichium laxum, commonly known as the lax leaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, yellowish to greenish flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Eritrichium laxum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in poor, well-drained conditions.
