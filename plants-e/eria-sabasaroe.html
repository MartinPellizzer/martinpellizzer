Eria sabasaroe

Eria sabasaroe, commonly known as the Sabasaroe orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and produces clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant's inflorescence consists of a single raceme bearing fragrant, yellow-green flowers with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
