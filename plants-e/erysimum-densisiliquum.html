Erysimum densisiliquum

Erysimum densisiliquum, commonly known as the dense silique mustard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by elongated, narrow pods called siliques. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a slightly hairy texture, and it typically grows in rocky or dry soils.

Erysimum densisiliquum is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
