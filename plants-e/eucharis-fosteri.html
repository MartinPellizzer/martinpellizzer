Eucharis fosteri

Eucharis fosteri, commonly known as the Foster's Amazonian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family (Araceae) and is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking white flowers with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the genus Eucharis. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eucharis fosteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
