Eriocaulon trimenii

Eriocaulon trimenii, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in marshy or wetland habitats, often submerged or partially submerged in water. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually arranged in dense clusters at the base. Its flowering stems are short and bear small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spikes.

Eriocaulon trimenii is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in shallow, still water environments.
