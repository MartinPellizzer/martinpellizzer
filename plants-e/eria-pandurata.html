Eria pandurata

Eria pandurata, commonly known as the orchid with the pandurata name, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are often violet or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria pandurata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
