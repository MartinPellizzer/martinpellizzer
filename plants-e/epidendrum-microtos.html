Epidendrum microtos

Epidendrum microtos, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features small, linear leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Epidendrum microtos is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
