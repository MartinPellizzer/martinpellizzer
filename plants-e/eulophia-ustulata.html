Eulophia ustulata

Eulophia ustulata, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Eulophia ustulata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
