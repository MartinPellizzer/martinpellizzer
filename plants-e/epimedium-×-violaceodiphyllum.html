Epimedium × violaceodiphyllum

Epimedium × violaceodiphyllum, commonly known as the purple-flowered bar sinister, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that appear in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant features glossy, dark green foliage with a leathery texture, which provides an ornamental effect throughout the growing season. It is typically found in woodland or shaded gardens, thriving in moist, well-drained soil.

Epimedium × violaceodiphyllum is valued for its hardiness and ability to add color to shady garden spaces.
