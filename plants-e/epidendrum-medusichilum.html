Epidendrum medusichilum

Epidendrum medusichilum, commonly known as the Medusichilum Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
