Exallage costata

Exallage costata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
