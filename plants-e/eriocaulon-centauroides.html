Eriocaulon centauroides

Eriocaulon centauroides, commonly known as the centaur's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and semi-aquatic ecosystems.
