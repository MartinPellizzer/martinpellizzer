Eustoma exaltatum f. albiflorum

Eustoma exaltatum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered lisianthus, is a hybrid cultivar of the lisianthus plant.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. The plant is a herbaceous annual with upright, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and commercial horticulture.
