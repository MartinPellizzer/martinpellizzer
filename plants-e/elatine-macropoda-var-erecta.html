Elatine macropoda var. erecta

Elatine macropoda var. erecta, commonly known as the upright water stitchwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and stream margins. The plant has a prostrate or erect growth habit, with slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is often found in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is adapted to fluctuating water levels.
