Elatostema monandrum var. zeylanicum

Elatostema monandrum var. zeylanicum, commonly known as the Zeylanic Elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive yellowish-green color.

It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
