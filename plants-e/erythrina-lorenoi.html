Erythrina lorenoi

Erythrina lorenoi, commonly known as the Lorenoi flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves composed of three to five leaflets, which are often bright green and provide a lush canopy. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by distinctive three-lobed pods. Erythrina lorenoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
