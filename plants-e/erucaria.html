Erucaria

Erucaria, commonly known as the erucaria plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. Erucaria is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate flowers and foliage.
