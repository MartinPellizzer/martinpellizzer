Epidendrum platyphylloserpens

Epidendrum platyphylloserpens, commonly known as the flat-leafed epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features broad, flat leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically borne in racemes and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

This epiphytic orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
