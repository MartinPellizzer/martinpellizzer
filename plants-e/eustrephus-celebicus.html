Eustrephus celebicus

Eustrephus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes starling, is a species of bird native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It is characterized by its striking black and white plumage, with a distinctive star-like pattern on its wings. The bird is known for its complex social behavior and vocalizations, often mimicking other sounds in its environment. It plays an important role in seed dispersal within its ecosystem.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species.
