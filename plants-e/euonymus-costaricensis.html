Euonymus costaricensis

Euonymus costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican euonymus, is a shrub native to Central America, particularly Costa Rica and Panama.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, followed by orange to red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
