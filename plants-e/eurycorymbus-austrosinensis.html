Eurycorymbus austrosinensis

Eurycorymbus austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese sandthorn, is a shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, orange fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
