Erica priori

Erica priori, commonly known as the common heather, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of western Asia.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes many shrubs and evergreen plants adapted to acidic soils. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, and they bloom in late summer to early autumn.
