Epiphyllum guedneyrii

Epiphyllum guedneyrii, commonly known as the night blooming cereus, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is a climbing cactus that produces large, showy flowers that open at night. Native to Mexico and parts of Central America, it thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant has flat, leaf-like stems that help it climb and store water.

Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance.
