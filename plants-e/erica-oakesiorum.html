Erica oakesiorum

Erica oakesiorum, commonly known as the Oakesii heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica oakesiorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
