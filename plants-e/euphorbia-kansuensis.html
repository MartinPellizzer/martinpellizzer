Euphorbia kansuensis

Euphorbia kansuensis, commonly known as the Kansu spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Euphorbia kansuensis is often found in rocky soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
