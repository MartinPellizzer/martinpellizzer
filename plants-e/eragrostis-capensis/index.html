Eragrostis Capensis

Eragrostis capensis is a hardy grass species native to southern Africa. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a common sight in savannas and rocky soils. This plant is known for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions. Its tufted growth habit and distinctive seed heads make it easily recognizable. Eragrostis capensis plays an important role in local ecosystems and is often used in landscaping for its resilience and ornamental value.
