Eriosema pilosum

Eriosema pilosum, commonly known as the hairy eriosema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, glandular leaves that are typically alternate and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in late summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Eriosema pilosum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
