Euonymus cochinchinensis

Euonymus cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese spindle tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cercidiphyllaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall.
