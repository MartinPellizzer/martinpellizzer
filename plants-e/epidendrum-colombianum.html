Epidendrum colombianum

Epidendrum colombianum, commonly known as the Colombian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features long, arching stems that bear numerous showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Epidendrum colombianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
