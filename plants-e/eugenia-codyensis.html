Eugenia codyensis

Eugenia codyensis, commonly known as the Cody's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Eugenia codyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
