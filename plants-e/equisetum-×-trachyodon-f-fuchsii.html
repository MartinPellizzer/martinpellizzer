Equisetum × trachyodon f. fuchsii

Equisetum × trachyodon f. fuchsii, commonly known as the Fuchs' horsetail, is a hybrid fern-like plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, jointed stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces spore-bearing cones at the tips of its stems, which are a key feature in its reproductive cycle. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ancient lineage and unique morphology make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
