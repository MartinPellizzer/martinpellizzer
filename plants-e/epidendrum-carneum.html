Epidendrum carneum

Epidendrum carneum, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems covered with fleshy, ovate leaves that are often reddish at the base. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with flowers that are typically pink to red in color, often with a white or yellow throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum carneum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
