Elsota lamarckii

Elsota lamarckii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Elsota lamarckii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
