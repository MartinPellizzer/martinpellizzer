Epidendrum tenuissimum

Epidendrum tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender leaved epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
