Elatine glaziovii

Elatine glaziovii, commonly known as the Mexican water starwort, is a small aquatic or semiaquatic plant native to South America.

It typically grows in shallow water bodies and moist soils, often in shaded environments. The plant has small, delicate leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern along its stems. Its flowers are tiny and clustered, producing small, inconspicuous fruits.

Elatine glaziovii is often found in wetlands, streams, and along the edges of ponds.
