Epilobium molle var. sabulonense

Epilobium molle var. sabulonense, commonly known as sandstem willow-herb, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils. The plant features slender, branching stems and produces showy, pink to red flowers in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, oval, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is often found in open habitats such as dunes, roadsides, and riverbanks.
