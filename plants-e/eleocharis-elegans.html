Eleocharis elegans

Eleocharis elegans, commonly known as slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often covered in fine hairs. Its rhizomes help it spread and establish dense colonies in waterlogged soils.

Eleocharis elegans plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
