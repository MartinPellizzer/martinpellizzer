Euphrasia × pratiuscola

Euphrasia × pratiuscola, commonly known as the hybrid eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish or brownish flowers that resemble eyes, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, woodlands, and stream banks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem.

Euphrasia × pratiuscola is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported soothing properties.
