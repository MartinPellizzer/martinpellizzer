Epidendrum trachypentatropis

Epidendrum trachypentatropis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

Epidendrum trachypentatropis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
