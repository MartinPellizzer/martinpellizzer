Esenbeckia febrifuga var. densiflora

Esenbeckia febrifuga var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered feverfew, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical features make it distinctive within its native ecosystems.
