Echeveria gudeliana

Echeveria gudeliana, commonly known as the Silver Cloud Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
