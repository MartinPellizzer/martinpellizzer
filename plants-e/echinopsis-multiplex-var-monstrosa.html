Echinopsis multiplex var. monstrosa

Echinopsis multiplex var. monstrosa, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a unique hybrid of the genus Echinopsis.

It is characterized by its flattened, segmented stems that resemble leaves, which are often brightly colored. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It is native to the coastal regions of South America and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and seasonal blooming, it is a popular houseplant.
