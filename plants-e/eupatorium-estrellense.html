Eupatorium estrellense

Eupatorium estrellense, commonly known as Estrelle's boneset, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has erect, branching stems covered in fine hairs. Its showy white to pale blue flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a strong, aromatic scent.
