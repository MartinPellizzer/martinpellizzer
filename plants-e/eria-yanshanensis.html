Eria yanshanensis

Eria yanshanensis, commonly known as the Yanshan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, preferring moist and well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Eria yanshanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
