Eragrostis exelliana

Eragrostis exelliana, commonly known as the slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, arching blades that form a dense, low-growing mat, often creating a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Eragrostis exelliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its delicate, flowing foliage.
