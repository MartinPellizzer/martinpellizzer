Eleusine oligostachya

Eleusine oligostachya, commonly known as slender panicgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Eleusine oligostachya is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and thrive in poor soil conditions.
