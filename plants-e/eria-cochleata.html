Eria cochleata

Eria cochleata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Eria cochleata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
