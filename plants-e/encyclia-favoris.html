Encyclia favoris

Encyclia favoris, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, pink to purple flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Its flowers are typically short-lived but attract various pollinators.

Encyclia favoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
