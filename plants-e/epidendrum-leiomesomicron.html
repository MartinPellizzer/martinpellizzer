Epidendrum leiomesomicron

Epidendrum leiomesomicron, commonly known as the miniature epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features dense, dark green leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is characterized by its compact size and unique, narrow leaf structure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
