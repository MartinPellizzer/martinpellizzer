Erica leucosepala

Erica leucosepala, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white flowers. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is well adapted to Mediterranean climates with mild, wet winters and dry summers.

Erica leucosepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
