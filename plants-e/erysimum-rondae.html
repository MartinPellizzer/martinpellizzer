Erysimum rondae

Erysimum rondae, commonly known as the Rondan mustard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features linear, hairy leaves. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Erysimum rondae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
