Etlingera dekockii

Etlingera dekockii, commonly known as the Dekock's torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking red and orange inflorescences, which resemble torches, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.
