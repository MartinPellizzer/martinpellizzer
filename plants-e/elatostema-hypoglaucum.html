Elatostema hypoglaucum

Elatostema hypoglaucum, commonly known as the silverleaf stellaria, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, with white petals that attract pollinators like flies and beetles.

Elatostema hypoglaucum is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
