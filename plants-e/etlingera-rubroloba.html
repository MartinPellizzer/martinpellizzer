Etlingera rubroloba

Etlingera rubroloba, commonly known as the red-lobed torch ginger, is a species of flowering plant in the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround its small, yellow flowers, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value.
