Ethuliopsis

Ethuliopsis, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, arid environments. It produces small, toxic berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
