Epidendrum chinchaoense

Epidendrum chinchaoense, commonly known as the Chinchaoense orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually brightly colored, featuring a combination of purple, yellow, and green hues.

Epidendrum chinchaoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
