Epidendrum pampatamboense

Epidendrum pampatamboense, commonly known as the Pampatambo orchid, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of Peru.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in cool, humid environments with well-draining soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both amateur and experienced growers.
