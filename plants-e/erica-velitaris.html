Erica velitaris

Erica velitaris, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Erica velitaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
