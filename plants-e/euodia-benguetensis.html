Euodia benguetensis

Euodia benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in montane forests. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
