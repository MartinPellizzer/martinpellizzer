Eryngium campestre f. angustiinvolucratum

Eryngium campestre f. angustiinvolucratum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flowers and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is often found in sunny, well-drained environments.

This variety is distinguished by its narrower involucres compared to the typical form of Eryngium campestre.
