Echinodorus tenellus var. ecostatus

Echinodorus tenellus var. ecostatus, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. This plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light intensity and requires stable water parameters. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.

The plant is also known for its rapid growth rate and ease of propagation through stem cuttings.
