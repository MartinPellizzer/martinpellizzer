Erythranthe filicaulis

Erythranthe filicaulis, commonly known as the slender-leaved erythranthe, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with small, cruciform flowers. The plant features delicate, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
