Eugenia trukensis

Eugenia trukensis, commonly known as the Truk eugenia, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caroline Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
