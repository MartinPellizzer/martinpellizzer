Eugenia elliptica var. levinervis

Eugenia elliptica var. levinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves with prominent, narrow veins. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
