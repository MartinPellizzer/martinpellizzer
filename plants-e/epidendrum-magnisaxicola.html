Epidendrum magnisaxicola

Epidendrum magnisaxicola, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has fleshy roots and thrives in well-drained soil, often growing in rocky or sunny environments. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the year.

Epidendrum magnisaxicola is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
