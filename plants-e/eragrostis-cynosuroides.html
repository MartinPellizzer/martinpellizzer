Eragrostis cynosuroides

Eragrostis cynosuroides, commonly known as silverleaf panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous slender, two-rowed spikelets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
