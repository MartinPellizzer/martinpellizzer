Elaphoglossum urophyllum

Elaphoglossum urophyllum, commonly known as the upright holly fern, is a small, delicate fern species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The fronds are typically dark green, with a leathery texture and finely divided, serrated leaflets. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
