Eryngium Integrifolium Var. Typicum

Eryngium integrifolium var. typicum is a striking member of the carrot family known for its spiky, blue-gray foliage and showy purple flowers. Native to the western United States, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes. The plant is also valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements. It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
