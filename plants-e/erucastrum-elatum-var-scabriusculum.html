Erucastrum elatum var. scabriusculum

Erucastrum elatum var. scabriusculum, commonly known as the rough bittercress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with hairy, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and gardens.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution across various habitats.
