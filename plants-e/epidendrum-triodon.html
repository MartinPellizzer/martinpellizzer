Epidendrum triodon

Epidendrum triodon, commonly known as the Triodonic Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Epidendrum triodon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
