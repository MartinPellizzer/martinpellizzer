Echeveria setchellii

Echeveria setchellii, commonly known as the Mexican rose or sunburst echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray to silver in color, with a subtle pink or red hue on the edges. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a favored ornamental plant among succulent enthusiasts.
