Embelia penangiana

Embelia penangiana, commonly known as Penang embelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Embelia penangiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
