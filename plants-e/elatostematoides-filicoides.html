Elatostematoides filicoides

Elatostematoides filicoides, commonly known as the lace fern, is a rare and delicate tropical plant belonging to the family Elatostemataceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that resemble those of true ferns, though it is not a true fern but a member of the monocot group. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in the understory. Its leaves are usually light green and have a soft, velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
