Erigeron nematophyllus

Erigeron nematophyllus, commonly known as the narrowleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
