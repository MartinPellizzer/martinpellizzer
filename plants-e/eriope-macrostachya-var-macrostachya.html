Eriope macrostachya var. macrostachya

Eriope macrostachya var. macrostachya, commonly known as the silverleaf eriope, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage and compact growth habit.
