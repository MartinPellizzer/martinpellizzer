Epilobium roseum subsp. roseum

Epilobium roseum subsp.

roseum, commonly known as the rose marsh willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in wet or damp habitats such as marshes, wet meadows, and riverbanks.

The plant features showy pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in fine hairs, while the stems are usually reddish in color.
