Eriostemma badaensis

Eriostemma badaensis, commonly known as the Bada Island bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the island of Bada in the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within montane forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Eriostemma badaensis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and potential ecological significance.
