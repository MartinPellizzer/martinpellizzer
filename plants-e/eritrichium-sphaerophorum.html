Eritrichium sphaerophorum

Eritrichium sphaerophorum, commonly known as the globe eritrichium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments.

Its unique spherical seed capsules make it a distinctive species within the Eritrichium genus.
