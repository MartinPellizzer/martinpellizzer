Eugenioides ramosissimum

Eugenioides ramosissimum, commonly known as the ramose eugenia, is a shrub native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its dense, branched structure and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Eugenioides ramosissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
