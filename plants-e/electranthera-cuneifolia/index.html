Electranthera Cuneifolia

Electranthera cuneifolia is a striking aquatic plant known for its unique leaf shape and vibrant coloration. Native to the wetlands of South America, it thrives in shallow, nutrient-rich waters. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves often display shades of green, red, and purple, making it a popular choice for aquariums. The plant is easy to care for, requiring moderate lighting and regular water changes. Its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance make it a favorite among both novice and experienced aquatic plant enthusiasts.
