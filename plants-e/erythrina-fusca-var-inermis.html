Erythrina fusca var. inermis

Erythrina fusca var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth flame tree, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are borne in dense, racemose inflorescences. The bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ornamental value.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
