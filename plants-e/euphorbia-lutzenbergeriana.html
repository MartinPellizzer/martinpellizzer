Euphorbia lutzenbergeriana

Euphorbia lutzenbergeriana, commonly known as the Lutzenberger's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with thick, fleshy stems and small, waxy leaves. The plant produces unique, cyathium-shaped inflorescences that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and leaves.

Euphorbia lutzenbergeriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
