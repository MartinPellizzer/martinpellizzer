Emarhendia

Emarhendia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Native to South Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid climates. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
