Eragrostis ovata

Eragrostis ovata, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands, prairies, and disturbed areas.

Eragrostis ovata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
