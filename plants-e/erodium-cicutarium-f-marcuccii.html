Erodium cicutarium f. marcuccii

Erodium cicutarium f. marcuccii, commonly known as the Italian bedstraw, is a variant of the common bedstraw.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with small, pinkish to reddish flowers. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are opposite and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

The species is characterized by its hairy stems and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
