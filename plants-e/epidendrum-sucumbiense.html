Epidendrum sucumbiense

Epidendrum sucumbiense, commonly known as the Guatemalan Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers with yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum sucumbiense is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
