Elatine brochonii

Elatine brochonii, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. The plant has a prostrate growth habit with numerous small, oval leaves that are often submerged or floating. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, emerging from the water surface on slender stalks.

Elatine brochonii is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich waters and is often used as an indicator of water quality.
