Epacris × hyacinthiflora-candissima

Epacris × hyacinthiflora-candissima, commonly known as the hybrid epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the heather family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces dense clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
