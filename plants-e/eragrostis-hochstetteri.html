Eragrostis hochstetteri

Eragrostis hochstetteri, commonly known as Hochstetter's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, hairless stems that often form dense tufts, and its leaves are narrow with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence is a loose panicle, bearing small, spikelet-like flowers.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
