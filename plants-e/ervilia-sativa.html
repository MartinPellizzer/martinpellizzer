Ervilia sativa

Ervilia sativa, commonly known as the common vervain, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, two-lipped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
