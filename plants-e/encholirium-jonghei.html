Encholirium jonghei

Encholirium jonghei, commonly known as the Jonghei's grass.

It is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Honduras. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
