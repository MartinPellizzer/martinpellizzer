Epidendrum cernuum

Epidendrum cernuum, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum cernuum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
