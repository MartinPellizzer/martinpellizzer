Erythranthe × hybrida

Erythranthe × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid fireweed, is a perennial plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, blue to purple flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often found in wet meadows, stream banks, and other damp environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
