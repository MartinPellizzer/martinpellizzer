Erysimum rizeense

Erysimum rizeense, commonly known as Rize wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers and is often found growing in rocky or dry habitats. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
