Euphorbia evonymicarpa

Euphorbia evonymicarpa, commonly known as the scarlet spurge, is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant orange-red flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its milky sap and diverse morphological features. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Euphorbia evonymicarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
