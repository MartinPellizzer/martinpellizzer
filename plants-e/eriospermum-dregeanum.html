Eriospermum dregeanum

Eriospermum dregeanum, commonly known as the silverleaf eriospermum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of silvery, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid habitat. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

Eriospermum dregeanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive foliage and drought tolerance.
