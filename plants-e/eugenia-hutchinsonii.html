Eugenia hutchinsonii

Eugenia hutchinsonii, commonly known as the mountain eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the highlands of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia hutchingtonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
