Elaeodendron japonicum

Elaeodendron japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese elaeodendron, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.
