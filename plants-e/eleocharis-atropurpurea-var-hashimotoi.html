Eleocharis atropurpurea var. hashimotoi

Eleocharis atropurpurea var. hashimotoi, commonly known as the purple spike-rush, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that often display a purplish hue. It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
