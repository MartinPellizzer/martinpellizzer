Exomicrum congestum

Exomicrum congestum, commonly known as the clustered exomicrum, is a unique plant species characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It typically features small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of white and pale pink, adding a delicate floral display. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
