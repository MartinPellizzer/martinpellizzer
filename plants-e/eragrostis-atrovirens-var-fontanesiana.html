Eragrostis atrovirens var. fontanesiana

Eragrostis atrovirens var. fontanesiana, commonly known as the dark-green lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It features narrow, dark green leaves with a rough texture and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental use.
