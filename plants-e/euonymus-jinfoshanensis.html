Euonymus jinfoshanensis

Euonymus jinfoshanensis, commonly known as the Jinfoshan beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant red berries that appear in clusters, making it a visually striking plant. The leaves are typically ovate with a smooth margin and a dark green upper surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural landscapes.
