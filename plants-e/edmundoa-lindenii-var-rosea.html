Edmundoa lindenii var. rosea

Edmundoa lindenii var. rosea, commonly known as the pink-flowered orchid, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant pink or rose-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its unique coloration and elegant appearance.
