Echidnopsis × atlantica

Echidnopsis × atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic euphorbia, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
