Erigeron swatensis

Erigeron swatensis, commonly known as the Swat fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer.

Erigeron swatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
