Elytranthe sphaerocarpa

Elytranthe sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular elytranthe, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its compact, globular shape. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of tiny, inconspicuous flowers. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
