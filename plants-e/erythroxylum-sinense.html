Erythroxylum sinense

Erythroxylum sinense, commonly known as the Chinese copal tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
