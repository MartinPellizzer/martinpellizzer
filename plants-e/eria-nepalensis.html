Eria nepalensis

Eria nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal, India, and Bhutan, typically growing in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Eria nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.

Its growth habits and floral characteristics make it a unique addition to the orchid diversity of the region.
