Epidendrum nocturnum var. panamense

Epidendrum nocturnum var. panamense, commonly known as the Panamanian night orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features showy, nocturnal flowers that open at night, attracting specific pollinators. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, aiding in water retention.

The orchid's flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that enhances its attractiveness to nocturnal insects.
