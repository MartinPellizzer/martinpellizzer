Euphorbia × nyaradyana

Euphorbia × nyaradyana, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in a cyathium, which is a unique floral structure characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
