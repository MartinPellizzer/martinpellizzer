Eurycoma harmandiana var. harmandiana

Eurycoma harmandiana var. harmandiana, commonly known as the Malayan dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, forested areas and thrives in tropical climates. It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other flowering plants with similar characteristics.
