Euterpe elegans

Euterpe elegans, commonly known as the golden palm or the elegant areca, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its slender, feather-like leaves and produces small, yellow fruits that are edible. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.

The fruit, though not widely cultivated, has potential for both culinary and commercial use.
