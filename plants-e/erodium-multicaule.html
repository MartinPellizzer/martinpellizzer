Erodium multicaule

Erodium multicaule, commonly known as common stork's bill, is an annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, bean-like seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
