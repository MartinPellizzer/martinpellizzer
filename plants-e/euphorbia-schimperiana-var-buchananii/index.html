Euphorbia Schimperiana Var. Buchananii

Euphorbia schimperiana var. buchananii is a unique and striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant green stems and striking yellow sap, it adds a bold touch to any garden or indoor space. This plant is highly drought-tolerant, making it ideal for arid environments or low-maintenance collections. Its compact growth habit and ability to form dense clusters make it both ornamental and resilient. Careful handling is essential due to its toxic latex, which can cause skin irritation.
