Erica triumphans

Erica triumphans, commonly known as the evergreen heather, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of small, evergreen leaves that are dark green and leathery.

Erica triumphans thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
