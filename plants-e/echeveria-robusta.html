Echeveria robusta

Echeveria robusta, commonly known as the Mexican rose or the robust echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
