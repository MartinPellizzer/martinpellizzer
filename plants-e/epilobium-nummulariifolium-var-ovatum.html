Epilobium nummulariifolium var. ovatum

Epilobium nummulariifolium var. ovatum, commonly known as the silverleaf willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, round-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and wildlife habitat.
