Epilobium × hybridum

Epilobium × hybridum, commonly known as the common willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Epilobium, which includes various species of willowherbs. The plant typically grows to a height of 60–120 cm and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and along riverbanks.
