Euphorbia leistneri

Euphorbia leistneri, commonly known as the Leistner's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branching stem system with a distinctive, spiny appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green花朵, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Euphorbia leistneri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
