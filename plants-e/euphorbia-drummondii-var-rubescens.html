Euphorbia drummondii var. rubescens

Euphorbia drummondii var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed spurge, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features a distinctive reddish stem and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to xeriscaped gardens.
