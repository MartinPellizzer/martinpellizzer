Echinopsis Bonnieae

Echinopsis bonnieae is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. It is known for its striking white spines and spherical shape that resembles a small globe. This plant thrives in extreme desert conditions with minimal water and intense sunlight. Its name honors Bonnie, a botanist who contributed significantly to its discovery and study. Echinopsis bonnieae is not only a fascinating subject for plant enthusiasts but also an important species for ecological research.
