Eurya ochnacea var. contracta

Eurya ochnacea var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eurya, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, followed by small, leathery fruits.

The species is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
