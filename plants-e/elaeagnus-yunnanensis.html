Elaeagnus yunnanensis

Elaeagnus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan silverberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are rich in nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Elaeagnus yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
