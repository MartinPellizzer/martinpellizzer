Eupatorium leptopodum

Eupatorium leptopodum, commonly known as slender boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium leptopodum is valued in native plant gardens for its ecological benefits and ornamental appeal.
