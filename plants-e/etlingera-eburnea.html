Etlingera eburnea

Etlingera eburnea, commonly known as the white torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of large, white, fragrant flowers that resemble torches. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a glossy texture.
