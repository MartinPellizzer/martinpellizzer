Eulophia macrorhiza

Eulophia macrorhiza, commonly known as the large-ridge eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and broad, elliptical leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with numerous pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Eulophia macrorhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
