Eragrostis intermedia

Eragrostis intermedia, commonly known as intermediate bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in open areas such as prairies, fields, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, blue-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with purple. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
