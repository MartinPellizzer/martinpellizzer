Euphorbia pseudovolkensii

Euphorbia pseudovolkensii, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, ribbed stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its stems, making it drought-tolerant. Its sap contains toxic compounds, which can cause irritation if touched.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
