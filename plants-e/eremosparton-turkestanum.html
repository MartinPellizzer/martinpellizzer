Eremosparton turkestanum

Eremosparton turkestanum, commonly known as the Turkestan sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub with gray-green, succulent leaves adapted to conserve water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its harsh environment. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access moisture from deeper soil layers.

Eremosparton turkestanum plays a vital role in stabilizing sandy soils and preventing erosion in its native habitat.
