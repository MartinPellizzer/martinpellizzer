Encyclia vazzoleri

Encyclia vazzoleri, commonly known as the Vazzoler's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, spurred orchid flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, depending on the specific variety. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Encyclia vazzoleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
