Elaeagnus jiangxiensis

Elaeagnus jiangxiensis, commonly known as Jiangxi silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruit is a small, dark brown drupe that ripens in late summer.

Elaeagnus jiangxiensis is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
