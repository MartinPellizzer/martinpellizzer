Eremopappus tadshicorum

Eremopappus tadshicorum, commonly known as the Tadshik Tumbleweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, herbaceous structure with small, gray-green leaves. The plant produces fluffy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Eremopappus tadshicorum is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
