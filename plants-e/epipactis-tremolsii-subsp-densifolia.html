Epipactis tremolsii subsp. densifolia

Epipactis tremolsii subsp.

densifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and its showy, greenish-yellow flowers that often have a purple or brownish hue.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and colonize new areas.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often found in calcareous soils.
