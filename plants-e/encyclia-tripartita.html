Encyclia tripartita

Encyclia tripartita, commonly known as the three-parted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in racemes. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Encyclia tripartita thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich substrates for optimal growth.
