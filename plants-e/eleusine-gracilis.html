Eleusine gracilis

Eleusine gracilis, commonly known as slender gothic grass, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Eleusine gracilis is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and ability to form dense vegetation.
