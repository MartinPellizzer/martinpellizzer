Epidendrum oppositifolium

Epidendrum oppositifolium, commonly known as the opposite-leaf epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, alternate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum oppositifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
