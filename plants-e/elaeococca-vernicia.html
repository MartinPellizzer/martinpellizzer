Elaeococca vernicia

Elaeococca vernicia, commonly known as the Chinese elaeocarp, is a flowering plant belonging to the Elaeocarpaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically reddish or black when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Elaeococca vernicia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
