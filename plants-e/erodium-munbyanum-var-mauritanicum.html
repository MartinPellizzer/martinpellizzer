Erodium munbyanum var. mauritanicum

Erodium munbyanum var. mauritanicum, commonly known as the Mauritania catchfly, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize disturbed soils.

This species is adapted to thrive in hot, dry climates with minimal water availability.
