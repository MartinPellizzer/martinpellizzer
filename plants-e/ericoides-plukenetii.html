Ericoides plukenetii

Ericoides plukenetii, commonly known as the Plukenet's heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and evergreen foliage, typically featuring small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink or white flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Ericoides plukenetii is well adapted to cool, moist climates and is relatively slow-growing.
