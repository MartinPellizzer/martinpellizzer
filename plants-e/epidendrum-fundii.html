Epidendrum fundii

Epidendrum fundii, commonly known as the pink-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces long, pendulous inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are showy, with a pinkish hue and a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Epidendrum fundii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
