Eupatorium ternatum

Eupatorium ternatum, commonly known as boneset, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Boneset is commonly found in moist meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
