Erythrodes valida

Erythrodes valida, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive red or purple stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Erythrodes valida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
