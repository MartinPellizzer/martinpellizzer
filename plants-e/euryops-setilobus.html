Euryops setilobus

Euryops setilobus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed bushveld daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Euryops setilobus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
