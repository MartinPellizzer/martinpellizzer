Eupatorium macrophyllum

Eupatorium macrophyllum, commonly known as giant thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its showy foliage and flowers make it a notable plant in ecological and ornamental contexts.
