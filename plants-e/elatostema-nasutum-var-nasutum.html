Elatostema nasutum var. nasutum

Elatostema nasutum var. nasutum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elatostema, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a distinctive, hairy stem. Its flowers are tiny and clustered in inflorescences, often greenish-white in color.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
