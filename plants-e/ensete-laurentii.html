Ensete laurentii

Ensete laurentii, commonly known as the African banana or giant bamboo, is a large, herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that resemble bamboo. The plant produces large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It is cultivated for its edible pseudostem and has cultural significance in many African communities.
