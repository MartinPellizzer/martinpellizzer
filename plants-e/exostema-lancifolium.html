Exostema lancifolium

Exostema lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf exostema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Exostema lancifolium is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
