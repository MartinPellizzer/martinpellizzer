Echeveria pfersdorffii

Echeveria pfersdorffii, commonly known as the Rosy Red Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pink to red in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact size and vibrant color make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
