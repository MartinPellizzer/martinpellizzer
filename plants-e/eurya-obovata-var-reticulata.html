Eurya obovata var. reticulata

Eurya obovata var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate evergreen holly, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a reticulate venation pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
