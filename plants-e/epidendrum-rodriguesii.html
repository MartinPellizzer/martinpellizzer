Epidendrum rodriguesii

Epidendrum rodriguesii, commonly known as the Rodrigues's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and produces striking, showy inflorescences with large, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and can bloom multiple times throughout the year.

Epidendrum rodriguesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
