Eria ventricosa var. benguetensis

Eria ventricosa var. benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high rainfall. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
