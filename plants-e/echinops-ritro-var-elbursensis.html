Echinops ritro var. elbursensis

Echinops ritro var. elbursensis, commonly known as the Elburs thistle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical seed heads that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant features a robust, branched stem covered in coarse hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough, serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environmental conditions.
