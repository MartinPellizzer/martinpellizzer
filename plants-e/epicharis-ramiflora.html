Epicharis ramiflora

Epicharis ramiflora, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Epicharis ramiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
