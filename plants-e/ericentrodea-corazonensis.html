Ericentrodea corazonensis

Ericentrodea corazonensis, commonly known as the Heartleaf Ericentrode, is a unique plant species characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and compact growth habit.

It belongs to the Ericentrodeaceae family and is native to specific regions with moist, acidic soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, adding to its ornamental value. Its foliage is dense and evergreen, making it a desirable choice for shaded garden spaces.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
