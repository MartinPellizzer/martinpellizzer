Eugenia argyrocalyx

Eugenia argyrocalyx, commonly known as the silverleaf eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
