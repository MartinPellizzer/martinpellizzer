Erysimum maremmanum

Erysimum maremmanum, commonly known as the Maremma wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, particularly in coastal areas.

Erysimum maremmanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
