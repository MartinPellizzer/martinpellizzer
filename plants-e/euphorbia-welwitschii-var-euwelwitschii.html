Euphorbia welwitschii var. euwelwitschii

Euphorbia welwitschii var. euwelwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and a spiky, branched structure that resembles a tree. The plant produces yellowish-green花朵 with a unique, spiky center, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its stems for survival.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
