Epidendrum haematanthum

Epidendrum haematanthum, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, velvety red-tipped leaves that contrast with its deep green foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum haematanthum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
