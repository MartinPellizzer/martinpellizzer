Epidendrum schimperi

Epidendrum schimperi, commonly known as the Schimper's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine roots. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in a loose inflorescence.

Epidendrum schimperi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
