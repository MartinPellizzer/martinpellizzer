Erica nivea

Erica nivea, commonly known as the snow heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Erica nivea thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded locations.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
