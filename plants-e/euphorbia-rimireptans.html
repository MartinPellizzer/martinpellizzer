Euphorbia rimireptans

Euphorbia rimireptans, commonly known as the red-tipped euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem covered in spiny, tuberculate structures and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.

Euphorbia rimireptans is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
