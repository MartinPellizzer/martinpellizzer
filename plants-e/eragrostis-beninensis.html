Eragrostis beninensis

Eragrostis beninensis, commonly known as Benin grass, is a species of grass native to West Africa.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in poor soil, making it a resilient species.

Botanically, it is part of the Poaceae family, which includes many other grasses.
