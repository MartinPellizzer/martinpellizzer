Echinodorus macrophyllus var. muricatus

Echinodorus macrophyllus var. muricatus, commonly known as the African sword plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize substrates in aquariums and natural waterways. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular nutrient supplementation.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological applications.
