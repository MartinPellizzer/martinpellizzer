Echiochilon chazaliei

Echiochilon chazaliei, commonly known as the Chazal's echinochloa, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Kenya and Tanzania.
