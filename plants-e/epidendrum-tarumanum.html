Epidendrum tarumanum

Epidendrum tarumanum, commonly known as the Tarumanum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored blooms that range from white to pink and purple. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it has long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Epidendrum tarumanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
