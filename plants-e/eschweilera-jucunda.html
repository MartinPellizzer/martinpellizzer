Eschweilera jucunda

Eschweilera jucunda, commonly known as the pink star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that resemble stars, hence its common name. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eschweilera jucunda is a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
