Evolvulus boliviensis

Evolvulus boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. The plant has a trailing growth habit and produces small, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its flowers open in the morning and close by midday, hence its common name.
