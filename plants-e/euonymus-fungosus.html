Euonymus fungosus

Euonymus fungosus, commonly known as the fungal euonymus, is a unique species within the Euonymus genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which often exhibits a fungal-like appearance due to its pale, almost translucent leaves. The plant is native to specific regions in eastern Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow, and it produces unique, inflated seed pods that resemble gourds.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
