Eragrostis castellaneana

Eragrostis castellaneana, commonly known as the Castellane's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Eragrostis castellaneana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
