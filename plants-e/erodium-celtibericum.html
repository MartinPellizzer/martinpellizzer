Erodium celtibericum

Erodium celtibericum, commonly known as the Iberian bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It is a small annual herb with slender stems and tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom from spring to summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and disturbed areas.

Botanically, it is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
