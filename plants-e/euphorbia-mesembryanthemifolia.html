Euphorbia mesembryanthemifolia

Euphorbia mesembryanthemifolia, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, spiky, red bracts that resemble a lion's tail, giving it its common name. The plant has a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, typical of succulents.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
