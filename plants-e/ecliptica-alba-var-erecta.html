Ecliptica alba var. erecta

Ecliptica alba var. erecta, commonly known as the white eclipta, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
