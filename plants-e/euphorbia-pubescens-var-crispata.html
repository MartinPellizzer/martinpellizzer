Euphorbia pubescens var. crispata

Euphorbia pubescens var. crispata, commonly known as the curly euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, fleshy, green stems with a waxy texture and a unique, curly growth pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its specialized inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its ornamental appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
