Epilobium cockayneanum

Epilobium cockayneanum, commonly known as the purple willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
