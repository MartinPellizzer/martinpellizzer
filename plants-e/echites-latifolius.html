Echites latifolius

Echites latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf echites, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.
