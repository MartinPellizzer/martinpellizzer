Eritrichium gracile

Eritrichium gracile, commonly known as the slender eritrichium, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically reaches heights of only a few centimeters, with slender stems and small, delicate flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features basal leaves that are often hairy and have a linear to lanceolate shape. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with five petals and a distinctive, elongated spur.

Eritrichium gracile thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
