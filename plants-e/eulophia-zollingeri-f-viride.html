Eulophia zollingeri f. viride

Eulophia zollingeri f. viride, commonly known as the green-flowered eulophia, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and dark green foliage. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers that lack the typical vivid colors of many orchids. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil with adequate light exposure.

This cultivar is valued for its unique coloration and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal.
