Eupatorium steviifolium var. itatiaiae

Eupatorium steviifolium var. itatiaiae, commonly known as Itatiaia eupatorium, is a perennial herb native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
