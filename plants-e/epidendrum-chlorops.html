Epidendrum chlorops

Epidendrum chlorops, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant has slender, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are a vibrant green. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with pale green petals and a yellowish lip.

Epidendrum chlorops is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
