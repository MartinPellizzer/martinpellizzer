Encyclia brassavolae

Encyclia brassavolae, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Encyclia brassavolae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
