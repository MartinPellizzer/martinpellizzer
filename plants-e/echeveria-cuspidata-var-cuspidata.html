Echeveria cuspidata var. cuspidata

Echeveria cuspidata var. cuspidata, commonly known as the Pointed Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with sharp, pointed tips. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of pink or purple flowers in the spring. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
