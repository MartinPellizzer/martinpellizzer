Elaeocarpus angustipes

Elaeocarpus angustipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that are typically covered in a thick, fleshy pericarp. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
