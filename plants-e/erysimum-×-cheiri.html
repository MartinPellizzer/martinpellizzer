Erysimum × cheiri

Erysimum × cheiri, commonly known as candytuft, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has linear, grass-like leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive, long-lasting blooms.
