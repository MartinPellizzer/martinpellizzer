Euphoria rimosa

Euphoria rimosa, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and a tall, straight trunk that can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant is known for producing latex, which has been used historically in the rubber industry. Its leaves are typically large, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Euphoria rimosa thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
