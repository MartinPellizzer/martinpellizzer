Epidendrum leiobolbon

Epidendrum leiobolbon, commonly known as the velvet epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is an epiphytic orchid, often growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both collectors and gardeners.
