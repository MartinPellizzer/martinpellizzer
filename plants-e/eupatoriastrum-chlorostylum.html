Eupatoriastrum chlorostylum

Eupatoriastrum chlorostylum, commonly known as the green-stem eupatoria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that are often tinged with a bluish hue. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatoriastrum chlorostylum is typically found in grasslands and rocky outcrops, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
