Epipactis viridiflora subsp. rechingeri

Epipactis viridiflora subsp.

rechingeri, commonly known as the green-flowered helleborine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and is typically found in moist, shaded habitats.

The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. Its leaves are broad and lanceolate, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and is often associated with calcareous soils.
