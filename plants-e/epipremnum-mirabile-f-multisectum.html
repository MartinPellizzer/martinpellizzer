Epipremnum mirabile f. multisectum

Epipremnum mirabile f. multisectum, commonly known as the variegated pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of light green and white patterns. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail from shelves and hanging baskets.

Its foliage is often used in interior design for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
