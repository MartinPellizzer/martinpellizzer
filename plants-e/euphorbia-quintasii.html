Euphorbia quintasii

Euphorbia quintasii, commonly known as the Quintas's spurge, is a succulent shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It features a compact, multi-stemmed growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its stems and leaves.

Euphorbia quintasii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
