Euphrasia officinalis var. vulgaris

Euphrasia officinalis var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in spikes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, downy hairs.

Euphrasia officinalis var. vulgaris is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies for eye conditions.
