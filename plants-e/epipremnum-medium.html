Epipremnum medium

Epipremnum medium, commonly known as the pothos plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that vary in color from solid green to variegated patterns of yellow and white. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and is known for its ability to purify indoor air. Its rapid growth rate makes it popular as a houseplant, often trained to climb supports or allowed to trail from hanging baskets.

Epipremnum medium is also valued for its resilience and ease of care, making it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
