Encyclia macrochila

Encyclia macrochila, commonly known as the large-flowered encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. The plant typically features broad, strap-like leaves and blooms during the warmer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia macrochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
