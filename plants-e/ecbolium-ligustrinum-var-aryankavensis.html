Ecbolium ligustrinum var. aryankavensis

Ecbolium ligustrinum var. aryankavensis, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a milky sap, which is the source of its common name. Its leaves are alternate and oval-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
