Erica banksii

Erica banksii, commonly known as the Cape heath or narrow-leaf heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica banksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
