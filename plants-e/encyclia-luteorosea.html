Encyclia luteorosea

Encyclia luteorosea, commonly known as the yellow rose orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and orange floral coloration, which is particularly vibrant during its flowering season. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant flowers, each featuring a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns. Encyclia luteorosea thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
