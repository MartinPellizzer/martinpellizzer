Eulophia hastata

Eulophia hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of white or pale yellow, and are known for their delicate appearance.

Eulophia hastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
