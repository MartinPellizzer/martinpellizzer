Eugeniopsis paniculata

Eugeniopsis paniculata, commonly known as the panicled eugeniopsis, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically eaten by birds and small mammals.
