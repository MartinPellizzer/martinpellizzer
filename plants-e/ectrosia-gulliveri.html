Ectrosia gulliveri

Ectrosia gulliveri, commonly known as the Gulliver's spider plant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to the remote island of Gulliver's Isle.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped inflorescence that resembles a miniature whirlpool, with small, white, star-shaped flowers arranged in a helical pattern. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has adapted to the specific microclimate of its native habitat. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curled, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, Ectrosia gulliveri is considered a botanical curiosity and a subject of scientific interest.
