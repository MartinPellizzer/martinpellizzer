Echinocactus Ingens F. Viznaga

The echinocactus ingens f. viznaga is a rare and striking variant of the hedgehog cactus native to northern mexico. it is known for its tall, columnar stems and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a favorite among cactus enthusiasts. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and sometimes endangered species in its native habitat.
