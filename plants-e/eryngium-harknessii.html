Eryngium harknessii

Eryngium harknessii, commonly known as Harkness' thistle, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and spiny foliage. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often covered in stiff bristles.

Eryngium harknessii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native pollinators.
