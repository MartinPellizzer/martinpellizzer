Epidendrum aristisepalum

Epidendrum aristisepalum, commonly known as the feathered lip orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, linear leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive, feather-like lip. Its inflorescence is often racemose, bearing multiple blooms in a loose cluster.

Epidendrum aristisepalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
