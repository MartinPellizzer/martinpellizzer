Eupatorium alternifolium subf. glabratum

Eupatorium alternifolium subf.

glabratum, commonly known as the smoothleaf boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States. It features aromatic, opposite leaves that are typically smooth or slightly hairy, depending on the subspecies.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
