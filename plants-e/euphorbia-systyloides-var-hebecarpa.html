Euphorbia systyloides var. hebecarpa

Euphorbia systyloides var. hebecarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spurge, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and branching stems covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are narrow and linear, with a distinctive blue-green coloration. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by specialized bracts.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
