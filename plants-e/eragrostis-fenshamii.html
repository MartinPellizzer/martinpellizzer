Eragrostis fenshamii

Eragrostis fenshamii, commonly known as the Fensham's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native ecosystems in its range.
