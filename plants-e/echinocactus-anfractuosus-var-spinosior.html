Echinocactus anfractuosus var. spinosior

Echinocactus anfractuosus var. spinosior, commonly known as the Spiny Echinocactus, is a succulent cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that are covered in sharp, spiny areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
