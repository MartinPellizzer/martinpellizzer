Exacum pumilum

Exacum pumilum, commonly known as dwarf African violet, is a small, evergreen perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the year under proper conditions. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and continuous flowering make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
