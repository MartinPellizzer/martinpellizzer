Epidendrum meliosmum

Epidendrum meliosmum, commonly known as the orchid of the forest, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow, elongated shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum meliosmum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
