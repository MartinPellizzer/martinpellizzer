Eurya pubicalyx

Eurya pubicalyx, commonly known as the silverleaf eurya, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. Eurya pubicalyx thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in temperate regions.
