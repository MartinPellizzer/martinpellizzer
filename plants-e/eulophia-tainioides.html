Eulophia tainioides

Eulophia tainioides, commonly known as the Tainoiid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust rhizome and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with purple or brown markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia tainioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
