Eriogonum Villiflorum Var. Typicum

Eriogonum villiflorum var. typicum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western united states. it is commonly known as the coastal buckwheat and thrives in coastal scrub habitats. this species is characterized by its finely divided leaves and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecosystem balance. due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival.
