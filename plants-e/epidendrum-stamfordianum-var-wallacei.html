Epidendrum stamfordianum var. wallacei

Epidendrum stamfordianum var. wallacei, commonly known as the Wallace's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and lush, dark green leaves.
