Elaeagnus stellipila

Elaeagnus stellipila, commonly known as the star-leaved elaeagnus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Elaeagnus stellipila is also valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various climates.
