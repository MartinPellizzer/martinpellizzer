Echidnopsis planiflora subsp. chrysantha

Echidnopsis planiflora subsp.

chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow pincushion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, yellowish-orange spherical inflorescence, which resembles a pincushion.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is a succulent species, adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy leaves that store water.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
