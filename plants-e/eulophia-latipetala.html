Eulophia latipetala

Eulophia latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia latipetala is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
