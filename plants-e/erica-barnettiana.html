Erica barnettiana

Erica barnettiana, commonly known as the Cape fynbos heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of the region.

Erica barnettiana is often found in coastal and mountainous areas, where it plays a significant role in local ecosystems.
