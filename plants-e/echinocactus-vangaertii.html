Echinocactus vangaertii

Echinocactus vangaertii, commonly known as the Vangaertii cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a solitary, columnar cactus with a cylindrical stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant features prominent areoles covered with white, glochids, which are small, barbed spines. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles during the spring and summer seasons.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
