Euphorbia cylindrifolia var. tuberifera

Euphorbia cylindrifolia var. tuberifera, commonly known as the tuberous euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it has a distinctive tuberous root system.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
