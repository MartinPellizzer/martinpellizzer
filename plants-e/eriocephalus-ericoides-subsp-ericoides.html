Eriocephalus ericoides subsp. ericoides

Eriocephalus ericoides subsp.

ericoides, commonly known as the coastal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form with dense, leathery leaves.
