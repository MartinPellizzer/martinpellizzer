Eriachne ovata var. pallida

Eriachne ovata var. pallida, commonly known as the pale eriachne, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The inflorescence consists of a spikelet cluster, with small, pale blue to white flowers. This variety is typically found in sandy or rocky soils in arid and semi-arid regions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
