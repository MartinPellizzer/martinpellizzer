Eugenia prasina

Eugenia prasina, commonly known as the green eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional cuisine and herbal remedies. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.

Eugenia prasina is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
