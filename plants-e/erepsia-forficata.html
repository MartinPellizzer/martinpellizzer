Erepsia forficata

Erepsia forficata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ericoid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves that are typically narrow and leathery. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Erepsia forficata is a hardy species that is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
