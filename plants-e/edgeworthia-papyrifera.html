Edgeworthia papyrifera

Edgeworthia papyrifera, commonly known as the paper lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, papery, lantern-shaped flower clusters that appear in early spring. The plant belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is valued for its ornamental foliage and unique floral display. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
