Eichwaldia oxiana

Eichwaldia oxiana, commonly known as the Oxian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Eichwaldia oxiana is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and drought conditions.
