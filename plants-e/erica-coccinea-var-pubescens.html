Erica coccinea var. pubescens

Erica coccinea var. pubescens, commonly known as the red heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are small, leathery, and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
