Elodes japonica

Elodes japonica, commonly known as Japanese waterweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It has slender, branching stems that are typically green to reddish in color. Its leaves are long and ribbon-like, often arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Elodes japonica is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes.
