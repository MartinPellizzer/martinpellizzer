Erigeron Alpinus Var. Oreades

Erigeron alpinus var. oreades is a striking alpine plant known for its delicate blue to purple flowers and finely divided leaves. Native to mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This variant of the common erigeron is often found in open meadows and scree slopes at high elevations. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in less-than-ideal conditions.
