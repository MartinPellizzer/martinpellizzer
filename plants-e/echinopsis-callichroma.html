Echinopsis callichroma

Echinopsis callichroma, commonly known as the Moon Cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stem and spiny exterior, which help it survive in arid environments. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental value. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ease of care and unique appearance.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts and desert gardeners alike.
