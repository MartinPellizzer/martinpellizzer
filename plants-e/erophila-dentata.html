Erophila dentata

Erophila dentata, commonly known as the toothed erophila, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny habitats. The plant features small, narrow leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are usually pale blue or purple in color. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Erophila dentata is known for its drought tolerance and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
