Elaeocarpus timikensis

Elaeocarpus timikensis, commonly known as the Timikensis elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruits are woody, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Elaeocarpus timikensis is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological importance.
