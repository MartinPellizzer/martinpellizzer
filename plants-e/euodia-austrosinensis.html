Euodia austrosinensis

Euodia austrosinensis, commonly known as the Chinese laurustin, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The tree prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for subtropical climates.
