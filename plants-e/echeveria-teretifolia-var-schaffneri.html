Echeveria teretifolia var. schaffneri

Echeveria teretifolia var. schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's stone rose, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy, blue-green to silver-gray leaves that form a rosette shape, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces striking clusters of pink to violet flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container cultivation due to its drought tolerance and minimal care requirements.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
