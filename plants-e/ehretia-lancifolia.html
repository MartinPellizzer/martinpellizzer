Ehretia lancifolia

Ehretia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ehretia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Ehretia lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
