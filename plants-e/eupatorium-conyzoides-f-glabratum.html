Eupatorium conyzoides f. glabratum

Eupatorium conyzoides f. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth blueweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowering heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are usually hairy.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive flowers.
