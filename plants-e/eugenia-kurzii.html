Eugenia kurzii

Eugenia kurzii, commonly known as the Kurz's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink, and it bears edible, dark purple to black berries. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a leathery texture.

Eugenia kurzii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit and medicinal properties.
