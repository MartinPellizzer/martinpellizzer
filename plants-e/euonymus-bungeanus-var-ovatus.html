Euonymus bungeanus var. ovatus

Euonymus bungeanus var. ovatus, commonly known as the ovate euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
