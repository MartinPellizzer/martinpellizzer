Eupatorium graciliflorum

Eupatorium graciliflorum, commonly known as slenderflower thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white to pale blue, and are arranged in dense, spherical clusters called heads.

It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its aromatic foliage.
