Epipactis helleborine var. viridens

Epipactis helleborine var. viridens, commonly known as the green helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a tall, unbranched stem that bears a single spike of greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. It produces a distinctive, spurred orchid flower with a labellum that is often green or yellowish.

Epipactis helleborine var. viridens is a perennial species that thrives in cool, humid conditions and is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora.
