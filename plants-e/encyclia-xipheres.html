Encyclia xipheres

Encyclia xipheres, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia xipheres is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
