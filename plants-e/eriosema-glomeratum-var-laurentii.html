Eriosema glomeratum var. laurentii

Eriosema glomeratum var. laurentii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are hairy and oval-shaped, while the stems are slender and often branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
