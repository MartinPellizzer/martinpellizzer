Erythronium dens-canis var. japonicum

Erythronium dens-canis var. japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese dog's tooth violet, is a bulbous perennial plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale yellow, often with a reddish tinge. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
