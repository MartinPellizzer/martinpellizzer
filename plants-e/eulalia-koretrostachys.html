Eulalia koretrostachys

Eulalia koretrostachys, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, arching stems. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that give it a distinctive, ethereal appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its visual appeal.
