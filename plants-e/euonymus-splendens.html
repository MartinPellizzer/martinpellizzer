Euonymus splendens

Euonymus splendens, commonly known as the shining euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright orange-red berries. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for gardeners.
