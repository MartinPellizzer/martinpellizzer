Epidendrum pseudocernuum

Epidendrum pseudocernuum, commonly known as the Showy Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Epidendrum pseudocernuum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
