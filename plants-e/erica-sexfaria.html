Erica sexfaria

Erica sexfaria, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in spring.

Erica sexfaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
