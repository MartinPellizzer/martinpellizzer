Encyclia xuxiana

Encyclia xuxiana, commonly known as the Xuxa orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple lip. Its pseudobulbs are short and thick, supporting its evergreen foliage.

Encyclia xuxiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
