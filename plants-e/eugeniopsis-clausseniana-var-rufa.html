Eugeniopsis clausseniana var. rufa

Eugeniopsis clausseniana var. rufa, commonly known as the red-flowered eugeniopsis, is a shrub native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in understorey layers of dense vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
