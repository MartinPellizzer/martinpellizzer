Erigeron thunbergii subsp. angustifolius

Erigeron thunbergii subsp.

angustifolius, commonly known as narrowleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America. It is a perennial herb with hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often grayish-green in color.

The plant produces daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

Its foliage and floral structure make it a valuable species for pollinators and ecological restoration projects.
