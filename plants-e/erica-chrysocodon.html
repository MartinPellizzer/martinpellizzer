Erica chrysocodon

Erica chrysocodon, commonly known as the yellow heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica chrysocodon is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
