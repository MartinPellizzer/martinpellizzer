Eulophia longipes

Eulophia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Eulophia longipes thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
