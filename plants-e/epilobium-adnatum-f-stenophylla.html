Epilobium adnatum f. stenophylla

Epilobium adnatum f. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaved willow herb, is a botanical variant of the willow herb species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy pink to purple flowers. This subspecies is typically found in moist, often calcareous soils in wetlands or along stream banks. It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
