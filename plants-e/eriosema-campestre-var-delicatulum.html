Eriosema campestre var. delicatulum

Eriosema campestre var. delicatulum, commonly known as the silky eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems covered in soft, white hairs, giving it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also bear a fine, silky coating.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
