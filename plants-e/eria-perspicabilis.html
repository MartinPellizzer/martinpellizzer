Eria perspicabilis

Eria perspicabilis, commonly known as the transparent orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent petals that reveal a vibrant inner color when light passes through them. Native to Southeast Asia, this orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments within the forest understory. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pink.

Due to its striking appearance, Eria perspicabilis is a sought-after species in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.
