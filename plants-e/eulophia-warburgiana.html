Eulophia warburgiana

Eulophia warburgiana, commonly known as the Warburg's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, dark green leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip that adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
