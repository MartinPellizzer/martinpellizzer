Eutassa luxurians

Eutassa luxurians, commonly known as the red-flowered spider orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and its showy, red-brown flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant produces intricate, spiral-shaped inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
