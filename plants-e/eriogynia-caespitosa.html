Eriogynia caespitosa

Eriogynia caespitosa, commonly known as the clustered eriogynia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in wet, swampy areas and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

Eriogynia caespitosa is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
