Echinopsis candicans var. tenuispina

Echinopsis candicans var. tenuispina, commonly known as the slender-spined moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stem and small, spiny areoles. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
