Eugenia sexangulata

Eugenia sexangulata, commonly known as the six-angled eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its fragrant white flowers are typically clustered in small inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, round berry that changes color from green to red as it matures.
