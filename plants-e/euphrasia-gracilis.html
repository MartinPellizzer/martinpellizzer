Euphrasia gracilis

Euphrasia gracilis, commonly known as slender eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Euphrasia gracilis is often found in association with other woodland flora and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
