Epidendrum manarae

Epidendrum manarae, commonly known as the Manarae orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Epidendrum manarae is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
