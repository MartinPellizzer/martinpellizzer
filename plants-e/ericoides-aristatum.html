Ericoides aristatum

Ericoides aristatum, commonly known as the tall heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly in California and Oregon, where it thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its low-growing shrub form, with evergreen leaves that are narrow and leathery. Its flowers are typically small, bell-shaped, and appear in clusters, often in shades of pink or white.
