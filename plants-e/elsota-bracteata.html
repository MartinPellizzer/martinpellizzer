Elsota bracteata

Elsota bracteata, commonly known as the golden bract fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that emit a subtle, sweet fragrance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by ornamental, papery bracts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.
