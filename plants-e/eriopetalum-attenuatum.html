Eriopetalum attenuatum

Eriopetalum attenuatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriopetalum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
