Echinopsilon stelligerus

Echinopsilon stelligerus, commonly known as the starry sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with silvery-gray leaves covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime climates.

Echinopsilon stelligerus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in coastal gardens.
