Eutriana polyodon

Eutriana polyodon, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant yellow, star-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in rocky crevices. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.

The fronds are typically lanceolate and emit a subtle fragrance when touched.
