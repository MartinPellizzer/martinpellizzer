Eria compacta

Eria compacta, commonly known as the compact eria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria compacta is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental flowers and ease of care.
