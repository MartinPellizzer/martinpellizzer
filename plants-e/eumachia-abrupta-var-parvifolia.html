Eumachia abrupta var. parvifolia

Eumachia abrupta var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eumachia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length.
