Epidendrum vigosi

Epidendrum vigosi, commonly known as the Crimson Lip Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features striking, bright red or orange-red flowers with a contrasting white lip, making it a visually appealing species. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a thick layer of white velvety pubescence.

Epidendrum vigosi is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
