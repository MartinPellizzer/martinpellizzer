Erica cedromontana

Erica cedromontana, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Erica cedromontana thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
