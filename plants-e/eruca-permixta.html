Eruca permixta

Eruca permixta, commonly known as the mixed garden cress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants like broccoli and mustard. The plant features small, yellow flowers and has pinnately divided leaves that are often used in culinary applications. Eruca permixta is typically found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.

Its seeds are edible and can be used to add a peppery flavor to dishes.
