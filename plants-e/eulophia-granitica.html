Eulophia granitica

Eulophia granitica, commonly known as the granite orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the warmer months. Eulophia granitica thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique habitat and floral structure make it a distinctive species within the orchid family.
