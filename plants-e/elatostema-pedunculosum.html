Elatostema pedunculosum

Elatostema pedunculosum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a short, upright stem with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Elatostema pedunculosum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
