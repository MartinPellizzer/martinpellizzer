Espeletia ruizii

Espeletia ruizii, commonly known as the Ruiz's espeletia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with dense, woolly leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to purple capitula, which are composed of many small florets. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats.

Espeletia ruizii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in cloud forests and paramo ecosystems.
