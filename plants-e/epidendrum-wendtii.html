Epidendrum wendtii

Epidendrum wendtii, commonly known as the Wendt's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple stems that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged spirally along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings, appearing in clusters at the tips of the stems.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.
