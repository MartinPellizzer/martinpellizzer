Escallonia × exoniensis

Escallonia × exoniensis, commonly known as the Exeter escallonia, is a hybrid shrub native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden borders and hedging. It is relatively low maintenance and resistant to common pests and diseases.

Escallonia × exoniensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
