Erica massonii

Erica massonii, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, dense clusters of white or pinkish flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica massonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its hardy nature and ability to attract pollinators.
