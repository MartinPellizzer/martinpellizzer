Eria kraenzliniana

Eria kraenzliniana, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers during the winter months. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Eria kraenzliniana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
