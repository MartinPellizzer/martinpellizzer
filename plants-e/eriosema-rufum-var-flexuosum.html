Eriosema rufum var. flexuosum

Eriosema rufum var. flexuosum, commonly known as the hairy legume, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has a creeping habit with hairy stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
