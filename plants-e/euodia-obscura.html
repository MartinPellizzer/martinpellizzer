Euodia obscura

Euodia obscura, commonly known as the dark euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue fruits that are edible but not widely consumed. Euodia obscura is known for its medicinal properties, with its leaves traditionally used in herbal remedies.
