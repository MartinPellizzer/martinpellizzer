Erica williamsiorum

Erica williamsiorum, commonly known as the Williams' heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Erica williamsiorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
