Erysimum falcatum

Erysimum falcatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Erysimum falcatum is often found growing on rocky slopes and in dry habitats.
