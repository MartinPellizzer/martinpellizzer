Epidendrum cleefii

Epidendrum cleefii, commonly known as the Cleef's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, erect growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptical, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Epidendrum cleefii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
