Epimedium sagittifolius

Epimedium sagittifolius, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant has distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are dark green and velvety in texture. Its flowers are small, yellow, and occur in clusters, typically blooming in early spring.

Epimedium sagittifolius is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to naturalize in woodland gardens.
