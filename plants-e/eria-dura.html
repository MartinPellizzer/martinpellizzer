Eria dura

Eria dura, commonly known as the hard eria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

Eria dura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
