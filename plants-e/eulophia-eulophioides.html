Eulophia eulophioides

Eulophia eulophioides, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It is a terrestrial orchid, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its flowers bloom during the summer months, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
