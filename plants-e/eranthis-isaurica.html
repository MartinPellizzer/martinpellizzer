Eranthis isaurica

Eranthis isaurica, commonly known as the Persian buttercup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and the Caucasus.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-like shape. The plant typically blooms in early spring, before the leaves fully develop. Its flowers are surrounded by a ring of petal-like sepals, giving it a unique appearance.

Eranthis isaurica is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
