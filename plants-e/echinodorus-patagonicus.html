Echinodorus patagonicus

Echinodorus patagonicus, commonly known as the Patagonian sword plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, making it a striking addition to freshwater aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular nutrient supplementation to maintain its vibrant green color. It is often used in planted aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

Echinodorus patagonicus is also known for its adaptability to a range of water conditions, making it a popular choice among aquarists.
