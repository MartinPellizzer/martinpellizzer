Evolvulus uniflorus

Evolvulus uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flower morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
