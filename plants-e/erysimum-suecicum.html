Erysimum suecicum

Erysimum suecicum, commonly known as Swedish wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Erysimum suecicum typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
