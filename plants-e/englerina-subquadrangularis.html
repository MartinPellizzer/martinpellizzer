Englerina subquadrangularis

Englerina subquadrangularis, commonly known as the quadrangular englerina, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive quadrangular stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
