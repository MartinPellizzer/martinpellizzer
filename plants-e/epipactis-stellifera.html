Epipactis stellifera

Epipactis stellifera, commonly known as the starry helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and its striking, star-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it produces small, dark brown seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Epipactis stellifera is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
