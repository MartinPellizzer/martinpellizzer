Echites axillaris

Echites axillaris, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are ovate and have a soft, velvety texture, which contributes to its common name.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance.
