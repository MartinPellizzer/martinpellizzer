Erythrina humei var. hastifolia

Erythrina humei var. hastifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf coral tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, trifoliate leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which are often followed by flat, pod-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its role in traditional medicine and as a source of dye.
