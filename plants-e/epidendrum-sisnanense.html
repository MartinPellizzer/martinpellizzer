Epidendrum sisnanense

Epidendrum sisnanense, commonly known as the Sisnans Island orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum sisnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
