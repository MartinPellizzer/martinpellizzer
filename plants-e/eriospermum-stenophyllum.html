Eriospermum stenophyllum

Eriospermum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriosperm, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its arid and semi-arid habitats.
