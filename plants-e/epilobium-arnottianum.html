Epilobium arnottianum

Epilobium arnottianum, commonly known as the Arnott's willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along waterways.

Epilobium arnottianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native pollinators.
