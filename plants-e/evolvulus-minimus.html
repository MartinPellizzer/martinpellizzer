Evolvulus minimus

Evolvulus minimus, commonly known as the small morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a trailing herbaceous plant with slender stems that often grow along the ground. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Evolvulus minimus is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
