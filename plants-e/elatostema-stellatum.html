Elatostema stellatum

Elatostema stellatum, commonly known as the starleaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. The plant features a rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, which are borne on slender stalks.

Elatostema stellatum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
