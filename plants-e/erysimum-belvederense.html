Erysimum belvederense

Erysimum belvederense, commonly known as the Belvedere wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features serrated, lance-shaped leaves and can reach heights of up to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
