Eremurus aitchisonii

Eremurus aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's desert candle, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing numerous yellowish-green flowers. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, enabling it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are narrow and reduced, minimizing water loss through transpiration.

Eremurus aitchisonii is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic appearance and resilience to poor soil conditions.
