Euphorbia provincialis

Euphorbia provincialis, commonly known as the Provincial spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branching stem system with spines and produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The plant has adapted to arid environments with its thick, fleshy leaves that store water. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its milky sap.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
