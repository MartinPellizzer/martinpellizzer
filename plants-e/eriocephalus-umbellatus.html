Eriocephalus umbellatus

Eriocephalus umbellatus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.
