Eugenia loeseneri

Eugenia loeseneri, commonly known as the Loesener's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
