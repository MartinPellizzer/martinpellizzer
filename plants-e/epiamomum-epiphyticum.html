Epiamomum epiphyticum

Epiamomum epiphyticum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is an epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on the trunks or branches of trees. The plant features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern, which helps attract pollinators. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Epiamomum epiphyticum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
