Epidendrum mutelianum var. mirabile

Epidendrum mutelianum var. mirabile, commonly known as the Mirable Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing structural support and aiding in water retention.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
