Embelia tessmannii

Embelia tessmannii, commonly known as the African star anise, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and dark, glossy berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
