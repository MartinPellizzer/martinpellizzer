Epidendrum cerinum

Epidendrum cerinum, commonly known as the silver vase orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum cerinum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
