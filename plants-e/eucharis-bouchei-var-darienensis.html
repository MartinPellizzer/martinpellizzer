Eucharis bouchei var. darienensis

Eucharis bouchei var. darienensis, commonly known as the Darienean arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family, Araceae, and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of many aroid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
