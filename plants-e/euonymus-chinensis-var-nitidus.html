Euonymus chinensis var. nitidus

Euonymus chinensis var. nitidus, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
