Epiphyllum cruentum

Epiphyllum cruentum, commonly known as the crimson night bloomer, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is a climbing cactus that produces large, showy flowers that open at night. The plant is native to Mexico and has a stem that can grow up to 10 meters long. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color and have a strong, sweet fragrance.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
