Eriocaulon blumei

Eriocaulon blumei, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale brown flowers. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds or streams.

Eriocaulon blumei is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal in aquatic gardens.
