Erysimum Ochroleucum Subsp. Helveticum

Erysimum ochroleucum subsp. helveticum is a rare and delicate plant native to parts of Europe. It is known for its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in many regions. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival.
