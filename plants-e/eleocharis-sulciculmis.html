Eleocharis sulciculmis

Eleocharis sulciculmis, commonly known as the zigzag sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, triangular leaves that often have a zigzag pattern along their edges. Eleocharis sulciculmis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
