Euonymus bungeanus var. semipersistens

Euonymus bungeanus var. semipersistens, commonly known as the evergreen euonymus, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year. The leaves are typically ovate or elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries that persist into winter.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
