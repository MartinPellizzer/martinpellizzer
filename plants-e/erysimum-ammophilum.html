Erysimum ammophilum

Erysimum ammophilum, commonly known as the sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in early spring. It has a compact growth habit with linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Erysimum ammophilum is adapted to harsh coastal environments, making it a resilient and hardy species.
