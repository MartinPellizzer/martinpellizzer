Echeveria misteca

Echeveria misteca, commonly known as the Misteca Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a slight powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring or early summer. It prefers well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
