Eriachne montana

Eriachne montana, commonly known as the mountain eriachne, is a grass-like plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its small, spike-like inflorescences bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers.

Eriachne montana is adapted to withstand drought and is an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
