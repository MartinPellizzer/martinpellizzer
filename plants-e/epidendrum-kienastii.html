Epidendrum kienastii

Epidendrum kienastii, commonly known as the Kienast's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with brightly colored, long-lasting flowers that range from white to violet. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence.

Epidendrum kienastii is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
