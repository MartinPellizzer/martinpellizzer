Elatostema acuminatum var. acuminatum

Elatostema acuminatum var. acuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are borne in clusters and are often greenish-white in color.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact growth habit.
