Erica transparens

Erica transparens, commonly known as the transparent heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica transparens thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh, coastal environments.
