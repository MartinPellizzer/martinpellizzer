Echinodorus australis

Echinodorus australis, commonly known as the Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Echinodorus australis is also known for its ability to absorb excess nutrients, making it beneficial for maintaining water quality in aquariums.
