Erysimum caespitosum var. pumilum

Erysimum caespitosum var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf wallflower, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with dense, compact foliage.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.

Its small, hairy leaves and short stems contribute to its drought-tolerant characteristics.
