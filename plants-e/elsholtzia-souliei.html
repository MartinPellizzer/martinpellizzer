Elsholtzia souliei

Elsholtzia souliei, commonly known as Chinese germander, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Elsholtzia souliei is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
