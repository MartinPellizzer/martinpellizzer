Epidendrum barbeyanum

Epidendrum barbeyanum, commonly known as the Barbey's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity to flourish.

Epidendrum barbeyanum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
