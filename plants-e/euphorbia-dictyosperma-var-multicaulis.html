Euphorbia dictyosperma var. multicaulis

Euphorbia dictyosperma var. multicaulis, commonly known as the multicaul euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, multi-stemmed growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It is important to note that all parts of this plant contain toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
