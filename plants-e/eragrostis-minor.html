Eragrostis minor

Eragrostis minor, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green leaves and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils. This plant is often found in prairies, open woodlands, and disturbed areas due to its adaptability. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and serves as a food source for various wildlife species.

Eragrostis minor is also valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
