Echinocactus lenninghausii var. cristatus

Echinocactus lenninghausii var. cristatus, commonly known as the Cristate Hedgehog Cactus, is a unique and striking cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, fleshy stems that form a dense, cushion-like structure. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. Its stems are covered in spines, which provide protection from herbivores and reduce water loss.

This variety is prized for its unusual growth form and is often cultivated in arid gardens and rockeries.
