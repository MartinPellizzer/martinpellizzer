Epicharis densiflora

Epicharis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered epicharis, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. Its leaves are opposite and often have a velvety texture, while the flowers range in color from white to pink.

Epicharis densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
