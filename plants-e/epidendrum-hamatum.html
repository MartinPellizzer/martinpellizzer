Epidendrum hamatum

Epidendrum hamatum, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Epidendrum hamatum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
