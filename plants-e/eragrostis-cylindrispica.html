Eragrostis cylindrispica

Eragrostis cylindrispica, commonly known as the cylindrical needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Eragrostis cylindrispica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
