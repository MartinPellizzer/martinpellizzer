Eruca erecta

Eruca erecta, commonly known as wall rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branching stems covered in small, lobed leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by slender, cylindrical seed pods. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor, similar to mustard greens.

Eruca erecta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and borders.
