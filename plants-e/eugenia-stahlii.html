Eugenia stahlii

Eugenia stahlii, commonly known as the Stahli's evergreen, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces edible, dark purple berries that are rich in antioxidants.

Eugenia stahlii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
