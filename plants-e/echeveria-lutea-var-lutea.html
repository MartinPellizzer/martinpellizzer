Echeveria lutea var. lutea

Echeveria lutea var. lutea, commonly known as the Orange Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bright green with a yellow or orange hue. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring or summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its compact size and striking color make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
