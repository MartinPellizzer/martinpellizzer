Euonymus chinensis

Euonymus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese spindle tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by distinctive red, berry-like fruits that persist into winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its adaptability and use in traditional Chinese medicine.
