Ericoides tenue

Ericoides tenue, commonly known as the slender ericoid, is a small evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and leathery, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, which bloom in early spring. Ericoides tenue thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is a hardy plant that is well adapted to coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
