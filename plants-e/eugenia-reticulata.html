Eugenia reticulata

Eugenia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a distinct net-like venation pattern. Its fragrant white flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, dark purple to black berries.

Eugenia reticulata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
