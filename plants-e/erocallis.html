Erocallis

Erocallis, commonly known as the snowdrop, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Amaryllidaceae.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. They are valued in horticulture for their delicate, early-blooming flowers and ability to thrive in woodland or shaded garden environments.
