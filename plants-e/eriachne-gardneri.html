Eriachne gardneri

Eriachne gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for native fauna.
