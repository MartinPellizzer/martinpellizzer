Eriosema erici-rosenii var. machadoi

Eriosema erici-rosenii var. machadoi, commonly known as the Machado's eriosema, is a species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, and its flowers are usually small and clustered. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in semi-arid environments.
