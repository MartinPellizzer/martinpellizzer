Epidendrum hutchisonii

Epidendrum hutchisonii, commonly known as the Mexican Flame Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Epidendrum hutchisonii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
