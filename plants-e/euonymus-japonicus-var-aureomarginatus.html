Euonymus japonicus var. aureomarginatus

Euonymus japonicus var. aureomarginatus, commonly known as the Japanese Spurge with Golden Margin, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive yellow or gold margin. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, which are followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and adaptability.

This variety thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
