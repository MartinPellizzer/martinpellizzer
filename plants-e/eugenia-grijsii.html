Eugenia grijsii

Eugenia grijsii, commonly known as the Grey's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. It typically grows as a small shrub or tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Eugenia grijsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
