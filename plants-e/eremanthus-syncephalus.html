Eremanthus syncephalus

Eremanthus syncephalus, commonly known as the white-flowered eremanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Eremanthus syncephalus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
