Eupatorium subulatum

Eupatorium subulatum, commonly known as slender boneset, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. It is part of the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads.

Eupatorium subulatum is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
