Eulalia viminea

Eulalia viminea, commonly known as the silky grass or silver grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces slender, drooping inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening. Native to South America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its unique texture and color make it a popular choice for landscape design.
