Eriocycla olivieri

Eriocycla olivieri, commonly known as the olive sea snail, is a species of sea snail in the family Eulimidae.

It is characterized by its elongated, spiral shell that is typically pale yellow to reddish-brown in color. The shell is often covered with a thin, delicate periostracum that can vary in texture. This marine gastropod is found in warm coastal waters, particularly in the Indo-Pacific region.

Its diet primarily consists of algae and other small marine organisms.
