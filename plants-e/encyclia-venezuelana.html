Encyclia venezuelana

Encyclia venezuelana, commonly known as the Venezuelan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia venezuelana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
