Ehretia thonningiana

Ehretia thonningiana, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.
