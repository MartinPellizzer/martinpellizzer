Eria atroferruginea

Eria atroferruginea, commonly known as the black rust orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown to blackish sepals and petals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of brown and orange.

Eria atroferruginea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
