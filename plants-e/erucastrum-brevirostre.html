Erucastrum brevirostre

Erucastrum brevirostre, commonly known as the narrow-beaked erucaceous, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, typical of the family. Its leaves are elongated and have a slightly hairy texture.
