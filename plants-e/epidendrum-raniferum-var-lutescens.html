Epidendrum raniferum var. lutescens

Epidendrum raniferum var. lutescens, commonly known as the Yellow Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, arching stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves that are a vibrant green. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are characterized by their yellow-orange coloration with red or brown markings.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in tropical gardens.
