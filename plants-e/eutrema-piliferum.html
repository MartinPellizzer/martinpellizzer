Eutrema piliferum

Eutrema piliferum, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the northern hemisphere.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright green, lobed leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it a common sight along shorelines and dunes.

Eutrema piliferum is also valued for its edible leaves and seeds, which have been used in traditional cuisines.
