Eriocaulon sikokianum var. atrosepalum

Eriocaulon sikokianum var. atrosepalum, commonly known as the dark-sepal rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
