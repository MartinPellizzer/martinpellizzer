Evaiezoa

Evaiezoa, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by small, round fruits.

Evaiezoa is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
