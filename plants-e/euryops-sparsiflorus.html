Euryops sparsiflorus

Euryops sparsiflorus, commonly known as the sparseflower dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. Euryops sparsiflorus is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.
