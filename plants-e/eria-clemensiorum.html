Eria clemensiorum

Eria clemensiorum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants rather than in soil.

Eria clemensiorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
