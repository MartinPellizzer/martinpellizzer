Etlingera cevuga

Etlingera cevuga, commonly known as the Cevuga torch ginger, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange inflorescence, which resembles a torch, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, typically with a waxy texture.
