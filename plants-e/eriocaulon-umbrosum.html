Eriocaulon umbrosum

Eriocaulon umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in spikelets. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine, hairy layer, which helps in retaining moisture.

Eriocaulon umbrosum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid and partially shaded conditions.
