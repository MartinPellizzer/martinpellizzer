Eurya stenophylla

Eurya stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
