Eriospermum capense subsp. stoloniferum

Eriospermum capense subsp.

stoloniferum, commonly known as the Cape eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and produces showy yellow flowers.

The plant is perennial and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. It is known for its stoloniferous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
