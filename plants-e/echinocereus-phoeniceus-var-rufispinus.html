Echinocereus phoeniceus var. rufispinus

Echinocereus phoeniceus var. rufispinus, commonly known as the red spiny hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, reddish-brown spines that give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
