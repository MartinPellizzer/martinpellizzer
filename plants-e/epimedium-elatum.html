Epimedium elatum

Epimedium elatum, commonly known as the barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a leathery texture, often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Epimedium elatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
