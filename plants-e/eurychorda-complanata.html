Eurychorda complanata

Eurychorda complanata, commonly known as the flatleaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale greenish-white flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
