Epipactis odemensis

Epipactis odemensis, commonly known as the Odem's helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are pollinated by bees and other insects, and it produces small, dark brown seeds.

Epipactis odemensis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
