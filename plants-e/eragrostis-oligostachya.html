Eragrostis oligostachya

Eragrostis oligostachya, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, tufted stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
