Euphrasia multicaulis

Euphrasia multicaulis, commonly known as the hairy eyebright, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
