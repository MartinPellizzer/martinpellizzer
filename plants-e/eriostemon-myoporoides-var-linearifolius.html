Eriostemon myoporoides var. linearifolius

Eriostemon myoporoides var. linearifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
