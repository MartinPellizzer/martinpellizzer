Erepsia villiersii

Erepsia villiersii, commonly known as the African rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually greenish-white or yellowish in color. It has a short, underground rhizome and fibrous roots, which help it survive in arid conditions.

Erepsia villiersii is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and adaptation to its harsh environment.
