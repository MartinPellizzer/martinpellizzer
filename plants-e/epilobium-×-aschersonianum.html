Epilobium × aschersonianum

Epilobium × aschersonianum, commonly known as the redstem filaree, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm. The plant features showy pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are reddish and often tinged with purple.

It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
