Eucalyptus goniocarpa

Eucalyptus goniocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallee, is a species of eucalyptus native to Western Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically growing up to 5 meters in height. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly rough texture. Its flowers are typically white or cream, arranged in clusters, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

This plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
