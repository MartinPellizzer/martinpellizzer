Eulophia rouxii

Eulophia rouxii, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with several flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its flowers have a distinct lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks.

Eulophia rouxii is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.
