Erica triceps

Erica triceps, commonly known as the three-leaf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica triceps typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal.
