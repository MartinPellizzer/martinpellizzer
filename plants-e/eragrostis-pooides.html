Eragrostis pooides

Eragrostis pooides, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its native range.
