Epidendrum oscar-perezii

Epidendrum oscar-perezii, commonly known as the Oscar Perez Orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a sympodial orchid that typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white to grayish hairs. Its flowers are borne in racemose inflorescences and are typically violet to purple in color.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
