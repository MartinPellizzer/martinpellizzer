Euphorbia ohiotica

Euphorbia ohiotica, commonly known as Ohio spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features a succulent, branching stem covered in small, green, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters called cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. Ohio spurge is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often found in open woodlands and prairie habitats.
