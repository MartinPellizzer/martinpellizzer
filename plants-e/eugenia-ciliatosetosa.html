Eugenia ciliatosetosa

Eugenia ciliatosetosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf guava, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered with fine, short hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow or red when ripe.

Eugenia ciliatosetosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some use in traditional medicine.
