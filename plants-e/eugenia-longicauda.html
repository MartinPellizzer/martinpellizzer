Eugenia longicauda

Eugenia longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Eugenia longicauda is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and potential medicinal properties.
