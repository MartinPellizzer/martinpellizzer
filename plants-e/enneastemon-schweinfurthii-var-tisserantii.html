Enneastemon schweinfurthii var. tisserantii

Enneastemon schweinfurthii var. tisserantii, commonly known as the Tisserant's flame bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tiny, spadix-like flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
