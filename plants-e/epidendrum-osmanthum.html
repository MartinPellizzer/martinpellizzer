Epidendrum osmanthum

Epidendrum osmanthum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Moon, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences adorned with fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that resemble moonlight. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum osmanthum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
