Evolvulus nummularius var. emarginatus

Evolvulus nummularius var. emarginatus, commonly known as the Margined Blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces small, blue or violet flowers. The leaves are typically round or ovate with a distinctive notched or lobed margin, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
