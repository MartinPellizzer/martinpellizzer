Epidendrum morimotoi

Epidendrum morimotoi, commonly known as the Morimoto's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features dense, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum morimotoi is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for experienced orchid growers.
