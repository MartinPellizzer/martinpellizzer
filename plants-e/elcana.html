Elcana

Elcana, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with a short stem and dense foliage. Elcana thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and yellow, making it a favorite among gardeners.
