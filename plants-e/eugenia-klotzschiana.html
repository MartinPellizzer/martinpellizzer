Eugenia klotzschiana

Eugenia klotzschiana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant produces bright red, bell-shaped fruits that are edible and often used in culinary applications. Eugenia klotzschiana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
