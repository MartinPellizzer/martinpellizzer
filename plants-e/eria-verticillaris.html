Eria verticillaris

Eria verticillaris, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, compact growth habit and its unique spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance.
