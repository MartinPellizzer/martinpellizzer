Eucalyptus muelleri

Eucalyptus muelleri, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallee, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It produces small, globular flower buds that range in color from white to pink.

The wood of Eucalyptus muelleri is hard and durable, making it valuable for timber and fuel.
