Epilobium pratense

Epilobium pratense, commonly known as willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant features showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in racemes from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are square and often reddish in color.

Epilobium pratense is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
