Echinocactus leucacanthus

Echinocactus leucacanthus, commonly known as the white-spined cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and collections.
