Epidendrum viridiochraceum

Epidendrum viridiochraceum, commonly known as the green-stem orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant green stems and leaves, with flowers that range from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and have a delicate, fragrant aroma.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
