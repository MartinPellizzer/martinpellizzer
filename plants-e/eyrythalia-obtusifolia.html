Eyrythalia obtusifolia

Eyrythalia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved erythronium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features basal leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, with a deep green color and a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces delicate, nodding white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Eyrythalia obtusifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early season blooms.
