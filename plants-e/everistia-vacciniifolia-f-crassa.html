Everistia vacciniifolia f. crassa

Everistia vacciniifolia f. crassa, commonly known as the thick-leaved everistia, is a rare and specialized plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in dense forest understories.
