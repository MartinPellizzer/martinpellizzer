Elymus shouliangiae

Elymus shouliangiae, commonly known as the Shouliang's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

Elymus shouliangiae is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
