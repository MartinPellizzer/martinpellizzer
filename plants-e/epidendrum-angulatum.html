Epidendrum angulatum

Epidendrum angulatum, commonly known as the angular epidendron, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with distinctive angular sepals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Epidendrum angulatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
