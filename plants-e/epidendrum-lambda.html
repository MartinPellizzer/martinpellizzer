Epidendrum lambda

Epidendrum lambda, commonly known as the violet-leaf epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Epidendrum lambda thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
