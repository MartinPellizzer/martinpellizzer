Encyclia albopurpurea

Encyclia albopurpurea, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that often displays a distinctive purple or reddish hue. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia albopurpurea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
