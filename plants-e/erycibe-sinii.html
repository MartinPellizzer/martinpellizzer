Erycibe sinii

Erycibe sinii, commonly known as the Chinese starfruit or golden apple.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and Malaysia. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a waxy texture, while its flowers are typically white or pale yellow.

Erycibe sinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for its fruit.
