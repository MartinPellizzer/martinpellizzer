Eulalia shrirangii

Eulalia shrirangii, commonly known as the Himalayan feather grass, is a perennial grass native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery plumes that give it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest to its natural habitat.

Eulalia shrirangii is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornate flowering structure and adaptability to challenging climates.
