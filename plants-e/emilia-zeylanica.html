Emilia zeylanica

Emilia zeylanica, commonly known as the blueweed or blue coneflower, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, with the leaves being lance-shaped and slightly serrated. Emilia zeylanica thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
