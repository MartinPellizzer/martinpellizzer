Eustephia brevituba

Eustephia brevituba, commonly known as the short-tubed euphorbia, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Eustephia brevituba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
