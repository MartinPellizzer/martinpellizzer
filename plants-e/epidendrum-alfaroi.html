Epidendrum alfaroi

Epidendrum alfaroi, commonly known as the Alfaroi orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum alfaroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
