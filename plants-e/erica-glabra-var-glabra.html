Erica glabra var. glabra

Erica glabra var. glabra, commonly known as heather, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

Erica glabra var. glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to form dense, hardy ground cover.
