Eria hirta

Eria hirta, commonly known as the hairy eria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Eria hirta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
