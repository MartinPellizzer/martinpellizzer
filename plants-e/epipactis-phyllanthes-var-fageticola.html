Epipactis phyllanthes var. fageticola

Epipactis phyllanthes var. fageticola, commonly known as the Fageticola helleborine, is a herbaceous perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, sparsely branched stems with lanceolate, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a distinctive lip that is pale yellow with darker markings.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
