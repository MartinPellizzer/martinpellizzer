Eugenia odorata var. longifolia

Eugenia odorata var. longifolia, commonly known as the fragrant guava, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits with a sweet, aromatic flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and culinary purposes.
