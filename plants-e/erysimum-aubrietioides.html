Erysimum aubrietioides

Erysimum aubrietioides, commonly known as the candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features basal rosettes of silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch of color to the landscape.
