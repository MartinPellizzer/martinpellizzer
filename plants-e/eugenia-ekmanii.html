Eugenia ekmanii

Eugenia ekmanii, commonly known as the Kauai hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal areas and lowland forests.
