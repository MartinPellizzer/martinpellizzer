Euphorbia unicornis

Euphorbia unicornis, commonly known as the unicorn's horn, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive single, spiny stem that resembles a horn, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces yellowish-green花朵 (flowers) surrounded by colorful bracts, which are often the primary attraction. Its leaves are fleshy and triangular, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Euphorbia unicornis is a slow-growing plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.
