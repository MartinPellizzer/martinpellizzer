Epilobium billardiereanum f. major

Epilobium billardiereanum f. major, commonly known as the large-flowered willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and riverbanks.

This species is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
