Eragrostis vulcanica

Eragrostis vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic grass, is a hardy plant adapted to extreme environments.

It is native to regions with volcanic soil and high temperatures, showcasing its resilience. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils, often found in arid or semi-arid climates.

Its botanical structure allows it to withstand harsh conditions, making it a notable species in ecological studies.
