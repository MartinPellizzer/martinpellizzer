Epidendrum × ormindoi

Epidendrum × ormindoi, commonly known as the Orchid Hybrid, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Epidendrum ibaguense and Epidendrum nocturnum, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parents. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a strong fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage is typically broad and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal in both indoor and outdoor settings.
