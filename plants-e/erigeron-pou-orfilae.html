Erigeron pou-orfilae

Erigeron pou-orfilae, commonly known as the Chinese blueweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is often found in grassy areas and is valued for its ornamental and traditional medicinal uses.
