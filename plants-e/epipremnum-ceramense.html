Epipremnum ceramense

Epipremnum ceramense, commonly known as the Ceram island philodendron, is a climbing vine native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.

Epipremnum ceramense is relatively easy to care for, requiring bright indirect light and moderate watering.
