Epidendrum elatius

Epidendrum elatius, commonly known as the tall epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum elatius is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
