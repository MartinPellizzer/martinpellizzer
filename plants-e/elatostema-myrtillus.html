Elatostema myrtillus

Elatostema myrtillus, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
