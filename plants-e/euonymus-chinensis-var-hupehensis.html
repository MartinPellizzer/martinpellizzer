Euonymus chinensis var. hupehensis

Euonymus chinensis var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese spindletree, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that persist into winter. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This species is often used in landscaping for its seasonal color changes and hardy nature.
