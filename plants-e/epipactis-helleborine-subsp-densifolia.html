Epipactis helleborine subsp. densifolia

Epipactis helleborine subsp.

densifolia, commonly known as the dense-leaved helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dark green, densely packed leaves.

The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Epipactis helleborine subsp.

densifolia is known for its ability to form dense colonies, making it a common sight in its native habitats.
