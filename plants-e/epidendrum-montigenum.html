Epidendrum montigenum

Epidendrum montigenum, commonly known as the Mexican flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum montigenum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
