Encyclia grammatoglossa

Encyclia grammatoglossa, commonly known as the Grammatoglossa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizomatous stem and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant has a compact stature and typically blooms during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

The orchid is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories.
