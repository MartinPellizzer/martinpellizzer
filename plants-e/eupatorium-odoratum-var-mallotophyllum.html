Eupatorium odoratum var. mallotophyllum

Eupatorium odoratum var. mallotophyllum, commonly known as the fragrant eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive, fragrant scent when crushed.

The flowers, which bloom in late summer, are often blue to purple in color and attract various pollinators.
