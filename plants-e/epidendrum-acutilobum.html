Epidendrum acutilobum

Epidendrum acutilobum, commonly known as the acute-lobed epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically features strap-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum acutilobum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
