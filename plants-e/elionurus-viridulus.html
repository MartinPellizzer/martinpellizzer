Elionurus viridulus

Elionurus viridulus, commonly known as the green elionurus, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

Elionurus viridulus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
