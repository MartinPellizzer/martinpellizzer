Eulalia yunnanensis

Eulalia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

Eulalia yunnanensis is also of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
