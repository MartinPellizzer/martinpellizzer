Erodium cicutarium f. praecox

Erodium cicutarium f. praecox, commonly known as early field bean or small field bean, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Leguminosae family.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and distinctive pink or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is adaptable to various climates.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.
