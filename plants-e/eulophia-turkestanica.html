Eulophia turkestanica

Eulophia turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, creeping rhizome and a rosette of broad, fleshy leaves. The plant produces a single, erect stem bearing several lance-shaped, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky substrates.

Eulophia turkestanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
