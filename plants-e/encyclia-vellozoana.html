Encyclia vellozoana

Encyclia vellozoana, commonly known as the Vellozoan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, often in shades of yellow and red, which bloom during the warmer months. Its pseudobulbs are segmented, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia vellozoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
