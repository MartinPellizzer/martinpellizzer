Epilobium × charbonnelianum

Epilobium × charbonnelianum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf willow herb, is a hybrid species within the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
