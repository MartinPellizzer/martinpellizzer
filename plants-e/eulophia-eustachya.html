Eulophia eustachya

Eulophia eustachya, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and possesses long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Eulophia eustachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
