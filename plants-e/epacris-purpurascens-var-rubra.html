Epacris purpurascens var. rubra

Epacris purpurascens var. rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed epacris, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant red or pink flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Epacris purpurascens var. rubra is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
