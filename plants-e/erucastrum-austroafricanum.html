Erucastrum austroafricanum

Erucastrum austroafricanum, commonly known as the African cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor, similar to that of mustard greens.
