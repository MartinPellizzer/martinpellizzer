Elatine fruticosa

Elatine fruticosa, commonly known as the water spurge, is a small, herbaceous plant that thrives in moist environments.

It is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit with tiny, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Elatine fruticosa is a member of the Elatinaceae family and is known for its ability to tolerate waterlogged soils.
