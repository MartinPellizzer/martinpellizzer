Encyclia moojenii

Encyclia moojenii, commonly known as the Moojen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, violet-colored blooms with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns. Encyclia moojenii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
