Epidendrum brevicernuum

Epidendrum brevicernuum, commonly known as the short-stemmed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
