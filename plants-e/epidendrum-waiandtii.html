Epidendrum waiandtii

Epidendrum waiandtii, commonly known as the Waiandt's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, often with a waxy coating that helps in water retention.

Epidendrum waiandtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
