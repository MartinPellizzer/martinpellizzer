Eclipta dubia

Eclipta dubia, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Eclipta dubia is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
