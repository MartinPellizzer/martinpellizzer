Erigeron hirtuosus

Erigeron hirtuosus, commonly known as the hairy fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of ray and disc flowers. Erigeron hirtuosus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ecological value and ornamental appeal.
