Excoecaria indica

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often toxic species. The plant is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with prominent veins and bright yellow flowers. Its sap contains toxic compounds, which can cause skin irritation and other adverse reactions.
