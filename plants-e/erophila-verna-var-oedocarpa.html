Erophila verna var. oedocarpa

Erophila verna var. oedocarpa, commonly known as the early purple orchid, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture efficiently.
