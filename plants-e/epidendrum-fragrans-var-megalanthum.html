Epidendrum fragrans var. megalanthum

Epidendrum fragrans var. megalanthum, commonly known as the fragrant epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often emit a sweet fragrance, especially in the evening. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
