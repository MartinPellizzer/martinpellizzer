Erythrorhipsalis Burchellii

Erythrorhipsalis burchellii is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant red and green foliage. Native to the arid regions of southern Africa, it thrives in harsh desert environments. This succulent is prized for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. Its unusual coloration makes it a popular choice among collectors and indoor gardeners. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and root rot.
