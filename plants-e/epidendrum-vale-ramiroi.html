Epidendrum vale-ramiroi

Epidendrum vale-ramiroi, commonly known as the Ramiro's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers due to its relatively easy care requirements.

Epidendrum vale-ramiroi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
