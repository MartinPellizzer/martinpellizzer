Euphorbia bothriosperma

Euphorbia bothriosperma, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that can be toxic if ingested. Its leaves are typically small and opposite, with a rough texture.

Euphorbia bothriosperma is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
