Epidendrum otuzcense

Epidendrum otuzcense, commonly known as the Otuzcense orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branched stem and numerous, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum otuzcense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
