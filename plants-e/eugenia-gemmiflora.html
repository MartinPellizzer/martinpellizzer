Eugenia gemmiflora

Eugenia gemmiflora, commonly known as the gemmy eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible berries that are typically red or yellow when mature, containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Eugenia gemmiflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
