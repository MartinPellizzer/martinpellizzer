Eranthemum splendens

Eranthemum splendens, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant produces tubular flowers with two lips, typical of the genus Eranthemum. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eranthemum splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
