Escobedia foliolosa

Escobedia foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy escobedia, is a unique lichen species belonging to the family Pilocarpaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, branched thallus that forms a cushion-like structure. The plant exhibits a symbiotic relationship between a green alga and a fungus, which together contribute to its survival in harsh environments. It typically grows on rocks and soil in arid and semi-arid regions.

Due to its specialized morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to mycologists and botanists studying lichen diversity.
