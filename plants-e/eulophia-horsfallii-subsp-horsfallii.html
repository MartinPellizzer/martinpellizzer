Eulophia horsfallii subsp. horsfallii

Eulophia horsfallii subsp.

horsfallii, commonly known as the Horsfall's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a cluster of lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces a single, elongated inflorescence bearing numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in montane regions.
