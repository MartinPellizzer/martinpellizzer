Epidendrum bisulcatum

Epidendrum bisulcatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often slightly curled and have a distinctive zigzag pattern along their edges. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Epidendrum bisulcatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
