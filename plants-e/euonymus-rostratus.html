Euonymus rostratus

Euonymus rostratus, commonly known as the scarlet beauty, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features showy, orange-red berries that persist through the winter, adding visual interest to the landscape. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and unique fruiting characteristics.
