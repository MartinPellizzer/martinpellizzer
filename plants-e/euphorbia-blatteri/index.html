Euphorbia Blatteri

Euphorbia blatteri is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, colorful bracts, it resembles a flower but is actually a modified leaf. This succulent thrives in arid conditions and requires minimal water to survive. Its unusual appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap that can cause skin irritation.
