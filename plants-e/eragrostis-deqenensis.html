Eragrostis deqenensis

Eragrostis deqenensis, commonly known as the Deqen grass, is a perennial grass species native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Tibet regions.

It thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in alpine meadows and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to cold climates and is often used for soil stabilization in its native habitat.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local ecosystem.
