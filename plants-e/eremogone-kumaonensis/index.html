Eremogone Kumaonensis

Eremogone kumaonensis is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It thrives in harsh, arid environments where few other species can survive. This plant is known for its striking yellow blooms that appear during the short growing season. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and delicate species.
