Erysimum ghaznicum

Erysimum ghaznicum, commonly known as the Ghazni wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its foliage is characterized by narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder.
