Eragrostis depallens

Eragrostis depallens, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often tinged with purple or pink hues. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
