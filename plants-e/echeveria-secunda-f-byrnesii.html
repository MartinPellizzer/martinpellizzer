Echeveria secunda f. byrnesii

Echeveria secunda f. byrnesii, commonly known as the Byrnesii Echeveria, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves with a waxy texture that help it retain moisture. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
