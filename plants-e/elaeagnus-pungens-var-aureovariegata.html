Elaeagnus pungens var. aureovariegata

Elaeagnus pungens var. aureovariegata, commonly known as silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-white bark and small, fragrant yellow flowers that appear in early spring. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive variegated pattern of green and yellow. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It also has a history of use in traditional medicine.
