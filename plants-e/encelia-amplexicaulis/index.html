Encelia Amplexicaulis

Encelia amplexicaulis is a unique flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its distinctive rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that wrap around its stem. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. Despite its modest size, Encelia amplexicaulis plays an important role in its ecosystem and is a symbol of resilience in desert landscapes.
