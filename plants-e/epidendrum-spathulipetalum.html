Epidendrum spathulipetalum

Epidendrum spathulipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and relatively broad, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum spathulipetalum is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
