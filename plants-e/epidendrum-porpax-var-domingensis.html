Epidendrum porpax var. domingensis

Epidendrum porpax var. domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically features elliptical, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
