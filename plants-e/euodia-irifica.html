Euodia irifica

Euodia irifica, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a resin that has been historically used in traditional medicine and incense. Its bark and leaves contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

Euodia irifica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
