Episcia densa

Episcia densa, commonly known as the dense episcia, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Episcia densa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush foliage.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
