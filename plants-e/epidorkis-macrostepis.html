Epidorkis macrostepis

Epidorkis macrostepis, commonly known as the giant stephanotis, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces fruit that turns from green to yellow as it matures. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
