Epidendrum silverstonei

Epidendrum silverstonei, commonly known as the Silverstone Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features erect, hairy stems that bear clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near streams or in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine, silver-gray pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
