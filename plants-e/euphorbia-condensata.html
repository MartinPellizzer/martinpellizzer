Euphorbia condensata

Euphorbia condensata, commonly known as the condensed spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin.

Euphorbia condensata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
