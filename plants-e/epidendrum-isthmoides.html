Epidendrum isthmoides

Epidendrum isthmoides, commonly known as the Isthmian Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum isthmoides is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
