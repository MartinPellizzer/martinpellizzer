Evolvulus paniculatus

Evolvulus paniculatus, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Evolvulus paniculatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive flowers and easy care requirements.
