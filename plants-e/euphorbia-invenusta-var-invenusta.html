Euphorbia invenusta var. invenusta

Euphorbia invenusta var. invenusta, commonly known as the "Coral Beauty" or "Coral Spurge," is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features striking, coral-colored bracts that surround its small, greenish-yellow flowers, giving it a vibrant and ornamental appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
