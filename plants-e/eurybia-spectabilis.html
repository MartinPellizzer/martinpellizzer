Eurybia spectabilis

Eurybia spectabilis, commonly known as showy goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small yellow daisies. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and stream banks. It produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall.
