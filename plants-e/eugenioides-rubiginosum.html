Eugenioides rubiginosum

Eugenioides rubiginosum, commonly known as the red-stained eugenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a reddish or purplish hue. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a fragrant aroma, and it produces small, edible berries that are rich in antioxidants.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
