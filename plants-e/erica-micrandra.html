Erica micrandra

Erica micrandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in late spring to early summer.

Erica micrandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
