Eriopsis altissima

Eriopsis altissima, commonly known as the tall eriopsis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Eriopsis altissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
