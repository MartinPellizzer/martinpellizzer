Erica kiwuensis

Erica kiwuensis, commonly known as the African heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with high rainfall. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and visual interest.
