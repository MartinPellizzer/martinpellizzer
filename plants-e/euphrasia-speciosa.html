Euphrasia speciosa

Euphrasia speciosa, commonly known as the showy eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. Euphrasia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has a long history of use in traditional herbal medicine.
