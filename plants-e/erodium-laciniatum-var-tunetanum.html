Erodium laciniatum var. tunetanum

Erodium laciniatum var. tunetanum, commonly known as the tunetanum erodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its finely dissected, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, pink or reddish-purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
