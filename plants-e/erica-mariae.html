Erica mariae

Erica mariae, commonly known as the Marie's heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of bell-shaped, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer.

Erica mariae is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.
