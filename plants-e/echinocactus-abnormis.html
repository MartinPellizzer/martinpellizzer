Echinocactus abnormis

Echinocactus abnormis, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its spine-like structures are modified stems, providing protection from herbivores and reducing water loss.

Echinocactus abnormis is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in desert environments.
