Euploca diffusa

Euploca diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf euploca, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of silver-gray foliage. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Euploca diffusa is often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.
