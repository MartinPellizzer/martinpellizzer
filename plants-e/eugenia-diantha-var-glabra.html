Eugenia diantha var. glabra

Eugenia diantha var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth eugenia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
