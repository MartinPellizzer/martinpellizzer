Echium mentagense var. versicolor

Echium mentagense var. versicolor, commonly known as the variable fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple and pink, depending on the variety. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
