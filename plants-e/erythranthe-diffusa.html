Erythranthe diffusa

Erythranthe diffusa, commonly known as the spreading fritillary, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it typically grows in moist, open habitats like meadows and streambanks.
