Emilia somalensis

Emilia somalensis, commonly known as the Somali emilia, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Emilia somalensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

It is often found growing in rocky soils and along dry streambeds.
