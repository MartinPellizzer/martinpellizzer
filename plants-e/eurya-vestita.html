Eurya vestita

Eurya vestita, commonly known as the woolly alder or silverberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has leathery, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is found in temperate regions of Asia.

Eurya vestita is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and modest flowers.
