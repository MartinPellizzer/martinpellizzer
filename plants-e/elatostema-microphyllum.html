Elatostema microphyllum

Elatostema microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tiny, ovate leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its compact size and unique leaf morphology make it a distinctive species within the Elatostema genus.
