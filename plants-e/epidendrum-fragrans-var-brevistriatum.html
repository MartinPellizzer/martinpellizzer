Epidendrum fragrans var. brevistriatum

Epidendrum fragrans var. brevistriatum, commonly known as the fragrant epidendrum, is a flowering orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its pleasant scent and elegant appearance.
