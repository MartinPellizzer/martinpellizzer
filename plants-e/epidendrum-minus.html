Epidendrum minus

Epidendrum minus, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its compact size and ornamental value.

Epidendrum minus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
