Epimedium platypetalum

Epimedium platypetalum, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves fully emerge. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium platypetalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to add color to shady landscapes.
