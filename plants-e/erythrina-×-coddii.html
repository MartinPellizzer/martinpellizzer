Erythrina × coddii

Erythrina × coddii, commonly known as the hybrid flame tree, is a tropical ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant red, orange, and yellow flowers.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Erythrina crista-galli and Erythrina indica, showcasing a mix of traits from both parent species. The plant features compound leaves with three leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its striking floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
