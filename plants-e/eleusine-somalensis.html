Eleusine somalensis

Eleusine somalensis, commonly known as Somali lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Eleusine somalensis is often used for forage in livestock farming due to its nutritional value and hardiness.
