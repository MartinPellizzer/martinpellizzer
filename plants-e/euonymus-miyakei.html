Euonymus miyakei

Euonymus miyakei, commonly known as the Miyake's spindle tree, is a shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, alternate leaves. The plant produces distinctive red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
