Epidendrum cupreum

Epidendrum cupreum, commonly known as the copper orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features pendant, sword-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum cupreum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
