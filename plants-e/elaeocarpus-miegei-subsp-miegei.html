Elaeocarpus miegei subsp. miegei

Elaeocarpus miegei subsp.

miegei, commonly known as the Miege's elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and has limited economic use.
