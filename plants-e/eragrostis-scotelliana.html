Eragrostis scotelliana

Eragrostis scotelliana, commonly known as the silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green leaves and tufted inflorescences that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.

Eragrostis scotelliana is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
