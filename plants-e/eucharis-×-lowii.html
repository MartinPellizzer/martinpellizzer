Eucharis × lowii

Eucharis × lowii, commonly known as the Amazonian white lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon basin, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features large, waxy, white flowers that are often surrounded by a showy spathe, giving it a striking appearance. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value.

Eucharis × lowii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its elegant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
