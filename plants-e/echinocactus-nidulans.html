Echinocactus nidulans

Echinocactus nidulans, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive crown of spiny, ribbed stems that resemble a hat. The plant features prominent, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments.

Echinocactus nidulans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
