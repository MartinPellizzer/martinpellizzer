Ehretia mollis

Ehretia mollis, commonly known as the silky tree or velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves and bark have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
