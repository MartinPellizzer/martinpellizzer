Erica acockii

Erica acockii, commonly known as the Acock's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica acockii is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable environments.
