Echinocactus hemifossus var. gracilispinus

Echinocactus hemifossus var. gracilispinus, commonly known as the narrow-spined hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in prominent, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the ends of the stems. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for desert gardens and rockeries.
