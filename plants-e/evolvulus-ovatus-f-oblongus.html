Evolvulus ovatus f. oblongus

Evolvulus ovatus f. oblongus, commonly known as the oblong leaf morning glory, is a variant of the species Evolvulus ovatus.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing as an annual or perennial depending on the climate.

Its unique leaf shape and vibrant flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
