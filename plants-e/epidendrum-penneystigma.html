Epidendrum penneystigma

Epidendrum penneystigma, commonly known as the Penneystigma Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to pale green pubescence. Its flowers are typically borne in racemes and are usually a mix of white, green, and yellow hues.

Epidendrum penneystigma is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
