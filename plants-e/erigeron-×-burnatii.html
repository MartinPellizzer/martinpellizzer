Erigeron × burnatii

Erigeron × burnatii, commonly known as the hybrid fleabane, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its fluffy, daisy-like composite flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It is native to the United States and is often found in prairies, open woodlands, and disturbed soils.

Erigeron × burnatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
