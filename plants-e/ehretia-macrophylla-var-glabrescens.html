Ehretia macrophylla var. glabrescens

Ehretia macrophylla var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed ehretia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
