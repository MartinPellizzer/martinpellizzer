Epipremnum elegans

Epipremnum elegans, commonly known as the Golden Pothos, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that often feature a striking yellow or gold variegation. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like philodendrons. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is known for its ability to purify indoor air.

Epipremnum elegans is a low-maintenance plant that can be grown in soil or as a hanging plant.
