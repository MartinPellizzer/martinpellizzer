Eriostemon ovatifolius

Eriostemon ovatifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
