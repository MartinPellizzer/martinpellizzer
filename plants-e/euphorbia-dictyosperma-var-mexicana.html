Euphorbia dictyosperma var. mexicana

Euphorbia dictyosperma var. mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican spurge, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a thick, branching stem covered in small, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its toxic sap, it requires careful handling.
