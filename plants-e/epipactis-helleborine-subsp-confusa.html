Epipactis helleborine subsp. confusa

Epipactis helleborine subsp.

confusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest floors. It produces small, yellowish-brown seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves and slightly different floral structure compared to other members of the *Epipactis helleborine* species complex.
