Eriosema filipendulum var. prostratum

Eriosema filipendulum var. prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate eriosema, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features prostrate stems that spread along the ground, often rooting at the nodes. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and covered with fine, silky hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
