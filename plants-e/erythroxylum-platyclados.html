Erythroxylum platyclados

Erythroxylum platyclados, commonly known as the broad-leaved erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
