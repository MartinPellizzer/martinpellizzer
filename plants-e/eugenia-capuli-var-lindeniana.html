Eugenia capuli var. lindeniana

Eugenia capuli var. lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenaugur, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, edible berries that are used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
