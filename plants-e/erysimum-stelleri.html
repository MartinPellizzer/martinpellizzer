Erysimum stelleri

Erysimum stelleri, commonly known as Steller's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched and upright.

Erysimum stelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
