Equisetum ramosissimum f. debile

Equisetum ramosissimum f. debile, commonly known as the slender horse tail, is a variant of the common horsetail.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the Equisetaceae family. This plant is characterized by its slender, segmented stems and reduced leaves, which are typically only a few millimeters in length. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

The plant reproduces through spores and is known for its ancient lineage, dating back to the Paleozoic era.
