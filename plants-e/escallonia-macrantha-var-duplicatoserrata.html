Escallonia macrantha var. duplicatoserrata

Escallonia macrantha var. duplicatoserrata, commonly known as the double-serrated escallonia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, leathery foliage with dark green leaves that have a distinctive double-serrated edge. The plant produces showy clusters of pinkish-white flowers in spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
