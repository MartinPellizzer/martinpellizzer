Echeveria pubescens

Echeveria pubescens, commonly known as the velvet echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fuzzy, bluish-green leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
