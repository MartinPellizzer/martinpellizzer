Eruca stenocarpa var. major

Eruca stenocarpa var. major, commonly known as wild rocket or sheep's cabbage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is an annual or biennial herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, featuring lobed leaves and yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family members. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor, often used in salads or as a seasoning.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
