Epidendrum kymatochilum

Epidendrum kymatochilum, commonly known as the "Coral Spike" orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of small, colorful flowers, often in shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum kymatochilum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
