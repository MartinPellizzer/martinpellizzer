Eupatorium conyzoides var. glabrescens

Eupatorium conyzoides var. glabrescens, commonly known as white boneset, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats and is often found in marshy areas or along riverbanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
