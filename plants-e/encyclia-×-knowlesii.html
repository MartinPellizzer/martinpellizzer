Encyclia × knowlesii

Encyclia × knowlesii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia recurvata.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico, characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
