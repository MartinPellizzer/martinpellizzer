Eurya ciliata

Eurya ciliata, commonly known as the silky eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like India, China, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silky texture. It produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible.

Eurya ciliata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
