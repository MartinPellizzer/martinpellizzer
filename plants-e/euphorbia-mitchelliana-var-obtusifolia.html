Euphorbia mitchelliana var. obtusifolia

Euphorbia mitchelliana var. obtusifolia, commonly known as Mitchell's spurge, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
