Epimedium × purpureum

Epimedium × purpureum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a deciduous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that turn coppery red in the fall. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, hairy stalks. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its foliage and early blooming habit.
