Erysimum capitatum var. angustatum

Erysimum capitatum var. angustatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant thrives in well-drained, sunny soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

This variety is closely related to other Erysimum species, sharing similar growth habits and floral characteristics.
