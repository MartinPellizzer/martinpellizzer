Euphorbia hofstaetteri

Euphorbia hofstaetteri, commonly known as the Hofstaetter's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with dense, spiny leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Euphorbia hofstaetteri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
