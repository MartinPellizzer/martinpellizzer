Eugenia skutchii

Eugenia skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the humid lowland forests of Central America, particularly in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red when mature.

Eugenia skutchii is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in tropical ecosystems.
