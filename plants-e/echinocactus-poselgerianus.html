Echinocactus poselgerianus

Echinocactus poselgerianus, commonly known as the Poselgerianus cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its cylindrical stem and spiny, ribbed structure. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the top of the plant.

This species is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
