Elatostema sinense

Elatostema sinense, commonly known as the Chinese snowflake plant, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, white flowers. Native to East Asia, particularly China, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.
