Eriogonum microtheca var. microtheca

Eriogonum microtheca var. microtheca, commonly known as the smallleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, nut-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in regions with low annual rainfall.
