Euphorbia heteropodum var. heteropodum

Euphorbia heteropodum var. heteropodum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features succulent, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in rocky or disturbed areas.
