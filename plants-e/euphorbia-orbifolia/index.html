Euphorbia Orbifolia

Euphorbia orbifolia is a striking succulent native to southern Africa known for its unique orb-shaped leaves. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its vibrant green foliage and occasional bright yellow flowers add visual interest to any landscape. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap that can cause skin irritation, requiring careful handling. With its compact growth habit and low maintenance needs, Euphorbia orbifolia is both a fascinating and rewarding plant for gardeners.
