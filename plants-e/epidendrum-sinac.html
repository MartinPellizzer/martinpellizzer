Epidendrum sinac

Epidendrum sinac, commonly known as the Sinac Epidendrum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Epidendrum sinac is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
