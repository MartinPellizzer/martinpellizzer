Eminium spiculatum var. tigrinum

Eminium spiculatum var. tigrinum, commonly known as the tiger's eye plant, is a striking succulent characterized by its unique, striped foliage.

The leaves are fleshy and thick, typically arranged in a rosette formation, and display a vibrant mix of green, orange, and red hues. Its variegated pattern resembles the stripes of a tiger, giving it its common name. The plant is native to arid regions and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

It is prized for its ornamental appeal and ability to store water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
