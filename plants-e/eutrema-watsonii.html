Eutrema watsonii

Eutrema watsonii, commonly known as the Watson's iceplant, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a succulent species that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and saline environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the winter months. Its fleshy leaves store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry conditions.

Eutrema watsonii is valued for its resilience and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
