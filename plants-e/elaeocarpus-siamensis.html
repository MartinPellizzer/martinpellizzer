Elaeocarpus siamensis

Elaeocarpus siamensis, commonly known as the Siam elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential ecological roles.
