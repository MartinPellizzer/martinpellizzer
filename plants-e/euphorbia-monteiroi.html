Euphorbia monteiroi

Euphorbia monteiroi, commonly known as the Monteiro's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
