Eugenia muelleri

Eugenia muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's clove, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in culinary applications. It typically grows in coastal areas and has glossy, narrow leaves that are dark green in color.

Eugenia muelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
