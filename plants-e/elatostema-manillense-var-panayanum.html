Elatostema manillense var. panayanum

Elatostema manillense var. panayanum, commonly known as the Philippine stellaria, is a small herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, hairy leaves.
