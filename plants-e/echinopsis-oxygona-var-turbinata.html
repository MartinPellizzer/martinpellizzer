Echinopsis oxygona var. turbinata

Echinopsis oxygona var. turbinata, commonly known as the Turbinated Hedgehog Cactus, is a cacti species native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, spiny areoles. The plant produces large, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance and unique features, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
