Eugenia longibracteata

Eugenia longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, edible berry that is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
