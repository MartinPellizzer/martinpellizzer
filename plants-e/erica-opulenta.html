Erica opulenta

Erica opulenta, commonly known as the showy heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. Erica opulenta typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to form compact, attractive shrubs.
