Ervatamia parviflora

Ervatamia parviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ervatamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
