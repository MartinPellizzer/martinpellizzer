Euphorbia fortuita

Euphorbia fortuita, commonly known as the lucky euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded shape with fleshy, green stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has a milky sap that can be toxic if ingested, so care should be taken when handling it. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
