Ectadiopsis nigritana

Ectadiopsis nigritana, commonly known as the black-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking dark green to black foliage. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
