Epipremnum grandifolium

Epipremnum grandifolium, commonly known as the giant arrowhead plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its rapid growth rate makes it an excellent choice for vertical gardening or as a hanging plant.

Epipremnum grandifolium is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a popular houseplant.
