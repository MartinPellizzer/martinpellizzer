Elatostema zhengyuanum

Elatostema zhengyuanum, commonly known as the Zhengyuan elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan. The plant is a perennial herb with small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

The species is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists studying plant diversity in the region.
