Echium modestum var. rifanum

Echium modestum var. rifanum, commonly known as the Rif evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of violet or purple flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in Mediterranean flora.
