Eragrostis trachyantha

Eragrostis trachyantha, commonly known as the red grass or red oat grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, reddish-brown inflorescence that becomes more prominent as the plant matures. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when young.

Eragrostis trachyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in some traditional medicinal practices.
