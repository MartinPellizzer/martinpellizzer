Elephantopus tomentosus f. rotundatus

Elephantopus tomentosus f. rotundatus, commonly known as the rounded elephantopus, is a variant of the elephantopus tomentosus species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rounded, broad leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, and it is often used in ornamental gardening.

The f. rotundatus form is distinguished by its unique leaf shape, which sets it apart from other forms of the species.
