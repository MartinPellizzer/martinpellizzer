Erica goatcheriana var. goatcheriana

Erica goatcheriana var. goatcheriana, commonly known as the Goatcher's heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is typically found in coastal fynbos vegetation and is adapted to the region's Mediterranean climate.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
