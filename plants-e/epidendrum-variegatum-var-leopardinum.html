Epidendrum variegatum var. leopardinum

Epidendrum variegatum var. leopardinum, commonly known as the Leopard Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green foliage with distinctive yellow and red markings resembling a leopard's spots. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
