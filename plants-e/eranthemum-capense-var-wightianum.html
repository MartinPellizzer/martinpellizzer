Eranthemum capense var. wightianum

Eranthemum capense var. wightianum, commonly known as the Wight's eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
