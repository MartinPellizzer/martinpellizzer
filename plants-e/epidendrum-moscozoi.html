Epidendrum moscozoi

Epidendrum moscozoi, commonly known as the Moscozoi Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in humid environments. Its leaves are typically elongated and glossy, adapted to capture moisture from the air.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
