Elatostema megaphyllum

Elatostema megaphyllum, commonly known as the giant leaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is notable for its striking foliage and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
