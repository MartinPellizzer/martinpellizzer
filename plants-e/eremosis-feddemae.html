Eremosis feddemae

Eremosis feddemae, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique evergreen conifer native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flattened, scale-like leaves and its overall appearance that resembles a cypress tree. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several centuries, making it a long-lived species. It is adapted to the arid and harsh conditions of its native habitat, with deep roots that help it access water.

The Socotra cypress is also notable for its ecological importance and is considered a key species in the island's unique ecosystem.
