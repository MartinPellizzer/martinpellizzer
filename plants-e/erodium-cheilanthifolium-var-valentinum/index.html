Erodium Cheilanthifolium Var. Valentinum

Erodium cheilanthifolium var. valentinum is a striking plant known for its vibrant red and purple flowers. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, often found along the California coast. This variety is a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its hardy nature. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a favorite among gardeners and conservationists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare and requires specific conditions to flourish.
