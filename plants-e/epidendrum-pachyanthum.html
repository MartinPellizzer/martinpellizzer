Epidendrum pachyanthum

Epidendrum pachyanthum, commonly known as the golden lips orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with distinctive red-brown lips, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum pachyanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
