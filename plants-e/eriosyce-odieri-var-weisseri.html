Eriosyce odieri var. weisseri

Eriosyce odieri var. weisseri, commonly known as the Weisser's rock cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape with a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
