Erucaria rupestris

Erucaria rupestris, commonly known as the rock erucaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, succulent shrub that typically grows in rocky, arid environments. The plant features fleshy, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a hardy species in harsh climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for study in xerophytic plant ecology.
