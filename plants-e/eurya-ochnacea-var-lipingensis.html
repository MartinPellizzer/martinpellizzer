Eurya ochnacea var. lipingensis

Eurya ochnacea var. lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping evergreen holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Guangxi and Guangdong provinces. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a glossy surface and a leathery texture.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
