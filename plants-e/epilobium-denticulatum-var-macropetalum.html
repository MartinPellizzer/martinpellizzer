Epilobium denticulatum var. macropetalum

Epilobium denticulatum var. macropetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as along riverbanks and in wet meadows. The plant features showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have serrated edges, while its stems are often covered in fine hairs.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
