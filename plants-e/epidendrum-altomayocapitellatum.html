Epidendrum altomayocapitellatum

Epidendrum altomayocapitellatum, commonly known as the Mayan helmet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces dense clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinct helmet-shaped lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
