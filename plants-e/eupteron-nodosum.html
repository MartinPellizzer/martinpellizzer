Eupteron nodosum

Eupteron nodosum, commonly known as the nodding euperteron, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its bell-shaped, nodding flowers that are typically blue or violet in color.
