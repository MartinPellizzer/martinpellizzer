Elytrigia spicata

Elytrigia spicata, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Elytrigia spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
