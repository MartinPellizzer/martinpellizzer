Eurya fangii var. megaphylla

Eurya fangii var. megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaf eurya, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often larger than those of the typical species. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
