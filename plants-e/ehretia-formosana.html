Ehretia formosana

Ehretia formosana, commonly known as the Formosan ehretia, is a small evergreen tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
