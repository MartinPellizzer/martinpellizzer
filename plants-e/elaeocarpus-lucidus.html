Elaeocarpus lucidus

Elaeocarpus lucidus, commonly known as the shining elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits that are edible for certain bird species. Its wood is hard and durable, making it valuable for construction and furniture.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitats by providing food and shelter for various wildlife.
