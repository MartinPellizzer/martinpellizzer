Epidendrum labiatum var. mossiae

Epidendrum labiatum var. mossiae, commonly known as the Moss's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features erect, branching stems that bear numerous showy, violet to purple flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture to retain moisture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
