Eugenia ixoroides

Eugenia ixoroides, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenia ixoroides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
