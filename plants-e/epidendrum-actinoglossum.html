Epidendrum actinoglossum

Epidendrum actinoglossum, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum actinoglossum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
