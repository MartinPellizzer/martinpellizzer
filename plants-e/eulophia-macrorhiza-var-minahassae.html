Eulophia macrorhiza var. minahassae

Eulophia macrorhiza var. minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa eulophia, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with multiple flowers in a loose cluster. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
