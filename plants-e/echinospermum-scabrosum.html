Echinospermum scabrosum

Echinospermum scabrosum, commonly known as the roughhead sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, bristly stems and leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, typical of the sunflower family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Echinospermum scabrosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
