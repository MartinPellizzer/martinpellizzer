Epimedium davidi var. hunanense

Epimedium davidi var. hunanense, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, erect pedicels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and early blooming habit.
