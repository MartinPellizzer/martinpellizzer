Echinodorus × schlueteri

Echinodorus × schlueteri, commonly known as the Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length, providing dense coverage in freshwater aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular CO₂ supplementation for optimal growth. It is known for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nitrates and other nutrients.

Echinodorus × schlueteri is often used in planted tanks for both aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
