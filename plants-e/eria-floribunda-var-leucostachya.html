Eria floribunda var. leucostachya

Eria floribunda var. leucostachya, commonly known as the white-stemmed orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often hairy along the edges. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouse cultivation.
