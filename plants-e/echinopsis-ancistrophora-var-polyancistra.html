Echinopsis ancistrophora var. polyancistra

Echinopsis ancistrophora var. polyancistra, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a columnar cactus with a short, thick stem and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has a spiny exterior with areoles that bear both spines and flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens.
