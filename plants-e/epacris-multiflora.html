Epacris multiflora

Epacris multiflora, commonly known as the mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in cool, moist environments such as heathlands and forested areas. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense canopy that supports various wildlife.

Epacris multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
