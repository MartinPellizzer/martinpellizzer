Echeveria gracillima

Echeveria gracillima, commonly known as the slender echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a textured surface. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its compact growth habit and low maintenance make it an ideal plant for both novice and experienced gardeners.
