Erodium juliani

Erodium juliani, commonly known as the coastal bird's-beak, is a flowering plant native to southern California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has tiny, ovate leaves and produces small, dry fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils along coastal areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Erodium juliani is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
