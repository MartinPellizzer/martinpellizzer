Eurotia pungens

Eurotia pungens, commonly known as the spiked spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters along the stem. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Eurotia pungens is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
