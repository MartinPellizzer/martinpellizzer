Euodia concinna

Euodia concinna, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces a fragrant resin, which has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it valuable in both traditional and modern pharmacological research.

Euodia concinna is also appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
