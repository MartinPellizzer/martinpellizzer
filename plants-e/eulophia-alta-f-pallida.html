Eulophia alta f. pallida

Eulophia alta f. pallida, commonly known as the pale-flowered eulophia, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, erect stems that bear large, showy flowers with pale to white petals and sepals. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy leaves and a distinctive, elongated inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its elegant appearance.
