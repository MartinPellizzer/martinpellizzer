Echinopsis arachnacantha var. vallegrandensis

Echinopsis arachnacantha var. vallegrandensis, commonly known as the Vallegrandensis cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its columnar stem and spiny, rib-like structures that help reduce water loss. The plant produces large, showy white or pink flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for desert environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
