Elytraria bissei

Elytraria bissei, commonly known as the slender panic grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy surface.

Elytraria bissei is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
