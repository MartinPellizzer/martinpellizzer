Eberhardtia tonkinensis

Eberhardtia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin evergreen.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its evergreen nature, with dense foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
