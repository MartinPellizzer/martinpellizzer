Encyclia fehlingii

Encyclia fehlingii, commonly known as the Fehling's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the highlands of Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, erect growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a darker throat. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, humid environments.

Encyclia fehlingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
