Euklisia albida

Euklisia albida, commonly known as the white euklisia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Euklisia albida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
