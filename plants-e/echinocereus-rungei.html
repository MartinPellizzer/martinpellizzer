Echinocereus rungei

Echinocereus rungei, commonly known as the Runge's hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, spiny cactus that typically grows to a height of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in white or pale blue spines. Its flowers, which are typically yellow or orange, bloom in the spring and are relatively short-lived.

Echinocereus rungei is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
