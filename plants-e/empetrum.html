Empetrum

Empetrum, commonly known as crowberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its small, dense clusters of dark blue berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It plays an important ecological role by providing food for birds and small mammals, and it is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
