Eriocereus martianus

Eriocereus martianus, commonly known as the Martian night-blooming cactus, is a species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a flowering cactus that produces fragrant, white flowers that open at night. The plant has a tall, spiny stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. Its flowers are typically short-lived, lasting only a single night.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
