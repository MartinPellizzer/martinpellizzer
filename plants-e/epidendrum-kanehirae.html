Epidendrum kanehirae

Epidendrum kanehirae, commonly known as the Kanehira's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in arid environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-shaped, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

Epidendrum kanehirae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
