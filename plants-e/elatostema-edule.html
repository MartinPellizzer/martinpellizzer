Elatostema edule

Elatostema edule, commonly known as the edible elatostema, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of India and Indonesia. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The leaves and stems are edible and are sometimes used in local cuisines for their mild, slightly acidic flavor.
