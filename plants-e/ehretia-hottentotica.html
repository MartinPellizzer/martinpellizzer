Ehretia hottentotica

Ehretia hottentotica, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
