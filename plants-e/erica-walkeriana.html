Erica walkeriana

Erica walkeriana, commonly known as the Walker's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica walkeriana typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
