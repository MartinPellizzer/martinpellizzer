Eremostachys acaulis

Eremostachys acaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed eremostachys, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems and dense clusters of small, often blue or purple flowers. The plant has adapted to harsh environments with its water-storing stems and reduced leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Eremostachys acaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
