Elatostema laetevirens

Elatostema laetevirens, commonly known as the happy green elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema laetevirens is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.
