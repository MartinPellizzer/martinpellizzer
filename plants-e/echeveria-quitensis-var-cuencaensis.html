Echeveria quitensis var. cuencaensis

Echeveria quitensis var. cuencaensis, commonly known as the Cuenca Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
