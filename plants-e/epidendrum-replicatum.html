Epidendrum replicatum

Epidendrum replicatum, commonly known as the Mexican flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features striking, bright red or orange flowers with a yellow throat, often appearing in clusters along its pendulous stems. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum replicatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
