Eriospermum horizontale

Eriospermum horizontale, commonly known as the horizontal eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a low-growing, spreading habit with fleshy, cylindrical stems that lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and covered in fine, hair-like structures.

Eriospermum horizontale is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
