Epidendrum pallatangae

Epidendrum pallatangae, commonly known as the Pallatanga Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, with flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Epidendrum pallatangae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
