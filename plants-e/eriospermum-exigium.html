Eriospermum exigium

Eriospermum exigium, commonly known as the narrowleaf eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Eriospermum exigium is typically found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its drought-tolerant nature.
