Epipremnum moszkowskii

Epipremnum moszkowskii, commonly known as the Moskowii philodendron, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies.

Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it well-suited for hanging baskets or trellises.
