Euryops rehmannii

Euryops rehmannii, commonly known as the coastal dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

Euryops rehmannii is well adapted to coastal environments, with a deep root system that helps it withstand strong winds and periodic salt spray.
