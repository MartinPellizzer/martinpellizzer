Elatostema suzukii

Elatostema suzukii, commonly known as the Japanese snowflake plant, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, white, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. It is native to Japan and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Elatostema suzukii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
