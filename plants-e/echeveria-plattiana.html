Echeveria plattiana

Echeveria plattiana, commonly known as the Plattiana Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its compact shape.

Echeveria plattiana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
