Elionurus latiflorus

Elionurus latiflorus, commonly known as the broadflower elionurus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a hairy texture.

Elionurus latiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
