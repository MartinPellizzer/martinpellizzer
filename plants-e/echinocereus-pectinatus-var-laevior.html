Echinocereus pectinatus var. laevior

Echinocereus pectinatus var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid climates.
