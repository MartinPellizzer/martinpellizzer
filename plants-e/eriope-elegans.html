Eriope elegans

Eriope elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetplant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Eriope elegans thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
