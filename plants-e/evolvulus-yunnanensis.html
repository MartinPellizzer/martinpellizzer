Evolvulus yunnanensis

Evolvulus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan morning glory, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Evolvulus yunnanensis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
