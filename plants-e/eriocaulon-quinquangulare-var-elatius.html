Eriocaulon quinquangulare var. elatius

Eriocaulon quinquangulare var. elatius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded soils, often in marshy or swampy habitats.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated leaves and is found in various regions of Southeast Asia.
