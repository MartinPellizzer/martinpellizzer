Elaeocarpus glabripetalus var. alatus

Elaeocarpus glabripetalus var. alatus, commonly known as the winged elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glabrous leaves and distinctive winged fruits. The plant belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is known for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit morphology. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood in some regions.
