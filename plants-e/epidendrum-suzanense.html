Epidendrum suzanense

Epidendrum suzanense, commonly known as the Suzan's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with numerous flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum suzanense is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
