Eulalia speciosa var. modesta

Eulalia speciosa var. modesta, commonly known as the modesta grass or dwarf silvergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features slender, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements due to their ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for xeriscaping and sustainable landscaping.
