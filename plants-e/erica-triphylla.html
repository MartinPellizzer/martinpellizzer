Erica triphylla

Erica triphylla, commonly known as the three-leaf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. Erica triphylla thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
