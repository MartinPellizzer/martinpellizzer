Eriochilus scaber subsp. orbifolius

Eriochilus scaber subsp.

orbifolius, commonly known as the Woolly Sun-orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southeastern Australia. It features dense, woolly grey-green foliage that is often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and upright, and the stems are often prostrate or spreading.

This subspecies is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open heathlands and coastal areas.
