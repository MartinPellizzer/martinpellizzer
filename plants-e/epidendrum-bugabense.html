Epidendrum bugabense

Epidendrum bugabense, commonly known as the Bugabens Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum bugabense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
