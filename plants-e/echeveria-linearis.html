Echeveria linearis

Echeveria linearis, commonly known as the linear echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of narrow, linear leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.

Echeveria linearis is popular as a decorative plant due to its compact size and aesthetic appeal.
