Eugenia rizzoana

Eugenia rizzoana, commonly known as the Rizzo's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy dark green surface.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature, attracting various bird species.
