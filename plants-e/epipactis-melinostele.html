Epipactis melinostele

Epipactis melinostele, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in woodland and shaded areas. It has a complex root system and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, humus-rich soils.

Epipactis melinostele is often found in association with other orchids and is a notable species in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological preferences.
