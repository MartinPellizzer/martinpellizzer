Exochaenium alatum

Exochaenium alatum, commonly known as the winged grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in small, three-lobed capsules, which are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Exochaenium alatum is often found in open, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
