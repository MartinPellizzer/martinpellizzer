Egenolfia × yunnanensis

Egenolfia × yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan yew, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It belongs to the Taxaceae family and is characterized by its coniferous appearance with scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, reddish berries that contain toxic seeds, making it important to handle with care. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
