Ericoila quadrifaria

Ericoila quadrifaria, commonly known as the four-leaf erica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial shade.

Ericoila quadrifaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, low-growing mats.
