Echinopsis aurea var. quinensis

Echinopsis aurea var. quinensis, commonly known as the Quinensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to yellow spines and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

It is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
