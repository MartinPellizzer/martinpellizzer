Etaeria

Etaeria, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals, often followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Etaeria is native to the Americas and is commonly found in wetlands and disturbed soils.

It plays a role in both ecological systems and has been used in traditional medicine.
