Euphorbia succulenta

Euphorbia succulenta, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green or blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

However, it contains toxic sap and should be handled with care.
