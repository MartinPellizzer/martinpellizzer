Euphorbia ebracteolata var. coreana

Euphorbia ebracteolata var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Korea.

It features a short, upright stem covered in small, green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green cyathia, which are its distinctive flower-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
