Episteira

Episteira, commonly known as the silver leaf or velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Episteira thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
