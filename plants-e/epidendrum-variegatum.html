Epidendrum variegatum

Epidendrum variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its strikingly variegated foliage, featuring a mix of green, white, and yellow tones. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow.

Epidendrum variegatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
