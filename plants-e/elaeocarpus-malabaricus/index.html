Elaeocarpus Malabaricus

Malabar elaeocarpus, scientifically known as Elaeocarpus malabaricus, is a rare and ancient tree species native to the Western Ghats of India. This evergreen tree is characterized by its glossy dark green leaves and striking white flowers that bloom in clusters. It plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species. Due to deforestation and habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve this unique plant for future generations.
