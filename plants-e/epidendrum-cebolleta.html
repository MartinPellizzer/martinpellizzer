Epidendrum cebolleta

Epidendrum cebolleta, commonly known as the small onion orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and often grow close to the soil surface.

Epidendrum cebolleta is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
