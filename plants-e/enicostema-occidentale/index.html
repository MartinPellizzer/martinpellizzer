Enicostema Occidentale

Enicostema occidentale is a unique plant native to western regions of Australia. It is known for its striking silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known gem in the world of horticulture.
