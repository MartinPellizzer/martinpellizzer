Epipactis varians

Epipactis varians, commonly known as the variable helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It thrives in woodland and shaded areas, often growing on the forest floor.

Epipactis varians is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
