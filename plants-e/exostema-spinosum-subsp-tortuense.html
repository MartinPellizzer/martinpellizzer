Exostema spinosum subsp. tortuense

Exostema spinosum subsp.

tortuense, commonly known as the twisted spiny exostema, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions of South America. It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in spines or bristles.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
