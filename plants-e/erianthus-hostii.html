Erianthus hostii

Erianthus hostii, commonly known as the golden bamboo or giant bamboo, is a tall, grass-like plant native to the Caribbean.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often bright yellow or golden in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant grows in dense clumps and can reach heights of up to 10 meters. It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
