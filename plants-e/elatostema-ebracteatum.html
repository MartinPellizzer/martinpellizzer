Elatostema ebracteatum

Elatostema ebracteatum, commonly known as the small-leaved elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a short, stout stem. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often with white or pale green petals.

Elatostema ebracteatum is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats, which helps in soil stabilization.
