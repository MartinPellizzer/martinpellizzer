Excoecaria biglandulosa var. aucuparia

Excoecaria biglandulosa var. aucuparia, commonly known as the African rubber plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by specialized structures called cyathia. It is native to tropical Africa and thrives in warm, humid environments.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental leaves and relatively low maintenance requirements.
