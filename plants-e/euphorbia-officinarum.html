Euphorbia officinarum

Euphorbia officinarum, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, woody stem with spines and produces yellow flowers in clusters. The plant has a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
