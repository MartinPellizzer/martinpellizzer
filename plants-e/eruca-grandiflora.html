Eruca grandiflora

Eruca grandiflora, commonly known as broadleaf rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features pinnately compound leaves with a peppery aroma, and its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in racemes. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides. It is valued in both culinary and ornamental horticulture for its edible leaves and vibrant blooms.

Eruca grandiflora is also known for its ability to attract beneficial insects such as bees and ladybugs.
