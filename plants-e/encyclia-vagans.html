Encyclia vagans

Encyclia vagans, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomes and pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or brown markings. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Encyclia vagans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
