Excoecaria agallocha var. dallachyana

Excoecaria agallocha var. dallachyana, commonly known as the Dallachy's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
