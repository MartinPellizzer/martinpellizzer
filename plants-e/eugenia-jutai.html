Eugenia jutai

Eugenia jutai, commonly known as the Jutai myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Amazon rainforest of Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, bright red berries. Eugenia jutai is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic foliage and fruit.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
