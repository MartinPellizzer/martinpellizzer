Elatostema nanchuanense var. schleroceras

Elatostema nanchuanense var. schleroceras, commonly known as the rock elatostema, is a rare variant of the Elatostema genus native to China.

It is characterized by its small, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters.
