Euphorbia yamashitae

Euphorbia yamashitae, commonly known as Yamashita's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are surrounded by colorful bracts, making it visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
