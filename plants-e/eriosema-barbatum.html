Eriosema barbatum

Eriosema barbatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its small, hairy pods contain seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural distribution.

Eriosema barbatum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
