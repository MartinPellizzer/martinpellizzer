Emilia kikuyorum

Emilia kikuyorum, commonly known as the Kikuyu daisy, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Emilia kikuyorum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
