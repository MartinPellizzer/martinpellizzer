Encyclia cochleata var. triandra

Encyclia cochleata var. triandra, commonly known as the triandra orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive greenish throat. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
