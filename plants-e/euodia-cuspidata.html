Euodia cuspidata

Euodia cuspidata, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood and fragrant leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, leathery fruits. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Euodia cuspidata is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in its native habitats.
