Eupatorium clibadioides

Eupatorium clibadioides, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and stream banks. The plant features showy, blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like butterflies and bees.
