Epidendrum atropurpureum var. roseum

Epidendrum atropurpureum var. roseum, commonly known as the Purple-veined Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, velvety leaves that are dark green with prominent purple veins. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
