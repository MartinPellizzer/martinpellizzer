Elatostema janowskyi

Elatostema janowskyi, commonly known as the small-leaved elatostema, is a member of the Urticaceae family, characterized by its small, often succulent leaves.

The flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in inflorescences, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits.
