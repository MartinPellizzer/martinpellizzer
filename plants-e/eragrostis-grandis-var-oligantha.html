Eragrostis grandis var. oligantha

Eragrostis grandis var. oligantha, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its drooping, fountain-like growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
