Epipactis rubiginosa var. stenopetala

Epipactis rubiginosa var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist woodlands and rocky habitats. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and moths.

The orchid has a complex root system and is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
