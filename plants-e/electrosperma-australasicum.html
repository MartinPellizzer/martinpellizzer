Electrosperma australasicum

Electrosperma australasicum, commonly known as the Australian star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or brown in color, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Electrosperma australasicum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands.
