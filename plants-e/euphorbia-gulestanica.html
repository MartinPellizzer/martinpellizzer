Euphorbia gulestanica

Euphorbia gulestanica, commonly known as the Golestan spurge, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species that thrives in dry conditions.

Euphorbia gulestanica is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, where it forms dense, low-growing clumps.
