Euodia octandra

Euodia octandra, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, reddish-brown fruits that contain aromatic seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Euodia octandra is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
