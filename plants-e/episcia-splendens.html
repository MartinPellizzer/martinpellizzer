Episcia splendens

Episcia splendens, commonly known as the flame violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the humid tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Episcia splendens is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
