Eulophia brunneorubra

Eulophia brunneorubra, commonly known as the brown-red eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or along stream banks. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small, clustered in racemes, and exhibit a range of colors from brown to reddish-orange.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
