Eria scortechinii

Eria scortechinii, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Eria, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its relatively easy care requirements.

Eria scortechinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
