Euodia phanerophlebia

Euodia phanerophlebia, commonly known as the glossy ash or Chinese ash, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lanceolate leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elliptical, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
