Epidendrum uleinanodes

Epidendrum uleinanodes, commonly known as the Uleinano Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a unique, intricate structure that attracts pollinators.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
