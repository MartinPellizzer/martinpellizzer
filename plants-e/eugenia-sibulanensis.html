Eugenia sibulanensis

Eugenia sibulanensis, commonly known as the Sibulan eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the provinces of Siquijor and Negros Oriental. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
