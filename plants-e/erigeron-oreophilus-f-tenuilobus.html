Erigeron oreophilus f. tenuilobus

Erigeron oreophilus f. tenuilobus, commonly known as the slender-lobed blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, grass-like leaves and small, blue to purple flowering heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It is native to the eastern United States and is often found in the Appalachian region.
