Eurya greenwoodii

Eurya greenwoodii, commonly known as the Chinese holly or dwarf holly, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a hedge.

Eurya greenwoodii thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, preferring cooler climates.
