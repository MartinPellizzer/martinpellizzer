Echinocactus robustus var. monstruosus

Echinocactus robustus var. monstruosus, commonly known as the giant hedgehog cactus, is a striking cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its spiny structure. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

The variety 'monstruosus' is noted for its particularly robust and sprawling growth habit.
