Eranthemum palatiferum var. levicapsa

Eranthemum palatiferum var. levicapsa, commonly known as the narrow-capsule eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers with tubular corollas. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant flower colors.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
