Erysimum greenei

Erysimum greenei, commonly known as Greene's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species with cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually bright yellow or orange, with four petals arranged in a cross shape.

Erysimum greenei is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments.
