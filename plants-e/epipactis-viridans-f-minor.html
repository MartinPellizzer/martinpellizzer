Epipactis viridans f. minor

Epipactis viridans f. minor, commonly known as the small green helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of dark green, fleshy leaves that help it survive in shaded environments. The plant produces slender, erect flowering stems that bear small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is often found in association with other orchid species.

The botanical features of this orchid make it a notable species in the Orchidaceae family.
