Epidendrum chioneum

Epidendrum chioneum, commonly known as the snow orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that resemble snowflakes. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom during the cooler months.

Epidendrum chioneum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a greenhouse or indoor setting.
