Euphorbia estevesii

Euphorbia estevesii, commonly known as the Cape Verde euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It features a distinctive, branched stem that is often covered in a waxy, pale green or yellowish coating. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Euphorbia estevesii is known for its striking appearance and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
