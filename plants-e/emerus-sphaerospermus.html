Emerus sphaerospermus

Emerus sphaerospermus, commonly known as the globe-spore grass, is a unique flowering plant characterized by its distinctive spherical seed heads.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is native to specific regions in southern Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that mature into round, spore-bearing structures.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
