Eriosyce chilensis var. chilensis

Eriosyce chilensis var. chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean eriosyce, is a succulent plant native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that store water. The plant features small, pale green to blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It produces yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
