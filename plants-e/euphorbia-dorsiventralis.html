Euphorbia dorsiventralis

Euphorbia dorsiventralis, commonly known as the warty euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, flattened, ribbed stems that are often covered in small, warty protrusions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in rock gardens and succulent collections.
