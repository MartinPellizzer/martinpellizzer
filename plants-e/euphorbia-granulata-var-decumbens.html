Euphorbia granulata var. decumbens

Euphorbia granulata var. decumbens, commonly known as the decumbent granulate spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a low-growing, spreading habit with fleshy, ribbed stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It has adapted to arid environments with succulent leaves that store water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
