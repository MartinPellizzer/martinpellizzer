Eugenia biflora var. wallenii

Eugenia biflora var. wallenii, commonly known as the Wallenii cherry, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by edible, bright red berries. These berries are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
