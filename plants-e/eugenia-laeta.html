Eugenia laeta

Eugenia laeta, commonly known as the white myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia laeta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
