Echinodorus tenellus var. parvulus

Echinodorus tenellus var. parvulus, commonly known as the dwarf echinodorus, is a compact aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle metallic sheen. This variety thrives in moderate to high light conditions and prefers soft to moderately hard water with a neutral to slightly acidic pH. It is often used in planted aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help oxygenate the water.

The plant grows slowly and is relatively easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for freshwater hobbyists.
