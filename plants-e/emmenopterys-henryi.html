Emmenopterys henryi

Emmenopterys henryi, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, dark green leaves that are often veined with a striking yellow or white color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which appear in clusters during the spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Emmenopterys henryi is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
