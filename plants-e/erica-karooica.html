Erica karooica

Erica karooica, commonly known as the Karoo heath, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is well adapted to dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. Its dense growth habit makes it an important component of the fynbos vegetation.

Erica karooica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
