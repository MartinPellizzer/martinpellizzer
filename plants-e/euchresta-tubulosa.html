Euchresta tubulosa

Euchresta tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular euphorbia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its unique floral structures and milky sap. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a suitable ornamental plant in xeriscaped gardens.
