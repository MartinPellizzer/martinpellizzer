Elionurus purpureus

Elionurus purpureus, commonly known as the purple grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Elionurus purpureus is often used in ornamental gardening for its colorful blooms and drought tolerance.
