Elatostema rostratum var. rostratum

Elatostema rostratum var. rostratum, commonly known as the narrowleaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
