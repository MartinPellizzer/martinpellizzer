Echinopsis acanthura subsp. faustiana

Echinopsis acanthura subsp.

faustiana, commonly known as the Faustiana moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to South America. It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, spiny areoles.

The plant produces striking white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and blooming habit make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
