Eremiris biglumis

Eremiris biglumis, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and small, narrow leaves that are adapted to conserve water. Eremiris biglumis is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.
