Eupatorium urubambense var. mantarense

Eupatorium urubambense var. mantarense, commonly known as the Mantarene eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stem and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
