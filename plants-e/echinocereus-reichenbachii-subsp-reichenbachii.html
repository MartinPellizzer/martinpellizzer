Echinocereus reichenbachii subsp. reichenbachii

Echinocereus reichenbachii subsp.

reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is a low-growing, spine-tipped cactus with a compact, globular shape.

The plant features pale green to bluish-green stems that are often covered in white, waxy hairs. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to orange, blooming in spring or early summer.

This subspecies is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
