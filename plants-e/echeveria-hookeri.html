Echeveria hookeri

Echeveria hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wool. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
