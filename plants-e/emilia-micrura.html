Emilia micrura

Emilia micrura, commonly known as the small emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Emilia micrura is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
