Epidendrum filicaule

Epidendrum filicaule, commonly known as the Sword Fern Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Epidendrum filicaule is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in a home setting.
