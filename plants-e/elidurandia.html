Elidurandia

Elidurandia, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a sweet fragrance. The leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the pale blossoms. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique flowering pattern makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
