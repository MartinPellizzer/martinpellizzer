Exochorda Grandiflora Var. Alberti

Alberti exochorda, scientifically known as Exochorda grandiflora var. alberti, is a striking ornamental shrub prized for its ornate floral displays. Native to China, this variety is celebrated for its showy white blooms that appear in early spring. Its compact growth habit and excellent cold tolerance make it a versatile choice for gardens in temperate climates. The plant features attractive foliage that provides visual interest throughout the growing season. With its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, Alberti exochorda is a popular choice among landscapers and garden enthusiasts.
