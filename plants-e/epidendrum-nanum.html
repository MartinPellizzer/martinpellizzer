Epidendrum nanum

Epidendrum nanum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Epidendrum nanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its compact size and ornamental value.
