Ebnerella wilcoxii

Ebnerella wilcoxii, commonly known as the Wilcox's cypress, is a rare and endangered species native to the island of Socotra.

It is a small, shrub-like plant with distinctive, scale-like leaves that are tightly clustered around its branches. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Ebnerella wilcoxii is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, exhibiting drought-resistant characteristics.
