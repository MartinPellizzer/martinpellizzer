Eriocephalus punctulatus var. brevifolius

Eriocephalus punctulatus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the dwarf African daisy, is a low-growing perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features small, daisy-like white flowers with yellow centers, often blooming in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are densely packed along its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
