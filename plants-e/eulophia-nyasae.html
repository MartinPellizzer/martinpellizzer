Eulophia nyasae

Eulophia nyasae, commonly known as the Malawi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the highlands of Malawi.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, which bloom during the rainy season. Eulophia nyasae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.

Its unique floral structure and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
