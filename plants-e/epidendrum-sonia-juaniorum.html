Epidendrum sonia-juaniorum

Epidendrum sonia-juaniorum, commonly known as the Sonia Juaniorum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features striking, long-lasting flowers that are typically pink or purple with a white throat, and it blooms during the summer months. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
