Epidendrum camargoi

Epidendrum camargoi, commonly known as the Camargue Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Epidendrum camargoi is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
