Eulophia friesii

Eulophia friesii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and leafy pseudobulbs. The plant produces erect, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Eulophia friesii thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
