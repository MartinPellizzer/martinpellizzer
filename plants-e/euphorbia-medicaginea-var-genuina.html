Euphorbia medicaginea var. genuina

Euphorbia medicaginea var. genuina, commonly known as the medicinal euphorbia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems with a distinctive greenish-gray bark and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.

Its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions makes it adaptable to various environments.
