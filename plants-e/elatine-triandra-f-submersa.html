Elatine triandra f. submersa

Elatine triandra f. submersa, commonly known as the submerged waterwort, is a aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is a submerged herb that typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, scale-like leaves that are adapted for underwater life. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or just at the water's surface.

This species is often found in ponds, lakes, and wetlands, where it plays a role in aquatic ecosystems.
