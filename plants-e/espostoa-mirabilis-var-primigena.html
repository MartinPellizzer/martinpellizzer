Espostoa mirabilis var. primigena

Espostoa mirabilis var. primigena, commonly known as the Giant Spider Plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers that emerge from a central spike, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and insects. Its unique structure allows it to collect rainwater in its leaf rosette, making it well adapted to humid, shaded environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
