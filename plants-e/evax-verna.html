Evax verna

Evax verna, commonly known as the spring gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Evax verna is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
