Erica mackayi var. watsonii

Erica mackayi var. watsonii, commonly known as the Watson's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
