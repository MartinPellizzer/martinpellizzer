Eugenia sasoana

Eugenia sasoana, commonly known as the Sasoana clove, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It produces small, reddish-brown fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

The essential oils extracted from its leaves and flowers are valued for their aromatic and therapeutic properties.
