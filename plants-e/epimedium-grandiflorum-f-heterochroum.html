Epimedium grandiflorum f. heterochroum

Epimedium grandiflorum f. heterochroum, commonly known as the variegated barrenwort, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It features rhizomatous growth and lance-shaped leaves with distinctive variegation, often displaying shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces showy, upright panicles of bright yellow flowers in early spring before the foliage emerges. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its unique foliage and early bloom make it a valuable addition to shade gardens.
