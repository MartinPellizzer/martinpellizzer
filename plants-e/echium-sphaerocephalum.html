Echium sphaerocephalum

Echium sphaerocephalum, commonly known as the globeflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, violet or blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Echium sphaerocephalum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
