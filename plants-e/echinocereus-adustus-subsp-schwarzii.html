Echinocereus adustus subsp. schwarzii

Echinocereus adustus subsp.

schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwerzii hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are typically green to bluish-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are often borne at the ends of the stems. It is a drought-tolerant plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in arid gardens and rockeries.
