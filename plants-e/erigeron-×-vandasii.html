Erigeron × vandasii

Erigeron × vandasii, commonly known as the hybrid fleabane, is a composite plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and roadsides, preferring full sun and well-drained soils. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its ability to spread and colonize new areas.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in the wild, though it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
