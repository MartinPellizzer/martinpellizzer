Euphorbia barbellata var. imaii

Euphorbia barbellata var. imaii, commonly known as the Imai's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
