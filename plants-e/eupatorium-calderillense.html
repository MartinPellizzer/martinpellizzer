Eupatorium calderillense

Eupatorium calderillense, commonly known as the Caldeira eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Eupatorium calderillense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
