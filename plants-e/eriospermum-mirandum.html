Eriospermum mirandum

Eriospermum mirandum, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orb-shaped flower heads that resemble tiny lanterns, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Eriospermum mirandum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
