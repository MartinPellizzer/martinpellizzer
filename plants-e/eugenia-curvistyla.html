Eugenia curvistyla

Eugenia curvistyla, commonly known as the curved-styled eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

Eugenia curvistyla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
