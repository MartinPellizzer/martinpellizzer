Eugenia kalahiensis

Eugenia kalahiensis, commonly known as the Kalahari eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Eugenia kalahiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
