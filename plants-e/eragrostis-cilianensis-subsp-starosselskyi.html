Eragrostis cilianensis subsp. starosselskyi

Eragrostis cilianensis subsp.

starosselskyi, commonly known as the Starosselsky's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
