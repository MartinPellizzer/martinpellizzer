Erica natalitia var. natalitia

Erica natalitia var. natalitia, commonly known as Natal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, dense clusters of flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It has needle-like leaves and is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Erica natalitia var. natalitia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
