Epidendrum montiargentatum

Epidendrum montiargentatum, commonly known as the silver mountain orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum montiargentatum is a popular choice for both indoor and greenhouse cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
