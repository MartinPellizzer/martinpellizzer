Eriocephalus tenuipes

Eriocephalus tenuipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriocephalus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
