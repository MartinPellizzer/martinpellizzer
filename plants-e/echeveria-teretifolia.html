Echeveria teretifolia

Echeveria teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
