Echinops delicatus

Echinops delicatus, commonly known as the delicate globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky seed heads that resemble small globes, which are a defining feature of the genus Echinops. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its unique appearance and ecological adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
