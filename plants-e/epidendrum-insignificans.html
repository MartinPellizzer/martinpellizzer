Epidendrum insignificans

Epidendrum insignificans, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its compact size and ease of cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
