Encephalartos macdonnellii

Encephalartos macdonnellii, commonly known as the Macdonnell's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically found in the Eastern Cape province. This cycad is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant produces both male and female cones, which are essential for its reproduction.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
