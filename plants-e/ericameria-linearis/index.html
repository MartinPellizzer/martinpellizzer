Ericameria Linearis

Ericameria linearis is a hardy shrub native to the western United States and northern Mexico. It is known for its slender, linear leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions highlights its adaptability. Ericameria linearis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
