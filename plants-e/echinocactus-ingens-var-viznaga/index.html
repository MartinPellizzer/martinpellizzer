Echinocactus Ingens Var. Viznaga

Echinocactus ingens var. viznaga is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its spiny, globular shape and vibrant green color, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. This variety is a natural mutation of the larger Echinocactus ingens, making it unique in appearance and care requirements. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens. Its beauty and resilience make it a standout addition to any collection of cacti.
