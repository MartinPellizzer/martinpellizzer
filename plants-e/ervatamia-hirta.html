Ervatamia hirta

Ervatamia hirta, commonly known as hairy ervatamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
