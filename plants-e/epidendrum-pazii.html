Epidendrum pazii

Epidendrum pazii, commonly known as the Pazii orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum pazii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
