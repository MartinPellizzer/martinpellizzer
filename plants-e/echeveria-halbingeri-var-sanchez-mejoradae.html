Echeveria halbingeri var. sanchez-mejoradae

Echeveria halbingeri var. sanchez-mejoradae, commonly known as the Sancheza or Sancheza Echeveria, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-gray to silver-green in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple-pink flowers on tall, branching stalks during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
