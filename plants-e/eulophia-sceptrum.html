Eulophia sceptrum

Eulophia sceptrum, commonly known as the royal eulophia, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, erect stem with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small, clustered blooms. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Eulophia sceptrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
