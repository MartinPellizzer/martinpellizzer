Echites obovatus

Echites obovatus, commonly known as the broad-leaved echites, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
