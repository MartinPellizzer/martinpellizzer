Eupatorium apollinairei

Eupatorium apollinairei, commonly known as the Apollinaire's eupatorium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the wetlands and marshy areas of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. Its leaves are typically opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.
