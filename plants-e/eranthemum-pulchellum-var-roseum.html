Eranthemum pulchellum var. roseum

Eranthemum pulchellum var. roseum, commonly known as the pink eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
