Elionurus latiflorus var. calvescens

Elionurus latiflorus var. calvescens, commonly known as the broadflower elionurus, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant grows in open grasslands and is often found in disturbed or sandy soils. It has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
