Epicycas Siamensis

Epicycas siamensis is a rare and ancient plant species native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is known for its unique, palm-like appearance. This plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and has been around for millions of years. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this living relic of prehistoric times.
