Euphorbia pithyusa var. bonifaciensis

Euphorbia pithyusa var. bonifaciensis, commonly known as the Bonifacius Spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, ribbed stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, with pale green to yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are the modified flower structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
