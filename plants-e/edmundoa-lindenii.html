Edmundoa lindenii

Edmundoa lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a complex inflorescence with multiple blooms arranged along a branched stem. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the nutrient-poor soils of its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
