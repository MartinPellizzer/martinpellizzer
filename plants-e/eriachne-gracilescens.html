Eriachne gracilescens

Eriachne gracilescens, commonly known as slender eriochloa, is a grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal dunes and heathlands. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow leaves that are usually hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Eriachne gracilescens is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
