Elatine hydropiper

Elatine hydropiper, commonly known as water pepper, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, rounded leaves that are often submerged or floating on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water bodies.

Elatine hydropiper is known for its ability to tolerate fluctuating water levels and is often found in nutrient-rich soils.
