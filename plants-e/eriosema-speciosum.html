Eriosema speciosum

Eriosema speciosum, commonly known as the ornate eriosema, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Eriosema speciosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
