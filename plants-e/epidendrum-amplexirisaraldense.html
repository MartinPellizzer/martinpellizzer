Epidendrum amplexirisaraldense

Epidendrum amplexirisaraldense, commonly known as the Saralda Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-shaped leaves that are often folded at the base. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
