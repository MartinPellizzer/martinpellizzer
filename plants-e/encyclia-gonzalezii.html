Encyclia gonzalezii

Encyclia gonzalezii, commonly known as the Gonzalez's Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-shaped leaves and clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia gonzalezii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
