Epidendrum cycnostachys

Epidendrum cycnostachys, commonly known as the orchid with the clustered flowers, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Its unique floral display makes it a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
