Encyclia ionocentra

Encyclia ionocentra, commonly known as the ionocentra orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces fragrant, yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. Encyclia ionocentra thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and conservation efforts.
