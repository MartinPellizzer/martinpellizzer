Eulophia congoensis

Eulophia congoensis, commonly known as the Congo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to central Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features a short rhizome with fleshy roots and produces erect, branching inflorescences. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Eulophia congoensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
