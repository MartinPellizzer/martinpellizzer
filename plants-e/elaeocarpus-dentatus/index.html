Elaeocarpus Dentatus

Elaeocarpus dentatus is a striking tropical tree known for its distinctive foliage and ornamental value. Native to regions across Southeast Asia and the Pacific, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its lance-shaped leaves and showy flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural gardens. The tree also produces small, edible fruits that are appreciated in local cuisines. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in global botanical discussions.
