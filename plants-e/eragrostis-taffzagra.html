Eragrostis taffzagra

Eragrostis taffzagra, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit, which makes it visually appealing in dry landscapes. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
