Ehretia namibiensis

Ehretia namibiensis, commonly known as the Namibian ehretia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
