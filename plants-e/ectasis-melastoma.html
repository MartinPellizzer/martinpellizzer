Ectasis melastoma

Ectasis melastoma, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough texture, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
