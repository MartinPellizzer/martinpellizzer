Eresda

Eresda, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions in Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Eresda is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in some areas.
