Etlingera elatior

Etlingera elatior, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

The flower structure includes a labellum that is highly specialized for attracting specific pollinators.
