Eugenia burkilliana var. garcinifolioides

Eugenia burkilliana var. garcinifolioides, commonly known as the broad-leafed eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often mistaken for those of Garcinia species. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
