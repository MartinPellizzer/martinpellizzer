Eragrostis riobrancensis

Eragrostis riobrancensis, commonly known as the Brazilian bluestem, is a grass species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and riparian zones. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale blue or greenish flowers.

Eragrostis riobrancensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife in its native environment.
