Eulophidium warneckeanum

Eulophidium warneckeanum, commonly known as the warnecke's eulophidium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eulophiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
