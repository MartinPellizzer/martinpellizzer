Epidendrum orthocaule

Epidendrum orthocaule, commonly known as the upright epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum orthocaule is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
