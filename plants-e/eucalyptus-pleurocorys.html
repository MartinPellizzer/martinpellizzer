Eucalyptus pleurocorys

Eucalyptus pleurocorys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallee, is a species of eucalyptus native to Western Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and smooth, often greyish bark. It produces small, globular flower clusters that are typically white or pink in color.

Eucalyptus pleurocorys is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
