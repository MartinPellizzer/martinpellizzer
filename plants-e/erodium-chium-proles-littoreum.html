Erodium chium proles littoreum

Erodium chium proles littoreum, commonly known as sea rocket or Mediterranean sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is an annual herb that thrives in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline. The plant features small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
