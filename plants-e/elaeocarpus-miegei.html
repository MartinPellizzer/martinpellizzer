Elaeocarpus miegei

Elaeocarpus miegei, commonly known as the Miege's elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, white or pale pink flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a thick, waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
