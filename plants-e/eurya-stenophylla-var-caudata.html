Eurya stenophylla var. caudata

Eurya stenophylla var. caudata, commonly known as the long-leaved eurya, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring cool and humid conditions. Its small, white flowers are followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
