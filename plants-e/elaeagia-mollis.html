Elaeagia mollis

Elaeagia mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, soft foliage that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often covered in fine hairs.

Elaeagia mollis is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal and sandy soils.
