Epidendrum altissimum

Epidendrum altissimum, commonly known as the sky orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence that can reach heights of up to 10 feet. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in clusters. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and cylindrical, with broad, leathery leaves that are arranged in two rows.

Epidendrum altissimum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
