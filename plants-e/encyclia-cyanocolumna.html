Encyclia cyanocolumna

Encyclia cyanocolumna, commonly known as the blue-columned orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
