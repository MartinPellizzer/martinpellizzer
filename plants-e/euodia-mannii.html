Euodia mannii

Euodia mannii, commonly known as the African laurustin, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its aromatic properties make it valuable in both culinary and medicinal applications.

Euodia mannii thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
