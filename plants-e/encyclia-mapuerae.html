Encyclia mapuerae

Encyclia mapuerae, commonly known as the Mapuera orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or yellowish flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia mapuerae is often found in cloud forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
