Eugenia websteri

Eugenia websteri, commonly known as the Webster's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. Its fruit, which is typically small and reddish, is edible and has a tart flavor.

Eugenia websteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
