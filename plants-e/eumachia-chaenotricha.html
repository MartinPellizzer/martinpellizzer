Eumachia chaenotricha

Eumachia chaenotricha, commonly known as the woolly thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a hairy, spiny stem and leaves with a rough, woolly texture. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in dense clusters.

Eumachia chaenotricha is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
