Erigeron conspicuus

Erigeron conspicuus, commonly known as the showy fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its daisy-like appearance, with white to pale blue ray florets surrounding yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, prairies, and disturbed soils. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that aid in its propagation.

Erigeron conspicuus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
