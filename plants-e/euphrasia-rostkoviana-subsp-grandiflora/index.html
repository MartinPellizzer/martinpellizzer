Euphrasia Rostkoviana Subsp. Grandiflora

Euphrasia rostkoviana subsp. grandiflora is a striking member of the Scrophulariaceae family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and woodland habitats. Its unique subspecies status highlights its distinct characteristics compared to other Euphrasia species. The plant is often admired for its delicate appearance and ecological significance. It plays an important role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
