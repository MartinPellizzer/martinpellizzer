Eriocnema fulva

Eriocnema fulva, commonly known as the red-flowered ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a robust, woody stem with a rough, fissured bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Eriocnema fulva is valued for its hard wood and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.
