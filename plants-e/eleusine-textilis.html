Eleusine textilis

Eleusine textilis, commonly known as African lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy texture.

Eleusine textilis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
