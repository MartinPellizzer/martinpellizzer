Epilobium × salcedoi

Epilobium × salcedoi, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Epilobium, which includes species commonly referred to as willow herbs or everlasting. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as streambanks and meadows. It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.
