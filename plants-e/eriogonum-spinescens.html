Eriogonum spinescens

Eriogonum spinescens, commonly known as the spiny buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which provide protection against herbivores. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Eriogonum spinescens plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
