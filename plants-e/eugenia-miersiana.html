Eugenia miersiana

Eugenia miersiana, commonly known as the Miers' eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia miersiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
