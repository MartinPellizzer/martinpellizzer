Epidendrum stenostachyum

Epidendrum stenostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple stems. The plant typically has elliptical or ovate leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme bearing numerous small, white or pale pink flowers.

Epidendrum stenostachyum is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical forests.
