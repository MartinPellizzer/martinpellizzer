Eulophia lujae

Eulophia lujae, commonly known as the Lujan's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive spurred lip. Eulophia lujae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
