Erlangea leptophylla

Erlangea leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaf erlangia, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are typically less than 5 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
