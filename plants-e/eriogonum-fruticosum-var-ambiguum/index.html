Eriogonum Fruticosum Var. Ambiguum

Eriogonum ambiguum, scientifically known as Eriogonum fruticosum var. ambiguum, is a unique flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open woodlands. This species is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth and clusters of small, showy flowers. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife. Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important component of its ecosystem.
