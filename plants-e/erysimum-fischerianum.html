Erysimum fischerianum

Erysimum fischerianum, commonly known as the Fischer's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. This species typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and waste grounds. It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
