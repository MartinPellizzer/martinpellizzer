Epidendrum sulphuroleucum

Epidendrum sulphuroleucum, commonly known as the sulfur-yellow epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum sulphuroleucum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
