Erica × craufordii

Erica × craufordii, commonly known as the hybrid heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, producing vibrant pink or purple hues. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is well-suited for coastal and rocky garden environments.

Erica × craufordii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climatic conditions.
