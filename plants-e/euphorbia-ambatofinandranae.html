Euphorbia ambatofinandranae

Euphorbia ambatofinandranae, commonly known as the Ambatofinandrahina euphorbia, is a rare and endemic species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiny stems and clustered, yellowish-green cyathia. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, adapting to the arid and semi-arid conditions of its native habitat.
