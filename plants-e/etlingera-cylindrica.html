Etlingera cylindrica

Etlingera cylindrica, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its striking, red-orange inflorescence that resembles a torch, hence its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its potential medicinal properties.
