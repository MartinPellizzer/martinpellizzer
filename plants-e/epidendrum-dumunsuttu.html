Epidendrum dumunsuttu

Epidendrum dumunsuttu, commonly known as the Showy Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, warm environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Epidendrum dumunsuttu is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
