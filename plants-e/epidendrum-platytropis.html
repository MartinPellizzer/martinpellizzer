Epidendrum platytropis

Epidendrum platytropis, commonly known as the flat-lip epidendron, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are broad and oval, often with a waxy texture, and the flowers have a distinctive flat, lip-like structure.

Epidendrum platytropis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
