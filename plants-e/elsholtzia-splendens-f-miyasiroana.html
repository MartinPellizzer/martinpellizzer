Elsholtzia splendens f. miyasiroana

Elsholtzia splendens f. miyasiroana, commonly known as the Splendid Elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
