Erodium neuradifolium subsp. linosae

Erodium neuradifolium subsp.

linosae, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of southern California. It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a characteristic species of Mediterranean-type climates.
