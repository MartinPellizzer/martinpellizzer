Eurybia tenuifolia

Eurybia tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, slender stems and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its common name.

Eurybia tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
