Encyclia kautskyi

Encyclia kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the highlands of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
