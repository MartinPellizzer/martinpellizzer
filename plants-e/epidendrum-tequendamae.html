Epidendrum tequendamae

Epidendrum tequendamae, commonly known as the Tequendama Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that feature vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
