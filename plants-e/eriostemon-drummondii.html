Eriostemon drummondii

Eriostemon drummondii, commonly known as the Drummond's eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows in shrubland and heathland environments, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a leathery texture.

Eriostemon drummondii is often found in the southwestern regions of Western Australia.
