Elatostema Monandrum F. Subincisum

Elatostema monandrum f. subincisum is a unique variant of the Elatostema genus known for its distinctive leaf morphology. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are characterized by a shallow incision, giving it a distinct appearance compared to other members of the genus. The plant is often found in dense undergrowth, contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
