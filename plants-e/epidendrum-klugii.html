Epidendrum klugii

Epidendrum klugii, commonly known as the Klug's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum klugii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
