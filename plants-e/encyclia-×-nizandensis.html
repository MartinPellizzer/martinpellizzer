Encyclia × nizandensis

Encyclia × nizandensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia frondosa.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America, particularly found in countries like Guatemala and Honduras. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers with yellow petals and a greenish-tinged lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its unique genetic makeup, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
