Eritrichium patens

Eritrichium patens, commonly known as the alpine bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features delicate, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Eritrichium patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
