Epidendrum panteonense

Epidendrum panteonense, commonly known as the Panteonense orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored flowers. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Epidendrum panteonense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
