Euphorbia myrsinites subsp. myrsinites

Euphorbia myrsinites subsp.

myrsinites, commonly known as the "Coral Plant," is a succulent shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of bright red bracts that resemble coral, giving it its common name.

The plant has thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
