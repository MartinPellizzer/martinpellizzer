Elsholtzia fruticosa var. parvifolia

Elsholtzia fruticosa var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elsholtzia, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It features aromatic, grey-green foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
