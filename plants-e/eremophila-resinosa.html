Eremophila resinosa

Eremophila resinosa, commonly known as the sticky leaf eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, resinous leaves that give it a distinctive scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Eremophila resinosa is often used in native gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
