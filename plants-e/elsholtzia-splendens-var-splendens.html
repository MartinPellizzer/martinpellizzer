Elsholtzia splendens var. splendens

Elsholtzia splendens var. splendens, commonly known as the golden elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
