Exostema revolutum

Exostema revolutum, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The leaves are typically green with a prominent midrib and are often covered in fine, short hairs. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often found in wetlands and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, tropical environments.
