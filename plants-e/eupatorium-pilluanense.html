Eupatorium pilluanense

Eupatorium pilluanense, commonly known as the Puerto Rican blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico.

Eupatorium pilluanense is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Caribbean herbal remedies.
