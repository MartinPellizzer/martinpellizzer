Eucharis sanderi var. multiflora

Eucharis sanderi var. multiflora, commonly known as the dwarf Amazon lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 12 inches and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care.
