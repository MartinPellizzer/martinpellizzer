Eragrostis milleflora

Eragrostis milleflora, commonly known as the slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and drooping inflorescences that give it a weeping appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its small, spike-like flowers are usually pale green to brown in color.

Eragrostis milleflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its unique visual texture.
