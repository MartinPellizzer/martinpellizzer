Epimedium campanulatum

Epimedium campanulatum, commonly known as the bell-shaped barrenwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Epimedium campanulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add early-season color to the landscape.
