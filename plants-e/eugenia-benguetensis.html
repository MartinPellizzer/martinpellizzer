Eugenia benguetensis

Eugenia benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Benguet and other northern provinces. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
