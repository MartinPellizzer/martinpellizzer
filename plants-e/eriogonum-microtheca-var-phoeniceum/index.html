Eriogonum Microtheca Var. Phoeniceum

Eriogonum microtheca var. phoeniceum is a striking wildflower native to the western United States. It is known for its vibrant red blooms that stand out against its green foliage. This plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in sagebrush steppe environments. Its unique coloration makes it a popular subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively understudied compared to more common flora.
