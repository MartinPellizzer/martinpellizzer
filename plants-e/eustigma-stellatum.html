Eustigma stellatum

Eustigma stellatum, commonly known as the star-like eustigma, is a unique flowering plant characterized by its striking, star-shaped blooms.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers, which bloom in clusters, come in shades of white and pale yellow, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
