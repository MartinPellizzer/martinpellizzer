Eruca vesicaria var. flaviflora

Eruca vesicaria var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered wall rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and pinnately divided, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found growing in rocky or disturbed soils.

This variety is closely related to other members of the Eruca genus, which are known for their peppery taste and culinary uses.
