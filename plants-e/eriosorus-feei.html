Eriosorus feei

Eriosorus feei, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant features small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
