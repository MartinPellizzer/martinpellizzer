Espadaea amoena var. nejasaensis

Espadaea amoena var. nejasaensis, commonly known as the Nejasaensis Spider Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated spur. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories where it thrives on mycorrhizal associations. Its flowers are usually a mix of white and green hues, with a subtle fragrance that attracts specific pollinators.
