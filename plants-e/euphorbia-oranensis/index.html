Euphorbia Oranensis

Euphorbia oranensis is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of northern Africa. Known for its spiky, branched stems and clusters of colorful, lantern-like flowers, it captivates with its unusual appearance. This plant thrives in harsh conditions, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its ability to store water in its thick stems allows it to survive in dry environments. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic, requiring caution when handling.
