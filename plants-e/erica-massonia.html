Erica massonia

Erica massonia, commonly known as the silver leaf heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, evergreen shrub form. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica massonia is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
