Elaeocarpus bellus

Elaeocarpus bellus, commonly known as the beautiful elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy white flowers. The tree produces small, woody fruits that are typically covered in a thick, leathery coat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
