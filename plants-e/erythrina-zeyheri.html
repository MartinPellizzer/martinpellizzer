Erythrina zeyheri

Erythrina zeyheri, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking leguminous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves composed of three to five oval-shaped leaflets. Its pods are long, slender, and contain several seeds.

Erythrina zeyheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
