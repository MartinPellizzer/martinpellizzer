Eriosyce crispa subsp. atroviridis

Eriosyce crispa subsp.

atroviridis, commonly known as the dark green eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that are typically a dark green color.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often followed by pale green fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
