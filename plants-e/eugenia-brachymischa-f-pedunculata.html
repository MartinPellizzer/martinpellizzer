Eugenia brachymischa f. pedunculata

Eugenia brachymischa f. pedunculata, commonly known as the pedunculate eugenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, red, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
