Eugenia elegans

Eugenia elegans, commonly known as the elegant guava, is a small evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Eugenia elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
