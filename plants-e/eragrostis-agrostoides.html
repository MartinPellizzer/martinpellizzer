Eragrostis agrostoides

Eragrostis agrostoides, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to the United States, particularly in the central and southern regions.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in prairies and open woodlands.

Eragrostis agrostoides is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
