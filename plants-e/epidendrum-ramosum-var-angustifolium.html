Epidendrum ramosum var. angustifolium

Epidendrum ramosum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a waxy texture and a deep green color. It produces pendulous inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under proper conditions.
