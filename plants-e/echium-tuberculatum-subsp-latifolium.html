Echium tuberculatum subsp. latifolium

Echium tuberculatum subsp.

latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer.

The subspecies is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to other Echium species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
