Euphorbia ebracteolata var. anhweiensis

Euphorbia ebracteolata var. anhweiensis, commonly known as the Anhwei spurge, is a succulent shrub native to China.

It features distinctive, fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typically surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and drought tolerance in landscaping.
