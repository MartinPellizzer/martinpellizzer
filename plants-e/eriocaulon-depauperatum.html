Eriocaulon depauperatum

Eriocaulon depauperatum, commonly known as the sparse eriocaulon, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and wetlands.

Due to its delicate structure and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its habitat.
