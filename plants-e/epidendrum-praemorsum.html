Epidendrum praemorsum

Epidendrum praemorsum, commonly known as the Black Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking violet to purple flowers with yellow markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are arranged in dense clusters and bloom during the wet season.

Epidendrum praemorsum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
