Eriocaulon huianum

Eriocaulon huianum, commonly known as the Huian spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence that bears small, white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Eriocaulon huianum plays a role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
