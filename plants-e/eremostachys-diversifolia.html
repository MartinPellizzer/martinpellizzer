Eremostachys diversifolia

Eremostachys diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaved eremostachys, is a perennial flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and gray-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Eremostachys diversifolia is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
