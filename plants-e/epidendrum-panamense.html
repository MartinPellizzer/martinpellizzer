Epidendrum panamense

Epidendrum panamense, commonly known as the Panamanian orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Epidendrum panamense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
