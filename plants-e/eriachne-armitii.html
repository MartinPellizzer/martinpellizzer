Eriachne armitii

Eriachne armitii, commonly known as the Australian grass or spear grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often reddish in color.

Eriachne armitii is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
