Elaphoglossum glaucescens

Elaphoglossum glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green holly fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, compact fern with finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically a bluish-green color. It produces spore-bearing leaves called sporangia, which are clustered in clusters along the fronds.

Elaphoglossum glaucescens is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
