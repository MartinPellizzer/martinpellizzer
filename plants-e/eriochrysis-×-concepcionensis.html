Eriochrysis × concepcionensis

Eriochrysis × concepcionensis, commonly known as the Concepcion grass.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Eriochrysis, characterized by its tufted growth habit and small, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, coastal environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils. It produces small, yellowish to greenish flowers that are clustered at the base of the plant.

Eriochrysis × concepcionensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging coastal conditions.
