Elatostema × hybrida

Elatostema × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid snowflake plant, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, typical of the genus Elatostema. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
