Erophila spathulata

Erophila spathulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a head. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Erophila spathulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
