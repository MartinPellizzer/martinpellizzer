Eriocaulon fenestratum

Eriocaulon fenestratum, commonly known as the fenestrated flat sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is often found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and pond edges, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions.

Its unique leaf structure, featuring fenestrations or small openings, is a distinctive botanical feature.
