Encyclia duboisiana

Encyclia duboisiana, commonly known as the Dubois's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow or greenish-white blooms with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia duboisiana is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
