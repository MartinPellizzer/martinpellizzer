Echium bifrons var. hierrense

Echium bifrons var. hierrense, commonly known as the Canary Island thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are generally gray-green, covered in fine hairs, and often have a waxy texture.

This variety is notable for its ecological importance and ornamental value in gardens.
