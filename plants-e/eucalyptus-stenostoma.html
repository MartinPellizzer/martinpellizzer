Eucalyptus stenostoma

Eucalyptus stenostoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and smooth, pale grey bark. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, globular flower clusters that are often tinged with pink or red.

Eucalyptus stenostoma is known for its aromatic oils, which are used in various traditional and commercial applications.
