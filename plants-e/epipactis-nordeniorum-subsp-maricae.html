Epipactis nordeniorum subsp. maricae

Epipactis nordeniorum subsp.

maricae, commonly known as the Maricae helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe. It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its showy, greenish-yellow flowers with purple markings.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces a single spike of flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is notable for its specialized pollination mechanism, attracting specific insect pollinators through its nectar-rich flowers.
