Eriosema lagoense

Eriosema lagoense, commonly known as the Lake Lago eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
