Emilia zeylanica var. zeylanica

Emilia zeylanica var. zeylanica, commonly known as the tropical blueweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
