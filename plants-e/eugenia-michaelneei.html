Eugenia michaelneei

Eugenia michaelneei, commonly known as the Michaelnei eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
