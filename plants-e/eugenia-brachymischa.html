Eugenia brachymischa

Eugenia brachymischa, commonly known as the small-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The fruit is a small, smooth, and edible berry that ripens to a dark purple color.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
