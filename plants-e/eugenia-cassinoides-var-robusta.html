Eugenia cassinoides var. robusta

Eugenia cassinoides var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit, it is cultivated in gardens and for commercial purposes.
