Eriosema pumilum var. pumilum

Eriosema pumilum var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf eriosema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and features a compact, bushy habit. The leaves are usually ovate or elliptic, with a soft, hairy texture on both surfaces. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.

This plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
