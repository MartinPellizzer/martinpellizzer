Elytranthe suberosa

Elytranthe suberosa, commonly known as the woolly elytranthe, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are thick and covered in fine hairs, which help to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Elytranthe suberosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and appearance.
