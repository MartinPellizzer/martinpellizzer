Epidendrum oncidioides var. profusum

Epidendrum oncidioides var. profusum, commonly known as the profuse epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
