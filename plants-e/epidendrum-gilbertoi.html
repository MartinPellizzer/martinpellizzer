Epidendrum gilbertoi

Epidendrum gilbertoi, commonly known as the Gilbert's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in limestone-rich soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Epidendrum gilbertoi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
