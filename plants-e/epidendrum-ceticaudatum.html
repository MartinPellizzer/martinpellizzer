Epidendrum ceticaudatum

Epidendrum ceticaudatum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs and arching, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense, white tomentum. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and wasps.

Epidendrum ceticaudatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
