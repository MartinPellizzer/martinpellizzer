Eugenia barrerensis

Eugenia barrerensis, commonly known as the Barrer's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically ovate and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Eugenia barrerensis is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
