Ebenus haussknechtii

Ebenus haussknechtii, commonly known as the Hausknecht's wormwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. Its flowering stems are usually branched and bear small, yellowish flowers.
