Eriachne hookeri

Eriachne hookeri, commonly known as the slender spinifex, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is typically found in sandy or coastal environments, where it forms dense mats. Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important component of arid and semi-arid ecosystems.

Eriachne hookeri plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
