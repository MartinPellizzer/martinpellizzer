Echinocactus × erubescens

Echinocactus × erubescens, commonly known as the Easter cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, ribbed stems that are typically green or red in color. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and blooming habits, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
