Enterolobium gummiferum

Enterolobium gummiferum, commonly known as the rubberwood tree, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and produces pods containing edible seeds. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, which enriches the soil. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in secondary forests and disturbed areas.

The tree is also valued for its timber and has been used in traditional medicine.
