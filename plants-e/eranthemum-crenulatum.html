Eranthemum crenulatum

Eranthemum crenulatum, commonly known as the zigzag geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
