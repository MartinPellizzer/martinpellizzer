Eriogonum densum

Eriogonum densum, commonly known as the denseleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, gray-green foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and chaparral areas. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes other buckwheats and related species.

Eriogonum densum is often found in California and parts of Nevada, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
