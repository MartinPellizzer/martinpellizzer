Ervatamia divaricata var. plena

Ervatamia divaricata var. plena, commonly known as the full-flowered ervatamia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its abundant flowering and compact form.
