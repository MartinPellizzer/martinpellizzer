Eupatorium Origanoides

Eupatorium origanoides, commonly known as boneset, is a native flowering plant of the sunflower family. It thrives in wetlands and moist woodlands across eastern North America. The plant is recognized for its tall, sturdy stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Boneset has a long history of traditional use in herbal medicine. Its resilience and ecological importance make it a notable species in native plant communities.
