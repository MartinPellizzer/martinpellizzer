Ericameria nelsonii

Ericameria nelsonii, commonly known as Nelson's ericameria, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. This plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as chaparral and coastal sage scrub. It produces small, yellowish to golden composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is characterized by its woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
