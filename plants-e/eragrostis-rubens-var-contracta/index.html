Eragrostis Rubens Var. Contracta

Eragrostis rubens var. contracta is a unique grass species known for its striking red hues and compact growth habit. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it thrives in environments with limited water and intense sunlight. This variety is often found in sandy soils and is commonly used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value. Its dense foliage and vibrant color make it a popular choice for erosion control and decorative purposes. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known plant in the broader horticultural community.
