Erythroxylum suave var. aneurum

Erythroxylum suave var. aneurum, commonly known as the smooth erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces elliptical, dark red berries that contain seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
