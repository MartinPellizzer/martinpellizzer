Elatostema rivulare

Elatostema rivulare, commonly known as the water shield, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It has small, heart-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating on the water surface. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Elatostema rivulare is characterized by its ability to thrive in shallow, freshwater environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic ecosystems.
