Euphorbia viguieri

Euphorbia viguieri, commonly known as the Viguier's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It has a spiky appearance with milky sap that can cause skin irritation.

Euphorbia viguieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and striking appearance.
