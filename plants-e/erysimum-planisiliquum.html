Erysimum planisiliquum

Erysimum planisiliquum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features bright yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves are linear and often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Erysimum planisiliquum is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
