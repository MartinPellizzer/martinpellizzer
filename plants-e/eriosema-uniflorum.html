Eriosema uniflorum

Eriosema uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in summer. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
