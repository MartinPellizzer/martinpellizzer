Ebermaiera thyrsoidea

Ebermaiera thyrsoidea, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, violet-colored flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
