Euonymus europaeus f. nanus

Euonymus europaeus f. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf European spindle tree, is a compact shrub native to Europe.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by orange-red, berry-like fruits. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This cultivar is valued for its dense growth habit and low maintenance requirements.
