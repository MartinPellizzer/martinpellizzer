Epidendrum ruizianum

Epidendrum ruizianum, commonly known as the Ruiz's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, featuring a pseudobulb and a single, elongated leaf. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Epidendrum ruizianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
