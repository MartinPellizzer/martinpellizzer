Epidendrum convergens

Epidendrum convergens, commonly known as the showy flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, providing a contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Epidendrum convergens thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.
