Eria hegdei

Eria hegdei, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread gradually across mountainous terrains.

Eria hegdei is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
