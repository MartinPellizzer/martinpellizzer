Eria sutepensis

Eria sutepensis, commonly known as the Sutep's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
