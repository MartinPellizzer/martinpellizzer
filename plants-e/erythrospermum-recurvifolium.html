Erythrospermum recurvifolium

Erythrospermum recurvifolium, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and recurved, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Erythrospermum recurvifolium thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland heathlands.
