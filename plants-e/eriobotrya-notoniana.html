Eriobotrya notoniana

Eriobotrya notoniana, commonly known as the Australian loquat, is a small evergreen tree native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. Its botanical features include a smooth, greyish bark and a tendency to form dense, rounded crowns.
