Epidendrum amayense

Epidendrum amayense, commonly known as the Amayense Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum amayense is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
