Euphorbia xianxialingensis

Euphorbia xianxialingensis, commonly known as the Xianxialing euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the genus Euphorbia. It is adapted to arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
