Epidendrum sculptum var. arevaloi

Epidendrum sculptum var. arevaloi, commonly known as the Arevalo's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
