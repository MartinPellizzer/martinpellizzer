Equisetum Californicum

Equisetum californicum is a unique plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family. It is commonly known as the California horse tail and is native to the western United States. This plant is notable for its ancient lineage, dating back over 300 million years. It has a distinctive appearance with a tall, spore-bearing stem and whorled branches. Despite its prehistoric origins, it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or streamside habitats.
