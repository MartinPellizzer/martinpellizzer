Eustoma russellianum f. leucantha

Eustoma russellianum f. leucantha, commonly known as the white lisianthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy white petals that resemble those of the common lisianthus but with a more pronounced white coloration. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 30 inches. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a delicate, fragrant appearance.

This cultivar is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements.
