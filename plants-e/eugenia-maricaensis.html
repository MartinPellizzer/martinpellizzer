Eugenia maricaensis

Eugenia maricaensis, commonly known as the Maricaensis tamarind, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
