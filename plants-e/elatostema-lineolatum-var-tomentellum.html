Elatostema lineolatum var. tomentellum

Elatostema lineolatum var. tomentellum, commonly known as the woolly leaf elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are densely covered with fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variant is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
