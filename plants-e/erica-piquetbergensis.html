Erica piquetbergensis

Erica piquetbergensis, commonly known as Piquetberg heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of pink or white flowers during the summer months.

Erica piquetbergensis thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
