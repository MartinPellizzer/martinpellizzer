Erysimum strictisiliquum

Erysimum strictisiliquum, commonly known as the narrow-siliqua wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous areas. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped, and the flowers are usually yellow or orange in color.

Erysimum strictisiliquum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
