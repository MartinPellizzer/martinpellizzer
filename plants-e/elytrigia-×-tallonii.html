Elytrigia × tallonii

Elytrigia × tallonii, commonly known as the tallonii grass, is a hybrid species within the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and typically forms dense, low-growing clumps. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with two to three florets per spikelet. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when fresh.

Elytrigia × tallonii is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
