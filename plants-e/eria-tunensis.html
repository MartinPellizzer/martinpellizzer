Eria tunensis

Eria tunensis, commonly known as the Tunisian eria, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Eria tunensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
