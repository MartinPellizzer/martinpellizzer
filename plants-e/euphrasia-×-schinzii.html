Euphrasia × schinzii

Euphrasia × schinzii, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, linear, and often hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
