Eugenia choungiensis

Eugenia choungiensis, commonly known as the Choung Island Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found on the island of Choung in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
