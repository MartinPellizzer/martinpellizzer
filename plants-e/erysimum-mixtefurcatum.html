Erysimum mixtefurcatum

Erysimum mixtefurcatum, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant features basal leaves that are usually lobed or divided, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Erysimum mixtefurcatum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
