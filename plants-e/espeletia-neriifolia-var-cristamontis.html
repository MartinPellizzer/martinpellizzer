Espeletia neriifolia var. cristamontis

Espeletia neriifolia var. cristamontis, commonly known as the Cristamontes heather, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in compact inflorescences. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
