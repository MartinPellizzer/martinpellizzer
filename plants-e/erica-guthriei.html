Erica guthriei

Erica guthriei, commonly known as Guthrie's heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late winter to early spring. Erica guthriei is a shrub that can grow up to 1 meter tall, with dense, evergreen foliage.

It is well adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat, requiring minimal water once established.
