Enkianthus rubicundus

Enkianthus rubicundus, commonly known as the red-flowered enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
