Epilobium alpinum var. alsinifolium

Epilobium alpinum var. alsinifolium, commonly known as the alpine willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in dense, terminal clusters, and bloom in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
