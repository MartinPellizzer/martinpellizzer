Ehrharta oreophila var. minor

Ehrharta oreophila var. minor, commonly known as the mountain fescue, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny grains. It is often found in open grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant suited to harsh, arid conditions.
