Encyclia stellata

Encyclia stellata, commonly known as the star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually dark green and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.
