Emblica Chayamaritiae

Emblica chayamaritiae is a rare and lesser-known species of the emblic myrobalan genus. Native to the remote regions of northeastern India, this plant thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its fruit, known for its tart flavor and high vitamin c content, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Despite its nutritional value, it remains underexplored in modern scientific research. This article aims to shed light on the significance and potential of emblica chayamaritiae.
