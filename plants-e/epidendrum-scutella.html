Epidendrum scutella

Epidendrum scutella, commonly known as the scarlet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange, with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum scutella is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
