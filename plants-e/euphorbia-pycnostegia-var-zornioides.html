Euphorbia pycnostegia var. zornioides

Euphorbia pycnostegia var. zornioides, commonly known as the Zorn's euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy stems and produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in cyathia. The plant has a dense, spiny texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
