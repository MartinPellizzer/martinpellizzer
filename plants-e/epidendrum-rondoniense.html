Epidendrum rondoniense

Epidendrum rondoniense, commonly known as the Rondoniense orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces elongated, pendulous inflorescences with colorful, showy flowers that range from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in two ranks, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Epidendrum rondoniense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
