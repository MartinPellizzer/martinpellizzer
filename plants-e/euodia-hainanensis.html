Euodia hainanensis

Euodia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan euodia, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
