Eriosema buchananii var. buchananii

Eriosema buchananii var. buchananii, commonly known as the velvetleaf crotalaria, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on slender stems. Its growth habit is sprawling, with the stems often rooting at the nodes.

It is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
