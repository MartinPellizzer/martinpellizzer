Eriocarpum blephariphyllum

Eriocarpum blephariphyllum, commonly known as the silky everlasting or silky everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

Eriocarpum blephariphyllum is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
