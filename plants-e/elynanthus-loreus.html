Elynanthus loreus

Elynanthus loreus, commonly known as the yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Elynanthus loreus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
