Erysimum baeticum

Erysimum baeticum, commonly known as the Iberian wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in rocky or disturbed soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Erysimum baeticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
