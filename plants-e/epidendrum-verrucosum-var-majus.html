Epidendrum verrucosum var. majus

Epidendrum verrucosum var. majus, commonly known as the Showy Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple with a yellow throat. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
