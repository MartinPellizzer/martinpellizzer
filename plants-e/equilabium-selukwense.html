Equilabium selukwense

Equilabium selukwense, commonly known as the Selukwense orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive labellum that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
