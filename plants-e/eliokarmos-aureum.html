Eliokarmos aureum

Eliokarmos aureum, commonly known as the golden elokarmos, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in open, sunny habitats. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry conditions.

Eliokarmos aureum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
