Elaeagnus longipes var. orbiculata

Elaeagnus longipes var. orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaved silverberry, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery leaves covered in fine, dense hairs, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
