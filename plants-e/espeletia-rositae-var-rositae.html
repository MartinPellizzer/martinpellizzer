Espeletia rositae var. rositae

Espeletia rositae var. rositae, commonly known as the Rosita's espeletia, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-like growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the cold, dry conditions of its native habitat.
