Elatostema puberulum

Elatostema puberulum, commonly known as the velvet leaf or silver leaf, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in the understory of forests. The plant features heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

Elatostema puberulum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
