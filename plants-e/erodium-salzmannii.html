Erodium salzmannii

Erodium salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and botanical studies.
