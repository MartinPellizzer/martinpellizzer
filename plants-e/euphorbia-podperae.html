Euphorbia podperae

Euphorbia podperae, commonly known as the Podpera's spurge, is a perennial plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the spurge family. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
