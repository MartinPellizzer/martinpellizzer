Epipactis Dunensis Subsp. Sancta

Epipactis dunensis subsp. sancta is a rare and elusive orchid native to specific regions of southern Europe. This sub-species is distinguished by its unique floral structure and coloration, setting it apart from other members of the Epipactis genus. Due to its limited distribution and habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation terms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rocky crevices. Despite its beauty, little is known about its reproductive habits and ecological role.
