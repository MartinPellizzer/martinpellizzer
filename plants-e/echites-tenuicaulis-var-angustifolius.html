Echites tenuicaulis var. angustifolius

Echites tenuicaulis var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved echites, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
