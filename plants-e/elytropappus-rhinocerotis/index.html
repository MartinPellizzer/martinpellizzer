Elytropappus Rhinocerotis

Elytropappus rhinocerotis is a small, annual plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring tiny, clustered flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in coastal or inland dunes. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in stabilizing sand dunes and supporting local wildlife. Its unique adaptations make it an interesting subject for ecological and botanical study.
