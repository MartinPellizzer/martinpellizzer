Euphrasia nemorosa var. tenuifolia

Euphrasia nemorosa var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale green coloration. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with five petals and a spurred structure at the back.

It is often found in colonies and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
