Eria lanigera

Eria lanigera, commonly known as the woolly orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Eria lanigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
