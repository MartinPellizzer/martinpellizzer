Euphorbia neostapelioides

Euphorbia neostapelioides, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces unique, cyathium-shaped inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers. Its sap contains toxic compounds, which can cause irritation if handled improperly.
