Echites odorifer

Echites odorifer, commonly known as the fragrant echites, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
