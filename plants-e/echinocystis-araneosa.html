Echinocystis araneosa

Echinocystis araneosa, commonly known as the spider gourd, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

The flowers are yellow and unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
