Eurycaulis tropaeoliflorus

Eurycaulis tropaeoliflorus, commonly known as the tropical nasturtium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other members like mustard and cabbage. The plant features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive spurred petal. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is known for its peppery scent and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.
