Epidendrum polystachyum

Epidendrum polystachyum, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, pink or red flowers during the holiday season, typically from December to February. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying environmental conditions.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
