Elaeocarpus colnettianus

Elaeocarpus colnettianus, commonly known as the Colnett's elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
