Eritrichium holopterum

Eritrichium holopterum, commonly known as the alpine mouse-eared cress, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in the spring months. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves that are adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.

Eritrichium holopterum is often found in alpine meadows and is characteristic of the flora in regions like the Himalayas and the Tibetan Plateau.
