Erica sphaerantha

Erica sphaerantha, commonly known as the globe heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer. Erica sphaerantha thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
