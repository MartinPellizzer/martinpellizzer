Eryngium spinosissimum

Eryngium spinosissimum, commonly known as the spiny eryngium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features striking, spiky blue to purple flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rugged appearance. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and limestone areas.

Eryngium spinosissimum is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
