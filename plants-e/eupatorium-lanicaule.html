Eupatorium lanicaule

Eupatorium lanicaule, commonly known as roughleaf boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks. The plant features tall, slender stems covered with stiff, hairy leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its small, white flowers bloom in clustered heads during the summer months.

The botanical characteristics of Eupatorium lanicaule make it a distinctive member of the Asteraceae family.
