Eugenia romana var. pickeli

Eugenia romana var. pickeli, commonly known as the Pickel's Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red when mature.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
