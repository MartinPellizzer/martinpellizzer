Erysimum dagestanicum

Erysimum dagestanicum, commonly known as the Dagestani wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features bright yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Erysimum dagestanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
