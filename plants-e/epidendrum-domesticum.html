Epidendrum domesticum

Epidendrum domesticum, commonly known as the domestic Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in dense clusters along the stem.

Epidendrum domesticum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
