Euphorbia carpatica

Euphorbia carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features dense, upright stems covered in a waxy, pale green coating. Its flowers are small and clustered in yellowish cyathia, which are surrounded by green bracts.

Euphorbia carpatica is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.
