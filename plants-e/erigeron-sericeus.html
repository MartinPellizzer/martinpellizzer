Erigeron sericeus

Erigeron sericeus, commonly known as silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas.

Erigeron sericeus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
