Epidendrum scytocladium

Epidendrum scytocladium, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces striking, raceme-like inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that have a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant and unique blooms.
