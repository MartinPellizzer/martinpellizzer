Eugenia floscellus

Eugenia floscellus, commonly known as the small-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, with a slightly aromatic scent.

Eugenia floscellus is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
