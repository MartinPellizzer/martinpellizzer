Epidendrum matutinum

Epidendrum matutinum, commonly known as the morning orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and appear in clusters, often in shades of white and pink.

Epidendrum matutinum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
