Erigeron turczaninowii

Erigeron turczaninowii, commonly known as the Turczaninow's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
