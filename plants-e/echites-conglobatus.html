Echites conglobatus

Echites conglobatus, commonly known as the globular echites, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a succulent appearance, with thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
