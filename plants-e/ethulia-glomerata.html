Ethulia glomerata

Ethulia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered flax.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Ethulia glomerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
