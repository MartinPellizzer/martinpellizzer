Elymus hongyuanensis

Elymus hongyuanensis, commonly known as Hongyuan grass, is a species of perennial grass native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow leaves. This plant is typically found in high-altitude meadows and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates. Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil, making it ecologically significant in its habitat.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
