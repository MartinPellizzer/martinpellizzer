Epipactis placentina var. robatschiana

Epipactis placentina var. robatschiana, commonly known as the Robatsch's marsh helleborine, is a rare and specialized orchid species.

It is characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with distinct lip structures. This plant is typically found in wet meadows and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it can derive some nutrients from its host through specialized roots.
