Elaterium gracile var. ottonianum

Elaterium gracile var. ottonianum, commonly known as the slender elaterium, is a variant of the elaterium plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the cooler months, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat endangered species.
