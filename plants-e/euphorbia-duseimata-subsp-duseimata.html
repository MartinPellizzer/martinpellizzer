Euphorbia duseimata subsp. duseimata

Euphorbia duseimata subsp.

duseimata, commonly known as the Duseimata spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a short, thick stem with a spiny crown, and its leaves are fleshy and often covered in a waxy coating.

The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
