Epidendrum jamiesonis

Epidendrum jamiesonis, commonly known as the Jamaica orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Caribbean.

It features long, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length, with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum jamiesonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
