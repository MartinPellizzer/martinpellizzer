Encyclia granitica

Encyclia granitica, commonly known as the granite orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, humid environment.

Encyclia granitica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
