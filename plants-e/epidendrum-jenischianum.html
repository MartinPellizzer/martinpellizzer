Epidendrum jenischianum

Epidendrum jenischianum, commonly known as the Jenisch's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural collections.
