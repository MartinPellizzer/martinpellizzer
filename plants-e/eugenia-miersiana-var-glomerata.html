Eugenia miersiana var. glomerata

Eugenia miersiana var. glomerata, commonly known as the clustered eugenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, dark purple fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal and lowland regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical areas.
