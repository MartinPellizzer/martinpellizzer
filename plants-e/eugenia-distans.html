Eugenia distans

Eugenia distans, commonly known as the distant eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia distans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
