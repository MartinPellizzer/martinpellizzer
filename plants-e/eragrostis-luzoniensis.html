Eragrostis luzoniensis

Eragrostis luzoniensis, commonly known as Luzon grass, is a species of grass native to the Philippines.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in lowland areas.

Its ecological role includes soil stabilization and providing forage for livestock.
