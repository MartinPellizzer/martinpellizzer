Echites polyanthus

Echites polyanthus, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant is evergreen and thrives in arid or semi-arid conditions, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to store water, while its flowers bloom throughout the year in some climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
