Evosmia obovata

Evosmia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed evosmia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Evosmia obovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
