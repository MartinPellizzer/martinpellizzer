Erysimum duranii

Erysimum duranii, commonly known as the Duran's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow and orange, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Erysimum duranii is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
