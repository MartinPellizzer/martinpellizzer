Encyclia caximboensis

Encyclia caximboensis, commonly known as the Caximbo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia caximboensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
