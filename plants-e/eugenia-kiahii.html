Eugenia kiahii

Eugenia kiahii, commonly known as the Kiahii hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a deep red color.

Eugenia kiahii is often found in coastal and lowland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
