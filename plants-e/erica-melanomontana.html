Erica melanomontana

Erica melanomontana, commonly known as the black mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Erica melanomontana typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous environments.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is known for its rich biodiversity and unique ecological significance.
