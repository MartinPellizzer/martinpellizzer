Eleusine barcinonensis

Eleusine barcinonensis, commonly known as the Barcino grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Eleusine barcinonensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in open, sunny habitats.
