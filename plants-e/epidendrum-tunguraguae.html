Epidendrum tunguraguae

Epidendrum tunguraguae, commonly known as the Tunguragua Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
