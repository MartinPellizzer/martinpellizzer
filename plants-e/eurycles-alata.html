Eurycles alata

Eurycles alata, commonly known as the winged begonia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winged leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
