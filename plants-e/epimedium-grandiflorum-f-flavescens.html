Epimedium grandiflorum f. flavescens

Epimedium grandiflorum f. flavescens, commonly known as the golden shade plant, is a unique variant of the Epimedium genus native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its deep green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to shaded gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it an excellent choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular selection among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
