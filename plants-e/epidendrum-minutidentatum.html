Epidendrum minutidentatum

Epidendrum minutidentatum, commonly known as the miniature toothed epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.
