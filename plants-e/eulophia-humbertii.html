Eulophia humbertii

Eulophia humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has a pseudobulb and linear leaves, with inflorescences bearing multiple showy blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia humbertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
