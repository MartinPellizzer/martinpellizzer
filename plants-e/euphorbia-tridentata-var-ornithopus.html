Euphorbia tridentata var. ornithopus

Euphorbia tridentata var. ornithopus, commonly known as the Bird's Beak Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, bird-beak-shaped inflorescences that are often brightly colored. The plant has a branched stem system with thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water in arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
