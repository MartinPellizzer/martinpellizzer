Eleocharis dombeyana

Eleocharis dombeyana, commonly known as the narrowleaf spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like clusters of flowers. Eleocharis dombeyana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
