Epidendrum moralesii

Epidendrum moralesii, commonly known as the Morales' Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Epidendrum moralesii is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
