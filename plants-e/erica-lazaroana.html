Erica lazaroana

Erica lazaroana, commonly known as the Cape Verde heath, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Erica lazaroana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to the arid and coastal conditions of its native habitat.

It is often found growing in rocky or sandy areas, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
