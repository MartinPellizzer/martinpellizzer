Ensete holstii

Ensete holstii, commonly known as the African elephant tree, is a large, ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its striking foliage. It belongs to the Musaceae family, which also includes bananas and plantains.
