Euonymus poilanei

Euonymus poilanei, commonly known as the creeping euonymus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cercidifolium.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by bright red, spherical fruits that are visually striking.
