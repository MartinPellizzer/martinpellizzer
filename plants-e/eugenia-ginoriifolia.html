Eugenia ginoriifolia

Eugenia ginoriifolia, commonly known as the pink eugenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Eugenia ginoriifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic foliage.
