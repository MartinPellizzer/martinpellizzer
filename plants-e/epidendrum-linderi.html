Epidendrum linderi

Epidendrum linderi, commonly known as the Linder's Epidendrum, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Epidendrum linderi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
