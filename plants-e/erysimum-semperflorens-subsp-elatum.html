Erysimum semperflorens subsp. elatum

Erysimum semperflorens subsp.

elatum, commonly known as the tall wallflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe. It produces showy, bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.

The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with linear, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking long-blooming, low-maintenance plants.
