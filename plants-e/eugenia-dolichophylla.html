Eugenia dolichophylla

Eugenia dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant white or pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia dolichophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
