Elaeocarpus mutabilis

Elaeocarpus mutabilis, commonly known as the variable elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that change color as they mature.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
