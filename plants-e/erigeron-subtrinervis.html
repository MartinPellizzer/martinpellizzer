Erigeron subtrinervis

Erigeron subtrinervis, commonly known as the slenderleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are small and white or pale blue.

It plays a role in supporting pollinators and is often found in prairie ecosystems.
