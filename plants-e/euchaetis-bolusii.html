Euchaetis bolusii

Euchaetis bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale blue, with a distinctive central disc.
