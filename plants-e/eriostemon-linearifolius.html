Eriostemon linearifolius

Eriostemon linearifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal or sandy soils and is often found in heathlands or open forests. It is a perennial shrub that produces small, woody fruits containing seeds.
