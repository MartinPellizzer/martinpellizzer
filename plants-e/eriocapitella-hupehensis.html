Eriocapitella hupehensis

Eriocapitella hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually basal, lance-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The flowers are inconspicuous, with yellow disc florets surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.
