Eragrostis alopecuroides

Eragrostis alopecuroides, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features fine, blue-green blades with a silky texture, and its leaves are typically narrow and slightly folded. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for lawns and ornamental gardens.
