Eranthemum angustifolium

Eranthemum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. Eranthemum angustifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
