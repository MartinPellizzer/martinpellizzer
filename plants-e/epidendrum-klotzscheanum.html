Epidendrum klotzscheanum

Epidendrum klotzscheanum, commonly known as the Klotzschean orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted for absorbing moisture from the air. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
