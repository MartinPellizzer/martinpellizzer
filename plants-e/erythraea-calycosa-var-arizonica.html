Erythraea calycosa var. arizonica

Erythraea calycosa var. arizonica, commonly known as Arizona thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, thimble-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight bluish tint.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
