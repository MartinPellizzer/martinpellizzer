Euphorbia nakaii var. hondoensis

Euphorbia nakaii var. hondoensis, commonly known as the Hondo euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by succulent enthusiasts.
