Eucnide hypomalaca

Eucnide hypomalaca, commonly known as the silverleaf acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, oval leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It produces yellow flowers arranged in clustered inflorescences, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is adapted to arid environments and is often used in traditional medicine.
