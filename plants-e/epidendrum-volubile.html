Epidendrum volubile

Epidendrum volubile, commonly known as the climbing epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a vigorous climber with long, flexible stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a waxy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical regions.
