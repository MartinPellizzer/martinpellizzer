Enodium caeruleum var. congestum

Enodium caeruleum var. congestum, commonly known as the Blue Enodium, is a strikingly colorful plant with dense clusters of sky-blue flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pea-like flowers and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated for both aesthetic and environmental purposes.
