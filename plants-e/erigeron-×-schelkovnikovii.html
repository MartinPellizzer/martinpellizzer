Erigeron × schelkovnikovii

Erigeron × schelkovnikovii, commonly known as the Schelkovnikov's fleabane, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments. Its foliage is generally gray-green with hairy surfaces, and it produces small white to pinkish flowers in the summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
