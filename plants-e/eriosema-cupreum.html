Eriosema cupreum

Eriosema cupreum, commonly known as copperleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Eriosema cupreum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
