Embelia arunachalensis

Embelia arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal embelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the northeastern region of India.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of tropical and subtropical forests.
