Epidendrum × pinheiroi

Epidendrum × pinheiroi, commonly known as the Pink Lady's Slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that resemble a slipper, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the tropical rainforests. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the summer months.

Epidendrum × pinheiroi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses and gardens.
