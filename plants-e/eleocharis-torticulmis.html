Eleocharis torticulmis

Eleocharis torticulmis, commonly known as the twisted spike rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. Eleocharis torticulmis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
