Echinomastus arizonicus

Echinomastus arizonicus, commonly known as the Arizona echinomastus, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, succulent stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. This species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and desert landscapes. The plant has adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native habitat.
