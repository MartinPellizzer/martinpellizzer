Ebenus montbretii var. cilicica

Ebenus montbretii var. cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician thornvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are often trifoliate or pinnate. It is characterized by its thorny stems and adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

The species is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a long history of use in traditional herbal medicine.
