Epidendrum microtos var. grandiflorum

Epidendrum microtos var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the Grandiflorum Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
