Erigeron pycnotrichus

Erigeron pycnotrichus, commonly known as the woolly fleabane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

Its flowering stems bear small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers, typically blooming in late summer.

Erigeron pycnotrichus is often found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.
