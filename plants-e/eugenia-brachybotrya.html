Eugenia brachybotrya

Eugenia brachybotrya, commonly known as the small-fruited eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a small, berry-like drupe that ripens to a deep red color.

Eugenia brachybotrya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
