Eugenia liebmannii

Eugenia liebmannii, commonly known as the red-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, while its fruit is a small, woody berry.

Eugenia liebmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
