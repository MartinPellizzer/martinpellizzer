Eugenia vanderwateri

Eugenia vanderwateri, commonly known as Vanderwater's guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. It produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.
