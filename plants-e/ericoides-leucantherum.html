Ericoides leucantherum

Ericoides leucantherum, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen shrub form and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland or woodland environments.

Ericoides leucantherum is well adapted to cooler climates and is relatively low maintenance once established.
