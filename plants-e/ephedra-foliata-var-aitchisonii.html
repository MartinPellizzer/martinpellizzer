Ephedra foliata var. aitchisonii

Ephedra foliata var. aitchisonii, commonly known as Aitchison's Ephedra, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the arid regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Ephedraceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated, and often branched stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. It has a slow growth rate and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
