Excoecaria formosana var. formosana

Excoecaria formosana var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan croton, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often asymmetrical leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its foliage.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
