Eugenia zuchowskiae

Eugenia zuchowskiae, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The flowers bloom in the winter months, making it a notable plant for its seasonal display.
