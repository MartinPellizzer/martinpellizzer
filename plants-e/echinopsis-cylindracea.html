Echinopsis cylindracea

Echinopsis cylindracea, commonly known as the cylindrical moon cactus, is a succulent native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stem and dense spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
