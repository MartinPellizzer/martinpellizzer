Eranthemum tenellum

Eranthemum tenellum, commonly known as the small eranthemum, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its compact size and extended blooming period make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
