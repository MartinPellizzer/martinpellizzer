Elaeocarpus obovatus var. foveolatus

Elaeocarpus obovatus var. foveolatus, commonly known as the glossy elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, often used in traditional medicine.

This variant is distinguished by its slightly depressed or pitted surface on the fruit's exterior.
