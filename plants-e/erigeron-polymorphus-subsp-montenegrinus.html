Erigeron polymorphus subsp. montenegrinus

Erigeron polymorphus subsp.

montenegrinus, commonly known as the Montenegro Fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region, particularly Montenegro. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets.
