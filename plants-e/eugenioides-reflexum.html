Eugenioides reflexum

Eugenioides reflexum, commonly known as the red-flowered eugenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of small, red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but often have a sour taste. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenioides reflexum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
