Epidendrum megalemmum

Epidendrum megalemmum, commonly known as the giant lemming orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, often in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum megalemmum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.
