Echeveria spathulifolia

Echeveria spathulifolia, commonly known as the spoon-leaf echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are usually blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
