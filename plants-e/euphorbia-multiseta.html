Euphorbia multiseta

Euphorbia multiseta, commonly known as the prickly euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, woody stem with numerous spines and branching structures. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flowers. Its leaves are typically small, fleshy, and covered in fine hairs.

Euphorbia multiseta is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for arid environments.
