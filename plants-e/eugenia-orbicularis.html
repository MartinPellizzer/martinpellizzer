Eugenia orbicularis

Eugenia orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

Eugenia orbicularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
