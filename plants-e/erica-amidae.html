Erica amidae

Erica amidae, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, particularly the Western and Eastern Cape provinces. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in late spring to early summer.

Erica amidae is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in gardens and natural landscapes.
