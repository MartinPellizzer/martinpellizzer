Erodium macradenum

Erodium macradenum, commonly known as the sticky everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces small, dry fruits called legumes, which contain several seeds. Its leaves are simple and often covered in fine, sticky hairs.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
