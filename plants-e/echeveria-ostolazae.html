Echeveria ostolazae

Echeveria ostolazae, commonly known as the Blue Elf or Blue Moon, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Due to its slow growth and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
