Eremostachys schugnanica

Eremostachys schugnanica, commonly known as the Schugnanica eremostachys, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, blooming in late summer. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
