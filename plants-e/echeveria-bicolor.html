Echeveria bicolor

Echeveria bicolor, commonly known as the Two-Colored Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves with a contrasting pink or red edge. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
