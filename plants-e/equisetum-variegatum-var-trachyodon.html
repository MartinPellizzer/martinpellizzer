Equisetum variegatum var. trachyodon

Equisetum variegatum var. trachyodon, commonly known as the variable horsetail, is a variant of the common horsetail that exhibits distinct botanical characteristics.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family, known for its whorled arrangement of small, spore-bearing cones. The plant features a tall, slender stem covered in small, scale-like structures, with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Its unique morphology and reproductive structures make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
