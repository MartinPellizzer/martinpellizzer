Echites schomburgkii

Echites schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's echites, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
