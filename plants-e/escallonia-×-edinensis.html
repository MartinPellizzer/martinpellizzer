Escallonia × edinensis

Escallonia × edinensis, commonly known as the Edinburgh escallonia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the UK.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or red flowers in spring. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is often used in gardens for hedging or as a specimen plant.

Escallonia × edinensis is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
