Eria berringtoniana

Eria berringtoniana, commonly known as the Berrington's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria berringtoniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
