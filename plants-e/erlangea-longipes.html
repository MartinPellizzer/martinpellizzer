Erlangea longipes

Erlangea longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed erlangea, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth, waxy surface.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and potential medicinal properties.
