Echeveria argentinensis var. kieslingii

Echeveria argentinensis var. kieslingii, commonly known as the Kieslingii Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Argentina.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact growth and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
