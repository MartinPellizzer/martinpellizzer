Euphorbia Alainii

Euphorbia alainii is a unique and striking succulent native to Madagascar. It is known for its vibrant, spiky appearance and its ability to thrive in arid environments. This plant is often mistaken for a cactus due to its similar look, but it belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family. Its distinctive features make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its toxic sap.
