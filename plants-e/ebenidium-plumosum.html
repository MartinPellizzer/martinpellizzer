Ebenidium plumosum

Ebenidium plumosum, commonly known as the feather moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pterobryaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like shoots that give it its common name. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. Ebenidium plumosum has a low-growing habit and forms dense mats that help retain soil moisture.

Its structure includes leafy shoots with a distinct central axis and specialized structures for water retention.
