Eryngium dusenii

Eryngium dusenii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiky, blue to purple flower heads surrounded by bracts that resemble a crown. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Eryngium dusenii is a perennial herb that blooms in late summer and early autumn.
