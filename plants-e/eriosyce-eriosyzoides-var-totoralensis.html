Eriosyce eriosyzoides var. totoralensis

Eriosyce eriosyzoides var. totoralensis, commonly known as the Totoralensis Eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white, woolly hairs. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
