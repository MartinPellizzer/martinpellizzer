Echinodorus amphibius

Echinodorus amphibius, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 20 inches in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can tolerate a range of water temperatures.

Echinodorus amphibius is also known for its ability to help maintain water quality by absorbing nutrients and reducing algae growth.
