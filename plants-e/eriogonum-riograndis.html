Eriogonum riograndis

Eriogonum riograndis, commonly known as the desert wild buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae) and is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves. Eriogonum riograndis is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats, contributing to local biodiversity.
