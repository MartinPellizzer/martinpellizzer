Eriogonum montanum

Eriogonum montanum, commonly known as mountain buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. Eriogonum montanum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing food for pollinators.
