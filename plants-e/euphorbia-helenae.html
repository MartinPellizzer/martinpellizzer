Euphorbia helenae

Euphorbia helenae, commonly known as Helen's spurge, is a succulent plant native to the island of Crete.

It features a distinctive appearance with its cylindrical, segmented stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its arid environment. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardiness.

Euphorbia helenae is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.
