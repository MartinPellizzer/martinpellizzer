Emilia lejolyana

Emilia lejolyana, commonly known as the goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats, including grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Emilia lejolyana is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
