Echium Buekii

Echium buekii is a striking plant native to the Canary Islands, known for its vibrant purple flowers and spiky foliage. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family, which includes other showy and often medicinal plants. This species thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes. Echium buekii is not only ornamental but also holds cultural significance in its native region.
