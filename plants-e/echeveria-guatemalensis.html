Echeveria guatemalensis

Echeveria guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Central America.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green or purple in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts for rock gardens and container planting.
