Epidendrum lorifolium

Epidendrum lorifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent purple sepals and petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum lorifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
