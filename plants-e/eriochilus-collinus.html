Eriochilus collinus

Eriochilus collinus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriochilus, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained environments.

Eriochilus collinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
