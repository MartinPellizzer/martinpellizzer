Eulophia Brevisepala

Eulophia with short sepals, scientifically known as Eulophia brevisepala, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Southeast Asia. This unique orchid is characterized by its compact size and distinctive floral structure, featuring short, narrow sepals that give it a refined appearance. Often found in shaded, moist forest environments, it thrives in humid climates with well-drained soil. Its elegant blooms, which range in color from white to pale yellow, make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Eulophia brevisepala is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
