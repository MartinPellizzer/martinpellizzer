Eria ustulata

Eria ustulata, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are borne on racemes and are known for their unique, flame-like appearance.

Eria ustulata requires high humidity and well-draining soil to thrive, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
