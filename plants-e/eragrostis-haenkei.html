Eragrostis haenkei

Eragrostis haenkei, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a distinctive ornamental plant. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in prairie and woodland habitats.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
