Elatostema pseudodissectum

Elatostema pseudodissectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, ovate leaves with a prominent midrib. The leaves are typically arranged alternately along the stem and may exhibit a slightly serrated margin. This plant produces tiny greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are often subtended by bracts.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
