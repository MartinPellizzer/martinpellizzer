Eriogonum ovalifolium var. bellum

Eriogonum ovalifolium var. bellum, commonly known as the Sierra buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and meadows. It has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

The Sierra buckwheat plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
