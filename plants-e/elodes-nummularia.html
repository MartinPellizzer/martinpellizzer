Elodes nummularia

Elodes nummularia, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually submerged, followed by small, greenish fruits.

Elodes nummularia plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and fish.
