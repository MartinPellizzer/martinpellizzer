Eulophia granducalis

Eulophia granducalis, commonly known as the giant eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia granducalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
