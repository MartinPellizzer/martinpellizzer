Eria neglecta

Eria neglecta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Eria neglecta thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
