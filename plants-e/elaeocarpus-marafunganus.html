Elaeocarpus marafunganus

Elaeocarpus marafunganus, commonly known as the Philippine elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest for conservation and horticultural studies.
