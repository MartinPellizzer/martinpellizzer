Epidendrum × obrienianum

Epidendrum × obrienianum, commonly known as the O'Brien's Epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with colorful, fragrant flowers that range from yellow to red. Its leaves are typically elongated and strap-like, with a smooth or slightly textured surface.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
