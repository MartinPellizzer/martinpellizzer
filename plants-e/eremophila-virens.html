Eremophila virens

Eremophila virens, commonly known as the green eremophila, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, which bloom in spring. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and a preference for well-drained soils.

Eremophila virens is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
