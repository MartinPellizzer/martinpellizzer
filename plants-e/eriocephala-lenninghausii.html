Eriocephala lenninghausii

Eriocephala lenninghausii, commonly known as the Leninghaus's eriocephala, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
