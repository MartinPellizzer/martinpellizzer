Eugenioides latiflorum

Eugenioides latiflorum, commonly known as the broadflower eugenioid, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a fragrant aroma. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The flowers are primarily pollinated by insects, and the plant produces small, dark berries as its fruit.
