Epidendrum lanceolatum

Epidendrum lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is epiphytic, often growing on trees in humid tropical environments. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum lanceolatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
