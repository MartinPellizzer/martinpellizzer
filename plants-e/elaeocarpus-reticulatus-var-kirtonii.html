Elaeocarpus reticulatus var. kirtonii

Elaeocarpus reticulatus var. kirtonii, commonly known as the Kirton's holly.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indo-Pacific region, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
