Encyclia purpurachyla

Encyclia purpurachyla, commonly known as the purple-tinged orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker.

Encyclia purpurachyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
