Etlingera muluensis

Etlingera muluensis, commonly known as the Mulu torch ginger, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, red-orange inflorescence that resembles a torch, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly waxy texture.
