Erythranthe procera

Erythranthe procera, commonly known as the tall rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. This perennial plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun. Its striking purple or pink flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
