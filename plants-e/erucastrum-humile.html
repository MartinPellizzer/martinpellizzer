Erucastrum humile

Erucastrum humile, commonly known as the small eructate, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. The plant features small, elongated leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in rocky or sandy environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
