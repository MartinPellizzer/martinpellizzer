Eriochilus helonomos

Eriochilus helonomos, commonly known as the pink mountain grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The species features slender, hairy stems and produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open heathlands and rocky areas.
