Eugenia demeusei

Eugenia demeusei, commonly known as the Demeuse's Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, spherical berries. Eugenia demeusei is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.

Its fruit is a food source for various native wildlife species.
