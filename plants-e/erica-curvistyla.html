Erica curvistyla

Erica curvistyla, commonly known as the curved-styled heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. Erica curvistyla typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
