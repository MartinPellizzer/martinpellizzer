Elaeocarpus dongnaiensis

Elaeocarpus dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the region's biodiversity.
