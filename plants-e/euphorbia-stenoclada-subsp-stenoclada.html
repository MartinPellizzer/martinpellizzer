Euphorbia stenoclada subsp. stenoclada

Euphorbia stenoclada subsp.

stenoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite, narrow, fleshy leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic flower-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact form and unique foliage.
