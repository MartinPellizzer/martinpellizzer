Ebnerella multiceps

Ebnerella multiceps, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that remain throughout the year, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. Ebnerella multiceps thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden settings.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to produce clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers.
