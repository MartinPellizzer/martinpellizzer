Eurya muricata var. muricata

Eurya muricata var. muricata, commonly known as the roughleaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. This evergreen shrub is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
