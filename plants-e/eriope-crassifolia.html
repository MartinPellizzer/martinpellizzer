Eriope crassifolia

Eriope crassifolia, commonly known as the thick-leaved eriope, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, often during the spring or summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
