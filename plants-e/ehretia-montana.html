Ehretia montana

Ehretia montana, commonly known as the mountain ehretia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Ehretia montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
