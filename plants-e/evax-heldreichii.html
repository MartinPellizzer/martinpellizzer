Evax heldreichii

Evax heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in mountainous regions. The plant features showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine hairy coating.

Evax heldreichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
