Erythraea ramosissima var. tenuiflora

Erythraea ramosissima var. tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered erythraea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens, though it is not widely cultivated.
