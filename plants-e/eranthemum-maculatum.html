Eranthemum maculatum

Eranthemum maculatum, commonly known as the spotted eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink or white. Its blooming period occurs during the warmer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
