Eugenia psidiiflora var. schultziana

Eugenia psidiiflora var. schultziana, commonly known as the Schultze's guava, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, pink to red berries. The fruit has a sweet, aromatic flavor and is often used in culinary applications.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is valued for both its ornamental and edible qualities.
