Eragrostis laxa

Eragrostis laxa, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its drooping, feathery panicles that give it a delicate, weeping appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Eragrostis laxa is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
