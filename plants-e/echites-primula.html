Echites primula

Echites primula, commonly known as the common primrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, white, and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually hairy as well.

Echites primula is a herbaceous perennial that blooms in early spring, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking early-season color.
