Echinocactus denudatus var. heuschkelianus

Echinocactus denudatus var. heuschkelianus, commonly known as the Heuschkelianus cactus, is a rare and striking member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of Mexico, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features a cylindrical stem with prominent, ribbed structures and spines that are typically reddish in color. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring to early summer.
