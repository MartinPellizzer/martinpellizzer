Eriogonum niveum var. greenei

Eriogonum niveum var. greenei, commonly known as the Whiteleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its white to pale green foliage and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and meadows. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, known for its unique seed structures and ecological importance.
