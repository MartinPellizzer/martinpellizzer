Erigeron dryophyllus

Erigeron dryophyllus, commonly known as the desert fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.
