Exarata

Exarata, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen plant native to South Africa. The plant features long, arching leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries.

Exarata is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions and is often used as an indoor plant.
