Etlingera rubrostriata

Etlingera rubrostriata, commonly known as the red-striated torch ginger, is a tropical plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the island of New Guinea and is characterized by its striking red and orange inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a strong, spicy aroma. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are typically used in traditional ceremonies and have cultural significance in indigenous communities.
