Embelia hainanensis

Embelia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan embelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
