Erysimum capitatum var. perenne

Erysimum capitatum var. perenne, commonly known as the perennial wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and hairy, and its stems are upright with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its early spring bloom and ability to attract pollinators.
