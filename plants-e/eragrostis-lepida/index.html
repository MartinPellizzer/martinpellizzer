Eragrostis Lepida

Eragrostis lepida, commonly known as pink needlegrass, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the southwestern United States. With its vibrant pink flowers and delicate, grass-like foliage, it stands out among native flora. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its unique appearance has made it a favorite among gardeners and landscapers seeking both beauty and drought tolerance. Eragrostis lepida is not only aesthetically pleasing but also supports local wildlife and contributes to ecological balance.
