Epimedium coelestre

Epimedium coelestre, commonly known as barrenwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to Asia.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring before the leaves emerge. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
