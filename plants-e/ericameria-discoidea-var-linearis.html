Ericameria discoidea var. linearis

Ericameria discoidea var. linearis, commonly known as the linearleaf goldenbush, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowering heads that appear in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It is part of the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its aromatic foliage.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
