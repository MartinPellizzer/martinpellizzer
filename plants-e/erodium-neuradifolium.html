Erodium neuradifolium

Erodium neuradifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf storm weed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb with slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are pink to purple, with five petals and are borne in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and fields.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize areas quickly.
