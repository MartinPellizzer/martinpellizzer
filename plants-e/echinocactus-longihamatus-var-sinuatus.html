Echinocactus longihamatus var. sinuatus

Echinocactus longihamatus var. sinuatus, commonly known as the Sinuatus Hedgehog Cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, spiny areoles that bear both radial and central spines. The plant has a distinctive ribbed structure with a greenish-gray coloration and a slightly ribbed surface. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.
