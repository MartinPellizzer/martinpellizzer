Epidendrum itacolomiense

Epidendrum itacolomiense, commonly known as the Itacolomi orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, which is characteristic of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with red or purple markings, and it produces a showy inflorescence.

Epidendrum itacolomiense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
