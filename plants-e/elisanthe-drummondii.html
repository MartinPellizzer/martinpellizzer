Elisanthe drummondii

Elisanthe drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming species that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its drought-tolerant appearance. Elisanthe drummondii is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
