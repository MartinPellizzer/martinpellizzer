Epidendrum cancanae

Epidendrum cancanae, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Epidendrum cancanae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
