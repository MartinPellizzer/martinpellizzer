Euphrasia altaica

Euphrasia altaica, commonly known as the Altai eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. Euphrasia altaica is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies for eye conditions.
