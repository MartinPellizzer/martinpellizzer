Euterpe ptariana

Euterpe ptariana, commonly known as the white palm, is a species of palm native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to three meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by bright red fruits containing a single seed. Euterpe ptariana is often found in moist, shaded areas of the Atlantic Forest.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is also used in traditional medicine.
