Eria tenuicaulis

Eria tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed eria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are linear and arranged spirally along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Eria tenuicaulis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in controlled indoor environments.
