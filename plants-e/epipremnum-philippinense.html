Epipremnum philippinense

Epipremnum philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure known as a spadix. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage.

Epipremnum philippinense is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for interior gardening.
