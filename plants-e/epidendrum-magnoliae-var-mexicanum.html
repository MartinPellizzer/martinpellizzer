Epidendrum magnoliae var. mexicanum

Epidendrum magnoliae var. mexicanum, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in warm climates.
