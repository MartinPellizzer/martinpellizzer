Elmerrillia vrieseana

Elmerrillia vrieseana, commonly known as the Vriesea orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that resemble orchids. The plant features rosettes of sword-shaped leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Elmerrillia vrieseana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
