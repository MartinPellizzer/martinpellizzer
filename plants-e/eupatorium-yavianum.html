Eupatorium yavianum

Eupatorium yavianum, commonly known as the Yavian boneset, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Eupatorium yavianum is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
