Elionurus gobariensis

Elionurus gobariensis, commonly known as the Gobariensis grass, is a perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.
