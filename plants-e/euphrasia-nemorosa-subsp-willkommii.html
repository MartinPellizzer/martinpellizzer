Euphrasia nemorosa subsp. willkommii

Euphrasia nemorosa subsp.

willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm's eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer.
