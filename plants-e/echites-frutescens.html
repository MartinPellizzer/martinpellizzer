Echites frutescens

Echites frutescens, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10-30 cm. The plant features rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Echites frutescens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
