Eragrostis lugens subsp. flaccida

Eragrostis lugens subsp.

flaccida, commonly known as the slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its drooping, pale green leaves and narrow, cylindrical seed heads.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its spread in suitable habitats.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important component of its native ecosystems.
