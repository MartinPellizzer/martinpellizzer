Eupatorium crenifolium

Eupatorium crenifolium, commonly known as roughleaf thoroughwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that have serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white to pale pink, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Eupatorium crenifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for pollinators.
