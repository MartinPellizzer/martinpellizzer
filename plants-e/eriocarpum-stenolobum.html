Eriocarpum stenolobum

Eriocarpum stenolobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed eriocarpum, is a species of flowering plant native to South Australia.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, hairy fruits that are characteristic of the genus Eriocarpum. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and provides habitat for various insect species.
