Epidendrum schlimii

Epidendrum schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, and the plant prefers bright, indirect light and high humidity.

Epidendrum schlimii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
