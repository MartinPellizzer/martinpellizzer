Eucalyptus microcorys

Eucalyptus microcorys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed shrubs with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, globular flower buds that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Eucalyptus microcorys plays an important role in the ecosystems of its native range, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
