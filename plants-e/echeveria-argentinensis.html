Echeveria argentinensis

Echeveria argentinensis, commonly known as the Argentinian rose or the silver rose, is a succulent plant native to Argentina.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a fine layer of white tomentum. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Echeveria argentinensis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
