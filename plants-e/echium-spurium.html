Echium spurium

Echium spurium, commonly known as purpleweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and can grow up to 1.5 meters tall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Echium spurium is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
