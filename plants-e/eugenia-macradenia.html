Eugenia macradenia

Eugenia macradenia, commonly known as the broad-leaved eugenia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
