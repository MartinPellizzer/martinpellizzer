Exemix

Exemix, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features slender, woody stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Exemix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
