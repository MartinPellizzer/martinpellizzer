Eulophia encyclioides

Eulophia encyclioides, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and a rosette of lanceolate, dark green leaves. The plant produces a tall, slender inflorescence bearing numerous fragrant, spiral-shaped flowers with white to pale pink petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia encyclioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique floral structure.
