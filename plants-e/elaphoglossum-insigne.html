Elaphoglossum insigne

Elaphoglossum insigne, commonly known as the noble holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, finely divided structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of its fertile fronds.
