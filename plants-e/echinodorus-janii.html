Echinodorus janii

Echinodorus janii, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

Echinodorus janii is also known for its moderate growth rate and ease of care, making it a favorite among hobbyists.
