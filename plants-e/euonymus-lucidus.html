Euonymus lucidus

Euonymus lucidus, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red, lantern-like fruits that persist into winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and attractive appearance make it suitable for hedges and landscape gardens.
