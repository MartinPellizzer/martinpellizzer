Erythrina corallodendron var. occidentalis

Erythrina corallodendron var. occidentalis, commonly known as the Western coral tree, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its striking red and orange flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to regions of southern Africa. The plant features palmately compound leaves and produces distinctive, coral-like inflorescences that attract pollinators such as birds and insects.

Its seeds are encased in a woody pod, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
