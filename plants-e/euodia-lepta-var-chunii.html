Euodia lepta var. chunii

Euodia lepta var. chunii, commonly known as Chinese laurustin, is a variant of the Euodia genus native to China.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its aromatic, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by dark, glossy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a valued addition to shaded garden spaces.
