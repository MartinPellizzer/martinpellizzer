Eulophia keniensis

Eulophia keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Kenya.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats, often near streams or in mountainous regions. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Eulophia keniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems.
