Eriosyce sandillon

Eriosyce sandillon, commonly known as the sandillon moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
