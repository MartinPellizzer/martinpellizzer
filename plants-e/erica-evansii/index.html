Erica Evansii

Erica evansii is a rare and striking evergreen shrub native to the rugged coastal regions of South Africa. With its dense, needle-like foliage and clusters of vibrant pink and white flowers, it adds a unique touch to any landscape. This plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. Known for its resilience and hardy nature, Erica evansii is a popular choice among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants. Its ecological importance also makes it a valuable addition to native plant conservation efforts.
