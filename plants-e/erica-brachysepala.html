Erica brachysepala

Erica brachysepala, commonly known as the short-sepal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color.

Erica brachysepala thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
