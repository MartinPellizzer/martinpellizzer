Elatostema shanglinense

Elatostema shanglinense, commonly known as the Shanglinenese Elatostema, is a member of the Urticaceae family, characterized by its small, often succulent leaves.

It is native to China, specifically the Guangxi region, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with a prostrate or ascending habit. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and may have a waxy coating.

Elatostema shanglinense is notable for its ability to form dense mats, contributing to the ecological structure of its habitat.
