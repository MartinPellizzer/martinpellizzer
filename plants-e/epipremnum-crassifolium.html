Epipremnum crassifolium

Epipremnum crassifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf philodendron, is a climbing tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly variegated appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its stems are sturdy and can climb using aerial roots, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.

Epipremnum crassifolium is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
