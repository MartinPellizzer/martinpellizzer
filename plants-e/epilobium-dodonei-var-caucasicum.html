Epilobium dodonei var. caucasicum

Epilobium dodonei var. caucasicum, commonly known as Caucasian willow, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom in the spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
