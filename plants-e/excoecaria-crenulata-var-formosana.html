Excoecaria crenulata var. formosana

Excoecaria crenulata var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
