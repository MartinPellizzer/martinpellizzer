Eryngium humile var. multiradiatum

Eryngium humile var. multiradiatum, commonly known as the lesser thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features blue to purple spiky flower heads surrounded by white or pale blue bracts. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
