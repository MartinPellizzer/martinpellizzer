Encyclia spondiada

Encyclia spondiada, commonly known as the Spondiada orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. Its inflorescence is a raceme that bears multiple blooms, often arranged in a spiral pattern.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
