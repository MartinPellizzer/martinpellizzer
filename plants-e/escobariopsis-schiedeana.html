Escobariopsis schiedeana

Escobariopsis schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiede's broom.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina, Bolivia, and Chile. The plant is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or green. Its leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a slightly hairy surface.

Escobariopsis schiedeana is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
