Euphorbia meyeriana

Euphorbia meyeriana, commonly known as Meyer's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of yellow-green flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has smooth, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
