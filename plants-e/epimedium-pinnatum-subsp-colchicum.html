Epimedium pinnatum subsp. colchicum

Epimedium pinnatum subsp.

colchicum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs.

The plant produces showy, yellowish-green to reddish flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, erect pedicels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
