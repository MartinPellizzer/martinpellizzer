Erica multicaulis

Erica multicaulis, commonly known as the many-stemmed heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, acidic soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions. Erica multicaulis produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often blooming in spring and summer.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for garden landscaping.
