Epimedium pteroceras

Epimedium pteroceras, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features striking, shield-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed and emit a faint fragrance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels.

Epimedium pteroceras is well-suited for woodland gardens and thrives in partial shade with moist, well-drained soil.
