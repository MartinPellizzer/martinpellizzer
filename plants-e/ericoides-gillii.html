Ericoides gillii

Ericoides gillii, commonly known as the Gill's ericoid, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and evergreen foliage. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color.

Ericoides gillii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.
