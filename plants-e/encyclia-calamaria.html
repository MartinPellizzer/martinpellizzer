Encyclia calamaria

Encyclia calamaria, commonly known as the Calamary orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia calamaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in suitable conditions.
