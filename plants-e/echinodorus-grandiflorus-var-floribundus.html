Echinodorus grandiflorus var. floribundus

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. floribundus, commonly known as the giant sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, providing dense coverage in aquariums. The plant produces striking white to pink flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding ornamental value. It thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light and nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its rapid growth, it is often used for aquatic gardening and water filtration.
