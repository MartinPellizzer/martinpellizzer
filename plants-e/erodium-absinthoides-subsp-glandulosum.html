Erodium absinthoides subsp. glandulosum

Erodium absinthoides subsp.

glandulosum, commonly known as sticky erodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing annual herb with small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple.

The plant features glandular hairs on its stems and leaves, which give it a sticky texture. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
