Erigeron luteus

Erigeron luteus, commonly known as the yellow fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. Erigeron luteus is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
