Etlingera elatior var. pileng

Etlingera elatior var. pileng, commonly known as the Pileng torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, red-orange flowers that resemble torches. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a slight sheen.
