Eriocaulon officinale

Eriocaulon officinale, commonly known as the medicinal grass or small water plant, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dense covering of fine hairs.

Eriocaulon officinale is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
