Epipactis Persica

Epipactis persica is a rare and fascinating orchid native to parts of southern Europe and western Asia. This delicate plant is known for its unique floral structure and striking violet-blue petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting Epipactis persica is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
