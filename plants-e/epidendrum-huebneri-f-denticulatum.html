Epidendrum huebneri f. denticulatum

Epidendrum huebneri f. denticulatum, commonly known as the toothed epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive toothed margin. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow with a contrasting lip, and it blooms during the warmer months.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
