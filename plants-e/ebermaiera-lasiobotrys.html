Ebermaiera lasiobotrys

Ebermaiera lasiobotrys, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Ebermaiera lasiobotrys is also known for its ability to grow rapidly, making it both a useful forage plant and a potential weed in agricultural settings.
