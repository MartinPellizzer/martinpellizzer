Euodia brombyana

Euodia brombyana, commonly known as the slender tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
