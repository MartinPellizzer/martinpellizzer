Euphorbia viguieri var. viguieri

Euphorbia viguieri var. viguieri, commonly known as the Viguier's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, stocky stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic flower-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
