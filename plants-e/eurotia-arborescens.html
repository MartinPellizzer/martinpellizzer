Eurotia arborescens

Eurotia arborescens, commonly known as the tree-like eurotia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Eurotia arborescens is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for drought-resistant landscaping.
