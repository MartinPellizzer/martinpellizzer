Eulophia ensata

Eulophia ensata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding substrate, and it is often found in rocky or mossy areas.

Eulophia ensata is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
