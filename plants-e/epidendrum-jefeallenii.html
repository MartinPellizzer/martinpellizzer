Epidendrum jefeallenii

Epidendrum jefeallenii, commonly known as the Jefe Allen's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
