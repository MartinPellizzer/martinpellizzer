Erigeron philippii f. pluriceps

Erigeron philippii f. pluriceps, commonly known as the Pluriceps Fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
