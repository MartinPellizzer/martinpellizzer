Epidendrum kerichilum

Epidendrum kerichilum, commonly known as the Kerichilum orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Epidendrum kerichilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
