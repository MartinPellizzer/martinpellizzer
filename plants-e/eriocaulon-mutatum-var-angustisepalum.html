Eriocaulon mutatum var. angustisepalum

Eriocaulon mutatum var. angustisepalum, commonly known as the narrow-sepal eriocaulon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, pointed leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white flowers. It typically grows in wet, marshy environments, preferring moist soils and partial shade.
