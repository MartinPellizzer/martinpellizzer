Erica hillburttii

Erica hillburttii, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter, adding a striking contrast to its dark green foliage. Erica hillburttii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
