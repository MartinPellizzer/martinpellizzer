Echinocereus durangensis var. nigrispinus

Echinocereus durangensis var. nigrispinus, commonly known as the Black-spined Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spine-covered cactus with a compact, rounded growth habit. The stems are typically green and ribbed, with clusters of white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its spines are short and often have a blackish or reddish tinge, giving it its common name.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
