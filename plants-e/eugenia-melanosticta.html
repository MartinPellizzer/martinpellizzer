Eugenia melanosticta

Eugenia melanosticta, commonly known as the black-streaked eucalypt, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its bark is often flaky and grey, while its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped.

The flowers are followed by small, woody capsules that contain the plant's seeds.
