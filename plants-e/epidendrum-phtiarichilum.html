Epidendrum phtiarichilum

Epidendrum phtiarichilum, commonly known as the orchid with silver-green leaves, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a unique, intricate pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
