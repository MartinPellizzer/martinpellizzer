Epidendrum odontospathum

Epidendrum odontospathum, commonly known as the toothed spathum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage. The plant produces striking inflorescences with large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.
