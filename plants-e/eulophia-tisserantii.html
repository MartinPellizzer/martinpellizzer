Eulophia tisserantii

Eulophia tisserantii, commonly known as the Tisserant's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with numerous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia tisserantii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
