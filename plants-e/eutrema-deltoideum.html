Eutrema deltoideum

Eutrema deltoideum, commonly known as delta iceplant, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers that range from white to pink. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its ability to store water in its leaves. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often found in disturbed soils and rocky areas.

Eutrema deltoideum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects and animals.
