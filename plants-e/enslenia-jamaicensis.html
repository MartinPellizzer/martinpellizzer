Enslenia jamaicensis

Enslenia jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican starfruit, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica. The plant is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are small, round, and resemble starfruits, hence its common name.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
