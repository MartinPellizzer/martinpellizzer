Eria herklotsii

Eria herklotsii, commonly known as the Herklots' orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Eria herklotsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
