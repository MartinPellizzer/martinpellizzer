Euthales peruviana

Euthales peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian holly, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are thick and leathery, providing a distinctive appearance.

Euthales peruviana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
