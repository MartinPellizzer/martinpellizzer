Eriosyce Subgibbosa Var. Nigrihorrida

Eriosyce subgibbosa var. nigrihorrida is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of central Chile. Known for its unique appearance, it features dark green stems covered in dense, white spines that give it a striking visual contrast. This variety is particularly noted for its thick, rounded body and the presence of small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. Due to its limited habitat and susceptibility to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
