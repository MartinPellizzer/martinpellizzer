Eriocaulon koynense

Eriocaulon koynense, commonly known as the Koynense spike-moss, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a smooth or slightly rough surface.

Eriocaulon koynense plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetlands and contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat.
