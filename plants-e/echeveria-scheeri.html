Echeveria scheeri

Echeveria scheeri, commonly known as the Scheeri Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-gray in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its color and shape.

Echeveria scheeri is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
