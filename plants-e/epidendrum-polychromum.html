Epidendrum polychromum

Epidendrum polychromum, commonly known as the rainbow epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flower spikes that can display a wide range of hues, including red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are borne on long, branching inflorescences and can last for several weeks.

Epidendrum polychromum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
