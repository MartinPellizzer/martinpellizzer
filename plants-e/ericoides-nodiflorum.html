Ericoides nodiflorum

Ericoides nodiflorum, commonly known as the clustered heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant is native to regions with acidic soils, often found in heathlands and moorlands. Its growth habit is low and compact, with multiple stems arising from the base.

Ericoides nodiflorum is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystem.
