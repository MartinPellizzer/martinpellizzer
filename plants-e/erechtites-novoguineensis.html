Erechtites novoguineensis

Erechtites novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
