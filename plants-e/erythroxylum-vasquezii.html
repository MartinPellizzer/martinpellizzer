Erythroxylum vasquezii

Erythroxylum vasquezii, commonly known as the Vasquez's erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It produces elliptical, red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals.
