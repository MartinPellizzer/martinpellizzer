Eriostemon myoporoides subsp. acutus

Eriostemon myoporoides subsp.

acutus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, glandular hairs.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in native gardens.
