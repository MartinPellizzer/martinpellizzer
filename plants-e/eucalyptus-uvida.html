Eucalyptus uvida

Eucalyptus uvida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallee, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and smooth, often greyish bark. The plant produces small, globular flower clusters that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resistance to drought and salinity.

Eucalyptus uvida is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
