Euphorbia decaryi var. decaryi

Euphorbia decaryi var. decaryi, commonly known as the Decary's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features a branching stem system with thick, fleshy stems that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are the specialized flower-like structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are often reduced to small, scale-like structures, further reducing water loss.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
