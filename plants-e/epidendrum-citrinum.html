Epidendrum citrinum

Epidendrum citrinum, commonly known as the Lemon Spike, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and upright, branching stems. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum citrinum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
