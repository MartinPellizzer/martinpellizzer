Eulophia stenophylla

Eulophia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
