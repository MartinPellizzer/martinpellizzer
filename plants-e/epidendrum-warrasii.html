Epidendrum warrasii

Epidendrum warrasii, commonly known as the Warrasii Vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing erect or pendant inflorescences. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum warrasii is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
