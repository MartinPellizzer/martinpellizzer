Elatostema surculosum var. pinnatifidum

Elatostema surculosum var. pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.
