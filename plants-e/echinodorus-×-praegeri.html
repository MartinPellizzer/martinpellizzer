Echinodorus × praegeri

Echinodorus × praegeri, commonly known as the Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate the water. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can be propagated through stem cuttings.

Its robust growth makes it an excellent choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes in aquatic habitats.
