Epilobium × schmidtianum

Epilobium × schmidtianum, commonly known as the Schmidt's willow herb, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and hairy stems, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in terminal clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often covered with fine hairs.

Epilobium × schmidtianum is often found in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
