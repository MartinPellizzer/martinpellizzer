Ehretia acuminata var. obovata

Ehretia acuminata var. obovata, commonly known as the broadleaf ehretia, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
