Epidendrum ibarrae

Epidendrum ibarrae, commonly known as the Ibarra's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Epidendrum ibarrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, humid conditions.
