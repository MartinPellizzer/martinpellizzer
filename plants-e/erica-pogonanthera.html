Erica pogonanthera

Erica pogonanthera, commonly known as the purple heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica pogonanthera is adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
