Eleocharis uniglumis var. watsonii

Eleocharis uniglumis var. watsonii, commonly known as the Watson's spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight sheen, while the flowers are small and inconspicuous.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
