Eugenioides martii

Eugenioides martii, commonly known as the mountain myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations. Its bark and leaves contain aromatic compounds that have been used in traditional medicine.

Eugenioides martii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
