Eriochrysis porphyrocoma

Eriochrysis porphyrocoma, commonly known as the purple-leafed sea lettuce, is a marine green alga found in coastal regions.

It thrives in shallow, intertidal zones where it attaches to rocks or other substrates. The plant has a filamentous structure with a bluish-green color, and its cells are rich in chlorophyll, enabling efficient photosynthesis. It plays a vital role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.

Eriochrysis porphyrocoma is adapted to withstand tidal fluctuations and saltwater exposure.
