Erysimum Glandulosum

Erysimum glandulosum is a striking member of the mustard family known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive hairy foliage. Native to the western united states, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. it is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions where it adapts well to harsh conditions. with its compact growth habit, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
