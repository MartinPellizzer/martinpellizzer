Encyclia enriquearcilae

Encyclia enriquearcilae, commonly known as the Enrique Arcilae orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces dark green, strap-like leaves and bears fragrant, yellowish-white flowers with a distinctive purple lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
