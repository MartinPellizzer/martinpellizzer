Eulophia foliosa

Eulophia foliosa, commonly known as the leafy eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the rainy season. Eulophia foliosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical research.
