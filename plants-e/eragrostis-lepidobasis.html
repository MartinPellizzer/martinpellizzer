Eragrostis lepidobasis

Eragrostis lepidobasis, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, drooping inflorescences that give it a weeping appearance, and it typically grows in open, sunny habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered with fine hairs, and its flowering stems are usually erect.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for dryland restoration projects.
