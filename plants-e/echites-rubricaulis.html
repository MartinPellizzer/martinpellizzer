Echites rubricaulis

Echites rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and yellowish-green leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and dry landscapes.

Due to its toxic sap, it is important to handle the plant with care.
