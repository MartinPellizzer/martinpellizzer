Epidendrum bogarinii

Epidendrum bogarinii, commonly known as the Bogarinii Vanda, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a sympodial orchid with a short, creeping rhizome and produces numerous pseudobulbs. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in two ranks. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow lip, and are borne on lateral inflorescences.

Epidendrum bogarinii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
