Eranthis albiflora

Eranthis albiflora, commonly known as the white winter aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It features bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers with white veins, which bloom early in the spring. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that emerge before the flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Eranthis albiflora is valued for its early blooming habit and ornamental appeal in gardens.
