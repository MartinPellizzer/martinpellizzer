Eriogonum microtheca var. johnstonii

Eriogonum microtheca var. johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
