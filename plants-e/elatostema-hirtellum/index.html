Elatostema Hirtellum

Elatostema hirtellum is a small, herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of eastern Asia. It is commonly found in swampy areas and along the edges of ponds and streams. This species is known for its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers. Its unique adaptations allow it to thrive in waterlogged soils where few other plants can survive. Elatostema hirtellum plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its wetland habitat.
