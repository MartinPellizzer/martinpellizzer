Elatostema muscicola

Elatostema muscicola, commonly known as the mossy elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rosette of fleshy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, moss-like coating. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color, and arranged in clusters.

Elatostema muscicola is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its preference for well-drained, nutrient-rich soils.
