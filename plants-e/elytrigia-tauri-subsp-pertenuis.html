Elytrigia tauri subsp. pertenuis

Elytrigia tauri subsp.

pertenuis, commonly known as the slender taurian grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and sandy soils.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a tufted growth habit.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to withstand drought conditions.
