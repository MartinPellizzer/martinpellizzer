Eruca vesicaria var. stenocarpa

Eruca vesicaria var. stenocarpa, commonly known as narrow-fruited wall rocket, is a biennial or perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features erect, branched stems that are hairy and often reddish in color. The leaves are pinnately divided, with a peppery aroma, and the flowers are small, yellow, and borne in clusters. The fruit is a slender, cylindrical silique that matures in the second year of growth.

This plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in rocky areas.
