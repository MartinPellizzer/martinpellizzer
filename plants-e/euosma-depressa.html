Euosma depressa

Euosma depressa, commonly known as the flat euosma, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant features small, aromatic leaves with a dense, leathery texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are borne in clusters.

Euosma depressa is often found in rocky or dry habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
