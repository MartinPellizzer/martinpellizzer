Epidendrum lindleyanum

Epidendrum lindleyanum, commonly known as the Lindley's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple stems that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant features showy, pendulous flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive red or brown lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum lindleyanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
