Epilepis rudis

Epilepis rudis, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
