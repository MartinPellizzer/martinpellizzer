Eugenia miquelii

Eugenia miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's guava, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are similar in appearance to guavas but have a distinct flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Eugenia miquelii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
