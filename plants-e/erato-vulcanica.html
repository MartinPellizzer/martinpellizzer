Erato vulcanica

Erato vulcanica, commonly known as the Flamevine, is a strikingly unique plant native to volcanic regions.

It thrives in nutrient-rich, well-drained soils found in areas with recent volcanic activity. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along its trailing stems. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is known for its adaptability to extreme environmental conditions.
