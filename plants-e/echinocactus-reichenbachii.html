Echinocactus reichenbachii

Echinocactus reichenbachii, commonly known as the Mexican hat cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its ribbed stems and prominent, spiny areoles. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and desert landscapes.
