Epipactis atrorubens f. pallens

Epipactis atrorubens f. pallens, commonly known as the pale epipactis, is a rare and distinctive orchid species native to parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its pale green to yellowish leaves and small, pale green or brownish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodland floors or rocky crevices. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and exhibits a complex floral structure typical of orchids.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in many regions.
