Etlingera urophylla

Etlingera urophylla, commonly known as the yellow torch ginger, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, orange-red inflorescence that resembles a torch, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.
