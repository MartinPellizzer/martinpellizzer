Eryngium amethystinum var. dilatatum

Eryngium amethystinum var. dilatatum, commonly known as the purple thistle or velvet thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flower heads surrounded by white to gray bracts. The plant has basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and open woodlands.

Its unique morphology and vibrant coloration make it a distinctive addition to native plant gardens.
