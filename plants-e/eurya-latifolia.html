Eurya latifolia

Eurya latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries.

Eurya latifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
