Erica rugegensis

Erica rugegensis, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained, acidic soils. Its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers make it a distinctive ornamental plant.

Erica rugegensis is well adapted to harsh climates and requires minimal water once established.
