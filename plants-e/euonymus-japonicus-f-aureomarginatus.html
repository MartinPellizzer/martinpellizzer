Euonymus japonicus f. aureomarginatus

Euonymus japonicus f. aureomarginatus, commonly known as the Japanese spurge with golden margin, is a cultivar of the Euonymus japonicus species.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to Japan, characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The leaves are glossy and dark green with a distinct yellow or orange margin, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive foliage.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
