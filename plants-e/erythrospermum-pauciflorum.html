Erythrospermum pauciflorum

Erythrospermum pauciflorum, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
