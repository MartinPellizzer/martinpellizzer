Echinospermum sinaicum var. flaviflorum

Echinospermum sinaicum var. flaviflorum, commonly known as the Yellowflower Sinaicum, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces small, spherical fruits. It is adapted to survive in dry environments with minimal water.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
