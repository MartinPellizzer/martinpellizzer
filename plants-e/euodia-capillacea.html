Euodia capillacea

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. Euodia capillacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
