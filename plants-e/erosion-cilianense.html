Erosion cilianense

Erosion cilianense, commonly known as the Cilician erosion plant, is a rare flowering species native to the Cilicia region of Turkey.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in rocky, arid environments and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions. Its root system helps stabilize soil, making it valuable in preventing erosion.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
