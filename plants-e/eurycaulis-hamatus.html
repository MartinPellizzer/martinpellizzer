Eurycaulis hamatus

Eurycaulis hamatus, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a unique species native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a waxy coating, providing additional protection against drought.

Eurycaulis hamatus is well adapted to survive in low-nutrient soils and can thrive in full sun conditions.
