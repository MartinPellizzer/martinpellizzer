Eria clavicaulis

Eria clavicaulis, commonly known as the club-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, pink or white blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria clavicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
