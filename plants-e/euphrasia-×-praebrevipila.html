Euphrasia × praebrevipila

Euphrasia × praebrevipila, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically narrow and arranged alternately along the stem, giving it a grass-like appearance. This plant is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
