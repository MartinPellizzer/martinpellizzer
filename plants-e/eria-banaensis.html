Eria banaensis

Eria banaensis, commonly known as the Banaensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Eria banaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
