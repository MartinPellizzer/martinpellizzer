Echinocactus strobiliformis

Echinocactus strobiliformis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, spiny stems that form a compact, bushy shape. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
