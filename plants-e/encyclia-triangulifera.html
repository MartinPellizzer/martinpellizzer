Encyclia triangulifera

Encyclia triangulifera, commonly known as the triangular orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to produce fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, while the flowers are borne on erect, branched inflorescences.

Encyclia triangulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
