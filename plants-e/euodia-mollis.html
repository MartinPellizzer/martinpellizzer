Euodia mollis

Euodia mollis, commonly known as the fragrant euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a leathery texture and a subtle, spicy fragrance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Euodia mollis prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
