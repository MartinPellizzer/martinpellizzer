Euphronia hirtelloides

Euphronia hirtelloides, commonly known as the woolly euphronia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of rainforests. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, with hairy foliage that gives it its common name. Its leaves are ovate and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Euphronia hirtelloides is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in warm, tropical climates.
