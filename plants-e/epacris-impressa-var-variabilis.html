Epacris impressa var. variabilis

Epacris impressa var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable heath, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically in spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
