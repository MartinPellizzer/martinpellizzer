Eucalyptus polita

Eucalyptus polita, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallees, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Western Australia. The plant is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed shrubs with small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. Eucalyptus polita produces small, globular flower spikes that are typically pink or pale purple in color.

It is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
