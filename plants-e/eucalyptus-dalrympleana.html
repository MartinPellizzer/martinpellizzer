Eucalyptus dalrympleana

Eucalyptus dalrympleana, commonly known as the Dalrymple's mallee, is a small to medium-sized tree native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its rough, fissured bark that peels in strips, and its lance-shaped leaves that are usually grey-green in color. The plant produces creamy-white flowers in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months.

It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and woodlands.
