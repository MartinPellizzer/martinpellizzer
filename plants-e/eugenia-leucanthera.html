Eugenia leucanthera

Eugenia leucanthera, commonly known as the white-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries.

Eugenia leucanthera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
