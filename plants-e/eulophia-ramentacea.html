Eulophia ramentacea

Eulophia ramentacea, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia ramentacea is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
