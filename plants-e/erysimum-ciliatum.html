Erysimum ciliatum

Erysimum ciliatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf wallflower, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has fuzzy, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive texture and help reduce water loss. It grows up to 30 cm tall and prefers well-drained soil in full sun.

Erysimum ciliatum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
