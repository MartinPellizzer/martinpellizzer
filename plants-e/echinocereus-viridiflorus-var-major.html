Echinocereus viridiflorus var. major

Echinocereus viridiflorus var. major, commonly known as the green-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a greenish stem and produces bright yellow flowers during the spring. The plant has spiny, cylindrical stems and is adapted to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in desert landscapes.
