Euonymus europaeus f. albus

Euonymus europaeus f. albus, commonly known as the white euonymus, is a cultivar of the common euonymus.

It is characterized by its striking white foliage, which contrasts with its dark green stems. This plant is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-drained soil.

The white leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
