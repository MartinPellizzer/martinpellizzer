Excoecaria biglandulosa var. hamata

Excoecaria biglandulosa var. hamata, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that are often followed by orange or red fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
