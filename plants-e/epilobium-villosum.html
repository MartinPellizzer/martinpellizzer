Epilobium villosum

Epilobium villosum, commonly known as silky willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Epilobium villosum is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp environments.
