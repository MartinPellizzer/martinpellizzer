Eucharis

Eucharis, commonly known as the Amazon lily, is a genus of flowering plants in the Araceae family.

These plants are native to South America, particularly the Amazon rainforest, and are known for their striking, showy flowers. Eucharis species typically feature large, waxy leaves and produce inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe. They are popular as houseplants due to their elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

The genus includes several species, such as Eucharis grandiflora and Eucharis amazonica, which are prized for their ornamental value.
