Eria lorentziana

Eria lorentziana, commonly known as the Lorentziana orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and display a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
