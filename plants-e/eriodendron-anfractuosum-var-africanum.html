Eriodendron anfractuosum var. africanum

Eriodendron anfractuosum var. africanum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has pinnate leaves with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The tree is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and its role in supporting local wildlife.
