Eulophia krebsii var. purpurata

Eulophia krebsii var. purpurata, commonly known as the Purple Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are borne on slender, erect spikes. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
