Eugenia lambii

Eugenia lambii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Eugenia lambii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
