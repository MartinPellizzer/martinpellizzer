Erucastrum gallicum

Erucastrum gallicum, commonly known as the French wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing along roadsides and in disturbed areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its delicate floral display.
