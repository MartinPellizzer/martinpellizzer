Echinocereus enneacanthus var. stramineus

Echinocereus enneacanthus var. stramineus, commonly known as the straw-colored hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, spiny cactus that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 20 inches. The plant features pale green to straw-colored stems covered in clusters of white to pale yellow spines. It produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This drought-tolerant plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
