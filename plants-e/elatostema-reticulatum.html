Elatostema reticulatum

Elatostema reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern, which is a key identifying characteristic. Its flowers are usually tiny and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with white or pale yellow petals.

Elatostema reticulatum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
