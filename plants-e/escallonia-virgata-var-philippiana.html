Escallonia virgata var. philippiana

Escallonia virgata var. philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine escallonia, is a flowering shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers during the summer months. The plant is evergreen, with a compact, rounded growth habit that makes it suitable for ornamental landscaping. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value in its native habitat.
