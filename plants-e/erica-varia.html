Erica varia

Erica varia, commonly known as the variable heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats. Its small, bell-shaped flowers can range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety.

Erica varia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
