Epidendrum clypeolum

Epidendrum clypeolum, commonly known as the shield orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its often-dry habitats. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
