Epidendrum macrothyrsus

Epidendrum macrothyrsus, commonly known as the large-leaf epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has broad, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum macrothyrsus is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
