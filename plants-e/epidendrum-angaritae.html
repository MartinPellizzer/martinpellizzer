Epidendrum angaritae

Epidendrum angaritae, commonly known as the Angarita orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a contrasting purple or reddish lip.

Epidendrum angaritae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
