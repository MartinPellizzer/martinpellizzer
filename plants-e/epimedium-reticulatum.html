Epimedium reticulatum

Epimedium reticulatum, commonly known as the barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish or bronze tint. It produces yellowish-green flowers in spring, which are clustered in upright racemes.

Epimedium reticulatum is well-suited for shady gardens and is valued for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
