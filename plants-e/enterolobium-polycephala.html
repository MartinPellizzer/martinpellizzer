Enterolobium polycephala

Enterolobium polycephala, commonly known as the velvet bean tree, is a tropical legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and produces pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural systems. It also has ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

However, its seeds contain toxic compounds, so they must be handled with care.
