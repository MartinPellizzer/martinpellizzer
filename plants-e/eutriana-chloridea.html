Eutriana chloridea

Eutriana chloridea, commonly known as the green-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its natural habitat.

Eutriana chloridea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper moisture and light conditions.
