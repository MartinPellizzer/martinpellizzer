Eriosyce eriosyzoides var. huascensis

Eriosyce eriosyzoides var. huascensis, commonly known as the Huascensis Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the coastal regions of Peru.

It features a dense, spiny coat that protects it from herbivores and harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate contrast to its otherwise rugged appearance. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid climates with minimal rainfall.

This variety is valued for its unique morphology and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry gardens.
