Eccremocactus bradei

Eccremocactus bradei, commonly known as the Brade's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, columnar cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a smooth, green stem that often develops a reddish or pinkish hue under sunlight. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived but highly attractive to pollinators. It has a slow growth rate and is well adapted to harsh, dry environments.

Due to its unique beauty and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized collections.
