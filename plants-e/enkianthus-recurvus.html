Enkianthus recurvus

Enkianthus recurvus, commonly known as the recurved enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters and features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Enkianthus recurvus thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
