Echeveria sturmiana

Echeveria sturmiana, commonly known as the Sturm's echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant violet or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Echeveria sturmiana is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and ease of care.
