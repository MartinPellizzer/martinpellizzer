Epidendrum schistostemum

Epidendrum schistostemum, commonly known as the stone-stemmed epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive, spurred shape.

Epidendrum schistostemum is known for its resilience and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
