Encyclia michuacana

Encyclia michuacana, commonly known as the Michuacan Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or wooded areas. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a few leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. Its flowers are characterized by their long, slender petals and a distinctive lip.

Encyclia michuacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
