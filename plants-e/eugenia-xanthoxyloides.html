Eugenia xanthoxyloides

Eugenia xanthoxyloides, commonly known as the golden eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Following flowering, it bears small, round, yellow or orange fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid climates make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
