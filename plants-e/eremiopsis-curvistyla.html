Eremiopsis curvistyla

Eremiopsis curvistyla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremis, is a succulent shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that help it conserve water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the rainy season, adding a subtle ornamental value. Its adaptability to drought and poor soils makes it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Eremiopsis curvistyla is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil in degraded landscapes.
