Epidendrum polygonatum var. latifolium

Epidendrum polygonatum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity to flourish.

This orchid is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
