Eugenia neomooniana

Eugenia neomooniana, commonly known as the New Moon Beauty, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the delicate blooms.

Eugenia neomooniana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.
