Emilia flaccida

Emilia flaccida, commonly known as the slender emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Emilia flaccida is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
