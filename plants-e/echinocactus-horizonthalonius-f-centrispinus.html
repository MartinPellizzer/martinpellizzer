Echinocactus horizonthalonius f. centrispinus

Echinocactus horizonthalonius f. centrispinus, commonly known as the Centrispina Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in thick, white to grayish spines that provide protection from predators and intense sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny form. It is well adapted to dry environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
