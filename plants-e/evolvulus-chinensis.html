Evolvulus chinensis

Evolvulus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trumpet-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
