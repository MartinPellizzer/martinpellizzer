Erdisia

Erdisia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed habitats. Erdisia species are known for their ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which benefits soil fertility.

They are also used in some traditional medicinal practices.
