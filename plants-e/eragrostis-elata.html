Eragrostis elata

Eragrostis elata, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a distinctive ornamental plant. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairie and woodland habitats.

Eragrostis elata is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
