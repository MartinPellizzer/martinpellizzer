Elatine minima

Elatine minima, commonly known as the dwarf water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and ditches. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often submerged, with tiny, opposite leaves that are oval to lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing tiny, greenish-white seeds.

Elatine minima is an important component of aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
