Eugenia psiloclada

Eugenia psiloclada, commonly known as the purple-leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark purple leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns red when mature.

Eugenia psiloclada is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
