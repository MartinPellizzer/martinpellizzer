Epimedium sutchuenense

Epimedium sutchuenense, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features attractive, leathery leaves that are typically reddish-brown to green in color. It produces yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in panicles.

Epimedium sutchuenense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to shaded garden environments.
