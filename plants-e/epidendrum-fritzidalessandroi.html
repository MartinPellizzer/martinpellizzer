Epidendrum fritzidalessandroi

Epidendrum fritzidalessandroi, commonly known as the Fritzidalessandroi orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters.

This orchid is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental qualities.
