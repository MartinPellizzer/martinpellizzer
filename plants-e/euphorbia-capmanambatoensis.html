Euphorbia capmanambatoensis

Euphorbia capmanambatoensis, commonly known as the Capmanambato euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Its unique morphology makes it a notable species in the study of succulent flora.
