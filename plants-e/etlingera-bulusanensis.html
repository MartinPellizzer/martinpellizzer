Etlingera bulusanensis

Etlingera bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan ginger, is a species of flowering plant in the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the province of Sorsogon. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in terminal inflorescences.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
