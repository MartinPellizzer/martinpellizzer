Euryops Athanasiae

Athanasi's euryops, scientifically known as Euryops athanasiae, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa. This species belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its vibrant yellow blooms. Despite its beauty, Euryops athanasiae is critically endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique species. Its survival is essential for maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystem.
