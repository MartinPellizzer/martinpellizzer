Euphorbia duseimata

Euphorbia duseimata, commonly known as the Cape York euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with a crown of fleshy, lobed leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized flower-like structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
