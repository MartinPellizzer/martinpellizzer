Elatostema lineolatum var. petiolare

Elatostema lineolatum var. petiolare, commonly known as the petioled leaf spurge, is a member of the Polygonaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a distinctive midrib that is prominently visible. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often surrounded by leaf-like bracts.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
