Echinopsis arachnacantha var. densiseta

Echinopsis arachnacantha var. densiseta, commonly known as the dense-hair echinopsis, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines that provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces striking white to pale yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collections and xeriscaping.
