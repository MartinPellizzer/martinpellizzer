Epicharis

Epicharis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Proteaceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly Brazil, and are characterized by their striking, showy flowers. Epicharis species typically have large, waxy petals that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. Their growth habit includes both shrub-like and tree-like forms, depending on the species.
