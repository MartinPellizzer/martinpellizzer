Elsholtzia eriocalyx

Elsholtzia eriocalyx, commonly known as the woolly elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in dense, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

Elsholtzia eriocalyx is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
