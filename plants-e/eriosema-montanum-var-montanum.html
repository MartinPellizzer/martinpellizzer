Eriosema montanum var. montanum

Eriosema montanum var. montanum, commonly known as the mountain eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to dry, well-drained conditions.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological niche.
