Echinocactus conoideus

Echinocactus conoideus, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and a dense covering of bright yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
