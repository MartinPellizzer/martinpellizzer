Escallonia pendula var. granatensis

Escallonia pendula var. granatensis, commonly known as the granate escallonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features attractive, glossy dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink flowers in spring. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its visual appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Escallonia pendula var. granatensis is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
