Epidendrum fagerlindii

Epidendrum fagerlindii, commonly known as the Fagerlind's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of yellow and green, often arranged in racemes. It has fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum fagerlindii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
