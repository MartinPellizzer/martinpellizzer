Epidendrum pepeportillae

Epidendrum pepeportillae, commonly known as the Pepeportilla Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, often with a waxy texture.

Epidendrum pepeportillae is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
