Erysimum humile subsp. penyalarense

Erysimum humile subsp.

penyalarense, commonly known as the Penyalarense wallflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring.

The plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is adapted to rocky or coastal environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and locally endemic species.
