Eria gagnepainii

Eria gagnepainii, commonly known as the Gagnepain's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Eria gagnepainii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
