Eriogonum deflexum var. gilvum

Eriogonum deflexum var. gilvum, commonly known as the yellow curlleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its yellowish to reddish flowers and its basal leaves that curl downward. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous small, clustered flowers.

This species is part of the Polygonaceae family and is known for its adaptability to arid environments.
