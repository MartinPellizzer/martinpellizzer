Eupatorium alternifolium var. vernoniopsis

Eupatorium alternifolium var. vernoniopsis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved boneset, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its slender, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar flowering plants.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine.
