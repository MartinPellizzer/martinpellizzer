Etlingera sphaerocephala var. grandiflora

Etlingera sphaerocephala var. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a striking member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of northeastern Australia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, showy red and orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
