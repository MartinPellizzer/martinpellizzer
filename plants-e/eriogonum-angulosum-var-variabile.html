Eriogonum angulosum var. variabile

Eriogonum angulosum var. variabile, commonly known as the variable leaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its variable morphology, particularly in leaf shape and size. It is a hardy perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
