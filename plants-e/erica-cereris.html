Erica cereris

Erica cereris, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a species of shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers in spring, which are highly attractive to pollinators.

Erica cereris is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
