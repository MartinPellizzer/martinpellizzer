Erysimum nuttallianum

Erysimum nuttallianum, commonly known as Nuttall's wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in prairies and open woodlands. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with four petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Erysimum nuttallianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
