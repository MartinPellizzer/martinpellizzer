Eugenia angustissima

Eugenia angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.

Eugenia angustissima is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
