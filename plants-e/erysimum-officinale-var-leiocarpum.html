Erysimum officinale var. leiocarpum

Erysimum officinale var. leiocarpum, commonly known as the smoothpod wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with four petals. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the fruit is a capsule that remains smooth and glabrous, giving the variety its name. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically in traditional medicine.
