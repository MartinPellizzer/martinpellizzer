Epidendrum parvilobum

Epidendrum parvilobum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Epidendrum parvilobum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
