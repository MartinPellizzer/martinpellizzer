Eria capitellata

Eria capitellata, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features compact, fleshy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its unique growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Eria capitellata is known for its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.
