Euphorbia deltoidea subsp. deltoidea

Euphorbia deltoidea subsp.

deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically triangular in shape, adapted to store water.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique floral structures. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance, it is cultivated as a decorative plant in dry climates.
