Epidendrum carinatum

Epidendrum carinatum, commonly known as the spine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a robust, upright stem with a distinctive keel-like ridge running along its length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its leaves are broad, sword-shaped, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Epidendrum carinatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
