Erycibe forbesii

Erycibe forbesii, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red, five-petaled flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Erycibe forbesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
