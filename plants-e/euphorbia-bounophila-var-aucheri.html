Euphorbia bounophila var. aucheri

Euphorbia bounophila var. aucheri, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are the modified inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
