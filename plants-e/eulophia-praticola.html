Eulophia praticola

Eulophia praticola, commonly known as the common eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along forest margins, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots each year. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Eulophia praticola is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in open habitats where it can receive adequate sunlight.
