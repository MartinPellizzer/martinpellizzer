Epipactis kamtschatica

Epipactis kamtschatica, commonly known as Kamtschata orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the cold climates of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically features a rosette of basal leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences with greenish-yellow to white flowers. Epipactis kamtschatica thrives in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its harsh, high-altitude environment.
