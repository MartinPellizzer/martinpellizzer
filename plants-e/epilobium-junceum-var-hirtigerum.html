Epilobium junceum var. hirtigerum

Epilobium junceum var. hirtigerum, commonly known as hairy willow herb, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and wet meadows. The plant features erect, branched stems covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are pink to reddish in color, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

It is often found in wetland habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
