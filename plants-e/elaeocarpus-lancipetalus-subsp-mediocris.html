Elaeocarpus lancipetalus subsp. mediocris

Elaeocarpus lancipetalus subsp.

mediocris, commonly known as the narrow-petaled elaeocarpus, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters, and the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
