Eriope macrostachya var. grandiflora

Eriope macrostachya var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered eriope, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are borne in dense clusters.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
