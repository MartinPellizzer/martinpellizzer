Eurya montana

Eurya montana, commonly known as the mountain eurya, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It typically grows in forested areas and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its fragrant flowers are white or pale yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, orange-red berries.

Eurya montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
