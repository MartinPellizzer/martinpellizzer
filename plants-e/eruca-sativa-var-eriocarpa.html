Eruca sativa var. eriocarpa

Eruca sativa var. eriocarpa, commonly known as the woolly cabbage or wild rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to southern Europe and western Asia. Its seeds are small and contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.

The plant is often used in traditional cuisine and has a peppery flavor similar to other members of the mustard family.
