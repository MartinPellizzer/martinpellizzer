Echinopsis eyriesii var. duvallii

Echinopsis eyriesii var. duvallii, commonly known as the Duvall's moon cactus, is a flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and bright, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is a succulent, adapted to arid environments with thick, fleshy stems that store water. It produces a single flower per stem, typically during the spring and early summer.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
