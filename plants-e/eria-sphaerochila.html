Eria sphaerochila

Eria sphaerochila, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded pseudobulbs and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces showy, white or pale pink blooms.

Eria sphaerochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
