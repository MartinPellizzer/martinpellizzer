Eleocharis austriaca

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense vegetation, which can help stabilize soil and provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Eleocharis austriaca is generally short-lived, with a lifespan of a few years, and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to thrive in disturbed wetland environments.
