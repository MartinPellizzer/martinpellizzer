Eugenioides decorum

Eugenioides decorum, commonly known as the glossy leaf myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenioides decorum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
