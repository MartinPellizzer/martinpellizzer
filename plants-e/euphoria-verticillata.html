Euphoria verticillata

Euphoria verticillata, commonly known as the clustered euphoria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, white pulp. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
