Eriogonum microtheca var. foliosum

Eriogonum microtheca var. foliosum, commonly known as the leafy buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellowish to reddish inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a hairy texture.
