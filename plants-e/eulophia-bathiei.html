Eulophia bathiei

Eulophia bathiei, commonly known as the Bath's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent spurred petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Eulophia bathiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
