Epidendrum mutelianum var. mirabilis

Epidendrum mutelianum var. mirabilis, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often feature a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing a sturdy structure for supporting its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
