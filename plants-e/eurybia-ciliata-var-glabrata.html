Eurybia ciliata var. glabrata

Eurybia ciliata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth yarrow, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves with a smooth surface, distinguishing it from other yarrow varieties. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological and ornamental purposes.
