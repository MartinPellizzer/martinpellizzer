Euanthe sanderiana f. albata

Euanthe sanderiana f. albata, commonly known as the white-flowered sander's euanthe, is a rare and striking cultivar of the genus Euanthe.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. This plant is a hybrid form, likely resulting from crosses between different Euanthe species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
