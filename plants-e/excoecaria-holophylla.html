Excoecaria holophylla

Excoecaria holophylla, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Excoecaria holophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
