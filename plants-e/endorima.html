Endorima

Endorima, commonly known as the fairy fan palm, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, fan-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a solitary stem with a crown of narrow, feather-like fronds. Endorima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in humid, well-drained soils.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
