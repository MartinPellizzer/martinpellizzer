Erica unicolor

Erica unicolor, commonly known as the singleflower heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the Cape Province of South Africa, where it typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and its single, showy white flower that blooms in spring. Erica unicolor prefers well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
