Euphorbia thelephora var. thelephora

Euphorbia thelephora var. thelephora, commonly known as the Mexican tree spurge, is a succulent shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It features a thick, branching stem covered in a waxy, pale green or grayish coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers surrounded by colorful bracts, typically in clusters. It has adapted to arid environments with its water-storing stems and reduced leaves.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
