Ebenidium sibthorpii

Ebenidium sibthorpii, commonly known as the African ebony, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony species known for their dense, dark wood. The plant features elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy coating. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, round fruits containing smooth, black seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, heavy wood and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
