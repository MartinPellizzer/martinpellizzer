Echinodorus quadricostatus var. xinguensis

Echinodorus quadricostatus var. xinguensis, commonly known as the Xingu River sword, is a aquatic plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, dark green leaves that often have a slight serration along the edges. This variety thrives in shallow, slow-moving waters with soft to moderately hard water conditions. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

The plant also plays a role in maintaining water quality by absorbing nutrients and oxygenating the water.
