Episcia ciliosa

Episcia ciliosa, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and silver. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

Episcia ciliosa is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
