Elcismia cordatifolia

Elcismia cordatifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf elcismia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warm seasons. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Elcismia cordatifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
