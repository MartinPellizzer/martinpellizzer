Echeveria sprucei

Echeveria sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's echeveria, is a species of flowering plant in the Crassulaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color and covered in a fine layer of white trichomes, giving it a frosty appearance. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring.

This succulent is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
