Eremostachys laciniata f. glabrifolia

Eremostachys laciniata f. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved eremostachys, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lanceolate leaves that are generally hairless, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, adding visual interest to its foliage. It is native to arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
