Elaeocarpus guillaumii

Elaeocarpus guillaumii, commonly known as the Guillaumii elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

Elaeocarpus guillaumii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
