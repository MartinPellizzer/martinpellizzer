Eugenia smaragdina

Eugenia smaragdina, commonly known as the green eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia smaragdina is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
