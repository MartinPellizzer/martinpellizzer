Euphorbia leachii

Euphorbia leachii, commonly known as the Leach's spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a short, thick stem with paired, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem.

Euphorbia leachii is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.
