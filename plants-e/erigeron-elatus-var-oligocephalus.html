Erigeron elatus var. oligocephalus

Erigeron elatus var. oligocephalus, commonly known as the slender fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and herbaceous growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and white to pale blue.

It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its propagation in arid environments.
