Exolobus grandiflorus

Exolobus grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered oxalis, is a South African plant belonging to the Oxalidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, bright yellow flowers with prominent red sepals. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three heart-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Exolobus grandiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
