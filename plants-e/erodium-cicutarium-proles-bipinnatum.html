Erodium cicutarium proles bipinnatum

Erodium cicutarium proles bipinnatum, commonly known as the two-pinnate field penny-cress, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves, which are often divided into three leaflets.
