Erica dodii

Erica dodii, commonly known as the Dodii heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Erica dodii typically thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
