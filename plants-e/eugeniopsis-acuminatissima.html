Eugeniopsis acuminatissima

Eugeniopsis acuminatissima, commonly known as the sharp-pointed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It typically grows as an understory shrub in humid, shaded environments.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
