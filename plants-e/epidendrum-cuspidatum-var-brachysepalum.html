Epidendrum cuspidatum var. brachysepalum

Epidendrum cuspidatum var. brachysepalum, commonly known as the short-sepal epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
