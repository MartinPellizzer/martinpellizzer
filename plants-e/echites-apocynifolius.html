Echites apocynifolius

Echites apocynifolius, commonly known as the roughleaf milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with rough textures and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its flowers are typically followed by follicles that contain seeds with silky hairs.

Echites apocynifolius is often found in moist, shaded environments and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
