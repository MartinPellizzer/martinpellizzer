Erythraea compressa subsp. majorcica

Erythraea compressa subsp.

majorcica, commonly known as the Majorcan erythranthe, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive red throat.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
