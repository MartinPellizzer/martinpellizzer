Euosma ovata

Euosma ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Euosma ovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
