Eugenia laxiuscula

Eugenia laxiuscula, commonly known as the small-leaved eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Eugenia laxiuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic foliage.
