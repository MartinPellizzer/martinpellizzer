Epidendrum utcuyacuense

Epidendrum utcuyacuense, commonly known as the Utcuyacu Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Amazon rainforest of Peru.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and long, arching stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered with a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Epidendrum utcuyacuense thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the rich, organic soil of the rainforest floor.
