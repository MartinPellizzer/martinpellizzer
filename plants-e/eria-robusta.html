Eria robusta

Eria robusta, commonly known as the robust eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple blooms with a distinctive spurred lip, which is a common feature in many orchids. Eria robusta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
