Eurya auriculata

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as India, China, and Malaysia. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical to ovate in shape, with a leathery texture and a distinctive auriculate base.
