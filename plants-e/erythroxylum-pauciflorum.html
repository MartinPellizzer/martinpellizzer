Erythroxylum pauciflorum

Erythroxylum pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Erythroxylaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elliptical leaves.
