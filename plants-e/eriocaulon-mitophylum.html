Eriocaulon mitophylum

Eriocaulon mitophylum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and swamps. The plant has slender, grass-like stems that bear small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a waxy coating.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
