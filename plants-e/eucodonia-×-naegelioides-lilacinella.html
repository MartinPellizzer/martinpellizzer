Eucodonia × naegelioides lilacinella

Eucodonia × naegelioides lilacinella, commonly known as the lilac-flowered phlox, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Polemoniaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features lance-shaped, aromatic leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and fragrance.
