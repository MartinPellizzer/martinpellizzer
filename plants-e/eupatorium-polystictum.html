Eupatorium polystictum

Eupatorium polystictum, commonly known as purple stem eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that are often purple-tinged, especially when mature. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium polystictum is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
