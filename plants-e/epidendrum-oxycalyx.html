Epidendrum oxycalyx

Epidendrum oxycalyx, commonly known as the "Copperleaf Orchid," is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen, often adorned with a reddish or copper-colored margin. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding surfaces.

Epidendrum oxycalyx is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor growers.
