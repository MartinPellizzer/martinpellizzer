Emilia caespitosa

Emilia caespitosa, commonly known as Common Emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. The plant has a bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and wildflower mixes.

Emilia caespitosa is also valued for its historical use in herbal medicine.
