Erica leucoclada

Erica leucoclada, commonly known as the white heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. Erica leucoclada thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
