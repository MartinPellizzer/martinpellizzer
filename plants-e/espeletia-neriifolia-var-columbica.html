Espeletia neriifolia var. columbica

Espeletia neriifolia var. columbica, commonly known as the Columbian heather, is a striking plant native to the Andean highlands of Colombia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat. Its unique morphology includes a rosette structure and a short, sturdy stem.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native ecosystem.
