Eranthemum strictum

Eranthemum strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Eranthemum strictum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
