Eugenia perpuncticulata var. brachythyrsa

Eugenia perpuncticulata var. brachythyrsa, commonly known as the broad-thorned eugenia, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers, followed by edible, fleshy berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are typically elliptical with prominent veins and can have a slightly aromatic scent.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
