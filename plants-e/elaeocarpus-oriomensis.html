Elaeocarpus oriomensis

Elaeocarpus oriomensis, commonly known as the Orior Mountain Elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers and elliptical, leathery leaves. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The fruit is a hard, woody capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential ecological roles in its native habitat.
