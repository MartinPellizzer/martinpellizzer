Echinopogon ovatus var. pubiglumis

Echinopogon ovatus var. pubiglumis, commonly known as the hairy-awned bulrush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often in temperate regions. Its leaves are narrow and grassy, with hairy structures along the base of the glumes.

This variant is distinguished by its pubescent (hairy) glumes, which give it its specific epithet "pubiglumis.".
