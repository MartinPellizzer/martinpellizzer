Epidendrum compressibulbum

Epidendrum compressibulbum, commonly known as the compressed bubble epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Epidendrum compressibulbum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
