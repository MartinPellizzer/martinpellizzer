Epidanthus wercklei

Epidanthus wercklei, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its attractive, daisy-like flowers. The plant features silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a compact cluster. Epidanthus wercklei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and decorative appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
