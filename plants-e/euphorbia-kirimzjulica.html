Euphorbia kirimzjulica

Euphorbia kirimzjulica, commonly known as the Kirimzjul euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It features a branching stem structure with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbia genus. Its spines and milky sap are characteristic defenses against herbivores.

Euphorbia kirimzjulica is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to extreme temperature fluctuations.
