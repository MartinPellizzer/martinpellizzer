Eugenia hintonii

Eugenia hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia hintonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
