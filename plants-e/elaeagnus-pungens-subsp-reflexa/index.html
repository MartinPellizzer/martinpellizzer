Elaeagnus Pungens Subsp. Reflexa

Elaeagnus pungens subsp. reflexa is a hardy shrub known for its silvery foliage and fragrant flowers. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements. Its unique leaf shape and winter interest make it a standout in garden design. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
