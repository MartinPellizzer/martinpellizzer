Eucalyptus mensalis

Eucalyptus mensalis, commonly known as the slender ribbonspike, is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbed leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the cooler months. Its bark is typically smooth and peels in thin, fibrous strips.

Eucalyptus mensalis is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in dry, open habitats.
