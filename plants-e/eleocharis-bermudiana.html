Eleocharis bermudiana

Eleocharis bermudiana, commonly known as Bermuda grass sedge, is a flowering plant native to Bermuda and the Caribbean.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant has slender, grass-like stems and produces small, triangular spikes of flowers. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in coastal areas.
