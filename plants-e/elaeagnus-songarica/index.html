Elaeagnus Songarica

Elaeagnus songarica is a shrub native to central Asia known for its hardy nature and ornamental value. It features small, fragrant flowers and dark green leaves that provide year-round interest. This plant thrives in poor soils and is often used for erosion control and landscaping. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment. Despite its resilience, it requires minimal care once established.
