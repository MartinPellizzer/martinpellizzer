Echium obtusifolium

Echium obtusifolium, commonly known as blunt-leaved fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and striking spikes of purple or blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate saline conditions.
