Epidendrum zanonii

Epidendrum zanonii, commonly known as the Zanonii Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb system that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces pendulous racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its foliage is typically dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
