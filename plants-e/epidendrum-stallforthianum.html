Epidendrum stallforthianum

Epidendrum stallforthianum, commonly known as the Stallforth's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendraceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive purple or reddish lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
