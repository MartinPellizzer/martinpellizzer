Echeveria delicata

Echeveria delicata, commonly known as the silver echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a silvery appearance. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color and can grow up to 2 inches in length. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months.

This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.
