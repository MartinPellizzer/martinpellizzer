Echinospermum semiglabrum var. bungei

Echinospermum semiglabrum var. bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's echinospermum, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.
