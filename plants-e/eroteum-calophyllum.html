Eroteum calophyllum

Eroteum calophyllum, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous pulp. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its timber, which is used in furniture and construction.
