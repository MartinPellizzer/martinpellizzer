Ephedra minuta var. dioeca

Ephedra minuta var. dioeca, commonly known as the dwarf ephedra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ephedraceae.

It is a dioecious plant, meaning that male and female flowers are found on separate plants. The plant is characterized by its small, branched stems and scale-like leaves. It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and rocky areas.

Ephedra minuta var. dioeca is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
