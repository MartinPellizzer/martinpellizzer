Eleocharis wichurae f. petasata

Eleocharis wichurae f. petasata, commonly known as the broad-leaved spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and shallow water bodies. Its morphology includes small, spike-like clusters of flowers that are often wind-pollinated.
