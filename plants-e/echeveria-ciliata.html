Echeveria ciliata

Echeveria ciliata, commonly known as the curled edge echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves with white or silver hairs along the edges. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its color and shape.

Echeveria ciliata is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
