Eragrostis chalcantha var. holstii

Eragrostis chalcantha var. holstii, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in grassland ecosystems. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
