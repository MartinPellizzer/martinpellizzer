Eriachne stricta

Eriachne stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand drought and salt spray, making it resilient in harsh environments.

Eriachne stricta plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
