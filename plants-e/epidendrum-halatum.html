Epidendrum halatum

Epidendrum halatum, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum halatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
