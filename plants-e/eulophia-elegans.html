Eulophia elegans

Eulophia elegans, commonly known as the elegant eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, violet or purple flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
