Encyclia saltensis f. maior

Encyclia saltensis f. maior, commonly known as the larger form of the saltensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its robust, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellowish-green to brownish coloration. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and has a unique floral structure with a spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
