Elatostema strictum

Elatostema strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features opposite, narrow, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and are pollinated primarily by insects.

Elatostema strictum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.
