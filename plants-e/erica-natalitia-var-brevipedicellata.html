Erica natalitia var. brevipedicellata

Erica natalitia var. brevipedicellata, commonly known as Natal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. It typically blooms in spring and summer, producing pink or red hues.

This variety is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
