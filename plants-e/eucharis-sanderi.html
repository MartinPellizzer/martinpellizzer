Eucharis sanderi

Eucharis sanderi, commonly known as the Sander's Amazon lily, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family and is characterized by its striking, waxy, white flowers that resemble lilies. The plant features broad, dark green leaves that are often used as a decorative element in indoor gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eucharis sanderi is popular for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
