Elaphoglossum uluguruense

Elaphoglossum uluguruense, commonly known as the Uluguru fern, is a species of polypody fern native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania.

It is a small, compact fern with delicate, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The fronds are typically upright and have a distinctive, glossy appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and restricted distribution.
