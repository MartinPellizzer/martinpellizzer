Eriphilema grandiflora

Eriphilema grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered eriphilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

Eriphilema grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
