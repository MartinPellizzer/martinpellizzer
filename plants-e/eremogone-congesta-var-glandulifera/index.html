Eremogone Congesta Var. Glandulifera

Eremogone congesta var. glandulifera is a rare and intriguing plant species known for its unique morphological traits. The plant's glandular structures on its stems and leaves play a crucial role in its survival strategies. Despite its ecological significance, it remains under-studied and often overlooked in botanical research.
