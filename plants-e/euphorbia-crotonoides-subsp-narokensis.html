Euphorbia crotonoides subsp. narokensis

Euphorbia crotonoides subsp.

narokensis, commonly known as the Narok euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to East Africa. It features a branched stem with smooth, greenish-gray bark and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers.

The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to full sun exposure.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry conditions.
