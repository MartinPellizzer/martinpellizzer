Eurya

Eurya, commonly known as evergreen holly or Chinese holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Ericaceae.

It consists of approximately 120 species, predominantly found in East Asia, with a few species in the Americas. These shrubs typically have small, leathery leaves and produce clusters of bright yellow flowers in the spring. The fruits are usually small, red or orange berries that mature in the fall.

Eurya species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are adapted to a variety of soil types and climates.
