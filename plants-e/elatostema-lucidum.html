Elatostema lucidum

Elatostema lucidum, commonly known as the shining elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a rosette of narrow, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or greenish flowers are borne on short pedicels and are often clustered near the base of the plant.

Elatostema lucidum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
