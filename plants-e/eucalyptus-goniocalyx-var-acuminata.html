Eucalyptus goniocalyx var. acuminata

Eucalyptus goniocalyx var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, acuminated leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, globular flower buds that range in color from white to pink, which are often clustered in groups of three. It is a hardy shrub or small tree that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is known for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various native wildlife.
