Epilobium glaucum var. stenophyllum

Epilobium glaucum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and arranged in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
