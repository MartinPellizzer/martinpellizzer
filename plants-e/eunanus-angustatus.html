Eunanus angustatus

Eunanus angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eunanus, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, and it is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Eunanus angustatus is considered a rare and somewhat endangered species.
