Epilobium × rubneri

Epilobium × rubneri, commonly known as the redstem willow herb, is a hybrid species belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils along streambanks and wetlands.

Epilobium × rubneri is often found in temperate regions of Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
