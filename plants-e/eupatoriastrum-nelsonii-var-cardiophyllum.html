Eupatoriastrum nelsonii var. cardiophyllum

Eupatoriastrum nelsonii var. cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heartleaf eupatoriastrum, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, such as deserts and coastal dunes.
