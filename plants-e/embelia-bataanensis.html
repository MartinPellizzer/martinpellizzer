Embelia bataanensis

Embelia bataanensis, commonly known as Bataan embelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its aromatic properties.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
