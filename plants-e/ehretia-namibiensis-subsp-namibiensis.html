Ehretia namibiensis subsp. namibiensis

Ehretia namibiensis subsp.

namibiensis, commonly known as the Namibian Ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of Namibia. It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a bluish-green hue.

The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the arid and semi-arid conditions of its native habitat.

The species is also notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
