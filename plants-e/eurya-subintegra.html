Eurya subintegra

Eurya subintegra, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the broadleaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

Eurya subintegra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
