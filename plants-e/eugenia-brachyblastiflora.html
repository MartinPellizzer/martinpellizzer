Eugenia brachyblastiflora

Eugenia brachyblastiflora, commonly known as the short-leafed eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties.
