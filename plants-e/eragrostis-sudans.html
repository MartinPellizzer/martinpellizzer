Eragrostis sudans

Eragrostis sudans, commonly known as Sudan grass, is a warm-season grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for various agricultural and ornamental uses.

Eragrostis sudans is often used for forage, erosion control, and as a cover crop due to its rapid growth and drought tolerance.
