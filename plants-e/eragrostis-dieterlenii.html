Eragrostis dieterlenii

Eragrostis dieterlenii, commonly known as the Dieterlen's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has a clump-forming habit and produces pale green to bluish-green foliage.

Eragrostis dieterlenii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
