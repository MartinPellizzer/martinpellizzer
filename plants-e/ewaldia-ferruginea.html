Ewaldia ferruginea

Ewaldia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered with fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Ewaldia ferruginea is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
