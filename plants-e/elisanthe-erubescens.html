Elisanthe erubescens

Elisanthe erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered elisanthe, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright red, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Elisanthe erubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
