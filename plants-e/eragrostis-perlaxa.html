Eragrostis perlaxa

Eragrostis perlaxa, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its drooping, arching inflorescences that give it a distinctive weeping appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and covered with fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Eragrostis perlaxa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its unique visual effect.
