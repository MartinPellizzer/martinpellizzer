Epithema brunonis var. scabridum

Epithema brunonis var. scabridum, commonly known as the rough-leaved epithema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
