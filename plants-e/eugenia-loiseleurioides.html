Eugenia loiseleurioides

Eugenia loiseleurioides, commonly known as the dwarf eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or purple when mature. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Eugenia loiseleurioides is relatively low maintenance and suitable for shady garden environments.
