Epimedium × setosum

Epimedium × setosum, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese smilacina, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped, deciduous leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and attractive foliage.
