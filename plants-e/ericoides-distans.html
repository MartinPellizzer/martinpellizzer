Ericoides distans

Ericoides distans, commonly known as the distant erica, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family, Ericaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in summer. Ericoides distans thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
