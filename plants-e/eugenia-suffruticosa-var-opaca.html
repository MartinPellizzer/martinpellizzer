Eugenia suffruticosa var. opaca

Eugenia suffruticosa var. opaca, commonly known as the dark eucalyptus, is a shrub or small tree native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically dark purple or black when mature. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and full sun.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
