Eulophia streptopetala var. stenophylla

Eulophia streptopetala var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
