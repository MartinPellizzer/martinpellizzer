Epipremnum pinnatum f. multisectum

Epipremnum pinnatum f. multisectum, commonly known as the Variegated Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, lobed leaves.

The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and exhibit a variegated pattern of green, white, and sometimes yellow. It is a climbing vine that thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. The plant is native to the rainforests of South America and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
