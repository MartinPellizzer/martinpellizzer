Eulophia dabia

Eulophia dabia, commonly known as the Dabia orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs and linear leaves are typical of many orchid species, and it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

Eulophia dabia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
