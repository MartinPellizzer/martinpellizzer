Erysimum glaberrimum

Erysimum glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has smooth, hairless leaves and stems, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum glaberrimum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
