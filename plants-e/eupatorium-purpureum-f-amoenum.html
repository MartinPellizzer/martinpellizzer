Eupatorium purpureum f. amoenum

Eupatorium purpureum f. amoenum, commonly known as the purple thoroughwort, is a cultivar of the purple thoroughwort species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States. The plant features tall, slender stems and clusters of small, purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
