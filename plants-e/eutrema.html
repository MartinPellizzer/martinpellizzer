Eutrema

Eutrema, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often found in coastal areas, thriving in salty and well-drained soils. Its seeds are small and contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal uses.
