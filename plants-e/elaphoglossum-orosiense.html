Elaphoglossum orosiense

Elaphoglossum orosiense, commonly known as the Orosi fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in areas with high humidity and shade. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and finely divided. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
