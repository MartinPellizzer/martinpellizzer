Euphorbia flanaganii

Euphorbia flanaganii, commonly known as the Blue Glow Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue-gray foliage and produces small, yellowish cyathia that resemble flowers. The plant has a branching stem structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia flanaganii is also known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves.
