Erysimum humile

Erysimum humile, commonly known as the dwarf wallflower, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erysimum humile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
