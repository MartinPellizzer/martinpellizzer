Elionurus hirtifolius

Elionurus hirtifolius, commonly known as the hairy-leafed elionurus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
