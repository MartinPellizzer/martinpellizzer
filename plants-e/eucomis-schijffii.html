Eucomis schijffii

Eucomis schijffii, commonly known as the African fountain grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear whorled clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, spiky flower heads that range in color from white to pink, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Eucomis schijffii is often used in landscape design for its architectural form and seasonal blooms.
