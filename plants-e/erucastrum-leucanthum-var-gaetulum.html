Erucastrum leucanthum var. gaetulum

Erucastrum leucanthum var. gaetulum, commonly known as the Gaetulan rocket, is a cultivated variety of the wild rocket plant.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, pinnately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its peppery flavor and is often used in culinary applications.
