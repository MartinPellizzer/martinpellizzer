Eupatorium vacciniifolium

Eupatorium vacciniifolium, commonly known as the redstem goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a reddish stem, giving it its common name.

It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall.
