Epidendrum foldatsii

Epidendrum foldatsii, commonly known as the Foldats' epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Epidendrum foldatsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
