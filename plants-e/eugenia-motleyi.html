Eugenia motleyi

Eugenia motleyi, commonly known as the Motley's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia motleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
