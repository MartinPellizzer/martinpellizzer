Epidendrum oncidioides var. graniticum

Epidendrum oncidioides var. graniticum, commonly known as the granite epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions contribute to its appeal in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
