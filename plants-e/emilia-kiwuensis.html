Emilia kiwuensis

Emilia kiwuensis, commonly known as the African emilia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warm season.

Emilia kiwuensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
