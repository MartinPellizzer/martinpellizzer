Eritrichium nanum var. argenteum

Eritrichium nanum var. argenteum, commonly known as silverweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with hairy, silver-gray leaves that help reduce water loss. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a distinctive member of the Boraginaceae family.
