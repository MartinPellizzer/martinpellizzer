Eriogonum sphaerocephalum var. brevifolium

Eriogonum sphaerocephalum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the Sierra buckwheat, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions. It has small, rounded leaves and produces rounded, pale green seed heads.

This variety is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
