Erysimum koeiei

Erysimum koeiei, commonly known as the Chinese wallflower, is a flowering plant native to China and neighboring regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with characteristic four-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm, featuring basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are bright yellow, often with a dark central stripe, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Erysimum koeiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ease of care.
