Erythrina bagshawei

Erythrina bagshawei, commonly known as the red torch tree, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking red, flame-like flowers that bloom in summer. Erythrina bagshawei is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in ecological restoration projects.
