Euphorbia boiteaui var. boiteaui

Euphorbia boiteaui var. boiteaui, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green or blue-gray in color. The plant produces striking yellow flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Euphorbia boiteaui var. boiteaui is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
