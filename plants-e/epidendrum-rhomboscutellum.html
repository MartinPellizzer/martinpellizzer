Epidendrum rhomboscutellum

Epidendrum rhomboscutellum, commonly known as the rhombic-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, violet or purple flowers that have a distinct, intricate pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
