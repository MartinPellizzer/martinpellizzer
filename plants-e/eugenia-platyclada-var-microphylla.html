Eugenia platyclada var. microphylla

Eugenia platyclada var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leafed eugenia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and emit a subtle aromatic scent. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by small, round, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedging plant.
