Epidendrum lima

Epidendrum lima, commonly known as the Lima orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that can grow up to several feet in length, supporting clusters of bright green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum lima is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
