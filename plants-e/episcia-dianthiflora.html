Episcia dianthiflora

Episcia dianthiflora, commonly known as the "Snowy Womb" or "White Womb," is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting red or purple throat. The plant has elongated, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Episcia dianthiflora is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and ease of care.
