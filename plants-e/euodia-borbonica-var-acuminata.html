Euodia borbonica var. acuminata

Euodia borbonica var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese frankincense or Chinese yellowwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark brown to black bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental value.

Euodia borbonica var. acuminata is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
