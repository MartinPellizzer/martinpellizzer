Erica junonia var. minor

Erica junonia var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its hardy nature makes it suitable for coastal gardens and rockeries.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
