Ehrharta paniciformis

Ehrharta paniciformis, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, arching stems that form dense clumps. The plant produces small, pale green to brownish flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its elegant appearance.

Ehrharta paniciformis is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in marginal areas.
