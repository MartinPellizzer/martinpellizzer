Euonymus yunnanensis

Euonymus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan spurge or Yunnan euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the family Cistaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
