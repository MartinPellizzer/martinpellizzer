Eriosema rhynchosioides var. rhynchosioides

Eriosema rhynchosioides var. rhynchosioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The leaves are typically narrow and elliptic, with a softly hairy surface and a slightly serrated margin. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, rounded, brownish seeds.

This plant is often found in grassy areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
