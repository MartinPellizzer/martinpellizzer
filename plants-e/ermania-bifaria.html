Ermania bifaria

Ermania bifaria, commonly known as the two-lobed ermania, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Ermania bifaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
