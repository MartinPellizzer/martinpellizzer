Erysimum duriaei subsp. neumannii

Erysimum duriaei subsp.

neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is a hardy perennial that is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
