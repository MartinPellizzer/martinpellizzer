Evelyna

Evelyna, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Evelyna is often found in wetland areas and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shady environments.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
