Eriosorus lechleri

Eriosorus lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. The plant is notable for its ability to produce latex, which has been historically used in the rubber industry. Its growth habit includes a straight trunk and a dense canopy of foliage.

Eriosorus lechleri thrives in warm, humid climates with high rainfall and well-drained soil.
