Euklisia longirostris

Euklisia longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, branching stem covered in soft, greenish-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm season. Its leaves are succulent, adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
