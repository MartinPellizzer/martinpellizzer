Esquiroliella

Esquiroliella, commonly known as the dwarf golden star, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Esquiroliella is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
