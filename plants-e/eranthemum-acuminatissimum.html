Eranthemum acuminatissimum

Eranthemum acuminatissimum, commonly known as the narrow-pointed eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.
