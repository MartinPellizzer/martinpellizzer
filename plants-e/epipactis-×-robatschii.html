Epipactis × robatschii

Epipactis × robatschii, commonly known as the hybrid marsh helleborine, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wet meadows and bogs. The plant features a rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It is a natural hybrid between Epipactis palustris and Epipactis helleborine.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations and is often found in regions where its parent species overlap.
