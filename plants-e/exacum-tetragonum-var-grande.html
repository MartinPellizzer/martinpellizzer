Exacum tetragonum var. grande

Exacum tetragonum var. grande, commonly known as the large-leafed African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
