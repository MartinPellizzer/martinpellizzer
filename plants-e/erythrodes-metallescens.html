Erythrodes metallescens

Erythrodes metallescens, commonly known as the metallic flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a fiery display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Erythrodes metallescens is also notable for its ability to produce nectar, attracting a variety of pollinators.
