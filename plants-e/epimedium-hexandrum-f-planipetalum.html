Epimedium hexandrum f. planipetalum

Epimedium hexandrum f. planipetalum, commonly known as the Japanese barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, evergreen leaves that are dark green and glossy, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
