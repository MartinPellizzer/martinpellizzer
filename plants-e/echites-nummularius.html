Echites nummularius

Echites nummularius, commonly known as the dollar plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, round leaves that are typically a bright green color with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact size and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
