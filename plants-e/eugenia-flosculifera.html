Eugenia flosculifera

Eugenia flosculifera, commonly known as the clustered eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible, red, berry-like fruits that are often consumed by native wildlife. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Eugenia flosculifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
