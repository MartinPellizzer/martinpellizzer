Encyclia bulbosa

Encyclia bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bulb-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Encyclia bulbosa is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and ornamental value.
