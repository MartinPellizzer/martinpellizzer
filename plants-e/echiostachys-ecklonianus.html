Echiostachys ecklonianus

Echiostachys ecklonianus, commonly known as the mountain reed, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Echiostachys ecklonianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
