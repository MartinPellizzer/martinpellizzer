Epipactis neerlandica var. renzii

Epipactis neerlandica var. renzii, commonly known as the Dutch helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species.

This variety is relatively rare and is of interest to botanists due to its specific ecological requirements.
