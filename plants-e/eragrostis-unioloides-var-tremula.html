Eragrostis unioloides var. tremula

Eragrostis unioloides var. tremula, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats such as meadows, roadsides, and woodland edges.

Its botanical features make it identifiable by its delicate appearance and specific growth patterns.
