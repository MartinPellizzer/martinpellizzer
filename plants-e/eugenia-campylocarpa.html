Eugenia campylocarpa

Eugenia campylocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, and it is often found in dry, open habitats.
