Epidendrum pamplonense

Epidendrum pamplonense, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
