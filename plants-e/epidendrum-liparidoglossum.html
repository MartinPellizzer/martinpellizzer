Epidendrum liparidoglossum

Epidendrum liparidoglossum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a golden-orange color and a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
