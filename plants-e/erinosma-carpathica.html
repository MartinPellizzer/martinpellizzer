Erinosma carpathica

Erinosma carpathica, commonly known as the Carpathian erinoid, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features a robust, upright stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, covered with dense, woolly hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent.

Erinosma carpathica is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
