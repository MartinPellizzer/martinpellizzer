Erigeron Uniflorus Var. Frigidus

Erigeron uniflorus var. frigidus is a rare and hardy plant native to the cold climates of northern regions. It thrives in harsh environments where few other species can survive. This variant is distinguished by its small, delicate flowers and low-growing habit. Often found in rocky or alpine areas, it plays a vital role in stabilizing fragile ecosystems. Its resilience makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
