Epipactis Biflora

Epipactis biflora is a rare and delicate orchid native to specific regions of Europe. It is known for its unique floral structure and striking appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
