Eriocaulon viride

Eriocaulon viride, commonly known as green spikegrass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale green flowers in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Eriocaulon viride is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in aquatic habitats.
