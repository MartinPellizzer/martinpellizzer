Eupatorium carnosifolium

Eupatorium carnosifolium, commonly known as roughleaf boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes. The plant features opposite, rough-textured leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
