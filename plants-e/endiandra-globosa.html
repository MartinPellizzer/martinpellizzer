Endiandra globosa

Endiandra globosa, commonly known as the globular endiandra, is a rare tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique globular shape and dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
