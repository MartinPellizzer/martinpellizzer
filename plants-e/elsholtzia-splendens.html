Elsholtzia splendens

Elsholtzia splendens, commonly known as the golden elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly aromatic scent. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from white to pale yellow.

Elsholtzia splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained, rocky soils.
