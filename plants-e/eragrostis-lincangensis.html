Eragrostis lincangensis

Eragrostis lincangensis, commonly known as the Lincang grass, is a species of annual grass native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets, which are wind-pollinated.

Eragrostis lincangensis is valued for its ornamental potential and its role in ecological restoration projects.
