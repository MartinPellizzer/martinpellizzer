Euphrasia Officinalis Var. Pratensis

Euphrasia officinalis var. pratensis is a delicate herbaceous plant known for its small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. This variety is closely related to the common eyebright, sharing similar medicinal properties. It has been used historically for its purported eye health benefits. Its presence in the wild is a sign of a healthy, undisturbed ecosystem.
