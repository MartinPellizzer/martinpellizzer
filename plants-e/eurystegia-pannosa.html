Eurystegia pannosa

Eurystegia pannosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and can grow up to 20 meters in height. The leaves are typically large, ovate, and have a smooth, waxy texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along riverbanks.
