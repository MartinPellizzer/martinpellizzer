Euonymus geloniifolius

Euonymus geloniifolius, commonly known as the glossy leaf euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by bright red, bead-like fruits that persist into winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Euonymus geloniifolius prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of growing conditions.
