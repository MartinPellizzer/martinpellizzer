Euphorbia epithymoides var. serratifolia

Euphorbia epithymoides var. serratifolia, commonly known as the serrated euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched stem covered in soft, greenish-gray foliage with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This species is often cultivated for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
