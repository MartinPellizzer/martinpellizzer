Epidendrum crassipes

Epidendrum crassipes, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, fleshy leaves and produces large, showy inflorescences with numerous flowers. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks rather than in soil. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a variety of colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
