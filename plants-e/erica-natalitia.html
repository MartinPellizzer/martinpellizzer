Erica natalitia

Erica natalitia, commonly known as Natal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica natalitia is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and requires minimal water once established.
