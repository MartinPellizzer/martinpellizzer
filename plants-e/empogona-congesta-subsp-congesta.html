Empogona congesta subsp. congesta

Empogona congesta subsp.

congesta, commonly known as the clustered passionvine, is a species of flowering plant in the family Passifloraceae. It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, colorful flowers.
