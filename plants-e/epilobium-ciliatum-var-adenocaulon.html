Epilobium ciliatum var. adenocaulon

Epilobium ciliatum var. adenocaulon, commonly known as the woolly willow or slender willow, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often boggy habitats. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a soft, woolly texture. Its flowers are small, pink to red, and clustered in racemes, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
