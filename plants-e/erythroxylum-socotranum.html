Erythroxylum socotranum

Erythroxylum socotranum, commonly known as Socotra tea, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Erythroxylaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds, and it is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
