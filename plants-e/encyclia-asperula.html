Encyclia asperula

Encyclia asperula, commonly known as the rough leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are strap-shaped and have a rough, textured surface, giving it its common name.

Encyclia asperula thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
