Eriogonum comosum var. playanum

Eriogonum comosum var. playanum, commonly known as Playa sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in desert and semi-arid regions.

It is a perennial herb with a low-growing, mat-like habit, often forming dense colonies on sandy or gravelly soils.
