Eucalyptus glomericassis

Eucalyptus glomericassis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallee, is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, peeling bark and lance-shaped, often narrow leaves. The plant produces small, globular flower clusters that are typically white or pale blue in color. Eucalyptus glomericassis is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
