Eranthemum nigrescens

Eranthemum nigrescens, commonly known as the black-flowered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers that contrast with its green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture.
