Eriocaulon manoharanii

Eriocaulon manoharanii, commonly known as the Manoharanii spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Sri Lanka. The plant is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
