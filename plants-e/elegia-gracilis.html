Elegia gracilis

Elegia gracilis, commonly known as slender water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant has delicate, branching stems and small, needle-like leaves that are often submerged. Its flowers are tiny and clustered, producing small, yellowish blooms.

Elegia gracilis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
