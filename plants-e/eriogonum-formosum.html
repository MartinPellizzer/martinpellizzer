Eriogonum formosum

Eriogonum formosum, commonly known as the showy buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Eriogonum formosum is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
