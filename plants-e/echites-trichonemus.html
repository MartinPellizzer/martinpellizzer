Echites trichonemus

Echites trichonemus, commonly known as the hairy echites, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
