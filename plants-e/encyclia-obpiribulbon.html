Encyclia obpiribulbon

Encyclia obpiribulbon, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.
