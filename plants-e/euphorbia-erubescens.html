Euphorbia erubescens

Euphorbia erubescens, commonly known as the red-spotted euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem covered in spiny, red-tinged bracts that give it its striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to many landscapes.
