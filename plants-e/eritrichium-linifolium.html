Eritrichium linifolium

Eritrichium linifolium, commonly known as the slender leaf alpine beauty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. It is known for its low-growing habit and is often found in high elevation habitats.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sensitive indicator species of alpine ecosystems.
