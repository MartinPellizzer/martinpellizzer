Ehretia catronga

Ehretia catronga, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 8 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight for optimal growth.
