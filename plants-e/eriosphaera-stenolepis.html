Eriosphaera stenolepis

Eriosphaera stenolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriosphaera, is a species of succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
