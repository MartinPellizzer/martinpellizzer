Elatostema platyphylloides

Elatostema platyphylloides, commonly known as the broad-leafed elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features trifoliate leaves with broad, ovate leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, which are borne on short pedicels.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
