Epidendrum subulatum

Epidendrum subulatum, commonly known as the needle orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, needle-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, spurred orchid flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum subulatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
