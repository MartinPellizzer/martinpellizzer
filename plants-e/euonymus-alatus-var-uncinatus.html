Euonymus alatus var. uncinatus

Euonymus alatus var. uncinatus, commonly known as the curlyleaf hedgehog plant, is a variant of the winged euonymus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, warty, and often curling leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and thrives in temperate climates. It produces small, red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds.

This evergreen shrub is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
