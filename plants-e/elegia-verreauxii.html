Elegia verreauxii

Elegia verreauxii, commonly known as the Verreaux's elegia, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Elegia verreauxii thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
