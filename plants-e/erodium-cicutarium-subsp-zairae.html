Erodium cicutarium subsp. zairae

Erodium cicutarium subsp.

zairae, commonly known as the African catchfly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, pinkish to reddish flowers.

The plant produces tiny, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous minute seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
