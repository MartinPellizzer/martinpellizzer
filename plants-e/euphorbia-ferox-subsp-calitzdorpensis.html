Euphorbia ferox subsp. calitzdorpensis

Euphorbia ferox subsp.

calitzdorpensis, commonly known as the Calitzdorp euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features spiny, cylindrical stems that are often greenish or reddish in color.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
