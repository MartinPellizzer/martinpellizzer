Eupatorium mairetianum f. elucens

Eupatorium mairetianum f. elucens, commonly known as the white eupatorium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
