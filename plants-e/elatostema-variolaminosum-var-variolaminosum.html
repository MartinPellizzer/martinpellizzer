Elatostema variolaminosum var. variolaminosum

Elatostema variolaminosum var. variolaminosum, commonly known as the variable leaf elatostema, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, often lobed leaves that vary in shape and size. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.
