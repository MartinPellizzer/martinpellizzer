Euphrasia × brevipila var. gracilior

Euphrasia × brevipila var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender marsh gentian, is a hybrid species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue or yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
