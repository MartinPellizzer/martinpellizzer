Eriocaulon alpinum

Eriocaulon alpinum, commonly known as the alpine ricegrass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to high-altitude regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes grass-like plants with specialized structures for water retention. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. Its tiny, spike-like inflorescence contains small, white to pale blue flowers.

Eriocaulon alpinum thrives in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous regions.
