Erysimum nasturtium

Erysimum nasturtium, commonly known as wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers and pinnately lobed leaves. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family members. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or gravelly habitats.

The plant is also valued for its peppery scent and edible leaves, making it both ornamental and culinary.
