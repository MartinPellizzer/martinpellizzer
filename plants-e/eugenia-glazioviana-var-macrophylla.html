Eugenia glazioviana var. macrophylla

Eugenia glazioviana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed eugenia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically large and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by edible, red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.

The leaves and fruits of this plant are used in traditional medicine and for ornamental purposes due to their aesthetic appeal and aromatic properties.
