Eriocephalus namaquensis

Eriocephalus namaquensis, commonly known as the Namaqua groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Eriocephalus namaquensis is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
