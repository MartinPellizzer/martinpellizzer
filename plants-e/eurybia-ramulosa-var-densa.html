Eurybia ramulosa var. densa

Eurybia ramulosa var. densa, commonly known as the dense eupatorium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and showy composite flowers. The plant typically features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Eurybia ramulosa var. densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
