Epiphyllum biformis

Epiphyllum biformis, commonly known as the moon cactus or night bloomer, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is a climbing cactus native to Mexico and parts of Central America. The plant has flat, fleshy stems that are often green or red in color, and it produces large, showy flowers at night. These flowers are typically white or pale pink and have a strong, sweet fragrance.

Epiphyllum biformis is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
