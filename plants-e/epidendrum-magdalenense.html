Epidendrum magdalenense

Epidendrum magdalenense, commonly known as the Magdalena Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

Epidendrum magdalenense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
