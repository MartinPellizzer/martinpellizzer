Euphrasia multifolia var. kirisimana

Euphrasia multifolia var. kirisimana, commonly known as the Kirisimana eyebright, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
