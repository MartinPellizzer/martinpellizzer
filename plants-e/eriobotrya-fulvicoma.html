Eriobotrya fulvicoma

Eriobotrya fulvicoma, commonly known as the Chinese white flowering ash, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in early spring, followed by yellow, edible fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to temperate climates.
