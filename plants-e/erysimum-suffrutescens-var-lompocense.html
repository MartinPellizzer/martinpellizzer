Erysimum suffrutescens var. lompocense

Erysimum suffrutescens var. lompocense, commonly known as Lompoc wallflower, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to California.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to Mediterranean climates.
