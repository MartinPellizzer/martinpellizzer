Elaeocarpus mingendensis

Elaeocarpus mingendensis, commonly known as the Mingendeng elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of China, India, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a smooth surface. Its small, white or pinkish flowers are clustered in inflorescences, and it produces small, hard fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
