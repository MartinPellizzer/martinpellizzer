Epacris Capitata

Epacris capitata, also known as the mountain heath, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the wetter regions of southeastern Australia. It is prized for its vibrant clusters of pink and white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a favorite in native landscaping. Epacris capitata not only adds visual interest to gardens but also supports local wildlife, enhancing the ecological value of the environment.
