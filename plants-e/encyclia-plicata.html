Encyclia plicata

Encyclia plicata, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores nutrients. The plant produces intricate, pleated leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Encyclia plicata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
