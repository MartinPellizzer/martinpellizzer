Eriosema lucipetum

Eriosema lucipetum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Eriosema lucipetum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
