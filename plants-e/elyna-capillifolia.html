Elyna capillifolia

Elyna capillifolia, commonly known as the slender hairgrass, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spikelet-like flowers.
