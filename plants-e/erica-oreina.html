Erica oreina

Erica oreina, commonly known as the pink heather, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. Erica oreina thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in garden landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
