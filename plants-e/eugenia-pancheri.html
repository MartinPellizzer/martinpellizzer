Eugenia pancheri

Eugenia pancheri, commonly known as the Pancheri guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. Its fruit is a small, yellowish-orange berry with a sweet and aromatic flavor.

Eugenia pancheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit in home gardens.
