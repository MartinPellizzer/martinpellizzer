Erythroxylum densinerve

Erythroxylum densinerve, commonly known as the dense-veined erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with dense venation patterns. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elliptical, reddish-brown berries.
