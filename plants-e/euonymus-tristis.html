Euonymus tristis

Euonymus tristis, commonly known as the spotted winterberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its bright red berries that appear in the fall and persist through winter, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

The plant is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
