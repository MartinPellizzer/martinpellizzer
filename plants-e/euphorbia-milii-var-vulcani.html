Euphorbia milii var. vulcani

Euphorbia milii var. vulcani, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy stems and clusters of bright red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its milky sap contains toxic compounds, so care should be taken when handling it.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
