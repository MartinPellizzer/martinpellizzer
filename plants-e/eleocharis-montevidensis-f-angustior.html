Eleocharis montevidensis f. angustior

Eleocharis montevidensis f. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a variant of the spike-rush genus Eleocharis.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Uruguay and parts of Argentina. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, clustered flowers. It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and pond margins.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
