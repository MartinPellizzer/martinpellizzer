Erodium triangulare var. hispanicum

Erodium triangulare var. hispanicum, commonly known as Spanish catchfly, is a low-growing annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
