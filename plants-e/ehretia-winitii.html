Ehretia winitii

Ehretia winitii, commonly known as the Chinese mahogany or Chinese evergreen, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.
