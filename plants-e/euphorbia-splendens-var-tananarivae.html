Euphorbia splendens var. tananarivae

Euphorbia splendens var. tananarivae, commonly known as the Madagascar candelabra euphorbia, is a striking succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a branched, tree-like structure with thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters tall. The plant produces vibrant yellow cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, spiny structures, and it thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and adaptability to arid environments.
