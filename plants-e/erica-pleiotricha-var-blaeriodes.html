Erica pleiotricha var. blaeriodes

Erica pleiotricha var. blaeriodes, commonly known as the Blaeriodes heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is typically found in fynbos vegetation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
