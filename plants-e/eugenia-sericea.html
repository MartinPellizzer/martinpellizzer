Eugenia sericea

Eugenia sericea, commonly known as silverleaf eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, edible berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

Eugenia sericea is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
