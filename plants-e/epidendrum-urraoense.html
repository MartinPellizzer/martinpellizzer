Epidendrum urraoense

Epidendrum urraoense, commonly known as the Urrao orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces linear, grass-like leaves and bears showy, fragrant flowers in a raceme. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings, and it blooms during the dry season.

Epidendrum urraoense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
