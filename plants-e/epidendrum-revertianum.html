Epidendrum revertianum

Epidendrum revertianum, commonly known as the pink-tinged epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive reddish tinge. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum revertianum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
