Eucalyptus aparrerinja

Eucalyptus aparrerinja, commonly known as the Northern Territory peppermint gum, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and smooth, often greyish bark. The leaves are lance-shaped and aromatic, with a strong mint-like scent. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.

This plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
