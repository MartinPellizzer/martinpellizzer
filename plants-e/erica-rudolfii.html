Erica rudolfii

Erica rudolfii, commonly known as the Natal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica rudolfii is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and strong winds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
