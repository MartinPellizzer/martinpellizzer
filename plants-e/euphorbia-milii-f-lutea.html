Euphorbia milii f. lutea

Euphorbia milii f. lutea, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green, though some varieties may have a yellowish hue. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
