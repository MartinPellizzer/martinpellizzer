Erica keetii

Erica keetii, commonly known as the Keet's heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. Erica keetii produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
