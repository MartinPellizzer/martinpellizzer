Eragrostis elegans

Eragrostis elegans, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its small, spike-like inflorescence contains numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Eragrostis elegans is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
