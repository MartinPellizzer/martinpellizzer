Eriosphaera apiculata

Eriosphaera apiculata, commonly known as the woolly globe cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of white to grayish hairs. The plant typically grows as a solitary or clustered shrub, with short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in spines. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the axils of the leaves.

Eriosphaera apiculata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
