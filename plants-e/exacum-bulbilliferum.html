Exacum bulbilliferum

Exacum bulbilliferum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often velvety to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Exacum bulbilliferum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
