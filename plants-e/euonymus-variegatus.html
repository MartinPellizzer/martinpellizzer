Euonymus variegatus

Euonymus variegatus, commonly known as the variegated euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Euonymus variegatus prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
