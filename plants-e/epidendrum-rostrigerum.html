Epidendrum rostrigerum

Epidendrum rostrigerum, commonly known as the Rosy Lip Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Epidendrum rostrigerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under proper conditions.
