Eupatorium dubium f. elutum

Eupatorium dubium f. elutum, commonly known as the slender eupatorium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands and moist habitats, showcasing its adaptability to humid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This botanical variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
