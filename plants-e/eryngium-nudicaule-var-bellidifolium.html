Eryngium nudicaule var. bellidifolium

Eryngium nudicaule var. bellidifolium, commonly known as the slender eryngo, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features a compact, spiny plant with blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
