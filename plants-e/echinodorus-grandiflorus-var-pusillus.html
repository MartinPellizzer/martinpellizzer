Echinodorus grandiflorus var. pusillus

Echinodorus grandiflorus var. pusillus, commonly known as the dwarf Amazon sword, is a compact aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green color, with a textured surface that helps it thrive in low-light conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding a decorative touch to aquariums. It is well-suited for planted tanks due to its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters.

Its slow growth rate makes it an excellent choice for maintaining a balanced and low-maintenance aquatic environment.
