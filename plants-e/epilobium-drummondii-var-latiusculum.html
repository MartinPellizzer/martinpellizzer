Epilobium drummondii var. latiusculum

Epilobium drummondii var. latiusculum, commonly known as Drummond's willow herb, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along streambanks and wet meadows. The plant produces showy pink to purple flowers in racemes during the summer months. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
