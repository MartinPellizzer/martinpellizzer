Elytraria frondosa

Elytraria frondosa, commonly known as the frondose elytraria, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feather-like foliage that gives it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Elytraria frondosa is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
