Eurya pickeringii

Eurya pickeringii, commonly known as the Chinese huckleberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, and it produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring. The berries, which are edible, are typically red or black when mature and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
