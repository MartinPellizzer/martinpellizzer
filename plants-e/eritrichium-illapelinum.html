Eritrichium illapelinum

Eritrichium illapelinum, commonly known as the Illapelin alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

Eritrichium illapelinum is known for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions, making it a notable species in alpine ecosystems.
