Encyclia silverarum

Encyclia silverarum, commonly known as the silver orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically in spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia silverarum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
