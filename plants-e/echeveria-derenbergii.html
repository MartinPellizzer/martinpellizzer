Echeveria derenbergii

Echeveria derenbergii, commonly known as the Deringen's Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or yellow during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
