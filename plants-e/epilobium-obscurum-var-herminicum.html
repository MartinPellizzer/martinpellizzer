Epilobium obscurum var. herminicum

Epilobium obscurum var. herminicum, commonly known as the dark willow herb, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes.

It is valued in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.
