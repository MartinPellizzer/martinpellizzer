Erica hottentotica

Erica hottentotica, commonly known as the Hottentot's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica hottentotica is well adapted to coastal and sandy environments, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
