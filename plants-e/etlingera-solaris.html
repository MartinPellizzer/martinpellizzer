Etlingera solaris

Etlingera solaris, commonly known as the sun orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant yellow or orange hues. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as coastal dunes and sandy soils. Its flowers are designed to attract specific pollinators, primarily bees and birds, due to their bright colors and nectar-rich structure.
