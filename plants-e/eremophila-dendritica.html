Eremophila dendritica

Eremophila dendritica, commonly known as the tree eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink and red. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

Eremophila dendritica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
