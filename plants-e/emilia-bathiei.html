Emilia bathiei

Emilia bathiei, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, dandelion-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it grows upright with a branching stem. Emilia bathiei is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
