Erigeron chiangii var. lamprocaulis

Erigeron chiangii var. lamprocaulis, commonly known as the silverstem fleabane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow central disk, blooming in the summer months. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
