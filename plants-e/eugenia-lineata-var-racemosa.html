Eugenia lineata var. racemosa

Eugenia lineata var. racemosa, commonly known as the racemose eugenia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub with smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
