Epilobium Alpinum F. Lactiflorum

Epilobium alpinum f. lactiflorum is a rare and delicate plant species native to high alpine regions. It belongs to the evening primrose family and is known for its striking white to pale pink flowers. This subspecies is characterized by its milky sap and compact growth habit. It thrives in rocky soils and harsh mountain environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for ecological and botanical study.
