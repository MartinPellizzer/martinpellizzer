Epilobium junceum var. cinereum

Epilobium junceum var. cinereum, commonly known as silverweed or silver hair grass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found along streambanks and in meadows.

Epilobium junceum var. cinereum is a herbaceous perennial that is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for insects.
