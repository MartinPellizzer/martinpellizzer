Eupatorium japonicum

Eupatorium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features tall, slender stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, slightly hairy leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Eupatorium japonicum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
