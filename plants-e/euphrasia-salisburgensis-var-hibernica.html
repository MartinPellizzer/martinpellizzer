Euphrasia salisburgensis var. hibernica

Euphrasia salisburgensis var. hibernica, commonly known as the Irish eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the British Isles and parts of northern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features small, yellowish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are oval-shaped, hairy, and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
