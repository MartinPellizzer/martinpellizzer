Eulophia abyssinica var. euantha

Eulophia abyssinica var. euantha, commonly known as the Abyssinian eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Ethiopia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a greenish or yellowish throat. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, adapted to the arid conditions of its native habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
