Encyclia ambigua

Encyclia ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, and the plant thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Encyclia ambigua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
