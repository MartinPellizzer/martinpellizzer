Eleocharis liouana

Eleocharis liouana, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and rhizomatous growth habit.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and cylindrical, with a smooth surface and a tendency to form dense stands.

Eleocharis liouana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms.
