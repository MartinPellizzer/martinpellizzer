Eria erosula

Eria erosula, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white, pink, or purple. Eria erosula thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
