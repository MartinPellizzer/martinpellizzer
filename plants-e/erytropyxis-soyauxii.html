Erytropyxis soyauxii

Erytropyxis soyauxii, commonly known as the African flame plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa, characterized by its striking red and orange tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
