Euphrasia × lundensis

Euphrasia × lundensis, commonly known as the hybrid eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Euphrasia × lundensis is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported eye-soothing properties.
