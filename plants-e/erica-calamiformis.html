Erica calamiformis

Erica calamiformis, commonly known as the flame heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. Erica calamiformis thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
