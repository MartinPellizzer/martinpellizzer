Ebermiera

Ebermiera, commonly known as the evergreen shrub, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

It typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, enhancing its drought resistance. The plant produces small yellow to orange flowers, which are arranged in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Ebermiera thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to flourish in temperate climates, contributing to its popularity among gardeners.
