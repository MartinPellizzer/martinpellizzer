Ermania albiflora

Ermania albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower ermania, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Ermania albiflora is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cooler, moist environments.
