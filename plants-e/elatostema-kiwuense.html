Elatostema kiwuense

Elatostema kiwuense, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a member of the Elatostemaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in humid conditions.

Elatostema kiwuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
