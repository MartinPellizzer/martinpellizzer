Eremostachys sarawschanica f. stellatipubescens

Eremostachys sarawschanica f. stellatipubescens, commonly known as the star-haired eremostachys, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. Its rosette growth form allows it to store water efficiently, adapting to extreme drought conditions.

This variety is noted for its unique pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance among other members of the eremostachys genus.
