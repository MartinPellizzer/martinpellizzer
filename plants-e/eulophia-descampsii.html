Eulophia descampsii

Eulophia descampsii, commonly known as the Campsii orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, and it prefers warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.

Eulophia descampsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
