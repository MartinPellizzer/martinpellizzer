Eugenia nutans var. pellucida

Eugenia nutans var. pellucida, commonly known as the translucent guava, is a variant of the Eugenia nutans species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and small, edible, translucent fruits that are often used in local cuisines. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a pleasant aroma when crushed.

The translucent guava is valued for its unique appearance and mild flavor, making it a popular ornamental and culinary plant in its native habitat.
