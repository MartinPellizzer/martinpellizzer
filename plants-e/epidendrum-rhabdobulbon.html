Epidendrum rhabdobulbon

Epidendrum rhabdobulbon, commonly known as the giant green epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, providing a lush, foliage-rich appearance. The plant produces striking inflorescences with clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
