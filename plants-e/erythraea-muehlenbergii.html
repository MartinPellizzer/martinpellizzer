Erythraea muehlenbergii

Erythraea muehlenbergii, commonly known as the redroot germander, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, pink to white flowers. The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
