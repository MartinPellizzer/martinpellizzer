Erysimum capitatum var. stellatum

Erysimum capitatum var. stellatum, commonly known as the star-flowered wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and vibrant yellow or orange flowers that are arranged in dense, showy clusters. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its long blooming period, often extending from spring to early summer.
