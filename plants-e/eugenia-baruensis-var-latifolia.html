Eugenia baruensis var. latifolia

Eugenia baruensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed eugenia, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
