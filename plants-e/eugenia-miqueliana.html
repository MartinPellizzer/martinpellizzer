Eugenia miqueliana

Eugenia miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia miqueliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
