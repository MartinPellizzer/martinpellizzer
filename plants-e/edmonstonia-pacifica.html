Edmonstonia pacifica

Edmonstonia pacifica, commonly known as the Pacific emerald tree.

It is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking emerald-green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
