Echinocodon primulifolium

Echinocodon primulifolium, commonly known as the primrose-leaved echinocodon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are characterized by their lobed shape and a soft, hairy texture.

Echinocodon primulifolium is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.
