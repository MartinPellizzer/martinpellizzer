Echeveria rubromarginata

Echeveria rubromarginata, commonly known as the Red Margined Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves with distinct red margins that intensify in sunlight. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
