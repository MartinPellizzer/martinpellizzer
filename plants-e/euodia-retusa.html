Euodia retusa

Euodia retusa, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its spiny branches and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-colored, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Euodia retusa is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
