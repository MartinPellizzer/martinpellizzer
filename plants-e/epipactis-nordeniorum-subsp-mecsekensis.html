Epipactis nordeniorum subsp. mecsekensis

Epipactis nordeniorum subsp.

mecsekensis, commonly known as the Mecsek marsh orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the Mecsek Mountains in Hungary. It is a terrestrial orchid with a unique morphology, characterized by its dark green, grass-like leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as wet meadows and marshes. It has a short flowering period in late spring to early summer, and its reproductive strategy relies on specific pollinators.
