Echeveria cuicatecana

Echeveria cuicatecana, commonly known as the Cuicatecana Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, bluish-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hair. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
