Echinopsis acanthura subsp. canetensis

Echinopsis acanthura subsp.

canetensis, commonly known as the Canetensis cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Argentina. It is a columnar cactus characterized by its smooth, pale green stems and dense clusters of white flowers.

The plant produces spiny areoles, typical of the Echinopsis genus, and is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

This subspecies is relatively rare and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.
