Epidendrum paniculovenezolanum

Epidendrum paniculovenezolanum, commonly known as the Venezuelan Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
