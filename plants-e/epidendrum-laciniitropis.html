Epidendrum laciniitropis

Epidendrum laciniitropis, commonly known as the lace-leaf epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
