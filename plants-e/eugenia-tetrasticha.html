Eugenia tetrasticha

Eugenia tetrasticha, commonly known as the sweet pepper tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, leathery leaves. It produces small, round fruits that are typically red or purple when mature.
