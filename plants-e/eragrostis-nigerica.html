Eragrostis nigerica

Eragrostis nigerica, commonly known as black panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, dark green leaves that often have a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed areas.

Eragrostis nigerica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a forage crop.
