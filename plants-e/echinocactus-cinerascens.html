Echinocactus cinerascens

Echinocactus cinerascens, commonly known as the blue haworthia, is a species of cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its distinctive blue-gray foliage. The plant features prominent, spiny areoles with clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
