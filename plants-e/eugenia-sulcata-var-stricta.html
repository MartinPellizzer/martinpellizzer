Eugenia sulcata var. stricta

Eugenia sulcata var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eucalypt, is a species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and smooth, often greyish bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
