Euonymus fortunei f. carrierei

Euonymus fortunei f. carrierei, commonly known as the Carriere's euonymus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dark green, waxy leaves with a slight sheen and produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers in early spring. The plant is noted for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is often used as a decorative hedge or ground cover due to its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and resilience.
