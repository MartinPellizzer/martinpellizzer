Elaphoglossum lineare

Elaphoglossum lineare, commonly known as the linear holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, linear fronds that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.
