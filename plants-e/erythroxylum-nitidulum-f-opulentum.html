Erythroxylum nitidulum f. opulentum

Erythroxylum nitidulum f. opulentum, commonly known as the opulentum form of the glossy evergreen.

This plant is a flowering shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces red berries that are a key feature of its botanical appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
