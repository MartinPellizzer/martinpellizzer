Eragrostis willdenoviana

Eragrostis willdenoviana, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice in native plant gardens.
