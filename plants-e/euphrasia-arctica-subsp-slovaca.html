Euphrasia arctica subsp. slovaca

Euphrasia arctica subsp.

slovaca, commonly known as the Slovaca eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer.
