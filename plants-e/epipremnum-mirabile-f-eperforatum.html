Epipremnum mirabile f. eperforatum

Epipremnum mirabile f. eperforatum, commonly known as the satin pothos, is a variegated cultivar of the Epipremnum mirabile species.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display a mix of light green and cream-white variegation. This plant is a trailing vine, often used as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ease of care. Its foliage is dense and glossy, with a textured surface that adds visual interest.

The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
