Elaphoglossum trichomallum

Elaphoglossum trichomallum, commonly known as the hairy elaphoglossum, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are often covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it its common name. Elaphoglossum trichomallum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
