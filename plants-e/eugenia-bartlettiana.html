Eugenia bartlettiana

Eugenia bartlettiana, commonly known as the Bartlett's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Eugenia bartlettiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
