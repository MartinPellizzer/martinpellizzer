Erodium × kolbianum

Erodium × kolbianum, commonly known as the hybrid common stork's-bill, is a flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Erodium corymbosum and Erodium arvense, characterized by its small, violet to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in regions of Australia. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive, spurred flowers that attract pollinators.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological and horticultural studies.
