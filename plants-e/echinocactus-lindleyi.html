Echinocactus lindleyi

Echinocactus lindleyi, commonly known as the Lindley's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the top of the stems. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
