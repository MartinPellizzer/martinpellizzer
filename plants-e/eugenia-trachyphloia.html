Eugenia trachyphloia

Eugenia trachyphloia, commonly known as the rough-bark eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a dark purple color.
