Eugenia tikalana

Eugenia tikalana, commonly known as the Tikalana guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Central America and parts of South America, particularly in tropical regions. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. Its fruits are typically small, round, and have a sweet, aromatic flavor.

Eugenia tikalana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit in local communities.
