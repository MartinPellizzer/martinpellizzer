Ehretia linifolia

Ehretia linifolia, commonly known as the silky tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
