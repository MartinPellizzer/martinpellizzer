Euphrasia rockii

Euphrasia rockii, commonly known as the rock euphrasia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with dark markings. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Euphrasia rockii is valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in challenging environments and its attractive floral display.
