Euphrasia tatarica var. brevipila

Euphrasia tatarica var. brevipila, commonly known as the short-stemmed tatarian eyebright, is a perennial herb native to eastern Europe and western Asia.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
