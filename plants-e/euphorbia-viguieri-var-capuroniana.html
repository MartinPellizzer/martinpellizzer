Euphorbia viguieri var. capuroniana

Euphorbia viguieri var. capuroniana, commonly known as the Capuron's spurge, is a succulent shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has a spiny appearance, with leaves that are often reduced to small, scale-like structures. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its thick stems.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
