Elytrigia pertenuis

Elytrigia pertenuis, commonly known as slender meadowgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist meadows, riverbanks, and other damp habitats. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Elytrigia pertenuis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
