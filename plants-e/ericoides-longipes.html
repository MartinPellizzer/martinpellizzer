Ericoides longipes

Ericoides longipes, commonly known as the slender heath orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in shaded forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green or brownish in color.

Ericoides longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
