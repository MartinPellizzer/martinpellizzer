Elytrigia elongata var. haifensis

Elytrigia elongata var. haifensis, commonly known as the Haifa bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with slender, erect stems. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a blue-green coloration that becomes more pronounced under stress. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers.

This variety is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
