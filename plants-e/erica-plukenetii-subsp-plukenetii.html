Erica plukenetii subsp. plukenetii

Erica plukenetii subsp.

plukenetii, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a shrub native to South Africa. It typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils.

The plant features dense, compact foliage with small, evergreen leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in spring, which are highly attractive to pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal environments.
