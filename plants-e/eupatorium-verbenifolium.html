Eupatorium verbenifolium

Eupatorium verbenifolium, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small, pale blue to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and prairies.

Eupatorium verbenifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
