Eriocaulon splendens

Eriocaulon splendens, commonly known as the glittering arrowgrass, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage with a striking silver or white sheen on the leaf surfaces. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Eriocaulon splendens is often used in ornamental water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of cultivation.
