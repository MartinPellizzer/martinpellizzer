Erigeron exilis

Erigeron exilis, commonly known as slender fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
