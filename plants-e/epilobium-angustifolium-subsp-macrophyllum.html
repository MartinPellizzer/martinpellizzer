Epilobium angustifolium subsp. macrophyllum

Epilobium angustifolium subsp.

macrophyllum, commonly known as narrowleaf willowherb, is a flowering plant native to North America. It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wet meadows, stream banks, and disturbed sites. It is a herbaceous perennial with erect, often branched stems.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems and a subject of interest in ecological studies.
