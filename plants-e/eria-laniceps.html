Eria laniceps

Eria laniceps, commonly known as the woolly orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and slender, branching stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Eria laniceps is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
