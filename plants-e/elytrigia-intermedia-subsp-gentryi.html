Elytrigia intermedia subsp. gentryi

Elytrigia intermedia subsp.

gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's intermediate brome, is a perennial grass native to the western United States. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a distinctive flowering structure.

It is often found in association with other native grasses and forbs in its ecological community.
