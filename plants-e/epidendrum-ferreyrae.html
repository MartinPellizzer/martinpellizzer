Epidendrum ferreyrae

Epidendrum ferreyrae, commonly known as the Ferreyra's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are elliptical and slightly hairy, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Epidendrum ferreyrae is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
