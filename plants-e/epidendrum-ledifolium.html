Epidendrum ledifolium

Epidendrum ledifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum ledifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
