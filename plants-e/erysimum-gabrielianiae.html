Erysimum gabrielianiae

Erysimum gabrielianiae, commonly known as the Gabrielian's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
