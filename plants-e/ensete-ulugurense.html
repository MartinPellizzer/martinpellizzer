Ensete ulugurense

Ensete ulugurense, commonly known as the African elephant grass, is a large, perennial grass native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, robust stalks that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant produces large, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional practices and as a source of fiber. Ensete ulugurense is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types and thrives in humid, warm climates.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
