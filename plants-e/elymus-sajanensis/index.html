Elymus Sajanensis

Elymus sajanensis is a rare grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. This plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions. Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important subject for botanical study. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Elymus sajanensis faces increasing threats to its survival.
