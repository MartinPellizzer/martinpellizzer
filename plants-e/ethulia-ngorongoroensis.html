Ethulia ngorongoroensis

Ethulia ngorongoroensis, commonly known as the Ngorongoro star grass, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude grasslands of Tanzania.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the cool, dry climate of its native habitat. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine by local communities.

Ethulia ngorongoroensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its ecosystem.
