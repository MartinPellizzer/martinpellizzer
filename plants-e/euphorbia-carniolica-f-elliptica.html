Euphorbia carniolica f. elliptica

Euphorbia carniolica f. elliptica, commonly known as the Carniolica euphorbia, is a unique variant of the Euphorbia carniolica species.

It is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves, which are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are the plant's modified flowers.

This succulent is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
