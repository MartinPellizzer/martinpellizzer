Erythrodes plantaginea

Erythrodes plantaginea, commonly known as the red-flowered plantain, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are upright and branching.
