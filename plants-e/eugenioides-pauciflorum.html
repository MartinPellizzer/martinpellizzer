Eugenioides pauciflorum

Eugenioides pauciflorum, commonly known as the little-flowered eugenia, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of dense forests. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are clustered in small inflorescences.

Eugenioides pauciflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
