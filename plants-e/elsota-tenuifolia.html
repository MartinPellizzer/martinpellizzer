Elsota tenuifolia

Elsota tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elsota, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a branching stem that supports its delicate foliage.

Elsota tenuifolia is often found in regions with high humidity and moderate temperatures.
