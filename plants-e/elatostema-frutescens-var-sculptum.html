Elatostema frutescens var. sculptum

Elatostema frutescens var. sculptum, commonly known as the sculptured prostrate elatostema, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive, raised venation patterns that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of small, greenish-white blooms that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
