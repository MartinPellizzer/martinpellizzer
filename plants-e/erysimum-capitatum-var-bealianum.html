Erysimum capitatum var. bealianum

Erysimum capitatum var. bealianum, commonly known as the Alpine honesty, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to cold and arid conditions.

The seeds are small and contained within a hairy, capsule-like structure, aiding in wind dispersal.
