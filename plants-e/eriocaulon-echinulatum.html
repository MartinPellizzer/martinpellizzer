Eriocaulon echinulatum

Eriocaulon echinulatum, commonly known as the spiny flat sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-poor soils. Its flowers are usually small and inconspicuous, with three stamens and a single pistil.
