Ehretia cymosa var. abyssinica

Ehretia cymosa var. abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian ehretia, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a serrated margin. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in cymes, giving it a delicate appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
