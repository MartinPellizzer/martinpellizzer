Erica keniensis

Erica keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan heather, is a flowering plant native to Kenya.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming during the dry season. Erica keniensis thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

It is adapted to high-altitude environments and is an important component of the local flora.
