Echinodorus parvulus

Echinodorus parvulus, commonly known as the small sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It has narrow, grass-like leaves that grow in dense, compact rosettes, making it ideal for foreground planting in aquariums. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is relatively easy to care for and can tolerate a range of water parameters.

Echinodorus parvulus also produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface, adding aesthetic value to both aquariums and ponds.
