Epidendrum motozintlense

Epidendrum motozintlense, commonly known as the Mexican flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant red and orange flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense, white pubescence.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.
