Euryops transvaalensis subsp. setilobus

Euryops transvaalensis subsp.

setilobus, commonly known as the Transvaal golden bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
