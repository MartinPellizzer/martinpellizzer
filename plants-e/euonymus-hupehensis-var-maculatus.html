Euonymus hupehensis var. maculatus

Euonymus hupehensis var. maculatus, commonly known as the spotted euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage that features bright yellow or orange spots on dark green leaves. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans and livestock. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is commonly used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, low-maintenance plant.
