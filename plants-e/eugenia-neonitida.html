Eugenia neonitida

Eugenia neonitida, commonly known as the pink hibiscus.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Eugenia neonitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
