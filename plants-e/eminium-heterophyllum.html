Eminium heterophyllum

Eminium heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eminium, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Eminium heterophyllum thrives in moist, shaded environments such as wet meadows and along stream banks.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
