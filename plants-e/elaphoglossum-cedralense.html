Elaphoglossum cedralense

Elaphoglossum cedralense, commonly known as the Cedralense holly fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, clustered sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.
