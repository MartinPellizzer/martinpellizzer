Eriachne melicacea

Eriachne melicacea, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystem.

The species is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
