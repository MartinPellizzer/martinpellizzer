Eriogonum latifolium var. harfordii

Eriogonum latifolium var. harfordii, commonly known as Harford's bush monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and cliffs. It is part of the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae) and is known for its drought tolerance.

This variety is often found in California and Oregon, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.
