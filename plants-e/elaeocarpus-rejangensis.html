Elaeocarpus rejangensis

Elaeocarpus rejangensis, commonly known as the Rejang elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves and showy, white or pink flowers. The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals.

This species is valued for its timber and is also studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
