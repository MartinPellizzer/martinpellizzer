Euphorbia petiolaris

Euphorbia petiolaris, commonly known as the coral euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with its branching stems and clusters of small, bright red cyathia, which are its modified flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and striking appearance.

Euphorbia petiolaris is also known for its ability to thrive in well-draining soil and full sun conditions.
