Euphrasia matsumurae

Euphrasia matsumurae, commonly known as the Japanese eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Euphrasia matsumurae is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
