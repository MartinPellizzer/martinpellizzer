Erythraea centaurium subsp. major

Erythraea centaurium subsp.

major, commonly known as the greater centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia. It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its blue to violet flowers and lance-shaped leaves.
