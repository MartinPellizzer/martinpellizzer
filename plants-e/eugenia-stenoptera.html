Eugenia stenoptera

Eugenia stenoptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and are clustered in small inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, and it is sometimes used in local traditional medicine.
