Euphorbia spirosticha

Euphorbia spirosticha, commonly known as the spiral euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and rockeries.
