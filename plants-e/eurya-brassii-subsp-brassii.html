Eurya brassii subsp. brassii

Eurya brassii subsp.

brassii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or red-berried eurya, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers.

The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
