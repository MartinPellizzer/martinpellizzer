Euphorbia nicholasii

Euphorbia nicholasii, commonly known as the Nicholas's spurge, is a striking succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with large, waxy, green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and collections of succulents.
