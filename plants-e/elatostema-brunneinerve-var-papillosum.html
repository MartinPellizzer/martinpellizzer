Elatostema brunneinerve var. papillosum

Elatostema brunneinerve var. papillosum, commonly known as the hairy nerve plant, is a small herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish-brown venation, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, typical of the Urticaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
