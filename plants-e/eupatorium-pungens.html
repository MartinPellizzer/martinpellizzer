Eupatorium pungens

Eupatorium pungens, commonly known as bone straw, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves and stems are aromatic and have a slightly sharp, pungent scent.

Eupatorium pungens is often found in moist woodlands and wet meadows, thriving in partial to full shade.
