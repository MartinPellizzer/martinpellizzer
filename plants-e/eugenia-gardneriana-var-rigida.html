Eugenia gardneriana var. rigida

Eugenia gardneriana var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid eugenia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

The rigid eugenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrance and fruit.
