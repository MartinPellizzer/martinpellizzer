Ervum

Ervum, commonly known as the cowpea, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. The plant is native to Africa but is now cultivated in many warm climates around the world. Ervum thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

It plays an important role in agricultural systems due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
