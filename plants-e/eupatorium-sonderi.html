Eupatorium sonderi

Eupatorium sonderi, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spherical, umbrella-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces a distinctive, aromatic scent.

Eupatorium sonderi is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
