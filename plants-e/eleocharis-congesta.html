Eleocharis congesta

Eleocharis congesta, commonly known as the clustered spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The flowers are usually unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate spikelets.
