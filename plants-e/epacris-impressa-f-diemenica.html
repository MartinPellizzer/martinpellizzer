Epacris impressa f. diemenica

Epacris impressa f. diemenica, commonly known as the Tasmanian native heath, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
