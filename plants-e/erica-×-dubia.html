Erica × dubia

Erica × dubia, commonly known as the common heather, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the western and southern regions of the United States, particularly in California and Oregon. This evergreen shrub typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant prefers well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
