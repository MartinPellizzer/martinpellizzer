Elatostema lineolatum var. falcigerum

Elatostema lineolatum var. falcigerum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a distinct midrib and a slightly wavy margin. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually greenish-white in color.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats, making it a common ground cover in its native habitat.
