Emilia schinzii

Emilia schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's emilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring full sun.

Emilia schinzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
