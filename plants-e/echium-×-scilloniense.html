Echium × scilloniense

Echium × scilloniense, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Echium × scilloniense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
