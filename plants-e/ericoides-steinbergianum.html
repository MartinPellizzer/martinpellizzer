Ericoides steinbergianum

Ericoides steinbergianum, commonly known as the Steinberg's ericoid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and somewhat elusive species.
