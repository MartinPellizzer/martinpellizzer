Epinetrum villosum

Epinetrum villosum, commonly known as the hairy epine.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Epinetrum villosum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
