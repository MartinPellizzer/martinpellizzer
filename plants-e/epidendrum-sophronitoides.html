Epidendrum sophronitoides

Epidendrum sophronitoides, commonly known as the Orchid of the Andes, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to South America.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Epidendrum sophronitoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
