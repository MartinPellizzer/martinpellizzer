Eria rigida

Eria rigida, commonly known as the rigid eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid surroundings.

Eria rigida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
