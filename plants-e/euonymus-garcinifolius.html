Euonymus garcinifolius

Euonymus garcinifolius, commonly known as the glossy leaf euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features attractive, dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in the spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal.

Euonymus garcinifolius is relatively low maintenance and adapts well to a variety of soil conditions.
