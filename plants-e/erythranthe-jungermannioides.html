Erythranthe jungermannioides

Erythranthe jungermannioides, commonly known as the Jungermann's erythranthe, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
