Echinocactus gibbosus var. leonensis

Echinocactus gibbosus var. leonensis, commonly known as the Leonese Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for desert gardens and rockeries.
