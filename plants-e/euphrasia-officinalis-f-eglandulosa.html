Euphrasia officinalis f. eglandulosa

Euphrasia officinalis f. eglandulosa, commonly known as the glandular eyebright, is a flowering plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are ovate and covered with fine, glandular hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
