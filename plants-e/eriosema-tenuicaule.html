Eriosema tenuicaule

Eriosema tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pale blue or violet, and it produces small, round pods that contain several seeds.

Eriosema tenuicaule is often found in open habitats and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
