Eleocharis mitrata var. africana

Eleocharis mitrata var. africana, commonly known as African spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in wetland environments such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spike-like clusters. Its roots help in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in aquatic habitats.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to tolerate varying water conditions.
