Epilobium billardiereanum var. simplex

Epilobium billardiereanum var. simplex, commonly known as the slender willow-herb, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in spikes, which bloom from late summer to autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along waterways.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland restoration.
