Ebnerella moelleriana

Ebnerella moelleriana, commonly known as the Moeller's rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine and arctic regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with four petals, arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a rough texture and a strong, pungent odor.

Ebnerella moelleriana is adapted to cold climates and is often found in high-altitude environments.
