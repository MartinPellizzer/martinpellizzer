Elatine brachysperma

Elatine brachysperma, commonly known as the narrow-ovary water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves and tiny, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and ditches. Its short-lived nature and rapid life cycle make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.

Elatine brachysperma is also noted for its ability to survive in nutrient-poor conditions, contributing to water purification.
