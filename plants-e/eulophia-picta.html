Eulophia picta

Eulophia picta, commonly known as the pictured eulophia, is a striking orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that often display intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually broad and sword-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Eulophia picta is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
