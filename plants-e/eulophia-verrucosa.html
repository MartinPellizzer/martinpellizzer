Eulophia verrucosa

Eulophia verrucosa, commonly known as the疣兰, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowering. The plant produces a single, erect raceme of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellowish-green lip. Its roots are often covered in a dense, hairy covering, which helps in moisture retention.

Eulophia verrucosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
