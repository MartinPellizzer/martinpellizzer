Eragrostis cretacea

Eragrostis cretacea, commonly known as Cretaceous grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Eragrostis cretacea is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal dunes.
