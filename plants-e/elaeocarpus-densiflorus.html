Elaeocarpus densiflorus

Elaeocarpus densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically reddish or yellowish when mature. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests and coastal areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
