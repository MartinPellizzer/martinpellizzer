Eurya chinensis var. glabra

Eurya chinensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth eurya, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptic leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in shaded garden environments.
