Euphorbia milii var. splendens

Euphorbia milii var. splendens, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and striking, colorful bracts that resemble flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

Its toxic sap can cause skin irritation, so caution is advised when handling the plant.
