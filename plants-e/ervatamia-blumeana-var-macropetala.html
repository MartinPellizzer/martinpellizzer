Ervatamia blumeana var. macropetala

Ervatamia blumeana var. macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled ervatamia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers with white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
