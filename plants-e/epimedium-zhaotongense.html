Epimedium zhaotongense

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels.
