Elaeodendron pyramidale

Elaeodendron pyramidale, commonly known as the pyramidal elaeodendron, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its dense, pyramidal growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Elaeodendron pyramidale is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
