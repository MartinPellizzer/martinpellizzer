Endiandra teschneri

Endiandra teschneri, commonly known as the Tasmanian ironwood, is a rare and endangered plant native to Tasmania.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub with dense, dark green foliage and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. Endiandra teschneri thrives in moist, shaded environments within temperate rainforests.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
