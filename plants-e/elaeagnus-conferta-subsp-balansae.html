Elaeagnus conferta subsp. balansae

Elaeagnus conferta subsp.

balansae, commonly known as the Balansae silverberry, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its silvery foliage, which results from dense pubescence on the leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its fruits are edible berries that ripen to a dark red or black color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
