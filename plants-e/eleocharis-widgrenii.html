Eleocharis widgrenii

Eleocharis widgrenii, commonly known as the narrowleaf spike-rush, is a species of grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and parts of Africa. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. Its botanical structure reflects its adaptation to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, with rhizomes that help in anchoring and nutrient absorption.
