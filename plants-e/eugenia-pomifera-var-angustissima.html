Eugenia pomifera var. angustissima

Eugenia pomifera var. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved feijoana, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, fleshy berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitats.
