Eria cylindripoda

Eria cylindripoda, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for epiphytic growth on trees or rocks.

Eria cylindripoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
