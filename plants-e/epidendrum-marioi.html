Epidendrum marioi

Epidendrum marioi, commonly known as the Mario's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal stem that produces new growths from the nodes. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum marioi is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
