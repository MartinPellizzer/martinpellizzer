Eupatorium mikaniifolium

Eupatorium mikaniifolium, commonly known as tropical blueweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium mikaniifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
