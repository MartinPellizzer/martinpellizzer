Epidendrum bletioides

Epidendrum bletioides, commonly known as the Bletioides Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs covered in dense, velvety foliage. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in racemes with a subtle fragrance.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
