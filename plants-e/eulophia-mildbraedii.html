Eulophia mildbraedii

Eulophia mildbraedii, commonly known as the Malawi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
