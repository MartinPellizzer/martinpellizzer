Eriocaulon tubiflorum

Eriocaulon tubiflorum, commonly known as the tuberous spike-rush, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its tuberous roots and dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its small, white to pale pink flowers are clustered in dense spikes, and the leaves are narrow and grass-like.

It plays an important ecological role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
