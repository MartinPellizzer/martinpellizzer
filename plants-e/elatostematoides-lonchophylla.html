Elatostematoides lonchophylla

Elatostematoides lonchophylla, commonly known as the rock spathiphyllum, is a member of the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique morphology.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous structure, often growing in rocky or crevice habitats. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It is adapted to arid environments and exhibits a slow growth rate.
