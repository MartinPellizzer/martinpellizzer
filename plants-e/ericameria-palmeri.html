Ericameria palmeri

Ericameria palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's goldenbush, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and silver-gray foliage, which provide visual interest throughout the growing season. The plant belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.

Ericameria palmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
