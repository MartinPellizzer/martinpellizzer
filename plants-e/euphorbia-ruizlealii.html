Euphorbia ruizlealii

Euphorbia ruizlealii, commonly known as the Ruizleal's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a distinctive stem structure with a swollen, often ribbed body that stores water, typical of desert plants. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
