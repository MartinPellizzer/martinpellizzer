Eria soronensis

Eria soronensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom from the base of the leaves. Its leaves are typically velvety and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

Eria soronensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
