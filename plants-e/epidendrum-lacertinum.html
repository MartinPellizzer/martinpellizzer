Epidendrum lacertinum

Epidendrum lacertinum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.

Epidendrum lacertinum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various growing conditions.
