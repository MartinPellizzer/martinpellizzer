Escallonia poeppigiana

Escallonia poeppigiana, commonly known as the Chilean fire bush, is a flowering shrub native to South America.

It features dense, evergreen foliage that provides a lush green backdrop. The plant produces clusters of showy, red or orange flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
