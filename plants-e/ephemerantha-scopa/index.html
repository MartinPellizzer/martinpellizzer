Ephemerantha Scopa

Ephemerantha scopa is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. Known for its short lifespan and ephemeral blooming cycle, it thrives in harsh, cold environments. The plant's unique structure includes small, cup-shaped flowers that open only briefly each day. Its limited distribution and ecological specificity make it a subject of scientific interest. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and climate change.
