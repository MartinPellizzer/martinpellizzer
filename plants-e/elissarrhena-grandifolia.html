Elissarrhena grandifolia

Elissarrhena grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered elissarrhena, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Elissarrhena grandifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
