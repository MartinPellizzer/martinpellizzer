Euphrasia × electa

Euphrasia × electa, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a hybrid species within the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. Euphrasia × electa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
