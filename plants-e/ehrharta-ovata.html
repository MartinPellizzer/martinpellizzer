Ehrharta ovata

Ehrharta ovata, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in summer, which are borne in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Ehrharta ovata is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a valued addition to gardens and landscapes.
