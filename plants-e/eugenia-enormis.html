Eugenia enormis

Eugenia enormis, commonly known as the giant eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, making it one of the tallest species in the Eugenia genus. Its leaves are large, elliptical, and have a glossy green surface.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, fleshy berries.
