Etlingera subulicalyx

Etlingera subulicalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers that resemble torches. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
