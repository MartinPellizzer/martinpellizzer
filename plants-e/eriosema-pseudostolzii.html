Eriosema pseudostolzii

Eriosema pseudostolzii, commonly known as the silverleaf eriosema, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage that gives it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Eriosema pseudostolzii is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, where it adapts well to dry and sunny conditions.
