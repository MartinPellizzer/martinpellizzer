Eriospermum lineare

Eriospermum lineare, commonly known as the linear eriosperm, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Eriospermum lineare is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
