Echeveria rosei

Echeveria rosei, commonly known as the 'Pink Pearl' or 'Rosea,' is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically pink to purple in color, often with a bluish or silverish hue. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant colors.

Echeveria rosei is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
