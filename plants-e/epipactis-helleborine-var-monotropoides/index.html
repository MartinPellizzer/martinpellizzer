Epipactis Helleborine Var. Monotropoides

Epipactis helleborine var. monotropoides is a unique orchid species known for its striking appearance and ecological significance. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forests across Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its dark green leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Despite its beauty, it is often mistaken for other orchid species due to its subtle coloration. The plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its habitat.
