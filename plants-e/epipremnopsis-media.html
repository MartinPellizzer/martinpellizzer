Epipremnopsis media

Epipremnopsis media, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that often have a silvery or variegated appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often used as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is also popular in the pet trade as it is known to be attractive to cats.
