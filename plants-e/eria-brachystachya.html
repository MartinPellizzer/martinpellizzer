Eria brachystachya

Eria brachystachya, commonly known as the short-stalked eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, delicate orchid flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in its natural habitat.

Eria brachystachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
