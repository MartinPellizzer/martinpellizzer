Elaeodendron croceum var. heterophyllum

Elaeodendron croceum var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered elaeodendron, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to its foliage. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
