Eupatorium procerum

Eupatorium procerum, commonly known as slender boneset, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and stream banks. The plant features tall, slender stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, white to pale blue flowers in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture with prominent veins.

Eupatorium procerum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.
