Elmerrillia papuana var. glaberrima

Elmerrillia papuana var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-leafed elmerrillia, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the humid lowland forests of Papua New Guinea.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense, drooping clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing near the forest floor.
