Erica plukenetii subsp. bredensis

Erica plukenetii subsp.

bredensis, commonly known as the Breda heath, is a shrub native to South Africa. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in spring and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings.
