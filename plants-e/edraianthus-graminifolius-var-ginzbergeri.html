Edraianthus graminifolius var. ginzbergeri

Edraianthus graminifolius var. ginzbergeri, commonly known as the Ginzberger's grass-leaved edraianthus, is a perennial plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear, arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to specialized botanical collections.
