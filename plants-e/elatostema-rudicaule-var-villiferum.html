Elatostema rudicaule var. villiferum

Elatostema rudicaule var. villiferum, commonly known as the creeping elatostema, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine, appressed hairs. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical structure is characterized by a rhizomatous root system that aids in its spreading growth habit.
