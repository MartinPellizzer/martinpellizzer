Epipactis tremolsii subsp. duriensis

Epipactis tremolsii subsp.

duriensis, commonly known as the Duriense Helleborine, is a rare orchid species native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive lip.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. It is known for its specialized pollination mechanism involving specific insect species.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several European countries.
