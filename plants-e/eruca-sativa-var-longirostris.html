Eruca sativa var. longirostris

Eruca sativa var. longirostris, commonly known as the long-beaked rocket, is a cultivated variety of the wild mustard plant.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and small, edible pods that contain seeds. The plant is typically grown for its peppery, mustard-like flavor, often used in culinary applications. It thrives in cool climates and well-drained soil, preferring full sun.

This variety is valued for both its nutritional content and its role in traditional agriculture.
