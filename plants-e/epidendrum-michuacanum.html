Epidendrum michuacanum

Epidendrum michuacanum, commonly known as the Michuacan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Mexico.

It features long, arching stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces pendulous clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers with a distinct three-lobed lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum michuacanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
