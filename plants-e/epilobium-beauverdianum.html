Epilobium beauverdianum

Epilobium beauverdianum, commonly known as the slender willow-herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils, such as those found in wet meadows and riverbanks. The plant features slender, erect stems covered with glandular hairs, and its leaves are lanceolate, often with a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and are typically pink to purple in color.

Epilobium beauverdianum is known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting various invertebrate species.
