Eriocaulon equisetoides

Eriocaulon equisetoides, commonly known as the needlegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale flowers. It typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, preferring moist and acidic soils.

Eriocaulon equisetoides is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, showcasing its adaptability to humid environments.
