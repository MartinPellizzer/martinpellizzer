Eragrostis leersioides

Eragrostis leersioides, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and bear small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
