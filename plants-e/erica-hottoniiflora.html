Erica hottoniiflora

Erica hottoniiflora, commonly known as the Hotton's heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. Erica hottoniiflora is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
