Eupatorium loniceroides

Eupatorium loniceroides, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. Eupatorium loniceroides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators like butterflies and bees.
