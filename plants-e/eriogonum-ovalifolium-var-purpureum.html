Eriogonum ovalifolium var. purpureum

Eriogonum ovalifolium var. purpureum, commonly known as the purpleleaf globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its rounded, globular inflorescence and purple-tinged leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with reddish-brown markings.

It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
