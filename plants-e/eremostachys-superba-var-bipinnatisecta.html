Eremostachys superba var. bipinnatisecta

Eremostachys superba var. bipinnatisecta, commonly known as the bipinnatisect eremostachys, is a striking member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, almost fern-like appearance. Native to arid or semi-arid regions, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its stems are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss in dry conditions.
