Eremophila platythamnos subsp. villosa

Eremophila platythamnos subsp.

villosa, commonly known as the silky eremophila, is a shrub native to Australia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and showy pink to red flowers.

The plant has a woody stem with hairy, grey-green leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
