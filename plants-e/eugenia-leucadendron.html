Eugenia leucadendron

Eugenia leucadendron, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support local wildlife.
