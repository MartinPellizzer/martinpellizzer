Euonymus sieboldianus var. nikoensis

Euonymus sieboldianus var. nikoensis, commonly known as the Niko's euonymus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red, urn-shaped fruits. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its hardiness and aesthetic qualities.
