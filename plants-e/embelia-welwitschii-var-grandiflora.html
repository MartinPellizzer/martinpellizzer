Embelia welwitschii var. grandiflora

Embelia welwitschii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered embelia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. These flowers are followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
