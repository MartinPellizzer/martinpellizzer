Erica dysantha

Erica dysantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes various shrubs and evergreen plants. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Erica dysantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
