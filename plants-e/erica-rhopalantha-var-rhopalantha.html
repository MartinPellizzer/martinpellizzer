Erica rhopalantha var. rhopalantha

Erica rhopalantha var. rhopalantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is a low-growing shrub with evergreen foliage, and its leaves are narrow and leathery.

This plant is well adapted to harsh, sunny environments and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
