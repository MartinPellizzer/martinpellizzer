Elaeocarpus robustus

Elaeocarpus robustus, commonly known as the robust elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
