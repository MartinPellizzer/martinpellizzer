Eriocaulon obtriangulare

Eriocaulon obtriangulare, commonly known as the triangular spike-rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its compact, triangular spikelets. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with a fine, hairy layer.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.
