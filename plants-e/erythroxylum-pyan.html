Erythroxylum pyan

Erythroxylum pyan, commonly known as the purple leaf erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking purple foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant leaf color and unique appearance.
