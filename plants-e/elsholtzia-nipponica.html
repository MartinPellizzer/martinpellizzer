Elsholtzia nipponica

Elsholtzia nipponica, commonly known as Japanese elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to temperate climates. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.

Elsholtzia nipponica is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
