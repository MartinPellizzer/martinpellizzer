Ehrharta pusilla

Ehrharta pusilla, commonly known as little love grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features fine, slender leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and can thrive in arid environments. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Ehrharta pusilla is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in degraded areas.
