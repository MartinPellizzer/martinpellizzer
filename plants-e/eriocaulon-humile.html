Eriocaulon humile

Eriocaulon humile, commonly known as the small spike-rush, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with a fine, silky coating.

Eriocaulon humile plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
