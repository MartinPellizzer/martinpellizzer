Epidendrum cilioccidentale

Epidendrum cilioccidentale, commonly known as the Ciliated Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually clustered in spikes, with showy, colorful blooms that can range from white to pink and purple.

Epidendrum cilioccidentale is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
