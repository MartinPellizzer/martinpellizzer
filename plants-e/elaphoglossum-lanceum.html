Elaphoglossum lanceum

Elaphoglossum lanceum, commonly known as the lance-leaf holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its fertile leaves, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

Elaphoglossum lanceum is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
