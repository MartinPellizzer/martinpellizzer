Effusiella sarcopetala

Effusiella sarcopetala, commonly known as the woolly leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Effusiella sarcopetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
