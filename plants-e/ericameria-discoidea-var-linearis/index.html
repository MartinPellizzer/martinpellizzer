Ericameria Discoidea Var. Linearis

Ericameria discoidea var. linearis is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of the western United States. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its unique linear leaves and yellowish flower heads. This variety is often found in dry, rocky habitats where it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Its striking appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
