Erythraea nudicaulis

Erythraea nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed erythraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features a slender, leafless stem that bears small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are small and alternate, usually appearing only at the base of the plant.

Erythraea nudicaulis is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
