Echinocactus acuatus var. spinosior

Echinocactus acuatus var. spinosior, commonly known as the spinous hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and dense clusters of spines, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for desert gardens.
