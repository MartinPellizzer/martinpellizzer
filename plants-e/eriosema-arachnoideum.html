Eriosema arachnoideum

Eriosema arachnoideum, commonly known as the spider-tailed eriosema, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its foliage is often covered in fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Eriosema arachnoideum is primarily found in the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces of South Africa.
