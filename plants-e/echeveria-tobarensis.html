Echeveria tobarensis

Echeveria tobarensis, commonly known as the Toba Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its compact size and aesthetic appeal, it is widely cultivated as an ornamental plant.
