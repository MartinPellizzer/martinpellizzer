Eupatorium lucayanum

Eupatorium lucayanum, commonly known as the Lucayan thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Eupatorium lucayanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various ailments.
