Eriostemon rhomboideus

Eriostemon rhomboideus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1-2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish-yellow in color. It is often found in sandy or loamy soils in coastal and inland regions.

Eriostemon rhomboideus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering habit.
