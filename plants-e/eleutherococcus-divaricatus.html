Eleutherococcus divaricatus

Eleutherococcus divaricatus, commonly known as the Siberian ginseng, is a deciduous shrub native to the forests of eastern Russia, China, and Korea.

The plant produces red berries that are rich in antioxidants and have been used in traditional medicine. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
