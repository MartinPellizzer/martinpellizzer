Erica copiosa var. linearisepala

Erica copiosa var. linearisepala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
