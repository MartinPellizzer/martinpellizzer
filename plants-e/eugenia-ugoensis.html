Eugenia ugoensis

Eugenia ugoensis, commonly known as the Ugoensis Myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. It produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when mature.

Eugenia ugoensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and fragrant foliage.
