Ecbolium racemosum

Ecbolium racemosum, commonly known as the clustered bladderpod, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in racemes. Due to its ecological role and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
