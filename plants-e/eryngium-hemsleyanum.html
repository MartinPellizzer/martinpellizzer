Eryngium hemsleyanum

Eryngium hemsleyanum, commonly known as the Mexican thistle, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

Eryngium hemsleyanum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.
