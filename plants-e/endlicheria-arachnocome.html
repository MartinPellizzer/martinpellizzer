Endlicheria arachnocome

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which features long, thin stems ending in a cluster of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base.
