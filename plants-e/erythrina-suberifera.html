Erythrina suberifera

Erythrina suberifera, commonly known as the red coral tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to parts of Africa and the Indian Ocean islands.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers and distinctive trifoliate leaves. The plant produces long, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Erythrina suberifera has also been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
