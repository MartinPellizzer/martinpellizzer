Erica cristiflora var. blanda

Erica cristiflora var. blanda, commonly known as the soft broom.

This low-growing evergreen shrub is native to South Africa and features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are narrow, leathery, and typically a deep green, providing a lush backdrop for the blooms. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal climates.
