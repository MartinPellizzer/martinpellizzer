Epidendrum totoroense

Epidendrum totoroense, commonly known as the Totoro orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features long, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It belongs to the Epidendrum genus, which includes over 1,000 species of orchids.

The Totoro orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
