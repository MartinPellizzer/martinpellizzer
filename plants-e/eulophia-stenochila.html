Eulophia stenochila

Eulophia stenochila, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers are small and clustered, usually in a spike, with a distinctive coloration that varies from white to pale yellow.
