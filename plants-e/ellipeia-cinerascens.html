Ellipeia cinerascens

Ellipeia cinerascens, commonly known as the ash-leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The leaves are compound, with a distinctive ash-gray color that gives the species its scientific name. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by round, fleshy fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
