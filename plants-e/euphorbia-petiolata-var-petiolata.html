Euphorbia petiolata var. petiolata

Euphorbia petiolata var. petiolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in a waxy coating, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The leaves are narrow, fleshy, and typically have a distinctive white or pale green coloration. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

This plant is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
