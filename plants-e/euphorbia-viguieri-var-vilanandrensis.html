Euphorbia viguieri var. vilanandrensis

Euphorbia viguieri var. vilanandrensis, commonly known as the Madagascar spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves, which are adapted to store water in its arid native environment. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
