Eragrostis millettii

Eragrostis millettii, commonly known as millet grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grassy areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control.

Eragrostis millettii is also a source of forage for livestock in some regions.
