Euphorbia phymatosperma

Euphorbia phymatosperma, commonly known as the smooth euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a unique cyathium structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are often reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it may have spiny or waxy coatings for protection.

Euphorbia phymatosperma is a hardy, low-maintenance plant suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
