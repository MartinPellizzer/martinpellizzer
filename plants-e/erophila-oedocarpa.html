Erophila oedocarpa

Erophila oedocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
