Etlingera australasica

Etlingera australasica, commonly known as the Australian torch ginger, is a tropical plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its striking, red-orange floral bracts that resemble torches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often clustered within the bracts. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Etlingera australasica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
