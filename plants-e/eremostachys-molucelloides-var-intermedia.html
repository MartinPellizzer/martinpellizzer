Eremostachys molucelloides var. intermedia

Eremostachys molucelloides var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate moluccan eremostachys, is a succulent plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and clustered, small, often blue or purple flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance through its thick, water-storing leaves. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
