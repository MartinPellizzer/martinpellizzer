Eupatorium microphyllum

Eupatorium microphyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The plant features slender stems and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive rough texture.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
