Echidnopsis scutellata subsp. planiflora

Echidnopsis scutellata subsp.

planiflora, commonly known as the flat-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a powdery coating.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging growing conditions.
