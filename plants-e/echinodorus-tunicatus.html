Echinodorus tunicatus

Echinodorus tunicatus, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant has a rhizomatic growth habit, allowing it to spread across the substrate in aquariums or ponds. It thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular fertilization to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Echinodorus tunicatus is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
