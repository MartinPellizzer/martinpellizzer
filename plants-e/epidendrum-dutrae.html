Epidendrum dutrae

Epidendrum dutrae, commonly known as the Dutra's epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum dutrae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
