Eupatorium multicaulum

Eupatorium multicaulum, commonly known as boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features tall, sturdy stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a serrated edge.

Boneset is often found in moist meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.
