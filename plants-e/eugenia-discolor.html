Eugenia discolor

Eugenia discolor, commonly known as the purple-leafed myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark purple leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenia discolor is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
