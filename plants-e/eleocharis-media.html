Eleocharis media

Eleocharis media, commonly known as spike rush, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical spikelets that are typically found in wet or marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered at the tips of the stems. Eleocharis media is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and improve water quality.

It is also a valuable food source for various wildlife species.
