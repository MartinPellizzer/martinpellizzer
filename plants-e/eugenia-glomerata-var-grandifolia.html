Eugenia glomerata var. grandifolia

Eugenia glomerata var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants. Its fruits, which are small and edible, have a sweet, aromatic flavor.

This species thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.
