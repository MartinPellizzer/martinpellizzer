Excoecaria agallocha var. muelleriana

Excoecaria agallocha var. muelleriana, commonly known as the muelleriana variant of the Indian rubber plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries, which are an important food source for birds. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.

This variant is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant due to its adaptability and visual appeal.
