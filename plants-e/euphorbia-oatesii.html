Euphorbia oatesii

Euphorbia oatesii, commonly known as the Oates' spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray. The plant produces small, yellowish flower clusters called cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbia genus. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
