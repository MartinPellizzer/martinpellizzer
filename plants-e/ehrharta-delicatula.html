Ehrharta delicatula

Ehrharta delicatula, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Ehrharta delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
