Elaphoglossum yourkeorum

Elaphoglossum yourkeorum, commonly known as the Yourke's holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

Elaphoglossum yourkeorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
