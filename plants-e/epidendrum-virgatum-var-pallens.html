Epidendrum virgatum var. pallens

Epidendrum virgatum var. pallens, commonly known as the pale epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences bearing numerous small, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
