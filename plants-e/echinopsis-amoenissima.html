Echinopsis amoenissima

Echinopsis amoenissima, commonly known as the "Moon Cactus," is a species of cactus native to the deserts of South America.

It is characterized by its short, spiny stems and a distinctive, rounded shape. The plant produces beautiful, night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
