Euphorbia bupleuroides

Euphorbia bupleuroides, commonly known as the blue-green euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
