Erythroxylum sparsiflorum

Erythroxylum sparsiflorum, commonly known as the sparse-flower erythroxylum, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree belonging to the Erythroxylaceae family, known for its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by elliptical berries that contain a single seed.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions.
