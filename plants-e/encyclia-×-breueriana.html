Encyclia × breueriana

Encyclia × breueriana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical orchid that belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia fraterna, resulting in a plant with unique morphological features. The plant typically produces compact, hairy pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. Its flowers are showy, with a range of colors from yellow to orange, and are often fragrant.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated in warm, humid environments.
