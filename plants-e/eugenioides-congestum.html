Eugenioides congestum

Eugenioides congestum, commonly known as the dense eugenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy appearance.

Eugenioides congestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
