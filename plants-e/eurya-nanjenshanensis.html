Eurya nanjenshanensis

Eurya nanjenshanensis, commonly known as the Nanjing Eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Jiangxi and Zhejiang. This evergreen shrub typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, while its small white flowers appear in clusters during the spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
