Echinopsis orozasana

Echinopsis orozasana, commonly known as the Orózsaña cactus, is a species native to Bolivia.

It is a spineless cactus with a globular shape and a dark green to bluish-green stem. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and flowering make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
