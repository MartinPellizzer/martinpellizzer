Echinops ilicifolius

Echinops ilicifolius, commonly known as the silver centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a rosette of silver-gray, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking spiny, spherical seed heads that are prominent in late summer and autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes.
