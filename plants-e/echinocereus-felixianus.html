Echinocereus felixianus

Echinocereus felixianus, commonly known as the Mexican Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, segmented stems that form a dense, bushy structure. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Echinocereus felixianus is adapted to arid environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
