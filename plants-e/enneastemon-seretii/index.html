Enneastemon Seretii

Enneastemon seretii is a rare and striking plant native to a small region in southern China. It is known for its unique, star-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and is protected in several conservation programs. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
