Epidendrum flammeum

Epidendrum flammeum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat, and are borne on long, drooping inflorescences. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. It requires high humidity and well-draining potting media to flourish.

Epidendrum flammeum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
