Eriocaulon albotetrandra

Eriocaulon albotetrandra, commonly known as the white-tipped spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and aquatic or wetland habitat.

It is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments and plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems.
