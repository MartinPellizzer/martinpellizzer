Eugenioides obovatum

Eugenioides obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed eugenia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that changes from green to red as it matures.

Eugenioides obovatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
