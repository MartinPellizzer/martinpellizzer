Exacum lanceolatum

Exacum lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Exacum lanceolatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
