Eugenia spruceana

Eugenia spruceana, commonly known as the Spruce's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Andes mountains of South America, particularly in regions of Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
