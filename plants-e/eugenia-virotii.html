Eugenia virotii

Eugenia virotii, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. These fruits are edible and often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia virotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
