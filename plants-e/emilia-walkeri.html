Emilia walkeri

Emilia walkeri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flower heads, which are composed of numerous small, daisy-like florets. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Emilia walkeri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
