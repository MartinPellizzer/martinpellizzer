Epilobium rigidum

Epilobium rigidum, commonly known as rigid willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils such as riverbanks and wet meadows. The plant features erect, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its showy, pink to purple flowers bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Epilobium rigidum is known for its ability to thrive in wet environments and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
