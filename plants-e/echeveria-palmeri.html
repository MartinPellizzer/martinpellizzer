Echeveria palmeri

Echeveria palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's Echeveria, is a succulent native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, often blue-green or grayish leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces striking clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its beauty and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
