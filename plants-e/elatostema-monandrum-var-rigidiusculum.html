Elatostema monandrum var. rigidiusculum

Elatostema monandrum var. rigidiusculum, commonly known as the rigid-leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its growth habit is low and sprawling, with a tendency to form dense mats.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
