Eclipta Alba Var. Longifolia

Eclipta alba var. longifolia is a lesser-known variant of the common eclipta plant, often overlooked for its medicinal potential. This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its long, slender leaves and clusters of yellow flowers make it an attractive addition to gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains underutilized in modern herbal medicine. Further research could unlock its many benefits for human health and traditional healing practices.
