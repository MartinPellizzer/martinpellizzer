Erythrina senegalensis var. camerounensis

Erythrina senegalensis var. camerounensis, commonly known as the Cameroon flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers, which bloom in dense, racemose inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its bark is often smooth with a reddish hue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
