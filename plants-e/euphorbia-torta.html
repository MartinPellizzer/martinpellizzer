Euphorbia torta

Euphorbia torta, commonly known as the twisted spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, branched stem covered in small, fleshy, greenish-yellow leaves. The plant produces striking, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
