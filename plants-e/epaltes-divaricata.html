Epaltes divaricata

Epaltes divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slight waxy coating.

Epaltes divaricata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
