Echinopsis stollenwerkiana

Echinopsis stollenwerkiana, commonly known as the Stollenwerk's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stems that are typically green with a yellowish or reddish bloom. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers during the cooler months, usually in the evening. It is a slow-growing, drought-tolerant species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and blooming habits, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and desert gardeners.
