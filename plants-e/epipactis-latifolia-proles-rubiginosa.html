Epipactis latifolia proles rubiginosa

Epipactis latifolia proles rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in association with other orchid species.
