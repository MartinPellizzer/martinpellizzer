Eragrostis dielsii var. pritzelii

Eragrostis dielsii var. pritzelii, commonly known as Pritzel's lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
