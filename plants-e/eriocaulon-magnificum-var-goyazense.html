Eriocaulon magnificum var. goyazense

Eriocaulon magnificum var. goyazense, commonly known as the Goyazense spikegrass, is a striking member of the Poaceae family native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of purple and pink. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with finely textured leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest in botanical research and horticulture.
