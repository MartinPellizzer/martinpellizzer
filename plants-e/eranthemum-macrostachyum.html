Eranthemum macrostachyum

Eranthemum macrostachyum, commonly known as the large-stemmed eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers along its elongated stems.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
