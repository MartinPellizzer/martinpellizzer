Elaeagnus conferta var. menghaiensis

Elaeagnus conferta var. menghaiensis, commonly known as the Menghai silverleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
