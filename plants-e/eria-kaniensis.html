Eria kaniensis

Eria kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniensis orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. This herbaceous plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in racemose inflorescences, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
