Epidendrum calceolariae

Epidendrum calceolariae, commonly known as the "Cup-leaf Orchid," is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, cup-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum calceolariae is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
