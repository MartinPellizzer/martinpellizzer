Elaeocarpus celebesianus

Elaeocarpus celebesianus, commonly known as the Celebes apple or Borneo apple, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain a single seed. Its fruit is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor, making it a valuable resource in local cuisines.

The tree is also noted for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
