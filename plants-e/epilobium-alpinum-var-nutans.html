Epilobium alpinum var. nutans

Epilobium alpinum var. nutans, commonly known as the Alpine willow herb, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in glandular hairs and produces small, pink to purple flowers in terminal clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a hairy texture.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.
