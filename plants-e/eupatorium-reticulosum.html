Eupatorium reticulosum

Eupatorium reticulosum, commonly known as roughleaf thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Eupatorium reticulosum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
