Elaeagnus magna var. nanchuanensis

Elaeagnus magna var. nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan silverleaf buckthorn, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-white bark and small, fragrant leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters, followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
