Eragrostis astrepta

Eragrostis astrepta, commonly known as silver panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Eragrostis astrepta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
