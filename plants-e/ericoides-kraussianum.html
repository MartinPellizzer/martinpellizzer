Ericoides kraussianum

Ericoides kraussianum, commonly known as the Cape heath.

It is a shrub native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Ericoides kraussianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal and sandy environments.
