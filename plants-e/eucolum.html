Eucolum

Eucolum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in spring and summer. Eucolum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
