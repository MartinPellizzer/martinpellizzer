Euphoria rubra

Euphoria rubra, commonly known as the red euphoria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features vibrant red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has palmate leaves with a glossy surface, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a pollinator attractor and its use in traditional medicine.
