Euphorbia xanti

Euphorbia xanti, commonly known as the White-spined Candelabra, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branched stem system with a distinctive, spiny appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic flower-like structures. Its leaves are typically reduced to small spines, giving it a unique and striking appearance.

Euphorbia xanti is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
