Euphrasia Salisburgensis Subsp. Portae

Euphrasia salisburgensis subsp. portae is a rare and delicate plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family, known for its parasitic and hemiparasitic species. This subspecies is distinguished by its small, yellow-orange flowers and hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh environments. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique ecological role makes it an important subject for botanical study and preservation efforts.
