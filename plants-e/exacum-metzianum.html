Exacum metzianum

Exacum metzianum, commonly known as the blue snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Exacum metzianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
