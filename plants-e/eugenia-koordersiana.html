Eugenia koordersiana

Eugenia koordersiana, commonly known as the Koorders' eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, dark purple fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
