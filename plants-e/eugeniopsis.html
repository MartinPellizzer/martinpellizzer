Eugeniopsis

Eugeniopsis, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. Eugeniopsis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its fruit, known as a drupe, contains a single seed and is typically small in size.
