Euphorbia yanjinensis

Euphorbia yanjinensis, commonly known as the Yanjin spurge, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, spine-tipped leaves and yellowish-green cyathia, which are its specialized flower-like structures. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
