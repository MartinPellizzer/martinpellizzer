Epilobium × intersitum

Epilobium × intersitum, commonly known as the common willow herb, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its erect or decumbent growth habit and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Epilobium × intersitum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil.
