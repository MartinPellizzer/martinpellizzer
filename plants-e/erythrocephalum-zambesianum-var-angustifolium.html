Erythrocephalum zambesianum var. angustifolium

Erythrocephalum zambesianum var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved erythrocephalum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
