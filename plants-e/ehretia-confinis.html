Ehretia confinis

Ehretia confinis, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or red flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin layers.
