Eremosis foliosa

Eremosis foliosa, commonly known as the leafy eremosis, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it an important species in ecological studies of desert flora.
