Epidendrum curimarcense

Epidendrum curimarcense, commonly known as the Crimson Sword orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of red and orange. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

The flowers are typically borne in racemes and can last for several weeks.
