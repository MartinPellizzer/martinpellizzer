Excoecaria laurocerasus var. genuina

Excoecaria laurocerasus var. genuina, commonly known as the Natal plum or Natal cherry, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and edible landscaping.

The species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
