Elmerrillia platyphylla

Elmerrillia platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf elmerrillia, is a unique plant species characterized by its broad, flat leaves that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant's foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems emerging from the base.

Elmerrillia platyphylla is also valued for its ornamental qualities, making it a popular choice in horticultural gardens.
