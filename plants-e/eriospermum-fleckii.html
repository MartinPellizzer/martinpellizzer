Eriospermum fleckii

Eriospermum fleckii, commonly known as the yellow eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers and hairy stems. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
