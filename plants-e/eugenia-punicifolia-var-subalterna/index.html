Eugenia Punicifolia Var. Subalterna

Eugenia punicifolia var. subalterna is a unique and lesser-known plant species that belongs to the Myrtaceae family. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid and shaded environments. This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and strikingly colorful foliage. Its leaves often display a vibrant mix of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. Due to its rarity and aesthetic value, it has gained interest among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
