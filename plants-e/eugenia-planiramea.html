Eugenia planiramea

Eugenia planiramea, commonly known as the flat-branch guava, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that develop into edible berries, which are typically red or yellow when mature.

Eugenia planiramea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
