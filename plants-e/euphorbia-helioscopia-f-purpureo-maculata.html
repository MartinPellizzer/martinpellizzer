Euphorbia helioscopia f. purpureo-maculata

Euphorbia helioscopia f. purpureo-maculata, commonly known as the purple-spotted spurge, is a flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that features striking purple and white spotted leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-green flowers that are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and unique floral structure.
