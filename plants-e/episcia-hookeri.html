Episcia hookeri

Episcia hookeri, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that come in shades of green, red, and purple. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color.

Episcia hookeri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
