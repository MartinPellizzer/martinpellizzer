Eulophia celebica

Eulophia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stem and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct lip. Eulophia celebica typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
