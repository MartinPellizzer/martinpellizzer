Elatostema surculosum var. zeylanicum

Elatostema surculosum var. zeylanicum, commonly known as the Zeylanic Elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are pollinated by insects.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is often used in traditional herbal medicine.
