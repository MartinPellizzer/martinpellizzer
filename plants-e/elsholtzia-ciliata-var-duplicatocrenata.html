Elsholtzia ciliata var. duplicatocrenata

Elsholtzia ciliata var. duplicatocrenata, commonly known as the double-crested elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and a distinctive, grayish-green color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy addition to xerophytic plant collections.
