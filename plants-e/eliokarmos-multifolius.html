Eliokarmos multifolius

Eliokarmos multifolius, commonly known as the many-leaved eliokarmos, is a species native to the temperate regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with numerous small, serrated leaves arranged in a compact rosette. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
