Escallonia spectabilis

Escallonia spectabilis, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Escallonia spectabilis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
