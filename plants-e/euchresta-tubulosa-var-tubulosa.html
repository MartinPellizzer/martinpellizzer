Euchresta tubulosa var. tubulosa

Euchresta tubulosa var. tubulosa, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet, featuring woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a beneficial addition to native plant landscapes.
