Euphorbia Splendens Subsp. Bojeri

Euphorbia splendens subsp. bojeri is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant green leaves and unique, spiky inflorescences, it adds a bold touch to any indoor or outdoor garden. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for beginners. Its resilience and aesthetic appeal have made it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic sap, so care should be taken when handling it.
