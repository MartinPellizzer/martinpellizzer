Epidendrum nocturnum var. latifolium

Epidendrum nocturnum var. latifolium, commonly known as the night orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that open at night, typically in shades of white and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique nocturnal blooming habit and ornamental value.
