Epidendrum platypetalum

Epidendrum platypetalum, commonly known as the flat-petaled orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically violet to purple in color, with a unique flat petal structure that distinguishes it from other Epidendrum species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
