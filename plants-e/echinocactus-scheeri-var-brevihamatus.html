Echinocactus scheeri var. brevihamatus

Echinocactus scheeri var. brevihamatus, commonly known as the short-stemmed hedgehog cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in prominent, sharp spines, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
