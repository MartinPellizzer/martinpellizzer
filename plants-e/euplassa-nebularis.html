Euplassa nebularis

Euplassa nebularis, commonly known as the nebular lichen, is a species of lichen that belongs to the family Verrucariaceae.

It is a foliose lichen, characterized by its leaf-like thallus structure and a distinct, often colorful appearance. The lichen typically grows on soil, rocks, or tree bark in temperate and boreal regions. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a common species in many ecosystems.

Its chemical composition includes various secondary metabolites that contribute to its ecological role and potential medicinal uses.
