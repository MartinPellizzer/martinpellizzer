Eragrostis thunbergii

Eragrostis thunbergii, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-blue foliage and tufted inflorescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-gray hue, and its seeds are small and wind-dispersed.

Eragrostis thunbergii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
