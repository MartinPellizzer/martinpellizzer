Elaphoglossum mannianum

Elaphoglossum mannianum, commonly known as the dwarf holly fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, creeping growth habit. The plant features finely divided, glossy green fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of brownish spore-bearing structures. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
