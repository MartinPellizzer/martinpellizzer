Euryale ferox

Euryale ferox, commonly known as the water caltrop, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, floating fruit that resembles a small chestnut. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and slow-moving rivers. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, while its flowers are small and typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit contains several hard, nut-like seeds that are often harvested for culinary or medicinal purposes.
