Erysimum ochroleucum subsp. duriaei

Erysimum ochroleucum subsp.

duriaei, commonly known as the yellow wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
