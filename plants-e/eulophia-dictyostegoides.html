Eulophia dictyostegoides

Eulophia dictyostegoides, commonly known as the clustered eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking, showy inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has a pseudobulbous structure with long, strap-like leaves that are often dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

Eulophia dictyostegoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
