Eutaxia ericoides var. filicaulis

Eutaxia ericoides var. filicaulis, commonly known as the fern-leaf eutaxia, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are leathery and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This plant is often used in coastal landscapes for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
