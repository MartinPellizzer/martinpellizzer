Epidendrum fragrans var. pachypus

Epidendrum fragrans var. pachypus, commonly known as the fragrant epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a yellow or green lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the old ones. Its leaves are broad, fleshy, and often covered in a waxy coating to retain moisture.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
