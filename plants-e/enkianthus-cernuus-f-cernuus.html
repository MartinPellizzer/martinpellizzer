Enkianthus cernuus f. cernuus

Enkianthus cernuus f. cernuus, commonly known as the drooping enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features striking, pendulous flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
