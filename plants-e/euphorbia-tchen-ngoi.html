Euphorbia tchen-ngoi

Euphorbia tchen-ngoi, commonly known as the Chinese spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its succulent, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the genus Euphorbia. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its toxic sap, it requires careful handling and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
