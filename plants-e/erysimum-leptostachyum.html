Erysimum leptostachyum

Erysimum leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in glandular hairs, and its leaves are pinnately lobed with a distinctive hairy texture. Its flowers are bright yellow, arranged in racemes, and bloom from spring to early summer.

Erysimum leptostachyum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
