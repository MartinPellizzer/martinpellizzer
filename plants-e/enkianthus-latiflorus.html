Enkianthus latiflorus

Enkianthus latiflorus, commonly known as the broad-flower enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

Enkianthus latiflorus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add visual interest to woodland gardens.
