Erica greyi

Erica greyi, commonly known as the Grey's heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers during the late summer and early autumn.

Erica greyi is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
