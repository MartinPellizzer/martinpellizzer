Equisetum Ramosissimum F. Elegans

Equisetum ramosissimum f. elegans is a striking variant of the common horsetail plant known for its elegant, spiky appearance. This fern-like plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in damp woodlands. Its unique structure features segmented stems and whorled leaves that give it a distinctive, almost architectural look. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for centuries in gardens and botanical collections. Despite its ancient lineage, it remains a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
