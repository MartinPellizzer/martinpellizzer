Eria sikkimensis

Eria sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments at high altitudes. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several elliptical leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Eria sikkimensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
