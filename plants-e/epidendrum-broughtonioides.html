Epidendrum broughtonioides

Epidendrum broughtonioides, commonly known as the Mexican Flame Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers and strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Epidendrum broughtonioides requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
