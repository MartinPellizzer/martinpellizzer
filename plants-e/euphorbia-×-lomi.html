Euphorbia × lomi

Euphorbia × lomi, commonly known as the hybrid euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often green or blue-green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers surrounded by brightly colored bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid environments.
