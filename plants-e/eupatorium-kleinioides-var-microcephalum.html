Eupatorium kleinioides var. microcephalum

Eupatorium kleinioides var. microcephalum, commonly known as narrow-headed boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and wet meadows. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture, and its flowers are usually white or pale purple.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
