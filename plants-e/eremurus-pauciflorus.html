Eremurus pauciflorus

Eremurus pauciflorus, commonly known as the desert candle, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Eremurus, which is part of the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the sunflower family. This plant is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, yellowish to white flowers. Eremurus pauciflorus is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
