Endlicheria arenosa

Endlicheria arenosa, commonly known as the sand dune morning glory, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous annual that thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
