Elymus Gmelinii

Elymus gmelinii is a hardy grass species native to the cold climates of northern Asia and North America. It thrives in harsh environments such as tundra and rocky soils where few plants can survive. This plant is known for its ability to withstand extreme temperatures and poor soil conditions. Its slender stems and narrow leaves make it a common sight in alpine regions. Elymus gmelinii plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife in its ecosystem.
