Epidendrum curtisii

Epidendrum curtisii, commonly known as the Curtis's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant typically features showy, colorful flowers that bloom in racemes, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in a waxy coating.

Epidendrum curtisii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
