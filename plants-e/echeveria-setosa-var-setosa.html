Echeveria setosa var. setosa

Echeveria setosa var. setosa, commonly known as the Silver Dollar Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features dense rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and tightly packed, giving the plant a compact and ornamental appearance. It produces clusters of pink or purple flowers during the spring or early summer.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
