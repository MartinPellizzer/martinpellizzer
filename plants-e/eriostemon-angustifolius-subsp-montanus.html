Eriostemon angustifolius subsp. montanus

Eriostemon angustifolius subsp.

montanus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and dry forest regions.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, pale yellow flowers during the autumn season. Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in local ecosystems.
