Eriocaulon sinicum

Eriocaulon sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese spike-rush, is a small herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including China, where it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Eriocaulon sinicum is an important species for ecological studies due to its role in wetland ecosystems.
