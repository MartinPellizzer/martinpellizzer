Erigeron Divergens

Erigeron divergens is a flowering plant known for its delicate white petals and fluffy yellow centers. It thrives in open fields and meadows across North America. This species is part of the aster family, which includes many familiar wildflowers. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plants. Despite its beauty, it can be mistaken for other similar species in the genus.
