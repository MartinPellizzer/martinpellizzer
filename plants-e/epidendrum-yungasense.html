Epidendrum yungasense

Epidendrum yungasense, commonly known as the Yungas Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to the humid cloud forests of Bolivia and Peru.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, often with darker markings. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum yungasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
