Erigeron uniflorus subsp. elborsensis

Erigeron uniflorus subsp.

elborsensis, commonly known as the Elborsen's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and sandy soils. It features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and locally endemic species.
