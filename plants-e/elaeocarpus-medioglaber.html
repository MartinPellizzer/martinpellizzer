Elaeocarpus medioglaber

Elaeocarpus medioglaber, commonly known as the smooth-fruited elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain a single seed.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
