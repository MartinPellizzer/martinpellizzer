Epidendrum gelisii

Epidendrum gelisii, commonly known as the Snowy Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear numerous white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Epidendrum gelisii is prized for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
