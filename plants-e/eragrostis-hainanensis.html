Eragrostis hainanensis

Eragrostis hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the island of Hainan in China and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open areas with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
