Exochorda × macrantha

Exochorda × macrantha, commonly known as the Chinese snowball bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

The species is often used in gardens for its winter interest due to its persistent red stems.
