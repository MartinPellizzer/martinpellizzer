Epidendrum Medusae

Epidendrum medusae is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. Known for its unique, coral-like blooms that resemble a sea anemone, this plant captivates botanists and enthusiasts alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. The species is highly sought after for its beauty and ecological significance. Its conservation status highlights the need for protecting its natural habitat.
