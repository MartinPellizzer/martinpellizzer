Eranthis hyemalis var. cilicica

Eranthis hyemalis var. cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician winter aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features striking yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves appear. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It prefers well-drained, moist soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its early blooming habit and vibrant floral display.
