Ephedra clusii

Ephedra clusii, commonly known as the African ephedra, is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ephedraceae family and is characterized by its jointed stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Ephedra clusii is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in areas with low rainfall.

Its botanical features make it a unique and resilient species in its native habitat.
