Echeveria meyraniana

Echeveria meyraniana, commonly known as the Mexican snowflake, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces striking clusters of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
