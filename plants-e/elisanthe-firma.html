Elisanthe firma

Elisanthe firma, commonly known as the common elisanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces fluffy, white to pinkish bracts that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Elisanthe firma is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant once established.
