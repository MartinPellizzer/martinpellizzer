Erythrina Humei Var. Raja

Erythrina humei var. raja is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant red flowers and unique, clustered pods, it stands out in any landscape. This variety is a cultivated form of the broader Erythrina humei species, often prized for its ornamental value. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves that add a lush green contrast to its showy blooms. The plant is not only visually appealing but also holds cultural significance in various regions.
