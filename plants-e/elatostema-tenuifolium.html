Elatostema tenuifolium

Elatostema tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in umbels. Elatostema tenuifolium has a short flowering period, usually in the spring or early summer.
