Eulophia rupestris

Eulophia rupestris, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows on rocky outcrops and in open, sunny habitats. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Eulophia rupestris is adapted to dry conditions, making it a hardy and resilient species.
