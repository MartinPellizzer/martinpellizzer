Eria micholitzii

Eria micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its roots for moisture and nutrients. Its flowers are small, clustered, and often have a unique coloration, with some varieties displaying purple or green hues.

Eria micholitzii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
