Erica clowieana

Erica clowieana, commonly known as the Clowes heath, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, needle-like leaves. It produces clusters of pink or pale red flowers during the summer months.

Erica clowieana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.
