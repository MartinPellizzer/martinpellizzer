Epidendrum astroselaginella

Epidendrum astroselaginella, commonly known as the star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
