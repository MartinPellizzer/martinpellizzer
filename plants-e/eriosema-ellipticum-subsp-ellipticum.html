Eriosema ellipticum subsp. ellipticum

Eriosema ellipticum subsp.

ellipticum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriosema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa. It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
