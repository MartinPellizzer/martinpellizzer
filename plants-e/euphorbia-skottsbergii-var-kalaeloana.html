Euphorbia skottsbergii var. kalaeloana

Euphorbia skottsbergii var. kalaeloana, commonly known as the Kalaeloana euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces a milky latex when damaged, a common trait among the Euphorbia genus.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
