Erythroxylum domingense

Erythroxylum domingense, commonly known as the Dominican erythroxylum, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the Erythroxylaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth texture, while its fruits are capsule-like and contain seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
