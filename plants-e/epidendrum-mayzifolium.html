Epidendrum mayzifolium

Epidendrum mayzifolium, commonly known as the pink-veined epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with prominent pink veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum mayzifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
