Eriosyce ceratistes var. mollesensis

Eriosyce ceratistes var. mollesensis, commonly known as the soft-hair cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in soft, white spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid environments.
