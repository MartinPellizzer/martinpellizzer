Ericoides tenellum

Ericoides tenellum, commonly known as the small heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and densely packed, giving it a dense, compact appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming in early spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Ericoides tenellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover in suitable climates.
