Euphorbia laciniata subsp. burmanica

Euphorbia laciniata subsp.

burmanica, commonly known as the Burmese leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Southeast Asia. It features a thick, fleshy stem that can grow up to several meters in height.

The plant produces striking, lobed leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. It blooms with small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
