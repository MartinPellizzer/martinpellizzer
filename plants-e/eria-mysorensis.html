Eria mysorensis

Eria mysorensis, commonly known as the Mysore orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown.

Eria mysorensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
