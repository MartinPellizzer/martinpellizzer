Encyclia linearifolioides

Encyclia linearifolioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pineapple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a rosette of linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces erect, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for horticultural purposes.
