Eriospermum brevipedunculatum

Eriospermum brevipedunculatum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed eriosperm, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, upright stems with small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
