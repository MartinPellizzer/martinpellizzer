Eugenia conglomerata var. paniculata

Eugenia conglomerata var. paniculata, commonly known as the paniculate eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant foliage and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in terminal panicles. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature, containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for its aromatic leaves and fruit.
