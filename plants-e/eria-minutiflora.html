Eria minutiflora

Eria minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered eria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, small flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, clustered inflorescences. It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its small size and ornamental flowers, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
