Echeveria vanvlietii

Echeveria vanvlietii, commonly known as the Van Vliet's echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of vibrant purple-pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
