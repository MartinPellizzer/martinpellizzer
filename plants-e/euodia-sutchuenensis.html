Euodia sutchuenensis

Euodia sutchuenensis, commonly known as Chinese boxwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Euodia sutchuenensis prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
