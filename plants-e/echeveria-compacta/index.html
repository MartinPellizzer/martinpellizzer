Echeveria Compacta

Echeveria compacta is a striking succulent native to Mexico known for its dense, rosette-shaped leaves. Its compact form makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting. The plant features vibrant green foliage with a bluish tint that often turns red or purple when exposed to full sun. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring season. This hardy plant is relatively low maintenance, making it ideal for both novice and experienced gardeners.
