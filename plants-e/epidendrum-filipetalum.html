Epidendrum filipetalum

Epidendrum filipetalum, commonly known as the Philippine velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, pendulous inflorescences with clusters of dark purple to violet flowers, which are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum filipetalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
