Eurya taronensis

Eurya taronensis, commonly known as the Taronese evergreen holly, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. Its berries are typically red or black and serve as a food source for various bird species.

Eurya taronensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
