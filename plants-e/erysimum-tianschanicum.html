Erysimum tianschanicum

Erysimum tianschanicum, commonly known as the Tian Shan wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with characteristic cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. It produces bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its foliage is usually dark green and covered in a fine layer of hair, helping to reduce water loss in arid conditions.
