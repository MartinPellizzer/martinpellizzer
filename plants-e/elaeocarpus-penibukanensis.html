Elaeocarpus penibukanensis

Elaeocarpus penibukanensis, commonly known as the Penibukan elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It is often found in lowland to montane forests, thriving in humid and shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
