Erica × wilmoreana

Erica × wilmoreana, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white or pink flowers during the winter months. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are evergreen and provide year-round interest.

Erica × wilmoreana is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salt spray and well-drained soils.
