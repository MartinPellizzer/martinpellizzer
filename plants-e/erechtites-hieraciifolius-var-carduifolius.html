Erechtites hieraciifolius var. carduifolius

Erechtites hieraciifolius var. carduifolius, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features distinctive purple-pink flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a soft, hairy texture. Its root system is fibrous and helps it thrive in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
