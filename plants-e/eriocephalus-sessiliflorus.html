Eriocephalus sessiliflorus

Eriocephalus sessiliflorus, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with a dense, rounded canopy. The leaves are thick, leathery, and covered in a silvery-white powder, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels.

It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-resistant species.
