Eriosyce paucicostata subsp. paucicostata

Eriosyce paucicostata subsp.

paucicostata, commonly known as the few-costate eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and is covered in dense, white to grayish spines.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring season. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in succulent collections.
