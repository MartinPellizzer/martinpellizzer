Eriospermum capense subsp. capense

Eriospermum capense subsp.

capense, commonly known as the Cape eriosperm, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant.

The subspecies capense is distinguished by its specific floral and foliar features compared to other subspecies of Eriospermum.
