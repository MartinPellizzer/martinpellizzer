Epidendrum niebliense

Epidendrum niebliense, commonly known as the Nieve's Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum niebliense is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
