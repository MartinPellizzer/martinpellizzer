Epidendrum utricularioides

Epidendrum utricularioides, commonly known as the "Cup-like Epidendron," is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, cup-shaped pseudobulbs and long, pendant-like inflorescences. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum utricularioides is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and botanical gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
