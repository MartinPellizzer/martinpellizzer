Eucharis × grandiflora

Eucharis × grandiflora, commonly known as the Amazonian white lily, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often used in floral arrangements due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eucharis × grandiflora is a popular ornamental plant in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
