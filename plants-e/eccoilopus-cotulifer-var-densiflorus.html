Eccoilopus cotulifer var. densiflorus

Eccoilopus cotulifer var. densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered cowpea, is a variant of the cowpea species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom along the stems. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with trifoliate leaves and hairy stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its edible seeds, which are used in various culinary traditions around the world.
