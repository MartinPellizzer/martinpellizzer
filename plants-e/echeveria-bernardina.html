Echeveria bernardina

Echeveria bernardina, commonly known as the Bernardina Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy, violet-pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
