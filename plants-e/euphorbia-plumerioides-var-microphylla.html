Euphorbia plumerioides var. microphylla

Euphorbia plumerioides var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature euphorbia or dwarf spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
