Eugenia myhendrae

Eugenia myhendrae, commonly known as the Myhendra eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The fruit, which is typically a berry, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
