Eriocoma uncinata

Eriocoma uncinata, commonly known as the hooked eriocoma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Eriocoma uncinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
