Euonymus europaeus f. aldenhamensis

Euonymus europaeus f. aldenhamensis, commonly known as the Aldenham euonymus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Europe.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red, lantern-like fruits that persist into winter. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This cultivar is often used in horticulture for its dense growth habit and seasonal color changes.
