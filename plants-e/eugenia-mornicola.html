Eugenia mornicola

Eugenia mornicola, commonly known as the Mornicola Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a strong, aromatic scent.

Eugenia mornicola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
