Eupatorium africanum

Eupatorium africanum, commonly known as African boneset, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium africanum is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
