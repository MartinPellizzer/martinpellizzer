Euphrasia gunnii subsp. deflexifolia

Euphrasia gunnii subsp.

deflexifolia, commonly known as the bent-leaved eyebright, is a flowering plant native to southern South America. It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, hairy stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring.

The leaves are lance-shaped and often curve downward, giving the plant its distinguishing appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
