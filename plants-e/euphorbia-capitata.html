Euphorbia capitata

Euphorbia capitata, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has spiny leaves and produces a milky sap that can be irritating. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Euphorbia capitata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardiness.
