Euodia densiflora

Euodia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flower euodia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are typically harvested when mature. Euodia densiflora is valued for its medicinal properties, with its leaves and fruits used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.
