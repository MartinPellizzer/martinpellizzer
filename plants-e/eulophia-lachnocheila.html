Eulophia lachnocheila

Eulophia lachnocheila, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale green with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, moist conditions.
