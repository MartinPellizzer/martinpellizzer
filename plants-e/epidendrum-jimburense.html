Epidendrum jimburense

Epidendrum jimburense, commonly known as the Jimburen orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
