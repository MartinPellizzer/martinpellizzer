Epipremnum glaucicephalum

Epipremnum glaucicephalum, commonly known as the Variegated Philodendron, is a climbing tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of green, white, and cream variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is primarily grown for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
