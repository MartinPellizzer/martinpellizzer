Echinofossulocactus crispatus f. anfractuosus

Echinofossulocactus crispatus f. anfractuosus, commonly known as the crinkled fissured cactus, is a unique and rare cactus species native to specific regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ribbed stems that are covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its textured foliage. It thrives in arid environments with well-draining soil and requires minimal water to survive.
