Epidendrum macrochilum var. albopurpurea

Epidendrum macrochilum var. albopurpurea, commonly known as the Purple-lipped Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that range from white to pale purple, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
