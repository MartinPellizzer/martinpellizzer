Erigeron patentisquama

Erigeron patentisquama, commonly known as the narrow-leaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.
