Epilobium × navasii

Epilobium × navasii, commonly known as the Navasii willowherb, is a hybrid species within the evening primrose family (Onagraceae).

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

This plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
