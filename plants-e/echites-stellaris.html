Echites stellaris

Echites stellaris, commonly known as the star echis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a succulent stem and produces small, spiny leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
