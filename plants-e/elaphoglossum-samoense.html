Elaphoglossum samoense

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories where it can receive adequate humidity and dappled sunlight. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

Elaphoglossum samoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitats.
