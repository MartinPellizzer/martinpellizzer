Empusa ferruginea

Empusa ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty ant.

This plant is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, reddish-brown flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and shelter for small insects.
