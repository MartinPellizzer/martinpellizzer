Encyclia lutzenbergeri

Encyclia lutzenbergeri, commonly known as the Lutzenberger's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb that supports its growth during dry periods. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow color and a prominent lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Encyclia lutzenbergeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
