Eugenia tussacii

Eugenia tussacii, commonly known as the Tussacii myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along riverbanks.

Eugenia tussacii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
