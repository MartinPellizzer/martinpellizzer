Epidendrum erythrostigma

Epidendrum erythrostigma, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the year. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and surrounding environment.

Epidendrum erythrostigma is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
