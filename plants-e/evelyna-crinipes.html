Evelyna crinipes

Evelyna crinipes, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its long, feathery leaves that give it its common name. It produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, often blooming during the rainy season.

Evelyna crinipes thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in forest understories.
