Eleocharis esculenta

Eleocharis esculenta, commonly known as the spike rush, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its rhizomes are often edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
