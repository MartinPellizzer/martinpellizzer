Echinops sphaerocephalus subsp. albidus

Echinops sphaerocephalus subsp.

albidus, commonly known as the white globemallow, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its spherical, spiky flower heads that are typically white or pale yellow in color.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is native to the Mediterranean region. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
