Epidendrum subtorquatum

Epidendrum subtorquatum, commonly known as the twisted-leaf epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage with a distinctive twisted or curling leaf shape. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum subtorquatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
