Euphorbia carinifolia

Euphorbia carinifolia, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, spiny stem with a crown-like arrangement of leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a striking addition to arid landscapes.
