Epidendrum chloranthum

Epidendrum chloranthum, commonly known as the green-flowered epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, often with a waxy coating.

Epidendrum chloranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
