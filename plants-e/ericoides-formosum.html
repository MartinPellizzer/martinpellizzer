Ericoides formosum

Ericoides formosum, commonly known as the Formosa heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan, where it thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is a shrub that grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with evergreen leaves that are leathery and dark green.

Ericoides formosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
