Erysimum hieraciifolium var. lanceolatum

Erysimum hieraciifolium var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar floral structures.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
