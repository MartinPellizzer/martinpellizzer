Erythrina poeppigiana f. redmondii

Erythrina poeppigiana f. redmondii, commonly known as the Redmond's Flame Tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a variant of the species Erythrina poeppigiana, characterized by its vibrant red flowers and distinctive, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces bright red, flame-like blooms that appear in clusters, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to gardens and landscapes.
