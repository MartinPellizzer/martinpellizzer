Echinopsis Eyriesii

Candelabra cactus, scientifically known as Echinopsis eyriesii, is a striking member of the cactus family native to South America. This plant is celebrated for its tall, branching structure that resembles a candlestick, hence its common name. It features clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months, adding a vibrant touch to its desert environment. The candelabra cactus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favorite in many arid landscapes and indoor gardens.
