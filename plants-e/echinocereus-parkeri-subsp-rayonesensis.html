Echinocereus parkeri subsp. rayonesensis

Echinocereus parkeri subsp.

rayonesensis, commonly known as the Rayonesensis cactus, is a species native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Mexico. It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a short, thick stem that often branches at the base.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and conservation efforts.
