Eupatorium hasslerianum

Eupatorium hasslerianum, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, blue-violet flower clusters that bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its attractive appearance makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
