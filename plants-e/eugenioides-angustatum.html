Eugenioides angustatum

Eugenioides angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenioid, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or pink flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.
