Ebnerella solisii

Ebnerella solisii, commonly known as the Solisii orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Ebnerella solisii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
