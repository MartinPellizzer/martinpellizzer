Erica heleogena

Erica heleogena, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica heleogena is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the unique biodiversity of the region.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
