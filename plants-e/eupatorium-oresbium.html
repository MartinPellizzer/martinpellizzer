Eupatorium oresbium

Eupatorium oresbium, commonly known as swamp milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to wetland habitats in North America.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Eupatorium oresbium plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and is a valuable plant for wildlife habitat.
