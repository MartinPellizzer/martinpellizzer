Eremostachys persimilis

Eremostachys persimilis, commonly known as the slender eremostachys, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and narrow, linear leaves adapted to conserve water. The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Eremostachys persimilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
