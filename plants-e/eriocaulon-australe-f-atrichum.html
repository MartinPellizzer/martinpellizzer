Eriocaulon australe f. atrichum

Eriocaulon australe f. atrichum, commonly known as the smooth-fruited flat sedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in areas with consistent moisture. Its leaves are usually smooth and lack the fine hairs found in other varieties of the species.

This subspecies is often found in subtropical and tropical regions, particularly in Australia and Southeast Asia.
