Escallonia pulverulenta var. glabra

Escallonia pulverulenta var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth escallonia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth to the touch, giving it its varietal name "glabra.". The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
