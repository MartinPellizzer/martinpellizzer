Echinocereus scheeri var. obscuriensis

Echinocereus scheeri var. obscuriensis, commonly known as the dark-stemmed hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically dark green to purplish in color, covered with spines and areoles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its dark foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
