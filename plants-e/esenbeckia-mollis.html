Esenbeckia mollis

Esenbeckia mollis, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often at the ends of its branches. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

Esenbeckia mollis is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
