Eupatorium oaxacanum

Eupatorium oaxacanum, commonly known as the Oaxaca eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
