Encyclia ekmanii

Encyclia ekmanii, commonly known as the Ekman's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high Andes of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive purple lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
