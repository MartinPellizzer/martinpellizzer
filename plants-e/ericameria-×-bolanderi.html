Ericameria × bolanderi

Ericameria × bolanderi, commonly known as the Bolander's goldenbush, is a hybrid shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Ericameria × bolanderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
