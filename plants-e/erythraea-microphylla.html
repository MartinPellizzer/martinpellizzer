Erythraea microphylla

Erythraea microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf erythraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense canopy that helps it thrive in arid conditions.

Erythraea microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
