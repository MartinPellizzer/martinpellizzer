Eupatorium lundellii

Eupatorium lundellii, commonly known as the Mexican blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as swamps and wetlands. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Eupatorium lundellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
