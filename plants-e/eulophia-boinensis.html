Eulophia boinensis

Eulophia boinensis, commonly known as the Bornean eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often near the base of trees or on rocky substrates. The plant produces compact, upright pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Eulophia boinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
