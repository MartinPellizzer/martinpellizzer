Epidendrum chloe

Epidendrum chloe, commonly known as the Blue Butterfly orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant blue and violet flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum chloe is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
