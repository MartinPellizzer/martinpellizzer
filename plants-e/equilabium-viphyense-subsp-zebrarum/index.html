Equilabium Viphyense Subsp. Zebrarum

Equilabium viphyense subsp. zebrarum is a rare and enigmatic plant species known for its striking striped foliage. Native to the remote highlands of Southeast Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of fascination among botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, the species remains poorly studied due to its limited distribution and elusive habitat. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this delicate and endangered flora from threats posed by habitat loss and climate change.
