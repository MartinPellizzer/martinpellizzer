Eriobotrya prinoides var. prinoides

Eriobotrya prinoides var. prinoides, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, edible fruits resembling pears.
