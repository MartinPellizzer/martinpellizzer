Eriocaulon transvaalicum subsp. dembianense

Eriocaulon transvaalicum subsp.

dembianense, commonly known as the Dembian flat sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, spike-like flowers.
