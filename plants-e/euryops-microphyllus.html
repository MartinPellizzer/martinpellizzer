Euryops microphyllus

Euryops microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaved torch bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in dense, spherical clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its compact growth habit and striking floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
