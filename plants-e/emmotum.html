Emmotum

Emmotum, commonly known as the emmotum plant, is a species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, thriving in humid climates with high rainfall. Emmotum produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
