Erythrospermum coronarium

Erythrospermum coronarium, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, with a woody stem and small, spiny leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color and long-lasting nature.
