Epacris miniata var. splendens

Epacris miniata var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
