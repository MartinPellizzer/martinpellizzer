Echeveria angustiflora

Echeveria angustiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaf echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
