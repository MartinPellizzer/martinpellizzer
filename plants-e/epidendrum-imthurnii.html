Epidendrum imthurnii

Epidendrum imthurnii, commonly known as the Imthurn's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs and has strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum imthurnii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
