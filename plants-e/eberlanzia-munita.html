Eberlanzia munita

Eberlanzia munita, commonly known as the Mexican starfruit, is a small evergreen tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by yellow, star-shaped fruits. The fruit is edible, with a sweet, juicy pulp similar to that of the common starfruit.
