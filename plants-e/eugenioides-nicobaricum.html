Eugenioides nicobaricum

Eugenioides nicobaricum, commonly known as the Nicobar laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. Its bark is thick and smooth, often exuding a mild aromatic scent.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its ecological significance in the region.
