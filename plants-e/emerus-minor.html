Emerus minor

Emerus minor, commonly known as the lesser emerus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
