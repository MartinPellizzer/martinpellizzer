Enkianthus ruber

Enkianthus ruber, commonly known as the red enkianthus, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a strong contrast to its floral display. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Enkianthus ruber is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and woodland settings.
