Epipactis Myodes

Epipactis myodes is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rocky crevices and forest floors. Known for its small, pale greenish-yellow flowers, this plant is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting Epipactis myodes is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile habitat.
