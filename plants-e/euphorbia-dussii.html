Euphorbia dussii

Euphorbia dussii, commonly known as the Desert Spurge, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its branching stems and small, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique flower-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Euphorbia dussii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional uses.
