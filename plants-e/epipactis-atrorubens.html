Epipactis atrorubens

Epipactis atrorubens, commonly known as the dark red helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it has a rhizomatous root system.

Epipactis atrorubens is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
