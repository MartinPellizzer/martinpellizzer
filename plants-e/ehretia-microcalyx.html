Ehretia microcalyx

Ehretia microcalyx, commonly known as the small-leaved ehretia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a faint fragrance. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
