Englerina longiflora

Englerina longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered engleria, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, elliptical seeds.
