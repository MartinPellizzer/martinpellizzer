Eriogonum Chrysocephalum Subsp. Mancum

Eriogonum chrysocephalum subsp. mancum is a striking member of the buckwheat family known for its vibrant yellow flowers. Found primarily in the western United States, this subspecies thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The plant's resilience to harsh conditions highlights its adaptability and ecological importance. Understanding its biology and habitat is key to its conservation and appreciation.
