Eugenia codyensis var. obovata

Eugenia codyensis var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate eugenia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
