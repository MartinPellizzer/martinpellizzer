Eutrema minutissimum

Eutrema minutissimum, commonly known as the dwarf sea rocket, is a small flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, clustered flowers and pungent odor. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in maritime climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
