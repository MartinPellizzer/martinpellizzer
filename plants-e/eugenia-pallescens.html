Eugenia pallescens

Eugenia pallescens, commonly known as the pale eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Eugenia pallescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
