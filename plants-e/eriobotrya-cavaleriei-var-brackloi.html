Eriobotrya cavaleriei var. brackloi

Eriobotrya cavaleriei var. brackloi, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and broad, ovate leaves. The tree produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers in early spring, followed by edible, sweet, yellowish fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and fruit production.
