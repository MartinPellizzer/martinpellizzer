Erophila verna var. boerhaavii

Erophila verna var. boerhaavii, commonly known as the early cow-wheat, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. It blooms early in the spring, producing clusters of yellowish-white flowers that attract pollinators.
