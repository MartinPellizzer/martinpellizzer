Eucalyptus bynoeana

Eucalyptus bynoeana, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Manna Gum, is a tall, slender tree native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often grey-green in color. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters along the branches. The tree produces small, woody capsules that contain aromatic, resinous seeds.

It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
