Elleanthus arpophyllostachys

Elleanthus arpophyllostachys, commonly known as the red-flowered bamboo, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and vibrant red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a deep green color that contrasts beautifully with the red bracts.

Elleanthus arpophyllostachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in containers.
