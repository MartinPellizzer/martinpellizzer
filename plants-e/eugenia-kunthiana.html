Eugenia kunthiana

Eugenia kunthiana, commonly known as the confetti bush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a decorative appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Eugenia kunthiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
