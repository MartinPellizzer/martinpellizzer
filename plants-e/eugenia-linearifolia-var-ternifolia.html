Eugenia linearifolia var. ternifolia

Eugenia linearifolia var. ternifolia, commonly known as the three-leaf eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in threes, giving it its common name. It produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that is typically red when mature.
