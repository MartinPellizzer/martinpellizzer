Elsholtzia splendens var. miyasiroana

Elsholtzia splendens var. miyasiroana, commonly known as the Yunnan germander, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and delicate floral display.
