Erica trimera subsp. keniensis

Erica trimera subsp.

keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan heath, is a shrub native to the highland regions of Kenya. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many evergreen shrubs and small trees.

The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth and small, leathery leaves. It produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers during the dry season, which are attractive to pollinators.

This subspecies is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.
