Eleutherocarpum escalloniifolium

Eleutherocarpum escalloniifolium, commonly known as the escalloniifolium horsegram, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods containing small, edible seeds that are used in traditional cuisines.

It is also valued for its potential medicinal properties, though further research is needed to confirm its therapeutic benefits.
