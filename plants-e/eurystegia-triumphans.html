Eurystegia triumphans

Eurystegia triumphans, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
