Eranthemum diantherum

Eranthemum diantherum, commonly known as the double-flowered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with two rows of petals, giving it the "double-flowered" appearance.
