Eupatorium Ligustrinum F. Weinmannianum

Blue mistflower, scientifically known as Eupatorium ligustrinum f. weinmannianum, is a striking perennial plant native to the southeastern United States. It is prized for its delicate, bluish-white flowers that create a mist-like appearance, hence its common name. This plant thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its unique foliage and blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value. Despite its ornamental appeal, it is also an important plant for pollinators and can support a variety of wildlife.
