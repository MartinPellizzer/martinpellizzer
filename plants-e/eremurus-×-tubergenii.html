Eremurus × tubergenii

Eremurus × tubergenii, commonly known as the hybrid foxtail lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Eremurus, which includes several species of tall, spiky flowering plants. The hybrid is characterized by its elongated, branched flower spikes that bear numerous small, tubular flowers. These flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a dramatic display of yellow to orange hues.

The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
