Erysimum comperianum

Erysimum comperianum, commonly known as the Comper's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its structure is compact with hairy stems and leaves, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
