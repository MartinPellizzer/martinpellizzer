Epidendrum ramirezii

Epidendrum ramirezii, commonly known as the Ramirez's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments within pine-oak forests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with numerous slender, erect pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with red or purple markings, and they are arranged in racemose inflorescences.

Epidendrum ramirezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
