Eleocharis macrostylis

Eleocharis macrostylis, commonly known as the tall spike rush, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence that rises on long, slender stems. The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are small and clustered in spikelets.

Eleocharis macrostylis plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
