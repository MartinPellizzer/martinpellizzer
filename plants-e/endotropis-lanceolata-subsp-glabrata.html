Endotropis lanceolata subsp. glabrata

Endotropis lanceolata subsp.

glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf endotropis, is a member of the Malvaceae family. It is characterized by its lanceolate, glabrous leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem.

It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
