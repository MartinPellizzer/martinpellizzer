Echeveria elegans var. simulans

Echeveria elegans var. simulans, commonly known as the "Pineapple Plant," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
