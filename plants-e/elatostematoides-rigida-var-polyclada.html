Elatostematoides rigida var. polyclada

Elatostematoides rigida var. polyclada, commonly known as the polyclad elatostema, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, allowing it to store water efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.
