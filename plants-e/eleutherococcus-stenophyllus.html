Eleutherococcus stenophyllus

Eleutherococcus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eleuthero, is a deciduous shrub native to the temperate regions of East Asia.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in umbel-like clusters, followed by red berries that are rich in antioxidants. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
