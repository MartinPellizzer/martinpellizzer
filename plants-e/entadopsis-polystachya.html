Entadopsis polystachya

Entadopsis polystachya, commonly known as the red-flowered entadopsis, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
