Eragrostis trichodes

Eragrostis trichodes, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in prairie and steppe ecosystems. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting wildlife.

Its seeds are a valuable food source for birds and small mammals.
