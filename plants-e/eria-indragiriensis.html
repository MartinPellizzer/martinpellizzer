Eria indragiriensis

Eria indragiriensis, commonly known as the Indragiri orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and green.

Eria indragiriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
