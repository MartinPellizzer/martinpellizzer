Eugenia lutescens

Eugenia lutescens, commonly known as the yellow eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, smooth, yellow drupe that ripens in late summer.
