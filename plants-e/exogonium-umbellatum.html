Exogonium umbellatum

Exogonium umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and a striking inflorescence that resembles an umbrella, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

The botanical features of Exogonium umbellatum make it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
