Elaeodendron angustifolium

Elaeodendron angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elaeodendron, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Elaeodendron angustifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
