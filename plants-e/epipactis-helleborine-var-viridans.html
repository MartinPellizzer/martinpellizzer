Epipactis helleborine var. viridans

Epipactis helleborine var. viridans, commonly known as the green helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is usually darker in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Epipactis helleborine var. viridans is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
