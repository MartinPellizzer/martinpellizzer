Erythrophleum africanum var. stenocarpum

Erythrophleum africanum var. stenocarpum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive red flowers and compound leaves. The tree produces woody pods containing flat, fibrous seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for reforestation and landscaping in warm climates.
