Epidendrum oraion

Epidendrum oraion, commonly known as the Orchid of the Moon, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and purple. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum oraion is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
