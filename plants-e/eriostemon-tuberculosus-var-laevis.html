Eriostemon tuberculosus var. laevis

Eriostemon tuberculosus var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved eriostemon, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features small, tuberculate (knob-like) leaves that are smooth and glabrous on the upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
