Elatostema tenuicaudatum var. tenuicaudatum

Elatostema tenuicaudatum var. tenuicaudatum, commonly known as the slender-tailed elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, arranged in clusters at the base of the stems.

It has a fibrous root system and is often found in wetland or riparian habitats.
