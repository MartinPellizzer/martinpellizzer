Elaeocarpus clementis var. borneensis

Elaeocarpus clementis var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, white to pink flowers. The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitat.

It is also sometimes cultivated for its timber and medicinal properties.
