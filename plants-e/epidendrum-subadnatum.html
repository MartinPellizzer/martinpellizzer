Epidendrum subadnatum

Epidendrum subadnatum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendant inflorescences with showy, velvety flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum subadnatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
