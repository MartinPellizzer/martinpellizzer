Epidendrum punctulatum

Epidendrum punctulatum, commonly known as the speckled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or red with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant has long, arching stems that often bear multiple flowering shoots. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Epidendrum punctulatum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.
