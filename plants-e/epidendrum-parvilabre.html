Epidendrum parvilabre

Epidendrum parvilabre, commonly known as the small-lipped epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically features broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum parvilabre is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
