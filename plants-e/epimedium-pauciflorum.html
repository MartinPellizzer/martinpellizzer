Epimedium pauciflorum

Epimedium pauciflorum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are typically clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, upright stalks. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland habitats.

Epimedium pauciflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and hardy nature.
