Encyclia amanda

Encyclia amanda, commonly known as Amanda's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Encyclia amanda is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
