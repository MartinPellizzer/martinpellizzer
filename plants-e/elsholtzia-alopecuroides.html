Elsholtzia alopecuroides

Elsholtzia alopecuroides, commonly known as silverleaf elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden environments.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a desirable addition to native plant gardens.
