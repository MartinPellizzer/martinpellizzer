Erica vagans var. rubra

Erica vagans var. rubra, commonly known as the red heather, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. Its foliage consists of narrow, evergreen leaves that are often covered in a fine, white bloom.

Erica vagans var. rubra thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.
