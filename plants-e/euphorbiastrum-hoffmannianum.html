Euphorbiastrum hoffmannianum

Euphorbiastrum hoffmannianum, commonly known as the Hoffmann's milkweed, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
