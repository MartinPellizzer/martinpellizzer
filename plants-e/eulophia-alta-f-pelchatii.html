Eulophia alta f. pelchatii

Eulophia alta f. pelchatii, commonly known as the Pelchat's tall eulophia, is a striking orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged along the stem, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
