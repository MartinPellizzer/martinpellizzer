Eria Suborbicularis

Eria suborbicularis is a unique orchid species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, orbicular-shaped leaves. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions. Its flowers, though small, add a delicate beauty to any collection. Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized addition for experienced orchid growers.
