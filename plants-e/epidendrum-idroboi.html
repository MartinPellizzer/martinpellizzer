Epidendrum idroboi

Epidendrum idroboi, commonly known as the Idrobo's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Andes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are usually pale green to yellowish in color.

Epidendrum idroboi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
