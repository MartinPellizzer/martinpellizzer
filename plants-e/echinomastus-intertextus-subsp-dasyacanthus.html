Echinomastus intertextus subsp. dasyacanthus

Echinomastus intertextus subsp.

dasyacanthus, commonly known as the spiny cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny tubercles.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
