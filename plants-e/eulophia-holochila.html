Eulophia holochila

Eulophia holochila, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, arranged along the stem.

Eulophia holochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
