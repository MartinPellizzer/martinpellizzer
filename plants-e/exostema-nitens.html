Exostema nitens

Exostema nitens, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, silver-green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its botanical features include long, narrow leaves and small, clustered inflorescences with white or pale yellow blooms.

Exostema nitens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
