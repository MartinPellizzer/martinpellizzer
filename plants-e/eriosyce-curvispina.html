Eriosyce curvispina

Eriosyce curvispina, commonly known as the curved-spined eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that are typically greenish-gray in color. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by pale yellow fruits. Eriosyce curvispina is adapted to grow in arid and semi-arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

It is often found in rocky soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in full sun.
