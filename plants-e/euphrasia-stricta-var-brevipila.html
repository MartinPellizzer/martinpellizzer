Euphrasia stricta var. brevipila

Euphrasia stricta var. brevipila, commonly known as the short-stemmed eyebright, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
