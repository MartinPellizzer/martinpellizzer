Eugenioides prionophyllum

Eugenioides prionophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fruit is a small, dark purple drupe that contains a single seed.
