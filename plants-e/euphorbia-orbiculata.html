Euphorbia orbiculata

Euphorbia orbiculata, commonly known as the orbicular spurges, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem with a distinctive orbicular shape, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters. The plant has a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

Euphorbia orbiculata is also known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun.
