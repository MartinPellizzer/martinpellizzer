Ehretia goetzei

Ehretia goetzei, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. The bark and leaves of Ehretia goetzei have been used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory and antifungal effects.
