Erica salteri

Erica salteri, commonly known as the Salter's heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other shrubs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica salteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
