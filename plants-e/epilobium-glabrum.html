Epilobium glabrum

Epilobium glabrum, commonly known as smooth willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are smooth and hairless. Its showy, pink to purple flowers appear in spikes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Epilobium glabrum is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist habitats.
