Echinocereus viereckii subsp. huastecensis

Echinocereus viereckii subsp.

huastecensis, commonly known as the Huastec Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico. It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny, ribbed stems and clusters of yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its subspecies status highlights its distinct morphological features compared to other Echinocereus species.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
