Eria oberoniiflora

Eria oberoniiflora, commonly known as the Oberon's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
