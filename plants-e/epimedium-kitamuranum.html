Epimedium kitamuranum

Epimedium kitamuranum, commonly known as Japanese barbatius, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside, often adorned with red or purple veins. Its flowers, which are typically yellow or orange, appear in early spring on short pedicels.

Epimedium kitamuranum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded woodland environments.
