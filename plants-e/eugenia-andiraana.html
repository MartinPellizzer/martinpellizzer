Eugenia andiraana

Eugenia andiraana, commonly known as the Andiraan ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes many aromatic and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its hard, heavy wood is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay.

The tree also produces small, edible berries that are consumed by various wildlife species.
