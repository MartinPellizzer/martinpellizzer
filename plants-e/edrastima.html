Edrastima

Edrastima, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive silver-gray foliage that reflects light, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Edrastima is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
