Eulophia ramosa

Eulophia ramosa, commonly known as the branched eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia ramosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
