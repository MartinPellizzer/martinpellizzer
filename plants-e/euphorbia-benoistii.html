Euphorbia benoistii

Euphorbia benoistii, commonly known as the Benoist's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in soft, greenish-gray hairs and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
