Euphorbia terracina var. modesta

Euphorbia terracina var. modesta, commonly known as the modest spurge, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branched stems covered in a waxy, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green花朵 (flowers) surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. Its sap is milky and toxic, containing compounds that can cause skin irritation.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
