Eremostachys thyrsiflora

Eremostachys thyrsiflora, commonly known as the desert bellflower, is a perennial herb native to arid regions of North America.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink.
