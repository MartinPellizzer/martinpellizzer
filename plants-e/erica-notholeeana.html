Erica notholeeana

Erica notholeeana, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in winter, adding ornamental value to gardens. Erica notholeeana prefers well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
