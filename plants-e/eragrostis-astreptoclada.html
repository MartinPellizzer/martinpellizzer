Eragrostis astreptoclada

Eragrostis astreptoclada, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
