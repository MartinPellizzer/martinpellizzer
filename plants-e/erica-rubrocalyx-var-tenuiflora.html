Erica rubrocalyx var. tenuiflora

Erica rubrocalyx var. tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered heath, is a dwarf shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically red or pink in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy environments.
