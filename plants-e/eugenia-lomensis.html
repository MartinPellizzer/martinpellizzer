Eugenia lomensis

Eugenia lomensis, commonly known as the Lomentosum Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

Eugenia lomensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
