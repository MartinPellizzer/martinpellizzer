Eugenia fraseri

Eugenia fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a specimen plant.
