Eugenia gracilenta

Eugenia gracilenta, commonly known as the slender eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, bright red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

The berries are often used in culinary applications, adding a unique flavor to dishes and beverages.
