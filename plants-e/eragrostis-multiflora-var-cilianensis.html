Eragrostis multiflora var. cilianensis

Eragrostis multiflora var. cilianensis, commonly known as Cilian grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and typically have a bluish-green coloration. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This plant is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
