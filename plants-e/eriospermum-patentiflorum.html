Eriospermum patentiflorum

Eriospermum patentiflorum, commonly known as the broad-flowered eriosperm, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features distinctive, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with five petals that open in a radial pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a notable species in the Aizoaceae family.
