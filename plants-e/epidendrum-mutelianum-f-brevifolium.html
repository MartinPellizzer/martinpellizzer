Epidendrum mutelianum f. brevifolium

Epidendrum mutelianum f. brevifolium, commonly known as the compact-leaf epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its short, broad leaves and compact growth habit, making it an attractive choice for indoor cultivation. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
