Eragrostis imberbis

Eragrostis imberbis, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It is often used for erosion control and as a forage plant for livestock.
