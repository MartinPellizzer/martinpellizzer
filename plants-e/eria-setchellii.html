Eria setchellii

Eria setchellii, commonly known as the Setchell's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, branching pseudobulbs and elongated, linear leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
