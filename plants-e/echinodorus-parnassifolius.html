Echinodorus parnassifolius

Echinodorus parnassifolius, commonly known as the Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, sword-like leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, providing excellent coverage in freshwater aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular nutrient supplementation to maintain its vibrant green color. It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help oxygenate the water.

Echinodorus parnassifolius is also known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a favorite among aquarium enthusiasts.
