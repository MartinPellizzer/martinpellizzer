Eriocaulon oinereum

Eriocaulon oinereum, commonly known as the silver spike sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with slender, cylindrical stems. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist soils. Its flowering spikes are usually pale green or silver, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Eriocaulon oinereum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities in wetland gardens.
