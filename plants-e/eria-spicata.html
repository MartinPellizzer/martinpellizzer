Eria spicata

Eria spicata, commonly known as the spike eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Eria spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
