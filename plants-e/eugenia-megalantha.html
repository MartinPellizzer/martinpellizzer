Eugenia megalantha

Eugenia megalantha, commonly known as the large-leaved eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that changes from green to red as it matures.

Eugenia megalantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
