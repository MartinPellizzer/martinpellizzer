Ecbolium pectorale

Ecbolium pectorale, commonly known as the heartleaf vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is a climbing vine that produces elongated, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Ecbolium pectorale has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
