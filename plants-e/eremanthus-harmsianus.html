Eremanthus harmsianus

Eremanthus harmsianus, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a snow-like appearance. It is typically found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Eremanthus harmsianus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in local traditional medicine.
