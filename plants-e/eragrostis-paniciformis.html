Eragrostis paniciformis

Eragrostis paniciformis, commonly known as panic grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial species native to various regions of Africa, including South Africa, where it thrives in dry, open habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of small spikelets, which produce tiny grains of seeds.

Eragrostis paniciformis is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
