Euphorbia nubigena var. rutilans

Euphorbia nubigena var. rutilans, commonly known as the Cloudy Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and striking yellowish-orange stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
