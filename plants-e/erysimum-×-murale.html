Erysimum × murale

Erysimum × murale, commonly known as wallflower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Erysimum × murale thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
