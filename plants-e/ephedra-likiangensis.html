Ephedra likiangensis

Ephedra likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan ephedra, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Ephedraceae and is characterized by its jointed stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant contains alkaloids such as ephedrine, which have been historically used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing at high elevations.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been the subject of both scientific research and conservation efforts.
