Epilobium × jinshaense

Epilobium × jinshaense, commonly known as the Jinsha hybrid willow herb, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the genus Epilobium, which includes species commonly referred to as willow herbs or everlastings. The plant is characterized by its erect or ascending stems, often hairy, and its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and typically have a rough texture.

Epilobium × jinshaense is often found in moist, open habitats such as riverbanks and meadows.
