Epilobium billardiereanum f. simplex

Epilobium billardiereanum f. simplex, commonly known as the simple willowherb, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, ascending stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late winter to early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, swampy areas, and along waterways.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland restoration efforts.
