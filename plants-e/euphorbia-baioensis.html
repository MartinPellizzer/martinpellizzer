Euphorbia baioensis

Euphorbia baioensis, commonly known as the Baio's spurge, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes a wide variety of plants characterized by their milky sap and unique inflorescences. The plant typically features succulent stems and leaves, adapted to survive in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are small and clustered in a structure called a cyathium, which is a defining feature of the genus Euphorbia.
