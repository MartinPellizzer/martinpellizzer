Encyclia schmidtii

Encyclia schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, often epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen foliage.

Encyclia schmidtii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
