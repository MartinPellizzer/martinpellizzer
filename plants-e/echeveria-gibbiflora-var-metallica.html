Echeveria gibbiflora var. metallica

Echeveria gibbiflora var. metallica, commonly known as the Silver Dust Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features dense, rosette-shaped foliage covered in a fine, silvery coating that gives it a metallic appearance. The leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, and are typically blue-gray in color. It produces striking pink or purple flowers during the spring or summer months.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
