Elymus Fibrosus

Elymus fibrosus is a hardy grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and dunes. This plant is known for its deep root system, which helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion. Its fibrous structure gives it a unique appearance, with long, narrow leaves and dense tufts of seed heads. Elymus fibrosus plays an important role in ecological restoration and is valued for its resilience in challenging environments.
