Excoecaria marginata var. intermedia

Excoecaria marginata var. intermedia, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, large, five-petaled flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with a distinctive orange or yellow throat. The plant has smooth, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow margins. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
