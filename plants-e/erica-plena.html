Erica plena

Erica plena, commonly known as the common heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Erica plena thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
