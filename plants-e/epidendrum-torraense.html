Epidendrum torraense

Epidendrum torraense, commonly known as the Torraense orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and bright indirect light.

Epidendrum torraense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
