Epidendrum culmiforme

Epidendrum culmiforme, commonly known as the cane Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically broad and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
