Escallonia multiflora

Escallonia multiflora, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year, providing dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Escallonia multiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
