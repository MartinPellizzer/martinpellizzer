Elymus mollis var. coreensis

Elymus mollis var. coreensis, commonly known as Corean soft wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and soft, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
