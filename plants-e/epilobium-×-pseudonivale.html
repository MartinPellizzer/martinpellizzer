Epilobium × pseudonivale

Epilobium × pseudonivale, commonly known as the silverweed or snowplant, is a hybrid species within the evening primrose family (Onagraceae).

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late summer. Epilobium × pseudonivale is typically found in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or streamside habitats.

It is a perennial herb that is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
