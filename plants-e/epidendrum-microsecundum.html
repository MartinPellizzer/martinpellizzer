Epidendrum microsecundum

Epidendrum microsecundum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
