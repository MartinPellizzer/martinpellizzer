Eria lineata

Eria lineata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a yellowish or greenish tinge. Eria lineata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
