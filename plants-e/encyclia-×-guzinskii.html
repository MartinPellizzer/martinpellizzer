Encyclia × guzinskii

Encyclia × guzinskii, commonly known as the Guzinskii's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow to orange petals and a distinctive lip. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
