Euphorbia nakaiana

Euphorbia nakaiana, commonly known as the Nakaiana spurges, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
