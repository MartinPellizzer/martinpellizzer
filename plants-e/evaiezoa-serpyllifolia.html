Evaiezoa serpyllifolia

Evaiezoa serpyllifolia, commonly known as common sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Evaiezoa serpyllifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging environments.
