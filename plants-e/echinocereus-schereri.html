Echinocereus schereri

Echinocereus schereri, commonly known as the Schereri cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in spiny areoles, and it produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its succulent stems and minimizing water loss through its thick, waxy cuticle. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Echinocereus schereri plays a role in its native ecosystem by providing food and shelter for various desert animals.
