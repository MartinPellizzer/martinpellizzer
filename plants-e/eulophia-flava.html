Eulophia flava

Eulophia flava, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive lip, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia flava is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and gardens.
