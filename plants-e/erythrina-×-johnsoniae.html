Erythrina × johnsoniae

Erythrina × johnsoniae, commonly known as the Johnson's coral tree, is a hybrid legume native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking red, flame-like flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to landscapes. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental species.

Erythrina × johnsoniae is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
