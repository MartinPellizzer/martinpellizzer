Eranthemum aciculare

Eranthemum aciculare, commonly known as the needleleaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Eranthemum aciculare is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
