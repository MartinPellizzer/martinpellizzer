Elaphoglossum ornatiforme

Elaphoglossum ornatiforme, commonly known as the ornate holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a distinctive, ornate pattern of sori along the margins. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense, low-growing mats.

The plant's intricate leaf structure and seasonal variability make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
