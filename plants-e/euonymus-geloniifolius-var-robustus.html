Euonymus geloniifolius var. robustus

Euonymus geloniifolius var. robustus, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that persist into winter. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The leaves are typically alternate and have a waxy texture, contributing to its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
