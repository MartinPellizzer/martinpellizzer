Epilobium alpinum var. gavei

Epilobium alpinum var. gavei, commonly known as the Alpine willow-herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant features erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive serrated margin. Its flowers are showy, with pink to purple petals, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators in high-altitude environments.
