Eugenia fluviatilis

Eugenia fluviatilis, commonly known as the river guava, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round, yellow or orange berry with a sweet, acidic flavor.

Eugenia fluviatilis is often found near water bodies and is valued for its ornamental and edible qualities.
