Eugenia mimica

Eugenia mimica, commonly known as the mimic eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a fragrant aroma, and it produces small, edible berries.

Eugenia mimica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
