Epidendrum jacarandichromum

Epidendrum jacarandichromum, commonly known as the Jacaranda Chromatic Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features strikingly colored flowers that range from yellow to red, with a distinctive violet throat. The plant has long, arching stems and produces numerous small, clustered blooms. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, with a smooth, waxy surface.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific humidity and light requirements.
