Eulophia streptopetala var. streptopetala

Eulophia streptopetala var. streptopetala, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that often display a zigzag pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is also considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
