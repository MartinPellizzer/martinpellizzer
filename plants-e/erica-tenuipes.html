Erica tenuipes

Erica tenuipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Erica tenuipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
