Euphorbiopsis lucidissima

Euphorbiopsis lucidissima, commonly known as the glossy euphorbia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
