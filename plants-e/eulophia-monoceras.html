Eulophia monoceras

Eulophia monoceras, commonly known as the single horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and several elongated pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive, elongated spur.

Eulophia monoceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
