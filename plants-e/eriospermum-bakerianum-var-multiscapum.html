Eriospermum bakerianum var. multiscapum

Eriospermum bakerianum var. multiscapum, commonly known as the many-stemmed eriosperm, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, grey-green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant ground cover.
