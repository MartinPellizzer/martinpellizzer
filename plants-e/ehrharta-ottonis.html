Ehrharta ottonis

Ehrharta ottonis, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It features slender, silver-gray blades with a fine texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to brownish spikelets that are arranged in dense clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Ehrharta ottonis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its elegant, feathery foliage.
