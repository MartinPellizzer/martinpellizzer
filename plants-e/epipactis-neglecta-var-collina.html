Epipactis neglecta var. collina

Epipactis neglecta var. collina, commonly known as the Alpine Helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow lip. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas. It has a complex root system that helps it anchor in nutrient-poor soils.

The orchid is pollinated by specific insects, showcasing a specialized ecological relationship.
