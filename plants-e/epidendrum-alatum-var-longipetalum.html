Epidendrum alatum var. longipetalum

Epidendrum alatum var. longipetalum, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features broad, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, arranged in racemes that can be quite elongated. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
