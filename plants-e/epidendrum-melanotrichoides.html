Epidendrum melanotrichoides

Epidendrum melanotrichoides, commonly known as the black-haired epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
