Epidendrum alopecurum

Epidendrum alopecurum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name, with leaves that are typically dark green and slightly fleshy. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum alopecurum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
