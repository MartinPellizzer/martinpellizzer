Eumachia sciadephora var. sciadephora

Eumachia sciadephora var. sciadephora, commonly known as the shade-leaf euphorbia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, often thorny foliage and unique floral structures. The leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a whorled pattern, providing dense shade. Its flowers are small and clustered, often yellow or greenish-yellow, and are borne in complex inflorescences.

The plant is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
