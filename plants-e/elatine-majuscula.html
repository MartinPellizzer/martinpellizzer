Elatine majuscula

Elatine majuscula, commonly known as the greater water stitchwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, branched stems that are often submerged, with small, opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually pink or white, with four petals.

Elatine majuscula is known for its ability to thrive in moist environments and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
