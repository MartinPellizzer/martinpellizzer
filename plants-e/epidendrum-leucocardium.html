Epidendrum leucocardium

Epidendrum leucocardium, commonly known as the white-heart epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum leucocardium is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
