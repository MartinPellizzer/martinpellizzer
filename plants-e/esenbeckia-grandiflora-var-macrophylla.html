Esenbeckia grandiflora var. macrophylla

Esenbeckia grandiflora var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved esenbeckia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
