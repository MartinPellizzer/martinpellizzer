Erigeron ibarii

Erigeron ibarii, commonly known as the Ibari fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. Erigeron ibarii is often found in open woodlands, grasslands, and along coastal areas.
