Erica astroites

Erica astroites, commonly known as the star heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica astroites typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
