Eulophia orthoplectra var. schweinfurthii

Eulophia orthoplectra var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in a raceme. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for greenhouse or terrarium cultivation.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
