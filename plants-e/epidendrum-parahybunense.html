Epidendrum parahybunense

Epidendrum parahybunense, commonly known as the Parahyba orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored flowers that are typically yellow or orange. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Epidendrum parahybunense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
