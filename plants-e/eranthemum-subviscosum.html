Eranthemum subviscosum

Eranthemum subviscosum, commonly known as the sticky eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

Eranthemum subviscosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
