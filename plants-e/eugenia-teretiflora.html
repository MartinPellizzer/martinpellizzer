Eugenia teretiflora

Eugenia teretiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or purple when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

Eugenia teretiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
