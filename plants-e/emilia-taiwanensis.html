Emilia taiwanensis

Emilia taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan emilia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its botanical features make it an attractive and ecologically significant species in its native environment.
