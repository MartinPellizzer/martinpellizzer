Echinodorus guayanensis

Echinodorus guayanensis, commonly known as the Guayana arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. This plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in the Amazon basin. Echinodorus guayanensis is a popular choice for aquariums due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.

It plays a role in providing habitat and oxygenation in its natural aquatic ecosystems.
