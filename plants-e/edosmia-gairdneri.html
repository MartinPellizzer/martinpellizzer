Edosmia gairdneri

Edosmia gairdneri, commonly known as the Gairdner's lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces showy blooms during the spring and early summer months.
