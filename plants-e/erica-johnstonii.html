Erica johnstonii

Erica johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's heath, is a species of shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, evergreen leaves. It produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months.

Erica johnstonii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
