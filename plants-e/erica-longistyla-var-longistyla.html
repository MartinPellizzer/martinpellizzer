Erica longistyla var. longistyla

Erica longistyla var. longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled heath, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes shrubs and evergreen plants adapted to acidic soils. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, thriving in well-drained, sandy soils.

Erica longistyla var. longistyla is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive floral display.
