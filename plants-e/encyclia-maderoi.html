Encyclia maderoi

Encyclia maderoi, commonly known as the Mader's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth in harsh, cold environments. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow color and a prominent, intricate lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat.

Encyclia maderoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
