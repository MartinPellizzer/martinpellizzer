Eragrostis aulacosperma

Eragrostis aulacosperma, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its drooping, arching inflorescences that give it a weeping appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its unique visual effect.
