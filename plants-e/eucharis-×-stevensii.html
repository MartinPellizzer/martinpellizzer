Eucharis × stevensii

Eucharis × stevensii, commonly known as the Amazon lily or stevensian Amazon lily, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family (Araceae) and is characterized by its striking, large, white or pale pink flowers. The plant features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or elliptical. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eucharis × stevensii is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
