Euphrasia × trikoviana

Euphrasia × trikoviana, commonly known as the Trikovian eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with prominent dark markings. The plant produces slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Native to the Balkan region, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners interested in cultivating native wildflowers.
