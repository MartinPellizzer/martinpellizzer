Elaeocarpus gaussenii

Elaeocarpus gaussenii, commonly known as the Gaussen's elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers and elliptical fruits. The plant typically grows in lowland forests and is known for its hardy, evergreen foliage. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and the fruits are woody, berry-like structures that contain a single seed.
