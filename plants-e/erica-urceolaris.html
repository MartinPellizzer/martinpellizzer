Erica urceolaris

Erica urceolaris, commonly known as the urn heath, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family, Ericaceae.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming in spring. Erica urceolaris is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter, with dense, leathery leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in acidic, nutrient-poor conditions.
