Epilobium billardiereanum

Epilobium billardiereanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, stream banks, and swampy areas. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are linear to lanceolate in shape. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red, with five petals.

The species is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and waterlogged soils.
