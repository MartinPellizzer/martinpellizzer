Eugenia odorata

Eugenia odorata, commonly known as the fragrant eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The fruit, which is small and berry-like, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for use in traditional medicine.
