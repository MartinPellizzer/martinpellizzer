Entandrophragma speciosum

Entandrophragma speciosum, commonly known as the African teak or African sandalwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Bombacaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
