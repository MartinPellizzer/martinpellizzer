Epidendrum porphyrospilum

Epidendrum porphyrospilum, commonly known as the flame-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with vibrant red and orange hues, often featuring a distinctive veined pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, providing a lush green contrast to its colorful blooms.

Epidendrum porphyrospilum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
