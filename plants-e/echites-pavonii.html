Echites pavonii

Echites pavonii, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, red-orange flowers resembling spiders, which bloom in late spring. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that emerge from a central bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable addition to many gardens.
