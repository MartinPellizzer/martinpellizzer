Emmotum glabrum

Emmotum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth emmotum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Emmotum glabrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
