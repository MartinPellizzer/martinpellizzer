Euphrasia × tenuis var. vestita

Euphrasia × tenuis var. vestita, commonly known as the slender eyebright, is a hybrid species within the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
