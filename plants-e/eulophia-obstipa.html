Eulophia obstipa

Eulophia obstipa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Eulophia obstipa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
