Epidendrum bakrense

Epidendrum bakrense, commonly known as the Bakrense Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum bakrense is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
