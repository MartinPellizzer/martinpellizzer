Erysimum brevistylum

Erysimum brevistylum, commonly known as the narrowleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
