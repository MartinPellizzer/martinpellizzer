Erica stelleriana

Erica stelleriana, commonly known as the Steller's heath, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly known as the heath or heather family. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica stelleriana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy coastal habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand coastal conditions.
