Eudolon

Eudolon, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces bulbous underground structures that store nutrients. Eudolon is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blossoms and unique appearance.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
