Erica multiflora

Erica multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or acidic soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in spring and summer. Erica multiflora is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

It is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
