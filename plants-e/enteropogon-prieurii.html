Enteropogon prieurii

Enteropogon prieurii, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense, fluffy inflorescences that range in color from red to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly and form dense stands.

Enteropogon prieurii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and is also valued for its role in traditional medicine.
