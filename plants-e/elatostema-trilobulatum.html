Elatostema trilobulatum

Elatostema trilobulatum, commonly known as the three-lobed snowflake, is a small herbaceous plant native to eastern Australia.

It features a dense rosette of fleshy, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rock crevices and forest floors.

Its unique leaf structure and flowering habit make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
