Elaeocarpus holosericeus

Elaeocarpus holosericeus, commonly known as the silky elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by distinctive, woody fruits that are often covered in a silky, hairy coating.

Its foliage and unique fruit structure make it an important species in its native ecosystems, though it is not widely cultivated for commercial purposes.
