Epidendrum praecox

Epidendrum praecox, commonly known as the Early Blooming Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the early part of the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum praecox is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
