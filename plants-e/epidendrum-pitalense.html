Epidendrum pitalense

Epidendrum pitalense, commonly known as the Pitalense orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features pseudobulbs that support its leaves and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple or red markings.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
