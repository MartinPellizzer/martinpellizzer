Eugenia bulusanensis

Eugenia bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan eggplant, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
