Eugenia herrerae

Eugenia herrerae, commonly known as the Herrera's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit, which is a small, smooth berry, is edible and has a sweet, acidic flavor.
