Epidendrum ramosum var. lanceolatum

Epidendrum ramosum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
