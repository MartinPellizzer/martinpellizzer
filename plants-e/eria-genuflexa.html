Eria genuflexa

Eria genuflexa, commonly known as the bent knee orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Eria genuflexa is valued for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
