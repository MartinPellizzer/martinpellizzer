Euphorbia deccanensis var. deccanensis

Euphorbia deccanensis var. deccanensis, commonly known as the Deccan spurge, is a succulent shrub native to the Indian subcontinent.

It features a thick, branched stem with a waxy, pale green or grayish appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by colorful bracts, typically in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or dry soils.
