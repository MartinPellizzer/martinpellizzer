Elongatia excelsa

Elongatia excelsa, commonly known as the giant bamboo, is a tall, fast-growing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family and can reach heights of over 30 meters. Its stems are hollow and segmented, with a smooth, greenish-brown surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically wind-pollinated.

It is valued for its structural strength and is often used in construction and traditional crafts.
