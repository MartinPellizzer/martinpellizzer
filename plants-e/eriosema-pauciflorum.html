Eriosema pauciflorum

Eriosema pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered eriosema, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which are often covered in a fine, woolly texture. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
