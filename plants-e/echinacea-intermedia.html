Echinacea intermedia

Echinacea intermedia, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with purple petals and a distinctive yellow-orange central cone. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves and grows up to 3 feet tall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Echinacea intermedia is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for supporting immune health.
