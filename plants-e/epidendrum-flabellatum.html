Epidendrum flabellatum

Epidendrum flabellatum, commonly known as the fan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fan-shaped flowers that can be pink, red, or yellow, depending on the variety. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with long, arching stems and broad, elliptical leaves. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum flabellatum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
