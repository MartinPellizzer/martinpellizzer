Erica filifolia

Erica filifolia, commonly known as the silky heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth and narrow, silvery-gray leaves. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring.

Erica filifolia thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
