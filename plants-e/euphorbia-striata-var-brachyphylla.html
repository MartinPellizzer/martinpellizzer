Euphorbia striata var. brachyphylla

Euphorbia striata var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the shortleaf spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with fleshy, ribbed stems that are often covered in a powdery white coating. The leaves are small, narrow, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
