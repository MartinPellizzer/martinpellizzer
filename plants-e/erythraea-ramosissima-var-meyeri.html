Erythraea ramosissima var. meyeri

Erythraea ramosissima var. meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's erythraea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, branched growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and seasonal fruit display.
