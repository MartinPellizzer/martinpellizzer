Eurycaulis cumulatus

Eurycaulis cumulatus, commonly known as the cloud-shaped fern, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive, fluffy, and cloud-like appearance.

The plant features delicate, feathery fronds that grow in a compact, clustered formation, giving it its common name. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are typically located on the underside of the fronds.
