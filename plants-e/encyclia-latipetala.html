Encyclia latipetala

Encyclia latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth during periods of dormancy. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with broad, strap-like petals and a prominent lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia latipetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
