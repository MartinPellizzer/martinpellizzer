Elytrigia littoralis

Elytrigia littoralis, commonly known as sea barley, is a grass species native to coastal regions.

It thrives in saline and sandy environments, often found along shorelines and dunes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for various coastal organisms.
