Euphorbia sparrmanni

Euphorbia sparrmanni, commonly known as the Sparrmann's euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves and stems. It produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin.

Euphorbia sparrmanni is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
