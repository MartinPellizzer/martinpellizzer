Epidendrum aura-usecheae

Epidendrum aura-usecheae, commonly known as the Useche's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and covered with a fine layer of white pubescence.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under appropriate conditions.
