Erodium cicutarium var. album

Erodium cicutarium var. album, commonly known as the white bedstraw, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides, in fields, and in waste areas.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open spaces.
