Eugenia tenuissima

Eugenia tenuissima, commonly known as the slender eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenia tenuissima is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
