Encyclia betancourtiana

Encyclia betancourtiana, commonly known as the Betancourt's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
