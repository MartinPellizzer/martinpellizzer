Eremophila elderi

Eremophila elderi, commonly known as the Grey Mallet, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its grey-green foliage and distinctive, mallet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to red. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand prolonged droughts. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Eremophila elderi is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
