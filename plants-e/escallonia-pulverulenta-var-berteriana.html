Escallonia pulverulenta var. berteriana

Escallonia pulverulenta var. berteriana, commonly known as the Berterian firebush, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features bright red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and hardiness make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
