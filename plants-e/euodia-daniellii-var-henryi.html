Euodia daniellii var. henryi

Euodia daniellii var. henryi, commonly known as Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its spiny branches and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Euodia genus.
