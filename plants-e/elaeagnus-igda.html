Elaeagnus igda

Elaeagnus igda, commonly known as silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by red berries. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Elaeagnus igda is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
