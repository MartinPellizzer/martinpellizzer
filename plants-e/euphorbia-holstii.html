Euphorbia holstii

Euphorbia holstii, commonly known as the Holst's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a thick, branching stem covered in small, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique flower-like structures. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
