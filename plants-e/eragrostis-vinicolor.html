Eragrostis vinicolor

Eragrostis vinicolor, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet to purple inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Eragrostis vinicolor is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
