Elatostema brachyurum

Elatostema brachyurum, commonly known as the small-leaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters at the base of the plant.

Elatostema brachyurum is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its preference for well-drained soils.
