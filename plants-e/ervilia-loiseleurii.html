Ervilia loiseleurii

Ervilia loiseleurii, commonly known as the Loiseleur's erica, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family (Ericaceae).

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Western Cape region, where it grows in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in spring. It is a low-growing shrub with evergreen foliage, adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat.

Ervilia loiseleurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
