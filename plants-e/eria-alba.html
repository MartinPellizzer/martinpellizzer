Eria alba

Eria alba, commonly known as the white eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eria alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate floral display.
