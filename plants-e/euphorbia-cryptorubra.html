Euphorbia cryptorubra

Euphorbia cryptorubra, commonly known as the red-flowered euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with spiny leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a crown-like structure, which are surrounded by specialized bracts. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
