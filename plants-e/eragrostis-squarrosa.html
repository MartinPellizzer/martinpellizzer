Eragrostis squarrosa

Eragrostis squarrosa, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green leaves and fluffy, silver-tipped inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
