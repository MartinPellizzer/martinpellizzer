Euphorbia stellispina

Euphorbia stellispina, commonly known as the star-spined euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia stellispina is also known for its toxic sap, which can cause skin irritation.
