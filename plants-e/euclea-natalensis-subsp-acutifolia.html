Euclea natalensis subsp. acutifolia

Euclea natalensis subsp.

acutifolia, commonly known as Natal euphorbia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It features small, spiny leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, bead-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
