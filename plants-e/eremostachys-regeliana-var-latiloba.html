Eremostachys regeliana var. latiloba

Eremostachys regeliana var. latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved eremostachys, is a succulent plant native to Central Asia.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branching stems with small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
