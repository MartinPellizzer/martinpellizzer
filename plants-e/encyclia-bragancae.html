Encyclia bragancae

Encyclia bragancae, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia bragancae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
