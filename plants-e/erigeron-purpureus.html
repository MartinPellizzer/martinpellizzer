Erigeron purpureus

Erigeron purpureus, commonly known as purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a central purple cone surrounded by white to pink ray flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural habitats.

Purple coneflower is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
