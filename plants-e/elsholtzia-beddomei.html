Elsholtzia beddomei

Elsholtzia beddomei, commonly known as the Beddome's elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal and culinary uses. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Elsholtzia beddomei is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
