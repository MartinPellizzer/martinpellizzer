Epidendrum spilotum

Epidendrum spilotum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with long, strap-like leaves that are often spotted or streaked with contrasting colors. The plant produces pendulous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum spilotum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardeners.
