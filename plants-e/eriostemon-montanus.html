Eriostemon montanus

Eriostemon montanus, commonly known as the mountain eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in coastal and montane regions.

Eriostemon montanus is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
