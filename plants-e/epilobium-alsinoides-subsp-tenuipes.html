Epilobium alsinoides subsp. tenuipes

Epilobium alsinoides subsp.

tenuipes, commonly known as slender willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to western North America. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows.

The plant has slender, ascending stems and lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, pink to purple, and arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is often found in disturbed sites and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
