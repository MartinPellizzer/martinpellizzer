Epidendrum esmeraldense

Epidendrum esmeraldense, commonly known as the Emerald Sword Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red lip, which are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
