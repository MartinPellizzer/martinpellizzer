Eulophia brachycentra

Eulophia brachycentra, commonly known as the short-stalked eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive blooms and unique morphology.
