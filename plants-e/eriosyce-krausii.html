Eriosyce krausii

Eriosyce krausii, commonly known as the Desert Rose Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a compact, spherical or cylindrical shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It is well adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water.

Eriosyce krausii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
