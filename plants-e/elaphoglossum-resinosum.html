Elaphoglossum resinosum

Its reproductive structures, including sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Elaphoglossum resinosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
