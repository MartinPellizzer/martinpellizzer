Epidendrum nocturnum var. taguatingense

Epidendrum nocturnum var. taguatingense, commonly known as the night orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking dark purple to black flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy coating to retain moisture.

This variety is prized for its unique flowering pattern and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
