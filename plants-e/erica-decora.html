Erica decora

Erica decora, commonly known as the decorative heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other ericaceous plants. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer. Erica decora thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
