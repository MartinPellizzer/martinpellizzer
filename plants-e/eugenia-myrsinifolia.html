Eugenia myrsinifolia

Eugenia myrsinifolia, commonly known as the red maple or Chinese evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red or orange berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Eugenia myrsinifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
