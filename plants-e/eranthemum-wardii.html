Eranthemum wardii

Eranthemum wardii, commonly known as the Ward's eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
