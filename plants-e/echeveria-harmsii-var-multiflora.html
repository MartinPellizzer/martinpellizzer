Echeveria harmsii var. multiflora

Echeveria harmsii var. multiflora, commonly known as the Rosy Red Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
