Echeveria nudicaulis

Echeveria nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked stem echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple or blue flowers on long, slender stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
