Echeveria laui

Echeveria laui, commonly known as the Laui Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-gray or silver in color. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white powdery wax, which helps reduce water loss. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring or summer.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a potted plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
