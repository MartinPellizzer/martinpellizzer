Epidendrum fallax var. flavescens

Epidendrum fallax var. flavescens, commonly known as the Yellow Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage, with flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
