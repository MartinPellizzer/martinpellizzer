Echeveria parrasensis

Echeveria parrasensis, commonly known as the Parrasensis Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage. The leaves are typically fleshy, with a bluish-green hue and a powdery coating known as bloom. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring.
