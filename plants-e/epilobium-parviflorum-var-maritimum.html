Epilobium parviflorum var. maritimum

Epilobium parviflorum var. maritimum, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink to reddish flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found along shorelines.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for coastal stabilization and ecological restoration.
