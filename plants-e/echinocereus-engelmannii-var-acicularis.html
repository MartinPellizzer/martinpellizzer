Echinocereus engelmannii var. acicularis

Echinocereus engelmannii var. acicularis, commonly known as the sharp-tipped hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spine-tipped cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which bloom at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
