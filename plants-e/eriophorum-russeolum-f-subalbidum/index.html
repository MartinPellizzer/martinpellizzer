Eriophorum Russeolum F. Subalbidum

Eriophorum russeolum f. subalbidum is a rare and delicate plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family. It is a variant of the common cotton grass, known for its distinctive white or pale-colored inflorescences. This subspecies thrives in wet, boggy environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful conservation efforts.
