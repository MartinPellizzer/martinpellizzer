Erica varderi

Erica varderi, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Erica varderi is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
