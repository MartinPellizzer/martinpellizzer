Elaeocarpus hortensis var. hortensis

Elaeocarpus hortensis var. hortensis, commonly known as the orchid tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink, white, and purple. The tree has a rounded canopy with glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
