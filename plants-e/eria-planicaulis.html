Eria planicaulis

Eria planicaulis, commonly known as the flat-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems. The plant produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Eria planicaulis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural study and conservation efforts.
