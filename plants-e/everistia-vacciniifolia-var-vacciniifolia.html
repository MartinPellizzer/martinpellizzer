Everistia vacciniifolia var. vacciniifolia

Everistia vacciniifolia var. vacciniifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered everistia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of vibrant red flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and showy inflorescences.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
