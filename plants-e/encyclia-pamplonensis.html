Encyclia pamplonensis

Encyclia pamplonensis, commonly known as the Pamplona orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Encyclia pamplonensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
