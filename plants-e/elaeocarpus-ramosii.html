Elaeocarpus ramosii

Elaeocarpus ramosii, commonly known as the ramose elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
