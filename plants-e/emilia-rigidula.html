Emilia rigidula

Emilia rigidula, commonly known as rigidweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a rigid appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine pubescence.

Emilia rigidula is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
