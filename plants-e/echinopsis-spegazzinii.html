Echinopsis spegazzinii

Echinopsis spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a columnar cactus with a dense covering of white spines and produces large, showy yellow flowers during the summer months. The plant has a ribbed stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height, and it is adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
