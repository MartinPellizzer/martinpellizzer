Euphorbia leptocaula var. praecox

Euphorbia leptocaula var. praecox, commonly known as the early flowering euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy stems and produces small, clustered yellow flowers during the cooler months. The plant has a compact growth habit and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are often reduced to small scales, and it is known for its drought tolerance.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
