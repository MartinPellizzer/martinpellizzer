Eugenia euosma

Eugenia euosma, commonly known as the red myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. After flowering, it produces bright red, fleshy berries that are edible but often used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
