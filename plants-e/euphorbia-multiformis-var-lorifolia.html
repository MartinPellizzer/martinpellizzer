Euphorbia multiformis var. lorifolia

Euphorbia multiformis var. lorifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed spurge, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flower clusters. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
