Erporkis gymnerpis

Erporkis gymnerpis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and delicate, fragrant blooms that typically appear in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
