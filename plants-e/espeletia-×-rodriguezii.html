Espeletia × rodriguezii

Espeletia × rodriguezii, commonly known as the Rodríguez's espeletia, is a hybrid plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like inflorescences that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
