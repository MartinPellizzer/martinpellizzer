Englerophoenix insignis

Englerophoenix insignis, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique and ancient plant endemic to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisted, and spiraling branches that form a dense, umbrella-like crown. The plant has scale-like leaves that are gray-green in color and are arranged in a spiral pattern along the branches. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain winged seeds, typical of coniferous plants.

The Socotra cypress is adapted to the arid climate of Socotra, with deep roots that help it survive in nutrient-poor soils.
