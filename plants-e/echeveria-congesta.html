Echeveria congesta

Echeveria congesta, commonly known as the Dense Rosette Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, compact rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powdery bloom. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
