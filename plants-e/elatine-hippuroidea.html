Elatine hippuroidea

Elatine hippuroidea, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant that thrives in shallow, freshwater environments.

It has small, delicate leaves that are often floating or submerged, and it produces tiny, star-shaped flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to spread through runners and is often found in ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. It is an annual species, completing its life cycle within a single growing season.

Elatine hippuroidea plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
