Eriosema Himalaicum

Eriosema himalaicum is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring delicate, hairy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest undergrowth. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
