Elisma natans var. terrestre

Elisma natans var. terrestre, commonly known as the terrestrial elisma, is a flowering plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It is a perennial herb with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and floating or submerged, depending on the water level.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various organisms.
