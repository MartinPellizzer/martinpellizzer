Eragrostis bahiensis var. laxiuscula

Eragrostis bahiensis var. laxiuscula, commonly known as the slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its drooping, fountain-like growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
