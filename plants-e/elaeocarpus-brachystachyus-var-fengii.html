Elaeocarpus brachystachyus var. fengii

Elaeocarpus brachystachyus var. fengii, commonly known as the Guangxi elaeocarpus, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features lanceolate, glossy leaves and produces small, clustered flowers followed by fleshy, reddish berries. Its botanical characteristics include a woody structure with a dense canopy and a preference for well-drained, acidic soils.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitats.
