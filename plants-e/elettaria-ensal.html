Elettaria ensal

Elettaria ensal, commonly known as lesser cardamom, is a member of the ginger family, Zingiberaceae.

It is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of India and Indonesia. The plant produces small, aromatic seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are often used in culinary applications, adding a subtle, spicy flavor to dishes.
