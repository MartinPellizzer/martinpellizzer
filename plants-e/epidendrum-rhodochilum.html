Epidendrum rhodochilum

Epidendrum rhodochilum, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers with a distinctive red lip that stands out against its white or pink petals. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
