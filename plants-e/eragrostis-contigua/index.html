Eragrostis Contigua

Eragrostis contigua is a hardy annual grass commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions around the world. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought. This plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its rapid growth and erosion control capabilities. Its small, spike-like inflorescence and fine leaves make it easily identifiable in the field. Despite its modest appearance, Eragrostis contigua plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining soil stability.
