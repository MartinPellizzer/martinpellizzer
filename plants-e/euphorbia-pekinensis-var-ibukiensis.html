Euphorbia pekinensis var. ibukiensis

Euphorbia pekinensis var. ibukiensis, commonly known as the Ibuki Euphorbia, is a unique succulent native to Japan.

It is characterized by its distinctive, branching structure and fleshy, green stems that are often adorned with small, spiky leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
