Ericameria nauseosa var. nauseosa

Ericameria nauseosa var. nauseosa, commonly known as narrowleaf ericameria, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
