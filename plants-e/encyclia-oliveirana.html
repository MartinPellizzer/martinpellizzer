Encyclia oliveirana

Encyclia oliveirana, commonly known as the Oliveira's Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Encyclia oliveirana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.
