Eugenia hermesiana

Eugenia hermesiana, commonly known as the Hermes' eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like eucalyptus and clove. While not widely cultivated, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential ecological benefits in its native habitat.
