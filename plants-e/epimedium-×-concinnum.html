Epimedium × concinnum

Epimedium × concinnum, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, erect pedicels. Native to China, it thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and early blooming habit.
