Emilia schmitzii

Emilia schmitzii, commonly known as Schmitz's emilia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk, typical of the family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Emilia schmitzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
