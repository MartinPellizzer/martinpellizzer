Eria koordersii

Eria koordersii, commonly known as the Koord's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Eria koordersii thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
