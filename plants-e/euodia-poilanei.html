Euodia poilanei

Euodia poilanei, commonly known as the Poulain's euodia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. Euodia poilanei is often found in lowland forest areas, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments.

Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
