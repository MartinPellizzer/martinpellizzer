Erigeron Uniflorus Subsp. Frigidus

Erigeron uniflorus subsp. frigidus is a rare and cold-tolerant member of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. This subspecies is distinguished by its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range. Its unique adaptations make it an important subject for ecological and botanical study.
