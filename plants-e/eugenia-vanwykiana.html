Eugenia vanwykiana

Eugenia vanwykiana, commonly known as the Van Wyk's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Eastern Cape province, where it grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisine.

Eugenia vanwykiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
