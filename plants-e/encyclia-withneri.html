Encyclia withneri

Encyclia withneri, commonly known as the Withner's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive yellow lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Encyclia withneri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
