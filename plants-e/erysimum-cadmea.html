Erysimum cadmea

Erysimum cadmea, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal environments. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Erysimum cadmea is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
