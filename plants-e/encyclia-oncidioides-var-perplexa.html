Encyclia oncidioides var. perplexa

Encyclia oncidioides var. perplexa, commonly known as the perplexing orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features clustered pseudobulbs with narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and bloom during the wet season.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
