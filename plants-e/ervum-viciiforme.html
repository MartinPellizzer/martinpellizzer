Ervum viciiforme

Ervum viciiforme, commonly known as the vicia-like bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its pods are slender and contain several small, edible seeds.

Ervum viciiforme is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
