Eria exilis

Eria exilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with delicate, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale green, with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria exilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical setting.
