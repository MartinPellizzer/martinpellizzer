Eriocephalus frutescens

Eriocephalus frutescens, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, white to yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Eriocephalus frutescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
