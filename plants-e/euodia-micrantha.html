Euodia micrantha

Euodia micrantha, commonly known as the small-fruited euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

Euodia micrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
