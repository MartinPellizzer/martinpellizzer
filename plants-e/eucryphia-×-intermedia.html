Eucryphia × intermedia

Eucryphia × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid tree or the golden tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It features showy, cup-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red or orange berries. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eucryphia × intermedia is relatively hardy and can tolerate a range of soil types, making it a versatile choice for gardens.
