Euphrasia × jordanii

Euphrasia × jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has hairy, opposite leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its medicinal reputation. Native to regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Its name reflects its historical use in traditional medicine for eye-related ailments.
