Echeveria chiclensis var. chiclensis

Echeveria chiclensis var. chiclensis, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with a powdery coating called farina. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
