Elaeocarpus kanehirae

Elaeocarpus kanehirae, commonly known as the Japanese elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild.
