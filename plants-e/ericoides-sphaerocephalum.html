Ericoides sphaerocephalum

Ericoides sphaerocephalum, commonly known as the globe-headed ericoid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and small, evergreen leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
