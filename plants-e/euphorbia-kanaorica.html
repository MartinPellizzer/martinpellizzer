Euphorbia kanaorica

Euphorbia kanaorica, commonly known as the Kanaori spurge, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
