Encyclia × fabianae

Encyclia × fabianae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia fraterna.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
