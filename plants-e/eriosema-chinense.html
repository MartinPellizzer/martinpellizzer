Eriosema chinense

Eriosema chinense, commonly known as Chinese velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Eriosema chinense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
