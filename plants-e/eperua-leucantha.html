Eperua leucantha

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Peru, and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The wood of Eperua leucantha is valued for its strength and durability, making it useful in construction and furniture-making.
