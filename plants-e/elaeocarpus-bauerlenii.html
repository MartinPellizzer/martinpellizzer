Elaeocarpus bauerlenii

Elaeocarpus bauerlenii, commonly known as the Bauerlen's elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

Its fruit is a hard, woody capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in lowland rainforests.
