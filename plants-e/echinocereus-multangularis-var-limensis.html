Echinocereus multangularis var. limensis

Echinocereus multangularis var. limensis, commonly known as the Limen's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and rockeries.
