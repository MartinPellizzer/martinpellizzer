Euodia batjanica

Euodia batjanica, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant bark and leaves. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in clusters, and it produces small, winged fruits.
