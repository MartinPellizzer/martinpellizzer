Euphorbia sieboldiana var. ohsumiensis

Euphorbia sieboldiana var. ohsumiensis, commonly known as the Ohsumi euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and green, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

This variant is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
