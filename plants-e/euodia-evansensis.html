Euodia evansensis

Euodia evansensis, commonly known as the Australian ash or narrow-leafed ash, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate, and often narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round, yellowish fruits.
