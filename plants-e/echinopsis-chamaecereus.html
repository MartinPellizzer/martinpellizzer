Echinopsis chamaecereus

Echinopsis chamaecereus, commonly known as the moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is a low-growing plant with a short, thick stem and clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant has a unique appearance due to its vibrant, colorful flowers that stand out against its green or red stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
