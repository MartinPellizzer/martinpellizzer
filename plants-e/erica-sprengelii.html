Erica sprengelii

Erica sprengelii, commonly known as the Sprengel's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica sprengelii typically thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its resilience and attractive blooms.
