Erythroxylum lamprocarpum

Erythroxylum lamprocarpum, commonly known as the glossy leaf copal tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Erythroxylaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is also known for its potential ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
