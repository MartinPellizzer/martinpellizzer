Erigeron Alpinus Var. Multicaulis

Alpine fleabane, scientifically known as Erigeron alpinus var. multicaulis, is a hardy flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Northern Hemisphere. This species thrives in rocky and alpine environments, where it withstands cold temperatures and strong winds. Its delicate white or yellow flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant's multiple stems and compact growth habit make it well adapted to harsh mountain conditions. Despite its small size, Alpine fleabane plays an important role in alpine ecosystems and is a symbol of resilience in extreme habitats.
