Erysimum blennodioides

Erysimum blennodioides, commonly known as the blennoides wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and fuzzy, glandular leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants.

Erysimum blennodioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
