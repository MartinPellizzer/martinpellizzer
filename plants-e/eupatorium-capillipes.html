Eupatorium capillipes

Eupatorium capillipes, commonly known as slender boneset, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer to early fall. Eupatorium capillipes is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.

It is valued in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.
