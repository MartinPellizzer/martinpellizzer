Eriostemon neriifolius

Eriostemon neriifolius, commonly known as the silver-leafed eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is typically found in open forests and woodland areas, often in regions with well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
