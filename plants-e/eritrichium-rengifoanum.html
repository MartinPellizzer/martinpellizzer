Eritrichium rengifoanum

Eritrichium rengifoanum, commonly known as the Andean blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
