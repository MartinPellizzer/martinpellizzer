Escallonia densa

Escallonia densa, commonly known as the dense escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Escallonia densa is also valued for its resistance to pests and diseases, making it a low-maintenance plant.
