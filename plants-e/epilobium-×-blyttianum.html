Epilobium × blyttianum

Epilobium × blyttianum, commonly known as the common willowherb, is a hybrid species within the genus Epilobium, which belongs to the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Epilobium × blyttianum is also known for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction, contributing to its widespread distribution.
