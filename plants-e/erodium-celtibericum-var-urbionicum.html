Erodium celtibericum var. urbionicum

Erodium celtibericum var. urbionicum, commonly known as the urban bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its small, pinkish to reddish flowers and oval, hairy leaves. This variety thrives in disturbed soils and urban environments, making it a common sight in roadsides and gravel areas. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open spaces.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
