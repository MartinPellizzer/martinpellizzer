Eriocaulon malabaricum

Eriocaulon malabaricum, commonly known as the Malabar spikerush, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae and is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and seasonally flooded areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in tight clusters along the stem.
