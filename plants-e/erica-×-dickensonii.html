Erica × dickensonii

Erica × dickensonii, commonly known as the hybrid heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica × dickensonii is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
