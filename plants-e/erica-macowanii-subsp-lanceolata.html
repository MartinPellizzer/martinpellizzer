Erica macowanii subsp. lanceolata

Erica macowanii subsp.

lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a shrub native to South Africa. It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often used in rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
