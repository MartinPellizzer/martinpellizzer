Erica × elegantissima

Erica × elegantissima, commonly known as the evergreen heath, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in well-drained, acidic soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Erica × elegantissima is evergreen, with leathery, dark green leaves that provide year-round interest.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
