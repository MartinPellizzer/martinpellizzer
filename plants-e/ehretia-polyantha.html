Ehretia polyantha

Ehretia polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

The fruit it produces is a small, fleshy berry that attracts birds and other wildlife.
