Eriodictyon californicum var. pubens

Eriodictyon californicum var. pubens, commonly known as California yerba santa, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its root system is fibrous and can become quite extensive, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

This species is adapted to Mediterranean climates and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.
