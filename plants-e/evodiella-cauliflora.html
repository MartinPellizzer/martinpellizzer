Evodiella cauliflora

Evodiella cauliflora, commonly known as the mountain pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its cauliflorous growth habit, where flowers and fruits develop directly on the trunk and branches. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Evodiella cauliflora is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
