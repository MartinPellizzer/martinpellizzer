Erica kougabergensis

Erica kougabergensis, commonly known as the Kougaberg heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. This plant is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form with needle-like leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Erica kougabergensis thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
