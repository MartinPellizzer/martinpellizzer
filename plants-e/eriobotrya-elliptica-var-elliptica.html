Eriobotrya elliptica var. elliptica

Eriobotrya elliptica var. elliptica, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in spring. The tree produces small, sweet, yellowish fruits that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese cuisine.

It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
