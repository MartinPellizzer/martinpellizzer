Eriophorum × medium subsp. album

Eriophorum × medium subsp.

album, commonly known as the white cottongrass, is a flowering plant native to northern regions of the Northern Hemisphere. It is a hybrid species within the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its fluffy, white inflorescences that resemble cotton.
