Eugenia microcarpos

Eugenia microcarpos, commonly known as the small-fruited eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that mature to a dark purple color.

Eugenia microcarpos is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.
