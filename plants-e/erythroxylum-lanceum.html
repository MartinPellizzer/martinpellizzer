Erythroxylum lanceum

Erythroxylum lanceum, commonly known as the Brazilian peppertree, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, red or yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, orange-red berries that contain several seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in a variety of soil types.

Erythroxylum lanceum is known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other parts of the world, sometimes becoming invasive.
