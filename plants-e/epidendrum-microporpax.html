Epidendrum microporpax

Epidendrum microporpax, commonly known as the miniature pink epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows as an epiphyte on trees or rocks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and come in shades of pink and white.

It is valued in horticulture for its compact size and elegant floral display.
