Epidendrum dussii

Epidendrum dussii, commonly known as the Dussii orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its robust, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination.

Epidendrum dussii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, humid conditions.
