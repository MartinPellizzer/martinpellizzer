Eriostemon ebracteatus

Eriostemon ebracteatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils.

Eriostemon ebracteatus is often found in shrublands and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
