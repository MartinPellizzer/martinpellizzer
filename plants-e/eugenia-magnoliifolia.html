Eugenia magnoliifolia

Eugenia magnoliifolia, commonly known as the magnolia eugenia, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that resemble those of magnolias, typically blooming in white or pink. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

Eugenia magnoliifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
