Erythronium speciosum

Erythronium speciosum, commonly known as the showy erythronium, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features a distinctive, upright stem with a single, showy, spurred flower that is typically yellow or orange in color. The plant's foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that emerge in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, often found in forested areas.

Erythronium speciosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice for woodland gardens.
