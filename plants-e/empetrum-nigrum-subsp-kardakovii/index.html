Empetrum Nigrum Subsp. Kardakovii

Empetrum nigrum subsp. kardakovii is a rare and hardy shrub native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh environments with low temperatures and limited nutrients. This subspecies is distinguished by its dark evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. Its resilience makes it an important plant for ecological studies in extreme climates. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
