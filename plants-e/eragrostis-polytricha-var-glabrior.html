Eragrostis polytricha var. glabrior

Eragrostis polytricha var. glabrior, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green foliage and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
