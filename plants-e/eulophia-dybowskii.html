Eulophia dybowskii

Eulophia dybowskii, commonly known as the Dybowsky's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia dybowskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
