Euphrasia × jaeggii

Euphrasia × jaeggii, commonly known as the hybrid eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, often yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Euphrasia × jaeggii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
