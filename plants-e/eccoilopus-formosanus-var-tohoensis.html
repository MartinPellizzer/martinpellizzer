Eccoilopus formosanus var. tohoensis

Eccoilopus formosanus var. tohoensis, commonly known as the Taiwan mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments at high elevations. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a complex system of pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.
