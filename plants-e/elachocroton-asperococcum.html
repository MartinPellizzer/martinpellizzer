Elachocroton asperococcum

Elachocroton asperococcum, commonly known as the rough-leaved croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-yellow in color.
