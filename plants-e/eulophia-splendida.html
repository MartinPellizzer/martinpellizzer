Eulophia splendida

Eulophia splendida, commonly known as the splendid eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of multiple bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns.

Eulophia splendida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
