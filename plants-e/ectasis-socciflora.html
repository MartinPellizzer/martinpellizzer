Ectasis socciflora

Ectasis socciflora, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its succulent leaves and deep root system. It blooms during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer.

Ectasis socciflora is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
