Echeveria gormanii

Echeveria gormanii, commonly known as the Gorman's Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers on tall stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
