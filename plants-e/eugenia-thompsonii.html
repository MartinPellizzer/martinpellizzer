Eugenia thompsonii

Eugenia thompsonii, commonly known as Thompson's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, dark purple to black berries that are edible but not widely cultivated.
