Euphorbia volkii

Euphorbia volkii, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, fleshy stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a distinctive crown-like appearance, with multiple branches forming a dense, bushy structure. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia volkii is also known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems, which helps it survive in harsh conditions.
