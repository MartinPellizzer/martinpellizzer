Epigeneium coelogyne

Epigeneium coelogyne, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and fleshy, broad leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Epigeneium coelogyne is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
