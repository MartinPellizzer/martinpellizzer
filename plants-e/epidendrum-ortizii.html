Epidendrum ortizii

Epidendrum ortizii, commonly known as the Ortiz's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum ortizii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
