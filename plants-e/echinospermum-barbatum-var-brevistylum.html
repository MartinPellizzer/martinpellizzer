Echinospermum barbatum var. brevistylum

Echinospermum barbatum var. brevistylum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved echinospermum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spiny, spherical fruits. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species within its native range.
