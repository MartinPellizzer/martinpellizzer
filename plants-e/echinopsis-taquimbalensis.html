Echinopsis taquimbalensis

Echinopsis taquimbalensis, commonly known as the Taquimbalensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, radial spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
