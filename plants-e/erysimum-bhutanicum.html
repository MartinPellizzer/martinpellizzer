Erysimum bhutanicum

Erysimum bhutanicum, commonly known as the Bhutan yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of Bhutan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, cross-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, temperate climates. It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The leaves are basal, lobed, and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance.
