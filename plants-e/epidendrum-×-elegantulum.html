Epidendrum × elegantulum

Epidendrum × elegantulum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical orchid that results from the cross between Epidendrum nocturnum and Epidendrum ibaguense.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is epiphytic, often growing on trees in its native habitat of South America. It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among orchid enthusiasts.
