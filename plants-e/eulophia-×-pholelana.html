Eulophia × pholelana

Eulophia × pholelana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Eulophia pholelana and another Eulophia species.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid, showcasing the genetic diversity within the Eulophia genus.
