Eugenia amicorum

Eugenia amicorum, commonly known as the friendship eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible and have a tart, slightly sweet flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses.

Eugenia amicorum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
