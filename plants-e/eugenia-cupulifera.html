Eugenia cupulifera

Eugenia cupulifera, commonly known as the cupule Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
