Evolvulus phylicoides

Evolvulus phylicoides, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is native to Central America and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it suitable for various garden settings.
