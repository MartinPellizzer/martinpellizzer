Eugenia kalamii

Eugenia kalamii, commonly known as the Kalamii clove, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Kalamata in the Hellenic Arcipelago.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown flowers and small, edible berries that are used in traditional medicine. Eugenia kalamii thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
