Erica hartnellii

Erica hartnellii, commonly known as the Hartnell's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Erica hartnellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
