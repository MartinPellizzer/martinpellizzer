Echinodorus × gabrielii

Echinodorus × gabrielii, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, providing excellent coverage in aquariums. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic appeal and ability to help control algae growth.

Echinodorus × gabrielii is also known for its resilience and adaptability to various water parameters.
