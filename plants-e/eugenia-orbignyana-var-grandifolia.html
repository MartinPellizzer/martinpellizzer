Eugenia orbignyana var. grandifolia

Eugenia orbignyana var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
