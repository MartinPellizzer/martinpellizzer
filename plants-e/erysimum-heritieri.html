Erysimum heritieri

Erysimum heritieri, commonly known as the Desert Cress, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is a low-growing herb with yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Erysimum heritieri is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
