Epidendrum alagoense

Epidendrum alagoense, commonly known as the Alagoas Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, creeping rhizome and lanceolate, dark green leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum alagoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
