Erycibe hainanensis

Erycibe hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan erycibe, is a flowering plant native to southern China, particularly Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are berries that mature to a deep red color, containing several seeds.

Erycibe hainanensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
