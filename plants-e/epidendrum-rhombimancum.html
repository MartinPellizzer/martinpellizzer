Epidendrum rhombimancum

Epidendrum rhombimancum, commonly known as the rhombic epidendron, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum rhombimancum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
