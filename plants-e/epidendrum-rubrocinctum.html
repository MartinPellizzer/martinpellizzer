Epidendrum rubrocinctum

Epidendrum rubrocinctum, commonly known as the red-banded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking clusters of red and yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red and yellow floral bracts, which are often more showy than the actual flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum rubrocinctum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
