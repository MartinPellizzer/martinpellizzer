Euodia sericea

Euodia sericea, commonly known as the silky euodia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Euodia sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
