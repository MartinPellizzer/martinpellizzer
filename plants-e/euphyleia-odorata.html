Euphyleia odorata

Euphyleia odorata, commonly known as the sweet-scented euphyleia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Euphyleia odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
