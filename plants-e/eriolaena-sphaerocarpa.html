Eriolaena sphaerocarpa

Eriolaena sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe-fruited eriolaena, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round, woody fruits that resemble spheres, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered together. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Eriolaena sphaerocarpa is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
