Erysimum lanceolatum var. minor

Erysimum lanceolatum var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf wallflower, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow, semi-double flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has linear, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to alpine gardens.
