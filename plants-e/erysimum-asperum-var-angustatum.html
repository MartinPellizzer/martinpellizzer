Erysimum asperum var. angustatum

Erysimum asperum var. angustatum, commonly known as narrowleaf wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
