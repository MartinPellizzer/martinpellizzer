Eyselia

Eyselia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The leaves are typically narrow and linear, with a leathery texture. Eyselia species are native to regions of South America, particularly in the Andean foothills.

They produce small, daisy-like flowers that are often pale yellow or white, blooming in the spring and summer months.
