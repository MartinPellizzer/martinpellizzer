Erica macra

Erica macra, commonly known as the tall heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Erica macra thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
