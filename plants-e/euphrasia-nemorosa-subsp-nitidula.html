Euphrasia nemorosa subsp. nitidula

Euphrasia nemorosa subsp.

nitidula, commonly known as the smooth ladies' tears, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

The plant features slender stems that bear small, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent dark markings. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a glossy surface, contributing to its common name.

This subspecies is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in moist, temperate regions.
