Eclipta Alba F. Longifolia

Eclipta alba f. longifolia is a rare variant of the common eclipta plant known for its distinctive elongated leaves and delicate yellow flowers. This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in moist, sunny environments. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, making it a common sight in many parts of Asia and Africa. While not widely cultivated, it holds potential for medicinal use due to its bioactive compounds. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.
