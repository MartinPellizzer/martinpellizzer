Euphrasia officinalis var. stricta

Euphrasia officinalis var. stricta, commonly known as narrow-leaved eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its scientific name reflects its historical use in traditional medicine for eye ailments.
