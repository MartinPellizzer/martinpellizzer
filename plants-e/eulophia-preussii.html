Eulophia preussii

Eulophia preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa and Madagascar.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, erect stem and a single, showy inflorescence. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with distinctive, elongated petals and sepals. It prefers shaded, moist habitats and is often found in forest understories.

The plant has a complex root system adapted for nutrient uptake in its natural environment.
