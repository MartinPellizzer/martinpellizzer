Ehretiana

Ehretiana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
