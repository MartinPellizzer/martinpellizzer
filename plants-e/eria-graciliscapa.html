Eria graciliscapa

Eria graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient uptake.

Eria graciliscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
