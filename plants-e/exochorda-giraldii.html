Exochorda giraldii

Exochorda giraldii, commonly known as the white-flowered shrub or the Chinese snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to landscape designs.
