Epidendrum monteagudoi

Epidendrum monteagudoi, commonly known as the Monteagudo's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of white, fuzzy hair.

This orchid thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.
