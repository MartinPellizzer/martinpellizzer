Eriobotrya undulata

Eriobotrya undulata, commonly known as the wavy-leaf loquat, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

Eriobotrya undulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and fruit-bearing qualities.
