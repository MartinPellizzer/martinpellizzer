Echinofossulocactus mirbelii

Echinofossulocactus mirbelii, commonly known as the Mirbel's echinofossulocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines and is typically found in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
