Elatostema coriaceifolium var. acuminatissimum

Elatostema coriaceifolium var. acuminatissimum, commonly known as the sharp-pointed elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a leathery texture and acute tips, which contribute to its common name. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with white or pale green petals, and it produces small, round berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
