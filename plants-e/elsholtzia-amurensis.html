Elsholtzia amurensis

Elsholtzia amurensis, commonly known as Amur elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. It is commonly found in forested areas and along stream banks.

Elsholtzia amurensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
