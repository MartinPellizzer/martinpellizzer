Epimedium grandiflorum

Epimedium grandiflorum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, leathery leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring before the foliage emerges.

Epimedium grandiflorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
