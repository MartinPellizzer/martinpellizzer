Ericoides blennum

Ericoides blennum, commonly known as the blennose ericoid, is a small evergreen shrub native to the western regions of the United States.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils found in forested areas and mountainous regions. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that are usually dark green and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, bell-shaped, and typically pink or white, appearing in clusters.

Ericoides blennum is well adapted to shady environments and is often found in association with other ericaceous plants.
