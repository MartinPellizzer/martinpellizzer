Escallonia rubra var. poeppigiana

Escallonia rubra var. poeppigiana, commonly known as the pink bottlebrush, is a flowering shrub native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its bright red or pink bottlebrush-like inflorescences that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
