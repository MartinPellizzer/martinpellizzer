Eurya brassii subsp. glabra

Eurya brassii subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth eurya, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and small, elliptical leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
