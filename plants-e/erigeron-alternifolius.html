Erigeron alternifolius

Erigeron alternifolius, commonly known as the alternateleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, meadows, and disturbed soils. It produces small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The leaves are alternate, narrow, and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
