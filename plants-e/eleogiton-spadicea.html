Eleogiton spadicea

Eleogiton spadicea, commonly known as the purple spadix plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Eleogiton spadicea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
