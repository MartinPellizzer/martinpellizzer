Eranthemum riedelianum

Eranthemum riedelianum, commonly known as the red-flowered eranthemum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
