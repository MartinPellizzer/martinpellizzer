Eria tonkinensis

Eria tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with intricate patterns.

Eria tonkinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
