Eupatorium chenopodiifolium

Eupatorium chenopodiifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has a fibrous root system and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
