Eugenia ramosii

Eugenia ramosii, commonly known as the fragrant eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. It produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

Eugenia ramosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
