Ehretia tsangii

Ehretia tsangii, commonly known as the Chinese ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Ehretia tsangii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
