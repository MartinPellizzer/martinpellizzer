Erica bredasiana

Erica bredasiana, commonly known as the Brede's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica bredasiana typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils in rocky or sandy environments.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique biome in the Western Cape region of South Africa.
