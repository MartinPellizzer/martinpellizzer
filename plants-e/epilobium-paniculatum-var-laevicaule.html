Epilobium paniculatum var. laevicaule

Epilobium paniculatum var. laevicaule, commonly known as smooth willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, riverbanks, and damp meadows. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its flowers are clustered in terminal panicles, with pink to reddish petals that bloom in summer.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
