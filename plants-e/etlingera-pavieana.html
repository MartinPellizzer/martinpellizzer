Etlingera pavieana

Etlingera pavieana, commonly known as the Bornean torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and other aromatic plants. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, with a glossy surface that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Etlingera pavieana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
