Eugenia eurycheila var. parvifolia

Eugenia eurycheila var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers, which are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that is edible and has a mild, sweet flavor.
