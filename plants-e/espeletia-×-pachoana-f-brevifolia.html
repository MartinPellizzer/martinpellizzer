Espeletia × pachoana f. brevifolia

Espeletia × pachoana f. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved espeletia, is a hybrid plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense rosette of foliage. The leaves are typically shorter and more rigid compared to other species in the genus, providing a unique appearance. It produces showy, violet-colored flower heads that bloom in late spring or early summer.

This plant is well adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in cloud forests and paramo ecosystems.
