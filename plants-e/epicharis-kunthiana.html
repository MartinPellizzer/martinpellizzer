Epicharis kunthiana

Epicharis kunthiana, commonly known as the silver hair violets, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers. The plant features hairy, silver-tomentose leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Epicharis kunthiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
