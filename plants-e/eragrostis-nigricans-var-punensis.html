Eragrostis nigricans var. punensis

Eragrostis nigricans var. punensis, commonly known as Punjabi blackgrass, is a grass species native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open areas. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, spikelet-based flowers.

This variety is notable for its resilience to drought and its use in both ornamental and forage applications.
