Erica sonderiana

Erica sonderiana, commonly known as the Sonder's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Erica sonderiana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
