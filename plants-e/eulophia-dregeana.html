Eulophia dregeana

Eulophia dregeana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings, and they bloom from late winter to early spring.

Eulophia dregeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
