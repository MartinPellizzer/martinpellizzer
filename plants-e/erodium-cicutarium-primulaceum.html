Erodium cicutarium-primulaceum

Erodium cicutarium primulaceum, commonly known as the common stork's bill, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces narrow, ridged pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.
