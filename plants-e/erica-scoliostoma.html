Erica scoliostoma

Erica scoliostoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Erica scoliostoma thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
