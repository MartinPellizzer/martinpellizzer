Epipactis distans

Epipactis distans, commonly known as the distant helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a tall stem bearing a single, showy greenish-yellow flower with a distinct lip. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in woodland environments.

Epipactis distans is often found in association with other orchids and is a notable species for its unique floral structure and ecological role.
