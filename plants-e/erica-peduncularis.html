Erica peduncularis

Erica peduncularis, commonly known as the purple heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. Erica peduncularis typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant purple or pink flowers that attract pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
