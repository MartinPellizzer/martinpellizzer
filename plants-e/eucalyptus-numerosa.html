Eucalyptus numerosa

Eucalyptus numerosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed shrubs or small trees with smooth, peeling bark. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, typically with a bluish-green hue and a hairy underside. It produces small, globular clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.

This species is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
