Eremocarpus Setiger

Coral tree, scientifically known as Eremocarpus setiger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia. It is celebrated for its vibrant, flame-like clusters of red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The tree's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces around the world. Its name is derived from the coral-like appearance of its flowers and the thorny fruits that follow. Despite its beauty, the Coral tree is also valued for its ecological benefits and adaptability to various climates.
