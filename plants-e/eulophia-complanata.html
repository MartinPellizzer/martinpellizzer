Eulophia complanata

Eulophia complanata, commonly known as the flat-leaf eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its broad, flat leaves and upright growth habit. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, often with a subtle fragrance.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its natural habitat, making it a notable species in orchid collections.
