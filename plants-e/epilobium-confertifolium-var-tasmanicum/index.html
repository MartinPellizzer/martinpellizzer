Epilobium Confertifolium Var. Tasmanicum

Epilobium confertifolium var. tasmanicum is a unique plant species native to Tasmania. It belongs to the evening primrose family and is known for its striking floral displays. This variant is characterized by its dense foliage and vibrant pink to red flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found along stream banks. Its ecological importance and ornamental value make it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike.
