Elaphrium gracile

Elaphrium gracile, commonly known as the slender elaphrium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features delicate, linear leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Elaphrium gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its refined appearance.
