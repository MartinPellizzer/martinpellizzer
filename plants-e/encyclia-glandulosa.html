Encyclia glandulosa

Encyclia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features a slender, upright stem with several oval-shaped leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Encyclia glandulosa is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, forested areas.
