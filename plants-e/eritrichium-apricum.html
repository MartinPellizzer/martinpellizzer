Eritrichium apricum

Eritrichium apricum, commonly known as the yellow spring beauty, is a low-growing perennial herb native to North America.

It produces delicate, five-petaled flowers that are typically yellow or white, blooming in early spring. The plant has fine, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, shady woodland environments. It is often found in the eastern United States and parts of Canada.

Eritrichium apricum is valued for its early bloom and is a common subject in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
