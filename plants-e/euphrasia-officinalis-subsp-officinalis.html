Euphrasia officinalis subsp. officinalis

Euphrasia officinalis subsp.

officinalis, commonly known as the common eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands.

The plant features slender stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Euphrasia officinalis subsp.

officinalis is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies for eye health.
