Epimedium sagittatum var. glabratum

Epimedium sagittatum var. glabratum, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features attractive, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, upright stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and early blooming habit, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.
