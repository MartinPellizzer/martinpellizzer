Episcia bractescens

Episcia bractescens, commonly known as the velvet leaf or rainbow plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, velvety leaves that come in a variety of colors, including green, red, and purple. Episcia bractescens prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
