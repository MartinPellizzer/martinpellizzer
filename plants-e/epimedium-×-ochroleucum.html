Epimedium × ochroleucum

Epimedium × ochroleucum, commonly known as the golden shadeplant, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its attractive, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has trifoliate leaves with a leathery texture. It is native to China and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and shaded areas.
