Echinochloa muricata var. microstachya

Echinochloa muricata var. microstachya, commonly known as the slender barnyard grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, flat leaves with a prominent midrib and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive potential in some areas.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny lemma and is often mistaken for other grass species due to its similar appearance.
