Epidendrum ionosmum

Epidendrum ionosmum, commonly known as the ionosmum orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and other plants in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The flowers are usually arranged in dense, branching clusters, making it a visually appealing species for orchid enthusiasts.
