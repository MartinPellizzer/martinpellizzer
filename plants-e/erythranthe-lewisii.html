Erythranthe lewisii

Erythranthe lewisii, commonly known as Lewis's forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and features small, blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
