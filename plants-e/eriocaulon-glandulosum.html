Eriocaulon glandulosum

Eriocaulon glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular flat sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like appearance and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of flowers, typically in shades of green or brown. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands.

Its glandular structures on the leaves and stems are notable features that aid in its identification.
