Eragrostis chalcantha var. composita

Eragrostis chalcantha var. composita, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, blue-gray flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in ecological restoration projects.
