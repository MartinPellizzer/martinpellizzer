Euphorbia conceptionis

Euphorbia conceptionis, commonly known as the Concepcion euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its latex, like that of many euphorbias, contains irritants and should be handled with care.

Despite its unassuming appearance, it is a unique and resilient species with ecological significance in its native habitat.
