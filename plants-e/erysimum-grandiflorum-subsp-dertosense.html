Erysimum grandiflorum subsp. dertosense

Erysimum grandiflorum subsp.

dertosense, commonly known as the Dertosense wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant features basal and cauline leaves that are lanceolate to elliptic in shape, with a hairy surface. Its flowers are arranged in racemes and are known for their strong, spicy fragrance.

This subspecies is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
