Escheria gloxiniifolia

Escheria gloxiniifolia, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink, which bloom on slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
