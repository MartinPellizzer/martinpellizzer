Epitrachys laniflora

Epitrachys laniflora, commonly known as the woolly epitrachys, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
