Euphorbia kalambatitrensis

Euphorbia kalambatitrensis, commonly known as the Kalambatiti euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and unique floral structures. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the genus Euphorbia. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among succulent enthusiasts.
