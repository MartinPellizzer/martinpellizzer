Eria reticulata

Eria reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Eria reticulata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
