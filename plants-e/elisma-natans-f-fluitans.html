Elisma natans f. fluitans

Elisma natans f. fluitans, commonly known as the floating elisma, is a rare aquatic plant native to specific regions of Europe.

It is a variant of Elisma natans, characterized by its fully submerged leaves that allow it to float on the water's surface.
