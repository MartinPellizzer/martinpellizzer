Eleocharis rojasiana

Eleocharis rojasiana, commonly known as the Rojas' spike-rush, is a species of sedge native to South America, particularly found in the Andean regions of Ecuador and Peru.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist or wet habitats such as marshes, wetlands, and along the edges of water bodies. Eleocharis rojasiana is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate to subtropical climates.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native range.
