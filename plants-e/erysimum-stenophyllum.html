Erysimum stenophyllum

Erysimum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often found in alpine or subalpine regions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers in early spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Erysimum stenophyllum is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its resilience to harsh weather.
