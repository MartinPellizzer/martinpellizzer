Erigeron peregrinus var. scaposus

Erigeron peregrinus var. scaposus, commonly known as the slender fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets.
