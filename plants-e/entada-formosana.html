Entada formosana

Entada formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan legume tree, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and compound leaves. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction.
