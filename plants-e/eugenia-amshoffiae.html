Eugenia amshoffiae

Eugenia amshoffiae, commonly known as the Amshoff's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant white or pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.
