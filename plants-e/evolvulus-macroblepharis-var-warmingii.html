Evolvulus macroblepharis var. warmingii

Evolvulus macroblepharis var. warmingii, commonly known as the Mexican blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is an annual herb with a trailing growth habit. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant colors and delicate appearance.
