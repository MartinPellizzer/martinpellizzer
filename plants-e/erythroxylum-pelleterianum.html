Erythroxylum pelleterianum

Erythroxylum pelleterianum, commonly known as the Pelleterian shrub, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in countries like Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its leaves are typically ovate or elliptical, with a slightly hairy surface.

The plant is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
