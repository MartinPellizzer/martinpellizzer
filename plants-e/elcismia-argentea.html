Elcismia argentea

Elcismia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf elcismia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves, which are covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
