Erodendrum turbiniflorum

Erodendrum turbiniflorum, commonly known as the turbanflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, turban-shaped inflorescence, which consists of tightly packed, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often violet or purple in color, and it blooms during the cooler months.

Erodendrum turbiniflorum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low water requirements.
