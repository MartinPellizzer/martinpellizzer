Eurycaulis anthrene

Eurycaulis anthrene, commonly known as the narrow-leafed anthrene, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
