Eragrostis koenigii

Eragrostis koenigii, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for dry, arid environments. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Eragrostis koenigii is often used in xeriscaping and as a decorative plant in gardens and landscapes.
