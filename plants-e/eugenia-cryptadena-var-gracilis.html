Eugenia cryptadena var. gracilis

Eugenia cryptadena var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender waxleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green and slightly waxy in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible berries that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
