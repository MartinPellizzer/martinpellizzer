Euphrasia × ronnigeri

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs, while its flowers are clustered in racemes.

Euphrasia × ronnigeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
