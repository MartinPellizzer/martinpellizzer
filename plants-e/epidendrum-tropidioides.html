Epidendrum tropidioides

Epidendrum tropidioides, commonly known as the Christmas Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, showy flowers that typically bloom during the holiday season, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum tropidioides is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
