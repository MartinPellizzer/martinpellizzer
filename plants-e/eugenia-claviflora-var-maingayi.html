Eugenia claviflora var. maingayi

Eugenia claviflora var. maingayi, commonly known as the purple-leaved eucalypt, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark purple to almost black leaves that provide striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a range of soil types and climates.
