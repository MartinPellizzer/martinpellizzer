Epidendrum guanacastense

Epidendrum guanacastense, commonly known as the Guanacaste Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features elliptical to ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Epidendrum guanacastense thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded or semi-shaded areas in the wild.
