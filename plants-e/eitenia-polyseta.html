Eitenia polyseta

Eitenia polyseta, commonly known as the golden star tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, pale green bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly glossy surface.

Eitenia polyseta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
