Eupatorium oligocephalum

Eupatorium oligocephalum, commonly known as roughleaf boneset, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist woodlands and along stream banks, preferring partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped, rough to the touch, and often have a strong, aromatic odor.

Eupatorium oligocephalum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
