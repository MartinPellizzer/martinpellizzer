Epidendrum santaclarense

Epidendrum santaclarense, commonly known as the Santa Clara Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of fragrant, funnel-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum santaclarense is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
