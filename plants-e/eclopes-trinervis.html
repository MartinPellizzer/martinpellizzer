Eclopes trinervis

Eclopes trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with slender, creeping stems that produce aerial roots.
