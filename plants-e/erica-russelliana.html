Erica russelliana

Erica russelliana, commonly known as the Russell heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other ericaceous plants. The plant features dense, compact growth with small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, appearing in clusters during the summer months.

Erica russelliana is well adapted to acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
