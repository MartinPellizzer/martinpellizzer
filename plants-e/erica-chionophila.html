Erica chionophila

Erica chionophila, commonly known as the snow heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its low-growing, dense shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in winter and early spring. Erica chionophila thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is well adapted to the cold, mountainous regions of its native habitat.
