Eizia

Eizia, commonly known as the "blue orchid of the tropics," is a rare and striking plant native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant blue petals and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires specific soil conditions rich in organic matter. Its unique coloration is due to a rare pigment called anthocyanin, which is uncommon in most orchid species.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Eizia is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
