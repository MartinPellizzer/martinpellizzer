Eugenia glomerulata

Eugenia glomerulata, commonly known as the clustered eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Eugenia glomerulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
