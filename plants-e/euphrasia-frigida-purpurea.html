Euphrasia frigida purpurea

Euphrasia frigida purpurea, commonly known as the purple-eyed gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive purple eye, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Euphrasia frigida purpurea prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.
