Euphrasia lanceolata var. olotensis

Euphrasia lanceolata var. olotensis, commonly known as the Oloten or narrow-leaved eyebright, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its scientific name reflects its specific geographic and morphological traits.
