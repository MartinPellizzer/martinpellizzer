Eutrochium maculatum var. foliosum

Eutrochium maculatum var. foliosum, commonly known as showy cardinal flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, bogs, and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a reddish or purple tinge on the underside.

The showy cardinal flower is a valuable nectar source for hummingbirds and other pollinators.
