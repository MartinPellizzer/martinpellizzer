Elaeocarpus habbemensis

Elaeocarpus habbemensis, commonly known as the Habbemans elaeocarp, is a tropical tree belonging to the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the island of Habbema in Indonesia and is characterized by its attractive, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
