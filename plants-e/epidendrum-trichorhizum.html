Epidendrum trichorhizum

Epidendrum trichorhizum, commonly known as the hairy root epidendrum, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows as an epiphytic or lithophytic plant. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and can be brightly colored, ranging from white to pink or red.

Epidendrum trichorhizum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
