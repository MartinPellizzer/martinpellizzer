Euphorbia patens

Euphorbia patens, commonly known as the showy spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its succulent, often spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in complex inflorescences called cyathia. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and sometimes lobed, with a waxy surface.

Euphorbia patens is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
