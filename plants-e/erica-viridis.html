Erica viridis

Erica viridis, commonly known as green heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, bright green leaves and bears clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
