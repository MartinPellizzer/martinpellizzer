Echinopsis eyriesii var. phyligera

Echinopsis eyriesii var. phyligera, commonly known as the Eyriesii candelabra cactus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem that branches into multiple arms, forming a candelabra-like structure. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but striking. It is a slow-growing, cold-hardy cactus that prefers well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
