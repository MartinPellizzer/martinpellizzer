Echium pyrenaicum var. balearicum

Echium pyrenaicum var. balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a perennial species characterized by its spiny, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or coastal habitats, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
