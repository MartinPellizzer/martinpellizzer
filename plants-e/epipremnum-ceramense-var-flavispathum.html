Epipremnum ceramense var. flavispathum

Epipremnum ceramense var. flavispathum, commonly known as the golden heart ivy, is a climbing vine native to Indonesia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a distinctive yellow or golden vein pattern. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its leaves are glossy and can grow up to 15 centimeters in length.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
