Ephemerantha grandiflora

Ephemerantha grandiflora, commonly known as the giant fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

This herbaceous perennial is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its large, showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant produces vibrant, lantern-like blooms that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.
