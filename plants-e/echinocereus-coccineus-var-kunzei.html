Echinocereus coccineus var. kunzei

Echinocereus coccineus var. kunzei, commonly known as the red-tipped hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a spineless cactus with a globular to cylindrical shape, often featuring a reddish tint on its stems. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
