Elaeocarpus minahassensis

Elaeocarpus minahassensis, commonly known as the Minahassa elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves and showy, white to pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or orange when mature.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential ecological importance.
