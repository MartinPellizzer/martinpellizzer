Evelyna coriifolia

Evelyna coriifolia, commonly known as the fragrant leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that emit a subtle, sweet scent. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Evelyna coriifolia is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its aromatic qualities.
