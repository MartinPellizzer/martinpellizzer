Erica drakensbergensis

Erica drakensbergensis, commonly known as the Drakensberg heath, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor, acidic soils. Erica drakensbergensis produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is well adapted to the cool, moist climate of the Drakensberg Mountains.
