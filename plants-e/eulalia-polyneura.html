Eulalia polyneura

Eulalia polyneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pampas grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, white plumes that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Eulalia polyneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
