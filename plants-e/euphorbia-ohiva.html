Euphorbia ohiva

Euphorbia ohiva, commonly known as the Canary Island spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has small, green, opposite leaves and produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stems.

Euphorbia ohiva is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
