Elyhordeum piperi

Elyhordeum piperi, commonly known as the Russian tamarisk, is a perennial grass native to Central Asia.

It features tufted, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Elyhordeum piperi is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
