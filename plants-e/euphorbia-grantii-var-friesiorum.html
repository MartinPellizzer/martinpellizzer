Euphorbia grantii var. friesiorum

Euphorbia grantii var. friesiorum, commonly known as the Friesii euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem covered in dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
