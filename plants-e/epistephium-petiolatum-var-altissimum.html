Epistephium petiolatum var. altissimum

Epistephium petiolatum var. altissimum, commonly known as the tall epistephium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and have a smooth, waxy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
