Eccremocarpus scaber

Eccremocarpus scaber, commonly known as the rough senna, is a climbing legume native to Australia.

It features compound leaves with small, serrated leaflets and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant's fruit is a distinctive, elongated pod that turns brown as it matures. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting species for ecological and horticultural study.
