Elatostema barbatum

Elatostema barbatum, commonly known as the hairy elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky crevices. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and fine hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually greenish-white and clustered in dense inflorescences, often accompanied by a strong, spicy odor.

Elatostema barbatum is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
