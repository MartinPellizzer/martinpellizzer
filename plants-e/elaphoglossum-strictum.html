Elaphoglossum strictum

Elaphoglossum strictum, commonly known as the rigid holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, rigid fronds that are typically dark green and covered in fine, silky hairs. Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
