Eragrostis oldfieldii

Eragrostis oldfieldii, commonly known as Oldfield's lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as sandy soils and disturbed areas. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Eragrostis oldfieldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
