Eleocharis reznicekii

Eleocharis reznicekii, commonly known as the narrowleaf spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. This perennial herb typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
