Echeveria moranii

Echeveria moranii, commonly known as the Moran's Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are densely packed and covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched stems with clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its color and shape.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
