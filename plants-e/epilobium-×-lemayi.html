Epilobium × lemayi

Epilobium × lemayi, commonly known as the hybrid fireweed, is a species native to North America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. The plant features showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it has a robust root system.

Epilobium × lemayi is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to stabilize soil and thrive in disturbed habitats.
