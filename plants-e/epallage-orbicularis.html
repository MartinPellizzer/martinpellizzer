Epallage orbicularis

Epallage orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly round, leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a loose spike. Epallage orbicularis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
