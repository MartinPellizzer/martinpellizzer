Euphorbia barrelieri subsp. barrelieri

Euphorbia barrelieri subsp.

barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar. It features a thick, fleshy stem with a distinctive green color and a spiny texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cyathium, which is typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stem for survival.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
