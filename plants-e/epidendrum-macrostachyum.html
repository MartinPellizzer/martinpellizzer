Epidendrum macrostachyum

Epidendrum macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with long, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with numerous flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
