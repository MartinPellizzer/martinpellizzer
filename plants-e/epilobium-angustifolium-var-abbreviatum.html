Epilobium angustifolium var. abbreviatum

Epilobium angustifolium var. abbreviatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved willowherb, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length. The plant produces showy pink to reddish-purple flowers in spikes during the summer months. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for ecological studies and native landscaping.
