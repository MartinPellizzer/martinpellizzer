Echinodorus tenellus var. latifolius

Echinodorus tenellus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf anubias, is a hardy aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green leaves that are often used in aquariums for their aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular nutrient supplementation to maintain its vibrant foliage. It is a slow-growing plant that can be propagated through stem cuttings.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both beginner and advanced aquarists.
