Ericoides blandfordium

Ericoides blandfordium, commonly known as the Blanford's ericoid, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, acidic environments. Its small, evergreen leaves are densely packed, and it produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers.

Ericoides blandfordium is often found in coastal and mountainous regions where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
