Eugenia cormiflora

Eugenia cormiflora, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange floral bracts that surround small, white flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Eugenia cormiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its role in ecological restoration projects.
