Elaphoglossum convolutum

Elaphoglossum convolutum, commonly known as the twisted holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, lanceolate fronds that are often curled or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. Elaphoglossum convolutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
