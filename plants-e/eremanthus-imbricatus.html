Eremanthus imbricatus

Eremanthus imbricatus, commonly known as the yellow-flowered eremanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

Eremanthus imbricatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
