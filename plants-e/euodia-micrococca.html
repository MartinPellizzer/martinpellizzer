Euodia micrococca

Euodia micrococca, commonly known as the small-fruited lauraceae, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like India, Thailand, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by small, dark berries.

Euodia micrococca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
