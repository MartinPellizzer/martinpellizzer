Echeveria diffractens

Echeveria diffractens, commonly known as the Rainbow Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces vibrant pink to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
