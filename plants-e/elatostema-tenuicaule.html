Elatostema tenuicaule

Elatostema tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant has thin, wiry stems and small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are usually greenish-white and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema tenuicaule is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
