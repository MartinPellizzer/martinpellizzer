Eugenia ottonis

Eugenia ottonis, commonly known as the Otto's eugenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a strong aromatic scent.

Eugenia ottonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
