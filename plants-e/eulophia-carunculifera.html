Eulophia carunculifera

Eulophia carunculifera, commonly known as the red-lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive red lip that resembles a lip-shaped structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in forested areas with high humidity. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a spike, with a unique coloration that ranges from white to pink and red.

Eulophia carunculifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty in horticultural settings.
