Eurya megatrichocarpa

Eurya megatrichocarpa, commonly known as the woolly evergreen holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. This shrub thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
