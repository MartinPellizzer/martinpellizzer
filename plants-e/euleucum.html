Euleucum

Euleucum, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Euleucum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.
