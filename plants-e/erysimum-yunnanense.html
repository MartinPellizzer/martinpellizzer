Erysimum yunnanense

Erysimum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other well-known flowering plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Erysimum yunnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
