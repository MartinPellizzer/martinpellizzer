Epidendrum obcordatum

Epidendrum obcordatum, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety green leaves with purple veins. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive purple throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum obcordatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
