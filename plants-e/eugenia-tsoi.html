Eugenia tsoi

Eugenia tsoi, commonly known as the Tsoi's evergreen tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are edible but not widely consumed. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Eugenia tsoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
