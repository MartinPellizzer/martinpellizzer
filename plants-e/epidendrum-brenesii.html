Epidendrum brenesii

Epidendrum brenesii, commonly known as the Brena's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically has broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum brenesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
