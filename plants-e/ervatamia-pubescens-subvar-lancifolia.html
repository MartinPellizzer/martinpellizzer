Ervatamia pubescens subvar. lancifolia

Ervatamia pubescens subvar. lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ervatamia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a bluish-green hue and a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
