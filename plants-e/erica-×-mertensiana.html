Erica × mertensiana

Erica × mertensiana, commonly known as the Heath Erica, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to the unique flora of these ecosystems.

Erica × mertensiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soils in challenging environments.
