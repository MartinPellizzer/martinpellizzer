Eriostemon halmaturorus

Eriostemon halmaturorus, commonly known as the Halmaturus eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of around 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, which bloom during the cooler months of spring and autumn. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, an adaptation to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Eriostemon halmaturorus is well-suited to dry conditions and is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands.
