Eria densa

Eria densa, commonly known as the dense eria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Eria densa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
