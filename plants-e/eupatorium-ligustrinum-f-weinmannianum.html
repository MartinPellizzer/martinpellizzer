Eupatorium ligustrinum f. weinmannianum

Eupatorium ligustrinum f. weinmannianum, commonly known as the Weinmannianum eupatorium, is a variant of the common boneset plant.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture. This form is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
