Elaeagnus umbellata f. rotundifolia

Elaeagnus umbellata f. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf silverberry, is a shrub characterized by its dense, silvery foliage and small, yellowish flowers.

It features elliptic to ovate leaves that are often rounded at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically orange to red in color. It is native to eastern Asia and thrives in well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
