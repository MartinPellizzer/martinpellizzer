Eupatorium donnell-smithii

Eupatorium donnell-smithii, commonly known as the Appalachian eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. Eupatorium donnell-smithii is considered a rare and endangered species due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
