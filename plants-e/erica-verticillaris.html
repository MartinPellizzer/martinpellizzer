Erica verticillaris

Erica verticillaris, commonly known as the spiral heath, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Province of South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom from late winter to early spring. Erica verticillaris thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental flowers.
