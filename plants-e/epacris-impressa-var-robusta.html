Epacris impressa var. robusta

Epacris impressa var. robusta, commonly known as the robust epacris, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a member of the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, are pollinated primarily by birds and insects.

This variant is notable for its hardiness and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
