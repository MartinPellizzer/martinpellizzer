Ekimia ozcan-secmenii

Ekimia ozcan-secmenii, commonly known as the Turkish rose or Rosa turcica, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems and serrated, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and fragrance, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
