Elatostema kaweesakii

Elatostema kaweesakii, commonly known as the Thai prostrate nettle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats on the forest floor. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and produces tiny greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to competitive understory conditions in its native habitat.
