Epidendrum prismatocarpum

Epidendrum prismatocarpum, commonly known as the prism-fruit orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum prismatocarpum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
