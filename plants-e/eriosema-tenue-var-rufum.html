Eriosema tenue var. rufum

Eriosema tenue var. rufum, commonly known as the red-stemmed eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, and it is often found in grassy or rocky habitats.
