Euphorbia pekinensis var. sinanensis

Euphorbia pekinensis var. sinanensis, commonly known as the Sinanensis spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor and rock garden settings.
