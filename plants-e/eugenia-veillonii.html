Eugenia veillonii

Eugenia veillonii, commonly known as the Veillonii eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically red or purple when ripe.
