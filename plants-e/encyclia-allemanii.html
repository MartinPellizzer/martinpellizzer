Encyclia allemanii

Encyclia allemanii, commonly known as the Alleman's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Encyclia allemanii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
