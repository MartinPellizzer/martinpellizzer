Epidendrum pernambucense

Epidendrum pernambucense, commonly known as the Pernambuco Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
