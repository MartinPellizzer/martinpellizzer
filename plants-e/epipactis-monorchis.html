Epipactis monorchis

Epipactis monorchis, commonly known as the single-ovary helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.

Epipactis monorchis is a perennial herb that produces a single ovary, hence its common name.
