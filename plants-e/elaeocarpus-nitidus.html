Elaeocarpus nitidus

Elaeocarpus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and showy white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. It is often found in lowland rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
