Eriocaulon henryanum f. viviparum

Eriocaulon henryanum f. viviparum, commonly known as the viviparous flat sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. This variety is notable for its vivipary, a reproductive trait where the seeds germinate while still attached to the parent plant.
