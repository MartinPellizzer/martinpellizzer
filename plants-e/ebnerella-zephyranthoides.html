Ebnerella zephyranthoides

Ebnerella zephyranthoides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and silver-tinted leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has a low, spreading growth habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silvery hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Ebnerella zephyranthoides is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
