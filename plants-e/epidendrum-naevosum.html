Epidendrum naevosum

Epidendrum naevosum, commonly known as the spotted Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum naevosum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
