Eragrostis olivacea

Eragrostis olivacea, commonly known as olive bluestem, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Eragrostis olivacea is also valued for its role in soil stabilization and erosion control.
