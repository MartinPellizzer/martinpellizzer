Euodia rugosa

Euodia rugosa, commonly known as the rough-leafed euodia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Thailand. Euodia rugosa is often grown for its ornamental value and has a long history of use in herbal remedies.
