Euphorbia × whitesloaneana

Euphorbia × whitesloaneana, commonly known as the White Sloane's Spurge, is a hybrid plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized flower-like structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
