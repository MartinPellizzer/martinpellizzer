Epipremnum meeboldii

Epipremnum meeboldii, commonly known as the Variegated Pothos, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that often display variegation of white and green. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to surfaces, making it a popular choice for indoor hanging baskets or shelves.

Epipremnum meeboldii is relatively low-maintenance, making it an ideal plant for beginners in the world of houseplants.
