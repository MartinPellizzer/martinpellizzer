Eustephia hugoei

Eustephia hugoei, commonly known as the Hugoe's mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often submerged or floating on the water surface. It thrives in shallow, still waters and is often found in marshy areas.

Eustephia hugoei plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
