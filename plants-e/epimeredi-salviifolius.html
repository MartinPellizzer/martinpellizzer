Epimeredi salviifolius

Epimeredi salviifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
