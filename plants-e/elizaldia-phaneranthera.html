Elizaldia phaneranthera

Elizaldia phaneranthera, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow panicles that bloom in the spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Elizaldia phaneranthera is also valued for its fast growth and ability to tolerate urban environments.
