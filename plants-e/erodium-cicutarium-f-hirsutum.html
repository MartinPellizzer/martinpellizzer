Erodium cicutarium f. hirsutum

Erodium cicutarium f. hirsutum, commonly known as hairy bedstraw, is a flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its hairy stems and small, pinkish-white flowers. The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds, making it a common weed in many regions.
