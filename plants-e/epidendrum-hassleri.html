Epidendrum hassleri

Epidendrum hassleri, commonly known as the Hassler's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are broad and pointed, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum hassleri is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
