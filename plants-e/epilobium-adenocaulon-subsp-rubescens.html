Epilobium adenocaulon subsp. rubescens

Epilobium adenocaulon subsp.

rubescens, commonly known as redstem willow herb, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils.

The plant features slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, especially when mature. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and are usually pink to purple in hue.

It is characterized by lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.
