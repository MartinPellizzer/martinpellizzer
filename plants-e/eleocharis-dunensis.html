Eleocharis dunensis

Eleocharis dunensis, commonly known as the Dun Mountain spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. This species typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and shallow water areas. Its roots help stabilize soil, contributing to the ecological health of its environment.

Eleocharis dunensis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
