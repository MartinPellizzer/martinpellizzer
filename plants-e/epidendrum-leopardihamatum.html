Epidendrum leopardihamatum

Epidendrum leopardihamatum, commonly known as the Leopard Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous growth habit, featuring a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright yellow and orange petals, often adorned with intricate patterns resembling a leopard's spots. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum leopardihamatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
