Eriogonum Corymbosum Var. Ambiguum

Ambiguous indian blanket, scientifically known as Eriogonum corymbosum var. ambiguum, is a striking wildflower native to the western united states. it thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in open habitats like grasslands and mountain slopes. the plant is named for its intricate and somewhat uncertain floral structure, which varies subtly between individuals. its vibrant colors and unique form make it a popular subject for botanists and gardeners alike. this plant plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and adding visual interest to natural landscapes.
