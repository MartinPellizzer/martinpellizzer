Euphrasia rostkoviana f. borealis

Euphrasia rostkoviana f. borealis, commonly known as the northern eyebright, is a subspecies of the eyebright plant native to northern regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing herbaceous plant with small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

Botanically, it is characterized by its medicinal properties and is part of the Scrophulariaceae family.
