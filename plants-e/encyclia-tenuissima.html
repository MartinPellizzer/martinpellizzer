Encyclia tenuissima

Encyclia tenuissima, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Encyclia tenuissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
