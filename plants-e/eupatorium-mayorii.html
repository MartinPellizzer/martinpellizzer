Eupatorium mayorii

Eupatorium mayorii, commonly known as the Chinese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Eupatorium mayorii is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
