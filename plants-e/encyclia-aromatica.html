Encyclia aromatica

Encyclia aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with brown or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia aromatica is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique aromatic properties.
