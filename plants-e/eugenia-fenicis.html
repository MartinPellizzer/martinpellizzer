Eugenia fenicis

Eugenia fenicis, commonly known as the flame tree or fire tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its fruits are small, round drupes that turn from green to red when mature.

Eugenia fenicis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
