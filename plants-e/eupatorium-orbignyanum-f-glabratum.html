Eupatorium orbignyanum f. glabratum

Eupatorium orbignyanum f. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

The smooth eupatorium is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
