Erysimum nuttallii

Erysimum nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its hardy and drought-resistant nature.
