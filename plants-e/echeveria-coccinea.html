Echeveria coccinea

Echeveria coccinea, commonly known as the red velvet sedum, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces showy, pinkish-red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and vibrant color, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
