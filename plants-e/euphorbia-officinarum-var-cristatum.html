Euphorbia officinarum var. cristatum

Euphorbia officinarum var. cristatum, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, branching structure that resembles a crown, hence its common name. The plant has thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its leaves are typically small and may be absent in some cultivars, giving it a more spiky appearance.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value to the plant.
