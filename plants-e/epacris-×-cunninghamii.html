Epacris × cunninghamii

Epacris × cunninghamii, commonly known as the hybrid epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and sclerophyllous forests, preferring well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and red, bloom in spring and attract nectar-feeding insects.

Epacris × cunninghamii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively low maintenance once established.
