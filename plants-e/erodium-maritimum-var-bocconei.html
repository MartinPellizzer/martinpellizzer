Erodium maritimum var. bocconei

Erodium maritimum var. bocconei, commonly known as the Mediterranean bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pinkish to purple flowers. The plant typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in maritime environments.
