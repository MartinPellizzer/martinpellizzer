Enemion biternatum

Enemion biternatum, commonly known as the three-leaved bittercress, is a small herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. Its small white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

The species is characterized by its hairy stems and capsule fruits that release tiny seeds.
