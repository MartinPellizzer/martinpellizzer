Erythronium dens-canis

Erythronium dens-canis, commonly known as the dense-canine erythronium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, nodding, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink or yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Erythronium dens-canis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring bloom.
