Eriocaulon sparganioides

Eriocaulon sparganioides, commonly known as the spike moss, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant typically grows in wet, acidic environments such as bogs and marshes. It has rhizomes that help anchor it in the soil and facilitate vegetative propagation.
