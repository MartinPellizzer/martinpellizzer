Eulophia mechowii

Eulophia mechowii, commonly known as the Mechow's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often in mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive, spurred lip.

Eulophia mechowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
