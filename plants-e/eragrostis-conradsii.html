Eragrostis conradsii

Eragrostis conradsii, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted inflorescences that produce small, spikelet-like flowers. This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
