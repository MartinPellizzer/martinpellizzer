Eutoca strictiflora

Eutoca strictiflora, commonly known as the rigid-flowered eutoca, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
