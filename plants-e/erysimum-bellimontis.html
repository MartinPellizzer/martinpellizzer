Erysimum bellimontis

Erysimum bellimontis, commonly known as the Bellmont's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat woody.

Erysimum bellimontis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
