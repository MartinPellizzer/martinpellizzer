Elaeocarpus jugahanus

Elaeocarpus jugahanus, commonly known as the Java elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are often dispersed by animals.

Elaeocarpus jugahanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
