Euphorbia cremersii var. cremersii

Euphorbia cremersii var. cremersii, commonly known as the Cape York euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its branched, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typically surrounded by leaf-like bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
