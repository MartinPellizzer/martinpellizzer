Erica carnea var. hybrida

Erica carnea var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid heather, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming throughout the year. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are dark green and arranged in a compact, bushy form. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
