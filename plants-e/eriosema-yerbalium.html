Eriosema yerbalium

Eriosema yerbalium, commonly known as the yellow eriosema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are often glandular, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a spurred structure at the base of the petals.

Eriosema yerbalium is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
