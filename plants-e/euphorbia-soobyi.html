Euphorbia soobyi

Euphorbia soobyi, commonly known as the Cape York euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branched stem structure with thick, fleshy leaves that help store water. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
