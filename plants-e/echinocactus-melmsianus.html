Echinocactus melmsianus

Echinocactus melmsianus, commonly known as the Melmsian hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in prominent, sharp spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which bloom at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
