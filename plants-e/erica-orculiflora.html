Erica orculiflora

Erica orculiflora, commonly known as the orchid heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Erica orculiflora thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
