Echinocystis wrightii

Echinocystis wrightii, commonly known as Wright's spiny cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its spiny stems and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by green, spiny fruits. The fruit's exterior is covered in small, rough tubercles, and it contains edible, fleshy seeds.

It is native to the southern United States and thrives in warm, humid climates.
