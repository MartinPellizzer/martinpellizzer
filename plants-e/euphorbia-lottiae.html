Euphorbia lottiae

Euphorbia lottiae, commonly known as the Lottia euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branched, cylindrical stem covered in small, spiny tubercles. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green cyathia, which are its modified inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
