Eria queenslandica

Eria queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland eriopsis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.
