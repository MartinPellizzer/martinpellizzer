Eulophia streptopetala var. rueppelii

Eulophia streptopetala var. rueppelii, commonly known as the Rueppel's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features striking, elongated inflorescences with numerous pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant has strap-like leaves and a rhizomatous root system, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
