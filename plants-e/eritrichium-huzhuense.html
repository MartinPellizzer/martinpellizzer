Eritrichium huzhuense

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. It has lance-shaped leaves with a fuzzy texture and produces small, clustered inflorescences.
