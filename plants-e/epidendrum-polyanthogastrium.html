Epidendrum polyanthogastrium

Epidendrum polyanthogastrium, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically has elongated, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Epidendrum polyanthogastrium is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
