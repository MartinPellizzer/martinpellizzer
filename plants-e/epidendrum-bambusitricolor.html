Epidendrum bambusitricolor

Epidendrum bambusitricolor, commonly known as the Bamboo Leaf Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that resemble bamboo leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
