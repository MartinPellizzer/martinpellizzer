Euonymus bodinieri

Euonymus bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese spindletree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features opposite, simple, ovate to elliptic leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Euonymus bodinieri is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
