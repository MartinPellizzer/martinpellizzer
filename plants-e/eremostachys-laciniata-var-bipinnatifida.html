Eremostachys laciniata var. bipinnatifida

Eremostachys laciniata var. bipinnatifida, commonly known as the finely dissected eremostachys, is a member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate, grass-like appearance. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or red, adding ornamental value.

It is often grown as a drought-tolerant ornamental in gardens and xeriscapes.
