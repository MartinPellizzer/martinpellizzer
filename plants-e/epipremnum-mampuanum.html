Epipremnum mampuanum

Epipremnum mampuanum, commonly known as the Mampuanum philodendron, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its ability to climb using aerial roots, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets or trellises.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a favored houseplant among indoor gardeners.
