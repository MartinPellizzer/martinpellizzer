Epigynum odoratum

Epigynum odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant epigynum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a mild aromatic scent.

The botanical features of Epigynum odoratum make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
