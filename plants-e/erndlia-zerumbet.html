Erndlia zerumbet

Erndlia zerumbet, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height, giving it a bamboo-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that eventually develop into bright red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are long and arching, contributing to its lush, ornamental look.

Erndlia zerumbet is well-suited for humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
