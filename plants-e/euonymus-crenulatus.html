Euonymus crenulatus

Euonymus crenulatus, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that have a waxy, glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are ornamental. It is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Euonymus crenulatus is also valued in horticulture for its use as a ground cover or in landscaping.
