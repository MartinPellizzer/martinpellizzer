Eriocaulon statices

Eriocaulon statices, commonly known as the marsh pennywort, is a small aquatic plant native to wetland environments in the Americas.

It has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in the summer.
