Elatine macrocarpa

Elatine macrocarpa, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are usually less than 1 cm in length. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are often submerged or floating on the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and ditches.

Elatine macrocarpa is typically found in temperate regions and is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed aquatic habitats.
