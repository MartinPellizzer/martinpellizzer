Epipactis palustris f. robusta

Epipactis palustris f. robusta, commonly known as the robust marsh helleborine, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often in shaded environments. The plant features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It is characterized by its robust growth compared to the typical form of Epipactis palustris.

This orchid is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
