Epipactis × trikalana

Epipactis × trikalana, commonly known as the hybrid marsh helleborine, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Epipactis palustris and Epipactis helleborine.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wet meadows and bogs. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent lips. It is characterized by its complex inflorescence and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
