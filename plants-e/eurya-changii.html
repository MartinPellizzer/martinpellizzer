Eurya changii

Eurya changii, commonly known as the Chinese holly or Chinese wax tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, showy flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Eurya changii thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
