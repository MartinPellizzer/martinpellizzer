Emilia hiernii

Emilia hiernii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Emilia hiernii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
