Epidendrum simile

Epidendrum simile, commonly known as the Christmas Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that bloom in a raceme during the holiday season. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal climates. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-like, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

Epidendrum simile is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
