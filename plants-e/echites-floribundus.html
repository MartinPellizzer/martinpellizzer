Echites floribundus

Echites floribundus, commonly known as the showy echites, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant is a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
