Epidendrum microrigidiflorum

Epidendrum microrigidiflorum, commonly known as the rigid-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, white to pale yellow blooms that are often adorned with intricate patterns. Its roots are epiphytic, allowing it to grow on trees in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum microrigidiflorum prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
