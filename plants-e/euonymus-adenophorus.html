Euonymus adenophorus

Euonymus adenophorus, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

Euonymus adenophorus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
