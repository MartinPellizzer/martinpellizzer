Eulophia pulchra var. actinomorpha

Eulophia pulchra var. actinomorpha, commonly known as the showy eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its flowers typically display a combination of white, yellow, and green hues, with a prominent lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its striking appearance.
