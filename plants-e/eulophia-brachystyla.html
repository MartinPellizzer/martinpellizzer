Eulophia brachystyla

Eulophia brachystyla, commonly known as the short-styled eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Thailand, Vietnam, and the Philippines. This terrestrial orchid typically grows in moist, shaded environments within forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a mix of white and green hues.

The plant's unique morphology and habitat preferences make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
