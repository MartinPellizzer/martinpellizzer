Erica natalensis

Erica natalensis, commonly known as the Natal heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related species. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica natalensis is well adapted to harsh, sunny environments and is an important component of fynbos vegetation.
