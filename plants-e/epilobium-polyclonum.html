Epilobium polyclonum

Epilobium polyclonum, commonly known as the clustered willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often calcareous soils. The plant features showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other notable species like willow herb and evening primrose.
