Eustrephus latifolius f. leucanthus

Eustrephus latifolius f. leucanthus, commonly known as the white-flowered broadleaf eustrephus, is a horticultural form of the broader Eustrephus latifolius species.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and striking white flowers that contrast with its green foliage. This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden environments.
