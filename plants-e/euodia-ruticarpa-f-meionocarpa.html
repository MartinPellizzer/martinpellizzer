Euodia ruticarpa f. meionocarpa

Euodia ruticarpa f. meionocarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited Chinese sandalwood, is a shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by fragrant, woody fruits. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The variety meionocarpa is distinguished by its smaller fruit size compared to the typical form.
