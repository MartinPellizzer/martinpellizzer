Elaeagnus Pungens Var. Reflexa

Elaeagnus pungens var. reflexa is a hardy shrub known for its ornamental value and adaptability. It features silvery foliage and fragrant flowers that attract pollinators. This variety is particularly noted for its reflexed leaves, which give it a unique appearance. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often used in landscaping. Its resilience makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized areas.
