Eriosema arenicola

Eriosema arenicola, commonly known as the sand eriosema, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, succulent stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, soft hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Eriosema arenicola is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
