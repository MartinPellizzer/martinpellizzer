Eriocaulon echinulatum var. tenue, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its flowers are usually inconspicuous, with small, three-parted perianths.
