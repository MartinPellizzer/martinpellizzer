Epidendrum embreei

Epidendrum embreei, commonly known as the Embree's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or green with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, helping it store water in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum embreei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
