Escallonia hypoglauca

Escallonia hypoglauca, commonly known as the silver-leaved escallonia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that has a distinctive silvery-blue hue on the underside of the leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it suitable for hedges or container planting.
