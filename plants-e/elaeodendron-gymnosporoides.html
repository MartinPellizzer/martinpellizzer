Elaeodendron gymnosporoides

Elaeodendron gymnosporoides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed elaeodendron, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, evergreen leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, a typical feature of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Elaeodendron gymnosporoides is often found in shaded, moist environments in tropical and subtropical regions.
