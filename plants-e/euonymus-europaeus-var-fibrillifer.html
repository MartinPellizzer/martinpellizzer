Euonymus europaeus var. fibrillifer

Euonymus europaeus var. fibrillifer, commonly known as the European spindle tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red, berry-like fruits that persist into winter. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, often peeling in thin, fibrous strips.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
