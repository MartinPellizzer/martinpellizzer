Eragrostis sporoboloides

Eragrostis sporoboloides, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Silver bluestem is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
