Encyclia flabellifera

Encyclia flabellifera, commonly known as the Flabellifera Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive flabellate (fan-shaped) lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia flabellifera is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
