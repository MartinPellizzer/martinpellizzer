Echeveria westii

Echeveria westii, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring or summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
