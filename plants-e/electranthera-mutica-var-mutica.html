Electranthera mutica var. mutica

Electranthera mutica var. mutica, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae, native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that often appear in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its attractive leaves.

The leaves are typically dark green and have a soft, textured surface that gives the plant its common name.
