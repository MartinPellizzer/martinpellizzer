Epidendrum rubrum

Epidendrum rubrum, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with yellow markings, which are typically borne on long, pendulous stems. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum rubrum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
