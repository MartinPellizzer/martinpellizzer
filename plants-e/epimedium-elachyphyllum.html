Epimedium elachyphyllum

Epimedium elachyphyllum, commonly known as the barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and possesses trifoliate leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Epimedium elachyphyllum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
