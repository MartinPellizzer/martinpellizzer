Eriosyce curvispina var. mutabilis

Eriosyce curvispina var. mutabilis, commonly known as the variable spiny cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it stores water in its fleshy stems for survival in dry conditions.

This variation is notable for its adaptability to different environmental conditions within its range.
