Euphorbia otjipembana subsp. otjipembana

Euphorbia otjipembana subsp.

otjipembana, commonly known as the Otjipemba euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to Namibia. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and lobed leaves.

The plant produces a unique yellowish-green cyathium, which is its specialized inflorescence. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and leaves to survive drought conditions.

This subspecies is often found in rocky and sandy soils, thriving in regions with low rainfall.
