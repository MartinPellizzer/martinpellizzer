Erysimum tenuissimum

Erysimum tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its cheerful blossoms.
