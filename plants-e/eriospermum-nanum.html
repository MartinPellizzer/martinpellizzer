Eriospermum nanum

Eriospermum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf eriosperm, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, creeping stem with dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has small, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its compact size and unique floral structure, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
