Eugenioides uniflorum

Eugenioides uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower eugenioid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and a single, showy floral cluster at the tip of each branch. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by small, edible berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Eugenioides uniflorum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
