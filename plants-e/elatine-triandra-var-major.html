Elatine triandra var. major

Elatine triandra var. major, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and ditches. The plant has delicate, branching stems with small, opposite leaves that are often submerged. It produces tiny, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
