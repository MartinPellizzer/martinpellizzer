Erophila americana var. bardinii

Erophila americana var. bardinii, commonly known as the Bardini's erophila, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers.
