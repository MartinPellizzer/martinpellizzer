Epacris impressa f. nivalis

Epacris impressa f. nivalis, commonly known as the Snowy Epacris, is a unique variant of the Epacris genus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its white to pale pink flowers, which contrast strikingly with its silver-green foliage. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments, often found in moist, well-drained soils within forested areas. The leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, silky hair, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
