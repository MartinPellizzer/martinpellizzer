Echeveria quitensis var. quitensis

Echeveria quitensis var. quitensis, commonly known as the Quillota echeveria, is a succulent native to the coastal regions of central Chile.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers on tall, branching stalks during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
