Encyclia acuta

Encyclia acuta, commonly known as the sharp-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, pendulous flowers with vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are hermaphroditic, meaning they contain both male and female reproductive structures.

Encyclia acuta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
