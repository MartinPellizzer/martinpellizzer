Euphrasia mollis var. laurentiana

Euphrasia mollis var. laurentiana, commonly known as Laurentian eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems with opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish-green flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
