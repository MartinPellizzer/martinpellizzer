Echites splendens

Echites splendens, commonly known as the splendour cactus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and clusters of vibrant yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Cactaceae family and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths.

Echites splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
