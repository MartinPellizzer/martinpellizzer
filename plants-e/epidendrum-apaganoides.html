Epidendrum apaganoides

Epidendrum apaganoides, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum apaganoides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
