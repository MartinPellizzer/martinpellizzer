Echeveria attenuata

Echeveria attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf jade plant, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are narrow and pointed. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Echeveria attenuata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
