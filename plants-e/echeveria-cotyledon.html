Echeveria cotyledon

Echeveria cotyledon, commonly known as the "Cotyledon Echeveria," is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often tinged with purple or pink at the edges. The plant produces tall, branching stems with clusters of vibrant purple or pink flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its compact shape.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
