Epidendrum alsum

Epidendrum alsum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and long, arching inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, velvety flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum alsum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
