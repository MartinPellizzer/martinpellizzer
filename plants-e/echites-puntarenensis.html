Echites puntarenensis

Echites puntarenensis, commonly known as the Puntarenas tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in coastal areas and dry forests. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in greenish-yellow inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
