Eragrostis nindensis

Eragrostis nindensis, commonly known as Nind's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and savannas. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a dense panicle of spikelets, which are small, clustered flower clusters.

Eragrostis nindensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fine texture and delicate appearance.
