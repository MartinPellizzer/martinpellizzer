Erica coccinea var. intermedia

Erica coccinea var. intermedia, commonly known as the red heather or pink heather, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, depending on the variety. The plant produces needle-like leaves that are typically a dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
