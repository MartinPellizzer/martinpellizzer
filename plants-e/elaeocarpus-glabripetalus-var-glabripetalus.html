Elaeocarpus glabripetalus var. glabripetalus

Elaeocarpus glabripetalus var. glabripetalus, commonly known as the glabrous-petaled elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds, typical of the genus Elaeocarpus.
