Elaphoglossum plumieri

Elaphoglossum plumieri, commonly known as the plumier's holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a finely divided structure. Elaphoglossum plumieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
