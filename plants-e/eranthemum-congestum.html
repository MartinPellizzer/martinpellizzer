Eranthemum congestum

Eranthemum congestum, commonly known as the clustered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has succulent leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Eranthemum congestum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
