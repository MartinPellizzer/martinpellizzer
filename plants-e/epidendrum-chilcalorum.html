Epidendrum chilcalorum

Epidendrum chilcalorum, commonly known as the Chilca orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum chilcalorum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
