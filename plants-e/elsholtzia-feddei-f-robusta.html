Elsholtzia feddei f. robusta

Elsholtzia feddei f. robusta, commonly known as the robust elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a strong, minty fragrance.

This species is adapted to dry climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
