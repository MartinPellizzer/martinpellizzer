Eulophia malangana

Eulophia malangana, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with red or purple markings. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in the region's seasonal climate.

Eulophia malangana is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
