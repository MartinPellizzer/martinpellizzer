Euphorbia macrostegia var. pauciradiata

Euphorbia macrostegia var. pauciradiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with dense, fleshy stems that are often tinged with reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by green bracts, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle, helping to reduce water loss in arid environments.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
