Erodium flexuosum

Erodium flexuosum, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.

Erodium flexuosum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
