Epidendrum stroebelii

Epidendrum stroebelii, commonly known as the Stroebel's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. Its blooms are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green hues, with a distinctive yellow lip.

Epidendrum stroebelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
