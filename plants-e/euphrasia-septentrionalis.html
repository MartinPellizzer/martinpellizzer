Euphrasia septentrionalis

Euphrasia septentrionalis, commonly known as northern eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent, spurred petals that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.
