Eragrostis cyperoides

Eragrostis cyperoides, commonly known as slender lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Eragrostis cyperoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
