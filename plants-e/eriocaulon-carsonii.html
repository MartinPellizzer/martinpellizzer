Eriocaulon carsonii

Eriocaulon carsonii, commonly known as Carson's needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to specific regions of the United States.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Eriocaulon carsonii is often found in wetland or moist soil environments, reflecting its hydrophytic adaptations.
