Eriogonum umbellatum var. tolmieanum

Eriogonum umbellatum var. tolmieanum, commonly known as the Tolmie's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink blooms in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its leaves are generally gray-green, and it is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
