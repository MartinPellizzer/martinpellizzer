Erica goatcheriana

Erica goatcheriana, commonly known as the coastal heath, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in sandy or rocky coastal habitats.

Erica goatcheriana is well adapted to harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
