Euterpe badiocarpa

Euterpe badiocarpa, commonly known as the red-barked palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, edible fruits. The fruit has a fibrous outer layer and a sweet, aromatic pulp.

It is often found in wet tropical forests and plays a role in local ecosystems and traditional uses.
