Enckea unguiculata

Enckea unguiculata, commonly known as the red-flowered enckea, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Enckea unguiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
