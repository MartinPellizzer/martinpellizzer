Euphorbia platyclada var. hardyi

Euphorbia platyclada var. hardyi, commonly known as the Hardy Euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique flower-like structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid climates.

Its slow growth rate and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
