Eriocaulon ligulatum var. dimerum

Eriocaulon ligulatum var. dimerum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flat sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its botanical structure and habitat make it an important component of aquatic and wetland ecosystems.
