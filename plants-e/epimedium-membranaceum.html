Epimedium membranaceum

Epimedium membranaceum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a distinctive white or pale bloom, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage.

Epimedium membranaceum is also noted for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its low maintenance requirements.
