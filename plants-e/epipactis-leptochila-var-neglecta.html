Epipactis leptochila var. neglecta

Epipactis leptochila var. neglecta, commonly known as the slender helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and meadows, favoring rich, loamy soils. The plant features a slender, leafless stem that bears a single, pale green to yellowish-green flower with a distinctive, spurred lip. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
