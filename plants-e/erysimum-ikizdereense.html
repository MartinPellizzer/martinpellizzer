Erysimum ikizdereense

Erysimum ikizdereense, commonly known as the Ikizdere wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The species is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers and basal rosette of leaves. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
