Euphorbia baleensis

Euphorbia baleensis, commonly known as the Bale Euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces unique cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences containing both male and female flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
