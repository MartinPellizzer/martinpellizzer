Eruca deserti

Eruca deserti, commonly known as the desert rocket, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has hairy, lobed leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. Eruca deserti is adapted to thrive in sandy soils and can tolerate extreme temperatures.
