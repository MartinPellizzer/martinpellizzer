Erodium × keichium

Erodium × keichium, commonly known as the common stinkcup, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its small, kidney-shaped leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, roadsides, and waste areas. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

The plant contains compounds that can cause irritation to the skin and mucous membranes, making it potentially toxic to humans and animals.
