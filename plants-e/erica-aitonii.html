Erica aitonii

Erica aitonii, commonly known as the Scottish heath, is a flowering plant native to the Scottish Highlands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica aitonii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and moorland environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
