Euphrasia hirtella var. paupera

Euphrasia hirtella var. paupera, commonly known as the poor euphrasia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink.
