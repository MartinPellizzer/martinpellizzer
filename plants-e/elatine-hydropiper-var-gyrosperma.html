Elatine hydropiper var. gyrosperma

Elatine hydropiper var. gyrosperma, commonly known as water pepper, is a small annual herb belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, stream banks, and disturbed soils.

Its seeds are small and often dispersed by water, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.
