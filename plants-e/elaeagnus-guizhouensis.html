Elaeagnus guizhouensis

Elaeagnus guizhouensis, commonly known as the Guizhou silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, followed by edible berries that are rich in nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Elaeagnus guizhouensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
