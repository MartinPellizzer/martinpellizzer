Euryale

Euryale, commonly known as the water caltrop, is a aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, floating fruit that resembles a nut. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its leaves are narrow and submerged, while its flowers are small and yellow.

Euryale is often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.
