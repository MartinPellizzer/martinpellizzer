Eria lasiopetala

Eria lasiopetala, commonly known as the rough-leaved eria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of white or pale yellow.

Eria lasiopetala is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
