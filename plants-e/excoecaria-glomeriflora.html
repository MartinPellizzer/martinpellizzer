Excoecaria glomeriflora

Excoecaria glomeriflora, commonly known as the cluster-flowered croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish or variegated in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and landscapes.
