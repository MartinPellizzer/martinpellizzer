Eupatorium nudispermum

Eupatorium nudispermum, commonly known as slender boneset, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
