Erigeron Latus

Erigeron latus, commonly known as the broadleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western united states and parts of mexico. it belongs to the asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. this plant is characterized by its showy blue to purple flowers and hairy stems that help it retain moisture. it thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. due to its beauty and adaptability, it is often found in wildflower gardens and natural landscapes.
