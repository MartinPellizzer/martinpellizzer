Epidendrum crenulidifforme

Epidendrum crenulidifforme, commonly known as the crested epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy-edged leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.

Epidendrum crenulidifforme is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its resilience and adaptability.
