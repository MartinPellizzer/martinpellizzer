Eragrostis milnei

Eragrostis milnei, commonly known as Milne's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a potential candidate for ecological restoration projects.
