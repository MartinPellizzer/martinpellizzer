Euonymus europaeus var. hamiltonianus

Euonymus europaeus var. hamiltonianus, commonly known as the European spindle tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive orange-red fruits that appear in clusters, which are a key feature in its botanical description. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly toothed margin. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden environments.
