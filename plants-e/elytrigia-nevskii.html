Elytrigia nevskii

Elytrigia nevskii, commonly known as Nevskii's bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the cold climates of Eastern Europe and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, blue-green inflorescences. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a notable species in ecological studies.

The plant's genetic diversity contributes to its resilience in fluctuating climatic conditions.
