Euphoria informis

Euphoria informis, commonly known as the informal euphorbia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its succulent, branching stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is often covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss.

Euphoria informis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
