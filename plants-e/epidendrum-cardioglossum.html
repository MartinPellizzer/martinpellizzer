Epidendrum cardioglossum

Epidendrum cardioglossum, commonly known as the heart-lipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that bear clusters of pendulous, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a distinctive red lip. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
