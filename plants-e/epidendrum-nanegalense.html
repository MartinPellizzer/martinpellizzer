Epidendrum nanegalense

Epidendrum nanegalense, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces a cluster of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late summer. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid rainforest environment.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a warm, humid climate.
