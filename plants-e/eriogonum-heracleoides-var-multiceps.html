Eriogonum heracleoides var. multiceps

Eriogonum heracleoides var. multiceps, commonly known as the multiceps leafcurl, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid environments.
