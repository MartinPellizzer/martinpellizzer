Eremocarya micrantha var. micrantha

Eremocarya micrantha var. micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremocarya, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh environments. It typically grows in rocky soils and is often found in steppe and desert landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local flora in its native habitat.
