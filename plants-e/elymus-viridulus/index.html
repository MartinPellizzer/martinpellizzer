Elymus Viridulus

Elymus viridulus is a grass species commonly found in moist environments across northern regions. It is known for its slender stems and distinctive blue-green foliage. This plant plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Often mistaken for other grasses, it has unique growth patterns that set it apart. Its ecological significance makes it a valuable subject for botanical study.
