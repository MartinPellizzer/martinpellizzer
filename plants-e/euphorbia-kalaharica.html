Euphorbia kalaharica

Euphorbia kalaharica, commonly known as the Kalahari euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant has adapted to arid environments with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
