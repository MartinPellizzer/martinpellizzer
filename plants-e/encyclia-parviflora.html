Encyclia parviflora

Encyclia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Encyclia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
