Erysimum linearifolium

Erysimum linearifolium, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to wildflower gardens.
