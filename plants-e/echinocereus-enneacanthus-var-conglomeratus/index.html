Echinocereus Enneacanthus Var. Conglomeratus

Echinocereus enneacanthus var. conglomeratus is a striking cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Known for its dense clusters of spiny, red to orange flowers, this plant is a favorite among desert gardeners. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well-suited for arid climates. The variety's compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it both a beautiful and resilient addition to any xeriscape landscape.
