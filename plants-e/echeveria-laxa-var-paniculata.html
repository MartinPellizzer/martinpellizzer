Echeveria laxa var. paniculata

Echeveria laxa var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicle echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces showy inflorescences of pink or purple flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its compact form.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
