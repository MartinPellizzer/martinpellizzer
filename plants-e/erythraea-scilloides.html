Erythraea scilloides

Erythraea scilloides, commonly known as the red lily or red bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are often clustered together. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erythraea scilloides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
