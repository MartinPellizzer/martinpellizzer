Elaeocarpus japonicus var. lantsangensis

Elaeocarpus japonicus var. lantsangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan elaeocarpus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain a single seed.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.
