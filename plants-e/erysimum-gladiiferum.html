Erysimum gladiiferum

Erysimum gladiiferum, commonly known as the swordleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum gladiiferum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
