Eriocaulon yaoshanense

Eriocaulon yaoshanense, commonly known as the Yaoshan spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, where it grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered with fine, hairy structures.

Eriocaulon yaoshanense is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
