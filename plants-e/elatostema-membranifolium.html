Elatostema membranifolium

Elatostema membranifolium, commonly known as the paper-leaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, membranous leaves that are often heart-shaped or oval in appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in terminal inflorescences.

Elatostema membranifolium is a member of the Elatostemaceae family and is known for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
