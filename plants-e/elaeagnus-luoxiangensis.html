Elaeagnus luoxiangensis

Elaeagnus luoxiangensis, commonly known as the Luoxiang silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Elaeagnus luoxiangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in arid landscapes.
