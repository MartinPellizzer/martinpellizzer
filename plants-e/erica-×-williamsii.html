Erica × williamsii

Erica × williamsii, commonly known as the silver-leaved heath, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with a silvery-gray hue. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica × williamsii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
