Eriosyce krainziana

Eriosyce krainziana, commonly known as the Krainz's eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
