Erica rubens

Erica rubens, commonly known as the common heather, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. Erica rubens is well adapted to harsh environments, often found in heathlands and moorlands.

Its evergreen foliage and early spring blooms make it a valuable component of many ecosystems.
