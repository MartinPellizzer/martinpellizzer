Echinops mosulensis

Echinops mosulensis, commonly known as the Mosul thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, spherical seed heads. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The flowers are small and yellow, appearing in the summer months.
