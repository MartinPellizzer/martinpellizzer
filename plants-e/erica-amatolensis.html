Erica amatolensis

Erica amatolensis, commonly known as the Amatole heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring.

The plant is adapted to acidic conditions and is often found in fynbos vegetation.
