Eriachne helmsii

Eriachne helmsii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy environments, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale blue or greenish flowers.

Eriachne helmsii is adapted to withstand salt spray and is often found in dune ecosystems.
