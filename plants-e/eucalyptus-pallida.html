Eucalyptus pallida

Eucalyptus pallida, commonly known as the white mallee, is a species of eucalyptus native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale grey to white bark that peels in long, narrow strips. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in groups, attracting various pollinators.
