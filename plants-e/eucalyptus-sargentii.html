Eucalyptus sargentii

Eucalyptus sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's malleeflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Eucalyptus sargentii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing nectar for various pollinators.
