Elegia fenestrata

Elegia fenestrata, commonly known as the window leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, window-like leaf patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Elegia fenestrata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
