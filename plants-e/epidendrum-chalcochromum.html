Epidendrum chalcochromum

Epidendrum chalcochromum, commonly known as the Copperleaf Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring broad, dark green leaves with a metallic sheen that reflects light. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This epiphytic orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
