Erysimum capitatum var. amoenum

Erysimum capitatum var. amoenum, commonly known as the California Wallflower, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of California and southern Oregon.

It is a low-growing annual herb with bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features alternate, pinnately lobed leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal dunes and grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
