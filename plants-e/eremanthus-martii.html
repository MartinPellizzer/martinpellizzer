Eremanthus martii

Eremanthus martii, commonly known as the mountain pittosporum, is a shrub native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in cloud forests.

Eremanthus martii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
