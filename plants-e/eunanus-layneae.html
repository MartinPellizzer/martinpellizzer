Eunanus layneae

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals and a unique floral structure that resembles a ghostly, ethereal form. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in the understory of forests. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with a distinct scent that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Eunanus layneae is considered critically endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
