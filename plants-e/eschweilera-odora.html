Eschweilera odora

Eschweilera odora, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen or the Guianan rosewood, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its aromatic properties, with a sweet, woody scent that is often used in perfumery. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and unique fragrance, making it a sought-after material in traditional crafts.

Eschweilera odora plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
