Eriospermum tortuosum var. depauperatum

Eriospermum tortuosum var. depauperatum, commonly known as the slender eriosperm, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun exposure. It is often found in rocky or coastal environments, where it adapts to harsh, arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in xerophytic plant adaptations.
