Euodia henryi

Euodia henryi, commonly known as Chinese frankincense or Chinese myrobalan, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain aromatic seeds. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and ornamental value.

Euodia henryi is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
