Erysimum burnatii

Erysimum burnatii, commonly known as the Burnat's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It produces bright yellow or orange flowers during the spring and early summer.

Erysimum burnatii is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
