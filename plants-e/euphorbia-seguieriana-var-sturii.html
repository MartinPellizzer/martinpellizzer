Euphorbia seguieriana var. sturii

Euphorbia seguieriana var. sturii, commonly known as the Sturii's euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, segmented stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, often surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is adapted to withstand drought due to its thick, fleshy stems that store water.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and resilience.
