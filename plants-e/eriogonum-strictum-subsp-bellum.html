Eriogonum strictum subsp. bellum

Eriogonum strictum subsp.

bellum, commonly known as the Sierra buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions.

The plant produces showy clusters of white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, and it is adapted to dry, high-altitude environments.
