Eranthemum decumbens

Eranthemum decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit with succulent, fleshy leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Eranthemum decumbens is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
