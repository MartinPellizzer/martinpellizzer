Epimedium violaceum var. grandiflorum

Epimedium violaceum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage composed of leathery, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate shade and its long-lasting foliage.
