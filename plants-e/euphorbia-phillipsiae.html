Euphorbia phillipsiae

Euphorbia phillipsiae, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a branched stem with small, star-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking yellow-green cyathia, which are specialized floral structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia phillipsiae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
