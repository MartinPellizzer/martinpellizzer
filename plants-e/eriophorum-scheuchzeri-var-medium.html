Eriophorum scheuchzeri var. medium

Eriophorum scheuchzeri var. medium, commonly known as the medium cottongrass, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy inflorescences that resemble cotton, which are composed of small, scale-like flowers. This plant typically thrives in moist, acidic environments such as bogs and wet meadows. Its leaves are grass-like, slender, and often covered in fine, silky hairs.

The species is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
