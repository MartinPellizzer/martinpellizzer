Eryngium vesiculosum var. elatior

Eryngium vesiculosum var. elatior, commonly known as the balloon eryngium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, spherical, spiky flower heads that are typically blue to purple in color, surrounded by silver-gray bracts. The plant has a rosette of narrow, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
