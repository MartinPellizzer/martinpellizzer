Epipactis formosana

Epipactis formosana, commonly known as the Formosan helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with dark markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring rich, organic soil. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor environments.

Epipactis formosana is listed as an endangered species due to habitat loss and over-collection.
