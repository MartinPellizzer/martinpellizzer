Eranthemum modestum var. minus

Eranthemum modestum var. minus, commonly known as the small eranthemum, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
