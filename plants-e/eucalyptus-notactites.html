Eucalyptus notactites

Eucalyptus notactites, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mallee, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-stemmed growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, globular flower buds that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and resistance to certain pests.

The bark of Eucalyptus notactites is often flaky and can vary in color from grey to reddish-brown.
