Exacum latifolium

Exacum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pink or purple. The plant has heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Exacum latifolium is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
