Erysimum kartalkayaense

Erysimum kartalkayaense, commonly known as the Kartalkaya mustard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard species. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with four petals arranged in a cross shape, a characteristic feature of the Brassicaceae family.

The plant is noted for its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
