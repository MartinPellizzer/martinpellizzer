Eugenia wullschlaegeliana

Eugenia wullschlaegeliana, commonly known as the Wullschlaegel's evergreen eugenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
