Encyclia panthera

Encyclia panthera, commonly known as the panther orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, and it produces small, fragrant blossoms during the wet season.

Encyclia panthera is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
