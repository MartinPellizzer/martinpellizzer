Eriobotrya Deflexa

Eriobotrya deflexa is a lesser-known species within the Rosaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This small evergreen shrub is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. Despite its ornamental potential, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles due to limited cultivation. Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in its natural habitat.
