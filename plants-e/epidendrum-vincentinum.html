Epidendrum vincentinum

Epidendrum vincentinum, commonly known as the Vincent's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

Epidendrum vincentinum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
