Evolvulus maximiliani var. acutifolius

Evolvulus maximiliani var. acutifolius, commonly known as the sharp-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy, branching stems and small, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a pointed tip, giving it its common name. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
