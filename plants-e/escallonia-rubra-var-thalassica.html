Escallonia rubra var. thalassica

Escallonia rubra var. thalassica, commonly known as the coastal escallonia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southern Australia.

It features glossy, dark green foliage and produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers in spring. The plant is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and as a hedging plant.
