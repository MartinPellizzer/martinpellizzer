Epidendrum cuencanum

Epidendrum cuencanum, commonly known as the Cuencanum Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often reddish or purple.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
