Escallonia britteniana

Escallonia britteniana, commonly known as the Britten's escallonia, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features attractive, glossy, dark green leaves that turn copper-red in the fall. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers in late winter to early spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Escallonia britteniana is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
