Eria aporoides var. celebica

Eria aporoides var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stem and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
