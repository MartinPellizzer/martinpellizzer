Erysimum Kunzeanum

Erysimum kunzeanum is a striking annual plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This species is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental value. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. Despite its beauty, it is relatively easy to grow and maintain, making it a popular choice among gardeners.
