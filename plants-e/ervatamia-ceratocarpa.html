Ervatamia ceratocarpa

Ervatamia ceratocarpa, commonly known as the horned milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-like seed pods that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its latex sap is milky and can be toxic, making it a plant to handle with care.
