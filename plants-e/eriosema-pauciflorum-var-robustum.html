Eriosema pauciflorum var. robustum

Eriosema pauciflorum var. robustum, commonly known as the rough eriosema, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are often covered in fine, white to grayish pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.
