Englerodendron explicans

Englerodendron explicans, commonly known as the white-flowered trumpet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
