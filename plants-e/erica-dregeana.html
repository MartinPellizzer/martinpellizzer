Erica dregeana

Erica dregeana, commonly known as the Cape heath or red heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related species. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to red. Erica dregeana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments.

It is well adapted to Mediterranean climates and is commonly used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
