Eugenioides neriifolium

Eugenioides neriifolium, commonly known as the white-flowered eugenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Eugenioides neriifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
