Eria lancifolia

Eria lancifolia, commonly known as the sword fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizome is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.
