Eugenia harrisii

Eugenia harrisii, commonly known as the Harris's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia harrisii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
