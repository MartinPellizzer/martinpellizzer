Echinospermum heteracanthum

Echinospermum heteracanthum, commonly known as the roughleaf sunflower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant produces small, cylindrical flower heads that mature into dry, spiky seed heads. Its seeds are enclosed in a pitted, hardened casing, which is typical of the genus Echinospermum.

The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to a wide range of environmental conditions.
