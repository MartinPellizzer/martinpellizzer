Epilobium nummulariifolium var. nerteroides

Epilobium nummulariifolium var. nerteroides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features small, orbicular leaves that are usually hairy, and its flowers are pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
