Elatinoides commutata

Elatinoides commutata, commonly known as the common elatin, is a small, annual herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland dunes. The plant has small, linear leaves and produces tiny, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.

Elatinoides commutata is often found in open habitats and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
