Epidendrum reniconfusum

Epidendrum reniconfusum, commonly known as the "Coral Spike Orchid," is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or gray pubescence. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
