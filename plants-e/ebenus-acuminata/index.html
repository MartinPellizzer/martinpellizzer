Ebenus Acuminata

Succulent ebenus, scientifically known as Ebenus acuminata, is a hardy plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. This resilient species is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements have made it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
