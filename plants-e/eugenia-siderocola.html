Eugenia siderocola

Eugenia siderocola, commonly known as the ironwood eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically red or purple when ripe.

Eugenia siderocola is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in humid, lowland forests.
