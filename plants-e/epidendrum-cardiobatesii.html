Epidendrum cardiobatesii

Epidendrum cardiobatesii, commonly known as the Cardiac Epidendrum, is a member of the orchid family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features showy, brightly colored flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in racemes. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

This epiphytic orchid requires well-draining soil and consistent humidity to flourish.
