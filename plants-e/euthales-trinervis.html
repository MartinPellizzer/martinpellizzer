Euthales trinervis

Euthales trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces striking, yellowish-green flowers that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
