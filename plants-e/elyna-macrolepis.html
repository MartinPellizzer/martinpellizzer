Elyna macrolepis

Elyna macrolepis, commonly known as the large-scale elyna, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. Its root system is robust, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Elyna macrolepis is native to specific regions with a preference for sunny to partially shaded environments.
