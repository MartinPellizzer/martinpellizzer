Ehretia exsoluta

Ehretia exsoluta, commonly known as the silky tree or silky hibiscus, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, typically blooming in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
