Egania

Egania, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Egania is native to various regions in Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
