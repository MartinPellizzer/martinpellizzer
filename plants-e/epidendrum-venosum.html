Epidendrum venosum

Epidendrum venosum, commonly known as the veiny orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, veined leaves that are often a deep green with prominent yellow or white markings. The plant produces pendulous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum venosum is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
