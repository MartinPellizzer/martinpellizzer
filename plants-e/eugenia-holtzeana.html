Eugenia holtzeana

Eugenia holtzeana, commonly known as the mountain eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

The fruit, which is edible, is typically small and berry-like, containing several seeds.
