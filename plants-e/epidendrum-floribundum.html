Epidendrum floribundum

Epidendrum floribundum, commonly known as the clustered epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum floribundum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
