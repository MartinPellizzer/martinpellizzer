Epidendrum euosmum

Epidendrum euosmum, commonly known as the fragrant epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has strap-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum euosmum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
