Epidendrum steinbachii

Epidendrum steinbachii, commonly known as the Steinbach's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences with numerous fragrant, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Epidendrum steinbachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under proper conditions.
