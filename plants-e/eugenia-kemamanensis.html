Eugenia kemamanensis

Eugenia kemamanensis, commonly known as the Kaimana eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the island of New Guinea and is found in moist, tropical rainforest environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, and it bears small, edible berries. Eugenia kemamanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruit.
