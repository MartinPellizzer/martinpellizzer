Eurya inaequilatera

Eurya inaequilatera, commonly known as the unequal-leaved eurya, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
