Euphorbia epithymoides f. glabrescens

Euphorbia epithymoides f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are the modified inflorescences typical of the genus Euphorbia. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

The f. glabrescens form is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stem and leaves, setting it apart from other forms of Euphorbia epithymoides.
