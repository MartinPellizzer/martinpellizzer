Echinocactus grusonii

Echinocactus grusonii, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of bright yellow spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for desert gardens and as a houseplant.
