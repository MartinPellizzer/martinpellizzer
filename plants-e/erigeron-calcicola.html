Erigeron calcicola

Erigeron calcicola, commonly known as the California fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Erigeron calcicola is well adapted to arid environments and is considered a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
