Erysimum feodorovii

Erysimum feodorovii, commonly known as the Siberian wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and can grow up to 30 centimeters tall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erysimum feodorovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
