Encyclia isochila

Encyclia isochila, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia isochila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
