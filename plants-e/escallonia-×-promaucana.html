Escallonia × promaucana

Escallonia × promaucana, commonly known as the evergreen honeysuckle, is a hybrid shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Escallonia × promaucana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
