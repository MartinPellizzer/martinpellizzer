Eriosphaera multifida

Eriosphaera multifida, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and its ability to store water in its fleshy, ribbed stems. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Eriosphaera multifida is well-suited for rock gardens and arid environments due to its drought tolerance.
