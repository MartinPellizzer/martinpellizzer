Ehretia acuminata var. polyantha

Ehretia acuminata var. polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically green with a slight pubescence. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
