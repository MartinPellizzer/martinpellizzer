Epidendrum villahermosaense

Epidendrum villahermosaense, commonly known as the Villa Hermosa orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features lanceolate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink.

Epidendrum villahermosaense is well adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.
