Euphrasia filicaulis

Euphrasia filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
