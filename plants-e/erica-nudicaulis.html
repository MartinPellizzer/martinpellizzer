Erica nudicaulis

Erica nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of pale pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica nudicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
