Eugenia oonophylla

Eugenia oonophylla, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Eugenia oonophylla thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
