Echeveria × scaphylla

Echeveria × scaphylla, commonly known as the hybrid echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are often blue-gray or green in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or yellow during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and collectors.
