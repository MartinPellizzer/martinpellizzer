Eria versteegii

Eria versteegii, commonly known as the Cape mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky and sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. It blooms during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and moths.

Eria versteegii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
