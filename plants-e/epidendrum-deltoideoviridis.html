Epidendrum deltoideoviridis

Epidendrum deltoideoviridis, commonly known as the Viridian Delta Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green color. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous small, greenish-white flowers that have a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum deltoideoviridis is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
