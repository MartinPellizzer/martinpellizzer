Epimedium rugosum

Epimedium rugosum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, matted foliage. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early spring before the leaves emerge. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium rugosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
