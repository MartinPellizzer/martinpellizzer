Epidendrum blepharichilum var. majus

Epidendrum blepharichilum var. majus, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
