Elatinoides elatine

Elatinoides elatine, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Elatinaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in length. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or just at the water's surface. It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams.

Elatinoides elatine is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed aquatic habitats.
