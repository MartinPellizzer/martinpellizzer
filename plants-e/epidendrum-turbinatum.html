Epidendrum turbinatum

Epidendrum turbinatum, commonly known as the Turban Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a striking addition to any collection. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environments.

Epidendrum turbinatum is relatively easy to care for, thriving in bright, indirect light and moderate humidity.
