Eugenia pearcei

Eugenia pearcei, commonly known as the pearson's eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its leaves are typically glossy and elliptical, with a pleasant aroma when crushed.

Eugenia pearcei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
