Eugenia willdenowii

Eugenia willdenowii, commonly known as the willdenow's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Eugenia willdenowii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and aromatic foliage.
