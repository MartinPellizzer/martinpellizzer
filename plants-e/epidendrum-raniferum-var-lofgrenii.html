Epidendrum raniferum var. lofgrenii

Epidendrum raniferum var. lofgrenii, commonly known as the Lofgren's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant features broad, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy bloom, enhancing its ornamental appeal. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

This species is appreciated for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
