Edraianthus hercegovinus

Edraianthus hercegovinus, commonly known as the Hercegovina fumaria, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, often pink or red, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Edraianthus hercegovinus is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
