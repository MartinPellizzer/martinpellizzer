Eragrostis lanipes

Eragrostis lanipes, commonly known as silky grama, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, hairy leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry to moist grasslands, prairies, and open woodlands. Its seeds are small and wind-dispersed, contributing to its spread across various habitats.

Eragrostis lanipes plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
