Erodium cicutarium var. cicutaefolium

Erodium cicutarium var. cicutaefolium, commonly known as broad-leaved erodium, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems that grow up to 30 cm in height, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive wavy margin. The flowers are small, pinkish-white, and arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. It produces small, kidney-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This plant is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
