Eucomis albomarginata

Eucomis albomarginata, commonly known as the white-edged pineapple lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, with a distinctive flowering stem that bears a pineapple-shaped inflorescence. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a striking white margin on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
