Eleiosina

Eleiosina, commonly known as the "butterfly plant," is a tropical species belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. Eleiosina thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
