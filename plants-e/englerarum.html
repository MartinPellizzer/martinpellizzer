Englerarum

Englerarum, commonly known as the Engler's orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, often fragrant flowers. Englerarum species typically have linear or strap-like leaves and produce small, clustered inflorescences.

They are valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
