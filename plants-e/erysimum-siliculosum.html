Erysimum siliculosum

Erysimum siliculosum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, and its flowers are bright yellow with four petals. It produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds.

Erysimum siliculosum is often found in disturbed habitats and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
