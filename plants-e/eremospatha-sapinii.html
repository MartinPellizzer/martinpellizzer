Eremospatha sapinii

Eremospatha sapinii, commonly known as the Alpine snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bell-shaped white or pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in cold, high-altitude environments with well-drained soil.
