Elionurus hirsutus

Elionurus hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy elionurus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, where it typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
