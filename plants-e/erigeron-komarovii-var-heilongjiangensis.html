Erigeron komarovii var. heilongjiangensis

Erigeron komarovii var. heilongjiangensis, commonly known as the Heilongjiang fleabane, is a flowering plant native to northeastern China.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
