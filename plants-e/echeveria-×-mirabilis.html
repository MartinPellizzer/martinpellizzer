Echeveria × mirabilis

Echeveria × mirabilis, commonly known as the "Fire Opal," is a hybrid succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces vibrant orange to yellow flowers in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
