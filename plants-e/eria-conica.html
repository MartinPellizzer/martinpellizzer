Eria conica

Eria conica, commonly known as the conical eria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms that are highly ornamental. Eria conica thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
