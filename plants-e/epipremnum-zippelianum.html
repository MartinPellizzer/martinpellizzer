Epipremnum zippelianum

Epipremnum zippelianum, commonly known as the Zippel's philodendron, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its rapid growth rate makes it ideal for vertical gardening or as a hanging plant.
