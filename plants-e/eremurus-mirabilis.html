Eremurus mirabilis

Eremurus mirabilis, commonly known as the giant spikerush, is a striking plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette at the base.

Eremurus mirabilis is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
