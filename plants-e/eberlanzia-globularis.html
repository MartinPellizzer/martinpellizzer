Eberlanzia globularis

Eberlanzia globularis, commonly known as the globularia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and produces clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique form.

Eberlanzia globularis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
