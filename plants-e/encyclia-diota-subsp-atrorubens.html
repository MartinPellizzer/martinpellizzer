Encyclia diota subsp. atrorubens

Encyclia diota subsp.

atrorubens, commonly known as the dark red encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its dark reddish-brown pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves.

The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green to orange flowers with red or purple markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts.
