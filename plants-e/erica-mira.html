Erica mira

Erica mira, commonly known as the pink heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. Erica mira thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its fragrant blooms, which range from white to pink, attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
