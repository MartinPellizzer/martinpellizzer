Elatostema lineolatum var. helferi

Elatostema lineolatum var. helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's gotu kola, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces tiny white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Urticaceae family.
