Epidendrum manta-birostris

Epidendrum manta-birostris, commonly known as the Manta-birostris orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescence that bears numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping to retain moisture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
