Eugenia oblongifolia var. robusta

Eugenia oblongifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ornamental value, with showy, red or pink berries that appear after flowering. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

The species is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine.
