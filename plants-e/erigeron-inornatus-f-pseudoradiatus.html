Erigeron inornatus f. pseudoradiatus

Erigeron inornatus f. pseudoradiatus, commonly known as the False Radiant Fleabane, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like appearance, featuring white ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has finely divided, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
