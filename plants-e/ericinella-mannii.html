Ericinella mannii

Ericinella mannii, commonly known as the African heather, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Ericinella mannii typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories.
