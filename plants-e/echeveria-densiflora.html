Echeveria densiflora

Echeveria densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
