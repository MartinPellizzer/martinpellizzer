Eulophia rauhii

Eulophia rauhii, commonly known as the Rauh's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia rauhii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
