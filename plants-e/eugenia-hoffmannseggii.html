Eugenia hoffmannseggii

Eugenia hoffmannseggii, commonly known as the Hoffmannsegg's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Andes mountains in South America, particularly in regions of Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. It produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or yellow when ripe.

Eugenia hoffmannseggii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and aromatic properties.
