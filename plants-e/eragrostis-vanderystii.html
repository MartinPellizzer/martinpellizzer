Eragrostis vanderystii

Eragrostis vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in regions with warm, dry climates.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a potential candidate for ecological restoration projects.
