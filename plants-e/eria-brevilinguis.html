Eria brevilinguis

Eria brevilinguis, commonly known as the short-leaved eria, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eria brevilinguis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
