Erigeron × enderi

Erigeron × enderi, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a hybrid perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

Erigeron × enderi is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
