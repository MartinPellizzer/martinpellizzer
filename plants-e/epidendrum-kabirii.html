Epidendrum kabirii

Epidendrum kabirii, commonly known as the Kabiri's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, bell-shaped blooms. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture.

Epidendrum kabirii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
