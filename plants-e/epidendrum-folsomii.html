Epidendrum folsomii

Epidendrum folsomii, commonly known as the Folsom's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy texture, aiding in water retention.

Epidendrum folsomii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
