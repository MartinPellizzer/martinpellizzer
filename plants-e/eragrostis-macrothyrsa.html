Eragrostis macrothyrsa

Eragrostis macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-panicle bluestem, is a perennial grass native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense, cylindrical inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Eragrostis macrothyrsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
