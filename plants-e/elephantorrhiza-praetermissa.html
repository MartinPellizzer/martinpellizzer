Elephantorrhiza praetermissa

Elephantorrhiza praetermissa, commonly known as the yellow burr bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces yellowish to brownish composite flowers that are often surrounded by bracts. Its leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.
