Eminium rauwolffii var. rauwolffii

Eminium rauwolffii var. rauwolffii, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with sprawling or climbing stems. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue to purple in color, blooming in the early morning. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its rapid growth and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
