Emilia malaisseana

Emilia malaisseana, commonly known as the Malayan emilia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia, and thrives in tropical climates. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Emilia malaisseana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
