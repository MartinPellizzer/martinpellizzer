Epilobium trichoneurum var. brachyphyllum

Epilobium trichoneurum var. brachyphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willowherb, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, often disturbed habitats such as meadows, riverbanks, and wetlands.
