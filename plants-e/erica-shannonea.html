Erica shannonea

Erica shannonea, commonly known as the Shannonea heath, is a flowering plant native to the western regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly known as the heath or heather family, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained, acidic conditions. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late summer and attract various pollinators.

Erica shannonea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable environmental conditions.
