Ebertia nana

Ebertia nana, commonly known as the dwarf snowbell, is a small herbaceous plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, hairy texture.
