Euphorbia munizii

Euphorbia munizii, commonly known as the Muniz's spurge, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
