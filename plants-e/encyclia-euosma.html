Encyclia euosma

Encyclia euosma, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Encyclia euosma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
