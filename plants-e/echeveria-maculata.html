Echeveria maculata

Echeveria maculata, commonly known as the "Tricolor Echeveria," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red and purple markings. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Echeveria maculata is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
