Echeveria amethystina

Echeveria amethystina, commonly known as the Amethystina Jade Plant, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features dense, rosette-shaped foliage with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray to purple in color. The plant produces striking violet-pink flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
