Erato

Erato, commonly known as the erato flower, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are usually opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Erato is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
