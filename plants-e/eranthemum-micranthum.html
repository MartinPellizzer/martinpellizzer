Eranthemum micranthum

Eranthemum micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive tubular corolla.

Eranthemum micranthum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
