Eulophia yunnanensis

Eulophia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to China.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing clusters of white to pale pink flowers with intricate patterns. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
