Endymion lacaillei

Endymion lacaillei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its blooms are often pink or purple, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Endymion lacaillei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
