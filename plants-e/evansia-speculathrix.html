Evansia speculathrix

Evansia speculathrix, commonly known as the silverleaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its root system is shallow but extensive, enabling it to absorb moisture from the soil efficiently.

Evansia speculathrix is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
