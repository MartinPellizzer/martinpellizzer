Elatostema tenuicaudatoides var. orientale

Elatostema tenuicaudatoides var. orientale, commonly known as the oriental thread-leaf, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive thread-like appearance, which are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
