Erica benguelensis

Erica benguelensis, commonly known as the Benguela heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. This shrub typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in spring.

The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in harsh, coastal environments with limited nutrients.
