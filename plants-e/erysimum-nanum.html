Erysimum nanum

Erysimum nanum, commonly known as dwarf wallflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, aiding in moisture retention. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum nanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
