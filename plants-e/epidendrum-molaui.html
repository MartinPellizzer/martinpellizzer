Epidendrum molaui

Epidendrum molaui, commonly known as the Molaui orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum molaui is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
