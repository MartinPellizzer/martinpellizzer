Entandrophragma leplaei

Entandrophragma leplaei, commonly known as African teak, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the family Bombacaceae and is characterized by its durable, heavy, and highly valued hardwood. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall with a straight trunk and smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, while the flowers are small and yellowish-green.
