Elodea Linearis

Elodea linearis is a common aquatic plant found in freshwater environments around the world. It is known for its long, slender leaves that float on the water's surface or hang beneath it. This plant plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems by providing oxygen and habitat for various organisms. Its simple structure makes it a popular choice for classroom experiments in biology. Despite its modest appearance, Elodea linearis is essential for maintaining water quality and supporting aquatic life.
