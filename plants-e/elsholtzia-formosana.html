Elsholtzia formosana

Elsholtzia formosana, commonly known as Taiwan elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, opposite leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pale blue to violet, clustered in whorls along the stems, and bloom during the summer months.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.
