Epidendrum lilijae

Epidendrum lilijae, commonly known as the Lilijae orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum lilijae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
