Eugenia brevistylis

Eugenia brevistylis, commonly known as the small-styled eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe.

Eugenia brevistylis is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
