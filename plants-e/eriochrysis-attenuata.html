Eriochrysis attenuata

Eriochrysis attenuata, commonly known as the slender sea fescue, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

Eriochrysis attenuata is adapted to salt spray and is an important component of coastal vegetation.
