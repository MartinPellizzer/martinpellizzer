Erigeron diversifolius

Erigeron diversifolius, commonly known as the diverse-leaved fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, producing a showy display that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
