Eugenia obtusifolia

Eugenia obtusifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers, which are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn from green to red when mature.

Eugenia obtusifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
