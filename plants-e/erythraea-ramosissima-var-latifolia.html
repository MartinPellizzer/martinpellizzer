Erythraea ramosissima var. latifolia

Erythraea ramosissima var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved erythraea, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and its clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a slightly serrated margin. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and evergreen foliage.
