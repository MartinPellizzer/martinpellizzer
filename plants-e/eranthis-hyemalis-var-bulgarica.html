Eranthis hyemalis var. bulgarica

Eranthis hyemalis var. bulgarica, commonly known as the Bulgarian winter aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It features early spring blooming, producing clusters of bright yellow, buttercup-like flowers. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that emerge before the flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
