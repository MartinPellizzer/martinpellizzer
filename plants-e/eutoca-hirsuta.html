Eutoca hirsuta

Eutoca hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy eutoca, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its hairy foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom during the dry season.

Eutoca hirsuta is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
