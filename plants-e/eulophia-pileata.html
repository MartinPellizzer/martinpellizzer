Eulophia pileata

Eulophia pileata, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the base.

Eulophia pileata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
