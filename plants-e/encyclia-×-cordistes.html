Encyclia × cordistes

Encyclia × cordistes, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia tatei.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of Central America. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded environments. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or orange lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
