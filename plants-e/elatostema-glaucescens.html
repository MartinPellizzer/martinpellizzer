Elatostema glaucescens

Elatostema glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green elatostema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually glaucous, giving them a bluish-gray appearance. Its flowers are inconspicuous, with tiny white petals and a distinct central spike.

Elatostema glaucescens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
