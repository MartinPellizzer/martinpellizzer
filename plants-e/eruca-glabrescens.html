Eruca glabrescens

Eruca glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its seeds are encased in a hard, triangular capsule, typical of the mustard family.

Eruca glabrescens is often cultivated for its edible leaves, which have a peppery flavor similar to arugula.
