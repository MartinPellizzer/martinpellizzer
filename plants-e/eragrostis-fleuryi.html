Eragrostis fleuryi

Eragrostis fleuryi, commonly known as Fleury's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues, adding to its ornamental appeal.
