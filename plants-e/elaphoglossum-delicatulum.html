Elaphoglossum delicatulum

Elaphoglossum delicatulum, commonly known as the delicate holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, delicate fronds that often have a pale or silvery underside. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, this fern thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's rhizomes are typically short and produce small, clustered sporangia.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, indoor conditions.
