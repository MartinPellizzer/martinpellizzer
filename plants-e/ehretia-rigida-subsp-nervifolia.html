Ehretia rigida subsp. nervifolia

Ehretia rigida subsp.

nervifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stems.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
