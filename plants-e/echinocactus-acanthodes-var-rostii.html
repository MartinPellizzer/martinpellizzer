Echinocactus acanthodes var. rostii

Echinocactus acanthodes var. rostii, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in prominent, yellowish spines that give it its common name. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for desert gardens and indoor collections.
