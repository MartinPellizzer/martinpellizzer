Euphorbia pithyusa var. ovalifolia

Euphorbia pithyusa var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaved spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic flower-like structures. The leaves are oval-shaped and often have a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
