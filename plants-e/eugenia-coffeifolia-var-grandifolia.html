Eugenia coffeifolia var. grandifolia

Eugenia coffeifolia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. These fruits are edible and often used in local cuisines.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
