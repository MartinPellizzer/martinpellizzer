Euphorbia zinniiflora

Euphorbia zinniiflora, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem with spines and produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers in clusters. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. It blooms during the summer months and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its milky sap contains toxic compounds, so it should be handled with care.
