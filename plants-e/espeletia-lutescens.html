Espeletia lutescens

Espeletia lutescens, commonly known as the yellow espeletia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, rosette-like foliage and showy yellow flowers. Espeletia lutescens plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
