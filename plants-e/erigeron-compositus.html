Erigeron compositus

Erigeron compositus, commonly known as the white fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It features showy white to pale blue ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers.

Erigeron compositus is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
