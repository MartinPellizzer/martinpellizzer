Erythrogyne

Erythrogyne, commonly known as the redstem or redroot plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its distinctive red or reddish stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetlands or along waterways. Erythrogyne species are shrubs or small trees that can reach heights of up to 3 meters.
