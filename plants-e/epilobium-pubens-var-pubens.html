Epilobium pubens var. pubens

Epilobium pubens var. pubens, commonly known as sticky willow or velvetleaf willow, is a flowering plant in the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems covered in fine, sticky hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often glandular, with a rough texture. It produces small, pink to red flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.
