Eria globifera

Eria globifera, commonly known as the globe orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Eria globifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
