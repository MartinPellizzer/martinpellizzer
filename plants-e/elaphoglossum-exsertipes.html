Elaphoglossum exsertipes

Elaphoglossum exsertipes, commonly known as the erect fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lanceolate fronds that are typically upright and rigid. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
