Elaphoglossum polypodium

Elaphoglossum polypodium, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and its ability to thrive in rocky, shaded environments. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinctive, serrated margin.

Elaphoglossum polypodium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
