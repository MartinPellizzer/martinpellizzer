Evelyna lindenii

Evelyna lindenii, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and fragrant, pale pink blooms. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after specimen in botanical gardens and among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is often protected in conservation programs.
