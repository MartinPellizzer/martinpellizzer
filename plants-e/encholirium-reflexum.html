Encholirium reflexum

Encholirium reflexum, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine, silvery pubescence.

Encholirium reflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
