Eugenia reinwardtiana f. lutea

Eugenia reinwardtiana f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow eugenia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is valued in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
