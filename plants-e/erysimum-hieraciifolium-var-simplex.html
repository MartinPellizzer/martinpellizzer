Erysimum hieraciifolium var. simplex

Erysimum hieraciifolium var. simplex, commonly known as the simple leaf wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is native to the western United States. It thrives in full sun and is often found in alpine or arid environments.

Its simple leaf structure and compact growth habit make it a distinctive and hardy ornamental plant.
