Eritrichium oxygonum

Eritrichium oxygonum, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and tundra environments.

Eritrichium oxygonum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in pollinator habitats.
