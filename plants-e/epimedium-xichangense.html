Epimedium xichangense

Epimedium xichangense, commonly known as the Xichang barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often reddish or bronze-colored leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
