Eugenia albotomentosa var. goyazensis

Eugenia albotomentosa var. goyazensis, commonly known as the Goiás eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Brazil, particularly found in the states of Goiás and Tocantins. The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers and hairy, glandular leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.

The fruit is small, fleshy, and often has a sweet aroma, making it an important food source for various wildlife species.
