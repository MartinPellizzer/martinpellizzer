Echinodorus quadricostatus var. magdalenensis

Echinodorus quadricostatus var. magdalenensis, commonly known as the Magdalenense Amazon Sword, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 12 inches in length. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical freshwater environments, preferring moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize large areas in ponds or aquariums.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
