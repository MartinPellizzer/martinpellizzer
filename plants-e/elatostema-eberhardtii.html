Elatostema eberhardtii

Elatostema eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. The plant features a rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema eberhardtii is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and its unique adaptations to humid, tropical climates.
