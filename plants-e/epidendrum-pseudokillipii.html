Epidendrum pseudokillipii

Epidendrum pseudokillipii, commonly known as the Killip's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum pseudokillipii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
