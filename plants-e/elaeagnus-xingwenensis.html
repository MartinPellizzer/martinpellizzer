Elaeagnus xingwenensis

Elaeagnus xingwenensis, commonly known as the Xingwen silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, reddish berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky areas.

Elaeagnus xingwenensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
