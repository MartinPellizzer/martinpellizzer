Eulophia geniculata

Eulophia geniculata, commonly known as the bent-stem orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often curved stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a pale yellow to greenish hue, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Eulophia geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
