Echinodorus sellowianus

Echinodorus sellowianus, commonly known as Amazon Sword, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, providing dense coverage in aquariums. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in planted tanks for its aesthetic value and ability to help control algae growth.

Echinodorus sellowianus is also known for its ease of care and adaptability to various water conditions.
