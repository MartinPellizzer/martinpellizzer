Euphrasia officinalis var. mollis

Euphrasia officinalis var. mollis, commonly known as the Downy Yellow-eyed Grass, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its delicate, hair-covered stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically blooms in early summer and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued in herbal medicine for its purported soothing properties.
