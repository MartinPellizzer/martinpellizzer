Euryops racemosus

Euryops racemosus, commonly known as the yellow dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow, dandelion-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is often found in disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and hairy.
