Eugenioides lenormandianum

Eugenioides lenormandianum, commonly known as the Lenormandian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The bark is smooth and may exude a mild aromatic scent when cut.
