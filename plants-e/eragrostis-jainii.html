Eragrostis jainii

Eragrostis jainii, commonly known as Jain's lovegrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and Nepal.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
