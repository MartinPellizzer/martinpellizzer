Eruca

Eruca, commonly known as garden rocket or Italian arugula, is a leafy green plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its peppery, slightly bitter taste and is often used in salads and as a garnish. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a rough texture. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

Eruca is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in cool climates.
