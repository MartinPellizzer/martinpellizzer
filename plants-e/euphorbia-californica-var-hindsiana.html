Euphorbia californica var. hindsiana

Euphorbia californica var. hindsiana, commonly known as Hinds' euphorbia, is a succulent perennial native to California.

It features a branching structure with thick, ribbed stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by specialized bracts. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white wax.

It is well adapted to dry, rocky habitats and requires minimal water once established.
