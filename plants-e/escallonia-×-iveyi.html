Escallonia × iveyi

Escallonia × iveyi, commonly known as the pink escallonia, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Escallonia × iveyi is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
