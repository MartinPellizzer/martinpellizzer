Erysimum stewartii

Erysimum stewartii, commonly known as Stewart's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, feathery appearance.
