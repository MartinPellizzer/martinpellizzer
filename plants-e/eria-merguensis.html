Eria merguensis

Eria merguensis, commonly known as the Merguens orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. Eria merguensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
