Eriostomum

Eriostomum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a distinctive waxy texture. The flowers are usually small and clustered, often with a yellow or white coloration.

These plants are native to various regions in the Americas and are sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
