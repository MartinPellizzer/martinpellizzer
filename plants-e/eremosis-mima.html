Eremosis mima

Eremosis mima, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Eremosis mima is adapted to extreme temperatures and low precipitation, making it a hardy species in desert ecosystems.
