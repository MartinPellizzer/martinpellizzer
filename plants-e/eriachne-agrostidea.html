Eriachne agrostidea

Eriachne agrostidea, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Eriachne agrostidea plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
