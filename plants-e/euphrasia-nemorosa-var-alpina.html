Euphrasia nemorosa var. alpina

Euphrasia nemorosa var. alpina, commonly known as the Alpine eyebright, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and alpine meadows. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
