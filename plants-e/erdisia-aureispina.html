Erdisia aureispina

Erdisia aureispina, commonly known as the golden spiny erdisia, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its spiny stems and leaves provide a natural defense against herbivores, while its root system helps in nitrogen fixation, enriching the soil.
