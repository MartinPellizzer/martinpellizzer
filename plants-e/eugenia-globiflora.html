Eugenia globiflora

Eugenia globiflora, commonly known as the globeflower, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescences that consist of numerous small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Eugenia globiflora is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
