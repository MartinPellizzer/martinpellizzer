Echinomastus krausei

Echinomastus krausei, commonly known as the Krause's milkweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It is adapted to arid environments and has a milky sap that is toxic to some animals.
