Evolvulus mollis

Evolvulus mollis, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped, silver-veined leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for gardeners looking for a quick, vibrant addition to their landscape.
