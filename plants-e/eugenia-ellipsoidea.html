Eugenia ellipsoidea

Eugenia ellipsoidea, commonly known as the elliptical eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its elliptical-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a small, edible berry that is often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia ellipsoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
