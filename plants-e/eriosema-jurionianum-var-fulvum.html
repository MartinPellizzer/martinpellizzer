Eriosema jurionianum var. fulvum

Eriosema jurionianum var. fulvum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered eriosema, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellow or orange-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has compound leaves with small, hairy stipules. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and are visited by various pollinators.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
