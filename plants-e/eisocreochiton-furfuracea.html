Eisocreochiton furfuracea

Eisocreochiton furfuracea, commonly known as the hairy shield bug, is a species of shield bug native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairy, and often reddish-brown body covering. The plant it inhabits typically includes a variety of woody shrubs and trees, particularly those in the families Rosaceae and Ericaceae. Its life cycle involves overwintering as an adult and emerging in the spring to feed on plant sap.

This species plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat through both predation and as a food source for other organisms.
