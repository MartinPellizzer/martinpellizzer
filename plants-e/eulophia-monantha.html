Eulophia monantha

Eulophia monantha, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, rhizomatous stem and a cluster of narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces a single, slender flower spike bearing a single, showy orchid flower with a distinctive, spurred lip. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Eulophia monantha thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
