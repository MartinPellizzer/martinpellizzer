Escobaria Hesteri

Escobaria hesteri is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the deserts of Texas and New Mexico. It belongs to the cactus family and is known for its small, spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. This species is highly adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves and stems. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and protected in many areas. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
