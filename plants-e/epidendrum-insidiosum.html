Epidendrum insidiosum

Epidendrum insidiosum, commonly known as the sneaky Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, often forming dense clumps. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, arranged in racemes. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a thick, leathery epidermis.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
