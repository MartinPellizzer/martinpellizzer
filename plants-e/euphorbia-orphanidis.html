Euphorbia orphanidis

Euphorbia orphanidis, commonly known as the Cape Verde euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is characterized by its spineless, fleshy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry climates. Its leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, which help minimize water loss.

Euphorbia orphanidis is often found growing on rocky soils, demonstrating its resilience to poor nutrient conditions.
