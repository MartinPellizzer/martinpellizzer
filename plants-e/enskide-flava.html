Enskide flava

Enskide flava, commonly known as the golden shield plant, is a rare flowering species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the rainy season. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat. Its unique structure includes a basal rosette of leaves that form a protective shield around the stem.

Enskide flava is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
