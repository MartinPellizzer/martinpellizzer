Elaphrium penicillatum

Elaphrium penicillatum, commonly known as the slender lady's-tresses, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowering period occurs in late summer, and it is known for its delicate, grass-like appearance.

Elaphrium penicillatum is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
