Epidendrum attenuatum

Epidendrum attenuatum, commonly known as the slender epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Epidendrum attenuatum is a hardy orchid that can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, though it requires consistent moisture and moderate temperatures.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a favored plant among orchid enthusiasts.
