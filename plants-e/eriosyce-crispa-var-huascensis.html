Eriosyce crispa var. huascensis

Eriosyce crispa var. huascensis, commonly known as the Huascensis Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to the high Andes of Peru.

It features a rounded, cushion-like stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, white flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid gardens.
