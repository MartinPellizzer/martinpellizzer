Eugenia calvinii

Eugenia calvinii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is known for its fragrant blooms, which appear in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Eugenia calvinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
