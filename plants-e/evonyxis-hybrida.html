Evonyxis hybrida

Evonyxis hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen holly, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense, spiny foliage with dark green leaves that provide year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental landscaping.
