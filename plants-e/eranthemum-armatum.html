Eranthemum armatum

Eranthemum armatum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its erect growth and small, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Eranthemum armatum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
