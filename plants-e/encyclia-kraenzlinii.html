Encyclia kraenzlinii

Encyclia kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It prefers humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Encyclia kraenzlinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
