Eulophia menelikii

Eulophia menelikii, commonly known as the Menelik's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, narrow leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. It is characterized by its unique floral structure and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
