Eria striolata

Eria striolata, commonly known as the streaked orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with distinct streaks or stripes. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Eria striolata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
