Echeveria megacalyx

Echeveria megacalyx, commonly known as the Blue Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or pink during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect sunlight.

Echeveria megacalyx is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
