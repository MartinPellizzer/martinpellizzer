Erica turbiniflora var. turbiniflora

Erica turbiniflora var. turbiniflora, commonly known as the turbiniflora heath, is a species of evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heaths and heathers, and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in whorls around the stems. Its flowers are usually pink or red, appearing in clusters and blooming in spring.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
