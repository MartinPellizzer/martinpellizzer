Eupatorium × arakianum

Eupatorium × arakianum, commonly known as the Chinese boneset, is a hybrid shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins, and it often grows in moist, well-drained soils. It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
