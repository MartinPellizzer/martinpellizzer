Elatostema filipes var. filipes

Elatostema filipes var. filipes, commonly known as the slender leaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of small, greenish-white blooms that appear in clusters.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread through rhizomes, making it a common sight in its natural habitat.
