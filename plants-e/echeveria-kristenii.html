Echeveria kristenii

Echeveria kristenii, commonly known as the "Pink Cloud," is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a compact rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with a pinkish or reddish tinge. The leaves are densely packed, creating a visually appealing, rounded shape. It produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
