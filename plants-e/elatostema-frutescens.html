Elatostema frutescens

Elatostema frutescens, commonly known as the silverleaf stellaria, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale pink, and arranged in clusters, making it a subtle but delicate ornamental plant.

Elatostema frutescens is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
