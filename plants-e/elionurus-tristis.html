Elionurus tristis

Elionurus tristis, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers, which bloom in early spring. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in sandy soils and access moisture from deeper layers.

Elionurus tristis is often found in rocky outcrops and coastal dunes, showcasing its adaptability to extreme conditions.
