Erysimum capitatum var. capitatum

Erysimum capitatum var. capitatum, commonly known as the rockcress or winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or coastal environments. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately divided, and its flowers are small, bright yellow, and arranged in a compact, upright inflorescence. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its early spring blooms.
