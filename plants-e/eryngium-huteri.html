Eryngium huteri

Eryngium huteri, commonly known as the New Zealand thistle, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb with a robust, spiny stem and silver-gray, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

Eryngium huteri is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, providing habitat for native insects and birds.
