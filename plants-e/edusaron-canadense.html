Edusaron canadense

Edusaron canadense, commonly known as Canadian edusaron, is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to specific regions of North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal practices, though its medicinal properties remain understudied.

Due to habitat loss, Edusaron canadense is considered endangered in many parts of its range.
