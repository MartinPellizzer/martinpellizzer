Eugenia sintenisii

Eugenia sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
