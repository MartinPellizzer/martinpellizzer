Ervatamia angustisepala

Ervatamia angustisepala, commonly known as the narrow-lobed ervatamia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

Ervatamia angustisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
