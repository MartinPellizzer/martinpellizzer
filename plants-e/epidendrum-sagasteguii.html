Epidendrum sagasteguii

Epidendrum sagasteguii, commonly known as the Sagastegui's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with numerous flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with a waxy coating.

Epidendrum sagasteguii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
