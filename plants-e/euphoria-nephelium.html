Euphoria nephelium

Euphoria nephelium, commonly known as the rambutan, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, spiny fruit covering, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically pollinated by insects. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

The fruit is typically harvested when it turns a bright red or yellow color, indicating maturity.
