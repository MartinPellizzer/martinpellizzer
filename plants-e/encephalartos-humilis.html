Encephalartos humilis

Encephalartos humilis, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. Its cones are small and inconspicuous, with the female cones bearing a distinctive red coloration.

Encephalartos humilis is a slow-growing, long-lived plant that is highly valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
