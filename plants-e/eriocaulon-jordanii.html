Eriocaulon jordanii

Eriocaulon jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's spikegrass, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and parts of Africa. The plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and tiny, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine, hairy coating.

Eriocaulon jordanii thrives in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks and in wetlands.
