Eupatorium torondoyense

Eupatorium torondoyense, commonly known as the Torondoy plant, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are usually opposite, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Eupatorium torondoyense is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Caribbean herbal remedies.
