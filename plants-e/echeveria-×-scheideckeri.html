Echeveria × scheideckeri

Echeveria × scheideckeri, commonly known as the Silver Dust Echeveria, is a hybrid succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and drought tolerance.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and ease of care.
