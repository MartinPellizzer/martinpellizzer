Elatostema biglomeratum

Elatostema biglomeratum, commonly known as the clustered elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with numerous small, clustered leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of small, greenish-white blooms that appear in early spring.

The plant is notable for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its role in supporting local insect populations.
