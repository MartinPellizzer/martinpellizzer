Elbunis Alba

Elbunis alba is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its striking white blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, the species faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Protecting Elbunis alba is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
