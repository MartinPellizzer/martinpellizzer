Epidendrum suborbiculare

Epidendrum suborbiculare, commonly known as the Orange-Flowered Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting green or yellow throat. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum suborbiculare is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
