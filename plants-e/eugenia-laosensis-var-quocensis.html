Eugenia laosensis var. quocensis

Eugenia laosensis var. quocensis, commonly known as the Quocensis Eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique flavor profile, it is cultivated in some horticultural gardens.
