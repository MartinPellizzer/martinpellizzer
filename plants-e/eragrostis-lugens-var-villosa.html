Eragrostis lugens var. villosa

Eragrostis lugens var. villosa, commonly known as silky lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often mistaken for flowering plants. It thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Silky lovegrass is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
