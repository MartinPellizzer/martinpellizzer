Epidendrum magnicallosum

Epidendrum magnicallosum, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central America.

It features long, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length, supporting clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red with yellow markings, blooming in summer and lasting for several weeks. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
