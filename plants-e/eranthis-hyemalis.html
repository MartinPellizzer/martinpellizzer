Eranthis hyemalis

Eranthis hyemalis, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically before the leaves fully emerge. The plant has basal leaves that are kidney-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its stems are usually short and stout, often branching near the base.

Eranthis hyemalis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or shaded gardens.
