Epilobium chlorifolium var. chlorifolium

Epilobium chlorifolium var. chlorifolium, commonly known as yellow sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, sandy or rocky soils, often found in wetlands, streambanks, and coastal areas. The plant features alternate, narrow, yellow-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, clustered flowers are typically pink to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wet environments.
