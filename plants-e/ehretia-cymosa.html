Ehretia cymosa

Ehretia cymosa, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or mountain evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in cymes and bloom in the spring.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
