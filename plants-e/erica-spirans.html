Erica spirans

Erica spirans, commonly known as the spiral heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. Erica spirans thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to coastal conditions.
