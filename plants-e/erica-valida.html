Erica valida

Erica valida, commonly known as the heather, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink, red, or white, blooming in spring and summer. Erica valida thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
