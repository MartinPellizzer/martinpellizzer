Emiliella zambiensis

Emiliella zambiensis, commonly known as the Zambian star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-like growth pattern and produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in grassland habitats. Emiliella zambiensis is adapted to arid conditions and can thrive in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
