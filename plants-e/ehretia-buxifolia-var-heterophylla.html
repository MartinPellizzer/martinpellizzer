Ehretia buxifolia var. heterophylla

Ehretia buxifolia var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf boxwood, is a variant of the boxwood plant characterized by its unique leaf morphology.

It is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features small, ovate leaves that vary in shape and size, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and foliage make it a desirable choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.
