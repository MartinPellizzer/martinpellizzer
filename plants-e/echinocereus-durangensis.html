Echinocereus durangensis

Echinocereus durangensis, commonly known as the Durango hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
