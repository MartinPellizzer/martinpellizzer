Erigeron subacaulis

Erigeron subacaulis, commonly known as the slender fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. This herbaceous plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.
