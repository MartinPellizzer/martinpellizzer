Elatostema weinlandii var. vestitum

Elatostema weinlandii var. vestitum, commonly known as the velvet elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are densely covered with fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne on short stalks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a notable species within the Elatostema genus.
