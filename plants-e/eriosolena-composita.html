Eriosolena composita

Eriosolena composita, commonly known as the composite eriosolena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its composite inflorescence, which consists of both showy ray flowers and smaller tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphological features and ecological significance in its native habitat.
