Espeletia brassicoidea f. contracta

Espeletia brassicoidea f. contracta, commonly known as the compact espeletia, is a unique plant species native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange composite flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cool, humid climates.
