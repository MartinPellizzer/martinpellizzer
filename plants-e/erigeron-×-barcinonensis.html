Erigeron × barcinonensis

Erigeron × barcinonensis, commonly known as the hybrid fleabane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically feature white ray florets surrounding a yellow disc. The plant has finely divided, gray-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as roadsides and prairies.

Erigeron × barcinonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
