Epidendrum hexagonum

Epidendrum hexagonum, commonly known as the hexagonal epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum hexagonum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
