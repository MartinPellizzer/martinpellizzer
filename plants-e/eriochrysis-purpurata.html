Eriochrysis purpurata

Eriochrysis purpurata, commonly known as the purple seaweed plant, is a member of the Chenopodiaceae family.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or coastal environments. The plant is characterized by its dense, rosette-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in saline conditions. Eriochrysis purpurata plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
