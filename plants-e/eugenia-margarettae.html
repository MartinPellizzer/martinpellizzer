Eugenia margarettae

Eugenia margarettae, commonly known as the Margaretta's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Eugenia margarettae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
