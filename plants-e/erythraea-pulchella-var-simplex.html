Erythraea pulchella var. simplex

Erythraea pulchella var. simplex, commonly known as the simple erythraea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, pale pink to white flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
