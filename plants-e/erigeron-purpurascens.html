Erigeron purpurascens

Erigeron purpurascens, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and upright.

Erigeron purpurascens is often found in prairies, open woods, and along roadsides, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
