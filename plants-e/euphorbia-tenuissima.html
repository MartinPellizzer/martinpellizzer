Euphorbia tenuissima

Euphorbia tenuissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its fleshy stems, making it drought-tolerant. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its unique floral structures called cyathia.

Euphorbia tenuissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and distinctive appearance.
