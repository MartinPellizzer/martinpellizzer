Escallonia leucantha

Escallonia leucantha, commonly known as the silverleaf escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Escallonia leucantha is also valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various climates.
