Epilobium × lambertianum

Epilobium × lambertianum, commonly known as the Lambert's willow herb, is a hybrid plant species belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect or spreading growth habit and is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Epilobium × lambertianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
