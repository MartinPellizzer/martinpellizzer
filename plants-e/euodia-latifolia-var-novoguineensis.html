Euodia latifolia var. novoguineensis

Euodia latifolia var. novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea laurel, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, orange-red berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for hedges and landscape gardening in warm climates.
