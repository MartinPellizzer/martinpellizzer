Epidendrum vieirae

Epidendrum vieirae, commonly known as the Vieira's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces numerous flowering spikes with showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with a contrasting lip, and it thrives in warm, humid environments.

Epidendrum vieirae is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
