Epidendrum × dellense

Epidendrum × dellense, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a popular epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, tropical conditions.

Epidendrum × dellense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
