Elizabetha macrostachya

Elizabetha macrostachya, commonly known as the giant lespedeza, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, reddish-purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Elizabetha macrostachya is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and wetlands.
