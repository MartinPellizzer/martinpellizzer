Eremurus aucherianus

Eremurus aucherianus, commonly known as the giant spirea, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, yellowish flowers. Eremurus aucherianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public landscapes.
