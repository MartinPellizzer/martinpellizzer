Epidendrum viridifuscatum

Epidendrum viridifuscatum, commonly known as the greenish-gray epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and often have a distinctive grayish-green coloration.

Epidendrum viridifuscatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
