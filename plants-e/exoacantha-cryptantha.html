Exoacantha cryptantha

Exoacantha cryptantha, commonly known as the hidden acantha, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Exoacantha cryptantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
