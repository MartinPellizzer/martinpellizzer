Eurybiopsis macrorhiza

Eurybiopsis macrorhiza, commonly known as the large-rooted eurybiopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along stream banks. The plant features a robust root system and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insect species.
