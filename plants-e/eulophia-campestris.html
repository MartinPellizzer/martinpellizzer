Eulophia campestris

Eulophia campestris, commonly known as the field eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth, with long, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, often with a distinctive reddish or brownish tint.

Eulophia campestris is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions, making it a resilient species.
