Epidorkis multiflora

Epidorkis multiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
