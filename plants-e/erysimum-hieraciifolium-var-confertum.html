Erysimum hieraciifolium var. confertum

Erysimum hieraciifolium var. confertum, commonly known as the dense-leaved wallflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
