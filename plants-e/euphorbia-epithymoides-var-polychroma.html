Euphorbia epithymoides var. polychroma

Euphorbia epithymoides var. polychroma, commonly known as the variegated spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a houseplant.
