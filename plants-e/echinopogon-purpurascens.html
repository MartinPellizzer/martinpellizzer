Echinopogon purpurascens

Echinopogon purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-headed hairgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. This grass is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its striking purple-tinted inflorescences make it a notable species in ecological and ornamental contexts.
