Erythroxylum indicum

Erythroxylum indicum, commonly known as the Indian holly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by red or orange berries. Its leaves are elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
