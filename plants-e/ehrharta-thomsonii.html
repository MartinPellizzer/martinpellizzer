Ehrharta thomsonii

Ehrharta thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, erect stems that produce small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. It has a dense root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Thomson's grass is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
