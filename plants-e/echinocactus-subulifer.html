Echinocactus subulifer

Echinocactus subulifer, commonly known as the needleleaf echinocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are often covered in small, needle-like spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
