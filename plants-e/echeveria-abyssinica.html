Echeveria abyssinica

Echeveria abyssinica, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to Ethiopia.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are densely packed and often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight to maintain its vibrant color.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
