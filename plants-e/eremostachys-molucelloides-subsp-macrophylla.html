Eremostachys molucelloides subsp. macrophylla

Eremostachys molucelloides subsp.

macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf eremostachys, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Moluccas. It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, pale blue to violet flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry garden conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
