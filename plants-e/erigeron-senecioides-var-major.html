Erigeron senecioides var. major

Erigeron senecioides var. major, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. Its blue to purple flower heads are composed of numerous small florets, and it has hairy, linear leaves.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
