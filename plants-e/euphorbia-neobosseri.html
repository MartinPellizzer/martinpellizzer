Euphorbia neobosseri

Euphorbia neobosseri, commonly known as the Bosser's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branching stem system with cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters called cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are often reduced to small scales, and it is adapted to arid environments.

Euphorbia neobosseri is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.
