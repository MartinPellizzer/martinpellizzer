Eriachne bleeseri

Eriachne bleeseri, commonly known as the Blesser's eriachne, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, showing resilience to drought.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native grassland ecosystems.
