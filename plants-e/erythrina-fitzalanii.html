Erythrina fitzalanii

Erythrina fitzalanii, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive bright red flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features pinnately compound leaves and produces showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping and reforestation projects.

The seeds are encased in long, slender pods that split open to release the seeds, aiding in natural propagation.
