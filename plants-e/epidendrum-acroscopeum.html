Epidendrum acroscopeum

Epidendrum acroscopeum, commonly known as the Acroscopium Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored blossoms. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a compact rosette.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
