Echinops sphaerocephalus subsp. sphaerocephalus

Echinops sphaerocephalus subsp.

sphaerocephalus, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spherical, spiky blue or purple flower heads, which are composed of many small, bristly florets.

The plant has a robust, branched stem and narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.

Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and xeriscaping.
