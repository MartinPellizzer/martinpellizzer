Elatostema litseifolium

Elatostema litseifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Elatostemaceae and is characterized by its rosette-like growth habit and hairy, silver-colored leaves. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a waxy surface that reflects light. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are pollinated by insects.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
