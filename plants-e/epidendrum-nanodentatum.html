Epidendrum nanodentatum

Epidendrum nanodentatum, commonly known as the miniature toothed epidendrum, is a compact orchid native to Mexico.

It features a short, upright stem with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has a dense, velvety foliage that gives it a unique texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
