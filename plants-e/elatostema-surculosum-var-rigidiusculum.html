Elatostema surculosum var. rigidiusculum

Elatostema surculosum var. rigidiusculum, commonly known as the rigid-leaved elatostema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a resilient and hardy species suitable for xerophytic gardens.
