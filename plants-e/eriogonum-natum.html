Eriogonum natum

Eriogonum natum, commonly known as the desert mullein, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, greenish flowers. Eriogonum natum is often found in sagebrush steppe and desert scrub ecosystems.
