Enicostema axillare subsp. latilobum

Enicostema axillare subsp.

latilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed enicostema, is a perennial herb native to eastern Australia. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in dense spikes, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
