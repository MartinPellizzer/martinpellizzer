Euphorbia orthoclada subsp. vepretorum

Euphorbia orthoclada subsp.

vepretorum, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique cyathium structure, characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its stems for survival.

The sap of this plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
