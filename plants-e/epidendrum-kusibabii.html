Epidendrum kusibabii

Epidendrum kusibabii, commonly known as the Kusibabii Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, densely packed flowers, making it an attractive choice for ornamental cultivation. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Epidendrum kusibabii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
