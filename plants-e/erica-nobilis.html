Erica nobilis

Erica nobilis, commonly known as the noble heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. Erica nobilis thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
