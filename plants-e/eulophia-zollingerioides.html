Eulophia zollingerioides

Eulophia zollingerioides, commonly known as the Zollinger's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia zollingerioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
