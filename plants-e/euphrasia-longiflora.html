Euphrasia longiflora

Euphrasia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features slender stems that bear opposite leaves with toothed edges. Its most distinctive characteristic is its clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers with prominent yellow spots.

Euphrasia longiflora is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to support eye health.
