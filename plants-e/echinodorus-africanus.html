Echinodorus africanus

Echinodorus africanus, commonly known as African swordplant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Africa.

It has long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a prominent midrib. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshes and ponds. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

Echinodorus africanus is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appeal and ability to improve water quality.
