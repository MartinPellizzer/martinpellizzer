Eupatorium nanchuanense

Eupatorium nanchuanense, commonly known as Nanchuan eupatorium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium nanchuanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
