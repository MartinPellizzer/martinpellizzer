Eulophia monile var. brevipetala

Eulophia monile var. brevipetala, commonly known as the short-petalled eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with long, narrow petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
