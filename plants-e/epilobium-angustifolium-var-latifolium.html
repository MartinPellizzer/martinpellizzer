Epilobium angustifolium var. latifolium

Epilobium angustifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf willowherb, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture and a serrated edge. It produces showy, pink to purple flowers in spikes during the summer months.

This plant thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wet meadows, streambanks, and along roadsides.
