Erica rinzii

Erica rinzii, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering shrub native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Erica rinzii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to coastal climates.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
