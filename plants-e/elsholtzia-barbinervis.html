Elsholtzia barbinervis

Elsholtzia barbinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pale blue to purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom during the summer months.

It is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
