Epimedium enshiense

Epimedium enshiense, commonly known as the Enshi barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and appear in early spring before the leaves fully emerge.

Epimedium enshiense is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
