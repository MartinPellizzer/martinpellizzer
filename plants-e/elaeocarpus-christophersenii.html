Elaeocarpus christophersenii

Elaeocarpus christophersenii, commonly known as the Christmas tree or Christmas bush, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive, woody fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental elements during festive seasons.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
