Elwendia vaginata

Elwendia vaginata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Elwendia vaginata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
