Enterosora enterosoroides

Enterosora enterosoroides, commonly known as the red root snakeroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features a fibrous root system and lance-shaped leaves with a distinct reddish tinge along the veins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically pollinated by insects. Its leaves and roots contain various chemical compounds, some of which have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

Enterosora enterosoroides thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
