Euodia daniellii var. laxiflora

Euodia daniellii var. laxiflora, commonly known as the fragrant white ash, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The fruit is a small, globose drupe that matures to a reddish color, containing a single seed.
