Elatostema goudotianum var. coursii

Elatostema goudotianum var. coursii, commonly known as the Cours' prostrate elatostema, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with a dense, mat-forming habit, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and clustered in inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact growth and foliage.
