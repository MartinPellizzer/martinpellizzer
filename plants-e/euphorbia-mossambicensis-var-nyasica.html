Euphorbia mossambicensis var. nyasica

Euphorbia mossambicensis var. nyasica, commonly known as the Nyasica spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia (cup-like structures) that contain the true flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
