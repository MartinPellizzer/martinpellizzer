Eugenia curvipilosa

Eugenia curvipilosa, commonly known as the warty eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, warty fruits that are typically red or purple in color. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, and it produces small white or pink flowers.

Eugenia curvipilosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
