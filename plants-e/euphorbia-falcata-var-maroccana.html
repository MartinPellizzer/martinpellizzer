Euphorbia falcata var. maroccana

Euphorbia falcata var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan spurge, is a succulent plant native to North Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often ribbed and can grow in a shrub-like form. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a bluish-green coloration. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
