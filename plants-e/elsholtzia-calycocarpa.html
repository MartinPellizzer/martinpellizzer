Elsholtzia calycocarpa

Elsholtzia calycocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a strong, minty scent. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a blue or purple hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
