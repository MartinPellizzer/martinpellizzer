Eupatorium sladenianum

Eupatorium sladenianum, commonly known as Sladen's thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its propagation. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and wetlands.
