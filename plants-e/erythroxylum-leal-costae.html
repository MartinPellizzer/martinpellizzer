Erythroxylum leal-costae

Erythroxylum leal-costae, commonly known as the Costae Erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
