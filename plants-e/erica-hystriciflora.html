Erica hystriciflora

Erica hystriciflora, commonly known as the spiny heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica hystriciflora typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates with dry summers.

It is often found in fynbos vegetation, which is a unique biome in South Africa.
