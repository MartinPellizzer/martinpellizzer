Eugenia astronioides

Eugenia astronioides, commonly known as the Astronoides eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
