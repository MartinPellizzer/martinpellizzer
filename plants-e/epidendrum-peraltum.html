Epidendrum peraltum

Epidendrum peraltum, commonly known as the Paltum Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a somewhat fleshy texture.

Epidendrum peraltum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
