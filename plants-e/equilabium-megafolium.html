Equilabium megafolium

Equilabium megafolium, commonly known as the giant leaf orchid, is a unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to two meters in length, making it one of the largest leafy plants in its habitat. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its botanical structure includes a sympodial growth pattern, with each stem producing a new growth from a lateral bud.

This species is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
