Epilobium × aboense

Epilobium × aboense, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in terminal spikes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Epilobium × aboense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
