Erysimum schneideri

Erysimum schneideri, commonly known as Schneider's wallflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features upright stems with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
