Einadia polygonoides

Einadia polygonoides, commonly known as the slender polygonella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, clustered leaves that are typically lance-shaped. Its flowers are greenish-white and appear in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
