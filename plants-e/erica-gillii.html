Erica gillii

Erica gillii, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation. Erica gillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its winter-blooming habit.

It is also an important plant in local ecosystems, supporting various pollinators and wildlife.
