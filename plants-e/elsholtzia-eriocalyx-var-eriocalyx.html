Elsholtzia eriocalyx var. eriocalyx

Elsholtzia eriocalyx var. eriocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Lamiaceae family.
