Encyclia magnicallosa

Encyclia magnicallosa, commonly known as the Mexican sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, grass-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip that is often marked with darker pigmentation. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Encyclia magnicallosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
