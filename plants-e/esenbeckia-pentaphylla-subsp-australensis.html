Esenbeckia pentaphylla subsp. australensis

Esenbeckia pentaphylla subsp.

australensis, commonly known as the Australian esenbeckia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
