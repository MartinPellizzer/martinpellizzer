Elatostema dersum

Elatostema dersum, commonly known as the small shield fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elatostemataceae.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are often shield-shaped, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
