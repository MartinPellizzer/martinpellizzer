Eulophia guineensis

Eulophia guineensis, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a complex structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia guineensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
