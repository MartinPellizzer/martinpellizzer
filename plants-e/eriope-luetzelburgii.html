Eriope luetzelburgii

Eriope luetzelburgii, commonly known as the Luetzelburg's eriope, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation.
