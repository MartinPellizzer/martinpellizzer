Erysimum chamaephyton

Erysimum chamaephyton, commonly known as the rock cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

Erysimum chamaephyton is adapted to harsh conditions and is often found in mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.
