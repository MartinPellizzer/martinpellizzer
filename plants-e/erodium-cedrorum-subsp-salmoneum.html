Erodium cedrorum subsp. salmoneum

Erodium cedrorum subsp.

salmoneum, commonly known as the pink erodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a small annual herb with slender stems and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it a characteristic species of the Mediterranean flora.
