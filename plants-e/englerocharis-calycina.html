Englerocharis calycina

Englerocharis calycina, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers that resemble flames, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its unique and showy inflorescence. Its leaves are lance-shaped and evergreen, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Englerocharis calycina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
