Euphrasia polyadena f. elata

Euphrasia polyadena f. elata, commonly known as the tall yellow burnet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant features narrow, slightly hairy leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
