Echinodorus uruguayensis var. minor

Echinodorus uruguayensis var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf Amazon sword, is a compact aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green with a slight metallic sheen. This variety thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light exposure and is often used in planted aquariums. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense underwater carpets.

It is relatively easy to care for and contributes to water oxygenation and algae control in aquascapes.
