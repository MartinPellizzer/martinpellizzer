Eragrostis olida

Eragrostis olida, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow spikelet with small, spike-like flowers.

Eragrostis olida is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
