Epidendrum commelinispathum

Epidendrum commelinispathum, commonly known as the yellow spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in many regions for its unique floral display.
