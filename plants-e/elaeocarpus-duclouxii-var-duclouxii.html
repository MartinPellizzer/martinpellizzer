Elaeocarpus duclouxii var. duclouxii

Elaeocarpus duclouxii var. duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux's elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive, woody fruits that contain a single seed. The plant belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is typically found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth margin, and it produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological importance in its native habitats.
