Erigeron tenuifolius

Erigeron tenuifolius, commonly known as slenderleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
