Epacris impressa f. lucida

Epacris impressa f. lucida, commonly known as the shining epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and glossy, contributing to its striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.
