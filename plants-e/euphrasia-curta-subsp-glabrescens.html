Euphrasia curta subsp. glabrescens

Euphrasia curta subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the short euphrasia, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family. It is a small herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, hairless stems and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers.
