Euphorbia maddenii

Euphorbia maddenii, commonly known as the silver euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with its pale green, fleshy stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a spiny texture and produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Euphorbia maddenii is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
