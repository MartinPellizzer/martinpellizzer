Elaeophora abutifolia

Elaeophora abutifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming a compact rosette structure. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Elaeophora abutifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
