Eupatorium rotundifolium var. rotundifolium

Eupatorium rotundifolium var. rotundifolium, commonly known as roundleaf boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 1.5 meters tall. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are rounded and often have a slightly wavy edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
