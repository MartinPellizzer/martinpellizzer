Epilobium strictum

Epilobium strictum, commonly known as upright willow herb, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows up to 1 meter tall, characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in spikes, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, riverbanks, and woodland edges.

Epilobium strictum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
