Erica echiiflora var. purpurea

Erica echiiflora var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple heath, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically evergreen, leathery, and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
