Electranthera

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Electranthera species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and as houseplants due to their striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
