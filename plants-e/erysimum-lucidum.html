Erysimum lucidum

Erysimum lucidum, commonly known as the brightleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum lucidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
