Eugenia scalarinervis

Eugenia scalarinervis, commonly known as the mountain eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a distinctive venation pattern. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color.

The fruit is a small, edible berry that is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
