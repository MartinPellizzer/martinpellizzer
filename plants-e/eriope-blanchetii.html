Eriope blanchetii

Eriope blanchetii, commonly known as the mountain eriope, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapted to the harsh mountain climate.

Eriope blanchetii produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
