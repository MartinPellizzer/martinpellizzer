Erica pillansii

Erica pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom in spring. Erica pillansii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
