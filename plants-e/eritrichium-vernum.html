Eritrichium vernum

Eritrichium vernum, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a small flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and rocky slopes, preferring cool, temperate climates. The plant produces delicate, nodding flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, hairy, and lance-shaped, often with a bluish-green hue.

Eritrichium vernum is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting early pollinators.
