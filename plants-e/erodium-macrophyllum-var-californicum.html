Erodium macrophyllum var. californicum

Erodium macrophyllum var. californicum, commonly known as California pusley, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in coastal areas and open fields.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread easily in various habitats.
