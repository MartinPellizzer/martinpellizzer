Eremophila rostrata subsp. rostrata

Eremophila rostrata subsp.

rostrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eremophila, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and spikes of pink to purple flowers.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

This subspecies is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
