Epidendrum lumbaquiense

Epidendrum lumbaquiense, commonly known as the Lumbaquiense orchid, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or other structures rather than in soil. The plant features a compact, branching structure with numerous pseudobulbs and fleshy leaves. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and are often brightly colored, showcasing a range of hues.

Epidendrum lumbaquiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
