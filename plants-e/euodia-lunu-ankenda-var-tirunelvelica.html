Euodia lunu-ankenda var. tirunelvelica

Euodia lunu-ankenda var. tirunelvelica, commonly known as the Ceylon cinnamon tree, is a species native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is highly valued for its medicinal and culinary uses. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, round fruits containing smooth, brown seeds. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly serrated margin and emit a strong, spicy fragrance when crushed.

This variety is particularly noted for its distinct flavor and is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
