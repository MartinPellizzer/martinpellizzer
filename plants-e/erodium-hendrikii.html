Erodium hendrikii

Erodium hendrikii, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, fern-like leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal areas. It is often found in open habitats such as fynbos vegetation.

Erodium hendrikii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
