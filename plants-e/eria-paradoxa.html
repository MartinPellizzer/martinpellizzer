Eria paradoxa

Eria paradoxa, commonly known as the paradox orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and striking appearance. The plant features a slender stem with a single, elongated leaf and a spike of small, fragrant flowers. Eria paradoxa is often grown as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.

Its growth habits and floral structure make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
