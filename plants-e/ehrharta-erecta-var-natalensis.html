Ehrharta erecta var. natalensis

Ehrharta erecta var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can tolerate poor soils.

This variety is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
