Epidendrum crescentilobum

Epidendrum crescentilobum, commonly known as the Crescent-lobed Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hair. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its elegant appearance and ease of care.
