Euonymus europaeus f. microphyllus

Euonymus europaeus f. microphyllus, commonly known as the European spindle tree, is a compact shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

It produces distinctive red-orange berries that persist through the winter, adding ornamental value to the landscape. The plant is native to Europe and thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight. It is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and seasonal color.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic appeal and low maintenance.
