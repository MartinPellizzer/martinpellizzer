Elatostema yungshunense

Elatostema yungshunense, commonly known as the Yungshun velvetleaf, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a small herbaceous plant characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It is native to China, specifically the Yungshun region, and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on tropical flora and plant morphology.
