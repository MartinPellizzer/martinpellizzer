Erica pilifera

Erica pilifera, commonly known as the silky heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly referred to as the heath or heather family. The plant is characterized by its dense, low-growing shrub form and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica pilifera thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
