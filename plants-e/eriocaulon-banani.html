Eriocaulon banani

Eriocaulon banani, commonly known as the banana marsh plant, is a member of the Eriocaulaceae family.

It is a small herbaceous plant that typically grows in wetland and marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in areas with high humidity.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.
