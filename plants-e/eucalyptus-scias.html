Eucalyptus scias

Eucalyptus scias, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallee, is a hardy Australian shrub or small tree that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-stemmed trunk and lance-shaped leaves that are typically grey-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Eucalyptus scias is well adapted to drought conditions and is often found in sandy or clay soils.

Its essential oils, derived from the leaves, have been used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy for their therapeutic properties.
