Euthemis elegantissima

Euthemis elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant starflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Euthemis elegantissima is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
