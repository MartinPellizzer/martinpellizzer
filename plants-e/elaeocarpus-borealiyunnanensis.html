Elaeocarpus borealiyunnanensis

Elaeocarpus borealiyunnanensis, commonly known as the northern Yunnan elaeocarpus, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain a single seed.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions of Yunnan Province.
