Epidendrum atropurpureum var. randii

Epidendrum atropurpureum var. randii, commonly known as the Purple Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking purple and green foliage, with flowers that range from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its blooms are relatively short-lived but often appear in clusters, adding ornamental value.

This variety is appreciated for its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
