Epidendrum insolatum

Epidendrum insolatum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum insolatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
