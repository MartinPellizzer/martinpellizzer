Epipactis × gauckleri

Epipactis × gauckleri, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and a long, spiral arrangement of flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Epipactis × gauckleri is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Epipactis palustris and Epipactis helleborine.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
