Epilobium sparsifolium

Epilobium sparsifolium, commonly known as the slender willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, riverbanks, and wetlands. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a hairy texture. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Epilobium sparsifolium is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
