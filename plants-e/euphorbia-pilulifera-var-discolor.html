Euphorbia pilulifera var. discolor

Euphorbia pilulifera var. discolor, commonly known as the Variegated Spurge, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, colorful cyathia, which are the modified inflorescences typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
