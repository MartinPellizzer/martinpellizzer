Euonymus bancanus

Euonymus bancanus, commonly known as the African boxwood, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, which is typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red, spherical fruits. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a leathery texture, making it suitable for use as a ornamental plant.

Euonymus bancanus is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
