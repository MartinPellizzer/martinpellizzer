Epipactis latifolia f. foliosa

Epipactis latifolia f. foliosa, commonly known as the broad-leaf helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. Native to Europe, this orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and is often found in acidic soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in botanical studies and ecological surveys.
