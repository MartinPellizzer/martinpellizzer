Erechtites elongatus

Erechtites elongatus, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.
