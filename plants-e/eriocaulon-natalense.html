Eriocaulon natalense

Eriocaulon natalense, commonly known as Natal grass, is a member of the Poaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a small, tufted grass with dense, fibrous roots that help it thrive in wetland environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in marshy areas and along the edges of water bodies.

Due to its adaptability, it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
