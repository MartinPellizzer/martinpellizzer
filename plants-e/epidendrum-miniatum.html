Epidendrum miniatum

Epidendrum miniatum, commonly known as the miniature phalaenopsis orchid, is a compact, epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining potting media. Its flowers are often arranged in a spike, and it is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.

Epidendrum miniatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
