Eriachne pallescens

Eriachne pallescens, commonly known as the pale eriochloa, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in coastal dunes and grasslands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand saline and arid conditions, making it a hardy plant in coastal environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native vegetation in its habitat.
