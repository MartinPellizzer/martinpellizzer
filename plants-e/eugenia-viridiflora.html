Eugenia viridiflora

Eugenia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, which bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Eugenia viridiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
