Erica berzelioides

Erica berzelioides, commonly known as the Berzelioides heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. Erica berzelioides thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is typically found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
