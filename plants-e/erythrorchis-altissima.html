Erythrorchis altissima

Erythrorchis altissima, commonly known as the tall red orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the wet tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. Its inflorescence typically consists of several flowers arranged in a spike, with the lower ones often being more vibrant.

Erythrorchis altissima thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
