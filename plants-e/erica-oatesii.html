Erica oatesii

Erica oatesii, commonly known as the Oates' heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica oatesii typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in coastal or mountainous regions.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in mild climates.
