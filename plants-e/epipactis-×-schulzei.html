Epipactis × schulzei

Epipactis × schulzei, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Epipactis helleborine and Epipactis purpurea.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe, particularly in moist woodland habitats. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow to brownish flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in a basal rosette.

The species is notable for its ability to hybridize and adapt to various environmental conditions.
