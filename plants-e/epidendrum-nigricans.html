Epidendrum nigricans

Epidendrum nigricans, commonly known as the Black Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking clusters of dark red to black flowers. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Epidendrum nigricans is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
