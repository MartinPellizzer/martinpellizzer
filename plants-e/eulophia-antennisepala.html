Eulophia antennisepala

Eulophia antennisepala, commonly known as the slender-lobed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia antennisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
