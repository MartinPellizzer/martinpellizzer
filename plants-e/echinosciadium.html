Echinosciadium

Echinosciadium, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiky, spherical seed heads that resemble a globe, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Echinosciadium species are often cultivated for their ornamental value and can thrive in full sun with minimal water.
