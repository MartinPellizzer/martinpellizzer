Euonymus ellipticus

Euonymus ellipticus, commonly known as the glossy euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features elliptic, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red, bead-like berries that persist into winter. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and winter interest.

Euonymus ellipticus is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
