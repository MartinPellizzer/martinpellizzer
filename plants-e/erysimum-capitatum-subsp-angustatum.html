Erysimum capitatum subsp. angustatum

Erysimum capitatum subsp.

angustatum, commonly known as narrowleaf wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States. It typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as slopes and cliffs.

The plant features upright stems with lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are bright yellow, clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences, and bloom in spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
