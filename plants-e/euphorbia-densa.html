Euphorbia densa

Euphorbia densa, commonly known as the dense euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, branching stem covered in small, greenish-yellow leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts that give it a striking appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia densa is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
