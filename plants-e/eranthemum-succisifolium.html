Eranthemum succisifolium

Eranthemum succisifolium, commonly known as the hairy leaf eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb with succulent, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique foliage and compact growth habit make it an attractive addition to ornamental plant collections.
