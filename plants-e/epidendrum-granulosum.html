Epidendrum granulosum

Epidendrum granulosum, commonly known as the granular epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant features fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum granulosum is a pseudobulbous orchid that requires well-draining soil and regular watering during its growing season.
