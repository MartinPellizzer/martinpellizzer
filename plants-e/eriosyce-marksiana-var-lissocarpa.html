Eriosyce marksiana var. lissocarpa

Eriosyce marksiana var. lissocarpa, commonly known as the silky eriosyce, is a species of cactus native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of pale green to grayish-blue hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its stems are cylindrical and segmented, often forming a low-growing rosette.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in succulent gardens.
