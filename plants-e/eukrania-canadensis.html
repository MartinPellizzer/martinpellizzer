Eukrania canadensis

Eukrania canadensis, commonly known as Canada bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
