Euphrasia officinalis subvar. gracilis

Euphrasia officinalis subvar. gracilis, commonly known as slender eyebright, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported eye-health benefits.
