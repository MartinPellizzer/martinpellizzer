Euonymus alatus var. striatus

Euonymus alatus var. striatus, commonly known as the winged euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winged stems that give it its common name. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth margin and a slightly serrated edge. In the fall, it produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
