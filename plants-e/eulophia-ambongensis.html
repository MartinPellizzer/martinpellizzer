Eulophia ambongensis

Eulophia ambongensis, commonly known as the Ambongensis orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces erect, leafy stems with clustered inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption from the soil.

Eulophia ambongensis is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
