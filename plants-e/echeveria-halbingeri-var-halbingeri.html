Echeveria halbingeri var. halbingeri

Echeveria halbingeri var. halbingeri, commonly known as the Halbinger's Echeveria, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray to silver-green in color, with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pinkish-white flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.

Its low maintenance and attractive appearance make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
