Epidendrum estrellense

Epidendrum estrellense, commonly known as the Estrellense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and open woodlands. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the summer months.

Epidendrum estrellense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
