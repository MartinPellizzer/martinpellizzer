Eupatorium omeiense

Eupatorium omeiense, commonly known as the Sichuan boneset, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Eupatorium omeiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
