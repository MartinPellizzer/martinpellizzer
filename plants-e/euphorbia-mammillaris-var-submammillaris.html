Euphorbia mammillaris var. submammillaris

Euphorbia mammillaris var. submammillaris, commonly known as the "Candelabra Euphorbia," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, branching structure with thick, ribbed stems that form a tree-like shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright sunlight.

Due to its spiny stems, it requires careful handling to avoid skin irritation.
