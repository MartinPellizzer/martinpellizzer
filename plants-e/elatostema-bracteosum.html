Elatostema bracteosum

Elatostema bracteosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf elatostema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and it produces tiny, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its stems are often prostrate or trailing, forming dense mats.

Elatostema bracteosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
