Elaeocarpus yunnanensis

Elaeocarpus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan elaeocarp, is a deciduous tree native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the branches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of tropical and subtropical flora.
