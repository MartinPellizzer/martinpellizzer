Epidendrum rhaibogyrum

Epidendrum rhaibogyrum, commonly known as the Rhaibogyrum Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.
