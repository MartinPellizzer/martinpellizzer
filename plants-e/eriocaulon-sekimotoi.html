Eriocaulon sekimotoi

Eriocaulon sekimotoi, commonly known as the Sekimoto's spike-moss, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which includes various species of spike-mosses. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, often in shallow water or moist soil. Its structure consists of a short stem with whorled leaves, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Eriocaulon sekimotoi is notable for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in water gardens.
