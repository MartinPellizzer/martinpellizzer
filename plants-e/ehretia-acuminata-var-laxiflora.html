Ehretia acuminata var. laxiflora

Ehretia acuminata var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered ehretia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its foliage and floral display.
