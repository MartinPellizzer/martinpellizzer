Erica esterhuyseniae var. tetramera

Erica esterhuyseniae var. tetramera, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate appearance to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
