Eustoma lacteum

Eustoma lacteum, commonly known as the milkflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant and long-lasting blooms.
