Epidendrum anastasioi

Epidendrum anastasioi, commonly known as the Anastasio's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features erect, hairy stems with numerous leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually in shades of white, yellow, or green.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
