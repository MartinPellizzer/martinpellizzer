Erythroxylum uniflorum

Erythroxylum uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It produces a reddish-brown fruit that contains seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
