Euphorbia stellata subsp. stellata

Euphorbia stellata subsp.

stellata, commonly known as the star spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a branched stem system with numerous small, spiny shoots that bear clusters of yellowish-green flowers.

The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. It produces cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
