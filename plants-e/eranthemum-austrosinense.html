Eranthemum austrosinense

Eranthemum austrosinense, commonly known as the southern China geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Eranthemum austrosinense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
