Epilobium Astonii

Epilobium astonii is a rare and striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern England. It is known for its distinctive pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This species thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dunes and coastal scrub. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is crucial for preserving this rare botanical treasure.
