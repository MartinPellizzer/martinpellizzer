Eugenia flavonigra

Eugenia flavonigra, commonly known as the golden eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit, which is typically a small, yellowish berry, is edible and has a sweet, aromatic flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for use in local cuisines.
