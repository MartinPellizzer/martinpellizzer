Elatostema glomeratum

Elatostema glomeratum, commonly known as the clustered elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually white or pale green, attracting insects for pollination.

Elatostema glomeratum is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is often found in low-light habitats.
