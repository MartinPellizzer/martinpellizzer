Eremanthus angustifolius

Eremanthus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eremanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Eremanthus angustifolius is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
