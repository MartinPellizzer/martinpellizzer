Epidendrum jose-alvarezii

Epidendrum jose-alvarezii, commonly known as the Jose Alvarez Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, light green to white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, demonstrating its epiphytic nature. Its leaves are usually dark green and slightly textured, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
