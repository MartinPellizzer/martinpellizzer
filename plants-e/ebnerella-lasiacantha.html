Ebnerella lasiacantha

Ebnerella lasiacantha, commonly known as the broad-leafed milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweeds, and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers.
