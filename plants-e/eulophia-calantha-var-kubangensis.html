Eulophia calantha var. kubangensis

Eulophia calantha var. kubangensis, commonly known as the Kubangensis Eulophia, is a rare orchid species native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant features elongated, lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and exhibit a range of colors, often with a mix of white, yellow, and green tones.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
