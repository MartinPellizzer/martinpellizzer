Eugenia hookeri

Eugenia hookeri, commonly known as the New Zealand holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. These flowers are followed by bright red berries that are edible but have a tart flavor.

Eugenia hookeri is well adapted to coastal environments and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils.
