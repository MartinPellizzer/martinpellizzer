Elatostema backeri

Elatostema backeri, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
