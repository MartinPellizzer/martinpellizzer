Echeveria michihuacana

Echeveria michihuacana, commonly known as the Michoacan sunset echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically blue-green to purple in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange during the spring. It prefers well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
