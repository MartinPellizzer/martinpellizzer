Ernestia confertiflora

Ernestia confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered ernestia, is a member of the Ericaceae family, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit.

It is native to South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features small, evergreen leaves that are typically leathery and glossy. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying shades of pink or white.

Ernestia confertiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
