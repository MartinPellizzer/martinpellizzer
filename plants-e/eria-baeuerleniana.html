Eria baeuerleniana

Eria baeuerleniana, commonly known as the Baeuerlen's orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
