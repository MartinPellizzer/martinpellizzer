Encyclia christii

Encyclia christii, commonly known as the Christ's Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence with several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Encyclia christii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
