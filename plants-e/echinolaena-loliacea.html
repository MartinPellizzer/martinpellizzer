Echinolaena loliacea

Echinolaena loliacea, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters, often accompanied by spiny calyces. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
