Epidendrum grayumii

Epidendrum grayumii, commonly known as the Gray's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and can grow up to several feet in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum grayumii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
