Eria reticosa

Eria reticosa, commonly known as the reticulate orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like pattern on its leaves, which gives it its scientific name. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors including white, yellow, and orange.

Eria reticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
