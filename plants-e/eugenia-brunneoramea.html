Eugenia brunneoramea

Eugenia brunneoramea, commonly known as the brown-stem eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish-brown stems and lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, red, berry-like fruits. Eugenia brunneoramea thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
