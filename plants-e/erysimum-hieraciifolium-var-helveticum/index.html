Erysimum Hieraciifolium Var. Helveticum

Erysimum hieraciifolium var. helveticum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is known for its small, bright yellow blooms that appear in early spring. The plant has finely divided leaves that resemble those of a dandelion, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in mountainous habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
