Exacum tricolor

Exacum tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tricolored flowers that typically display shades of purple, white, and pink. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions. Its leaves are velvety and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating.

Exacum tricolor is well-suited for indoor cultivation and is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value.
