Eriachne obtusa var. glauca

Eriachne obtusa var. glauca, commonly known as bluebulrush, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with moist soil conditions.
