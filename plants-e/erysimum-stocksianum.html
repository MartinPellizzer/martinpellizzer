Erysimum stocksianum

Erysimum stocksianum, commonly known as the heartleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum stocksianum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
