Epilobium × huteri

Epilobium × huteri, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the genus Epilobium, which is part of the evening primrose family (Onagraceae). The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are showy, with five pink or purple petals, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Epilobium × huteri is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
