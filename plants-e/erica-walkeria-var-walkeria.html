Erica walkeria var. walkeria

Erica walkeria var. walkeria, commonly known as the common heather, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are needle-like and evergreen, providing a dense, foliage-covered structure.

Erica walkeria var. walkeria thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
