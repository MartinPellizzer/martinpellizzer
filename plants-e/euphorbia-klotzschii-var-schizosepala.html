Euphorbia klotzschii var. schizosepala

Euphorbia klotzschii var. schizosepala, commonly known as the Schizosepala spurge, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with thick, fleshy stems that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow花朵, which are surrounded by specialized leaf-like structures called bracts. Its leaves are typically narrow and may bear small, white or yellowish spots.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
