Epidendrum gnomoides

Epidendrum gnomoides, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a short, compact stem with a few leaves and a single, delicate flower that is usually white or pale green. Its flowers are small and clustered, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal.

Epidendrum gnomoides is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate light and regular watering.
