Elsholtzia pseudocristata var. saxatilis

Elsholtzia pseudocristata var. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and spikes of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or gravelly habitats. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
