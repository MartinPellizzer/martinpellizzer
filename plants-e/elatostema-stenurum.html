Elatostema stenurum

Elatostema stenurum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-white in color, and it produces small, round berries.

Elatostema stenurum is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
