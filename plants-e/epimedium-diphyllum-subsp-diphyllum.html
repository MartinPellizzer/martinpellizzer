Epimedium diphyllum subsp. diphyllum

Epimedium diphyllum subsp.

diphyllum, commonly known as the barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, upright stems. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodlands and rocky slopes.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
