Euphoria elongata

Euphoria elongata, commonly known as the elongated euphoria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems that can reach lengths of several meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Euphoria elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
