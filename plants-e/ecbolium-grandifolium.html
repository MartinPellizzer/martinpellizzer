Ecbolium grandifolium

Ecbolium grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf ecbolium, is a member of the Leguminosae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits.

Ecbolium grandifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological roles in supporting pollinators.
