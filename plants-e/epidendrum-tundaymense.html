Epidendrum tundaymense

Epidendrum tundaymense, commonly known as the Tunday Mountain Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit and epiphytic tendencies. The plant produces showy, colorful flowers that are typically clustered in racemes, with a distinctive appearance that attracts pollinators. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum tundaymense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
