Eutaxia virgata var. diffusa

Eutaxia virgata var. diffusa, commonly known as the spreading eutaxia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, spreading growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight bluish-green hue.

This variety is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
