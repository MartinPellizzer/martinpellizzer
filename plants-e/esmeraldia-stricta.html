Esmeraldia stricta

Esmeraldia stricta, commonly known as the Emerald Stem Plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and clusters of small, vibrant green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming during the warmer months. Esmeraldia stricta prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
