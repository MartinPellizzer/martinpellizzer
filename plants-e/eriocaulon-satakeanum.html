Eriocaulon satakeanum

Eriocaulon satakeanum, commonly known as the Satake's spike-moss, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curved, with a soft, velvety texture.

Eriocaulon satakeanum is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for microorganisms.
