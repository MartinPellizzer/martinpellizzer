Erica lituiflora var. breviflora

Erica lituiflora var. breviflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, needle-like leaves that are typically a glossy green. The plant produces fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense, compact growth in coastal and sandy environments.
