Eugenia oleoides

Eugenia oleoides, commonly known as the Australian brush cherry, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, cherry-like fruits. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract wildlife.
