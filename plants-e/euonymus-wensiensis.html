Euonymus wensiensis

Euonymus wensiensis, commonly known as the Chinese burning bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright red foliage in autumn, which makes it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that turn vibrant colors in the fall. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red capsules that contain seeds.

Euonymus wensiensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
