Echeveria cuspidata var. gemmula

Echeveria cuspidata var. gemmula, commonly known as the Gemmula Echeveria, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to Mexico.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a slight powdery coating. The leaves are often adorned with tiny, sharp teeth along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces vibrant purple-pink flowers in late spring to early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to well-drained soil and bright sunlight.
