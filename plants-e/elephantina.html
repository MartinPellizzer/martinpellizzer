Elephantina

Elephantina, commonly known as the elephant's foot plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that resembles an elephant's foot, which stores water to help it survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, white flowers during the growing season, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are narrow and often turn reddish in full sun, enhancing its visual appeal.

Elephantina is prized for its resilience and ability to thrive in low-water environments.
