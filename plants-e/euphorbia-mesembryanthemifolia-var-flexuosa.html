Euphorbia mesembryanthemifolia var. flexuosa

Euphorbia mesembryanthemifolia var. flexuosa, commonly known as the Blue Glow euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are the specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbia genus. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
