Elaphrium galeottianum

Elaphrium galeottianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Elaphrium galeottianum is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
