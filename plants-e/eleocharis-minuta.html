Eleocharis minuta

Eleocharis minuta, commonly known as the minute spike-rush, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and tiny, spike-like inflorescences that produce small, wind-pollinated flowers. Eleocharis minuta is an important component of wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
