Elaeodendron lycioides

Elaeodendron lycioides, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a small, deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. Its bark and leaves contain milky sap, which is characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family.
