Epidendrum maculosum

Epidendrum maculosum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or purple flowers with yellow markings. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty and longevity.

Epidendrum maculosum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
