Eucryphia billardierei var. milliganii

Eucryphia billardierei var. milliganii, commonly known as the Milligan's eucryphia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated for its visual appeal and nectar-rich flowers.
