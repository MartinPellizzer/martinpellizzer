Euphorbia ruiziana

Euphorbia ruiziana, commonly known as the Ruiz's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique cyathium structure. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia ruiziana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
