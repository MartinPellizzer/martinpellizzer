Ehretia serrata var. pyrifolia

Ehretia serrata var. pyrifolia, commonly known as the Chinese mahogany or Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are serrated along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden environments.

Its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban conditions make it a popular choice for landscaping.
