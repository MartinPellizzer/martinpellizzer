Euphrasia × valentinii

Euphrasia × valentinii, commonly known as the Valentine's eye or the heart's eye, is a hybrid plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. Native to the British Isles, this species is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and hedgerows. The plant grows to a height of about 10–30 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It blooms from late spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a charming and early-flowering plant.
