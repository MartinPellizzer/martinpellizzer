Erucastrum palustre

Erucastrum palustre, commonly known as swamp crucifer, is a flowering plant native to wetland areas in North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant typically grows in marshy or swampy habitats, thriving in moist, acidic soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
