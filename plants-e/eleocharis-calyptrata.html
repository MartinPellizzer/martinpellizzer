Eleocharis calyptrata

Eleocharis calyptrata, commonly known as the common spike-rush, is a species of grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Eleocharis calyptrata plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms.
