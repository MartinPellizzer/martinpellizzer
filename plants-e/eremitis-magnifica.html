Eremitis magnifica

Eremitis magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent eremite, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture.

Eremitis magnifica thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish.
