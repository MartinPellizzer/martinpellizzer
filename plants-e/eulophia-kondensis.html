Eulophia kondensis

Eulophia kondensis, commonly known as the Kondh eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of India.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
