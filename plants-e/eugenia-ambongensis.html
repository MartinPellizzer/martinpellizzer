Eugenia ambongensis

Eugenia ambongensis, commonly known as the Ambongensis Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are typically small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
