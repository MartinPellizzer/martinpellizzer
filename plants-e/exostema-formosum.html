Exostema formosum

Exostema formosum, commonly known as the Taiwan fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce clusters of small, green, fan-shaped leaves. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture for optimal growth.
