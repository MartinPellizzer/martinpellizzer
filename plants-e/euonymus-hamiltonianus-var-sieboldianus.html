Euonymus hamiltonianus var. sieboldianus

Euonymus hamiltonianus var. sieboldianus, commonly known as the Siebold's euonymus, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers in spring, followed by distinctive red, lantern-like fruits in autumn. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
