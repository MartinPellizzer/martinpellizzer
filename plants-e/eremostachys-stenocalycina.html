Eremostachys stenocalycina

Eremostachys stenocalycina, commonly known as the narrow-calyx eremostachys, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, hairy stems, and small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of pale blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperatures.

Eremostachys stenocalycina is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
