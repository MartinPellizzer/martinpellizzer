Eugenia sargentii

Eugenia sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine.
