Eriostemon hillebrandii var. brevifolius

Eriostemon hillebrandii var. brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
