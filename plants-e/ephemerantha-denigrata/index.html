Ephemerantha Denigrata

Ephemerantha denigrata is a rare and elusive flowering plant known for its brief blooming period. It thrives in specific, often remote habitats, making it difficult to study in the wild. The plant's name reflects its fleeting presence, as it only flowers for a short time each year. Despite its small size, it plays a vital role in its ecosystem. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from environmental threats.
