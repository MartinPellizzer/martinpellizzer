Eleocharis soloniensis

Eleocharis soloniensis, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. This species is typically found in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and the edges of ponds. Eleocharis soloniensis has small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in dense spikes.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
