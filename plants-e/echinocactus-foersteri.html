Echinocactus foersteri

Echinocactus foersteri, commonly known as the golden barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of bright yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
