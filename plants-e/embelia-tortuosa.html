Embelia tortuosa

Embelia tortuosa, commonly known as the zigzag embelia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
