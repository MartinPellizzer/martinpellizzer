Eragrostis Tatarica

Eragrostis tatarica is a hardy annual grass native to eastern Asia. It is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and extreme climates. This plant is commonly found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its slender stems and small spikelets make it a distinctive sight in the wild. Despite its humble appearance, it plays an important role in ecological systems and has been used in traditional medicine.
