Epilobium bongardii f. umbrosum

Epilobium bongardii f. umbrosum, commonly known as the shade-loving willow herb, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem.
