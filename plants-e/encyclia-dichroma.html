Encyclia dichroma

Encyclia dichroma, commonly known as the dichromatic orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its compact appearance.

Encyclia dichroma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
