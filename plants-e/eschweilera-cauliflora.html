Eschweilera cauliflora

Eschweilera cauliflora, commonly known as the pink-veined wax palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are often pink-veined. The plant is notable for its cauliflorous flowering habit, where flowers develop directly on the trunk and branches rather than at the tips of stems. Its unique morphology makes it a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.

Eschweilera cauliflora plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting diverse wildlife and contributing to the rainforest's biodiversity.
