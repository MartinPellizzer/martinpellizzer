Epidendrum campylorhachis

Epidendrum campylorhachis, commonly known as the curly lip orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, colorful flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered with a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Epidendrum campylorhachis is often cultivated as a decorative orchid due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
