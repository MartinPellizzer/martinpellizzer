Eugenia anthacanthoides

Eugenia anthacanthoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Eugenia anthacanthoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
