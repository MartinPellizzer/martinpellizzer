Epipactis scariosa

Epipactis scariosa, commonly known as the scarious helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. It has a complex root system and produces a single, erect stem with several flower spikes.

Epipactis scariosa is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments and is often found in association with other orchid species.
