Echites torulosus

Echites torulosus, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
