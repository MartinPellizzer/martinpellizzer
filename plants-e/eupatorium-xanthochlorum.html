Eupatorium xanthochlorum

Eupatorium xanthochlorum, commonly known as yellow false fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
