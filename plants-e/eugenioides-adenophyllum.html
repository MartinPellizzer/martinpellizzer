Eugenioides adenophyllum

Eugenioides adenophyllum, commonly known as the hairy-leaved eugenioid, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
