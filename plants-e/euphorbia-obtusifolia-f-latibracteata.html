Euphorbia obtusifolia f. latibracteata

Euphorbia obtusifolia f. latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that are often tinged with red or purple, and its leaves are typically oval or spatulate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by colorful bracts that are broader than those of the typical species. It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in arid or semi-arid conditions, making it well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
