Eriopetalum

Eriopetalum, commonly known as the silky mistflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family.

These plants are characterized by their soft, hairy foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. They are native to Australia and are often found in dry, rocky habitats. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance.

Eriopetalum species are valued for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
