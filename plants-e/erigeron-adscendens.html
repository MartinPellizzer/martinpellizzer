Erigeron adscendens

Erigeron adscendens, commonly known as the ascending fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The showy white to pale blue flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
