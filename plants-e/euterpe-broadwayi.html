Euterpe broadwayi

Euterpe broadwayi, commonly known as the broadway palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforest canopy. Its fruit, which is edible, is often consumed by local wildlife and has been used by indigenous communities.

Euterpe broadwayi plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing food and shelter for various species.
