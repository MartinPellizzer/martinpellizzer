Epidendrum chauvetii

Epidendrum chauvetii, commonly known as the Chauvet's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually elliptical and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Epidendrum chauvetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for horticultural purposes.
