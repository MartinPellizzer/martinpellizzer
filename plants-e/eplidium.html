Eplidium

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Eplidium thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
