Erythraea longiflora

Erythraea longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered erythraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Erythraeeae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

Erythraea longiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
