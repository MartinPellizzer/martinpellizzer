Eryngium kermanense

Eryngium kermanense, commonly known as the Kerman thistle, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny leaves and produces blue to purple flowers, which attract pollinators. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to harsh climatic conditions.
