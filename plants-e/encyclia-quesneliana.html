Encyclia quesneliana

Encyclia quesneliana, commonly known as the Quesnel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, grass-like growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It typically blooms in the spring and is found in moist, shaded environments.

Encyclia quesneliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
