Eugenia foetida

Eugenia foetida, commonly known as the stinking eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and has a distinctive, somewhat unpleasant odor.
