Etlingera metriocheilos var. major

Etlingera metriocheilos var. major, commonly known as the giant flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its large, showy, red and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphytic or lithophytic species, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

The species is noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for various pollinators in its native habitat.
