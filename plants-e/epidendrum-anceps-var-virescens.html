Epidendrum anceps var. virescens

Epidendrum anceps var. virescens, commonly known as the Emerald Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking emerald-green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It prefers warm temperatures and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its foliage and blooms make it a visually appealing addition to both gardens and greenhouses.
