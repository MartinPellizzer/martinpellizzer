Encyclia ensiformis

Encyclia ensiformis, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, spurred flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of several showy flowers that bloom during the spring and summer months.

Encyclia ensiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
