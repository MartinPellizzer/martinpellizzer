Eulophia amplipetala

Eulophia amplipetala, commonly known as the broad-leaved eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This plant typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade. Its leaves are broad and elongated, often reaching lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

The flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are characterized by their intricate, fragrant structure.
