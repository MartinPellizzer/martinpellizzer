Epidendrum forcipatoides

Epidendrum forcipatoides, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum forcipatoides is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
