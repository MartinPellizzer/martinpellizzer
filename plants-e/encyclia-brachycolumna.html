Encyclia brachycolumna

Encyclia brachycolumna, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, columnar growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with white markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia brachycolumna is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
