Eulophia woodii

Eulophia woodii, commonly known as the Wood's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia woodii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
