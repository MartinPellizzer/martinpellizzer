Eriocaulon sphagnoides

Eriocaulon sphagnoides, commonly known as the bog moss, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and is typically found in wet, acidic environments such as bogs and marshes. Eriocaulon sphagnoides plays an important role in nutrient cycling and soil stabilization in its habitat.
