Euphrasia × reuteri var. subcurta

Euphrasia × reuteri var. subcurta, commonly known as the short-stemmed eyebright, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs.
