Elatine triandra

Elatine triandra, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating on the water surface. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Elatine triandra plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
