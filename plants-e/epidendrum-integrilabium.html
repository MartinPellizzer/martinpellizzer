Epidendrum integrilabium

Epidendrum integrilabium, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces pendulous flower spikes with showy, violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
