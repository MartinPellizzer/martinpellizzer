Eriope foetida

Eriope foetida, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a species of flowering plant in the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. Eriope foetida is typically found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and thickets.
