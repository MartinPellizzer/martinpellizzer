Epidendrum microbulbon

Epidendrum microbulbon, commonly known as the miniature bulb orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, bulb-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a fragrant scent. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Epidendrum microbulbon is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
