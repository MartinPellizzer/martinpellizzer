Etlingera pauciflora

Etlingera pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange inflorescence, which resembles a torch, and its narrow, sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
