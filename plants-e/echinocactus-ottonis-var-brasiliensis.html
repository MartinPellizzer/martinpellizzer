Echinocactus ottonis var. brasiliensis

Echinocactus ottonis var. brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brasiliensis Cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a spineless cactus with a globular shape and bright green to bluish-green stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
