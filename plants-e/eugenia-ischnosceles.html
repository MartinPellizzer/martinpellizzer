Eugenia ischnosceles

Eugenia ischnosceles, commonly known as the small-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are small, red berries that are edible but not widely cultivated for commercial use.

Eugenia ischnosceles is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
