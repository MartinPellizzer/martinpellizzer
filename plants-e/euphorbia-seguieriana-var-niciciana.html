Euphorbia seguieriana var. niciciana

Euphorbia seguieriana var. niciciana, commonly known as the Seguier's spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
