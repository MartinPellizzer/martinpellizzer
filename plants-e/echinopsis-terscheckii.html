Echinopsis terscheckii

Echinopsis terscheckii, commonly known as the Terscheck's cactus, is a species of cactus native to northern South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the cooler months, typically in the evening. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its structure and appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
