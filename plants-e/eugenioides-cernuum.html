Eugenioides cernuum

Eugenioides cernuum, commonly known as the drooping eugenia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping inflorescences and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Eugenioides cernuum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in tropical gardens.
