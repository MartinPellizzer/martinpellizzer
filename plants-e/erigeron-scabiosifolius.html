Erigeron scabiosifolius

Erigeron scabiosifolius, commonly known as the scabiousleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and disturbed soils. It produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its leaves are usually narrow and grayish-green, with a slightly hairy texture.
