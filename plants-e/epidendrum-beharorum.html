Epidendrum beharorum

Epidendrum beharorum, commonly known as the Behar's Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Caribbean.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically found growing on trees in humid tropical environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or silver pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are usually a mix of white and pink hues, with a pleasant fragrance.

It is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
