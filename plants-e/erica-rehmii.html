Erica rehmii

Erica rehmii, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. Erica rehmii produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the winter months, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
