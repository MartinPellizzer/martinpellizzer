Evolvulus Alsinoides Var. Normalis

Blue morning glory, scientifically known as Evolvulus alsinoides var. normalis, is a vibrant flowering plant native to the Americas. It is celebrated for its striking blue petals that bloom in the early morning, adding a touch of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This annual plant thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed soils, making it a common sight in fields and along roadsides. Its rapid growth and easy care make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance yet visually appealing plant. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not properly managed, highlighting the need for responsible cultivation.
