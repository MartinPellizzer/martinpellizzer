Epidendrum homeieri

Epidendrum homeieri, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.

Its ornamental value and seasonal blooming make it a sought-after addition to indoor gardens.
