Euphrasia formosissima subsp. formosissima

Euphrasia formosissima subsp.

formosissima, commonly known as the "beautiful euphrasia," is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange.
