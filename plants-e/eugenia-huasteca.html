Eugenia huasteca

Eugenia huasteca, commonly known as the Huastec hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a fragrant scent, while its fruits are small, round, and edible.

Eugenia huasteca is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils.
