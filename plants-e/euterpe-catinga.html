Euterpe catinga

Euterpe catinga, commonly known as the buriti palm, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It is a tall, slender palm that can grow up to 20 meters in height, with long, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clusters of bright red berries. Its fruit is an important food source for various wildlife, including birds and mammals.

The trunk of the buriti palm is also valued for its lightweight, durable wood.
