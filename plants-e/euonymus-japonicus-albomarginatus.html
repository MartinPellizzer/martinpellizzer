Euonymus japonicus albomarginatus

Euonymus japonicus albomarginatus, commonly known as the Japanese euonymus with white margins, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinct white or yellow margins. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for hedges or as a specimen plant in gardens.
