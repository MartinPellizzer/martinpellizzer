Epidendrum caucae

Epidendrum caucae, commonly known as the Cauca Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Andes. The plant features upright, cylindrical pseudobulbs covered in a dense layer of brownish-gray roots. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Epidendrum caucae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
