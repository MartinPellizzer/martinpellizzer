Epimedium × perralchicum

Epimedium × perralchicum, commonly known as barrenwort or bishop's hat, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of delicate, nodding yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to China, this plant thrives in partially shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and early bloom. The leaves are typically leathery and have a distinctive, shield-like shape, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
