Eugenia agasthiyamalayana

Eugenia agasthiyamalayana, commonly known as the Nilgiri eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
