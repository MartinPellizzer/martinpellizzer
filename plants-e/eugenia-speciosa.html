Eugenia speciosa

Eugenia speciosa, commonly known as the showy eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or purple when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to humid, tropical climates.

Eugenia speciosa is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
