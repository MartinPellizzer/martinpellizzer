Echeveria whitei

Echeveria whitei, commonly known as the White's Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces vibrant violet to pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
