Epidendrum mora-retanae

Epidendrum mora-retanae, commonly known as the Mora-Retana Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the production of elongated, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
