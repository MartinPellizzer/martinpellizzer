Epidendrum papallactense

Epidendrum papallactense, commonly known as the Papallacte Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, slender pseudobulbs. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
