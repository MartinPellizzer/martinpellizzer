Eupatorium multifidum

Eupatorium multifidum, commonly known as the New Zealand everlasting, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Eupatorium multifidum is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils.
