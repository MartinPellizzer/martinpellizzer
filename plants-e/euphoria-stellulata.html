Euphoria stellulata

Euphoria stellulata, commonly known as the starlight euphoria, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emit a subtle fragrance when touched.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes many species with unique floral structures.
