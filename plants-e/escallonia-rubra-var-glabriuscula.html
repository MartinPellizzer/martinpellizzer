Escallonia rubra var. glabriuscula

Escallonia rubra var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leaved escallonia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Australia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth and leathery, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped pink flowers in spring, which attract pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a versatile plant for both landscape design and container gardening.
