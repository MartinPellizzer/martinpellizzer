Eugenia naraveana

Eugenia naraveana, commonly known as the narave or naravilla, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia naraveana is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
