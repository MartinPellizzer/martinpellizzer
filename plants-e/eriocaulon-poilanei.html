Eriocaulon poilanei

Eriocaulon poilanei, commonly known as the Madagascar rice grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically inconspicuous.
