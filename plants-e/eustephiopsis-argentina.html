Eustephiopsis argentina

Eustephiopsis argentina, commonly known as the Argentine stephanocactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is typically covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within the Cactaceae family.
