Eatonia robusta

Eatonia robusta, commonly known as the hardy evergreen shrub, is a tropical plant characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and compact growth habit.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. Its root system is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in well-drained soils.

Due to its resilience and ornamental value, it is popular in landscaping and as a houseplant.
