Erica comptoniana

Erica comptoniana, commonly known as the Compton's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Erica comptoniana typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
