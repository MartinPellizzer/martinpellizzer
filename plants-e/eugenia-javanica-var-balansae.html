Eugenia javanica var. balansae

Eugenia javanica var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Eugenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinct aromatic scent.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that is edible and often used in local cuisines.
