Encyclia moebusii

Encyclia moebusii, commonly known as the Moebius orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Encyclia moebusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
