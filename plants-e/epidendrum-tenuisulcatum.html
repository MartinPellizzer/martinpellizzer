Epidendrum tenuisulcatum

Epidendrum tenuisulcatum, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture and produces pendulous, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
