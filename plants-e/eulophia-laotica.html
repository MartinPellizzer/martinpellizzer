Eulophia laotica

Eulophia laotica, commonly known as the Laotian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Laos and Vietnam.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

Eulophia laotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
