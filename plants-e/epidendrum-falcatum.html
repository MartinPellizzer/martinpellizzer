Epidendrum falcatum

Epidendrum falcatum, commonly known as the banana orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, banana-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow or white flowers with a distinctive, banana-like scent. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum falcatum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
