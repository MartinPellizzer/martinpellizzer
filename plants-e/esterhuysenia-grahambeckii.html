Esterhuysenia grahambeckii

Esterhuysenia grahambeckii, commonly known as the Grahambeck's ericoid, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
