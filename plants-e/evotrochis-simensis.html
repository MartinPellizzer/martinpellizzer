Evotrochis simensis

Evotrochis simensis, commonly known as the African horned vlei grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and savanna habitats, often in areas with well-drained soils. The plant has distinctive, flattened, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Evotrochis simensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
