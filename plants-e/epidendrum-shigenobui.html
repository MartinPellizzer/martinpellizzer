Epidendrum shigenobui

Epidendrum shigenobui, commonly known as the Shigenobu Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting orchid flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum shigenobui is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
