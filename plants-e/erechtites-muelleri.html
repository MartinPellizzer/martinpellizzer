Erechtites muelleri

Erechtites muelleri, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, wooded areas and along stream banks. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. Its flowering period occurs during the late spring to early summer months.

Erechtites muelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
