Eucryphia wilkiei

Eucryphia wilkiei, commonly known as the Wilkie's tree-fern, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and distinctive, often pinkish or reddish, inflorescences that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in coastal environments, where it is adapted to salt-laden winds and well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.

Eucryphia wilkiei plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various native wildlife species.
