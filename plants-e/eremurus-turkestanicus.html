Eremurus turkestanicus

Eremurus turkestanicus, commonly known as the Turkestan spirea, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescences that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Eremurus turkestanicus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
