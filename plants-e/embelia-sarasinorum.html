Embelia sarasinorum

Embelia sarasinorum, commonly known as the Indian embelia, is a small shrub native to the tropical regions of India and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is valued for its medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an important species in traditional herbal medicine.
