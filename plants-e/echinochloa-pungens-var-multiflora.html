Echinochloa pungens var. multiflora

Echinochloa pungens var. multiflora, commonly known as slender barnyardgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and wetlands.

Due to its rapid growth and competitive nature, it can become a significant weed in cultivated areas.
