Erythranthe corallina

Erythranthe corallina, commonly known as coral pink, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it often grows in rocky or sandy soils.
