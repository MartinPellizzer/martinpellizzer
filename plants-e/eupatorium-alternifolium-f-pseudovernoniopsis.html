Eupatorium alternifolium f. pseudovernoniopsis

Eupatorium alternifolium f. pseudovernoniopsis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thoroughwort, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, alternate leaves and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to parts of North America and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Eupatorium genus.
