Eugenia flamingensis

Eugenia flamingensis, commonly known as the Flame Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Eugenia flamingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
