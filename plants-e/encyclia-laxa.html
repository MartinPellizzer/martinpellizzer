Encyclia laxa

Encyclia laxa, commonly known as the loose orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with several showy, spurred flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its flowers have a distinctive, elongated lip that is often marked with contrasting coloration.

Encyclia laxa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
