Echiochilon arenarium

Echiochilon arenarium, commonly known as sand couch grass, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Echiochilon arenarium is often found in coastal dunes and other dry habitats where it plays a role in stabilizing sand dunes.
