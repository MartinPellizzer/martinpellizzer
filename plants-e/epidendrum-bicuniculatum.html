Epidendrum bicuniculatum

Epidendrum bicuniculatum, commonly known as the two-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and are characterized by their showy, spurred petals.

Epidendrum bicuniculatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
