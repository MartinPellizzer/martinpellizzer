Ericoila Nivalis

Ericoila nivalis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. It thrives in cold, rocky environments where few other species can survive. Its small, bell-shaped flowers bloom in shades of white and pink, adding a touch of beauty to the otherwise stark landscape. The plant is known for its resilience and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions. Due to its limited habitat and increasing environmental threats, Ericoila nivalis is considered a vulnerable species.
