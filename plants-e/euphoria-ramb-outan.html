Euphoria ramb-outan

Euphoria ramb-outan, commonly known as the tropical orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy coating to retain moisture.

The flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, and are often used in traditional floral arrangements.
