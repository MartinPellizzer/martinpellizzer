Erythroxylum guanchezii

Erythroxylum guanchezii, commonly known as the Canary Islands copal tree, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its woody structure and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas.
