Erica crinita

Erica crinita, commonly known as the silky heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica crinita is a low-growing shrub that produces small, leathery leaves and is adapted to harsh, sunny environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
