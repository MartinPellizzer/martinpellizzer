Ehretia membranifolia

Ehretia membranifolia, commonly known as the paperleaf ehretia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
