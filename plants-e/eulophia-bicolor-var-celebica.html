Eulophia bicolor var. celebica

Eulophia bicolor var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, contrasting pink and green foliage and its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
