Echthronema maculata

Echthronema maculata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spotted leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is adapted to a variety of soil types. Its blooming period occurs during the summer months, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
