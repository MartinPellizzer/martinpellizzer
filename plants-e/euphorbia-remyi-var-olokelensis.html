Euphorbia remyi var. olokelensis

Euphorbia remyi var. olokelensis, commonly known as the Olokelensis Spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in water retention. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
