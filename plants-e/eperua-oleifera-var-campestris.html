Eperua oleifera var. campestris

Eperua oleifera var. campestris, commonly known as the Campestris eperua, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree is known for its valuable timber, which is hard and durable, making it useful for construction and furniture. It also has medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional remedies.

The species thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
