Epicharis altissima

Epicharis altissima, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has a stout, trunk-like stem with smooth, greyish bark, and its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets. Epicharis altissima thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also known for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various soil types.
