Encyclia bipapularis

Encyclia bipapularis, commonly known as the papular orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with distinctive papillae on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Encyclia bipapularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
