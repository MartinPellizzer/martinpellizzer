Eupatorium microstemon var. perennans

Eupatorium microstemon var. perennans, commonly known as the perennial blue stem, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often tinged with blue or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and wetland habitats.
