Ectemis

Ectemis, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom at night. The plant produces small, round berries that contain the seeds. Ectemis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its nocturnal blooming habit makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
