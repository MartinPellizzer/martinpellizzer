Elymus qingnanensis

Elymus qingnanensis, commonly known as the Qingnan feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Elymus qingnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
