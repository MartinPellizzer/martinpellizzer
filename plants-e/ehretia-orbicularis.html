Ehretia orbicularis

Ehretia orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf ehretia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its orbicular (rounded) leaves, which are typically leathery and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
