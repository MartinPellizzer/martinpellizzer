Eugenia salzmannii

Eugenia salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and local cuisines.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
