Euphorbia fiherenensis

Euphorbia fiherenensis, commonly known as the Fiheren's Spurge, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers surrounded by colorful bracts, which are often mistaken for petals. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
