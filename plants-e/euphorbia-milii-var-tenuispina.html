Euphorbia milii var. tenuispina

Euphorbia milii var. tenuispina, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a succulent shrub native to Madagascar.

It features thick, spiny stems that are often tinged with red or purple, and it produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has adapted to arid environments with its ability to store water in its stems and leaves. Its leaves are typically narrow and pointed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is commonly cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and striking visual appeal.
