Elaeocarpus robustus var. robustus

Elaeocarpus robustus var. robustus, commonly known as the robust elaeocarpus, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by hard, woody fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
