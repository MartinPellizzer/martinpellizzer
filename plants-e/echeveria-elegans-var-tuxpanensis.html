Echeveria elegans var. tuxpanensis

Echeveria elegans var. tuxpanensis, commonly known as the Tuxpan Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often tinged with purple at the edges. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
