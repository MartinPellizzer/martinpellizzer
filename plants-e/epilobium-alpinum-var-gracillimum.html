Epilobium alpinum var. gracillimum

Epilobium alpinum var. gracillimum, commonly known as the alpine willow herb, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pink to purple flowers. This variant is notable for its compact growth form and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
