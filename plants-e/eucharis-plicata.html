Eucharis plicata

Eucharis plicata, commonly known as the pleated arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Aroid family and is characterized by its striking, pleated leaves that resemble a fan. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eucharis plicata is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
