Erysimum elatum

Erysimum elatum, commonly known as the tall wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces a rosette of basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Erysimum elatum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
