Echites sagittatus

Echites sagittatus, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
