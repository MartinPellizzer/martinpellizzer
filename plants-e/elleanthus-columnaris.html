Elleanthus columnaris

Elleanthus columnaris, commonly known as the slender spike grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous tiny white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight sheen.

Elleanthus columnaris is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
