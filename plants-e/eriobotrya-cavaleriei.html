Eriobotrya cavaleriei

Eriobotrya cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by yellow, juicy fruits that are highly valued for their flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.
