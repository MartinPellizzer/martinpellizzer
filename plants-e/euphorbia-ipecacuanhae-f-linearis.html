Euphorbia ipecacuanhae f. linearis

Euphorbia ipecacuanhae f. linearis, commonly known as the linear euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, ribbed stems that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow or green, typical of the Euphorbia genus. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique form and resilience.
