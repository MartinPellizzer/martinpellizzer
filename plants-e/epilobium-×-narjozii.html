Epilobium × narjozii

Epilobium × narjozii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willow herb, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Epilobium in the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun. It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
