Echium oederifolium

Echium oederifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thornplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking purple or blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
