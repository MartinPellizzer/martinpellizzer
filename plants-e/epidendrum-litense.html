Epidendrum litense

Epidendrum litense, commonly known as the litense orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum litense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under proper conditions.
