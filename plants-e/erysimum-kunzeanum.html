Erysimum kunzeanum

Erysimum kunzeanum, commonly known as the yellow wallflower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves and grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
