Eragrostis eragrostis subvar. leersioides

Eragrostis eragrostis subvar. leersioides, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a hairy texture.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
