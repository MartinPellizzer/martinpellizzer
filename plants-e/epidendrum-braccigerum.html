Epidendrum braccigerum

Epidendrum braccigerum, commonly known as the bract-bearing orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, tropical environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant features elongated, grass-like leaves and produces striking, pendulous flower spikes with numerous small, fragrant blossoms. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with a contrasting lip, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Epidendrum braccigerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
