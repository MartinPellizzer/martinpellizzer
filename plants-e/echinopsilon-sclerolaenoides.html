Echinopsilon sclerolaenoides

Echinopsilon sclerolaenoides, commonly known as the rigid-leaved burweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb with hairy, spiny stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to brown flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native region.
