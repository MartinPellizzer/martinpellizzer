Eugenioides foliosum

Eugenioides foliosum, commonly known as the broadleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by edible berries that range in color from red to black. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
