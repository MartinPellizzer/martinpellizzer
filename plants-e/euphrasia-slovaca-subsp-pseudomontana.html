Euphrasia slovaca subsp. pseudomontana

Euphrasia slovaca subsp.

pseudomontana, commonly known as the Slovak eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.
