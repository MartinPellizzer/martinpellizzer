Epicycas Lindstromii

Epicycas lindstromii is a rare and ancient plant species that dates back millions of years. It is a member of the cycad family, known for its distinctive palm-like appearance and slow growth. Found primarily in remote areas of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, tropical climates. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now critically endangered. Its survival is a key indicator of the health of its ecosystem.
