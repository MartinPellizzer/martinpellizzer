Eulophia concolor

Eulophia concolor, commonly known as the uniform orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale pink, often arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia concolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens.
