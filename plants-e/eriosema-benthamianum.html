Eriosema benthamianum

Eriosema benthamianum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
