Elatostema saxatile

Elatostema saxatile, commonly known as the rock elatostem, is a small, herbaceous plant native to rocky habitats in southern Africa.

It typically grows in crevices or on rocky outcrops, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

Elatostema saxatile is characterized by its ability to form dense mats, which help stabilize soil and prevent erosion in its native habitat.
