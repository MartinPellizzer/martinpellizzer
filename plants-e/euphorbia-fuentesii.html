Euphorbia fuentesii

Euphorbia fuentesii, commonly known as the Fuentes' spurge, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features a distinctive, branched stem covered in small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
