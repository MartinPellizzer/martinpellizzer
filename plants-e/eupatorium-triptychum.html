Eupatorium triptychum

Eupatorium triptychum, commonly known as boneset, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Boneset is often found in moist, wooded areas and is known for its medicinal properties.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.
