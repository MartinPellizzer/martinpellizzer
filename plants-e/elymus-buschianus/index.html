Elymus Buschianus

Elymus buschianus is a hardy grass species native to the arid and semi-arid regions of North America. It thrives in harsh environments, often found in dry plains and rocky soils. This plant is known for its deep root system, which helps it survive in drought conditions. Its tall, slender stalks and small, spike-like inflorescences make it a distinctive feature of its habitat. Despite its resilience, it is often overlooked for its ecological and ornamental value.
