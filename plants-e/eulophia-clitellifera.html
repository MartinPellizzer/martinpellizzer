Eulophia clitellifera

Eulophia clitellifera, commonly known as the broad-leafed eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Eulophia clitellifera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
