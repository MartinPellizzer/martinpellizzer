Etlingera minor

Etlingera minor, commonly known as the dwarf torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, red-orange bracts that resemble torches. Its leaves are narrow and sword-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Etlingera minor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
