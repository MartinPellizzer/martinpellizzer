Epidendrum bungerothii

Epidendrum bungerothii, commonly known as the Bungeroth's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum bungerothii is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
