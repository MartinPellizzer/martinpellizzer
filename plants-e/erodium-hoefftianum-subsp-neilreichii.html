Erodium hoefftianum subsp. neilreichii

Erodium hoefftianum subsp.

neilreichii, commonly known as Neilreich's bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to California. It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pinkish-white flowers.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in coastal sage scrub habitats.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
