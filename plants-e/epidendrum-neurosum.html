Epidendrum neurosum

Epidendrum neurosum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and its striking, spider-like flower structure. The plant typically grows epiphytically on trees or rocks, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Epidendrum neurosum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
