Ehretia seyrigii

Ehretia seyrigii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre mahogany, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
