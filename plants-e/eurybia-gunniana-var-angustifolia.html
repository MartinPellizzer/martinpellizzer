Eurybia gunniana var. angustifolia

Eurybia gunniana var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf sunflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, bogs, and stream banks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in the Carolinas and extends into parts of Georgia and Florida.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
