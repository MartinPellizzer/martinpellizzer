Euphrasia × tremolsii

Euphrasia × tremolsii, commonly known as the hybrid eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically appearing in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Native to regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add ornamental value to shady garden spaces.
