Ericameria nauseosa var. latisquamea

Ericameria nauseosa var. latisquamea, commonly known as the broad-scale ericameria, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves with a distinctive, waxy coating. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
