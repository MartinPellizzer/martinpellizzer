Eriocaulon kolhapurense

Eriocaulon kolhapurense, commonly known as the Kolhapur marsh grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to the wetlands and marshy areas of southern India, particularly in the state of Maharashtra. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in spikelets. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in seasonal wetlands.

Due to its ecological importance in maintaining wetland biodiversity, it is considered a valuable plant for conservation efforts.
