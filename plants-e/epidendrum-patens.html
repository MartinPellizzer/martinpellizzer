Epidendrum patens

Epidendrum patens, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white or gray pubescence. It produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

Epidendrum patens is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
