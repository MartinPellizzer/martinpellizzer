Elatostema pulchellum

Elatostema pulchellum, commonly known as the pretty elatostema, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elatostemaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
