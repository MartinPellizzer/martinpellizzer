Eugenia wightii

Eugenia wightii, commonly known as the Wight's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly India and Sri Lanka. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and smooth, dark green leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Eugenia wightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
