Euphorbia remyi var. kauaiensis

Euphorbia remyi var. kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a cluster called a cyathium, which is typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or rocky environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
