Eriostomum polystachyum

Eriostomum polystachyum, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a woody stem that becomes more branched as it matures. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
