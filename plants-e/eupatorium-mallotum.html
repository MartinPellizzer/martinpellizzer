Eupatorium mallotum

Eupatorium mallotum, commonly known as the Chinese boneset, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Eupatorium mallotum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
