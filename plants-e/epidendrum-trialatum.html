Epidendrum trialatum

Epidendrum trialatum, commonly known as the Pink Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum trialatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
