Eugenia luzonensis

Eugenia luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Luzon. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. It produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, dark purple berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
