Epipactis erecta

Epipactis erecta, commonly known as the erect helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with purple or brown markings. Epipactis erecta thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is a perennial herb that plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
