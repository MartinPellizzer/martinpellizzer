Eragrostis thunbergiana

Eragrostis thunbergiana, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields, roadsides, and disturbed sites.

Eragrostis thunbergiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
