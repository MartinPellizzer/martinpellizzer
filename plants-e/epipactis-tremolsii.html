Epipactis tremolsii

Epipactis tremolsii, commonly known as the Tremols' helleborine, is a rare orchid species native to parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent purple markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as woodlands and rocky areas. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its specialized pollination mechanisms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered in many regions.
