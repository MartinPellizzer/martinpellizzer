Echinops major

Echinops major, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky seed heads that resemble small globes, which are a striking feature of the plant. The plant produces blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Echinops major is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
