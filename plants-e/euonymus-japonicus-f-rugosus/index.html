Euonymus Japonicus F. Rugosus

Japanese euonymus, scientifically known as Euonymus japonicus f. rugosus, is a unique and ornamental shrub prized for its distinctive foliage. This variety features deeply lobed, waxy leaves that give it a rugged, almost sculptural appearance. Native to Japan, it has gained popularity in horticulture for its adaptability and visual appeal. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for small gardens, rockeries, or as a container plant. With its striking texture and seasonal color changes, it adds a dramatic element to any landscape.
