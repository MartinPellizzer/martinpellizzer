Epidendrum nocturnum var. minus

Epidendrum nocturnum var. minus, commonly known as the night blooming epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America, particularly found in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that open at night, often in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are typically broad and dark green, arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique nocturnal blooming habit.
