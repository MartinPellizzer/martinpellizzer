Eugenioides coronatum

Eugenioides coronatum, commonly known as the Crowned Eugenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in rainforests or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural gardens.
