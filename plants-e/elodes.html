Elodes

Elodes, commonly known as waterweed, is an aquatic plant belonging to the Hydrocharitaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, simple leaves that are typically arranged in whorls. It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing oxygen and habitat for various aquatic organisms.
