Erechtites tenuiflorus

Erechtites tenuiflorus, commonly known as slenderflower false sunflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in wetlands and along stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow disk flowers surrounded by ray florets.

Erechtites tenuiflorus is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ecological benefits.
