Eriachne minuta

Eriachne minuta, commonly known as the dwarf eriachne, is a small grass-like plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Eriachne minuta is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along coastal dunes.
