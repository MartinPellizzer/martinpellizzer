Echidnopsis planiflora

Echidnopsis planiflora, commonly known as the flat-flowered stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of purple or pink during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
