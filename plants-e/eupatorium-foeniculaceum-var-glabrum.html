Eupatorium foeniculaceum var. glabrum

Eupatorium foeniculaceum var. glabrum, commonly known as smooth white boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is often found in wet meadows or along stream banks.

Smooth white boneset is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
