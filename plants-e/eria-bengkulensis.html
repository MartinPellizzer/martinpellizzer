Eria bengkulensis

Eria bengkulensis, commonly known as the Bengkulu orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

Eria bengkulensis is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.
