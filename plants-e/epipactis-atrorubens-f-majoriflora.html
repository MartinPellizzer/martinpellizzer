Epipactis atrorubens f. majoriflora

Epipactis atrorubens f. majoriflora, commonly known as the showy helleborine, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically dark red or purple with a yellow throat. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers that can reach up to 30 cm in height.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
