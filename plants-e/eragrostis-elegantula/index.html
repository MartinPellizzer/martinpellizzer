Eragrostis Elegantula

Eragrostis elegantula is a striking grass species known for its elegant, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery seed heads. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. While it is drought tolerant, it benefits from occasional watering during dry periods. This plant not only adds visual interest to gardens but also supports local wildlife by providing habitat and nectar sources.
