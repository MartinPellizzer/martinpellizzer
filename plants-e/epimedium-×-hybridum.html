Epimedium × hybridum

Epimedium × hybridum, commonly known as barberry fern or bishop's hat, is a deciduous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features heart-shaped, leathery leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish or bronze tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white during early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Epimedium × hybridum is valued for its ornamental foliage and early blooming habit.
