Eriocaulon nubigenum

Eriocaulon nubigenum, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species of aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, characterized by its small, delicate leaves and specialized root systems. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often in marshy or swampy habitats. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a dense covering of fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Eriocaulon nubigenum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and aiding in water filtration.
