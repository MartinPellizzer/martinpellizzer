Eugenia brevistyla

Eugenia brevistyla, commonly known as the narrow-styled eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, berry-like structure that ripens to a dark purple color.
