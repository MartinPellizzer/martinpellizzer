Erythraea exaltata

Erythraea exaltata, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erythraea exaltata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
