Encyclia guanahacabibensis

Encyclia guanahacabibensis, commonly known as the Guanahacabib Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Cuba and the Isle of Pines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers, which are usually small and clustered in racemes. Its flowers are often pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral characteristics.
