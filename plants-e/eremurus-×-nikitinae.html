Eremurus × nikitinae

Eremurus × nikitinae, commonly known as the Nikitina's Spearthistle, is a hybrid plant in the genus Eremurus, native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence composed of numerous yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features a robust, branched stem and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by gardeners interested in unique and hardy plants.
