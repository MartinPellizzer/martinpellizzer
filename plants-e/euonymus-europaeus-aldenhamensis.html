Euonymus europaeus aldenhamensis

Euonymus europaeus aldenhamensis, commonly known as Aldenham euonymus, is a rare subspecies of the common euonymus.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
