Eryngium planum

Eryngium planum, commonly known as the flat thistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple or blue flower heads that resemble thistles. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun.

Eryngium planum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
