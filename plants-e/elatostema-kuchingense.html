Elatostema kuchingense

Elatostema kuchingense, commonly known as the Kuching dewplant, is a small herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and swampy areas. The plant has a rosette of fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain water in its humid habitat. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Elatostema kuchingense is part of the Urticaceae family, which includes many species with stinging hairs.
