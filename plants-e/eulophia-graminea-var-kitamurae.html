Eulophia graminea var. kitamurae

Eulophia graminea var. kitamurae, commonly known as the Kitamura's grass orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and small, clustered orchid flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its inflorescence is distinctive, with flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like structure.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its rarity and beauty.
