Eriostemon buxifolius

Eriostemon buxifolius, commonly known as the waxleaf eriostemon, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for cultivation in gardens and is often used as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Eriostemon buxifolius prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
