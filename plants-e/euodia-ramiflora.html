Euodia ramiflora

Euodia ramiflora, commonly known as the fragrant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and often fragrant, and its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. Euodia ramiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
