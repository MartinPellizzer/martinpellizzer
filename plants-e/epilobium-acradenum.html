Epilobium acradenum

Epilobium acradenum, commonly known as narrow-leaved willowherb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows, riverbanks, and woodland edges. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are pink to red, arranged in spikes, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Epilobium acradenum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
