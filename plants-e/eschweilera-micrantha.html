Eschweilera micrantha

Eschweilera micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered eschweilera, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small berries that contain seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
