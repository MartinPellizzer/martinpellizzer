Erythrina corallodendron var. bicolor

Erythrina corallodendron var. bicolor, commonly known as the red and white coral tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that display a striking red and white coloration. The plant produces distinctive pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its vibrant floral display makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces.
