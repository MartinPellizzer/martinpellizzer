Erythrospermum latifolium

Erythrospermum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf erythrospermum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Erythrospermum latifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
