Echinocereus rigidissimus subsp. rigidissimus

Echinocereus rigidissimus subsp.

rigidissimus, commonly known as the rigid hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny areoles.

The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
