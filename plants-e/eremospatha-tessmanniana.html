Eremospatha tessmanniana

Eremospatha tessmanniana, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. Eremospatha tessmanniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in stabilizing sandy soils.
