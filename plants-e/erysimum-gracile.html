Erysimum gracile

Erysimum gracile, commonly known as slender wallflower, is a low-growing annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Erysimum gracile is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
