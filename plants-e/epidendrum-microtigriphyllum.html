Epidendrum microtigriphyllum

Epidendrum microtigriphyllum, commonly known as the miniature tiger-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive mottled pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
