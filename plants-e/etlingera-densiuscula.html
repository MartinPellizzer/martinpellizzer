Etlingera densiuscula

Etlingera densiuscula, commonly known as the dense-stemmed torch ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Its inflorescence consists of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling a torch.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional medicine in some regions.
