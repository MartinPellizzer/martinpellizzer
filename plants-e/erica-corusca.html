Erica corusca

Erica corusca, commonly known as the silver heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica corusca is a low-growing shrub that produces berries which are an important food source for local wildlife.
