Encyclia ortizii

Encyclia ortizii, commonly known as the Ortiz's pineapple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces compact, pseudobulbous stems with fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Encyclia ortizii is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests.
