Epilobium × salicifolium

Epilobium × salicifolium, commonly known as the willowleaf evening primrose, is a hybrid species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, willow-like leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has a creeping growth habit with reddish stems and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
