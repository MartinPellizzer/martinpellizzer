Encyclia delacruzii

Encyclia delacruzii, commonly known as Delacruz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that bear lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Encyclia delacruzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care in suitable conditions.
