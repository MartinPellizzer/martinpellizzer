Erica ekloniana

Erica ekloniana, commonly known as the Eklon heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. This species is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in well-drained, acidic soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer.

Erica ekloniana is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
