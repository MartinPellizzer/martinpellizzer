Epigeneium tsangianum

Epigeneium tsangianum, commonly known as the Tsaungian arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its spatulate leaves and inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
