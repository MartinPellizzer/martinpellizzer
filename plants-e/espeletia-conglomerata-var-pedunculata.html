Espeletia conglomerata var. pedunculata

Espeletia conglomerata var. pedunculata, commonly known as the "Páramo Umbrella Plant," is a striking plant native to the páramo ecosystems of South America.

The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences that can reach several feet in height, often with white or pinkish flowers.
