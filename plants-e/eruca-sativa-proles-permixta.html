Eruca sativa proles permixta

Eruca sativa proles permixta, commonly known as wild rocket or garden cress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pinnately lobed leaves that are edible and often used in salads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into siliques containing numerous seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both culinary and ecological purposes.
