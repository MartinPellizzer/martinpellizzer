Eulophia triceras

Eulophia triceras, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia triceras is valued for its ornamental beauty and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
