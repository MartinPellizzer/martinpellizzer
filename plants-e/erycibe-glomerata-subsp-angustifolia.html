Erycibe glomerata subsp. angustifolia

Erycibe glomerata subsp.

angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed erycibe, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in humid, tropical environments.
