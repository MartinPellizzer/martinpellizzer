Epidendrum nutantirhachis

Epidendrum nutantirhachis, commonly known as the hanging epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, arching stems that often dangle from trees, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with reddish-brown markings. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
