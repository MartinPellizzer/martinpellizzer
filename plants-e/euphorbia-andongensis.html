Euphorbia andongensis

Euphorbia andongensis, commonly known as the Andong spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique cyathium structure, typical of the genus Euphorbia. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
