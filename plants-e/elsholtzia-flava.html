Elsholtzia flava

Elsholtzia flava, commonly known as yellow elsholtzia, is a flowering plant native to regions of China and the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
