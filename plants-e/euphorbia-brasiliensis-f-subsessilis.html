Euphorbia brasiliensis f. subsessilis

Euphorbia brasiliensis f. subsessilis, commonly known as the Brazilian spurge, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its fleshy, branching stems and small, clustered yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are typically reduced or absent, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
