Evolvulus canus

Evolvulus canus, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has hairy, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver underside. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Evolvulus canus is an annual herb that blooms throughout the growing season.
