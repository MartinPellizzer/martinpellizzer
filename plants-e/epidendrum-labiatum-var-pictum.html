Epidendrum labiatum var. pictum

Epidendrum labiatum var. pictum, commonly known as the Painted Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

The species is valued in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
