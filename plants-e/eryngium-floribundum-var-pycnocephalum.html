Eryngium floribundum var. pycnocephalum

Eryngium floribundum var. pycnocephalum, commonly known as the dense-headed thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features spiky, blue to purple flower heads that are surrounded by dense, overlapping bracts. The plant has a basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance makes it a striking addition to rock gardens and native plant collections.
