Euphorbia Hyberna Subsp. Canuti

Euphorbia hyberna subsp. canuti is a rare and unique plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature, thriving in harsh, rocky environments. This subspecies is often mistaken for other euphorbias due to its similar leaf structure. Despite its tough exterior, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Its cultivation is challenging but rewarding for plant enthusiasts.
