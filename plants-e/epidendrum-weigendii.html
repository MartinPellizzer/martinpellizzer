Epidendrum weigendii

Epidendrum weigendii, commonly known as the Weigend's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum weigendii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
