Erysimum canum

Erysimum canum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its small, yellow flowers appear in early spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged surroundings.

Erysimum canum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
