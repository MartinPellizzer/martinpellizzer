Encyclia pflanzii

Encyclia pflanzii, commonly known as the Pflanz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with several pink to purple blooms, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. Encyclia pflanzii prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical regions.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
