Euonymus tibeticus

Euonymus tibeticus, commonly known as the Tibetan spurge, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of the Tibetan Plateau.

Euonymus tibeticus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in alpine ecosystems.
