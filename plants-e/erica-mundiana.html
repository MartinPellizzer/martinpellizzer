Erica mundiana

Erica mundiana, commonly known as the common heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This shrub typically grows in sandy or acidic soils and thrives in coastal or mountainous regions. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in spring and summer.

Erica mundiana is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
