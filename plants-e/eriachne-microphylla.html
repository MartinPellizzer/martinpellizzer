Eriachne microphylla

Eriachne microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved eriachne, is a low-growing grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its small leaves and compact growth habit make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
