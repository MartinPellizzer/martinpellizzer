Editeles

Editeles, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that appear to shimmer in the light, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant has a unique reproductive system, relying on a specific species of beetle for pollination. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in the humid environment.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, Editeles is considered a conservation priority in its native habitat.
