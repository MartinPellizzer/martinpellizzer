Euphorbia ancyrensis

Euphorbia ancyrensis, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, woody stem with dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique inflorescence called a cyathium. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia ancyrensis is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
