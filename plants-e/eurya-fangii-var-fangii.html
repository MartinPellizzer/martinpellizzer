Eurya fangii var. fangii

Eurya fangii var. fangii, commonly known as the Fang's waxleaf eurya, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of wax, giving them a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
