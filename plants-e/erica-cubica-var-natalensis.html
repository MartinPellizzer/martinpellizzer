Erica cubica var. natalensis

Erica cubica var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Natal heath is well adapted to coastal and mountainous regions, displaying resilience to coastal winds and salt spray.
