Erysimum hieraciifolium var. patulum

Erysimum hieraciifolium var. patulum, commonly known as the broadleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces a compact, bushy growth habit.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
