Eugenia surigaensis

Eugenia surigaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the region of Mindanao. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically growing up to 5 meters in height. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a pleasant aromatic scent.

The flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are followed by edible, red, fleshy berries that are used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
