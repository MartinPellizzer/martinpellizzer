Eragrostis elongata var. brachyclados

Eragrostis elongata var. brachyclados, commonly known as slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its growth habit is clump-forming, with stems that are often prostrate or ascending.

The botanical features of this grass make it an important component of native plant communities in its habitat.
