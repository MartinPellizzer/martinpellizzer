Erysimum neglectum

Erysimum neglectum, commonly known as the common wall rocket, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnately divided leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing in rocky or disturbed areas.

Erysimum neglectum is a hardy plant that is often used in garden settings for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
