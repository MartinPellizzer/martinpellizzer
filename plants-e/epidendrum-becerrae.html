Epidendrum becerrae

Epidendrum becerrae, commonly known as the Berrera's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often in clusters. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
