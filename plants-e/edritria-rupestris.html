Edritria rupestris

Edritria rupestris, commonly known as the rock stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in drought conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
