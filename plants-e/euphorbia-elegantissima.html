Euphorbia elegantissima

Euphorbia elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, branching structure with smooth, waxy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish cyathia, which are specialized flower-like structures typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
