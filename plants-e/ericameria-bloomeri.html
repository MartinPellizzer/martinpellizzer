Ericameria bloomeri

Ericameria bloomeri, commonly known as the California goldenbush, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-green foliage and produces small, yellow flowers in the spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is often found in coastal and inland areas, particularly in California and Oregon.

Ericameria bloomeri is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in gardens.
