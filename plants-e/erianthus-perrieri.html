Erianthus perrieri

Erianthus perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri grass, is a tall perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves with a distinctive blue-green color. The plant produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, silver-white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and ecological landscaping.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is increasingly cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
