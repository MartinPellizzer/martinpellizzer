Erysimum baytopiae var. baytopiae

Erysimum baytopiae var. baytopiae, commonly known as the Baytop's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers, which range in color from yellow to orange, bloom in early spring, adding vibrant hues to its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
