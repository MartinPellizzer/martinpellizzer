Eria andamanica

Eria andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman skyflower, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in coastal forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
