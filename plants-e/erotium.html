Erotium

Erotium, commonly known as the "love plant," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a velvety texture.

The fruit, a small, round berry, contains seeds that are dispersed by animals and birds.
