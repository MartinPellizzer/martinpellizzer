Euphorbia clivicola subsp. clivicola

Euphorbia clivicola subsp.

clivicola, commonly known as the cliff euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found growing on cliffs or slopes. It has adapted to arid environments with its ability to store water in its stems.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
