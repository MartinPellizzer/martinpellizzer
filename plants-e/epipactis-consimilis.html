Epipactis consimilis

Epipactis consimilis, commonly known as the similar helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and a tall stem that bears a single, showy flower with a distinctive hooded labellum. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It produces a small, greenish-brown capsule as its fruit, containing numerous tiny seeds.

Epipactis consimilis is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in association with other orchid species.
