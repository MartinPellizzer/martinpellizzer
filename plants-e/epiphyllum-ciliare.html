Epiphyllum ciliare

Epiphyllum ciliare, commonly known as the Crested Night Blooming Cereus, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is a climbing cactus native to Mexico and parts of Central America. The plant is characterized by its flat, fleshy stems that are often adorned with white or pale green spines. Its flowers, which bloom at night, are large, showy, and typically white or pale pink.

Epiphyllum ciliare thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
