Ebenus andamanica

Ebenus andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-white and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Ebenus andamanica is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.
