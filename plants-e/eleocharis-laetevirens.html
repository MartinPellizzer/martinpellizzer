Eleocharis laetevirens, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact, spike-like inflorescences. Eleocharis laetevirens is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
