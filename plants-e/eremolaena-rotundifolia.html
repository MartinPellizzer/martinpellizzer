Eremolaena rotundifolia

Eremolaena rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf eremolaena, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments.

Eremolaena rotundifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
