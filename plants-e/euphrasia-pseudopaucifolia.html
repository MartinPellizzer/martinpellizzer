Euphrasia pseudopaucifolia

Euphrasia pseudopaucifolia, commonly known as the slender eyebright, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has thin, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Euphrasia pseudopaucifolia is part of the Scrophulariaceae family, known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
