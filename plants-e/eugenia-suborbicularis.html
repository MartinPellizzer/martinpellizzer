Eugenia suborbicularis

Eugenia suborbicularis, commonly known as the small-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, orbicular-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
