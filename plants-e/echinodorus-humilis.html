Echinodorus humilis

Echinodorus humilis, commonly known as the dwarf arrowhead, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It features narrow, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and is often used in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. It prefers moderate to high light conditions and can grow in both submerged and floating forms.

Echinodorus humilis is also known for its ability to help improve water quality by absorbing nutrients.
