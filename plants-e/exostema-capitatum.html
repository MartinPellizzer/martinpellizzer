Exostema capitatum

Exostema capitatum, commonly known as the "Crown of Thorns," is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that features dense, spiny foliage and produces clusters of small, white flowers. The plant's leaves are leathery and often covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with branching patterns that support its distinctive appearance.

Exostema capitatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique visual appeal.
