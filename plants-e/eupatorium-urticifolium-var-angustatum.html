Eupatorium urticifolium var. angustatum

Eupatorium urticifolium var. angustatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved boneset, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a stout, branching stem that often becomes woody with age.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.
