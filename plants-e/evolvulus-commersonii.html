Evolvulus commersonii

Evolvulus commersonii, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Evolvulus commersonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
