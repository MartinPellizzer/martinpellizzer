Euphorbia dulcis f. longeradiata

Euphorbia dulcis f. longeradiata, commonly known as the sweet euphorbia or the milkweed euphorbia, is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, thick stems and leaves.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to certain regions of Africa. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its latex sap is toxic and can cause skin irritation, making it important to handle with care.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
