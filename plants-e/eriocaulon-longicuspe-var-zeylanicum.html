Eriocaulon longicuspe var. zeylanicum

Eriocaulon longicuspe var. zeylanicum, commonly known as the Zeylanic spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and compact inflorescences that bear small, spike-like clusters of flowers. This variety is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and supporting aquatic biodiversity.
