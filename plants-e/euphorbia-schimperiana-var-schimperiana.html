Euphorbia schimperiana var. schimperiana

Euphorbia schimperiana var. schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
