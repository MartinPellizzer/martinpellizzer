Eugenia smaragdina var. rigida

Eugenia smaragdina var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid evergreen hibiscus, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The rigid form of this variety gives it a compact, upright growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening.
