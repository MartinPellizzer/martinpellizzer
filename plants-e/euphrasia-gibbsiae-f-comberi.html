Euphrasia gibbsiae f. comberi

Euphrasia gibbsiae f. comberi, commonly known as the Comber's eyebright, is a variant of the eyebright plant native to the British Isles.

The plant typically thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland edges and grassy areas.
