Eragrostis baguirmiensis

Eragrostis baguirmiensis, commonly known as the Baguirmi grass, is a perennial grass species native to central Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and savannas. The plant has narrow, erect leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid conditions and can thrive with minimal water.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing forage for livestock.
