Elaphoglossum kusaiense

Elaphoglossum kusaiense, commonly known as the Kusaiense holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, brownish layer of rhizome sheaths.

The spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the fronds, which are typically small and inconspicuous.
