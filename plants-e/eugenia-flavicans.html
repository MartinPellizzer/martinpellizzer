Eugenia flavicans

Eugenia flavicans, commonly known as the yellow eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Eugenia flavicans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
