Eulophus californicus var. sanctorum

Eulophus californicus var. sanctorum, commonly known as the Sanctorum Eulophus, is a variant of the Eulophus californicus, a species of wasp.

It is primarily found in the western United States, particularly in California. This plant, though not a plant in the traditional sense, is a type of parasitic wasp that plays a role in biological control of other insect pests.
