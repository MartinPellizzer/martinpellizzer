Erepsia dunensis

Erepsia dunensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean thyme, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the spring.

Erepsia dunensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
