Epidendrum aloisii

Epidendrum aloisii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking addition to any collection. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
