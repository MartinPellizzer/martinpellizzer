Erodium × willkommianum

Erodium × willkommianum, commonly known as the common stinkcup, is a hybrid plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10–30 cm. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are encased in a distinctive, inflated pod that splits open when mature.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural and garden settings.
