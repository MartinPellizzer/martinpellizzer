Epimedium × longifolium f. violaceum

Epimedium × longifolium f. violaceum, commonly known as the purple-leaf barrenwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its delicate, nodding yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, preferring rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a distinctive heart-shaped form.

This cultivar is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to woodland gardens.
