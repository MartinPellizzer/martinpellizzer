Elaeocarpus nitidus var. nitidus

Elaeocarpus nitidus var. nitidus, commonly known as the shining elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that are typically red or purple in color. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance.

The species is also noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
