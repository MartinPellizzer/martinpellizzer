Echinolaena Nemorosa

Echinolaena nemorosa is a lesser-known yet fascinating plant species found in tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, commonly known as the daisy or sunflower family. This plant is characterized by its unique inflorescence and adaptability to various soil types. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its study offers insights into plant evolution and ecological resilience.
