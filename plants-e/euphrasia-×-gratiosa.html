Euphrasia × gratiosa

Euphrasia × gratiosa, commonly known as the pretty willow herb, is a hybrid plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Euphrasia × gratiosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
