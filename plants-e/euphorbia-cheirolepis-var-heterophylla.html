Euphorbia cheirolepis var. heterophylla

Euphorbia cheirolepis var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variegated euphorbia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, gelatinous yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the Euphorbia genus. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
