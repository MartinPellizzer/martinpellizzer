Eugenioides laurinum

Eugenioides laurinum, commonly known as the laurinum myrtle, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure containing numerous seeds.

Eugenioides laurinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
