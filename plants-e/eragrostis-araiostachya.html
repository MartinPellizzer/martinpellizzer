Eragrostis araiostachya

Eragrostis araiostachya, commonly known as the silvergrass or blackseed grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, dark brown seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodland areas.

Eragrostis araiostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
