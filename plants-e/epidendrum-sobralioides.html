Epidendrum sobralioides

Epidendrum sobralioides, commonly known as the Sobral's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and a rosette of elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
