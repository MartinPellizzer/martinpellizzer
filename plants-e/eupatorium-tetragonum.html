Eupatorium tetragonum

Eupatorium tetragonum, commonly known as squarestem thoroughwort, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its square, rigid stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, sunny habitats such as wetlands and streambanks.

Eupatorium tetragonum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
