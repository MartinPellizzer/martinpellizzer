Exoacantha heterophylla

Exoacantha heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes, which can vary from ovate to linear. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
