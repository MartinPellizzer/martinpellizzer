Echites tubulosus

Echites tubulosus, commonly known as the slender pipevine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Aristolochiaceae and is characterized by its tubular, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, purple flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
