Escallonia alpina var. alpina

Escallonia alpina var. alpina, commonly known as the Alpine Escallonia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and provide dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and alpine settings due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

Escallonia alpina var. alpina prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
