Eugenia sulcistyla

Eugenia sulcistyla, commonly known as the ridged eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that have distinct longitudinal ridges. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and are clustered in small inflorescences.

Eugenia sulcistyla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
