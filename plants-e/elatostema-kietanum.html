Elatostema kietanum

Elatostema kietanum, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth, waxy surface and a distinctive white or pale green coloration. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, often appearing in the summer months.

Elatostema kietanum is known for its ability to form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization in its native habitat.
