Eulalia speciosa var. glauca

Eulalia speciosa var. glauca, commonly known as the blue glauca grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features slender, bluish-gray foliage that gives it a unique and soft appearance. The plant produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that add movement and texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements in landscaping.
