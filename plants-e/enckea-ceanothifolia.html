Enckea ceanothifolia

Enckea ceanothifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf enckea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Enckea ceanothifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage.
