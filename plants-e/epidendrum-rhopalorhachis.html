Epidendrum rhopalorhachis

Epidendrum rhopalorhachis, commonly known as the Sword Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum rhopalorhachis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
