Epidendrum sneidernii

Epidendrum sneidernii, commonly known as the Pink Ghost orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom during the warmer months of the year.

Epidendrum sneidernii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
