Eria stenostachya

Eria stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stalked erythrina, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are followed by small, woody pods that contain several seeds.

Eria stenostachya is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
