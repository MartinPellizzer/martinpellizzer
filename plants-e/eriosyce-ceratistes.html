Eriosyce ceratistes

Eriosyce ceratistes, commonly known as the horned euphorbia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its unique, spiny stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers.
