Eriospermum dielsianum subsp. molle

Eriospermum dielsianum subsp.

molle, commonly known as the soft eriosperm, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
