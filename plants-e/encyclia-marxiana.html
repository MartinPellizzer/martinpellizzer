Encyclia marxiana

Encyclia marxiana, commonly known as the Marx's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia marxiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
