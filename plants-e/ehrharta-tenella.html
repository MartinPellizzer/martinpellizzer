Ehrharta tenella

Ehrharta tenella, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Ehrharta tenella is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in landscaping applications.
