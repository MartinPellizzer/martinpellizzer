Erica lactea

Erica lactea, commonly known as the milk heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Erica lactea typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is a shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter, with evergreen foliage that remains dense throughout the year.
