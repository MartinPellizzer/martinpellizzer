Epimedium macranthum f. niveum

Epimedium macranthum f. niveum, commonly known as the white-flowered barrenwort, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It is a hybrid form of Epimedium macranthum, characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is considered a valuable addition to specialized botanical gardens.
