Eragrostis terecaulis

Eragrostis terecaulis, commonly known as slender false brome, is a perennial grass native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological restoration projects.
