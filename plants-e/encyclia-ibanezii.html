Encyclia ibanezii

Encyclia ibanezii, commonly known as the Ibanez's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or yellow-green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia ibanezii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
