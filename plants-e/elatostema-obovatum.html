Elatostema obovatum

Elatostema obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, with white or pale green petals.

Elatostema obovatum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
