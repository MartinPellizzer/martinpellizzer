Echeveria brachyantha

Echeveria brachyantha, commonly known as the silver rose or Mexican snowflake, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are fleshy and covered in a fine, silvery-blue waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
