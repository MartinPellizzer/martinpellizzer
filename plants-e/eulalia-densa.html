Eulalia densa

Eulalia densa, commonly known as the dense grass or dense reed, is a species of grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it forms dense stands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Eulalia densa plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland organisms.
