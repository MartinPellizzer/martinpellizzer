Eria kandariana

Eria kandariana, commonly known as the Kandarian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eria kandariana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
