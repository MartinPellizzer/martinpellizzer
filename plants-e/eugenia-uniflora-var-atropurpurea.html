Eugenia uniflora var. atropurpurea

Eugenia uniflora var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple passionfruit, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple, fleshy fruits that contain a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruit.
