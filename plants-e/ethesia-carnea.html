Ethesia carnea

Ethesia carnea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in a fleshy, orange-red capsule that splits open when mature.

Ethesia carnea is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
