Epidendrum labiatum

Epidendrum labiatum, commonly known as the lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, lip-like petals. The plant typically has strap-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, and are often fragrant.

Epidendrum labiatum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
