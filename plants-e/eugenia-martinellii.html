Eugenia martinellii

Eugenia martinellii, commonly known as the Martinellii Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a smooth texture. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments at moderate altitudes.
