Epidendrum lasiostachyum

Epidendrum lasiostachyum, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum lasiostachyum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
