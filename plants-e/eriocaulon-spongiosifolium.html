Eriocaulon spongiosifolium

Eriocaulon spongiosifolium, commonly known as the spongy-leaf rush, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a spongy texture, which is a distinguishing feature.

Eriocaulon spongiosifolium thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in swampy or seasonally flooded areas.
