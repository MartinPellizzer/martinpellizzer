Erophila conferta

Erophila conferta, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils. The plant features slender stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple and are clustered in dense heads.
