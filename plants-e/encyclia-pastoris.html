Encyclia pastoris

Encyclia pastoris, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Encyclia pastoris thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil and partial shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
