Eria thao

Eria thao, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a compact growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Eria thao is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
