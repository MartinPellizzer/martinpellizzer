Erodium × hybridum

Erodium × hybridum, commonly known as the common stork's bill, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple, and its distinctive seed pods that resemble a stork's bill. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates. It has finely divided, trifoliate leaves and produces small, edible seeds.

Erodium × hybridum is often found in gardens and along roadsides, where it spreads through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
