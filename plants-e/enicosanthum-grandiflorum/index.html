Enicosanthum Grandiflorum

Cape jasmine, scientifically known as Enicosanthum grandiflorum, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is prized for its showy, fragrant blooms that range from white to pink and red. The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, Cape jasmine requires careful attention to ensure optimal growth and longevity.
