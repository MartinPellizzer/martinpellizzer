Euphorbia kwebensis

Euphorbia kwebensis, commonly known as the Kwebeni spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, branched stem covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are usually reduced to small, scale-like structures, which help minimize transpiration.

Euphorbia kwebensis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
