Eugenia adenantha

Eugenia adenantha, commonly known as the red-leaved eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark red to purple foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Eugenia adenantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
