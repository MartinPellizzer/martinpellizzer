Einadia hastata

Einadia hastata, commonly known as the narrowleaf false hellebore, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in rich, well-drained soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
