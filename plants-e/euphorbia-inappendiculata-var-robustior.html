Euphorbia inappendiculata var. robustior

Euphorbia inappendiculata var. robustior, commonly known as the robust euphorbia, is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Namibia and South Africa. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its succulent nature allows it to store water, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for rocky or well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
