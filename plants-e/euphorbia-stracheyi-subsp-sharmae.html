Euphorbia stracheyi subsp. sharmae

Euphorbia stracheyi subsp.

sharmae, commonly known as the Sharma's spurge, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of India. It features a short, thick stem with spiny, fleshy leaves that help it store water.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low rainfall, making it a resilient species.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
