Epidendrum aemulum var. brevistriatum

Epidendrum aemulum var. brevistriatum, commonly known as the Short-stemmed Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features compact, evergreen pseudobulbs that support strap-like, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
