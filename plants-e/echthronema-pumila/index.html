Echthronema Pumila

Echthronema pumila is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its small, white petals that resemble tiny clouds. This species is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making it a valuable indicator of ecosystem health. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Echthronema pumila is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique botanical treasure.
