Eria limenophylax

Eria limenophylax, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with few leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its roots are fibrous and often form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native ecosystems.
