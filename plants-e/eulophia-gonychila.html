Eulophia gonychila

Eulophia gonychila, commonly known as the narrow-leafed eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing small, white to pale green flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia gonychila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
