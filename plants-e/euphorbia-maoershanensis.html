Euphorbia maoershanensis

Euphorbia maoershanensis, commonly known as Maoershan spurge, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its succulent, spiny stems and distinctive flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually small and alternate, while its flowers are clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the genus Euphorbia.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological significance and potential medicinal properties.
