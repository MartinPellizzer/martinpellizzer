Eriocaulon melanocephalum var. longipes

Eriocaulon melanocephalum var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed black-headed pipewort, is a rare aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are typically surrounded by a dense tuft of fine, hair-like structures. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with ample sunlight.
