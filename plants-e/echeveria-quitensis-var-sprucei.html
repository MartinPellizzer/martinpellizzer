Echeveria quitensis var. sprucei

Echeveria quitensis var. sprucei, commonly known as the Spruce's Echeveria, is a succulent native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
