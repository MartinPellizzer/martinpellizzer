Elatine spathulata

Elatine spathulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically oval or spatulate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds, ditches, and wetlands.

Elatine spathulata is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed aquatic habitats.
