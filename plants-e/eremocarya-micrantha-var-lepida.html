Eremocarya micrantha var. lepida

Eremocarya micrantha var. lepida, commonly known as the smooth eremocarya, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically narrow and hairy, adapted to arid environments. This variety is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in regions with low rainfall.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
