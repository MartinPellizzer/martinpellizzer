Eurycaulis perula

Eurycaulis perula, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage. Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it versatile for various landscaping applications.

However, it is important to note that it can become invasive in certain environments if not properly managed.
