Eucryphia milliganii subsp. milliganii

Eucryphia milliganii subsp.

milliganii, commonly known as the Milligan's eucryphia, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically appearing in spring.

The plant has smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
