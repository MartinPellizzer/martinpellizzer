Echites atropurpureus

Echites atropurpureus, commonly known as the purple heart, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple to reddish-pink stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
