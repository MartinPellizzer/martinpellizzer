Erica cyrilliflora

Erica cyrilliflora, commonly known as the Cyrilliflora heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Erica cyrilliflora is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and attractive appearance.
