Euonymus japonicus f. aureovariegatus

Euonymus japonicus f. aureovariegatus, commonly known as the Japanese Spurge Variegated, is a decorative shrub characterized by its striking variegated foliage.

The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a yellow-green margin and a deep green center, creating a vibrant contrast. It is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family and is native to Japan. This plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

It is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases, making it a popular choice for gardens.
