Echeveria sessiliflora

Echeveria sessiliflora, commonly known as the Rosette Echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is relatively low maintenance once established.
