Euphorbia polygona var. exilis

Euphorbia polygona var. exilis, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
