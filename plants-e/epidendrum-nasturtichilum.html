Epidendrum nasturtichilum

Epidendrum nasturtichilum, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum nasturtichilum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
