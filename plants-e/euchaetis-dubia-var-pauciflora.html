Euchaetis dubia var. pauciflora

Euchaetis dubia var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered euchaetis, is a lesser-known variant of the euchaetis genus.

It is characterized by its reduced floral display, with fewer flowers compared to other varieties within the species. The plant typically exhibits small, clustered flowers that are often pale in color, contributing to its subtle aesthetic. It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
