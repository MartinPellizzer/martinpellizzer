Eleocharis × subangulata

Eleocharis × subangulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved spikerush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and shallow water areas. Its morphology includes a robust stem with a clustered arrangement of spikelets.

Eleocharis × subangulata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
