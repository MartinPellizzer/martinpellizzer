Epimedium × warleyense

Epimedium × warleyense, commonly known as the barrenwort or Chinese barrenwort, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Berberidaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant produces showy, nodding flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically appearing in early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Epimedium × warleyense is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
