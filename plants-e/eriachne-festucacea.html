Eriachne festucacea

Eriachne festucacea, commonly known as slender false grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. It is well adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system to access water.

Eriachne festucacea plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
