Epidendrum remotiflorum

Epidendrum remotiflorum, commonly known as the Purple Rain Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forests or along riverbanks. Its flowers are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Epidendrum remotiflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
