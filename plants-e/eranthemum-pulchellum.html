Eranthemum pulchellum

Eranthemum pulchellum, commonly known as the beautiful eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Eranthemum pulchellum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
