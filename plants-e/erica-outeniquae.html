Erica outeniquae

Erica outeniquae, commonly known as the Outeniqua heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. This plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in acidic, well-drained soils. Erica outeniquae produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late winter to early spring.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
