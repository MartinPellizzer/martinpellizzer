Elatostema prunifolium

Elatostema prunifolium, commonly known as the cherry-leaved elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky outcrops. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, often resembling the shape of cherry leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, which are usually borne on short pedicels.

Elatostema prunifolium is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and its preference for well-drained soils.
