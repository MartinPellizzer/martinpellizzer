Euphrasia olotensis

Euphrasia olotensis, commonly known as the Olot wild gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
