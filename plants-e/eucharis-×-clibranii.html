Eucharis × clibranii

Eucharis × clibranii, commonly known as the Amazon lily or white elephant plant, is a hybrid species native to South America.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a rhizomatous herb, with broad, strap-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

The flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a striking visual element to indoor spaces.
