Eriocaulon dioecum

Eriocaulon dioecum, commonly known as the dioecious spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dimorphic reproductive structures, with separate male and female plants. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in wetlands and marshy areas. Its inflorescence consists of a dense spike of small, scale-like flowers.
