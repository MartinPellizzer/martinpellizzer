Eragrostis caroliniana

Eragrostis caroliniana, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and a distinctive silver-tan color during the winter months. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a common sight in prairies and open woodlands. It plays an important role in ecological systems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Eragrostis caroliniana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
