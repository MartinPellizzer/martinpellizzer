Erythrodes lindleyana

Erythrodes lindleyana, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and sepals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom during the rainy season, adding vibrant color to the undergrowth.

Erythrodes lindleyana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
