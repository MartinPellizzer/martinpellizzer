Erymophyllum glossanthus

Erymophyllum glossanthus, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Erymophyllum glossanthus is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a resilient species in semi-arid climates.
