Eria maboroensis

Eria maboroensis, commonly known as the Maboro orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are broad and glossy, while its flowers are usually pale pink or white with a distinctive yellow lip.

Eria maboroensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
