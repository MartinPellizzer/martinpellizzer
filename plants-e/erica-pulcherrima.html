Erica pulcherrima

Erica pulcherrima, commonly known as the Christmas or holiday flower, is a shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant is evergreen, with dense, leathery leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Erica pulcherrima is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year, especially during the holiday season.
