Epidendrum guerrerense

Epidendrum guerrerense, commonly known as the Guerrerense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and bears inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum guerrerense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
