Exochaenium Pumilum

Exochaenium pumilum is a small, hardy grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and nutrient availability. This plant is known for its compact growth and resilience to extreme conditions. Its unique adaptations make it an important component of desert ecosystems. Despite its modest size, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
