Equisetum Ramosissimum Var. Elegans

Equisetum ramosissimum var. elegans is a strikingly ancient plant that has thrived for millions of years. Known for its distinctive, fern-like fronds and spore-producing cones, it belongs to the Equisetaceae family. This variety is particularly prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses. It requires well-drained soil and partial sunlight to flourish. Its unique morphology and historical significance make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant lovers alike.
