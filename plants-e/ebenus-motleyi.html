Ebenus motleyi

Ebenus motleyi, commonly known as Motley's bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, inflated seed pods. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in its native region.
