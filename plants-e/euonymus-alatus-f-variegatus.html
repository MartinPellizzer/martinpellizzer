Euonymus alatus f. variegatus

Euonymus alatus f. variegatus, commonly known as the winged euonymus or variegated spindle tree, is a cultivar of the winged euonymus species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, flattened, wing-like branches and variegated foliage that typically features white or yellow markings. The leaves are usually oval-shaped and have a glossy texture, adding ornamental value to gardens. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that thrives in full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
