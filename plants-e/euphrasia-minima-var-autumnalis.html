Euphrasia minima var. autumnalis

Euphrasia minima var. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn eyebright, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features delicate, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in herbal medicine for its purported eye-health benefits.
