Eriogonum ainsliei

Eriogonum ainsliei, commonly known as Ainslie's buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ainslie's buckwheat is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
