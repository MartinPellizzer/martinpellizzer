Eupatorium mairei var. heterophyllum

Eupatorium mairei var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the Maire's eupatorium, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has opposite, serrated leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. Its showy, clustered inflorescences produce small, white to pale blue flowers that attract pollinators.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and wetlands.
