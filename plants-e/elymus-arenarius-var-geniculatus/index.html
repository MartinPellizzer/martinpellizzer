Elymus Arenarius Var. Geniculatus

Elymus arenarius var. geniculatus is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. This variant is characterized by its distinctive growth habit and unique morphological features. It is commonly found in sandy soils and coastal regions where it plays a vital ecological role. Despite its resilience, it remains underappreciated in both scientific and horticultural circles. Understanding this plant can offer insights into plant adaptation and ecosystem dynamics.
