Eugenia flavovirens var. obscura

Eugenia flavovirens var. obscura, commonly known as the dark-eyed eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of aromatic wood.
