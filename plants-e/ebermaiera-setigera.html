Ebermaiera setigera

Ebermaiera setigera, commonly known as the slender-leaved evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its woody stem is often covered in a thin, smooth bark.

Ebermaiera setigera is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
