Euphorbia australis var. canescens

Euphorbia australis var. canescens, commonly known as the silver euphorbia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its gray-green, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
