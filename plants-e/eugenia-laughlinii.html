Eugenia laughlinii

Eugenia laughlinii, commonly known as the Laughlin's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
