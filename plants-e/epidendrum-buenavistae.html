Epidendrum buenavistae

Epidendrum buenavistae, commonly known as the Good View Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, light yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, thriving in well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, pale pubescence.

Epidendrum buenavistae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
