Encyclia virens

Encyclia virens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip that is often lighter in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Encyclia virens is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
