Epidendrum fuscatum var. viridipurpureum

Epidendrum fuscatum var. viridipurpureum, commonly known as the Purple-leaf Epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green to purple foliage, which often features a striking purplish hue on the underside. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
