Echinopsis chacoana var. spinosior

Echinopsis chacoana var. spinosior, commonly known as the Chacoan moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to Argentina.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in spines, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
