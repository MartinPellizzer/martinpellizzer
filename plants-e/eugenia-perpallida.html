Eugenia perpallida

Eugenia perpallida, commonly known as the white eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically reddish when mature. Eugenia perpallida is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

It has also been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
