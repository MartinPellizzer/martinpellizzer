Elatostema pentaneurum

Elatostema pentaneurum, commonly known as the five-veined elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in wet or damp environments, such as rainforests or swampy areas. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with five distinct veins, which is a key identifying feature. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in inflorescences.

Elatostema pentaneurum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
