Echinopsis eyriesii var. tettavii

Echinopsis eyriesii var. tettavii, commonly known as the Tettavii moon cactus, is a cactus species native to South America, specifically Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and striking white to pale blue flowers. The plant has a dense covering of spines and is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
