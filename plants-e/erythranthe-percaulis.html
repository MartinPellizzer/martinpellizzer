Erythranthe percaulis

Erythranthe percaulis, commonly known as the Sierra wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, often in alpine or subalpine regions. It has basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, and its stems are usually unbranched.
