Erigeron bellidiastrum var. robustus

Erigeron bellidiastrum var. robustus, commonly known as the robust hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb with a basal rosette of leaves and produces numerous yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The species is noted for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
