Epidendrum croatii

Epidendrum croatii, commonly known as the Croatian Vanda, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to Central and South America, particularly found in the Caribbean and parts of Mexico. The plant has thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are typically showy, with vibrant colors and a complex structure typical of orchids.

Epidendrum croatii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
