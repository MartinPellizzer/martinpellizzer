Elaphoglossum paulistanum

Elaphoglossum paulistanum, commonly known as the Brazilian holly fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of its fertile fronds.
