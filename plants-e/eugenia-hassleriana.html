Eugenia hassleriana

Eugenia hassleriana, commonly known as the Hassler's eugenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, red, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
