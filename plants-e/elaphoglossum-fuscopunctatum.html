Elaphoglossum fuscopunctatum

Elaphoglossum fuscopunctatum, commonly known as the spotted holly fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds. The leaves are typically dark green with a silvery or pale underside, and the sporangia are clustered in sori along the margins of the fronds. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shady garden settings.
