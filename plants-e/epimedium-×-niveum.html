Epimedium × niveum

Epimedium × niveum, commonly known as the Moonshade, is a deciduous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, nodding, cream-colored flowers that appear in early spring. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Epimedium × niveum is valued for its ornamental qualities and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
