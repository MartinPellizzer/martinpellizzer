Eriolaena linearifolia

Eriolaena linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriolaena, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. The plant features linear, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, attracting various pollinators.

Eriolaena linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
