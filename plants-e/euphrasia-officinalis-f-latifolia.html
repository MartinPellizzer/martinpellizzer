Euphrasia officinalis f. latifolia

Euphrasia officinalis f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved eyebright, is a variant of the eyebright plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies for eye conditions.

The f. latifolia form is distinguished by its wider foliage compared to the typical species.
