Elsholtzia ocimoides

Elsholtzia ocimoides, commonly known as silver sage or narrowleaf silver sage, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, pale blue to lavender flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its essential oils contain compounds like thymol and carvacrol, which contribute to its strong scent and medicinal properties.

Elsholtzia ocimoides is often used in herbal remedies and as a companion plant in gardens.
