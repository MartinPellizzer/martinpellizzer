Epidendrum dayseae

Epidendrum dayseae, commonly known as the Dayseae Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, branching stems and numerous, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing clusters of violet to purple flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in two rows along the stem, and it prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
