Euphrasia stricta subsp. pectinata

Euphrasia stricta subsp.

pectinata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, glandular hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface, and it produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in umbels.

It is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
