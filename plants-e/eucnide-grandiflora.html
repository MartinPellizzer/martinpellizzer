Eucnide grandiflora

Eucnide grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered eucnide, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and conservation efforts.
