Ehretia spinifex

Ehretia spinifex, commonly known as the spiny leaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its woody shrub structure and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Ehretia spinifex is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun.
