Echinodorus bracteatus var. efenestratus

Echinodorus bracteatus var. efenestratus, commonly known as the Fenestrated Sword, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, pleated leaves that often feature a distinctive fenestrated or perforated pattern. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments and is well-suited for pond and aquarium settings. It produces small, white flowers that emerge above the water surface, adding ornamental value.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily, making it a popular choice for water gardeners.
