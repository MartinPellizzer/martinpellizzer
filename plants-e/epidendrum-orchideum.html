Epidendrum orchideum

Epidendrum orchideum, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the production of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive red or orange lip.

Epidendrum orchideum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
