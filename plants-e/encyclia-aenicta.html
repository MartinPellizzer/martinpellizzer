Encyclia aenicta

Encyclia aenicta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in the spring. Encyclia aenicta has linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
