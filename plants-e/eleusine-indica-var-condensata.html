Eleusine indica var. condensata

Eleusine indica var. condensata, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a variant of the grass species Eleusine indica.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
