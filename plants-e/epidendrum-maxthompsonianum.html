Epidendrum maxthompsonianum

Epidendrum maxthompsonianum, commonly known as the Thompson's Epidendrum, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Mexico, Guatemala, and Honduras. This epiphytic plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale green with a slight pinkish tinge, and it produces a fragrant scent.

The plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
