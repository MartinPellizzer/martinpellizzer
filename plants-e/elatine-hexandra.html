Elatine hexandra

Elatine hexandra, commonly known as the common water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and streams. The plant has small, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually submerged or floating. Its flowers are tiny and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with four petals.

Elatine hexandra is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed aquatic habitats.
