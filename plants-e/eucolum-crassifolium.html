Eucolum crassifolium

Eucolum crassifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf eucolum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green in color. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
