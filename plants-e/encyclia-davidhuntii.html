Encyclia davidhuntii

Encyclia davidhuntii, commonly known as David Hunt's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of light yellow to orange-red blooms with a distinctive white lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
