Echinops boranensis

Echinops boranensis, commonly known as Boran thistle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical crown and is part of the Asteraceae family. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Echinops boranensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
