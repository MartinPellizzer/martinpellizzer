Epipactis × nicolosii

Epipactis × nicolosii, commonly known as the hybrid lady's tresses, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Epipactis helleborine and Epipactis palustris.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves and long, spiraled inflorescences bearing numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. It has a rhizomatous root system and is often found in the understory of temperate forests.

Epipactis × nicolosii is a perennial herb that thrives in regions with cool, humid climates.
