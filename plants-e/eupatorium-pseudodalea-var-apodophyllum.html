Eupatorium pseudodalea var. apodophyllum

Eupatorium pseudodalea var. apodophyllum, commonly known as the slender boneset, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, sunny habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Its scientific name reflects its distinctive morphological features and geographic distribution.
