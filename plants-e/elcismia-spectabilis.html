Elcismia spectabilis

Elcismia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy elcismia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive violet or purple hue at the base. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Elcismia spectabilis is valued in native plant gardens for its beauty and ecological benefits to local pollinators.
