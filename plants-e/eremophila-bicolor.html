Eremophila bicolor

Eremophila bicolor, commonly known as the pink ertan, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species.

Eremophila bicolor is often used in native gardens due to its ornamental value and low water requirements.
