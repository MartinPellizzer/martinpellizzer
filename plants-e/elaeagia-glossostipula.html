Elaeagia glossostipula

Elaeagia glossostipula, commonly known as the glossy-stippled elaeagia, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with prominent white or silver stippling on the leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry woodland habitats.

Elaeagia glossostipula is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
