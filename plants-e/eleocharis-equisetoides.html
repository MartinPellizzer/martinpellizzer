Eleocharis equisetoides

Eleocharis equisetoides, commonly known as the spike rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial grass-like plant that typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it often forms dense stands in moist environments.
