Echinopsis densispina f. cupreoviridis

Echinopsis densispina f. cupreoviridis, commonly known as the copper-veined echinopsis, is a succulent cactus native to South America.

It features a globular stem with a ribbed structure and spiny areoles, typical of the Echinopsis genus. The plant produces vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
