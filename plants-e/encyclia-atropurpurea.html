Encyclia atropurpurea

Encyclia atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple and green foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia atropurpurea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
