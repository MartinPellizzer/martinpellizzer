Ethanium thyrsodeum

Ethanium thyrsodeum, commonly known as the silver thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and tall, spiny stems that support its showy purple or pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. Ethanium thyrsodeum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for drought-resistant gardens.
