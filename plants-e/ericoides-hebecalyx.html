Ericoides hebecalyx

Ericoides hebecalyx, commonly known as the slender calyx heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland or moorland environments.

Ericoides hebecalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
