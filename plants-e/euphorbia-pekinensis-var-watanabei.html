Euphorbia pekinensis var. watanabei

Euphorbia pekinensis var. watanabei, commonly known as the Watanabei Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to Japan.

It features a distinctive, branched stem structure with spines that protect it from herbivores. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are surrounded by colorful bracts. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
