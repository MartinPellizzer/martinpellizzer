Erythrodes chinensis var. chinensis

Erythrodes chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese red-flowered orchid, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and yellow sepals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
