Erica aghillana var. aghillana

Erica aghillana var. aghillana, commonly known as the Aghillan heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Scottish Highlands.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, white waxy coating. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Erica aghillana var. aghillana is well adapted to harsh coastal and mountainous environments.
