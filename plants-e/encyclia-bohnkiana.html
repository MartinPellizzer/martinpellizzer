Encyclia bohnkiana

Encyclia bohnkiana, commonly known as the Bohnken's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are broad and strap-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Encyclia bohnkiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
