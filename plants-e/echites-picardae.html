Echites picardae

Echites picardae, commonly known as the picard's echites, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
