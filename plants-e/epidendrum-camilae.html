Epidendrum camilae

Epidendrum camilae, commonly known as the Camila's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, showy inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom sequentially along the spike. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum camilae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
