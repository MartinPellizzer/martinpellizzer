Epilobium montanum var. carpetanum

Epilobium montanum var. carpetanum, commonly known as the Alpine willow herb, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine meadows and slopes.

Epilobium montanum var. carpetanum is a herbaceous perennial that plays a role in stabilizing soil in high-altitude environments.
