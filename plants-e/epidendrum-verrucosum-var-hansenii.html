Epidendrum verrucosum var. hansenii

Epidendrum verrucosum var. hansenii, commonly known as the Hansen's velvet epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
