Epipactis helleborine subsp. leutei

Epipactis helleborine subsp.

leutei, commonly known as the yellow helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and showy, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It produces a single spike of flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
