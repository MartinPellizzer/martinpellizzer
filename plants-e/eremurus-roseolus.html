Eremurus roseolus

Eremurus roseolus, commonly known as the pink desert candle, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous pale pink to white flowers. Eremurus roseolus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and unique architectural form.
