Euphorbia petala

Euphorbia petala, commonly known as the petal euphorbia, is a species native to parts of Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, often spiny stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to store water in its thick stems.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
