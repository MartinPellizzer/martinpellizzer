Epidendrum envigadoense

Epidendrum envigadoense, commonly known as the Envigado orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a fine pubescence.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
