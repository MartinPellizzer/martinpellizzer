Epidendrum × belmillerae

Epidendrum × belmillerae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Epidendrum ibaguense and Epidendrum nocturnum.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces numerous pseudobulbs. The plant features long, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. It produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, spicy scent.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
