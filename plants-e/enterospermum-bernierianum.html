Enterospermum bernierianum

Enterospermum bernierianum, commonly known as the broadleaf nightshade, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other notable plants like tomatoes and tobacco. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct scent. Its flowers are typically yellow or white, with five petals, and it produces berries that contain toxic compounds.
