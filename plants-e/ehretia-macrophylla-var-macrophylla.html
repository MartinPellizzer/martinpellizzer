Ehretia macrophylla var. macrophylla

Ehretia macrophylla var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf ehretia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 15 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
