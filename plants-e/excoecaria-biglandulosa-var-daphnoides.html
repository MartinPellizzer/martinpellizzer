Excoecaria biglandulosa var. daphnoides

Excoecaria biglandulosa var. daphnoides, commonly known as the Daphnoides croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
