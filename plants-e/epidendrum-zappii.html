Epidendrum zappii

Epidendrum zappii, commonly known as the Zappi's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Epidendrum zappii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
