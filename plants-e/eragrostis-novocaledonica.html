Eragrostis novocaledonica

Eragrostis novocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia grass, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
