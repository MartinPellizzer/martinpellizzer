Eriocaulon buchananii var. buchananii

Eriocaulon buchananii var. buchananii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf marsh plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in wet, boggy environments and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

This variety is notable for its delicate appearance and its ability to tolerate waterlogged soils.
