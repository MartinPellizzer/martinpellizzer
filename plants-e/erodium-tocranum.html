Erodium tocranum

Erodium tocranum, commonly known as the coastal bird's-beak, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky coastal areas. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in the spring. Its seeds are enclosed in a slender, curved pod that splits open when mature.

Erodium tocranum is adapted to withstand salt spray and harsh coastal conditions.
