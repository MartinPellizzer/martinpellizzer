Epidendrum × monteverdense

Epidendrum × monteverdense, commonly known as the Monteverde Epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it well-suited for tropical greenhouse environments. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
