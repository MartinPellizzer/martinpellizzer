Eugenia umbrosa

Eugenia umbrosa, commonly known as the dark Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a small, reddish berry that ripens in the summer months.

Eugenia umbrosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
