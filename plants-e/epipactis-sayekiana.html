Epipactis sayekiana

Epipactis sayekiana, commonly known as the Sayek's helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent lips. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects.

Epipactis sayekiana is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, herbaceous growth habit.
