Echinospermum oligacanthum

Echinospermum oligacanthum, commonly known as the roughleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant features serrated, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a pioneer species in ecological succession.
