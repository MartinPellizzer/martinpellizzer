Euodia hortensis var. odorifera

Euodia hortensis var. odorifera, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, resinous bark and small, leathery leaves. The plant is valued for its aromatic properties, with the bark often used in traditional medicine. It produces small white flowers that are followed by spherical, green fruits.

The tree is typically grown for its ornamental value and its historical use in herbal remedies.
