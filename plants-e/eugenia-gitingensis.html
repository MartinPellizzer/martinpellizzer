Eugenia gitingensis

Eugenia gitingensis, commonly known as the Giting's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, reddish-purple berries.

The plant is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
