Eriocapitella × hybrida

Eriocapitella × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid pincushion flower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, fluffy spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, white or pale yellow florets. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique, cotton-like appearance, which adds visual interest to garden landscapes.
