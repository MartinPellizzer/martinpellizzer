Etlingera calycina

Etlingera calycina, commonly known as the flame spike ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its striking red and orange spathes that enclose a cluster of small, fragrant flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The botanical features of Etlingera calycina make it a notable ornamental and horticultural plant.
