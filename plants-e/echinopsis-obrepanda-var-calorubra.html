Echinopsis obrepanda var. calorubra

Echinopsis obrepanda var. calorubra, commonly known as the red-flowered moon cactus, is a hybrid cactus species originating from the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant features a columnar stem with spines and a swollen, colorful tuberous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
