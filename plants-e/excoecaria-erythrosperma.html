Excoecaria erythrosperma

Excoecaria erythrosperma, commonly known as the red-seed croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and diverse leaf structures. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in various cultures.
