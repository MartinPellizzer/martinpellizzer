Epidendrum trapeziinocturnum

Epidendrum trapeziinocturnum, commonly known as the nightshade orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences. The plant produces striking, night-blooming flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum trapeziinocturnum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
