Eragrostis Airoides

Eragrostis airoides is a unique grass species known for its striking silver-blue foliage. Native to parts of Africa and the Middle East, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance. Its feathery plumes add a delicate touch to garden designs. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish.
