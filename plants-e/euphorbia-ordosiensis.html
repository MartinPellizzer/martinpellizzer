Euphorbia ordosiensis

Euphorbia ordosiensis, commonly known as the Ordos spurge, is a succulent plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in a structure called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its stems for survival.

Euphorbia ordosiensis is often found in rocky or sandy soils in its natural habitat.
