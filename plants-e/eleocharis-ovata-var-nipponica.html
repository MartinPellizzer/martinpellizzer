Eleocharis ovata var. nipponica

Eleocharis ovata var. nipponica, commonly known as Japanese spike-rush, is a flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to East Asia, including Japan, Korea, and parts of China. The plant is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of small, pale green to brownish flowers. It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, pond edges, and along streams.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and improve water quality.
