Eugenia toddalioides

Eugenia toddalioides, commonly known as the Togian eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Togian Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance in its native habitat.
