Erodium oxyrhinchum var. hohenackeri

Erodium oxyrhinchum var. hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to ecological studies and native plant gardens.
