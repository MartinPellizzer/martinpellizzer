Erica meuronii

Erica meuronii, commonly known as the Cape heath or narrow-leaf heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, particularly the Western and Northern Cape provinces. This evergreen shrub typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica meuronii features dense, needle-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and red.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
