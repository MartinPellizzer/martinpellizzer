Eulophia Picta

Eulophia picta is a striking orchid native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. It is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that often feature intricate patterns and contrasting hues. This plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and collectors. Eulophia picta is not only beautiful but also an important species in ecological and horticultural studies.
