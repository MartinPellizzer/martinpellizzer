Exacum nummulariifolium

Exacum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, delicate, white or pinkish flowers. The leaves are rounded and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving the plant its common name. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
