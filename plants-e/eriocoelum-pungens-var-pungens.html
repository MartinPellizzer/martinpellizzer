Eriocoelum pungens var. pungens

Eriocoelum pungens var. pungens, commonly known as the spiny poinsettia, is a unique variant of the poinsettia species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with woody stems and serrated, dark green leaves. Its foliage is often covered in fine, spiny hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant during the holiday season.
