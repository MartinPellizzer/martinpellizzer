Eugenia linocieroidea

Eugenia linocieroidea, commonly known as the pink wax myrtle, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or purple when mature. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Eugenia linocieroidea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
