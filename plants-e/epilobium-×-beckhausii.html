Epilobium × beckhausii

Epilobium × beckhausii, commonly known as Beckhaus' willowherb, is a hybrid plant in the evening primrose family (Onagraceae).

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and disturbed sites.

Epilobium × beckhausii is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
