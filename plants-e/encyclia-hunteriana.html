Encyclia hunteriana

Encyclia hunteriana, commonly known as the Hunter's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia hunteriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
