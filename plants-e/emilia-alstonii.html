Emilia alstonii

Emilia alstonii, commonly known as the yellow dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. Emilia alstonii is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its resilience to drought conditions.
