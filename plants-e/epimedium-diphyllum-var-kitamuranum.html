Epimedium diphyllum var. kitamuranum

Epimedium diphyllum var. kitamuranum, commonly known as the Japanese barrenwort, is a deciduous perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its attractive, leathery, heart-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the autumn. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-green flowers in early spring before the leaves emerge. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and early bloom, adding interest to the early spring garden.
