Eragrostis lichiangensis

Eragrostis lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lovegrass, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The leaves are narrow and often hairy, with a bluish-green hue, and the inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

This plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological roles in its native environment.
