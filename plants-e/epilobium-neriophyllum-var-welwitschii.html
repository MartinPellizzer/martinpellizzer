Epilobium neriophyllum var. welwitschii

Epilobium neriophyllum var. welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture and a bluish-green hue.

The flowers are pink to purple, arranged in spike-like clusters, and bloom in late summer to early autumn.
