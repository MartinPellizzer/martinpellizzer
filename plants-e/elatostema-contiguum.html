Elatostema contiguum

Elatostema contiguum, commonly known as the creeping elatostema, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with thin, fleshy stems that often root at the nodes. This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks.
