Eulophia virens

Eulophia virens, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Eulophia virens thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
