Eugenia amoena var. rotundata

Eugenia amoena var. rotundata, commonly known as the rounded beauty apple, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features glossy, elliptical to rounded leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often followed by edible, round, yellowish fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential fruit production in suitable climates.
