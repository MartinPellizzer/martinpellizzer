Epidendrum colorans

Epidendrum colorans, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with contrasting patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and often have a waxy texture, adapting to high humidity and indirect light conditions.

Epidendrum colorans is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
