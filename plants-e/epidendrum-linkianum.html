Epidendrum linkianum

Epidendrum linkianum, commonly known as the Linkian orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with contrasting markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Epidendrum linkianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
