Elytranthe affinis

Elytranthe affinis, commonly known as the rock fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It typically grows on rocky outcrops and in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it anchor to rocky substrates.

Elytranthe affinis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
