Erica × cylindrica

Erica × cylindrica, commonly known as the Cape heath or coastal heath, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming from late summer to early autumn. Erica × cylindrica is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salt spray and strong winds.

Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round interest in the landscape.
