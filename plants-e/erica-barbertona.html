Erica barbertona

Erica barbertona, commonly known as the Barberton heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. The plant produces small, dense clusters of white or pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Erica barbertona is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
