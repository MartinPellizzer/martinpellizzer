Erythroxylum pulchrum

Erythroxylum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking red flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
