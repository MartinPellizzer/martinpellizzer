Epiblastus ornithidioides

Epiblastus ornithidioides, commonly known as the bird's nest fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive rosette-shaped fronds that resemble a bird's nest, hence its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically large, leathery, and have a glossy surface.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
