Enkianthus cernuus f. rubens

Enkianthus cernuus f. rubens, commonly known as the red-flowered enkianthus, is a striking shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that range from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is evergreen, with dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and woodland settings.
