Erodium × viscosum

Erodium × viscosum, commonly known as sticky erodium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive red or purple calyx. The plant produces sticky, glandular secretions on its stems and leaves, which can make it difficult to handle. It is typically found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.
