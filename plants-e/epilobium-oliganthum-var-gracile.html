Epilobium oliganthum var. gracile

Epilobium oliganthum var. gracile, commonly known as slender willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, including China and the Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as stream banks and forest edges. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink to purple, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
