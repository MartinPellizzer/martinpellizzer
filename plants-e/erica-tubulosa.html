Erica tubulosa

Erica tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions. Erica tubulosa is evergreen, with compact, shrub-like growth and leathery foliage.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and floral display.
