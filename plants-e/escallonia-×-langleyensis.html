Escallonia × langleyensis

Escallonia × langleyensis, commonly known as the hybrid escallonia, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and features red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Escallonia × langleyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
