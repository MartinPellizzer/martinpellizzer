Epidendrum porphyreonocturnum

Epidendrum porphyreonocturnum, commonly known as the night-flowering epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark purple to almost black flowers that bloom at night, a unique trait among orchids. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish tint. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its striking appearance and nocturnal blooming habit.
