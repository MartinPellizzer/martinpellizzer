Embelia basankusuensis

Embelia basankusuensis, commonly known as the Congo embelia, is a shrub native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of white flowers followed by small, red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in secondary forests.

The species is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
