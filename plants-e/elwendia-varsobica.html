Elwendia varsobica

Elwendia varsobica, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Elwendia varsobica is also known for its toxic properties, containing alkaloids that can be harmful to humans and animals.
