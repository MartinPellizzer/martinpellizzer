Eriosema kraussianum

Eriosema kraussianum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Eriosema kraussianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
