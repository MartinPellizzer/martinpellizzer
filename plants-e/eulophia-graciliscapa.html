Eulophia graciliscapa

Eulophia graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-leafed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white with yellowish markings. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia graciliscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
