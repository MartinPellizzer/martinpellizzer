Epidendrum sylvettei

Epidendrum sylvettei, commonly known as the Sylvette's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Epidendrum sylvettei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
