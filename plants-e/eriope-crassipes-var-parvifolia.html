Eriope crassipes var. parvifolia

Eriope crassipes var. parvifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriope, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
