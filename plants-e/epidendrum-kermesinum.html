Epidendrum kermesinum

Epidendrum kermesinum, commonly known as the Kermesina Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a sympodial orchid that typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and good air circulation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and unique growth habit.
