Epipactis pinovica

Epipactis pinovica, commonly known as the slender helleborine, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, pleated leaves and a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates.

Its flowers bloom from June to August, attracting various pollinators with their nectar.
