Eleocharis uniglumis var. typica

Eleocharis uniglumis var. typica, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that often exceed 1 meter in height, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.
