Eucharis ulei

Eucharis ulei, commonly known as the Amazonian white lily, is a bulbous plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are strap-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking white, bell-shaped flowers with a pleasant fragrance, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in humid, well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Eucharis ulei is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
