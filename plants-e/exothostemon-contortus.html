Exothostemon contortus

Exothostemon contortus, commonly known as the twisted stem myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its twisted, gnarled stems and small, fragrant white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, often covered in a waxy coating.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
