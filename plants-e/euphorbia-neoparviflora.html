Euphorbia neoparviflora

Euphorbia neoparviflora, commonly known as the dwarf fairy duster, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by bright red bracts, which are often mistaken for petals. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
