Epidendrum hardingiae

Epidendrum hardingiae, commonly known as the Hardy Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Epidendrum hardingiae is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and good air circulation.
