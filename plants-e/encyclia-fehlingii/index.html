Encyclia Fehlingii

Encyclia fehlingii is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. Known for its vibrant green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media to flourish. The species is named after the German botanist Friedrich Fehling, who contributed significantly to orchid taxonomy. Its unique beauty and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
