Eria monostachya var. pleiostachya

Eria monostachya var. pleiostachya, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate petal structures typical of the Orchidaceae family.
