Eupatorium radula var. stenophyllum

Eupatorium radula var. stenophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved boneset, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in loose panicles, and are usually white or pale blue in color.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
