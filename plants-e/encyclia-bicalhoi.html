Encyclia bicalhoi

Encyclia bicalhoi, commonly known as the Bicalho's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia bicalhoi is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
