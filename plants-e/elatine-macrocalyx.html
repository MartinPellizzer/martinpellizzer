Elatine macrocalyx

Elatine macrocalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved water starwort, is a small aquatic plant that thrives in shallow, freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its root system is rhizomatous, allowing it to spread and colonize new areas efficiently.

Elatine macrocalyx plays a role in stabilizing water bodies and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
