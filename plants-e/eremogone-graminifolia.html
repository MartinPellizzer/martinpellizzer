Eremogone graminifolia

Eremogone graminifolia, commonly known as the desert grass or narrowleaf eremogone, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or purple in color.
