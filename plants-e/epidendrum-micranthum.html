Epidendrum micranthum

Epidendrum micranthum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum micranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
