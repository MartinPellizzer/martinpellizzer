Eragrostis warmingii

Eragrostis warmingii, commonly known as Warming's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often bend over. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to brown in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture and prominent veins.

Eragrostis warmingii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in dry climates.
