Epidendrum alfredii

Epidendrum alfredii, commonly known as the Alfred's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Epidendrum alfredii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
