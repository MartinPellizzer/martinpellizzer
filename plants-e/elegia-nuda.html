Elegia nuda

Elegia nuda, commonly known as the naked elegance, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, leafless stems that bear small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Elegia nuda is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
