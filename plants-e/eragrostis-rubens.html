Eragrostis rubens

Eragrostis rubens, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems that often display a reddish tinge, especially under certain environmental conditions. The leaves are narrow, linear, and have a rough, hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This plant is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in grasslands, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.
