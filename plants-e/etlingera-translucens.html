Etlingera translucens

Etlingera translucens, commonly known as the translucent ginger, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other economically important plants like ginger and turmeric. The species is valued both for its potential medicinal properties and as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
