Euphorbia × soanieranensis

Euphorbia × soanieranensis, commonly known as the hybrid euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and yellowish, cup-shaped flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves and stems. It produces a milky sap that can be toxic if ingested, making it important to handle with care.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
