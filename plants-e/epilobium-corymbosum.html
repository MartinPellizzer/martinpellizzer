Epilobium corymbosum

Epilobium corymbosum, commonly known as the showy willow herb, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. The plant features erect, branched stems covered in soft, glandular hairs. Its showy, pink to red flowers are arranged in dense, terminal clusters, making it a striking addition to gardens.

The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly serrated margin.
