Eupatorium fendleri

Eupatorium fendleri, commonly known as Mexican boneset, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. Eupatorium fendleri is often found in open habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
