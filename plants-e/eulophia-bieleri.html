Eulophia bieleri

Eulophia bieleri, commonly known as the Bieler's eulophia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Eulophia bieleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
