Eriocaulon mamfeense

Eriocaulon mamfeense, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and dense spikes of small, white flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.

The plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
