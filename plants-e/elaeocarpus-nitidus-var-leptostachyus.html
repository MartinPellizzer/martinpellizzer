Elaeocarpus nitidus var. leptostachyus

Elaeocarpus nitidus var. leptostachyus, commonly known as the slender-stalked elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are typically small, woody drupes that contain a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
