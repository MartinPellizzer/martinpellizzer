Echeveria kimnachii

Echeveria kimnachii, commonly known as the Kimnachii haworthia, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Echeveria kimnachii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and striking appearance.
