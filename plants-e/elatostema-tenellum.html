Elatostema tenellum

Elatostema tenellum, commonly known as the small leaf elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows as a low-growing, spreading plant with small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Elatostema tenellum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of cultivation.
