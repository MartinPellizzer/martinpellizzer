Epidendrum dermatanthum

Epidendrum dermatanthum, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, hairy pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum dermatanthum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
