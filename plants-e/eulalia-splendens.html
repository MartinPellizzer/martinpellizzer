Eulalia splendens

Eulalia splendens, commonly known as the golden feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features slender, arching leaves that are bright golden in color, creating a dramatic visual effect in gardens. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes that add movement and texture to the landscape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for modern landscaping and container gardening.
