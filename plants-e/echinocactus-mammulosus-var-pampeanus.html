Echinocactus mammulosus var. pampeanus

Echinocactus mammulosus var. pampeanus, commonly known as the Pampas hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in prominent, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
