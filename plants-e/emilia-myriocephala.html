Emilia myriocephala

Emilia myriocephala, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, yellow-orange flower heads that resemble a crown, giving it its common name. The plant produces narrow, linear leaves and has a bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Emilia myriocephala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
