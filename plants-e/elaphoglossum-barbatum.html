Elaphoglossum barbatum

Elaphoglossum barbatum, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Elaphoglossum barbatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
