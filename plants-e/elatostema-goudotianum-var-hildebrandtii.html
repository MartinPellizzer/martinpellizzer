Elatostema goudotianum var. hildebrandtii

Elatostema goudotianum var. hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's prostrate elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and produces tiny, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its stems are often prostrate or trailing, allowing it to spread along the ground.

This species is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its preference for well-drained, acidic soils.
