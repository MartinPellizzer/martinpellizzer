Epilobium nitidum

Epilobium nitidum, commonly known as the smooth willow herb, is a flowering plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in fine hairs. Epilobium nitidum is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for pollinators.
