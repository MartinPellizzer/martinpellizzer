Elatostema cupulare var. pedunculatum

Elatostema cupulare var. pedunculatum, commonly known as the spurred elatostema, is a member of the Elatostemaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its small, succulent leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, which aid in water retention.

The flowers are usually borne on short pedicels, giving the plant its varietal name "pedunculatum.".
