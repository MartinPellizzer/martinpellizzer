Eurya nitida var. nanjenshanensis

Eurya nitida var. nanjenshanensis, commonly known as the Nanjing mountain eurya, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles, followed by small, red berries. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is occasionally cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
