Erigeron amplexicaulis

Erigeron amplexicaulis, commonly known as the clasping blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Erigeron amplexicaulis thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
