Eriocaulon Parviflorum

Eriocaulon parviflorum is a small, delicate plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in wetland habitats. This species is known for its compact growth and unique floral structures that resemble tiny umbrellas. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences. Despite its modest size, Eriocaulon parviflorum plays an important role in its ecosystem and is a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
