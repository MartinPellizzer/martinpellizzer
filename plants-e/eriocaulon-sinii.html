Eriocaulon sinii

Eriocaulon sinii, commonly known as the Chinese spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white flowers. Native to China, this plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds and streams.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and ecological studies.
