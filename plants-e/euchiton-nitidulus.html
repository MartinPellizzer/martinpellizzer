Euchiton nitidulus

Euchiton nitidulus, commonly known as the smooth bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its simple, smooth leaves and small, bladder-like structures that aid in trapping small aquatic organisms. The plant typically grows in shallow, still waters such as ponds and ditches. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, appearing in clusters.
