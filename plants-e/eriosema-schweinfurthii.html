Eriosema schweinfurthii

Eriosema schweinfurthii, commonly known as the white-flowered eriosema, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers with prominent, showy stamens. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Eriosema schweinfurthii is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
