Encholirium pernambucanum

Encholirium pernambucanum, commonly known as the Pernambuco lily, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces showy inflorescences that can reach up to 3 meters in height, making it a dramatic ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that form a rosette, contributing to its unique appearance.
