Echinopsis densispina f. pectinifera

Echinopsis densispina f. pectinifera, commonly known as the Pectinifera Echinopsis, is a rare and striking cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and a compact, globular shape. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically in spring. It is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

However, it requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
