Eria bicornis

Eria bicornis, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-horned lip, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and colorful, often in shades of yellow or orange.

Eria bicornis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
