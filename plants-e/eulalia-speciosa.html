Eulalia speciosa

Eulalia speciosa, commonly known as the showy grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, arching culms that produce feathery, silver-green plumes during the late summer and fall. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and its ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add texture to garden designs.

Eulalia speciosa is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various soil types, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial landscapes.
