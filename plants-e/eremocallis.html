Eremocallis

Eremocallis, commonly known as the goatweed or shaggy beard, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its robust, herbaceous growth and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. These plants are native to various regions of Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and they often thrive in dry, sunny environments. Eremocallis species typically have tall, sturdy stems and are known for their ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
