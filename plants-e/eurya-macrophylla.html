Eurya macrophylla

Eurya macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, Japan, and Korea, where it thrives in temperate forest environments. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. Eurya macrophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
