Epidendrum calagrense

Epidendrum calagrense, commonly known as the Calagrense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features erect, branching stems covered with oval, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive purple or brown lip.

Epidendrum calagrense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
