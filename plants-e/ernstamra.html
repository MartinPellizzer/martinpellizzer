Ernstamra

Ernstamra, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a striking plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features feathery, delicate fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silvery coating, which helps reflect sunlight.

Ernstamra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in indoor gardening and landscape design.
