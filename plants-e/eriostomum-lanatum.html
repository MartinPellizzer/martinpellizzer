Eriostomum lanatum

Eriostomum lanatum, commonly known as the woolly eriostomum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Eriostomum lanatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
