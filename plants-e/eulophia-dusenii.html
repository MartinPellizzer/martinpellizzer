Eulophia dusenii

Eulophia dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Eulophia dusenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
