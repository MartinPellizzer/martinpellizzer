Erica aristata var. brenesii

Erica aristata var. brenesii, commonly known as the Brenesii heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has needle-like leaves that are gray-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
