Echeveria utcubambensis

Echeveria utcubambensis, commonly known as the UTCubambensis Echeveria, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, bluish-green leaves with a subtle waxy coating, which help it retain moisture in arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique coloration and form make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
