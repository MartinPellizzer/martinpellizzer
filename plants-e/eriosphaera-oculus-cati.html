Eriosphaera oculus-cati

Eriosphaera oculus-cati, commonly known as the cat's eye eriosphaera, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features distinctive, rounded, greenish-gray leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
