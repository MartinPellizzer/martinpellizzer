Eragrostis pubescens f. leiorachis

Eragrostis pubescens f. leiorachis, commonly known as the silky fescue, is a variant of the silky bluegrass species.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its fine, soft foliage and dense growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in prairie or wetland environments. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of hairs, giving it a silky texture.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
