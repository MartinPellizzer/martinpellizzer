Elaeagnus nanchuanensis

Elaeagnus nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray bark and pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, followed by edible, red berries that are a food source for various wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Elaeagnus nanchuanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential ecological benefits.
