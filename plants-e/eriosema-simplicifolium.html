Eriosema simplicifolium

Eriosema simplicifolium, commonly known as the simple-leaved eriosema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Eriosema simplicifolium thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grassland or rocky habitats.
