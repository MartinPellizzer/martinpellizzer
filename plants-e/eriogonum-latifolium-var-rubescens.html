Eriogonum latifolium var. rubescens

Eriogonum latifolium var. rubescens, commonly known as the redleaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its broad, reddish-tinged leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like sorrel and knotweed.
