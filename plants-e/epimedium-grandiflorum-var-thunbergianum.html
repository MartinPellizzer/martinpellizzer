Epimedium grandiflorum var. thunbergianum

Epimedium grandiflorum var. thunbergianum, commonly known as the Thunberg's barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage composed of lanceolate to ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
