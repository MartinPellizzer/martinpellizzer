Epimedium rhizomatosum

Epimedium rhizomatosum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features rhombic or ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. It produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in panicles.
