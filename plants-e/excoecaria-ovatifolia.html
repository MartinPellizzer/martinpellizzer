Excoecaria ovatifolia

Excoecaria ovatifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and vibrant green color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
