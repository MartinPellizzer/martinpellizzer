Erianthus balansae

Erianthus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and savannas. It produces small, clustered inflorescences with spikelets that contain the reproductive structures.

Erianthus balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
