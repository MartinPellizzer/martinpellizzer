Eria wenzelii

Eria wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming during the rainy season. Eria wenzelii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its botanical features make it a unique and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
