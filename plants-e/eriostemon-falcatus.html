Eriostemon falcatus

Eriostemon falcatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf eriostemon, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often aromatic leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal or sandy soils and is adapted to dry conditions. Its flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.

Eriostemon falcatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
