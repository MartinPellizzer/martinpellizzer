Elateriospermum tokbrai

Elateriospermum tokbrai, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-green leaves with a waxy texture, which help reduce water loss in its arid environment. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its unique leaf structure and adaptability to drought conditions make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Elateriospermum tokbrai is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
