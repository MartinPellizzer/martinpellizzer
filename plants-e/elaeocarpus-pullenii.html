Elaeocarpus pullenii

Elaeocarpus pullenii, commonly known as the Australian holly, is a flowering plant species native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in subtropical and tropical regions. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often bright red or yellow when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Elaeocarpus pullenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
