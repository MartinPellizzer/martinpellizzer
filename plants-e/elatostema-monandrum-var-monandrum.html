Elatostema monandrum var. monandrum

Elatostema monandrum var. monandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved elatostema, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and often exhibits a trailing or upright growth habit. Its flowers are usually greenish-white, with four petals and are borne in clusters at the leaf axils.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
