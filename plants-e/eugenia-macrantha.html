Eugenia macrantha

Eugenia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered eugenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically reddish when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Eugenia macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
