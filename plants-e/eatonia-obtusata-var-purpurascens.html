Eatonia obtusata var. purpurascens

Eatonia obtusata var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leafed evergreen, is a striking shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that exhibits a deep purple to reddish hue, especially under full sun exposure. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit and vibrant leaf color make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The leaves are evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in both indoor and outdoor settings.
