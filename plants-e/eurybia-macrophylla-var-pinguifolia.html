Eurybia macrophylla var. pinguifolia

Eurybia macrophylla var. pinguifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
