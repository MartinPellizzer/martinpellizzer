Earina valida

Earina valida, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural collections.

Earina valida requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.
