Echinodorus palifolius

Echinodorus palifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a species of aquatic plant native to South America.

It features broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

Echinodorus palifolius is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to help maintain water quality.
