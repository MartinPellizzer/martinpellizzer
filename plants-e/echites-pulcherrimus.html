Echites pulcherrimus

Echites pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful echites, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange blooms that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a striking appearance. It typically grows in moist, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
