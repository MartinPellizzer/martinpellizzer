Ephedra alte

Ephedra alte, commonly known as the tall ephedra, is a shrub-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the family Ephedraceae and is characterized by its jointed stems and scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Ephedra alte is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in areas with low rainfall.

Its structure and growth habits make it a distinctive component of desert ecosystems.
