Eleocharis bernardina

Eleocharis bernardina, commonly known as the Bernard's spike-rush, is a species of grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Eleocharis bernardina plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems and can serve as a habitat for various small aquatic organisms.
