Erica trichroma var. trichroma

Erica trichroma var. trichroma, commonly known as the three-colored heath, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family (Ericaceae).

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of tiny, brightly colored flowers that range from white to pink and red. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to harsh, sunny conditions.

Erica trichroma var. trichroma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
