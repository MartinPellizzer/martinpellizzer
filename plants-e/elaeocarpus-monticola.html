Elaeocarpus monticola

Elaeocarpus monticola, commonly known as the mountain elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of tropical ecosystems.
