Eriocaulon Atrum Var. Nakasimanum

Eriocaulon atrum var. nakasimanum is a rare and delicate aquatic plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan. It is known for its striking dark green leaves and unique, finely textured appearance. This variety thrives in cool, clear water and is often found in shaded stream environments. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural habitat. Its beauty and rarity make it a sought-after plant among aquatic enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
