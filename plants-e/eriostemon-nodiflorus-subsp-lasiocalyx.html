Eriostemon nodiflorus subsp. lasiocalyx

Eriostemon nodiflorus subsp.

lasiocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eriostemon, is a small shrub native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers.

The plant typically grows in well-drained soils in open forest or heathland habitats. It is often found in regions with a temperate climate and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly hairy stems and narrower leaves compared to other subspecies of Eriostemon nodiflorus.
