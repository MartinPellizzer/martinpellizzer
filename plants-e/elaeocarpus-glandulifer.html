Elaeocarpus glandulifer

Elaeocarpus glandulifer, commonly known as the glandular elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
