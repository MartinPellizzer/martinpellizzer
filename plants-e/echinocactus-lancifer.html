Echinocactus lancifer

Echinocactus lancifer, commonly known as the lancifer cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the top of the stems. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments.

Echinocactus lancifer is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
