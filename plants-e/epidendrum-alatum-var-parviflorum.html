Epidendrum alatum var. parviflorum

Epidendrum alatum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale green flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

This variety is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
