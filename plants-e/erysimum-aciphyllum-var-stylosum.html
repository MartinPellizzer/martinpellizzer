Erysimum aciphyllum var. stylosum

Erysimum aciphyllum var. stylosum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
