Equilabium stenosiphon

Equilabium stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon labellum, is a unique orchid species characterized by its specialized floral structure.

The plant features a distinctive labellum, or lip, that is narrow and elongated, which plays a crucial role in pollination by guiding pollinators. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with intricate patterns that enhance their visual appeal. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex reproductive mechanisms and adaptability to specific ecological niches.
