Epilobium dodonaei var. stenophyllum

Epilobium dodonaei var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willowherb, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny habitats. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pink to red in color, and arranged in spikes, blooming in the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
