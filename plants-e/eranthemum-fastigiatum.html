Eranthemum fastigiatum

Eranthemum fastigiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal or wetland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
