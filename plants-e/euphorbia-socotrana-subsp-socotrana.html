Euphorbia socotrana subsp. socotrana

Euphorbia socotrana subsp.

socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cactus, is a unique succulent native to the island of Socotra. It is characterized by its thick, spiny stems and produces a milky latex when damaged.

The plant has a distinctive appearance with its bright green, fleshy stems and yellowish flowers. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and low rainfall.

This species is notable for its ecological importance and is considered a flagship plant of the Socotra archipelago.
