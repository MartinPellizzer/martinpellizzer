Epilobium chionanthum

Epilobium chionanthum, commonly known as the snowstem willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as tundra and alpine meadows. The plant features slender, often hairy stems that may be prostrate or ascending. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or white, blooming in late summer.
