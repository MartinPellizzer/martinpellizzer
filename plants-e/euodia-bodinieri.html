Euodia bodinieri

Euodia bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic leaves and small white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain dark brown seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the essential oils extracted from its leaves.

Euodia bodinieri is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
