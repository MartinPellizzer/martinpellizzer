Erodium Saxatile

Erodium saxatile is a small flowering plant native to arid and rocky regions. It is commonly found in Mediterranean climates and parts of southern Europe. This plant is known for its delicate pink or purple flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh environments. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local insect populations. Due to its unique adaptations, it is often studied for its ecological resilience.
