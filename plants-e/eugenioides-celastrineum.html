Eugenioides celastrineum

Eugenioides celastrineum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental tree.

Eugenioides celastrineum is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local wildlife.
