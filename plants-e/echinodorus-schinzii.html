Echinodorus schinzii

Echinodorus schinzii, commonly known as the Amazon Sword Plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 20 inches in length. This plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions and prefers soft to moderately hard water. Echinodorus schinzii is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

It also plays a role in maintaining water quality by absorbing nutrients and reducing algae growth.
