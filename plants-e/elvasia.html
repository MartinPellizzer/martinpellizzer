Elvasia

Elvasia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a rare and striking species native to the Canary Islands.

It features striking silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Elvasia thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native environment.
