Eugenia fadyenii var. glabra

Eugenia fadyenii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth eugenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark and elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
