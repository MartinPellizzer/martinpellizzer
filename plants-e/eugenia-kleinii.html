Eugenia kleinii

Eugenia kleinii, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, edible fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Eugenia kleinii is also valued for its ability to attract birds and insects due to its nectar-rich flowers.
