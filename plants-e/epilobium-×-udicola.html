Epilobium × udicola

Epilobium × udicola, commonly known as the sticky willow or redstem sedges, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Onagraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, often reddish stems that are covered in fine, sticky hairs. Epilobium × udicola is often found in temperate regions of North America and is known for its ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
