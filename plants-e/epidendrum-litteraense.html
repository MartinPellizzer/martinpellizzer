Epidendrum litteraense

Epidendrum litteraense, commonly known as the Litteraense Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored blooms. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Epidendrum litteraense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
