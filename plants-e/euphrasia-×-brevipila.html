Euphrasia × brevipila

Euphrasia × brevipila, commonly known as the short-stemmed eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.
