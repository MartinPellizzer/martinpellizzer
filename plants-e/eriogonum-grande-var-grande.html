Eriogonum grande var. grande

Eriogonum grande var. grande, commonly known as the large leaf buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family and is known for its adaptability to arid conditions.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and stabilizing soil.
