Eugenia kamelii

Eugenia kamelii, commonly known as the Kamelii eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia kamelii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
