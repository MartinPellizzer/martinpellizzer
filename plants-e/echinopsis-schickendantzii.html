Echinopsis schickendantzii

Echinopsis schickendantzii, commonly known as the Schickendantzii moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and a dense covering of white spines. The plant produces large, showy white flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

This species is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
