Eurya brassii subsp. psilocladus

Eurya brassii subsp.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often a vibrant yellow-green in color. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by red, fleshy berries.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative shrub.
