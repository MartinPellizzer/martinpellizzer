Emilia mbagoi

Emilia mbagoi, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to white. Emilia mbagoi typically grows in open grasslands and is often found in disturbed soils.
