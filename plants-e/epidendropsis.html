Epidendropsis

Epidendropsis, commonly known as the orchid mantis, is a unique plant that exhibits remarkable mimicry of orchid flowers.

Epidendropsis is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it partially depends on its host for nutrients while also photosynthesizing.
