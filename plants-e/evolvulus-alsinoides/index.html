Evolvulus Alsinoides

Evolvulus alsinoides is a small flowering plant known for its delicate blue or violet flowers. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. This annual herb is often grown as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some areas, requiring careful management.
