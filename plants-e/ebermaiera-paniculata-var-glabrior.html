Ebermaiera paniculata var. glabrior

Ebermaiera paniculata var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved evergreen, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth to the touch, giving it its varietal name "glabrior.". The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the wet season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use in ornamental landscaping.

The species is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions.
