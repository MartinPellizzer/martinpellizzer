Eschscholzia californica var. luxurians

Eschscholzia californica var. luxurians, commonly known as the California poppy variant 'Luxurians', is a cultivar of the California poppy, characterized by its dense, lush foliage and vibrant orange flowers.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to California, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. The variety 'Luxurians' is notable for its increased leaf density and more prolific flowering compared to the standard species. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its role in supporting local pollinators and its historical significance in California's flora.
