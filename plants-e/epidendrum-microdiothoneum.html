Epidendrum microdiothoneum

Epidendrum microdiothoneum, commonly known as the miniature yellow epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elliptical and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Epidendrum microdiothoneum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
