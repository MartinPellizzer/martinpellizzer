Enallagma obovata

Enallagma obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate fairy shrimp, is a small crustacean belonging to the order Anostraca.

It is characterized by its elongated, segmented body and a pair of long, feathery antennae. This species is typically found in temporary freshwater habitats such as ponds and vernal pools.
