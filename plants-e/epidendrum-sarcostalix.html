Epidendrum sarcostalix

Epidendrum sarcostalix, commonly known as the pink-stemmed epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are characterized by their vibrant pink to red hues.

Epidendrum sarcostalix is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
