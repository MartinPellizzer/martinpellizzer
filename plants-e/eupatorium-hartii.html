Eupatorium hartii

Eupatorium hartii, commonly known as white boneset, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Eupatorium hartii is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many familiar flowering plants such as daisies and sunflowers.
