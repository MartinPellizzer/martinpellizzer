Elatostema variolaminosum var. lonchophyllum

Elatostema variolaminosum var. lonchophyllum, commonly known as the variable leaf elatostema, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, often variegated leaves. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are usually borne on short pedicels.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
