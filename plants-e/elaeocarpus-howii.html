Elaeocarpus howii

Elaeocarpus howii, commonly known as the Philippine elaeocarp, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and showy white flowers. The tree produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Its growth habit includes a straight trunk and a dense crown, making it a valuable ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
