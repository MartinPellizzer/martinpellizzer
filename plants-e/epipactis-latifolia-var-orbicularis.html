Epipactis latifolia var. orbicularis

Epipactis latifolia var. orbicularis, commonly known as the broadleaf helleborine, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and clusters of greenish-yellow flowers with prominent, showy lips. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces showy, spurred flowers that attract pollinators like bees and wasps.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
