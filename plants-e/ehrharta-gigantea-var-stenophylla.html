Ehrharta gigantea var. stenophylla

Ehrharta gigantea var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to survive in arid or semi-arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a potential candidate for ecological restoration projects.
