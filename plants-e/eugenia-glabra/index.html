Eugenia Glabra

Eugenia glabra is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its fragrant leaves and small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in humid climates and is commonly found in coastal areas and lowland forests. Its leaves are used to make tea and have been valued for their medicinal properties for centuries. This versatile plant plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts.
