Eugenia polypora

Eugenia polypora, commonly known as the purple-leaved eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green to purple foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eugenia polypora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive leaves and subtle floral scent.
