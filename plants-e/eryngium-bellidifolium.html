Eryngium bellidifolium

Eryngium bellidifolium, commonly known as the bellidifolium thistle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, blue-green foliage and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in its native range.

Eryngium bellidifolium is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
