Eriocaulon oliveri

Eriocaulon oliveri, commonly known as Oliver's spike-rush, is a member of the Poales order and the Eriocaulaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet or marshy habitats. The plant features dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a short, thick base.

Eriocaulon oliveri is characterized by its unique morphology and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
