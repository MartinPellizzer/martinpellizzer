Erythrina klainei

Erythrina klainei, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces vibrant red flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its seed pods are long and woody, containing several seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Erythrina klainei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and urban landscapes.
