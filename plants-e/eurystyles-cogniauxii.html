Eurystyles cogniauxii

Eurystyles cogniauxii, commonly known as the African fern tree, is a unique plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the fern family and is characterized by its striking, feathery fronds that resemble those of a fern. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are compound, with multiple narrow, elongated segments that give it a delicate appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or forested areas.
