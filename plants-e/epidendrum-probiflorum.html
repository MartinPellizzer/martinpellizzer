Epidendrum probiflorum

Epidendrum probiflorum, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, brightly colored blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Epidendrum probiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
