Epidendrum cochleatum

Epidendrum cochleatum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Epidendrum cochleatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
