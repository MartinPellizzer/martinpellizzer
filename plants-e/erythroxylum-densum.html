Erythroxylum densum

Erythroxylum densum, commonly known as the dense erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. It produces a milky latex sap, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Erythroxylum.

This species is often found in humid, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological significance.
