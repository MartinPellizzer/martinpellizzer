Encyclia contrerasii

Encyclia contrerasii, commonly known as the Mexican sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Encyclia contrerasii thrives in shaded, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
