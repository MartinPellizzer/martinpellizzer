Eugenia laevis

Eugenia laevis, commonly known as the smooth eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are typically small, round, and edible, often used in local cuisines.

Eugenia laevis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid forest environments.
