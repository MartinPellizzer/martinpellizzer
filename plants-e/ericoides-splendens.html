Ericoides splendens

Ericoides splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. It produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety.

Ericoides splendens thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
