Encyclia atropurpurea var. rosea

Encyclia atropurpurea var. rosea, commonly known as the pink-flowered encyclia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking violet to pinkish-purple flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically dark green and lance-shaped, providing a lush backdrop for its showy blossoms.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
