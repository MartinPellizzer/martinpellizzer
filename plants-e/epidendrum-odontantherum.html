Epidendrum odontantherum

Epidendrum odontantherum, commonly known as the toothed lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive toothed lip. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
