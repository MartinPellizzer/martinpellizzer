Elvira biflora

Elvira biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered elvira, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in cloud forests and is adapted to high humidity and cool temperatures.
