Erythraea ramosissima var. altaica

Erythraea ramosissima var. altaica, commonly known as the Altai erythraea, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branching structure and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers during the summer months. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to cold climates.
