Eragrostis leprieurii

Eragrostis leprieurii, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence on the leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Eragrostis leprieurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and visual interest.
