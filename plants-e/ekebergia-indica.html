Ekebergia indica

Ekebergia indica, commonly known as the Indian flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Asia.

The leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and the tree can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often exfoliating in thin, papery layers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and urban landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
