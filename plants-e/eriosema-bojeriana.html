Eriosema bojeriana

Eriosema bojeriana, commonly known as the Bojer's eriosema, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive floral structure. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it a fuzzy appearance. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or white, with a unique spurred shape on the petals.
