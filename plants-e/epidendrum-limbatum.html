Epidendrum limbatum

Epidendrum limbatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant features strap-like, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum limbatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
