Eria warnementiae

Eria warnementiae, commonly known as the orchid of the rainforests, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often forming a rosette shape to retain moisture.

The flowers, which bloom in clusters, are usually white or pale pink with intricate patterns.
