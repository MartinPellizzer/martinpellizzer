Everardia revoluta

Everardia revoluta, commonly known as the red mangrove, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rhizophoraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive red bark and its ability to thrive in saline and brackish water environments. Everardia revoluta plays an important ecological role in coastal ecosystems by stabilizing shorelines and providing habitat for marine and terrestrial organisms.
