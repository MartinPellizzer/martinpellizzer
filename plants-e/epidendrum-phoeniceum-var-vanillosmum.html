Epidendrum phoeniceum var. vanillosmum

Epidendrum phoeniceum var. vanillosmum, commonly known as the Vanilla orchid, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a epiphytic orchid that thrives in warm, humid environments with high light exposure. The plant produces fragrant, vanilla-scented flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow to orange. It requires well-draining potting media and consistent moisture to flourish.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique fragrance and beauty.
