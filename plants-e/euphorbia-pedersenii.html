Euphorbia pedersenii

Euphorbia pedersenii, commonly known as the Pedersen's spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features a compact, low-growing structure with fleshy, greenish-gray leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

Euphorbia pedersenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
