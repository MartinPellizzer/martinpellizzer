Eriachne muelleri

Eriachne muelleri, commonly known as the slender false brome, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grassy areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native ecosystems in its range.
