Erica blenna var. grandiflora

Erica blenna var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered heath, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the western coast of South Africa.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
