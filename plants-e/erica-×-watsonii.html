Erica × watsonii

Erica × watsonii, commonly known as the Watson's heath, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Erica × watsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
