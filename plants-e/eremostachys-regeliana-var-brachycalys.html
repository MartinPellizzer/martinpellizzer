Eremostachys regeliana var. brachycalys

Eremostachys regeliana var. brachycalys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremostachys, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and grayish-blue foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is well adapted to poor soil conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is occasionally cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscaped landscapes.
