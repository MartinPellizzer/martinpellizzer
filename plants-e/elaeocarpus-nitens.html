Elaeocarpus nitens

Elaeocarpus nitens, commonly known as the slender satinash, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its lanceolate, glossy leaves that are often arranged in whorls. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers followed by distinctive, woody, spherical fruits that are typically covered in a waxy coating. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and unique fruiting structures.

Elaeocarpus nitens is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
