Englerastrum diffusum

Englerastrum diffusum, commonly known as the spreading englerastra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped with serrated edges. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads.

Englerastrum diffusum is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
