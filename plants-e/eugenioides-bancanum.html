Eugenioides bancanum

Eugenioides bancanum, commonly known as the Bankan eugenia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, making it an important species in local ecosystems.
