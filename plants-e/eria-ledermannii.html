Eria ledermannii

Eria ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This herbaceous plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
