Eryngium tenue

Eryngium tenue, commonly known as slender thistle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its blue to purple spiky flower heads and silver-gray foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Eryngium tenue is also known for its medicinal properties, though it is generally not cultivated for commercial use.
