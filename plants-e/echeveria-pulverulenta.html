Echeveria pulverulenta

Echeveria pulverulenta, commonly known as the Dusty Echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
