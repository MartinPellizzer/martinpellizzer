Elaeocarpus taprobanicus

Elaeocarpus taprobanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon elaeocarpus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The tree produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
