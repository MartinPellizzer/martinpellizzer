Euphorbia balbisii

Euphorbia balbisii, commonly known as the Balbis's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with numerous branching stems that bear small, spiny leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathium structures, which are its specialized flower-like inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Euphorbia balbisii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
