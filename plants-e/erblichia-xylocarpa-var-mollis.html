Erblichia xylocarpa var. mollis

Erblichia xylocarpa var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-fruit xylocarp, is a tropical plant characterized by its woody structure and distinctive fruit morphology.

It produces small, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange in color when ripe. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture, contributing to its unique appearance. This plant is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Amazon Basin.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
