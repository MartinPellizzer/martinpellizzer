Euphrasia stricta var. puberula

Euphrasia stricta var. puberula, commonly known as the hairy eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and features small, yellowish-orange flowers with prominent yellow spots. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name.
