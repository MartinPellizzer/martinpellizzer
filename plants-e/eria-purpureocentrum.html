Eria purpureocentrum

Eria purpureocentrum, commonly known as the purple-centered erythrina, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, typical of its family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Eria purpureocentrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
