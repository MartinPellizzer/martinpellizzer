Epicycas miquelii

Epicycas miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant belonging to the cycad family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. Native to the Philippines, this species is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates. Epicycas miquelii is known for its slow growth and long lifespan, often reaching several decades.
