Erythroxylum praecox

Erythroxylum praecox, commonly known as the early redleaf shrub, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive red or orange hue.

The plant is part of the Erythroxylaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
