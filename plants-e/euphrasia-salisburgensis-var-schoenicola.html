Euphrasia salisburgensis var. schoenicola

Euphrasia salisburgensis var. schoenicola, commonly known as the Salzburg eyebright, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
