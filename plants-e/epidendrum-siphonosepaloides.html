Epidendrum siphonosepaloides

Epidendrum siphonosepaloides, commonly known as the Siphonosepal Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing epiphytically on trees or rocks. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air, and it thrives in well-draining potting media.

The flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
