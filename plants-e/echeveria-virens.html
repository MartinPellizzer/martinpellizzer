Echeveria virens

Echeveria virens, commonly known as the Green Chandelier, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with blue or purple. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers on tall stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its color.

Echeveria virens is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
