Epidendrum auropurpureum

Epidendrum auropurpureum, commonly known as the Purple Flame Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet and purple flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and relatively easy care requirements.
