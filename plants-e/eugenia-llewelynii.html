Eugenia llewelynii

Eugenia llewelynii, commonly known as the Llewelyn's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and oval-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
