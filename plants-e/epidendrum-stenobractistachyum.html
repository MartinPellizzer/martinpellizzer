Epidendrum stenobractistachyum

Epidendrum stenobractistachyum, commonly known as the narrow-bract epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, elongated flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with a unique floral structure that includes a narrow, elongated lip. The plant grows as an epiphyte, often found on trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
