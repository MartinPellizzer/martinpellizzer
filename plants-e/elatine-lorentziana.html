Elatine lorentziana

Elatine lorentziana, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the Elatine genus.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish in color.

Elatine lorentziana is often used in ornamental water gardens due to its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
