Elaeocarpus chinensis

Elaeocarpus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain a single seed.

It is native to southern China and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
