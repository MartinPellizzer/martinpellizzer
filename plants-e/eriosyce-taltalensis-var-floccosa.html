Eriosyce taltalensis var. floccosa

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of white or grayish hairs, which helps protect it from intense sunlight and salt spray. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils along the coastline, where it forms compact, low-growing clumps. Its flowers, when they appear, are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, adding a subtle beauty to its otherwise muted appearance.
