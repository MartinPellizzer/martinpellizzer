Eremophila decipiens subsp. linearifolia

Eremophila decipiens subsp.

linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eremophila, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
