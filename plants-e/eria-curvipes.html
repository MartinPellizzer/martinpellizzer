Eria curvipes

Eria curvipes, commonly known as the curly-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often curling stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Eria curvipes thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
