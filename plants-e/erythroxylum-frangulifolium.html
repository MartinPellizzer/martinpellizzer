Erythroxylum frangulifolium

Erythroxylum frangulifolium, commonly known as the red leaf erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking red foliage, which becomes more intense as the leaves mature. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elliptical, greenish-brown capsules containing seeds.

This species is often found in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests.
