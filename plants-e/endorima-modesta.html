Endorima modesta

Endorima modesta, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a spadix and a spathe.
