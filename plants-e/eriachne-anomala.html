Eriachne anomala

Eriachne anomala, commonly known as the slender grassvetch, is a small annual grass native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale green or brown, and it relies on wind pollination.

Eriachne anomala is an important component of the local flora, contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
