Eugenia phillyreifolia f. obscurifolia

Eugenia phillyreifolia f. obscurifolia, commonly known as the dark-leaved eugenia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, red, edible berries. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
