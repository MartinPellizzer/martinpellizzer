Eulophia sinensis

Eulophia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species native to eastern Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces inflorescences with several fragrant, white to pale yellow blossoms. Eulophia sinensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in traditional Chinese horticulture.
