Epidendrum carthagenense

Epidendrum carthagenense, commonly known as the Carthage Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect or pendulous growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive white lip, and bloom during the warmer months.

Epidendrum carthagenense is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
