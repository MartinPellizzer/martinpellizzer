Erysimum insulare var. angustatum

Erysimum insulare var. angustatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or coastal environments. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is well adapted to arid conditions and has a compact growth habit.

This variety is often found in coastal cliffs and sandy soils, where it contributes to the unique flora of its endemic region.
