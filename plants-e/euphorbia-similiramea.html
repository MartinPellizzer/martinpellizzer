Euphorbia similiramea

Euphorbia similiramea, commonly known as the red-stemmed euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive red or purple stem with thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
