Erodium Cicutarium Var. Maculatum

Erodium cicutarium var. maculatum is a small flowering plant known for its distinctive appearance and adaptability. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in urban and rural environments worldwide. The plant features tiny, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple, adding a delicate touch to its surroundings. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture. Despite its beauty, it is considered a weed in many areas due to its rapid spread and tenacity.
