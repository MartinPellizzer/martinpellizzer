Epiphronitis × veitchii

Epiphronitis × veitchii, commonly known as the veitch's lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display a mix of white, pink, and red hues. The plant produces narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Epiphronitis × veitchii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
