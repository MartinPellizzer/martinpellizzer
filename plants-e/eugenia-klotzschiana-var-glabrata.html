Eugenia klotzschiana var. glabrata

Eugenia klotzschiana var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth eugenia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by edible, dark purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
