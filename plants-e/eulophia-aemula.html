Eulophia aemula

Eulophia aemula, commonly known as the slender eulophia, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, often branching stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a spike, which are typical of the Eulophia genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Eulophia aemula is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
