Eperua rubiginosa var. rubiginosa

Eperua rubiginosa var. rubiginosa, commonly known as the red eperua, is a species of tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
