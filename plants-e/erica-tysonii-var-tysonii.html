Erica tysonii var. tysonii

Erica tysonii var. tysonii, commonly known as the Tyson's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related shrubs. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in areas with acidic conditions. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.
