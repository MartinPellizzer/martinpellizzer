Echinophora lamondiana

Echinophora lamondiana, commonly known as the Lamond's thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, spherical seed heads that are adapted for wind dispersal.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
