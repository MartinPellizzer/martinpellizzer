Eremurus korshinskyi

Eremurus korshinskyi, commonly known as the desert candle, is a striking plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, candle-like flower stalks. Eremurus korshinskyi is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil and full sun.
