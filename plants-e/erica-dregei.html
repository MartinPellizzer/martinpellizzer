Erica dregei

Erica dregei, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of pale pink or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Erica dregei is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
