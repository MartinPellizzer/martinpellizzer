Echeveria oreophila

Echeveria oreophila, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada echeveria, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
