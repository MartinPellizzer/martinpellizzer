Epidendrum porphyreum

Epidendrum porphyreum, commonly known as the Purple Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, with flowers that range from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence is often branched, bearing numerous small, fragrant blooms.

Epidendrum porphyreum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
