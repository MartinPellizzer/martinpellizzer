Elaphoglossum lankesteri

Elaphoglossum lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankester's holly fern, is a small, delicate fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
