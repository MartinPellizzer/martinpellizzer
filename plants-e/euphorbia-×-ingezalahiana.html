Euphorbia × ingezalahiana

Euphorbia × ingezalahiana, commonly known as the hybrid spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers surrounded by colorful bracts, which are often mistaken for petals. Its leaves are typically spiny or reduced, contributing to its defensive adaptations.

Euphorbia × ingezalahiana is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
