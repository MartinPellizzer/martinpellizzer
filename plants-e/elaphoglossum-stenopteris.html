Elaphoglossum stenopteris

Elaphoglossum stenopteris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnately compound leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The fronds are usually erect and have a delicate, feathery appearance, with pinnae that are narrowly lanceolate. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests and rocky habitats.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant foliage.
