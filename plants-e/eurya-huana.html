Eurya huana

Eurya huana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Eurya huana thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a valuable addition to horticultural landscapes.
