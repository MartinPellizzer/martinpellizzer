Eustephia coccinea var. multiflora

Eustephia coccinea var. multiflora, commonly known as the red-flowered stephia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with simple, opposite leaves that are oval in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and relatively low maintenance requirements.
