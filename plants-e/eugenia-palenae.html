Eugenia palenae

Eugenia palenae, commonly known as the Palea Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for wildlife.
