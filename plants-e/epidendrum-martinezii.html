Epidendrum martinezii

Epidendrum martinezii, commonly known as the Martinez's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its leaves and flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
