Encyclia Alagoensis

Encyclia alagoensis is a unique orchid species native to the humid forests of Brazil. It is known for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in challenging environments. This plant belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae. Its flowers are often vividly colored, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, Encyclia alagoensis is considered a threatened species requiring conservation efforts.
