Eupatorium crocodilium

Eupatorium crocodilium, commonly known as crocodile plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique appearance.

The flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.
