Erigeron palmeri

Erigeron palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Palmer's fleabane is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
