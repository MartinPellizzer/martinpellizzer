Echinopsis torrefluminensis

Echinopsis torrefluminensis, commonly known as the Torre Fumosa cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny stems and ability to store water efficiently. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces white or pale pink flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
