Euphorbia distinctissima

Euphorbia distinctissima, commonly known as the red-spined euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid environments. It produces a milky sap that can be irritating to the skin.

Euphorbia distinctissima is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
