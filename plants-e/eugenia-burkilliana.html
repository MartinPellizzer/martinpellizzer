Eugenia burkilliana

Eugenia burkilliana, commonly known as the Burkill's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
