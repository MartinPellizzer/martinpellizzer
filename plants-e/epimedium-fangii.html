Epimedium fangii

Epimedium fangii, commonly known as the Chinese barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are clustered in umbels.

Epimedium fangii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to partial shade.
