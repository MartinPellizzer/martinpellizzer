Eruca vesicaria var. longirostris

Eruca vesicaria var. longirostris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wall rocket, is a biennial or annual plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a distinctive peppery aroma. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by elongated, cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found growing along roadsides, in fields, and near walls.

This variety is valued for its culinary use, particularly in salads and as a seasoning.
