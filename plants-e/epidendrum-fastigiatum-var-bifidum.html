Epidendrum fastigiatum var. bifidum

Epidendrum fastigiatum var. bifidum, commonly known as the bifid epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, arranged in racemes along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
