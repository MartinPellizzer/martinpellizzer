Epimedium yinjiangense

Epimedium yinjiangense, commonly known as the Yinjiang barrenwort, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features attractive, shield-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are followed by red berries.

Epimedium yinjiangense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
