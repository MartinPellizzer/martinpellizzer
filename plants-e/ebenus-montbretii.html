Ebenus montbretii

Ebenus montbretii, commonly known as the mountain thyme, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their essential oils. The plant features small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Ebenus montbretii thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
