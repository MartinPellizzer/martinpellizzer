Eria palmifolia var. rotundata

Eria palmifolia var. rotundata, commonly known as the rounded-leaf eria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often rounded at the ends. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
