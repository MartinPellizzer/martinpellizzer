Encephalartos delucanus

It belongs to the cycad family, known for their primitive characteristics and gymnosperm nature. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk with a fibrous, spongy bark. Its leaves are pinnate, with long, narrow, leathery leaflets that are often twisted or curled.
