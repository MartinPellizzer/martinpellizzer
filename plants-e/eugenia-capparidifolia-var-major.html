Eugenia capparidifolia var. major

Eugenia capparidifolia var. major, commonly known as the large-leaved eugenia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
