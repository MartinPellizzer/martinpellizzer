Eragrostis nitida

Eragrostis nitida, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Eragrostis nitida is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in landscaping and restoration projects.
