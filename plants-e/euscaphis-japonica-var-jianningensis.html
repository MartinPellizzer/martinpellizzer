Euscaphis japonica var. jianningensis

Euscaphis japonica var. jianningensis, commonly known as the Jianning boxwood, is a variant of the Japanese boxwood native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its slow growth rate and evergreen foliage make it a popular choice for hedging and topiary.
