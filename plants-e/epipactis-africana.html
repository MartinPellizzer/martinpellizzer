Epipactis africana

Epipactis africana, commonly known as the African helleborine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip petal, typical of many orchid species. Epipactis africana thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
