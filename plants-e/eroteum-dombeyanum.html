Eroteum dombeyanum

Eroteum dombeyanum, commonly known as the Dombey's ericaceous plant, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal floral display.
