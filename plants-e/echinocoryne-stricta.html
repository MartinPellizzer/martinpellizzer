Echinocoryne stricta

Echinocoryne stricta, commonly known as the rigid echinocoryne, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, spiny tubercles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Echinocoryne stricta is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
