Epilobium × brevipilum nothosubsp. nebrodense

Epilobium × brevipilum nothosubsp.

nebrodense, commonly known as the Nebrodensis Willowherb, is a hybrid plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are typically pink or red, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
