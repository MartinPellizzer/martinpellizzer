Espeletia petiolata f. paniculata

Espeletia petiolata f. paniculata, commonly known as the paniculate espeletia, is a striking plant native to the Andean highlands of Colombia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grayish-green leaves that are densely packed at the base. Its most distinctive feature is the tall, branched inflorescence that bears numerous small yellow flowers.

This species is adapted to high-altitude environments, exhibiting succulent traits to conserve water in arid conditions.
