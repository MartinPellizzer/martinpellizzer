Erysimum radicale

Erysimum radicale, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and hairy, with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in rocky or well-drained soils.

Erysimum radicale is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as slopes and gravelly areas.
