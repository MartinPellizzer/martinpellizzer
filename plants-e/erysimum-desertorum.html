Erysimum desertorum

Erysimum desertorum, commonly known as the desertflix, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, adapted to thrive in dry, hot environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and linear, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Erysimum desertorum is often found in desert scrub and creosote bush communities, showcasing its resilience to harsh desert conditions.
