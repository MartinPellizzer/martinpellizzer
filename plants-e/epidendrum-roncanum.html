Epidendrum roncanum

Epidendrum roncanum, commonly known as the Roncanum orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, featuring long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum roncanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced orchid growers.
