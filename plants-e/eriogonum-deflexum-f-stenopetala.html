Eriogonum deflexum f. stenopetala

Eriogonum deflexum f. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Polygonaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, white to pale pink flowers.
