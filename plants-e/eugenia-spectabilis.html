Eugenia spectabilis

Eugenia spectabilis, commonly known as the beautiful eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
