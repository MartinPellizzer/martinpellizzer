Euphorbia cactus

Euphorbia cactus, commonly known as the spurge or milkweed plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent stems and often has a spiny or thorny appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green. Euphorbias are native to various regions, including Africa, the Americas, and Asia.

They are adapted to arid environments and store water in their thick, fleshy stems.
