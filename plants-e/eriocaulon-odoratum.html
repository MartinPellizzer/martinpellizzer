Eriocaulon odoratum

Eriocaulon odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and marshy areas.

The species is noted for its subtle, aromatic scent, which is most noticeable when the plant is in bloom.
