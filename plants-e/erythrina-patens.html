Erythrina patens

Erythrina patens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a popular ornamental species. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.

Erythrina patens is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting local wildlife.
