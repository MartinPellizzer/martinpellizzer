Eugenioides tetrandrum

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
