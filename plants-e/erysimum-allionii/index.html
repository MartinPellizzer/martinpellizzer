Erysimum Allionii

Erysimum allionii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its vibrant yellow blooms and its unique ecological niche. This species thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy yet beautiful addition to specialized gardens. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats of extinction, prompting conservation efforts. Its beauty and scarcity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
