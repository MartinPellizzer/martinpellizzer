Erigeron thunbergii f. kirigishiensis

Erigeron thunbergii f. kirigishiensis, commonly known as the Japanese fleabane, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its foliage is generally hairy and has a slightly rough texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
