Epidendrum stamfordianum var. parviflorum

Epidendrum stamfordianum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a compact epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features narrow, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a desirable addition to indoor gardens and terrariums.
