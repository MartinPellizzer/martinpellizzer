Erica Globulifera

Erica globulifera is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. Known for its compact growth and vibrant foliage, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. This plant is prized for its ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions and its year-round aesthetic appeal. Its small, clustered flowers add a touch of elegance to any garden setting. Erica globulifera is an excellent choice for rock gardens, coastal landscapes, and as a hardy ornamental plant.
