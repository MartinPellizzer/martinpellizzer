Eremurus soogdianus

Eremurus soogdianus, commonly known as the Soogdian sago palm, is a striking plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like flower stalks that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

Eremurus soogdianus is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
