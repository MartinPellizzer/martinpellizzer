Eriosyce multicolorispina

Eriosyce multicolorispina, commonly known as the multicolored spiny ereiosyce, is a species of cactus native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and dense, spiny areoles that bear clusters of white or yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain seeds. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by cactus enthusiasts.
