Epidendrum macrocyphum

Epidendrum macrocyphum, commonly known as the giant wax plant, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy leaves and showy, long-lasting flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in length. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil. It is an epiphytic species, often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Epidendrum macrocyphum is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
