Eugenia demeusei f. lukolelaensis

Eugenia demeusei f. lukolelaensis, commonly known as the Lukolela eugenia, is a rare botanical variant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, rounded berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical rainforests.
