Eragrostis dolichostachya

Eragrostis dolichostachya, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, spike-like flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and savanna habitats.

Eragrostis dolichostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
