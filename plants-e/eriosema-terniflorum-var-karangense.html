Eriosema terniflorum var. karangense

Eriosema terniflorum var. karangense, commonly known as the Karangense False Broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often covered with a fine, velvety pubescence.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
