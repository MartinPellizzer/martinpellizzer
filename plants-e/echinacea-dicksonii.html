Echinacea dicksonii

Echinacea dicksonii, commonly known as the narrowleaf coneflower, is a perennial herb native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with purple petals and a prominent yellow central cone. Echinacea dicksonii is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional use in herbal remedies.
