Erymophyllum compactum

Erymophyllum compactum, commonly known as the compact thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features a dense, spiny stem with narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers appear in compact clusters, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise rugged appearance.

Erymophyllum compactum is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy species in challenging climates.
