Elatine triandra f. terrestris

Elatine triandra f. terrestris, commonly known as the creeping waterwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous species that typically grows in moist or aquatic environments. The plant features prostrate stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the surface of wet soil or water. Its small, green leaves are opposite and often covered with fine hairs.

It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.
