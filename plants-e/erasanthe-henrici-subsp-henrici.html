Erasanthe henrici subsp. henrici

Erasanthe henrici subsp.

henrici, commonly known as the Henrici's erasanthe, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its upright stem and clusters of small, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.
