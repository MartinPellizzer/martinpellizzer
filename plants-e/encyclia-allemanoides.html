Encyclia allemanoides

Encyclia allemanoides, commonly known as the Alleman's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that grows on trees and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, often featuring a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Encyclia allemanoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
