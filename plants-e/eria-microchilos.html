Eria microchilos

Eria microchilos, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, wiry stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, delicate, and usually white or pale yellow in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its compact size and ornamental appeal.
