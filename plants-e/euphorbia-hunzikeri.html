Euphorbia hunzikeri

Euphorbia hunzikeri, commonly known as the Hunziker's spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a unique cup-like structure called a cyathium. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stem for survival.

Euphorbia hunzikeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
