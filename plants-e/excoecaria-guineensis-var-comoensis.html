Excoecaria guineensis var. comoensis

Excoecaria guineensis var. comoensis, commonly known as the African rubber plant, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its latex sap contains toxic compounds and is used in traditional medicine.
