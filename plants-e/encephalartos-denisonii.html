Encephalartos denisonii

Encephalartos denisonii, commonly known as the Denison's cycad, is a species of cycad native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and its feather-like, compound leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces cones, with the male cones being cylindrical and the female cones being more rounded and woody. Encephalartos denisonii is a slow-growing, long-lived plant that thrives in well-drained, acidic soil.

It is also noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and conservatories.
