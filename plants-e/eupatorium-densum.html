Eupatorium densum

Eupatorium densum, commonly known as blackweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist habitats such as wetlands, stream banks, and marshes. It has a fibrous root system and can form dense stands, often dominating its environment.

Eupatorium densum is known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
