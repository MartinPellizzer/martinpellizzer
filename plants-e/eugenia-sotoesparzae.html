Eugenia sotoesparzae

Eugenia sotoesparzae, commonly known as the Soto Esparza's evergreen guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and dark blue to black berries. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
