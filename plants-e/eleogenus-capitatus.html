Eleogenus capitatus

Eleogenus capitatus, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant produces small, green, berry-like fruits that contain the seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
