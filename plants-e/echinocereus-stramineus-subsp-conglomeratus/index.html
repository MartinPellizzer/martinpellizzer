Echinocereus Stramineus Subsp. Conglomeratus

Echinocereus stramineus subsp. conglomeratus is a striking cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and dense clusters of spines, it thrives in arid environments with well-drained soil. This subspecies is prized by desert gardeners for its resilience and aesthetic appeal. It requires minimal water and full sun to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and succulent collections.
