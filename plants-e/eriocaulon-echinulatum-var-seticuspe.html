Eriocaulon echinulatum var. seticuspe

Eriocaulon echinulatum var. seticuspe, commonly known as the woolly spike sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental qualities in aquatic and bog gardens.
