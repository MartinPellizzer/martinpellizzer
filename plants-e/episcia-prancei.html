Episcia prancei

Episcia prancei, commonly known as the Prance's waxplant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to violet. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Episcia prancei is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
