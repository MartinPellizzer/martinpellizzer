Eugenia biflora var. ludibunda

Eugenia biflora var. ludibunda, commonly known as the laughing eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its fragrant, small pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, and it produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
