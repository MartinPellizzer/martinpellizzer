Elatostema hunanense

Elatostema hunanense, commonly known as the Hunan stellaria, is a member of the Urticaceae family and is native to China.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and delicate, star-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically oval to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and the flowers are usually white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
