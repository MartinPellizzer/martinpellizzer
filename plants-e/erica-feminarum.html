Erica feminarum

Erica feminarum, commonly known as the ladies' heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Erica feminarum thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
