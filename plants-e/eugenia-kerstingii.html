Eugenia kerstingii

Eugenia kerstingii, commonly known as the Kersting's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and unique characteristics, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
