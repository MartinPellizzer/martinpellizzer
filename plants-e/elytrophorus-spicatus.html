Elytrophorus spicatus

Elytrophorus spicatus, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescence and vibrant, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Elytrophorus spicatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
