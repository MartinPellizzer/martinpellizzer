Eranthemum erectum

Eranthemum erectum, commonly known as the upright eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Eranthemum erectum thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight for optimal growth.
