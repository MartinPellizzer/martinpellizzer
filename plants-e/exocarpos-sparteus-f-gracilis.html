Exocarpos sparteus f. gracilis

Exocarpos sparteus f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender holly.

It is a shrub native to Australia, characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and red or orange berries. The plant belongs to the family Rutaceae and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a container plant.
