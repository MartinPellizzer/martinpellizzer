Eragrostis cambessediana

Eragrostis cambessediana, commonly known as the Cambessediana grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
