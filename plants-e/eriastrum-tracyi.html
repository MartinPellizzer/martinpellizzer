Eriastrum tracyi

Eriastrum tracyi, commonly known as Tracy's eriocephalus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing annual herb with hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish to reddish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal sage scrub habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
