Eragrostis nightingaleae

Eragrostis nightingaleae, commonly known as the Nightingale grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, particularly in Namibia and South Africa. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Eragrostis nightingaleae is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
