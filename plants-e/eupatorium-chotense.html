Eupatorium chotense

Eupatorium chotense, commonly known as the Chotense thoroughwort, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The showy purple to pink flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the summer months.
