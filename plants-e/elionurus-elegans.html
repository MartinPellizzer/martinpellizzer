Elionurus elegans

Elionurus elegans, commonly known as the slender grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Elionurus elegans is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
