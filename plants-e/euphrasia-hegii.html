Euphrasia hegii

Euphrasia hegii, commonly known as the hairy eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. The plant features small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
