Epidendrum ramosum var. lineare

Epidendrum ramosum var. lineare, commonly known as the linear epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence is racemose, with numerous small flowers arranged along a central stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
