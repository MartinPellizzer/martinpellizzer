Echeveria nuda

Echeveria nuda, commonly known as the naked echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a powdery coating. The plant produces showy clusters of pinkish-purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
