Epilobium kiusianum

Epilobium kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusian willow herb, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, often in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its showy, pink to purple flowers appear in dense clusters during the summer months.

Epilobium kiusianum is part of the Onagraceae family, which includes other willow herbs and flowering plants adapted to harsh, high-altitude conditions.
