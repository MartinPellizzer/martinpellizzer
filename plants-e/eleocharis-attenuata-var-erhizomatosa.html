Eleocharis attenuata var. erhizomatosa

Eleocharis attenuata var. erhizomatosa, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, thriving in temperate climates. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense colonies.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
