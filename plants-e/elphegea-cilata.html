Elphegea cilata

Elphegea cilata, commonly known as the slender leaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Elphegea cilata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
