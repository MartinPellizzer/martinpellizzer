Eriocaulon parkeri

Eriocaulon parkeri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulbous grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense spikes. It is typically found in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

Eriocaulon parkeri is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and adaptability to wet conditions.
