Elaphoglossum madagascariense

Elaphoglossum madagascariense, commonly known as the Madagascar holly fern, is a species of fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it thrives in the understory. This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
