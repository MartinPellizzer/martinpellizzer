Echinopsis nealeana

Echinopsis nealeana, commonly known as the Neale's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its globular shape and spiny exterior. The plant produces vibrant flowers, typically in shades of yellow or orange, during the warmer months. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance requirements and striking appearance.

Echinopsis nealeana thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert-like environments.
