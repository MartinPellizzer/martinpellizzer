Epidendrum peraltense

Epidendrum peraltense, commonly known as the Peralta's Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Epidendrum peraltense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
