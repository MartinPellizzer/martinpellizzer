Erica onosmiflora

Erica onosmiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many shrubs and evergreen plants adapted to acidic soils. The plant is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pale pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Erica onosmiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
