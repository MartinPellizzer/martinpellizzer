Erysimum dyplopodum

Erysimum dyplopodum, commonly known as the narrowleaf wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are bright yellow and appear in clusters, blooming from spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
