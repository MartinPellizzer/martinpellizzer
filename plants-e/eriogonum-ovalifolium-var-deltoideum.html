Eriogonum ovalifolium var. deltoideum

Eriogonum ovalifolium var. deltoideum, commonly known as the delta buckwheat, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its deltoid-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and sagebrush steppe. It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, known for its ability to thrive in arid environments.
