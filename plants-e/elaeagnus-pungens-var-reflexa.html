Elaeagnus pungens var. reflexa

Elaeagnus pungens var. reflexa, commonly known as silverleaf buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves that are reflexed, or curled downward, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, followed by dark red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its hardy nature makes it adaptable to a variety of environmental conditions.
