Euodia robusta

Euodia robusta, commonly known as the Chinese frankincense tree, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is native to southern China, Vietnam, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

Euodia robusta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual and olfactory characteristics.
