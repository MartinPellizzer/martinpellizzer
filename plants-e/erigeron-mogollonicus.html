Erigeron mogollonicus

Erigeron mogollonicus, commonly known as the Mogollon blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
