Echinopsis gemmata var. schelhasii

Echinopsis gemmata var. schelhasii, commonly known as the Schelhasii moon cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to South America.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem with a distinctive red or orange coloration that intensifies under strong sunlight. The plant produces small, white flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low water requirements.

Its unique coloration and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.
