Erythroxylum suave var. compactum

Erythroxylum suave var. compactum, commonly known as the compact velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Erythroxylaceae and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub with upright stems and elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive appearance in gardens.

The species is also of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphological features and ecological adaptations.
