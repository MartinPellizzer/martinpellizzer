Euadenia major

Euadenia major, commonly known as the large-flowered euadenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems that are covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough texture.

The showy yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late winter to early spring.
