Eriosema volubile f. densiore

Eriosema volubile f. densiore, commonly known as the dense hairy morning glory, is a vigorous climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is characterized by its dense pubescence, which gives it its specific epithet "densiore.". It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening for its rapid growth and ornate blooms.

The flowers are typically borne in clusters and bloom throughout the growing season.
