Epidendrum belvederense var. brevifolium

Epidendrum belvederense var. brevifolium, commonly known as the Short-leaf Epidendrum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a waxy surface. This epiphytic orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining substrates.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
