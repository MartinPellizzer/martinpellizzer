Eria larutensis

Eria larutensis, commonly known as the Larut orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Malaysia and Indonesia. This terrestrial orchid typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color.

Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.
