Epidendrum rectopedunculatum f. denticulatum

Epidendrum rectopedunculatum f. denticulatum, commonly known as the denticulate epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudobulbs. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its foliage consists of elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This epiphytic species thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
