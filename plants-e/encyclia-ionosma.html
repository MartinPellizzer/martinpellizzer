Encyclia ionosma

Encyclia ionosma, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant has a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth during dry periods. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Encyclia ionosma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
