Eulophia schimperiana

Eulophia schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots and leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often more brightly colored.

Eulophia schimperiana is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and is often found in association with other epiphytic and terrestrial orchids in its habitat.
