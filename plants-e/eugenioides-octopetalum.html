Eugenioides octopetalum

Eugenioides octopetalum, commonly known as the eight-petaled eugenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its growth habit includes a compact form with dense foliage, making it suitable for ornamental purposes.
