Euphorbia kazerouni var. stricta

Euphorbia kazerouni var. stricta, commonly known as the Kazeroun euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to Iran.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique floral structures. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in xeriscaping and rock gardens.
