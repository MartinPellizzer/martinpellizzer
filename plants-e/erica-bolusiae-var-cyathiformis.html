Erica bolusiae var. cyathiformis

Erica bolusiae var. cyathiformis, commonly known as the Bolus's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
