Epidendrum amphistomum f. rubrifolium

Epidendrum amphistomum f. rubrifolium, commonly known as the red-leafed epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, dark green to red-brown leaves, which are often adorned with intricate veining patterns. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking foliage and delicate blooms.
