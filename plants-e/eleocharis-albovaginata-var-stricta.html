Eleocharis albovaginata var. stricta

Eleocharis albovaginata var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, white or pale-colored inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and often have a whitish or pale sheath, giving it its common name.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
