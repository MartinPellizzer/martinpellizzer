Epiphyllum phyllanthus var. schnetteri

Epiphyllum phyllanthus var. schnetteri, commonly known as the Schnetter's Christmas cactus, is a hybrid epiphytic cactus native to Brazil.

It features striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has flat, leaf-like stems that are segmented and can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and seasonal blooming.
