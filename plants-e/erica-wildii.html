Erica wildii

Erica wildii, commonly known as the Wild's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer. Erica wildii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
