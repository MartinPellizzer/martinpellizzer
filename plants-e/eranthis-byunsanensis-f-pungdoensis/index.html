Eranthis Byunsanensis F. Pungdoensis

Eranthis byunsanensis f. pungdoensis is a rare and captivating plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. This frost-tolerant species is known for its early blooming habit, often emerging in late winter or early spring. Its delicate yellow flowers are adorned with intricate patterns that make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a unique addition to temperate gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for conservation efforts and botanical study.
