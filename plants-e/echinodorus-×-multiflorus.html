Echinodorus × multiflorus

Echinodorus × multiflorus, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often cultivated in ponds and aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate water.

Echinodorus × multiflorus also plays a role in providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
