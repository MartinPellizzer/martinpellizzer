Elaphoglossum ellipsoideum

Elaphoglossum ellipsoideum, commonly known as the elliptic holly fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile pinnae that produce spores on the underside. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shady garden settings.
