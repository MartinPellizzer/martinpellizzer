Euonymus acanthocarpus var. lushanensis

Euonymus acanthocarpus var. lushanensis, commonly known as the Lushan euonymus, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The variety is noted for its adaptability and hardiness in temperate climates.
