Ethulia alata

Ethulia alata, commonly known as the winged fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that have a wing-like appearance on the underside. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are typically long and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Ethulia alata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
