Elatostema acrophilum

Elatostema acrophilum, commonly known as the mountain elatostema, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a distinctive waxy coating. Its flowers are tiny and clustered in dense inflorescences, often greenish-white in color.

Elatostema acrophilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shady garden settings.
