Eulophia coleae

Eulophia coleae, commonly known as the Colean Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia coleae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
