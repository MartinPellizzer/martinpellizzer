Ericoides trichroma

Ericoides trichroma, commonly known as the rainbow heath, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored flowers that range from violet to white and yellow. The plant is native to the wetlands and bogs of southern Africa, where it thrives in acidic, nutrient-poor soils. Its foliage consists of small, leathery leaves that are typically green, with a waxy surface to reduce water loss.

Ericoides trichroma is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and vibrant floral display.
