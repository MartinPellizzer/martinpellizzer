Elaphoglossum schiedei

Elaphoglossum schiedei, commonly known as the Schied's holly fern, is a small, evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial to full shade. This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
