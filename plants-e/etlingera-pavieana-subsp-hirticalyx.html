Etlingera pavieana subsp. hirticalyx

Etlingera pavieana subsp.

hirticalyx, commonly known as the hairy-leaf torch ginger, is a species of flowering plant in the Zingiberaceae family. It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines.
