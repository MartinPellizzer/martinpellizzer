Ericoides Lycopodioides

Ericoides lycopodioides is a rare and ancient plant species that belongs to the family Lycopodiaceae. It is commonly found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. This plant is known for its small, scale-like leaves and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils. Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an important subject for botanical study. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a delicate indicator of environmental health and biodiversity.
