Eupatorium conyzoides var. foliatum

Eupatorium conyzoides var. foliatum, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, blue to purple flowers that appear in clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and marshes. It has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
