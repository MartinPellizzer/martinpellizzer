Erianthera echioides

Erianthera echioides, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically green and glossy. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Erianthera echioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
