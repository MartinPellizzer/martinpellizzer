Eusteralis yatabeana

Eusteralis yatabeana, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

Eusteralis yatabeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
