Epilobium tetragonum nothosubsp.

semiadnatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved willow herb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its slender, quadrangular stems and lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface.
