Euphrasia regelii subsp. regelii

Euphrasia regelii subsp.

regelii, commonly known as Regel's eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It features erect, branched stems covered in fine, hairy texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Euphrasia regelii subsp.

regelii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
