Eranthemum acanthodes

Eranthemum acanthodes, commonly known as the rough eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and serrated edges. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
