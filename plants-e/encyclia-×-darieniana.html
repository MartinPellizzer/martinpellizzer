Encyclia × darieniana

Encyclia × darieniana, commonly known as the Darien orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to orange-red blooms with intricate markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Encyclia × darieniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
