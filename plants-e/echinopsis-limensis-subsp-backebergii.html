Echinopsis limensis subsp. backebergii

Echinopsis limensis subsp.

backebergii, commonly known as the Backeberg echinopsis, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa. It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white, woolly spines and produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem. It blooms during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
