Emilia sonchifolia var. sonchifolia

Emilia sonchifolia var. sonchifolia, commonly known as common emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets, typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
