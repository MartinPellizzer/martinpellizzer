Erinus

Erinus, commonly known as frostweed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Polemoniaceae.

These plants are native to the western United States and parts of Mexico, where they thrive in rocky and dry environments. They are often used in xeriscaping due to their drought tolerance and ornamental value.
