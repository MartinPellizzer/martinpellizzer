Eugenia koikokoensis

Eugenia koikokoensis, commonly known as the Kauai hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Eugenia koikokoensis is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of Hawaii and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
