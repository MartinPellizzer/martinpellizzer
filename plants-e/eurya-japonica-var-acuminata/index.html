Eurya Japonica Var. Acuminata

Eurya japonica var. acuminata is a unique evergreen shrub known for its vibrant foliage and ornamental appeal. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in forested areas. This variety is distinguished by its elongated, pointed leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It is prized in horticulture for its ability to add color and texture to gardens. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both landscape design and indoor cultivation.
