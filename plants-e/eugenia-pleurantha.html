Eugenia pleurantha

Eugenia pleurantha, commonly known as the white-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface.

Eugenia pleurantha is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic properties.
