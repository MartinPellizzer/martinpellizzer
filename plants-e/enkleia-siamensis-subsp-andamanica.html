Enkleia siamensis subsp. andamanica

Enkleia siamensis subsp.

andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman rock orchid, is a rare orchid species native to the Andaman Islands. It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and typically produces a single, slender stem.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
