Ehretia pyrifolia

Ehretia pyrifolia, commonly known as the flame tree or Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in spring, forming clusters that resemble flames. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical with a smooth margin. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain three winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
