Encephalartos villosus f. intermedius

Encephalartos villosus f. intermedius, commonly known as the Intermediate Natal Cycad, is a rare and unique variant of the Natal cycad.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves that are typically a deep green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that are essential for its reproduction. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a humid, subtropical climate.

Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered a conservation concern.
