Episcia melittifolia

Episcia melittifolia, commonly known as the honeybee plant, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow throat. Episcia melittifolia is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
