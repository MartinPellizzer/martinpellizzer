Eranthemum palatiferum var. elatum

Eranthemum palatiferum var. elatum, commonly known as the tall eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
