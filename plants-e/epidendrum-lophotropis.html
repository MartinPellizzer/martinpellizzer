Epidendrum lophotropis

Epidendrum lophotropis, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Epidendrum lophotropis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
