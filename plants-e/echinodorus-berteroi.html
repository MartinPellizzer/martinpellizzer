Echinodorus berteroi

Echinodorus berteroi, commonly known as the Amazon sword plant, is a popular aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. This plant thrives in freshwater environments and prefers moderate to high light conditions. It is often used in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality.

Echinodorus berteroi is also known for its hardy nature and ease of care, making it a favorite among aquarists.
