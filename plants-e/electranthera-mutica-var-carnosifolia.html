Electranthera mutica var. carnosifolia

Electranthera mutica var. carnosifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a tropical plant native to South America, characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The leaves are typically dark green with a prominent midrib and can grow up to 20 centimeters in length. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its unique foliage and ease of care.
