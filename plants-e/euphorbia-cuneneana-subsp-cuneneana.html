Euphorbia cuneneana subsp. cuneneana

Euphorbia cuneneana subsp.

cuneneana, commonly known as the Cunene spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a short, thick stem with prominent, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a crown-like structure, which are surrounded by modified leaves called bracts. It is well adapted to arid environments and can store water in its stems and leaves.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
