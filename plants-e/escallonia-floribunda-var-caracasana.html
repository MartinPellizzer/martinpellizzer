Escallonia floribunda var. caracasana

Escallonia floribunda var. caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas Escallonia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features glossy dark green leaves that are oval in shape and emit a mild aromatic scent. The plant produces clusters of bright red, bell-shaped flowers in the spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements in gardens.
