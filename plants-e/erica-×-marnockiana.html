Erica × marnockiana

Erica × marnockiana, commonly known as the Marnock's heath, is a hybrid shrub native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and related plants. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of small, leathery leaves that are usually evergreen, and it produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple.

Erica × marnockiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
