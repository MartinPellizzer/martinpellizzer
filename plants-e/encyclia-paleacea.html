Encyclia paleacea

Encyclia paleacea, commonly known as the pale pink orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, pale pink to white flowers with a distinct lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it has a short, rhizomatous stem.

Encyclia paleacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
