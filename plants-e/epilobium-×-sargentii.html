Epilobium × sargentii

Epilobium × sargentii, commonly known as Sargent's willow herb, is a hybrid species within the Onagraceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, often branched stems and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and covered with fine hairs.

Epilobium × sargentii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
