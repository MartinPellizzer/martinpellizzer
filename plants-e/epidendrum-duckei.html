Epidendrum duckei

Epidendrum duckei, commonly known as the Ducke's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically has long, slender leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Epidendrum duckei thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
