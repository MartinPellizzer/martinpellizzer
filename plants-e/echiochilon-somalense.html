Echiochilon somalense

Echiochilon somalense, commonly known as the Somali thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It has a rosette growth form, with dense foliage that helps reduce water loss.

Echiochilon somalense is also noted for its medicinal properties, used traditionally in local remedies.
