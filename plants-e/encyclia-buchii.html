Encyclia buchii

Encyclia buchii, commonly known as the Buchi's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its leaves and flowering stems, which are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are usually white or pale green with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the rainy season.

Encyclia buchii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
