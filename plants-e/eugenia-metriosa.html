Eugenia metriosa

Eugenia metriosa, commonly known as the broadleaf eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Eugenia metriosa thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
