Echinopsis sucrensis

Echinopsis sucrensis, commonly known as the Sucrensis Cactus, is a species native to the high Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Echinopsis sucrensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
