Epidendrum spectabile

Epidendrum spectabile, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, brightly colored flowers, often in shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native arid environments.

Epidendrum spectabile is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
