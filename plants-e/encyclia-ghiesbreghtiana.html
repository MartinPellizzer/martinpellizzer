Encyclia ghiesbreghtiana

Encyclia ghiesbreghtiana, commonly known as the Ghiesbreght's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces a raceme of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several regions.
