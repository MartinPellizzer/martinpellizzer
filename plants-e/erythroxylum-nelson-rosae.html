Erythroxylum nelson-rosae

Erythroxylum nelson-rosae, commonly known as Nelson-Rosa's erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, followed by elliptical or oval-shaped fruits. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
