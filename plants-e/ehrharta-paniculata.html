Ehrharta paniculata

Ehrharta paniculata, commonly known as silky grass or silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
