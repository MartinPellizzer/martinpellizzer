Eriocaulon lacustre

Eriocaulon lacustre, commonly known as the lake spikerush, is a small aquatic plant native to wetlands and marshy areas.

It typically grows in shallow water, forming dense stands that help stabilize sediment. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of white or pale flowers. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

Eriocaulon lacustre plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
