Epimedium leptorrhizum

Epimedium leptorrhizum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, deciduous leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in panicles.

Epimedium leptorrhizum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to shaded garden environments.
