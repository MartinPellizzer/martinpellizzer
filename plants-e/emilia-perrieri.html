Emilia perrieri

Emilia perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's emilia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a central disc, typical of the family, and has hairy, narrow leaves. Emilia perrieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
