Echium mentagense

Echium mentagense, commonly known as the Mentagene thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant features spiny leaves and produces striking violet or purple flowers. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
