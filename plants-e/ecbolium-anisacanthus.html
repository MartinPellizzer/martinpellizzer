Ecbolium anisacanthus

Ecbolium anisacanthus, commonly known as the desert honeysuckle, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. The plant has woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to arid environments. It produces small, red fruits that contain seeds dispersed by animals.
