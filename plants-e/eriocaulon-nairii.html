Eriocaulon nairii

Eriocaulon nairii, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Eriocaulaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences.
