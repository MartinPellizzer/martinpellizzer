Eulophia massiei

Eulophia massiei, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.

Eulophia massiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
