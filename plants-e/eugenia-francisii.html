Eugenia francisii

Eugenia francisii, commonly known as the Franklin's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, providing dense foliage that offers habitat for native wildlife.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
