Epilobium leiospermum

Epilobium leiospermum, commonly known as silky willow herb, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Epilobium leiospermum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
