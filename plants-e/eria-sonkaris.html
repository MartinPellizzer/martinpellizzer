Eria sonkaris

Eria sonkaris, commonly known as the Sonkari orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a subtle fragrance.

Eria sonkaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
