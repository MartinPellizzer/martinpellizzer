Eritrichium nipponicum

Eritrichium nipponicum, commonly known as the Japanese mouse-ear cress, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks.
