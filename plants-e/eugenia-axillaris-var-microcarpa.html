Eugenia axillaris var. microcarpa

Eugenia axillaris var. microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited eugenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
