Elaeocarpus mindanaensis

Elaeocarpus mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao elaeocarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Mindanao. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 15 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.
