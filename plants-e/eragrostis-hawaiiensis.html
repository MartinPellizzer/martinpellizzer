Eragrostis hawaiiensis

Eragrostis hawaiiensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian panicgrass, is a native grass species of the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and upland habitats. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Eragrostis hawaiiensis plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife in its endemic ecosystem.
