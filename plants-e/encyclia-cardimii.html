Encyclia cardimii

Encyclia cardimii, commonly known as the Cardimii Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Encyclia cardimii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
