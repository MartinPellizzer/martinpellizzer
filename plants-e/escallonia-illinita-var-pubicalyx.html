Escallonia illinita var. pubicalyx

Escallonia illinita var. pubicalyx, commonly known as the pubicalyx escallonia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a slightly hairy texture on the underside of its leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden borders or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
