Elaeocarpus ovalis

Elaeocarpus ovalis, commonly known as the oval flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by distinctive orange or red fruits. The fruits are woody, indehiscent capsules that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in traditional medicine for its various properties.
