Ehretia pingbianensis

Ehretia pingbianensis, commonly known as the Pingbian ehretia, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to grow in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
