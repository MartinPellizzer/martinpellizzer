Eria stenobulba

Eria stenobulba, commonly known as the narrow-bulb orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of a short, fleshy rhizome. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Eria stenobulba is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.
