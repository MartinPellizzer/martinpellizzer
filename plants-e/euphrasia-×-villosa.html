Euphrasia × villosa

Euphrasia × villosa, commonly known as the hairy eyebright, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its hairy stems and leaves are distinctive features, contributing to its common name.

Euphrasia × villosa is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported healing properties.
