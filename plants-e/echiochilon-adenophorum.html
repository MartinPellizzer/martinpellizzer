Echiochilon adenophorum

Echiochilon adenophorum, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to livestock and humans if ingested. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.

Due to its invasive nature, it is considered a weed in many regions.
