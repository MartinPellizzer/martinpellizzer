Emilia sinica

Emilia sinica, commonly known as Chinese emilia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and hairy leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with prominent, showy, violet-blue petals. Emilia sinica is often grown as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, colorful addition to their landscape.
