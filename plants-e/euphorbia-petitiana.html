Euphorbia petitiana

Euphorbia petitiana, commonly known as the miniature spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and small, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces yellowish-green cyathia, which are specialized inflorescences typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
