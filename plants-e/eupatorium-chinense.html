Eupatorium chinense

Eupatorium chinense, commonly known as Chinese boneset, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and marshes. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Eupatorium chinense is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
