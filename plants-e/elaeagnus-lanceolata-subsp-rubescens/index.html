Elaeagnus Lanceolata Subsp. Rubescens

Elaeagnus lanceolata subsp. rubescens is a unique shrub native to eastern Asia known for its striking red foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This subspecies is distinguished by its lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its hardy nature allows it to tolerate a range of environmental conditions. Despite its beauty, it requires minimal maintenance once established.
