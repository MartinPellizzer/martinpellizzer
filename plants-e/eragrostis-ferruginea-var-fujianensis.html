Eragrostis ferruginea var. fujianensis

Eragrostis ferruginea var. fujianensis, commonly known as the Fujian browntop grass, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a reddish-brown hue. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
