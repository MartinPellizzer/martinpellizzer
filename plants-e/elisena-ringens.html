Elisena ringens

Elisena ringens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink with intricate patterns.

Elisena ringens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
