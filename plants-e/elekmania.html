Elekmania

Elekmania, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa, characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Elekmania species are known for their intricate floral structures and vibrant colors, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and orange hues.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, several species are now threatened with extinction.
