Encyclia × verboonenii

Encyclia × verboonenii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a natural hybrid between Encyclia cochleata and Encyclia fraterna.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Jamaica and the Greater Antilles. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow with red or orange markings, arranged in a raceme.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
