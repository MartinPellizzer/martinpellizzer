Eulophia stenantha

Eulophia stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved eulophia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green with a contrasting lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eulophia stenantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
