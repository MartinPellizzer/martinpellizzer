Epimedium pumilum

Epimedium pumilum, commonly known as barrenwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Epimedium pumilum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and hardiness.
