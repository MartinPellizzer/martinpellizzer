Elaeocarpus thouarsii

Elaeocarpus thouarsii, commonly known as the New Caledonian elaeocarp, is a tropical tree native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

Its ecological significance is notable in the unique ecosystems of New Caledonia, where it contributes to biodiversity and habitat structure.
