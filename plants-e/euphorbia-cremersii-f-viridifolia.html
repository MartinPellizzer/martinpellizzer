Euphorbia cremersii f. viridifolia

Euphorbia cremersii f. viridifolia, commonly known as the green-leaved spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green, giving it its distinguishing appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique floral structures. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and collections.
