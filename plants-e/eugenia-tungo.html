Eugenia tungo

Eugenia tungo, commonly known as Tungó or Tungó tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Peru, and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and glossy, and its clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.

It is often used in traditional medicine and for its aromatic properties.
