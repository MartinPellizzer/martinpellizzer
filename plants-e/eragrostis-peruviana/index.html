Eragrostis Peruviana

Eragrostis peruviana is a unique grass species native to the Andean regions of South America. It is known for its distinctive silver-blue foliage and compact growth habit. This plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh conditions. Its ornamental qualities have made it popular among gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
