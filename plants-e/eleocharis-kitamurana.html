Eleocharis kitamurana

Eleocharis kitamurana, commonly known as the Kitamura spike-rush, is a species of sedge native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical spikes that bear small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture.

Eleocharis kitamurana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
