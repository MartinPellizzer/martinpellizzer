Eriochloa sericea

Eriochloa sericea, commonly known as silky bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-blue foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically pink to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Eriochloa sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in ecological restoration projects.
