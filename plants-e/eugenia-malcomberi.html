Eugenia malcomberi

Eugenia malcomberi, commonly known as the Malcomber's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia, and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a humid, subtropical climate.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
