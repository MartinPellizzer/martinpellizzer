Eleorchis japonica

Eleorchis japonica, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Japan.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a slender, erect stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
