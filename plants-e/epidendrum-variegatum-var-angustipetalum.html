Epidendrum variegatum var. angustipetalum

Epidendrum variegatum var. angustipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, often found growing on trees or rocks. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip petal. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
