Eremopoa nephelochloides

Eremopoa nephelochloides, commonly known as the cloudland grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Eremopoa nephelochloides is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
