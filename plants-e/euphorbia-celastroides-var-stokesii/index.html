Euphorbia Celastroides Var. Stokesii

Euphorbia celastroides var. stokesii is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant blue-green foliage and unique, spiky growth pattern, it adds a dramatic touch to any garden. This variety is prized for its low maintenance and ability to thrive in arid conditions. Its waxy leaves and compact form make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. While it is relatively hardy, it requires well-draining soil and minimal watering to flourish.
