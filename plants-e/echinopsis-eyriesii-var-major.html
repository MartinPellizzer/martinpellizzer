Echinopsis eyriesii var. major

Echinopsis eyriesii var. major, commonly known as the Giant Pencil Cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in summer. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in harsh conditions make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
