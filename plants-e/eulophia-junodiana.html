Eulophia junodiana

Eulophia junodiana, commonly known as the Junod's eulophia, is a tropical orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its foliage, and its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Eulophia junodiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
