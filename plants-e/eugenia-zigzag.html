Eugenia zigzag

Eugenia zigzag, commonly known as the zigzag hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Eugenia zigzag is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in its native habitat.
