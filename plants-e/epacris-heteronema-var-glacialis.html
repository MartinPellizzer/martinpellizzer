Epacris heteronema var. glacialis

Epacris heteronema var. glacialis, commonly known as the Snowy Epacris, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of pale pink to white tubular flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in heathlands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
