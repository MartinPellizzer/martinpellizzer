Euphoria undulata

Euphoria undulata, commonly known as the wavy euphoria, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater in dry conditions.

Euphoria undulata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
