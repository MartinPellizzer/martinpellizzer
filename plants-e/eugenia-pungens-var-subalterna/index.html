Eugenia Pungens Var. Subalterna

Eugenia pungens var. subalterna is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant red foliage and fragrant white flowers. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value, adding a dramatic touch to gardens and landscapes. Its leaves turn a brilliant crimson in the fall, making it a popular choice for seasonal displays. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering and frost damage.
