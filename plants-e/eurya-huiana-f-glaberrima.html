Eurya huiana f. glaberrima

Eurya huiana f. glaberrima, commonly known as the smoothleaf eurya, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its specific epithet. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are followed by small, orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
