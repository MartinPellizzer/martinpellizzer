Epidendrum burtonii

Epidendrum burtonii, commonly known as the Burton's Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are typically elliptic and covered in a fine, velvety pubescence.

Epidendrum burtonii thrives in shaded, humid environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in cultivation.
