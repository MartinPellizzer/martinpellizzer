Equisetum humboldtii

Equisetum humboldtii, commonly known as the giant horsetail, is a perennial plant belonging to the Equisetaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stems that can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant features whorls of small, needle-like leaves along its stem, which are typically green and slightly curved. It produces spore-bearing cones at the top of the stem, which are often reddish in color.

Equisetum humboldtii is native to South America and thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
