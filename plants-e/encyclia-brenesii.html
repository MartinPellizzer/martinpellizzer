Encyclia brenesii

Encyclia brenesii, commonly known as the Brenes's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that bear elliptical leaves and spikes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Encyclia brenesii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
