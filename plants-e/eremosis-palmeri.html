Eremosis palmeri

Eremosis palmeri, commonly known as the desert bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and can withstand periods of drought.

Eremosis palmeri plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is considered a symbol of the desert landscape.
