Evrardianthe asraoa

Evrardianthe asraoa, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers bloom during the dry season, showcasing a unique adaptation to its arid habitat.

Evrardianthe asraoa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
