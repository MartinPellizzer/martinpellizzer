Eurybia ramulosa var. elongata

Eurybia ramulosa var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated sunflower.

This variant is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive elongated leaves. It produces showy yellow flower heads that are typically surrounded by ray florets. The plant is native to North America and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
