Eugenia krapovickasiana

Eugenia krapovickasiana, commonly known as the Krapovickasian eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and local cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
