Epidendrum costaricense

Epidendrum costaricense, commonly known as the Costarican Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that often feature a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces numerous small, fragrant flowers.

Epidendrum costaricense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
