Epidendrum janeirense

Epidendrum janeirense, commonly known as the Janeirense Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Epidendrum janeirense is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
