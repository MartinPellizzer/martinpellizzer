Erica bokkeveldia

Erica bokkeveldia, commonly known as the Bokkeveld heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erica bokkeveldia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native environment.
