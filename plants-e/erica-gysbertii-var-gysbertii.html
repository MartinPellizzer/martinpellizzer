Erica gysbertii var. gysbertii

Erica gysbertii var. gysbertii, commonly known as the Gysbert's heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
