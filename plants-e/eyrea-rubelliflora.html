Eyrea rubelliflora

Eyrea rubelliflora, commonly known as the red-flowered eye-plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red petals and unique, eye-like markings on its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Eyrea rubelliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a rare houseplant.
