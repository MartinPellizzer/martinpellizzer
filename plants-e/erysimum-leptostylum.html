Erysimum leptostylum

Erysimum leptostylum, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that are covered in fine, hairy texture. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, often growing in a basal rosette.

Erysimum leptostylum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing on rocky or sandy soils in sunny locations.
