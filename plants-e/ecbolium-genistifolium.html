Ecbolium genistifolium

Ecbolium genistifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ecbolium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and trifoliate, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
