Epidendrum cepiforme

Epidendrum cepiforme, commonly known as the Cup-shaped Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that are often rounded or cylindrical in shape. The plant typically has narrow, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and can range in color from white to pink or purple.

Epidendrum cepiforme is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
