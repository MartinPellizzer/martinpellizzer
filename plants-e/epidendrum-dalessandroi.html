Epidendrum dalessandroi

Epidendrum dalessandroi, commonly known as the Dalessandro's Epidendrum, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are typically epiphytic, meaning they grow on the surfaces of trees or other structures rather than in soil.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
