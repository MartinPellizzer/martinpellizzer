Esula tenuifolia

Esula tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf esula, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
