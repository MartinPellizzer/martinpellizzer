Eranthemum coccineum

Eranthemum coccineum, commonly known as the red-flowered eranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or pink tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Eranthemum coccineum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
