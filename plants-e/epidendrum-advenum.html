Epidendrum advenum

Epidendrum advenum, commonly known as the showy epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often dark green and can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum advenum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
