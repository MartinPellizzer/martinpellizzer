Ebenus seychellarum

Ebenus seychellarum, commonly known as the Seychelles thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Seychelles archipelago.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and narrow, woody stems. It typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.
