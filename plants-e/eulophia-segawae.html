Eulophia segawae

Eulophia segawae, commonly known as the Segawa's eulophia, is a rare orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features a single or few upright pseudobulbs, each supporting a pair of strap-like leaves. Its flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are often pink or white with a yellow lip.

Eulophia segawae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
