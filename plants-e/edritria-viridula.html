Edritria viridula

Edritria viridula, commonly known as the green orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant greenish-yellow flowers and strap-like leaves. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a tuberous root system that stores nutrients, aiding in its survival during dry periods.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical studies.
