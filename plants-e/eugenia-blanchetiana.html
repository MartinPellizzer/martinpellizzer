Eugenia blanchetiana

Eugenia blanchetiana, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Eugenia blanchetiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual beauty.
