Eurybia axillaris var. exaltata

Eurybia axillaris var. exaltata, commonly known as the tall New Zealand bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with showy blue or purple bell-shaped flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
