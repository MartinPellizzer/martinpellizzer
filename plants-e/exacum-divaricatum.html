Exacum divaricatum

Exacum divaricatum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of purple, pink, and white.

Exacum divaricatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
