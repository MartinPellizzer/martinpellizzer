Eriocaulon regnellii

Eriocaulon regnellii, commonly known as the silver thread grass, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, thread-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, and its roots are often submerged. It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance and is often found in wetlands and marshy areas.

Eriocaulon regnellii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid environments.
