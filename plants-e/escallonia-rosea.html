Escallonia rosea

Escallonia rosea, commonly known as the common escallonia, is a shrub native to South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces showy clusters of pink or red flowers in the spring. The plant is evergreen, with a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for hedges or ornamental planting. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Escallonia rosea is also valued for its fragrant foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
