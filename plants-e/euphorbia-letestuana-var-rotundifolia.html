Euphorbia letestuana var. rotundifolia

Euphorbia letestuana var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaved spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically rounded or oval in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green花朵 surrounded by bright red bracts, which are often the most noticeable part of the plant. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
