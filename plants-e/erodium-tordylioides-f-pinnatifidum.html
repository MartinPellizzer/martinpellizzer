Erodium tordylioides f. pinnatifidum

Erodium tordylioides f. pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stormweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnatifid leaves that give it a lacy appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Its growth habit is low and spreading, with hairy stems and small, oval-shaped leaves.
