Eugenia vicozensis

Eugenia vicozensis, commonly known as the Vicozensis Eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
