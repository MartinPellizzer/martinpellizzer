Epidendrum × gracile

Epidendrum × gracile, commonly known as the slender epidendrum, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a smooth texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Epidendrum × gracile is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
