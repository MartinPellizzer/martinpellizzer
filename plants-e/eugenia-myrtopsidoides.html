Eugenia myrtopsidoides

Eugenia myrtopsidoides, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a fragrant scent, while its flowers are small and white or pink in color.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that is edible and contains several seeds.
