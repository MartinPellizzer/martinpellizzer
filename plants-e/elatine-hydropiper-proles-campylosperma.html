Elatine hydropiper proles campylosperma

Elatine hydropiper proles campylosperma, commonly known as the slender water plantain, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic herbaceous plant belonging to the Elatinaceae family.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and wetlands, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a dense network of veins.
