Eugenia sparsiflora

Eugenia sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flower eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the year in some regions. Its fruits are small, red berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans.

Eugenia sparsiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some areas.
