Echeveria paniculata var. paniculata

Echeveria paniculata var. paniculata, commonly known as the panicle echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear clusters of small, yellowish flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its compact form.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
