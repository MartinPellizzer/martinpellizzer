Eulophia dilecta

Eulophia dilecta, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Eulophia dilecta thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
