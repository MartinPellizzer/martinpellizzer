Evelyna oeconomica

Evelyna oeconomica, commonly known as the economic herb, is a perennial plant native to temperate regions.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its roots are valued for their medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Evelyna oeconomica thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
