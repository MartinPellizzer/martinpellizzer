Erica obconica

Erica obconica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other shrubs. The plant features small, densely packed flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Erica obconica thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
