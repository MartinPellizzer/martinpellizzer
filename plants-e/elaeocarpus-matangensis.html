Elaeocarpus matangensis

Elaeocarpus matangensis, commonly known as the Matang tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Elaeocarpaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, white or pink flowers. The tree produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature, containing a single seed. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and ornamental qualities.

The plant plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitats.
