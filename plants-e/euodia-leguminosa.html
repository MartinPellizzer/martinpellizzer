Euodia leguminosa

Euodia leguminosa, commonly known as the Chinese prickly ash, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Euodia leguminosa is valued for its aromatic bark and leaves, which contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.
