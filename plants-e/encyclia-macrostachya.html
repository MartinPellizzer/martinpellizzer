Encyclia macrostachya

Encyclia macrostachya, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia macrostachya is typically grown as a houseplant or in greenhouse conditions due to its specific care requirements.
