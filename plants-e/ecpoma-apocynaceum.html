Ecpoma apocynaceum

Ecpoma apocynaceum, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and emits a strong, unpleasant odor. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with five petals and a spurred structure.

The plant contains toxic compounds and is often used in traditional medicine.
