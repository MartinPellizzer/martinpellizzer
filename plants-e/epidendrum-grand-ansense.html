Epidendrum grand-ansense

Epidendrum grand-ansense, commonly known as the Giant Anansi orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter, often displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant features long, arching stems that can grow up to several meters in length, supporting clusters of fleshy, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are typically borne in racemes and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

Epidendrum grand-ansense thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.
