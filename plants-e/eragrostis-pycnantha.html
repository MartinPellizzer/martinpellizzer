Eragrostis pycnantha

Eragrostis pycnantha, commonly known as needlegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and savanna habitats.

Eragrostis pycnantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
