Epidendrum calamarium var. brevifolium

Epidendrum calamarium var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved sea anemone orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a humid environment.
