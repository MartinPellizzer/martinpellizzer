Epidendrum refractum

Epidendrum refractum, commonly known as the refracted epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or gray streaks. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

Epidendrum refractum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
