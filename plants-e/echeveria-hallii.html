Echeveria hallii

Echeveria hallii, commonly known as the Hallii Echeveria, is a species of flowering plant in the Crassulaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white powder. This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
