Erica shannoneana

Erica shannoneana, commonly known as the Shannon heath, is a flowering plant native to Ireland.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Erica shannoneana is often found in coastal and upland areas, contributing to the biodiversity of its native environment.
