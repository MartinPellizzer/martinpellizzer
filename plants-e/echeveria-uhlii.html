Echeveria uhlii

Echeveria uhlii, commonly known as the Silver Star, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a silvery-blue hue. The leaves are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a frosty appearance. It produces clusters of pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and bright sunlight.
