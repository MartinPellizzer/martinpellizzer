Eschweilera chartaceifolia

Eschweilera chartaceifolia, commonly known as the paper-leaf evergreen, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that are thin and papery in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forests.

Due to its unique foliage and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
