Erodium ciconium var. tenuisectum

Erodium ciconium var. tenuisectum, commonly known as slenderleaf bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are narrow and finely divided, giving the plant a delicate, lace-like appearance. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
