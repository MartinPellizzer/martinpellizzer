Echinopsis mamillosa var. kermesina

Echinopsis mamillosa var. kermesina, commonly known as the Kermesina cactus, is a columnar cactus native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It features a tall, spindly stem with a bluish-green color and is covered in clusters of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in the evening. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens.

Its unique appearance and nocturnal flowering make it a striking addition to any collection.
