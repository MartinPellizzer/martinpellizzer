Eugenia orbiculata

Eugenia orbiculata, commonly known as the red-eyed wonder, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, red-tinged flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

Eugenia orbiculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and attractive flowers.
