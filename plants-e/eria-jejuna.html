Eria jejuna

Eria jejuna, commonly known as the small orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
