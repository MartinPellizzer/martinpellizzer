Eugenia roseomarginata

Eugenia roseomarginata, commonly known as the pink-margin guava, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers with distinctive pink margins. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The fruit, when mature, is a small, yellowish-red berry with a sweet and aromatic flavor.
