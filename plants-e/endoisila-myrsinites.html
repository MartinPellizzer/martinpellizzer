Endoisila myrsinites

Endoisila myrsinites, commonly known as the golden myrtle, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
