Eupatorium firmum

Eupatorium firmum, commonly known as stiff boneset, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has tall, sturdy stems that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in clustered inflorescences, which bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Eupatorium firmum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes many flowering plants with composite flower heads.
