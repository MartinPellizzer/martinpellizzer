Eriocoma coronata

Eriocoma coronata, commonly known as the crowned eriocoma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a rosette of dense, silver-gray leaves covered in fine, woolly hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pale green to white in color.

Eriocoma coronata is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
