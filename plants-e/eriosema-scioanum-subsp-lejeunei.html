Eriosema scioanum subsp. lejeunei

Eriosema scioanum subsp.

lejeunei, commonly known as the slender eriosema, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats, often in areas with moderate to high rainfall.

The plant features slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is a relatively rare subspecies, with limited distribution and specific ecological requirements.
