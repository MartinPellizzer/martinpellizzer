Erica nigrita

Erica nigrita, commonly known as the black heath, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces dense, evergreen foliage and bears small, dark purple to black flowers during the winter months. Erica nigrita is well adapted to harsh, arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
