Erythrina stricta var. yunnanensis

Erythrina stricta var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan flame tree, is a striking tropical shrub or small tree native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically clustered in panicles. The seeds are enclosed in elongated, woody pods that split open when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
