Elynanthus aristatus

Elynanthus aristatus, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Elynanthus aristatus thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
