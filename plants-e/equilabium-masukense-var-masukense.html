Equilabium masukense var. masukense

Equilabium masukense var. masukense, commonly known as the Masukense orchid, is a species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and exhibit a range of colors, often with a distinctive labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphological features and limited distribution.
