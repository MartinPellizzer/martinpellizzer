Elachopappus rudallii

Elachopappus rudallii, commonly known as the roughhead sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
