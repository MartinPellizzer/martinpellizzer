Euodia officinalis

Euodia officinalis, commonly known as Chinese prickly ash, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its spiny branches and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves and bark contain compounds such as lignans and flavonoids, which are valued for their medicinal properties.

Euodia officinalis is also used in aromatherapy and as a source of essential oils.
