Eugenia bordenii

Eugenia bordenii, commonly known as the Borden's eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and for horticultural purposes.
