Elaeocarpus stellaris

Elaeocarpus stellaris, commonly known as the starfruit or carambola, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which is typically five-lobed and yellow when ripe. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, and the fruit develops from these flowers.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and the edible fruit it produces.
