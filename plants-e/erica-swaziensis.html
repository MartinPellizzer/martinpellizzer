Erica swaziensis

Erica swaziensis, commonly known as the Swazi heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and cranberries. This evergreen shrub typically grows in well-drained, acidic soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in late summer.

Erica swaziensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
