Eria nana

Eria nana, commonly known as the dwarf eria, is a small flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features delicate, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
