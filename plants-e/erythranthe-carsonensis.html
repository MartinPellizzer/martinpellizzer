Erythranthe carsonensis

Erythranthe carsonensis, commonly known as Carson's monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada.

It is a member of the Phrymaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as streambanks and meadows, and it has slender, hairy stems with opposite, ovate leaves.
