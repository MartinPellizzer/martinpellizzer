Epidendrum hippium

Epidendrum hippium, commonly known as the silver sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Epidendrum hippium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
