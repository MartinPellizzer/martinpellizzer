Erythrospermum erythroxyloides

Erythrospermum erythroxyloides, commonly known as the red-flowered hibiscus, is a species native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
