Eupatorium angustissimum

Eupatorium angustissimum, commonly known as narrowleaf boneset, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp habitats.

Eupatorium angustissimum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
