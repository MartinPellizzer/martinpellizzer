Encyclia naranjapatensis

Encyclia naranjapatensis, commonly known as the Orange Peel Encyclia, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which are often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in two rows along the stem.

Encyclia naranjapatensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
