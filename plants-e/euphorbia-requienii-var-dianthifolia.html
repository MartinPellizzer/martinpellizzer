Euphorbia requienii var. dianthifolia

Euphorbia requienii var. dianthifolia, commonly known as the pink spurge, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems that are often tinged with pink or red hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in a cup-like structure called a cyathium. It has a low-growing habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.

Euphorbia requienii var. dianthifolia is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight.
