Eurya integrifolia

Eurya integrifolia, commonly known as the Pacific silver fir or silverleaf eurya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ericaceae.

It is native to the western coast of North America, particularly in regions with moist, cool climates. It produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by dark blue berries.

Eurya integrifolia is often found in forest understories and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
