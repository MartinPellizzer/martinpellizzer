Eriocaulon australe

Eriocaulon australe, commonly known as the Australian needlegrass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Eriocaulaceae, which is characterized by its grass-like appearance and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet, swampy areas and is often found in coastal regions. Eriocaulon australe plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
