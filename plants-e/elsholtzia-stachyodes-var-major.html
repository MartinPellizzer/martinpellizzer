Elsholtzia stachyodes var. major

Elsholtzia stachyodes var. major, commonly known as the broad-leaved elsholtzia, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features aromatic, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and long blooming period.
