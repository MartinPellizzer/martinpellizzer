Erythroxylum santosii

Erythroxylum santosii, commonly known as Santos's erythroxylum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a capsule that contains elliptical, smooth seeds.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its morphological features and potential ecological roles in its native habitats.
