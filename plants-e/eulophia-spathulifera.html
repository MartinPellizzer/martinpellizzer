Eulophia spathulifera

Eulophia spathulifera, commonly known as the broad-leafed eulophia, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with numerous pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Eulophia spathulifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
