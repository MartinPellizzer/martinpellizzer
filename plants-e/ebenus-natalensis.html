Ebenus natalensis

Ebenus natalensis, commonly known as Natal's thornveld, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in the coastal regions of KwaZulu-Natal.

Ebenus natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
