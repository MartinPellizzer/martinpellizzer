Encyclia bicamerata

Encyclia bicamerata, commonly known as the two-chambered orchid, is a species native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive white lip.

Encyclia bicamerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
