Eulalia leschenaultiana

Eulalia leschenaultiana, commonly known as the leschenault's grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Eulalia leschenaultiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
