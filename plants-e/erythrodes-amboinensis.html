Erythrodes amboinensis

Erythrodes amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina redwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. It typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
