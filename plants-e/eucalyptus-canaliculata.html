Eucalyptus canaliculata

Eucalyptus canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallees, is a species of eucalyptus native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed form and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, globular flower buds that are typically yellow or cream in color. Eucalyptus canaliculata is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Its bark is often flaky and peels in thin layers, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
