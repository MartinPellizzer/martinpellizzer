Eugenia laeteviridis

Eugenia laeteviridis, commonly known as the green eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The fruit is a small, edible berry that is often used in culinary applications.
