Eulophia brenanii

Eulophia brenanii, commonly known as the Brenan's eulophia, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

Eulophia brenanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
