Exellodendron barbatum

Exellodendron barbatum, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its showy, winter-blooming flowers.
