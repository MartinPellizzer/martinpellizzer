Echeveria eurychlamys

Echeveria eurychlamys, commonly known as the Blue Elf or Blue Echeveria, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to Mexico.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, with a powdery coating that gives them a unique appearance. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers on tall, branching stems during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight to maintain its color and shape.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among collectors and gardeners who grow it in arid or semi-arid climates.
