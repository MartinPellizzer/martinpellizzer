Erianthemum linguiforme

Erianthemum linguiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.

Erianthemum linguiforme prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
