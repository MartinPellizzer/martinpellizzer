Elaphoglossum latifolium var. cuencanum

Elaphoglossum latifolium var. cuencanum, commonly known as the Cuencanum holly fern, is a variant of the broad-leaved holly fern native to Central America.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and finely divided, lanceolate leaves. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
