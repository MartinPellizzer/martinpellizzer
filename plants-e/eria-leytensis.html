Eria leytensis

Eria leytensis, commonly known as the Leyte orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Eria leytensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
