Echinochloa spectabilis

Echinochloa spectabilis, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink plumes that appear during the flowering season. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Echinochloa spectabilis is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
