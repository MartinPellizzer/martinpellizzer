Eria stenophylla

Eria stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of small, white or pale pink blossoms.

Eria stenophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
