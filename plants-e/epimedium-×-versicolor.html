Epimedium × versicolor

Epimedium × versicolor, commonly known as barrenwort, is a deciduous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, mounded growth habit. The plant produces showy, upright clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers in early spring, which appear before the leaves. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall.

Epimedium × versicolor is well-suited for shady garden environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
