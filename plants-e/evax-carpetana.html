Evax carpetana

Evax carpetana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented stems that bear clusters of vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, which is why it is popular as a festive decoration. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, and it prefers indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Evax carpetana is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
