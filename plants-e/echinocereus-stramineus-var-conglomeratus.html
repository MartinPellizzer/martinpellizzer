Echinocereus stramineus var. conglomeratus

Echinocereus stramineus var. conglomeratus, commonly known as the Desert Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, clumping cactus with cylindrical stems that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

Its structure and appearance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
