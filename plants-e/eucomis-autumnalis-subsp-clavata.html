Eucomis autumnalis subsp. clavata

Eucomis autumnalis subsp.

clavata, commonly known as the purple fountain grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa. It features tall, slender stems that bear dense clusters of pinkish-purple bracts, which resemble a fountain of flowers.

The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
