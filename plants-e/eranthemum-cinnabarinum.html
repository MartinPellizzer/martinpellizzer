Eranthemum cinnabarinum

Eranthemum cinnabarinum, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

The scarlet pimpernel is known for its rapid flowering cycle, often blooming in response to changes in light and temperature.
