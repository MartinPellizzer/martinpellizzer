Erysimum × allionii

Erysimum × allionii, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a cross between Erysimum cheiri and Erysimum hybrida.

It is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has compound leaves with serrated edges.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
