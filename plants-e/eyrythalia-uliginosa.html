Eyrythalia uliginosa

Eyrythalia uliginosa, commonly known as the bog bean, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating in water. It is adapted to nutrient-poor, acidic soils and plays a role in wetland ecosystems.

Eyrythalia uliginosa is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.
