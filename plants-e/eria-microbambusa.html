Eria microbambusa

Eria microbambusa, commonly known as the miniature bamboo orchid, is a unique hybrid plant that combines characteristics of both orchids and bamboo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its elegant, grass-like foliage and small, delicate orchid blooms. The plant typically grows in a compact, clumping form, making it suitable for container gardening. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous texture, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink.

Eria microbambusa thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
