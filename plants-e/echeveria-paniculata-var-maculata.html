Echeveria paniculata var. maculata

Echeveria paniculata var. maculata, commonly known as the variegated panicle echeveria, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green with distinctive red or pink markings. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
