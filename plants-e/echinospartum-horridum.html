Echinospartum horridum

Echinospartum horridum, commonly known as the desert star or spiny echinospartum, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, woody stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it well adapted to harsh desert environments. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a striking addition to xeriscapes and rock gardens.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
