Eriospermum Cooperi Var. Natalense

Eriospermum cooperi var. natalense is a unique and rare plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking yellow flowers. This variety is distinguished by its compact growth habit and vibrant bloom color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a desirable addition to rock gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is often sought after by botanists and gardeners alike.
