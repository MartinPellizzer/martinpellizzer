Echium laurentii

Echium laurentii, commonly known as the Laurentian fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Echium laurentii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
