Eriocoma occidentalis subsp. californica

Eriocoma occidentalis subsp.

californica, commonly known as the California eriocoma, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats, such as hillsides and slopes. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of pink or white.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is valued for its ornamental qualities in native plant gardens.
