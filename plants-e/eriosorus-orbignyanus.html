Eriosorus orbignyanus

Eriosorus orbignyanus, commonly known as the velvet fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering of fine, soft hairs, which gives it a velvety appearance. This fern is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil, Argentina, and Paraguay. Its rhizome is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
