Elaeocarpus batudulangii

Elaeocarpus batudulangii, commonly known as the Batudulangii elaeocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is typically found in lowland to montane rainforests. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree that can reach heights of up to 15 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy dark green upper surface.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant plays a role in the ecological diversity of its habitat.
