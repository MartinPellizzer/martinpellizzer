Euphorbia sieboldiana var. grandifolia

Euphorbia sieboldiana var. grandifolia, commonly known as the Japanese spurge or grandiflorous spurge, is a perennial shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers surrounded by red bracts. The plant has smooth, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs. It is known for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking floral display.

Euphorbia sieboldiana var. grandifolia is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
