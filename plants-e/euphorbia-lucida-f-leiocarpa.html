Euphorbia lucida f. leiocarpa

Euphorbia lucida f. leiocarpa, commonly known as the white-spined euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are typical of the Euphorbia genus. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to dry conditions.
