Erica chlorosepala

Erica chlorosepala, commonly known as the yellow heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the heath family, Ericaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in coastal and mountainous regions. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Erica chlorosepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
