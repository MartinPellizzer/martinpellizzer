Echinocereus tulensis

Echinocereus tulensis, commonly known as the Tulip Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that form a rounded or shrub-like structure. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid gardens and rockeries.
