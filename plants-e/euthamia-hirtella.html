Euthamia hirtella

Euthamia hirtella, commonly known as hairy goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows, prairies, and along stream banks. Its hairy stems and leaves give it a distinctive appearance, and it blooms from late summer into early autumn.
