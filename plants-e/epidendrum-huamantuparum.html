Epidendrum huamantuparum

Epidendrum huamantuparum, commonly known as the Huamantuparum Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
