Echinospermum sericeum

Echinospermum sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features a slender, branching stem covered in fine, silver-white hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped, also hairy, and often have a bluish-gray hue. It produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white ray florets, typically blooming from late spring to early fall.

This plant is adapted to drought conditions and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
