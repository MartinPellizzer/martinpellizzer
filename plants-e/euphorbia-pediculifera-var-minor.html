Euphorbia pediculifera var. minor

Euphorbia pediculifera var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its unique inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid gardens.
