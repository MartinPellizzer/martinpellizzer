Eucharis bouchei var. dressleri

Eucharis bouchei var. dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's Amazon lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that resemble lilies, often blooming in clusters. The plant features broad, glossy green leaves that are oval in shape and can grow quite large. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
