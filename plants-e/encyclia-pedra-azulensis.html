Encyclia pedra-azulensis

Encyclia pedra-azulensis, commonly known as the Blue Rock Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rock surfaces or in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky substrates, while its leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Encyclia pedra-azulensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
