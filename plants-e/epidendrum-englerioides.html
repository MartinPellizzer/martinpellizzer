Epidendrum englerioides

Epidendrum englerioides, commonly known as the Engler's Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
