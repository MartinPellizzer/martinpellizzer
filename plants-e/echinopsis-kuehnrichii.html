Echinopsis kuehnrichii

Echinopsis kuehnrichii, commonly known as the Kuehnrich's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and clusters of vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has spiny areoles and is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. It prefers full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
