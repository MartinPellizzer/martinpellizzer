Echidnopsis scutellata subsp. australis

Echidnopsis scutellata subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to Australia. It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

This subspecies is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
