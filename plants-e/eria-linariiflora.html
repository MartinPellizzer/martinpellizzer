Eria linariiflora

Eria linariiflora, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Eria linariiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens.
