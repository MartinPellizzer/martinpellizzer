Epidendrum cuatrecasasii

Epidendrum cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas's Epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and blooms in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
