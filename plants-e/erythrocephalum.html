Erythrocephalum

Erythrocephalum, commonly known as the redhead or redhead flower, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Australia and features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plants typically have hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. They are often found in dry, sandy soils and are adapted to arid environments.

Erythrocephalum species are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
