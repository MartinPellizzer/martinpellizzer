Eria mindanaensis

Eria mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao eria, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland to montane forest areas, often in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Eria mindanaensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
