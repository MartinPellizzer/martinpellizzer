Erigeron myosotis f. pluriceps

Erigeron myosotis f. pluriceps, commonly known as the clustered blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and a compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small, clustered flower heads. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky soils.

This variant is notable for its distinctive floral arrangement and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
