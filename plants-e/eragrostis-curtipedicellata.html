Eragrostis curtipedicellata

Eragrostis curtipedicellata, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, drooping inflorescences and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
