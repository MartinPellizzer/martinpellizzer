Eurybia persoonioides var. lanceolata

Eurybia persoonioides var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf sunflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape, with a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces yellow composite flowers with ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular florets. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often found in prairies, open woods, and disturbed sites.
