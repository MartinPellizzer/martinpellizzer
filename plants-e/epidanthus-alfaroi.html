Epidanthus alfaroi

Epidanthus alfaroi, commonly known as the Alfaroi's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
