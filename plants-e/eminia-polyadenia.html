Eminia polyadenia

Eminia polyadenia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Eminia polyadenia is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
