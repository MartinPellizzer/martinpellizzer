Eremurus robustus

Eremurus robustus, commonly known as the giant spirea, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen in some varieties.

Eremurus robustus is valued in horticulture for its dramatic floral display and hardy nature.
