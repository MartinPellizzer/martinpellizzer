Eryngium duriaei subsp. juresianum

Eryngium duriaei subsp.

juresianum, commonly known as the Juresian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its spiny, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in its native habitat.
