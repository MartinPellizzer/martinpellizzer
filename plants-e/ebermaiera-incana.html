Ebermaiera incana

Ebermaiera incana, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, hairy texture.

Ebermaiera incana is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
