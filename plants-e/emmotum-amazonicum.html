Emmotum amazonicum

Emmotum amazonicum, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking spatulate inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Emmotum amazonicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
