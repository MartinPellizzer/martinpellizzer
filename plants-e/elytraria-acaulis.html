Elytraria acaulis

Elytraria acaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed elytraria, is a rare and unique plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves on the stem, which is a distinguishing botanical feature. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with white to pale yellow flowers. Its roots are adapted to nutrient-poor soils, reflecting its specialized ecological niche.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
