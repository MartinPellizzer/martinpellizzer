Epilobium stereophyllum var. kiwuense

Epilobium stereophyllum var. kiwuense, commonly known as the Kiwuense willowherb, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal applications.
