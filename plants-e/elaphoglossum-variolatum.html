Elaphoglossum variolatum, commonly known as the variable harts-tongue fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. Elaphoglossum variolatum is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
