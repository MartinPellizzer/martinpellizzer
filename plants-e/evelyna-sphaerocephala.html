Evelyna sphaerocephala

Evelyna sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Evelyna sphaerocephala is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
