Eugenia bayatensis

Eugenia bayatensis, commonly known as the Bayat's eugenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, with a strong aromatic scent when crushed.

Eugenia bayatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
