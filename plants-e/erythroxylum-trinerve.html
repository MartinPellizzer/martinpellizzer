Erythroxylum trinerve

Erythroxylum trinerve, commonly known as the red veined or crimson veined plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Erythroxylaceae.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and red berries, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
