Epidendrum varicosum

Epidendrum varicosum, commonly known as the Variable Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and other structures in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

Epidendrum varicosum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
