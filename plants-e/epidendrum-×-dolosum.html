Epidendrum × dolosum

Epidendrum × dolosum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is a hybrid between Epidendrum ibaguense and Epidendrum nocturnum, resulting in a plant with distinctive dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically fragrant and appear in clusters, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil and regular watering for optimal growth.
