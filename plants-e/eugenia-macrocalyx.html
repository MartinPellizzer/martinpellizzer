Eugenia macrocalyx

Eugenia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
