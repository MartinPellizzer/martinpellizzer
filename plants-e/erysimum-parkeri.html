Erysimum parkeri

Erysimum parkeri, commonly known as the Sierra primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Erysimum parkeri is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
