Eremomastax crossandriflora

Eremomastax crossandriflora, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spiny stems and leaves that are adapted to arid conditions. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which attract pollinators like birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens in regions with Mediterranean climates.
