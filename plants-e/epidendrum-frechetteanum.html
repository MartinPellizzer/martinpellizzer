Epidendrum frechetteanum

Epidendrum frechetteanum, commonly known as the Frechette's orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in forested areas with high humidity. The plant features long, slender stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or orange lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
