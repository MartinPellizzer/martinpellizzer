Epilobium angustifolium f. spectabile

Epilobium angustifolium f. spectabile, commonly known as the showy willow herb, is a striking flowering plant native to North America.

It features slender, willow-like stems that are often reddish in color and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to red flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
