Elatostema sinense var. sinense

Elatostema sinense var. sinense, commonly known as the Chinese elatostema, is a small herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky hillsides. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with fleshy, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, round berries that are edible.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
