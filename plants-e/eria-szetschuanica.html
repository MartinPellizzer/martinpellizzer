Eria szetschuanica

Eria szetschuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink.

Eria szetschuanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
