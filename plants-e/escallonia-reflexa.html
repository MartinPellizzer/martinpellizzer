Escallonia reflexa

Escallonia reflexa, commonly known as the red escallonia, is a flowering shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is evergreen, with a compact, rounded shape that makes it suitable for hedges or ornamental gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Escallonia reflexa is also valued for its fragrant foliage and resistance to pests and diseases.
