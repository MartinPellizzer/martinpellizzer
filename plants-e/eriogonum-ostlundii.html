Eriogonum ostlundii

Eriogonum ostlundii, commonly known as the San Diego fairy duster, is a flowering plant native to southern California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions.
