Euphorbia obesa

Euphorbia obesa, commonly known as the football cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a swollen, globular stem that resembles a football, which stores water to help it survive in arid conditions. The plant has a smooth, waxy surface and produces small yellow flowers during the growing season. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
