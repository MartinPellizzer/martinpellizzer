Eritrichium mite

Eritrichium mite, commonly known as the common eritrichium mite, is a small arthropod belonging to the order Trombidiformes.

It is not a plant but a type of mite that feeds on plant material, particularly on the leaves of certain flowering plants. These mites are often found in gardens and agricultural settings where their host plants are present. They can cause damage to foliage by feeding on the surface cells, leading to discoloration and reduced plant vigor.

Due to their microscopic size, they are often overlooked but can have significant ecological impacts on plant health.
