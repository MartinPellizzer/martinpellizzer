Echeveria × pulchella

Echeveria × pulchella, commonly known as the hybrid echeveria, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often fleshy and covered in a fine layer of white hair. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, with a powdery coating that gives them a distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring or summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
