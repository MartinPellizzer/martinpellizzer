Epidendrum sceptrum

Epidendrum sceptrum, commonly known as the Crowned Epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored, often pink or red flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are usually broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a waxy appearance.

Epidendrum sceptrum is a hardy species that thrives in warm, humid environments with good air circulation.
