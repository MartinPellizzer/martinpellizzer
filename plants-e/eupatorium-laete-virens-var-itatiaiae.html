Eupatorium laete-virens var. itatiaiae

Eupatorium laete-virens var. itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia eupatorium, is a perennial herb native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
