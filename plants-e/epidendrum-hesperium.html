Epidendrum hesperium

Epidendrum hesperium, commonly known as the Mexican flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central America.

It features vibrant orange and red flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has long, arching stems with numerous racemes of showy, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Epidendrum hesperium thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.
