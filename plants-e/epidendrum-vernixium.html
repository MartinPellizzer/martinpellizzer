Epidendrum vernixium

Epidendrum vernixium, commonly known as the white-flowered epidendrum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Epidendrum vernixium is a hardy orchid that is relatively easy to care for, requiring regular watering and moderate humidity.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
