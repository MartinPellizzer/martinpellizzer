Eucalyptus oligantha

Eucalyptus oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallee, is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and smooth, often aromatic bark. The plant produces small, globular flower spikes that are typically white or pink in color. Eucalyptus oligantha is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various native animals.
