Euphorbia obtusifolia subsp. regis-jubae

Euphorbia obtusifolia subsp.

regis-jubae, commonly known as the royal crown euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a short, thick, branched stem covered in dense, woolly foliage.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green cyathia, which are its characteristic floral structures. Its leaves are typically rounded and often have a bluish or grayish hue.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
