Eucomea elata

Eucomea elata, commonly known as the slender hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually large and heart-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

Eucomea elata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various garden settings.
