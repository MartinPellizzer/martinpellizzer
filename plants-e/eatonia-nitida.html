Eatonia nitida

Eatonia nitida, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Eatonia nitida is considered endangered in the wild.
