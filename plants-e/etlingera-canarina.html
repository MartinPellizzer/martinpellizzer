Etlingera canarina

Etlingera canarina, commonly known as the Canary ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its ornamental, strap-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value.
