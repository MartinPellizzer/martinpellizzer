Elaeagnus cumingii subsp. perrottetii

Elaeagnus cumingii subsp.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in poor soils. It is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties, making it useful in reforestation and soil improvement.
