Encyclia confusa

Encyclia confusa, commonly known as the confused orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive purple or brown lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Encyclia confusa is a popular choice for tropical gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
