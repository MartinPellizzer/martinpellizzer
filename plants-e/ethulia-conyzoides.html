Ethulia conyzoides

Ethulia conyzoides, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, characterized by its hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and disturbed soils, and is often found in coastal areas.

Ethulia conyzoides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in African herbal medicine.
