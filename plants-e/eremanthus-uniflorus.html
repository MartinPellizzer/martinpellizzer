Eremanthus uniflorus

Eremanthus uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flowered eremanthus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Eremanthus uniflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
