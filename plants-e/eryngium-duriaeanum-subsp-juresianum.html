Eryngium duriaeanum subsp. juresianum

Eryngium duriaeanum subsp.

juresianum, commonly known as the Juresian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region. It is a perennial herb with spiny, blue-green foliage and produces purple to pink flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, tubular flowers surrounded by bracts.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
