Eugenia iwahigensis

Eugenia iwahigensis, commonly known as the Iwahig eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Luzon. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are typically red when mature.

Eugenia iwahigensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
