Epidendrum valenzuelae

Epidendrum valenzuelae, commonly known as the Valenzuela Epidendrum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, narrow pseudobulbs that support a cluster of elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in the ecosystems of its native habitat.
