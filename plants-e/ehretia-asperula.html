Ehretia asperula

Ehretia asperula, commonly known as roughleaf ehretia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, dark green foliage with rough, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
