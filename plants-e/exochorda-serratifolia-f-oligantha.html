Exochorda serratifolia f. oligantha

Exochorda serratifolia f. oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved snowball bush, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The leaves are narrow and serrated, contributing to its distinctive foliage. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in partial shade.

It is relatively low maintenance and suitable for USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8.
