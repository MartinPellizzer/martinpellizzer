Elaphoglossum fuertesii

Elaphoglossum fuertesii, commonly known as the Fuertes' holly fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a serrated margin. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth, where it thrives in humid conditions. Elaphoglossum fuertesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
