Eugenia ypanemensis

Eugenia ypanemensis, commonly known as the Ypanemense myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and glossy, elliptical leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
