Erica spectabilis

Erica spectabilis, commonly known as the showy heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Erica spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes.
