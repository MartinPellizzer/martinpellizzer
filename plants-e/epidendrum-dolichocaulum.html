Epidendrum dolichocaulum

Epidendrum dolichocaulum, commonly known as the long-stemmed epidendrum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Epidendrum dolichocaulum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right growing conditions.
