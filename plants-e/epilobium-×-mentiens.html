Epilobium × mentiens

Epilobium × mentiens, commonly known as the hybrid willow herb, is a flowering plant in the evening primrose family (Onagraceae).

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and woodland edges.

Epilobium × mentiens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for pollinators.
