Eriosema stanerianum var. flavescens

Eriosema stanerianum var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered eriosema, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are pubescent on the underside, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and vibrant floral display.
