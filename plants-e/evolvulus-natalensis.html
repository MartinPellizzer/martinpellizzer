Evolvulus natalensis

Evolvulus natalensis, commonly known as Natal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, and white. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, sunny habitats. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glory species.

The flowers open in the morning and close by midday, hence its common name.
