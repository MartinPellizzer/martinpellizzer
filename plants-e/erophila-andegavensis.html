Erophila andegavensis

Erophila andegavensis, commonly known as the Andegavensis sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Erophila andegavensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in dry landscapes.
