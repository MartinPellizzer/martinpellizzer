Elleanthus robustus

Elleanthus robustus, commonly known as the robust everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. Elleanthus robustus is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its long-lasting blooms and hardy nature.
