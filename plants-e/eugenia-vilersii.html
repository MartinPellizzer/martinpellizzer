Eugenia vilersii

Eugenia vilersii, commonly known as the Vilers' eugenia, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, glossy leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
