Eragrostis breviculmis

Eragrostis breviculmis, commonly known as the short-branched lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to brownish flowers.

Eragrostis breviculmis is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, making it a resilient plant in dry environments.
