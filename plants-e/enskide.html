Enskide

Enskide, commonly known as the "garden orchid," is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy blossoms that often come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Enskide is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticultural gardens.

Its growth requires a warm, humid environment with well-draining soil.
