Epacris paludosa

Epacris paludosa, commonly known as the swamp epacris, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Heath family (Ericaceae) and is typically found in wet or swampy habitats. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in heathlands and forested areas.
