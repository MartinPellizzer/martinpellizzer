Elatostema jabiense

Elatostema jabiense, commonly known as the Jabiense snowflake plant, is a member of the Urticaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a creeping habit, with runners that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the forest floor.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental addition to botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
