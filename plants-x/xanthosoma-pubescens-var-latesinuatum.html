Xanthosoma pubescens var. latesinuatum

Xanthosoma pubescens var. latesinuatum, commonly known as the red-veined elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with prominent red or purple veins. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Xanthosoma pubescens var. latesinuatum is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
