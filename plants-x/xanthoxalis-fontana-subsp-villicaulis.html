Xanthoxalis fontana subsp. villicaulis

Xanthoxalis fontana subsp.

villicaulis, commonly known as the slenderleaf false oxalis, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America. It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
