Xiphopteris skutchii

Xiphopteris skutchii, commonly known as Skutch's sword fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and slightly serrated along the edges. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of moisture and moderate temperatures. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through spore dispersal rather than seeds.

Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to environmental changes, Xiphopteris skutchii is considered a vulnerable species.
