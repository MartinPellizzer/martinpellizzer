Xanthe parviflora

Xanthe parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered xanthe, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in dry, open areas with well-drained soil.

Xanthe parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
