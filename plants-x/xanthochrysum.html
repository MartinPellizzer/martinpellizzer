Xanthochrysum

Xanthochrysum, commonly known as the golden everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant is known for its long-lasting blooms, which retain their color and form even when dried. Xanthochrysum is often used in floral arrangements and as a natural dye due to its vibrant hue.
