Xerophyta nutans var. nutans

Xerophyta nutans var. nutans, commonly known as the nodding iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its ability to survive in drought conditions makes it a hardy and low-maintenance plant.
