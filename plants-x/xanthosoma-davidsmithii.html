Xanthosoma davidsmithii

Xanthosoma davidsmithii, commonly known as the David Smith's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Xanthosoma davidsmithii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and vibrant floral displays.
