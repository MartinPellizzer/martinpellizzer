Xysmalobium alatum

Xysmalobium alatum, commonly known as the winged lily, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble lilies, with petals that are often brightly colored, ranging from white to pink. The plant has distinctive, narrow, wing-like leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Xysmalobium alatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
