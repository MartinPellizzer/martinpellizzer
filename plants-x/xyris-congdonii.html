Xyris congdonii

Xyris congdonii, commonly known as Congdon's goldeneye, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Xyridaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in wetland environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.
