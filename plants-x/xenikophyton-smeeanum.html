Xenikophyton smeeanum

Xenikophyton smeeanum, commonly known as the Smeeanum spurge, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many other spurge species.

Xenikophyton smeeanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
