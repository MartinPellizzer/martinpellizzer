Xanthium chinense var. globuliforme

Xanthium chinense var. globuliforme, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiny seed heads that resemble small globes, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are typically lobed with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
