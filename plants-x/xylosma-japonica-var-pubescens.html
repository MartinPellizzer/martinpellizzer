Xylosma japonica var. pubescens

Xylosma japonica var. pubescens, commonly known as the Japanese xylosma, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark is typically pale to reddish and has a smooth texture.

It is often found in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade to full sun.
