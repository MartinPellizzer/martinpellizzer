Xylinabaria minutiflora

Xylinabaria minutiflora, commonly known as the tiny-flowered xylinabaria, is a rare and delicate plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, capsule-like fruits that contain its seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous forests.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
