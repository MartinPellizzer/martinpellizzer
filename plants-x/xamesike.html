Xamesike

Xamesike, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with a rosette of leaves that form a dense, cushion-like structure. Its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year makes it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
