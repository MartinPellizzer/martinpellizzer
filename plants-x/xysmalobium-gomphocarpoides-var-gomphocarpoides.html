Xysmalobium gomphocarpoides var. gomphocarpoides

Xysmalobium gomphocarpoides var. gomphocarpoides, commonly known as the Gomphocarpoid Glandular Cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
