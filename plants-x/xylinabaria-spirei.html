Xylinabaria spirei

Xylinabaria spirei, commonly known as the spiralwood, is a unique flowering plant native to the humid tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its spirally arranged leaves, which give it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming during the wet season. Its woody stem is fibrous and often used in traditional crafts and construction.

Xylinabaria spirei is also valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in herbal remedies.
