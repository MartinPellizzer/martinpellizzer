Xanthosoma brasiliense

Xanthosoma brasiliense, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in humid tropical regions.

Xanthosoma brasiliense is valued both for its aesthetic appeal and for its potential use in traditional medicine.
