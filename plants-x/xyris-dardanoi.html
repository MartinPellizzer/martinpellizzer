Xyris dardanoi

Xyris dardanoi, commonly known as the Dardanian goldeneye, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flower clusters that bloom in the summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Xyris dardanoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
