Xylopia ferruginea var. oxyantha

Xylopia ferruginea var. oxyantha, commonly known as the red sandalwood or redwood, is a tropical tree species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its dark reddish-brown heartwood, which is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. Due to its economic value, it is often harvested for timber, leading to conservation concerns in its native habitats.
