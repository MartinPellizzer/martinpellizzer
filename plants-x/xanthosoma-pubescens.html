Xanthosoma pubescens

Xanthosoma pubescens, commonly known as the velvet leaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, velvety leaves. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. Its rhizomes are edible and used as a starchy food source in various cuisines.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
