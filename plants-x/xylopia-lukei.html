Xylopia lukei

Xylopia lukei, commonly known as the African star anise, is a tropical evergreen tree native to central and eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and compound leaves. Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species in its native environment.
