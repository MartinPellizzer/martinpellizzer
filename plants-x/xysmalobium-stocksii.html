Xysmalobium stocksii

Xysmalobium stocksii, commonly known as the Stocksii lily, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive spurred petal at the base. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its blooming period usually occurs in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
