Xylopia zenkeri

Xylopia zenkeri, commonly known as the African allspice or Zenker's xylopia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are typically globose or ellipsoid and contain several seeds enclosed in a fleshy, aromatic aril.

Xylopia zenkeri is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
