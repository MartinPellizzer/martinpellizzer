Xanthosoma sagittifolium

Xanthosoma sagittifolium, commonly known as arrowleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a glossy surface and are arranged alternately along the stem. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers followed by orange or red fruits containing numerous seeds.
