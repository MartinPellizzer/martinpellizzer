Xysmalobium bellum

Xysmalobium bellum, commonly known as the bell-shaped peacock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly through underground stems.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its ecosystem.
