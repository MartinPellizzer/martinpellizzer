Xeranthemum spinosum

Xeranthemum spinosum, commonly known as the spiny xeranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Xeranthemum spinosum is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
