Xeraea Scapigera

Xeraea scapigera is a unique and resilient plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in harsh environments where few other plants can survive. Known for its distinctive appearance and adaptive features, it has captured the interest of botanists and gardeners alike. This species is often overlooked despite its ecological importance.
