Xiphotheca tomentosa

Xiphotheca tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly coral tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Xiphotheca tomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
