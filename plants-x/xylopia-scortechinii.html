Xylopia scortechinii

Xylopia scortechinii, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a member of the Anacardiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. Its fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that contains a single seed.

The plant is known for its aromatic leaves and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
