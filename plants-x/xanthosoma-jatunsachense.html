Xanthosoma jatunsachense

Xanthosoma jatunsachense, commonly known as the Andean arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Xanthosoma jatunsachense is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local communities.
