Xanthosoma vargasii

Xanthosoma vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas giant taro, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces striking yellow spatheal inflorescences, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Xanthosoma vargasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its edible leaves in some regions.
