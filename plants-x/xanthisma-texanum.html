Xanthisma texanum

Xanthisma texanum, commonly known as Texas sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Texas.

It is a low-growing annual herb with narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing desert soils and providing nectar for pollinators.

Xanthisma texanum is also used in traditional medicine by some Native American tribes.
