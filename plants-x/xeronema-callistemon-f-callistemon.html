Xeronema callistemon f. callistemon

Xeronema callistemon f. callistemon, commonly known as the flame drumstick, is a compact shrub native to New Zealand.

It features striking red-orange flowers that bloom in winter, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its tolerance of salt spray and moderate frost.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in well-drained soils.
