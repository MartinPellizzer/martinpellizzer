Xylosteon cordatum

Xylosteon cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, followed by orange or red fruits containing edible seeds. Xylosteon cordatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
