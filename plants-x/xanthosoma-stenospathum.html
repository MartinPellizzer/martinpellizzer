Xanthosoma stenospathum

Xanthosoma stenospathum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
