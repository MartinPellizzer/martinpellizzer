Xanthosoma oyuelae

Xanthosoma oyuelae, commonly known as the Oyuela arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Xanthosoma oyuelae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by local communities.
