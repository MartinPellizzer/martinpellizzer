Xylopia wilwerthii var. cuneata

Xylopia wilwerthii var. cuneata, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a rough, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
