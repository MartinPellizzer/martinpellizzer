Xeranthemum rigidum

Xeranthemum rigidum, commonly known as the rigid thyme-leaf alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with compact, bushy foliage and produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Xeranthemum rigidum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
