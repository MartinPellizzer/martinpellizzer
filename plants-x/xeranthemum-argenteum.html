Xeranthemum argenteum

Xeranthemum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf xeranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Xeranthemum argenteum is often used as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
