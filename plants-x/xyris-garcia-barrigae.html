Xyris garcia-barrigae

Xyris garcia-barrigae, commonly known as the Garcia-Barriga yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a fan shape at the base.

Xyris garcia-barrigae is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and swampy areas.
