Ximenia elliptica

Ximenia elliptica, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a snowball-like appearance. Its fruit is a yellowish, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

Ximenia elliptica is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
