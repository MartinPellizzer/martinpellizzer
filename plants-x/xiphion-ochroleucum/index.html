Xiphion Ochroleucum

Xiphion ochroleucum is a rare and striking plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that stand out against its green foliage. This plant thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Due to its limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
