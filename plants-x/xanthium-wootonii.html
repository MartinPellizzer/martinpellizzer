Xanthium wootonii

Xanthium wootonii, commonly known as rough cocklebur, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its spiny, rough-textured leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces spherical, prickly fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. Xanthium wootonii is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
