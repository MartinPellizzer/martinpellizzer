Xiphion stocksii

Xiphion stocksii, commonly known as the Stocks flower or the Garden Stocks, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Xiphion stocksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
