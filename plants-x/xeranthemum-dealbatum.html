Xeranthemum dealbatum

Xeranthemum dealbatum, commonly known as the bushy rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive, slightly hairy appearance. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and thrives in well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its foliage is typically gray-green, with leaves that are narrow and covered in fine hairs.
