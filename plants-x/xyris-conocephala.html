Xyris conocephala

Xyris conocephala, commonly known as the narrowleaf yellow-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes various species of yellow-eyed grasses. The plant typically grows in wet to moist habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are yellow and clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

Xyris conocephala is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
