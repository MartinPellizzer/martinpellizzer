Xanthophyllum flavescens var. angustifolium

Xanthophyllum flavescens var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goldenbush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to specific regions in South America, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique foliage.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to horticultural collections.
