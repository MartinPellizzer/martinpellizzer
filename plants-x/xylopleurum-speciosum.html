Xylopleurum speciosum

Xylopleurum speciosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Xylopleurum speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
