Xolisma latifolia

Xolisma latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf xolisma, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Xolisma latifolia is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun, making it well adapted to desert conditions.
