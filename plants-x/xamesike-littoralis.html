Xamesike littoralis

Xamesike littoralis, commonly known as the coastal grass.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in sandy or saline soils along coastal areas. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

Xamesike littoralis plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion.
