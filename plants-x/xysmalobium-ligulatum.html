Xysmalobium ligulatum

Xysmalobium ligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its unique, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange. The plant produces strap-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
