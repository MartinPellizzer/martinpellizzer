Xipholepis aspera

Xipholepis aspera, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
