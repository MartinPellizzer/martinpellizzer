Xanthosia pilosa var. glabra

Xanthosia pilosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth xanthosia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
