Xyris ridleyi var. penicillata

Xyris ridleyi var. penicillata, commonly known as the Penicillate Yellow-eyed Grass, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plant species.
