Xylopia cristalensis

Xylopia cristalensis, commonly known as the Crystal Mountain wax tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, waxy leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces smooth, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
