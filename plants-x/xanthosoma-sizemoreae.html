Xanthosoma sizemoreae

Xanthosoma sizemoreae, commonly known as the Sizemore's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by edible, starchy tubers that are used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.

Xanthosoma sizemoreae is valued for its adaptability and nutritional content in tropical agriculture.
