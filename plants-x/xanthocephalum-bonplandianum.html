Xanthocephalum bonplandianum

Xanthocephalum bonplandianum, commonly known as the bonplandian goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in clusters.
