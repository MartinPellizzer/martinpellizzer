Xanthium macrocarpum var. glabratum

Xanthium macrocarpum var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rough, spiny stems and leaves with a distinctive lobed shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the dandelion family. Its fruits are hairy, three-angled follicles that contain seeds with pappus for wind dispersal.

It is native to North America and often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
