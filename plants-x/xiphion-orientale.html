Xiphion orientale

Xiphion orientale, commonly known as the oriental bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue or violet bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Xiphion orientale is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
