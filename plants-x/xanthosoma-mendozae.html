Xanthosoma mendozae

Xanthosoma mendozae, commonly known as the Mendoza arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the humid lowland forests of South America.

Xanthosoma mendozae is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
