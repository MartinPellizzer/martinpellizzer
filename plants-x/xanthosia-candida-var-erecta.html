Xanthosia candida var. erecta

Xanthosia candida var. erecta, commonly known as the erect white daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its delicate appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
