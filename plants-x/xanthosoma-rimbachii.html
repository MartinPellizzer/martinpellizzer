Xanthosoma rimbachii

Xanthosoma rimbachii, commonly known as the red-veined arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Xanthosoma rimbachii is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine.
