Xanthosoma yarumalense

Xanthosoma yarumalense, commonly known as the Yarumal palm, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces an inflorescence with small, greenish-white flowers, which are typically enclosed in a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe.
