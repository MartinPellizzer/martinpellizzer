Xanthosoma daulense

Xanthosoma daulense, commonly known as the Daulen's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically green with a yellow or orange spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma daulense is also valued for its edible leaves, which are used in traditional cuisines.
