Xyris flexuosa

Xyris flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in wet, sandy soils along stream banks and in marshy areas.

Xyris flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland habitats.
