Xanthoxalis Stricta Subsp. Villicaulis

Yellow oxalis, scientifically known as Xanthoxalis stricta subsp. villicaulis, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive leaf structure. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Often used in ornamental gardening, it adds a touch of color to gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
