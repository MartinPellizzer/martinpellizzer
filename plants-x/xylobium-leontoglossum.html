Xylobium leontoglossum

Xylobium leontoglossum, commonly known as the leopard orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that resemble leopards' spots. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are zygomorphic, with three sepals and three petals, and it produces small, round seed pods.

Xylobium leontoglossum is a perennial herb that is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and vibrant floral display.
