Xanthosia pilosa var. pannosa

Xanthosia pilosa var. pannosa, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to dry conditions.

The variety pannosa is distinguished by its more pronounced woolly covering compared to other varieties.
