Xantolis psammophila

Xantolis psammophila, commonly known as the sand dune lily, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand harsh, dry conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve water.

Xantolis psammophila plays an important role in stabilizing sand dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
