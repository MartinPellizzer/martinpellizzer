Xanthosoma munchiquense

Xanthosoma munchiquense, commonly known as the Munchique arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped inflorescences with yellow spadices and white to greenish flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests and humid lowland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
