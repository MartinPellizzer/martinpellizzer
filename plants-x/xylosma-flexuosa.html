Xylosma flexuosa

Xylosma flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible xylosma, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal uses.

Xylosma flexuosa is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
