Xanthosoma holtonianum

Xanthosoma holtonianum, commonly known as the Holton's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive inflorescence. The plant features a short, stout rhizome and produces showy, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma holtonianum is also valued for its edible leaves, which are used in traditional cuisines.
