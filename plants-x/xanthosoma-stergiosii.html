Xanthosoma stergiosii

Xanthosoma stergiosii, commonly known as the Stergios' arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma stergiosii is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
