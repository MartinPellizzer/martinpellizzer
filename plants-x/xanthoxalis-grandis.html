Xanthoxalis grandis

Xanthoxalis grandis, commonly known as yellow oxalis, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves with bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Xanthoxalis grandis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate partial shade and is relatively easy to care for.
