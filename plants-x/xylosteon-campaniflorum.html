Xylosteon campaniflorum

Xylosteon campaniflorum, commonly known as the bellflower xylosteon, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth texture.

The botanical features of Xylosteon campaniflorum make it a notable species in the study of tropical flora.
