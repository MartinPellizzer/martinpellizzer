Xiphotheca guthriei

Xiphotheca guthriei, commonly known as the Guthrie's sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

Xiphotheca guthriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
