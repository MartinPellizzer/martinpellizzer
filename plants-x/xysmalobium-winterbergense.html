Xysmalobium winterbergense

Xysmalobium winterbergense, commonly known as the Winterberg's ghost orchid, is a rare and unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South Africa, specifically the Western Cape. This plant is characterized by its slender, leafless stems and striking, white to pale purple flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
