Xylopia hastarum

Xylopia hastarum, commonly known as the Caribbean star anise, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, white flowers that are often clustered in umbels. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a rough texture.

The fruit, which resembles a star, contains multiple seeds and is used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
