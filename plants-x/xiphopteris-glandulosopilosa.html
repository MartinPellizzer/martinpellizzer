Xiphopteris glandulosopilosa

Xiphopteris glandulosopilosa, commonly known as the glandular-pilose holly fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features small, glandular hairs on its leaves, which give it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
