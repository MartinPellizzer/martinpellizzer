Xerophyta stenophylla

Xerophyta stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and stems, which help it survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Xerophyta stenophylla is adapted to withstand drought and extreme temperatures, showcasing its role as a xerophyte.
