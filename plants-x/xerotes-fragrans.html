Xerotes fragrans

Xerotes fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant desert orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, fragrant blossoms that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant has adapted to survive in extreme drought conditions through deep root systems and waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Its flowers bloom during brief periods of rainfall, making it a symbol of resilience in harsh environments.

Xerotes fragrans is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators in desert ecosystems.
