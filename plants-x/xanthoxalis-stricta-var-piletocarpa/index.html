Xanthoxalis Stricta Var. Piletocarpa

Yellow oxalis, scientifically known as Xanthoxalis stricta var. piletocarpa, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. It is prized for its vibrant yellow blooms and distinctive leaf patterns, which make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of climates. Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season. With proper care, Yellow oxalis can add a touch of elegance and color to any garden setting.
