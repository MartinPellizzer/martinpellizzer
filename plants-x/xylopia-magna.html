Xylopia magna

Xylopia magna, commonly known as the giant xylopia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its large, simple leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, hard seeds. Xylopia magna is known for its aromatic bark and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
