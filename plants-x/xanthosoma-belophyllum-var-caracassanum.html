Xanthosoma belophyllum var. caracassanum

Xanthosoma belophyllum var. caracassanum, commonly known as the Caracassanum arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow-orange flowers that bloom during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to add a dramatic touch to gardens and indoor spaces.
