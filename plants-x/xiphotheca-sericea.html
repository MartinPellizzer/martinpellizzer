Xiphotheca sericea

Xiphotheca sericea, commonly known as the silky xiphotheca, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Xiphotheca sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
