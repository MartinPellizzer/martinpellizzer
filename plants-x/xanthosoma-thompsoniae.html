Xanthosoma thompsoniae

Xanthosoma thompsoniae, commonly known as the Thompson's arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
