Xanthocercis Zambesiaca

Xanthocercis zambesiaca is a strikingly beautiful tree native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, it adds a burst of color to its natural habitat. This species is often found in riverine forests and along seasonal watercourses. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to many plant enthusiasts worldwide.
