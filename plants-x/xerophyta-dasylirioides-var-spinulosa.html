Xerophyta dasylirioides var. spinulosa

Xerophyta dasylirioides var. spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny lily, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage composed of thick, fleshy, spiny leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, tall flower stalks that bear showy, white or pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is valued both ornationally and for its ecological benefits.
