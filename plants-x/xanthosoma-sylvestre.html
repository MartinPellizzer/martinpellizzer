Xanthosoma sylvestre

Xanthosoma sylvestre, commonly known as the wild elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers enclosed in a spathe, which is typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
