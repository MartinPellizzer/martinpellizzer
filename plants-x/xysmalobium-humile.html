Xysmalobium humile

Xysmalobium humile, commonly known as the dwarf fairy lantern, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its unique, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its compact size and striking floral display make it a notable species in its native habitat.
