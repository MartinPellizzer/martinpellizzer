Xamesike depressa

Xamesike depressa, commonly known as the African violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, waxy leaves that are typically green with silver or purple markings. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white, which bloom from the center of the leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Xamesike depressa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
