Ximenesia hirta

Ximenesia hirta, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its woody, shrub-like structure and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Ximenesia hirta is valued for its hardy nature and has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties.
