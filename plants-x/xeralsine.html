Xeralsine

Xeralsine, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The leaves are narrow and covered in a silvery, waxy coating, which helps to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Xeralsine thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among botanists and gardeners alike.
