Xanthorrhoea preissii

Xanthorrhoea preissii, commonly known as the grass tree, is a unique plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, fibrous trunk and a dense crown of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a distinctive yellowish resin, which gives it its scientific name. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and can survive in harsh, arid conditions.

Xanthorrhoea preissii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
