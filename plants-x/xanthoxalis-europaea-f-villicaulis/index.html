Xanthoxalis Europaea F. Villicaulis

Yellow oxalis, scientifically known as Xanthoxalis europaea f. villicaulis, is a striking member of the Oxalidaceae family that captivates with its vibrant yellow flowers and delicate foliage. Native to parts of Europe, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its unique leaf structure, which resembles a rosette of small, heart-shaped leaves, adds to its ornamental appeal. The plant's ability to flourish in cool climates makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of wild beauty. With its cheerful blooms and easy care requirements, Yellow oxalis is both a botanical curiosity and a valuable addition to any garden.
