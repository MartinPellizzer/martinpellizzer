Xerochlamys tampoketsensis var. rupestris

Xerochlamys tampoketsensis var. rupestris, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful floral displays. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a strong, spicy fragrance.
