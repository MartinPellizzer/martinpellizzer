Xiphopteris allosuroides

Xiphopteris allosuroides, commonly known as the slender chain fern, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow fronds that grow in a cascading pattern, often forming dense, hanging clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous short, upright sporangium-bearing stems.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
