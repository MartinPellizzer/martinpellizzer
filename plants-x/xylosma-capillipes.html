Xylosma capillipes

Xylosma capillipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed xylosma, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Tiliaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
