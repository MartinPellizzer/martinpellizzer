Xanthophyllum adenotus var. lineare

Xanthophyllum adenotus var. lineare, commonly known as the linear xanthophyllum, is a variant of the genus Xanthophyllum, which belongs to the family Arecaceae.

This plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. It is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often yellow or orange in color, followed by fruit that contains edible seeds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
