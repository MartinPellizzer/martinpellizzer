Xanthosoma trilobum

Xanthosoma trilobum, commonly known as the arrowleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

The plant belongs to the Araceae family and produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
