Ximenia americana var. oxypyrena

Ximenia americana var. oxypyrena, commonly known as the red ximenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant produces small, round, yellow to orange fruits that contain several seeds. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures for medicinal purposes.
