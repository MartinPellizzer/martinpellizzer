Xanthosoma cremersii

Xanthosoma cremersii, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, making it a striking addition to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma cremersii is also valued for its edible tubers, which are used in traditional cuisines.
