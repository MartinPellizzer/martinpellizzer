Xanthoxalis ortgiesii

Xanthoxalis ortgiesii, commonly known as the Ortgies' goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are typically lobed or divided, and its stems are often hairy. Xanthoxalis ortgiesii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
