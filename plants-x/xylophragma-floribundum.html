Xylophragma floribundum

Xylophragma floribundum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, waxy leaves. Xylophragma floribundum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
