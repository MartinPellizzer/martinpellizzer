Xantholinum nodiflorum

Xantholinum nodiflorum, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique floral display.
