Xylosma tweediana

Xylosma tweediana, commonly known as the African xylosma, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
