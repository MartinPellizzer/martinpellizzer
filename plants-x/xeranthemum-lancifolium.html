Xeranthemum lancifolium

Xeranthemum lancifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf erinacea, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It is drought-tolerant and has a compact growth habit, which makes it ideal for border plantings.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
