Xerophyta equisetoides

Xerophyta equisetoides, commonly known as the needleleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, needle-like leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Xerophyta equisetoides is also known for its ability to tolerate frost and is often used in coastal landscapes due to its resilience.
