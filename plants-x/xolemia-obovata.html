Xolemia obovata

Xolemia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed xolemia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the cooler months. Xolemia obovata is adapted to survive in low-water environments, with deep root systems that allow it to access groundwater.
