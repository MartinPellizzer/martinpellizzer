Xanthocephalum amoenum var. intermedium

Xanthocephalum amoenum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flower heads and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It produces small, tubular flowers that are arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

The variety intermedium is distinguished by its intermediate characteristics between the typical var. amoenum and var. glabrum.
