Xysmalobium reticulatum

Xysmalobium reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and open habitats. The plant features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with a distinctive net-like venation pattern that gives it its scientific name.
