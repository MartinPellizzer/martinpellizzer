Xanthosoma silverstonei

Xanthosoma silverstonei, commonly known as the silver stone plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Xanthosoma silverstonei is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
