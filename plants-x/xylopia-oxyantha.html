Xylopia oxyantha

Xylopia oxyantha, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.
