Xylopia salicifolia

Xylopia salicifolia, commonly known as the "willow-leaved xylopia," is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Xylopia salicifolia is valued for its aromatic wood, which is used in traditional craftsmanship, and its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland tropical forests.
