Xeranthemum neumayeri

Xeranthemum neumayeri, commonly known as the Neumayer's erinaceus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, and it has a compact, shrub-like growth habit. Xeranthemum neumayeri is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
