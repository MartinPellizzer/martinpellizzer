Xanthosoma barinasense

Xanthosoma barinasense, commonly known as the Barinasense arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

It is a member of the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow spatheous flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Xanthosoma barinasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique foliage in horticultural settings.
