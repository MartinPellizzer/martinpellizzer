Xeraea regeliana

Xeraea regeliana, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are typically small and fleshy, aiding in water retention.

Xeraea regeliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in drought-prone regions.
