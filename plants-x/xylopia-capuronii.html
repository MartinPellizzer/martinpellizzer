Xylopia capuronii

Xylopia capuronii, commonly known as the African star anise, is a tropical plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise used in cooking. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, contributing to its ornamental value.
