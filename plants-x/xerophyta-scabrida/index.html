Xerophyta Scabrida

Xerophyta scabrida is a hardy succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features spiky leaves and produces small, delicate flowers. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements. This resilient species is a favorite among desert gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
