Xerophyta pulchra

Xerophyta pulchra, commonly known as the "Rainbow Flower," is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding its survival in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Xerophyta pulchra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
