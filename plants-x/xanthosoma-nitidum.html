Xanthosoma nitidum

Xanthosoma nitidum, commonly known as the velvetleaf plant, is a tropical perennial herb belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy, with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant produces inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the arum family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Xanthosoma nitidum is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
