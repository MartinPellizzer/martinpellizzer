Xeroderris chevalieri

Xeroderris chevalieri, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic wood that is highly valued for its durability and unique coloration. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
