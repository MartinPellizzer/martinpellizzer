Xyris morii

Xyris morii, commonly known as the Morii yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes various species of grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Xyris morii typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and along the edges of streams.
