Xiquexique

Xiquexique, commonly known as the "flower of the devil," is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces large, showy flowers with vibrant red and orange hues. The plant's botanical name, *Pteroglossum alatum*, reflects its unique wing-like leaves that are broad and heart-shaped. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
