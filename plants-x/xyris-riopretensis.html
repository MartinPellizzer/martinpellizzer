Xyris riopretensis

Xyris riopretensis, commonly known as the Rio Pretense Yellow-eyed Grass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and wetlands.
