Xiphopteris rigescens

Xiphopteris rigescens, commonly known as the rigid fern, is a species of fern characterized by its stiff, upright fronds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries like India, China, and Japan. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Xiphopteris rigescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
