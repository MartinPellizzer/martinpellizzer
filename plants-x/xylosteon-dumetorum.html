Xylosteon dumetorum

Xylosteon dumetorum, commonly known as the dwarf false nettle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Xylosteon dumetorum is notable for its adaptive features that allow it to thrive in arid environments.
