Xysmalobium undulatum var. ensifolium

Xysmalobium undulatum var. ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and sword-shaped, giving the plant its common name, and are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

The plant is part of the Ranunculaceae family, known for its showy flowers and often toxic properties.
