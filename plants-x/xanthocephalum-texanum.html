Xanthocephalum texanum

Xanthocephalum texanum, commonly known as Texas goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, while the flowers are yellow with a prominent central disk.
