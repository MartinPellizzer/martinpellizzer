Xanthisma Gymnocephalum

Desert sandmat, scientifically known as Xanthisma gymnocephalum, is a resilient plant adapted to thrive in harsh desert environments. This member of the Amaranthaceae family is characterized by its compact growth and pale, sand-colored foliage. It is commonly found in arid regions of North America, particularly in the Great Basin and Mojave Desert. Despite its unassuming appearance, the plant plays a vital role in stabilizing sand dunes and supporting local wildlife. Its unique adaptations make it a fascinating subject for ecological and botanical study.
