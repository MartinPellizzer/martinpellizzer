Xeranthemum frigidum

Xeranthemum frigidum, commonly known as the cold weather anthem, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a hardy perennial that thrives in cool climates and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, showy blooms in shades of yellow and white. Xeranthemum frigidum is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its resilience to cold temperatures makes it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate and colder regions.
