Xyris dusenii

Xyris dusenii, commonly known as the dusen's goldeneye, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wetland habitats. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to its environment. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of ponds.

Xyris dusenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
