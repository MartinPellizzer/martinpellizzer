Xyris papuana

Xyris papuana, commonly known as the Papuan yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to Papua New Guinea.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in wet, swampy areas and is adapted to tropical climates.

Xyris papuana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
