Xanthoxalis Fontana Subsp. Villicaulis

Yellow oxalis, scientifically known as Xanthoxalis fontana subsp. villicaulis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the wetlands of South America. Its delicate yellow blooms and heart-shaped leaves make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and water gardens. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden conditions. The plant's unique foliage and vibrant flowers add both color and texture to any landscape. With its easy care requirements and aesthetic appeal, Yellow oxalis is a versatile addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
