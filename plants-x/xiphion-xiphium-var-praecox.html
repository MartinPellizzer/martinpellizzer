Xiphion xiphium var. praecox

Xiphion xiphium var. praecox, commonly known as the early purple coneflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, spiky flower head composed of numerous small, tubular florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological landscapes.
