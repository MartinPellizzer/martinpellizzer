Xylophacos amphioxys

Xylophacos amphioxys, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor firmly in sandy or rocky soils.
