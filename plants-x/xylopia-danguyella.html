Xylopia danguyella

Xylopia danguyella, commonly known as the African star anise, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is native to western and central Africa, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and compound leaves with a distinct aromatic scent. Its fruit is a star-shaped, five-lobed capsule that contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it valuable for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
