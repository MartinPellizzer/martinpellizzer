Xanthosoma hoffmannii

Xanthosoma hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the genus Xanthosoma. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
