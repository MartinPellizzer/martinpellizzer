Xiphion aitchisonii

Xiphion aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Xiphion aitchisonii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegance and resilience.
