Xylopia degeneri

Xylopia degeneri, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a globose, fleshy berry that turns yellow when mature.

The plant has a distinctive aromatic scent and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
