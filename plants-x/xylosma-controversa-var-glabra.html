Xylosma controversa var. glabra

Xylosma controversa var. glabra, commonly known as smooth xylosma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and resilience.
