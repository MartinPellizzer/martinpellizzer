Xylophragma tenue

Xylophragma tenue, commonly known as the slender wood-fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces both sterile and fertile fronds. Its leaves are pinnately compound with a soft, grass-like texture.

Xylophragma tenue prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
