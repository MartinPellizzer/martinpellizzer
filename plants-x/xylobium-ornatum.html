Xylobium ornatum

Xylobium ornatum, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Xylobium ornatum is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
