Xanthosoma bakhuisense

Xanthosoma bakhuisense, commonly known as the Bakhuis arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, producing a yellowish-green color.

Xanthosoma bakhuisense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
