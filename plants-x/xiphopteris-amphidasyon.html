Xiphopteris amphidasyon

Xiphopteris amphidasyon, commonly known as the broad-leafed huperzia, is a species of clubmoss native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact, low-growing habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often broader at the base, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Xiphopteris amphidasyon is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in maintaining ecological balance in its native habitats.
