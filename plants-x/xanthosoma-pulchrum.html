Xanthosoma pulchrum

Xanthosoma pulchrum, commonly known as the velvetleaf taro, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers that are typically borne on upright stems. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

Xanthosoma pulchrum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
