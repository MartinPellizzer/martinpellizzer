Xyladenius

Xyladenius, commonly known as the goldenrod hawkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and grasslands. Xyladenius has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in temperate regions.
