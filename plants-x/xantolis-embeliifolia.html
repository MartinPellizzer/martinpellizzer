Xantolis embeliifolia

Xantolis embeliifolia, commonly known as the emerald leaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It thrives in humid environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect sunlight. The plant's leaves are typically broad and elongated, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its lush, green foliage.

Xantolis embeliifolia is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
