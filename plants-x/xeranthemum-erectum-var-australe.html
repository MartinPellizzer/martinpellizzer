Xeranthemum erectum var. australe

Xeranthemum erectum var. australe, commonly known as the Australian upright thyme, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing herb with grey-green, aromatic foliage and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.

The variety 'australe' is distinguished by its upright growth habit and compact form compared to other varieties.
