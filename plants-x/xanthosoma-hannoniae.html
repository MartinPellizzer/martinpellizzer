Xanthosoma hannoniae

Xanthosoma hannoniae, commonly known as the Hannon's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Xanthosoma hannoniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
