Xanthomyrtus prostrata

Xanthomyrtus prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate myrtle, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces small, fleshy berries.

Xanthomyrtus prostrata is often used in landscaping for its dense, ground-covering growth habit.
