Xanthium Spinosum F. Inerme

Xanthium spinosum f. inerme is a unique variant of the common cocklebur plant known for its reduced spines. This form exhibits a more delicate appearance compared to its spiky relatives. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides where it thrives in open environments. The plant's smooth leaves and smaller seeds make it distinct from the typical Xanthium species. Despite its mild appearance, it still possesses the same ecological role as its spiny counterparts.
