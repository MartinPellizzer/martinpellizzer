Xeropetalum multiflorum

Xeropetalum multiflorum, commonly known as the multibloom xeropetalum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage consists of narrow, evergreen leaves that remain lush throughout the growing season.

Xeropetalum multiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance, making it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
