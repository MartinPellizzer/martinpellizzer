Xyris delicatula

Xyris delicatula, commonly known as the slender yellow-eyed grass, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Xyris delicatula typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or swampy areas.

Its compact size and delicate appearance make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
