Xanthosoma mafaffa var. typicum

Xanthosoma mafaffa var. typicum, commonly known as the African arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma mafaffa var. typicum is also used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.
