Xysmalobium tysonianum

Xysmalobium tysonianum, commonly known as the Tyson's ghost orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. The plant produces unique, ghostly white flowers with intricate patterns that resemble skeletal structures, giving it its common name. It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters.
