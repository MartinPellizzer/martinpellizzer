Xanthosoma galianoi

Xanthosoma galianoi, commonly known as the Galiano elephant ear, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma galianoi is also valued for its edible leaves, which are used in traditional cuisines.
