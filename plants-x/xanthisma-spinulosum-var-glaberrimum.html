Xanthisma spinulosum var. glaberrimum

Xanthisma spinulosum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smoothleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. Its leaves are generally smooth and glabrous, distinguishing it from other varieties of Xanthisma.

This species is often found in desert environments, contributing to the ecological diversity of such habitats.
