Xerochloa cheribon

Xerochloa cheribon, commonly known as the desert grass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit, which help it conserve water in harsh environments. Xerochloa cheribon is well adapted to withstand high temperatures and low precipitation, making it a resilient component of desert ecosystems.
