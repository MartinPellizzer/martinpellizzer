Xylon rivieri

Xylon rivieri, commonly known as the riverwood plant, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Xylon rivieri is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
