Xanthomyrtus koebrensis

Xanthomyrtus koebrensis, commonly known as the yellow myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Xanthomyrtus koebrensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
