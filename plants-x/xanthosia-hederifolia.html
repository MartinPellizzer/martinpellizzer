Xanthosia hederifolia

Xanthosia hederifolia, commonly known as the ivy-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy leaves that resemble those of ivy, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Xanthosia hederifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
