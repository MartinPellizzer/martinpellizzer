Xolemia latifolia

Xolemia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf xolemia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom during the summer months. Xolemia latifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and pleasant floral scent.
