Xanthophytum glomeratum

Xanthophytum glomeratum, commonly known as the clustered yellowweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit. The plant produces yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Xanthophytum glomeratum is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas.
