Xylopiastrum taiense

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests, characterized by its dense canopy and rapid growth rate. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
