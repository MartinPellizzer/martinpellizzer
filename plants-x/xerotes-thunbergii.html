Xerotes thunbergii

Xerotes thunbergii, commonly known as the Japanese evergreen holly, is a shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces white flowers in spring, followed by red berries that attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and gardens.
