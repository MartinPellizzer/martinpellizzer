Xeranthemum nobile

Xeranthemum nobile, commonly known as the noble xeranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces showy, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in summer. Xeranthemum nobile is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
