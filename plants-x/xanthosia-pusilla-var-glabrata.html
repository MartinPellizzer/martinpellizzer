Xanthosia pusilla var. glabrata

Xanthosia pusilla var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth dwarf sunflower, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features compact, upright growth with narrow, linear leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in a cluster at the tip of the stem, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
