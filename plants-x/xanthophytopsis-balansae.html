Xanthophytopsis balansae

Xanthophytopsis balansae, commonly known as the Balansae lichen, is a unique foliose lichen that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green thallus, which contains a high concentration of xanthophylls, giving it its distinctive color. The lichen forms a symbiotic relationship with a green alga, which provides photosynthetic capabilities. It is typically found on tree trunks and rocks in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
