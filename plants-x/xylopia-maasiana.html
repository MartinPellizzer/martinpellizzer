Xylopia maasiana

Xylopia maasiana, commonly known as the African star anise, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and Eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, pungent fruits that resemble star anise. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are followed by dark, star-shaped berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The tree is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its antispasmodic and digestive benefits.
