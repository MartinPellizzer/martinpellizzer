Xantolis boniana var. boniana

Xantolis boniana var. boniana, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, bright yellow to orange fronds that radiate from a central stem, creating a striking, star-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, black berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and ease of care in indoor settings.
