Xanthophyllum virens

Xanthophyllum virens, commonly known as the green flame plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant green leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
