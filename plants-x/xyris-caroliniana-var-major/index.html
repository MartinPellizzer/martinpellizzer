Xyris Caroliniana Var. Major

Xyris caroliniana var. major is a strikingly unique plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the grass family and is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. this variant is distinguished by its larger size and more robust growth compared to its close relatives. it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and forest edges. due to its ecological importance and aesthetic appeal, it is a valuable addition to native plant gardens and conservation efforts.
