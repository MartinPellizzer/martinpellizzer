Xanthosia peduncularis

Xanthosia peduncularis, commonly known as the slender sandblow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, upright stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Xanthosia peduncularis is often found in coastal and inland regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
