Xyridanthe stricta

Xyridanthe stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf xyrid, is a perennial herb native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in disturbed sites such as roadsides and dry open woodlands.

Xyridanthe stricta plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insect species.
