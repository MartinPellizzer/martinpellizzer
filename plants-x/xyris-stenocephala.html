Xyris stenocephala

Xyris stenocephala, commonly known as narrow-headed yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Xyridaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

The leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.
