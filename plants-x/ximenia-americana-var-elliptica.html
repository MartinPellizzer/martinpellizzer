Ximenia americana var. elliptica

Ximenia americana var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic ximenia, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It features elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing a single seed. Its bark is thick and fissured, often exuding a milky sap when damaged.

This species is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.
