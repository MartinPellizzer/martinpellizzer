Xylopia polyantha var. nicaraguensis

Xylopia polyantha var. nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan xylopia, is a species of flowering plant in the Annonaceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in Nicaragua and adjacent regions. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, woody stems and simple, alternate leaves. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
