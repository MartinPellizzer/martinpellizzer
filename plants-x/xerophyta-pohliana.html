Xerophyta pohliana

Xerophyta pohliana, commonly known as the Pohliana ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its drought-resistant foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Xerophyta pohliana is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion in dry landscapes.
