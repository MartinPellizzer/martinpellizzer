Xanthosoma jaramilloi

Xanthosoma jaramilloi, commonly known as the Jaramillo's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the humid lowland forests of Central and South America. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and yellow markings. It produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Xanthosoma jaramilloi is cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
