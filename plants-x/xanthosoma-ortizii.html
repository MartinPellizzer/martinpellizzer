Xanthosoma ortizii

Xanthosoma ortizii, commonly known as the Ortiz's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a spathe, a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The plant's botanical features make it a unique addition to tropical landscapes.
