Xanthophyllum excelsum

Xanthophyllum excelsum, commonly known as the golden bell plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its striking yellow bracts that surround the small white flowers, creating a visually appealing display. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. The leaves are typically broad and arrow-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces due to its vibrant appearance and ease of care.
