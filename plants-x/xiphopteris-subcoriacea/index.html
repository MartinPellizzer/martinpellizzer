Xiphopteris Subcoriacea

Xiphopteris subcoriacea is a rare and unique fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive, leathery fronds that give it a rugged appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its slow growth and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its ecological importance, Xiphopteris subcoriacea remains poorly studied and faces threats from habitat loss.
