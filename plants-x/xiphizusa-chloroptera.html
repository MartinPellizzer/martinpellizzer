Xiphizusa chloroptera

Xiphizusa chloroptera, commonly known as the greenleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. Xiphizusa chloroptera thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
