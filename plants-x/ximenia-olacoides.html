Ximenia olacoides

Ximenia olacoides, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Olacaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and clusters of small, white flowers that resemble snowballs. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry woodland and savanna habitats.

Ximenia olacoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
