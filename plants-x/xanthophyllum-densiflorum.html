Xanthophyllum densiflorum

Xanthophyllum densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower sun-plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are clustered densely along the stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

Xanthophyllum densiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
