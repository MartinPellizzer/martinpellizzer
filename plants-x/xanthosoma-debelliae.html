Xanthosoma debelliae

Xanthosoma debelliae, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers enclosed in a leaf-like structure called a spadix, which is often surrounded by a colorful bract. Xanthosoma debelliae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
