Xanthosoma daguense var. daguense

Xanthosoma daguense var. daguense, commonly known as the Daguense arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces striking yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Xanthosoma daguense var. daguense is also valued for its edible leaves, which are used in local cuisines.
