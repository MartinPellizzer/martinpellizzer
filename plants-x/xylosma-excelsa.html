Xylosma excelsa

Xylosma excelsa, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stem and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in the summer months. Xylosma excelsa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
