Xiphopteris alternidens

Xiphopteris alternidens, commonly known as the zigzag water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that exhibit a distinctive zigzag pattern along their length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in marshy areas or along stream banks. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that release spores for propagation.

Xiphopteris alternidens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens.
