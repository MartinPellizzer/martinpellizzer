Xyris stenotera

Xyris stenotera, commonly known as narrowleaf yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes many species of grass-like plants. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in wetlands.

Xyris stenotera is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
