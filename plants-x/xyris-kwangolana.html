Xyris kwangolana

Xyris kwangolana, commonly known as the Kwangolana yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Xyris kwangolana typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats.

It is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.
