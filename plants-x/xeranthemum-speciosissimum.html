Xeranthemum speciosissimum

Xeranthemum speciosissimum, commonly known as the showy xeranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in gray-green, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically yellow with purple centers, blooming in summer.

Xeranthemum speciosissimum is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
