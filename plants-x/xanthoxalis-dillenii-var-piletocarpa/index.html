Xanthoxalis Dillenii Var. Piletocarpa

Yellow oxalis, scientifically known as Xanthoxalis dillenii var. piletocarpa, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves that give it a unique and eye-catching appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its delicate blooms make it a popular addition to both ornamental gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to flourish.
