Xiphopteris sodiroi

Xiphopteris sodiroi, commonly known as the Mexican sword fern, is a species of fern native to Central America and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. Xiphopteris sodiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and indoor settings.
