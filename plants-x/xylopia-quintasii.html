Xylopia quintasii

Xylopia quintasii, commonly known as the Madagascar nutmeg.

It is a member of the Annonaceae family and is native to Madagascar. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. Its fruit is a globose capsule that contains aromatic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine.
