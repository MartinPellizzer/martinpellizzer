Xylosma latifolia

Xylosma latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf xylosma, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically smooth and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Xylosma latifolia thrives in well-drained soils and is adaptable to a range of environmental conditions.
