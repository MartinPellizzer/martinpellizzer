Xanthophyllum fragrans

Xanthophyllum fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant yellow-flowered plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in a composite head. Xanthophyllum fragrans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
