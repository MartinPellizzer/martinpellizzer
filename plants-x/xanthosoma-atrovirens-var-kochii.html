Xanthosoma atrovirens var. kochii

Xanthosoma atrovirens var. kochii, commonly known as the dark green arrow leaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, starchy tubers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and culinary uses.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both gardens and agricultural systems.
