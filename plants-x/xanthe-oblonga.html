Xanthe oblonga

Xanthe oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically gray-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, elongated inflorescences. Xanthe oblonga is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
