Xanthosoma helleborifolium f. variegatum

Xanthosoma helleborifolium f. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated elephant ear, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive variegation.

The foliage often features a mix of green, white, and yellow hues, creating an eye-catching appearance. It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is typically grown as an ornamental due to its dramatic foliage, though it can also be used in some culinary applications.

It prefers well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
