Xeranthemum squamosum

Xeranthemum squamosum, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in dry, rocky environments, showcasing its drought-resistant adaptations. The plant features upright stems covered in small, scale-like leaves that reduce water loss. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Xeranthemum squamosum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
