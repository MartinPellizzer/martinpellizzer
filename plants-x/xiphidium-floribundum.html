Xiphidium floribundum

Xiphidium floribundum, commonly known as the showy goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and dry habitats, such as deserts and arid grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs that help reduce water loss.

Xiphidium floribundum is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
