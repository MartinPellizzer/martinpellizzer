Xuris

Xuris, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features silvery-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs. Xuris produces delicate, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appearance.
