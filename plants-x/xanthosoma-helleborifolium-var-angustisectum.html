Xanthosoma helleborifolium var. angustisectum

Xanthosoma helleborifolium var. angustisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in humid climates.

Its foliage is often used in landscaping for its dramatic visual impact.
