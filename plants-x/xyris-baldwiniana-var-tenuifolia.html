Xyris baldwiniana var. tenuifolia

Xyris baldwiniana var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf yellowroot, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes many species of grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in wet to moist soil conditions and is often found in swampy or marshy areas.

The botanical characteristics of this variety make it an important species for wetland ecosystems.
