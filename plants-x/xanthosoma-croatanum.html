Xanthosoma croatanum

Xanthosoma croatanum, commonly known as the Croatan arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Xanthosoma croatanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its attractive foliage in botanical gardens and private collections.
