Xiphion verum

Xiphion verum, commonly known as the true lily of the Nile, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous root system. Xiphion verum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
