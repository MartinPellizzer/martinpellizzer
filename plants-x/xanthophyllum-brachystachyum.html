Xanthophyllum brachystachyum

Xanthophyllum brachystachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed yellow-flowered plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and narrow, upright stems. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Xanthophyllum brachystachyum is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
