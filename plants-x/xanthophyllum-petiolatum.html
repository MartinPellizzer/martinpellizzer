Xanthophyllum petiolatum

Xanthophyllum petiolatum, commonly known as the yellowleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow petals and elongated, leafy stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
