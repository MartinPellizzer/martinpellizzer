Xanthosoma brevispathaceum

Xanthosoma brevispathaceum, commonly known as the elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its distinctive inflorescence structure. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
