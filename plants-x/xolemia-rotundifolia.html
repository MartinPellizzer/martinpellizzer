Xolemia rotundifolia

Xolemia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed xolemia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Xolemia rotundifolia prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and pleasant aroma.
