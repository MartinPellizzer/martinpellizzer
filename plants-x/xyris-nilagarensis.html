Xyris nilagarensis

Xyris nilagarensis, commonly known as the Nilagaren yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, preferring moist, acidic soils.

Xyris nilagarensis is often found in lowland areas and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
