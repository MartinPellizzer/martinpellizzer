Xanthosoma rupununiense

Xanthosoma rupununiense, commonly known as the Rupununi arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela and Guyana.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive inflorescence. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are broad and can reach up to 1 meter in length, making it a striking addition to botanical gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential in traditional medicine.
