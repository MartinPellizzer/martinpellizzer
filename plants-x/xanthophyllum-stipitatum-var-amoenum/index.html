Xanthophyllum Stipitatum Var. Amoenum

Xanthophyllum stipitatum var. amoenum is a rare and intriguing plant species known for its unique floral structure and vibrant yellow hue. This variant is particularly notable for its elongated, stalked flowers that stand out against its foliage.
