Xanthosoma cubense

Xanthosoma cubense, commonly known as the Cuban arrowleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its flowers are small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Xanthosoma cubense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in warm climates.
