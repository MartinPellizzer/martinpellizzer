Xanthophyllon

Xanthophyllon, commonly known as the golden leaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant contains xanthophylls, which are carotenoids responsible for its distinctive golden color. Xanthophyllon thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically beneficial.
