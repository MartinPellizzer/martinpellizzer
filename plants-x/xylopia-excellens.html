Xylopia excellens

Xylopia excellens, commonly known as the African allspice or African star anise, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, spindle-shaped fruits. The plant produces small white flowers that are often clustered in leaf axils, followed by elongated, ribbed berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a strong, spicy scent.

Xylopia excellens is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for treating various ailments.
