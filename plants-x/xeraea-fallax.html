Xeraea fallax

Xeraea fallax, commonly known as the desert star, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its shallow root system enables it to absorb moisture from the surface soil efficiently.

Xeraea fallax is well adapted to full sun and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for xeriscaping.
