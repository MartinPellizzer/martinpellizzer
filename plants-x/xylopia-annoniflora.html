Xylopia annoniflora

Xylopia annoniflora, commonly known as the West African star anise, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple, star-shaped berries. Its fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to star anise.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
