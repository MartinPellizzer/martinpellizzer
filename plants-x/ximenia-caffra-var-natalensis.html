Ximenia caffra var. natalensis

Ximenia caffra var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal ximenia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by small, fleshy, orange-red berries. Natal ximenia is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
