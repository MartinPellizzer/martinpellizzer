Xylopia sessiliflora

Xylopia sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flower xylopia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its rough, bark-like stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, followed by woody, spiny fruits.
