Xanthosoma daguense

Xanthosoma daguense, commonly known as the Daguense arrowleaf.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by edible, starchy tubers.

Xanthosoma daguense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential as a food source in certain regions.
