Xylopia dehiscens

Xylopia dehiscens, commonly known as the African pepper tree, is a member of the Annonaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

Xylopia dehiscens is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
