Xerophyta schnizleinia

Xerophyta schnizleinia, commonly known as the Schizlein's ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer. Its growth habit is prostrate, forming dense mats that help reduce soil erosion.

Xerophyta schnizleinia is well adapted to high sunlight and low rainfall, making it a resilient component of its ecosystem.
