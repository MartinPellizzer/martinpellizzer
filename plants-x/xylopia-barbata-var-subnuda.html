Xylopia barbata var. subnuda

Xylopia barbata var. subnuda, commonly known as the smooth xylopia, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and elliptical leaves with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark, fleshy berries. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
