Xylopia meridensis

Xylopia meridensis, commonly known as the Mexican tamarind tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central America and Mexico.

Xylopia meridensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
