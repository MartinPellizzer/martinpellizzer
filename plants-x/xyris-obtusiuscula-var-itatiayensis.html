Xyris obtusiuscula var. itatiayensis

Xyris obtusiuscula var. itatiayensis, commonly known as the Itatiaya yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herb native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
