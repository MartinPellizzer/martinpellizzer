Xanthosoma isabellanum

Xanthosoma isabellanum, commonly known as the Isabelline arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking yellow flowers arranged in a spadix, surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its foliage is used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in horticulture.
