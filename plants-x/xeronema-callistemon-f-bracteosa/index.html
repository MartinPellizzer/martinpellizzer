Xeronema Callistemon F. Bracteosa

Xeronema callistemon f. bracteosa is a unique variant of the native New Zealand plant known for its striking floral display. This cultivar features elongated, bract-like structures that resemble flowers, creating an eye-catching appearance. It thrives in coastal environments and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental qualities. The plant is often used in gardens to add visual interest and biodiversity. Its distinctive form makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and landscape designers.
