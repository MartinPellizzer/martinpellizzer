Xeraea mucronata

Xeraea mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed xeraea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Xeraea mucronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
