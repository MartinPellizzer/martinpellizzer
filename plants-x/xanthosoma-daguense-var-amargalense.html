Xanthosoma daguense var. amargalense

Xanthosoma daguense var. amargalense, commonly known as the Amargalense arrowleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
