Xanthochymus ovalifolius

Xanthochymus ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaf goldenroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branched stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The leaves are oval-shaped, with a smooth surface and a distinct midrib. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
