Xolemia longiflora

Xolemia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered beauty, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of showy, elongated flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant blooms. This species thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Xolemia longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
