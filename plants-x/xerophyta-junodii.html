Xerophyta junodii

Xerophyta junodii, commonly known as the Junod's ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, a key adaptation for surviving in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
