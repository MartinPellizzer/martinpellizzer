Xerosphaera fragifera subsp. bonannii

Xerosphaera fragifera subsp.

bonannii, commonly known as the Bonannii brittlestem, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are usually small and scale-like, reducing water loss through transpiration.

This subspecies is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.
