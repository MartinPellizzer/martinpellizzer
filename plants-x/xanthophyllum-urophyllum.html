Xanthophyllum urophyllum

Xanthophyllum urophyllum, commonly known as the goldenleaf sunplant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it its common name. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Xanthophyllum urophyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
