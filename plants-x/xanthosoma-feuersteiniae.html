Xanthosoma feuersteiniae

Xanthosoma feuersteiniae, commonly known as the Feuerstein's arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is a common feature in the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Xanthosoma feuersteiniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by some indigenous communities.
