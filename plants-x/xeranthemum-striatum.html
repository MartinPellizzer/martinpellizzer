Xeranthemum striatum

Xeranthemum striatum, commonly known as the striped carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters, producing a striking visual effect.

Xeranthemum striatum is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
