Xanthosoma longepedunculum

Xanthosoma longepedunculum, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its foliage.

Xanthosoma longepedunculum is also valued for its edible leaves, which are used in various traditional cuisines.
