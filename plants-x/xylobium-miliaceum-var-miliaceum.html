Xylobium miliaceum var. miliaceum

Xylobium miliaceum var. miliaceum, commonly known as the millet mallow, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a velvety texture and a soft green color. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its fibrous roots are notable for their ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
