Xylopia ambanjensis

Xylopia ambanjensis, commonly known as the Madagascar pepper tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture and emits a strong, spicy scent when crushed. Xylopia ambanjensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in local culinary practices.
