Xylopia barbata var. dolichopetala

Xylopia barbata var. dolichopetala, commonly known as the long-petioled xylopia, is a species native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a berry that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.
