Xyris guillenii

Xyris guillenii, commonly known as the Guillen's yellowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats.

Xyris guillenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
