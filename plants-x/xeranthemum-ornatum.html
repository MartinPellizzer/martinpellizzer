Xeranthemum ornatum

Xeranthemum ornatum, commonly known as the showy sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Xeranthemum ornatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
