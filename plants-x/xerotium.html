Xerotium

Xerotium, commonly known as the "desert lily," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Amaryllidaceae.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and showy, often fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. These plants are popular in xeriscaping due to their drought tolerance and ornamental value.
