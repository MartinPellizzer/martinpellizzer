Xanthocephalum grande

Xanthocephalum grande, commonly known as the large yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Xanthocephalum grande is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
