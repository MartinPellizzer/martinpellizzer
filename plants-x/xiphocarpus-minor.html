Xiphocarpus minor

Xiphocarpus minor, commonly known as the dwarf pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, leathery foliage with dark green leaves that are often slightly aromatic. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by yellowish-orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Xiphocarpus minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its compact growth habit.
