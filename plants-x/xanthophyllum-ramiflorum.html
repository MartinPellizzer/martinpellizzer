Xanthophyllum ramiflorum

Xanthophyllum ramiflorum, commonly known as the golden-flowered broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, drought-tolerant plants.
