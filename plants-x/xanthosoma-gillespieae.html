Xanthosoma gillespieae

Xanthosoma gillespieae, commonly known as the Gillespie's arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the wet tropical regions of Central America.

Xanthosoma gillespieae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
