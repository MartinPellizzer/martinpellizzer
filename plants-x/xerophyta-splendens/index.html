Xerophyta Splendens

Xerophyta splendens is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers that bloom in late winter. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Xerophyta splendens is both a hardy and visually appealing addition to any drought-tolerant landscape.
