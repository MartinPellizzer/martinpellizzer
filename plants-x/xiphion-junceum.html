Xiphion junceum

Xiphion junceum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Xiphion junceum is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-drained conditions.
