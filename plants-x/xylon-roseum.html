Xylon roseum

Xylon roseum, commonly known as the rosewood tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic wood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. The plant has large, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

Xylon roseum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the timber it provides.
