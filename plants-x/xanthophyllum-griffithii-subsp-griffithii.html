Xanthophyllum griffithii subsp. griffithii

Xanthophyllum griffithii subsp.

griffithii, commonly known as the Griffith's goldenrods, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish to golden inflorescences.
