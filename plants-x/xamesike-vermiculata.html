Xamesike vermiculata

Xamesike vermiculata, commonly known as the silver leaf fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions.

It features delicate, feathery fronds that are covered in a fine, silvery powder, giving it its characteristic appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize trees and rocky surfaces.

Xamesike vermiculata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in horticultural displays.
