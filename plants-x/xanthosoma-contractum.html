Xanthosoma contractum

Xanthosoma contractum, commonly known as the arrow leaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Xanthosoma contractum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and vibrant floral display.
