Xeralis

Xeralis, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a succulent species native to arid regions of Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the warmer months. Its ability to survive with minimal water makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Xeralis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
