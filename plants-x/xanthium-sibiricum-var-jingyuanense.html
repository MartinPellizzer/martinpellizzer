Xanthium sibiricum var. jingyuanense

Xanthium sibiricum var. jingyuanense, commonly known as the Jingyuan cocklebur, is a variant of the common cocklebur native to China.

It is a biennial plant characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and rough, hairy stems. The plant produces spherical, spiky fruits that contain seeds with a hard, bony coat. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
