Xanthophyllum griffithii var. montanum

Xanthophyllum griffithii var. montanum, commonly known as the mountain yellowwood, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, cool environments at high altitudes. It has a woody stem and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in alpine regions.
