Xyris desertorum

Xyris desertorum, commonly known as the desert flat-sedge, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy, or rocky soils. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. Xyris desertorum is adapted to arid environments and has a shallow root system for efficient water absorption.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects and animals.
