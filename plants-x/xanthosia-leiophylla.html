Xanthosia leiophylla

Xanthosia leiophylla, commonly known as the silky everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Xanthosia leiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and winter flowering.
