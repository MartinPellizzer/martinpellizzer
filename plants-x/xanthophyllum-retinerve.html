Xanthophyllum retinerve

Xanthophyllum retinerve, commonly known as the net-veined goldenbush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its finely net-veined leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and a subject of interest in botanical research.
