Xerochlamys arenaria

Xerochlamys arenaria, commonly known as the sand lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Xerochlamys arenaria is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
