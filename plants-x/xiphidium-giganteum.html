Xiphidium giganteum

Xiphidium giganteum, commonly known as the giant sedge, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Xiphidium giganteum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
