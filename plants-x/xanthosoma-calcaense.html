Xanthosoma calcaense

Xanthosoma calcaense, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Xanthosoma calcaense is also valued for its edible leaves, which are commonly consumed in local cuisines.
