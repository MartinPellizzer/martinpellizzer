Xerophyta wentzeliana var. wentzeliana

Xerophyta wentzeliana var. wentzeliana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, exhibiting xerophytic traits such as reduced leaf surface area and thick cuticles. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
