Xanthoxalis europaea var. bushii

Xanthoxalis europaea var. bushii, commonly known as the European yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves composed of five to seven oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
