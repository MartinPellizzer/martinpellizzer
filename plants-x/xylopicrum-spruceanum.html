Xylopicrum spruceanum

Xylopicrum spruceanum, commonly known as the Spruceleaf xylopicrum, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.
