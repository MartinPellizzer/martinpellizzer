Xeranthemum erectum var. meyeri

Xeranthemum erectum var. meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's upright thyme, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant, lavender-blue flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has narrow, aromatic leaves that are gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
