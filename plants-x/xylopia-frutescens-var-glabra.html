Xylopia frutescens var. glabra

Xylopia frutescens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth xylopia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

The fruit is a berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature, containing several seeds.
