Xeraea cylindrica

Xeraea cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical xeraea, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America.

It features thick, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the growing season. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, minimizing water loss through transpiration.

Xeraea cylindrica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
