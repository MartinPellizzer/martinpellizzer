Xanthophyllum laeve

Xanthophyllum laeve, commonly known as the smooth yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

The species is notable for its vibrant color and relatively low maintenance requirements.
