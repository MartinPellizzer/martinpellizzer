Xanthophyllum sarawakensis

Xanthophyllum sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak goldenleaf, is a unique plant species native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its striking yellow-green foliage. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and possess a glossy texture with a distinct aromatic scent.

Xanthophyllum sarawakensis plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various local wildlife species.
