Xyris paraensis

Xyris paraensis, commonly known as the Paraense Yellow-eyed Grass, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes many species of grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

Xyris paraensis is often found in wetland or swampy habitats, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils.
