Xanthosoma belophyllum var. kunthii

Xanthosoma belophyllum var. kunthii, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, providing a striking visual appearance. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers, followed by edible, starchy tubers that are a staple in some cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

This variant is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its culinary uses.
