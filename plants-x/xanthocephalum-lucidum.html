Xanthocephalum lucidum

Xanthocephalum lucidum, commonly known as the shining everlasting pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is often found in sandy or gravelly soils, thriving in arid or semi-arid environments. It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
