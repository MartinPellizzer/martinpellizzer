Xeilyathum altissimum

Xeilyathum altissimum, commonly known as the Skyreach Fern, is a rare and tall fern species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its elongated, feathery fronds that can grow up to three meters in length. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with high humidity and well-draining soil. Its spores are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize remote mountainous areas.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
