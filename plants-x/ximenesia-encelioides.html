Ximenesia encelioides

Ximenesia encelioides, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
