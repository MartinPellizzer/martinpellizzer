Xantho

Xantho, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a striking plant characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features long, slender stems and broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid environment. Xantho blooms during the rainy season, producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique coloration and role in local ecosystems.
