Xyris nubigena

Xyris nubigena, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various species of yellow-flowered plants. The plant is characterized by its grass-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.
