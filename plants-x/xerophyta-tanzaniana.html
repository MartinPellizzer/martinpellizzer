Xerophyta tanzaniana

Xerophyta tanzaniana, commonly known as the Tanzanian ice plant, is a succulent plant native to Tanzania.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Xerophyta tanzaniana is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
