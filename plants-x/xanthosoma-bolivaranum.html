Xanthosoma bolivaranum

Xanthosoma bolivaranum, commonly known as the Bolivian arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Bolivia and neighboring countries. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. It produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, which are often yellow or white in color.

Xanthosoma bolivaranum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
