Xenophontia obovata

Xenophontia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate xenophontia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically hairy and grayish in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in the summer months. Xenophontia obovata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
