Xanthophyllum lateriflorum

Xanthophyllum lateriflorum, commonly known as the golden-flowered fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has delicate, feathery leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Xanthophyllum lateriflorum is valued for its unique floral display and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
