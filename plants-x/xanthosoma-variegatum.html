Xanthosoma variegatum

Xanthosoma variegatum, commonly known as the variegated arrowleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with distinctive variegation. The plant is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.
