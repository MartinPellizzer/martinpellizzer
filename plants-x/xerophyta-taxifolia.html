Xerophyta taxifolia

Xerophyta taxifolia, commonly known as the needleleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, needle-like leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring. It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptations to dry conditions.
