Xanthium × shullii

Xanthium × shullii, commonly known as shull's cocklebur, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical fruits and palmate leaves with lobed margins. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to North America. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

Xanthium × shullii is often considered a weed due to its aggressive growth and ability to thrive in various environments.
