Xylopia sclerophylla

Xylopia sclerophylla, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a tropical plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is characterized by its woody, evergreen structure and small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged oppositely on the stems. Xylopia sclerophylla is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
