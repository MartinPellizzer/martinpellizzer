Xanthosoma hastifolium

Xanthosoma hastifolium, commonly known as the arrowleaf taro, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking yellow spathe flowers, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its edible corms.

Xanthosoma hastifolium is native to Central and South America and is used both ornamentally and for its nutritional value.
