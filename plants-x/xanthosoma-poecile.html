Xanthosoma poecile

Xanthosoma poecile, commonly known as the spotted arrowleaf.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive spotted pattern. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Xanthosoma poecile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
