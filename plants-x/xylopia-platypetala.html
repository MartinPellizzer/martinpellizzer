Xylopia platypetala

Xylopia platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled xylopia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Annonaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody stems and compound leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
