Xeranthemum orientale

Xeranthemum orientale, commonly known as the oriental xeranthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Xeranthemum orientale is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
