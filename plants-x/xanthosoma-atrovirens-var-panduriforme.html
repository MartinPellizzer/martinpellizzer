Xanthosoma atrovirens var. panduriforme

Xanthosoma atrovirens var. panduriforme, commonly known as the black-leaved arrowleaf taro, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking purple or red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

This variant is valued both for its aesthetic appeal and its potential agricultural uses.
