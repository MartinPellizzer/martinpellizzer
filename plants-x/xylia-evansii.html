Xylia evansii

Xylia evansii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark wood that is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. The plant has a straight, cylindrical trunk with smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green upper surface.

Xylia evansii produces small, white flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.
