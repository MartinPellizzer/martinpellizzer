Xylosma venosa var. populneum

Xylosma venosa var. populneum, commonly known as the slender xylosma, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Elaeocarpaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release tiny seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
