Xerophyta trichophylla

Xerophyta trichophylla, commonly known as the woolly pincushion, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months.

Xerophyta trichophylla is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, making it a hardy species for xeriscaping.
