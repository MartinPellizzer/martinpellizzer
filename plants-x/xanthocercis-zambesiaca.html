Xanthocercis zambesiaca

Xanthocercis zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesi flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The tree has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often pale in color.
