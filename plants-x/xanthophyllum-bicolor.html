Xanthophyllum bicolor

Xanthophyllum bicolor, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking yellow and green coloration.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to regions with warm, humid climates. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers with a distinctive yellow hue. Xanthophyllum bicolor thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
