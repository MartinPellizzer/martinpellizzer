Xyris goyazensis

Xyris goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazense yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Xyridaceae, which includes various species of grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers clustered in umbels. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands.
