Xanthosoma huilense

Xanthosoma huilense, commonly known as the Huilense arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Xanthosoma huilense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
