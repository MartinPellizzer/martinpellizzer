Xiphotheca elliptica

Xiphotheca elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves and striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, and it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
