Xiphion Serotinum

Xiphion serotinum is a striking plant known for its vibrant orange and red flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. This perennial plant is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
