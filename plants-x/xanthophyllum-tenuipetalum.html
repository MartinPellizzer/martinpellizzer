Xanthophyllum tenuipetalum

Xanthophyllum tenuipetalum, commonly known as the slender-petaled goldenbell, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters.
