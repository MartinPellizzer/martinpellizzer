Xeranthemum erucifolium

Xeranthemum erucifolium, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silvery-green leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and chrysanthemums.

Xeranthemum erucifolium is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
