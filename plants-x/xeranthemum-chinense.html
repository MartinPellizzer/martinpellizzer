Xeranthemum chinense

Xeranthemum chinense, commonly known as Chinese sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, drought-tolerant growth habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Xeranthemum chinense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
