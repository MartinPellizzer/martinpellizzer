Xolisma lucida var. rubra

Xolisma lucida var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered desert lupine, is a perennial herb native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking red to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and produces slender, erect stems. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to drought conditions.

Xolisma lucida var. rubra plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing nectar for pollinators in its native habitat.
