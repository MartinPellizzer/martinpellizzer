Xanthium fruticosum

Xanthium fruticosum, commonly known as prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and has been introduced to other regions. The plant has hairy, ribbed stems and leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Xanthium fruticosum is often found in disturbed soils and can become a weed in agricultural settings.
