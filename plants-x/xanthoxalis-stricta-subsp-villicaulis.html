Xanthoxalis stricta subsp. villicaulis

Xanthoxalis stricta subsp.

villicaulis, commonly known as the slender yellow oxalis, is a perennial herb native to South America. It features a low-growing habit with slender, hairy stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval, pointed leaflets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest edges.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
