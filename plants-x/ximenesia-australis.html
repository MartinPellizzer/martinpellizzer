Ximenesia australis

Ximenesia australis, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval in shape and often have a slightly hairy texture. Ximenesia australis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
