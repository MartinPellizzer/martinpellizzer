Xanthosoma panguiense

Xanthosoma panguiense, commonly known as the Guianese arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive white midrib. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Xanthosoma panguiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
