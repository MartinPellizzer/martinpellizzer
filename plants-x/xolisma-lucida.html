Xolisma lucida

Xolisma lucida, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in harsh, dry conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to minimize water loss.

Xolisma lucida is often found in rocky soils and is an important part of the desert ecosystem.
