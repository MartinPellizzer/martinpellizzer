Xeraea duriuscula

Xeraea duriuscula, commonly known as the hardy xeraea, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
