Xanthosoma schunkei

Xanthosoma schunkei, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces a spadix inflorescence surrounded by a showy spathe, typical of many aroid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its starchy, edible corms.

Xanthosoma schunkei is also valued for its ornamental foliage, making it a popular choice in tropical gardens.
