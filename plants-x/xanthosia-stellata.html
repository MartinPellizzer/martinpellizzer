Xanthosia stellata

Xanthosia stellata, commonly known as the starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Xanthosia stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
