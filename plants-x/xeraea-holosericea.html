Xeraea holosericea

Xeraea holosericea, commonly known as the silky glassy bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost glass-like appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
