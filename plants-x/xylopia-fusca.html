Xylopia fusca

Xylopia fusca, commonly known as the African allspice or wild allspice, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits and leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are often clustered together. Its fruit, which is a dark purple to black berry, contains several seeds and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
