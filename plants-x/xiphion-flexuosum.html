Xiphion flexuosum

Xiphion flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often slightly curved, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged alternately along the stem.

Xiphion flexuosum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
