Xylosma heterophylla

Xylosma heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf xylosma, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf morphology, with leaves varying in shape, size, and texture across different parts of the plant. The plant typically has smooth, greyish bark and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Xylosma heterophylla thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
