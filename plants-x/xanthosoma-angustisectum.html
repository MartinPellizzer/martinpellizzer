Xanthosoma angustisectum

Xanthosoma angustisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf elephant ear, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually yellow or green, followed by edible, starchy tubers.
