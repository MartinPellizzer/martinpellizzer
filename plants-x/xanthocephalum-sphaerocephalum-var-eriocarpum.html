Xanthocephalum sphaerocephalum var. eriocarpum

Xanthocephalum sphaerocephalum var. eriocarpum, commonly known as the woolly globular bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The leaves are narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hairs, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in native landscaping.
