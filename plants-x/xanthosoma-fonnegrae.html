Xanthosoma fonnegrae

Xanthosoma fonnegrae, commonly known as the black-leafed arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Xanthosoma fonnegrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by local communities.
