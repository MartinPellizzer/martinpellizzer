Xanthosoma roseum

Xanthosoma roseum, commonly known as the pink elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other familiar genera such as Colocasia and Caladium. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
