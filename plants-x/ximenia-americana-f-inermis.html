Ximenia americana f. inermis

Ximenia americana f. inermis, commonly known as the smooth ximenia, is a variant of the ximenia tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree with smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit.

The plant also has traditional uses in some cultures for medicinal and culinary purposes.
