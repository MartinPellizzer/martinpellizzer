Xylopia ferruginea

Xylopia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty xylopia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Xylopia ferruginea is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
