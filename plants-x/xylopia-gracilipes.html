Xylopia gracilipes

Xylopia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed xylopia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Annonaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems and alternate, simple, ovate leaves. Xylopia gracilipes is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
