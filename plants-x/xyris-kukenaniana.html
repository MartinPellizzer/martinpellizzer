Xyris kukenaniana

Xyris kukenaniana, commonly known as the Kukenaniana yellowroot, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Xyridaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants known for their ornamental and medicinal value. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
