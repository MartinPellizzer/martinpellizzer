Xanthosoma foreroi

Xanthosoma foreroi, commonly known as the Forero's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking yellow spadices surrounded by broad, green bracts, which are typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Xanthosoma foreroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
