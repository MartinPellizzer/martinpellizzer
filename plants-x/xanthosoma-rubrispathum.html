Xanthosoma rubrispathum

Xanthosoma rubrispathum, commonly known as the red-veined arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with striking red veins. The plant produces an inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Its rhizomes are edible and used in various culinary applications.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
