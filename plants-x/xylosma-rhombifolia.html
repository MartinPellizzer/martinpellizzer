Xylosma rhombifolia

Xylosma rhombifolia, commonly known as the rhombic xylosma, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically rhombic or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or yellowish in color. Its woody stems are often covered with a rough, bark-like texture.

Xylosma rhombifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
