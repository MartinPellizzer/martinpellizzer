Xenopoma bolivianum var. tarijense

Xenopoma bolivianum var. tarijense, commonly known as the Tarijense Bursera, is a species of flowering plant in the Burseraceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of Bolivia and is characterized by its aromatic, resinous bark. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and exhibits a shrub-like growth form. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a leathery texture.

The flowers are small and clustered, producing a distinctive fragrance that attracts pollinators.
