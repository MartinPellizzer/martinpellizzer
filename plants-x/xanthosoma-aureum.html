Xanthosoma aureum

Xanthosoma aureum, commonly known as the golden arrow plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a yellowish tinge. The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Xanthosoma aureum is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
