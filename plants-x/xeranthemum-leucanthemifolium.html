Xeranthemum leucanthemifolium

Xeranthemum leucanthemifolium, commonly known as the white fleur-de-lis, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble the fleur-de-lis symbol. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.

Xeranthemum leucanthemifolium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
