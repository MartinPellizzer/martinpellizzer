Xerophyta schnizleinia var. brevifolia

Xerophyta schnizleinia var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its low-growing habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive and easy-to-care-for ornamental plant.
