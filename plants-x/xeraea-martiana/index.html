Xeraea Martiana

Xeraea martiana is a rare and fascinating plant native to the arid regions of southern Mexico. It is known for its unique, spiny leaves and striking red and yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. This plant has adapted to survive in extreme conditions with minimal water and high temperatures. Its resilience makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Despite its beauty, Xeraea martiana remains under threat due to habitat loss and over-collection.
