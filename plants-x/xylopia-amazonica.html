Xylopia amazonica

Xylopia amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian star anise, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, spiny branches and palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into woody, star-shaped fruits. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and have a strong, licorice-like aroma.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
