Xerobotrys tomentosus

Xerobotrys tomentosus, commonly known as the woolly xerobotrys, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
