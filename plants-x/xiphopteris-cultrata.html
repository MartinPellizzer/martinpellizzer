Xiphopteris cultrata

Xiphopteris cultrata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, arching fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are wiry and help it spread through underground runners.

Xiphopteris cultrata is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often used in horticulture and landscaping.
