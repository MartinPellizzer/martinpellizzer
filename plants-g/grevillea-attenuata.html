Grevillea attenuata

Grevillea attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are usually covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces striking, long, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea attenuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
