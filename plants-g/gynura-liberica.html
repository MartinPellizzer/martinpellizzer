Gynura liberica

Gynura liberica, commonly known as the African crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver or white underside, giving it a striking appearance. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Gynura liberica is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
