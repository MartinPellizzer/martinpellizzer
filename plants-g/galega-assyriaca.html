Galega assyriaca

Galega assyriaca, commonly known as Assyrian goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in racemes. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
