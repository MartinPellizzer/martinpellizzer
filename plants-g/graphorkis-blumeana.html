Graphorkis blumeana

Graphorkis blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
