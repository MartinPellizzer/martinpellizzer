Gardenia rotunda

Gardenia rotunda, commonly known as the round gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green oval leaves and fragrant, waxy white flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia rotunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.
