Gymnadenia flexuosa

Gymnadenia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous grasslands and meadows, often in shaded areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a zigzag pattern along the stem, giving it its common name.

Gymnadenia flexuosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
