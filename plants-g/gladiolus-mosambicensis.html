Gladiolus mosambicensis

Gladiolus mosambicensis, commonly known as the Mozambique gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.
