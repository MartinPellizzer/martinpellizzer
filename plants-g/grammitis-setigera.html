Grammitis setigera

Grammitis setigera, commonly known as the feather moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth and dark green, soft foliage. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinctive midrib that is more prominent on the upper surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forests and along stream banks.

This moss plays an important role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.
