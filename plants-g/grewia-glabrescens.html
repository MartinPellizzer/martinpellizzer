Grewia glabrescens

Grewia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Grewia glabrescens is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
