Goniopteris affinis

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically divided into small, rounded lobes. The plant thrives in freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. Goniopteris affinis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
