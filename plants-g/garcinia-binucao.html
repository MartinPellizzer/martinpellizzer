Garcinia binucao

Garcinia binucao, commonly known as the purple mangostin, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, reddish-brown bark and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, segmented fruits. The fruit's pericarp contains a white, juicy aril that is rich in xanthones and other bioactive compounds.

Due to its medicinal properties, Garcinia binucao is widely studied for its potential health benefits.
