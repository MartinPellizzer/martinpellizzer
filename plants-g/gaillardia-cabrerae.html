Gaillardia cabrerae

Gaillardia cabrerae, commonly known as the Mexican blanket flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like blooms. The plant typically features bright red or orange petals with a yellow center, and its foliage is covered in fine, hairy leaves. Gaillardia cabrerae thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
