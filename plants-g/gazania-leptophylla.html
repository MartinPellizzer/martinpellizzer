Gazania leptophylla

Gazania leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers with a central disk. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania leptophylla is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
