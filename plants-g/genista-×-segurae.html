Genista × segurae

Genista × segurae, commonly known as the Segura broom, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in difficult environments.
