Gardenia chartacea var. latifolia

Gardenia chartacea var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are broader than those of the typical Gardenia chartacea. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its attractive foliage and flowers, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in subtropical regions.
