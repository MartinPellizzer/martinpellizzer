Garcinia Staneriana

Garcinia staneriana is a rare and fascinating plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking red fruits and unique medicinal properties. This species belongs to the Rutaceae family and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are glossy and its flowers are small but fragrant. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research.
