Gymnosporia maritima

Gymnosporia maritima, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and its ability to thrive in coastal environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. Gymnosporia maritima is often found in sandy or gravelly soils along shorelines.
