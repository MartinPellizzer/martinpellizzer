Goodenia redacta

Goodenia redacta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathland and shrubland habitats.

Goodenia redacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
