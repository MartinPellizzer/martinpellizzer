Gynura aspera

Gynura aspera, commonly known as rough gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical heads. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gynura aspera is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive foliage.
