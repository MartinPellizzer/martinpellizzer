Gynura sarcobasis

Gynura sarcobasis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or gynura.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is known for its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, heart-shaped or oval outline.

Gynura sarcobasis is also noted for its ornamental value, making it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists.
