Gymnopetalum leucostictum

Gymnopetalum leucostictum, commonly known as the white-streaked orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with prominent green veins, which are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
