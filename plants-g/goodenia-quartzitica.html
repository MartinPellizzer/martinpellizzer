Goodenia quartzitica

Goodenia quartzitica, commonly known as the quartzite goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers and is typically found growing in rocky, arid environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is often found in quartzite formations.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
