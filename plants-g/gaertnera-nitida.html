Gaertnera nitida

Gaertnera nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically small, elliptical, and have a leathery texture. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
