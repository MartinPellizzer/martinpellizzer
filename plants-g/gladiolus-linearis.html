Gladiolus linearis

Gladiolus linearis, commonly known as the slender gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces striking, sword-shaped flowers in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with its inflorescence consisting of a spike of showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The botanical characteristics of Gladiolus linearis make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
