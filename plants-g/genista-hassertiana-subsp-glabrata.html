Genista hassertiana subsp. glabrata

Genista hassertiana subsp.

glabrata, commonly known as the hairless genista, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring.

The subspecies glabrata is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, which set it apart from other members of the Genista species. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
