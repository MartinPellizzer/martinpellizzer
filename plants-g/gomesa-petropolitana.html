Gomesa petropolitana

Gomesa petropolitana, commonly known as the Brazilian aster, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.

Gomesa petropolitana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
