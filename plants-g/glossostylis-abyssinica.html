Glossostylis abyssinica

Glossostylis abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and yellow.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
