Gardenia megalocarpa

Gardenia megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia megalocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.
