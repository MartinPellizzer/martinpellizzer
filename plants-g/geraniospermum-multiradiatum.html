Geraniospermum multiradiatum

Geraniospermum multiradiatum, commonly known as the radiate geranium, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
