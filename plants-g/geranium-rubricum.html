Geranium rubricum

Geranium rubricum, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, where it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a reddish tinge. Its showy flowers, which range from pink to deep red, bloom in clusters during the summer months.

Geranium rubricum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant colors and ease of care.
