Grevillea minutiflora

Grevillea minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered grevillea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bright red or orange flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
