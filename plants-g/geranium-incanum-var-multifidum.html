Geranium incanum var. multifidum

Geranium incanum var. multifidum, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
