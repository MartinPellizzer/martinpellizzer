Goniophlebium amoenum var. latedeltoideum

Goniophlebium amoenum var. latedeltoideum, commonly known as the broad-winged fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, deltoid-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its fronds are often divided into lobes, giving it a feathery appearance.
