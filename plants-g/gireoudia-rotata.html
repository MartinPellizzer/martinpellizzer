Gireoudia rotata

Gireoudia rotata, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
