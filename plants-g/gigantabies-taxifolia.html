Gigantabies taxifolia

Gigantabies taxifolia, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a slender, unbranched stem with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are composed of numerous radiating leaflets, giving it a distinctive, palm-like appearance.
