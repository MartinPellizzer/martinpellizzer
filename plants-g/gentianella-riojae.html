Gentianella riojae

Gentianella riojae, commonly known as the Rioja gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
