Gnaphalium rigidum

Gnaphalium rigidum, commonly known as stiff everlasting or rigid cudweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and small, fluffy flower heads. The plant produces yellowish to brownish flowers that are surrounded by bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and coastal areas.

Gnaphalium rigidum is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
