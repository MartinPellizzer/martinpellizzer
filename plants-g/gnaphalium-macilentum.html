Gnaphalium macilentum

Gnaphalium macilentum, commonly known as the slender cudweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its delicate appearance.
