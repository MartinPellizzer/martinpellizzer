Gayophytum racemosum var. typicum

Gayophytum racemosum var. typicum, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to eastern Africa and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, ovate leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically purple or violet in color, with five petals.

It is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.
