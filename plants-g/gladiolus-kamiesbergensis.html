Gladiolus kamiesbergensis

Gladiolus kamiesbergensis, commonly known as the Kamiesberg gladiolus, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, colorful flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing spikes of vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape.

Gladiolus kamiesbergensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
