Goodenia argentea

Goodenia argentea, commonly known as silver goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in winter, adding visual interest during cooler months. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
