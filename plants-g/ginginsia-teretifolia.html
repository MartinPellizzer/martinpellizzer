Ginginsia teretifolia

Ginginsia teretifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
