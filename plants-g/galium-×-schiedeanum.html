Galium × schiedeanum

Galium × schiedeanum, commonly known as the silverweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, spreading stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. Galium × schiedeanum is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
