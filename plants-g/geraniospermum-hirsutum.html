Geraniospermum hirsutum

Geraniospermum hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy geraniospermum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Geraniaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is native to regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it prefers full sun and well-drained conditions.
