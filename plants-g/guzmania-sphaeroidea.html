Guzmania sphaeroidea

Guzmania sphaeroidea, commonly known as the globe bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red bracts that remain for an extended period. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Guzmania sphaeroidea is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
