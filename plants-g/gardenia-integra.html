Gardenia integra

Gardenia integra, commonly known as the smooth gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blossoms.

Gardenia integra requires partial shade and regular watering to maintain its health and vigor.
