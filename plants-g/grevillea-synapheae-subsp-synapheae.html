Grevillea synapheae subsp. synapheae

Grevillea synapheae subsp.

synapheae, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and produces bright red or orange flowers in clusters.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators. Grevillea synapheae subsp.

synapheae is also noted for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
