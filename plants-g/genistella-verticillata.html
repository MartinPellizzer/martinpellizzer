Genistella verticillata

Genistella verticillata, commonly known as the whorled genista, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically arranged in whorls along the stems. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
