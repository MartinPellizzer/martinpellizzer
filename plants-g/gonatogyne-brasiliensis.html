Gonatogyne brasiliensis

Gonatogyne brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant with slender, wiry stems and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive star-like pattern on the petals and lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
