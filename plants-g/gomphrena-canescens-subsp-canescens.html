Gomphrena canescens subsp. canescens

Gomphrena canescens subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the common gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, papery bracts that range in color from pink to red.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often surrounded by showy, leaf-like structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

This species is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
