Gnidia cuneata

Gnidia cuneata, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has palmate leaves and produces small, red berries that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Gnidia cuneata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine.
