Galium × honoratae

Galium × honoratae, commonly known as the evergreen wood sorrel, is a hybrid plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of 10–30 cm. The plant features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy coating, and produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a shade-tolerant, evergreen ground cover.
