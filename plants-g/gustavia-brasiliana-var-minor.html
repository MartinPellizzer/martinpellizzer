Gustavia brasiliana var. minor

Gustavia brasiliana var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf poinsettia, is a compact shrub native to Brazil.

It features small, colorful bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and seasonal color changes.
