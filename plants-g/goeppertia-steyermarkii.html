Goeppertia steyermarkii

Goeppertia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, while the small, inconspicuous flowers are usually hidden beneath the leaves.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
