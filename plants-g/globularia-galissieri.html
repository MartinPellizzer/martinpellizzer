Globularia galissieri

Globularia galissieri, commonly known as the Galician globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense, spherical clumps of foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.
