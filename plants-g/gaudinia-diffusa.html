Gaudinia diffusa

Gaudinia diffusa, commonly known as the silverleaf gaudinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Gaudinia diffusa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
