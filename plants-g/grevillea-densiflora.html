Grevillea densiflora

Grevillea densiflora, commonly known as the denseflowered grevillea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Grevillea densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds and insects.
