Gymnocalycium grandiflorum

Gymnocalycium grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It has a thick, waxy cuticle that helps it retain water in arid environments.

Gymnocalycium grandiflorum is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
