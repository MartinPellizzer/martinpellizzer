Gardenia noumeensis

Gardenia noumeensis, commonly known as the New Caledonia gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers bloom in summer and are notable for their strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia noumeensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
