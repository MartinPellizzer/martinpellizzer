Gymnadenia rhellicani var. robusta

Gymnadenia rhellicani var. robusta, commonly known as the robust lady's tresses, is a perennial orchid native to certain regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and its distinctive, fragrant, white to pinkish flower spikes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers bloom in late summer, producing a delicate, sweet scent that attracts pollinators like bees and moths.

This variety is noted for its robust growth compared to other members of the Gymnadenia genus.
