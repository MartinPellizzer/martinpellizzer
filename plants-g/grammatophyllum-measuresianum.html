Grammatophyllum measuresianum

Grammatophyllum measuresianum, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, spider-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns. The plant has long, thin pseudobulbs and produces numerous narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

This species is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
