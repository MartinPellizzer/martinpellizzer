Gaultheria yunnanensis

Gaultheria yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan wintergreen, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces dark blue to black berries that are edible and have a sharp, acidic flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with acidic soils.

Gaultheria yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
