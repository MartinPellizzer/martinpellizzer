Gazania schinzii

Gazania schinzii, commonly known as the Cape gold or Schinz's gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive central disk. The plant typically grows in a low, spreading form with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania schinzii is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the year in suitable conditions.
