Gasteria subnigricans

Gasteria subnigricans, commonly known as the Blackleaf Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and often have a distinctive dark purple or blackish hue on the underside. It produces small, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
