Gardenia florida var. maruba

Gardenia florida var. maruba, commonly known as the Maruba gardenia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is highly valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its slow growth and compact form, it is an ideal choice for small spaces and container gardening.
