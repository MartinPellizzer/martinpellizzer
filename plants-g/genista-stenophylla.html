Genista stenophylla

Genista stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its small yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
