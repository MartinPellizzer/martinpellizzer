Guzmania × litaensis

Guzmania × litaensis, commonly known as the hybrid bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports a micro-ecosystem. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can display a wide variety of colors.

Guzmania × litaensis is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
