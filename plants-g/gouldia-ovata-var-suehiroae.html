Gouldia ovata var. suehiroae

Gouldia ovata var. suehiroae, commonly known as the Suehiroae Gouldia, is a compact, flowering plant native to Australia.

It features dense, grass-like foliage with narrow, dark green leaves that form a low, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
