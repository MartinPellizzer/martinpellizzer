Guilandina volkensii

Guilandina volkensii, commonly known as the African snake plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. Guilandina volkensii is also noted for its hardy nature and adaptability to various soil types.
