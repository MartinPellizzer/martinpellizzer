Gastrochilus niveus

Gastrochilus niveus, commonly known as the snow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its white to pale pink flowers and its preference for shaded, moist environments. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The orchid's delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for conservation and horticultural cultivation.
