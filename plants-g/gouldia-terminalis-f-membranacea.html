Gouldia terminalis f. membranacea

Gouldia terminalis f. membranacea, commonly known as the membranaceous gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, papery bracts that range in color from red to orange, which are highly ornamental and attract pollinators. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with a short, woody stem and dense foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal color display make it a popular choice in horticulture.
