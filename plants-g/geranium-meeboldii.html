Geranium meeboldii

Geranium meeboldii, commonly known as the Meebold geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It blooms in late winter to early spring, producing vibrant pink or purple flowers that attract pollinators.

Geranium meeboldii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to well-drained soils.
