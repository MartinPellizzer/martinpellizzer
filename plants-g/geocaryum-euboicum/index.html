Geocaryum Euboicum

Geocaryum euboicum is a rare and elusive plant species native to the rocky coastlines of southern Europe. It thrives in harsh, saline environments where few other plants can survive. This unique species has adapted to extreme conditions through specialized root systems and salt-excreting leaves. Despite its ecological importance, it remains poorly studied due to its limited distribution and difficult habitat. Understanding Geocaryum euboicum could provide valuable insights into plant resilience in coastal ecosystems.
