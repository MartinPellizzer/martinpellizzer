Geum × jankae

Geum × jankae, commonly known as the hybrid garden buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with basal leaves that are palmately divided and covered in fine hairs. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.

Geum × jankae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
