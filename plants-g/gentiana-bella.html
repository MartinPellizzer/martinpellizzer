Gentiana bella

Gentiana bella, commonly known as the beautiful gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana bella is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
