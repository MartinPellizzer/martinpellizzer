Gardenia augusta

Gardenia augusta, commonly known as the golden gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade. Its flowers, which bloom in summer, are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty and scent.

Gardenia augusta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
