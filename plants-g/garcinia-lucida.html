Garcinia lucida

Garcinia lucida, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The tree produces yellow-orange fruits with a thick, juicy pulp that contains numerous seeds. The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its nutritional content and potential health benefits.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
