Gardenia letestui

Gardenia letestui, commonly known as the Madagascar gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia letestui is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
