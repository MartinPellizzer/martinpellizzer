Gentiana loureiroi subsp. loureiroi

Gentiana loureiroi subsp.

loureiroi, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are often woody at the base.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
