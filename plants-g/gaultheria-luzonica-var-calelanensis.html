Gaultheria luzonica var. calelanensis

Gaultheria luzonica var. calelanensis, commonly known as the Luzon wintergreen, is a species of shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically red or dark purple when mature, and it thrives in cool, moist, acidic soils.

It is often found in montane forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
