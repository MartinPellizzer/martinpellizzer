Gerascanthus sprucei

Gerascanthus sprucei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its blooms are often pink or purple and can last for several weeks during the summer months.

Gerascanthus sprucei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
