Gastrochilus pulchellus

Gastrochilus pulchellus, commonly known as the beautiful stomach orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
