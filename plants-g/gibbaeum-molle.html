Gibbaeum molle

Gibbaeum molle, commonly known as the soft-topped sand mat, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming structure with fleshy, pale green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact form and ability to store water in its leaves make it an adaptable and resilient species.
