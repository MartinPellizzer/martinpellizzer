Globularia spinosa var. major

Globularia spinosa var. major, commonly known as the large globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
