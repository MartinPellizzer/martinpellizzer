Goodenia purpurascens var. minima

Goodenia purpurascens var. minima, commonly known as the small-flowered goodenia, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has linear, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Goodeniaceae family.
