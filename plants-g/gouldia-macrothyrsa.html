Gouldia macrothyrsa

Gouldia macrothyrsa, commonly known as the Cape York lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery fuzz. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing near streams or in dense undergrowth.

Gouldia macrothyrsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
