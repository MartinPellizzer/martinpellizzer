Garnotia muricola var. abuensis

Garnotia muricola var. abuensis, commonly known as the Abu Dhabi garnet orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and purple flowers, which are adorned with intricate patterns resembling gemstones. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a warm, arid climate.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
