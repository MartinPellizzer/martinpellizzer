Gleichenia conversa

Gleichenia conversa, commonly known as the slender wood fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically dark green and have a soft, leathery texture. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in small, rounded clusters called sori. Gleichenia conversa is often found growing in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
