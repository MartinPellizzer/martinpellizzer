Gladiolus versicolor var. major

Gladiolus versicolor var. major, commonly known as the giant rainbow gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, often displaying intricate patterns and vibrant hues. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with tall, sturdy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spike of brightly colored, spurred flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The botanical features of this variety make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
