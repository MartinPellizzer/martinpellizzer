Goswamia gujaratensis

Goswamia gujaratensis, commonly known as the Gujarat grass, is a species of grass native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, showing adaptability to harsh environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous structure that contributes to its resilience.

Goswamia gujaratensis plays a minor role in local ecosystems and is sometimes used in traditional practices.
