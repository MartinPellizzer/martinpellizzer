Grammatotheca bergiana var. eckloniana

Grammatotheca bergiana var. eckloniana, commonly known as the Bergian grammatea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, and it produces tall, branched flower stalks with small, white or pale pink flowers.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
