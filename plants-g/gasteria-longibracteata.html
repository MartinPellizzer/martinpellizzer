Gasteria longibracteata

Gasteria longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract gasteria, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences bearing small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid conditions, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Gasteria longibracteata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
