Gymnocalycium mostii

Gymnocalycium mostii, commonly known as the Mostii's cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
