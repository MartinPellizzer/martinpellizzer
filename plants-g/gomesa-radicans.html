Gomesa radicans

Gomesa radicans, commonly known as the hanging mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a trailing perennial that produces small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant has succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Gomesa radicans is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its trailing growth habit.
