Goniopteris Gonophora

Goniopteris gonophora is a prehistoric fern that thrived during the late Paleozoic era. It is known for its large, intricate fronds that once dominated ancient forests. Fossils of this plant provide valuable insights into the evolution of early vascular plants. Its structure suggests it played a significant role in early terrestrial ecosystems. Studying Goniopteris helps scientists understand the transition from aquatic to land-based life.
