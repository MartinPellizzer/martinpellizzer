Gardenia hansemannii

Gardenia hansemannii, commonly known as the Hansemann's gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia hansemannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
