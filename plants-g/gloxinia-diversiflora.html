Gloxinia diversiflora

Gloxinia diversiflora, commonly known as the rainbow gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia diversiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
