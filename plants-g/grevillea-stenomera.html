Grevillea stenomera

Grevillea stenomera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Grevillea stenomera is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
