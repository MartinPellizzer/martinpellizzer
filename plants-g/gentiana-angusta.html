Gentiana angusta

Gentiana angusta, commonly known as the narrow gentian, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue.

Gentiana angusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
