Gasteria maculata

Gasteria maculata, commonly known as the spotted gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria maculata is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
