Gilia Latiflora Subsp. Davyi

Gilia latiflora subsp. davyi is a lesser-known yet striking member of the evening primrose family. Native to the western United States, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. Its unique appearance makes it a valuable addition to native plant gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in botanical discussions.
