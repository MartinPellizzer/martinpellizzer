Goeppertia coccinea, commonly known as the red-tipped pineapple plant, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking red-tipped leaves. Goeppertia coccinea is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
