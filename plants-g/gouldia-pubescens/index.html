Gouldia Pubescens

Gouldia pubescens is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of Australia. Known for its vibrant red and yellow flowers, it adds a bold splash of color to any landscape. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native habitat.
