Gentiana tenuifolia

Gentiana tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and the plant is often found in rocky or grassy areas.

Gentiana tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
