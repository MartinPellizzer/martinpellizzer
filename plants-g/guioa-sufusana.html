Guioa sufusana

Guioa sufusana, commonly known as the white guioa, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Guioa sufusana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
