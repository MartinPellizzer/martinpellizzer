Gagea maeotica

Gagea maeotica, commonly known as the Crimean spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus and southern Russia.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy or loamy soils. It is typically found in open woodlands and rocky areas, preferring full sun to partial shade.

Gagea maeotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its early blooming habit.
