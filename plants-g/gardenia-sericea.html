Gardenia sericea

Gardenia sericea, commonly known as silky gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia sericea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blooms.
