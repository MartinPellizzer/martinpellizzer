Geranium sylvaticum var. glabriusculum

Geranium sylvaticum var. glabriusculum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed wood geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in woodland habitats and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its compact growth and ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
