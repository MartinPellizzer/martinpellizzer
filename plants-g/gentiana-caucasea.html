Gentiana caucasea

Gentiana caucasea, commonly known as the Caucasian gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Gentiana caucasea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
