Gesneria fawcettii

Gesneria fawcettii, commonly known as the Fawcett's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria fawcettii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
