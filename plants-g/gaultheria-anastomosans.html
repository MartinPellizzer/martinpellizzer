Gaultheria anastomosans

Gaultheria anastomosans, commonly known as the mountain cranberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Andes Mountains.

The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and have a tart flavor, often used in local cuisines. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
