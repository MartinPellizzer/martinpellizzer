Geanthus stenophyllus

Geanthus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geanthus, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale green, blooming in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy texture.

Geanthus stenophyllus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
