Gossypium asiaticum

Gossypium asiaticum, commonly known as the Asiatic cotton plant, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family, Malvaceae.

It is native to South and Southeast Asia and is characterized by its soft, fluffy fibers that are used in textile production. Gossypium asiaticum is an important crop in certain regions due to its economic value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
