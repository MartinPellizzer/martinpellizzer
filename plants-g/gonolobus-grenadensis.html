Gonolobus grenadensis

Gonolobus grenadensis, commonly known as the Grenada morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, showy flowers that typically bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, green fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Gonolobus grenadensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
