Gesneria nitida

Gesneria nitida, commonly known as the silver gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the spring.

Gesneria nitida is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
