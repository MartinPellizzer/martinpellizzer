Geijera paniculata

Geijera paniculata, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces bright orange to red flowers in clusters, which are borne on long, drooping panicles. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth surface.

The tree is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its striking floral display.
