Gossia diversifolia

Gossia diversifolia, commonly known as the variable gossia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Australia, particularly in Queensland and New South Wales. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys.

Gossia diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local wildlife.
