Geranium harmsii

Geranium harmsii, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pentagonal flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium harmsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited for rock gardens and containers.
