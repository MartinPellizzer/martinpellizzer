Gentiana ochroleuca var. intermedia

Gentiana ochroleuca var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variant is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
