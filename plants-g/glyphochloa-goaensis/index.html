Glyphochloa Goaensis

Glyphochloa goaensis is a rare and unique grass species native to the coastal regions of Goa, India. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dune environments along the western coast. This plant is notable for its distinctive leaf structure and limited geographic distribution. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant threats to its survival. Studying Glyphochloa goaensis provides valuable insights into ecological adaptation and conservation efforts in coastal ecosystems.
