Garayanthus rostellatus

Garayanthus rostellatus, commonly known as the red-flowered rock heath.

It is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa. The plant features small, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to orange. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

Garayanthus rostellatus thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
