Gnaphalium germanicum var. pyramidatum

Gnaphalium germanicum var. pyramidatum, commonly known as the pyramidal everlasting, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, fluffy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry meadows and rocky areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in marginal environments.
