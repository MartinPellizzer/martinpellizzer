Galactites tomentosus var. elegans

Galactites tomentosus var. elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a soft, hairy texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appearance.
