Goodenia hederacea var. hypotephra

Goodenia hederacea var. hypotephra, commonly known as the woolly goodenia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with dense, woolly foliage that is typically grey-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas.

Its distinctive appearance and drought tolerance make it a notable species in its native region.
