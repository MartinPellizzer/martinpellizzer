Gynura winkleri

Gynura winkleri, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense heads, often yellow or orange in color.

Gynura winkleri is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
