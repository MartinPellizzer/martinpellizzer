Grammitis billardierei f. nana

Grammitis billardierei f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf grammitis, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, compact plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. The fronds are usually short and curled, with a dense, mat-like appearance. It has a simple rhizome system and produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
