Gardenia gordonii

Gardenia gordonii, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces yellow-orange berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. Gardenia gordonii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
