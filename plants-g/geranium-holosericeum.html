Geranium holosericeum

Geranium holosericeum, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Geranium holosericeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
