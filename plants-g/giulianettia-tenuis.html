Giulianettia tenuis

Giulianettia tenuis, commonly known as the slender leaf giulianettia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in cloud forests. The plant features narrow, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in greenish-white or pale yellow hues.

Giulianettia tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in specialized gardens.
