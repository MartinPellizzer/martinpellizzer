Gelasine rigida

Gelasine rigida, commonly known as the rigid gelasine, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It features upright, rigid stems that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough texture.

Gelasine rigida is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
