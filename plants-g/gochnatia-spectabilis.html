Gochnatia spectabilis

Gochnatia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy gochnatia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with fine hairs.

Gochnatia spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
