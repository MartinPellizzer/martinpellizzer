Geonoma maxima subsp. camptoneura

Geonoma maxima subsp.

The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a dense arrangement of small, serrated pinnae. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing on tree trunks or in the understory of rainforests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and plays a role in the ecological structure of its habitat.
