Gazania scabra

Gazania scabra, commonly known as the sand gazania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is well adapted to arid and sandy environments. It thrives in full sun and requires minimal water once established.

Gazania scabra is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
