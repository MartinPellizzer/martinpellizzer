Greenwayodendron usambaricum

Greenwayodendron usambaricum, commonly known as Usambaran flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to the Usambara Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate, with elongated leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in rocky or limestone soils.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
