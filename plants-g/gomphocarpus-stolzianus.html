Gomphocarpus stolzianus

Gomphocarpus stolzianus, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling a starburst pattern. The plant has a hairy stem and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and woodland habitats.

Gomphocarpus stolzianus is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
