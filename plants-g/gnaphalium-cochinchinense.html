Gnaphalium cochinchinense

Gnaphalium cochinchinense, commonly known as the Vietnamese feather grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Vietnam and Cambodia, and thrives in moist, open habitats.

Gnaphalium cochinchinense is often found in grasslands, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
