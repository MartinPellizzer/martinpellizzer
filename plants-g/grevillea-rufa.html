Grevillea rufa

Grevillea rufa, commonly known as the common grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea rufa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract native birds.
