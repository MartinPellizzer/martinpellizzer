Galianthe brasiliensis

Galianthe brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in shades of yellow, orange, and red.

Galianthe brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
