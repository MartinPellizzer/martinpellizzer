Gentiana trichostemma

Gentiana trichostemma, commonly known as the three-horned gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in a dense inflorescence.

Gentiana trichostemma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
