Geranium berezovcaeanum

Geranium berezovcaeanum, commonly known as the Berezovcaean geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowering period typically occurs in late summer to early autumn.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
