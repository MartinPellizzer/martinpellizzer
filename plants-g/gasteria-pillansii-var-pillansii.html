Gasteria pillansii var. pillansii

Gasteria pillansii var. pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
