Gomphocarpus macropus

Gomphocarpus macropus, commonly known as the large-seeded milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Gomphocarpus macropus is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, where it thrives in full sun.
