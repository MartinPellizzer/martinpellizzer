Geum heldreichii

Geum heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan avens, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and hairy, with a distinctive reddish or pinkish hue. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Geum heldreichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native ecosystems.
