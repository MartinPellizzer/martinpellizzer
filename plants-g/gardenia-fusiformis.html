Gardenia fusiformis

Gardenia fusiformis, commonly known as the spindle gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, spindle-shaped leaves and produces fragrant white flowers. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in humid, tropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Gardenia fusiformis prefers well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
