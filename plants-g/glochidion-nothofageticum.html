Glochidion nothofageticum

Glochidion nothofageticum, commonly known as the Tasmanian holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
