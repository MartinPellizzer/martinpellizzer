Galium verum lusus tomentosum

Galium verum lusus tomentosum, commonly known as the yellow bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its botanical name reflects its close relation to *Galium verum*, with the specific epithet *lusus tomentosum* highlighting its tomentose (fuzzy) foliage.
