Genista monosperma var. bovei

Genista monosperma var. bovei, commonly known as the Bove's genista, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its role in stabilizing soils in arid environments.
