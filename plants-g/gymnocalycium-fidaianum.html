Gymnocalycium fidaianum

Gymnocalycium fidaianum, commonly known as the Fidaianus cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
