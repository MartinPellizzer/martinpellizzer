Gongora truncata var. donckelaariana

Gongora truncata var. donckelaariana, commonly known as the Donckelaariana Orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Colombia.

This variant is characterized by its striking, elongated flower spikes that bear multiple violet or purple flowers with intricate patterns. The plant has broad, dark green leaves and produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts who appreciate its beauty and rarity.
