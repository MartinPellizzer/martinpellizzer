Gentiana stricta

Gentiana stricta, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue.

Gentiana stricta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
