Goodenia trichophylla

Goodenia trichophylla, commonly known as the silky goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Goodenia trichophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
