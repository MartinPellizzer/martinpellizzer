Geranium knysnaense

Geranium knysnaense, commonly known as Knysna geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant features upright stems and lobed leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium knysnaense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
