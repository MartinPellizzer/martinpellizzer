Gentiana itzershanensis

Gentiana itzershanensis, commonly known as the Itzershan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, which is characterized by its showy, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool and humid conditions. Its flowers bloom in the summer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
