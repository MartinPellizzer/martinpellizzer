Grosourdya lobata

Grosourdya lobata, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Grosourdya lobata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
