Grevillea micrantha

Grevillea micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, thriving in well-drained environments.

Grevillea micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
