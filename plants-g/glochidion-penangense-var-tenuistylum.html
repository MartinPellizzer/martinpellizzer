Glochidion penangense var. tenuistylum

Glochidion penangense var. tenuistylum, commonly known as the Penang Glochidion, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its small, simple leaves that are usually alternate and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
