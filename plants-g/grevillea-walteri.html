Grevillea walteri

Grevillea walteri, commonly known as the Walter's waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are dark green and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Grevillea walteri thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a standout in the botanical world.
