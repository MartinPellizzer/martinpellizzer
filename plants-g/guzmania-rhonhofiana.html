Guzmania rhonhofiana

Guzmania rhonhofiana, commonly known as the Rhonhof's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports various microorganisms. Its unique structure allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Guzmania rhonhofiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify the air in indoor spaces.
