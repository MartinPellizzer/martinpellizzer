Geraniospermum crispum

Geraniospermum crispum, commonly known as the silverleaf geraniospermum, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight and reduces water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in dry climates.
