Grevillea umbellifera

Grevillea umbellifera, commonly known as the umbrella grevillea, is a flowering plant native to western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Grevillea umbellifera is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy soils.
