Genistoides linifolia

Genistoides linifolia, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
