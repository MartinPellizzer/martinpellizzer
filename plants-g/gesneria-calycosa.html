Gesneria calycosa

Gesneria calycosa, commonly known as the calycosa gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Gesneria calycosa requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
