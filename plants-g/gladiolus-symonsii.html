Gladiolus symonsii

Gladiolus symonsii, commonly known as the Cape Town gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically vibrant in color, ranging from red to orange, and are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value. The plant is also noted for its resilience to drought conditions.
