Geodorum formosanum

Geodorum formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has strap-like leaves and blooms during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Geodorum formosanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
