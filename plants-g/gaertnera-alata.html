Gaertnera alata

Gaertnera alata, commonly known as the winged bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and oval, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside.

Gaertnera alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
