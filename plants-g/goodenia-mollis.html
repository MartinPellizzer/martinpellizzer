Goodenia mollis

Goodenia mollis, commonly known as the soft goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, woolly foliage and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Goodenia mollis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
