Geigeria ornativa subsp. rhodesiana

Geigeria ornativa subsp.

rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodesian ornamental geigeria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
