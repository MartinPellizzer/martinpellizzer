Globba pendula var. elegans

Globba pendula var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant hanging globba, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its gracefully drooping inflorescences that bear clusters of small, vividly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant display and unique growth habit.
