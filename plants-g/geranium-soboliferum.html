Geranium soboliferum

Geranium soboliferum, commonly known as the purple geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of purple and pink, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Geranium soboliferum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
