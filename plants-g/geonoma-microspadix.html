Geonoma microspadix

Geonoma microspadix, commonly known as the small-spadix geonoma, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which can collect water. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful bract, typical of many bromeliads.

Geonoma microspadix is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid environments.
