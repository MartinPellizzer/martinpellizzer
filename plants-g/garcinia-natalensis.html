Garcinia natalensis

Garcinia natalensis, commonly known as Natal Garcinia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange-red in color when ripe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests and riverine areas.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
