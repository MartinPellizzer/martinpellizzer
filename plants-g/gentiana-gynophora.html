Gentiana gynophora

Gentiana gynophora, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its stems are erect and branched.

Gentiana gynophora is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
