Gazania canescens

Gazania canescens, commonly known as the silver gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and silvery-gray foliage. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms make it a resilient and attractive addition to gardens.
