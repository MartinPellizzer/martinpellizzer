Geranium raii

Geranium raii, commonly known as the Sierra geranium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically blooms in the summer months and prefers well-drained, sandy soils. Its leaves are usually lobed and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium raii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
