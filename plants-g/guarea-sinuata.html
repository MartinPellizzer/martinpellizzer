Guarea sinuata

Guarea sinuata, commonly known as the "screw tree," is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. Guarea sinuata is valued for its durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
