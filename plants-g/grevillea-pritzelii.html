Grevillea pritzelii

Grevillea pritzelii, commonly known as the Pritzel's waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its vivid red, flame-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea pritzelii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
