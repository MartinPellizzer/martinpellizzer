Gomphostemma heyneanum

Gomphostemma heyneanum, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elliptical, yellowish fruits. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
