Genista scopolii

Genista scopolii, commonly known as the Turkish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Genista scopolii is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
