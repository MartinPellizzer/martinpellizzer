Glycine wilmsii

Glycine wilmsii, commonly known as the Wilmsii evergreen bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Glycine wilmsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
