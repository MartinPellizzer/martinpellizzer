Gladiolus alatus var. algoensis

Gladiolus alatus var. algoensis, commonly known as the Alagoas gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, colorful inflorescences that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its unique varietal characteristics include a distinctive, elongated flower spike and a variety of color forms.

This species is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in floral arrangements and landscape design.
