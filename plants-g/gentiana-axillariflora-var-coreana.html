Gentiana axillariflora var. coreana

Gentiana axillariflora var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
