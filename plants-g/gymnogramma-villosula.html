Gymnogramma villosula

Gymnogramma villosula, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and features fronds that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. This fern is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
