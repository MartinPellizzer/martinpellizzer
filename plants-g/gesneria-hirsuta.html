Gesneria hirsuta

Gesneria hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color.

Gesneria hirsuta is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
