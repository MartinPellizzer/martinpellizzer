Gaertnera × gardneri

Gaertnera × gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's gaertnera, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gaertnera bignonioides and Gaertnera macrophylla.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive reddish-brown stem. Its flowers are typically orange to yellow, appearing in clusters and blooming in late spring to early summer.

Gaertnera × gardneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
