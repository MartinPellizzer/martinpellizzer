Gnaphalium ecklonianum

Gnaphalium ecklonianum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and is native to southern Africa. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces a profusion of tiny white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant is also noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
