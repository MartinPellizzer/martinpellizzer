Gomphrena fallax

Gomphrena fallax, commonly known as the Mexican feather grass, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Gomphrena fallax is drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping, making it a low-maintenance addition to gardens.
