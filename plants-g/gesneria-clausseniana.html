Gesneria clausseniana

Gesneria clausseniana, commonly known as the Claußen’s gesneria, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Gesneria clausseniana is popular among plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
