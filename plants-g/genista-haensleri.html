Genista haensleri

Genista haensleri, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
