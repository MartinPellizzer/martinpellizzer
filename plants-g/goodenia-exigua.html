Goodenia exigua

Goodenia exigua, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Goodenia exigua is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
