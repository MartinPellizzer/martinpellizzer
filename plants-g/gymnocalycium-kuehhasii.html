Gymnocalycium kuehhasii

Gymnocalycium kuehhasii, commonly known as the Kuehne's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green or blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, usually in spring or early summer. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

Gymnocalycium kuehhasii is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
