Genista radiata

Genista radiata, commonly known as the yellow broom.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, yellow-orange flower clusters that bloom in spring. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.

Genista radiata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
