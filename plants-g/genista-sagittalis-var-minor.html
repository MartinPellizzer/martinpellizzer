Genista sagittalis var. minor

Genista sagittalis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser arrowhead broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, scale-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
