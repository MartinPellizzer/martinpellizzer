Gastrochilus anomalus

Gastrochilus anomalus, commonly known as the strange stomach orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, Vietnam, and Thailand. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

The species is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
