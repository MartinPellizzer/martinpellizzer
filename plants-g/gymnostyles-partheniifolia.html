Gymnostyles partheniifolia

Gymnostyles partheniifolia, commonly known as the golden buttonwood, is a small evergreen tree native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale grey bark and dense, rounded crown. Gymnostyles partheniifolia is well adapted to coastal conditions, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.
