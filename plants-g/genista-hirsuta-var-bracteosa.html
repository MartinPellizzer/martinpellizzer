Genista hirsuta var. bracteosa

Genista hirsuta var. bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteate broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems covered in dense, yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
