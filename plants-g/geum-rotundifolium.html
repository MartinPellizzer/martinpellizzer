Geum rotundifolium

Geum rotundifolium, commonly known as roundleaf avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features basal leaves that are round or heart-shaped, with a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
