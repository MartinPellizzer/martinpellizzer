Goodyera maximowicziana var. commelinoides

Goodyera maximowicziana var. commelinoides, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically borne on the upper surface of the leaves. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
