Gastrodia × nippouraiensis

Gastrodia × nippouraiensis, commonly known as the Japanese thunder god vine, is a hybrid orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often reddish-brown rhizomes, which are prized in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid species and its medicinal properties.
