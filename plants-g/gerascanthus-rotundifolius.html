Gerascanthus rotundifolius

Gerascanthus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rounded, velvety leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its blooming period typically occurs during the summer months, adding vibrant color to gardens.

Gerascanthus rotundifolius is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
