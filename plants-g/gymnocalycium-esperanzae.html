Gymnocalycium esperanzae

Gymnocalycium esperanzae, commonly known as the Esperanza Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus that typically forms a dense, rounded clump. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
