Galega berteroi

Galega berteroi, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 1 meter in height, characterized by its slender stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in the summer months. Its root system is known for containing alkaloids, which have been historically used in traditional medicine.

Galega berteroi thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.
