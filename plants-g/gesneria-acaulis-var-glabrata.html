Gesneria acaulis var. glabrata

Gesneria acaulis var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its specific epithet. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in the summer months. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light and moderate watering to thrive.
