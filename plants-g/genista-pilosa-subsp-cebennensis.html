Genista pilosa subsp. cebennensis

Genista pilosa subsp.

cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal. It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
