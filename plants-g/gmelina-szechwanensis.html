Gmelina szechwanensis

Gmelina szechwanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan gum tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Oleaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions and is valued for its timber and ornamental qualities.
