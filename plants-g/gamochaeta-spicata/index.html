Gamochaeta Spicata

Spined panicgrass, scientifically known as Gamochaeta spicata, is a resilient perennial grass native to North America. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in prairies and along roadsides. Its distinctive spiny bracts and narrow, grass-like leaves make it easily identifiable. Despite its rugged appearance, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. This plant is both a common sight and an important component of native ecosystems.
