Geranium africanum

Geranium africanum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with palmate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Geranium africanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
