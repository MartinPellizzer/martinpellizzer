Goniothalamus gabriacianus var. coriaceifolius

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive fragrance.
