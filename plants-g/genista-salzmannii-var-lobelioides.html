Genista salzmannii var. lobelioides

Genista salzmannii var. lobelioides, commonly known as the Salzmann's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
