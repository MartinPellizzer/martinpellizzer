Gouldia antiqua var. acuta

Gouldia antiqua var. acuta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often adorned with vibrant red or orange markings. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically yellow or orange, adding a splash of color to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
