Geonoma insignis

Geonoma insignis, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth, where it thrives in high humidity. The leaves are usually evergreen and can reach lengths of up to 1 meter, making it a striking addition to tropical gardens.
