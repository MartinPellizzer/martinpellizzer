Galeandra baueri

Galeandra baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. It typically grows as an epiphytic orchid, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

Galeandra baueri prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
