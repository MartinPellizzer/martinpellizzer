Grevillea miqueliana

Grevillea miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's glory, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in sandy or rocky soils. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid climates.

Grevillea miqueliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
