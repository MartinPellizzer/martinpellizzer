Guettarda viburnoides var. rhombifolia

Guettarda viburnoides var. rhombifolia, commonly known as the rhombifoliate guettarda, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptic to rhombic leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
