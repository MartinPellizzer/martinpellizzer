Gladiolus sprengelianus

Gladiolus sprengelianus, commonly known as the dwarf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features upright, strap-like leaves and produces spikes of colorful, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, blooming from late spring to early summer.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
