Grandiphyllum kraenzlinianum

Grandiphyllum kraenzlinianum, commonly known as the African sarsaparilla, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, purple-pink flowers. The plant produces legume pods that contain small, round seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodland and savanna habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
