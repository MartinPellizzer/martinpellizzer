Goodenia purpurascens

Goodenia purpurascens, commonly known as the red-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and woodlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
