Grevillea nana

Grevillea nana, commonly known as the dwarf grevillea, is a low-growing shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are densely arranged along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Grevillea nana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
