Garcinia urophylla

Garcinia urophylla, commonly known as the white mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white flowers that are often clustered together. The plant bears edible fruits that are typically round and have a thin, white rind. The fruit's interior contains segmented, juicy segments that are sweet and aromatic.

It is valued not only for its culinary uses but also for its potential medicinal properties.
