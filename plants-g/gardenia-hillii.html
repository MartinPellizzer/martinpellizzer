Gardenia hillii

Gardenia hillii, commonly known as the Hawaiian gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Hawaii.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and pleasant scent.

Gardenia hillii is also valued for its cultural significance in Hawaiian traditions.
