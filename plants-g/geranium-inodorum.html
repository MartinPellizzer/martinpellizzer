Geranium inodorum

Geranium inodorum, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
