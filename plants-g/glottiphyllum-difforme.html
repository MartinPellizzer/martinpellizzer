Glottiphyllum difforme

Glottiphyllum difforme, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its native arid environment. Its flowers are typically showy, with five petals and a distinctive spur.
