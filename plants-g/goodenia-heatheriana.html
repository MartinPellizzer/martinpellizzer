Goodenia heatheriana

Goodenia heatheriana, commonly known as the Heathen Goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathland environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
