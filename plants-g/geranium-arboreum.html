Geranium arboreum

Geranium arboreum, commonly known as the tree geranium, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a woody perennial that can grow up to 10 feet tall, forming a shrub-like structure with multiple stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture with a serrated margin. The plant produces showy pink to purple flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, woodland habitats.
