Gastrochilus carnosus

Gastrochilus carnosus, commonly known as the fleshy lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and a distinctive, showy orchid flower with a prominent, fleshy lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color, with a strong, spicy scent.

Gastrochilus carnosus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
