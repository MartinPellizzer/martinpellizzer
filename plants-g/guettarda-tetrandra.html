Guettarda tetrandra

Guettarda tetrandra, commonly known as the four-stamen guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with four stamens, which bloom throughout the year in some regions. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.

Guettarda tetrandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping in warm climates.
