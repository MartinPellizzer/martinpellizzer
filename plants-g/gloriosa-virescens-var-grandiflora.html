Gloriosa virescens var. grandiflora

Gloriosa virescens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the flame lily or golden glory, is a striking tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from orange to red, with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
