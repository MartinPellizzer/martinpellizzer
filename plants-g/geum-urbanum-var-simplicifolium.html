Geum urbanum var. simplicifolium

Geum urbanum var. simplicifolium, commonly known as the slender wood avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in soft, hairy pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a heart-shaped or ovate form with a serrated edge.

It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded or partially shaded areas.
