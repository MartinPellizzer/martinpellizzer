Gesneria leichlinii

Gesneria leichlinii, commonly known as the Leichlin's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria leichlinii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
