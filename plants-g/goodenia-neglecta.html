Goodenia neglecta

Goodenia neglecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Goodenia neglecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
