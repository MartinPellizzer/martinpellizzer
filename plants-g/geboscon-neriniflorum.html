Geboscon neriniflorum

Geboscon neriniflorum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery down. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale purple to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a small, toxic berry that turns from green to black as it matures.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
