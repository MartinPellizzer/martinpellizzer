Gerardia dulcis var. floridana

Gerardia dulcis var. floridana, commonly known as Florida sweet gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, showy pink or red flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds and streams.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
