Genista peloponnesiaca

Genista peloponnesiaca, commonly known as the Peloponnesian broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are typical of the genus Genista. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
