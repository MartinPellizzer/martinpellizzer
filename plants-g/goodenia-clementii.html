Goodenia clementii

Goodenia clementii, commonly known as the Clement's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, foliage. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Goodenia clementii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
