Gentiana Scabra Var. Buergeri

Gentiana scabra var. buergeri is a striking blue flowering plant native to eastern Asia. It is known for its vivid sky-blue petals that resemble the color of the sky. This variety of gentiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its beauty makes it a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts and botanists alike.
