Gymnacranthera cryptocaryoides

Gymnacranthera cryptocaryoides, commonly known as the blackwood tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its dark, dense wood and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. Its leaves are ovate, glossy, and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.
