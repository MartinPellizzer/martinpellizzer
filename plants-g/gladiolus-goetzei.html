Gladiolus goetzei

Gladiolus goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, erect stems. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful summer displays.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
