Gymnanthocereus microspermus

Gymnanthocereus microspermus, commonly known as the small-seeded night bloomer, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gymnanthocereus microspermus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming habit.
