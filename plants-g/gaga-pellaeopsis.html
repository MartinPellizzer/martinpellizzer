Gaga pellaeopsis

Gaga pellaeopsis, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact size and drought tolerance make it suitable for various landscaping applications.

The flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
