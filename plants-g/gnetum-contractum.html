Gnetum contractum

Gnetum contractum, commonly known as the African gnetum, is a member of the Gnetophyta division, which includes gymnosperms.

It is a tropical plant native to Africa, characterized by its woody stems and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and has a unique reproductive system involving both male and female cones.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of ancient plant lineages and their evolutionary adaptations.
