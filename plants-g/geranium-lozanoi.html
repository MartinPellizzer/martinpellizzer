Geranium lozanoi

Geranium lozanoi, commonly known as the Lozanoi geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink to red blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Geranium lozanoi is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
