Gesneria merckii

Gesneria merckii, commonly known as the Merck's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, fleshy, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Gesneria merckii requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
