Galinsoga semicalva var. percalva

Galinsoga semicalva var. percalva, commonly known as common gallinsoga, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features small, yellow flowers with five petals and hairy, alternate leaves that are usually heart-shaped. The plant produces numerous tiny seeds enclosed in a spiny, spherical capsule. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and invasive tendencies make it a common weed in many regions.
