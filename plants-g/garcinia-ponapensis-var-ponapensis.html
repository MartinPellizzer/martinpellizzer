Garcinia ponapensis var. ponapensis

Garcinia ponapensis var. ponapensis, commonly known as the Ponapen Garcinia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands, particularly the Caroline Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces bright orange, fleshy fruits that are typically covered in a thin, waxy coating. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal or lowland forest areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
