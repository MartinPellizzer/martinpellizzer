Glossodia major

Glossodia major, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking plant native to the eastern Cape Province of South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, making it one of the largest orchids in the world. The plant has a tall, erect stem that can grow up to two meters in height, supporting its impressive floral display. Its leaves are long, narrow, and covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Glossodia major thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
