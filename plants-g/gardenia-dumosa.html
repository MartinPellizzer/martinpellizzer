Gardenia dumosa

Gardenia dumosa, commonly known as the bush gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the white blooms.

Gardenia dumosa thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
