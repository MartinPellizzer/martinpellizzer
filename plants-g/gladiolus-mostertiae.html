Gladiolus mostertiae

Gladiolus mostertiae, commonly known as the Zanzibar gladiolus, is a striking flowering plant native to the island of Zanzibar in Tanzania.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
