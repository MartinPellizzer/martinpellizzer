Garcinia rubriflora

Garcinia rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Garcinia rubriflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
