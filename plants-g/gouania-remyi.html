Gouania remyi

Gouania remyi, commonly known as the Mexican gooseberry, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub native to Mexico and Central America, characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. Gouania remyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
