Grevillea scortechinii subsp. scortechinii

Grevillea scortechinii subsp.

scortechinii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Grevillea, is a shrub native to southwestern Western Australia. It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically bright green and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
