Gaillardia × grandiflora

Gaillardia × grandiflora, commonly known as the blanket flower, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the United States.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright orange and red petals, often with a yellow center. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It is drought-tolerant and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its foliage is gray-green and becomes more prominent in the fall, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.
