Geranium tuberosum var. debile

Geranium tuberosum var. debile, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom from summer to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both garden and landscape settings.
