Gurania velutina

Gurania velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf gurania, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in pale yellow or greenish hues.
