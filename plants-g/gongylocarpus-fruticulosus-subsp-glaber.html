Gongylocarpus fruticulosus subsp. glaber

Gongylocarpus fruticulosus subsp.

glaber, commonly known as the smooth thimble cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in small, smooth spines.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures, typical of many cacti.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for arid garden environments.
