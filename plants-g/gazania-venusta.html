Gazania venusta

Gazania venusta, commonly known as the showy gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features a basal rosette of fleshy, gray-green leaves that help it conserve water. It produces showy ray florets surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers.

Gazania venusta is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
