Grona rubra

Grona rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered grona, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species with showy flowers and aromatic leaves. The plant is characterized by its striking red petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Grona rubra thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in mountainous forests.
