Gibasis linearis var. graminifolia

Gibasis linearis var. graminifolia, commonly known as the slender gibasis, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its delicate foliage and compact growth habit, it is often used in ground cover or as a filler plant in mixed borders.
