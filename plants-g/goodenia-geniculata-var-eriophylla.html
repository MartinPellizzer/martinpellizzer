Goodenia geniculata var. eriophylla

Goodenia geniculata var. eriophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

The variety eriophylla is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Goodenia geniculata.
