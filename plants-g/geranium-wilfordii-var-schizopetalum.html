Geranium wilfordii var. schizopetalum

Geranium wilfordii var. schizopetalum, commonly known as the Schizopetalum geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a serrated margin, and the plant produces distinctive, two-lobed seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
