Grewia obovata

Grewia obovata, commonly known as the roundleaf globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are broad, oval, and often have a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

The plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
