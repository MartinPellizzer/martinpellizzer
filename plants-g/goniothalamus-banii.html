Goniothalamus banii

Goniothalamus banii, commonly known as the Bornean goniothalamus, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive floral structure that includes a prominent perianth.

The fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
