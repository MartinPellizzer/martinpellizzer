Geniostoma grandifolium

Geniostoma grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaved geniostoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Geniostoma grandifolium is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
