Geranium Eriostemon F. Onoei

Japanese geranium, scientifically known as Geranium eriostemon f. onoei, is a popular ornamental plant native to Japan. It is prized for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features delicate, palmate leaves and compact growth habits, making it ideal for gardens and containers. Its ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil adds to its versatility in landscaping. With its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, it remains a favorite among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
