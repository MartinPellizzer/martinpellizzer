Geranium core-core

Geranium core-core, commonly known as the ivy-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
