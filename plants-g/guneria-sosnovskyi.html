Guneria sosnovskyi

Guneria sosnovskyi, commonly known as the Sosnovsky's gunnera, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gunneraceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it requires specific care to maintain its health.

The plant also plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
