Guettarda crispiflora var. sabiceoides

Guettarda crispiflora var. sabiceoides, commonly known as the coastal guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface.

It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
