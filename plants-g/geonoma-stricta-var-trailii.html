Geonoma stricta var. trailii

Geonoma stricta var. trailii, commonly known as the Trail's bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a dense rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that give it a bamboo-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are a characteristic feature of the genus Geonoma. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance for moderate watering.

Its unique foliage and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both residential and commercial settings.
