Goeppertia altissima

Goeppertia altissima, commonly known as the giant calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
