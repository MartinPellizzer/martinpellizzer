Gymnocalycium spegazzinii subsp. bayrianum

Gymnocalycium spegazzinii subsp.

bayrianum, commonly known as the Bayrianum cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Argentina. It features a globular to cylindrical stem that is typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and hardiness make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
