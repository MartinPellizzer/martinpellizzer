Glossonema erlangeri

Glossonema erlangeri, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-yellow in color, and it produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Glossonema erlangeri is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain environments.
