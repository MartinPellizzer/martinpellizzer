Gerbera cineraria

Gerbera cineraria, commonly known as the silver gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has hairy, grayish-green foliage that gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera cineraria is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in cut flower production.
