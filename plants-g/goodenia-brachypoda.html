Goodenia brachypoda

Goodenia brachypoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and sometimes hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia brachypoda is often used in native gardens due to its ornamental value and adaptability to dry conditions.
