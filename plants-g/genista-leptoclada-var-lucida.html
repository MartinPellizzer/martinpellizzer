Genista leptoclada var. lucida

Genista leptoclada var. lucida, commonly known as the shining broom.

This shrub is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
