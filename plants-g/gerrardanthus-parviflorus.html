Gerrardanthus parviflorus

Gerrardanthus parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered gerrardanthus, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

Gerrardanthus parviflorus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
