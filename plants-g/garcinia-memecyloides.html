Garcinia memecyloides

Garcinia memecyloides, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, yellow rind and a segmented, white or pinkish pulp. The fruit is often consumed fresh or used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and phytotherapy.
