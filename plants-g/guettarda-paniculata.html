Guettarda paniculata

Guettarda paniculata, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in panicles, giving it a striking appearance during the flowering season. The plant has opposite, simple, and often leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda paniculata is also valued for its ability to withstand coastal conditions, making it suitable for coastal landscaping.
