Gnaphalium armenium

Gnaphalium armenium, commonly known as Armenian cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Europe.

Gnaphalium armenium is often found in open, sunny habitats and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
