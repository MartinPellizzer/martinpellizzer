Gasteria kirsteana

Gasteria kirsteana, commonly known as the Cape goldfish plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings resembling a goldfish. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria kirsteana is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
