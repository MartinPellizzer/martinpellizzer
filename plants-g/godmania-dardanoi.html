Godmania dardanoi

Godmania dardanoi, commonly known as the Dardani orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks, and requires specific conditions to thrive. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it a unique example of an epiphytic species.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
