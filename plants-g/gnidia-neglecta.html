Gnidia neglecta

Gnidia neglecta, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Gnidia neglecta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
