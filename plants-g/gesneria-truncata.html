Gesneria truncata

Gesneria truncata, commonly known as the truncated gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

Gesneria truncata prefers bright, indirect light and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
