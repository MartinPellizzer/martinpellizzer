Gaillardia megapotamica var. megapotamica

Gaillardia megapotamica var. megapotamica, commonly known as the Mexican firewheel, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of orange to brown petals. The plant typically grows in full sun and well-drained soil, thriving in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

Gaillardia megapotamica var. megapotamica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
