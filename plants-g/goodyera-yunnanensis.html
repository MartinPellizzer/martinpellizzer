Goodyera yunnanensis

Goodyera yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and intricate, grass-like leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in late summer, typically arranged in a loose raceme with violet or pale blue petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Goodyera yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its ecological and medicinal potential.
