Gymnocalycium marsoneri subsp. matoense

Gymnocalycium marsoneri subsp.

matoense, commonly known as the Matoense Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in white or pale blue spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne on the areoles. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
