Goodenia mitchellii

Goodenia mitchellii, commonly known as Mitchell's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Mitchell's goodenia is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
