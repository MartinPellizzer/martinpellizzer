Guzmania kalbreyeri

Guzmania kalbreyeri, commonly known as the red-tipped guzmania, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that typically displays red or orange hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports small aquatic life. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Guzmania kalbreyeri is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
