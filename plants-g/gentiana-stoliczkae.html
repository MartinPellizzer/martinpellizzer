Gentiana stoliczkae

Gentiana stoliczkae, commonly known as the Stoliczka's gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is erect and usually unbranched.

Gentiana stoliczkae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
