Gasteria crassifolia

Gasteria crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal watering.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
