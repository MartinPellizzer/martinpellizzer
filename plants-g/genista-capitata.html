Genista capitata

Genista capitata, commonly known as the yellow broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, creeping stems and small, leathery leaves. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

Genista capitata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
