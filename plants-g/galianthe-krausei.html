Galianthe krausei

Galianthe krausei, commonly known as the Krause's galianthe, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Galianthe krausei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
