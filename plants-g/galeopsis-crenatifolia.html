Galeopsis crenatifolia

Galeopsis crenatifolia, commonly known as the wavy-leaved hedge-nettle, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Galeopsis crenatifolia is an annual plant that is sometimes considered a weed but has historical uses in traditional medicine.
