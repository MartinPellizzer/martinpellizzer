Geranium roseum

Geranium roseum, commonly known as the rose geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often aromatic, and it produces small, cylindrical fruits containing seeds. Geranium roseum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and easy care requirements contribute to its widespread cultivation.
