Geonoma ramosa

Geonoma ramosa, commonly known as the ramose fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

Geonoma ramosa is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in forest understories, showcasing its adaptability to various microhabitats.
