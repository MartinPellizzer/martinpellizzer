Guajava mollis

Guajava mollis, commonly known as the velvet guava, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.

The leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.
