Gentiana carpatica

Gentiana carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian gentian, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentiana carpatica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
