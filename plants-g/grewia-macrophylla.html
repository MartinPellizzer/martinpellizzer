Grewia macrophylla

Grewia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved grewia, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that can reach up to 20 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and fruit.
