Gnaphalium proteoides

Gnaphalium proteoides, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a cut flower.
