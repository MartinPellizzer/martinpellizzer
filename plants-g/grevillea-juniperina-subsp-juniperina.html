Grevillea juniperina subsp. juniperina

Grevillea juniperina subsp.

juniperina, commonly known as the Juniperina Grevillea, is a shrub native to southern Western Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.

The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces bright orange or yellow flowers in clusters. It is well-suited for coastal gardens due to its salt tolerance and drought resistance.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and ornamental value.
