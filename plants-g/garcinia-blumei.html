Garcinia blumei

Garcinia blumei, commonly known as the Blume's garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, reddish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Garcinia blumei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within its native habitat.
