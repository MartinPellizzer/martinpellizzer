Gentiana yunnanensis subsp. kialensis

Gentiana yunnanensis subsp.

kialensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along streambanks. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are usually unbranched.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
