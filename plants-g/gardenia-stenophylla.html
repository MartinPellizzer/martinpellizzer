Gardenia stenophylla

Gardenia stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and elongated. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Gardenia stenophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
