Gymnocoronis attenuata

Gymnocoronis attenuata, commonly known as the slender false dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
