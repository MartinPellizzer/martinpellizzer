Gesneria yamuriensis

Gesneria yamuriensis, commonly known as the Yamuriensis Gesneria, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking foliage and flowering habits. The plant typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and vibrant green coloration. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, often during the summer months.

Gesneria yamuriensis is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
