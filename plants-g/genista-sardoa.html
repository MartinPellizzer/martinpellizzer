Genista sardoa

Genista sardoa, commonly known as the Sardinian broom, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sardinia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista sardoa is notable for its hardy nature and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
