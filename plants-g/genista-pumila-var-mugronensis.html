Genista pumila var. mugronensis

Genista pumila var. mugronensis, commonly known as the Mugronese broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, needle-like leaves and produces yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xerophytic environments. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics include a compact growth habit and a long flowering period.
