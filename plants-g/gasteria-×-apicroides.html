Gasteria × apicroides

Gasteria × apicroides, commonly known as the Zulu candle or Zulu torch, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy coating that helps it store water. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, giving the plant a candle-like appearance. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, upright stems during the spring or summer.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
