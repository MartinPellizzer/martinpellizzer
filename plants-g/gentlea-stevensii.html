Gentlea stevensii

Gentlea stevensii, commonly known as the silverleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are silver-green, giving it its common name, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
