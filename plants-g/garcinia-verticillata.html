Garcinia verticillata

Garcinia verticillata, commonly known as the African starfruit or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, yellow-orange fruits that resemble small star-shaped berries. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, white flowers with a strong scent. Its fruits are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of Garcinia verticillata make it an interesting subject for both ecological and medicinal research.
