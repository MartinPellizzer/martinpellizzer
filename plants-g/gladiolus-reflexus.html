Gladiolus reflexus

Gladiolus reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with reflexed (bent backward) petals that give the plant its common name. It blooms during the summer months and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
