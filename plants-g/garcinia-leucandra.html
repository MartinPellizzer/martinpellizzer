Garcinia leucandra

Garcinia leucandra, commonly known as the white Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
