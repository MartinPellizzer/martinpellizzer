Galium pastorale

Galium pastorale, commonly known as bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, opposite leaves that are often hairy in texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
