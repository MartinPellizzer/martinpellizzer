Goodyera mairei

Goodyera mairei, commonly known as the Maire's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a unique slipper-like pouch, which is a feature common in certain orchid species. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Goodyera mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
