Goodenia glabrata

Goodenia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

Goodenia glabrata is often found in open woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native gardens.
