Gladiolus rehmannii

Gladiolus rehmannii, commonly known as the Chinese gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features tall, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped, bright yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants.
