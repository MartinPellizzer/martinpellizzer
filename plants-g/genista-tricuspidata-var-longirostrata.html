Genista tricuspidata var. longirostrata

Genista tricuspidata var. longirostrata, commonly known as the long-beaked broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, needle-like leaves. Its flowers are yellow and pea-shaped, typical of the legume family.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
