Gymnema macrothyrsa

Gymnema macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-leaved gymnema, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a prominent midrib. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Gymnema macrothyrsa is notable for its ability to thrive in humid tropical environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological and medicinal research.
