Grewia Mollis Var. Trichocarpa

Cape honeysuckle, scientifically known as Grewia mollis var. trichocarpa, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is renowned for its vibrant orange-red blooms that appear in clusters during the summer months. This versatile plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions. Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping. Despite its beauty, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and prevent overgrowth.
