Garcinia macrantha

Garcinia macrantha, commonly known as the African yellow wood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, yellowish bark and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into orange-red berries. Garcinia macrantha is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and ornamental purposes.
