Galeandra camptoceras

Galeandra camptoceras, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil.

Galeandra camptoceras is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable climate.
