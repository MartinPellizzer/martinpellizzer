Geraniospermum dasycaule

Geraniospermum dasycaule, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its hairy, succulent stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to dry conditions.

Geraniospermum dasycaule is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
