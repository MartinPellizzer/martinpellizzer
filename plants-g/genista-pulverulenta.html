Genista pulverulenta

Genista pulverulenta, commonly known as the dusty broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch of color to its habitat. The plant has slender, branched stems covered in fine, grayish hairs, giving it a dusty appearance. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in pairs along the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.
