Grevillea synapheae subsp. latiloba

Grevillea synapheae subsp.

latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in cylindrical clusters, which are typical of the Grevillea genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
