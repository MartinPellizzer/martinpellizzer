Graphardisia nigrovirens

Graphardisia nigrovirens, commonly known as the black-green orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green leaves and striking, dark purple to black flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and moths.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex floral structure and specialized adaptations for its ecological niche.
