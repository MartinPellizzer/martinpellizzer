Grevillea pilulifera

Grevillea pilulifera, commonly known as the red globular grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red, globular flower heads that appear in spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces nectar-rich flowers that attract birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea pilulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
