Guettarda finlaysoniana

Guettarda finlaysoniana, commonly known as the Hawaiian guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Hawaii.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Guettarda finlaysoniana is well adapted to coastal and dry forest environments, showcasing resilience to salt spray and drought.
