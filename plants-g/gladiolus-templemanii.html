Gladiolus templemanii

Gladiolus templemanii, commonly known as the Templeman's gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and summer blooming cycle. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive in dry conditions, making it adaptable to various soil types.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
