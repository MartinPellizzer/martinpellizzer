Gonostemon × marginatus

Gonostemon × marginatus, commonly known as the hybrid mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dense, leathery leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by edible, segmented fruits resembling those of the true mangosteen. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
