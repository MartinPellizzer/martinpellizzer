Gothofreda grandiflora

Gothofreda grandiflora, commonly known as the royal flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Gothofreda grandiflora is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
