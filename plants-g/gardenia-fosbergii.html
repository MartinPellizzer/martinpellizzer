Gardenia fosbergii

Gardenia fosbergii, commonly known as Fosberg's gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Maui in Hawaii.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique fragrance.

Gardenia fosbergii is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
