Grewia evrardii

Grewia evrardii, commonly known as the African starwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a velvety underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds and some mammals. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Grewia evrardii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
