Gentiana Kingdonii

Gentiana kingdonii is a rare and striking alpine plant native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China. Known for its deep blue to violet flowers, it thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. This species is part of the gentian family, which is renowned for its vibrant blooms and ecological importance. Due to its limited habitat and threats from climate change, Gentiana kingdonii is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and fragile species for future generations.
