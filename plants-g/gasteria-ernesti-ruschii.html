Gasteria ernesti-ruschii

Gasteria ernesti-ruschii, commonly known as the Erniet's gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy coating, which help it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
