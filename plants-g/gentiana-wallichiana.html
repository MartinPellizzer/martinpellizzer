Gentiana wallichiana

Gentiana wallichiana, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are often hairy.

Gentiana wallichiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
