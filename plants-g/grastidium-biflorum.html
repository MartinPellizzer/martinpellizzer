Grastidium biflorum

Grastidium biflorum, commonly known as the two-flowered grass.

It is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in pairs, giving it its scientific name.

Grastidium biflorum is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and riverbanks.
