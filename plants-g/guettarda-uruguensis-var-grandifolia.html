Guettarda uruguensis var. grandifolia

Guettarda uruguensis var. grandifolia, commonly known as the Uruguayan guettarda, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The species is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to tolerate partial shade.
