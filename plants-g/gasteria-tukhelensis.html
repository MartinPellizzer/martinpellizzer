Gasteria tukhelensis

Gasteria tukhelensis, commonly known as the Tukhelen's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall stems, which are usually white or pale yellow in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Gasteria tukhelensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
