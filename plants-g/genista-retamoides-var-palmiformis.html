Genista retamoides var. palmiformis

Genista retamoides var. palmiformis, commonly known as the palmate genista, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are palmately divided, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
