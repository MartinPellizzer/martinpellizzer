Globularia vulgaris subsp. montserratensis

Globularia vulgaris subsp.

montserratensis, commonly known as the Montserrat globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats. It is a perennial species with succulent-like leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
