Genista Aspalathoides Var. Mauritanica

Genista aspalathoides var. mauritanica is a hardy shrub native to North Africa and the Canary Islands. It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. This plant is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it suitable for gardeners in Mediterranean climates. Despite its modest appearance, it holds cultural significance in regions where it is traditionally used.
