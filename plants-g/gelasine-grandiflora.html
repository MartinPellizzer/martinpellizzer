Gelasine grandiflora

Gelasine grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered gelasine, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Gelasine grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
