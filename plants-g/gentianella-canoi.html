Gentianella canoi

Gentianella canoi, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, and it is often found in open grasslands or meadows.

The species is notable for its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
