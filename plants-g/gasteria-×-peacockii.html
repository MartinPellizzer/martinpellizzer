Gasteria × peacockii

Gasteria × peacockii, commonly known as the Peacock Gasteria, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow marginal markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular, white to yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
