Geonoma latifolia

Geonoma latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf geonoma, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Geonoma latifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
