Galbulimima baccata

Galbulimima baccata, commonly known as the yellow bell.

It is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Galbulimima baccata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
