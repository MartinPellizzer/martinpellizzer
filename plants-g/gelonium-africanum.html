Gelonium africanum

Gelonium africanum, commonly known as the African gelonium, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.
