Gladiolus tigrinus

Gladiolus tigrinus, commonly known as the tiger gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a fan-like pattern around the stem.

The plant prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and floral arrangements.
