Gomesa stricta

Gomesa stricta, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Gomesa stricta is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
