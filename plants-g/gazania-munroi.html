Gazania munroi

Gazania munroi, commonly known as the yellow gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Gazania munroi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its bright, long-lasting blooms.
