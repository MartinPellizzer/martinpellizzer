Gleichenia koordersii

Gleichenia koordersii, commonly known as the Koord's fern, is a species of fern native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, strap-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Gleichenia koordersii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
