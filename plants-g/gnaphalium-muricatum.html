Gnaphalium muricatum

Gnaphalium muricatum, commonly known as the rough sandwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.
