Geranium moorei

Geranium moorei, commonly known as the Cape vermillion, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant red flowers with a distinctive black center, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in soft, velvety leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium moorei is also valued for its long flowering period and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
