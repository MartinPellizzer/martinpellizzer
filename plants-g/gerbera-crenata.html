Gerbera crenata

Gerbera crenata, commonly known as the zigzag gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Gerbera crenata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
