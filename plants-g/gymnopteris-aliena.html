Gymnopteris aliena

Gymnopteris aliena, commonly known as the alien fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Gymnopteris aliena has small, spore-producing sporangia that are clustered on the underside of its fronds.

This fern is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
