Gonostemon glabricaulis

Gonostemon glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed gonostemon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, smooth stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
