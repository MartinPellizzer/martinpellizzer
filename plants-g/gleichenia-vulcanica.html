Gleichenia vulcanica

Gleichenia vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic fern, is a member of the Gleicheniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found near volcanic soils, which provide the necessary nutrients for its growth. The plant has a unique ability to absorb minerals from the soil, which contributes to its survival in nutrient-rich volcanic regions. Its spores are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

Gleichenia vulcanica plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitats.
