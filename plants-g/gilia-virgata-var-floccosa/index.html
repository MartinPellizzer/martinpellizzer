Gilia Virgata Var. Floccosa

Gilia virgata var. floccosa is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the western united states. it is known for its fluffy, cotton-like blooms that add a soft and whimsical touch to its surroundings. this variety of gilia thrives in rocky and open habitats, often found in mountainous regions. its delicate flowers come in shades of pink and white, creating a vivid contrast against the rugged landscape. it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value.
