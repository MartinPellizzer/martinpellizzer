Geranium baschkyzylsaicum

Geranium baschkyzylsaicum, commonly known as the Bashkir geranium, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and serrated leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
