Goodenia muelleriana

Goodenia muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
