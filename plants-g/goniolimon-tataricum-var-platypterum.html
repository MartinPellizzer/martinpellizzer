Goniolimon tataricum var. platypterum

Goniolimon tataricum var. platypterum, commonly known as the flat-winged tatarian goniolimon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the steppes of Eastern Europe and Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological adaptations and potential horticultural value.
