Gastrodia elata f. viridis

Gastrodia elata f. viridis, commonly known as the green-stemd tiger tongue, is a rare and distinctive variant of the gastrodia plant.

It is characterized by its unique greenish stems and is native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This fern-like plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered a protected species in several regions.
