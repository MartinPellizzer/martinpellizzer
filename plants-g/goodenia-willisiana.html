Goodenia willisiana

Goodenia willisiana, commonly known as the willisiana goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is found in regions such as Western Australia. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, and the flowers come in shades of pink, purple, or white.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
