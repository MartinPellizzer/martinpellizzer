Gymnadeniopsis nivea

Gymnadeniopsis nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia. This herbaceous perennial has a distinctive appearance with its slender, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, acidic soils.

Its blooming period occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a unique ornamental value to its habitat.
