Gladiolus × lewisiae

Gladiolus × lewisiae, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is native to South Africa, where it thrives in diverse habitats. The plant features sword-shaped leaves and tall, erect stems that support its vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its bold floral display and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to flourish optimally.
