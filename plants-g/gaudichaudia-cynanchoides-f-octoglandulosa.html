Gaudichaudia cynanchoides f. octoglandulosa

Gaudichaudia cynanchoides f. octoglandulosa, commonly known as the hairy gaudichaudia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
