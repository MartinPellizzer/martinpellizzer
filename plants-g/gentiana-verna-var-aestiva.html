Gentiana verna var. aestiva

Gentiana verna var. aestiva, commonly known as the early gentian, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It produces small, tubular flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully develop.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
