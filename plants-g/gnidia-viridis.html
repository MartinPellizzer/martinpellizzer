Gnidia viridis

Gnidia viridis, commonly known as the green gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
