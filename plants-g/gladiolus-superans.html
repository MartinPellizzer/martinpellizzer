Gladiolus superans

Gladiolus superans, commonly known as the tall gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with brightly colored flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a fan-like pattern along the stem.

Gladiolus superans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
