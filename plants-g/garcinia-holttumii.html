Garcinia holttumii

Garcinia holttumii, commonly known as the yellow garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is notable for its elongated, orange-red fruits, which are covered in a thick, waxy coating. Garcinia holttumii is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually striking member of the Clusiaceae family.
