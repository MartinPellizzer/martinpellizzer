Guzmania manzanaresiorum

Guzmania manzanaresiorum, commonly known as the Manzanares guzmania, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can last for several months. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports small aquatic life. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow.

Guzmania manzanaresiorum thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
