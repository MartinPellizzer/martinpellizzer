Glandora nitida

Glandora nitida, commonly known as the glossy leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a shiny appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Glandora nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
