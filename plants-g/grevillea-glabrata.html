Grevillea glabrata

Grevillea glabrata, commonly known as the smooth grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Grevillea glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
