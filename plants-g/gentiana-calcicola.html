Gentiana calcicola

Gentiana calcicola, commonly known as the limestone gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Gentiana calcicola is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
