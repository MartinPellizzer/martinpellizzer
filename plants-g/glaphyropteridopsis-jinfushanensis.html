Glaphyropteridopsis jinfushanensis

Glaphyropteridopsis jinfushanensis, commonly known as the Jinfushan glaphyropterid, is a rare fossil plant species discovered in China.

It belongs to the order Sphenophyta and is characterized by its unique, leaf-like structures preserved in sedimentary rock. The plant exhibits features typical of early vascular plants, with a complex arrangement of vascular tissues. Its morphology provides important insights into the evolution of early land plants during the late Paleozoic era.

This species is significant for understanding the transition from aquatic to terrestrial life in plant evolution.
