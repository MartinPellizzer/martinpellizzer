Gladiolus oatesii

Gladiolus oatesii, commonly known as the Oates' gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, branched stems that bear clusters of vibrant, colorful flowers. The flowers typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed petal structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
