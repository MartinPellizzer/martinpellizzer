Geranium drakensbergense

Geranium drakensbergense, commonly known as the Drakensberg geranium, is a striking perennial herb native to the Drakensberg mountain range in South Africa.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink and purple, typically blooming from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
