Genista capitellata var. tunetana

Genista capitellata var. tunetana, commonly known as the Tunisian broom, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and is adapted to arid and rocky environments. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Its botanical features make it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping and coastal landscaping.
