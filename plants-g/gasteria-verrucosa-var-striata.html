Gasteria verrucosa var. striata

Gasteria verrucosa var. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale green stripes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
