Grevillea brachyantha

Grevillea brachyantha, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Grevillea brachyantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-water conditions.
