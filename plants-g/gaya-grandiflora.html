Gaya grandiflora

Gaya grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered gaya, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Gaya grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
