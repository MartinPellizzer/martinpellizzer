Goeppertia grandis

Goeppertia grandis, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Goeppertia grandis is also valued for its ability to purify the air and its potential medicinal properties.
