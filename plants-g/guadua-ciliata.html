Guadua ciliata

Guadua ciliata, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, palm-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid lowland areas.

Guadua ciliata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine and construction.
