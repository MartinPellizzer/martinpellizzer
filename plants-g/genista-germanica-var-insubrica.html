Genista germanica var. insubrica

Genista germanica var. insubrica, commonly known as the Italian broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
