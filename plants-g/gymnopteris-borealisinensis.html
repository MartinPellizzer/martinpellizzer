Gymnopteris borealisinensis

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is typically found in alpine or subarctic environments. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are clustered on the underside of the fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Gymnopteris borealisinensis plays a minor but ecologically significant role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitat.
