Gentiana sabbatioides

Gentiana sabbatioides, commonly known as the Kashmir gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana sabbatioides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
