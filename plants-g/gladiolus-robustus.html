Gladiolus robustus

Gladiolus robustus, commonly known as the robust gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white, with a distinctive sword-shaped leaf structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
