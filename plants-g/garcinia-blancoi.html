Garcinia blancoi

Garcinia blancoi, commonly known as the white Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine. Garcinia blancoi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties have contributed to its cultivation in some regions.
