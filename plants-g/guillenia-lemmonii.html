Guillenia lemmonii

Guillenia lemmonii, commonly known as the Lemmon's guillenia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue to purple and bloom in late spring or early summer.

Guillenia lemmonii is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often found in desert scrub habitats.
