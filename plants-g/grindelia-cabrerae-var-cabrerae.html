Grindelia cabrerae var. cabrerae

Grindelia cabrerae var. cabrerae, commonly known as the Cabrera's gumweed, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray, woolly foliage and produces yellow to orange composite flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and grassland habitats.

Grindelia cabrerae var. cabrerae is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is sometimes used in traditional herbal practices.
