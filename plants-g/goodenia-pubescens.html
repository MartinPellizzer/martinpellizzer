Goodenia pubescens

Goodenia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the warmer months.

Goodenia pubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
