Gleichenia brittonii

Gleichenia brittonii, commonly known as the Britton's Gleichenia, is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is a small, evergreen plant with a creeping rhizome and delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinct, glossy appearance and a pale underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
