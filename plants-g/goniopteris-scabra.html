Goniopteris scabra

Goniopteris scabra, commonly known as the rough huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rugged, and often hairy fronds that are typically lance-shaped. Goniopteris scabra is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in forest understories, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and ecological balance.
