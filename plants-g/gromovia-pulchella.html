Gromovia pulchella

Gromovia pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful gromovia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gromoviacaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Gromovia pulchella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
