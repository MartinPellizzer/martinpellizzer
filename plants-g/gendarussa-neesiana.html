Gendarussa neesiana

Gendarussa neesiana, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near rivers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and public spaces for its showy floral display.
