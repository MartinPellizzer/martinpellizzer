Globimetula elegantiflora

Globimetula elegantiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf globeweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a waxy texture that reflects sunlight.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.
