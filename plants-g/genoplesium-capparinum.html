Genoplesium capparinum

Genoplesium capparinum, commonly known as the Capparinum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as understorey forests. It produces a single spike of flowers, each bearing a single, elongated bloom.
