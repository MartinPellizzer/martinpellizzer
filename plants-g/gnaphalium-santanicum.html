Gnaphalium santanicum

Gnaphalium santanicum, commonly known as the sacred everlasting flower, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
