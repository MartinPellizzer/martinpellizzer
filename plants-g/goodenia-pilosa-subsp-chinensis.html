Goodenia pilosa subsp. chinensis

Goodenia pilosa subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese goodenia, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, upright stems and small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
