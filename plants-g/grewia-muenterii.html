Grewia muenterii

Grewia muenterii, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescences that bear small, bright red flowers. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.
