Gouldia terminalis f. russii

Gouldia terminalis f. russii, commonly known as the Russii form of the Gouldia terminalis, is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange, giving it a dramatic visual appeal. It thrives in humid, bright indirect light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in indoor and greenhouse settings.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small spaces, adding a tropical flair to any collection.
