Goodenia heterochila

Goodenia heterochila, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as woodlands and heathlands. It is a perennial herb that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
