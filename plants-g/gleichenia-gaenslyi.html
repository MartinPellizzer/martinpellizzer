Gleichenia gaenslyi

Gleichenia gaenslyi, commonly known as the Gaenslyi spleenwort, is a species of moss belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, carpet-like growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant has elongated, strap-shaped leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. It is typically found in forest floors, rock crevices, and other humid habitats.

Gleichenia gaenslyi plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various small organisms.
