Gynerium argenteum

Gynerium argenteum, commonly known as silverstem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported diuretic properties.

Gynerium argenteum is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking appearance.
