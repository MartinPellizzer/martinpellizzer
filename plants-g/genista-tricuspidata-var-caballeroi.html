Genista tricuspidata var. caballeroi

Genista tricuspidata var. caballeroi, commonly known as the Caballero's buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves, which are compound and consist of three leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal flowering.
