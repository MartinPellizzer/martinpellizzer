Gynochthodes litseifolia

Gynochthodes litseifolia, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
