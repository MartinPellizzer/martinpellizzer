Grammitis javanica

Grammitis javanica, commonly known as the Javan hound's-tongue, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.
