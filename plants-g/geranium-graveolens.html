Geranium graveolens

Geranium graveolens, commonly known as the sweet geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Geranium graveolens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
