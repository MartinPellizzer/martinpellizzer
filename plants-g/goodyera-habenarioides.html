Goodyera habenarioides

Goodyera habenarioides, commonly known as the broad-leafed slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. It has a short rhizome and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom from spring to summer.

Goodyera habenarioides is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its delicate appearance.
