Grevillea linearifolia

Grevillea linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features long, narrow, linear leaves that are typically bright green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea linearifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
