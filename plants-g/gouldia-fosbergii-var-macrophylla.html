Gouldia fosbergii var. macrophylla

Gouldia fosbergii var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange and yellow, often with contrasting dark centers. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are glossy and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like birds and insects.
