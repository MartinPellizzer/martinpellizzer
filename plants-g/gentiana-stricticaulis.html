Gentiana stricticaulis

Gentiana stricticaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stem gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

Gentiana stricticaulis is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
