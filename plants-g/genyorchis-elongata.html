Genyorchis elongata

Genyorchis elongata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It has a sympodial growth habit, with new growths arising from the base of the previous stem.

Genyorchis elongata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
