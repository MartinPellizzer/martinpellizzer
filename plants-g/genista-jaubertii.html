Genista jaubertii

Genista jaubertii, commonly known as the Jaubert's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in small, needle-like leaves. Its yellow flowers appear in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments.
