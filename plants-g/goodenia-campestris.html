Goodenia campestris

Goodenia campestris, commonly known as the field goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are often narrow and hairy, and the flowers are usually pale blue or purple in color.

Goodenia campestris is an annual or biennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
