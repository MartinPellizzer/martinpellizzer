Galeorchis cyclochila

Galeorchis cyclochila, commonly known as the cyclochila orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow blooms with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown spots. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many parts of its range.
