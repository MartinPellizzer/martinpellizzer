Garcinia lowryi

Garcinia lowryi, commonly known as the Lowry's garcinia, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain a single seed.

Garcinia lowryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic features.
