Grewia flavescens var. flavescens

Grewia flavescens var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of yellow, globular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, arid environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and drought tolerance.
