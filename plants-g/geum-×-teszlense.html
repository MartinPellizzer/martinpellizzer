Geum × teszlense

Geum × teszlense, commonly known as the hybrid garden buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed, with a hairy texture, and upright, branched stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or wetlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
