Geranium gracile

Geranium gracile, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, where it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, rounded leaves and produces clusters of delicate, pink or purple flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its compact size and attractive blooms.

Geranium gracile is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained conditions.
