Geissorhiza exscapa

Geissorhiza exscapa, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that come in shades of purple and white. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves and has a fibrous root system adapted to its arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Geissorhiza exscapa is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
