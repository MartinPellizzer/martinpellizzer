Gymnocalycium schickendantzii

Gymnocalycium schickendantzii, commonly known as the Schickendantzii moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to southern South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stem for survival in arid environments.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and unique appearance.
