Gynura cusimbua var. petiolata

Gynura cusimbua var. petiolata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or horseleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
