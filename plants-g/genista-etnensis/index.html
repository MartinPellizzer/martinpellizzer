Genista Etnensis

Genista etnensis is a rare and striking plant native to the volcanic slopes of Sicily. Known for its vivid yellow flowers and delicate foliage, it thrives in harsh, sun-drenched environments. This shrub is a symbol of resilience, adapting to the extreme conditions of its mountainous habitat. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and nature enthusiasts. Protecting Genista etnensis is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of its fragile ecosystem.
