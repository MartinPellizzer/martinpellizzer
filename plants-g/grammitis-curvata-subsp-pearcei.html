Grammitis curvata subsp. pearcei

Grammitis curvata subsp.

pearcei, commonly known as the pearce's filmy fern, is a species of epiphytic fern characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are thin and translucent, with a distinctive, finely divided structure that enhances its ability to absorb moisture from the air.
