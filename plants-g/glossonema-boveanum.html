Glossonema boveanum

Glossonema boveanum, commonly known as the Bovean's ghost plant, is a unique aquatic plant characterized by its translucent, ribbon-like leaves that resemble ghostly apparitions.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant's leaves are typically thin and elongated, with a waxy surface that aids in water retention. It is often found in shaded areas, where it can absorb sufficient light for photosynthesis.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental aquatic gardens.
