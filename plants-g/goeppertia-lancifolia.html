Goeppertia lancifolia

Goeppertia lancifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive dark green color and intricate veining patterns. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often adorned with variegation, adding visual interest to indoor spaces.

Goeppertia lancifolia is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for shade-loving houseplants.
