Garcinia cambogioides var. pictoria

Garcinia cambogioides var. pictoria, commonly known as the painted Garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful fruits that range from yellow to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are typically yellow or white and are clustered in small inflorescences.

The fruit contains a single large seed surrounded by a fleshy, aromatic pulp.
