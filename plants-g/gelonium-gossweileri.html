Gelonium gossweileri

Gelonium gossweileri, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces bright yellow or orange blooms.

Gelonium gossweileri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
