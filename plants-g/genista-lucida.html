Genista lucida

Genista lucida, commonly known as the bright buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense canopy that supports various wildlife.

Genista lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
