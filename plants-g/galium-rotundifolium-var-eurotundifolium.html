Galium rotundifolium var. eurotundifolium

Galium rotundifolium var. eurotundifolium, commonly known as the European wood spindle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The leaves are rounded and often have a distinctive waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
