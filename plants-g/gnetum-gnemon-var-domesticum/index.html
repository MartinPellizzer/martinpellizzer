Gnetum Gnemon Var. Domesticum

Gnetum gnemon var. domesticum is a unique species of plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the gymnosperm group, making it one of the few non-flowering plants that produce seeds. This variety is known for its large, fleshy fruits that contain a sticky, edible pulp. The plant has been traditionally used in local communities for both food and medicinal purposes. Its distinctive appearance and versatile uses make it an important subject of study in botany and ethnobotany.
