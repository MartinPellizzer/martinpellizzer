Genista hirsuta var. trichoacantha

Genista hirsuta var. trichoacantha, commonly known as the hairy bursch or hairy genista, is a shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal and rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
