Glochidion dasyanthum

Glochidion dasyanthum, commonly known as the hairy glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glandular hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that release tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
