Genista thebaica

Genista thebaica, commonly known as the Thessalian broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, spiny stems and produces small yellow flowers in early spring. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with each leaflet being elliptical in shape.

Genista thebaica is often found in rocky or arid soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
