Gentianella pulla

Gentianella pulla, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentianella pulla is known for its short flowering period, usually occurring in late summer.
