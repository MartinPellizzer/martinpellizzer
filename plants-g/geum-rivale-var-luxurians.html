Geum rivale var. luxurians

Geum rivale var. luxurians, commonly known as the water avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and woodland edges. The plant features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and reddish in color, often with a velvety texture. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, upright inflorescences.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
