Grammitis torricelliana

Grammitis torricelliana, commonly known as the Torricelli's filmy fern, is a tropical fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, thin, and often translucent fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests and along stream banks. Its rhizomes are usually short and horizontal, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Grammitis torricelliana is notable for its intricate leaf venation and its ability to retain moisture in its leaf blades.
