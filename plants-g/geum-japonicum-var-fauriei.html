Geum japonicum var. fauriei

Geum japonicum var. fauriei, commonly known as Japanese avens, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and naturalized landscapes.
