Goyazianthus tetrastichus

Goyazianthus tetrastichus, commonly known as the Brazilian aster, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces numerous small, yellow to orange flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
