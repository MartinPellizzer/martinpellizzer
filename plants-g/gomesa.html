Gomesa

Gomesa, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that bear vibrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, producing clusters of bright red, pink, or white flowers. Its leaves are usually modified into rounded, leaf-like structures that help reduce water loss.

Gomesa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
