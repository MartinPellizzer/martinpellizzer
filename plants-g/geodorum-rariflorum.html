Geodorum rariflorum

Geodorum rariflorum, commonly known as the slender white orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, narrow leaf and produces a spike of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
