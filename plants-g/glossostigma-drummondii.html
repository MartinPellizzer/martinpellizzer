Glossostigma drummondii

Glossostigma drummondii, commonly known as the African water plant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically less than 1 cm wide and can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and lakes. It is known for its ability to form dense mats on the water surface, which can help control algae growth.

Glossostigma drummondii is also valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
