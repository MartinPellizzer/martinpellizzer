Geranium nervosum

Geranium nervosum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with distinct veins and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers. Its blooming season occurs in late spring to early summer.

Geranium nervosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
