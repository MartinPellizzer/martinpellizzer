Gahnia preissii

Gahnia preissii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tussock grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in wetland and swampy environments, often forming dense mats that stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife.
