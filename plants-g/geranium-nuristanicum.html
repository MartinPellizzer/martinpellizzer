Geranium nuristanicum

Geranium nuristanicum, commonly known as the Nuristan geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Afghanistan and northern Pakistan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Geranium nuristanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
