Gnaphalium sylvaticum var. montanum

Gnaphalium sylvaticum var. montanum, commonly known as mountain everlasting, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe.

It typically grows in open, rocky or gravelly habitats at high elevations. The plant features slender stems that are often covered with fine, white hairs, and it produces small, daisy-like yellow flower heads. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and arranged alternately along the stem.

The plant is known for its aromatic, fragrant foliage and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
