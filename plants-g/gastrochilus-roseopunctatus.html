Gastrochilus roseopunctatus

Gastrochilus roseopunctatus, commonly known as the rose-spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem with several oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually pink or purple, with a distinctive rose-colored spotting on the petals.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
