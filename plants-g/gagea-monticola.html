Gagea monticola

Gagea monticola, commonly known as the mountain gagea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, grass-like foliage and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. Gagea monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native ecosystems.
