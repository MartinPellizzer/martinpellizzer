Guatteria pubens

Guatteria pubens, commonly known as the glossy cacao tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval, fleshy berries.
