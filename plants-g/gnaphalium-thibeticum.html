Gnaphalium thibeticum

Gnaphalium thibeticum, commonly known as the Tibetan feather grass, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, dry conditions. Gnaphalium thibeticum is often used in traditional medicine in Tibet for its purported health benefits.
