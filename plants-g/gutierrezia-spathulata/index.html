Gutierrezia Spathulata

Gutierrezia spathulata is a flowering plant native to the southwestern united states and northern mexico. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its yellow blooms that appear in late summer. this species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils and rocky areas. its common name, "desert goldenweed," reflects its hardy nature and vibrant color. gutierrezia spathulata plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and stabilizing desert ecosystems.
