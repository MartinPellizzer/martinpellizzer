Graphorkis nutans

Graphorkis nutans, commonly known as the nodding orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, nodding flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive spurred structure.

The leaves are linear and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its graceful appearance.
