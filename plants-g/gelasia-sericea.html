Gelasia sericea

Gelasia sericea, commonly known as silverleaf gelasia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gelasia sericea is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
