Genista umbellata var. microcephala

Genista umbellata var. microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf broom.

This plant is a compact shrub characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants. The variety microcephala is distinguished by its smaller, more compact growth compared to the typical form of Genista umbellata.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
