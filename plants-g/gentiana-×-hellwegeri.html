Gentiana × hellwegeri

Gentiana × hellwegeri, commonly known as the hybrid gentian, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine down.

Gentiana × hellwegeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
