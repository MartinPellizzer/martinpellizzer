Galactia minor

Galactia minor, commonly known as the lesser milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, green pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Galactia minor is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
