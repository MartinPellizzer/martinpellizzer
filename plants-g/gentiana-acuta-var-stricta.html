Gentiana acuta var. stricta

Gentiana acuta var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a waxy coating that helps retain moisture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
