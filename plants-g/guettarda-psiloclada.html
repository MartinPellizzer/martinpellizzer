Guettarda psiloclada

Guettarda psiloclada, commonly known as the red guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that turn red when mature, containing seeds enclosed in a fleshy pulp. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Guettarda psiloclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
