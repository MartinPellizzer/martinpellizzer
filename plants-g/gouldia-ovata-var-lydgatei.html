Gouldia ovata var. lydgatei

Gouldia ovata var. lydgatei, commonly known as the Lydgate's gouldia, is a striking member of the Commelinaceae family native to Australia.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in gardens and landscaping projects.
