Gloxinia × gesnerioides

Gloxinia × gesnerioides, commonly known as the hybrid gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors such as red, pink, and white. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and grows as an herbaceous perennial. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia × gesnerioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
