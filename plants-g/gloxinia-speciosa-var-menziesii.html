Gloxinia speciosa var. menziesii

Gloxinia speciosa var. menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' gloxinia, is a striking hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with intricate patterns. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia speciosa var. menziesii is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
