Gravesia setifera

Gravesia setifera, commonly known as the silky gravesia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
