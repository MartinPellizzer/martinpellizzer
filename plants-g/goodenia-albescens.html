Goodenia albescens

Goodenia albescens, commonly known as the white goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia albescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
