Goeppertia cannoides

Goeppertia cannoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Goeppertia cannoides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and shaded outdoor spaces.
