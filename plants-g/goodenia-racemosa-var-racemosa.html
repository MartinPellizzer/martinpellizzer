Goodenia racemosa var. racemosa

Goodenia racemosa var. racemosa, commonly known as the clustered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, clustered inflorescence of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are usually hairy, and it produces yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
