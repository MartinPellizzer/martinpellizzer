Garcinia Roxburghii

Garcinia roxburghii is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and bright red berries that attract wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil. Its unique appearance and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
