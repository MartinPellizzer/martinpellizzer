Gurania eriantha

Gurania eriantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist lowland areas.

Gurania eriantha is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
