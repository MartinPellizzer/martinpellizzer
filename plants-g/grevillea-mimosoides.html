Grevillea mimosoides

Grevillea mimosoides, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features slender, wiry stems and small, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which are highly attractive to birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in native plant gardens.
