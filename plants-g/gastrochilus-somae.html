Gastrochilus somae

Gastrochilus somae, commonly known as the Soma orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with reddish markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrates.

Gastrochilus somae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
