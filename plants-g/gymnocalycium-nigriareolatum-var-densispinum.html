Gymnocalycium nigriareolatum var. densispinum

Gymnocalycium nigriareolatum var. densispinum, commonly known as the Black Spined Cactus, is a species native to South America, particularly Argentina.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a gray-green coloration. The plant features prominent, dark brown areolas covered with dense, black spines. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring to early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
