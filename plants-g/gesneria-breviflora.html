Gesneria breviflora

Gesneria breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or red, blooming during the warmer months.

Gesneria breviflora is appreciated as a decorative plant for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
