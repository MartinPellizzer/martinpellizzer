Garcinia oxyedra

Garcinia oxyedra, commonly known as the white mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The fruit, which is typically oval or spherical, has a thick, white rind and a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge.

Its botanical features make it a popular subject in horticultural studies and landscape design.
