Geranium rigidum

Geranium rigidum, commonly known as the rigid geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically narrow and have a slightly serrated edge.

Geranium rigidum is well adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
