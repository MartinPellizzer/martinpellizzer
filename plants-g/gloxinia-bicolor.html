Gloxinia bicolor

Gloxinia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored gloxinia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia bicolor is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
