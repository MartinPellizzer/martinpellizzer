Gentiana campestris var. nuriensis

Gentiana campestris var. nuriensis, commonly known as the Korean gentian, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. It has lance-shaped leaves and a short, stout stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
