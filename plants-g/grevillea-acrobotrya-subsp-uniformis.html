Grevillea acrobotrya subsp. uniformis

Grevillea acrobotrya subsp.

uniformis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its smooth, grey-green stems and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, which bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

This subspecies is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
