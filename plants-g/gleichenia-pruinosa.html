Gleichenia pruinosa

Gleichenia pruinosa, commonly known as the powdered fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are covered in a powdery, whitish coating, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Gleichenia pruinosa is found in various regions across the Americas and is known for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.
