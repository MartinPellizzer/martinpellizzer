Grewia orientalis var. latifolia

Grewia orientalis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf grownia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

The fruit, which is a small, fleshy capsule, is edible and has a tart flavor.
