Guatteria poeppigiana

Guatteria poeppigiana, commonly known as the Mexican beautyberry, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

Guatteria poeppigiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
