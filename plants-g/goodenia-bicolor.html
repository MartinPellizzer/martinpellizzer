Goodenia bicolor

Goodenia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive two-toned flowers, typically featuring a yellow center and purple or pink outer petals. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It blooms during the spring and summer months, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Goodenia bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
