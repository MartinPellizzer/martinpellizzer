Gomphocarpus pulchellus

Gomphocarpus pulchellus, commonly known as the milkweed or the yellow milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Gomphocarpus pulchellus is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
