Garuga pierrei

Garuga pierrei, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that appear in late winter or early spring. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Garuga pierrei is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its unique floral display.
