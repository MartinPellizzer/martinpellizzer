Goodenia elaiosoma

Goodenia elaiosoma, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinators.

The species is noted for its resilience and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
