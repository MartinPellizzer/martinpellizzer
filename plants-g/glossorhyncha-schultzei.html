Glossorhyncha schultzei

Glossorhyncha schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are often pale or translucent, contributing to its ethereal appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
