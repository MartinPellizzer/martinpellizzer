Gymnocalycium valnicekianum var. bicolor

Gymnocalycium valnicekianum var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor moon cactus, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact size make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
