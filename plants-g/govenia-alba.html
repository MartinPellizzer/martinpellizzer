Govenia alba

Govenia alba, commonly known as the white govenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its pale flowers.

Govenia alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
