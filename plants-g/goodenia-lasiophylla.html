Goodenia lasiophylla

Goodenia lasiophylla, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

It is often found in sandy or rocky soils in arid and semi-arid regions.
