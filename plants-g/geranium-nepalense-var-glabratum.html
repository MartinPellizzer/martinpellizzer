Geranium nepalense var. glabratum

Geranium nepalense var. glabratum, commonly known as the smooth geranium, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features upright stems covered with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its showy flowers, which range from pink to red, bloom in late summer and are clustered in umbels.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
