Gesneria × chromatella

Gesneria × chromatella, commonly known as the hybrid gesneria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gesneria xerophila and Gesneria obtusifolia.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, often displaying shades of green, red, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria × chromatella is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
