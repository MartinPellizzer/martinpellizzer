Gendarussa orchioides var. latifolia

Gendarussa orchioides var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved gendarussa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, orchid-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant features broad, strap-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
