Glomera obovata

Glomera obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate glomera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple.
