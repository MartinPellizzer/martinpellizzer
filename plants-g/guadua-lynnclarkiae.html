Guadua lynnclarkiae

Guadua lynnclarkiae, commonly known as the Lynn Clark's bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil.

Guadua lynnclarkiae is valued for its strong, lightweight structure, making it useful in construction and traditional crafts.
