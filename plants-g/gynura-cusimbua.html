Gynura cusimbua

Gynura cusimbua, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gynura cusimbua is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated as a houseplant in many parts of the world.
