Geranium macbridei

Geranium macbridei, commonly known as the Mexican geranium, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pink or red petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
