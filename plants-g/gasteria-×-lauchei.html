Gasteria × lauchei

Gasteria × lauchei, commonly known as the hybrid gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or silver markings. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers on tall, erect stems during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria × lauchei is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental foliage.
