Gnaphalium diamantinense

Gnaphalium diamantinense, commonly known as diamond dust plant, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences. Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
