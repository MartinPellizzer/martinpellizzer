Griffinia ornata

Griffinia ornata, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped bloom. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their beauty.

Griffinia ornata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
