Galeopsis × haussknechtii

Galeopsis × haussknechtii, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial herb native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring square stems and opposite leaves with serrated edges. Its small, blue to purple flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, blooming from June to August.

Galeopsis × haussknechtii is often found in moist meadows and along stream banks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
