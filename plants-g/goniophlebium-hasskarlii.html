Goniophlebium hasskarlii

Goniophlebium hasskarlii, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are typically divided into small, linear segments, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Goniophlebium hasskarlii is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.
