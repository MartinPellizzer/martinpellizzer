Gouldia terminalis var. antiqua

Gouldia terminalis var. antiqua, commonly known as the ancient gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, providing an attractive foliage contrast. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms, which can persist for several months during the warmer seasons.
