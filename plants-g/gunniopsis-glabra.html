Gunniopsis glabra

Gunniopsis glabra, commonly known as the smooth gunniopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
