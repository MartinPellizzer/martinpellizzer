Garcinia hessii

Garcinia hessii, commonly known as the African yellow wood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain a single seed. Garcinia hessii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
