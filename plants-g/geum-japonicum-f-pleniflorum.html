Geum japonicum f. pleniflorum

Geum japonicum f. pleniflorum, commonly known as Japanese avens, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, semidouble to fully double pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and wetland restoration projects.
