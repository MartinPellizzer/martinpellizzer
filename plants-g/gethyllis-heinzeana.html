Gethyllis heinzeana

Gethyllis heinzeana, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, flowering structure. The plant produces delicate, violet-colored flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Gethyllis heinzeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
