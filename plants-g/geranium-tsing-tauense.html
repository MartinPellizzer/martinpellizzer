Geranium tsing-tauense

Geranium tsing-tauense, commonly known as the Tsingtao geranium, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pentagonal flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in temperate climates.
