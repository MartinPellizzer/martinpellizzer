Gymnocarpos argenteus

Gymnocarpos argenteus, commonly known as silverleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub with silver-gray foliage due to dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Gymnocarpos argenteus is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
