Galanthus elwesii var. stenophyllus

Galanthus elwesii var. stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved snowdrop, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in woodland and shaded garden environments, preferring moist, well-drained soil. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and early blooming habit.
