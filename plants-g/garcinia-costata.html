Garcinia costata

Garcinia costata, commonly known as the golden apple or yellow mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny rind and bright yellow, edible fruit that contains a high concentration of xanthones. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while its flowers are small and white with a subtle fragrance.

Garcinia costata is valued not only for its fruit but also for its potential medicinal properties.
