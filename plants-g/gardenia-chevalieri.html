Gardenia chevalieri

Gardenia chevalieri, commonly known as the Madagascar gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia chevalieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blooms.
