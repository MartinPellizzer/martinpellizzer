Gastrochilus deltoglossus

Gastrochilus deltoglossus, commonly known as the pink-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique pinkish-purple lip and its delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive three-lobed structure.

Gastrochilus deltoglossus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
