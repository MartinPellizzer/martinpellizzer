Grevillea granulifera

Grevillea granulifera, commonly known as the rough-stemmed Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems covered in rough, granular texture and its striking, bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.

Grevillea granulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
