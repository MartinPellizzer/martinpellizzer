Gaultheria leucocarpa var. pingbienensis

Gaultheria leucocarpa var. pingbienensis, commonly known as the Pingbian starflower, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant produces dark blue berries that are edible and have a tart, slightly sweet flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded landscapes.
