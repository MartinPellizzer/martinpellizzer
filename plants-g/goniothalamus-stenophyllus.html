Goniothalamus stenophyllus

Goniothalamus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed goniothalamus, is a member of the family Annonaceae, native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are berries that contain several seeds.

It is often found in lowland tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental foliage and potential medicinal properties.
