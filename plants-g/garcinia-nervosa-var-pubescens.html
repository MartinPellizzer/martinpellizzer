Garcinia nervosa var. pubescens

Garcinia nervosa var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a bright orange berry with a thick, leathery rind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
