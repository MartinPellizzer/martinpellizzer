Goldbachia pendula

Goldbachia pendula, commonly known as the weeping goldbachia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble tiny, delicate lanterns. The plant features broad, glossy green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Goldbachia pendula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
