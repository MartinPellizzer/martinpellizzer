Guarianthe aurantiaca

Guarianthe aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers, which are typically clustered in inflorescences. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

Guarianthe aurantiaca thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
