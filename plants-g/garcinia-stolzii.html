Garcinia stolzii

Garcinia stolzii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and East Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, yellowish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries containing a single seed. Garcinia stolzii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for cultivation in warm, humid climates.
