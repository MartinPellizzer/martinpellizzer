Goodenia grandiflora var. albiflora

Goodenia grandiflora var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered goodenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features slender, erect stems that bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native gardens and conservation efforts.
