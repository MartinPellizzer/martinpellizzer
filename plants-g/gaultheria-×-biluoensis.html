Gaultheria × biluoensis

Gaultheria × biluoensis, commonly known as the biluoensis wintergreen, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China, where it thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments. The plant produces small, evergreen leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. Its flowers are bell-shaped and usually pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Gaultheria × biluoensis is valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes or for its potential medicinal properties.
