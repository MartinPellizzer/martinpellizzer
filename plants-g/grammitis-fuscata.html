Grammitis fuscata

Grammitis fuscata, commonly known as the dark grammitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce clusters of spore-bearing leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Grammitis fuscata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
