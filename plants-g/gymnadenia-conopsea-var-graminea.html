Gymnadenia conopsea var. graminea

Gymnadenia conopsea var. graminea, commonly known as the slender grass-pink, is a perennial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking pink or purple flowers in late summer. It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that are often arranged in a loose spike.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
