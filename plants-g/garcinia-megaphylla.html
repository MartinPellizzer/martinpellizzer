Garcinia megaphylla

Garcinia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red fruits resembling small cherries. The fruit contains a single seed enclosed in a fleshy, aromatic pulp.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
