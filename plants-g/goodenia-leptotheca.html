Goodenia leptotheca

Goodenia leptotheca, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for native pollinators.
