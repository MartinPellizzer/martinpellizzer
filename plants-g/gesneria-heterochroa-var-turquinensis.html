Gesneria heterochroa var. turquinensis

Gesneria heterochroa var. turquinensis, commonly known as the Turquin's gesneria, is a strikingly colorful plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, contrasting leaf colors, with some leaves displaying deep green and others a striking red or orange. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts.
