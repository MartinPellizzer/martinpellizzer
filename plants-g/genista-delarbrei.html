Genista delarbrei

Genista delarbrei, commonly known as the Delarbre's broom, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Genista delarbrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems.
