Gonolobus absalonensis

Gonolobus absalonensis, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often opening in the morning.
