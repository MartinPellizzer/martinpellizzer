Geranium subumbelliforrme

Geranium subumbelliforrme, commonly known as the clustered geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are often hairy and bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
