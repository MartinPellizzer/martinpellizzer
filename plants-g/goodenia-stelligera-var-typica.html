Goodenia stelligera var. typica

Goodenia stelligera var. typica, commonly known as the coastal goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal dunes and sandy soils, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is well-suited to saline and well-drained soils, making it a hardy species in coastal ecosystems.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and nectar for native pollinators.
