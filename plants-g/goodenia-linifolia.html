Goodenia linifolia

Goodenia linifolia, commonly known as the silky goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it its common name. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Goodenia linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
