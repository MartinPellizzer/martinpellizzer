Grammitis rigens

Grammitis rigens, commonly known as the rigid fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in rainforests. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a distinctive, narrow, and rigid structure.

Grammitis rigens is known for its rapid growth and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
