Garcinia zenkeri

Garcinia zenkeri, commonly known as the African yellow wood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, yellowish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries. Garcinia zenkeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
