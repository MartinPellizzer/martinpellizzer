Gomphrena connata

Gomphrena connata, commonly known as the Mexican plume or red plume, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery inflorescences that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is often grown as an ornamental due to its striking appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
