Gentiana Affinis Var. Forwoodii

Gentiana affinis var. forwoodii is a rare and striking variant of the gentian genus native to the mountainous regions of western Europe. This plant is known for its vibrant blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
