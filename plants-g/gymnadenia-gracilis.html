Gymnadenia gracilis

Gymnadenia gracilis, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Gymnadenia gracilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
