Gynetra volubilis

Gynetra volubilis, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a climbing plant characterized by its unique helical growth pattern.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and emits a faint, citrus-like fragrance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. Its fruit is a rounded, fleshy berry that ripens to a deep orange color.

Gynetra volubilis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
