Globba aurea

Globba aurea, commonly known as golden globba, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features striking, bright yellow to orange-red inflorescences that resemble small globes, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a metallic sheen.

Globba aurea is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
