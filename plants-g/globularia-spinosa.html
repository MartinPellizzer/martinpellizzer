Globularia spinosa

Globularia spinosa, commonly known as the spiny globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant features hairy, spiny stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
