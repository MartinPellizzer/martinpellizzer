Goniolimon speciosum var. strictum

Goniolimon speciosum var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goniolimon, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, adding visual interest to its otherwise low profile. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
