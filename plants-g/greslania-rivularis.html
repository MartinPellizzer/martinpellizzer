Greslania rivularis

Greslania rivularis, commonly known as the river greslania, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as riverbanks and forest floors. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize soil along water edges.

Greslania rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
