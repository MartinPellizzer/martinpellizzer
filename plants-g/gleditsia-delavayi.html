Gleditsia delavayi

Gleditsia delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, green pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.

Gleditsia delavayi is also valued for its hard wood and is used in traditional medicine.
