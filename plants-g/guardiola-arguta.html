Guardiola arguta

Guardiola arguta, commonly known as the sharp-leafed broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy plant adapted to Mediterranean climates.
