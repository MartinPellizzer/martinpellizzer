Gentiana × billingtonii

Gentiana × billingtonii, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained, rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stem is usually unbranched and somewhat hairy.

Gentiana × billingtonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
