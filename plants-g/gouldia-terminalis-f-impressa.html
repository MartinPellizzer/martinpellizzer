Gouldia terminalis f. impressa

Gouldia terminalis f. impressa, commonly known as the impressa gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It features dense, compact growth with vibrant, colorful inflorescences that resemble fireworks, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
