Gardenia lanepoolei

Gardenia lanepoolei, commonly known as the Lanepoole's gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage and produces large, waxy, white flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and unique scent.

Gardenia lanepoolei is relatively slow-growing and can reach heights of up to 10 feet in suitable conditions.
