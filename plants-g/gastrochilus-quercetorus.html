Gastrochilus quercetorus

Gastrochilus quercetorus, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in some botanical gardens.
