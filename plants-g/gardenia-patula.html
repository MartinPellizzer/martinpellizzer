Gardenia patula

Gardenia patula, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant scent.

Gardenia patula is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
