Gentianopsis barbata var. ovatodeltoidea

Gentianopsis barbata var. ovatodeltoidea, commonly known as the broad-leaf gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate to deltoid in shape, with a rough texture and prominent veins.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
