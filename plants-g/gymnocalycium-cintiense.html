Gymnocalycium cintiense

Gymnocalycium cintiense, commonly known as the Cintiense Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular, spiny stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the spring. Gymnocalycium cintiense is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
