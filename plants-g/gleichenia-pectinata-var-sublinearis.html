Gleichenia pectinata var. sublinearis

Gleichenia pectinata var. sublinearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the Gleichenia pectinata species.

It is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae, characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and linear, with a distinctive pinnate structure.

The varietal name 'sublinearis' refers to the narrow, elongated shape of its foliage.
