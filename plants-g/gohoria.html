Gohoria

Gohoria, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Gohoria is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and ability to bloom throughout the year.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
