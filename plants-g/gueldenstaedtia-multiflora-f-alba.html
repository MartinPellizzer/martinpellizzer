Gueldenstaedtia multiflora f. alba

Gueldenstaedtia multiflora f. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered multiflora vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands, often in temperate regions. Its trifoliate leaves and slender, climbing stems are typical of the genus Gueldenstaedtia.

This variety is distinguished by its lighter flower color compared to other forms of the species.
