Grammitis asahinae

Grammitis asahinae, commonly known as the Japanese basket fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in forest understories.

Grammitis asahinae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
