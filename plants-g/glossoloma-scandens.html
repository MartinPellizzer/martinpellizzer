Glossoloma scandens

Glossoloma scandens, commonly known as the climbing orchid, is a tropical plant native to regions of Central and South America.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant blooms. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture.
