Grevillea miqueliana subsp. moroka

Grevillea miqueliana subsp.

moroka, commonly known as Moroka grevillea, is a shrub native to South Africa. It features distinctive, needle-like leaves that are dense and evergreen.

The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in winter, attracting birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal flowering.
