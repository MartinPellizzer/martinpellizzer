Glochidion punctatum

Glochidion punctatum, commonly known as the punctate glabrous leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a woody vine characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a distinctive punctuated texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain minute, hairy seeds.

Glochidion punctatum is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
