Goodyera hachijoensis var. yakushimensis

Goodyera hachijoensis var. yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimen lady's slipper, is a rare orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves and unique, pinkish-white flowers with a prominent lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rock surfaces or in crevices. Its small size and delicate appearance make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
