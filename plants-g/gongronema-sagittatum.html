Gongronema sagittatum

Gongronema sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowleaf gongronema, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elliptical, berry-like fruits. Gongronema sagittatum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitats.
