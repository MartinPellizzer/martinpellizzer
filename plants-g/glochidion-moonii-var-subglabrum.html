Glochidion moonii var. subglabrum

Glochidion moonii var. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved glochidion, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, often white or pale yellow in color.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
