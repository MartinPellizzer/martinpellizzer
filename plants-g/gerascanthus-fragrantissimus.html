Gerascanthus fragrantissimus

Gerascanthus fragrantissimus, commonly known as the fragrant geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy due to their pleasant scent.

Gerascanthus fragrantissimus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
