Gasteria intermedia var. laevior

Gasteria intermedia var. laevior, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, narrow, dark green leaves with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
