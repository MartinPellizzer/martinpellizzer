Geranium wlassovianum var. hattae

Geranium wlassovianum var. hattae, commonly known as the Hattae geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture. The flowers are typically pink to purple, clustered in dense, showy inflorescences, and bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to temperate climates.
