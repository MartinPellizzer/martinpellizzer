Guarea amaris

Guarea amaris, commonly known as the red guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and compound leaves with elongated leaflets. The tree is valued for its durable timber and is often found in lowland rainforests.
