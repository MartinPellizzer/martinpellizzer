Gnaphalium rosillense

Gnaphalium rosillense, commonly known as the Rosillense featherweed, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

The plant produces fluffy, white to pale yellow inflorescences that are often mistaken for cotton or wool. Gnaphalium rosillense is also noted for its ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
