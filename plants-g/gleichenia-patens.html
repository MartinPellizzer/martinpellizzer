Gleichenia patens

Gleichenia patens, commonly known as the common fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, strap-like fronds that are usually dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Gleichenia patens is widely distributed across temperate regions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
