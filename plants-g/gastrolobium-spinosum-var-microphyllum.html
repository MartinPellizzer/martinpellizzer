Gastrolobium spinosum var. microphyllum

Gastrolobium spinosum var. microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaf burr legume, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces distinctive yellow flowers in spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is part of the Fabaceae family.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in native gardens.
