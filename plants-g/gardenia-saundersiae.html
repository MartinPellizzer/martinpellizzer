Gardenia saundersiae

Gardenia saundersiae, commonly known as the Saunders' gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and pleasant scent.

Gardenia saundersiae is also valued for its potential in horticulture and botanical research.
