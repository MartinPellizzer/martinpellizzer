Gymnanthemum blandum

Gymnanthemum blandum, commonly known as the pale aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, pale blue to white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Gymnanthemum blandum is known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators like bees and butterflies.
