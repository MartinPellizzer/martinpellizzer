Gymnocalycium spegazzinii subsp. spegazzinii

Gymnocalycium spegazzinii subsp.

spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered at the top of the stems. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts and desert gardeners.
