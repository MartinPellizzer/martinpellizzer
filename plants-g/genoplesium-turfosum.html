Genoplesium turfosum

Genoplesium turfosum, commonly known as the Turf Leek Orchid, is an Australian native orchid species characterized by its unique botanical features.

It typically grows in heathlands and wetlands, thriving in moist, acidic soils. The plant produces a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense mat, with a single flower spike emerging from the center. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

This orchid is part of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its subtle and delicate appearance.
