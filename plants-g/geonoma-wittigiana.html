Geonoma wittigiana

Geonoma wittigiana, commonly known as the Wittigian sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, rosette-like structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its rhizome is covered with dense, fibrous roots that help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.
