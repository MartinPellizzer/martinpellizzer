Gynura zeylanica

Gynura zeylanica, commonly known as the Indian nettle or velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves with a distinctive velvety texture. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, dry fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gynura zeylanica is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
