Gomphrena lundii

Gomphrena lundii, commonly known as the pink gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, bract-like leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Gomphrena lundii is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can persist throughout the growing season.
