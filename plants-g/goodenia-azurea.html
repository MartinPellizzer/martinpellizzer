Goodenia azurea

Goodenia azurea, commonly known as the blue goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are usually erect.

Goodenia azurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
