Geranium ocellatum var. africanum

Geranium ocellatum var. africanum, commonly known as the African geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features striking, palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive central mark. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in gardens across the globe.
