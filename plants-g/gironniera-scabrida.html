Gironniera scabrida

Gironniera scabrida, commonly known as the rough gironniera, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
