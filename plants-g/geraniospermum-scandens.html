Geraniospermum scandens

Geraniospermum scandens, commonly known as the climbing geraniosperm, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit. The plant features small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are often hairy and adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments.

Geraniospermum scandens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
