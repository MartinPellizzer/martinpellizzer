Grosourdya callifera

Grosourdya callifera, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has smooth, grayish-brown bark and dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Grosourdya callifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
