Gnetum schwackeanum

Gnetum schwackeanum, commonly known as the African tropical gnetum, is a gymnosperm native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a straight, unbranched stem and opposite, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and is notable for its reproductive structures, including male and female cones. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various forest organisms.
