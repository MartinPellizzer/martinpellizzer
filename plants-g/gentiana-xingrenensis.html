Gentiana xingrenensis

Gentiana xingrenensis, commonly known as the Xingren gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
