Gangila

Gangila, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy yellow or orange flower heads with white ray florets, surrounded by a central disk of tiny flowers. Gangila typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
