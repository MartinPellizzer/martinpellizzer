Grewia densiserrulata

Grewia densiserrulata, commonly known as the serrated grownia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The leaves are ovate with serrated edges, giving the plant its scientific name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
