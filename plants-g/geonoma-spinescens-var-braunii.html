Geonoma spinescens var. braunii

Geonoma spinescens var. braunii, commonly known as the spiny geonoma, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaf margins, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as rainforests, where it thrives in the understory. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a deep green color, with a complex, divided structure.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage.
