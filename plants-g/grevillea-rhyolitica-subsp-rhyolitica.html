Grevillea rhyolitica subsp. rhyolitica

Grevillea rhyolitica subsp.

rhyolitica, commonly known as the Red Grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
