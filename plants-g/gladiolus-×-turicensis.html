Gladiolus × turicensis

Gladiolus × turicensis, commonly known as the Swiss gladiolus, is a hybrid plant native to the Alps of Switzerland.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
