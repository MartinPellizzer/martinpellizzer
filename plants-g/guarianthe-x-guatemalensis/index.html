Guarianthe × Guatemalensis

Guatemalan orchid, scientifically known as Guarianthe × guatemalensis, is a rare and exquisite flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. This hybrid orchid is prized for its striking, large, and colorful blooms that often feature vibrant shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is closely related to several other orchid species found in the region. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species, making conservation efforts essential for its survival. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both scientific interest and botanical admiration.
