Gladiolus canaliculatus

Gladiolus canaliculatus, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, colorful flowers. The flowers bloom in summer and are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens.
