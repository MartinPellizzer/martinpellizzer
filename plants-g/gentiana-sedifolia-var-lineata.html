Gentiana sedifolia var. lineata

Gentiana sedifolia var. lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
