Gentiana germanica var. minor

Gentiana germanica var. minor, commonly known as the lesser gentian, is a perennial herb native to Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
