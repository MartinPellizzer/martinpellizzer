Gasteria × squarrosa

Gasteria × squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa gasteria, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or grayish markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria × squarrosa is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
