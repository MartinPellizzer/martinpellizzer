Goodyera robusta

Goodyera robusta, commonly known as the hardy wintergreen, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as under forest canopies. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces small, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with prominent purple or brown markings, and they bloom during the winter season.

Goodyera robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
