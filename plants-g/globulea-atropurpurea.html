Globulea atropurpurea

Globulea atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple globular heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, adapted to thrive in well-drained, acidic soils.

Globulea atropurpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
