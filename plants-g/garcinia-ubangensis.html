Garcinia ubangensis

Garcinia ubangensis, commonly known as the Ubangensis Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
