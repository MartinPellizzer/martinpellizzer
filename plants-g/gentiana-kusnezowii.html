Gentiana kusnezowii

Gentiana kusnezowii, commonly known as the Kusnezow's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Gentiana kusnezowii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
