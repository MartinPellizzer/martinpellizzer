Graphistylis itatiaiae

Graphistylis itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia grass.

It is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae, native to Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers.
