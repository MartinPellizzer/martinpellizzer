Gasteria triebneriana

Gasteria triebneriana, commonly known as the Zulu bulbous plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow tubercles. The plant produces short, upright flower stalks that bear small, tubular, white or yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria triebneriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and resilience.
