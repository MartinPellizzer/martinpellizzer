Gynostemma pubescens

Gynostemma pubescens, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or "tian qi," is a climbing vine native to Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its purported health benefits and is used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Cucurbitaceae family.
