Gymnosteris pulchella

Gymnosteris pulchella, commonly known as the pretty gymnosteris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of a central disk surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. Gymnosteris pulchella is known for its ornamental value due to its vibrant yellow petals and attractive foliage.

It is a perennial species that blooms in the summer months.
