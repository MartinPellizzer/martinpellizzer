Gentiana wangchukii

Gentiana wangchukii, commonly known as the Bhutan sky gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of Bhutan.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in alpine meadows and rocky areas. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, often blue or purple flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
