Gymnopteris neglecta

Gymnopteris neglecta, commonly known as the neglected sword fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce both sterile and fertile fronds.

Gymnopteris neglecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its lush, green foliage.
