Geranium urbanum

Geranium urbanum, commonly known as the town geranium, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with erect, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and urban environments.

Geranium urbanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
