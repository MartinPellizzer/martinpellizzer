Garcinia pungens

Garcinia pungens, commonly known as the African bitter apple, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits that have a sharp, acidic taste. The plant produces elliptical leaves with a slightly rough texture and emits a faint, spicy aroma. Garcinia pungens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Clusiaceae family.
