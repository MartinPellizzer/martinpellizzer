Gumira leucostoma

Gumira leucostoma, commonly known as the white-leaf gumira, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically white or pale green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
