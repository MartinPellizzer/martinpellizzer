Goodenia hispida

Goodenia hispida, commonly known as the rough goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal regions.

Goodenia hispida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
