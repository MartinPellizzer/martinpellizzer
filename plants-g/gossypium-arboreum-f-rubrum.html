Gossypium arboreum f. rubrum

Gossypium arboreum f. rubrum, commonly known as the red tree cotton, is a rare and ornamental variant of the tree cotton plant.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which contrast with its green foliage and woody stems. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Its botanical features include a shrub-like structure with fibrous bark and capsule fruits containing fluffy seeds.

This cultivar is valued for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture and its unique coloration.
