Gongora jennyi

Gongora jennyi, commonly known as Jenny's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Gongora jennyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical research.
