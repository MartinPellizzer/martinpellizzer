Geraniospermum × urbanum

Geraniospermum × urbanum, commonly known as the urban geranium, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of its parent species, which are typically members of the Geraniaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial with showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red, often blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate, with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for urban gardening and ornamental landscapes.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in parks and along sidewalks in urban environments.
