Gardenia volkensii subsp. spathulifolia

Gardenia volkensii subsp.

spathulifolia, commonly known as the East African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa. It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, fragrant white flowers.

The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are showy and bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
