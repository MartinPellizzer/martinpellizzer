Gardenia sessilis

Gardenia sessilis, commonly known as the sessile gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia sessilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic blooms.
