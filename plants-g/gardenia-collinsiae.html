Gardenia collinsiae

Gardenia collinsiae, commonly known as the Collinsia gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are composed of multiple petals and are often used in traditional medicine.

Gardenia collinsiae thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
