Gazania krebsiana subsp. serrulata

Gazania krebsiana subsp.

serrulata, commonly known as the serrated gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive central disk and ray florets.

The plant typically grows in a compact, bushy form with hairy, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season add to its ornamental value.
