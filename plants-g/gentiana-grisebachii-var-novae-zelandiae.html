Gentiana grisebachii var. novae-zelandiae

Gentiana grisebachii var. novae-zelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features showy, sky-blue to violet-blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
