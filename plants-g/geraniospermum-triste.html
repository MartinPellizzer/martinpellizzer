Geraniospermum triste

Geraniospermum triste, commonly known as the weeping geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and hanging baskets.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
