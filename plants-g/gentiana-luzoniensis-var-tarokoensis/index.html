Gentiana Luzoniensis Var. Tarokoensis

Taroko gentian, scientifically known as Gentiana luzoniensis var. tarokoensis, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of Taiwan. Found primarily in the Taroko National Park, this species thrives in cool, moist environments with rich, well-drained soil. Its vivid blue flowers, which bloom in late spring, make it a standout among the region's flora. Despite its beauty, the plant faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered species for future generations.
