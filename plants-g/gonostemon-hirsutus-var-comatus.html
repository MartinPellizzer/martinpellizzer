Gonostemon hirsutus var. comatus

Gonostemon hirsutus var. comatus, commonly known as the hairy gonostemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
