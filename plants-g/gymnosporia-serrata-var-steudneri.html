Gymnosporia serrata var. steudneri

Gymnosporia serrata var. steudneri, commonly known as the serrated gymnosporia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, typical of the genus Gymnosporia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
