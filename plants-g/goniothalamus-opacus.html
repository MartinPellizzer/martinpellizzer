Goniothalamus opacus

Goniothalamus opacus, commonly known as the dark goniothalamus, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Goniothalamaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
