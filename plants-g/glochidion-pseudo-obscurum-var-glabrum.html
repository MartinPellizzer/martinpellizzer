Glochidion pseudo-obscurum var. glabrum

Glochidion pseudo-obscurum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth glochidion, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is a small shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, clustered flowers with yellowish-green petals, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This variant is distinguished by its lack of pubescence, giving it a smooth appearance compared to other varieties in the species.
