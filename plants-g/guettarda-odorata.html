Guettarda odorata

Guettarda odorata, commonly known as the fragrant guettarda, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight. Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and continuous flowering.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
