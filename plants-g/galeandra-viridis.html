Galeandra viridis

Galeandra viridis, commonly known as the green orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking greenish-yellow floral bracts. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Galeandra viridis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique coloration.
