Goniophlebium kuhnii

Goniophlebium kuhnii, commonly known as the Kuhn's rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, lanceolate leaves with a serrated margin. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the leaves, a common reproductive feature in ferns.

Goniophlebium kuhnii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
