Gymnosphaera melleri

Gymnosphaera melleri, commonly known as the Mexican pineapple cactus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spineless, segmented stems that resemble a pineapple. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. Gymnosphaera melleri thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to poor soil conditions.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for cultivation in dry climates.
