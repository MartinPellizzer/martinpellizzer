Gnaphalium bellidifolium

Gnaphalium bellidifolium, commonly known as the bellidifolium everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, showy yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

The plant is known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
