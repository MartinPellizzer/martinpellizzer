Gynostemma

Gynostemma, commonly known as Chinese ginseng, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, with its leaves containing compounds similar to ginseng. It is often cultivated for its purported health benefits, including antioxidant and adaptogenic effects.

Gynostemma thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vine growth.
