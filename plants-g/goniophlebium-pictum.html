Goniophlebium pictum

Goniophlebium pictum, commonly known as the painted fern, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that display a unique pattern of light and dark green, resembling a painted design. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking texture and visual interest.

Goniophlebium pictum is also valued for its ability to tolerate wet soil conditions, making it suitable for bog gardens and rain gardens.
