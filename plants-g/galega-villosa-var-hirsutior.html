Galega villosa var. hirsutior

Galega villosa var. hirsutior, commonly known as the hairy goat's rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems covered in dense, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets that are also hairy.

Galega villosa var. hirsutior is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
