Guettarda apiculata

Guettarda apiculata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are typically borne in pendulous inflorescences, adding an ornamental value to the plant.

Guettarda apiculata is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
