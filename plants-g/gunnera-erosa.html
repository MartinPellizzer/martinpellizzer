Gunnera erosa

Gunnera erosa, commonly known as the purple gunnera, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gunneraceae family and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate rainforests. Its flowers, which appear in late summer, are small and clustered in a spadix surrounded by bracts.

Gunnera erosa is valued in horticulture for its dramatic foliage and ability to add a bold statement to gardens.
