Gentiana stylophora subsp. yunnanensis

Gentiana stylophora subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat branched.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique ecological adaptations.
