Goldfussia hancockii

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. It is a perennial species that prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.
