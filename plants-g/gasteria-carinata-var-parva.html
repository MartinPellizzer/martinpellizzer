Gasteria carinata var. parva

Gasteria carinata var. parva, commonly known as the dwarf gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive central ridge. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
