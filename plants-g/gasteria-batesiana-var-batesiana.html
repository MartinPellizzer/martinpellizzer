Gasteria batesiana var. batesiana

Gasteria batesiana var. batesiana, commonly known as the Natal carpet plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white veins and a textured surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
