Gentianella calcarea

Gentianella calcarea, commonly known as the limestone gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in calcareous soils, often in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Gentianella calcarea is part of the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, often blue or purple flowers.
