Globularia liouvillei

Globularia liouvillei, commonly known as the desert globularia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its dense, globular clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its harsh, dry environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Globularia liouvillei is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
