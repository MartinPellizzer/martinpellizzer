Gazania lichtensteinii

Gazania lichtensteinii, commonly known as the Cape gold flower, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange daisy-like flowers. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Gazania lichtensteinii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
