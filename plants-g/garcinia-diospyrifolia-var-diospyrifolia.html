Garcinia diospyrifolia var. diospyrifolia

Garcinia diospyrifolia var. diospyrifolia, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a broad, spreading canopy. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its fruit is a yellow or orange drupe, often covered in a whitish, powdery bloom, and contains a single seed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
