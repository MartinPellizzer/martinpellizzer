Grevillea spinosa

Grevillea spinosa, commonly known as the spiny grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, needle-like leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Grevillea spinosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
