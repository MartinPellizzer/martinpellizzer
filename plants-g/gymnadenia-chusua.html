Gymnadenia chusua

Gymnadenia chusua, commonly known as the sweet-flag orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in moist meadows, woodlands, and wetlands. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, flowering stems with showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white. Gymnadenia chusua is characterized by its specialized root system and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
