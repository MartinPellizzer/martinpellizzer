Grevillea meisneri var. argyrophylla

Grevillea meisneri var. argyrophylla, commonly known as the silver-leafed grevillea, is a striking shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, silver-blue leaves that provide a striking contrast to its bright red or orange flowers. The plant produces clusters of long, tubular flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
