Geraniopsis yemensis

Geraniopsis yemensis, commonly known as Yemen geranium, is a flowering plant native to Yemen.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually simple and often have a hairy texture, while the flowers come in shades of pink or red.

Geraniopsis yemensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with a similar climate.
