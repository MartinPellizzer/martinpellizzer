Gentiana Luteoviridis

Gentiana luteoviridis is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant yellow-green blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species belongs to the Gentianaceae family, which is renowned for its showy and often blue or purple flowers. Its unique coloration makes it a standout in botanical gardens and natural habitats. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and requires specific conditions to flourish.
