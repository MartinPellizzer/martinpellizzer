Grammitis sarawakensis

Grammitis sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that often form a dense, mat-like structure. Grammitis sarawakensis plays a role in the ecological balance of its ecosystem by providing habitat for microorganisms and small invertebrates.
