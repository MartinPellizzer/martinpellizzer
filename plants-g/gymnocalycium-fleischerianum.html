Gymnocalycium fleischerianum

Gymnocalycium fleischerianum, commonly known as the Fleischer's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its rounded, globular stems that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the warmer months. It is a succulent plant that stores water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Gymnocalycium fleischerianum is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
