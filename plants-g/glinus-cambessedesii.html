Glinus cambessedesii

Glinus cambessedesii, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it well adapted to dry environments. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in desert scrublands.

Glinus cambessedesii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
