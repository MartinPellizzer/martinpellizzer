Garnotia fragilis var. parcitorta

Garnotia fragilis var. parcitorta, commonly known as the slender garnet bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its fragility, it requires careful handling and protection from strong winds.
