Gladiolus ecklonii subsp. vinosomaculatus

Gladiolus ecklonii subsp.

vinosomaculatus, commonly known as the spotted gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.
