Gentianella detonsa var. elegans

Gentianella detonsa var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
