Gingidium monroi

Gingidium monroi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. Gingidium monroi is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its relatively easy care requirements.
