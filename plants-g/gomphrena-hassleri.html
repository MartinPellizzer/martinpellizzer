Gomphrena hassleri

Gomphrena hassleri, commonly known as the red gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to pink. Gomphrena hassleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
