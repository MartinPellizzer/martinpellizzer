Godetia cylindrica var. tracyi

Godetia cylindrica var. tracyi, commonly known as Tracy's cylinderleaf, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its botanical features include small, clustered inflorescences and a compact growth habit.

It is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping.
