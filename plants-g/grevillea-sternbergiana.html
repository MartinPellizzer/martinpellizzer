Grevillea sternbergiana

Grevillea sternbergiana, commonly known as the pink and white grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features vibrant, feather-like flowers that range from pink to white, blooming throughout the warmer months. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea sternbergiana is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract native pollinators.
