Gesneria gollmeriana

Gesneria gollmeriana, commonly known as the Gollmer's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, thriving in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, with a slightly waxy texture.

The flowers, which bloom in spring, come in shades of orange and red, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
