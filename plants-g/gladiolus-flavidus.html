Gladiolus flavidus

Gladiolus flavidus, commonly known as the yellow gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The flowers are typically arranged in spike-like inflorescences and bloom during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
