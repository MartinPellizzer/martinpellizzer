Genista africana

Genista africana, commonly known as the African broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Genista africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
