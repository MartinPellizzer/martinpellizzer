Gossypium Jamaicense F. Janiphifolium

Gossypium jamaicense f. janiphifolium is a rare and unique cotton plant native to the Caribbean. This variant of the cotton species is known for its distinctive leaf morphology and historical significance in local agriculture. Unlike common cotton varieties, it thrives in specific tropical climates and requires particular care for cultivation. Its leaves are broader and more lobed, giving it a striking appearance that sets it apart from other cotton plants. Despite its rarity, it remains an important subject of botanical study and conservation efforts.
