Genista clavata var. webbiana

Genista clavata var. webbiana, commonly known as the Webb's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, woody stems covered in small, sharp spines, typical of the Genista genus. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a slight pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
