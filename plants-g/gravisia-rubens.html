Gravisia rubens

Gravisia rubens, commonly known as the crimson flame lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals that resemble flames, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers. Gravisia rubens thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add dramatic visual interest to their landscapes.
