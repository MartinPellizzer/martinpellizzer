Grevillea vieillardii var. emarginata

Grevillea vieillardii var. emarginata, commonly known as the Emarginate Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often adorned with distinctive red or orange markings. The plant produces striking, bright red or orange flowers in clusters, which are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
