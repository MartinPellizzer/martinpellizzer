Gaultheria Fragrantissima Subsp. Punctata

Gaultheria fragrantissima subsp. punctata is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its striking appearance and distinctive red berries that attract various wildlife. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in dense forests at high altitudes. Its leaves are covered in tiny white spots, giving it a speckled look that sets it apart from other species. Despite its beauty, this subspecies remains poorly studied and is considered a botanical curiosity.
