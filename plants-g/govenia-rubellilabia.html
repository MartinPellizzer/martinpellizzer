Govenia rubellilabia

Govenia rubellilabia, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a combination of pink, white, and deep red hues.

Govenia rubellilabia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
