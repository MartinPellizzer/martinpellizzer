Gentiana kwansiensis

Gentiana kwansiensis, commonly known as the Kwansian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, which is known for its showy, blue or violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers are tubular with prominent blue petals, and it blooms during the summer months.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
