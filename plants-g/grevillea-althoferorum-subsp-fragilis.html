Grevillea althoferorum subsp. fragilis

Grevillea althoferorum subsp.

fragilis, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a species native to Western Australia. It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and striking red or orange flowers that appear in summer.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and threatened species.
