Geum × rivaliurbanum

Geum × rivaliurbanum, commonly known as the urban garden geum, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features basal leaves that are palmately divided with serrated edges. Its adaptability to urban environments makes it a popular choice for gardeners in city settings.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil conditions.
