Gonocormus palmatifidus

Gonocormus palmatifidus, commonly known as the broadleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that split into several lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in humid, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
