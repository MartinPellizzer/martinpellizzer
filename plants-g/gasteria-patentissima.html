Gasteria patentissima

Gasteria patentissima, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically upright and pointed. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria patentissima is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
