Gentiana yunnanensis subsp. yunnanensis

Gentiana yunnanensis subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are usually unbranched and hairy.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
