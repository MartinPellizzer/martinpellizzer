Gentiana burseri var. biloba

Gentiana burseri var. biloba, commonly known as the African gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
