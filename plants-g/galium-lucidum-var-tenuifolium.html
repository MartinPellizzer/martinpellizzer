Galium lucidum var. tenuifolium

Galium lucidum var. tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, ascending stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and they are arranged alternately along the stem. Small, white to pale yellow flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
