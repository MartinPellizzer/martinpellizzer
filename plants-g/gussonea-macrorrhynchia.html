Gussonea macrorrhynchia

Gussonea macrorrhynchia, commonly known as the large-beaked gussonea, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright stem and large, colorful bracts that resemble petals. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and often covered in a waxy coating.
