Gymnocalycium valnicekianum subsp. prochazkanum

Gymnocalycium valnicekianum subsp.

prochazkanum, commonly known as the Prochazka's globose cactus, is a species native to Argentina. It is characterized by its rounded, spiny stems that can grow up to 20 cm in diameter.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and botanists.
