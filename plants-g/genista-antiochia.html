Genista antiochia

Genista antiochia, commonly known as the Antioch broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
