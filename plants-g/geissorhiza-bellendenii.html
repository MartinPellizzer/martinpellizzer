Geissorhiza bellendenii

Geissorhiza bellendenii, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Xanthorrhoeaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spike-like inflorescences that bear bright orange to red flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both garden settings and botanical collections.
