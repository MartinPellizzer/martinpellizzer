Grevillea hystrix

Grevillea hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped stems and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Grevillea hystrix thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract native birds and insects.
