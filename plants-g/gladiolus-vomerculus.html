Gladiolus vomerculus

Gladiolus vomerculus, commonly known as the dwarf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves and produces tall, sturdy stems that bear clusters of vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, showcasing a wide range of colors including red, pink, yellow, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden beds and borders.

Due to its compact size, it is ideal for small spaces and container gardening.
