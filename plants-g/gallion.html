Gallion

Gallion, commonly known as the gallion plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological importance.
