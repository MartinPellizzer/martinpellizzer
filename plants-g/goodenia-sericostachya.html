Goodenia sericostachya

Goodenia sericostachya, commonly known as the silky-stemmed goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
