Gundelia tenuisecta

Gundelia tenuisecta, commonly known as the desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in harsh, dry environments, showcasing remarkable adaptations to survive in extreme conditions.
