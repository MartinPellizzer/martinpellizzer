Gladiolus guthriei

Gladiolus guthriei, commonly known as the Guthrie's gladiolus, is a rare and striking member of the Iridaceae family native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces large, showy flowers with vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late summer, adding a dramatic visual impact to its natural habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
