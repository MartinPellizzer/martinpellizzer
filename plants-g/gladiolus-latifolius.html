Gladiolus latifolius

Gladiolus latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, stiff stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its ability to produce numerous blooms throughout the growing season.
