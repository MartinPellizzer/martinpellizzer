Goeppertia mendesiana

Goeppertia mendesiana, commonly known as the Mendesiana Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America, specifically Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, often featuring intricate patterns that change with light exposure. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.

This ornamental plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
