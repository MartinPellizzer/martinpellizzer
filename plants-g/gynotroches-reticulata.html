Gynotroches reticulata

Gynotroches reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, net-like venation pattern on its leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
