Gardenia corymbosa

Gardenia corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia corymbosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.
