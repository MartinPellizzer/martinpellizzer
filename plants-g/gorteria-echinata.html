Gorteria echinata

Gorteria echinata, commonly known as the spiked gorteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Gorteria echinata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
