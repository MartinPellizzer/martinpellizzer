Gymnacranthera farquhariana var. major

Gymnacranthera farquhariana var. major, commonly known as the Farquharian myrtle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is dense and evergreen, making it a desirable ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
