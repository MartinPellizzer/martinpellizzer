Gesneria pallida

Gesneria pallida, commonly known as the white gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant green, with some varieties displaying a pale or white underside. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom during the warm seasons.

Gesneria pallida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
