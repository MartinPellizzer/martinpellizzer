Gnaphalium likiangense

Gnaphalium likiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan feather grass, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, fluffy flower heads that resemble dandelion blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, mountainous regions and has delicate, hairy leaves. It produces a large number of tiny seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
