Griffinia liboniana

Griffinia liboniana, commonly known as the Philippine violet, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Griffinia liboniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
