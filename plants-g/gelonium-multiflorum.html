Gelonium multiflorum

Gelonium multiflorum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for indirect light and consistently moist soil. Its compact growth habit and long blooming season make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Gelonium multiflorum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires regular care to maintain its vibrant appearance.
