Gandasulium coccineum

Gandasulium coccineum, commonly known as the crimson flame vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Gandasulium coccineum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and vibrant floral display.
