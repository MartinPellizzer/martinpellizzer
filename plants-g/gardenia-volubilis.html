Gardenia volubilis

Gardenia volubilis, commonly known as the climbing gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem that allows it to climb or trail, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets. It prefers warm, humid climates and well-draining soil.

Gardenia volubilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
