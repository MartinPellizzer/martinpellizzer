Gloxinia attenuata

Gloxinia attenuata, commonly known as the slender gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Gloxinia attenuata is also popular for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
