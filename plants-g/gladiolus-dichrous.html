Gladiolus dichrous

Gladiolus dichrous, commonly known as the dichrous gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically vibrant in color, often displaying a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
