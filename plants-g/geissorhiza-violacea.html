Geissorhiza violacea

Geissorhiza violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in areas with good drainage. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms during the summer months.

Geissorhiza violacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
