Gazania × splendens

Gazania × splendens, commonly known as the splendour gazania, is a hybrid plant in the Aizoaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Gazania × splendens is known for its long blooming period, typically from spring to early autumn.
