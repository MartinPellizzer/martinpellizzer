Gnaphalium confusum

Gnaphalium confusum, commonly known as the confused featherweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are often clustered in branched arrays. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and covered in fine, white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance.

Native to South America, it is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
