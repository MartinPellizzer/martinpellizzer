Goodenia calogynoides

Goodenia calogynoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces distinctive seed pods that are often ribbed and contain numerous tiny seeds.

Goodenia calogynoides is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often found in coastal or inland dunes.
