Gladiolus stenosiphon

Gladiolus stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-lipped gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, sword-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with narrow lips that distinguish it from other gladiolus species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for cut flowers.
