Galium rubioides

Galium rubioides, commonly known as redstem bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often reddish in color, especially when young. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Galium rubioides is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
