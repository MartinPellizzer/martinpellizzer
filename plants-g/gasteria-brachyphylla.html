Gasteria brachyphylla

Gasteria brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features short, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria brachyphylla is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
