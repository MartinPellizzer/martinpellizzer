Gurania dumortieri

Gurania dumortieri, commonly known as the desert cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its thick, spiny stems that store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It is adapted to survive in extreme desert conditions with minimal water.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it an important species in its ecological niche.
