Gonzalagunia petesia

Gonzalagunia petesia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Gonzalagunia petesia is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
