Goodenia macrocalyx

Goodenia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered goodenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and shrublands. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a hairy surface, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia macrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
