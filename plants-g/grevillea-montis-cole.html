Grevillea montis-cole

Grevillea montis-cole, commonly known as the Mount Cole grevillea, is a shrub native to the alpine regions of southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like foliage and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, and the leaves are narrow and linear.

Grevillea montis-cole is well adapted to cool, temperate climates and is often found in open forest or heathland environments.
