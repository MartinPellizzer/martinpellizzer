Gentiana × burseri-punctata

Gentiana × burseri-punctata, commonly known as the Blue Gentian, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded habitats. It has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana × burseri-punctata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
