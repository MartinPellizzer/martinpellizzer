Gelasia ensifolia

Gelasia ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf gelasia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, hence its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Gelasia ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
