Gentiana manshurica

Gentiana manshurica, commonly known as the Manchurian gentian, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gentiana manshurica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
