Grammitis ludens

Grammitis ludens, commonly known as the silver fern, is a species of fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lacy fronds that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a unique appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually narrow and elongated, with a delicate, feathery structure.

Grammitis ludens is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
