Gloxinia purpurea

Gloxinia purpurea, commonly known as the purple gloxinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces vibrant purple or pink blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gloxinia purpurea is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
