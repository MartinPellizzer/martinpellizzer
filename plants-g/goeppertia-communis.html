Goeppertia communis

Goeppertia communis, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Goeppertia communis is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other plants like peace lilies and calla lilies.
