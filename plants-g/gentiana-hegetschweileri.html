Gentiana hegetschweileri

Gentiana hegetschweileri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is a perennial herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or streamside habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
