Gladiolus × willmoreanus

Gladiolus × willmoreanus, commonly known as the Willmorean gladiolus, is a hybrid plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy, colorful flowers in shades of red, pink, white, and yellow, typically arranged in dense spikes. Its foliage consists of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
