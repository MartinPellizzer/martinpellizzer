Gurania latifolia

Gurania latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gurania, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Violaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in shaded forest understories and along stream banks.
