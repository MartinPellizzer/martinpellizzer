Gymnogramma laucheana var. gigantea

Gymnogramma laucheana var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant gymnogramma, is a variant of the gymnogramma genus native to South America.

It is a fern characterized by its large, leathery, evergreen leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, upright growth habit. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
