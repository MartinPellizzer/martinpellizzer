Gymnostillingia acutifolia

Gymnostillingia acutifolia, commonly known as the acute-leaved gymnostillingia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa.

Gymnostillingia acutifolia thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.
