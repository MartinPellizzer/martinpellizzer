Gentiana ovalis

Gentiana ovalis, commonly known as the oval gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky slopes. The plant features basal leaves that are oval-shaped with a distinctive white or pale green midrib. Its flowers are striking, blue to violet in color, with five petals and a prominent blue throat.

Gentiana ovalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
