Genista webbii

Genista webbii, commonly known as the Webb's broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by elongated, bean-like pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval leaflets.

Genista webbii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
