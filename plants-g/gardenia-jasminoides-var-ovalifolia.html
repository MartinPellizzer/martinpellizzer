Gardenia jasminoides var. ovalifolia

Gardenia jasminoides var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the ovalleaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. The plant produces fragrant, waxy white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
