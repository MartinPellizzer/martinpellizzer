Globba × intermedia

Globba × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid ginger, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is a rhizomatous herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
