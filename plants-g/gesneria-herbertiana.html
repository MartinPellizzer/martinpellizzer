Gesneria herbertiana

Gesneria herbertiana, commonly known as Herbert's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage is often a deep green with a waxy texture, while its flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Gesneria herbertiana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
