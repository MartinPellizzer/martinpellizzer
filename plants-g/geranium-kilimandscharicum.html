Geranium kilimandscharicum

Geranium kilimandscharicum, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro geranium, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often found in mountainous areas with cool, moist conditions.

Geranium kilimandscharicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in temperate climates.
