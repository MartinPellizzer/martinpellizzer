Gentiana tenuissima

Gentiana tenuissima, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of down.

The slender gentian is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
