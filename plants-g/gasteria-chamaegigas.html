Gasteria chamaegigas

Gasteria chamaegigas, commonly known as the Cape mountain gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Gasteria chamaegigas is well-suited to full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.
