Gentiana doxiongshangensis

Gentiana doxiongshangensis, commonly known as the Doxiongshang gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
