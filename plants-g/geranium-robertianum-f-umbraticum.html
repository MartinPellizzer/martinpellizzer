Geranium robertianum f. umbraticum

Geranium robertianum f. umbraticum, commonly known as the shade geranium, is a compact perennial plant characterized by its dark green, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is native to Europe, particularly thriving in moist, shaded woodland environments. The foliage is often variegated, with a distinctive dark underside that adds visual interest. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or container planting.

Its botanical features make it a unique choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants with subtle beauty.
