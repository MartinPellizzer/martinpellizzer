Gentiana oschtenica

Gentiana oschtenica, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana oschtenica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural environment.
