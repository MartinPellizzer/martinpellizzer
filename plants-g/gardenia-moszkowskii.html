Gardenia moszkowskii

Gardenia moszkowskii, commonly known as the Moskowicz gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, waxy white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces yellowish-orange berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Gardenia moszkowskii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and strong, sweet scent.
