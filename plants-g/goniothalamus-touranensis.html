Goniothalamus touranensis

Goniothalamus touranensis, commonly known as the Touranensis genus, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the tropical regions of Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
