Gazania caespitosa

Gazania caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted gazania, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow or orange daisy-like flowers with dark centers, blooming throughout the warmer months. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit, with hairy, gray-green leaves that help reduce water loss. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
