Goeppertia panamensis

Goeppertia panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture and intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
