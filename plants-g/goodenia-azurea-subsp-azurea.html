Goodenia azurea subsp. azurea

Goodenia azurea subsp.

azurea, commonly known as the blue goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
