Gladiolus dalenii subsp. dalenii

Gladiolus dalenii subsp.

dalenii, commonly known as the Dalen's gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences that bear vibrant, colorful flowers.

The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
