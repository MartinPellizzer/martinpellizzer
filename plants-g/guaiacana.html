Guaiacana

Guaiacana, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the family Marantaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are rich in starch. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are broad, arrow-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.
