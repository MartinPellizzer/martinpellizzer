Goodyera wuana

Goodyera wuana, commonly known as the Chinese basket plant, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a thick layer of white, woolly hair. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a distinctive yellowish or reddish hue at the base.

Goodyera wuana thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in mountainous regions.
