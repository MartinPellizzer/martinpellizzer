Grevillea helmsiana

Grevillea helmsiana, commonly known as the Orange Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its bright orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea helmsiana is also noted for its ability to attract native pollinators such as birds and insects.
