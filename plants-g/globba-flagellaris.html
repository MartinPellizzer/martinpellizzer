Globba flagellaris

Globba flagellaris, commonly known as the narrowleaf globba, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers.
