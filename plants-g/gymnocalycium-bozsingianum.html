Gymnocalycium bozsingianum

Gymnocalycium bozsingianum, commonly known as the Bozsing's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its globular shape and spiny stems. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived. Its fleshy stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Gymnocalycium bozsingianum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
