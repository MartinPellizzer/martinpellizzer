Gemmingia chinensis f. aureoflora

Gemmingia chinensis f. aureoflora, commonly known as the golden-flowered Chinese gemmingia, is a unique cultivar of the gemmingia plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth texture, while the flowers bloom in clusters during the spring season.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
