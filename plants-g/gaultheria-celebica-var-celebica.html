Gaultheria celebica var. celebica

Gaultheria celebica var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes wintergreen, is a species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy in appearance. Its flowers are white and clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red berries that are edible.

The plant is valued for its aromatic leaves and has traditional uses in local medicine.
