Glochidion styliferum

Glochidion styliferum, commonly known as the slender glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptic leaves with a leathery texture. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
