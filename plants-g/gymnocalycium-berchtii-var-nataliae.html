Gymnocalycium berchtii var. nataliae

Gymnocalycium berchtii var. nataliae, commonly known as Natal's moon cactus, is a compact cactus species native to southern Africa.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in white, woolly spines that provide protection against herbivores and extreme temperatures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its gray-green foliage. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem for survival during droughts.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
