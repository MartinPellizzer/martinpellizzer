Galaxia grandiflora

Galaxia grandiflora, commonly known as the largeflower galaxia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Galaxia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
