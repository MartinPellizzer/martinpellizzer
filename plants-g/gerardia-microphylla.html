Gerardia microphylla

Gerardia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Geraniaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring. Gerardia microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
