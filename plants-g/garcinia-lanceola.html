Garcinia lanceola

Garcinia lanceola, commonly known as the African yellow mangosteen, is a tropical shrub native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange fruits that have a thick, leathery rind. The plant produces elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly waxy texture. The flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in greenish-white clusters.

Garcinia lanceola is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
