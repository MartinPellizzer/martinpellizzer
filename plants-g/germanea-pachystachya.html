Germanea pachystachya

Germanea pachystachya, commonly known as the giant pachystachya, is a striking member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of southern Africa, particularly Namibia and South Africa. The plant is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, clustered inflorescences that produce vibrant red or orange flowers. Its leaves are typically thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native dry environment.

Germanea pachystachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh conditions.
