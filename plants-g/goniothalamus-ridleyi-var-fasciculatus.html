Goniothalamus ridleyi var. fasciculatus

Goniothalamus ridleyi var. fasciculatus, commonly known as the fasciculate goniothalamus, is a member of the Annonaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Borneo. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-white in color, and are borne in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a globose berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
