Geranium platyanthum

Geranium platyanthum, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of silvery, fuzzy leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in both horticulture and botanical gardens.
