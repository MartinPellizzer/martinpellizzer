Gentiana verna subsp. willkommiana

Gentiana verna subsp.

willkommiana, commonly known as the spring gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as meadows and forest floors. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
