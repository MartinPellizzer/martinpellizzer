Grammitis Adspersa

Grammitis adspersa is a delicate fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific. This small, evergreen plant is known for its intricate, lace-like fronds that shimmer with a fine, silvery coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or in dense undergrowth. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species within the fern family.
