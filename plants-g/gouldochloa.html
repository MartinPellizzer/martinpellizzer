Gouldochloa

Gouldochloa, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a genus of grasses native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Gouldochloa species are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
