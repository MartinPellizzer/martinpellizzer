Guyonia humilis

Guyonia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually yellow or orange, attracting various pollinators.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.
