Gonocaryum poilanei

Gonocaryum poilanei, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique and striking floral structures. The plant typically features showy, colorful flowers with intricate petal arrangements. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
