Gymnadenia nigra subsp. eggeriana

Gymnadenia nigra subsp.

eggeriana, commonly known as the Black Reticulated Orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family. It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish floral lips and elongated, grass-like leaves.

This subspecies is native to the alpine regions of Central Europe, particularly in Austria and the Czech Republic. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as mountain meadows and forest edges.

The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which blooms in late summer.
