Gomphrena arborescens

Gomphrena arborescens, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescences that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched and somewhat woody.

Gomphrena arborescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
