Gorteria beguinotii

Gorteria beguinotii, commonly known as the Cape dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has hairy leaves and produces fluffy, seed-filled capsules that disperse its seeds by wind.
