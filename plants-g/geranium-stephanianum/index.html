Geranium Stephanianum

Stephanian's geranium, scientifically known as Geranium stephanianum, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. With its vibrant purple blooms and velvety foliage, it stands out as a unique addition to any garden or collection. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a versatile choice for horticultural enthusiasts. Its delicate flowers, which resemble tiny bells, bloom throughout the growing season, adding a touch of elegance to landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering, ensuring its health and longevity.
