Gentiana ornata

Gentiana ornata, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are often erect and branched.

Gentiana ornata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
