Genista tricuspidata subsp. sparsiflora

Genista tricuspidata subsp.

sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features slender, thorny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves with three to five leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland scrub habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
