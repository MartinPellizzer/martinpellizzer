Gesneria elliptica var. lutea

Gesneria elliptica var. lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Gesneria, is a hybrid cultivar of the Gesneria genus native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually orange or red, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
