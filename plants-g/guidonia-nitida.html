Guidonia nitida

Guidonia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf guillemot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its ornamental value comes from its distinctive foliage and long blooming period.

Guidonia nitida is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
