Gymnogramma rutifolia var. australasica

Gymnogramma rutifolia var. australasica, commonly known as the Australian sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern genus native to Australia.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its lance-shaped, serrated leaves that form dense, upright clumps. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with spore-producing structures on the underside.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
