Geodorum regnieri

Geodorum regnieri, commonly known as the red-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often reddish-veined, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, with prominent purple sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Geodorum regnieri is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
