Grona laosensis

Grona laosensis, commonly known as the Laos mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Laos.

It is a terrestrial orchid with delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, preferring well-drained soil rich in organic matter. Its root system is fibrous, and it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Grona laosensis is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
