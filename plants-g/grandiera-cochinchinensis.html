Grandiera cochinchinensis

Grandiera cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Solanaceae, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
