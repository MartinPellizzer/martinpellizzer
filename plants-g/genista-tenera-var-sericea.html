Genista tenera var. sericea

Genista tenera var. sericea, commonly known as silky broom.

This plant is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit. Its leaves are small, narrow, and covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The flowers are yellow, clustered in small racemes, and bloom in early spring.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
