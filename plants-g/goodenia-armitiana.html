Goodenia armitiana

Goodenia armitiana, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in arid and semi-arid regions. It has small, linear leaves and produces capsules as its fruiting structure.

Goodenia armitiana is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
