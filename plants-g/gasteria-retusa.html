Gasteria retusa

Gasteria retusa, commonly known as the cannonball plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on tall stalks, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria retusa is valued for its ornamental appearance and resilience in low-maintenance gardening.
