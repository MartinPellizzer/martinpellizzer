Goodenia ovata var. latifolia

Goodenia ovata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
