Geranium vitifolium

Geranium vitifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
