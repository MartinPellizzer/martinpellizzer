Gomphrena meyeniana var. caulescens

Gomphrena meyeniana var. caulescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and vibrant, feathery inflorescences that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements.
