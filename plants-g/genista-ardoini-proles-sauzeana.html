Genista ardoini proles sauzeana

Genista ardoini proles sauzeana, commonly known as the Sauzean broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It produces yellow flowers in spring, followed by small, legume-like pods that contain seeds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in arid climates.
