Graptopetalum amethystinum

Graptopetalum amethystinum, commonly known as the Blue Elf Flower, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
