Gynomphis argentea

Gynomphis argentea, commonly known as the silverleaf gynomphis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Gynomphis argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
