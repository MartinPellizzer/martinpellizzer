Gymnopetalum tubiflorum

Gymnopetalum tubiflorum, commonly known as the woolly bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, which bloom in late summer. Gymnopetalum tubiflorum thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.
