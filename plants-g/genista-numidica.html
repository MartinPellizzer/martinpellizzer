Genista numidica

Genista numidica, commonly known as the Algerian broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves, each composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it adaptable to arid environments.

Genista numidica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
