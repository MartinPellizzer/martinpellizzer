Gentiana boliviana

Gentiana boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
