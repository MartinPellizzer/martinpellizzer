Grosourdya elegans

Grosourdya elegans, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Grosourdya elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
