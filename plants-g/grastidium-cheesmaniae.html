Grastidium cheesmaniae

Grastidium cheesmaniae, commonly known as the cheesman's grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features slender, grass-like stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in clusters.

The species is adapted to waterlogged soils and plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems.
