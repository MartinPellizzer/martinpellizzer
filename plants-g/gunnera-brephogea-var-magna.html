Gunnera brephogea var. magna

Gunnera brephogea var. magna, commonly known as the giant broadleaf gunnera, is a striking plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its massive, shield-shaped leaves that can reach several feet in diameter, making it a dramatic focal point in gardens. The plant belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants like buckwheat and sorrel. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique foliage and robust growth make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialty landscapes.
