Geraniospermum althaeodes

Geraniospermum althaeodes, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower colors, which range from pink to purple.
