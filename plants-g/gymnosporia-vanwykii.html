Gymnosporia vanwykii

Gymnosporia vanwykii, commonly known as the Van Wyk's burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain small, hard seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Gymnosporia vanwykii is also noted for its use in traditional medicine and its role in local ecosystems.
