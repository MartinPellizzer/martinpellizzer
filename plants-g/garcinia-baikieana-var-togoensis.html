Garcinia baikieana var. togoensis

Garcinia baikieana var. togoensis, commonly known as Togo yellow mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface.
