Gymnadenia nigra subsp. cenisia

Gymnadenia nigra subsp.

cenisia, commonly known as the dark pink orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its striking dark pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It has a rhizomatous root system and lanceolate leaves that are often hairy.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
