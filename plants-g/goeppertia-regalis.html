Goeppertia regalis

Goeppertia regalis, commonly known as the Royal Bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its large, colorful leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Goeppertia regalis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
