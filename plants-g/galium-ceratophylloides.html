Galium ceratophylloides

Galium ceratophylloides, commonly known as the horned bedstraw, is a perennial herb belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Galium ceratophylloides is typically found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
