Gynura incana

Gynura incana, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Gynura incana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
