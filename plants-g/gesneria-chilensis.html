Gesneria chilensis

Gesneria chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean gesneria, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Chile.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features broad, glossy leaves and produces clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Gesneria chilensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
