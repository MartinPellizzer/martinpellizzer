Genista × praecox

Genista × praecox, commonly known as the early buckthorn, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, elliptical pods containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
