Gouinia polygama var. major

Gouinia polygama var. major, commonly known as the giant gouinia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family native to southern Africa.

This plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. It produces showy, pale yellow to orange flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. The plant is typically found in rocky outcrops and well-drained soils, thriving in full sun.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in arid and semi-arid regions.
