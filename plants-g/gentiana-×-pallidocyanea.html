Gentiana × pallidocyanea

Gentiana × pallidocyanea, commonly known as the pale sky gentian, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to sky-blue flowers, which are typically tubular with a wide, open throat. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gentiana × pallidocyanea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
