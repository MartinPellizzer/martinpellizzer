Gasteria carinata var. falcata

Gasteria carinata var. falcata, commonly known as the sword-shaped gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, sword-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The leaves are often curved, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
