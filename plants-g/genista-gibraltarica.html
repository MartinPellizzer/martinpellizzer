Genista gibraltarica

Genista gibraltarica, commonly known as Gibraltar rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the rocky landscapes of southern Europe and North Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub with slender, spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

Genista gibraltarica is often used in horticulture for its hardiness and ornamental value in gardens.
