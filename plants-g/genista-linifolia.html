Genista linifolia

Genista linifolia, commonly known as the slender broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and are followed by small, bean-like pods. It is native to southern Europe and is adapted to dry, rocky soils.

Genista linifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
