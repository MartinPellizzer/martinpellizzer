Grindelia gymnospermoides

Grindelia gymnospermoides, commonly known as the desert gumweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

Grindelia gymnospermoides is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects.
