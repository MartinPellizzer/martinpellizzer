Geniostoma kasyotense

Geniostoma kasyotense, commonly known as the Kasyotense fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has delicate, feather-like fronds that are often submerged or partially emergent. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the substrate.

It plays a role in maintaining water quality by filtering sediments and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
