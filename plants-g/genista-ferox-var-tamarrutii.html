Genista ferox var. tamarrutii

Genista ferox var. tamarrutii, commonly known as the Tamarrutii shrub, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

This variety is noted for its resilience and is often used in ecological restoration projects in its native habitat.
