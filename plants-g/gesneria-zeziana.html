Gesneria zeziana

Gesneria zeziana, commonly known as the Zanzibar gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and vibrant orange-red flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It produces showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

Gesneria zeziana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
