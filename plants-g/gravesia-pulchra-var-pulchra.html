Gravesia pulchra var. pulchra

Gravesia pulchra var. pulchra, commonly known as the sweet shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
