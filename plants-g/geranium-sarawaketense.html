Geranium sarawaketense

Geranium sarawaketense, commonly known as the Sarawak geranium, is a species native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while the flowers range in color from pink to red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in tropical climates.
