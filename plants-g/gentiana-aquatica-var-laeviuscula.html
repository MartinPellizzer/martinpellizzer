Gentiana aquatica var. laeviuscula

Gentiana aquatica var. laeviuscula, commonly known as the smooth water gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in ponds, marshes, and along stream edges.

Its flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, and it plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
