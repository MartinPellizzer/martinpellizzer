Gerascanthus molundensis

Gerascanthus molundensis, commonly known as the Molund's geranium, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a slightly hairy texture, and the stems are upright and branched.

Gerascanthus molundensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
