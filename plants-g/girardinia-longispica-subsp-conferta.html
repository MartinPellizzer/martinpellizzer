Girardinia longispica subsp. conferta

Girardinia longispica subsp.

conferta, commonly known as the dense-spined girardinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features dense, spiny leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
