Gesneria tweedieana

Gesneria tweedieana, commonly known as the Tweedie's gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the humid lowland forests of Ecuador.

It features dark green, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact rosette. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Gesneria tweedieana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and vibrant floral display.
