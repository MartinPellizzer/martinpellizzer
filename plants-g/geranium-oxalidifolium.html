Geranium oxalidifolium

Geranium oxalidifolium, commonly known as the oxalidifolium geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
