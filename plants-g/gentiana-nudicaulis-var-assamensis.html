Gentiana nudicaulis var. assamensis

Gentiana nudicaulis var. assamensis, commonly known as the Assam gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northeastern India.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
