Gladiolus overbergensis

Gladiolus overbergensis, commonly known as the Overberg gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing spikes of flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is appreciated both for its aesthetic value and its adaptability to various growing conditions.
