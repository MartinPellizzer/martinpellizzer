Grammitis frigida

Grammitis frigida, commonly known as the cold grammitis, is a fern native to temperate regions of the Southern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, moss-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. Its fronds are small, narrow, and often have a velvety texture.
