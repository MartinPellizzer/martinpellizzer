Geranium hupehanum

Geranium hupehanum, commonly known as Chinese geranium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink or red flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Geranium hupehanum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
