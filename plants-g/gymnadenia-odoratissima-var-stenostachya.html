Gymnadenia odoratissima var. stenostachya

Gymnadenia odoratissima var. stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is noted for its aromatic foliage and is of interest to botanists and gardeners alike.
