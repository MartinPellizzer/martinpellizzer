Gravesia antongiliana

Gravesia antongiliana, commonly known as the Madagascar star flower, is a striking tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Gravesia antongiliana is primarily found in humid, lowland forest areas and is known for its ornamental value.
