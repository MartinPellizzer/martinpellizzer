Gomphrena decumbens subvar. foliatissima

Gomphrena decumbens subvar. foliatissima, commonly known as the leafy gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, spiky flower heads that range in color from pink to red. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its long blooming period and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal in both garden and landscape settings.
