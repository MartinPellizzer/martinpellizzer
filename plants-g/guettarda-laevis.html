Guettarda laevis

Guettarda laevis, commonly known as the smooth guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and forest understories. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture, which contributes to its common name.

Guettarda laevis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its attractive foliage and continuous flowering.
