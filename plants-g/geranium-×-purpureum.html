Geranium × purpureum

Geranium × purpureum, commonly known as the purple geranium, is a hybrid species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pentagonal flowers. The leaves are typically rounded or heart-shaped, with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It produces vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is well-suited to full sun to partial shade conditions.
