Galega maxima

Galega maxima, commonly known as the greater goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets with serrated edges. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Galega maxima is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
