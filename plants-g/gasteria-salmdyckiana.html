Gasteria salmdyckiana

Gasteria salmdyckiana, commonly known as the Zulu bulbous plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences that are often pink or orange in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria salmdyckiana is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
