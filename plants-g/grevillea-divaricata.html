Grevillea divaricata

Grevillea divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, zigzagging leaves that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in clusters, which are rich in nectar and attract various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea divaricata is also valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
