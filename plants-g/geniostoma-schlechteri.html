Geniostoma schlechteri

Geniostoma schlechteri, commonly known as the velvetleaf or false dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant features alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a velvety texture, and it produces small, yellowish to greenish composite flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is considered a weed in some regions.
