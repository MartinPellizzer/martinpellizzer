Grevillea didymobotrya

Grevillea didymobotrya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions.

Grevillea didymobotrya is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
