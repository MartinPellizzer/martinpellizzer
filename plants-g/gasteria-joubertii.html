Gasteria joubertii

Gasteria joubertii, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers on tall, slender stems during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Gasteria joubertii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
