Guettarda calyptrata var. ferruginea

Guettarda calyptrata var. ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty guettarda, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers, which are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its woody stems are covered in a rough, rusty-colored bark, giving it its common name.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy soils.
