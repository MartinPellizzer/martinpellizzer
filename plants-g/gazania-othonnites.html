Gazania othonnites

Gazania othonnites, commonly known as the yellow gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Gazania othonnites blooms throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to gardens in warm climates.
