Garcinia edulis

Garcinia edulis, commonly known as the African mango, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The fruit is oval-shaped, with a thick, yellow rind and a soft, white, fleshy pulp. It contains a single or multiple seeds enclosed in a hard, woody shell.

The plant is valued for its fruit, which is used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
