Genista cupanii

Genista cupanii, commonly known as the Turkish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring slender, wiry stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters during the spring and early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody structure and a preference for well-drained soils.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, thriving in full sun conditions.
