Gerbera hederifolia

Gerbera hederifolia, commonly known as the ivy gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white petals. The plant features heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil.

Gerbera hederifolia is a popular choice for garden borders and containers due to its vibrant blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
