Galium × schneebergense

Galium × schneebergense, commonly known as the Schneeberg bedstraw, is a hybrid species native to Central Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender stems that are often prostrate or ascending, covered with fine, soft hairs. Its small, white to pale pink flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal in shady garden settings.
