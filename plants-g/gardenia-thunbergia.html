Gardenia thunbergia

Gardenia thunbergia, commonly known as the Thai gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to tropical and subtropical climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Gardenia thunbergia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
