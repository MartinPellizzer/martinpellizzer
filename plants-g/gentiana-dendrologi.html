Gentiana dendrologi

Gentiana dendrologi, commonly known as the tree gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Gentiana dendrologi is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
