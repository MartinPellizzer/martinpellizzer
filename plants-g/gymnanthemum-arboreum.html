Gymnanthemum arboreum

Gymnanthemum arboreum, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, woody stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and savanna habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
