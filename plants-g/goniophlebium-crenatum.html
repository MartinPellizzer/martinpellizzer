Goniophlebium crenatum

Goniophlebium crenatum, commonly known as the serrated fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that have serrated edges. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Goniophlebium crenatum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate foliage and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
