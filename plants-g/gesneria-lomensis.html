Gesneria lomensis

Gesneria lomensis, commonly known as the Lomentos Gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features broad, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months.

Gesneria lomensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
