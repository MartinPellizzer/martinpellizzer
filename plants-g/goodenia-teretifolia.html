Goodenia teretifolia

Goodenia teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Goodenia teretifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
