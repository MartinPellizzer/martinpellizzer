Gynoxys glabriuscula

Gynoxys glabriuscula, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Gynoxys glabriuscula is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
