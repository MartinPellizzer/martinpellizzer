Gladiolus ferrugineus

Gladiolus ferrugineus, commonly known as the rusty gladiolus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink to red, with a distinctive rust-colored stripe down the center of each petal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This plant is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its resilience in various climatic conditions.
