Grevillea variifolia

Grevillea variifolia, commonly known as the variable grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery foliage that ranges from dark green to silver-blue in color. The plant produces bright red or orange cylindrical flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea variifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
