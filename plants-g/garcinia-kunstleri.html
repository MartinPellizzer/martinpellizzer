Garcinia kunstleri

Garcinia kunstleri, commonly known as the African yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, with a thick, aromatic rind. Garcinia kunstleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticultural and ecological contexts.
