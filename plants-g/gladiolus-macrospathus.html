Gladiolus macrospathus

Gladiolus macrospathus, commonly known as the giant sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 meters, making it one of the tallest species in the gladiolus genus. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of flowers arranged in a spike, with each bloom having three prominent petals and three sepals.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering and moderate fertilization for optimal growth.
