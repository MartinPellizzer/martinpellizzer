Guarea convergens

Guarea convergens, commonly known as the greenheart tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and dense, glossy leaves. The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
