Gunnera insularis

Gunnera insularis, commonly known as the giant curlleaf gunnera, is a striking plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and deeply lobed leaves that can reach up to 1.5 meters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers in dense, umbrella-like clusters during the growing season. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in temperate climates.
