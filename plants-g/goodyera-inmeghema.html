Goodyera inmeghema

Goodyera inmeghema, commonly known as the purple rosette orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of dark green, glossy leaves that form a dense, circular pattern. The plant produces striking purple or violet flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Goodyera inmeghema is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
