Gleichenia tenera

Gleichenia tenera, commonly known as the soft Gleichenia, is a fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, soft fronds that are typically less than 1 meter in length. The plant has a creeping rhizome and forms dense, low-growing mats, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately divided with a distinctive, glossy appearance.

Gleichenia tenera is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded, humid conditions.
