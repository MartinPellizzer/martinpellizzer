Gongora atropurpurea

Gongora atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple night orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking purple and violet flowers that bloom at night, attracting specific pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Gongora atropurpurea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
