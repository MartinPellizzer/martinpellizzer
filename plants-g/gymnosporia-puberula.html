Gymnosporia puberula

Gymnosporia puberula, commonly known as the woolly burr acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, yellowish-green foliage and thorny stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical heads. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Gymnosporia puberula is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
