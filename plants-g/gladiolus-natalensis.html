Gladiolus natalensis

Gladiolus natalensis, commonly known as the Natal gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with striking, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with colorful, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
