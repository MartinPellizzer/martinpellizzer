Gomesa paranensoides

Gomesa paranensoides, commonly known as the Parana grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow leaves. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense, attractive turf.
