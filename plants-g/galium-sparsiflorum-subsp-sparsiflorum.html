Galium sparsiflorum subsp. sparsiflorum

Galium sparsiflorum subsp.

sparsiflorum, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to North America. It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces, and its stems are often prostrate or ascending. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Galium sparsiflorum subsp.

sparsiflorum is typically found in the eastern United States and parts of Canada.
