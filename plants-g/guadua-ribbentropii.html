Guadua ribbentropii

Guadua ribbentropii, commonly known as the pink bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Colombia and Venezuela. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. Its leaves are long and narrow, often with a distinctive pinkish hue on the underside.

Guadua ribbentropii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic qualities.
