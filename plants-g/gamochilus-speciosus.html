Gamochilus speciosus

Gamochilus speciosus, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Gamochilus speciosus is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
