Gorteria gracilis

Gorteria gracilis, commonly known as the slender gorteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with small, hairy leaves.

Gorteria gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
