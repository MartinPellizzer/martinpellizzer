Garcinia leggeae

Garcinia leggeae, commonly known as the Malagasy Garcinia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces orange-red berries that are typically harvested when fully mature. Garcinia leggeae is often found in humid, lowland rainforests, where it thrives in the warm and moist climate.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
