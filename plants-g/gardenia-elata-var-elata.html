Gardenia elata var. elata

Gardenia elata var. elata, commonly known as the Chinese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are five-petaled and have a waxy texture, while the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and scent.
