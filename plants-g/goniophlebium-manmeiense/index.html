Goniophlebium Manmeiense

Goniophlebium manmeiense is a rare and fascinating fern native to the mountainous regions of China. This plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is known for its unique leaf structure and delicate appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this plant for future generations.
