Gynerium zeelandicum

Gynerium zeelandicum, commonly known as Zeeland's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring a robust stem covered in coarse hairs. The plant has large, heart-shaped basal leaves that are often lobed and covered in a dense layer of white, silky hairs. Its flowers are yellow and appear in dense, terminal clusters, forming a distinctive spherical shape.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
