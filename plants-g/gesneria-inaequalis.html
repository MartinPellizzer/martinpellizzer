Gesneria inaequalis

Gesneria inaequalis, commonly known as the unequal gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange, typically blooming during the warmer months.

Gesneria inaequalis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
