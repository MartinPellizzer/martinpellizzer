Gymnocalycium asterium var. paucispinum

Gymnocalycium asterium var. paucispinum, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular or cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the warm season, typically in spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
