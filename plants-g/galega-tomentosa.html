Galega tomentosa

Galega tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly goatweed, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green foliage and erect, branching stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Galega tomentosa is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
