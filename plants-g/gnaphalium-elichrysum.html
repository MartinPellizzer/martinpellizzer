Gnaphalium elichrysum

Gnaphalium elichrysum, commonly known as silverleaf toothache plant, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has a compact growth habit and produces numerous tiny flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat various ailments.
