Geum × kolbianum

Geum × kolbianum, commonly known as the hybrid garden buttercup, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with trifoliate leaves that are alternate and serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and woodland edges.

Geum × kolbianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in riparian areas.
