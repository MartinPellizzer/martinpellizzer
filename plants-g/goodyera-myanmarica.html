Goodyera myanmarica

Goodyera myanmarica, commonly known as the Myanmar moss orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, moss-like foliage and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing in forest understories. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Goodyera myanmarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
