Gaultheria steyermarkii

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by bright red berries that are rich in aromatic compounds. Gaultheria steyermarkii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and unique fragrance.
