Gentianella liniflora

Gentianella liniflora, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. It has slender, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit.

Gentianella liniflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
