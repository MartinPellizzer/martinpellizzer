Gymnostachyum kanthanense

Gymnostachyum kanthanense, commonly known as the Thai starfruit or Thai wild starfruit, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, and is characterized by its distinctive yellowish-orange fruit with a star-like appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a rough texture, while the flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is edible, though it is less commonly cultivated compared to other starfruit varieties.
