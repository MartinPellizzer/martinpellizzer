Geranium monsonia

Geranium monsonia, commonly known as the rock geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it well-suited for rocky or arid environments. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Geranium monsonia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
