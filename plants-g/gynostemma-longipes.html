Gynostemma longipes

Gynostemma longipes, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng or yangshan ginseng, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical fruits containing numerous seeds. It is valued for its medicinal properties, containing compounds similar to ginseng.

Its botanical features make it an important species in traditional medicine and ecological studies.
