Gelonium microcarpum

Gelonium microcarpum, commonly known as the dwarf geranium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with dark purple markings. The plant has succulent leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped, adapted to retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it an attractive addition to gardens with limited space.
