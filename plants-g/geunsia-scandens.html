Geunsia scandens

Geunsia scandens, commonly known as the climbing morning glory, is a vine species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures using its tendrils. It blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to autumn.

Geunsia scandens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
