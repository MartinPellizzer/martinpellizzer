Geranium collinum var. londesii

Geranium collinum var. londesii, commonly known as the limestone geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils, often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features upright stems with opposite, palmately lobed leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are pink to purple, with five petals, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging soil conditions.
