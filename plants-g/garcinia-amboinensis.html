Garcinia amboinensis

Garcinia amboinensis, commonly known as the mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple, segmented fruit that contains a white, juicy aril surrounding the seeds. The plant belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is known for its ornamental value due to its glossy leaves and attractive flowers. The fruit is rich in xanthones, which have been studied for their potential health benefits.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental appeal.
