Gasteria fasciata var. polyspila

Gasteria fasciata var. polyspila, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinct white or silver markings that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces offsets and has a compact growth habit, making it ideal for small spaces. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
