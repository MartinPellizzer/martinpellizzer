Gymnadenia mac-owaniana

Gymnadenia mac-owaniana, commonly known as the MacOwan's grass-pink, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of pale pink to lilac-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or grassy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the summer months.

Gymnadenia mac-owaniana is a perennial herb that plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native environment.
