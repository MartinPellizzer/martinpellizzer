Genista longirostrata

Genista longirostrata, commonly known as the long-beaked broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, yellow pea-like flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista longirostrata is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to stabilize slopes.
