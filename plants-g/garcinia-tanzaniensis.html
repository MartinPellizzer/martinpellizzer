Garcinia tanzaniensis

Garcinia tanzaniensis, commonly known as Tanzanian garcinia, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Garcinia.

It is native to East Africa, particularly Tanzania, and is found in tropical rainforests. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, yellow-orange fruits that contain a single seed. Garcinia tanzaniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.

Its botanical study contributes to the understanding of tropical flora and potential medicinal uses.
