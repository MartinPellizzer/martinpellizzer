Gasteria disticha var. conspurcata

Gasteria disticha var. conspurcata, commonly known as the speckled gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive brown or gray speckles. The plant produces orange or red flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
