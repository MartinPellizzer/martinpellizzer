Gethyllis oligophylla

Gethyllis oligophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, and its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is a perennial herb that produces flowers throughout the year under suitable conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
