Grevillea wickhamii

Grevillea wickhamii, commonly known as the Wicking Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical spikes. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea wickhamii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
