Graptopetalum filiferum

Graptopetalum filiferum, commonly known as the silver rose or moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
