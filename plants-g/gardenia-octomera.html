Gardenia octomera

Gardenia octomera, commonly known as the eight-lobed gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elliptical, yellowish fruits that contain multiple seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Gardenia octomera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and aromatic blossoms.
