Galarhoeus pekinensis var. fauriei

Galarhoeus pekinensis var. fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
