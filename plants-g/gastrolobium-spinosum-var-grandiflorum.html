Gastrolobium spinosum var. grandiflorum

Gastrolobium spinosum var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered spear-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, spiny leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
