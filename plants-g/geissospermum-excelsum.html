Geissospermum excelsum

Geissospermum excelsum, commonly known as the tall milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its latex sap is milky and can be toxic, containing compounds that deter herbivores.

Geissospermum excelsum is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
