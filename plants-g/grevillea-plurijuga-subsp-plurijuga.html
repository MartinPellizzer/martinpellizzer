Grevillea plurijuga subsp. plurijuga

Grevillea plurijuga subsp.

plurijuga, commonly known as the broad-leafed waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia. It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

The plant has leathery, narrow to broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Grevillea plurijuga subsp.

plurijuga is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
