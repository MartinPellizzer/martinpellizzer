Gaura decorticans

Gaura decorticans, commonly known as the slender gaura, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to white, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to pollinator-friendly habitats.
