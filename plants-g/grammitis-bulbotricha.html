Grammitis bulbotricha

Grammitis bulbotricha, commonly known as the hairy-veined fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, intricate fronds with finely divided leaves. Grammitis bulbotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
