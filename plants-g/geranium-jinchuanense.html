Geranium jinchuanense

Geranium jinchuanense, commonly known as the Jinchuan geranium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers bloom in shades of pink to purple, adding visual interest to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
