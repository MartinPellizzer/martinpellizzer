Godetia nutans var. sonomensis

Godetia nutans var. sonomensis, commonly known as the Sonoma godetia, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in native plant gardens.
