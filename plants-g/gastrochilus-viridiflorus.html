Gastrochilus viridiflorus

Gastrochilus viridiflorus, commonly known as the green-flowered gastrochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
