Gasteria pallescens

Gasteria pallescens, commonly known as the pale gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy, light green leaves that are often tinged with blue or gray. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, erect stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
