Gymnocalycium monvillei subsp. achirasense

Gymnocalycium monvillei subsp.

achirasense, commonly known as the Achira Moon Cactus, is a succulent cactus native to Argentina. It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water, and its distinctive, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange.

The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
