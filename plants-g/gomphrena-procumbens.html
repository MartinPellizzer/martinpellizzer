Gomphrena procumbens

Gomphrena procumbens, commonly known as the red leaf gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and vibrant, spiky flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Gomphrena procumbens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
