Gesneria trianae

Gesneria trianae, commonly known as the Trianas gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features broad, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow. It produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months.

Gesneria trianae is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
