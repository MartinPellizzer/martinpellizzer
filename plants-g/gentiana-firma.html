Gentiana firma

Gentiana firma, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana firma is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
