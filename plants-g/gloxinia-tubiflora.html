Gloxinia tubiflora

Gloxinia tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots that store nutrients. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Gloxinia tubiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
