Gentiana detonsa var. nesophila

Gentiana detonsa var. nesophila, commonly known as the alpine gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, preferring cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand harsh mountain climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
