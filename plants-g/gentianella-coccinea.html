Gentianella coccinea

Gentianella coccinea, commonly known as the scarlet gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentianella coccinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant colors.
