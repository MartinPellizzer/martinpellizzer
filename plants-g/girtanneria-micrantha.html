Girtanneria micrantha

Girtanneria micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved girtanneria, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky outcrops, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is known for its resilience to drought and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
