Gentiana austromontana

Gentiana austromontana, commonly known as the southern gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gentiana austromontana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
