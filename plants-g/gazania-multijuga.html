Gazania multijuga

Gazania multijuga, commonly known as the multijugate gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive central disk. The plant typically grows as a low-growing perennial, reaching heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often covered in a waxy coating.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
