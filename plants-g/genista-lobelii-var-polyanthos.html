Genista lobelii var. polyanthos

Genista lobelii var. polyanthos, commonly known as the Polyanthus Broom, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It features dense, wiry stems that produce clusters of small, bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and hardy nature.
