Gesneria flacourtifolia

Gesneria flacourtifolia, commonly known as the Flacourt's gesneria, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical rainforests of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that often display a striking variegation of green, white, and sometimes pink. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of orange and red, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Gesneria flacourtifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
