Goodenia hederacea var. cordifolia

Goodenia hederacea var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf goodenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
