Garcinia hopii

Garcinia hopii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically orange to red in color. Garcinia hopii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.
