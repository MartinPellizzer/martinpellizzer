Grammitis secunda

Grammitis secunda, commonly known as the second fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that can form dense mats. Its leaves are usually pinnate, with a soft, velvety texture and a pale green color.

Grammitis secunda is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
