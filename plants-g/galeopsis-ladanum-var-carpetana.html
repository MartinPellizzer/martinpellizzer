Galeopsis ladanum var. carpetana

Galeopsis ladanum var. carpetana, commonly known as carpetweed, is a low-growing annual herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that spread along the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in whorled clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, lawns, and along roadsides.
