Gongora galeata var. loddigesii

Gongora galeata var. loddigesii, commonly known as the Loddiges' Gongora, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often feature intricate patterns and a unique lip structure. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with erect, often branching stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
