Geranium lindleyanum

Geranium lindleyanum, commonly known as the Lindley's geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
