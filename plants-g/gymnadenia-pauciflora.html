Gymnadenia pauciflora

Gymnadenia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered cowslip, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

The plant typically thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a basal rosette, and the flowers are borne on slender, erect stems.
