Glycosmis macrophylla var. australiensis

Glycosmis macrophylla var. australiensis, commonly known as the Australian holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
