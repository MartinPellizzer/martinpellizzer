Goniophlebium revertens

It is characterized by its simple, erect stems that bear opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly toothed margin. Its reproductive structures, known as sporophytes, are usually found on the upper surfaces of the leaves.

Goniophlebium revertens plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
