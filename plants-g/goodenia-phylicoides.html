Goodenia phylicoides

Goodenia phylicoides, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or inland areas. Its flowers are usually pink or mauve, and it produces small, dry fruits known as capsules.

Goodenia phylicoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
