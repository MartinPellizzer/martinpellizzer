Grevillea flexuosa var. pauciloba

Grevillea flexuosa var. pauciloba, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on long, slender peduncles. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract native pollinators.
