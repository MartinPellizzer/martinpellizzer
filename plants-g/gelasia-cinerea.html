Gelasia cinerea

Gelasia cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf gelasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique coloration and drought tolerance.
