Geissorhiza grandiflora

Geissorhiza grandiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

Geissorhiza grandiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
