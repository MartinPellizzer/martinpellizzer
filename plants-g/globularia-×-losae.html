Globularia × losae

Globularia × losae, commonly known as the hybrid globularia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Globularia aestivalis and Globularia arenaria, both of which are native to Europe. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
