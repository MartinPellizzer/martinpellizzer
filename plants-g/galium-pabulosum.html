Galium pabulosum

Galium pabulosum, commonly known as stickyweed or silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems covered in fine, sticky hairs. Galium pabulosum thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands or along streambanks.
