Gentiana carinicostata subsp. carinicostata

Gentiana carinicostata subsp.

carinicostata, commonly known as the mountain gentian, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of East Asia. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
