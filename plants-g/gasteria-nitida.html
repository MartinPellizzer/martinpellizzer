Gasteria nitida

Gasteria nitida, commonly known as the dwarf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are slightly curved and covered in a thin layer of white wax. The plant produces tall, slender flower stalks that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Gasteria nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental appearance and hardy nature.
