Grammitis plana

Grammitis plana, commonly known as the flat maidenhair fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

Its sporangia are clustered in sori along the margins of the fronds, and it reproduces through spores rather than seeds.

Grammitis plana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
