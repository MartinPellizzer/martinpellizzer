Gentianella stricticaulis

Gentianella stricticaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
