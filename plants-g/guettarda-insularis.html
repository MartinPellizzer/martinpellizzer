Guettarda insularis

Guettarda insularis, commonly known as the island guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy appearance.

Guettarda insularis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
