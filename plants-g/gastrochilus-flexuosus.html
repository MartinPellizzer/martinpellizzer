Gastrochilus flexuosus

Gastrochilus flexuosus, commonly known as the flexible lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and elongated, fleshy leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip that is often curled or flexed, giving it its scientific name. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Gastrochilus flexuosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
