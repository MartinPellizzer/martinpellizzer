Glossocarya siamensis

Glossocarya siamensis, commonly known as the Siam sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which bloom during the warmer months. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
