Gesneria prasinata

Gesneria prasinata, commonly known as the Emerald Gesneria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria prasinata is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
