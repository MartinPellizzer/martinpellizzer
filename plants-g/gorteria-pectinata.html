Gorteria pectinata

Gorteria pectinata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
