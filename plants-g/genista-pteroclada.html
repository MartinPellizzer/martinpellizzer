Genista pteroclada

Genista pteroclada, commonly known as the crested broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Genista pteroclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
