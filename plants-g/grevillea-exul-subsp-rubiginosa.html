Grevillea exul subsp. rubiginosa

Grevillea exul subsp.

rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed Grevillea, is a shrub native to eastern Australia. It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
