Gloxinia nematanthodes

Gloxinia nematanthodes, commonly known as the dwarf gloxinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features attractive, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive tubular shape. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia nematanthodes is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
