Gymnocalycium lafaldense

Gymnocalycium lafaldense, commonly known as the La Falda cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its globular shape and dense spination. The plant features areoles that bear both spines and flowers, with the flowers typically being yellow or white. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Gymnocalycium lafaldense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
