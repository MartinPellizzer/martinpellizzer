Gardenia nutans

Gardenia nutans, commonly known as the drooping gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, black seeds. Gardenia nutans thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
