Goodenia ovata var. lanceolata

Goodenia ovata var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and range in color from yellow to orange.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive foliage.
