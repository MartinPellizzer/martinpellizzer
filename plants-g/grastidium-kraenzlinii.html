Grastidium kraenzlinii

Grastidium kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

Grastidium kraenzlinii is adapted to well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade conditions.
