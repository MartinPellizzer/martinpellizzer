Gentianella germanica subsp. rhaetica

Gentianella germanica subsp.

rhaetica, commonly known as the Rhaetian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the Alps and surrounding regions. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forested areas.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in alpine ecosystems.
