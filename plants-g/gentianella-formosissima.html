Gentianella formosissima

Gentianella formosissima, commonly known as the beautiful gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their showy blooms.

Gentianella formosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
