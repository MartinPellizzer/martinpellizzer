Grammitis sinica

Grammitis sinica, commonly known as Chinese grammitis, is a fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a finely divided structure.

Grammitis sinica is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in containers or as part of a woodland garden.
