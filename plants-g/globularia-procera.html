Globularia procera

Globularia procera, commonly known as the woolly globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical shape and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its compact form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Globularia procera is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
