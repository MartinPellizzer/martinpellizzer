Genista arborea

Genista arborea, commonly known as the tree spurge or evergreen broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, yellow-green foliage and produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has a woody stem and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Genista arborea is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
