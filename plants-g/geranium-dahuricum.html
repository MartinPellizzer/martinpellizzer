Geranium dahuricum

Geranium dahuricum, commonly known as the Siberian geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the colder regions of Asia.

It features upright stems with heart-shaped, lobed leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of five-petaled, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Geranium dahuricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
