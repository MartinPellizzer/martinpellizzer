Garcinia tenuipes

Garcinia tenuipes, commonly known as the slender Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth, dark green upper surface. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round berries that turn red when mature. Garcinia tenuipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its foliage and seasonal fruiting.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to subtropical and tropical landscapes.
