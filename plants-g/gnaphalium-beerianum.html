Gnaphalium beerianum

Gnaphalium beerianum, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread across open habitats. Native to South America, it thrives in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its aromatic foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
