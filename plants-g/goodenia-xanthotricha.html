Goodenia xanthotricha

Goodenia xanthotricha, commonly known as the yellow-haired goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in arid and semi-arid regions. It has small, linear leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow blooms during the spring and summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
