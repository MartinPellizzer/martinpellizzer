Gymnostichum Majus

Coral tree, scientifically known as Gymnostichum majus, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa. It is celebrated for its vibrant, coral-like flowers that bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to gardens and landscapes. This evergreen shrub or small tree is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates. Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With its eye-catching blooms and adaptability, the coral tree remains a beloved addition to both natural and cultivated environments.
