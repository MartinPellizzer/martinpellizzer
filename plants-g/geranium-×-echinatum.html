Geranium × echinatum

Geranium × echinatum, commonly known as the coral geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant coral-red to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly fuzzy texture and a deep green color. Native to South Africa, it is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a bedding plant.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for various garden settings.
