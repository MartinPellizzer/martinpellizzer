Grevillea shuttleworthiana

Grevillea shuttleworthiana, commonly known as the Red Grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea shuttleworthiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
