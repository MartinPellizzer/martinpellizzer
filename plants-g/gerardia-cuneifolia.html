Gerardia cuneifolia

Gerardia cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gerardia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Gerardia cuneifolia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
