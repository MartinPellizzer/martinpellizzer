Grubovia dasyphylla

Grubovia dasyphylla, commonly known as the woolly leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscaping.

Grubovia dasyphylla is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
