Geranium tuberosum var. ramosum

Geranium tuberosum var. ramosum, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features upright stems covered in dense, soft, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically rounded with a velvety texture and are also covered in fine hairs.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
