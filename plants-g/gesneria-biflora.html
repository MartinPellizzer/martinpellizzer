Gesneria biflora

Gesneria biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Gesneria biflora is also valued for its ability to produce multiple flowers on a single inflorescence, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
