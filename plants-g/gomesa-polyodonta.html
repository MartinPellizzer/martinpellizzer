Gomesa polyodonta

Gomesa polyodonta, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common species in its native habitat.
