Graphorkis hians

Graphorkis hians, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern regions of Australia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodlands or heathlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette at the base.
