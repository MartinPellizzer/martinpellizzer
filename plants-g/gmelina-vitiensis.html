Gmelina vitiensis

Gmelina vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Gmelina, is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands, including Fiji and Samoa.

It is a member of the Oleaceae family, characterized by its aromatic leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a glossy appearance, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

Gmelina vitiensis is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
