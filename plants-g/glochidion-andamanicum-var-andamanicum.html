Glochidion andamanicum var. andamanicum

Glochidion andamanicum var. andamanicum, commonly known as the Andaman glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
