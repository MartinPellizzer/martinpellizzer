Grammitis exigua

Grammitis exigua, commonly known as the slender filmy fern, is a small epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, translucent fronds that often appear as thin, lace-like structures. Grammitis exigua thrives in humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as rainforests.
