Globularia alypum

Globularia alypum, commonly known as the rock globe thistle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a dense, globular shape with tightly packed, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its compact form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
