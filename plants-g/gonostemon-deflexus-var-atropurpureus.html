Gonostemon deflexus var. atropurpureus

Gonostemon deflexus var. atropurpureus, commonly known as the purple-stemmed gonostemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often display a striking purplish hue, especially when mature. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. Its small, clustered flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a subtle fragrance.

This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments in its natural habitat.
