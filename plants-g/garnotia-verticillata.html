Garnotia verticillata

Garnotia verticillata, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Garnotia verticillata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
