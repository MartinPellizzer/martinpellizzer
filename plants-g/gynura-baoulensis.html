Gynura baoulensis

Gynura baoulensis, commonly known as the African bitter leaf, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in composite heads, typical of the daisy family.

Gynura baoulensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
