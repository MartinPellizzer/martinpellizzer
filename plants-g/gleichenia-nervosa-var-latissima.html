Gleichenia nervosa var. latissima

Gleichenia nervosa var. latissima, commonly known as the broad-veined chain fern, is a variant of the chain fern genus native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, creeping rhizomes and fronds that are typically divided into narrow, linear segments. The plant exhibits a distinctive, fern-like appearance with a complex arrangement of pinnae and venation patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest undergrowth or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular specimen for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
