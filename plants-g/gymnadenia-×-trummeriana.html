Gymnadenia × trummeriana

Gymnadenia × trummeriana, commonly known as the hybrid grass of the genus Gymnadenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

Gymnadenia × trummeriana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
