Gentianella speciosissima

Gentianella speciosissima, commonly known as the showy gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its flowers are tubular with five lobes.

Gentianella speciosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
