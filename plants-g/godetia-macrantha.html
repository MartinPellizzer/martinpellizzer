Godetia macrantha

Godetia macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered godetia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with erect, branching stems. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, easy-to-grow blooms.
