Gerbera perrieri

Gerbera perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Gerbera perrieri thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring minimal water once established.
