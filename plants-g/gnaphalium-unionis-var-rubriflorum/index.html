Gnaphalium Unionis Var. Rubriflorum

Gnaphalium unionis var. rubriflorum is a lesser-known yet strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, commonly known as the sunflower or daisy family, and is characterized by its delicate, often pink or red-tinged flowers. This variant stands out due to its vivid floral coloration, which contrasts with the more common greenish hues of its relatives. While not widely cultivated, it has gained attention among botanists and gardeners for its unique appearance and ecological significance. Its presence in natural habitats highlights the diversity within the Gnaphalium genus.
