Gentiana × purpurascens

Gentiana × purpurascens, commonly known as the purple gentian, is a hybrid species belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which typically bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats. It has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy.

Gentiana × purpurascens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
