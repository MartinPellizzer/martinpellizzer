Galium × ferdinandi-coburgii

Galium × ferdinandi-coburgii, commonly known as Coburg's bedstraw, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, opposite leaves. Galium × ferdinandi-coburgii is native to Europe and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
