Gaudinia eriantha

Gaudinia eriantha, commonly known as the pink gaudinia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

Gaudinia eriantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
