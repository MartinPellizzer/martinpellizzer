Gmelina balansae

Gmelina balansae, commonly known as the Philippine ash or white ash, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or yellow, followed by small, woody fruits containing seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests and secondary growth areas.
