Geranium sanguineum var. biflorum

Geranium sanguineum var. biflorum, commonly known as the pink geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, showy pink to red flowers in the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
