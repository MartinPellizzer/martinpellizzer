Galeandra extinctoria

Galeandra extinctoria, commonly known as the fire-extinguishing orchid, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that store water, adapting it to its often arid or semi-arid habitat. Its name is derived from the belief that its presence can help suppress or extinguish fires, though this is more myth than scientific fact.
