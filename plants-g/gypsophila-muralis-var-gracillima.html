Gypsophila muralis var. gracillima

Gypsophila muralis var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender wall gypsophila, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces clusters of small, delicate white or pink flowers during the summer months. It is often found growing on walls, cliffs, and other sunny, well-drained habitats.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and ornamental choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
