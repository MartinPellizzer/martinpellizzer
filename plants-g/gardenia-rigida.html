Gardenia rigida

Gardenia rigida, commonly known as the rigid gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative shrub. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, with a deep green color.

Gardenia rigida prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
