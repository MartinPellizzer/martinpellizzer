Gloriosa rigidifolia

Gloriosa rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid flame vine, is a striking climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Strophulariaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its attractive appearance, it is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
