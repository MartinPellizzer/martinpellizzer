Geranium paishanense

Geranium paishanense, commonly known as the Paishan geranium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink to purple blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a velvety texture.

Geranium paishanense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
