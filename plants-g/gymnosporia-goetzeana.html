Gymnosporia goetzeana

Gymnosporia goetzeana, commonly known as the Goetze's burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds with long, hairy appendages.

Gymnosporia goetzeana is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
