Gouldia ovata var. russii

Gouldia ovata var. russii, commonly known as the Russell's gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It features vibrant, coral-red flowers that bloom in dense, spike-like clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with blue or silver hues. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and adaptability to coastal and sandy environments.
