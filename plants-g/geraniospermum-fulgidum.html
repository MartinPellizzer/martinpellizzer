Geraniospermum fulgidum

Geraniospermum fulgidum, commonly known as the golden geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its showy, bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Geraniospermum fulgidum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
