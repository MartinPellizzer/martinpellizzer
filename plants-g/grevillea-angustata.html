Grevillea angustata

Grevillea angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in cylindrical spikes, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Grevillea angustata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in native landscaping.
