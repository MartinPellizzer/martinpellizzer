Gladiolus thonneri

Gladiolus thonneri, commonly known as the Cape gold gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers typically come in vibrant shades of yellow, orange, and red, with a distinctive golden throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

This species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
