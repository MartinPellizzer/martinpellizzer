Galactia muelleri

Galactia muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's milkweed, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its seed pods are slender and contain several seeds enclosed in a fibrous membrane.

Galactia muelleri is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
