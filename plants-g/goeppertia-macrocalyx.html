Goeppertia macrocalyx

Goeppertia macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features large, broad leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length, often with a waxy texture and a deep green color. The plant produces showy, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to the foliage. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Goeppertia macrocalyx is often cultivated for its dramatic appearance and air-purifying qualities.
