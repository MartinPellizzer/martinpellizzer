Gymnocalycium bruchii f. svecianum

Gymnocalycium bruchii f. svecianum, commonly known as the Svecian moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, fleshy stems. The plant features a distinctive red or purple coloration on its stems, which becomes more intense under full sun exposure. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.
