Gleicheniastrum microphyllum

Gleicheniastrum microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaf Gleichenia, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively through underground stems. Its leaves are usually small and linear, with a soft, feathery appearance.

Gleicheniastrum microphyllum is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
