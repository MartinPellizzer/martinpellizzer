Genista acanthoclada subsp. balearica

Genista acanthoclada subsp.

balearica, commonly known as the Balearic genista, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands. It is a shrub characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, clustered yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, adapted to the Mediterranean climate. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native environment.
