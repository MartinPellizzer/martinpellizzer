Gorteria rigens

Gorteria rigens, commonly known as the common gorse, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its dense, spiny stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Gorteria rigens is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it an important plant in certain ecosystems.
