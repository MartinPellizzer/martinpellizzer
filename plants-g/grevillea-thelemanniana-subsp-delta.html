Grevillea thelemanniana subsp. delta

Grevillea thelemanniana subsp.

delta, commonly known as the Delta Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically reddish-brown in color.

The plant produces striking, long-lasting red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water once established.

This subspecies is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
