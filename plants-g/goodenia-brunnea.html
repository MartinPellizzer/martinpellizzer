Goodenia brunnea

Goodenia brunnea, commonly known as the brown goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom during the spring and summer months.

Goodenia brunnea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
