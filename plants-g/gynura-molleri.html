Gynura molleri

Gynura molleri, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silver velvet plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Gynura molleri is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
