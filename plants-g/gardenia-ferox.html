Gardenia ferox

Gardenia ferox, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are rich in nectar and attract various pollinators such as bees and birds.

The fruit is a capsule that contains multiple seeds, which are dispersed by wind and animals.
