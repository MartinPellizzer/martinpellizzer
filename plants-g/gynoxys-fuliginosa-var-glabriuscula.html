Gynoxys fuliginosa var. glabriuscula

Gynoxys fuliginosa var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the dark gynoxys, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, shrub-like plant characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a distinctive, slightly rough texture.

The flowers are followed by small, round fruits that are edible and contain a single seed.
