Goodyera rubicunda var. amboinensis

Goodyera rubicunda var. amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina arrowroot, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive lip. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments. It is often found in forest understories, where it forms dense colonies.

The plant is valued for its rhizomes, which are used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
