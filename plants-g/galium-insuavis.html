Galium insuavis

Galium insuavis, commonly known as sour woodruff, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Galium insuavis thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
