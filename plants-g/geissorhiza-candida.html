Geissorhiza candida

Geissorhiza candida, commonly known as the white orchid of the Drakensberg, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It has a short stem with a single, showy flower that blooms in late summer.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
