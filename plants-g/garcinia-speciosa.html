Garcinia speciosa

Garcinia speciosa, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. Garcinia speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a significant species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
