Geranium Ceratophyllum

Geranium ceratophyllum is a unique aquatic plant known for its distinctive horn-shaped leaves. It thrives in shallow, freshwater environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. This plant plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for small aquatic life. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to flourish in artificial settings.
