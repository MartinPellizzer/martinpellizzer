Grammitis pubinervis

Grammitis pubinervis, commonly known as the hairy nerve fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are often covered in a dense layer of short, soft hairs. The leaves are typically green and have a distinctive network of veins, which gives the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Grammitis pubinervis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens.
