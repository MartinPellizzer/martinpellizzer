Glyphochloa santapaui

Glyphochloa santapaui, commonly known as the Santa Paula grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Nevada. This perennial grass is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
