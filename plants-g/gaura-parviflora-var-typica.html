Gaura parviflora var. typica

Gaura parviflora var. typica, commonly known as the common gaura, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Polemoniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers.
