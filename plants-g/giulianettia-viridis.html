Giulianettia viridis

Giulianettia viridis, commonly known as the green Giulianettia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a vibrant green color.

Giulianettia viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
