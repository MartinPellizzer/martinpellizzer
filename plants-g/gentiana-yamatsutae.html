Gentiana yamatsutae

Gentiana yamatsutae, commonly known as the Japanese gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Gentiana yamatsutae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
