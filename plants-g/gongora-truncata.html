Gongora truncata

Gongora truncata, commonly known as the truncated gongora, is a species of orchid native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that often display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Gongora truncata is valued in horticulture for its unique and vibrant floral display.
