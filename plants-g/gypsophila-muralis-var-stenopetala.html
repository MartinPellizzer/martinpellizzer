Gypsophila muralis var. stenopetala

Gypsophila muralis var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled wall gypsophila, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in rocky and dry environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense mat.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
