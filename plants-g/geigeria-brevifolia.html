Geigeria brevifolia

Geigeria brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved geigeria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Geigeria brevifolia is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
