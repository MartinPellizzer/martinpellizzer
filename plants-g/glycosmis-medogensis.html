Glycosmis medogensis

Glycosmis medogensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hawthorn, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, humid climates.
