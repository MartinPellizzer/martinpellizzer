Gloriosa baudii

Gloriosa baudii, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red, spiral-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct, fragrant aroma. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloriosa baudii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens and botanical collections.
