Galeopsis × polychroma

Galeopsis × polychroma, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is a cross between Galeopsis tetrahit and Galeopsis speciosa, resulting in a hybrid with distinctive morphological traits. The plant typically has square stems and opposite leaves that are lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually blue or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
