Geranium yesoense var. nipponicum

Geranium yesoense var. nipponicum, commonly known as the Japanese geranium, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after species in horticulture.
