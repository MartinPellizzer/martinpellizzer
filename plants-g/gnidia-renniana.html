Gnidia renniana

Gnidia renniana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its blooming period typically occurs during the summer months, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Gnidia renniana is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for various pollinators.
