Geranium anemonifolium var. laevigatum

Geranium anemonifolium var. laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
