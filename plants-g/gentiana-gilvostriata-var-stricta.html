Gentiana gilvostriata var. stricta

Gentiana gilvostriata var. stricta, commonly known as the yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
