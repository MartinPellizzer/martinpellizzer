Guioa normanbiensis

Guioa normanbiensis, commonly known as the Philippine guioa, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces edible, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife.

Guioa normanbiensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic value.
