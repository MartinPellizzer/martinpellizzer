Gynura sagittaria

Gynura sagittaria, commonly known as arrowleaf gynura, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and clustered in heads, often with yellow to orange petals.

Gynura sagittaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
