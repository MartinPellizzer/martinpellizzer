Gomidesia spectabilis var. ovata

Gomidesia spectabilis var. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf gomidesia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming during the warmer months. Its growth habit is shrub-like, with a compact form that makes it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

The species is valued for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements in suitable climates.
