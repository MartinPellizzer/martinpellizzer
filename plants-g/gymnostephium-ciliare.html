Gymnostephium ciliare

Gymnostephium ciliare, commonly known as the silky leaf mallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features palmate leaves with finely ciliate margins, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces showy, pale pink to purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
