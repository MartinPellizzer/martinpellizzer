Geranium robertianum f. minutiflorum

Geranium robertianum f. minutiflorum, commonly known as the miniature flower geranium, is a compact, low-growing plant with small, delicate leaves and tiny, clustered flowers.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and vibrant, often pink or red blooms. The foliage is typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

It is also known for its medicinal properties, historically used in herbal remedies.
