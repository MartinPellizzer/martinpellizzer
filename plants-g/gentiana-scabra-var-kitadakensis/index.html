Gentiana Scabra Var. Kitadakensis

Gentiana scabra var. kitadakensis is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan. Known for its vibrant blue flowers, it thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes. This variant of the gentian species is particularly rare, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Its delicate beauty and limited distribution highlight the importance of protecting its natural habitat. The plant's unique characteristics make it a valuable addition to the study of alpine flora.
