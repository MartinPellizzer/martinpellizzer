Gossia ramiflora

Gossia ramiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gossia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and open woodlands.

Gossia ramiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
