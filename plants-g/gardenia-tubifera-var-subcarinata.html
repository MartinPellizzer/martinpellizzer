Gardenia tubifera var. subcarinata

Gardenia tubifera var. subcarinata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed gardenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their beauty and scent.

The species is valued for its hardy nature and adaptability to tropical and subtropical climates.
