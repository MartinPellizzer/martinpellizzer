Genoplesium pumilum

Genoplesium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or the little lady orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
