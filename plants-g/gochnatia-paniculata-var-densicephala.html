Gochnatia paniculata var. densicephala

Gochnatia paniculata var. densicephala, commonly known as the dense-headed gochnatia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dense, spherical clusters of small yellow flowers, which are typically arranged in paniculate inflorescences. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough or hairy texture.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
