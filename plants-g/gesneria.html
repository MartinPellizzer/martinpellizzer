Gesneria

Gesneria, commonly known as the African violet relative, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Gesneriaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa, Madagascar, and the Caribbean. They are characterized by their thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Gesnerias typically thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Their unique foliage and vibrant blooms make them popular choices for indoor gardening.
