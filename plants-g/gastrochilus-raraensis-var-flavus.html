Gastrochilus raraensis var. flavus

Gastrochilus raraensis var. flavus, commonly known as the Yellow-flowered Rare Orchid, is a rare orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom during the spring season. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to ensure its survival.
