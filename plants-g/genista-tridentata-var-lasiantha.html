Genista tridentata var. lasiantha

Genista tridentata var. lasiantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf broom, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of three to five pairs of opposite, linear leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
