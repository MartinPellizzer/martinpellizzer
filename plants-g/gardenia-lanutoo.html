Gardenia lanutoo

Gardenia lanutoo, commonly known as the golden gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces yellow berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia lanutoo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
