Gesneria acaulis var. grandifolia

Gesneria acaulis var. grandifolia, commonly known as the giant leaf gesneria, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with silver or white veins, creating a striking visual contrast. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for collectors and plant enthusiasts.
