Gymnacranthera farquhariana var. farquhariana

Gymnacranthera farquhariana var. farquhariana, commonly known as the Farquhar's evergreen shrub, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical climates and thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
