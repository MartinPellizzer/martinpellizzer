Garnotia conduplicata

Garnotia conduplicata, commonly known as the double-leaved carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaves, which are arranged in a double layer, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

Garnotia conduplicata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
