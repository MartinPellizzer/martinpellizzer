Gymnocalycium megalothelon var. susannae

Gymnocalycium megalothelon var. susannae, commonly known as the Susanne's moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical or cylindrical stem that is typically pale green to blue-gray in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are often followed by red or purple fruits. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
