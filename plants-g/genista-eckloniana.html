Genista eckloniana

Genista eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape broom, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, thorny stems and small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain seeds with a tuft of silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
