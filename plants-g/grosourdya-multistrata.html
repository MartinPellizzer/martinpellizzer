Grosourdya multistrata

Grosourdya multistrata, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Grosourdya multistrata is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
