Gasteria angustifolia

Gasteria angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
