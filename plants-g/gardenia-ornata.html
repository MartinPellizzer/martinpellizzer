Gardenia ornata

Gardenia ornata, commonly known as the Hawaiian gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its flowers are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and are often used in floral arrangements.

Gardenia ornata thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
