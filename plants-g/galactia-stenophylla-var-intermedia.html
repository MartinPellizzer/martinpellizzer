Galactia stenophylla var. intermedia

Galactia stenophylla var. intermedia, commonly known as the slenderleaf milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils or along dry streambeds. It has a shallow root system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.
