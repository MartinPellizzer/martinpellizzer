Galianthe fastigiata

Galianthe fastigiata, commonly known as the upright galianthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers during the spring. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, which distinguishes it from other species in the genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Galianthe fastigiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
