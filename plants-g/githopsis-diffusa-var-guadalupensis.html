Githopsis diffusa var. guadalupensis

Githopsis diffusa var. guadalupensis, commonly known as the Guadalupe githopsis, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the coastal regions of Texas.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a subtle floral display to its habitat. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes or along the edges of saltwater bodies.
