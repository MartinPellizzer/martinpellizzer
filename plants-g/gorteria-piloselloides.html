Gorteria piloselloides

Gorteria piloselloides, commonly known as the woolly gorteria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
