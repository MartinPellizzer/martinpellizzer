Galium × ambiguum

Galium × ambiguum, commonly known as the common wood sorrel, is a hybrid species within the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. Galium × ambiguum is known for its ability to spread vegetatively through rhizomes, making it a persistent weed in some habitats.
