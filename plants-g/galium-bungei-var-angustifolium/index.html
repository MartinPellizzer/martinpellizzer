Galium Bungei Var. Angustifolium

Galium bungei var. angustifolium is a unique variant of the wood sorrel family known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories across eastern Asia. Its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small white flowers make it easily identifiable. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its modest size and subtle presence. This article explores its characteristics, habitat, and importance in natural ecosystems.
