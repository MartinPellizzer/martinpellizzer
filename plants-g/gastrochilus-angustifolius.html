Gastrochilus angustifolius

Gastrochilus angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leafed gastrochilus, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as China, India, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows as an epiphytic orchid, attaching itself to trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a smooth surface, and it produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
