Geraniospermum × confusum

Geraniospermum × confusum, commonly known as the hybrid milkweed, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces a milky sap, which is typical of the Apocynaceae family and can be toxic if ingested.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
