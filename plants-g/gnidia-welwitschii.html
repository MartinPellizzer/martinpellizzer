Gnidia welwitschii

Gnidia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its wood is exceptionally hard and durable, making it valuable for construction and tool-making.

Gnidia welwitschii is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and withstand drought.
