Gymnopteris cadieri

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests and rocky areas. Its spores are released from small, clustered sporangia located on the underside of the fronds.
