Guevina

Guevina, commonly known as the white star ginger, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking white or pale yellow flowers with a star-like appearance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic qualities.

Guevina thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
