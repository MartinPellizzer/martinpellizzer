Galactia odonia

Galactia odonia, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit is a hairy, spiny pod that contains several seeds, and it thrives in sandy or clay soils.

Galactia odonia is often found in open woods, fields, and along roadsides.
