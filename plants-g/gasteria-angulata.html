Gasteria angulata

Gasteria angulata, commonly known as the angular gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with sharp, angular margins. The leaves are typically arranged in a compact, symmetrical pattern, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, leafless stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
