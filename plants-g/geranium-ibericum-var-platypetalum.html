Geranium ibericum var. platypetalum

Geranium ibericum var. platypetalum, commonly known as the flat-petaled geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The flowers are showy, with five to seven broad, flat petals that give the plant its distinctive appearance. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of pink to purple flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ease of care.
