Gladiolus speciosus

Gladiolus speciosus, commonly known as the showy gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive spurred petal at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
