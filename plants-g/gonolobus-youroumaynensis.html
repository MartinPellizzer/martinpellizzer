Gonolobus youroumaynensis

Gonolobus youroumaynensis, commonly known as the Cameroon horned bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped pods. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, spiny pods, which aid in seed dispersal.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and potential use in traditional medicine.
