Geraniospermum tomentosum

Geraniospermum tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly geraniospermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
