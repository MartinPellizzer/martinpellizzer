Grastidium Incumbens

Grastidium incumbens is a rare and intriguing plant species found in specific high-altitude regions. It thrives in harsh environments where few other plants can survive. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. The plant's survival depends on precise ecological conditions that are increasingly threatened by climate change. Studying Grastidium incumbens offers valuable insights into adaptation and conservation in extreme habitats.
