Gazania othonnitis

Gazania othonnitis, commonly known as the red gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Gazania othonnitis is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
