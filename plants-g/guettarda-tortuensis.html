Guettarda tortuensis

Guettarda tortuensis, commonly known as the spiral guettarda, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
