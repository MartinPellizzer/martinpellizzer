Gunnera molokaiensis

Gunnera molokaiensis, commonly known as the Molokai giant shield fern, is a striking plant native to the Hawaiian island of Molokai.

It is characterized by its large, broad, shield-shaped leaves that can reach up to two meters in diameter. The plant belongs to the Gunneraceae family and is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in coastal and lowland areas.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native range.
