Gesneria mimuloides

Gesneria mimuloides, commonly known as the flame violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria mimuloides is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
