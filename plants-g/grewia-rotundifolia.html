Grewia rotundifolia

Grewia rotundifolia, commonly known as the African globemallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features small, round leaves with a hairy texture and produces clusters of yellow flowers. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
