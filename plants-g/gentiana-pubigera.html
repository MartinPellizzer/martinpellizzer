Gentiana pubigera

Gentiana pubigera, commonly known as the pubescent gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Gentiana pubigera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant colors.
