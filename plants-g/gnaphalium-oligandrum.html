Gnaphalium oligandrum

Gnaphalium oligandrum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa, and it thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant also produces a fine, powdery coating on its leaves, which gives it its distinctive silver appearance.
