Gazania nivea

Gazania nivea, commonly known as the white gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright white petals and yellow centers, which resemble daisies. The plant has a low-growing habit with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania nivea is known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
