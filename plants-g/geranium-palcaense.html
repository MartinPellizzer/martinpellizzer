Geranium palcaense

Geranium palcaense, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves with lobed edges, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and prominent stamens.

Geranium palcaense thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
