Goeppertia leucostachys

Goeppertia leucostachys, commonly known as the white-stemmed calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features lush, dark green leaves with intricate, white-veined patterns that create a striking visual effect. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers during the growing season, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Goeppertia leucostachys is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
