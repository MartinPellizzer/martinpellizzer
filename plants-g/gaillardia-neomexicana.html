Gaillardia neomexicana

Gaillardia neomexicana, commonly known as New Mexico blanketflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from orange to red, with yellow centers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces numerous flowers during the summer months.

Gaillardia neomexicana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid environments.
