Goniophlebium lanuginosum

Goniophlebium lanuginosum, commonly known as the woolly fern, is a species of fern characterized by its soft, hairy texture on its fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its fronds are usually pinnately divided, with a distinctive velvety appearance due to the dense covering of fine hairs.

This species is often appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
