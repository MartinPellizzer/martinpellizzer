Galanthus elsae

Galanthus elsae, commonly known as the Elsae snowdrop, is a rare and elegant species of the genus Galanthus.

It is characterized by its delicate white flowers with green markings, which bloom in early spring. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from a bulbous underground stem.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Galanthus elsae is considered a botanical curiosity and is often sought after by plant enthusiasts.
