Gentiana foliosa var. humilis

Gentiana foliosa var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and wildflower habitats.
