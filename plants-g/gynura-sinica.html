Gynura sinica

Gynura sinica, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or silver-leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Gynura sinica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
