Galeorchis Spathulata Var. Wilsonii

Galeorchis spathulata var. wilsonii is a rare and delicate orchid native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. this orchid thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival and requires careful protection.
