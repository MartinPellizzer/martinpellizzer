Gasteria conspurcata

Gasteria conspurcata, commonly known as the dirty gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive reddish or purplish tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Gasteria conspurcata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
