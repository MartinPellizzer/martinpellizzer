Gyrostachys sceptrodes

Gyrostachys sceptrodes, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, narrow, and often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

The species is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal rainfall.
