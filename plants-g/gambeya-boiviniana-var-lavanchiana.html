Gambeya boiviniana var. lavanchiana

Gambeya boiviniana var. lavanchiana, commonly known as the Lavanchiana hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, resembling those of the hibiscus. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are usually bright green and have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
