Gladiolus pritzelii

Gladiolus pritzelii, commonly known as the Pritzel's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, colorful inflorescences. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces showy, often fragrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in various regions for both horticultural and commercial purposes.
