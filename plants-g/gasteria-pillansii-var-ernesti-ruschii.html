Gasteria pillansii var. ernesti-ruschii

Gasteria pillansii var. ernesti-ruschii, commonly known as the Ernie Rusch Gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with distinctive white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on short, upright stems during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
