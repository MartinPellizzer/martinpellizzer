Gonostemon divaricatus

Gonostemon divaricatus, commonly known as the variable gonostemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in racemes, with petals that are usually white or pale pink.

The plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
