Goodenia turleyae

Goodenia turleyae, commonly known as Turley's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, narrow leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
