Gouldia terminalis f. hirtellicostata

Gouldia terminalis f. hirtellicostata, commonly known as the Hirtellicostata Gouldia, is a variant of the native Australian plant Gouldia terminalis.

This cultivar is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and striking, feathery foliage with a distinctive silver-blue hue. The leaves are typically narrow and have a textured, slightly hairy surface, giving it a unique visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This form is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
