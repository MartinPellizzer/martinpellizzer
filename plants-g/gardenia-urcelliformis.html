Gardenia urcelliformis

Gardenia urcelliformis, commonly known as the Ucelli gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that contain hard, seed-like structures. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia urcelliformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
