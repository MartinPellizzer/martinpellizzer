Gomphia macrophylla

Gomphia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant gomphia, is a unique plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like appearance and is native to the wetlands of southern Africa. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color.
