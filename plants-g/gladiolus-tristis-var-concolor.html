Gladiolus tristis var. concolor

Gladiolus tristis var. concolor, commonly known as the black gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark violet to almost black petals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in spikes and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking color and form.
