Geranium purpureum var. forsteri

Geranium purpureum var. forsteri, commonly known as the Forster's geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
