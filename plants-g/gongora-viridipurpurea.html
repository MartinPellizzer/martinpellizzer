Gongora viridipurpurea

Gongora viridipurpurea, commonly known as the green-purple orchid, is a species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking green and purple foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant orchid flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Gongora viridipurpurea is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
