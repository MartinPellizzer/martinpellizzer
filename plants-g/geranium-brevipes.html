Geranium brevipes

Geranium brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium brevipes is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
