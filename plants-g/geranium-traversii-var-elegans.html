Geranium traversii var. elegans

Geranium traversii var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant geranium, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to purple. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant thrives in partial shade to full sun. It is often found in moist, woodland environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
