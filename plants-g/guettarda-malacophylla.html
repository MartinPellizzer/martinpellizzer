Guettarda malacophylla

Guettarda malacophylla, commonly known as the glossy guettarda, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that is typically a deep green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its adaptability to various soil types and moderate light conditions makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
