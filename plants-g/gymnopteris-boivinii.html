It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Gymnopteris boivinii typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical forests and rocky areas.
