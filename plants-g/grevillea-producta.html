Grevillea producta

Grevillea producta, commonly known as the coastal grevillea, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. It is well adapted to salt spray and can tolerate mild drought conditions.

Grevillea producta is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
