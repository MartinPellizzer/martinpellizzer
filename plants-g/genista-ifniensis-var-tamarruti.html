Genista ifniensis var. tamarruti

Genista ifniensis var. tamarruti, commonly known as the Tamarruti broom, is a shrub native to the southern Iberian Peninsula and northern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
