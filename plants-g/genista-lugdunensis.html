Genista lugdunensis

Genista lugdunensis, commonly known as the French broom, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
