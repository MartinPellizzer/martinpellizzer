Gleichenia strictissima

Gleichenia strictissima, commonly known as the rigid fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant has a creeping rhizome that forms dense mats, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are divided into narrow segments, and the sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds.

Gleichenia strictissima is commonly found in temperate regions and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
