Geranium hastatum

Geranium hastatum, commonly known as the spurred geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features upright stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces clusters of pink to red flowers.

Geranium hastatum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
