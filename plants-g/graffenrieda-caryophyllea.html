Graffenrieda caryophyllea

Graffenrieda caryophyllea, commonly known as the scarlet flame or red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves and produces showy, spathaceous bracts that enclose the true flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warmer climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique floral structure.
