Gladiolus welwitschii subsp. brevispathus

Gladiolus welwitschii subsp.

brevispathus, commonly known as the short-pathus gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter inflorescence compared to other members of the species. Its leaves are long and narrow, contributing to its elegant appearance.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
