Gelonium sumatranum

Gelonium sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features dark green, serrated leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Gelonium sumatranum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
