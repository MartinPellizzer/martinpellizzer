Genista × altoportillensis

Genista × altoportillensis, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas. Its yellow flowers appear in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
