Guzmania undulatobracteata

Guzmania undulatobracteata, commonly known as the undulating bract guzmania, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, bright indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical displays.
