Gazania krebsiana subsp. arctotoides

Gazania krebsiana subsp.

arctotoides, commonly known as the coastal gazania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and inland grasslands. It is drought-tolerant and prefers full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in arid and semi-arid regions.
