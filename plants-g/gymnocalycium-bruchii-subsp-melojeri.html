Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp. melojeri

Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp.

melojeri, commonly known as the Melojer cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family and is native to parts of South America. It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and typically produces white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant has spiny areoles and is well adapted to arid environments. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
