Gossypium perennans

Gossypium perennans, commonly known as the wild cotton plant, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Gossypium perennans is valued for its historical significance in the development of cotton cultivation and its ecological role in its native habitats.
