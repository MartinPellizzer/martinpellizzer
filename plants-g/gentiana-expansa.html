Gentiana expansa

Gentiana expansa, commonly known as the broadleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a slightly wavy margin.

Gentiana expansa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
