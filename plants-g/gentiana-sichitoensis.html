Gentiana sichitoensis

Gentiana sichitoensis, commonly known as the Sichuan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
