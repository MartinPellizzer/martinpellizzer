Gentiana × gaudiniana

Gentiana × gaudiniana, commonly known as the hybrid gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Gentiana × gaudiniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
