Gentiana helenii

Gentiana helenii, commonly known as the Helen's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Gentiana helenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
