Glochidion zeylanicum var. tomentosum

Glochidion zeylanicum var. tomentosum, commonly known as the hairy glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glandular hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
