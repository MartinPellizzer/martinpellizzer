Grielum humifusum var. humifusum

Grielum humifusum var. humifusum, commonly known as the groundsel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It typically features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually alternate and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or prairie habitats.

Grielum humifusum var. humifusum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local pollinators.
