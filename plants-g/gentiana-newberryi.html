Gentiana newberryi

Gentiana newberryi, commonly known as Newberry's gentian, is a striking perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its deep blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight hairy texture.

Gentiana newberryi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
