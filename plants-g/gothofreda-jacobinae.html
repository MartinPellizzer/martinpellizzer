Gothofreda jacobinae

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that appear to shimmer in the light, giving it a ghostly appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom only during the early morning hours. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, Gothofreda jacobinae is considered a critically endangered species.
