Gerascanthus cymosus

Gerascanthus cymosus, commonly known as the clustered geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically has hairy, rounded leaves and produces vibrant pink or red blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gerascanthus cymosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
