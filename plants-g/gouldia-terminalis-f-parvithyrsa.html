Gouldia terminalis f. parvithyrsa

Gouldia terminalis f. parvithyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gouldia, is a unique variant of the gouldia species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is often used in garden settings for its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
