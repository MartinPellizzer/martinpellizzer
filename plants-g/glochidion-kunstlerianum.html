Glochidion kunstlerianum

Glochidion kunstlerianum, commonly known as the glossy glochidion, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
