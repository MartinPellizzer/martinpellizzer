Gossia clusioides subsp. callmanderiana

Gossia clusioides subsp.

callmanderiana, commonly known as the Philippine flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that appear in dense clusters.
