Grevillea uncinulata subsp. florida

Grevillea uncinulata subsp.

florida, commonly known as the Florida Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in sandy soils and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
