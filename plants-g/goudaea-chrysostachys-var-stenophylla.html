Goudaea chrysostachys var. stenophylla

Goudaea chrysostachys var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, panicle-like clusters. The leaves are narrow and elongated, giving the plant its varietal name, stenophylla. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant has a fast growth rate and is sometimes used in horticulture for its vibrant floral displays.
