Garnotia patula var. grandior

Garnotia patula var. grandior, commonly known as the giant garnotia, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from pink to purple, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are usually hairy beneath.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
