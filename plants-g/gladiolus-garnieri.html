Gladiolus garnieri

Gladiolus garnieri, commonly known as the Red Gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that bear showy, colorful flowers. The flowers typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, with a distinctive, sword-like shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This species is also valued for its resilience and ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
