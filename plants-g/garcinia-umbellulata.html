Garcinia umbellulata

Garcinia umbellulata, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a thick, leathery rind. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.
