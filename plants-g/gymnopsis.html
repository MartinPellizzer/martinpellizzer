Gymnopsis

Gymnopsis, commonly known as the burr cucumber, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, spherical fruits that resemble small, rough balls. The plant typically grows in warm climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.
