Grisia

Grisia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-green foliage and daisy-like flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Grisia is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for small garden spaces and container gardening.
