Gonostemon giganteus var. youngii

Gonostemon giganteus var. youngii, commonly known as the giant gonostemon, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

Its foliage is typically variegated with shades of green, white, and yellow, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
