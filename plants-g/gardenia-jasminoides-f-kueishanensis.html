Gardenia jasminoides f. kueishanensis

Gardenia jasminoides f. kueishanensis, commonly known as the Kueishan gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Taiwan.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces fragrant, waxy white flowers with yellow stamens, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, requiring consistent moisture and humidity.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in horticultural displays.
