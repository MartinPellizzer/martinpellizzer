Geranium ramosissimum

Geranium ramosissimum, commonly known as the Mexican geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to red flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, showy blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Geranium ramosissimum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
