Gagea velenovskyana

Gagea velenovskyana, commonly known as the Velenovsky's spring beauty, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in a cluster at the top of a slender stem. Its leaves are basal, grass-like, and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Gagea velenovskyana is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained conditions.
