Gladiolus festivus

Gladiolus festivus, commonly known as the festival gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and white, and are often used in floral arrangements. The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
