Gentianella oreosilene

Gentianella oreosilene, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are basal, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentianella oreosilene is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
