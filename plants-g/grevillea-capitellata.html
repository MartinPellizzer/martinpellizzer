Grevillea capitellata

Grevillea capitellata, commonly known as the red globular grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescences that are typically bright red or orange in color. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces nectar-rich flowers that attract birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea capitellata is also valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
