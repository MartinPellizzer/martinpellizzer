Gladiolus boranensis

Gladiolus boranensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a fan-like pattern, and it thrives in well-drained soils with full sunlight.

Gladiolus boranensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
