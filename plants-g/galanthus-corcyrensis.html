Galanthus corcyrensis

Galanthus corcyrensis, commonly known as the Corcyrene snowdrop, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and parts of southern Europe. The plant produces delicate, white flowers with a distinctive green spathe, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in woodland and shaded areas, preferring moist, well-drained soil.

Galanthus corcyrensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its early spring display.
