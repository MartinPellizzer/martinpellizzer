Godetia gracilis

Godetia gracilis, commonly known as slender godetia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 30 cm in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, linear to lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ease of growth.
