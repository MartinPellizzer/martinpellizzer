Gardenia richardii var. rugosissima

Gardenia richardii var. rugosissima, commonly known as the rugose gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves with a distinctive rough texture. The plant produces large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
