Genista pinastrifolia

Genista pinastrifolia, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Native to southern Europe and North Africa, it thrives in dry, sunny conditions.

Its foliage consists of compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.
