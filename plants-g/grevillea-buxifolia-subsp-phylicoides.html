Grevillea buxifolia subsp. phylicoides

Grevillea buxifolia subsp.

phylicoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
