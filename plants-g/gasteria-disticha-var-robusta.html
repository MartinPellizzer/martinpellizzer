Gasteria disticha var. robusta

Gasteria disticha var. robusta, commonly known as the Zulu candle or robust gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically bluish-green with white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear small, star-shaped white flowers. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
