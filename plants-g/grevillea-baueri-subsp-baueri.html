Grevillea baueri subsp. baueri

Grevillea baueri subsp.

baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color.

The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters, which bloom in spring. It is well adapted to sandy soils and is drought-tolerant. Grevillea baueri subsp.

baueri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
