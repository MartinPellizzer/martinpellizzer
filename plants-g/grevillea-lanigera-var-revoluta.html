Grevillea lanigera var. revoluta

Grevillea lanigera var. revoluta, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and vibrant floral displays.
