Geranium molle var. macropetalum

Geranium molle var. macropetalum, commonly known as the large-petaled geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early autumn.
