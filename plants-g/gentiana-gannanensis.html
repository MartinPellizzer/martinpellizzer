Gentiana gannanensis

Gentiana gannanensis, commonly known as the Gannan gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana gannanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
