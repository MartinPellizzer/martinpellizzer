Goeppertia verecunda

Goeppertia verecunda, commonly known as the silver queen calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green patterns. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
