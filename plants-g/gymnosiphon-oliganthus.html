Gymnosiphon oliganthus

Gymnosiphon oliganthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gymnosiphon, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Gymnosiphon oliganthus is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and rocky outcrops.
