Gethyllis multifolia

Gethyllis multifolia, commonly known as the starry flaxflower, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Gethyllis multifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
