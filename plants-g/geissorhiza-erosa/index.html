Geissorhiza Erosa

Geissorhiza erosa is a striking member of the Iridaceae family native to southern Africa. This plant is known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water once established. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of botanical circles.
