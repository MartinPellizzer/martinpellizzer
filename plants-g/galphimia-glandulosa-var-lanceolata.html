Galphimia glandulosa var. lanceolata

Galphimia glandulosa var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf galphimia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The species is part of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes.
