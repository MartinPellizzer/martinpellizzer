Gladiolus laccatus

Gladiolus laccatus, commonly known as the lacquer gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different growing conditions.
