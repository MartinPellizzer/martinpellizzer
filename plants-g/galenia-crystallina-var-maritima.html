Galenia crystallina var. maritima

Galenia crystallina var. maritima, commonly known as the sea galenia, is a perennial flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Its ability to tolerate salt spray and strong winds makes it well adapted to maritime climates.

The species is also noted for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and resilience.
