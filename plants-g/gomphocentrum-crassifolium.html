Gomphocentrum crassifolium

Gomphocentrum crassifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf gomphocentrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features large, broad leaves that are often heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, usually with yellow or greenish petals.

It plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing food and shelter for various insect species.
