Ghinia hirsuta

Ghinia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it produces small, violet-colored flowers. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.
