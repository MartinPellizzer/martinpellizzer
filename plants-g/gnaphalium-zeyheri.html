Gnaphalium zeyheri

Gnaphalium zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's feather grass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Gnaphalium zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
