Gardenia jasminoides f. ovalifolia

Gardenia jasminoides f. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf gardenia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and densely arranged along the stems. The plant produces large, fragrant, white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and as a potted plant.
