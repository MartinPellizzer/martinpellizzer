Geraniospermum trifoliatum

Geraniospermum trifoliatum, commonly known as the three-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

Geraniospermum trifoliatum is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with succulent leaves that store water and reduce transpiration.
