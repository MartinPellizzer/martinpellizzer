Gardenia concolor

Gardenia concolor, commonly known as the white gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper sunlight and watering.

The flowers are also used in perfumery due to their strong, sweet scent.
