Gesneria regeliana

Gesneria regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features thick, glossy leaves with a waxy texture and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria regeliana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
