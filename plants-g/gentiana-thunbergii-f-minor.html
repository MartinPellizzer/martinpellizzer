Gentiana thunbergii f. minor

Gentiana thunbergii f. minor, commonly known as the lesser gentian, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in cooler climates.
