Geraniospermum anethifolium

Geraniospermum anethifolium, commonly known as the anise-leaf geraniospermum, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its aromatic, anise-scented leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Geraniospermum anethifolium is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
