Genista rhiphaea

Genista rhiphaea, commonly known as the yellow broom.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are small, needle-like, and evergreen, providing a lush green appearance throughout the year.

Genista rhiphaea is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.
