Galenia crystallina

Galenia crystallina, commonly known as the crystalline galenia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, bead-like fruits that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Galenia crystallina is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
