Grammitis nhatrangensis

Grammitis nhatrangensis, commonly known as the Nhatrang fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are wiry and often covered in a thin layer of moss-like tissue.

Grammitis nhatrangensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
