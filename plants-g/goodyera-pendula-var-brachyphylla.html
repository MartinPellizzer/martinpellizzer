Goodyera pendula var. brachyphylla

Goodyera pendula var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-leaf basket orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dense clusters of dark green, fleshy leaves. The plant produces delicate, pendant flowers that are typically white or pale green with a slight pinkish tinge. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rock crevices.

Its unique morphology and specialized habitat make it a distinctive member of the Orchidaceae family.
