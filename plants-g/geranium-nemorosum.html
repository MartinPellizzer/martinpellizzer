Geranium nemorosum

Geranium nemorosum, commonly known as the wood geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in woodland areas and shaded environments, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite leaves that are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture. Its flowers are pink to purple, with five petals and a distinctive spurred lower petal.

Geranium nemorosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to support local pollinators.
