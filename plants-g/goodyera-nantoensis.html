Goodyera nantoensis

Goodyera nantoensis, commonly known as the Nanto orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant typically features a rosette of dark green, leathery leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and green. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing in forest undergrowth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
