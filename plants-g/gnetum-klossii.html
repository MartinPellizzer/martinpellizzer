Gnetum klossii

Gnetum klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's gnetum, is a unique gymnosperm native to the tropical rainforests of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its woody stems, scale-like leaves, and distinctive male and female cones. The plant exhibits a form of vascular tissue similar to that of angiosperms, which is unusual for gymnosperms. It plays a significant role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat, providing shelter and food for various species.

Due to its restricted distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
