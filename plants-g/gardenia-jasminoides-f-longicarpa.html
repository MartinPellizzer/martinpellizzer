Gardenia jasminoides f. longicarpa

Gardenia jasminoides f. longicarpa, commonly known as the longipedicellate gardenia, is a cultivar prized for its large, elongated, and fragrant white flowers.

This cultivar is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic fragrance.
