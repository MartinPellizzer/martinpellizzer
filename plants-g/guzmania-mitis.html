Guzmania mitis

Guzmania mitis, commonly known as the pink flamingo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, pinkish-red bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Guzmania mitis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
