Globba tricolor

Globba tricolor, commonly known as the three-color globba, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features vibrant, tri-colored bracts that range from green to red and yellow, creating a visually appealing display. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in suitable conditions.

Globba tricolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
