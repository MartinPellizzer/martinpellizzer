Geranium onaei f. yezoense

Geranium onaei f. yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo geranium, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright red or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
