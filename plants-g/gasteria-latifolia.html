Gasteria latifolia

Gasteria latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are broader than they are long. The plant produces tall, branching stems with small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Gasteria latifolia is often used in rock gardens and as a houseplant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
