Glossocalyx brevipes var. letouzeyi

Glossocalyx brevipes var. letouzeyi, commonly known as the narrow-leaved glossocalyx, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
