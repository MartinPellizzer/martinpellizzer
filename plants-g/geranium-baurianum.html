Geranium baurianum

Geranium baurianum, commonly known as the Baur's geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a slightly serrated margin, and they are often covered in a fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
