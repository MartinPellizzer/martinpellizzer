Gynura albicaulis

Gynura albicaulis, commonly known as the white-stemmed gynura, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are borne on slender pedicels. Its leaves are typically serrated and have a slightly rough texture.

Gynura albicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
