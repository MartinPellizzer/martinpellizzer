Geranium rivale

Geranium rivale, commonly known as the water geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and forest floors. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Geranium rivale is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
