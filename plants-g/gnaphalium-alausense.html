Gnaphalium alausense

Gnaphalium alausense, commonly known as the alpine cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Gnaphalium alausense is adapted to harsh environments, with a compact growth form and a thick, waxy coating on its leaves to reduce water loss.
