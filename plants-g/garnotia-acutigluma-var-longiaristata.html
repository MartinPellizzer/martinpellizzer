Garnotia acutigluma var. longiaristata

Garnotia acutigluma var. longiaristata, commonly known as the long-stemmed garnotia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
