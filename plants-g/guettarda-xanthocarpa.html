Guettarda xanthocarpa

Guettarda xanthocarpa, commonly known as the yellow fruit guettarda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by bright yellow, fleshy fruits that are edible. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and fruit.
