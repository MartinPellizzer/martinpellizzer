Guarianthe patinii

Guarianthe patinii, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically broad and elliptical, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Guarianthe patinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
