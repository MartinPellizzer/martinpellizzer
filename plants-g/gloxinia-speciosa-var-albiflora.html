Gloxinia speciosa var. albiflora

Gloxinia speciosa var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered gloxinia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are predominantly white with a slight pink or red tinge at the base. The plant features velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are a deep green in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia speciosa var. albiflora is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in indoor gardens and as a decorative plant.
