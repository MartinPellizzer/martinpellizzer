Gaultheria paulsmithii

Gaultheria paulsmithii, commonly known as the Paul Smith's wintergreen, is a species of shrub native to the temperate rainforests of southern Chile.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by edible, red berries in the autumn.
