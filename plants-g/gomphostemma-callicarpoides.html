Gomphostemma callicarpoides

Gomphostemma callicarpoides, commonly known as the sweetbriar vine, is a climbing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features woody stems with thorn-like projections and trifoliate leaves that are opposite or alternate in arrangement.
