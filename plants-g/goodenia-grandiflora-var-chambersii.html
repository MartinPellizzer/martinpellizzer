Goodenia grandiflora var. chambersii

Goodenia grandiflora var. chambersii, commonly known as the Heath Goodenia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and resilient addition to native gardens.
