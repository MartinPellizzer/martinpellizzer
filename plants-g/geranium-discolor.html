Geranium discolor

Geranium discolor, commonly known as the pale geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink or purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and prefers full sun. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Geranium discolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
