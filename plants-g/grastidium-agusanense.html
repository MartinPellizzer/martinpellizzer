Grastidium agusanense

Grastidium agusanense, commonly known as the Agusan mountain fern, is a rare and endemic species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that grow in a compact, clustered form. The plant typically thrives in shaded, humid environments within montane forests. Its leaves are bioluminescent under certain conditions, a unique trait that aids in its survival.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
