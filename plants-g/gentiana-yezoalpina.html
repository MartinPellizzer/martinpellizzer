Gentiana yezoalpina

Gentiana yezoalpina, commonly known as the Yezo gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It thrives in cool, moist environments, typically found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Gentiana yezoalpina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
