Goodenia anfracta

Goodenia anfracta, commonly known as the zigzag goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal regions.

Goodenia anfracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
