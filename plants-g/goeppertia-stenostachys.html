Goeppertia stenostachys

Goeppertia stenostachys, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves that often display a silvery or metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the growing season, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Goeppertia stenostachys is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
