Gouldia macrocarpa var. cuneata

Gouldia macrocarpa var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a vibrant red or orange, especially under full sun. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add color to the garden during the cooler months.
