Glechoma Cephalotes

Glechoma cephalotes is a unique plant known for its creeping growth habit and vibrant leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in woodland areas. The plant's ability to spread rapidly makes it both a useful ground cover and a potential weed. Its small, tubular flowers bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape. Despite its modest appearance, Glechoma cephalotes holds ecological significance in supporting local wildlife.
