Goodenia albiflora

Goodenia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its striking white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or heathland environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Goodenia albiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
