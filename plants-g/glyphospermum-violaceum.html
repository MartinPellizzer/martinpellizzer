Glyphospermum violaceum

Glyphospermum violaceum, commonly known as the violet starfruit or violet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant features palmate leaves with a distinctive violet hue, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into star-shaped fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
