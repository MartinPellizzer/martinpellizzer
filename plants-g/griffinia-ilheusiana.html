Griffinia ilheusiana

Griffinia ilheusiana, commonly known as the Ilheus spider orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring intricate spathes and specialized pollination mechanisms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its small, delicate flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive, spider-like appearance.

Due to habitat loss, Griffinia ilheusiana is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
