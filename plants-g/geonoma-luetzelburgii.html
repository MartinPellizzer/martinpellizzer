Geonoma luetzelburgii

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and mountainous areas. Its rhizome is often covered in a dense mat of roots, which helps in nutrient absorption.
