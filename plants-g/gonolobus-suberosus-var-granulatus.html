Gonolobus suberosus var. granulatus

Gonolobus suberosus var. granulatus, commonly known as the rough-stemmed gonolobus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems covered in small, rough, granular protrusions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry, open habitats.
