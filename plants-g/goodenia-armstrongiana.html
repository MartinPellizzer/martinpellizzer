Goodenia armstrongiana

Goodenia armstrongiana, commonly known as Armstrong's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, narrow leaves and produces seed pods that split open when mature.

Armstrong's goodenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
