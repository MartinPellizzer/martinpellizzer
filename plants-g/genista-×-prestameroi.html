Genista × prestameroi

Genista × prestameroi, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various climates.

Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it is often managed carefully in natural habitats.
