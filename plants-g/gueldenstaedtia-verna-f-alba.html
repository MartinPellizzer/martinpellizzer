Gueldenstaedtia verna f. alba

Gueldenstaedtia verna f. alba, commonly known as the white spring vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are white or pale pink, arranged in small clusters, and are characterized by their pea-like structure typical of the Fabaceae family.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators.
