Grevillea australis var. erecta

Grevillea australis var. erecta, commonly known as the erect grevillea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically red or orange in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in dense, cylindrical spikes, which bloom from spring to autumn. It is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
