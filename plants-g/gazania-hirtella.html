Gazania hirtella

Gazania hirtella, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers with a central disk. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania hirtella is also known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the year in suitable conditions.
