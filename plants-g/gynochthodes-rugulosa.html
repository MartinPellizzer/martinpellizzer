Gynochthodes rugulosa

It is characterized by its rough, irregularly shaped thallus and a distinctive, pale to yellowish-green color. It is often found in temperate regions and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.

Due to its ecological role in nutrient cycling and as an indicator species, it is of interest to mycologists and ecologists.
