Gesneria sceptrum var. flavida

Gesneria sceptrum var. flavida, commonly known as the golden flame gesneria, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, lance-shaped leaves that display a distinctive yellow to orange variegation. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 feet, with clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its unique foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
