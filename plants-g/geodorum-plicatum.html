Geodorum plicatum

Geodorum plicatum, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pleated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Geodorum plicatum is a perennial herb that prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture.
