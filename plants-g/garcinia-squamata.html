Garcinia squamata

Garcinia squamata, commonly known as the scarlet flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the appearance of a fiery display. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a faint, pleasant fragrance. Its fruit is a small, yellowish drupe that contains a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Garcinia squamata is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitats.
