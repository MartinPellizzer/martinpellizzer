Gladiolus rubens

Gladiolus rubens, commonly known as the red gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically vibrant red, though some varieties may exhibit other colors. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking blooms.
