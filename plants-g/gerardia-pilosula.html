Gerardia pilosula

Gerardia pilosula, commonly known as the silky gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Gerardia pilosula is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
