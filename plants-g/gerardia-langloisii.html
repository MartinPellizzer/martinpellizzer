Gerardia langloisii

Gerardia langloisii, commonly known as the Langlois' gerardia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or alpine habitats. The plant features a rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink.

Gerardia langloisii is noted for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
