Grammitis aspidioides

Grammitis aspidioides, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinct, narrow shape.
