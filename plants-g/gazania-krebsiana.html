Gazania krebsiana

Gazania krebsiana, commonly known as the Cape gold bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in a compact shrub form, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Gazania krebsiana is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
