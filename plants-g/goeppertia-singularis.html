Goeppertia singularis

Goeppertia singularis, commonly known as the singular goeppertia, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia singularis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
