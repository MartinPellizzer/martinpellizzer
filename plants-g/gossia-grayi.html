Gossia grayi

Gossia grayi, commonly known as the gray gossia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the rainforests of eastern Australia, where it typically grows in shaded, moist environments. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, brown seeds.

Gossia grayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it prefers cool, humid conditions.
