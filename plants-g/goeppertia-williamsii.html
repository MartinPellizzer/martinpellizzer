Goeppertia williamsii

Goeppertia williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, mottled foliage with deep green and silver patterns. Goeppertia williamsii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
