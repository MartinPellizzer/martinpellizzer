Gelasine azurea

Gelasine azurea, commonly known as the Azure Gelasine, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue and purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy, providing a contrast to its colorful blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
