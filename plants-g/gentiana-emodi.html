Gentiana emodi

Gentiana emodi, commonly known as the Himalayan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, adapting to the harsh mountain climate.

Gentiana emodi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
