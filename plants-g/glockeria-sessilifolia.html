Glockeria sessilifolia

Glockeria sessilifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Glockeria sessilifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
