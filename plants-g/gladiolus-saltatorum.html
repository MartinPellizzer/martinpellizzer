Gladiolus saltatorum

Gladiolus saltatorum, commonly known as the jumping gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sturdy stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting vibrant, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements.

Due to its unique appearance and blooming habits, it is a popular choice for both horticultural displays and botanical study.
