Guettarda gehrigeri

Guettarda gehrigeri, commonly known as the silver mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to saline and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. Its leaves are elliptical and have a silvery-white underside, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Guettarda gehrigeri is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and resilience to coastal conditions.
