Goodenia mimuloides

Goodenia mimuloides, commonly known as the mimuloides goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to red. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia mimuloides is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
