Gesneria confertifolia

Gesneria confertifolia, commonly known as the clustered gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, often blooming in spring or early summer.

Gesneria confertifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
