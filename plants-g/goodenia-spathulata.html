Goodenia spathulata

Goodenia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spathulate (spade-shaped) leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia spathulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
