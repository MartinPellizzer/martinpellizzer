Gentiana gilgiana

Gentiana gilgiana, commonly known as the Gilgian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or moist soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana gilgiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
