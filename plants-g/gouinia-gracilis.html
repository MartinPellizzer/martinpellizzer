Gouinia gracilis

Gouinia gracilis, commonly known as the slender gouinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
