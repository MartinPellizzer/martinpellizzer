Geranium stephanianum

Geranium stephanianum, commonly known as the purple geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces vibrant purple or pink blooms. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Geranium stephanianum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
