Globba cernua

Globba cernua, commonly known as the nodding globba, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.
