Gagea iberica var. lacaitae

Gagea iberica var. lacaitae, commonly known as the Iberian spring beauty, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and clusters of white or pale pink flowers.
