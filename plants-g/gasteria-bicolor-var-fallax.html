Gasteria bicolor var. fallax

Gasteria bicolor var. fallax, commonly known as the Blue Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often adorned with white or grayish markings. The plant produces short, segmented stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of red or orange. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its compact size and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
