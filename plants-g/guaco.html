Guaco

Guaco, commonly known as *Piper aduncum*, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other plants like pepper and sarsaparilla. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its stems and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Guaco thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.
