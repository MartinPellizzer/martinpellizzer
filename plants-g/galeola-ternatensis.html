Galeola ternatensis

Galeola ternatensis, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, velvety-textured leaves and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the rainy season.

Galeola ternatensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
