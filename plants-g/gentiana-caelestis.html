Gentiana caelestis

Gentiana caelestis, commonly known as the sky gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana caelestis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
