Galactia berteroana

Galactia berteroana, commonly known as the yellow burr bean, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by hairy, spiny pods containing small, hard seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
