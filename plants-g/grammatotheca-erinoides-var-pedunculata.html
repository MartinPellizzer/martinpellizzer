Grammatotheca erinoides var. pedunculata

Grammatotheca erinoides var. pedunculata, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It features succulent, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, which are borne on elongated peduncles. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Euphorbiaceae family.
