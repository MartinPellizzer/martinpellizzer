Gazania uniflora

Gazania uniflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant features a basal rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that help it conserve water. Gazania uniflora is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its flowers bloom in spring and early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens.
