Gibasis venustula subsp. robusta

Gibasis venustula subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust gibasis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in both garden and horticultural settings.
