Goodenia integerrima

Goodenia integerrima, commonly known as the smooth goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia integerrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
