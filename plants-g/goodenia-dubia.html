Goodenia dubia

Goodenia dubia, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and it blooms during the spring and summer months.

Goodenia dubia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
