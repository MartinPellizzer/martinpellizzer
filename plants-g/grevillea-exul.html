Grevillea exul

Grevillea exul, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green with a reddish or brownish underside. The plant produces striking, feather-like inflorescences that are usually red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea exul is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
