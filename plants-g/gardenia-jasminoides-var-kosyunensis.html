Gardenia jasminoides var. kosyunensis

Gardenia jasminoides var. kosyunensis, commonly known as the Korean gardenia, is a compact shrub native to Korea.

It features glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and produces fragrant, white, waxy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, and it requires consistent moisture to thrive.

The variety is noted for its smaller size compared to the standard gardenia, making it suitable for smaller spaces.
