Glycosmis macrophylla var. macrophylla

Glycosmis macrophylla var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf glycosmis, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree with glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries containing several seeds. Its leaves are typically large, which gives it its common name, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
