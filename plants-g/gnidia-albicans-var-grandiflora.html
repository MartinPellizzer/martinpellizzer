Gnidia albicans var. grandiflora

Gnidia albicans var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered gnidia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation areas.
