Gourliea decorticans var. subtropicalis

Gourliea decorticans var. subtropicalis, commonly known as the subtropical gourliea, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its fruit is a small, round pod that splits open to release seeds.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
