Geranium ciconium

Geranium ciconium, commonly known as the royal geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium ciconium is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
