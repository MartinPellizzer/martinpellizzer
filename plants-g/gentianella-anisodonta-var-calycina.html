Gentianella anisodonta var. calycina

Gentianella anisodonta var. calycina, commonly known as the Calycine Gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
