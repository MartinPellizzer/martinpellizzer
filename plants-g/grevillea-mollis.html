Grevillea mollis

Grevillea mollis, commonly known as the soft grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and showy, bright orange or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is well adapted to sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Grevillea mollis prefers full sun and is drought tolerant once established.
