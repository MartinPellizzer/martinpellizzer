Gasteria trigona

Gasteria trigona, commonly known as the Cape foxtail, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings. The plant produces striking, tall, spiky flower stalks that bear small, tubular flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Gasteria trigona is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
