Geniostoma haemospermum var. elongatum

Geniostoma haemospermum var. elongatum, commonly known as the bloodseed plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange seed pods, which contain seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often in shades of yellow or green.

The species is notable for its unique seed dispersal mechanism, which involves the explosive release of seeds upon maturity.
