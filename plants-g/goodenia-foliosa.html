Goodenia foliosa

Goodenia foliosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is adapted to arid environments.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
