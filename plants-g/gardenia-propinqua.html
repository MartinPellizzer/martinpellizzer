Gardenia propinqua

Gardenia propinqua, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with prominent yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
