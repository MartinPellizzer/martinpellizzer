Gentianella muelleriana subsp. muelleriana

Gentianella muelleriana subsp.

muelleriana, commonly known as Mueller's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of southern Australia. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
