Grammatotheca erinoides var. eckloniana

Grammatotheca erinoides var. eckloniana, commonly known as the Ecklon's featherleaf, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear alternate, finely divided, feathery leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
