Geodorum Pictum

Geodorum pictum is a striking member of the orchid family known for its vibrant, colorful flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors. The flowers often display intricate patterns and hues that resemble paintings, hence its common name. Cultivating Geodorum pictum can be rewarding for those who appreciate both beauty and botanical interest.
