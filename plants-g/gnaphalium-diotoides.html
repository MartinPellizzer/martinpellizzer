Gnaphalium diotoides

Gnaphalium diotoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf mouseear, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and typical sunflower-like structure. Gnaphalium diotoides produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators.
