Genianthus valvatus

Genianthus valvatus, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy habit. Its leaves are linear and grayish-green, while the flowers are borne in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Genianthus valvatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
