Glomera fruticulosa

Glomera fruticulosa, commonly known as the clustered glomera, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Glomera fruticulosa is known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
