Genyorchis macrantha

Genyorchis macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor it in loose, nutrient-poor soils.

Genyorchis macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
