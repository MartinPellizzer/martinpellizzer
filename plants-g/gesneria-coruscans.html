Gesneria coruscans

Gesneria coruscans, commonly known as the rainbow gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gesneria coruscans is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
