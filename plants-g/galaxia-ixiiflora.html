Galaxia ixiiflora

Galaxia ixiiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf galaxia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Galaxia ixiiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
