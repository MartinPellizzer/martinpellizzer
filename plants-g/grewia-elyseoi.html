Grewia elyseoi

Grewia elyseoi, commonly known as the Elyse's globemallow, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.

Grewia elyseoi is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and shelter for small wildlife.
