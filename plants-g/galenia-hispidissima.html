Galenia hispidissima

Galenia hispidissima, commonly known as the hairy galenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and also covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall rugged look.

Galenia hispidissima is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.
