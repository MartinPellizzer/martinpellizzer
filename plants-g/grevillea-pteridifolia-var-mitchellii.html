Grevillea pteridifolia var. mitchellii

Grevillea pteridifolia var. mitchellii, commonly known as Mitchell's Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in spikes, which are clustered at the ends of its branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
