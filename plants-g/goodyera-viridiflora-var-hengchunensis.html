Goodyera viridiflora var. hengchunensis

Goodyera viridiflora var. hengchunensis, commonly known as the Hengchun green-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a short rhizome and produces a single inflorescence with several flowers.

This variety is noted for its unique foliage and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
