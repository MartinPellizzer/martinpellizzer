Gorteria araneosa

Gorteria araneosa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its long, thin, hair-like petals. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Gorteria araneosa is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its elegant, delicate flowers.
