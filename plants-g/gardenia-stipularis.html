Gardenia stipularis

Gardenia stipularis, commonly known as the Chinese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen and typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Gardenia stipularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
