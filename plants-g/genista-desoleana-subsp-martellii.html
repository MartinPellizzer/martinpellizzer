Genista desoleana subsp. martellii

Genista desoleana subsp.

martellii, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and North Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits.

The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its slender, branched stems are often covered in small, scale-like leaves.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.
