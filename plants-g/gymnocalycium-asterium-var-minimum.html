Gymnocalycium asterium var. minimum

Gymnocalycium asterium var. minimum, commonly known as the miniature star cactus, is a compact cactus species native to the Atacama Desert in Chile.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. The plant has spiny areoles and is well adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance needs.

Its small size and unique floral display make it a striking addition to succulent collections.
