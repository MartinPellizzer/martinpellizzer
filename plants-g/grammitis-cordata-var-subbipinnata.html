Grammitis cordata var. subbipinnata

Grammitis cordata var. subbipinnata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grammitis, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features delicate, bi-pinnate leaves that are typically narrow and elongated, giving it a feathery appearance. The fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in humid, shaded gardens.
