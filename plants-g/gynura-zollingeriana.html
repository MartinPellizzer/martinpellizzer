Gynura zollingeriana

Gynura zollingeriana, commonly known as the silver arrow or arrowhead plant, is a tropical species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically followed by fluffy, white seed heads. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
