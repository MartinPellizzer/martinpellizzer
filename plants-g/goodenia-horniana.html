Goodenia horniana

Goodenia horniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia horniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
