Goeppertia taeniosa

Goeppertia taeniosa, commonly known as the Zebra Canna, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that often display a zebra-like pattern of dark green and silver stripes. The plant produces large, showy orange or red flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Goeppertia taeniosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
