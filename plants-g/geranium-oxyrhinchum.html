Geranium oxyrhinchum

Geranium oxyrhinchum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are narrow, elliptical, and have a distinctive wavy margin. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
