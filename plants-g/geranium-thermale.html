Geranium thermale

Geranium thermale, commonly known as the thermal geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to regions with warm climates.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
