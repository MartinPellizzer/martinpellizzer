Gaya disticha

Gaya disticha, commonly known as the fan palm, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive fan-shaped leaves that radiate from a central stem. The plant has a stout trunk covered with persistent leaf bases, giving it a rugged appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
