Garcinia gnetoides

Garcinia gnetoides, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gnetaceae and is characterized by its smooth, fissured bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red berries. Garcinia gnetoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in various cultures.
