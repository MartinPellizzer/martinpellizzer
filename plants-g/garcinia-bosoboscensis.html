Garcinia bosoboscensis

Garcinia bosoboscensis, commonly known as the Boso-boso tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds. Garcinia bosoboscensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its habitat is typically moist lowland forests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
