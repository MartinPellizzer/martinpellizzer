Gentiana multibracteolata

Gentiana multibracteolata, commonly known as the many-bract gentian, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers cool, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
