Grammatotheca bergiana var. foliosa

Grammatotheca bergiana var. foliosa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in various colors. The leaves are typically rounded and arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a lush, compact appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

The plant's botanical features make it both ornamental and relatively easy to care for in indoor environments.
