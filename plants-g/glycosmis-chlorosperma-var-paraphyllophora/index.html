Glycosmis Chlorosperma Var. Paraphyllophora

Glycosmis chlorosperma var. paraphyllophora is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the Rutaceae family. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments within dense forests. Its unique foliage features a striking combination of green and yellow hues, making it visually distinctive. This plant holds potential for both ecological and medicinal significance, warranting further exploration.
