Genista thunbergii

Genista thunbergii, commonly known as Thunberg's broom, is a shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

It is often used in hedges and as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
