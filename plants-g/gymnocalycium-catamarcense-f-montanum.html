Gymnocalycium catamarcense f. montanum

Gymnocalycium catamarcense f. montanum, commonly known as the Montane Gymnocalycium, is a succulent cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
