Gochnatia elliptica var. inermis

Gochnatia elliptica var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth gochnatia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, elliptical leaves that are usually hairless, giving it its varietal name "inermis," meaning unarmed. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typical of the Asteraceae family.

This species is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
