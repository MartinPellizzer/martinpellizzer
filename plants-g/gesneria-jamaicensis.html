Gesneria jamaicensis

Gesneria jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Jamaica.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant in shaded, humid environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.

Its growth habits and floral structure reflect adaptations to its native habitat.
