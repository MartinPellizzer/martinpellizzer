Goeppertia blanda

Goeppertia blanda, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and prefers bright, indirect light. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Goeppertia blanda is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage.
