Glossoma

Glossoma, commonly known as the velvet leaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often a deep green or reddish-purple in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Glossoma is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
