Gentiana muscicola

Gentiana muscicola, commonly known as the moss gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Gentiana muscicola is a perennial herb that thrives in cool, humid climates.
