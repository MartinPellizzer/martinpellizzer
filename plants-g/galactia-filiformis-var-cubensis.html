Galactia filiformis var. cubensis

Galactia filiformis var. cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces slender, hairy pods that contain small seeds.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
