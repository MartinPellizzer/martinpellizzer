Genista sophoroides

Genista sophoroides, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring slender, often thorny stems. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista sophoroides is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal and rocky areas.
