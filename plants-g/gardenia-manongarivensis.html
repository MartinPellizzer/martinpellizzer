Gardenia manongarivensis

Gardenia manongarivensis, commonly known as the Manongarivensis gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant produces elliptical, yellowish-orange berries that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Gardenia manongarivensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
