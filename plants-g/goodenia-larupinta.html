Goodenia larupinta

Goodenia larupinta, commonly known as the Northern Territory goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, often in rocky soils. It has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous small flowers arranged in a spike-like cluster.

Goodenia larupinta is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
