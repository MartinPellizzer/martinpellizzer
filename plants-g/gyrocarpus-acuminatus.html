Gyrocarpus acuminatus

Gyrocarpus acuminatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to lanceolate in shape. The tree is valued for its hard wood and has traditional uses in various indigenous communities.
