Germanea

Germanea, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. Germanea thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
