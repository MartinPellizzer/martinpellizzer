Gladiolus duvigneaudii

Gladiolus duvigneaudii, commonly known as the Duvigneaud's gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves and has a fibrous root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant hues and elegant appearance.
