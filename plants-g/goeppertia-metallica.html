Goeppertia metallica

Goeppertia metallica, commonly known as the Silver Star or Metallica Calathea, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, silver-veined, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia metallica is prized for its ornamental value and is often used as a decorative houseplant.
