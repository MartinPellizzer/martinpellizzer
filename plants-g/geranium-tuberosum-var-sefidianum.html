Geranium tuberosum var. sefidianum

Geranium tuberosum var. sefidianum, commonly known as the Persian geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Botanically, it belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and showy inflorescences.
