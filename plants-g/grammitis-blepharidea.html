Grammitis blepharidea

Grammitis blepharidea, commonly known as the silver fern, is a fern species belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Grammitis blepharidea is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks, where it can absorb moisture from the air.
