Garcinia × guacopary

Garcinia × guacopary, commonly known as the hybrid mangosteen, is a tropical plant belonging to the Clusiaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Garcinia indica and Garcinia mangostana, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers and evergreen leaves, which contribute to its ornamental value. Its fruit, though less commonly consumed than that of pure Garcinia mangostana, is prized for its medicinal properties.

The hybrid is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and potential health benefits.
