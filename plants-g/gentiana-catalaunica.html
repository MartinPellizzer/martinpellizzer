Gentiana catalaunica

Gentiana catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Gentiana catalaunica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
