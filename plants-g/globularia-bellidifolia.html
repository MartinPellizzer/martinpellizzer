Globularia bellidifolia

Globularia bellidifolia, commonly known as the rock globewort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and clusters of small, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Globularia bellidifolia is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
