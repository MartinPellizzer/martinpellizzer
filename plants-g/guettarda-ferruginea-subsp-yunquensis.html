Guettarda ferruginea subsp. yunquensis

Guettarda ferruginea subsp.

yunquensis, commonly known as the Yunquense Guettarda, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
