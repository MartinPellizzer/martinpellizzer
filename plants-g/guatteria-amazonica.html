Guatteria amazonica

Guatteria amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian guatteria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon rainforest. This evergreen shrub or small tree typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy dark green upper surface.
