Glycine javanica var. laurentii

Glycine javanica var. laurentii, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cascading clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces flat, seed pods that split open to release seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
