Gyrostachys congesta

Gyrostachys congesta, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has small, narrow leaves and a bushy growth habit, adapted to survive in hot, dry environments.
