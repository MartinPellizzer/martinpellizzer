Gloxinia × hybrida

Gloxinia × hybrida, commonly known as the gloxinia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves and produces tuberous roots that store nutrients. Gloxinias are popular as ornamental plants due to their striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making them suitable for indoor cultivation.
