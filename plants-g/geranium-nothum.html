Geranium nothum

Geranium nothum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium nothum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
