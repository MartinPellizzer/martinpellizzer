Gloriosa abyssinica var. graminifolia

Gloriosa abyssinica var. graminifolia, commonly known as the grassy flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its long, grass-like foliage and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
