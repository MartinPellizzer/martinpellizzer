Gentianella kuntzei

Gentianella kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's gentian, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Gentianella kuntzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
