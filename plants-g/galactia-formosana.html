Galactia formosana

Galactia formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its roots contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
