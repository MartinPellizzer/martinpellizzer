Genista macrobotrys

Genista macrobotrys, commonly known as the large-flowered broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
