Gentianella carpatica

Gentianella carpatica, commonly known as the Carpathian gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its leaves are opposite and often have a velvety texture.

Gentianella carpatica is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
