Glebionis coronaria

Glebionis coronaria, commonly known as the crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
