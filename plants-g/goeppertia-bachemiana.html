Goeppertia bachemiana

Goeppertia bachemiana, commonly known as the Bachemiana Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green and silver patterns. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that enhances their ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
