Gentiana verna f. schleicheri

Gentiana verna f. schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's spring gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountain meadows and rocky slopes.

This subspecies is notable for its compact growth and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
