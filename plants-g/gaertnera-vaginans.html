Gaertnera vaginans

Gaertnera vaginans, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom during the warmer months. Gaertnera vaginans is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
