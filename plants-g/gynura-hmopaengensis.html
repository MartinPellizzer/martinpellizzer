Gynura hmopaengensis

Gynura hmopaengensis, commonly known as the Hmopaeng gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is notable for its ornamental value, featuring bright yellow ray florets and a central disk. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Gynura hmopaengensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
