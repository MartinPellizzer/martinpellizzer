Gonystylus lucidulus

Gonystylus lucidulus, commonly known as the bright-stemmed ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, aromatic wood and smooth, often shiny bark. The plant belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are typically elliptical and dark green, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Gonystylus lucidulus is valued for both its timber and its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
