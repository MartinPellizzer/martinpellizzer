Goodyera glabra

Goodyera glabra, commonly known as the lesser pigmy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, rosette-like growth habit and features broad, dark green leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with prominent, showy lips. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in forest understories.

Goodyera glabra is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is often found growing on rocks or in leaf litter.
