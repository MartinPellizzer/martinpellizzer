Gerardia scabrosa

Gerardia scabrosa, commonly known as the rough gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are rough and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Gerardia scabrosa is known for its yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.
