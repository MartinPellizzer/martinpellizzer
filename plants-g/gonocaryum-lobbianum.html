Gonocaryum lobbianum

Gonocaryum lobbianum, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
