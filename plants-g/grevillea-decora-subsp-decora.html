Grevillea decora subsp. decora

Grevillea decora subsp.

decora, commonly known as the showy grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features striking, feather-like leaves and vibrant, red or orange flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is well adapted to arid environments, with deep roots that help it survive in dry conditions. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
