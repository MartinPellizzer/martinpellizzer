Grevillea huegelii

Grevillea huegelii, commonly known as the West Australian Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features vibrant red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, attracting native birds and insects. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or grayish tint. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea huegelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
