Gerardia tenuifolia subsp. typica

Gerardia tenuifolia subsp.

typica, commonly known as the slender leaf gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is adapted to the arid and coastal environments of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
