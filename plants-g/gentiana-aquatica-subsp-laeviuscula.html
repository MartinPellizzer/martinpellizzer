Gentiana aquatica subsp. laeviuscula

Gentiana aquatica subsp.

laeviuscula, commonly known as the smooth gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to moist meadows and stream banks in northern Europe. It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils and requires a cool, moist environment.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
