Gulubia crenata

Gulubia crenata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Gulubia crenata has a compact growth habit and is well-suited for indoor gardening.
