Gleichenia hermannii

Gleichenia hermannii, commonly known as Hermann's wood fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The fronds are typically dark green, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, rugged appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

This plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
