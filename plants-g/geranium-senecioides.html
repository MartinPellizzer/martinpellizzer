Geranium senecioides

Geranium senecioides, commonly known as the downy geranium, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a downy texture and produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous root system.

Geranium senecioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators and wildlife.
