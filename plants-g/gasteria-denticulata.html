Gasteria denticulata

Gasteria denticulata, commonly known as the toothed gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often marked with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or yellow during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria denticulata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
