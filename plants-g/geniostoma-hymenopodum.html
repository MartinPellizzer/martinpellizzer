Geniostoma hymenopodum

Geniostoma hymenopodum, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming throughout the warmer months. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a favored plant among gardeners in arid climates.
