Gongora maculata var. latibasis

Gongora maculata var. latibasis, commonly known as the spotted gongora, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the highlands of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and its showy, violet to purple flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in cool, well-drained soil. Its flowers are often adorned with a distinctive white or yellow marking near the base of the lip, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized by orchid growers for its unique appearance and relatively ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
