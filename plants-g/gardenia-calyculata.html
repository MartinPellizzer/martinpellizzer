Gardenia calyculata

Gardenia calyculata, commonly known as the Chinese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Gardenia calyculata prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
