Geniostoma rupestre var. tongense

Geniostoma rupestre var. tongense, commonly known as the Tongean rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern China.

It typically grows in rocky or limestone habitats, showcasing its adaptability to poor soil conditions. The plant features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with small, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a potted plant.
