Grevillea wittweri

Grevillea wittweri, commonly known as the West Australian flame flower, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters along the stems. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea wittweri is also known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract native birds and insects.
