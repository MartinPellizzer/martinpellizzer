Gongora longipes

Gongora longipes, commonly known as the long-footed orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated lip that sets it apart from other orchid species. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually a vibrant mix of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing species.

Gongora longipes is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitat.
