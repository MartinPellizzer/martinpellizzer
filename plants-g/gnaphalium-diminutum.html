Gnaphalium diminutum

Gnaphalium diminutum, commonly known as the narrowleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly in countries like South Africa and Namibia. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
