Globularia anatolica

Globularia anatolica, commonly known as Anatolian globularia, is a flowering plant native to Turkey and surrounding regions.

It is a low-growing herb with a compact, globular shape, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Globularia anatolica is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
