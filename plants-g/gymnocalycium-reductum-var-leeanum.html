Gymnocalycium reductum var. leeanum

Gymnocalycium reductum var. leeanum, commonly known as the miniature moon cactus, is a compact cactus species native to South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown as a houseplant.
