Gynostemma pentaphyllum f. grandiflorum

Gynostemma pentaphyllum f. grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant gynostemma, is a variant of the gynostemma plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and robust, climbing growth habit. The plant possesses opposite, pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable species within the Cucurbitaceae family.
