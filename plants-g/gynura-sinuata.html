Gynura sinuata

Gynura sinuata, commonly known as the silver velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and it produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Gynura sinuata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

It prefers warm, humid climates and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
