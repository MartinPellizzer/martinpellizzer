Gynura variifolia

Gynura variifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Gynura variifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
