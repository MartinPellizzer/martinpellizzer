Goodyera nebularum

Goodyera nebularum, commonly known as the cloud-fern or nebular fern, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features delicate, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with intricate patterns, and it produces small, red or orange berries.

Goodyera nebularum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in montane forests.
