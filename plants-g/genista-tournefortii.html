Genista tournefortii

Genista tournefortii, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Genista tournefortii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
