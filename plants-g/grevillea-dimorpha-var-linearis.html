Grevillea dimorpha var. linearis

Grevillea dimorpha var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which are highly attractive to birds and nectar-feeding insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Its unique foliage and striking blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
