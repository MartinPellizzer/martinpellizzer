Goniothalamus nitidus

Goniothalamus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy goniothalamus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
