Galium argenteum

Galium argenteum, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. This species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
