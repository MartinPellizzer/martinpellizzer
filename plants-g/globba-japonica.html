Globba japonica

Globba japonica, commonly known as Japanese globba, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. Globba japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
