Glossopetalum glabrum

Glossopetalum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved glossopetalum, is a succulent plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
