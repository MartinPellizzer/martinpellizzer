Gardenia forsteniana

Gardenia forsteniana, commonly known as the Forsten's gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia forsteniana prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
