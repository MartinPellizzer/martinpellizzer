Gleichenia bolanica

Gleichenia bolanica, commonly known as the Bolan fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fronds that are typically lanceolate in shape. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its fronds, which are clustered in strobila. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gleichenia bolanica is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
