Gymnadenia × borisii

Gymnadenia × borisii, commonly known as the Borisii grass-pink, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its overall appearance.

Gymnadenia × borisii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
