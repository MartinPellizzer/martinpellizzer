Guapira rufescens var. rufescens

Guapira rufescens var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed guapira, is a shrub native to South America.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in greenish-white or reddish hues.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering characteristics.
