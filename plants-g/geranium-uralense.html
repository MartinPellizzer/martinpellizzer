Geranium uralense

Geranium uralense, commonly known as the Ural geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and Eastern Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features upright stems with heart-shaped leaves that have a distinctive serrated edge. Its flowers are typically pink or red, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Geranium uralense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
