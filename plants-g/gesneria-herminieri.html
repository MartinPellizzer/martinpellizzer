Gesneria herminieri

Gesneria herminieri, commonly known as the Herminier's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with dark green, glossy leaves. It prefers humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Gesneria herminieri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
