Gelonium rubrum

Gelonium rubrum, commonly known as the red gelonium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gelonium rubrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
