Gardenia gjellerupii

Gardenia gjellerupii, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the island of Socotra.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is adapted to arid conditions, with thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and aromatic blooms make it a standout in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
