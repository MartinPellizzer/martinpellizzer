Gastrodia pubilabiata f. castanea

Gastrodia pubilabiata f. castanea, commonly known as the chestnut-flowered gastrodia, is a rare and unique plant species belonging to the orchid family.

The plant is native to specific regions in China, where it thrives in cool, humid, and shaded forest environments. Its botanical features include thick, leathery leaves and a short, erect stem.
