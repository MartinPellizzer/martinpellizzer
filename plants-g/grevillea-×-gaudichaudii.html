Grevillea × gaudichaudii

Grevillea × gaudichaudii, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, feathery flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is well adapted to sandy or well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Grevillea × gaudichaudii is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
