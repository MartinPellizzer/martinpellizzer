Genista tanaitica

Genista tanaitica, commonly known as the Tanaitic broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Genista tanaitica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in arid environments.
