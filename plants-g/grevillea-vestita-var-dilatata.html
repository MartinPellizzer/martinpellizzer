Grevillea vestita var. dilatata

Grevillea vestita var. dilatata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silky white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds and insects.
