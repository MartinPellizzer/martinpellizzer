Goodenia quadrifida

Goodenia quadrifida, commonly known as the four-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive four-lobed leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia quadrifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
