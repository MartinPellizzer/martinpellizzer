Garcinia oreophila

Garcinia oreophila, commonly known as the mountain garcinia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when ripe, containing a single seed.

Garcinia oreophila is often found in humid, shaded environments such as montane forests.
