Gasteria disticha

Gasteria disticha, commonly known as the chicken's comb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, flowering stems with small white or yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Gasteria disticha is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
