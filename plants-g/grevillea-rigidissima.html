Grevillea rigidissima

Grevillea rigidissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved waratah, is a striking evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and striking red or pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is part of the Proteaceae family and is well adapted to dry, sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and requires good drainage to prevent root rot.

Grevillea rigidissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens and public landscapes.
