Gaultheria luzonica var. luzonica

Gaultheria luzonica var. luzonica, commonly known as the Luzon wintergreen, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible and have a sharp, peppery flavor. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Ericaceae family.
