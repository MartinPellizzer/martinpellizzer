Gymnoluma

Gymnoluma, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Gymnoluma is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often found in disturbed areas and can become invasive in certain regions.
