Gordonia bidoupensis

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Vietnam, particularly in the Bidoup-Nui Ba National Park. This evergreen shrub or small tree is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
