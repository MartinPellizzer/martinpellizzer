Grumilea sphaerocarpa

Grumilea sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small tree or shrub that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, spherical, fleshy fruits that are typically yellow or green when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique fruiting habit.
