Gentiana lineata

Gentiana lineata, commonly known as the narrowleaf gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a prominent central vein.

Gentiana lineata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
