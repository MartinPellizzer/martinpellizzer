Genista maderensis

Genista maderensis, commonly known as the Mader's broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.

Genista maderensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
