Galega nervosa

Galega nervosa, commonly known as the narrowleaf goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense, branched inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Galega nervosa is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
