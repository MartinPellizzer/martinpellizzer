Graphorkis dregeana

Graphorkis dregeana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces spikes of bright pink or purple flowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
