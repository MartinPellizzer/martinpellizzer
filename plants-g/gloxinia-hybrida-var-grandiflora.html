Gloxinia hybrida var. grandiflora

Gloxinia hybrida var. grandiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf gloxinia, is a hybrid plant prized for its showy, velvety flowers.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are large, trumpet-shaped, and come in a variety of colors including red, pink, and white.
