Geranium rubens

Geranium rubens, commonly known as the scarlet geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium rubens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
