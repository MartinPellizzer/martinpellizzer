Galenia rigida

Galenia rigida, commonly known as rigid galena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Galenia rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
