Gymnocalycium platense var. parvulum

Gymnocalycium platense var. parvulum, commonly known as the Parvulum Moon Cactus, is a small, columnar cactus native to Argentina.

It features a short, thick stem with a distinctive white or light green color, often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the top of the stem. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
