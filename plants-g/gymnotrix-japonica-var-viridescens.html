Gymnotrix japonica var. viridescens

Gymnotrix japonica var. viridescens, commonly known as the Japanese green gymnotrix, is a unique variant of the gymnotrix plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green foliage and compact, bushy growth habit. The leaves are typically lance-shaped with a waxy surface that reflects light, enhancing its visual appeal. This variety thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value has led to its popularity in horticultural settings.
