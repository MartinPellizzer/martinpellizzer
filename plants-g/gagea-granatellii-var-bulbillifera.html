Gagea granatellii var. bulbillifera

Gagea granatellii var. bulbillifera, commonly known as the granulate gagea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its bulbous underground stem and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring. It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Spain and Portugal.
