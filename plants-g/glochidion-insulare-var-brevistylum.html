Glochidion insulare var. brevistylum

Glochidion insulare var. brevistylum, commonly known as the slender-leaf glabrous tree, is a variant of the species native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its simple, alternate, and elliptic leaves. The leaves are typically glossy and have a smooth surface, with a pointed tip and a narrow base. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

This plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
