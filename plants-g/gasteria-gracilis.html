Gasteria gracilis

Gasteria gracilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria gracilis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
