Genista tournefortii subsp. tournefortii

Genista tournefortii subsp.

tournefortii, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, yellowish-green stems that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in terminal clusters during the spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
