Gerbera Rotundifolia

Gerbera rotundifolia is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms that come in a variety of colors including red, yellow, and orange. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its rounded leaves and showy flowers make it a standout addition to any floral display. With its easy care requirements and long blooming season, Gerbera rotundifolia is a favorite among both amateur and professional gardeners.
