Gunnera reniformis

Gunnera reniformis, commonly known as the giant rhubarb, is a striking plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped leaves that can reach up to three feet in diameter. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy surface and serrated edges. This plant produces clusters of small, reddish-pink flowers in the summer months.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its dramatic appearance.
