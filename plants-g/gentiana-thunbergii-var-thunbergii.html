Gentiana thunbergii var. thunbergii

Gentiana thunbergii var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
