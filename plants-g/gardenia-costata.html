Gardenia costata

Gardenia costata, commonly known as the Costate Gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Gardenia costata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
