Gerbera media

Gerbera media, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern. Gerbera media thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Its blooms are long-lasting and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
