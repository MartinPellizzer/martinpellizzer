Gasteria carinata var. retusa

Gasteria carinata var. retusa, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or pale green longitudinal stripes. The plant produces pale yellow or greenish-white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
