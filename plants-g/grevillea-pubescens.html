Grevillea pubescens

Grevillea pubescens, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its common name.

Grevillea pubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
