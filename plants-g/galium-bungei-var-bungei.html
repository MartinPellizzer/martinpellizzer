Galium bungei var. bungei

Galium bungei var. bungei, commonly known as Bunge's bedstraw, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features slender, hairy stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and covered with fine hairs.

Galium bungei var. bungei is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.
