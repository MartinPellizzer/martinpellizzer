Guajava itanareensis

Guajava itanareensis, commonly known as the Itanare mango, is a tropical fruit tree native to the Caribbean.

It produces large, yellow-orange fruits with a sweet and aromatic flavor. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in panicles.

The tree is valued for its fruit and is often grown in warm, humid climates.
