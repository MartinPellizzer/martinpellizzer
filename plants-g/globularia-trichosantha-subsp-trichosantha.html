Globularia trichosantha subsp. trichosantha

Globularia trichosantha subsp.

trichosantha, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its globular, yellowish-orange fruit that resembles a small, round tomato.

The plant produces small, green, ovate leaves and has a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The fruit is edible and has a tangy, sweet flavor, making it a popular ingredient in culinary applications.
