Guapira obtusiloba

Guapira obtusiloba, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood or velvetwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside.
