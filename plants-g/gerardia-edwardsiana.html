Gerardia edwardsiana

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.
