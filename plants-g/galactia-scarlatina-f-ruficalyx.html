Galactia scarlatina f. ruficalyx

Galactia scarlatina f. ruficalyx, commonly known as the red-leafed scarlet bean, is a variant of the scarlet bean plant native to Central and South America.

It is a leguminous species characterized by its striking red foliage and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain small, edible seeds used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
