Geranium holosericeum var. typicum

Geranium holosericeum var. typicum, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
