Geissois pruinosa

Geissois pruinosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with five petals and a distinctive central spur.

Geissois pruinosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
