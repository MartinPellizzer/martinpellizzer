Gardenia tomentosa

Gardenia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, white, woolly pubescence. The plant produces large, fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Gardenia tomentosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
