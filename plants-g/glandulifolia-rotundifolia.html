Glandulifolia rotundifolia

Glandulifolia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf glandular plant, is a species characterized by its distinctive round, lobed leaves.

The plant is native to specific regions in the Mediterranean and is known for its glandular structures on the leaves and stems. These glands secrete a variety of compounds, which contribute to its aromatic properties. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
