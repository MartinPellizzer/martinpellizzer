Gnidia wilmsii

Gnidia wilmsii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and rocky habitats, where it adapts well to dry conditions.

Gnidia wilmsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
