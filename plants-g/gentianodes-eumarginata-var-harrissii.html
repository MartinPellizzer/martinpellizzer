Gentianodes eumarginata var. harrissii

Gentianodes eumarginata var. harrissii, commonly known as the Harrissii gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically arranged in dense inflorescences. The species is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in montane forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
