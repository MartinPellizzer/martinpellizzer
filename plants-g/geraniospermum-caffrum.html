Geraniospermum caffrum

Geraniospermum caffrum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or toothed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Geraniospermum caffrum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
