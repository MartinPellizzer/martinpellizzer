Graphephorum melicoides var. triflorum

Graphephorum melicoides var. triflorum, commonly known as the three-flowered grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and wetland conservation efforts.
