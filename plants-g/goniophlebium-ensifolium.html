Goniophlebium ensifolium

Goniophlebium ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near streams or in dense forests. Its rhizome system allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense clusters.
