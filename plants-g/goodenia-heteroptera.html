Goodenia heteroptera

Goodenia heteroptera, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as shrublands and grasslands.

Goodenia heteroptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
