Grumilea chalconeura

Grumilea chalconeura, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers. The plant features long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Grumilea chalconeura thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique coloration and striking appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
