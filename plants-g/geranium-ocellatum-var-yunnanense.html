Geranium ocellatum var. yunnanense

Geranium ocellatum var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features striking, palmately lobed leaves with a velvety texture and bright green coloration. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central spot. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
