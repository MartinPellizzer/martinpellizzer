Gymnadenia hygrophila

Gymnadenia hygrophila, commonly known as the marsh gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in Europe.

Gymnadenia hygrophila plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates.
