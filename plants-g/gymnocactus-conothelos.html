Gymnocactus conothelos

Gymnocactus conothelos, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a member of the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its unique appearance, featuring a tall, cylindrical stem with a spiny exterior. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-draining soil and full sunlight. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the top of the stem during the warmer months.

Gymnocactus conothelos is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking visual appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
