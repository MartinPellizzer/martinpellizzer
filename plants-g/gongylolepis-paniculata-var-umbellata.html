Gongylolepis paniculata var. umbellata

Gongylolepis paniculata var. umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
