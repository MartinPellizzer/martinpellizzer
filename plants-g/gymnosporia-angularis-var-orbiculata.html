Gymnosporia angularis var. orbiculata

Gymnosporia angularis var. orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaved burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, angular stems and orbiculate (rounded) leaves. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds, typical of the gymnosperm-like structure in its name. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife.
