Gentiana rigescens

Gentiana rigescens, commonly known as the rigid gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and sturdy.

Gentiana rigescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
