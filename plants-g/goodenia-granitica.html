Goodenia granitica

Goodenia granitica, commonly known as the granite goodenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Goodenia granitica is a member of the Goodeniaceae family, known for its ecological importance in supporting local insect populations.
