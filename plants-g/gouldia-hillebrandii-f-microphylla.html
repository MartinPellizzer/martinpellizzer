Gouldia hillebrandii f. microphylla

Gouldia hillebrandii f. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf gouldia, is a compact, low-growing plant native to Australia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is well-suited for rock gardens and coastal areas due to its tolerance of salty air and well-drained soils.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
