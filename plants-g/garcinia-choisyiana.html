Garcinia choisyiana

Garcinia choisyiana, commonly known as the African mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The fruit is small, round, and has a thick, purple rind that turns yellow when ripe. Inside, the fruit contains several segments of juicy, white, segmented flesh.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.
