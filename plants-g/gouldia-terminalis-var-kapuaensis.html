Gouldia terminalis var. kapuaensis

Gouldia terminalis var. kapuaensis, commonly known as the Kapuaensis Gouldia, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange and yellow, creating a striking visual display. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.

Its foliage is broad and grass-like, contributing to its overall lush appearance.
