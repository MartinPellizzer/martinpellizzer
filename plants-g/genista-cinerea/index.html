Genista Cinerea

Genista cinerea is a hardy shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It is known for its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal addition to xeriscaped gardens. Despite its modest appearance, Genista cinerea plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and wildlife.
