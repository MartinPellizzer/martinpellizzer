Gonzalagunia brenesii

Gonzalagunia brenesii, commonly known as the Guanacaste tree, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, spreading canopy and smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, spiny fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Gonzalagunia brenesii plays an important ecological role in providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
