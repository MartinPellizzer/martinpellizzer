Goodenia gracilis

Goodenia gracilis, commonly known as slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Goodenia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
