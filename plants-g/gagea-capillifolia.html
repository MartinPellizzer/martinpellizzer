Gagea capillifolia

Gagea capillifolia, commonly known as the slenderleaf gagea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Gagea capillifolia is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
