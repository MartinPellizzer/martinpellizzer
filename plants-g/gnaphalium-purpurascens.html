Gnaphalium purpurascens

Gnaphalium purpurascens, commonly known as the purple ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Gnaphalium purpurascens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
