Gastrochilus curvifolius

Gastrochilus curvifolius, commonly known as the curl-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features elongated, curling leaves that give it its common name. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
