Galactites duriaei

Galactites duriaei, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisine. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
