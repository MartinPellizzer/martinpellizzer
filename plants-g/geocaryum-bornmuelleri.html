Geocaryum bornmuelleri

Geocaryum bornmuelleri, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a hairy texture.

Geocaryum bornmuelleri is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
