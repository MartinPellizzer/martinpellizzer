Gravisia

Gravisia, commonly known as the heavy-weight orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their robust stems and showy, often fragrant flowers. Gravisia species are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Their leaves are usually broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in their native habitats.

The flowers exhibit a wide range of colors, often with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.
