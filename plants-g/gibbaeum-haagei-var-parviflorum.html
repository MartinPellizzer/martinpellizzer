Gibbaeum haagei var. parviflorum

Gibbaeum haagei var. parviflorum, commonly known as the dwarf starfish plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
