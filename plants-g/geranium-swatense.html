Geranium swatense

Geranium swatense, commonly known as the Swat geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright pink or red blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Geranium swatense is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
