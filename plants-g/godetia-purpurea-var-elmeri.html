Godetia purpurea var. elmeri

Godetia purpurea var. elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's godetia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological restoration projects.
