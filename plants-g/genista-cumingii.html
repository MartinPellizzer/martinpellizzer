Genista cumingii

Genista cumingii, commonly known as Cuming's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
