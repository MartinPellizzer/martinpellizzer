Geraniospermum antidysentericum

Geraniospermum antidysentericum, commonly known as the dysentery geranium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its basal leaves and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat gastrointestinal ailments. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and traditional medicine contexts.
