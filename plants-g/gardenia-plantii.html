Gardenia plantii

Gardenia plantii, commonly known as the Hawaiian gardenia, is a tropical shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil and partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.

Gardenia plantii is also valued for its use in traditional Hawaiian culture and horticulture.
