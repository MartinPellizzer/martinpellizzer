Garnotia laevis

Garnotia laevis, commonly known as the smooth garnet, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, Garnotia laevis is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
