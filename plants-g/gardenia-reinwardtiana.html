Gardenia reinwardtiana

Gardenia reinwardtiana, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and strong floral scent.

The fruit is a bright red berry that contains several seeds.
