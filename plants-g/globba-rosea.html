Globba rosea

Globba rosea, commonly known as the pink ginger, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with a spicy fragrance. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy surface.
