Glaucidium palmatum var. leucanthum

Glaucidium palmatum var. leucanthum, commonly known as the white-flowered glaucidium, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its slender, palmate leaves with a distinctive blue-green hue on the upper surface and a silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.
