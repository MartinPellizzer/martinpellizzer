Gloriosa superba f. grandiflora

Gloriosa superba f. grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Colocasiaceae and is characterized by its large, showy, orange-red flowers with a distinctive spurred shape. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for its visual appeal, though it requires specific care to thrive.
