Galium tenuissimum f. tenuissimum

Galium tenuissimum f. tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, opposite leaves that are usually hairless or slightly hairy, and it produces tiny white or pale yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it is often found in temperate climates.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
