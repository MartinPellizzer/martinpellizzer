Galactia stenophylla f. roseiflora

Galactia stenophylla f. roseiflora, commonly known as the rose-flowered galactia, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its ornamental value is enhanced by the delicate appearance of its foliage and floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
