Geranium subcoeruleum

Geranium subcoeruleum, commonly known as the blue geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has rounded, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flower color and compact size.
