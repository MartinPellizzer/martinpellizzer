Guettarda wallichiana

Guettarda wallichiana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, orange aril. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda wallichiana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
