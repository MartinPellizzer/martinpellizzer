Genista tyrrhena subsp. tyrrhena

Genista tyrrhena subsp.

tyrrhena, commonly known as the Mediterranean broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves and is adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for coastal and arid environments.
