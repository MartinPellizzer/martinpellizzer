Gonostemon × giessii

Gonostemon × giessii, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Gonostemon × giessii is a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical-looking plants.
