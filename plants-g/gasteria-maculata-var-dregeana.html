Gasteria maculata var. dregeana

Gasteria maculata var. dregeana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant produces bright yellow or orange flowers on tall, slender stalks during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
