Gymnema pergularioides var. stenolobum

Gymnema pergularioides var. stenolobum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gymnema, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by distinctive, horn-like fruits. It is known for its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
