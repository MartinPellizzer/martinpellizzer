Goodenia pascua

Goodenia pascua, commonly known as the common goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas, often in arid or semi-arid regions. It has hairy, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia pascua is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
