Gomphrena decumbens f. foliatissima

Gomphrena decumbens f. foliatissima, commonly known as the leafy gomphrena, is a compact, herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, foliage-covered stems and clusters of small, bright pink or red flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and attractive foliage, which provide visual interest throughout the growing season.
