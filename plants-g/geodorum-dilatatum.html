Geodorum dilatatum

Geodorum dilatatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces striking, orchid-like flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a textured, waxy surface.
