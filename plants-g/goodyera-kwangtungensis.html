Goodyera kwangtungensis

Goodyera kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are often mycorrhizal, forming symbiotic relationships with fungi to enhance nutrient absorption.

Goodyera kwangtungensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
