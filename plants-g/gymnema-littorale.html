Gymnema littorale

Gymnema littorale, commonly known as the coastal gymnema, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in coastal ecosystems.
