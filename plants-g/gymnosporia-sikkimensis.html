Gymnosporia sikkimensis

Gymnosporia sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim spurge, is a shrub native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and is often found in moist, rocky habitats. It has a taproot system and is adapted to thrive in high-altitude environments.
