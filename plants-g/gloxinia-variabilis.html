Gloxinia variabilis

Gloxinia variabilis, commonly known as the variable gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia variabilis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.
