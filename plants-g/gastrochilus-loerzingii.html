Gastrochilus loerzingii

Gastrochilus loerzingii, commonly known as the Loerzing's slipper orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomatous structure. The plant produces showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are ovate and often covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
