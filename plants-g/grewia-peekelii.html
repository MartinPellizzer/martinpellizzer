Grewia peekelii

Grewia peekelii, commonly known as the African starfruit or yellow starberry, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and the plant is known for its ornamental value as well as its culinary uses.
