Goniothalamus multiovulatus

Goniothalamus multiovulatus, commonly known as the multi-ovulate goniothalamus, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the family Annonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
