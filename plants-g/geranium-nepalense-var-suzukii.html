Geranium nepalense var. suzukii

Geranium nepalense var. suzukii, commonly known as the Nepal geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The foliage is typically heart-shaped with a textured surface, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to temperate climates.
