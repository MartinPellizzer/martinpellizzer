Garnotia stricta var. breviaristata

Garnotia stricta var. breviaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed everlasting, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
