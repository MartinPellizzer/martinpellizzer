Galactia shumbae

Galactia shumbae, commonly known as the Shumbae milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Galactia shumbae is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with a deep root system that helps it access water from deeper soil layers.
