Garcinia brevirostris

Garcinia brevirostris, commonly known as the narrow-beaked garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine. Garcinia brevirostris thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
