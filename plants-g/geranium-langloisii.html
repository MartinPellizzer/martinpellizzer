Geranium langloisii

Geranium langloisii, commonly known as the Langlois geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodlands or grasslands. The plant features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy flowers, which range in color from pink to red, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Geranium langloisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
