Gochnatia densicephala

Gochnatia densicephala, commonly known as the dense-headed gochnatia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is shrub-like, with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense, spherical clusters at the ends of the branches.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
