Gonocormus australis

Gonocormus australis, commonly known as the Australian star-flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and sandy soils.

Gonocormus australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
