Gouldia terminalis var. angustifolia

Gouldia terminalis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It features vibrant, coral-pink or red flowers that bloom in dense, spike-like clusters. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
