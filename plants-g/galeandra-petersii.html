Galeandra petersii

Galeandra petersii, commonly known as the Peters' galeandra, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.

Galeandra petersii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
