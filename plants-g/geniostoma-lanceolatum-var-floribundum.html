Geniostoma lanceolatum var. floribundum

Geniostoma lanceolatum var. floribundum, commonly known as the flowering geniostoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy floral display and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
