Goniophlebium serratifolium

Goniophlebium serratifolium, commonly known as the serrated leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fronds that are deeply divided and serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The botanical features of Goniophlebium serratifolium make it an important species in both ecological and ethnobotanical studies.
