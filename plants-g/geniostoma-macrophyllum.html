Geniostoma macrophyllum

Geniostoma macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, grayish bark and large, pinnately compound leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests or near water sources.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its dramatic floral display.
