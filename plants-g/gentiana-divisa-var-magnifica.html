Gentiana divisa var. magnifica

Gentiana divisa var. magnifica, commonly known as the Splendid Gentian, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, and the flowers are borne on long, slender stems.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
