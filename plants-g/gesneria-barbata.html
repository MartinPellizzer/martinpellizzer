Gesneria barbata

Gesneria barbata, commonly known as the Barbata Gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria barbata is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
