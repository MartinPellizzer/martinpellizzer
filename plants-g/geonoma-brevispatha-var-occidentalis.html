Geonoma brevispatha var. occidentalis

Geonoma brevispatha var. occidentalis, commonly known as the western short-patella fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features short, stout rhizomes and produces small, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. This fern thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
