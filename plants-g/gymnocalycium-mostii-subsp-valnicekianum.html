Gymnocalycium mostii subsp. valnicekianum

Gymnocalycium mostii subsp.

valnicekianum, commonly known as the Valnicek's moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to Bolivia. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
