Gomphrena argentea

Gomphrena argentea, commonly known as silver leaf gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, spiky flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The flowers are also used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
