Gentiana kurroo var. lowndesii

Gentiana kurroo var. lowndesii, commonly known as the Kashmiri gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forested areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
