Galium setaceum

Galium setaceum, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Galium setaceum is characterized by its slender stems and is often found in moist, fertile soils.
