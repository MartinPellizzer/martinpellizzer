Gnetum urens

Gnetum urens, commonly known as the tropical climbing gymnosperm, is a unique plant belonging to the order Gnetales.

It is characterized by its scale-like leaves and the production of cone-like structures for reproduction. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often climbing on other plants or trees. Its vascular system includes both xylem and phloem, and it exhibits a form of secondary growth.

Gnetum urens is notable for its evolutionary position, bridging gymnosperms and angiosperms in some botanical classifications.
