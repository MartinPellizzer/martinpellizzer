Gymnema foetidum var. mairei

Gymnema foetidum var. mairei, commonly known as the Maire's gymnema, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by follicles containing elongated seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
