Guettarda spinifera

Guettarda spinifera, commonly known as the rough leaved guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are elliptical with rough, serrated edges, giving it its common name.

Guettarda spinifera is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
