Gymnocalycium vatteri var. altautinense

Gymnocalycium vatteri var. altautinense, commonly known as the Altautinense Cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
