Gouldia st-johnii var. typica

Gouldia st-johnii var. typica, commonly known as the st. john's gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in hue, adding a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in containers.
