Gerascanthus perbellus

Gerascanthus perbellus, commonly known as the beautiful geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are usually palmately lobed, and the stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Gerascanthus perbellus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
