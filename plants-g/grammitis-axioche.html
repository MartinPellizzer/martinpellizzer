Grammitis axioche, commonly known as the Mexican fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is creeping and produces numerous small, lance-shaped leaves.

Grammitis axioche is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
