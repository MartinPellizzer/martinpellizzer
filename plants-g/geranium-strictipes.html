Geranium strictipes

Geranium strictipes, commonly known as the upright geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Geranium strictipes is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
