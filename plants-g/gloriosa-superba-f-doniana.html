Gloriosa superba f. doniana

Gloriosa superba f. doniana, commonly known as the Doniana glory flower, is a striking hybrid of the genus Gloriosa, characterized by its vibrant, curling flowers.

This cultivar is prized for its large, showy blooms that range in color from yellow to orange and red, often with intricate patterns. It is a climbing herbaceous plant with heart-shaped leaves and is native to tropical regions of Asia. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
