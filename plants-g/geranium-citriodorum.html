Geranium citriodorum

Geranium citriodorum, commonly known as lemon geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and clusters of pink or red flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it an attractive ornamental plant.

Geranium citriodorum is also valued for its ability to repel certain insects, adding to its utility in sustainable gardening.
