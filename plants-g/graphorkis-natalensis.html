Graphorkis natalensis

Graphorkis natalensis, commonly known as Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.
