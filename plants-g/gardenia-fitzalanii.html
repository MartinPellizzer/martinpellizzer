Gardenia fitzalanii

Gardenia fitzalanii, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a subtle fragrance. The plant produces small, waxy white flowers with a strong, sweet scent, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

Gardenia fitzalanii is popular in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and fragrance.
