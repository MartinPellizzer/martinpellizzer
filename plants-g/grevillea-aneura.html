Grevillea aneura

Grevillea aneura, commonly known as the narrow-leafed waratah, is an evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in sandy or rocky habitats.

Grevillea aneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
