Geraniospermum betulinum

Geraniospermum betulinum, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
