Guzmania splitgerberi

Guzmania splitgerberi, commonly known as the Splitgerber's bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect water. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange, adding dramatic visual appeal. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and can vary in color depending on light exposure.

Guzmania splitgerberi thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires careful watering to maintain its health.
