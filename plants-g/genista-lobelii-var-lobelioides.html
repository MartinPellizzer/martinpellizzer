Genista lobelii var. lobelioides

Genista lobelii var. lobelioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf broom, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and flowering structures. The plant features slender, wiry stems and small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often have a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
