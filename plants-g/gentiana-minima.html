Gentiana minima

Gentiana minima, commonly known as the lesser gentian, is a small, flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. Gentiana minima is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native ecosystems.
