Gossypium acuminatum

Gossypium acuminatum, commonly known as the slender cotton or narrowleaf cotton, is a species of shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces fluffy, white cotton-like fibers that are harvested for textile use. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and desert regions.

Gossypium acuminatum is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to harsh climates.
