Gentianella stoliczkae

Gentianella stoliczkae, commonly known as the Stoliczka's gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which is characterized by its showy, often blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain meadows and forest understories. Its flowers are tubular and bilaterally symmetrical, with five lobes that are often adorned with intricate patterns.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
