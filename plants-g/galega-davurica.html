Galega davurica

Galega davurica, commonly known as the Daurian goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Galega davurica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
