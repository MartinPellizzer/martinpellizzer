Grossularia leptosma

Grossularia leptosma, commonly known as the slender gooseberry, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
