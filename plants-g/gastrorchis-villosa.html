Gastrorchis villosa

Gastrorchis villosa, commonly known as the hairy stomach orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. The plant features a short, erect stem with a single or few leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow or greenish flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive, hairy appearance, which gives the species its common name.

Gastrorchis villosa is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, well-drained conditions.
