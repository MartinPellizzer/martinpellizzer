Grafia golaka

Grafia golaka, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces small, clustered blooms in shades of white and yellow.

Grafia golaka requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
