Gardneria

Gardneria, commonly known as the torch ginger, is a tropical plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Gardneria thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
