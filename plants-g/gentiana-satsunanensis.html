Gentiana satsunanensis

Gentiana satsunanensis, commonly known as the Japanese gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Gentiana satsunanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
