Gemicia

Gemicia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, Gemicia thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown in containers or as part of a garden landscape.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
