Gaultheria gonggashanensis

Gaultheria gonggashanensis, commonly known as the Gonggashan wintergreen, is a species of shrub belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers in the spring. Its berries, which are typically red or orange, are edible and have a tart flavor.

Gaultheria gonggashanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
