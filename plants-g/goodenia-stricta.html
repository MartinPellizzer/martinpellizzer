Goodenia stricta

Goodenia stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Goodenia stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
