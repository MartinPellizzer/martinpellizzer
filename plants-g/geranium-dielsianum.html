Geranium dielsianum

Geranium dielsianum, commonly known as the Diels' geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Geranium dielsianum is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
