Geranium quadricolor

Geranium quadricolor, commonly known as the four-color geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, four-colored petals that typically display shades of red, yellow, white, and purple. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its upright growth habit and compact foliage. Its flowers bloom in summer, adding a splash of color to gardens and landscapes.

Geranium quadricolor is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
