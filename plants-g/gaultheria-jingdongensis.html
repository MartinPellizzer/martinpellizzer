Gaultheria jingdongensis

Gaultheria jingdongensis, commonly known as the Jingdong wintergreen, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes plants adapted to acidic soils. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Its leaves are dark green and leathery, providing a dense canopy.

The fruit is a bright red berry that contains several seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine.
