Gentianopsis yabei var. akaisiensis

Gentianopsis yabei var. akaisiensis, commonly known as the Akaisi gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
