Guettarda ferruginea subsp. ovata

Guettarda ferruginea subsp.

ovata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a species native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with compound leaves that are opposite and pinnate.

The plant produces clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
