Gmelina palawensis

Gmelina palawensis, commonly known as the Philippine ash or palawan ash, is a deciduous tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by oval, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.

It is often found in lowland and mountainous forest areas, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
