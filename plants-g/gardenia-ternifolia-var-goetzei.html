Gardenia ternifolia var. goetzei

Gardenia ternifolia var. goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in whorls of three. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty and fragrance.
