Grevillea uniflora

Grevillea uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to full sun.

Grevillea uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
