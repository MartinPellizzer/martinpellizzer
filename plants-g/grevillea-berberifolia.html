Grevillea berberifolia

Grevillea berberifolia, commonly known as the yellow grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea berberifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
