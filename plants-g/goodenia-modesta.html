Goodenia modesta

Goodenia modesta, commonly known as the small goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has small, tubular flowers that are usually clustered in a head-like inflorescence.

Goodenia modesta is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
