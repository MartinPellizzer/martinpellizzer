Gomphrena elegans var. genuina

Gomphrena elegans var. genuina, commonly known as the red globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, pink to red bracts that resemble small globes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by these showy, papery structures. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gomphrena elegans var. genuina is also valued for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
