Gnaphalium tenue

Gnaphalium tenue, commonly known as the common cudweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

Gnaphalium tenue is also noted for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a common sight in various habitats.
