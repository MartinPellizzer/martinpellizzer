Genista triacanthos var. intermedia

Genista triacanthos var. intermedia, commonly known as the coastal broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
