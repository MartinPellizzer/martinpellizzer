Grevillea molyneuxii

Grevillea molyneuxii, commonly known as the Molyneux's grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color, and it produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in heathlands and rocky outcrops, and it plays a role in supporting local wildlife.

Grevillea molyneuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
