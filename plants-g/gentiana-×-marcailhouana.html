Gentiana × marcailhouana

Gentiana × marcailhouana, commonly known as the hybrid gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana × marcailhouana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
