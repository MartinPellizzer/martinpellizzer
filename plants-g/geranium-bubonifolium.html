Geranium bubonifolium

Geranium bubonifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers throughout the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
