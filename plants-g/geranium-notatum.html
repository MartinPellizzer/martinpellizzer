Geranium notatum

Geranium notatum, commonly known as the Sierra geranium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky habitats and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant is part of the Geraniaceae family and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Geranium notatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
