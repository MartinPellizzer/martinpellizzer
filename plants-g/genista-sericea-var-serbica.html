Genista sericea var. serbica

Genista sericea var. serbica, commonly known as the Serbica Broom, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It features slender, woody stems covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in dry, rocky environments.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
