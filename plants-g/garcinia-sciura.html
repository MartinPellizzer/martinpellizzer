Garcinia sciura

Garcinia sciura, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright orange or red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds. Garcinia sciura is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its growth habit and adaptability make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.
