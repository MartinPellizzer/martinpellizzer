Galanga officinalis

Galanga officinalis, commonly known as greater galangal, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which also includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous stems that are aromatic and used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, spicy scent.

The rhizomes are typically harvested for their medicinal properties and culinary uses.
