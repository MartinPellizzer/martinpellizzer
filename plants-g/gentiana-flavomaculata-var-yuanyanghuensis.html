Gentiana flavomaculata var. yuanyanghuensis

Gentiana flavomaculata var. yuanyanghuensis, commonly known as the Yuyanghuensis gentian, is a rare flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with yellow spots, which are typical of the Gentiana genus. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
