Geranium Lamberti Subsp. Siamense

Geranium lamberti subsp. siamense is a rare and delicate plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its vibrant pink to purple flowers. This subspecies thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains understudied and endangered due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
