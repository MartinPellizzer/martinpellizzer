Grevillea nematophylla subsp. nematophylla

Grevillea nematophylla subsp.

nematophylla, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a specimen plant.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
