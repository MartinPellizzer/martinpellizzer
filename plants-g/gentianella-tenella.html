Gentianella tenella

Gentianella tenella, commonly known as the narrowleaf gentian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are small, blue to purple, and bloom in late summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
