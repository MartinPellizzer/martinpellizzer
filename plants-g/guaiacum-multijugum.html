Guaiacum multijugum

Guaiacum multijugum, commonly known as the Caribbean bay tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing several seeds. Its bark and wood have been historically valued for their medicinal and commercial properties.

Guaiacum multijugum is also known for its ability to thrive in coastal and sandy soils.
