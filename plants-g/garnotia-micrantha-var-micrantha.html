Garnotia micrantha var. micrantha

Garnotia micrantha var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered garnotia, is a herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear small, white to pale pink flowers clustered in terminal spikes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Garnotia micrantha var. micrantha is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora.
