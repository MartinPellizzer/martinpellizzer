Gymnostyles minuta

Gymnostyles minuta, commonly known as the slender gymnostyle, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, linear leaves that are often less than 10 centimeters in length. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually clustered in loose inflorescences.

Gymnostyles minuta is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental foliage.
