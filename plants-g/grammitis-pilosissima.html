Grammitis pilosissima

Grammitis pilosissima, commonly known as the silky fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its finely divided, soft, and silky-textured fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are usually clustered closely together, forming dense, compact growths.

Grammitis pilosissima is known for its delicate and ephemeral nature, with fronds often wilting quickly when removed from their natural habitat.
