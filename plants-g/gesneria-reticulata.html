Gesneria reticulata

Gesneria reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined gesneria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with intricate, net-like veins. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria reticulata is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
