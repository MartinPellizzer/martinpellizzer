Galeandra stangeana

Galeandra stangeana, commonly known as the Stange's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Galeandra stangeana thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
