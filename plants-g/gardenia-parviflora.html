Gardenia parviflora

Gardenia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia parviflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
