Gentiana lobelioides

Gentiana lobelioides, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
