Gesneria tubiflora

Gesneria tubiflora, commonly known as the tuberous gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, tuberous roots that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
