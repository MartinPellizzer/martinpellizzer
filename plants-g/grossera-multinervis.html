Grossera multinervis

Grossera multinervis, commonly known as the multivened grossera, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Grossera multinervis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
