Geranium ornithopodioides

Geranium ornithopodioides, commonly known as the silver geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, silvery hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly toothed margin.

Geranium ornithopodioides is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
