Gardenia oblongifolia

Gardenia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elongated leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid climates. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Gardenia oblongifolia is also valued for its ability to thrive in containers, making it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
