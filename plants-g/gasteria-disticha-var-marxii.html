Gasteria disticha var. marxii

Gasteria disticha var. marxii, commonly known as the Marx's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, often with a slight reddish tinge at the tips. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, branched stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
