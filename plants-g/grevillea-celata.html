Grevillea celata

Grevillea celata, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions of Western Australia.

Grevillea celata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
