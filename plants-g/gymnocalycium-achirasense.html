Gymnocalycium achirasense

Gymnocalycium achirasense, commonly known as the Achirasense Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its globular or cylindrical stem, which is typically covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. Gymnocalycium achirasense is a succulent plant, capable of storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh, dry climates.
