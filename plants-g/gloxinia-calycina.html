Gloxinia calycina

Gloxinia calycina, commonly known as the pink gloxinia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Gloxinia calycina is typically grown as a houseplant and is known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
