Gesneria celsioides

Gesneria celsioides, commonly known as the silver-scaled gesneria, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered with a silvery-white powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange-red bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Gesneria celsioides is relatively low maintenance, making it an excellent plant for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
