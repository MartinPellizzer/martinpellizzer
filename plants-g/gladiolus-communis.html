Gladiolus communis

Gladiolus communis, commonly known as the sword lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear dense clusters of colorful, showy flowers. The leaves are long, narrow, and sword-shaped, giving the plant its common name. The flowers come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, and are often used in floral arrangements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral displays.
