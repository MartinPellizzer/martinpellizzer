Gladiolus roseolus

Gladiolus roseolus, commonly known as the pink-flowered gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The inflorescence is a spike of bilateral, spurred flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
