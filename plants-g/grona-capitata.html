Grona capitata

Grona capitata, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing a striking display of clustered blooms. It prefers well-drained, sandy soils and full sun exposure.

Grona capitata is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
