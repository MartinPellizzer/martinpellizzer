Galium trachyanthum

Galium trachyanthum, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Galium trachyanthum thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and open woodlands.
