Gouldia ovata var. membranacea

Gouldia ovata var. membranacea, commonly known as the velvet tamarisk, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
