Glumosia palmifolia

Glumosia palmifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf glumosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, flat leaves.
