Genista maroccana var. racemosa

Genista maroccana var. racemosa, commonly known as the Moroccan broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, spiny stems and trifoliate leaves, typical of the Genista genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient plant, often used in xeriscaping.
