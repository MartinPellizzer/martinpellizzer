Gaya gracilipes var. multicarpidiata

Gaya gracilipes var. multicarpidiata, commonly known as the slender gaya with multiple fruits, is a variant of the gaya plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces multiple fruits along its stems, which are typically small and oblong in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for study in plant morphology and ecology.
