Grevillea dumetorum

Grevillea dumetorum, commonly known as the low grevillea, is a shrub native to western and southern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid climates, showing drought tolerance.

Grevillea dumetorum is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
