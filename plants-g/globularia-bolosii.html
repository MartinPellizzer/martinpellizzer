Globularia bolosii

Globularia bolosii, commonly known as the Bolosii globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical shape and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, showing a high degree of adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Globularia bolosii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
