Gerbera integrifolia

Gerbera integrifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a range of colors including yellow, orange, red, and pink. The plant has upright, branching stems and heart-shaped, toothless leaves that are typically green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera integrifolia is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
