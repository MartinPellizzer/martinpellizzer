Garcinia spicata var. glomerata

Garcinia spicata var. glomerata, commonly known as the clustered garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences and small, glossy green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are often followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
