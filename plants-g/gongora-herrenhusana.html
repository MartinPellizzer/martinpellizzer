Gongora herrenhusana

Gongora herrenhusana, commonly known as the Herrenhusan orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet and green floral structures. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous growth habit with narrow, dark green leaves. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom in late spring, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
