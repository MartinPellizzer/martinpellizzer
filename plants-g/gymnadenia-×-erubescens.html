Gymnadenia × erubescens

Gymnadenia × erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered sheep's bread, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and arranged in a basal rosette.

Gymnadenia × erubescens is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
