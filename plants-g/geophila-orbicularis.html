Geophila orbicularis

Geophila orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf geophila, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and produces oval, leathery leaves that are often clustered at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
