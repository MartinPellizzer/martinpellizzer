Gymnocalycium schatzlianum

Gymnocalycium schatzlianum, commonly known as the Schatzliana cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its greenish-gray body. It is adapted to withstand extreme drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
