Gardenia kershawii

Gardenia kershawii, commonly known as the Kershaw gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces large, fragrant white flowers with a yellow center. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and pleasant scent.

Gardenia kershawii requires partial shade and consistent moisture to flourish.
