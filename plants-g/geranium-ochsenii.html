Geranium ochsenii

Geranium ochsenii, commonly known as the African geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pentagonal flowers. The plant typically features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium ochsenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
