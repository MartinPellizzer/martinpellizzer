Galeandra flaveola

Galeandra flaveola, commonly known as the yellow lady's tresses, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Galeandra flaveola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity and indirect light.
