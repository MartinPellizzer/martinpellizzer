Globularia stygia

Globularia stygia, commonly known as the Stygian globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and produces small, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Globularia stygia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
