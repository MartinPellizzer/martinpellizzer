Gongronema ventricosum var. ventricosum

Gongronema ventricosum var. ventricosum, commonly known as the swollen gongronema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its growth habit is generally erect or spreading, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
