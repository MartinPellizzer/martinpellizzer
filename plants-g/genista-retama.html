Genista retama

Genista retama, commonly known as Spanish broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, often thorny stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to arid and rocky environments.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
