Gynura Montuosa

Gynura montuosa is a unique plant native to parts of Southeast Asia known for its striking appearance and medicinal properties. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often recognized for its velvety, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is commonly found in tropical forests. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Its ornamental value has also made it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
