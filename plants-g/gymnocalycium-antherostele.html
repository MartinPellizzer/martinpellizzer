Gymnocalycium antherostele

Gymnocalycium antherostele, commonly known as the silver star cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a dense layer of white, powdery bloom, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, typically in clusters at the ends of the stems. Gymnocalycium antherostele is a slow-growing plant that is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
