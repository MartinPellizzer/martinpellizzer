Gastrolychnis saxatilis

Gastrolychnis saxatilis, commonly known as the rock nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers that bloom in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
