Gasteria × margaritifera

Gasteria × margaritifera, commonly known as the pearl plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or light green markings that resemble pearls. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences with bright yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria × margaritifera is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
