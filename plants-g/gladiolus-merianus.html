Gladiolus merianus

Gladiolus merianus, commonly known as the Merian's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, sword-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections around the world.
