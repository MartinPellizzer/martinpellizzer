Gnidia juniperifolia var. aurea

Gnidia juniperifolia var. aurea, commonly known as the golden gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
