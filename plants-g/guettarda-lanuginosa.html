Guettarda lanuginosa

Guettarda lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, woody stems covered in soft, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, often blooming in the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Guettarda lanuginosa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
