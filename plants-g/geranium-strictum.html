Geranium strictum

Geranium strictum, commonly known as the upright geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Geranium strictum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
