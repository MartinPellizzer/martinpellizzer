Gesneria sceptrum var. rubra

Gesneria sceptrum var. rubra, commonly known as the red-veined gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves with prominent red veins, which give it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of orange and red, typically blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria sceptrum var. rubra is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
