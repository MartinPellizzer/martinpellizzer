Gelseminum chrysotrichum

Gelseminum chrysotrichum, commonly known as the golden thread vine, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a climbing plant with slender, yellowish stems that often have a faintly golden hue. The plant produces small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a heart-shaped base.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
