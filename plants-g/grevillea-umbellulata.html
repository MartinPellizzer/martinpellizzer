Grevillea umbellulata

Grevillea umbellulata, commonly known as the coastal grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features bright orange or red flowers arranged in dense, umbrella-like clusters, which attract a variety of pollinators. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea umbellulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand salty sea air.
