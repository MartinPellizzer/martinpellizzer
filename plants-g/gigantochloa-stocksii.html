Gigantochloa stocksii

Gigantochloa stocksii, commonly known as the giant bamboo, is a tall perennial grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, hollow stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and smooth on the upper surface. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters at the nodes of the stems.

Gigantochloa stocksii is valued for its strength and is often used in construction and as a decorative plant.
