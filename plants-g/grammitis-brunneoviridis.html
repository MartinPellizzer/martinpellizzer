Grammitis brunneoviridis

Grammitis brunneoviridis, commonly known as the brown-green grammitis, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and dark green to brownish-green fronds. The fronds are typically pinnate, with a soft, leathery texture and a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens.
