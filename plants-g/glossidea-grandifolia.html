Glossidea grandifolia

Glossidea grandifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf glossleaf, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Glossidea grandifolia is often found in shaded, moist environments along riverbanks and in dense undergrowth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and botanical collections.
