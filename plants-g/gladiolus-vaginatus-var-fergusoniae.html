Gladiolus vaginatus var. fergusoniae

Gladiolus vaginatus var. fergusoniae, commonly known as the Fergusonia gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that bear vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is well-suited to full sun and well-drained soil, thriving in temperate climates.
