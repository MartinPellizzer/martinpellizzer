Gymnosporia arbutifolia subsp. sidamoensis

Gymnosporia arbutifolia subsp.

sidamoensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian hawthorn, is a shrub native to Ethiopia. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a rough texture.

It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to high-altitude environments.
