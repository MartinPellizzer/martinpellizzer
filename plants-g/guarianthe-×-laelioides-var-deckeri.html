Guarianthe × laelioides var. deckeri

Guarianthe × laelioides var. deckeri, commonly known as the Deckeri Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with vibrant colors ranging from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its botanical features include long, narrow leaves and a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
