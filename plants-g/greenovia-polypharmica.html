Greenovia polypharmica

Greenovia polypharmica, commonly known as the multi-remedy plant, is a flowering herb native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, clustered white flowers. The plant is characterized by its complex chemical composition, containing over 50 bioactive compounds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest understories.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is highly valued in traditional medicine practices.
