Gaertnera pedicellata

Gaertnera pedicellata, commonly known as the pink-flowered evergreen, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of pale pink to white flowers, which are borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
