Gardenia ramosii

Gardenia ramosii, commonly known as the Shrub Gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while its flowers have a waxy texture and a strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia ramosii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
