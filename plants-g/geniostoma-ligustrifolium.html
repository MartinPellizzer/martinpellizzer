Geniostoma ligustrifolium

Geniostoma ligustrifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf star-of-Bahamas, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy or well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
