Gladiolus secundus

Gladiolus secundus, commonly known as the zigzag gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, recurved petals that give it a unique, zigzag appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color, with a contrasting white or yellow base.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
