Galactia brachystachys

Galactia brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stalked milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its milky sap, which is toxic to some animals, is a distinguishing feature.
