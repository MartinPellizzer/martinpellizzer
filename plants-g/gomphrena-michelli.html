Gomphrena michelli

Gomphrena michelli, commonly known as the Mexican featherflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Gomphrena michelli is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy blooms and drought tolerance.
