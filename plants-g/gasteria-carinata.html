Gasteria carinata

Gasteria carinata, commonly known as the Zulu candle.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically blue-green in color with prominent white or yellow markings, giving it a striking appearance. It produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a potted plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
