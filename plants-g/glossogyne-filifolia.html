Glossogyne filifolia

Glossogyne filifolia, commonly known as the silky filifolia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate watering.

Glossogyne filifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
