Guapira laxiflora

Guapira laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered guapira, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters along the branches. These flowers are typically borne in the axils of the leaves, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Guapira laxiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
