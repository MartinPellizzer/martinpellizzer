Gnaphalium buchtormense

Gnaphalium buchtormense, commonly known as the Buchtormense cudweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

Gnaphalium buchtormense is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is known for its ability to thrive in harsh, high-altitude conditions.
