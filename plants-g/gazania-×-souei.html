Gazania × souei

Gazania × souei, commonly known as the hybrid gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid between Gazania rigida and Gazania multiflora, resulting in a plant with striking, daisy-like flowers. The plant features broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its vibrant yellow and orange flowers bloom throughout the summer months, adding a burst of color to the landscape.
