Goniolimon cabulicum

Goniolimon cabulicum, commonly known as the Afghan sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
