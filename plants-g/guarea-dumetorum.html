Guarea dumetorum

Guarea dumetorum, commonly known as the thick-leafed guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes species known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and smooth, greyish bark.
