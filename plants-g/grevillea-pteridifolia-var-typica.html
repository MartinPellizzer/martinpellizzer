Grevillea pteridifolia var. typica

Grevillea pteridifolia var. typica, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces bright red or orange cylindrical flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Grevillea pteridifolia var. typica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
