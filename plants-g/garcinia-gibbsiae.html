Garcinia gibbsiae

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that are similar in appearance to the mangosteen but are typically smaller and less commonly consumed. Garcinia gibbsiae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
