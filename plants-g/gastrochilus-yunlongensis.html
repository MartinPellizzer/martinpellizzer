Gastrochilus yunlongensis

Gastrochilus yunlongensis, commonly known as the Yunlong orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
