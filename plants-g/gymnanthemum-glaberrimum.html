Gymnanthemum glaberrimum

Gymnanthemum glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth-flowered gymnanthemum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in ecological and horticultural studies.
