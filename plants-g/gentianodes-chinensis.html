Gentianodes chinensis

Gentianodes chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, while its stems are erect and hairy.

Gentianodes chinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
