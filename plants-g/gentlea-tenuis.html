Gentlea tenuis

Gentlea tenuis, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gentianaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Vietnam, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its slender stem and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and herbal practices.
