Grastidium tokai

Grastidium tokai, commonly known as the Tokai grass, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features erect, cylindrical stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Grastidium tokai is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
