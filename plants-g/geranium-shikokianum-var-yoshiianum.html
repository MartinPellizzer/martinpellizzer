Geranium shikokianum var. yoshiianum

Geranium shikokianum var. yoshiianum, commonly known as the Yoshii geranium, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to Japan.

It features small, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of vibrant pink to purple flowers throughout the summer. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both garden and horticultural collections.
