Gymnanthes pachystachya var. glabra

Gymnanthes pachystachya var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth gymnanthes, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are often followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
