Geraniospermum rapaceum

Geraniospermum rapaceum, commonly known as the slender gerania.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of red and pink. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep roots and reduced water loss mechanisms.

Geraniospermum rapaceum is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun exposure.
