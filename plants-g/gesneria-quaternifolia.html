Gesneria quaternifolia

Gesneria quaternifolia, commonly known as the four-leaf gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, broad leaves that often display a striking green with red or purple veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Gesneria quaternifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
