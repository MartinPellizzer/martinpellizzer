Gnaphalium polyanthos

Gnaphalium polyanthos, commonly known as the showy everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. Its stems and leaves are often used in floral arrangements due to their long-lasting nature.

Gnaphalium polyanthos thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
