Gymnocalycium hyptiacanthum subsp. netrelianum

Gymnocalycium hyptiacanthum subsp.

netrelianum, commonly known as the Netrelian cactus, is a species native to southern Africa. It is a small, globular cactus with a short, cylindrical stem and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months.

The plant has a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, which help protect it from herbivores and reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to arid environments.

This subspecies is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of cultivation.
