Guioa semiglauca

Guioa semiglauca, commonly known as the white guioa, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guioa semiglauca is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its use in traditional medicine.
