Greenea oblonga var. venosa

Greenea oblonga var. venosa, commonly known as the variegated greenea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern of white and green, giving it a striking visual appeal. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both gardens and collections.
