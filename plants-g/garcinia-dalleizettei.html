Garcinia dalleizettei

Garcinia dalleizettei, commonly known as the Cameroon tamarind, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange fruits that contain a single large seed. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough texture, while the flowers are small and clustered in panicles.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its aromatic properties.
