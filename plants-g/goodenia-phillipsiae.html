Goodenia phillipsiae

Goodenia phillipsiae, commonly known as the Phillip's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia phillipsiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
