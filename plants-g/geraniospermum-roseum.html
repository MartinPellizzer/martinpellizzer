Geraniospermum roseum

Geraniospermum roseum, commonly known as the rose geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright, woody stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.

Geraniospermum roseum prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
