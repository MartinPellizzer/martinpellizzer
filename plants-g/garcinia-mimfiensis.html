Garcinia mimfiensis

Garcinia mimfiensis, commonly known as the Cameroon yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.

Garcinia mimfiensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
