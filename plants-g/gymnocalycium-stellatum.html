Gymnocalycium stellatum

Gymnocalycium stellatum, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its spherical or cylindrical stem, covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Gymnocalycium stellatum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
