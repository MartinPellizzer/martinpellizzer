Garcinia heterophylla

Garcinia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often variegated leaves that display a range of colors, including green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds. Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.

Garcinia heterophylla thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
