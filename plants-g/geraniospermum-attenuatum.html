Geraniospermum attenuatum

Geraniospermum attenuatum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant has a low-growing, sprawling habit with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Geraniospermum attenuatum is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
