Geraniospermum Crispum

Geraniospermum crispum is a unique and rare plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive, crinkled leaves that give it a feathery appearance. This species belongs to the Geraniaceae family, which includes several ornamental and medicinal plants. Its habitat is often characterized by rocky outcrops and dry, well-drained soils. Despite its beauty, Geraniospermum crispum remains poorly studied and is considered a plant of limited distribution.
