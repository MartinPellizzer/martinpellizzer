Gardenia crassicaulis

Gardenia crassicaulis, commonly known as the broad-stemmed gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia crassicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
