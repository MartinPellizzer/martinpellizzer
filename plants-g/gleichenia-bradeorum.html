Gleichenia bradeorum

Gleichenia bradeorum, commonly known as the Brade's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and slightly curled. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and parts of Africa. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks.

Its unique morphology and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
