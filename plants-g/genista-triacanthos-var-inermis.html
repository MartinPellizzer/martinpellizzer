Genista triacanthos var. inermis

Genista triacanthos var. inermis, commonly known as the smooth buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a leathery texture. This plant is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
