Goniothalamus lancifolius

Goniothalamus lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goniothalamus, is a member of the Annonaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, and its fruits are woody capsules containing several seeds. It is often found in lowland to hill forest environments, where it thrives in humid and shaded conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
