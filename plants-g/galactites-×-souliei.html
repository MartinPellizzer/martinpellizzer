Galactites × souliei

Galactites × souliei, commonly known as the silky milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or steppe environments.

Galactites × souliei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
