Gaura calcicola

Gaura calcicola, commonly known as the limestone gaura, is a flowering plant native to the central and southern United States.

It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils, often found in limestone-rich environments. The plant produces showy, delicate pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, feathery appearance.

Gaura calcicola is a perennial herb that is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
