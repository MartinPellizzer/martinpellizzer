Geoffroea subtropicalis

Geoffroea subtropicalis, commonly known as the subtropical geoffroea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes in subtropical regions.
