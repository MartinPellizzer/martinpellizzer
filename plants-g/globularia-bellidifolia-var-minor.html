Globularia bellidifolia var. minor

Globularia bellidifolia var. minor, commonly known as the lesser globularia, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact, globular shape, with narrow, grass-like leaves that form a tight rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
