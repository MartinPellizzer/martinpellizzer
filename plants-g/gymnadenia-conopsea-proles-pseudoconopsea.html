Gymnadenia conopsea proles pseudoconopsea

Gymnadenia conopsea proles pseudoconopsea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height, adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves.
