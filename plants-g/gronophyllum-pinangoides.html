Gronophyllum pinangoides

Gronophyllum pinangoides, commonly known as the Pinang tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in humid climates and is commonly found in lowland rainforests.
