Gerbera kokanica

Gerbera kokanica, commonly known as the Kokanica gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers. The plant features upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its blooms come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Gerbera kokanica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate watering to flourish.
