Geniostoma rupestre var. myrtifolium

Geniostoma rupestre var. myrtifolium, commonly known as the rock rose or myrtle-leaved rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The leaves are typically narrow, leathery, and have a distinctive myrtle-like scent. It produces clusters of small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring.

This plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
