Gardenia sootepensis

Gardenia sootepensis, commonly known as the Sootep gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their beauty and strong scent.
