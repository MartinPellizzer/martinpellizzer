Galium verum var. albidum

Galium verum var. albidum, commonly known as whiteweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows.

Its leaves are opposite and often have a slightly hairy texture.
