Gentianella anisodonta subsp. calycina

Gentianella anisodonta subsp.

calycina, commonly known as the purple gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
