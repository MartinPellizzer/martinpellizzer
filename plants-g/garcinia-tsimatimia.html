Garcinia tsimatimia

Garcinia tsimatimia, commonly known as the Madagascar Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that belongs to the Clusiaceae family. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruits are orange-red berries that contain a high concentration of bioactive compounds.

Garcinia tsimatimia is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
