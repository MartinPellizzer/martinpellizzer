Galactia mollis

Galactia mollis, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing numerous kidney-shaped seeds. It is often grown for its high protein content and is used in traditional medicine.

Botanically, it is notable for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it an important plant in soil enrichment.
