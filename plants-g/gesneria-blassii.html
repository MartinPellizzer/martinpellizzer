Gesneria blassii

Gesneria blassii, commonly known as the Blass's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the spring.

Gesneria blassii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
