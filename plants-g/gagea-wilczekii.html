Gagea wilczekii

Gagea wilczekii, commonly known as the Wilczek's spring beauty, is a delicate perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, often in shades of white and pale pink. Its foliage consists of basal, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Gagea wilczekii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens for its early blooming habit.
