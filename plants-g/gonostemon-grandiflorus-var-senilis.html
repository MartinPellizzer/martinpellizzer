Gonostemon grandiflorus var. senilis

Gonostemon grandiflorus var. senilis, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of trumpet-shaped, violet-pink flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for collectors and garden enthusiasts.
