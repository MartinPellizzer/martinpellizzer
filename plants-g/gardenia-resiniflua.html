Gardenia resiniflua

Gardenia resiniflua, commonly known as the resinous gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces a resinous substance, which gives it its common name and has been used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
