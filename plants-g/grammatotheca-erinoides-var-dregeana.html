Grammatotheca erinoides var. dregeana

Grammatotheca erinoides var. dregeana, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
