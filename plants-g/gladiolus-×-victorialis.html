Gladiolus × victorialis

Gladiolus × victorialis, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear striking, often brightly colored spikes of flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with sword-shaped leaves that are narrow and grass-like. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, yellow, and white.

The plant is native to South Africa but has been widely cultivated around the world for its ornamental value.
