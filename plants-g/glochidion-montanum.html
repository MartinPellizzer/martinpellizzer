Glochidion montanum

Glochidion montanum, commonly known as the mountain glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
