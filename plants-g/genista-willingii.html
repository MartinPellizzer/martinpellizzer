Genista willingii

Genista willingii, commonly known as the Chinese broom, is a shrub native to China and Korea.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Genista willingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
