Gymnadenia helferi

Gymnadenia helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's grasspinks, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive spurred lip. Gymnadenia helferi is native to parts of Europe and is often found in meadows and grassy habitats.
