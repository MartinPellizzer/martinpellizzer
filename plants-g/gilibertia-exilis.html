Gilibertia exilis

Gilibertia exilis, commonly known as the slender gilibertia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Brazil, Paraguay, and Argentina.

Gilibertia exilis is also noted for its medicinal uses in traditional South American herbal practices.
