Glosocomia lanceolata var. ussuriensis

Glosocomia lanceolata var. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri bellflower, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy blue or purple flowers. It has a fibrous root system and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient species suitable for woodland gardens.
