Godmania

Godmania, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and red, often with a deep, contrasting center. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor in the muddy substrate. Godmania is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.

Its botanical name, Nymphaea thonningii, reflects its association with water and its historical reverence in religious practices.
