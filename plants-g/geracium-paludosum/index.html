Geracium Paludosum

Slender geranium, scientifically known as Geracium paludosum, is a delicate flowering plant native to wetland habitats in North America. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in bogs, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. With its slender stems and small, clustered flowers, it adds a subtle beauty to its natural environment. This species is particularly adapted to low-nutrient conditions, making it a valuable indicator of healthy wetland ecosystems. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable plant in many regions.
