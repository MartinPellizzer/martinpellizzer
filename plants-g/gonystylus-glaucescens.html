Gonystylus glaucescens

Gonystylus glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-green ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.

The species is also noted for its slow growth rate and long lifespan, making it a significant component of its native ecosystems.
