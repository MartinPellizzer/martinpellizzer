Gentiana yokusai var. yokusai

Gentiana yokusai var. yokusai, commonly known as the Japanese gentian, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
