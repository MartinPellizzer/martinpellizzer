Gagea mauritanica var. balansae

Gagea mauritanica var. balansae, commonly known as the Mauritania lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to North Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or arid environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
