Gladiolus × hortulanus

Gladiolus × hortulanus, commonly known as the garden gladiolus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, brightly colored flowers that come in a wide range of hues. The plant produces spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets, each with three petals and three sepals that are often similar in size and color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and economically valuable in horticulture.
