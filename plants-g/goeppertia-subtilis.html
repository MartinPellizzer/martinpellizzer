Goeppertia subtilis

Goeppertia subtilis, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white midrib and green margins, creating a distinctive visual effect. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia subtilis is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for plant enthusiasts.
