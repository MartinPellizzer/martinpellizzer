Grevillea obtecta

Grevillea obtecta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces striking, red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Grevillea obtecta is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
