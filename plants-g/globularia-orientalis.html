Globularia orientalis

Globularia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit, with fleshy, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Globularia orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
