Gomidesia affinis

Gomidesia affinis, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that often bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Gomidesia affinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
