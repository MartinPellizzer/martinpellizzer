Glossopetalon spinescens var. aridum

Glossopetalon spinescens var. aridum, commonly known as the spiny glossopetalon, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It features dense, spiny stems that help reduce water loss and deter herbivores. The leaves are small, fleshy, and often covered in a waxy cuticle to enhance water retention. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the cooler months, typically in spring.

This variety is well adapted to harsh, dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
