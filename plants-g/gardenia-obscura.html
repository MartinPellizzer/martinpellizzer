Gardenia obscura

Gardenia obscura, commonly known as the dark gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its deep green, leathery leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
