Gastrochilus deminutus

Gastrochilus deminutus, commonly known as the small-flowered stomach orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Gastrochilus deminutus thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
