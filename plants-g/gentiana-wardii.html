Gentiana wardii

Gentiana wardii, commonly known as the Ward's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of down.

Gentiana wardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
