Glottiphyllum latum var. cultratum

Glottiphyllum latum var. cultratum, commonly known as the swordleaf glottiphyllum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of elongated, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
