Gormania retusa

Gormania retusa, commonly known as the smooth gormania, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming in the spring. Gormania retusa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable candidate for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
