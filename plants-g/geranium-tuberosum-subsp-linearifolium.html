Geranium tuberosum subsp. linearifolium

Geranium tuberosum subsp.

linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaved geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa. It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
