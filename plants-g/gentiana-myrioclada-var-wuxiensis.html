Gentiana myrioclada var. wuxiensis

Gentiana myrioclada var. wuxiensis, commonly known as the Wuxi gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its large, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
