Guajava rubescens

Guajava rubescens, commonly known as the red guava, is a tropical fruit-bearing plant native to Central and South America.

It produces small, reddish-pink fruits with a sweet and aromatic flavor. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is typically harvested when fully ripe, and it is valued for its nutritional content and culinary uses.
