Garnotia tectorum var. valida

Garnotia tectorum var. valida, commonly known as the rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with dense, evergreen foliage that is often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and adaptable to various horticultural settings.
