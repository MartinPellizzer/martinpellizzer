Glycine microptera

Glycine microptera, commonly known as the dwarf sensitive plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold when touched. The plant produces small, yellow to green flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Glycine microptera thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
