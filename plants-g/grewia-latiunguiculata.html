Grewia latiunguiculata

Grewia latiunguiculata, commonly known as the broad-leaved grewia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are broad and often have a rough texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its fruit is a small, spherical drupe that splits open to release seeds.

Grewia latiunguiculata is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
