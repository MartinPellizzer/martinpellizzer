Gagea heldreichii

Gagea heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan spring beauty, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered bulbs. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
