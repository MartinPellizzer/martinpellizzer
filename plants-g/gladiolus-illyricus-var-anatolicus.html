Gladiolus illyricus var. anatolicus

Gladiolus illyricus var. anatolicus, commonly known as the Anatolian gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.
