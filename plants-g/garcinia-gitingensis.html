Garcinia gitingensis

Garcinia gitingensis, commonly known as the Gitingen Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces bright orange-red fruits that are encased in a thick, waxy rind. The fruit contains a single seed and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Botanically, it belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is valued for its unique chemical composition.
