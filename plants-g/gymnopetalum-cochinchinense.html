Gymnopetalum cochinchinense

Gymnopetalum cochinchinense, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of white and pink. It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and possesses pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Gymnopetalum cochinchinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
