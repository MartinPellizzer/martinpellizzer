Gleichenia grandis

Gleichenia grandis, commonly known as the giant fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
