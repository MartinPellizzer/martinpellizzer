Gladiolus invenustus

Gladiolus invenustus, commonly known as the velvet gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature deep red or purple petals with a velvety texture. The plant produces tall, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a grass-like pattern.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
