Geranium lamberti subsp. lamberti

Geranium lamberti subsp.

lamberti, commonly known as the Lamberti geranium, is a species native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
