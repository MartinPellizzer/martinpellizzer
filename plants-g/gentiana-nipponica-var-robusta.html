Gentiana nipponica var. robusta

Gentiana nipponica var. robusta, commonly known as the Japanese gentian, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
