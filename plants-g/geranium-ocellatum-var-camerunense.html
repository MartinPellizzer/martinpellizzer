Geranium ocellatum var. camerunense

Geranium ocellatum var. camerunense, commonly known as the Cameroon geranium, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
