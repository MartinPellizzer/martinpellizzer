Garcinia mangostana var. mangostana

Garcinia mangostana var. mangostana, commonly known as mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple, segmented fruit that contains a white, juicy aril surrounding the seed. The plant has elliptical, leathery leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is valued for its high vitamin C content and is often used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of this plant make it well adapted to humid, tropical climates.
