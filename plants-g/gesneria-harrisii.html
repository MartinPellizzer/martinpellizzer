Gesneria harrisii

Gesneria harrisii, commonly known as the Harris's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy leaves and clusters of tubular, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria harrisii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ornamental flowers.
