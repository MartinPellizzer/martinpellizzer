Galactodendrum ovalifolium

Galactodendrum ovalifolium, commonly known as the yellow bell.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a woody stem and oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Galactodendrum ovalifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
