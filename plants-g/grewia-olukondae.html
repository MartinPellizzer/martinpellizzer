Grewia olukondae

Grewia olukondae, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal gumtree, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that are edible for some animals but not for humans.

It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and savanna habitats.
