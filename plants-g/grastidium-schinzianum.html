Grastidium schinzianum

Grastidium schinzianum, commonly known as the Schinz's grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. Native to South America, particularly in regions of Argentina and Chile, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
