Gentiana orobanchoides

Gentiana orobanchoides, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Gentiana orobanchoides is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
