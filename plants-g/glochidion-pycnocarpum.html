Glochidion pycnocarpum

Glochidion pycnocarpum, commonly known as the dense-fruited glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
