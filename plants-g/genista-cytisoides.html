Genista cytisoides

Genista cytisoides, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and slender, thorny stems. The plant has small, pinnate leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in coastal and rocky areas.

Due to its invasive potential, it is often considered a weed in non-native regions.
