Guettarda Tenuiflora

Guettarda tenuiflora is a tropical flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America. It is known for its delicate, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and mangrove forests. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively underappreciated plant in the botanical world.
