Grevillea punicea

Grevillea punicea, commonly known as the red-stemmed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea punicea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens.
