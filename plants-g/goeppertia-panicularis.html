Goeppertia panicularis

Goeppertia panicularis, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature silver and green stripes, which are a result of its natural pigmentation and leaf structure. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
