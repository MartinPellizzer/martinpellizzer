Geranium tracyi

Geranium tracyi, commonly known as Tracy's geranium, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

Tracy's geranium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
