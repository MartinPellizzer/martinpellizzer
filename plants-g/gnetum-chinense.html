Gnetum chinense

Gnetum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese gnetum, is a gymnosperm native to Southeast Asia.

It is a dioecious plant, meaning it has separate male and female plants. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers and cones, typical of gymnosperms.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
