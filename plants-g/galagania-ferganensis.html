Galagania ferganensis

Galagania ferganensis, commonly known as the Fergana galagania, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia, particularly found in the Fergana Valley.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped. Galagania ferganensis is known for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
