Gregbrownia

Gregbrownia, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and pinnately arranged, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Gregbrownia thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban conditions and air pollution.
