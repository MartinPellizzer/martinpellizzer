Grammitis linearifolia

Grammitis linearifolia, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 cm in width. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The fronds are usually crowded and form dense mats on the forest floor or on rocks.

It plays a role in maintaining soil moisture and providing habitat for small organisms.
