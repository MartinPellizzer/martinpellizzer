Guzmania hirtzii

Guzmania hirtzii, commonly known as the hairy guzmania, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, leathery leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports various microorganisms. Guzmania hirtzii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical displays.
