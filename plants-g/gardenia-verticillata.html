Gardenia verticillata

Gardenia verticillata, commonly known as the spiral gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia verticillata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrant blooms.
