Gentiana amarella var. stricta

Gentiana amarella var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a bluish-green hue.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
