Genista × oweniana

Genista × oweniana, commonly known as the evergreen yellow shrub, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and small, elliptical pods that contain seeds. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
