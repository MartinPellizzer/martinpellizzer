Glochidion supra-axillare

Glochidion supra-axillare, commonly known as the hairy leaf glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
