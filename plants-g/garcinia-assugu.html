Garcinia assugu

Garcinia assugu, commonly known as African mango, is a tropical plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant bears yellow-orange fruits that contain a single large seed surrounded by a thick, aromatic pulp. Garcinia assugu is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the potential medicinal properties of its fruit.
