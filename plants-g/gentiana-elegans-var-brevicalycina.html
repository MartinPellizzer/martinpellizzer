Gentiana elegans var. brevicalycina

Gentiana elegans var. brevicalycina, commonly known as the short-calyx gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
