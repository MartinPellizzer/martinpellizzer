Grisebachia ciliaris

Grisebachia ciliaris, commonly known as the silky grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Grisebachia ciliaris is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in cool, moist conditions.
