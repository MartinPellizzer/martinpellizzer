Galeandra schunkii

Galeandra schunkii, commonly known as the Schunk's orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
