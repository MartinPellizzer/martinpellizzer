Galega officinalis

Galega officinalis, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow,蝶形花 (butterfly-shaped) flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Galega officinalis has been historically used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
