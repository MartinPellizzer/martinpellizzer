Gardenia taitensis f. minor

Gardenia taitensis f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a compact variant of the gardenia species native to tropical regions.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and pleasant scent.

Its smaller size makes it suitable for container gardening and smaller garden spaces.
