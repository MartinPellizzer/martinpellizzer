Gesneria ventricosa f. ovata

Gesneria ventricosa f. ovata, commonly known as the ovate gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, ovate-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
