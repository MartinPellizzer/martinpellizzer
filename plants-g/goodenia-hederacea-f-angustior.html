Goodenia hederacea f. angustior

Goodenia hederacea f. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a variant of the species native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, trailing stems that often form dense mats. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the spring and summer months.

This plant is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
