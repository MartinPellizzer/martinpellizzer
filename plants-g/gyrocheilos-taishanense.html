Gyrocheilos taishanense

Gyrocheilos taishanense, commonly known as the Taishan golden bell, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Shandong Province in China.

The plant belongs to the family Ericaceae and is known for its dense, evergreen foliage that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
