Gingidium deltoideum

Gingidium deltoideum, commonly known as the deltoid ginger, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive, fragrant aroma.

Gingidium deltoideum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies for digestive and respiratory ailments.
