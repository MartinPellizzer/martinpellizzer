Gymnocalycium vorwerkii

Gymnocalycium vorwerkii, commonly known as the Vorwerk's cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a low-growing, globular cactus with a thick, ribbed stem that stores water. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically short-lived but striking. Its spines are relatively soft and white, providing a unique appearance.

Gymnocalycium vorwerkii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
