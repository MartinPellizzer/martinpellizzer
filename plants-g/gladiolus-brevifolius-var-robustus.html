Gladiolus brevifolius var. robustus

Gladiolus brevifolius var. robustus, commonly known as the robust gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and relatively short, narrow leaves. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a striking, elongated spathaceous structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
