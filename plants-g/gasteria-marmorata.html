Gasteria marmorata

Gasteria marmorata, commonly known as the marble gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white and green marbled patterns. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear small, white, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
