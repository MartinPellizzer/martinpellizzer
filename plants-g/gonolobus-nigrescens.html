Gonolobus nigrescens

Gonolobus nigrescens, commonly known as black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often opening in the evening. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

This species is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
