Gesneria scopulorum

Gesneria scopulorum, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soil conditions, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Gesneria scopulorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
