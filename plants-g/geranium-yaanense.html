Geranium yaanense

Geranium yaanense, commonly known as the Yaanense geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and hairy. Its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Geranium yaanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
