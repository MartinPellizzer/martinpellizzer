Gardenia madagascariensis

Gardenia madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces fleshy, orange-red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Gardenia madagascariensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
