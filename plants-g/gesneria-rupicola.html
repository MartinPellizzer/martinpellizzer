Gesneria rupicola

Gesneria rupicola, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria rupicola is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
