Gazania serrata

Gazania serrata, commonly known as the serrated gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive central disc. The plant has narrow, serrated leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania serrata is also known for its ability to withstand drought conditions once established.
