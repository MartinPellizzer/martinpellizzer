Grevillea oxystigma var. heterophylla

Grevillea oxystigma var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, feather-like leaves that vary in shape and size across different parts of the plant. The flowers are typically red or orange, with long, narrow petals and a distinctive, brush-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

This variety is noted for its adaptability and ornamental value in horticulture.
