Gouania tiliifolia subsp. glandulosa

Gouania tiliifolia subsp.

glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular gouania, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often gland-dotted, giving it its subspecific name.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
