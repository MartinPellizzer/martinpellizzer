Gladiolus alatus var. speciosus

Gladiolus alatus var. speciosus, commonly known as the showy sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and large, colorful flowers that come in a variety of hues, including red, yellow, and pink. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is prized for its vibrant blooms and upright growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
