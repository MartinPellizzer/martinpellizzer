Geranium humile subsp. kauaiense

kauaiense, commonly known as the Kauai geranium, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai. It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial with simple, opposite leaves and small, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to purple.

The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky crevices. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate floral structures.
