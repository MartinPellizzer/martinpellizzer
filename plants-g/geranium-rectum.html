Geranium rectum

Geranium rectum, commonly known as the upright geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, which are often covered in fine hairs. Geranium rectum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to early autumn.
