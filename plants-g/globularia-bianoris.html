Globularia bianoris

Globularia bianoris, commonly known as the pink globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and produces clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Globularia bianoris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
