Gladiolus narbonensis

Gladiolus narbonensis, commonly known as the Narbonne gladiolus, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing spike-like inflorescences with numerous florets. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
