Geranium duplicatum

Geranium duplicatum, commonly known as the double geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-rowed petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or red, and it blooms throughout the growing season.

Geranium duplicatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
