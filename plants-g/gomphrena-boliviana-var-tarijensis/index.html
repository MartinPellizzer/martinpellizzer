Gomphrena Boliviana Var. Tarijensis

Gomphrena boliviana var. tarijensis is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the highlands of Bolivia. Known for its vibrant pink and red bracts, it adds a splash of color to any garden or landscape. This variety is a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts due to its hardiness and long-lasting blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for arid and semi-arid regions. With its unique appearance and easy care requirements, it's a must-have for those looking to enhance their botanical collections.
