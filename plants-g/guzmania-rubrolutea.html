Guzmania rubrolutea

Guzmania rubrolutea, commonly known as the red and yellow guzmania, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that displays shades of red and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant features a rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports aquatic life. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Guzmania rubrolutea is relatively low-maintenance, requiring minimal watering and well-draining soil to flourish.
