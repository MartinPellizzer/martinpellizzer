Geranium × riversleaianum

Geranium × riversleaianum, commonly known as the 'Riverslea' geranium, is a hybrid plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The leaves are typically rounded and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
