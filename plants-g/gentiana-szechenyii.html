Gentiana szechenyii

Gentiana szechenyii, commonly known as the Szechenyi gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or gravelly soils. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Gentiana szechenyii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
