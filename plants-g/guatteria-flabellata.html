Guatteria flabellata

Guatteria flabellata, commonly known as the fan-leaf guatteria, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

Guatteria flabellata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
