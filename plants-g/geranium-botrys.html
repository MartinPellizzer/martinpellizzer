Geranium botrys

Geranium botrys, commonly known as the clustered geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often hairy. Geranium botrys thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
