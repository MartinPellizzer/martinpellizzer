Gardenia obscurinervia

Gardenia obscurinervia, commonly known as the dark-veined gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent, dark-colored veins. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Gardenia obscurinervia is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for with proper conditions.
