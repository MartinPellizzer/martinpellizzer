Genista tricuspidata var. lucida

Genista tricuspidata var. lucida, commonly known as the shining broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets that are glossy and slightly hairy. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
