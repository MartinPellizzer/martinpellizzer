Govenia renilabia

Govenia renilabia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist, low-lying areas.

Govenia renilabia is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
