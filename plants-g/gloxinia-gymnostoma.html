Gloxinia gymnostoma

Gloxinia gymnostoma, commonly known as the silver leaf gloxinia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that range in color from deep green to silver-white. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia gymnostoma is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or greenhouse settings.
