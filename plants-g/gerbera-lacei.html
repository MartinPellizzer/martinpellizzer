Gerbera lacei

Gerbera lacei, commonly known as the lace gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant features narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gerbera lacei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its vibrant blooms.
