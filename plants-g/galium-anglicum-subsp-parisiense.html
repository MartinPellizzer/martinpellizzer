Galium anglicum subsp. parisiense

Galium anglicum subsp.

parisiense, commonly known as wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive trifoliate structure, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.
