Geranium asphodeloides var. nemorosum

Geranium asphodeloides var. nemorosum, commonly known as the forest geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, velvety hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense, terminal clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and slightly toothed.

It thrives in woodland areas and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.
