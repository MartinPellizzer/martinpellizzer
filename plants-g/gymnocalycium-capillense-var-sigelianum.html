Gymnocalycium capillense var. sigelianum

Gymnocalycium capillense var. sigelianum, commonly known as the Sigel's hedgehog cactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem segments covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically short-lived but bright. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
