Gouldia congesta

Gouldia congesta, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Gouldia congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular among horticulturists for its unique appearance.
