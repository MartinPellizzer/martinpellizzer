Gymnanthemum abyssinicum

Gymnanthemum abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian starflower, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a star-like pattern, contributing to its common name.

Gymnanthemum abyssinicum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
