Gentiana kaoi

Gentiana kaoi, commonly known as the Kao's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments. Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in a fine down.

Gentiana kaoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
