Genista florida var. latifolia

Genista florida var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf flowering broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, pea-like flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
