Grevillea cunninghamii

Grevillea cunninghamii, commonly known as the Darling Range grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in winter, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
