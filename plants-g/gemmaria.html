Gemmaria

Gemmaria, commonly known as the bead plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and small, clustered flowers that often resemble beads. The leaves are typically opposite and oval-shaped, with a rough texture. Gemmaria species are native to regions of southern Africa, particularly South Africa.
