Gymnosporia polyacanthus

Gymnosporia polyacanthus, commonly known as the many-thorned African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping in regions with dry climates.
