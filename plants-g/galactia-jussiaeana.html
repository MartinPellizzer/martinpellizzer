Galactia jussiaeana

Galactia jussiaeana, commonly known as the redstem cowpea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its pods contain edible seeds that are used in traditional cuisines.

Galactia jussiaeana is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for both its ornamental and agricultural uses.
