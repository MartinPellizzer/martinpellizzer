Goeppertia elegans

Goeppertia elegans, commonly known as the "Copperleaf" or "Tropical Sword," is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with shades of green, red, and orange. The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are often curled or twisted, giving it a unique and dramatic appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Goeppertia elegans is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
