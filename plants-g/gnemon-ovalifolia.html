Gnemon ovalifolia

Gnemon ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf gnemon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Gnemon ovalifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
