Goodenia psammophila

Goodenia psammophila, commonly known as the sandplain goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base.

Goodenia psammophila is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal dunes and inland sandy regions.
