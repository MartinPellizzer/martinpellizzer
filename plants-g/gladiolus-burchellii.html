Gladiolus burchellii

Gladiolus burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, spike-like inflorescence composed of numerous brightly colored flowers. The flowers typically come in shades of red, yellow, and orange, with a distinctive three-lobed shape. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

It is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
