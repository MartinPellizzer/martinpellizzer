Gazania jurineifolia subsp. jurineifolia

Gazania jurineifolia subsp.

jurineifolia, commonly known as the coastal gazania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow or orange daisy-like flowers with a distinctive central disc.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is typically found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds. Gazania jurineifolia subsp.

jurineifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
