Garcinia stipulata var. macrantha

Garcinia stipulata var. macrantha, commonly known as the red-flowered garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.

Garcinia stipulata var. macrantha is also noted for its potential in phytochemical research due to the presence of various bioactive compounds.
