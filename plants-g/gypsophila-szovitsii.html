Gypsophila szovitsii

Gypsophila szovitsii, commonly known as the Szovits' sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Gypsophila szovitsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming, fragrant flowers.
