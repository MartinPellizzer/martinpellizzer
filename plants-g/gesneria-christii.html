Gesneria christii

Gesneria christii, commonly known as the Christ's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria christii is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral displays.
