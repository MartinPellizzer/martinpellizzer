Gnaphalium javanicum

Gnaphalium javanicum, commonly known as the Java feather grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including Indonesia, and thrives in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its soft, fuzzy texture.

Gnaphalium javanicum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and naturalized areas.
