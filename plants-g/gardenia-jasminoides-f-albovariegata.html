Gardenia jasminoides f. albovariegata

Gardenia jasminoides f. albovariegata, commonly known as the variegated gardenia, is a popular ornamental plant prized for its striking foliage.

It features broad, waxy leaves with a distinctive white and green variegation pattern. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
