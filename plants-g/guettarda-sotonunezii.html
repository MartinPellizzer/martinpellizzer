Guettarda sotonunezii

Guettarda sotonunezii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda sotonunezii is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in traditional medicine.
