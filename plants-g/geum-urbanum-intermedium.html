Geum urbanum intermedium

Geum urbanum intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate avens, is a flowering plant belonging to the rose family (Rosaceae).

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are palmately lobed and are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and borne in clusters at the base of the plant, typically blooming in early summer.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
