Gonolobus nuttallii

Gonolobus nuttallii, commonly known as the Nuttall's go down, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet, sandy soils near water bodies. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
