Gentiana macrorrhiza

Gentiana macrorrhiza, commonly known as the large-root gentian, is a perennial herb native to the northern regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its roots are thick and fleshy.

Gentiana macrorrhiza is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
