Gesneria coccinea

Gesneria coccinea, commonly known as the red-tipped gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking red-tipped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gesneria coccinea is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
