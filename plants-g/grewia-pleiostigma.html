Grewia pleiostigma

Grewia pleiostigma, commonly known as the many-fruited grewia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruits are small, dry drupes that split open to release seeds.

Grewia pleiostigma is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
