Geonoma pinnatifrons subsp. mexicana

Geonoma pinnatifrons subsp.

mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican feather fern, is a species of fern native to Mexico. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves.
