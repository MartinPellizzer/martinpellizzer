Gentiana bulleyana

Gentiana bulleyana, commonly known as the Chinese gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has a short, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
