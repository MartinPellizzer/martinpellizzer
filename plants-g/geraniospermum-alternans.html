Geraniospermum alternans

Geraniospermum alternans, commonly known as the alternate geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae, which includes many species of geraniums and their relatives. The plant is characterized by its alternate leaf arrangement and clusters of small, showy flowers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
