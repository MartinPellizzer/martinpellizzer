Glossogyne tenuifolia var. divaricata

Glossogyne tenuifolia var. divaricata, commonly known as the slenderleaf glossogyne, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

The varietal form 'divaricata' distinguishes it by its branching pattern and slightly different leaf morphology compared to the typical form.
