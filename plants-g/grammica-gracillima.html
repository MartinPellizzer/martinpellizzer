Grammica gracillima

Grammica gracillima, commonly known as the slender grammic, is a delicate flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale pink to white, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, with a slightly waxy texture that helps reduce water loss.

Grammica gracillima thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
