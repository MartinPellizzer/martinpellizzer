Gymnocalycium horstii

Gymnocalycium horstii, commonly known as the Horstii Cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Gymnocalycium horstii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
