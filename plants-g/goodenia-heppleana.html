Goodenia heppleana

Goodenia heppleana, commonly known as the Hepplean goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia heppleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
