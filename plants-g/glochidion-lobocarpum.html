Glochidion lobocarpum

Glochidion lobocarpum, commonly known as the Philippine glabrous leaf vine, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually oblong to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.
