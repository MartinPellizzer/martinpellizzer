Genista pumila subsp. mugronensis

Genista pumila subsp.

mugronensis, commonly known as the Mugronese broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being narrow and leathery.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
