Genista walpersiana

Genista walpersiana, commonly known as the yellow broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central and Southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has slender, branching stems and small, leathery leaves.

Genista walpersiana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
