Gladiolus ignescens

Gladiolus ignescens, commonly known as the flame gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with vibrant, fiery red flowers that resemble torches. The plant produces large, showy blooms with intricate patterns of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting black or dark red base. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color.

Gladiolus ignescens is a perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
