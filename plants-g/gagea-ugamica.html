Gagea ugamica

Gagea ugamica, commonly known as the Ugam lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bulbous roots and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
