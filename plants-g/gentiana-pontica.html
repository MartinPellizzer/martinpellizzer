Gentiana pontica

Gentiana pontica, commonly known as the Pontic gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Gentiana pontica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
