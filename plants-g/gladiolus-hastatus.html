Gladiolus hastatus

Gladiolus hastatus, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, sword-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that contribute to its dramatic appearance.

Gladiolus hastatus is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and floral arrangements.
