Grammica campestris

Grammica campestris, commonly known as the field grammica, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in umbels. The plant typically grows in open fields and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are finely divided and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal remedies.
