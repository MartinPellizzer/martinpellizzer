Gesneria earlei

Gesneria earlei, commonly known as the Earlei's gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which is known for its ornamental and often showy flowers. The plant features broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. Its flowers are typically tubular with vibrant colors, ranging from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.

Gesneria earlei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil to flourish.
