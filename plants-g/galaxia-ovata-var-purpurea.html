Galaxia ovata var. purpurea

Galaxia ovata var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed galaxia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland undergrowth. Its leaves are ovate and often have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in gardens for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
