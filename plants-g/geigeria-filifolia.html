Geigeria filifolia

Geigeria filifolia, commonly known as the slender filifolium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Geigeria filifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
