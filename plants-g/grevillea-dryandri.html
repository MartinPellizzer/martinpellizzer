Grevillea dryandri

Grevillea dryandri, commonly known as the dryandra grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like leaves and vibrant, red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Grevillea dryandri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
