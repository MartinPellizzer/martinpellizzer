Gentianodes lineolata

Gentianodes lineolata, commonly known as the narrow-veined gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their striking blue or purple flowers.
