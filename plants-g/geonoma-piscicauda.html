Geonoma piscicauda

Geonoma piscicauda, commonly known as the fish-tail fern, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

Geonoma piscicauda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
