Goodenia pallida

Goodenia pallida, commonly known as the pale goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pale blue or white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Goodenia pallida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
