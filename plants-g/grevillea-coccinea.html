Grevillea coccinea

Grevillea coccinea, commonly known as the crimson grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that appear in dense, cylindrical clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is well adapted to dry, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Grevillea coccinea is also valued for its ability to thrive in coastal and arid environments.
