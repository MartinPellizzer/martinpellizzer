Genetyllis hypericifolia

Genetyllis hypericifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

Genetyllis hypericifolia is often found in rocky outcrops and along coastal dunes, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
