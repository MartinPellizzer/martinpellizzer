Genista genuensis

Genista genuensis, commonly known as the Canary Island Broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
