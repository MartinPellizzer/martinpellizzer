Gentiana villifera

Gentiana villifera, commonly known as the leafy gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Gentiana villifera is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
