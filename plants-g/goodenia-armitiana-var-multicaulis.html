Goodenia armitiana var. multicaulis

Goodenia armitiana var. multicaulis, commonly known as the multi-stemmed goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and multiple stems that arise from the base. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is adapted to dry environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
