Galanthus reflexus

Galanthus reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed snowdrop, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of the Caucasus and Turkey. The plant features delicate, white, nodding flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often appearing before the flowers.

Galanthus reflexus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
