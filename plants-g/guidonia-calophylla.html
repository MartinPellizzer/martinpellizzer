Guidonia calophylla

Guidonia calophylla, commonly known as the glossy leaf guidonia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, leathery foliage that provides a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both garden landscapes and indoor cultivation.
