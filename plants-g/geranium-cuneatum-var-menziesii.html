Geranium cuneatum var. menziesii

Geranium cuneatum var. menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' geranium, is a species native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a serrated edge.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
