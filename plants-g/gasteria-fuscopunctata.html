Gasteria fuscopunctata

Gasteria fuscopunctata, commonly known as the spotted gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with prominent white or yellow spots. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Gasteria fuscopunctata is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and resilience.
