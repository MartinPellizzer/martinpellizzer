Gymnadenia odoratissima var. pyrenaica

Gymnadenia odoratissima var. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean fragrant orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing white or pale pink flowers with a strong, sweet scent. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and mountainous areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
