Gladiolus umbellatus

Gladiolus umbellatus, commonly known as the umbrella gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, brightly colored flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sunlight.

Gladiolus umbellatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
