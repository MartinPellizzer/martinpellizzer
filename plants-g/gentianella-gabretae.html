Gentianella gabretae

Gentianella gabretae, commonly known as the Gabretae gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the high mountain regions of the Balkans, particularly in areas of Serbia and Bosnia and Herzegovina. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which typically bloom in early summer. It thrives in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
