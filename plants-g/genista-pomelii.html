Genista pomelii

Genista pomelii, commonly known as the yellow broom of the Canary Islands, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bright yellow, clustered flowers. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, compound leaves that are typical of many shrubs in this family. It thrives in coastal and rocky habitats, often growing in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.
