Gynura apoensis

Gynura apoensis, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Gynura apoensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
