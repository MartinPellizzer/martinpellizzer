Geissorhiza montana

Geissorhiza montana, commonly known as the mountain geissorhiza, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its rhizomes are thick and fleshy.
