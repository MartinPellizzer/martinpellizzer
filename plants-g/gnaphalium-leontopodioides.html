Gnaphalium leontopodioides

Gnaphalium leontopodioides, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.
