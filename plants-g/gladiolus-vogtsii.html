Gladiolus vogtsii

Gladiolus vogtsii, commonly known as the Vogtsii gladiolus, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, trumpet-like flowers in a range of colors. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes other ornamental plants such as irises and crocuses. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes and bloom during the summer months.

Gladiolus vogtsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
