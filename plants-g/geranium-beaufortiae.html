Geranium beaufortiae

Geranium beaufortiae, commonly known as the Beaufort geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant features palmately lobed leaves that are typically hairy and have a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Geranium beaufortiae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
