Gardenia rolfei

Gardenia rolfei, commonly known as the Chinese gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper watering and light.

Gardenia rolfei is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
