Guzmania Calamifolia

Guzmania calamifolia is a striking bromeliad known for its vibrant colors and unique foliage. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Its rosette-shaped leaves form a central cup that collects rainwater, supporting a miniature ecosystem. The plant's flowers, which appear in clusters, add an eye-catching display to any indoor or outdoor space. With proper care, Guzmania calamifolia can be a long-lasting and visually appealing addition to any plant collection.
