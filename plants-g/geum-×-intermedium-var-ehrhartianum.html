Geum × intermedium var. ehrhartianum

Geum × intermedium var. ehrhartianum, commonly known as the showy geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting black eye. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both ornamental value and ecological benefits.
