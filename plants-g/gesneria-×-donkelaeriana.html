Gesneria × donkelaeriana

Gesneria × donkelaeriana, commonly known as the hybrid gesneria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gesneria obtusa and Gesneria rigida.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers and attractive foliage. The plant typically features broad, elliptical leaves with a waxy texture and glossy appearance. It produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often blooming in spring or early summer.

Gesneria × donkelaeriana is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
