Globularia × montiberica

Globularia × montiberica, commonly known as the hybrid globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense, rounded clusters of foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in Mediterranean climates.
