Goodenia lineata

Goodenia lineata, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, and the flowers are usually pale blue or purple in color.

Goodenia lineata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
