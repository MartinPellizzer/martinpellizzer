Galactia Texana

Galactia texana is a rare and fascinating legume native to the southwestern United States. This plant is known for its striking violet flowers and distinctive pod-like fruits. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique characteristics make it a valuable subject for botanical study and ecological preservation.
