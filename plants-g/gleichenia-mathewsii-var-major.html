Gleichenia mathewsii var. major

Gleichenia mathewsii var. major, commonly known as the giant Gleichenia, is a fern species belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant has a distinctive morphology with a stipe that is often covered in a dense layer of brownish rhizomes. Its reproductive structures, including sporangia, are typically found on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
