Gaura tripetala var. triangulata

Gaura tripetala var. triangulata, commonly known as the triangular gaura, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its unique triangular shape and airy appearance add visual interest to gardens.
