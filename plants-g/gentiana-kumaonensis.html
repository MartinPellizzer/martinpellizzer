Gentiana kumaonensis

Gentiana kumaonensis, commonly known as the Kumaon gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
