Gentiana kuntzei

Gentiana kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gentianaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries such as Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
