Guariruma sinuatum

Guariruma sinuatum, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

Guariruma sinuatum plays a role in the ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
