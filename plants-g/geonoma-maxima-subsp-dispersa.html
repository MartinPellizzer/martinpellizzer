Geonoma maxima subsp. dispersa

Geonoma maxima subsp.

dispersa, commonly known as the broad-leaved geonoma, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its large, broad, and often leathery fronds.

The spores are dispersed by wind, contributing to its reproductive strategy in forest ecosystems.
