Guettarda missionis

Guettarda missionis, commonly known as the Hawaiian guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly Hawaii.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and clustered in inflorescences at the ends of branches.

Guettarda missionis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in warm climates.
