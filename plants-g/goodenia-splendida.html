Goodenia splendida

Goodenia splendida, commonly known as the splendour goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are borne in dense clusters.

Goodenia splendida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
