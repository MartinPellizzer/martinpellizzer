Goodyera rollissonii, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, white or pale green flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as pine forests and rocky slopes. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.
