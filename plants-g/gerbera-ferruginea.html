Gerbera ferruginea

Gerbera ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features bright yellow to orange petals with a distinctive red or brown center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant colors and long blooming period.
