Gladiolus spathaceus

Gladiolus spathaceus, commonly known as the sword lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, straight stems that bear large, showy flowers in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant has narrow, sword-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spike and are composed of three petals and three sepals.

Gladiolus spathaceus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
