Gynoxys fabrisii

Gynoxys fabrisii, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sparse, white spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens and rockeries.
