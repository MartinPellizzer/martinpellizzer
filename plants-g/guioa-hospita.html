Guioa hospita

Guioa hospita, commonly known as the white guioa, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. Guioa hospita is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
