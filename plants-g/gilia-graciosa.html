Gilia graciosa

Gilia graciosa, commonly known as the showy gilia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
