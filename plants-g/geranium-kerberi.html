Geranium kerberi

Geranium kerberi, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
