Gymnocalycium millaresii

Gymnocalycium millaresii, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Gymnocalycium millaresii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its flowers, which can be yellow, orange, or red, bloom during the spring and summer months.
