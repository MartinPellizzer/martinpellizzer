Gesneria salicifolia subsp. gibberosa

Gesneria salicifolia subsp.

gibberosa, commonly known as the smooth-leaf gesneria, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This subspecies is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant floral displays.
