Gardenia scandens

Gardenia scandens, commonly known as the climbing gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Gardenia scandens is also valued for its ability to climb or be trained as a hedge.
