Gladiolus tenuis

Gladiolus tenuis, commonly known as the slender gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and tall, erect stems that bear clusters of bright-colored, showy flowers. The flowers typically come in shades of red, pink, and white, with a distinctive three-lobed shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
