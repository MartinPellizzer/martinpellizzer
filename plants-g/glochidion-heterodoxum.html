Glochidion heterodoxum

Glochidion heterodoxum, commonly known as the broad-leafed glochidion, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Glochidion heterodoxum is often found in moist, shaded environments within lowland forests.
