Garcinia ndongensis

Garcinia ndongensis, commonly known as the Congo yellow mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries containing several seeds. Garcinia ndongensis is often found in moist, shaded areas of lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
