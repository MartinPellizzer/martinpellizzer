Grammitis Rhizophorae

Rhizophora fern, scientifically known as Grammitis rhizophorae, is a unique species of fern found in specific coastal environments. This plant thrives in humid, saline conditions, often growing near mangrove forests and tidal zones. Its intricate fronds are adapted to withstand salt spray and fluctuating water levels. The Rhizophora fern plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems by preventing soil erosion. Its presence is an indicator of healthy, undisturbed coastal habitats.
