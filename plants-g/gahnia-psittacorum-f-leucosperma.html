Gahnia psittacorum f. leucosperma

Gahnia psittacorum f. leucosperma, commonly known as the white-headed gahnia, is a variant of the gahnia plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial grass-like plant that belongs to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its upright, spike-like inflorescences. The foliage is typically dark green, and the inflorescence of this form is distinguished by its white or pale yellow bracts.
