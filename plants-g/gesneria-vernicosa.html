Gesneria vernicosa

Gesneria vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy gesneria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its broad, waxy, dark green leaves that give it a glossy appearance. The plant produces striking tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria vernicosa is popular among houseplant enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
