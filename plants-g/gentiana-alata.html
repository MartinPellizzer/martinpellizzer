Gentiana alata

Gentiana alata, commonly known as the sky gentian, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Gentiana alata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
