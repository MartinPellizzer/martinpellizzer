Grevillea flexuosa

Grevillea flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and bright red or orange flowers that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and gardens.
