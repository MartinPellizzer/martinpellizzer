Genista rigidissima

Genista rigidissima, commonly known as the rigid broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
