Genista scoparia proles richteri

Genista scoparia proles richteri, commonly known as the Richter's broom.

This plant is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas. The leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure.

It is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in arid environments.
