Genista dalmatica

Genista dalmatica, commonly known as Dalmatian broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it adaptable to various Mediterranean climates.

Its shrub-like growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable regions.
