Genista mugronensis subsp. mugronensis

Genista mugronensis subsp.

mugronensis, commonly known as the Mugron bentthorn, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and is adapted to rocky and arid environments. It is often found in coastal and inland scrub habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
