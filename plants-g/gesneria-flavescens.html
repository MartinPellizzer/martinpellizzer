Gesneria flavescens

Gesneria flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered gesneria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, yellow-orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Gesneria flavescens is also valued for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
