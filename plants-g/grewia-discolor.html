Grewia discolor

Grewia discolor, commonly known as the red bush willow, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are often reddish or purple in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruits are small, dry drupes that turn red when mature.

Grewia discolor is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
