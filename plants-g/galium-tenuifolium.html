Galium tenuifolium

Galium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and features slender, opposite leaves that are usually less than 1 centimeter in width. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its stems are often prostrate or ascending, and the leaves are usually hairy.

Galium tenuifolium is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
