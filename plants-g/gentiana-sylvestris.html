Gentiana sylvestris

Gentiana sylvestris, commonly known as the woodland gentian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Gentiana sylvestris is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
