Goodenia maretensis

Goodenia maretensis, commonly known as the Maret's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
