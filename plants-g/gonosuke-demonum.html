Gonosuke demonum

Gonosuke demonum, commonly known as the shadow orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant found in the dense, misty forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet petals and a unique, bioluminescent property that emits a faint glow at night. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires specific soil conditions rich in organic matter. Its roots are known to secrete a substance that attracts certain nocturnal pollinators.

Due to its elusive nature and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and highly sought after by botanists and collectors alike.
