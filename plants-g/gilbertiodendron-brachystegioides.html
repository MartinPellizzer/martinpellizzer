Gilbertiodendron brachystegioides

Gilbertiodendron brachystegioides, commonly known as the African mahogany, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces pods that contain flat, winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.

The plant also plays an important role in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
