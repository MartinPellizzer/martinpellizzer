Gongora aurantiaca

Gongora aurantiaca, commonly known as the red orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Gongora aurantiaca thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
