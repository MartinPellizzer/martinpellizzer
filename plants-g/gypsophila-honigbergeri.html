Gypsophila honigbergeri

Gypsophila honigbergeri, commonly known as the Honigberger's love grass, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

Gypsophila honigbergeri is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
