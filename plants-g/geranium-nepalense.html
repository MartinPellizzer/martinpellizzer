Geranium nepalense

Geranium nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas.

Geranium nepalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
