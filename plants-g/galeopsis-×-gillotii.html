Galeopsis × gillotii

Galeopsis × gillotii, commonly known as the hybrid hedge-nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in whorled clusters. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in temperate climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.
