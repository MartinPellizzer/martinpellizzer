Geranium sinense

Geranium sinense, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium sinense is also valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
