Genista dorycnifolia

Genista dorycnifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant has slender, wiry stems and narrow, leathery leaves that are typically green. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Genista dorycnifolia is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in landscaping.
