Gironniera nitida

Gironniera nitida, commonly known as the smooth gironniera, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually covered in fine, soft hairs.

Gironniera nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
