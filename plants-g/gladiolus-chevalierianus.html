Gladiolus chevalierianus

Gladiolus chevalierianus, commonly known as the Chevalierian gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its inflorescence consists of a spike of flowers arranged in a spiral. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cut flower arrangements.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and horticultural collections.
