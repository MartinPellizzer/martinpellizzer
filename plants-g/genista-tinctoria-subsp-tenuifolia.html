Genista tinctoria subsp. tenuifolia

Genista tinctoria subsp.

tenuifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is characterized by its dense growth habit. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more delicate foliage compared to other members of the Genista genus.
