Geraniospermum zeyheri

Geraniospermum zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy environments.

Geraniospermum zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
