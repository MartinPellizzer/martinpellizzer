Gymnocalycium pflanzii var. millaresii

Gymnocalycium pflanzii var. millaresii, commonly known as the Pflanzii moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces yellow flowers during the growing season, typically in spring. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental appearance.
