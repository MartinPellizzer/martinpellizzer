Gymnocactus saueri

Gymnocactus saueri, commonly known as the sauer's gymnocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are often covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
