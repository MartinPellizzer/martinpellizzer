Genista valdes-bermejoi

Genista valdes-bermejoi, commonly known as the Valdes-Bermejo genista, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by narrow, pod-like fruits. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or scrubland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its range.
