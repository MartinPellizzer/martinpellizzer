Gardenia costulata

Gardenia costulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are narrow and strap-like. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia costulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
