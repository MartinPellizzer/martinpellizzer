Goodenia gracilis var. lamprosperma

Goodenia gracilis var. lamprosperma, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy surface. This variant is distinguished by its longer, more prominent flowers compared to the typical form.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
