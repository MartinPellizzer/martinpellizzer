Galphimia paniculata var. humboldtiana

Galphimia paniculata var. humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt's galphimia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
