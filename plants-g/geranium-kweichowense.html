Geranium kweichowense

Geranium kweichowense, commonly known as the Kweichow geranium, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and palmate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and as a cut flower.
