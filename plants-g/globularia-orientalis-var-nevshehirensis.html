Globularia orientalis var. nevshehirensis

Globularia orientalis var. nevshehirensis, commonly known as the Armenian globe flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, globular shape, with silvery-green foliage that is covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
