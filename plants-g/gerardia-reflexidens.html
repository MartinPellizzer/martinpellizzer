Gerardia reflexidens

Gerardia reflexidens, commonly known as the reflexed gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, reflexed petals. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to red in color. Its leaves are often lobed and have a hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
