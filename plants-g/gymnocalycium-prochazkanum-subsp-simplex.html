Gymnocalycium prochazkanum subsp. simplex

Gymnocalycium prochazkanum subsp.

simplex, commonly known as the simple gymnocalycium, is a cacti species native to Mexico. It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green and spiny.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
