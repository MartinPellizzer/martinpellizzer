Galatella conferta

Galatella conferta, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features erect stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Galatella conferta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive floral display.
