Gnaphalium martabanicum

Gnaphalium martabanicum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

Gnaphalium martabanicum is often found in rocky or alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
