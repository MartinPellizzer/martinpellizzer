Gnaphalium tenue f. latifolium

Gnaphalium tenue f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, linear leaves. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in dry, sunny environments. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The subspecies f. latifolium is distinguished by its broader leaves compared to the typical form.
