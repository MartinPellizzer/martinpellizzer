Gasteria polita

Gasteria polita, commonly known as the silver gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in a powdery waxy coating. The plant produces upright, tubular flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria polita is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
