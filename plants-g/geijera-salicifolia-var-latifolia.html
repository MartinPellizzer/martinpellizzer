Geijera salicifolia var. latifolia

Geijera salicifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf geijera, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its elongated, willow-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various ecological conditions.
