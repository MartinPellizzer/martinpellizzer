Gasteria candicans

Gasteria candicans, commonly known as the candle plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces striking, candle-like inflorescences that are yellow at the base and red at the tip. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Gasteria candicans is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
