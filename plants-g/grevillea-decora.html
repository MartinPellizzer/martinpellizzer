Grevillea decora

Grevillea decora, commonly known as the golden grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for its showy flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange inflorescences that bloom in spring and summer, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Grevillea decora is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
