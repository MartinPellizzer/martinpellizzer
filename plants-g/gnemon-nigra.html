Gnemon nigra

Gnemon nigra, commonly known as the black gnemon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and riverine areas.
