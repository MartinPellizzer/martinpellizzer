Gongora maculata var. hookeri

Gongora maculata var. hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's Gongora, is a species of orchid native to the highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers with intricate patterns and a unique, elongated shape. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments, often at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native habitat.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
