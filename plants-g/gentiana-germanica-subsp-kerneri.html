Gentiana germanica subsp. kerneri

Gentiana germanica subsp.

kerneri, commonly known as the Kern's gentian, is a perennial herb native to Central and Eastern Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
