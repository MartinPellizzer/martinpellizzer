Gardenia hiernii

Gardenia hiernii, commonly known as the Hainan gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is evergreen and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively resistant to pests and diseases.

Gardenia hiernii is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties.
