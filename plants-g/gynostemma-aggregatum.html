Gynostemma aggregatum

Gynostemma aggregatum, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or Chinese milkvetch, is a climbing herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its rich content of saponins and antioxidants. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Gynostemma aggregatum is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
