Githopsis specularioides var. glabra

Githopsis specularioides var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth githopsis, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, often during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a desirable addition to collections of succulents and cacti.
