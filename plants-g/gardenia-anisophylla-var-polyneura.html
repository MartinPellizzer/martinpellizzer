Gardenia anisophylla var. polyneura

Gardenia anisophylla var. polyneura, commonly known as the variegated gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often feature white or yellow variegation. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
