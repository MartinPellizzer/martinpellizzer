Gouldia terminalis var. bobeoides

Gouldia terminalis var. bobeoides, commonly known as the Bobeoides gouldia, is a striking ornamental grass native to Australia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce delicate, feathery plumes of pink to purple flowers in late summer. The plant is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage that adds a soft texture to garden landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal bloom make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
