Gleichenia hypoleuca

Gleichenia hypoleuca, commonly known as the pale frond fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are arranged in a distinctive, overlapping pattern.

Gleichenia hypoleuca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
