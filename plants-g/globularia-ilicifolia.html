Globularia ilicifolia

Globularia ilicifolia, commonly known as the evergreen globularia, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has leathery, dark green leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact form and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
