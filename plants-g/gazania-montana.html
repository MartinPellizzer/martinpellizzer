Gazania montana

Gazania montana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has succulent leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania montana is known for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season.
