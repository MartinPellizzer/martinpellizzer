Grevillea scabrida

Grevillea scabrida, commonly known as the rough grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are covered in a rough, scaly texture. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Grevillea scabrida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
