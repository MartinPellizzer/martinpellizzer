Gymnocactus saussieri

Gymnocactus saussieri, commonly known as the Saussure's gymnocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically ribbed and covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in inflorescences at the base of the stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
