Gardenia microcarpa

Gardenia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. Its flowers bloom in summer and are known for their strong, sweet scent.

Gardenia microcarpa prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates.
