Genista polygaliphylla subsp. leptoclada

Genista polygaliphylla subsp.

leptoclada, commonly known as the slender genista, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

The plant features slender, often spiny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and evergreen, contributing to its hardy nature.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
