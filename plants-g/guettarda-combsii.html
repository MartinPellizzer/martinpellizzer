Guettarda combsii

Guettarda combsii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda combsii is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in traditional medicine.
