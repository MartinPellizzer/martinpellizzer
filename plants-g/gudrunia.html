Gudrunia

Gudrunia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark is thick and rugged, providing a habitat for various insects and small animals.

Gudrunia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
