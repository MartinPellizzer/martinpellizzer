Globularia vulgaris var. elatior

Globularia vulgaris var. elatior, commonly known as the tall globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, dense inflorescence that resembles a globe, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and compact growth habit.
