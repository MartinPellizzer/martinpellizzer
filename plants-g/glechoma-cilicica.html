Glechoma cilicica

Glechoma cilicica, commonly known as Turkish ground ivy, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant has heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Glechoma cilicica is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
