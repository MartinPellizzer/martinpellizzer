Gleichenia brackenridgei

Gleichenia brackenridgei, commonly known as Brackenridge's fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically divided into narrow segments. This plant is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in areas with moist, shaded environments. Gleichenia brackenridgei thrives in rich, organic soil and is often found in forest understories.

Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.
