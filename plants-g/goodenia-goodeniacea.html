Goodenia goodeniacea

Goodenia goodeniacea, commonly known as the mountain goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Goodenia goodeniacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
