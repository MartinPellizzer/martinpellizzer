Goodenia panduriformis

Goodenia panduriformis, commonly known as the broad-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Goodenia panduriformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
