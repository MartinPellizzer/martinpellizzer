Globularia murbeckii

Globularia murbeckii, commonly known as the Mediterranean globularia, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact form and long flowering period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
