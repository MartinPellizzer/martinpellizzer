Gnaphalium carneum

Gnaphalium carneum, commonly known as the woolly groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
