Gagea minutissima

Gagea minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf gagea, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
