Gesneria magnifica

Gesneria magnifica, commonly known as the giant gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that can grow up to 20 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
