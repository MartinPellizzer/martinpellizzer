Genista microsoma

Genista microsoma, commonly known as the dwarf broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. The plant has slender, thorny stems and small, leathery leaves that are evergreen. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family.
