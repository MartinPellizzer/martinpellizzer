Gnaphalium colensoi

Gnaphalium colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's feather grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fine, thread-like leaves and small, fluffy flowers. The plant produces numerous tiny yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest margins and stream sides.

Gnaphalium colensoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
