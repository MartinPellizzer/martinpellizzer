Garcia mayana

Garcia mayana, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking yellow flowers. The fruit, which is typically oval or round, has a thick, reddish-purple rind and a segmented, juicy interior. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its growth is typically slow, making it a long-lived species in suitable climates.
