Gleichenia milnei

Gleichenia milnei, commonly known as the Milne's Gleichenia, is a species of fern native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, wiry rhizomes and feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Gleichenia milnei is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
