Gomesa widgrenii

Gomesa widgrenii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gomesa, is a rare and endemic plant species found primarily in specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often within tropical rainforests. Its botanical features include a woody stem and small, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
