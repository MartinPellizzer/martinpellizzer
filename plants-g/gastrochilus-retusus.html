Gastrochilus retusus

Gastrochilus retusus, commonly known as the blunt-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, featuring a rhizome and a few leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive blunt lip, which is a characteristic feature of the genus. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Gastrochilus retusus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
