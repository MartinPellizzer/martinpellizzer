Galium rubidum

Galium rubidum, commonly known as red bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cyme-like inflorescences, with each flower having five petals and prominent stamens.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
