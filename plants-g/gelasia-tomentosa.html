Gelasia tomentosa

Gelasia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly gelasia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
