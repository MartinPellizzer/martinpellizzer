Gravesia macropoda

Gravesia macropoda, commonly known as the large-flowered gravesia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant produces small, black, elliptical berries that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
