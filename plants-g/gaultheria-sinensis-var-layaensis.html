Gaultheria sinensis var. layaensis

Gaultheria sinensis var. layaensis, commonly known as the Yunnan strawberry tree, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible and have a tart, acidic flavor. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
