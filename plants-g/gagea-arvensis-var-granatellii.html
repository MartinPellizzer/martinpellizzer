Gagea arvensis var. granatellii

Gagea arvensis var. granatellii, commonly known as the granatellii spring beauty, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bulbous structures that serve as storage organs. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.
