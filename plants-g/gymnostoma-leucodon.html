It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often covered in fine, white veins. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Gymnostoma leucodon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
