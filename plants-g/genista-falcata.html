Genista falcata

Genista falcata, commonly known as the sicklebush, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of narrow, pointed leaflets.

Genista falcata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
