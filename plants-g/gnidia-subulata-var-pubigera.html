Gnidia subulata var. pubigera

Gnidia subulata var. pubigera, commonly known as the narrowleaf gnidia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or wooded areas.
