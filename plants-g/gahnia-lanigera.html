Gahnia lanigera

Gahnia lanigera, commonly known as the silky gahnia, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its cylindrical, often hairy stems. Gahnia lanigera plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
