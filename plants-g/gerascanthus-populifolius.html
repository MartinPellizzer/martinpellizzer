Gerascanthus populifolius

Gerascanthus populifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a member of the Geraniaceae family, which includes other geranium species.

Gerascanthus populifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
