Gentiana abaensis

Gentiana abaensis, commonly known as the ABA gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine layer of white hairs.

Gentiana abaensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
