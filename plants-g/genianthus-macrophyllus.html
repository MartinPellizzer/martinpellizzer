Genianthus macrophyllus

Genianthus macrophyllus, commonly known as the large-leaf genianthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, brightly colored flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners in Mediterranean climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
