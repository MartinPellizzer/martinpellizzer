Goulardia praecaespitosa

Goulardia praecaespitosa, commonly known as the red-flowered goulardia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and forms a compact, clump-forming growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
