Geranium angelina

Geranium angelina, commonly known as Angelina geranium, is a compact, flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly velvety texture. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Geranium angelina prefers well-drained soil and is relatively low maintenance once established.
