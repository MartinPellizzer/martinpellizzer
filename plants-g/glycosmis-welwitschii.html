Glycosmis welwitschii

Glycosmis welwitschii, commonly known as the African starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a tangy, acidic flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and rocky areas.
