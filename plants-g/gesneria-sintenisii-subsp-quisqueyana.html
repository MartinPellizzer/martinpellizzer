Gesneria sintenisii subsp. quisqueyana

Gesneria sintenisii subsp.

quisqueyana, commonly known as the Quisqueyana Gesneria, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America. It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It produces small, bright orange or yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Gesneria sintenisii subsp.

quisqueyana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
