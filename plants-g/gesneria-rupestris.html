Gesneria rupestris

Gesneria rupestris, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features lance-shaped, glossy leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Gesneria rupestris is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
