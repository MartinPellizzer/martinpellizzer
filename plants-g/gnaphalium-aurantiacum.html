Gnaphalium aurantiacum

Gnaphalium aurantiacum, commonly known as the Orange Ragwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright orange to yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, hairy leaves and has a sprawling growth habit.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally by Indigenous peoples.
