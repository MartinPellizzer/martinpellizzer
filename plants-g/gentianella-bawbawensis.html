Gentianella bawbawensis

Gentianella bawbawensis, commonly known as the Bawbaw gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which includes many species of gentians known for their vibrant blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and mountain slopes. Its flowers are usually blue or purple and are clustered in inflorescences, attracting various pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
