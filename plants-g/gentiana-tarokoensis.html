Gentiana tarokoensis

Gentiana tarokoensis, commonly known as the Taroko gentian, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest floors. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana tarokoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
