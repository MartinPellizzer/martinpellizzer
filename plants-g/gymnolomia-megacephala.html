Gymnolomia megacephala

Gymnolomia megacephala, commonly known as the giant umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-like leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Gymnolomia megacephala is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions where it can outcompete native vegetation.
