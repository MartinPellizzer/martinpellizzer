Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp. atroviride

Gymnocalycium bruchii subsp.

atroviride, commonly known as the dark green moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America. It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive dark green body and a white or pale colored areola.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
