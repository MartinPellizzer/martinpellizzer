Gesneria glandulosa

Gesneria glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in small, glandular hairs, which give it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value.

Gesneria glandulosa requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
