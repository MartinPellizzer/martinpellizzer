Glochidion delticola

Glochidion delticola, commonly known as the narrow-leaf glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers, followed by capsule fruits that release tiny seeds. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
