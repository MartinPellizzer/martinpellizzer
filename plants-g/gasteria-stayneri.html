Gasteria stayneri

Gasteria stayneri, commonly known as the Zulu bulb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces red or orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on tall, slender stems. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Gasteria stayneri is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
