Galega trifoliata

Galega trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaf goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Galega trifoliata is also known for its medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
