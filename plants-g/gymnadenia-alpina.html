Gymnadenia alpina

Gymnadenia alpina, commonly known as the alpine gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

Gymnadenia alpina plays an important ecological role in alpine ecosystems, providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a food source for certain herbivores.
