Guettarda crispiflora

Guettarda crispiflora, commonly known as the delicate guettarda, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and humid environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

Guettarda crispiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
