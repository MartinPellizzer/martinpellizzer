Gouania polygama

Gouania polygama, commonly known as the many-flowered gouania, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Gouania polygama is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
