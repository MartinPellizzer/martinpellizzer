Gymnocalycium ambatoense subsp. plesnikii

Gymnocalycium ambatoense subsp.

plesnikii, commonly known as the Ambato moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to the Andes mountains of South America. It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a single, often spiny areole.

The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a thick, water-storing stem.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
