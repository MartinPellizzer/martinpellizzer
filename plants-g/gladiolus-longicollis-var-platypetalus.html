Gladiolus longicollis var. platypetalus

Gladiolus longicollis var. platypetalus, commonly known as the flat-petaled gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, showy flowers with broad, flat petals that give it its distinctive appearance. The flowers typically come in shades of pink, red, and white, adding vibrant color to gardens. This variety is prized for its unique petal structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
