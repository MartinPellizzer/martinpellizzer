Givotia moluccana

Givotia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan givotia, is a flowering plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

Givotia moluccana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
