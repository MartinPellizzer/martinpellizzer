Guarea obtusifolia

Guarea obtusifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf guarea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its large, simple, and often heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant is valued for its durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
