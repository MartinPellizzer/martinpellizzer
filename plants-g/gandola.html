Gandola

Gandola, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with white petals and a yellow center, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Gandola is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
