Greenovia dodrantalis

Greenovia dodrantalis, commonly known as the flameleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like orange and red foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Greenovia dodrantalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both gardens and as a houseplant.
