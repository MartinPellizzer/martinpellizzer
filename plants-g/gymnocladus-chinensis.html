Gymnocladus chinensis

Gymnocladus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese smoke tree, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnately arranged and have a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a spherical pod that splits open to release smooth, dark seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
