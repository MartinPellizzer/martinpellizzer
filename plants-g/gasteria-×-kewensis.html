Gasteria × kewensis

Gasteria × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew Gasteria, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with white or grayish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria × kewensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
