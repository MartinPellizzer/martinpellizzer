Gerbera viridifolia subsp. natalensis

Gerbera viridifolia subsp.

natalensis, commonly known as Natal Gerbera, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to South Africa. It features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white to pale blue petals.

The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
