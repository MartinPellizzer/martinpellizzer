Gumira lucidula

Gumira lucidula, commonly known as the glossy gumira, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that reflect light, giving it a shiny appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Gumira lucidula is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
