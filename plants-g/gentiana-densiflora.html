Gentiana densiflora

Gentiana densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Gentiana densiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
