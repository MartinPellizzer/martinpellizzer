Galanthus bortkewitschianus

Galanthus bortkewitschianus, commonly known as the Bortkewitsch snowdrop, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that emerge from a basal rosette. It thrives in woodland and shady garden environments, preferring moist, well-drained soil.
