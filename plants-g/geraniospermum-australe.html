Geraniospermum australe

Geraniospermum australe, commonly known as the Australian geranium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and open woodlands.

Geraniospermum australe is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
