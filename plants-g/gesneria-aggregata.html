Gesneria aggregata

Gesneria aggregata, commonly known as the clustered gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months.

Gesneria aggregata is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
