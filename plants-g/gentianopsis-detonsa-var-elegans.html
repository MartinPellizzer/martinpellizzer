Gentianopsis detonsa var. elegans

Gentianopsis detonsa var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive blue-green coloration, and its flowers are striking, often blue or violet, with five petals. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
