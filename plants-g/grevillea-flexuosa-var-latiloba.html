Grevillea flexuosa var. latiloba

Grevillea flexuosa var. latiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers in dense, cylindrical spikes, which are typical of the Grevillea genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh climatic conditions.
