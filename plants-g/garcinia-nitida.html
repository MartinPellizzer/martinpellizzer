Garcinia nitida

Garcinia nitida, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale yellow bark and opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries containing a single seed. Garcinia nitida is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of timber.
