Gnaphalium crispum

Gnaphalium crispum, commonly known as the silverleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery flowers. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
