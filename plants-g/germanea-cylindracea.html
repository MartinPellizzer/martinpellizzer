Germanea cylindracea

Germanea cylindracea, commonly known as the slender Germanea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters, which are usually pink or white in hue. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Germanea cylindracea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
