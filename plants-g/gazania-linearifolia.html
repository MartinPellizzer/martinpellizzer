Gazania linearifolia

Gazania linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Gazania linearifolia is known for its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months.
