Gongora maculata var. bufonia

Gongora maculata var. bufonia, commonly known as the bufonia orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking, colorful flowers with a unique, intricate pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in humid tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, while its inflorescence bears several showy, lip-shaped flowers.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
