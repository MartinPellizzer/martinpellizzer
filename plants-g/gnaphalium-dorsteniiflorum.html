Gnaphalium dorsteniiflorum

Gnaphalium dorsteniiflorum, commonly known as the Dorsten's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white. The plant produces fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds, which contribute to its ability to colonize various habitats. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Its common name reflects its long-lasting flowers, which remain intact for extended periods after blooming.
