Geranium holm-nielsenii

Geranium holm-nielsenii, commonly known as the Holm-Nielsen geranium, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features serrated leaves and produces clusters of small, pink to purple blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is appreciated by horticulturists and botanists alike.
