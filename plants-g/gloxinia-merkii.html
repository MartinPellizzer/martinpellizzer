Gloxinia merkii

Gloxinia merkii, commonly known as the Merkii gloxinia, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its humid, tropical environment. Gloxinia merkii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
