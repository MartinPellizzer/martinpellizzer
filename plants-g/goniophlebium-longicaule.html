Goniophlebium longicaule

Its flowers are small and white, arranged in umbrella-like clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.

Goniophlebium longicaule is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
