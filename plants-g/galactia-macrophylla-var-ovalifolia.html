Galactia macrophylla var. ovalifolia

Galactia macrophylla var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm climate with adequate sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it valuable for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
