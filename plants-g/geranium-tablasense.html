Geranium tablasense

Geranium tablasense, commonly known as the Tablas geranium, is a perennial herb native to the island of Tablas in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Geranium tablasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
