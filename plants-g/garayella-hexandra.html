Garayella hexandra

Garayella hexandra, commonly known as the purple star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers with a star-like appearance. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky substrates, while its leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Garayella hexandra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
