Galeola nudifolia

Galeola nudifolia, commonly known as the naked-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often hairless stems. The plant produces small, greenish or white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
