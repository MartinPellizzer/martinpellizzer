Guneria mirabilis

Guneria mirabilis, commonly known as the silverleaf gunnera, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It features large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter, with a silvery-white underside that gives it its common name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs that help retain moisture and reflect sunlight.

Guneria mirabilis is valued in horticulture for its dramatic foliage and ability to add texture to garden landscapes.
