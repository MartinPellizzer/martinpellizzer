Gladiolus atrorubens

Gladiolus atrorubens, commonly known as the dark red gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting large, showy flowers in shades of deep red and purple. The flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes and are composed of three petals and three sepals, with the petals often being larger and more prominent. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both gardens and floral arrangements.
