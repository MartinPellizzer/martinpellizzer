Gomphrena regeliana

Gomphrena regeliana, commonly known as the velvet flower or red gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, bract-like flowers that are typically pink, red, or purple in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with upright stems and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant. Its foliage is usually gray-green, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Gomphrena regeliana is known for its long blooming period, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
