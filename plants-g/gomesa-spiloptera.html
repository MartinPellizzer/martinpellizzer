Gomesa spiloptera

Gomesa spiloptera, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet and green veined petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. Its flowers are small but fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and moths.

Gomesa spiloptera is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its role in local ecosystems.
