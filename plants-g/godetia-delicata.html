Godetia delicata

Godetia delicata, commonly known as the delicate godetia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and small, showy flowers. Godetia delicata is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
