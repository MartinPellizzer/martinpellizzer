Galega apollinea

Galega apollinea, commonly known as the Apollo goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, compound leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in racemes, and bloom in early summer.

Galega apollinea is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
