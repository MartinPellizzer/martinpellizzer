Gesneria craniolaria

Gesneria craniolaria, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Gesneria craniolaria thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.
