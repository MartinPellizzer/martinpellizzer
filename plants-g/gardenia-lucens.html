Gardenia lucens

Gardenia lucens, commonly known as the shining gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in tropical gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The fruit of Gardenia lucens is a bright red berry that contains several seeds.
