Gesneria libanensis

Gesneria libanensis, commonly known as the Lebanon gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Lebanon and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a glossy surface and a prominent midrib.

Gesneria libanensis thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas with rich, well-drained soil.
