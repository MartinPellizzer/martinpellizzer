Geranium eriostemon f. yezoense

Geranium eriostemon f. yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo geranium, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy beneath. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers with five petals, blooming from late spring to early summer. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers.
