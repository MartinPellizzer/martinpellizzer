Grammatophyllum giganteum

Grammatophyllum giganteum, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that can reach up to 3 meters in height, featuring numerous fragrant, spider-like flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, aiding in nutrient absorption.

Grammatophyllum giganteum is a perennial herb that can live for several decades, often found in lowland rainforests.
