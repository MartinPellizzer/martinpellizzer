Geum urbanum var. stenolobum

Geum urbanum var. stenolobum, commonly known as narrow-lobed avens, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its basal leaves that are often divided into narrow, elongated lobes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, woodland soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and habitat restoration.
