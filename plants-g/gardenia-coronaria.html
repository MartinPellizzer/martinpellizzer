Gardenia coronaria

Gardenia coronaria, commonly known as the crown gardenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Gardenia coronaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
