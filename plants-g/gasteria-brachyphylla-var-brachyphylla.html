Gasteria brachyphylla var. brachyphylla

Gasteria brachyphylla var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the dwarf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white to yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, flowering stalks with small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is well adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal watering.

Its compact size and striking appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
