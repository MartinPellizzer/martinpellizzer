Geum klettianum var. semperflorens

Geum klettianum var. semperflorens, commonly known as the everblooming avens, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and branched. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and prolonged blooming period.
