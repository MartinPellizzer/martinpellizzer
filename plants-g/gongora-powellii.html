Gongora powellii

Gongora powellii, commonly known as the Powell's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Gongora powellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
