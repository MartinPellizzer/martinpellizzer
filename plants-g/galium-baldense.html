Galium baldense

Galium baldense, commonly known as bald mountain bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial herb native to North America.

It typically thrives in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and mountain slopes. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Galium baldense is part of the Rubiaceae family, which includes many species with similar morphological characteristics.
