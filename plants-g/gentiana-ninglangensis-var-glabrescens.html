Gentiana ninglangensis var. glabrescens

Gentiana ninglangensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the Ninglang gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with a fine layer of hair that becomes less prominent as the plant matures.

This variant is noted for its ecological importance and is often found in alpine meadows where it contributes to biodiversity.
