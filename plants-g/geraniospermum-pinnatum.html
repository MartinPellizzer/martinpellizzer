Geraniospermum pinnatum

Geraniospermum pinnatum, commonly known as the slender geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often pink or red, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Geraniospermum pinnatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
