Goodenia janamba

Goodenia janamba, commonly known as the Janamba goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

Goodenia janamba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
