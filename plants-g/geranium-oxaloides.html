Geranium oxaloides

Geranium oxaloides, commonly known as the oxalate geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium oxaloides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
