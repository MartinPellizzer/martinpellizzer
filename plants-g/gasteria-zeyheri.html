Gasteria zeyheri

Gasteria zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's gasteria, is a succulent perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or silver markings. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Gasteria zeyheri is appreciated for its compact growth and ornamental appeal in gardens and indoor settings.
