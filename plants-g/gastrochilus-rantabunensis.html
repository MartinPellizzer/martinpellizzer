Gastrochilus rantabunensis

Gastrochilus rantabunensis, commonly known as the Rantabun orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration, typically featuring a combination of white and purple hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich substrates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native region.
