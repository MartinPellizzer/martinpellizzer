Gleichenia klotzschii

Gleichenia klotzschii, commonly known as the Klotzsch's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bright green and have a soft, velvety texture. This plant is native to regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Gleichenia klotzschii prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and ability to add a touch of subtlety to shaded landscapes.
