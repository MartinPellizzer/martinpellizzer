Gentianopsis stenocalyx

Gentianopsis stenocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-calyx gentian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae, which is characterized by showy, often blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are tubular with five lobes and are usually a striking blue or purple color.

It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
