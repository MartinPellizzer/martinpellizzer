Genista spartioides var. macrobotrys

Genista spartioides var. macrobotrys, commonly known as the large-flowered broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features wiry, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in xeriscaping and as a ornamental plant in gardens.
