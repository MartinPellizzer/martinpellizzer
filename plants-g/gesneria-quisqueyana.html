Gesneria quisqueyana

Gesneria quisqueyana, commonly known as the Ecuadorian gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the humid lowland forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria quisqueyana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
