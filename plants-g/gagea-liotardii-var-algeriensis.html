Gagea liotardii var. algeriensis

Gagea liotardii var. algeriensis, commonly known as the Algerian gagea, is a bulbous plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Algeria.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered bulbs and slender, grass-like foliage.
