Gastrochilus setosus

Gastrochilus setosus, commonly known as the hairy gastrochilus, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Gastrochilus setosus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
