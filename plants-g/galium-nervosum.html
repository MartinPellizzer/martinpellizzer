Galium nervosum

Galium nervosum, commonly known as slender bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and slender, ascending stems. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.

Galium nervosum is part of the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 13,000 species of flowering plants.
