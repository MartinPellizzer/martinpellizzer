Goldfussia speciosa

Goldfussia speciosa, commonly known as the showy goldfussia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. Goldfussia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
