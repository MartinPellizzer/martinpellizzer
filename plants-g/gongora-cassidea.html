Gongora cassidea

Gongora cassidea, commonly known as the Cassidea orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as rainforests. Its leaves are broad and often have a waxy texture, while its flowers come in shades of white and pink.
