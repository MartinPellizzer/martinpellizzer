Galium minutissimum

Galium minutissimum, commonly known as lesser bedstraw, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has slender, creeping stems and tiny, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers appear in clusters and bloom in early summer.

Galium minutissimum is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in low-light, damp habitats.
