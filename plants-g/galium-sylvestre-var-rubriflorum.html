Galium sylvestre var. rubriflorum

Galium sylvestre var. rubriflorum, commonly known as redflower bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit. The plant features small, opposite leaves and produces clusters of red or pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its medicinal properties have been historically utilized in traditional herbal remedies.
