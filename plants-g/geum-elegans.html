Geum elegans

Geum elegans, commonly known as elegant sandwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils along streambanks and woodland edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Geum elegans is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
