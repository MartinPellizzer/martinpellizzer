Globba mogokensis

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. Globba mogokensis is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
