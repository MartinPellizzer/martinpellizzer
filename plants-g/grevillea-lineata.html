Grevillea lineata

Grevillea lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that give it a distinctive appearance, and its flowers are typically red or orange, with long, slender petals. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract nectar-feeding birds.

Grevillea lineata is also noted for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
