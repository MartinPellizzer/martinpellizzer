Garnotia maxima

Garnotia maxima, commonly known as the giant garnotia, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a robust stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

Garnotia maxima is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
