Gnaphalium sandwicensium var. hawaiiense

Gnaphalium sandwicensium var. hawaiiense, commonly known as the Hawaiian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and stream banks. It has hairy leaves and produces a distinctive, fragrant resin.
