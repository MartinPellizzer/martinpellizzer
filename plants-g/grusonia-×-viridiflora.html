Grusonia × viridiflora

Grusonia × viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered grusonia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits a mix of characteristics from its parent species.

It typically features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a unique visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its aesthetic contribution to landscapes.
