Gazania heterochaeta

Gazania heterochaeta, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Gazania heterochaeta is known for its ability to withstand drought conditions, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
