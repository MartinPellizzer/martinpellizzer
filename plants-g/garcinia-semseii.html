Garcinia semseii

Garcinia semseii, commonly known as the African yellow wood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are highly ornamental. Garcinia semseii is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a specimen plant due to its vibrant foliage and unique fruit.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.
