Gesneria faucialis

Gesneria faucialis, commonly known as the Brazilian flame plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Gesneria faucialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
