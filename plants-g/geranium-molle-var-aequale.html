Geranium molle var. aequale

Geranium molle var. aequale, commonly known as the soft geranium or velvet geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. It has a compact growth habit and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
