Glycosmis pseudosapindoides

Glycosmis pseudosapindoides, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus or Indian hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.
