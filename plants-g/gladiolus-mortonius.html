Gladiolus mortonius

Gladiolus mortonius, commonly known as the Morton's gladiolus, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Gladiolus mortonius thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring regular watering during its growing season.
