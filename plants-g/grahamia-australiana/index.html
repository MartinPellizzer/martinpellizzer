Grahamia Australiana

Grahamia australiana is a unique and striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its vivid red and orange flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes and coastal heathlands. Its ability to withstand salt spray and strong winds makes it an important part of the local ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the broader horticultural world.
