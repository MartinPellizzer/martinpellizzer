Grevillea × semperflorens

Grevillea × semperflorens, commonly known as the everlasting grevillea, is a hybrid plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with narrow, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Due to its hardy nature and prolonged flowering period, it is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes.
