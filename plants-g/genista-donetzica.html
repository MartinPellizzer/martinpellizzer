Genista donetzica

Genista donetzica, commonly known as Donetz bentthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to parts of Eastern Europe and western Asia, particularly in regions with dry, rocky soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista donetzica is often found in steppe habitats and is adapted to survive in harsh, arid conditions.
