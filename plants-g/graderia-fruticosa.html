Graderia fruticosa

Graderia fruticosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its flowers are typically arranged in panicles and are produced in late spring to early summer.

Graderia fruticosa is well-suited to tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
