Gnaphalium tenellum

Gnaphalium tenellum, commonly known as the slender everlasting, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is native to regions of southern Africa and is often found in dry, open habitats. It produces a fine, powdery bloom that can be easily dispersed by the wind.

Gnaphalium tenellum is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
