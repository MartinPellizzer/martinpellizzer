Gardneria linifolia

Gardneria linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardneria linifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
