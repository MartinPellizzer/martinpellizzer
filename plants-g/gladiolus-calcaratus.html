Gladiolus calcaratus

Gladiolus calcaratus, commonly known as the rock gladiolus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous showy, spurred flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color and have a distinctive, recurved shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
