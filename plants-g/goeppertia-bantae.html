Goeppertia bantae

Goeppertia bantae, commonly known as the Bantai Calathea, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled foliage with deep green veins and variegated leaf patterns. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental leaves and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Goeppertia bantae prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
