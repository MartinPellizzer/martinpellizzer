Gaultheria cinnabarina

Gaultheria cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-flowered wintergreen, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the forests of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Gaultheria cinnabarina is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
