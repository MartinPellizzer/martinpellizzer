Garnotiella philippinensis

Garnotiella philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine thornapple, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its spiny stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
