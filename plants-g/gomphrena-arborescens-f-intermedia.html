Gomphrena arborescens f. intermedia

Gomphrena arborescens f. intermedia, commonly known as the red or pink globe thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and is native to South America. The plant produces dense, spherical clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, adding texture to the landscape.
