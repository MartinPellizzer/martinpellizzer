Glechoma perretii

Glechoma perretii, commonly known as creeping Jenny, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Glechoma perretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet conditions.
