Gandasulium

Gandasulium, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique leaf structure helps it retain moisture in its native habitat.

Gandasulium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
