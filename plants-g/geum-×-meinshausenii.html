Geum × meinshausenii

Geum × meinshausenii, commonly known as the hybrid garden buttercup, is a perennial plant native to the United Kingdom.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Geum × meinshausenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant color and easy maintenance.
