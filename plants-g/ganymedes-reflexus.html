Ganymedes reflexus

Ganymedes reflexus, commonly known as the reflexed gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Ganymedes reflexus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
