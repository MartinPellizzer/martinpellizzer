Gasteria nigricans var. crassifolia

Gasteria nigricans var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are broader and more robust compared to other gasteria varieties. The leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which appear on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
