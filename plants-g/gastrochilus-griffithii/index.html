Gastrochilus Griffithii

Griffith's stomach orchid, scientifically known as Gastrochilus griffithii, is a rare and fascinating member of the Orchidaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance, with velvety leaves and delicate flowers, makes it a standout among orchids. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making conservation efforts crucial. This plant offers both scientific interest and ecological importance in its native habitat.
