Gerardia flammea

Gerardia flammea, commonly known as the flame gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in rocky or sandy soils. It is typically found in coastal areas and is adapted to the warm, arid climate of its native habitat.

Gerardia flammea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
