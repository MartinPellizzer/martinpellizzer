Gesneria leptopus

Gesneria leptopus, commonly known as the slender-leaf gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Gesneria leptopus is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
