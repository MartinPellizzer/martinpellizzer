Gomphrena paranensis subsp. paranensis

Gomphrena paranensis subsp.

paranensis, commonly known as the Brazilian plume flower, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescences that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.
