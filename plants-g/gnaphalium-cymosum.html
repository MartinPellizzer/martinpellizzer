Gnaphalium cymosum

Gnaphalium cymosum, commonly known as silverleaf gumweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Gnaphalium cymosum is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
