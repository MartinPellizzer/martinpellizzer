Glochidion breynioides

Glochidion breynioides, commonly known as the broadleaf glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Glochidion breynioides is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
