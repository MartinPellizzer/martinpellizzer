Grewia gracillima

Grewia gracillima, commonly known as the slender starfruit, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers followed by small, round fruits that resemble starfruits in appearance. Grewia gracillima thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
