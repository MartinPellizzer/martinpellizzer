Gymnosporia Keniensis

Gymnosporia keniensis is a rare and intriguing plant native to Kenya's arid regions. It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is known for its unique adaptations to survive in harsh environments. The plant features spiny stems and small, pod-like fruits that protect its seeds. Its ecological role is significant, providing shelter and food for local wildlife. Due to habitat loss, it faces threats that highlight the need for conservation efforts.
