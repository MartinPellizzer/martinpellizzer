Gueldenstaedtia mirpoureana

Gueldenstaedtia mirpoureana, commonly known as the Armenian bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

Gueldenstaedtia mirpoureana is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
