Gossypium niveum

Gossypium niveum, commonly known as the cotton plant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fluffy, white fibers that are harvested for textile production. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, green flowers that develop into bolls containing the cotton fibers. It thrives in warm, dry climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Gossypium niveum plays a significant role in global agriculture due to its economic importance.
