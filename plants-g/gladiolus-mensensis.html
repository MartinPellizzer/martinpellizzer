Gladiolus mensensis

Gladiolus mensensis, commonly known as the Mountain Gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, colorful flowers. Gladiolus mensensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
