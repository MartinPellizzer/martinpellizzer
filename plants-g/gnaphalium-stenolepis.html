Gnaphalium stenolepis

Gnaphalium stenolepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, surrounded by narrow, linear leaves. It is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
