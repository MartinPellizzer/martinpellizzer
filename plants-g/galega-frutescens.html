Galega frutescens

Galega frutescens, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows up to 1.5 meters tall, with slender, branching stems and alternate, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Galega frutescens is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in herbal remedies.
