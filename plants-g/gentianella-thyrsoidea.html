Gentianella thyrsoidea

Gentianella thyrsoidea, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Gentianella thyrsoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vivid floral display.
