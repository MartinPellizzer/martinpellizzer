Genista polytricha

Genista polytricha, commonly known as the creeping broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typical of the legume family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Genista polytricha is noted for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
