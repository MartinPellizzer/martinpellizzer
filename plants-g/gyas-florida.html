Gyas florida

Gyas florida, commonly known as the Florida flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a notable addition to botanical collections.
