Gymnanthes brasiliensis var. obovata

Gymnanthes brasiliensis var. obovata, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange fruits with a star-shaped appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy coating.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruit.
