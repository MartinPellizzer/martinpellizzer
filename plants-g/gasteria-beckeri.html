Gasteria beckeri

Gasteria beckeri, commonly known as the African tussock, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria beckeri is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
