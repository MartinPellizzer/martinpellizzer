Gesneria salicifolia subsp. spathulata

Gesneria salicifolia subsp.

spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved gesneria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family. It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces striking orange-red flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom during the warmer months.

This species is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
