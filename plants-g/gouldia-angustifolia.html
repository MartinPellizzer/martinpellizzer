Gouldia angustifolia

Gouldia angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gouldia, is a species of flowering plant native to the wet tropical regions of Australia.

The plant typically grows in swampy or seasonally waterlogged soils, thriving in humid, subtropical climates. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
