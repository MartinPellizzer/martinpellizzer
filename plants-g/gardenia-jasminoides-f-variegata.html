Gardenia jasminoides f. variegata

Gardenia jasminoides f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated gardenia, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or cream variegation patterns. The plant produces fragrant, waxy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in tropical gardens.
