Gentiana pleurogynoides

Gentiana pleurogynoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Gentiana pleurogynoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
