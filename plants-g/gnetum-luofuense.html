Gnetum luofuense

Gnetum luofuense, commonly known as the Luofu gnetum, is a gymnosperm native to China.

It is characterized by its unique morphological features, including needle-like leaves and a distinct reproductive structure. The plant exhibits a woody stem with a rough, fissured surface. It produces small, scale-like leaves and has male and female cones on separate plants.

Gnetum luofuense is considered a relict species, reflecting ancient evolutionary traits in the gymnosperm lineage.
