Garcinia hermonii

Garcinia hermonii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and glossy, dark green leaves. Its growth habit and unique fruit color make it a notable species in its native habitat.
