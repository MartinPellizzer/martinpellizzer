Gomesa gardneri

Gomesa gardneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, velvety flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a central boss of stamens. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Gomesa gardneri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
