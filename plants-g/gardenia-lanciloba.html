Gardenia lanciloba

Gardenia lanciloba, commonly known as the slender gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Gardenia lanciloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
