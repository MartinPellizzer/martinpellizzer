Guettarda poasana

Guettarda poasana, commonly known as the coastal guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture.

Guettarda poasana is known for its adaptability to salty air and is often used in coastal landscaping.
