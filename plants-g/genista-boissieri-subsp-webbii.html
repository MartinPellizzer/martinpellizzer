Genista boissieri subsp. webbii

Genista boissieri subsp.

webbii, commonly known as the Webb's broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has slender, woody stems and trifoliate leaves, typical of the Genista genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to ecological restoration projects in arid environments.
