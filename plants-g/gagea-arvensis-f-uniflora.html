Gagea arvensis f. uniflora

Gagea arvensis f. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered field gagea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is a bulbous perennial that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. The plant produces a single, upright flower stem that bears a single, bell-shaped flower with six petals. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often persist through the winter.
