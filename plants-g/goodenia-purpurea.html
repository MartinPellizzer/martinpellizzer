Goodenia purpurea

Goodenia purpurea, commonly known as purple goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Purple goodenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
