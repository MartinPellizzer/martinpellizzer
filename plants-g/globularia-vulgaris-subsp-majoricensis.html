Globularia vulgaris subsp. majoricensis

Globularia vulgaris subsp.

majoricensis, commonly known as the Majorcan globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense foliage, which gives it a unique appearance.

The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
