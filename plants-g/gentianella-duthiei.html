Gentianella duthiei

Gentianella duthiei, commonly known as the Duthie's gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Spain and Portugal. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
