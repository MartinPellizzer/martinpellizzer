Goodenia convexa

Goodenia convexa, commonly known as the warty goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive warty tubercles on the stems and leaves. The plant produces small, delicate blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions.

Goodenia convexa is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and dry heathlands.
