Goodyera rubicunda var. celebica

Goodyera rubicunda var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forest floors and mountainous areas. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
