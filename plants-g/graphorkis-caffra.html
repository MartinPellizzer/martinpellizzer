Graphorkis caffra

Graphorkis caffra, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It prefers full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
