Grammangis ellisii var. dayanum

Grammangis ellisii var. dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces woody pods containing flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
