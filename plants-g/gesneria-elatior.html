Gesneria elatior

Gesneria elatior, commonly known as the showy gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with thick, waxy leaves that are often dark green in color. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Gesneria elatior is relatively easy to care for, though it requires consistent moisture and moderate temperatures to thrive.
