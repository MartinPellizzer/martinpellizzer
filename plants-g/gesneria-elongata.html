Gesneria elongata

Gesneria elongata, commonly known as the elongated gesneria, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces vibrant orange or red tubular flowers, which bloom in clusters at the tips of its stems. Gesneria elongata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and greenhouses.
