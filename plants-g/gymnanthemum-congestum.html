Gymnanthemum congestum

Gymnanthemum congestum, commonly known as the clustered star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. Gymnanthemum congestum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to add a touch of elegance to rock gardens.
