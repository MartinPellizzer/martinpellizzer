Garcinia hunsteinii

Garcinia hunsteinii, commonly known as the Hunstein's garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy green leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces orange-red berries that are rich in bioactive compounds, including hydroxycitric acid.
