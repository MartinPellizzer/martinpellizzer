Geranium verbasciflorum

Geranium verbasciflorum, commonly known as the woolly geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-gray leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
