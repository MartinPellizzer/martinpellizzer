Galium cryptanthum

Galium cryptanthum, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, branching stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Galium cryptanthum thrives in moist, wooded areas and is often found in the understory of forests.
