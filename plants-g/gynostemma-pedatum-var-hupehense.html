Gynostemma pedatum var. hupehense

Gynostemma pedatum var. hupehense, commonly known as Chinese ginseng or "huang qin," is a climbing herb native to China.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it distinct from other ginseng species, with unique chemical compositions and growth habits.
