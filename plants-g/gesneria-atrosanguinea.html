Gesneria atrosanguinea

Gesneria atrosanguinea, commonly known as the Black Blood Gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria atrosanguinea is popular among collectors for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
