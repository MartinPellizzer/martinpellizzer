Guizotia scabra

Guizotia scabra, commonly known as the rough guizotia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and Asia.

It has slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, oval seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep roots and minimal water requirements.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and sustainable agriculture.
