Gladiolus fragrans

Gladiolus fragrans, commonly known as the sweet gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, fragrant blooms that come in a variety of colors. The flowers typically have three petals and three sepals, forming a distinctive sword-like shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
