Gloriosa doniana

Gloriosa doniana, commonly known as the giant flame vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange-red, often with a distinctive red throat. The plant produces long, slender, tubular flowers that are typically borne in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture.

Gloriosa doniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
