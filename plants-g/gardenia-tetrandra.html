Gardenia tetrandra

Gardenia tetrandra, commonly known as the four-thread gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain hard, black seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Gardenia tetrandra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and aromatic foliage.
