Gasteria disticha var. major

Gasteria disticha var. major, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, often with distinctive dark green stripes and a bluish-gray hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular white or pale green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Gasteria disticha var. major is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor and outdoor settings.
