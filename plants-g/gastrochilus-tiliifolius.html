Gastrochilus tiliifolius

Gastrochilus tiliifolius, commonly known as the tiliifolius orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically arranged in clusters, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
