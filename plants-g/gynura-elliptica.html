Gynura elliptica

Gynura elliptica, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gynura elliptica is valued for its ornamental leaves and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
