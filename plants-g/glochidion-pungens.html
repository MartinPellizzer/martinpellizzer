Glochidion pungens

Glochidion pungens, commonly known as the spiny hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Glochidion pungens is also noted for its ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
