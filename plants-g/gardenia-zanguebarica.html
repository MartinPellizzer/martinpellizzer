Gardenia zanguebarica

Gardenia zanguebarica, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and produces large, fragrant white flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and pleasant scent.

Gardenia zanguebarica is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
