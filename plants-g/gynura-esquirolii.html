Gynura esquirolii

Gynura esquirolii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese evergreen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Gynura esquirolii is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its resilience to low light conditions.
