Geonoma spruceana var. tuberculata

Geonoma spruceana var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate geonoma, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The fronds are typically leathery and have a distinctive, serrated or lobed margin. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant's unique tuberculate (wart-like) structures on its leaves are a key identifying feature.
