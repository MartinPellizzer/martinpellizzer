Geranium eriostemon var. onoei

Geranium eriostemon var. onoei, commonly known as the Japanese geranium, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms from late spring to early summer, producing clusters of five-petaled flowers. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
