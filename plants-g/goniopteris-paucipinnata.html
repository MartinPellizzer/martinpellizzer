Goniopteris paucipinnata

Goniopteris paucipinnata, commonly known as the few-pinnate water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Marsileaceae.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically divided into four to six pairs of pinnules. Goniopteris paucipinnata plays a role in maintaining water quality by filtering nutrients and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
