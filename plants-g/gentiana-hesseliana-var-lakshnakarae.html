Gentiana hesseliana var. lakshnakarae

Gentiana hesseliana var. lakshnakarae, commonly known as the Lakshnakara gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of down, aiding in water retention.

This variant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with specific alpine conditions.
