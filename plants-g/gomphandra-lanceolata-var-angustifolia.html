Gomphandra lanceolata var. angustifolia

Gomphandra lanceolata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gomphandra, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

This variety is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Gomphandra lanceolata.
