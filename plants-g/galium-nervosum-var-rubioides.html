Galium nervosum var. rubioides

Galium nervosum var. rubioides, commonly known as scarlet bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with slender, creeping stems that often root at the nodes. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
