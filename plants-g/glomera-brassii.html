Glomera brassii

Glomera brassii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Glomera brassii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
