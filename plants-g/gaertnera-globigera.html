Gaertnera globigera

Gaertnera globigera, commonly known as the globigerous hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Gaertnera globigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
