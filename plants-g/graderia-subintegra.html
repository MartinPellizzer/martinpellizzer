Graderia subintegra

Graderia subintegra, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence that consists of numerous small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually white or pale green with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Graderia subintegra thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
