Gnaphalium pes-cati

Gnaphalium pes-cati, commonly known as cat's foot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to various regions of Europe, Asia, and Africa, often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically woolly and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Gnaphalium pes-cati is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
