Gardenia lacciflua

Gardenia lacciflua, commonly known as the lacquer gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

The fruit of the plant is a fleshy, yellowish berry that contains several seeds.
