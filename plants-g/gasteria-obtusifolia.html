Gasteria obtusifolia

Gasteria obtusifolia, commonly known as the elephant's foot plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched stalks with small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for arid environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
