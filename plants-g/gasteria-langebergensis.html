Gasteria langebergensis

Gasteria langebergensis, commonly known as the Langeberg gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped arrangement of fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear small, white, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria langebergensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
