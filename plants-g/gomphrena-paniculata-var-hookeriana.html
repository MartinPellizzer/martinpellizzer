Gomphrena paniculata var. hookeriana

Gomphrena paniculata var. hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, spherical flower heads that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
