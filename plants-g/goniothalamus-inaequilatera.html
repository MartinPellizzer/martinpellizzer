Goniothalamus inaequilatera

Goniothalamus inaequilatera, commonly known as the unequal-sided goniothalamus, is a member of the family Annonaceae and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
