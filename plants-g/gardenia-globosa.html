Gardenia globosa

Gardenia globosa, commonly known as the globe gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces spherical, yellowish fruit that contains several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Gardenia globosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticulture for its elegant appearance.
