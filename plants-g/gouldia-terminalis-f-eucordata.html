Gouldia terminalis f. eucordata

Gouldia terminalis f. eucordata, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its thick, segmented stems and showy, colorful flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.

Its foliage remains evergreen, adding visual interest throughout the year.
