Gleichenia glabra

Gleichenia glabra, commonly known as the common Gleichenia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is a terrestrial plant characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, dark green fronds. The fronds are usually pinnately compound, with a distinctive, elongated shape and a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and along stream banks.

The plant is noted for its ability to form extensive mats, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
