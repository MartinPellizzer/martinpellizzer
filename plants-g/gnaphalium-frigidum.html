Gnaphalium frigidum

Gnaphalium frigidum, commonly known as the alpine cudweed, is a flowering plant native to cold regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Gnaphalium frigidum is known for its ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient component of high-altitude ecosystems.
