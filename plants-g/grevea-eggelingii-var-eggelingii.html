Grevea eggelingii var. eggelingii

Grevea eggelingii var. eggelingii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The species is known for its ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year under proper conditions.
