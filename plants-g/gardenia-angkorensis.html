Gardenia angkorensis

Gardenia angkorensis, commonly known as the Cambodian gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces large, fragrant white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking appearance and pleasant aroma. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia angkorensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
