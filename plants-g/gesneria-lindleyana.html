Gesneria lindleyana

Gesneria lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's gesneria, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, while the flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Gesneria lindleyana is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
