Glandularia kuntzeana

Glandularia kuntzeana, commonly known as the Kuntze's glandular verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers. Glandularia kuntzeana is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
