Guzmania recurvobracteata

Guzmania recurvobracteata, commonly known as the recurved bract guzmania, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

Guzmania recurvobracteata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
