Goodyera anomala

Goodyera anomala, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces intricate, spiral-shaped inflorescences with small, white to pale green flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories.

Goodyera anomala is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
