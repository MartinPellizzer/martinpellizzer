Gomphia revoluta

Gomphia revoluta, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Gomphaceae.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and elegant appearance. Gomphia revoluta plays a role in ecosystem stability by contributing to soil structure and providing habitat for small organisms.
