Geranium oenotherae

Geranium oenotherae, commonly known as the Evening Primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange flowers that open in the evening, hence its common name. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium oenotherae is also known for its medicinal properties, particularly the oils extracted from its seeds.
