Gazania longiscapa var. ovalis

Gazania longiscapa var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow or orange daisy-like flowers with a central brown disk. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture. This variety thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
