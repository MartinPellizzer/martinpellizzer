Graphorkis Exaltata

Graphorkis exaltata is a rare and intriguing orchid native to the mountainous regions of New Guinea. Known for its striking violet and white petals, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and faces threats from deforestation and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful plant for future generations.
