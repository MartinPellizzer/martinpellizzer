Geitonoplesium angustifolium

Geitonoplesium angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starfruit, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into the edible, star-shaped fruits. The fruit has a sweet, acidic flavor and is often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications.
