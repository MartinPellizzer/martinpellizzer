Gymnocalycium achirasense subsp. chacrasense

Gymnocalycium achirasense subsp.

chacrasense, commonly known as the Chacrasense Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Bolivia and Argentina. It is characterized by its globular to cylindrical stem that is typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. It has spiny areoles with clusters of white to yellowish spines.

This subspecies is well adapted to harsh desert conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
