Gymnotrix crinita

Gymnotrix crinita, commonly known as the curly hair fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Gymnotrix crinita is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
