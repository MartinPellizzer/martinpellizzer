Guettarda pungens

Guettarda pungens, commonly known as the spiny guettarda, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has spiny leaves and stems, which provide natural protection against herbivores. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Guettarda pungens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
