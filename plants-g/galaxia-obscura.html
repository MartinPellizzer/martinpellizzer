Galaxia obscura

Galaxia obscura, commonly known as the dark galaxia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Galaxia obscura is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
