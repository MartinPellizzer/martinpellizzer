Gladiolus papilio

Gladiolus papilio, commonly known as the Papilio Gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, sword-shaped flowers in a range of vibrant colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes and are known for their unique, butterfly-like appearance. This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It prefers full sun and is often cultivated for garden displays and cut flower arrangements.
