Gueldenstaedtia santapaui

Gueldenstaedtia santapaui, commonly known as the Santapaui bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments.
