Guatteria cinnamomea

Guatteria cinnamomea, commonly known as cinnamon leaf guatteria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. Guatteria cinnamomea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and ecological significance.
