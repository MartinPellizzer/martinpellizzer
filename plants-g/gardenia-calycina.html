Gardenia calycina

Gardenia calycina, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Gardenia calycina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
