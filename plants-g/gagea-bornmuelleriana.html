Gagea bornmuelleriana

Gagea bornmuelleriana, commonly known as the Bornmueller's gagea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of white to pale blue flowers. The plant produces small, rounded bulbs that serve as storage organs for nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
