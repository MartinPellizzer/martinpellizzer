Gladiolus murielae

Gladiolus murielae, commonly known as the Muriel's gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear clusters of vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, making it a prominent feature in gardens and floral arrangements. Its foliage consists of narrow, sword-like leaves that provide a lush backdrop to the showy blooms.

Gladiolus murielae thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, requiring regular watering and moderate fertilization for optimal growth.
