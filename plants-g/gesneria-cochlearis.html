Gesneria cochlearis

Gesneria cochlearis, commonly known as the spiral gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. It produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often in clusters.

Gesneria cochlearis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
