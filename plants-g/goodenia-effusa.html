Goodenia effusa

Goodenia effusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf goodenia, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia effusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
