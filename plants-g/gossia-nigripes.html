Gossia nigripes

Gossia nigripes, commonly known as the black-veined gossia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia, particularly Queensland and New South Wales. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent black veins. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

Gossia nigripes is often found in rainforest or moist woodland habitats, where it thrives in shaded, humid conditions.
