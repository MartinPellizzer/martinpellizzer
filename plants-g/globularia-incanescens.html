Globularia incanescens

Globularia incanescens, commonly known as the silver globularia, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has hairy, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive, frosty appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Globularia incanescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
