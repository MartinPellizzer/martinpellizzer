Gentiana intricata

Gentiana intricata, commonly known as the intricate gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its deep blue to violet flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in dense clusters. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountain meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine pubescence, aiding in moisture retention.

Gentiana intricata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
