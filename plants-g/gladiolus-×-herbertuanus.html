Gladiolus × herbertuanus

Gladiolus × herbertuanus, commonly known as the Herbertian gladiolus, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting large, showy flowers in a wide range of colors. The flowers are typically arranged in a spike with each bloom having three petals and three sepals, forming a distinctive sword-like shape. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and floral arrangements.

Its ornamental value has led to the development of numerous cultivars with varying flower sizes and colors.
