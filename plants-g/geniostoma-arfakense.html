Geniostoma arfakense

Geniostoma arfakense, commonly known as the Arfak mountain orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the highlands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
