Gymnadenia conopsea var. caucasica

Gymnadenia conopsea var. caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian goat's rue, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its tall, sparsely leafed stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces striking, fragrant, pale yellow to orange-red flowers in late summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its native flora.
