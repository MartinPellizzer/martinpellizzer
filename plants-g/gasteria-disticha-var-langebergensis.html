Gasteria disticha var. langebergensis

Gasteria disticha var. langebergensis, commonly known as the Langeberg gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or pale markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
