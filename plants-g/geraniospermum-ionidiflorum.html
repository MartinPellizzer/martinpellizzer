Geraniospermum ionidiflorum

Geraniospermum ionidiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Geraniospermum ionidiflorum is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
