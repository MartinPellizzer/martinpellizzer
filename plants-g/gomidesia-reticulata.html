Gomidesia reticulata

Gomidesia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate net-like venation patterns on the petals. Native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the canopy layer of rainforests.

Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
