Gleichenia linearis var. latiloba

Gleichenia linearis var. latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a vascular fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae, characterized by its delicate, arching fronds. The leaves are typically longer and broader than those of the typical variety, with a distinctive, serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
