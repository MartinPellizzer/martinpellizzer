Genista pilosa var. nodosa

Genista pilosa var. nodosa, commonly known as the nodding genista, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by small, pod-like fruits. Its leaves are typically small, opposite, and trifoliate, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
