Gardenia angustifolia var. kosyunensis

Gardenia angustifolia var. kosyunensis, commonly known as the narrowleaf gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is evergreen and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
