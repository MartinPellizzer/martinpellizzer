Goodia polysperma

Goodia polysperma, commonly known as the coastal goodia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 1 meter. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong coastal winds.

Goodia polysperma is often found in sandy soils along shores and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
