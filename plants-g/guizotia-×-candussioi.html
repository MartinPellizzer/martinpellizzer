Guizotia × candussioi

Guizotia × candussioi, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a cross between Guizotia abyssinica and Helianthus annuus.

It is an annual herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, showy yellow composite flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The seeds are small, oval, and have a hard, smooth coat, similar to those of sunflowers.
