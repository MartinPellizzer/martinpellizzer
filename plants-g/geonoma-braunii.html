Geonoma braunii, commonly known as the Braun's fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that form a central cavity. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
