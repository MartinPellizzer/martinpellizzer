Grevillea Dissecta

Dissected grevillea, scientifically known as Grevillea dissecta, is a striking native Australian plant renowned for its unique and intricate foliage. Native to the coastal regions of southern Australia, it thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal scrub. Its leaves are deeply lobed, giving it a feathery appearance that sets it apart from other members of the Proteaceae family. This plant is not only visually appealing but also plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife. Due to its ornamental value and conservation status, it is a popular choice for gardeners and conservationists alike.
