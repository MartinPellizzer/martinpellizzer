Gerbera hieracioides

Gerbera hieracioides, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
