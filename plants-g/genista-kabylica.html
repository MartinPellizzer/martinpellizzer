Genista kabylica

Genista kabylica, commonly known as the Kabylian broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its slender, shrub-like structure features thorned stems and small, leathery leaves.

Genista kabylica is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
