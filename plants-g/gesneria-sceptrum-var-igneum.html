Gesneria sceptrum var. igneum

Gesneria sceptrum var. igneum, commonly known as the Flame Gesneria, is a strikingly colorful hybrid plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, fiery orange to red foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with broad, glossy leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months.

This variety is popular among succulent and tropical plant enthusiasts due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
