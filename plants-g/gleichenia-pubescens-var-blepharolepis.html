Gleichenia pubescens var. blepharolepis

Gleichenia pubescens var. blepharolepis, commonly known as the hairy Gleichenia, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, lobed fronds.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a creeping rhizome with dense, velvety pubescence on its leaves, giving it a soft, fuzzy texture. Its fronds are typically short and broad, with a unique, intricate venation pattern.

This variety is often found growing on shaded, moist rock surfaces or in shaded forest understories.
