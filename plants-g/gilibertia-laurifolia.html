Gilibertia laurifolia

Gilibertia laurifolia, commonly known as the Brazilian wax tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Gilibertia laurifolia is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
