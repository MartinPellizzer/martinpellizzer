Gouinia brasiliensis

Gouinia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing plant. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Gouinia brasiliensis is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in gardens with well-drained soil.
