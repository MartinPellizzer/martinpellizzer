Gleichenia boryi var. madagascariensis

Gleichenia boryi var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar Gleichenia, is a fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features lanceolate, serrated leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its reproductive structures include sporangia that produce spores for propagation.
