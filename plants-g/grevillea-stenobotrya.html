Grevillea stenobotrya

Grevillea stenobotrya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters. The plant is adapted to arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for dry gardens. Its flowers attract nectar-feeding birds and insects, contributing to local biodiversity.

Grevillea stenobotrya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscaping.
