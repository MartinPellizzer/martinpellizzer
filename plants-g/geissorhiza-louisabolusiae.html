Geissorhiza louisabolusiae

Geissorhiza louisabolusiae, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, stout stem with several leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
