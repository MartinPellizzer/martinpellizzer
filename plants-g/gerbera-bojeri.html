Gerbera bojeri

Gerbera bojeri, commonly known as the Bojer's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features bright yellow to orange petals with a central disk of brown or black florets. Gerbera bojeri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various horticultural settings.
