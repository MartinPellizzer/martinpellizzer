Grevillea kenneallyi

Grevillea kenneallyi, commonly known as the Kenneally's waratah, is a striking Australian plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Native to the coastal regions of New South Wales, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its unique floral structure and vivid coloration make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
