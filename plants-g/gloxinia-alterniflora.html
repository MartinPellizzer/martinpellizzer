Gloxinia alterniflora

Gloxinia alterniflora, commonly known as the zigzag gloxinia, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and a distinctive zigzag pattern along its stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gloxinia alterniflora is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and unique appearance.
