Gagea linearifolia

Gagea linearifolia, commonly known as the slender gagea, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Gagea linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
