Grevillea manglesii subsp. ornithopoda

Grevillea manglesii subsp.

ornithopoda, commonly known as the Crimson Bird's Nest, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces distinctive, urn-shaped flower clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in native plant gardens.
