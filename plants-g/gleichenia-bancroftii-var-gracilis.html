Gleichenia bancroftii var. gracilis

Gleichenia bancroftii var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender Gleichenia, is a fern species native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Gleicheniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to the substrate, while its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is noted for its graceful appearance and is often found in humid, temperate regions.
