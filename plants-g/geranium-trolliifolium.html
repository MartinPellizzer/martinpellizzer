Geranium trolliifolium

Geranium trolliifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved geranium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and southwestern Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
