Glochidion decorum

Glochidion decorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf hibiscus, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. Glochidion decorum thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate partial shade.
