Grevillea lullfitzii

Grevillea lullfitzii, commonly known as the Lullfitz's grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in dense, cylindrical clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife.
