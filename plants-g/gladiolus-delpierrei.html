Gladiolus delpierrei

Gladiolus delpierrei, commonly known as the Delpierre's gladiolus, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically vibrant in color, ranging from red to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its beauty and used in floral arrangements.
