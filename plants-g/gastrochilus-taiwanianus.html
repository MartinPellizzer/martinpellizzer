Gastrochilus taiwanianus

Gastrochilus taiwanianus, commonly known as the Taiwan stomach-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive lip that is often yellow or orange in color.

Gastrochilus taiwanianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
