Gasteria acinacifolia var. nitens

Gasteria acinacifolia var. nitens, commonly known as the Natal carpet plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with a slight blueish hue. The leaves are narrow and sword-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
