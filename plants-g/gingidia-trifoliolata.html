Gingidia trifoliolata

Gingidia trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaf ginger, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and are borne on slender inflorescences.
