Galaxia parva

Galaxia parva, commonly known as the dwarf galaxia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Galaxia parva is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
