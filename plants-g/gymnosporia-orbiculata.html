Gymnosporia orbiculata

Gymnosporia orbiculata, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, orbicular leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by woody, spherical fruits containing a single seed. Gymnosporia orbiculata is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to drought.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a subject of study in plant ecology.
