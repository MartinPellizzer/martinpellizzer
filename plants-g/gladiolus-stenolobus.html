Gladiolus stenolobus

Gladiolus stenolobus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that contribute to its overall ornamental appeal.
