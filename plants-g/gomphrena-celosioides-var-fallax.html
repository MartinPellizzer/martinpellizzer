Gomphrena celosioides var. fallax

Gomphrena celosioides var. fallax, commonly known as the fallax gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, plump, spherical blooms that range in color from pink to red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.

Gomphrena celosioides var. fallax is well-suited for gardens, containers, and as a border plant.
