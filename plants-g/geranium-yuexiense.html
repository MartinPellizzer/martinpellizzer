Geranium yuexiense

Geranium yuexiense, commonly known as the Yuexi geranium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often have a velvety texture.

Geranium yuexiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
