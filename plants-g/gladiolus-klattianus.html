Gladiolus klattianus

Gladiolus klattianus, commonly known as the Klattian gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant produces showy flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown for its beauty and used in floral arrangements.
