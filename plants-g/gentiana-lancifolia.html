Gentiana lancifolia

Gentiana lancifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Gentiana lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
