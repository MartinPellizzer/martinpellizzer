Gomphostemma crinitum

Gomphostemma crinitum, commonly known as the hairy gomphostemma, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color.
