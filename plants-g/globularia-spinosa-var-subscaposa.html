Globularia spinosa var. subscaposa

Globularia spinosa var. subscaposa, commonly known as the scaly globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial with a dense, globular shape, often forming a compact cushion of foliage. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, scaly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or violet flowers during the summer months.

This species is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
