Goodenia drummondii

Goodenia drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Drummond's goodenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
