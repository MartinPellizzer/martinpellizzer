Gastrodia pubilabiata f. viridis

Gastrodia pubilabiata f. viridis, commonly known as the green-flowered form of the mountain gastrodia, is a rare and unique variant of the gastrodia plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive greenish-yellow flowers, which differ from the typical brownish hues of the species. This fern-like orchid is native to the high-altitude regions of China, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is known for its fleshy, tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine.
