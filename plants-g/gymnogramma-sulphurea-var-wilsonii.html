Gymnogramma sulphurea var. wilsonii

Gymnogramma sulphurea var. wilsonii, commonly known as the golden fern, is a variant of the species Gymnogramma sulphurea.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, pinnately divided fronds with a distinctive golden hue on the underside. Its leaves are usually slender and have a soft, feathery appearance.

This variant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, shaded habitats.
