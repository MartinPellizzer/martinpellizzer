Gymnadenia × schweinfurthii

Gymnadenia × schweinfurthii, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a natural hybrid between Gymnadenia conopsea and Gymnadenia pygmaea.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in wet meadows, bogs, and other moist habitats across Europe. Its flowers are usually pale pink to lilac in color and are arranged in a loose, racemose inflorescence.

Gymnadenia × schweinfurthii is known for its ecological importance as a nectar source for various pollinators.
