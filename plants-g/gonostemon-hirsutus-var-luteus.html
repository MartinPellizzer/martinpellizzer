Gonostemon hirsutus var. luteus

Gonostemon hirsutus var. luteus, commonly known as the yellow hirsute gonostemon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are typically covered in fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its botanical structure includes a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems arising from the base.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
