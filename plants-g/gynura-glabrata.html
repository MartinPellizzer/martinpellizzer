Gynura glabrata

Gynura glabrata, commonly known as the smooth gynura, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Gynura glabrata is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
