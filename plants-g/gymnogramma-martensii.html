Gymnogramma martensii

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Gymnogramma martensii is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
