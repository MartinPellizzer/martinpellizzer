Gynura rusisiensis

Gynura rusisiensis, commonly known as the African silverleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-white underside on its leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its unique foliage.
