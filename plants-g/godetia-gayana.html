Godetia gayana

Godetia gayana, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems that grow up to 1 meter in height, covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and alternate along the stem, often with a slightly pubescent surface.

Godetia gayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
