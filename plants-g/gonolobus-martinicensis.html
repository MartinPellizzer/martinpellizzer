Gonolobus martinicensis

Gonolobus martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to warm, humid climates.

The flowers typically bloom in the morning, giving the plant its common name.
