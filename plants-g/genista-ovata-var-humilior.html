Genista ovata var. humilior

Genista ovata var. humilior, commonly known as the dwarf yellow broom, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
