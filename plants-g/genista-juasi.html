Genista juasi

Genista juasi, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Genista juasi is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive spring blooms.
