Gravesia longifolia

Gravesia longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered gravesia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Gravesia longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
