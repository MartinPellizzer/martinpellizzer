Guidonia hirsuta var. glabrata

Guidonia hirsuta var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth guidonia, is a variant of the hairy guidonia species native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, glandular foliage and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in spring. The leaves are usually smooth and glossy, distinguishing it from its varietal counterpart. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
