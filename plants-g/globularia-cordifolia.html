Globularia cordifolia

Globularia cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaved globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Globularia cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
