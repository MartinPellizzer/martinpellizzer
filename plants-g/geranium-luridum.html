Geranium luridum

Geranium luridum, commonly known as the yellow geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and hairy, ovate leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its compact growth habit.

Geranium luridum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
