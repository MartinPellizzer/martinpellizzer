Gerardia domingensis

Gerardia domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and simple, lance-shaped leaves. Gerardia domingensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
