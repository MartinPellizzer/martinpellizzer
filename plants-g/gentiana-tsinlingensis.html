Gentiana tsinlingensis

Gentiana tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and streamside areas. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
