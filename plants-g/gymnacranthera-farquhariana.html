Gymnacranthera farquhariana

Gymnacranthera farquhariana, commonly known as the Farquharian tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes other aromatic plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
