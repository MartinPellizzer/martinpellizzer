Galactia fiebrigiana var. sericophylla

Galactia fiebrigiana var. sericophylla, commonly known as the silky-leaf galactia, is a species native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and alternate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The flowers are small and clustered in racemes, typically ranging in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, flat, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
