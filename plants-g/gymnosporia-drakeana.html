Gymnosporia drakeana

Gymnosporia drakeana, commonly known as the Drakes' thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, hard-coated seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind and animals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
