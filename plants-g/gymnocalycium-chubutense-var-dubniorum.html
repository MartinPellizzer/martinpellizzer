Gymnocalycium chubutense var. dubniorum

Gymnocalycium chubutense var. dubniorum, commonly known as the Chubut moon cactus, is a succulent native to Argentina.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
