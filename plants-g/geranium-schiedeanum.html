Geranium schiedeanum

Geranium schiedeanum, commonly known as the shieldleaf geranium, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, shield-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
