Gonocaryum monostachyum

Gonocaryum monostachyum, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spikelet at the top of each culm.

Gonocaryum monostachyum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
