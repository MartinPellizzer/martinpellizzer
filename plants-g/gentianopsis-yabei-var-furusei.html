Gentianopsis yabei var. furusei

Gentianopsis yabei var. furusei, commonly known as the Yabei gentian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty.
