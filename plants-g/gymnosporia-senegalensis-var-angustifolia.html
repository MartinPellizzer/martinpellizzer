Gymnosporia senegalensis var. angustifolia

Gymnosporia senegalensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gymnosporia, is a shrub native to the Sahel region of Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and linear. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by woody, cone-like structures that contain the seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

The species is often found in sandy soils and is part of the Fabaceae family.
