Guzmania brachycephala

Guzmania brachycephala, commonly known as the compact pineapple plant, is a tropical bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces a striking inflorescence with vibrant red bracts and yellow flowers, adding ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
