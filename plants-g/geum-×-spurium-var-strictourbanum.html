Geum × spurium var. strictourbanum

Geum × spurium var. strictourbanum, commonly known as the compact garden buttercup, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It features bright yellow, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage, which helps it retain moisture. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders and containers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for small spaces and rock gardens.
