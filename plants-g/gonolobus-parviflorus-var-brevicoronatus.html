Gonolobus parviflorus var. brevicoronatus

Gonolobus parviflorus var. brevicoronatus, commonly known as the small-flowered go-on-lubus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces a distinctive, waxy fruit that contains several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
