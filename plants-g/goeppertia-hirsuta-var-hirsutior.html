Goeppertia hirsuta var. hirsutior

Goeppertia hirsuta var. hirsutior, commonly known as the Hairy Sword Fern, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silvery-white underside, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant is characterized by its dense, fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.

Its foliage remains lush and vibrant throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
