Genista Occidentalis

Genista occidentalis is a flowering plant native to the western united states and parts of mexico. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its bright yellow blooms that appear in spring. this shrub is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. it has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. due to its hardiness and beauty, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping and native gardens.
