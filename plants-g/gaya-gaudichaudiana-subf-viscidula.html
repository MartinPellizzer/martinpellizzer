Gaya gaudichaudiana subf. viscidula

Gaya gaudichaudiana subf.

viscidula, commonly known as the viscid gaya, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, winged seeds.

The leaves are often covered in a fine, sticky resin, which gives the plant its common name.
