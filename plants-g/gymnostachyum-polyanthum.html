Gymnostachyum polyanthum

Gymnostachyum polyanthum, commonly known as the multibract orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Gymnostachyum polyanthum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation in botanical gardens and private collections.
