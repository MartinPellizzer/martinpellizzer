Gasteria nitida var. armstrongii

Gasteria nitida var. armstrongii, commonly known as the Armstrong's gasteria, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture, often adorned with white or pale green markings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers on slender stalks, usually in shades of yellow or orange, during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for dry environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
