Gynochthodes motleyi

Gynochthodes motleyi, commonly known as the Motley's ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Brazil.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, blooming in late summer or early autumn.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Gynochthodes motleyi is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists.
