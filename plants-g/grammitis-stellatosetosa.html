Grammitis stellatosetosa

Grammitis stellatosetosa, commonly known as the star-setose fern, is a species of epiphytic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are often covered in fine, star-shaped hairs. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical and subtropical forests. Its spores are produced in small, clustered structures called sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds.

Grammitis stellatosetosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
