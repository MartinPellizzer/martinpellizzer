Grangea galamensis

Grangea galamensis, commonly known as the Galamensian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blossoms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Grangea galamensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
