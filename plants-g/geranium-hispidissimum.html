Geranium hispidissimum

Geranium hispidissimum, commonly known as the rough geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in stiff, hairy textures, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, hairy surface.

Geranium hispidissimum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
