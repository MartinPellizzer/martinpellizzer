Gomidesia langsdorffii

Gomidesia langsdorffii, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.
