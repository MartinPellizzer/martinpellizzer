Gastrolobium bennettsianum

Gastrolobium bennettsianum, commonly known as Bennetts wattle, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in dry, open habitats. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native landscapes.
