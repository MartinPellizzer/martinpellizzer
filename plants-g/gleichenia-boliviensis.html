Gleichenia boliviensis

Gleichenia boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Gleicheniaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in strobila, a feature typical of many ferns. Gleichenia boliviensis is native to South America, particularly Bolivia, and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical structure reflects adaptations to its tropical and subtropical habitats.
