Goodenia filiformis var. glaucoides

Goodenia filiformis var. glaucoides, commonly known as the glaucous goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variant is distinguished by its bluish-gray foliage, which gives it its common name.
