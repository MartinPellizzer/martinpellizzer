Geranium popovii

Geranium popovii, commonly known as the Popov's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the Geraniaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly found in regions of South Africa and Namibia. This perennial herb typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, featuring upright stems and clusters of small, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
