Geranium shensianum

Geranium shensianum, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant pink or red blooms during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Geranium shensianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
