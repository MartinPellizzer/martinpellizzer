Graffenrieda latifolia subsp. latifolia

Graffenrieda latifolia subsp.

latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf graffenrieda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white.

The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, warm climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical areas.
