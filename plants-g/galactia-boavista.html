Galactia boavista

Galactia boavista, commonly known as the Boavista milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Galactia boavista is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it valuable in ecological restoration projects.
