Garcinia linii

Garcinia linii, commonly known as the Himalayan Garcinia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces bright orange, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed, which is often used in traditional medicine. Garcinia linii thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found at high altitudes.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Garcinia genus.
