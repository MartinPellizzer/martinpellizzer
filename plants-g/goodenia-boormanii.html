Goodenia boormanii

Goodenia boormanii, commonly known as the Boorman's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves and has a compact, shrub-like growth habit.

Goodenia boormanii is often found in open woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native gardens.
