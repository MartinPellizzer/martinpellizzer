Gaillardia nervosa

Gaillardia nervosa, commonly known as the slender blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with bright orange and red petals. The plant typically grows in prairies and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often found in regions with full sun exposure.

Gaillardia nervosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
