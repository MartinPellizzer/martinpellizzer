Globulea atropurpurea var. smutsii

Globulea atropurpurea var. smutsii, commonly known as the Purple Globular Bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, spherical clusters of small, purple to pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and striking floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
