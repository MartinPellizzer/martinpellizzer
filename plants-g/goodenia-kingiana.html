Goodenia kingiana

Goodenia kingiana, commonly known as the King's goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers during the spring and summer months.

Goodenia kingiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
