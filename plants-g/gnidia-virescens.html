Gnidia virescens

Gnidia virescens, commonly known as the green knopper, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Gnidia virescens is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
