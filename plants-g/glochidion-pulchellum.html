Glochidion pulchellum

Glochidion pulchellum, commonly known as the beautiful glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits containing several seeds.
