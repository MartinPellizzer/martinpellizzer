Gynostemma blumei

Gynostemma blumei, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, containing various bioactive compounds. Its fruits are edible and often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Gynostemma blumei is also valued for its role in promoting health and vitality in traditional medicine practices.
