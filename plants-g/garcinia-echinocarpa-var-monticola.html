Garcinia echinocarpa var. monticola

Garcinia echinocarpa var. monticola, commonly known as the mountain Garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and produces yellow-orange fruits with a thick, waxy rind. The fruit contains a single seed enclosed in a fleshy, aromatic pulp. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
