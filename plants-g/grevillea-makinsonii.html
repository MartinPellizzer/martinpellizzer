Grevillea makinsonii

Grevillea makinsonii, commonly known as the Sydney panicled grevillea, is a striking shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in dense, panicle-like clusters, attracting native birds and insects. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and suburban gardens.

Grevillea makinsonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits in native landscaping.
