Grevillea treueriana

Grevillea treueriana, commonly known as the pink grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils. It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters in height, and it is well adapted to the arid and semi-arid climates of its native region.

Grevillea treueriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
