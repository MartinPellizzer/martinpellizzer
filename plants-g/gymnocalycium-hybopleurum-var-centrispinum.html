Gymnocalycium hybopleurum var. centrispinum

Gymnocalycium hybopleurum var. centrispinum, commonly known as the Centrispinum Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered with dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to survive in dry conditions.

This cactus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in harsh environments.
