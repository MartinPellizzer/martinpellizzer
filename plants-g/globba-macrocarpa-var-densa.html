Globba macrocarpa var. densa

Globba macrocarpa var. densa, commonly known as the dense-flowered globba, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright yellow to orange inflorescences, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, enhancing its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
