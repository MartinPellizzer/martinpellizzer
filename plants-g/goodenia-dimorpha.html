Goodenia dimorpha

Goodenia dimorpha, commonly known as the two-form goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually blue or purple and bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

Goodenia dimorpha is often found in heathlands and rocky areas, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
