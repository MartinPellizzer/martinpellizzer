Goeppertia ornata

Goeppertia ornata, commonly known as the Golden Canna, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green or variegated with yellow and white patterns. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming from summer to fall. Goeppertia ornata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
