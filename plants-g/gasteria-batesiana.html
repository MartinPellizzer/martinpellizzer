Gasteria batesiana

Gasteria batesiana, commonly known as the "Ponytail Plant," is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, often with a bluish-green hue and white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Gasteria batesiana is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
