Grevillea podalyriifolia

Grevillea podalyriifolia, commonly known as the silver-leaf grevillea, is a shrub native to western Australia.

It features narrow, silver-grey leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant floral displays make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
