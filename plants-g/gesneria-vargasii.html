Gesneria vargasii

Gesneria vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria vargasii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
