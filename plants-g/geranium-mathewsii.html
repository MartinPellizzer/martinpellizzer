Geranium mathewsii

Geranium mathewsii, commonly known as Mathews' geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features upright stems with opposite, serrated leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are showy, usually pink or purple, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Geranium mathewsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
