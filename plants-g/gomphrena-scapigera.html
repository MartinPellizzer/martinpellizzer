Gomphrena scapigera

Gomphrena scapigera, commonly known as the scapigerous gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often pink or red, spherical flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Gomphrena scapigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
