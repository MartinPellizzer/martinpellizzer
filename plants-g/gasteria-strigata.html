Gasteria strigata

Gasteria strigata, commonly known as the silver leaf gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a distinctive silver or grayish hue. The leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in small, stiff hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It produces pale green or yellowish flowers on slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
