Gesneria libanensis var. corrugata

Gesneria libanensis var. corrugata, commonly known as the wrinkled gesneria, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with a distinctive corrugated or ridged texture, which helps it store water in arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically blooming in spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
