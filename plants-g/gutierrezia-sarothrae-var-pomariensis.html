Gutierrezia sarothrae var. pomariensis

Gutierrezia sarothrae var. pomariensis, commonly known as silverleaf gumplant, is a variant of the silverleaf ironweed.

It is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features silver-gray, fuzzy foliage and produces yellow composite flowers in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert and semi-desert environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid conditions and it is sometimes used in xeriscaping.
