Gesneria × naegelioides

Gesneria × naegelioides, commonly known as the hybrid gesneria, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Gesneria obtusifolia and Gesneria rigida.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, characterized by its tropical origin and preference for humid, well-drained conditions. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and can produce showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
