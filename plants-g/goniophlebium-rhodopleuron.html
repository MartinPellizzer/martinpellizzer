Goniophlebium rhodopleuron

Goniophlebium rhodopleuron, commonly known as the red-stemmed fern, is a member of the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a small, terrestrial fern characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and delicate, pinnate fronds.
