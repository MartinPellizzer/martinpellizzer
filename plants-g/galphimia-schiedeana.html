Galphimia schiedeana

Galphimia schiedeana, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
