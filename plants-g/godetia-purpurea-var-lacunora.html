Godetia purpurea var. lacunora

Godetia purpurea var. lacunora, commonly known as the purple flax or scarlet flax, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant purple to red flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is known for its showy blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
