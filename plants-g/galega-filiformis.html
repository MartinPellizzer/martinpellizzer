Galega filiformis

Galega filiformis, commonly known as the slender goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Galega filiformis is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
