Geranium × hendersonii

Geranium × hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting dark veins. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are palmately lobed with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Geranium × hendersonii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
