Glochidion pubicapsa var. pubicapsa

Glochidion pubicapsa var. pubicapsa, commonly known as the hairy capsule glochidion, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves, which are usually odd-pinnate with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, and the fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds.
