Gomphogyne cirromitrata f. minor

Gomphogyne cirromitrata f. minor, commonly known as the lesser orchid of the genus Gomphogyne, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically feature a unique combination of colors and patterns. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its foliage consists of narrow, strap-like leaves that are adapted to conserve moisture.
