Guzmania sneidernii

Guzmania sneidernii, commonly known as the pink guzmania, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that often displays shades of pink, red, and orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect moisture and nutrients from the air. Its flowers are borne on a central spike and can last for several months.

Guzmania sneidernii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.
