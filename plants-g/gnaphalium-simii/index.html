Gnaphalium Simii

Gnaphalium simii is a unique flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau. It is known for its small, delicate white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. This species thrives in harsh environmental conditions, showcasing remarkable resilience and adaptability. Its scientific name honors the botanist who first documented it, highlighting its significance in botanical research. Despite its beauty, Gnaphalium simii remains a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Asteraceae family.
