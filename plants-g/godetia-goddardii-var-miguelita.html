Godetia goddardii var. miguelita

Godetia goddardii var. miguelita, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It features vibrant orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows in a bushy, upright form. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental and pollinator-friendly gardens.
