Gentiana biebersteinii

Gentiana biebersteinii, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Gentiana biebersteinii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vivid color and resilience in harsh environments.
