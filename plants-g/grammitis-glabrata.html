Grammitis glabrata

Grammitis glabrata, commonly known as the smooth grammitis, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features linear, glossy green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical forests.
