Guerkea gracillima

Guerkea gracillima, commonly known as the slender guerke, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 10 meters and has smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, narrow fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Guerkea gracillima is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
