Gnidia caniflora

Gnidia caniflora, commonly known as the knife-leaf gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that resemble a knife's blade, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Gnidia caniflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its unique foliage.
