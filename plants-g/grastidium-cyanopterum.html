Grastidium cyanopterum

Grastidium cyanopterum, commonly known as the blue-winged fern, is a strikingly beautiful plant characterized by its vibrant blue-green fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features intricate, finely divided leaves that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity to maintain its lush, vibrant coloration.

The unique coloration of its fronds makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
