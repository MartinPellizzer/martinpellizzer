Globularia alypum var. vesceritensis

Globularia alypum var. vesceritensis, commonly known as the Sicilian globularia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, globular clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
