Geocaryum tuberosum

Geocaryum tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous geocaryum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

Geocaryum tuberosum is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
