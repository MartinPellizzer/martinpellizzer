Grammatophyllum martae

Grammatophyllum martae, commonly known as the Christmas star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in a vibrant red and yellow coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a compact, clustered pattern.

Grammatophyllum martae thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
