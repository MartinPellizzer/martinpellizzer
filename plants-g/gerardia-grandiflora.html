Gerardia grandiflora

Gerardia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Gerardia grandiflora thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
