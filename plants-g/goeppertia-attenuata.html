Goeppertia attenuata

Goeppertia attenuata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Goeppertia attenuata is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
