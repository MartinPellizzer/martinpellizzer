Gladiolus striatus formosissimus

Gladiolus striatus formosissimus, commonly known as the Chinese gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, showy flowers in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant's botanical characteristics include narrow, grass-like leaves and a distinctive, sword-shaped flower spike. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
