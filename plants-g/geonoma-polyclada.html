Geonoma polyclada

Geonoma polyclada, commonly known as the clustered arrowroot, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright, sometimes branched stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. This plant is native to tropical regions of South America and is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage.
