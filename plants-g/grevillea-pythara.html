Grevillea pythara

Grevillea pythara, commonly known as the red-stemmed grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often red or purple at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Grevillea pythara is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
