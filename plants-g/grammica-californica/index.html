Grammica Californica

Grammica californica is a rare and delicate plant native to the coastal regions of California. It is known for its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes or along the coastline. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive. Its unique appearance and limited range make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
