Gerardia strictiflora

Gerardia strictiflora, commonly known as the rigid-flowered gerardia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Gerardia strictiflora thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
