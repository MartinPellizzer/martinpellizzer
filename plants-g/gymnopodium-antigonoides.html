Gymnopodium antigonoides

Gymnopodium antigonoides, commonly known as the smoothpod antelope bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees.

The seeds are enclosed in a smooth, leathery pod that splits open when mature.
