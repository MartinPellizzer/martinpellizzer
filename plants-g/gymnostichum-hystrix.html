Gymnostichum hystrix

Gymnostichum hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag or undulating leaf arrangement, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Gymnostichum hystrix is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
