Gladiolus arnoldianus

Gladiolus arnoldianus, commonly known as the Arnold's gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and blooms during the summer months. Its inflorescence is a spike of brightly colored flowers, which are often used in floral arrangements.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
