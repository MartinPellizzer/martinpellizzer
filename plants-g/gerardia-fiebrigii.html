Gerardia fiebrigii

Gerardia fiebrigii, commonly known as the Fiebrig's gerardia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

Gerardia fiebrigii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
