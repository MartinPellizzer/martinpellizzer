Galvezia limensis var. grandiflora

Galvezia limensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered galvezia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Galvezia limensis var. grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
