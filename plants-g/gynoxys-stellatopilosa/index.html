Gynoxys Stellatopilosa

Gynoxys stellatopilosa is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. Known for its unique star-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is now classified as endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival. Its beauty and ecological importance highlight the need for greater awareness and protection of such rare flora.
