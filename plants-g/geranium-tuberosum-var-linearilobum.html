Geranium tuberosum var. linearilobum

Geranium tuberosum var. linearilobum, commonly known as the slender-lobed geranium, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that have serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to red, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
