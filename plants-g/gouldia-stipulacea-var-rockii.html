Gouldia stipulacea var. rockii

Gouldia stipulacea var. rockii, commonly known as the rock gouldia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant, showy inflorescences that display a range of colors from red to orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
