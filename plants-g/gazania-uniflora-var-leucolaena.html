Gazania uniflora var. leucolaena

Gazania uniflora var. leucolaena, commonly known as the White Gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright white to pale yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it an attractive ornamental plant.
