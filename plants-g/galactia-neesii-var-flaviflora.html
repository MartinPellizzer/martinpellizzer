Galactia neesii var. flaviflora

Galactia neesii var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered galactia, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing legume that produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically borne on the stems. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
