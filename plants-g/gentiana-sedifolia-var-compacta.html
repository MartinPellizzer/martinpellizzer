Gentiana sedifolia var. compacta

Gentiana sedifolia var. compacta, commonly known as the compact gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
