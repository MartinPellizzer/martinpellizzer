Guettardella Attenuata

Guettardella attenuata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and swamps. Known for its small, pale yellow flowers, this species is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by supporting local insect populations. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss and invasive species.
