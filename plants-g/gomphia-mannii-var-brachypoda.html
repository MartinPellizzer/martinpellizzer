Gomphia mannii var. brachypoda

Gomphia mannii var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed gomphia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in grassland habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and restricted distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
