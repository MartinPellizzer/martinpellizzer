Gaertnera breviflora

Gaertnera breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered gaertnera, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool and moist environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gaertnera breviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
