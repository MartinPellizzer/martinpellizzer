Gymnocactus roseanus

Gymnocactus roseanus, commonly known as the roseate gymnocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces vibrant pink to rose-colored flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a shallow root system and thrives in well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid gardens.
