Gerascanthus lasiocalyx

Gerascanthus lasiocalyx, commonly known as the rough-hairy geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Gerascanthus lasiocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
