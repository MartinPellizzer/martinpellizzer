Globularia valentina

Globularia valentina, commonly known as the Valentine's globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that produces small, white or pink flowers in spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and delicate blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
