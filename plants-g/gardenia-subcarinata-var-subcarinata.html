Gardenia subcarinata var. subcarinata

Gardenia subcarinata var. subcarinata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed gardenia, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.
