Garcinia novoguineensis

Garcinia novoguineensis, commonly known as the African yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant's fruits are round, fleshy, and typically turn from green to yellow or red as they mature. Garcinia novoguineensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
