Gladiolus ecklonii subsp. rehmannii

Gladiolus ecklonii subsp.

rehmannii, commonly known as the Cape gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its striking appearance and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
