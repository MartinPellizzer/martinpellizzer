Gentiana swertioides

Gentiana swertioides, commonly known as the Swertii gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountain meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Gentiana swertioides is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
