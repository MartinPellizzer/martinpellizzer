Gymnocalycium hossei subsp. hossei

Gymnocalycium hossei subsp.

hossei, commonly known as the Hossi's moon cactus, is a succulent cactus native to the arid regions of South America. It is characterized by its globular, spiny stems that are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating.

The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it stores water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This species is popular among cactus enthusiasts due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
