Gamochaeta oligantha

Gamochaeta oligantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Gamochaeta oligantha is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
