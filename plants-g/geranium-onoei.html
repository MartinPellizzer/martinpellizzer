Geranium onoei

Geranium onoei, commonly known as the Japanese geranium, is a species native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge and produces clusters of small, pink or red flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Geranium onoei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
