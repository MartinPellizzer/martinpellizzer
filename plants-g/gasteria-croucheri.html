Gasteria croucheri

Gasteria croucheri, commonly known as the Croucher's gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Gasteria croucheri is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
