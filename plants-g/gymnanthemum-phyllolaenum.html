Gymnanthemum phyllolaenum

Gymnanthemum phyllolaenum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf starwort, is a small flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces small yellow flowers in the summer months.
