Gentiana septemfida subsp. overinii

Gentiana septemfida subsp.

overinii, commonly known as the Sevenleaf gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the family Gentianaceae. It is native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes.

The subspecies is distinguished by its finely divided, seven-lobed leaves, which give it its common name.
