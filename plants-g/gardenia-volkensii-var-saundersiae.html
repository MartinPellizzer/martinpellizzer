Gardenia volkensii var. saundersiae

Gardenia volkensii var. saundersiae, commonly known as the East African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a compact growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
