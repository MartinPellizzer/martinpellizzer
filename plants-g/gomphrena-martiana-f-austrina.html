Gomphrena martiana f. austrina

Gomphrena martiana f. austrina, commonly known as the Australian gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of bright pink to purple, globular flowers. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which can persist through the summer months.
