Genianthus crassifolius

Genianthus crassifolius, commonly known as the thick-leaved genianthus, is a perennial shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with thick, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
