Geum japonicum f. iyoanum

Geum japonicum f. iyoanum, commonly known as the Japanese avens or Iyoanum garden bell, is a compact perennial plant native to Japan.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and ecological landscapes.
