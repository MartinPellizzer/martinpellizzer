Geissorhiza esterhuyseniae

Geissorhiza esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species.
