Gentiana swertia

Gentiana swertia, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in China, India, and the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. Gentiana swertia thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered with a fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
