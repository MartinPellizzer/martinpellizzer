Genista suanica

Genista suanica, commonly known as the Chinese broom.

It is a flowering plant native to China and belongs to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista suanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
