Gloriosa homblei

Gloriosa homblei, commonly known as the Hombli's flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Colocasiaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from orange to red. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces long, climbing stems that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
