Gentiana rotundifolia

Gentiana rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Gentiana rotundifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
