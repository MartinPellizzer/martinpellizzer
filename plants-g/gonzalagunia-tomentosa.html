Gonzalagunia tomentosa

Gonzalagunia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly gonzalagunia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

Gonzalagunia tomentosa is primarily valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
