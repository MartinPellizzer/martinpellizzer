Gynochthodes hispida

Gynochthodes hispida, commonly known as the hispid gynochthodes, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
