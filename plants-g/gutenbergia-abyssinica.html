Gutenbergia abyssinica

Gutenbergia abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian geranium, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features dark green, serrated leaves and produces small, clustered fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Gutenbergia abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
