Gastrosulum annuum

Gastrosulum annuum, commonly known as the annual gastrosulum, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for dry climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and public landscapes.
