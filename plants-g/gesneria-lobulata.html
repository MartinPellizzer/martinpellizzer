Gesneria lobulata

Gesneria lobulata, commonly known as the spotted gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its broad, fleshy leaves with distinctive yellow spots. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Gesneria lobulata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
