Gynura valeriana

Gynura valeriana, commonly known as the velvetleaf or broadleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, spherical fruits.

Gynura valeriana is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
