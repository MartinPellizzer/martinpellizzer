Germainia thailandica

Germainia thailandica, commonly known as Thai geranium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Germainia thailandica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
