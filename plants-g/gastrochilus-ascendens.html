Gastrochilus ascendens

Gastrochilus ascendens, commonly known as the ascending stomach orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a single, erect, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, pale yellow to greenish-white, and are borne on a single, slender stem.

It is notable for its unique morphology and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils.
