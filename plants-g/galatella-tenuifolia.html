Galatella tenuifolia

Galatella tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf galatella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Galatella tenuifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
