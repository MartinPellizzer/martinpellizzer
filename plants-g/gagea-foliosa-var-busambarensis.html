Gagea foliosa var. busambarensis

Gagea foliosa var. busambarensis, commonly known as the Busambarensis Gagea, is a rare bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped white or pale blue flowers. The plant is adapted to cool, moist environments and thrives in well-drained soils. Its bulbs are small and often used in traditional medicine by local communities.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species.
