Grahamia kurtzii

Grahamia kurtzii, commonly known as the Kirtz's orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that often feature intricate patterns and vivid colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Grahamia kurtzii is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
