Glinus dictamnoides

Glinus dictamnoides, commonly known as false dictamnus, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement, and its flowers are small and clustered in racemes. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
