Gentiana occidentalis var. aragonensis

Gentiana occidentalis var. aragonensis, commonly known as the Aragon gentian, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as meadows and forest floors. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
