Gmelina magnifica

Gmelina magnifica, commonly known as the golden gmelina, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The tree produces woody capsules containing numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and reforestation projects.
