Genista Hirta Subsp. Elongata

Genista hirta subsp. elongata is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Fabaceae family. This subshrub is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in rocky and coastal environments. Its slender, branching stems and small yellow flowers make it a unique addition to Mediterranean landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and soil stabilization. Further study of this plant could offer insights into its ecological significance and conservation status.
