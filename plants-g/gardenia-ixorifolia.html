Gardenia ixorifolia

Gardenia ixorifolia, commonly known as the Madagascar gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are prominent and often have a waxy texture, which contributes to their ornamental appeal.

Gardenia ixorifolia prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
