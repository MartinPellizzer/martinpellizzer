Gentianella campestris var. suecica

Gentianella campestris var. suecica, commonly known as the Swedish gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in meadows and woodland edges. It has lanceolate leaves that are usually arranged in a basal rosette.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
