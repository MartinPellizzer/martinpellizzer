Geonoma wilsonii

Geonoma wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's bryophyte fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic fern that typically grows on trees in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has delicate, finely divided fronds that are usually less than 30 centimeters in length. Its spores are produced in small, clustered clusters on the underside of the fronds.

Geonoma wilsonii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.
