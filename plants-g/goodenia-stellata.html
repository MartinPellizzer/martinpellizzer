Goodenia stellata

Goodenia stellata, commonly known as the star goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and the flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Goodenia stellata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
