Gothofreda deltoidea

Gothofreda deltoidea, commonly known as the delta leaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant blooms that typically appear in late spring. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered in its natural range.
