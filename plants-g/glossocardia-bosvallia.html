Glossocardia bosvallia

Glossocardia bosvallia, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and hairy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae) and is known for its milky sap, which is toxic to some animals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.
