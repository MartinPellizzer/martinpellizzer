Grevillea decurrens

Grevillea decurrens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green with a reddish or brownish underside. The plant produces striking red or orange cylindrical flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry gardens.

Grevillea decurrens is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native birds.
