Gentianella Cerina

Gentianella cerina is a striking member of the gentian family known for its vivid blue flowers. Native to the alpine regions of Europe, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience in harsh mountain climates. The species is often found in rocky soils and open meadows at high elevations. It plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
