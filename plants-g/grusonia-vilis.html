Grusonia vilis

Grusonia vilis, commonly known as the slender grusonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Grusonia vilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in moderate climates.
