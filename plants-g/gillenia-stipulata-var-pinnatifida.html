Gillenia stipulata var. pinnatifida

Gillenia stipulata var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the pinnate gillenia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale yellow, umbel-like inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with a pinnate arrangement, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. It thrives in moist, rich soils and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
