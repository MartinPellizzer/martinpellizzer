Gorteria diffusa

Gorteria diffusa, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow petals and a central disk of darker yellow, resembling a daisy. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Gorteria diffusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
