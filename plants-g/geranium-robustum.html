Geranium robustum

Geranium robustum, commonly known as the hardy geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or ovate, with serrated edges, and are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is known for its resilience and ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
