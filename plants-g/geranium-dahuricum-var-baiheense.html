Geranium dahuricum var. baiheense

Geranium dahuricum var. baiheense, commonly known as the Baihe geranium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and borders.
