Gueldenstaedtia verna f. multiflora

Gueldenstaedtia verna f. multiflora, commonly known as the spring bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
