Galaxia multiflora

Galaxia multiflora, commonly known as the California galax, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Galaxia multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
