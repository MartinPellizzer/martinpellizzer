Goodenia eatoniana

Goodenia eatoniana, commonly known as the purple goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

Goodenia eatoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
