Galium × jubilaeare

Galium × jubilaeare, commonly known as the jubilee bedstraw, is a hybrid plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, ascending stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually oval to ovate in shape. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Galium × jubilaeare is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
