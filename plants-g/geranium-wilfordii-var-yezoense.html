Geranium wilfordii var. yezoense

Geranium wilfordii var. yezoense, commonly known as the Yezo geranium, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
