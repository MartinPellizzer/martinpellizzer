Genista Tinctoria Var. Plena

Genista tinctoria var. plena is a striking flowering plant known for its dense clusters of yellow blooms. It belongs to the legume family and is native to southern Europe and western Asia. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and historical use in dye production. Its compact growth makes it ideal for gardens and containers. With its vibrant color and hardy nature, it remains a popular choice among horticulturists.
