Gonocarpus confertifolius var. confertifolius

Gonocarpus confertifolius var. confertifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf gonocarpus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often arranged in a dense, bushy form. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are fibrous and can develop into a thick, woody structure.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along riverbanks.
