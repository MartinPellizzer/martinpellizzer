Garcinia platyphylla

Garcinia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange or red berries. Its fruit contains compounds that have been studied for potential health benefits.

Garcinia platyphylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
