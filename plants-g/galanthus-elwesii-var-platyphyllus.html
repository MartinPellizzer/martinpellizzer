Galanthus elwesii var. platyphyllus

Galanthus elwesii var. platyphyllus, commonly known as the broad-leaved snowdrop, is a perennial bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It produces delicate white flowers with a greenish tinge, typically blooming in early spring. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that emerge from the base of the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and graceful appearance.
