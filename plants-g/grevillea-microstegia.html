Grevillea microstegia

Grevillea microstegia, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland regions, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions.

Grevillea microstegia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
