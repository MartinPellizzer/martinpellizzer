Gentianella clarenii

Gentianella clarenii, commonly known as the Claren's gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Gentianella clarenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
