Gardenia florida var. boninensis

Gardenia florida var. boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin gardenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Bonin Islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, waxy white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elliptical, yellowish-orange fruits that contain black, hard seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
