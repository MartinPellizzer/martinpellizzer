Goodenia pulchella

Goodenia pulchella, commonly known as the pretty goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

Goodenia pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
