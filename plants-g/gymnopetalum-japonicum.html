Gymnopetalum japonicum

Gymnopetalum japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese star anise.

It is a flowering plant native to Japan and belongs to the family Lauraceae. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, often used in traditional herbal medicine.

The fruit, which resembles a star, is harvested for its aromatic properties and is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
