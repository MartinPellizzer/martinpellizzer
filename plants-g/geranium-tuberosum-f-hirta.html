Geranium tuberosum f. hirta

Geranium tuberosum f. hirta, commonly known as the hairy geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
