Galium × pseudoboreale

Galium × pseudoboreale, commonly known as the northern bedstraw, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped. Galium × pseudoboreale is known for its fragrant foliage and is often found in the northern regions of North America.
