Gmelina palawensis subsp. palawensis

Gmelina palawensis subsp.

palawensis, commonly known as the Palawan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines. It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability.

The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by oval-shaped fruits.

This species is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
