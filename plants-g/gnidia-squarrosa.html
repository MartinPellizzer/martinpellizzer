Gnidia squarrosa

Gnidia squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly knobthorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, which are often covered in a woolly or hairy texture. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually covered with fine, white hairs.

The plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid or coastal environments.
