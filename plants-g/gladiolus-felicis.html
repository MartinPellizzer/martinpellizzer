Gladiolus felicis

Gladiolus felicis, commonly known as the Happy Gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant is also valued for its resilience and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
