Guarea sericea

Guarea sericea, commonly known as the silky guarea, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and elliptical leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically white or pale yellow, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.
