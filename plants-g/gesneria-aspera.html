Gesneria aspera

Gesneria aspera, commonly known as the rough gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, rough texture. It produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months.

Gesneria aspera is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant blooms.
