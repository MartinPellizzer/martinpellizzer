Gynura caerulea

Gynura caerulea, commonly known as blue gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-green leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
