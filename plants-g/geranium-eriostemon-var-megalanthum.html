Geranium eriostemon var. megalanthum

Geranium eriostemon var. megalanthum, commonly known as the giant geranium, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive silver-white underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its upright growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
