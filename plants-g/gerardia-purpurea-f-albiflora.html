Gerardia purpurea f. albiflora

Gerardia purpurea f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered gerardia, is a variant of the common gerardia species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its most distinguishing characteristic is its pale to white flowers, which contrast with the green leaves.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky habitats.
