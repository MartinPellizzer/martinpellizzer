Gardenia augusta f. shanpinensis

Gardenia augusta f. shanpinensis, commonly known as the Shanpinensis gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is favored in gardens for its elegant appearance. It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil.

The fruit of this plant is a hard, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
