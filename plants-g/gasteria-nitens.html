Gasteria nitens

Gasteria nitens, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with tubular, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Gasteria nitens is popular in rock gardens and as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
