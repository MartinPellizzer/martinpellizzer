Galeopsis intermedia subsp. cebennensis

Galeopsis intermedia subsp.

cebennensis, commonly known as the Alpine hedge-nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants.

The species is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves with serrated edges, and small, pale blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, often at elevations above 1,500 meters.
