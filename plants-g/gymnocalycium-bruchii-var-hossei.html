Gymnocalycium bruchii var. hossei

Gymnocalycium bruchii var. hossei, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Cactus, is a species of cactus native to northern Argentina.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical stem that often turns blue or bluish-green when mature. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, with spines that provide protection and reduce water loss.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscape designs.
