Globularia spinosa var. minor

Globularia spinosa var. minor, commonly known as the lesser globularia, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to southern Europe.

It features small, spherical or globose flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
