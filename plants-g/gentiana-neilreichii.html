Gentiana neilreichii

Gentiana neilreichii, commonly known as Neilreich's gentian, is a striking alpine plant native to the high mountains of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at elevations above 2,000 meters. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
