Graptophyllum hortense var. rubrum

Graptophyllum hortense var. rubrum, commonly known as the red-flowered garden graptophyllum, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family, characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, fuzzy leaves that provide a lush, textured backdrop to its colorful blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold visual impact.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity in both garden and container settings.
