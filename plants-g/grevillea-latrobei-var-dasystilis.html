Grevillea latrobei var. dasystilis

Grevillea latrobei var. dasystilis, commonly known as the narrow-styled grevillea, is a shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
