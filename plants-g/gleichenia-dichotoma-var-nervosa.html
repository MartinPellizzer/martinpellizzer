Gleichenia dichotoma var. nervosa

Gleichenia dichotoma var. nervosa, commonly known as the veined maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a small, epiphytic fern typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant has delicate, lace-like fronds with a distinct veining pattern. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in humid, indoor environments.
