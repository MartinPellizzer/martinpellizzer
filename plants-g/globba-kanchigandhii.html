Globba kanchigandhii

Globba kanchigandhii, commonly known as Kanchi fragrant globba, is a herbaceous plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-colored flowers. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.
