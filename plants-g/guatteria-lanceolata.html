Guatteria lanceolata

Guatteria lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf guatteria, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, fleshy berries. Guatteria lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
