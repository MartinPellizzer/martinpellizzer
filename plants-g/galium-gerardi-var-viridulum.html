Galium gerardi var. viridulum

Galium gerardi var. viridulum, commonly known as greenleaf wood-remover, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often prostrate stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves that are usually smooth and dark green. Its leaves and stems are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
