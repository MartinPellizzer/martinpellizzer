Goodenia pilosa

Goodenia pilosa, commonly known as the hairy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Goodenia pilosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
