Galium puncticulosum

Galium puncticulosum, commonly known as the punctuated bedstraw, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit and features small, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. Galium puncticulosum is often found in moist, disturbed soils in woodland or meadow habitats.
