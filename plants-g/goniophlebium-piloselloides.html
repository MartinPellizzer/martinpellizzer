Goniophlebium piloselloides

Goniophlebium piloselloides, commonly known as the silky centipede plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Native to South America, particularly Brazil, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique texture and foliage.
