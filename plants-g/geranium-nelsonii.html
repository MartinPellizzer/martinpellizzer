Geranium nelsonii

Geranium nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium nelsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
