Grewia goetzeana

Grewia goetzeana, commonly known as the yellow globemallow, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
