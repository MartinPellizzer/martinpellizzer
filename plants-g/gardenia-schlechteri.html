Gardenia schlechteri

Gardenia schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's gardenia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia schlechteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its pleasant citrus-like scent.
