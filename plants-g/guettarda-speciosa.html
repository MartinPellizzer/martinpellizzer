Guettarda speciosa

Guettarda speciosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its dense, glossy foliage. The plant produces clusters of yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a striking ornamental species. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Guettarda speciosa is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to coastal conditions.
