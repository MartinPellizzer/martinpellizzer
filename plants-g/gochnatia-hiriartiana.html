Gochnatia hiriartiana

Gochnatia hiriartiana, commonly known as the Hiriart's gochnatia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its inflorescence consists of small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators.

Gochnatia hiriartiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
