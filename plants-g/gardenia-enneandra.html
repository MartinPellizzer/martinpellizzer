Gardenia enneandra

Gardenia enneandra, commonly known as the African gardenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and are often used in floral arrangements.

Gardenia enneandra prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
