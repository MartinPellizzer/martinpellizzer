Galatella tianschanica

Galatella tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It has lanceolate leaves that are often hairy, and its stems are erect and branching.

Galatella tianschanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
