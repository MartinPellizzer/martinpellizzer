Gladiolus vernus

Gladiolus vernus, commonly known as the spring gladiolus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of brightly colored flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in early spring. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
