Gynura latifolia

Gynura latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gynura, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively easy to care for.

Gynura latifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
