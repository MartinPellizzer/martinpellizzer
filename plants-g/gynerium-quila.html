Gynerium quila

Gynerium quila, commonly known as the giant reed, is a tall, reed-like plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It is characterized by its hollow, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is often found in shallow water or damp soil, where it forms dense stands. Gynerium quila is a perennial herb that can grow up to several meters in height.

It plays an important role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
