Grewia hirsuta var. helicterifolia

Grewia hirsuta var. helicterifolia, commonly known as the silky bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The leaves are palmately lobed and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
