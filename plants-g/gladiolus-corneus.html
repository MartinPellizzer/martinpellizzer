Gladiolus corneus

Gladiolus corneus, commonly known as the horned gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
