Gossypium roseum

Gossypium roseum, commonly known as the rose cotton or pink cotton, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant produces fluffy, pink or white fibers that are harvested for textile use. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a soft, fuzzy texture.

Gossypium roseum is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its economic importance in the cotton industry.
