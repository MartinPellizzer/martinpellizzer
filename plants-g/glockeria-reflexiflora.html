Glockeria reflexiflora

Glockeria reflexiflora, commonly known as the spiral grass, is a unique member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves.

Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often curl in a helical pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by narrow, cylindrical seed spikes.

Glockeria reflexiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant, spiral foliage.
