Gomphrena nana

Gomphrena nana, commonly known as dwarf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous annual with compact, bushy growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a distinctive, spiky appearance that resembles a pom-pom. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
