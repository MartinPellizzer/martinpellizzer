Gurania seemanniana

Gurania seemanniana, commonly known as the yellow sand mat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in harsh environments.

Gurania seemanniana is often found growing on sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
