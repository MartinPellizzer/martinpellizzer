Grammitis linearis

Grammitis linearis, commonly known as the slender chain fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter wide. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its small, clustered spore-producing structures are located on the underside of the leaves.

Grammitis linearis is well adapted to epiphytic growth, often growing on trees or rocks in humid forests.
