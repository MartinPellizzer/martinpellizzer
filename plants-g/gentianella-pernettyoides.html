Gentianella pernettyoides

Gentianella pernettyoides, commonly known as the alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. It is a member of the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, often blue or yellow flowers.

Gentianella pernettyoides is often found in open meadows and on slopes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
