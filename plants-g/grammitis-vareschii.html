Grammitis vareschii

Grammitis vareschii, commonly known as the tropical fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it reproduces through spore dispersal rather than seeds.

Grammitis vareschii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
