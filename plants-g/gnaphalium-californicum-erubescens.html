Gnaphalium californicum erubescens

Gnaphalium californicum erubescens, commonly known as the California rose-gold gumweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and the flowers are small, yellowish to reddish in color.
