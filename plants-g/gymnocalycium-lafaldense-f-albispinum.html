Gymnocalycium lafaldense f. albispinum

Gymnocalycium lafaldense f. albispinum, commonly known as the white spined moon cactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to Argentina.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a vibrant contrast to its otherwise muted appearance. Its unique morphology makes it a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
