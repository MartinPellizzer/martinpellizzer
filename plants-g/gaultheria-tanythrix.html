Gaultheria tanythrix

Gaultheria tanythrix, commonly known as the slender waxbush, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by bright red berries that are edible for certain bird species. Gaultheria tanythrix is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
