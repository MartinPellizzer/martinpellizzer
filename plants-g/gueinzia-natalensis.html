Gueinzia natalensis

Gueinzia natalensis, commonly known as Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns and can tolerate drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the region's flora and ecosystem.
