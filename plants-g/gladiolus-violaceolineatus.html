Gladiolus violaceolineatus

Gladiolus violaceolineatus, commonly known as the purple-veined gladiolus, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, spike-like inflorescences composed of numerous petals. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with intricate veining that adds to their visual appeal. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both horticultural collections and botanical studies.
