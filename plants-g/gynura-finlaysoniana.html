Gynura finlaysoniana

Gynura finlaysoniana, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically light green with a silvery sheen, enhancing its visual appeal.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic value.
