Gnetum cavaleriei

Gnetum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese gnetum, is a gymnosperm native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, scale-like seeds. The plant exhibits a unique combination of traits, including vessel elements in its xylem, which is rare among gymnosperms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ecological significance lies in its role as a living fossil, providing insights into the evolution of seed plants.
