Galeopsis ochroleuca

Galeopsis ochroleuca, commonly known as yellow hedge nettle, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, and it produces small, dry fruits called nutlets.
