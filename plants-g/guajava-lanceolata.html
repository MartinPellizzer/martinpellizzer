Guajava lanceolata

Guajava lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf guajava, is a species of flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that contains a single seed.
