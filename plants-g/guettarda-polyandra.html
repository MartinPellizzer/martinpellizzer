Guettarda polyandra

Guettarda polyandra, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the warmer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
