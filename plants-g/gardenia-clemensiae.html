Gardenia clemensiae

Gardenia clemensiae, commonly known as the Philippine gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty and long-lasting scent.
