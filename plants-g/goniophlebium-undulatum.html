Goniophlebium undulatum

Goniophlebium undulatum, commonly known as the wavy-veined moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. This moss is notable for its resilience and ability to survive in nutrient-poor conditions.
