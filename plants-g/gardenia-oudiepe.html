Gardenia oudiepe

Gardenia oudiepe, commonly known as the golden gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white to yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their striking color and long-lasting blooms.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid environments.
