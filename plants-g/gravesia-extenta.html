Gravesia extenta

Gravesia extenta, commonly known as the giant gravesia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, often in shades of red, yellow, or orange. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
