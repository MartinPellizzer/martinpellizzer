Garcinia basacensis

Garcinia basacensis, commonly known as the Borneo Garcinia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the rainforests of Borneo.

It is a member of the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces bright red fruits that are visually striking and contain a high concentration of bioactive compounds. Garcinia basacensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for potential medicinal properties due to its phytochemical composition.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important component of the tropical ecosystems it inhabits.
