Garnotia himalayensis var. lanceolata

Garnotia himalayensis var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Himalayan garnet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to red. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to high-altitude conditions.
