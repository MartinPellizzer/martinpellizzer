Gesneria fascialis

Gesneria fascialis, commonly known as the Zebra Gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a zebra-like pattern of green and white. It produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in the spring.

Gesneria fascialis is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
