Grevillea baueri subsp. asperula

Grevillea baueri subsp.

asperula, commonly known as the rough-leaved grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, rough-textured leaves that are typically grey-green in color.

The plant produces striking red or orange inflorescences that bloom in spring. It is well-suited to dry, well-drained soils and full sun. Grevillea baueri subsp.

asperula is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
