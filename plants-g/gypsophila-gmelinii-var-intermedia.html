Gypsophila gmelinii var. intermedia

Gypsophila gmelinii var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate grass-of-Parnassus, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, branched stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
