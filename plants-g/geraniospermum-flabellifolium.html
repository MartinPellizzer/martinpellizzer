Geraniospermum flabellifolium

Geraniospermum flabellifolium, commonly known as the fan-leaved geraniosperm, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that resemble a fan. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep root systems and waxy leaf surfaces.

Geraniospermum flabellifolium is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
