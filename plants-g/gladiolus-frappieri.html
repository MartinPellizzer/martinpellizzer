Gladiolus frappieri

Gladiolus frappieri, commonly known as the Frappier's gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with tall, sturdy stems. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.
