Geranium natalense

Geranium natalense, commonly known as Natal geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in summer. The plant has upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
