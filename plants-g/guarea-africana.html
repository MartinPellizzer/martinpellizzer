Guarea africana

Guarea africana, commonly known as African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The tree is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.
