Goodenia reinwardtii

Goodenia reinwardtii, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy, and it produces pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months.
