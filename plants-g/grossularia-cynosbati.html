Grossularia cynosbati

Grossularia cynosbati, commonly known as the Chinese hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by red or orange berries that are edible but often sour.
