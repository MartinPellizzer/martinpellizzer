Gaertnera obovata var. sphaerocarpa

Gaertnera obovata var. sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular guava, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is distinctive, with a rounded, spherical shape and a thick, leathery skin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
