Gladiolus albens

Gladiolus albens, commonly known as the white gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces white or pale pink blooms with a distinctive, narrow, grass-like leaf structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.
