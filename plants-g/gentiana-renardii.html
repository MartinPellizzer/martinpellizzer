Gentiana renardii

Gentiana renardii, commonly known as Renard's gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana renardii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
