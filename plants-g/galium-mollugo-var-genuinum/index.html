Galium Mollugo Var. Genuinum

Galium mollugo var. genuinum is a lesser-known variant of the common bedstraw plant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. This plant is characterized by its small, white flowers and sprawling growth habit. Though not widely cultivated, it holds ecological significance in its native habitats. Its unique traits make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
