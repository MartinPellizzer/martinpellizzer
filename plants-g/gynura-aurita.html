Gynura aurita

Gynura aurita, commonly known as the velvetleaf or golden nettle, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with yellow or orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Gynura aurita is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
