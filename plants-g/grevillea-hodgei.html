Grevillea hodgei

Grevillea hodgei, commonly known as the Hodge's grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features distinctive, feather-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in spikes, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for native landscaping.
