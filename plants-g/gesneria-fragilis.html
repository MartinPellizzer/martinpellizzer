Gesneria fragilis

Gesneria fragilis, commonly known as the fragrant gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Gesneria fragilis is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
