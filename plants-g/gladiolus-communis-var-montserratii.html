Gladiolus communis var. montserratii

Gladiolus communis var. montserratii, commonly known as the Montserrat gladiolus, is a rare and striking botanical species native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is closely related to other gladiolus species found in tropical regions.
