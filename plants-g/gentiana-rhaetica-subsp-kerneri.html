Gentiana rhaetica subsp. kerneri

Gentiana rhaetica subsp.

kerneri, commonly known as the Rhaetian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in mountain meadows.
