Garcinia desrousseauxii

Garcinia desrousseauxii, commonly known as the African yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of West Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, yellow-orange fruits with a thick, aromatic rind and a segmented, juicy pulp. Garcinia desrousseauxii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking component of its native ecosystems.
