Grevillea deflexa

Grevillea deflexa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Grevillea deflexa is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
