Geranium fragile

Geranium fragile, commonly known as the fragrant geranium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, hairy leaves and small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which release a sweet, spicy scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in temperate climates with minimal care.
