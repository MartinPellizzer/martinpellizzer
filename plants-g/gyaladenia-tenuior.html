Gyaladenia tenuior

Gyaladenia tenuior, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its slender stems and bell-shaped flowers. Native to Europe and western Asia, it typically grows in moist, grassy habitats. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
