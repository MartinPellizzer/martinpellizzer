Gardenia pomifera

Gardenia pomifera, commonly known as the tropical gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its fruits are small, round, and contain a single seed, which is used in traditional medicine.

Gardenia pomifera is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in perfumery.
