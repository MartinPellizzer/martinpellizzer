Grenacheria nebulosa

Grenacheria nebulosa, commonly known as the misty grenacher, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
