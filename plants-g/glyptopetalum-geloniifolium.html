Glyptopetalum geloniifolium

Glyptopetalum geloniifolium, commonly known as the geloniifolium bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
