Galium sublutaeum

Galium sublutaeum, commonly known as the smooth bedstraw, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, prostrate stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization in its native range.
