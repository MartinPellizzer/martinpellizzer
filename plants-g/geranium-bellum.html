Geranium bellum

Geranium bellum, commonly known as the bell-shaped geranium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or rounded with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
