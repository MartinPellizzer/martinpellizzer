Gossypium puniceum

Gossypium puniceum, commonly known as the crimson cotton or red cotton, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, the Middle East, and southern Asia. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and its production of fluffy, pinkish or reddish cotton fibers. These fibers are used in textile manufacturing, similar to other cotton species.

Gossypium puniceum is also valued for its ornamental qualities due to its vibrant flower colors and unique foliage.
