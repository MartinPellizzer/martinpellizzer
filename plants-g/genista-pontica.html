Genista pontica

Genista pontica, commonly known as the Pontic broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its slender, branched stems are covered in fine, hairy leaves.

Genista pontica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
