Gnaphalium dioiecium

Gnaphalium dioiecium, commonly known as the male and female flowering plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and is typically found in moist, open habitats.
