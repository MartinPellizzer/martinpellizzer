Grammitis silvicola

Grammitis silvicola, commonly known as the forest fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Its small size and intricate structure make it an important component of the understory vegetation in its native habitats.
