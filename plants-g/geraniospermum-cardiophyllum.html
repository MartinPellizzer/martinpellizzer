Geraniospermum cardiophyllum

Geraniospermum cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heartleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
