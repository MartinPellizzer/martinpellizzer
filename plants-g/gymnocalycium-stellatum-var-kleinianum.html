Gymnocalycium stellatum var. kleinianum

Gymnocalycium stellatum var. kleinianum, commonly known as the Kleinian star cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Argentina.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in dense, white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
