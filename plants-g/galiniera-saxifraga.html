Galiniera saxifraga

Galiniera saxifraga, commonly known as the rock heath, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. Galiniera saxifraga is adapted to arid conditions and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
