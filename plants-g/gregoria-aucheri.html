Gregoria aucheri

Gregoria aucheri, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its striking, shield-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Gregoria aucheri thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to indoor or outdoor spaces.
