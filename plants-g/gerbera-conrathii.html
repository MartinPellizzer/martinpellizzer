Gerbera conrathii

Gerbera conrathii, commonly known as the Conrath's gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Gerbera conrathii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
