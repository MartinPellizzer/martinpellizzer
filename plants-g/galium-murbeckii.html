Galium murbeckii

Galium murbeckii, commonly known as Murbeck's bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, often hairy stems that are often prostrate or ascending. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Small white or pale pink flowers are clustered in cymes, blooming from late spring to early summer.
