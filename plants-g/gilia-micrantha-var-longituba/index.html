Gilia Micrantha Var. Longituba

Gilia micrantha var. longituba is a lesser-known yet fascinating flowering plant native to the western United States. This variant of Gilia micrantha is distinguished by its elongated floral tubes, which set it apart from other members of the genus. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces delicate, blue or purple flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty and ecological importance, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in botanical studies.
