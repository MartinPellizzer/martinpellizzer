Geranium × magnificum

Geranium × magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent geranium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with silver or purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
