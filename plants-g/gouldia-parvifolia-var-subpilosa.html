Gouldia parvifolia var. subpilosa

Gouldia parvifolia var. subpilosa, commonly known as the slender gouldia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, grass-like foliage with a distinctive bluish-green hue and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
