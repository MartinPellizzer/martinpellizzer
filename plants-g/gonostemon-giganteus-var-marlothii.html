Gonostemon giganteus var. marlothii

Gonostemon giganteus var. marlothii, commonly known as the Marloth's giant gonostemon, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
