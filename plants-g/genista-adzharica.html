Genista adzharica

Genista adzharica, commonly known as the Caucasian broom, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

Genista adzharica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and early spring blooms.
