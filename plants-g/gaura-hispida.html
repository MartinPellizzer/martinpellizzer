Gaura hispida

Gaura hispida, commonly known as rough gaura, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open woodlands and prairies. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its foliage is covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving it a distinctive, rough texture.

Gaura hispida is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
