Genista sericea

Genista sericea, commonly known as silverbush or silky broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its fine, silvery-gray foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, contributing to its silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Genista sericea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
