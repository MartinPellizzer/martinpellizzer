Gesneria houttei

Gesneria houttei, commonly known as the Houtte's gesneria, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, broad-leafed foliage. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are showy, with bright colors and tubular shapes, often attracting pollinators like hummingbirds.

Gesneria houttei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
