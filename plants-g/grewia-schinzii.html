Grewia schinzii

Grewia schinzii, commonly known as the red gum tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish bark and small, clustered white flowers that appear in summer. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
