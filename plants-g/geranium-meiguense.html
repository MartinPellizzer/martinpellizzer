Geranium meiguense

Geranium meiguense, commonly known as the Chinese geranium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces vibrant pink or red blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Geranium meiguense is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
