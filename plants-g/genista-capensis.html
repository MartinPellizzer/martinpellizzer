Genista capensis

Genista capensis, commonly known as the Cape broom, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption.

Its ornamental value makes it popular in gardens, though it can be invasive in some regions.
