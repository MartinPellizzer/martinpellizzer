Gesneria elliptica

Gesneria elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen, strap-like leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria elliptica is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
