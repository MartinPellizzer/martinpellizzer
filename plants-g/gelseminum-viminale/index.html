Gelseminum Viminale

Gelseminum viminale is a unique and striking plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and climbing growth habit. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its leaves and flowers contain toxic alkaloids, making it both fascinating and potentially dangerous. Despite its risks, Gelseminum viminale remains a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
