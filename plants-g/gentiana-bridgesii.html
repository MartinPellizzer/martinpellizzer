Gentiana bridgesii

Gentiana bridgesii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is a perennial herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Gentiana bridgesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of ecological importance to local pollinators.
