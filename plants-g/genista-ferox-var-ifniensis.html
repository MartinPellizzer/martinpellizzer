Genista ferox var. ifniensis

Genista ferox var. ifniensis, commonly known as the Ifni spurgebush, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

It is an important species for local biodiversity and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
