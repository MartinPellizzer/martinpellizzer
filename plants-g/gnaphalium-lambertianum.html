Gnaphalium lambertianum

Gnaphalium lambertianum, commonly known as Lambert's feather grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Gnaphalium lambertianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
