Grevillea juncifolia subsp. temulenta

Grevillea juncifolia subsp.

temulenta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically grey-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast.

The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers in clusters, which attract nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and semi-arid gardens.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
