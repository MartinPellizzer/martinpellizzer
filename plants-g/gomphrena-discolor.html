Gomphrena discolor

Gomphrena discolor, commonly known as the purple top or redtop smartweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bract-like flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting central area. The plant typically grows in moist soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are erect and often branched.

Gomphrena discolor is a hardy annual that thrives in full sun and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
