Gentiana insubrica

Gentiana insubrica, commonly known as the Italian gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Gentiana insubrica is often found in meadows, woodlands, and rocky slopes at high altitudes.
