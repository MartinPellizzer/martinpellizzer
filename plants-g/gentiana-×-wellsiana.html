Gentiana × wellsiana

Gentiana × wellsiana, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and dark green, often with a hairy texture.

Gentiana × wellsiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
