Geranium capitatum

Geranium capitatum, commonly known as the Cape geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink or purple flowers atop its stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and compact size.

Geranium capitatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
