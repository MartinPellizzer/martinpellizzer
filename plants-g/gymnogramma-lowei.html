Gymnogramma lowei

Gymnogramma lowei, commonly known as the lowei rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. Gymnogramma lowei is often found growing on rocky substrates in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular species among fern enthusiasts and botanists.
