Goodenia melanoptera

Goodenia melanoptera, commonly known as the black-leaved goodenia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive dark green, almost black foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia melanoptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
