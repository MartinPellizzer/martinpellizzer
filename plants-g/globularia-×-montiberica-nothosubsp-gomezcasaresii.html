Globularia × montiberica nothosubsp.

gomezcasaresii, commonly known as the Canary Island globularia, is a hybrid plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
