Gomidesia spruceana var. obtusifolia

Gomidesia spruceana var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved spruce pine, is a rare evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by woody, cone-like structures containing edible, fleshy seeds.
