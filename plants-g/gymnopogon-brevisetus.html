Gymnopogon brevisetus

Gymnopogon brevisetus, commonly known as the short-stemmed grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and short, sturdy stems. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and is adapted to dry conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Gymnopogon brevisetus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for local wildlife.
