Gynerium rudiusculum

Gynerium rudiusculum, commonly known as the small roughdock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks. It has rough, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer.
