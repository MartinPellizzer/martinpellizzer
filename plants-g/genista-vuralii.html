Genista vuralii

Genista vuralii, commonly known as Turkish broom, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Turkey and parts of the Middle East, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, yellowish stems and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista vuralii is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
