Grevillea cooljarloo

Grevillea cooljarloo, commonly known as the Cooljarloo grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features striking, feather-like leaves and produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Grevillea cooljarloo is also an important plant for local wildlife, providing nectar for birds and insects.
