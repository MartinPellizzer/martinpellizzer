Gesneria stricta

Gesneria stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gesneria, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a compact, evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

Gesneria stricta is relatively low-maintenance and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
