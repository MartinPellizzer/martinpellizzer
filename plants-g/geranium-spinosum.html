Geranium spinosum

Geranium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are often covered in stiff, bristly hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically lobed and have a rough, textured surface.

Geranium spinosum is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in grassland and shrubland habitats.
