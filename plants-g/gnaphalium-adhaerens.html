Gnaphalium adhaerens

Gnaphalium adhaerens, commonly known as the sticky everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South America.

Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating that gives them a silvery appearance.

Gnaphalium adhaerens is often used in floral arrangements due to its long-lasting flowers and unique texture.
