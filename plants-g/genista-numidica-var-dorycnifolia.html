Genista numidica var. dorycnifolia

Genista numidica var. dorycnifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, needle-like leaves. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
