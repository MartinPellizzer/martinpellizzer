Goodenia pusilliflora

Goodenia pusilliflora, commonly known as the small-flowered goodenia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Australia.

It features small, often blue or purple, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often seen in coastal regions.

Goodenia pusilliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
