Geonoma spruceana

Geonoma spruceana, commonly known as the Spruce's fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
