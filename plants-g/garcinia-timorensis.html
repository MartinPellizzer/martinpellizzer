Garcinia timorensis

Garcinia timorensis, commonly known as Timor garcinia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Timor in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy green leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically yellow or orange in color. Garcinia timorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and fruit.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
