Gossia sankowskyorum

Gossia sankowskyorum, commonly known as the mountain gum.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to the cool temperate regions of southern Australia. The plant features lanceolate to ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by woody, spherical fruits.

Gossia sankowskyorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscapes.
