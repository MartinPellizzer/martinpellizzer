Genista tricuspidata var. crebrispina

Genista tricuspidata var. crebrispina, commonly known as the slender broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being narrow and pointed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
