Globularia × cunyi

Globularia × cunyi, commonly known as the hybrid globularia, is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is native to the Mediterranean region, where it thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant produces pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
