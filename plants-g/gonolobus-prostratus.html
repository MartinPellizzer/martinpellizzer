Gonolobus prostratus

Gonolobus prostratus, commonly known as the prostrate gonolobus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and the Pacific Islands.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gonolobus prostratus is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
