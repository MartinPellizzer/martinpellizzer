Georgeantha

Georgeantha, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy texture.

Georgeantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
