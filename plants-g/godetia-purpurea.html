Godetia purpurea

Godetia purpurea, commonly known as the purple gloriosa, is an annual flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful annuals.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and unique floral structure.
