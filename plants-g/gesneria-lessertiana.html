Gesneria lessertiana

Gesneria lessertiana, commonly known as the Lessert's gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its attractive, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Gesneria lessertiana is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
