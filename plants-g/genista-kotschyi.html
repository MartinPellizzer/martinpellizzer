Genista kotschyi

Genista kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by narrow, bean-like pods.

Its shrub-like form makes it a hardy and resilient plant suitable for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
