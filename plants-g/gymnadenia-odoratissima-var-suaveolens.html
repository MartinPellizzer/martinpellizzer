Gymnadenia odoratissima var. suaveolens

Gymnadenia odoratissima var. suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented grass of the genus Gymnadenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that emit a sweet, pleasant fragrance.
