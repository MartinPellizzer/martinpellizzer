Genista × norpalentina

Genista × norpalentina, commonly known as the Spanish broom.

It is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its dense, spiny growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and coastal areas. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are clustered in racemes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
