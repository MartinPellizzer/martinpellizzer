Goodenia barbata

Goodenia barbata, commonly known as the barbed goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, barbed bracts that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Goodenia barbata is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
