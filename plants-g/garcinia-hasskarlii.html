Garcinia hasskarlii

Garcinia hasskarlii, commonly known as the African yellow wood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, yellowish bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain a single seed. Garcinia hasskarlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both ornamental gardening and reforestation projects.
