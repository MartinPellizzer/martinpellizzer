Gnaphalium lanceolatum

Gnaphalium lanceolatum, commonly known as the narrowleaf everlasting, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It produces copious amounts of fine, white, powdery seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind.

This species is also known for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
