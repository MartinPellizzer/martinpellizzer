Gulubia

Gulubia, commonly known as the "blue bean" or *Psoralea quadrifaria*, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that produces small, yellow flowers and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple seed pods. The plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid climates. Its leaves are compound, with several pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Gulubia is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
