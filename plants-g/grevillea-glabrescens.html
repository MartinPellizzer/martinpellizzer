Grevillea glabrescens

Grevillea glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves and vibrant, red or orange flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
