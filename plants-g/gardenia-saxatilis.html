Gardenia saxatilis

Gardenia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock gardenia, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its hardy nature allows it to withstand salty sea air and sandy soils.

Gardenia saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
