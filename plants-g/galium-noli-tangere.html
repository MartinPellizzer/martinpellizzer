Galium noli-tangere

Galium noli-tangere, commonly known as touch-me-not or false nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Galium noli-tangere is known for its stinging hairs, which can cause skin irritation upon contact.
