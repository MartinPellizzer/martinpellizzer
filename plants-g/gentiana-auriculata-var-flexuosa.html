Gentiana auriculata var. flexuosa

Gentiana auriculata var. flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, aiding in water retention.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
