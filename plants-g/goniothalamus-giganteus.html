Goniothalamus giganteus

Goniothalamus giganteus, commonly known as the giant goniothalamus, is a member of the Annonaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a large evergreen tree that can grow up to 30 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, grey bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny fruits containing edible seeds.

It thrives in humid, tropical lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
