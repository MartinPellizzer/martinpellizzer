Grona rubra var. rubra

Grona rubra var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered grona, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a deep green color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
