Gymnosporia pancheriana

Gymnosporia pancheriana, commonly known as the Indian burr bush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of India and Pakistan.

Gymnosporia pancheriana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
