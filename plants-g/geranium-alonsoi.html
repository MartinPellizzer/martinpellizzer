Geranium alonsoi

Geranium alonsoi, commonly known as the Alonso's geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant features upright stems and rounded, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
