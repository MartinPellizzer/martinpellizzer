Georgeantha hexandra

Georgeantha hexandra, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant features a slender, erect stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers bloom in clusters, emitting a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Georgeantha hexandra is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
