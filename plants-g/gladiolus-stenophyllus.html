Gladiolus stenophyllus

Gladiolus stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with striking, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, colorful flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically arranged in dense spikes at the top of the stems. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cut flower arrangements.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and horticultural collections.
