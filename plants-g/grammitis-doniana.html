Grammitis doniana

Grammitis doniana, commonly known as the tropical fern, is a species belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Grammitis doniana is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in controlled indoor settings.
