Gentianella muelleriana subsp. jingerensis

Gentianella muelleriana subsp.

jingerensis, commonly known as the Jingerensis gentian, is a perennial herb native to the alpine regions of China. It typically grows in rocky, moist habitats at high elevations.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
