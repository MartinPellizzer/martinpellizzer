Geniostoma uninervium

Geniostoma uninervium, commonly known as the slender leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, elongated leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, pear-shaped fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
