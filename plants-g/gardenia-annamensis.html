Gardenia annamensis

Gardenia annamensis, commonly known as the Annam gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are highly valued for their beauty and are often used in ornamental horticulture.

Gardenia annamensis is also appreciated for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
