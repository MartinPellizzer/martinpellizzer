Gastrochilus minor

Gastrochilus minor, commonly known as the small stomach orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich substrates. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and pale green or white, often with a faintly colored lip.

Gastrochilus minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
