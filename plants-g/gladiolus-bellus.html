Gladiolus bellus

Gladiolus bellus, commonly known as the beautiful sword lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that support showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a wide range of colors, including white, pink, red, and purple, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and botanically interesting.
