Gouinia latifolia

Gouinia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf gouinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.

Gouinia latifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
