Guzmania densispica

Guzmania densispica, commonly known as the dense-spiked guzmania, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that often features red, orange, or yellow bracts. The plant possesses a central rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms and small animals. Its foliage is typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Guzmania densispica is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
