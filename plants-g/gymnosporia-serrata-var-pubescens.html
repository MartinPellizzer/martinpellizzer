Gymnosporia serrata var. pubescens

Gymnosporia serrata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy serrated gymnosporia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, serrated leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by small, woody capsules containing seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
