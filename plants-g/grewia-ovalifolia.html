Grewia ovalifolia

Grewia ovalifolia, commonly known as the ovalleaf grownia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a round, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature.

Grewia ovalifolia is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
