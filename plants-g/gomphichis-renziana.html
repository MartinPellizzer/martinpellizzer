Gomphichis renziana

Gomphichis renziana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features erect, slender stems and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Gomphichis renziana is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in dry conditions.
