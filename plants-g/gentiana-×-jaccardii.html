Gentiana × jaccardii

Gentiana × jaccardii, commonly known as the hybrid gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Gentiana × jaccardii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
