Goodia lotifolia

Goodia lotifolia, commonly known as the slender goodia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Goodia lotifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
