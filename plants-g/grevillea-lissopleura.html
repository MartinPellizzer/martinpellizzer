Grevillea lissopleura

Grevillea lissopleura, commonly known as the silky grevillea, is a striking shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea lissopleura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and flowering display.
