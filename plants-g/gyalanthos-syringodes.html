Gyalanthos syringodes

Gyalanthos syringodes, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Gyalanthos syringodes is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it demonstrates adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
