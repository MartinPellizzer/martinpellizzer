Gladiolus venustus

Gladiolus venustus, commonly known as the beautiful gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, and are often used in ornamental gardening. It is a perennial herb that produces corms as its storage organs, which allow it to survive adverse conditions.
