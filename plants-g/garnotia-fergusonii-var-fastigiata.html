Garnotia fergusonii var. fastigiata

Garnotia fergusonii var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright garnotia, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, upright stems and small, glossy green leaves that are arranged in a dense, compact form. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
