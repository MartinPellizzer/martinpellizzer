Gelasia circumflexa

Gelasia circumflexa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem. Gelasia circumflexa is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
