Garcinia pulvinata

Garcinia pulvinata, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and large, glossy green leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, orange-red fruits with a sweet and tangy flavor. Its fruit is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
