Guettarda calyptrata

Guettarda calyptrata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling a shower of golden beads. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Guettarda calyptrata is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and poor soil, making it a resilient choice for landscaping.
