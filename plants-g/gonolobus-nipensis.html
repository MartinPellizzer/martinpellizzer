Gonolobus nipensis

Gonolobus nipensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian tamarillo, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

Gonolobus nipensis is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and plays a role in local ecosystems.
