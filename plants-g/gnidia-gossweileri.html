Gnidia gossweileri

Gnidia gossweileri, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a South African plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white, ball-shaped inflorescences that bloom in winter. Its leaves are pinnately compound and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.
