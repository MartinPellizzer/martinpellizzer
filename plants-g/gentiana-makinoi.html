Gentiana makinoi

Gentiana makinoi, commonly known as the Makino's gentian, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

Gentiana makinoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
