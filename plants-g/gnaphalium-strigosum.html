Gnaphalium strigosum

Gnaphalium strigosum, commonly known as silverleaf woollyhead, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and fluffy, pale yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, which give it a distinctive silvery appearance.

It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
