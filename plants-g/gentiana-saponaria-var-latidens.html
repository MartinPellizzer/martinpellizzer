Gentiana saponaria var. latidens

Gentiana saponaria var. latidens, commonly known as the broadleaf gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
