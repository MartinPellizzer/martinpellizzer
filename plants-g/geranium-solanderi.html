Geranium solanderi

Geranium solanderi, commonly known as the Solander's geranium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features upright stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated in gardens across the world.
