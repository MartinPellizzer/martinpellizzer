Guneria latifolia

Guneria latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf guneria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences, typically appearing in shades of pink or red.

Guneria latifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
