Geum urbanum f. mauritanicum

Geum urbanum f. mauritanicum, commonly known as the Mauritanian avens, is a variant of the common avens.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of southern Europe and North Africa. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
