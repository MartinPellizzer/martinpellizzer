Grewia celle

Grewia celle, commonly known as the sour fig or wild sour plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, simple leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
