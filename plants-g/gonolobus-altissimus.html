Gonolobus altissimus

Gonolobus altissimus, commonly known as the tall morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces long, serpentine fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Gonolobus altissimus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
