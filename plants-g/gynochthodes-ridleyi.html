Gynochthodes ridleyi

Gynochthodes ridleyi, commonly known as the Malayan orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and possesses a unique floral structure with a single, showy flower per stem. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded forest environments. Its flowers are pollinated by specific insect species, highlighting its ecological niche.
