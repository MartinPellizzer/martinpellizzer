Galium erectum f. falcatum

Galium erectum f. falcatum, commonly known as the cat's-tail bedstraw, is a flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, ascending stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves. Its leaves and stems are often used in traditional herbal remedies for their purported medicinal properties.
