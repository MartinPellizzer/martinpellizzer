Gentiana luzoniensis var. tarokoensis

Gentiana luzoniensis var. tarokoensis, commonly known as the Taroko gentian, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist conditions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
