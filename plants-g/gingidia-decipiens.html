Gingidia decipiens

Gingidia decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its aromatic rhizomes and striking, ornamental foliage. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a unique structure that resembles a ginger plant, leading to its common name.
