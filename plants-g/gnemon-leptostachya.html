Gnemon leptostachya

Gnemon leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, which are typical of many grasses.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings for its delicate appearance.
