Genista demnatensis var. mouilleronii

Genista demnatensis var. mouilleronii, commonly known as the Mouilleron bentthorn, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellowish or golden flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is native to southern Europe, particularly France and Spain, and thrives in rocky or dry soils.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
