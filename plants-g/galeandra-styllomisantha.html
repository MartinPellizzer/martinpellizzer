Galeandra styllomisantha

Galeandra styllomisantha, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Galeandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

It requires specific environmental conditions to thrive, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
