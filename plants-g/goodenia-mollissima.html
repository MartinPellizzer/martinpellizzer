Goodenia mollissima

Goodenia mollissima, commonly known as the silky goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It has a low-growing habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
