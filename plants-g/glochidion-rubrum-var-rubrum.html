Glochidion rubrum var. rubrum

Glochidion rubrum var. rubrum, commonly known as the red glochidion, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pink in color, followed by smooth, oval-shaped fruits. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface with prominent veins.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
