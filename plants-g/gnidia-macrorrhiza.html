Gnidia macrorrhiza

Gnidia macrorrhiza, commonly known as the giant gnidia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a woody stem and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
