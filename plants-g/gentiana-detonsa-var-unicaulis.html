Gentiana detonsa var. unicaulis

Gentiana detonsa var. unicaulis, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and alpine meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are slender and often branched.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
