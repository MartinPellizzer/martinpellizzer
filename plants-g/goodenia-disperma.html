Goodenia disperma

Goodenia disperma, commonly known as the scattered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from pale blue to pink. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal areas.

Goodenia disperma is known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions, making it a hardy species in its native environment.
