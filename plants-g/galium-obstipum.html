Galium obstipum

Galium obstipum, commonly known as woolly bedstraw, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, ascending stems covered in dense, white to grayish hairs, giving it a woolly appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and also covered in fine hairs.

Galium obstipum thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
