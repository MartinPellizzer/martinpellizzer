Galactites tomentosus var. integrifolius

Galactites tomentosus var. integrifolius, commonly known as the silky milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, greyish stems and leaves that are typically entire, giving it the species name *integrifolius*. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid environments and has a milky sap that is toxic to some animals.
