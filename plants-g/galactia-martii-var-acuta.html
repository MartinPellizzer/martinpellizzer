Galactia martii var. acuta

Galactia martii var. acuta, commonly known as the sharpleaf galactia, is a species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a leguminous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant ecosystems.
