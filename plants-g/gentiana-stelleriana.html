Gentiana stelleriana

Gentiana stelleriana, commonly known as Steller's gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowering stems are usually unbranched.

Gentiana stelleriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological benefits.
