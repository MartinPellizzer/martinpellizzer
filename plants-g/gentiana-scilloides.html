Gentiana scilloides

Gentiana scilloides, commonly known as the scarlet gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana scilloides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
