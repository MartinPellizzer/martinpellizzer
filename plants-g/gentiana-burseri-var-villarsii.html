Gentiana burseri var. villarsii

Gentiana burseri var. villarsii, commonly known as the Burseri gentian, is a striking perennial plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is valued for its unique color and is often cultivated in specialized gardens for its beauty and rarity.
