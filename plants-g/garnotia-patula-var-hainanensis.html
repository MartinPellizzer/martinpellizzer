Garnotia patula var. hainanensis

Garnotia patula var. hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan garnet, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of China.

It is characterized by its striking, deep red to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic visual appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in mountainous areas with rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.

Due to its limited distribution and unique floral characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
