Gazania linearis

Gazania linearis, commonly known as the linear gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive central dark center, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Gazania linearis is relatively drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
