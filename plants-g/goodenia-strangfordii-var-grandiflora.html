Goodenia strangfordii var. grandiflora

Goodenia strangfordii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in dry, sunny conditions. It features linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
