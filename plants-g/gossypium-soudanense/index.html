Gossypium Soudanense

Gossypium soudanense is a rare and lesser-known species of cotton native to the Sahel region of Africa. This plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to harsh conditions. Its fibrous seeds have historically been used by local communities for both nutritional and textile purposes. Despite its potential, Gossypium soudanense remains under-researched and overlooked in global agricultural discussions. Understanding this plant could offer valuable insights into sustainable farming in drought-prone areas.
