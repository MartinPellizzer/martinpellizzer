Gouldia terminalis f. euglabra

Gouldia terminalis f. euglabra, commonly known as the smooth gouldia, is a cultivar of the gouldia plant, which is native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often used in ornamental horticulture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

The plant is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a decorative yet easy-to-care-for plant.
