Guzmania morreniana var. tenuifolia

Guzmania morreniana var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed torch plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, red-bronze inflorescence that emerges from a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant's foliage is typically dark green with a waxy texture, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.

The plant's unique appearance makes it a standout addition to any collection of tropical plants.
