Gaura longiflora

Gaura longiflora, commonly known as whorled gaura, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of delicate, pink or white, fragrant flowers. The plant has basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
