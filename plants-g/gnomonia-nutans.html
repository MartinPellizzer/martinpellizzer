Gnomonia nutans

Gnomonia nutans, commonly known as the nodding gnomonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy or well-drained soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Gnomonia nutans is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and coastal dunes.
