Geranium purpureum var. minutiflorum

Geranium purpureum var. minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf purple geranium, is a compact, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple and pink. The leaves are typically ovate to heart-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
