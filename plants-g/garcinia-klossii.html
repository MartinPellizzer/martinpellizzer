Garcinia klossii

Garcinia klossii, commonly known as the Kloss garcinia, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces orange-red berries that contain a high concentration of hydroxycitric acid, which has been studied for its potential health benefits. Garcinia klossii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the extraction of its active compounds.
