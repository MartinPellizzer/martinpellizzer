Glicophyllum jussieuanum

Glicophyllum jussieuanum, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Glicophyllum jussieuanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
