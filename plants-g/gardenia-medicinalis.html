Gardenia medicinalis

Gardenia medicinalis, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, white or yellow flowers. The plant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
