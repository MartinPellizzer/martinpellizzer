Galeana hastata

Galeana hastata, commonly known as the Mexican firecracker, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.
