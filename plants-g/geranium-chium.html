Geranium chium

Geranium chium, commonly known as the Indian Geranium, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb with upright stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various climates.
