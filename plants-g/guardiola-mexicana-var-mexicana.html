Guardiola mexicana var. mexicana

Guardiola mexicana var. mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican guardiola, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions. It produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
