Genista salzmannii

Genista salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's broom, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces yellow, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat. Its leaves are small, leathery, and evergreen, contributing to its hardy nature.

Genista salzmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
