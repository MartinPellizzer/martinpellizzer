Gentiana ornata var. acutifolia

Gentiana ornata var. acutifolia, commonly known as the acuteleaf gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and alpine meadows. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, giving it the common name "acuteleaf.".

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
