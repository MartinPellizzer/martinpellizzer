Gmelina dalrympleana var. schlechteri

Gmelina dalrympleana var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
