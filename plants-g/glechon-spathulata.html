Glechon spathulata

Glechon spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed glechon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of broad, spoon-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Glechon spathulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and borders.
