Gorteria squarrosa

Gorteria squarrosa, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
