Gymnogramma philippinensis

Gymnogramma philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered with a dense layer of roots, which help in anchoring the plant and absorbing nutrients.

Gymnogramma philippinensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
