Galinsoga × mixta

Galinsoga × mixta, commonly known as the common galinsoga, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural and garden settings. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive potential in various regions.

Galinsoga × mixta is often found in temperate climates and can be challenging to control due to its vigorous growth habit.
