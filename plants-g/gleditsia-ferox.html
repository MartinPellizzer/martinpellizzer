Gleditsia ferox

Gleditsia ferox, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to China.

It belongs to the genus Gleditsia in the family Fabaceae, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a broad, spreading crown. Its bark is thick and fissured, often appearing grayish-brown, while its leaves are pinnately compound with small, oval leaflets.

The plant is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
