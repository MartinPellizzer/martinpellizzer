Gymnadenia densiflora var. candida

Gymnadenia densiflora var. candida, commonly known as the white-flowered helmet orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in calcareous grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems. Its flowers are white or pale pink, with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip that gives it its common name.

It blooms from June to August and is often found in sunny, open habitats.
