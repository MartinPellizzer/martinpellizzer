Germainia schmidiana

Germainia schmidiana, commonly known as the Schmidian germander, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
