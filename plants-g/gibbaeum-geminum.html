Gibbaeum geminum

Gibbaeum geminum, commonly known as the twin-lobed sand mat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with dense clusters of small, white flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found on sandy or gravelly soils. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh conditions.

Gibbaeum geminum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
