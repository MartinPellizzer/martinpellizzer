Gelonium stenophyllum

Gelonium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
