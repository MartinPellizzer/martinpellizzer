Goodenia rotundifolia var. typica

Goodenia rotundifolia var. typica, commonly known as the round-leaf goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, round, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
