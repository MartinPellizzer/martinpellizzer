Gentiana dshimilensis

Gentiana dshimilensis, commonly known as the Dshimilensis gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool and moist conditions. Its flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
