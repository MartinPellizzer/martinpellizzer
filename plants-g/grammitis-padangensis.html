Grammitis padangensis

Grammitis padangensis, commonly known as the Padang fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually small, with a soft, velvety texture.
