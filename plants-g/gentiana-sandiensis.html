Gentiana sandiensis

Gentiana sandiensis, commonly known as the sand gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Gentiana sandiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
