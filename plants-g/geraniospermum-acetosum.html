Geraniospermum acetosum

Geraniospermum acetosum, commonly known as the sour apple tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with woody stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a rough, slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, sour-tasting berry that contains several seeds.
