Gnaphalium saturejoides var. flavescens

Gnaphalium saturejoides var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellow to golden-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has hairy, aromatic leaves and produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
