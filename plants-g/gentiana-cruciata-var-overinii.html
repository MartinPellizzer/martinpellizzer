Gentiana cruciata var. overinii

Gentiana cruciata var. overinii, commonly known as the Overin's gentian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
