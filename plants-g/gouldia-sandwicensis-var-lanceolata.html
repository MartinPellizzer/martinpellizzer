Gouldia sandwicensis var. lanceolata

Gouldia sandwicensis var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Hawaiian flame flower, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange bracts that surround its small, white flowers, creating a dramatic visual display.
