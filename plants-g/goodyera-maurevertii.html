Goodyera maurevertii

Goodyera maurevertii, commonly known as the Maurevert's filmy fern, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and translucent, waxy leaves that give it a unique, almost ghostly appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees in rainforests. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in a spike and bloom during the wet season.

Due to habitat loss and its elusive nature, Goodyera maurevertii is considered a rare and endangered species.
