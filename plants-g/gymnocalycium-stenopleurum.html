Gymnocalycium stenopleurum

Gymnocalycium stenopleurum, commonly known as the narrow-pleurum cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a dense covering of white, fuzzy hairs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
