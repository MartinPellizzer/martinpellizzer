Gardenia elata var. longipedicellata

Gardenia elata var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the longipedicellate gardenia, is a species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers have a high number of petals and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
