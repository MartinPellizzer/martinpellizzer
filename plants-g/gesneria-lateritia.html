Gesneria lateritia

Gesneria lateritia, commonly known as the rock gesneria, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Mexico.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Gesneria lateritia is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
