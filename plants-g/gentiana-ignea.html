Gentiana ignea

Gentiana ignea, commonly known as the flame gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, known for its showy, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are tubular with a bright blue or purple corolla, often featuring a yellow or orange spot at the base.

Gentiana ignea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ecological importance.
