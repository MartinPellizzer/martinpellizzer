Gonypetalum juruanum

Gonypetalum juruanum, commonly known as the Jurua nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its flowers are usually violet or purple, with a distinctive shape and a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
