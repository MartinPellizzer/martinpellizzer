Glochidion dumicola

Glochidion dumicola, commonly known as the rock glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine, glandular hairs. Glochidion dumicola is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
