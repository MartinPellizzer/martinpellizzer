Galenia namaensis

Galenia namaensis, commonly known as the Namibian galenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with tubular shapes that attract pollinators like bees and birds.

Galenia namaensis is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a hardy species in its native habitat.
