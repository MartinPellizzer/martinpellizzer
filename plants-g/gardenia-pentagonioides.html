Gardenia pentagonioides

Gardenia pentagonioides, commonly known as the five-angled gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, five-angled leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces fragrant white flowers with yellow stamens, which bloom in summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and aromatic qualities.

Gardenia pentagonioides prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
