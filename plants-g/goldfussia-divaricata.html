Goldfussia divaricata

Goldfussia divaricata, commonly known as the variable goldfussia, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Goldfussia divaricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
