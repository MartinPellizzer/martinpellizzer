Geniostoma remyanum

Geniostoma remyanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, warm environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Geniostoma remyanum is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
