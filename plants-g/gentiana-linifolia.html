Gentiana linifolia

Gentiana linifolia, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched and upright.

Gentiana linifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
