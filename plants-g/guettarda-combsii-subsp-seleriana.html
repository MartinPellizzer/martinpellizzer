Guettarda combsii subsp. seleriana

Guettarda combsii subsp.

seleriana, commonly known as the Selerian guettarda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean. It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and limestone soils.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
