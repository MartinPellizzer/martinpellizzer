Gongora maculata var. atropurpurea

Gongora maculata var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the Purple Spotted Gongora, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica and Panama.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and green foliage, with flowers that feature intricate patterns of purple and white. The plant produces pendant, spurred flowers that are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, requiring well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
