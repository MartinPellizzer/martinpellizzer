Glochidion tomentosum var. talbotii

Glochidion tomentosum var. talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's glochidion, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The leaves are typically opposite, elliptical, and have a waxy texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
