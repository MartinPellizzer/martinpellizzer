Gandasulium speciosum

Gandasulium speciosum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a silvery-white bloom, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections worldwide.
