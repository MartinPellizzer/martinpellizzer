Genyorchis micropetala

Genyorchis micropetala, commonly known as the small-petalled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the wet tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose spike. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Genyorchis micropetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist gardens.
