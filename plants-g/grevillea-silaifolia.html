Grevillea silaifolia

Grevillea silaifolia, commonly known as the slender grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to dry conditions.

Grevillea silaifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular in native gardens.
