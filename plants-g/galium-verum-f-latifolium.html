Galium verum f. latifolium

Galium verum f. latifolium, commonly known as yellow bedstraw, is a variant of the common bedstraw species.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a distinctive waxy coating. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it identifiable within the Rubiaceae family.
