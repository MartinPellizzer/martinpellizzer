Gymnolomia multiflora

Gymnolomia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered gymnolomia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stem and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Gymnolomia multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
