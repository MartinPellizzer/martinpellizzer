Gastrochilus apiculatus

Gastrochilus apiculatus, commonly known as the apiculate stomach orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and specific habitat requirements make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
