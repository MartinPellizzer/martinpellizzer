Gomphrena vaga var. effusa

Gomphrena vaga var. effusa, commonly known as the common gomphrena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bract-based flower heads that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers surrounded by leaf-like bracts, which give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental use in floral arrangements and as a ground cover.
