Gladiolus leptophyllus

Gladiolus leptophyllus, commonly known as the slender-leaf gladiolus, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall, straight stems with showy, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom during the summer months. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern at the base.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.
