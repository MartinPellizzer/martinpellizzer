Gongora dressleri

Gongora dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Gongora dressleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
