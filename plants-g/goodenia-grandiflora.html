Goodenia grandiflora

Goodenia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are often visited by birds and insects, contributing to its ecological importance.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
