Godetia lassenensis var. concolor

Godetia lassenensis var. concolor, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique flowering pattern.
