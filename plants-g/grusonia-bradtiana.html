Grusonia bradtiana

Grusonia bradtiana, commonly known as the Bradtian rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its arid environment.

Grusonia bradtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
