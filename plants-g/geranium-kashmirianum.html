Geranium kashmirianum

Geranium kashmirianum, commonly known as the Kashmir geranium, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often hairy as well. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
