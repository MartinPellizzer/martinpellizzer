Gentiana serra

Gentiana serra, commonly known as the Alpine gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.

Gentiana serra is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
