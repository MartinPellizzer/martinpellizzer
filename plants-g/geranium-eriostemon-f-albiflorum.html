Geranium eriostemon f. albiflorum

Geranium eriostemon f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered geranium, is a horticultural form of the species Geranium eriostemon.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with its deep green foliage. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is a compact perennial with a low-growing habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

The flowers bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch of white to shaded garden spaces.
