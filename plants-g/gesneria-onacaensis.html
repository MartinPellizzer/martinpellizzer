Gesneria onacaensis

Gesneria onacaensis, commonly known as the Onacaensis Gesneria, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which is known for its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features thick, waxy leaves that are adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

Gesneria onacaensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
