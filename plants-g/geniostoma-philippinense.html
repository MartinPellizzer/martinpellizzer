Geniostoma philippinense

Geniostoma philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant has strap-like leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or branches. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.

However, it is considered a rare and endangered species due to habitat loss and over-collection.
