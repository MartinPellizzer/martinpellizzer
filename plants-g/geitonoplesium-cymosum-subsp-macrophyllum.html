Geitonoplesium cymosum subsp.

macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant starfruit or large-leafed starfruit, is a tropical plant native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length.

The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers, which are followed by the distinctive starfruit-like fruits. The fruits are typically yellow when ripe and have a sweet, tangy flavor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit, thriving in warm, humid climates.
