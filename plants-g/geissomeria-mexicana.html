Geissomeria mexicana

Geissomeria mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican thimbleweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin, while its fruits are small, dry achenes.

Geissomeria mexicana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
