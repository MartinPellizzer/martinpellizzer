Grevillea hakeoides subsp. stenophylla

Grevillea hakeoides subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Grevillea, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers that appear in dense clusters.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments. Its floral structure is typical of the Proteaceae family, with showy, petal-like structures that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
