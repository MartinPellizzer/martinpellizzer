Gasteria subcarinata

Gasteria subcarinata, commonly known as the Zulu spear or the Cape speartip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with white or yellowish markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Gasteria subcarinata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
