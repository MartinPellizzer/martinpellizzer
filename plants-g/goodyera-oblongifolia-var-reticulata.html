Goodyera oblongifolia var. reticulata

Goodyera oblongifolia var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulated lady's slipper, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a reticulated pattern on the underside. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. It is typically found in shaded, moist forest environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
