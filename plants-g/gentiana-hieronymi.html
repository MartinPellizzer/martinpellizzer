Gentiana hieronymi

Gentiana hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are erect and branched.

Gentiana hieronymi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
