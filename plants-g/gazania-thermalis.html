Gazania thermalis

Gazania thermalis, commonly known as the thermal gazania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features basal leaves that are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are upright and branching. Gazania thermalis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also drought-tolerant, which enhances its adaptability to arid environments.
