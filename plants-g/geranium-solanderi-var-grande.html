Geranium solanderi var. grande

Geranium solanderi var. grande, commonly known as the large-leaf geranium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
