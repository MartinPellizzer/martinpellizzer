Geranium hookerianum var. incisum

Geranium hookerianum var. incisum, commonly known as the Hooker's geranium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features upright stems and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and foliage.
