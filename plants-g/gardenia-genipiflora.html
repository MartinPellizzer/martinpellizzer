Gardenia genipiflora

Gardenia genipiflora, commonly known as the Genipifoliate Gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Gardenia genipiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic foliage.
