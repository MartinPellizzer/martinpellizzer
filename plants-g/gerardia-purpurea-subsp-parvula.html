Gerardia purpurea subsp. parvula

Gerardia purpurea subsp.

parvula, commonly known as the purple gerardia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It is a member of the Geraniaceae family, characterized by its distinctive, often showy flowers.
