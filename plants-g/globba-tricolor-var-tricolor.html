Globba tricolor var. tricolor

Globba tricolor var. tricolor, commonly known as the three-color globba, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored leaves that display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
