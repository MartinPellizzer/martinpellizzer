Genista ephedrifolia

Genista ephedrifolia, commonly known as the thorny broom, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets, and its flowers are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Genista ephedrifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
