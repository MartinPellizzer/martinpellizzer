Gilia Latiflora Subsp. Purpusii

Purple gilia, scientifically known as Gilia latiflora subsp. purpusii, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is celebrated for its vibrant violet to purple blooms that appear in late spring and early summer. This subspecies thrives in rocky soils and open habitats, often found in desert and mountainous regions. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its resilience in harsh environments. The plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity in its ecosystem.
