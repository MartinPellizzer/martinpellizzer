Gomphia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved gomphia, is a unique member of the Restionaceae family, native to South Africa.

Gomphia microphylla is adapted to nutrient-poor, sandy soils and is typically found in fynbos vegetation, showcasing its ecological significance in its native habitat.
