Grevillea epicroca

Grevillea epicroca, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or greyish tinge. The plant produces striking, feather-like inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Grevillea epicroca is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
