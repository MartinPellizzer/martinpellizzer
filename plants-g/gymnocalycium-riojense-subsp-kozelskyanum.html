Gymnocalycium riojense subsp. kozelskyanum

Gymnocalycium riojense subsp.

kozelskyanum, commonly known as the Kozelsky's moon cactus, is a succulent plant native to Argentina. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white or pale blue spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
