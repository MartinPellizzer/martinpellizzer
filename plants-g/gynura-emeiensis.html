Gynura emeiensis

Gynura emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain gynura, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, lobed leaves and clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Gynura emeiensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
