Geranium shikokianum var. yamatense

Geranium shikokianum var. yamatense, commonly known as the Yamatense geranium, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
