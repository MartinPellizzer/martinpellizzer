Geum urbanum var. strictum

Geum urbanum var. strictum, commonly known as narrowleaf avens, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in loose, terminal heads, blooming from late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional herbal medicine.
