Gelseminum hirtum

Gelseminum hirtum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

The berries it produces are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.
