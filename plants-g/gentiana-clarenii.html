Gentiana clarenii

Gentiana clarenii, commonly known as the Clarenii gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hairs, which helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Gentiana clarenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural growing conditions.
