Gamochaeta americana var. linearifolia

Gamochaeta americana var. linearifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its linear, grass-like leaves are usually less than 10 cm in length.

The small blue to purple flowers appear in late summer, contributing to its ornamental value.
