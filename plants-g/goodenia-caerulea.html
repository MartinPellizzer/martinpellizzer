Goodenia caerulea

Goodenia caerulea, commonly known as the blue goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a hairy texture, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Goodenia caerulea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
