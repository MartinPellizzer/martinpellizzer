Gnaphalium micranthum

Gnaphalium micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered cudweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic, woolly stems and is often found in moist, disturbed soils such as roadsides, meadows, and wetlands.
