Genista ferox var. salditana

Genista ferox var. salditana, commonly known as the Salditana broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by pods containing seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, elliptical leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
