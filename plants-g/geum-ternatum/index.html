Geum Ternatum

Geum ternatum is a delicate perennial plant known for its clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Native to the northeastern United States and parts of Canada, it thrives in moist, shady environments like woodlands and stream sides. This species is often overlooked but plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. Its trifoliate leaves and compact growth make it a unique addition to native plant gardens. Despite its modest appearance, Geum ternatum is a valuable component of diverse natural habitats.
