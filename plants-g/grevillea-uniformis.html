Grevillea uniformis

Grevillea uniformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grevillea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and vibrant red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Grevillea uniformis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
