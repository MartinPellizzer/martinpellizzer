Genista ulicina var. densiflora

Genista ulicina var. densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it its varietal name. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, needle-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil.
