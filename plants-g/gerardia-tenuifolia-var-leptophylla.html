Gerardia tenuifolia var. leptophylla

Gerardia tenuifolia var. leptophylla, commonly known as the slender-leaf gerardia, is a rare and specialized plant species native to specific alpine environments.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, where it forms dense mats to retain moisture and withstand strong winds.
