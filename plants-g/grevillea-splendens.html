Grevillea splendens

Grevillea splendens, commonly known as the golden grevillea, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright golden-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, dark green, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in coastal regions.

Grevillea splendens is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and its low maintenance requirements.
