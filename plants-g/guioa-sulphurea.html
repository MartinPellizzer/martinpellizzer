Guioa sulphurea

Guioa sulphurea, commonly known as the golden guioa, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is a small, spherical drupe that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.
