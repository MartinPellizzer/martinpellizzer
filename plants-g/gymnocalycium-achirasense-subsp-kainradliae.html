Gymnocalycium achirasense subsp. kainradliae

Gymnocalycium achirasense subsp.

kainradliae, commonly known as the Kainradliae moon cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to arid regions of South America. It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its fleshy stem.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
