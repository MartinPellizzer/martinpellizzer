Goodenia careyi

Goodenia careyi, commonly known as the slender goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in dry, sunny conditions. Its flowers are usually pale blue to purple in color and bloom during the cooler months.

Goodenia careyi is often found in coastal and inland regions, contributing to the biodiversity of its native habitat.
