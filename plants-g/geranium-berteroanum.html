Geranium berteroanum

Geranium berteroanum, commonly known as the Bertero's geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and purple. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Geranium berteroanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
