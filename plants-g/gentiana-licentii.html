Gentiana licentii

Gentiana licentii, commonly known as the Licent's gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its flowering stems are often branched.

Gentiana licentii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
