Grumilea rubiginosa

Grumilea rubiginosa, commonly known as the rusty grumilea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are simple and opposite, often with a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
