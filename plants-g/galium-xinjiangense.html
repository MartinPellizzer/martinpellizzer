Galium xinjiangense

Galium xinjiangense, commonly known as Xinjiang bedstraw, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia, particularly Xinjiang in China.

It is a low-growing herbaceous perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, wiry stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Galium xinjiangense is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.
