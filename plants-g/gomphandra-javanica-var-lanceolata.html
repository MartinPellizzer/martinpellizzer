Gomphandra javanica var. lanceolata

Gomphandra javanica var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Javanese lanceleaf gomphandra, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
