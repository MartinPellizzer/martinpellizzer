Gladiolus hollandii

Gladiolus hollandii, commonly known as the Dutch gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, spike-like inflorescences with showy, colorful flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, with flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and valuable for horticultural study.
