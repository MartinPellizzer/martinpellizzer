Geocaryum euboicum

Geocaryum euboicum, commonly known as the Euboic sponge gourd, is a climbing vine native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It produces large, fleshy, sponge-like fruits that are typically green when mature. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and bears yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a robust stem that can climb up to several meters.

The fruit is often used in local cuisines and has historical significance in traditional medicine.
