Gentiana tatsienensis

Gentiana tatsienensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
