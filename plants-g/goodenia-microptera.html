Goodenia microptera

Goodenia microptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and narrow, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions. It produces small, usually yellow or white flowers that attract various pollinators.

Goodenia microptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
