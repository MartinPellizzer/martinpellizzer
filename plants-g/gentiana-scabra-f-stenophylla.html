Gentiana scabra f. stenophylla

Gentiana scabra f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
