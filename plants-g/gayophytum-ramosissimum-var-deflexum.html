Gayophytum ramosissimum var. deflexum

Gayophytum ramosissimum var. deflexum, commonly known as the curled gayophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, branching stems that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The leaves are typically small, ovate, and covered in a fine, powdery coating. It produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
