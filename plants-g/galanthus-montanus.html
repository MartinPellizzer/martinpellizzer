Galanthus montanus

Galanthus montanus, commonly known as the mountain snowdrop, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant produces delicate, white flowers with a distinctive yellow-green spathe, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in woodland and rocky habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soil.

Galanthus montanus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
