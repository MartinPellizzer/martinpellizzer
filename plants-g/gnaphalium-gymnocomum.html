Gnaphalium gymnocomum

Gnaphalium gymnocomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are surrounded by narrow, grass-like leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance.
