Gentiana diffusa

Gentiana diffusa, commonly known as the blue gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are tubular and often have a prominent blue or violet throat.

Gentiana diffusa is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
