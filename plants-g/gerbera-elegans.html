Gerbera elegans

Gerbera elegans, commonly known as the elegant gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with vibrant colors such as red, yellow, and orange. The plant features upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Gerbera elegans thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical beauty and ease of cultivation contribute to its popularity among horticultural enthusiasts.
