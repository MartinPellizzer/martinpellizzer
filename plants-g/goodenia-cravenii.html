Goodenia cravenii

Goodenia cravenii, commonly known as Craven's goodena, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to purple, bloom in spring and attract various pollinators.
