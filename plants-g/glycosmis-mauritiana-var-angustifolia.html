Glycosmis mauritiana var. angustifolia

Glycosmis mauritiana var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mastic tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible but not widely consumed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
