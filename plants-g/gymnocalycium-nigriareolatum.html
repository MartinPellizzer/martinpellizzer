Gymnocalycium nigriareolatum

Gymnocalycium nigriareolatum, commonly known as the black-spotted cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and dark green to bluish-gray stems. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its surface is covered with white, short spines and distinctive black or dark brown areolas.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
