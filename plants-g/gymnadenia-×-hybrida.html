Gymnadenia × hybrida

Gymnadenia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Gymnadenia × hybrida is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
