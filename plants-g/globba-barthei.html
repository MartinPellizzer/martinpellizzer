Globba barthei

Globba barthei, commonly known as the velvetleaf globba, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both its aesthetic appeal and its ability to thrive in humid conditions.
