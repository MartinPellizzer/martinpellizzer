Gnetum hainanense

Gnetum hainanense, commonly known as the Hainan gnetum, is a unique gymnosperm native to the tropical regions of southern China.

It belongs to the family Gnetaceae, which is closely related to flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its scale-like leaves and male and female cones that are typically borne on separate branches. Its vascular system exhibits features that bridge the gap between gymnosperms and angiosperms.

Gnetum hainanense is of particular interest to botanists due to its evolutionary significance and adaptive traits.
