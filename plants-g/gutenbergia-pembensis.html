Gutenbergia pembensis

Gutenbergia pembensis, commonly known as the Pembensian orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, silky texture.
