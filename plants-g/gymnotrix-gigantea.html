Gymnotrix gigantea

Gymnotrix gigantea, commonly known as the giant nightshade, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. Its fruit is typically a fleshy berry that contains seeds enclosed in a protective coating.

Gymnotrix gigantea is also known for its toxic properties, making it a plant to be handled with care.
