Gentiana crispata subsp. bosnjakii

Gentiana crispata subsp.

bosnjakii, commonly known as the Bosnian gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkans. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky slopes.

The plant features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique ecological adaptations.
