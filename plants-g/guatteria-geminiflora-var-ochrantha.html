Guatteria geminiflora var. ochrantha

Guatteria geminiflora var. ochrantha, commonly known as the orange-flowered guatteria, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that turn orange as they mature.
