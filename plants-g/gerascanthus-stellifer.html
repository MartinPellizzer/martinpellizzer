Gerascanthus stellifer

Gerascanthus stellifer, commonly known as the star-bearing geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that resemble stars. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are often lobed and have a slightly hairy texture, while the stems are erect and branching.

Gerascanthus stellifer is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
