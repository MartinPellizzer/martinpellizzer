Gymnocalycium artigas var. roseiflorum

Gymnocalycium artigas var. roseiflorum, commonly known as the rose-flowered prickly pear, is a cactus species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it is adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
