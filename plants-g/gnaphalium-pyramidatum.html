Gnaphalium pyramidatum

Gnaphalium pyramidatum, commonly known as the pyramidal groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Its small, yellowish flowers bloom in late summer, and it produces fluffy, white seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
