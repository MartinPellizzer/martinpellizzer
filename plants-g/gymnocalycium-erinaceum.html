Gymnocalycium erinaceum

Gymnocalycium erinaceum, commonly known as the spiny moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered in spiny areoles. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

Gymnocalycium erinaceum is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and unique appearance.
