Gentiana hyalina

Gentiana hyalina, commonly known as the glass gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to sky-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and alpine meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its stem is slender and erect.

Gentiana hyalina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
