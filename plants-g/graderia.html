Graderia

Graderia, commonly known as the orchid tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters along the branches. The plant has a woody stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Graderia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
