Gaya lyallii var. ribifolia

Gaya lyallii var. ribifolia, commonly known as the red-flowered gaya, is a variant of the gaya lyallii species native to the Himalayas.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and clusters of bright red flowers. The plant thrives in high-altitude regions with cool, moist climates and well-drained soils. Its flowers are typically borne on short pedicels and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
