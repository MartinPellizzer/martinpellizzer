Geophila croatii

Geophila croatii, commonly known as the Croatian mosquito plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are delicate, white or pale blue, and appear in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Geophila croatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in damp garden settings.
