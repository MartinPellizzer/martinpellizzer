Globularia cambessedesii

Globularia cambessedesii, commonly known as the Canary Islands globularia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant features dense, globular clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact, low-growing habit.

Globularia cambessedesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
