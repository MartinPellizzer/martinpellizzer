Genista scorpioides

Genista scorpioides, commonly known as the creeping thorned broom, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It features slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves and is well adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Genista scorpioides is also known for its ability to thrive in coastal areas and sandy environments.
