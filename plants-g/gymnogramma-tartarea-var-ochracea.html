Gymnogramma tartarea var. ochracea

Gymnogramma tartarea var. ochracea, commonly known as the ochraceous rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Gymnogramma.

It is a small, perennial fern typically found in rocky or shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lanceolate leaves with a distinctive ochraceous (yellowish-orange) coloration on the underside. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often seen in moist, calcareous soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded gardens.
