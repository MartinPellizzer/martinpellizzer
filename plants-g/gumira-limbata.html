Gumira limbata

Gumira limbata, commonly known as the winged gumira, is a climbing shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Gumira limbata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
