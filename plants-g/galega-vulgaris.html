Galega vulgaris

Galega vulgaris, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. Its root system is fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
