Gethyllis herrei

Gethyllis herrei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in regions with a Mediterranean climate. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
