Genista × arizagae

Genista × arizagae, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, often thorned stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and erosion control.
