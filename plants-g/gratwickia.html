Gratwickia

Gratwickia, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plants typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, often found on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Gratwickia species are known for their vibrant colors, ranging from white to deep purple, and their intricate floral structures.

These plants are popular among orchid enthusiasts due to their unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
