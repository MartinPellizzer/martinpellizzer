Gomphrena fuscipellita

Gomphrena fuscipellita, commonly known as the red or pink globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescences that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.

Gomphrena fuscipellita is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
