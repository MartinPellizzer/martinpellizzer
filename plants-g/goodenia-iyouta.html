Goodenia iyouta

Goodenia iyouta, commonly known as the mountain goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Goodenia iyouta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
