Guatteria flexilis

Guatteria flexilis, commonly known as the flexible guatteria, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often glossy and elliptical in shape. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries.

Guatteria flexilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
