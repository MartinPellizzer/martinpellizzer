Goodenia heterochila var. foliosa

Goodenia heterochila var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy goodenia, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a lobed or divided appearance, while the flowers are arranged in clusters.

This species is often found in open woodlands and scrublands, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
