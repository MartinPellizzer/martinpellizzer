Geranium longipes

Geranium longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Geranium longipes is known for its long flowering period, extending from spring through early summer.
