Gentiana Favratii

Gentiana favratii is a rare and striking alpine plant known for its deep blue flowers that bloom in high mountain environments. Native to the Balkans, this species thrives in rocky and gravelly soils where few other plants can survive. Its unique coloration and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Gentiana favratii faces increasing threats to its survival. Protecting this delicate species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in alpine ecosystems.
