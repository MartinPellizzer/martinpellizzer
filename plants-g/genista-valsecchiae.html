Genista valsecchiae

Genista valsecchiae, commonly known as the Valsecchia buckthorn, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring sunny locations. Its small, yellow flowers appear in spring, followed by elongated seed pods.

It is noted for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
