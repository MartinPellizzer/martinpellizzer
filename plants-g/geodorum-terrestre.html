Geodorum terrestre

Geodorum terrestre, commonly known as the terrestrial ginger, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous underground stem that supports its growth and propagation. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a prominent midrib. Its flowers are fragrant, with white petals and a yellow center, often blooming in clusters.

Geodorum terrestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.
