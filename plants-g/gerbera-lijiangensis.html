Gerbera lijiangensis

Gerbera lijiangensis, commonly known as the Liangjiang gerbera, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically has bright-colored petals in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with a central disk of smaller florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Gerbera lijiangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
