Genista gracilis

Genista gracilis, commonly known as slender broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features small, needle-like leaves and produces yellow, pea-like flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.

Genista gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
