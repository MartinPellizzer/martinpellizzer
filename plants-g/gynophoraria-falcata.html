Gynophoraria falcata

Gynophoraria falcata, commonly known as the sickleleaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, sickle-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that can spread across the forest floor. Its fronds are usually pinnately divided, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
