Garcinia moulmeinensis

Garcinia moulmeinensis, commonly known as Moulmein garcinia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Garcinia moulmeinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some pharmacological properties.
