Geranium molle var. album

Geranium molle var. album, commonly known as the white geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early autumn.
