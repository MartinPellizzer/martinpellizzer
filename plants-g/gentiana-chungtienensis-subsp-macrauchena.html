Gentiana chungtienensis subsp. macrauchena

Gentiana chungtienensis subsp.

macrauchena, commonly known as the Macrauchena gentian, is a flowering plant species native to high-altitude regions of China. It belongs to the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its showy, blue or purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and bear clusters of small, vibrant flowers.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
