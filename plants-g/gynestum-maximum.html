Gynestum maximum

Gynestum maximum, commonly known as the large gynestum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that are deeply lobed and its clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fruits are dry, three-lobed schizocarps that release tiny seeds when mature.

Gynestum maximum is noted for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
