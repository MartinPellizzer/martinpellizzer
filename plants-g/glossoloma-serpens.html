Glossoloma serpens

Glossoloma serpens, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal houseplant. Glossoloma serpens produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or green, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.

Its resilience and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
