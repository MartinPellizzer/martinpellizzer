Goodenia hirsuta

Goodenia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Goodeniaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Goodenia hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
