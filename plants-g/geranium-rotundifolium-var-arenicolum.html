Geranium rotundifolium var. arenicolum

Geranium rotundifolium var. arenicolum, commonly known as sand geranium, is a perennial herb native to sandy soils of the southeastern United States.

It features small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has rounded, fleshy leaves that are adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to xeriscaped gardens.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a valuable ornamental and ecological plant in native plant landscapes.
