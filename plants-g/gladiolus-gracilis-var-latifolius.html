Gladiolus gracilis var. latifolius

Gladiolus gracilis var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf gladiolus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is prized for its elegance and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
