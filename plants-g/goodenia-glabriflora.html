Goodenia glabriflora

Goodenia glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.

Goodenia glabriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
