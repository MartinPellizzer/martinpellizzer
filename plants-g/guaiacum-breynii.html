Guaiacum breynii

Guaiacum breynii, commonly known as the yellow guaiacwood or Caribbean cedar, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves. It produces yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
