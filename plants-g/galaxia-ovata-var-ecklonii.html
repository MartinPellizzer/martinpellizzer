Galaxia ovata var. ecklonii

Galaxia ovata var. ecklonii, commonly known as the Ecklon's galaxia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
