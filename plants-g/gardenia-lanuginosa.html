Gardenia lanuginosa

Gardenia lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky gardenia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain hard, smooth seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Gardenia lanuginosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant scent.
