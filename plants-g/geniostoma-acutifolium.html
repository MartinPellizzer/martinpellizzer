Geniostoma acutifolium

Geniostoma acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved geniostoma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a distinctive acuminate tip.

Geniostoma acutifolium is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
