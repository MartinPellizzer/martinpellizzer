Genianthus laurifolius

Genianthus laurifolius, commonly known as the laurel-leaf genianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which resembles that of laurel plants. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its attractive foliage and vibrant floral displays.
