Gymnocalycium friedrichii

Gymnocalycium friedrichii, commonly known as the Friedrich's moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are typically covered in white or light-colored spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its otherwise muted appearance. Gymnocalycium friedrichii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.
