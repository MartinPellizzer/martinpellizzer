Genista desiderata

Genista desiderata, commonly known as the scarlet broom, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by elongated seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
