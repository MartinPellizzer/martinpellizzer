Gaerdtia argentea

Gaerdtia argentea, commonly known as silverleaf acanthus, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, spiny bracts that enclose clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique foliage and architectural form make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
