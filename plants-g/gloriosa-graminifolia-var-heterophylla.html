Gloriosa graminifolia var. heterophylla

Gloriosa graminifolia var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf glorybower, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its diverse leaf morphology.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can vary in size and shape depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting yellow or orange throats. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a specimen plant in botanical collections.
