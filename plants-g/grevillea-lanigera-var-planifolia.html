Grevillea lanigera var. planifolia

Grevillea lanigera var. planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leafed grevillea, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, flat leaves and bright orange to red flowers that appear in clusters. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
