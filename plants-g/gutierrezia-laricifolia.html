Gutierrezia laricifolia

Gutierrezia laricifolia, commonly known as silverleaf erigeron, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Gutierrezia laricifolia is also valued for its role in supporting pollinators and its potential use in landscaping due to its ornamental qualities.
