Gasteria batesiana var. dolomitica

Gasteria batesiana var. dolomitica, commonly known as the Dolomite Gasteria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
