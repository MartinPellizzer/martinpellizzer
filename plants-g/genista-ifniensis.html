Genista ifniensis

Genista ifniensis, commonly known as the Ifni broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing its resilience in harsh conditions. Its growth habit includes spiny stems and compact foliage, making it well-suited for coastal and mountainous regions.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
