Gardenia rivularis

Gardenia rivularis, commonly known as the stream gardenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments along streams and in forests. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
