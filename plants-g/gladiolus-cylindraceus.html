Gladiolus cylindraceus

Gladiolus cylindraceus, commonly known as the cylindrical gladiolus, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall, sturdy stems with lance-shaped leaves. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. Its inflorescence is a spike of flowers that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
