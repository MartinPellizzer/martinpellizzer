Gynura vaniotii

Gynura vaniotii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or purple-leaf plant, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical, greenish fruits. Gynura vaniotii is known for its ornamental value, particularly due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
