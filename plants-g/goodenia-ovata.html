Goodenia ovata

Goodenia ovata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved goodenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Goodeniaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and often hairy, adapted to the dry conditions of its natural habitat.

Goodenia ovata is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in arid and semi-arid regions.
