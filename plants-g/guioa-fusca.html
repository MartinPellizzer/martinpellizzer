Guioa fusca

Guioa fusca, commonly known as the rough-leaved guioa, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often rough to the touch. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is commonly found in lowland forests.
