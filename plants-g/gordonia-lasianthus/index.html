Gordonia Lasianthus

Gordonia lasianthus is a striking evergreen shrub native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. the plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and partial to full shade. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. it also plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and ecosystems.
