Garcinia diospyrifolia var. minor

Garcinia diospyrifolia var. minor, commonly known as the lesser flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.
