Gentianella propinqua

Gentianella propinqua, commonly known as the slender gentian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Gentianella propinqua is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
