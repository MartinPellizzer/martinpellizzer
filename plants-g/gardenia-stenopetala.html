Gardenia stenopetala

Gardenia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled gardenia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Gardenia stenopetala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.
