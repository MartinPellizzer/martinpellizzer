Gentiana mirandae

Gentiana mirandae, commonly known as the fairy gentian, is a striking perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its deep blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in high-altitude environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Gentiana mirandae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
