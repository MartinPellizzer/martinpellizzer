Gesneria hypoclada

Gesneria hypoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gesneria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are typically tubular and come in shades of pink or red, blooming during the warmer months.

Gesneria hypoclada is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and moderate watering requirements.
