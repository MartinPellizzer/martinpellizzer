Gouldia purpurea

Gouldia purpurea, commonly known as the purple gouldia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its striking purple flowers and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Gouldia purpurea is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
