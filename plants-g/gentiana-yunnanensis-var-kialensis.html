Gentiana yunnanensis var. kialensis

Gentiana yunnanensis var. kialensis, commonly known as the Yunnan gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in high-altitude meadows and rocky habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are usually unbranched and hairy.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its beauty in gardens.
