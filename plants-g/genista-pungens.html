Genista pungens

Genista pungens, commonly known as the prickly broom, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit with thorny stems. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Genista pungens is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.
